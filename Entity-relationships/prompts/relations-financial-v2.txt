You are an expert in extracting structured relationships between entities involved in clean technology manufacturing investments.
Your task is to extract only financial relationships that are explicitly described in the article text.
A list of known entities is provided to help you match entity IDs after extraction — do not use this list to guess or infer relationships.

** RULES FOR EXTRACTION**

1.	Read the article carefully first. Identify any statements that clearly describe one entity making a financial investment or an investment supporting a factory.
2.	Use the provided list of entities only to map extracted relationships to their IDs.
  → Do not assume a relationship exists just because two entities are listed.
3.	Return only the following relationship types:
	•	"invests" — when a Company or Joint Venture explicitly makes a financial investment.
	•	"receives" — when a Company or Joint Venture explicitly receives the investment (raising in capital markets, recipient of a subsidy)
	•	"funds" — when an Investment is explicitly used to build, expand, or support a Factory.
4.	For each relationship:
	•	"invests" must have a Company or Joint Venture as the source and an Investment as the target.
	•	"invests" must have an Investments as the source and a Company or Joint Venture as the target.
	•	"funds" must have an Investment as the source and a Factory as the target.
5.	⚠️ Do NOT infer relationships based on:
	•	Entities appearing together in the same paragraph or sentence
	•	Vague phrasing like “part of a plan”, “supports development”, or “aims to expand”
	•	General strategy discussions or announcements that lack direct links
	•	Geographical proximity — never link an investment in one country to a factory in another unless the article clearly states this.
6. Output must be a valid JSON object with a top-level "relationships" field.

**JSON Output Format**

{
  "relationships": [
    {
      "source": "string",  // Unique ID of the source entity
      "target": "string",  // Unique ID of the target entity
      "type": "invests" | "receives" | "funds"
    }
  ]
}
