# 🌍 General News Search APIs

## [GNews API](https://gnews.io)
- ✅ Simple keyword + region search  
- 🌍 Region and language filters  
- 📅 Date filtering supported  
- 🔑 Free plan (limited), paid tiers for more requests
- ✅ Sample created

---

## [NewsData.io](https://newsdata.io)
- ✅ Advanced filters (country, category, language)  
- 📰 Returns metadata + full content (if available)  
- 📚 Multiple news sources globally  
- 🔑 Free & paid plans 
- ✅ Sample created

---

## [NewsCatcher API](https://newscatcherapi.com)
- 🧠 AI-powered deduplication and relevance sorting  
- 🌍 Localized content support  
- 🔁 Real-time and archive search  
- 📊 Free plan for devs, full-text in paid  
- ❌Requires a business email for account

---

## [ContextualWeb News API (via RapidAPI)](https://rapidapi.com/contextualwebsearch/api/web-search)
- 🚀 Fast and simple  
- 🧪 Web + news + image search  
- 🔍 Basic filtering options  
- 🆓 Free through RapidAPI with limited quota 
- ❌Requires booking a demo

---

## [Bing News Search API (Microsoft Azure)](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/bing/search-apis/bing-news-search)
- 🤖 Good metadata (publisher, snippet, etc.)  
- 📅 Sort by freshness  
- 💵 Requires Azure subscription  

---

# 🧠 AI & NLP-Enhanced News APIs

## [AYLIEN News API](https://newsapi.aylien.com/)
- 🧠 NLP features like sentiment, entity extraction  
- 📅 Historical archive + real-time updates  
- 🔍 Filter by category, sentiment, entities, etc.  
- 💰 Paid, but powerful for large-scale workflows  

---

## [Webz.io (formerly Webhose)](https://webz.io)
- 🕵️ Huge web-scale crawler  
- 📰 News, blogs, dark web, forums, etc.  
- 💰 Premium only, but highly customizable  

---

# 🔐 Free + Lightweight Options (Good for Prototyping)

## [MediaStack API](https://mediastack.com/)
- 🌍 Global news with language/region filters  
- ⏳ Basic real-time functionality  
- ✅ Good for keyword search and sorting  
- ✅ Sample created
---

## [NewsAPI.org](https://newsapi.org)
- 🌍 Global + regional news sources  
- ❌ Does not provide full content (just metadata)  
- ✅ Fast, easy to integrate  
- 🔑 Free for development; limited by source licenses  
- ✅ Sample created
---
