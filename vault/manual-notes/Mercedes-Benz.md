#### Vehicle plants

###### Bremen (Germany)
- Began producing passenger cars in 1978. 11,500 employees. 
- Has produced plug-in hybrids and electric vehicles for several years. 
###### Rastatt (Germany)
- Began producion in 1992. 6,100 employees. 
- EVs on the same assembly line as conventional. 
##### Sindelfingen (Germany)
- Founded in 1915. 21,500 employees.
- Manufactures all vehicle types.
###### Kecskemét (Hungary)
- Opened in 2012. 4,500 employees. 
- EVs on the same assembly line as vehicles with conventional drives
