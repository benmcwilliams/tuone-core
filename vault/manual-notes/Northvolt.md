Skelleftea, Sweden is the battery gigafactory (also called Northvolt Ett). This does both cathode active material and cell production
- Revolt Ett is next door which is battery recycling.

Vasteras, Sweden is the R&D facility. 

Gdansk, Poland is the battery assembly site. 

Heide, Germany. Northvolt Drei - initiative to produce green batteries. 

Gothenburg, Sweden. Battery cells tailor made for Volvo.
