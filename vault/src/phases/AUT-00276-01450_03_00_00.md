## AUT-00276-01450

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Andritz Hydro Facility in Dubai, AUT

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** August 2019
   - **Details:** The facility is part of the Hatta hydroelectric power plant project, which will produce 250 MW of hydropower using pumped storage technology. The design allows for water to be stored in an upper reservoir at the Hatta Dam, which will be reused to generate power. The project is notable as the first of its kind in the Gulf region and is designed to last at least 80 years.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** August 2019
   - **Details:** The Dubai Electricity and Water Authority (DEWA) awarded a construction contract valued at Dh1.437 billion ($391 million) for the Hatta hydroelectric project. This investment is part of the Dubai Clean Energy Strategy 2050, aiming to increase the share of clean energy in Dubai's power generation.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** August 2019
   - **Details:** The construction contract for the Hatta hydroelectric power plant was awarded to a consortium that includes Andritz Hydro. The project is scheduled to be commissioned in early 2024.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** August 2019
   - **Details:** DEWA is supporting job creation for UAE nationals as part of the project, which is also linked to the development of tourist facilities in the Hatta area.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** August 2019
   - **Details:** The facility is part of a consortium that includes Andritz Hydro, STRABAG Dubai LLC, STRABAG AG, and Ozkar. Therefore, Andritz Hydro does not own the facility exclusively but is part of a joint venture for the project. 

This timeline captures the key developments related to the Andritz Hydro facility in Dubai, focusing on its technology, investment, project milestones, labor implications, and ownership structure.
