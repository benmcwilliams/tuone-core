### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fisker Facility in Graz, Austria

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2022**: Production of the Fisker Ocean electric SUV commenced at Magna's facility in Graz, Austria. The initial production plan included 300 units for Q1 2023, ramping up to 42,400 units by the end of the year.
- **August 2022**: Fisker announced plans to potentially increase production capacity at Magna beyond the initially planned 50,000 units per year starting in 2024, due to high demand for the Ocean SUV.
- **February 2023**: As of late February, 65,000 reservations for the Fisker Ocean had been recorded, with 56 units produced by that time.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2024**: Fisker announced significant price reductions for the 2023 model year of the Fisker Ocean, indicating financial struggles and a need to boost sales.
- **April 2024**: The company warned of potential bankruptcy, indicating a dire financial situation that could impact future investments and operations.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 17, 2022**: Official start of production for the Fisker Ocean at Magna in Graz.
- **July 2022**: Fisker announced that the introductory edition of the Ocean, called Fisker Ocean One, was sold out within 30 days of pre-sale.
- **February 2022**: Fisker presented the Ocean in Europe for the first time at the Mobile World Congress, revealing pricing and specifications.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2024**: Fisker reported a reduction in workforce, with 1,135 employees remaining, down from 1,560 at the end of December 2023. This indicates job losses as a result of the company's financial struggles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Fisker does not own the Graz facility exclusively; production is carried out by Magna, a contract manufacturer. Fisker operates with a lean business model, focusing on design and development while outsourcing manufacturing.

This timeline captures the key developments and challenges faced by the Fisker facility in Graz, Austria, highlighting its production capabilities, financial status, and operational strategies.
