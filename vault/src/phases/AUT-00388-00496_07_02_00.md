### Evolutionary Timeline of the Magna Facility in Graz, Austria

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **11/2016**: Magna Steyr confirmed it will manufacture the electric SUV I-Pace for Jaguar Land Rover at its Graz facility.
- **03/2019**: Magna Steyr is in talks to produce electric vehicles for Evelozcity, a startup planning to launch three electric models starting in 2021.
- **10/2020**: Magna announced a partnership with Fisker to manufacture the electric SUV Ocean, with production scheduled to start in Q4 2022.
- **11/2022**: Production of the Fisker Ocean commenced at Magna in Graz, with plans for 50,000 units in the first full production year.
- **04/2023**: Magna announced plans to manufacture an electric SUV for Ineos Automotive, with production set to begin in 2026.
- **11/2023**: Magna confirmed it will develop two electric models for Volkswagen’s US brand Scout, with production expected to start by late 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **10/2020**: Magna's partnership with Fisker includes a significant investment in developing a new electric vehicle architecture for the Fisker Ocean.
- **11/2023**: The contract with Volkswagen for developing electric models for Scout is valued at 450 million euros.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **11/2016**: Announcement of Magna's role in producing the Jaguar I-Pace.
- **03/2019**: Letter of intent signed with Evelozcity for potential vehicle production.
- **10/2020**: Announcement of the Fisker Ocean production partnership.
- **11/2022**: Official start of production for the Fisker Ocean.
- **04/2023**: Announcement of future production for Ineos Automotive.
- **11/2023**: Confirmation of the contract with Volkswagen for Scout models.

#### 4. Labour
- **11/2022**: The start of production for the Fisker Ocean is expected to create jobs, although specific numbers were not disclosed.
- **04/2023**: The announcement of new projects (Ineos and Scout) suggests potential job creation, but exact figures are not provided.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Magna Steyr operates as a contract manufacturer, indicating that it does not own the brands it produces for (e.g., Fisker, Jaguar Land Rover, Ineos, Volkswagen). The facility in Graz is utilized for multiple partnerships, showcasing a collaborative ownership model rather than exclusive ownership.
