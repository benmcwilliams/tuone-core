## AUT-01350-00589

### Evolutionary Timeline of the OMV Facility in Vienna, Austria

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2023**: OMV and Wien Energie formed a joint venture to explore, develop, and utilize deep geothermal energy in the Greater Vienna area. The first deep geothermal plant is planned for Aspern, with a production capacity of up to 20 MW of climate-neutral district heating, aimed at supplying up to 20,000 households.
- **07/2023**: The joint venture, named “deeep,” is established to decarbonize district heating in Vienna. The partners plan to implement up to seven deep geothermal plants in the districts of Donaustadt and Simmering, with the first plant in Aspern scheduled to go into operation in 2027.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07/2023**: The joint investment for the planning of the deep geothermal projects is approximately EUR 20 million. This investment is tied to the development of the first geothermal plant in Aspern and the subsequent projects that will follow based on the learnings from this pilot plant.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/2023**: The joint venture aims to build the first deep geothermal plant in Aspern by 2026, with drilling expected to start towards the end of 2024. The plant is scheduled to commence operations in 2027.
- **07/2023**: The partners have already explored and proven the geothermal potential in the eastern Vienna Basin as part of the “GeoTief Wien” research project, which supports the foundation for the joint venture.

#### 4. Labour
- **07/2023**: The establishment of the joint venture and the planned geothermal projects are expected to create jobs in the region, although specific job creation numbers have not been detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **07/2023**: The facility is part of a joint venture between OMV and Wien Energie, indicating that it is not exclusively owned by OMV but rather a collaborative effort between the two companies. 

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic initiatives related to the OMV facility in Vienna, Austria, focusing on deep geothermal energy production and the collaborative efforts with Wien Energie.
