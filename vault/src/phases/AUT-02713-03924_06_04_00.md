## AUT-02713-03924

### Evolutionary Timeline of Energetica Industries Facility in Liebenfels, Austria

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2018**: Energetica Industries announces the establishment of a gigawatt-scale solar module factory in Liebenfels, Austria. The facility is expected to produce high-efficiency solar PV modules with a production capacity exceeding 1 GW per year. The factory will utilize fully automated production lines and aims to be a zero-emission facility by using a 2.7 MW PV plus storage system and waste heat.
- **November 2019**: Manufacturing begins at the facility. The first high-performance modules are produced on September 17, 2019. The facility is designed to produce powerful, intelligent, and visually appealing high-end photovoltaic modules.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2018**: Energetica Industries invests in the construction of the factory in the two-digit million range. Economic development funds from FFG, KWF, and AIT Austria are involved in bringing Energetica’s technologies to market maturity.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2018**: Groundbreaking for the gigawatt-scale solar module fab in Liebenfels is announced.
- **September 2019**: Production starts at the facility, with the first modules running off the production line.
- **October 2021**: Energetica files for insolvency, indicating financial difficulties and the inability to meet payment obligations. The company aims to restructure and continue operations despite the insolvency proceedings.

#### 4. Labour
- **November 2019**: Approximately 100 employees are reported to be working at the facility during the initial production phase.
- **October 2021**: The insolvency proceedings affect 112 employees, as the company seeks to restructure and optimize production costs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The articles do not specify any joint ventures or partnerships; therefore, it is assumed that Energetica Industries GmbH owns the facility exclusively.
