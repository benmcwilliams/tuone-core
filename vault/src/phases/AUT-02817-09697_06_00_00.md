### Evolutionary Timeline of the PVI GmbH Facility in Cres, AUT

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2020
   - **Details:** The facility, known as the SE Cres solar power plant, is designed to have a production capacity of 6.5 MW. It will consist of 17 sub-units, each with a capacity of 500 kW, utilizing a total of 20,330 PV panels supplied by Croatian manufacturer Solvis d.o.o. The plant is strategically located on a 17-hectare plot to ensure sufficient electricity supply during the peak tourist season.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2020
   - **Details:** The project is part of HEP's broader plan to expand its solar power generation portfolio, which includes an investment of approximately HRK 80 million ($11.8 million) for the initial four PV plants with a combined capacity of 11.6 MW. HEP plans to invest HRK 150 million annually in solar projects until 2023, aiming for a total investment of around HRK 750 million and annual capacity additions of about 20 MW.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** June 2020
   - **Details:** Construction of the SE Cres solar power plant has begun, with commercial operations scheduled to start by the end of 2020. The project was awarded to a consortium led by PVI GmbH through a tender process.
