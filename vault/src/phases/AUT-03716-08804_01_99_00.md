### Enerox Facility Timeline - Austria, AUT

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Enerox facility, trading as CellCube, specializes in the production of vanadium flow batteries (VFBs). 
   - The facility is involved in the development of a 100 MW battery energy storage system (BESS) that can operate for 4 and 8 hours, utilizing VFB technology.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The facility is part of Project Lumina, which has received significant attention and investment for its potential in the energy storage market. 
   - The project aims to deliver a competitive levelized cost of storage (LCOS) of $274 (USD) / MWh for a 4-hour VFB BESS and $251 / MWh for an 8-hour VFB BESS.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
