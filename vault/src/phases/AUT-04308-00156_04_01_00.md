1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 08-05-2023
   - **Details:** The facility is involved in the production of hydrogen through a 2 MW proton-exchange-membrane electrolyzer provided by Cummins. The electrolyzer will initially operate off baseload power to fill a former natural gas reservoir with hydrogen, with a storage capacity of 1.2 million cubic meters (equivalent to 4.2 GWh of power).
