### Evolutionary Timeline of the RAG Facility in Rubensdorf, Austria

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 08-05-2023
   - **Details:** RAG launched the world’s first underground hydrogen storage pilot at a former natural gas reservoir in Rubensdorf. The facility will store 1.2 million cubic meters of hydrogen, equivalent to 4.2 GWh of power. The hydrogen will be produced using a 2 MW proton-exchange-membrane electrolyzer provided by Cummins. The project aims to demonstrate the technical and economic feasibility of underground hydrogen storage, with a total site capacity of up to 12 million cubic meters.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 08-05-2023
   - **Details:** The pilot project is supported by the Austrian Federal Ministry for Climate Action, Environment, Energy, Mobility, Innovation, and Technology. It is also part of the European Commission’s Hydrogen Strategy, which aims to establish a hydrogen economy in Europe.

   - **Date:** 08-05-2023
