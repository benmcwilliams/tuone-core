### Evolutionary Timeline of INNIO Facility in Jenbach, Austria

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2023**: INNIO ordered two electrolyzers with a total capacity of 2 MW from H-TEC SYSTEMS for its hydrogen production plant in Jenbach. This facility aims to convert green electricity into green hydrogen (H2) as part of its power-to-hydrogen-to-power project. The electrolyzers will produce up to 900 kg of green hydrogen daily, sufficient to power the 2 MW Jenbacher H2 engine test bench. The entire Jenbacher engine product line is expected to be ready for 100% hydrogen operation by 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2023**: The investment in the two electrolyzers represents a significant step in advancing INNIO's sustainable hydrogen product strategy. The construction of the 2 MW system will take place in a container design at the Achensee power plant of TIWAG - Tiroler Wasserkraft AG, with necessary compressor and storage terminals being built by TINEXT.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
