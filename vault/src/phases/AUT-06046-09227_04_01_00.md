### Evolutionary Timeline of the Innio Group Facility in Gampern, Austria

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2024**: Innio Group commissioned a 1 MW hydrogen combined heat and power (CHP) plant at RAG Austria's site in Gampern. This facility is the first of its kind in Europe and is designed to provide green electricity and heat for the industrial plant. The Jenbacher CHP plant utilizes green hydrogen produced through water electrolysis during the summer months, which is then stored in an underground hydrogen storage facility in Rubensdorf, Austria. The storage capacity is up to 4.2 GWh of surplus solar power converted to hydrogen.
