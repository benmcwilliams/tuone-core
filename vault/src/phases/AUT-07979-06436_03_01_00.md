### Evolutionary Timeline of the Andritz Facility in Ebensee, Austria

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2023
   - **Details:** Andritz has been commissioned to provide electromechanical equipment for the Ebensee pumped storage power plant. The facility will feature a 170MW variable-speed reversible pump turbine, a generator, and associated automation. The plant is designed to store excess energy by pumping water from Traunsee lake to a mountain reservoir located 500m higher at Grosser Sonnstein. It is projected to provide green electricity to approximately 280,000 households once operational.

   - **Details:** This project represents the largest single investment by Upper Austrian utility Energie AG, aimed at advancing climate neutrality in the region.
