### Evolutionary Timeline of the EVN Facility in Grafenworth, Austria

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: The EVN facility in Grafenworth, in collaboration with ECOwind (a subsidiary of BayWa), completed the installation of a 24MW floating photovoltaic (PV) plant. This facility is noted as the largest floating PV plant in Austria and Central Europe, covering approximately 14 hectares on the water surface of a former sand and gravel pit. The plant is designed to produce 26,700 MWh of green electricity annually, sufficient to supply around 7,500 Austrian households.
