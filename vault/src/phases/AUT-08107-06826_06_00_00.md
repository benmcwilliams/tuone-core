#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: The ECOwind facility, a subsidiary of BayWa, launched a 24MW floating photovoltaic (PV) plant in Grafenworth, Lower Austria. This facility is noted as the largest floating PV plant in Austria and Central Europe, and the fourth largest in Europe overall. The floating PV system spans approximately 14 hectares and is situated on the water surface of a former sand and gravel pit across two lakes. It is designed to produce 26,700 MWh of green electricity annually, sufficient to supply around 7,500 Austrian households.
