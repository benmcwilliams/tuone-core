- **March 2021**: Announcement of the construction of the Limberg III pumped storage power plant in Kaprun, designed to support the energy transition towards a CO2-free power supply. The facility will be built underground between the existing reservoirs of Mooserboden and Wasserfallboden. Limberg III is expected to enhance energy efficiency and provide significant storage capacity for peak electricity consumption.
- **July 2023**: Excavation work for Limberg III is nearing completion, with the plant scheduled to be operational by 2025. The facility aims to store power to address peaks in electricity consumption and adapt to changing weather patterns, including irregular rainfall.
