### Evolutionary Timeline of VERBUND Hydro Power GmbH Facility in Kaprun, Austria

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2022:** The facility is in the process of constructing the Limberg III pumped storage power plant with a production capacity of 480 MW. This plant is part of the Kaprun power plant group and is designed to store electricity efficiently during surplus times and feed it back into the electricity system when required. The commissioning of the plant is planned for 2025.
