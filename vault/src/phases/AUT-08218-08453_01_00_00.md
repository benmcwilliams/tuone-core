#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2023**: Burgerland Energie began construction of a 300 MWh storage project utilizing the “Organic SolidFlow” technology developed by CMBlu. This technology employs high-performance organic energy storage molecules derived from lignin, a by-product from pulp mills.
