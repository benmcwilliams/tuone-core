### Evolutionary Timeline of Enerox Holdings Limited Facility at Vametco Alloys Mine, South Africa

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2020
   - **Details:** Enerox Holdings Limited is providing a vanadium redox flow storage system for a solar micro-grid at the Vametco Alloys mine. The system will have a capacity of 1 MW/4 MWh and is part of a larger 3.5 MW solar micro-grid project. This project aims to supply nearly 10% of the mine's electrical energy consumption and demonstrates the capability of hybrid mini-grids using solar PV and vanadium redox flow battery (VRFB) technology.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2020
   - **Details:** The Vametco hybrid mini-grid project is structured as a separately funded independent power producer, which will sell electrical energy to the mine. This project is significant as it is one of the first solar generation projects with long-duration storage to be financed off-balance sheet in Africa.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2020
   - **Details:** The project was announced and is noted as the world's largest hybrid solar plant incorporating vanadium redox flow storage. The installation is designed to operate independently or as part of a fully functional mini-grid.

5. **Ownership Structure:**

   - **Details:** Enerox Holdings Limited is a unit of Bushveld Minerals, which indicates that the facility is not exclusively owned by Enerox but is part of a larger corporate structure under Bushveld Minerals.
