## BEL-00138-00799

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Umicore Facility in Brussels, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2021**: Umicore announced a research and development collaboration with Anglo American to develop PGM-based catalysts for liquid organic hydrogen carrier (LOHC) applications, which could enhance hydrogen storage and usage in fuel cell electric vehicles (FCEVs).
- **September 2022**: Umicore and Volkswagen's battery subsidiary PowerCo established a joint venture for the production of cathode and precursor materials in Europe. The joint venture aims to produce materials sufficient for 160 GWh of battery cell capacity per year, equating to around 2.2 million fully electric vehicles.
- **March 2023**: The joint venture received all regulatory approvals and is set to begin preparations for large-scale industrial production of cathode materials and precursor materials in Europe.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2022**: The joint venture is expected to invest €3 billion in developing and producing cathodes and other primary materials.
- **March 2023**: The joint venture aims to cover a significant portion of PowerCo's EU requirements for cathode materials, which are crucial for battery performance and cost.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2021**: Announcement of the collaboration with Anglo American for catalyst development.
- **September 2022**: Establishment of the joint venture with PowerCo, with plans to start production in 2025.
- **March 2023**: Receipt of regulatory approvals for the joint venture, allowing for swift preparations for production.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2022**: The joint venture is expected to create jobs related to the production of cathode materials, although specific job creation numbers have not been disclosed.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **September 2022**: The facility operates as a joint venture between Umicore and PowerCo, indicating shared ownership rather than exclusive ownership by Umicore.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Umicore facility in Brussels, focusing on its technological advancements, investments, project statuses, labor implications, and ownership structure.
