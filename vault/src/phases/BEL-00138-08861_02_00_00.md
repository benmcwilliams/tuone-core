- **Date: 20-08-2024**: The European Heat Pump Association is involved in the GEOFLEXheat project, which aims to harness waste geothermal heat for industrial use. This project will develop a system that combines a heat pipe heat exchanger, a high-temperature heat pump, and thermal energy storage to utilize low-temperature geothermal brine efficiently.

#### 2. Investment and Development

- **Date: 20-08-2024**: The GEOFLEXheat project is set to start on 1 October 2024 and will run for three years. It aims to demonstrate the potential applications of geothermal heat extraction in various industrial sectors.
