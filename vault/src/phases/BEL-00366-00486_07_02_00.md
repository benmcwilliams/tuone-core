### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lynk & Co Facility in Ghent, Belgium

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2018:** Lynk & Co announced that production of their second model, the 02, will take place in Belgium. The model is a crossover SUV that will be available in Europe as a Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) starting in 2020, with a purely electric version to follow. The facility will also produce the Lynk & Co 01 and the Volvo XC40, utilizing a shortened, broader, and lowered version of the CMA platform.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **March 2018:** Volvo purchased 30% of shares in Lynk & Co, indicating a significant investment and a commitment to cooperative production in Ghent.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 2018:** It was confirmed that Lynk & Co models will be produced in Europe at the Ghent factory by the end of 2019. The facility was selected over other potential locations, including Charleston, USA.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **March 2018:** The facility is not exclusively owned by Lynk & Co; it is part of a cooperative production arrangement with Volvo, which holds a 30% stake in Lynk & Co.
