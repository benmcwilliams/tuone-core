### Evolutionary Timeline of the Derbigum Facility in Ghent, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2010**: Derbigum integrated 1.87MW of Energy Conversion Devices' (ECD) Uni-Solar amorphous-silicon thin-film photovoltaic (PV) laminates on the rooftop of the Flanders Expo in Ghent. This installation is part of a building-integrated PV (BIPV) solution known as Derbisolar, which combines a durable roofing membrane with solar technology. The system is expected to produce approximately 1.5 million kWh of clean energy annually, sufficient for around 454 families.
