### Evolutionary Timeline of the Engie Facility in Hoboken, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2020**: The Engie Solutions and Iemants joint venture began the fabrication of the topside for the 1.5GW Hollandse Kust Noord offshore wind farm. The topside will undergo final assembly at the Engie Solutions yard in Hoboken, scheduled for February 2021. Outfitting, commissioning, and testing are planned from June 2021 to May 2022, with offshore installation set for mid-2022.
- **June 2021**: The joint venture commenced cutting the first steel for the topside of the offshore transformer station for the Hollandse Kust West Alpha wind farm. The assembly of the topside will begin in July 2021, with final outfitting, commissioning, and testing scheduled from March 2022 until summer 2023, followed by offshore installation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2020**: The contract for the Hollandse Kust Noord offshore transformer station was awarded to the Engie Solutions and Iemants joint venture by TenneT in March 2020. This contract includes engineering, procurement, construction, offshore installation, connection, and testing.
- **June 2021**: The contract for the Hollandse Kust West Alpha project was also awarded to the same joint venture, indicating ongoing investment in offshore wind infrastructure.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2020**: The first steel cutting ceremony for the Hollandse Kust Noord topside was held, marking the start of fabrication.
- **February 2021**: Final assembly of the topside for Hollandse Kust Noord is scheduled to take place at the Engie Solutions yard in Hoboken.
- **June 2021**: The first steel for the Hollandse Kust West Alpha topside was cut, marking another significant milestone in the project timeline.
