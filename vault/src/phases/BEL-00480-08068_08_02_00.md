### Evolutionary Timeline of the Smulders Facility in Hoboken, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2020**: Smulders, in partnership with Sif, was awarded a contract to manufacture 190 monopiles and transition pieces for the Dogger Bank A and B offshore wind farm project, with production scheduled to start in May 2021 and assembly in September 2021 at the Hoboken facility.
- **June 2021**: The facility began cutting the first steel for the topside of the offshore transformer station for the Hollandse Kust West Alpha wind farm, marking the start of fabrication for this project.
- **July 2024**: Smulders secured reservation agreements for the manufacturing of 100 transition pieces for the Bałtyk II and Bałtyk III offshore wind projects in Poland, with final assembly to take place at the Hoboken facility.
- **November 2024**: Production of the last transition piece for the Dogger Bank Wind Farm commenced, with Smulders having delivered 276 of the 277 transition pieces required for the project.
