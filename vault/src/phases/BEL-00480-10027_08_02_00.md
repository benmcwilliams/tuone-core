- **November 2020**: Smulders Projects Belgium, in a consortium with Sif Netherlands, was awarded a contract to manufacture all monopiles and transition pieces for the Dogger Bank A & B offshore wind farm project. The facility in Hoboken is responsible for the assembly, coating, and testing of the transition pieces. Production at the Hoboken facility is scheduled to start in September 2021 and will take approximately ten months.
