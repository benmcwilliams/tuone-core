### Evolutionary Timeline of Punch Powertrain PSA e-transmissions Facility in Sint-Truiden, Belgium

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **September 2020:** The Punch Powertrain PSA e-transmissions facility officially commenced operations on September 17, 2020. The facility produces the DT2 dual-clutch transmission Hybrid, designed for mild and plug-in hybrids, utilizing 48V and High Voltage hybrid infrastructures for full integration and optimization of the hybrid system.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **September 2020:** The joint venture was established following official approvals, with Punch Powertrain holding a 61% majority stake. The initial agreement included the contribution of Punch Powertrain's DT2-related business unit to the new entity.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **September 2020:** The facility began operations, marking the official start of production for the DT2 transmission. This facility is part of a broader strategy, as it follows the establishment of the 'Punch Powertrain PSA e-transmissions assembly' in Metz, France, earlier in July 2020.

4. **Labour:**
   - **September 2020:** The facility commenced operations with over 300 employees, who were transferred from Punch Powertrain.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **September 2020:** The facility operates as a joint venture between Punch Powertrain and the PSA Group, with Punch Powertrain holding a 61% majority stake in the new company.
