### Evolutionary Timeline of the Parkwind Facility in Zeebrugge, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2020**: The Northwester 2 offshore wind farm, operated by Parkwind, became fully operational. It has a production capacity of 219 MW, consisting of 23 Vestas (MHI Vestas) V164-9.5 MW wind turbines connected to Elia’s Modular Offshore Grid (MOG).

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2021**: RWE signed a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with ASML for the supply of over 250 gigawatt-hours of green electricity annually. A portion of this electricity will be sourced from the Northwester 2 offshore wind farm.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2021**: RWE announced the PPA with ASML, confirming that the Northwester 2 offshore wind farm is part of the agreement. This agreement is part of ASML's commitment to source 100% of its energy from renewable sources by 2025.
