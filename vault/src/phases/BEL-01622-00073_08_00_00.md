### Evolutionary Timeline of the BASF Facility in Antwerp, Belgium

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2021
   - **Details:** BASF is set to benefit from the Hollandse Kust Zuid (HKZ) offshore wind project, which will host 140 wind turbines and is expected to be the largest offshore wind farm globally upon completion. The facility in Antwerp will utilize renewable electricity generated from this wind farm, significantly enhancing its operations.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2021
   - **Details:** BASF has committed approximately €1.6 billion towards the HKZ project, which includes a purchase of a 49.5% stake for €300 million. This investment marks BASF's first major foray into renewable power facilities, aimed at securing significant volumes of renewable electricity for its operations.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** Offshore construction of the HKZ wind farm is scheduled to commence in July 2021, with the project expected to become fully operational in 2023. The transaction for BASF's stake is anticipated to close in the fourth quarter of 2021, pending regulatory approvals.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
