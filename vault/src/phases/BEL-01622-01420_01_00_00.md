### Evolutionary Timeline of TotalEnergies Facility in Antwerp, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: TotalEnergies launched a 25MW/75MWh battery energy storage project at its Antwerp refinery. This facility will consist of 40 Intensium Max High Energy lithium-ion battery containers supplied by Saft, TotalEnergies' battery subsidiary. The project is designed to help smooth power fluctuations in the national grid, guarantee grid security, and allow for greater integration of renewable electricity. It is set to be operational by the end of 2024.
