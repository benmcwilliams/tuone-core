### Evolutionary Timeline of the Engie Facility in Antwerp, BEL

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: March 2021
   - **Details**: The facility is involved in a pilot project where hydrogen is used in a commercial-scale cogeneration plant at the INEOS Phenol site in Doel. The initial plan is to replace 10% of the natural gas feed with hydrogen, with a potential increase to 20% based on the success of the pilot. This project marks the first industrial-scale tests of hydrogen usage in Belgium, aimed at reducing CO₂ emissions and transitioning towards climate-neutral energy.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: March 2021
   - **Details**: The project is part of a broader roadmap defined by INEOS to achieve climate neutrality by 2050 and reduce emissions by 55% by 2030 compared to 1990 levels. This includes investments in electrification, the use of recycled or bio-based raw materials, and renewable energy.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: March 2021
   - **Details**: The pilot project was announced, marking a significant milestone in the energy transition of the chemical industry. ENGIE is responsible for the design, installation, and operation of the hydrogen technology at the INEOS site.
