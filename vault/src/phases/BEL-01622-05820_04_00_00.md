### Evolutionary Timeline of the Port of Antwerp Facility, Antwerp, BEL

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: The Port of Antwerp is focusing on becoming a hub for green hydrogen, collaborating with the Port of Zeebrugge and the Chilean government to facilitate the import of green hydrogen produced in Chile.
- **Capacity and Infrastructure**: The facility aims to establish a logistics chain for hydrogen distribution between South America and Western Europe, leveraging its infrastructure as a major energy hub.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Collaborative Agreement**: On November 4, 2021, a memorandum of understanding (MoU) was signed between the Port of Antwerp, Port of Zeebrugge, and the Chilean energy ministry during COP26 in Glasgow. This agreement aims to explore and develop the logistics and infrastructure necessary for green hydrogen flows.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **MoU Signing**: The MoU was signed on November 4, 2021, marking a significant step towards establishing a green hydrogen corridor between Chile and Belgium.
- **Future Plans**: The parties involved are committed to removing barriers to the effective start-up of green hydrogen production in Chile and establishing a logistics chain for its distribution.

#### 4. Labour
