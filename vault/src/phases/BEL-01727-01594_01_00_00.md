### EStor-Lux Facility Timeline - Bastogne, Belgium

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** Mid-2021
   - **Details:** The EStor-Lux facility in Bastogne is a 10MW/20MWh battery energy storage system. It is the largest battery energy storage project in Belgium and the first of its kind in the Walloon region. The facility operates as a virtual power plant, providing environmentally friendly backup to the grid and enabling the storage of renewable energy for use during peak demand.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** Announced on 12-01-2021
   - **Details:** The project is a partnership between UK-based Centrica Business Solutions and Belgian company EStor-Lux. Centrica played a significant role in the feasibility of the project, contributing to its development and operational strategy.
