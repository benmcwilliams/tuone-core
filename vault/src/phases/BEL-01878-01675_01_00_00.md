### Evolutionary Timeline of the Giga Storage Facility in Dilsen-Stokkem, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2024**: Giga Storage announces the development of the GIGA Green Turtle battery park in Dilsen-Stokkem. The facility will feature 20 batteries, each with an inverter, 185 medium voltage transformers, and five high-voltage transformers with a total capacity of 1,500 MVA. The facility is designed to store the average energy consumption of 330,000 families annually and will feed this energy back into the electricity grid.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2024**: The project is positioned adjacent to a new 380 kV high-voltage station run by Elia, indicating strategic investment in infrastructure to support the battery storage facility. Giga Storage aims to deliver 5 GW of Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) projects in Europe by 2030, highlighting a broader investment strategy in energy storage.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2024**: Approval for the GIGA Green Turtle project is secured. Construction is expected to start in **2025**, with an estimated completion date in **2028**. This timeline marks significant milestones in the project’s development.
