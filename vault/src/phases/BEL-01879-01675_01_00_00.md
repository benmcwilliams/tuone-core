### Evolutionary Timeline of the Giga Storage Facility in Kinrooi, Belgium

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **April 2024:** Giga Storage announced plans for a new battery storage project named GIGA Green Turtle, which will feature 20 batteries, each with an inverter, 185 medium voltage transformers, and five high-voltage transformers with a total capacity of 1,500 MVA. The facility aims to store energy equivalent to the average consumption of 330,000 families annually and will feed this energy back into the electricity grid.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **April 2024:** The project is positioned adjacent to a new 380 kV high-voltage station run by Elia, indicating significant infrastructure investment. The facility is part of Giga Storage's broader goal to deliver 5 GW of Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) projects in Europe by 2030.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **April 2024:** The project has secured approval, with construction expected to start in 2025 and estimated completion in 2028. This facility is set to be the largest battery storage project in Europe.

4. **Labour:**
   - **April 2024:** Specific job creation or job loss details have not been mentioned in the article, but the scale of the project suggests potential job creation during the construction and operational phases.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **April 2024:** Giga Storage is the developer and owner of the facility, having acquired the land from the previous owners of a zinc factory, indicating full ownership of the GIGA Green Turtle project.
