### Evolutionary Timeline of Jan De Nul Group Facility in Eemshaven, BEL

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2024**: Jan De Nul Group has been contracted for various transportation and installation activities for foundations and turbines related to the OranjeWind offshore wind project. The facility will support the offloading, storage, handling, and pre-assembly of major components, including 53 turbines provided by Vestas, each with a capacity of 15 megawatts (MW). The project is expected to generate around 3 terawatt hours of green electricity annually, sufficient to supply over one million Dutch households.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2024**: The Buss Terminal Eemshaven is designated as the construction base for the OranjeWind project, which will utilize a marshalling area of 35-40 hectares. This facility will also support concurrent projects, including the Nordseecluster B.

- **December 2024**: RWE and TotalEnergies announce the selection of Eemshaven as the base for the OranjeWind project. Construction activities are set to begin in early 2026, with turbine installation scheduled for early 2027 and full commissioning expected by the end of 2027.
