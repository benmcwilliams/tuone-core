### Evolutionary Timeline of the RWE Facility in North Sea, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2022**: RWE, in partnership with SolarDuck, announced the construction of the Merganser project, a pilot offshore floating solar plant with a capacity of **500 kW**. This facility is designed to operate in the challenging offshore environment of the North Sea, specifically off the coast of Ostend, Belgium. The project aims to provide valuable experience for future commercial applications of floating solar technology.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2022**: RWE committed to investing in the Merganser pilot project to accelerate the development and commercialization of SolarDuck’s offshore floating solar technology. This investment is part of RWE's broader strategy to explore innovative renewable energy solutions.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2022**: The Merganser project was officially announced, with expectations for it to go online in **2023**. The project is positioned as a pilot to gather insights and data from one of the most challenging offshore environments, which will facilitate faster commercialization of the technology.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- RWE and SolarDuck are collaborating on the Merganser project, indicating a **joint venture** structure for this facility. RWE is not the sole owner but is partnering with SolarDuck to develop and implement the technology.
