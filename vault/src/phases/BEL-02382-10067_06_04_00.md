### Evolutionary Timeline of the Soltech NV Facility in North Sea, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2019**: The facility is part of a consortium developing the first offshore photovoltaic (PV) floating project in the North Sea. The project includes a €2 million pilot floating solar array designed to withstand harsh offshore conditions, such as salty water, strong currents, and high waves. Soltech NV is responsible for developing marine-suitable PV panels.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2019**: The project is supported by the Agency for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (VLAIO), a Flemish governmental organization, and the Blue Cluster, which promotes economic activities linked to the North Sea.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2019**: Announcement of the pilot project, which is a collaboration between Tractebel, Jan De Nul Group, DEME, Soltech NV, and Ghent University. The project is planned to be located near an aquaculture and an offshore wind power facility.
