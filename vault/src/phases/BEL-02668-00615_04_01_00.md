### Evolutionary Timeline of the Plug Power Facility in Ostend, Belgium

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 01-07-2023
   - **Details:** Plug Power is set to design and deliver a 10-megawatt proton exchange membrane (PEM) electrolyzer system for the HOPE (Hydrogen Offshore Production Europe) project. The facility aims to produce up to four tons of green hydrogen (H2) per day, which will be sent via pipeline to the shore for compression and distribution to consumers in Belgium, the southern Netherlands, and northern France. The facility will utilize renewable energy under power purchase agreements (PPA) and will source water directly from the North Sea for the electrolyzer.

   - **Date:** 01-07-2023
   - **Details:** The HOPE project received a $21.8 million grant from the European Commission to support the construction of the offshore hydrogen production plant. This funding is crucial for the project's development and implementation.
