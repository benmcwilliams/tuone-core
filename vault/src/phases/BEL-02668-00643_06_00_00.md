### Evolutionary Timeline of the RWE Facility in Ostend, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2022**: RWE, in collaboration with SolarDuck, announced the development of the Merganser pilot project, which will utilize floating solar technology. The facility will have a production capacity of **500 kW** and is set to be installed off the coast of Ostend in the Belgian North Sea. This project marks SolarDuck's first offshore pilot project.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2022**: RWE committed to investing in the Merganser pilot project to explore and commercialize SolarDuck's floating solar PV technology. This investment aims to accelerate the learnings from deploying the technology in harsh North Sea conditions, facilitating faster commercialization from **2023**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2022**: The Merganser pilot project was officially announced, with plans for installation in **2023**. The project is part of a broader strategy to integrate floating solar technology with offshore wind farms, specifically targeting the Hollandse Kust West (HKW) offshore wind farm for future developments.

### Summary
The RWE facility in Ostend, Belgium, is focused on the Merganser pilot project, which aims to deploy innovative floating solar technology with a capacity of 500 kW. The project is a collaboration between RWE and SolarDuck, with significant investment aimed at commercializing this technology in challenging offshore environments. The pilot is set to be operational in 2023, laying the groundwork for future developments in hybrid renewable energy systems.
