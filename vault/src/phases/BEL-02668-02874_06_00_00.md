## BEL-02668-02874

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Equans Facility in Ostend, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2023**: The SeaVolt consortium, which includes Equans, announced the launch of an offshore floating photovoltaic (PV) demonstrator off the Belgian port of Ostend. This facility is designed to test floating solar technology specifically tailored for rough sea conditions. The installation will gather data on the performance of various solar panel configurations under real marine conditions, including the impact of waves, rain, and salt spray.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2023**: The project is supported by the Blue Cluster funded research project MPVAQUA and additional backing from the federal government via BELSPO. The consortium aims to collect data for at least a year to advance the development of offshore photovoltaics on a large scale, indicating significant investment in research and technology development.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2023**: The construction of the SeaVolt marine floating solar test installation is underway, with Equans serving as the main contractor. The project is described as a "solar lab at sea," and it is expected to provide crucial insights for future large-scale offshore solar energy projects. The facility is not a full-scale prototype but rather a laboratory for technological advancement.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2023**: The project is expected to contribute to job creation in the region, as highlighted by various officials, including Vincent Van Quickenborne, Minister of the North Sea, who noted that the project would contribute to green economic growth and create more jobs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2023**: The facility is part of a consortium that includes Equans, Tractebel, DEME, and Jan De Nul. While Equans is the main contractor responsible for the construction and follow-up of the project, the ownership structure appears to be collaborative rather than exclusive, indicating a joint venture among the consortium members.

This timeline captures the key developments related to the Equans facility in Ostend, focusing on its technological advancements, investments, project milestones, labor implications, and ownership structure.
