### Evolutionary Timeline of the Tree Composites Facility in Ostend, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2023**: The Tree Composites facility is involved in the construction of the SeaVolt marine floating solar test installation in the port of Ostend. This facility is designed to test solar panels specifically tailored for offshore conditions, utilizing a modular design suitable for rough seas. The installation will gather data on the performance of various solar panel configurations under real-life marine conditions.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2023**: The SeaVolt project is supported by the Blue Cluster funded research project MPVAQUA and additional funding from the federal government via BELSPO. This investment aims to advance the development of offshore solar energy technology.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2023**: The SeaVolt test installation is under construction, marking a significant milestone as it is the first installation in the Belgian North Sea aimed at large-scale offshore solar energy development. This project serves as a laboratory for testing and advancing the technology.
