### Evolutionary Timeline of the Alfen Facility in Ostend, Belgium

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 03-05-2023
   - **Details:** Alfen is set to supply a 24MW/54MWh battery energy storage system for Centrica’s project in Ostend. The facility will store enough clean energy to power 48,000 Belgian homes for two hours. The storage system will participate in Belgium’s capacity market, aimed at supporting the transition to renewable energy as the country plans to close five of its seven nuclear reactors by 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 03-05-2023
   - **Details:** The project is part of Centrica's strategy to deliver 900MW of flexible energy assets by 2026. The investment in this facility is part of a broader initiative to develop and acquire flexible energy assets in Belgium, which is becoming strategically important for Centrica.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 03-05-2023
