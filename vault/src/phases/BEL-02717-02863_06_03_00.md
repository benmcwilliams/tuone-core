### Evolutionary Timeline of the EnergyVille Facility in Genk, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: EnergyVille, a joint venture involving KU Leuven, VITO, imec, and Hasselt University, is constructing two testing facilities for photovoltaic (PV) noise barriers as part of the Rolling Solar project. The facility in Genk is designed to assess various PV technologies and aims to develop large-scale solar-equipped noise barriers. The installation measures 13m wide and 5m high and is currently hosting solar panels utilizing three different cell technologies: 
  - Monocrystalline PERC modules
  - Thin-film panels based on cadmium-telluride (CdTe)
  - Copper, indium, gallium, and selenium (CIGS) solar modules.

#### 2. Investment and Development
