## BEL-04032-04546

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Avery Dennison Facility in Turnhout, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2023**: Avery Dennison commissioned the construction of a concentrated solar thermal (CST) platform and thermal storage unit at its production plant in Turnhout. The facility will utilize 2,240 surface mirrors to achieve a solar field peak yield of 2.7 gigawatt-hours (GWh) of thermal power. It will also feature six thermal storage modules with a capacity of five megawatt-hours (MWh) of thermal power. The installation is designed to provide heat equivalent to 2.3 GWh of gas consumption, covering 100% of the factory's heat demand during peak summer months.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2023**: Financing for the CST project was secured through multiple sources, including the Belgian cooperative Campina Energie, the European Union’s Horizon 2020 research and innovation program, and the Flemish Government via the Green Heat program. Additionally, the Balance of Plant (BoP) was partly funded by the German Ministry for Economic Affairs and Climate Action.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2023**: The CST platform and thermal storage unit were officially commissioned, marking a significant milestone in Avery Dennison's sustainability efforts. The project is part of the company's broader initiative to decarbonize its operations and reduce reliance on fossil fuels.
