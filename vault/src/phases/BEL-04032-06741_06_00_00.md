### Evolutionary Timeline of the Azteq Facility in Turnhout, Belgium

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2023
   - **Details:** The Azteq facility, in collaboration with Energynest and Campina Energie, is developing Europe’s largest concentrated solar and thermal storage unit. The facility will utilize 2,240 surface mirrors to generate a peak yield of 2.7 gigawatt-hours (GWh) of thermal power. It will also feature six thermal storage modules with a capacity of five megawatt-hours of thermal power. The installation aims to provide heat for Avery Dennison’s factory, reducing emissions by an average of 9% annually and covering 100% of the factory's heat demand during peak summer months.
