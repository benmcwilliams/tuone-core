### Evolutionary Timeline of the Energynest Facility in Turnhout, Belgium

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2023
   - **Details:** The facility, developed by Energynest in collaboration with Azteq and Campina Energie, is focused on concentrated solar power (CSP) technology. It features 2,240 surface mirrors and a solar field with a peak yield of 2.7 gigawatt-hours (GWh) of thermal power. Additionally, it includes six thermal storage modules with a capacity of five megawatt-hours of thermal power. The installation is designed to provide heat for Avery Dennison's factory, covering approximately 5,540m², and is noted as the largest installation of parabolic mirrors combined with thermal energy storage in an industrial setting in Europe.
