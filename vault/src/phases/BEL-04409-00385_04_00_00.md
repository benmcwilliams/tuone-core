### Evolutionary Timeline of the INEOS Facility in Doel, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **02-03-2021**: INEOS launched a hydrogen pilot project at its cogeneration plant in Doel. The project aims to replace 10% of the natural gas feed with hydrogen, with plans to increase this to 20% if successful. The facility is designed to generate electricity and heat from natural gas, and this pilot marks the first industrial-scale tests of hydrogen use in Belgium. INEOS Phenol produces 300,000 tons of hydrogen annually as a co-product of its chemical processes, which is used as a low-carbon fuel and raw material.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **02-03-2021**: The pilot project is part of INEOS's broader roadmap to achieve climate neutrality by 2050 and reduce emissions by 55% by 2030 compared to 1990 levels. This includes investments in electrification, the use of recycled or bio-based raw materials, and renewable energy.
