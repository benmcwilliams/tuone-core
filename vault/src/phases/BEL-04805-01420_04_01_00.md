1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2023:** TotalEnergies and Holcim Belgium announced the development of a floating solar farm at the Obourg cement plant. The solar farm will consist of 55,000 photovoltaic panels with a total power capacity of 31MWp. This initiative aims to supply clean power to the facility and reduce CO2 emissions by 110,000 tons over the project's lifespan. The project is part of a broader strategy to decarbonize the cement production facility, which is one of the largest in Europe.
