## BEL-04805-04007

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Holcim Facility in Obourg, Belgium

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **03/2023:** Holcim Belgium, in collaboration with TotalEnergies, initiated the development of a floating solar farm at the Obourg cement plant. The solar farm will consist of 55,000 photovoltaic panels with a total power capacity of 31MWp. This project aims to supply renewable energy to the facility and reduce CO2 emissions by 110,000 tons over its lifespan. Additionally, the renewable energy produced will power a new oxyfuel kiln and an electrolyzer for green hydrogen production.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **10/2022:** A memorandum of understanding (MoU) was signed between TotalEnergies and Holcim Belgium to collaborate on the full decarbonization of the Obourg cement production facility. This agreement included the assessment of renewable projects to support the facility's transition to cleaner energy sources.
