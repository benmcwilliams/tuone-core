### Evolutionary Timeline of the Janssen Pharmaceutica Facility in Beerse, Belgium

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **October 2023:** The geothermal heating project at the Janssen Pharmaceutica facility was inaugurated. This facility utilizes deep geothermal energy, pumping hot water at 85 °C from a depth of 2.4 kilometers. The heat is distributed through a 3.5-kilometer heat network to provide clean and sustainable heat for the buildings and production processes.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The geothermal project received financial support from the Flemish Energy and Climate Agency and the European Union, amounting to EUR 1.5 million from the European Regional Development Fund (ERDF Flanders). This investment is part of a broader initiative to reduce carbon emissions and promote clean technologies.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Mid-2018:** Plans for the geothermal heating project were first announced.
   - **End of 2019:** Drilling operations for the geothermal project commenced.
   - **October 2023:** The geothermal heating project was officially inaugurated.
