### CMI Group Facility Evolution Timeline - Seraing, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2018**: CMI Group inaugurated Europe’s largest energy storage pilot project at its facility in Seraing. The project combines 2MWp of rooftop solar with a total of 4.2MWh of energy storage, utilizing a lithium-ion battery system and two flow batteries. This integration is part of the microgrid ‘MiRIS’ (Micro Réseau Intégré Seraing), aimed at demonstrating advanced integration of renewable energy resources and addressing the intermittency of renewable energy production.
