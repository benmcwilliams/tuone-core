### Evolutionary Timeline of the MHI Vestas Facility at Norther Offshore Wind Farm, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **01-02-2019**: The Norther offshore wind farm, with a total capacity of 370MW, will consist of 44 MHI Vestas V164-8.4MW turbines. Installation work commenced with the Van Oord jack-up vessel Aeolus loading the first four turbines for transport to the site. The facility is designed to significantly contribute to Belgium's climate objectives.

- **25-02-2019**: The facility delivered its first power to the Belgian grid from the MHI Vestas V164-8.4MW turbines. By this date, 12 turbines had been installed, with four operational. The project is on track to be fully operational by summer 2019.
