### Evolutionary Timeline of the Elicio Facility - Norther Offshore Wind Farm, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **01-02-2019**: The Norther offshore wind farm, with a total capacity of 370MW, will consist of 44 MHI Vestas V164-8.4MW turbines. Installation work commenced with the Van Oord jack-up vessel Aeolus loading the first four turbines for transport to the site. The project is significant for Belgium's climate objectives and is expected to be fully operational by summer 2019.

- **25-02-2019**: The first power was delivered to the Belgian grid from the installed MHI Vestas turbines. At this point, 12 turbines had been installed, with four already operational. The project continued to progress towards full operational status by summer 2019.
