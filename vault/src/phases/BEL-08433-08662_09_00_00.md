### E.On Facility in Willebroek, Belgium - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2023
   - **Details:** E.On, in collaboration with Imerys, is constructing an energy recovery plant at Imerys’ production facility in Willebroek. The plant will utilize syngas, a by-product from the production of carbon black, to generate electricity. The expected production capacity of the plant is 29MW, which will power Imerys’ site and supply electricity to 40,000 surrounding homes.
