### Evolutionary Timeline of the Smulders Facility in Balen, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2020**: The Smulders facility, in collaboration with Engie Solutions and Iemants, began the fabrication of the topside for TenneT's platform for the 1.5GW Hollandse Kust Noord offshore wind farm. The steel fabrication process commenced at the Smulders’ facilities in Arendonk and Balen, with assembly scheduled to start in October 2020.
- **June 2021**: The facility continued its operations by starting the fabrication of the topside for the Hollandse Kust West Alpha wind farm. Steel fabrication was ongoing at the Smulders’ facilities in Arendonk and Balen, as well as Hoboken.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2020**: TenneT awarded the contract for the engineering, procurement, construction, offshore installation, connection, and testing of the offshore transformer station for the Hollandse Kust Noord wind farm zone to the joint venture of Engie Solutions and Iemants. Specific investment amounts were not detailed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2020**: The first steel cutting ceremony for the Hollandse Kust Noord offshore wind farm took place, marking the official start of the project.
- **October 2020**: Assembly of the topside for the Hollandse Kust Noord project was scheduled to begin.
- **June 2021**: The first steel was cut for the Hollandse Kust West Alpha platform, marking another significant milestone in the project timeline.
