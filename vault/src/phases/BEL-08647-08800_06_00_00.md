### Evolutionary Timeline of the EnergyVision Facility at Ostend-Bruges Airport, Belgium

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: EnergyVision announced plans to construct a 40 MW photovoltaic (PV) facility at Ostend-Bruges Airport. The facility will consist of 66,200 solar modules, each with an output of 605 W. The expected annual electricity production is nearly 37,000 MWh, which will exceed the airport's consumption, allowing excess power to be diverted to Antwerp Airport and local residents.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: The project is valued at €35 million (approximately $37.9 million). EnergyVision is also setting up a platform for local citizens to co-invest in the project through an eight-year loan with a fixed annual interest rate of 5%.
