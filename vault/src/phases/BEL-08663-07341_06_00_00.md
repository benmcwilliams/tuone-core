### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enfinity Facility in Brasschaat, Belgium

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2011
   - **Details:** Enfinity completed a 3MW solar installation on the rooftop of a high-speed rail tunnel in Belgium. The facility features 16,000 solar panels, which are expected to produce electricity for the railway infrastructure, including signaling, lighting, and heating for railway stations, as well as powering both conventional and high-speed trains. The installation is capable of powering approximately 4,000 trains per year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2011
   - **Details:** The total investment for the solar installation was €14.5 million. This project was a collaborative effort involving Enfinity, the municipalities of Brasschaat and Schoten, financing companies FINEA and IKA, and solar construction company Solar Power Systems.
