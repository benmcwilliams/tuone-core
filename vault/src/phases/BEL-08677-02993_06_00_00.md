### Heliatek Facility in Linkebeek, Belgium - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: July 2017
   - **Details**: Heliatek's organic OPV (Organic Photovoltaic) thin-film technology was deployed on the façade of Engie's research center in Linkebeek. The installation covered a total surface area of 50 m² and was designed to generate an estimated annual power output of 2,300 kWh, which is equivalent to the electricity consumption of a small household in Europe. This installation serves as a real-world testing facility for OPV systems integrated into building façades.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: 2016
   - **Details**: The ENGIE Group acquired a 6.6% stake in Heliatek, indicating a financial investment in the company that supports the development and deployment of Heliatek's technologies.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: July 2017
   - **Details**: The completion of the OPV façade installation at Engie's research center marked a significant milestone in the application of Heliatek's technology. Plans were also announced to install OPV technology on Engie’s Fabricom buildings in Antwerp by October 2017.
