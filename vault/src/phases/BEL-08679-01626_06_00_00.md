### Evolutionary Timeline of the Engie Facility in Lommel, Belgium

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2019
   - **Details:** Engie completed the 99.5 MW Crystal Solar Park in Lommel, making it Belgium's largest solar project at that time. The facility is designed to sell power primarily to Nyrstar, a local zinc manufacturer, through a direct line to their technical installations. The expected annual generation from the solar park is around 85 GWh.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2019
   - **Details:** The project received a premium of €0.0479 per kWh for the power injected into the grid, which was a reduction from the previously set tariff of €0.078 per kWh. This reduction indicates a more efficient project requiring 34% less support than initially calculated.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** June 2019
   - **Details:** The construction of the Lommel solar park began in October 2018, and the facility was officially commissioned in June 2019.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** June 2019
   - **Details:** The article does not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** June 2019
   - **Details:** The facility is owned by Limburgse investeringsmaatschappij, a Flemish investment company, rather than Engie.
