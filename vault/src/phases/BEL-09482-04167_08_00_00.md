### Evolutionary Timeline of the Parkwind Facility in IJmuiden Ver Alpha, BEL

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 12-06-2024**: The IJmuiden Ver Alpha area, secured by the Noordzeker consortium (SSE Renewables and AGP), is designated for a 2 GW offshore wind farm. The project aims to incorporate nature-positive measures, including bird protection solutions and artificial reefs for marine wildlife.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: 12-06-2024**: The Noordzeker consortium is set to pay a total of EUR 21 million per year for 40 years for the rights to develop the wind farm and will cover the environmental impact assessment costs. The consortium must provide a bank guarantee or a deposit of EUR 200 million within four weeks to proceed with the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date: 12-06-2024**: The IJmuiden Ver Alpha project was part of the largest Dutch offshore wind tender, with winning bids announced. The Noordzeker consortium's proposal was selected, and they are required to refine their project before making a final decision on development by July 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **No specific information** regarding job creation or job loss was mentioned in the article.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date: 12-06-2024**: The facility is part of a joint venture under the Noordzeker consortium, which includes SSE Renewables and AGP, indicating that Parkwind does not exclusively own the facility. A separate bid was submitted by Parkwind, but it was not selected.
