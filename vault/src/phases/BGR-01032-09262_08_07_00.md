### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iv-Offshore & Energy Facility in Sofia, BGR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** Iv-Offshore & Energy is involved in the engineering for the Sofia offshore wind farm's HVDC platform, which has a production capacity of 1400MW. The facility focuses on the detailed design for both the topside and jacket foundation, as well as the procurement of topside equipment. The offshore converter platform will convert electricity generated by 100 turbines from 66kV AC to 320kV DC for transmission to shore.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** The project is part of a larger investment by RWE, which has selected Sembcorp Marine and GE Renewable Energy as preferred bidders for the transmission system. The full contract for the engineering works is contingent upon the project's final investment decision, expected in the first quarter of 2021.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** Iv-Offshore & Energy signed an early works contract with Sembcorp Marine Offshore Platforms to commence engineering work for the Sofia HVDC platform. The project is in the early design phase, with a full contract pending the final investment decision.
