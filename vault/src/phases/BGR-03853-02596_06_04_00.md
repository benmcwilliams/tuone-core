### AE Solar Facility in Plovdiv, Bulgaria - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2021
   - **Details:** AE Solar plans to construct a solar panel factory in the Trakia Economic Zone (TEZ) near Plovdiv, Bulgaria. The facility will have an annual production capacity of 1 GW and will include a research and development hub as well as a carbon-neutral industrial park.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2021
   - **Details:** AE Solar will invest more than EUR 150 million in the construction of the solar panel factory.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** December 2021
   - **Details:** The construction of the facility is set to begin in 2022, as announced by Bulgarian energy minister Alexander Nikolov.
