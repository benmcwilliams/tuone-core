### Eberspächer Facility in Ruse, Bulgaria - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: Eberspächer began construction of a new production plant for electric vehicle heaters in Ruse, Bulgaria. The facility was designed to have an annual production capacity of approximately 2.4 million heaters. The building covers 17,000 square meters and includes production, logistics areas, and an office and canteen complex. The facility was intended to start production in early 2024.
- **January 2024**: Eberspächer sold the new plant, which was intended for high-voltage heaters, to GG Group before it opened due to lower-than-expected demand for e-mobility products. The plant was initially planned to produce electric vehicle heaters for the European market.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: The construction of the facility was initiated, indicating a significant investment in expanding Eberspächer's manufacturing capabilities in response to the growing demand for electric vehicle components.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2023**: Eberspächer opened an interim production facility in Ruse to meet high market demand, which employed around 100 workers.
- **April 2023**: Construction of the new plant commenced.
- **January 2024**: The planned new plant was sold to GG Group before it could begin operations.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2023**: Eberspächer announced the creation of approximately 180 jobs in 2023, with plans to expand to up to 500 employees by 2025.
- **January 2024**: Following the sale of the new plant, the interim facility was to become a regular plant, maintaining around 100 employees, with plans to double the volume at the site in 2024 without increasing the number of employees.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2023**: Eberspächer was in the process of establishing full ownership of the new facility in Ruse.
- **January 2024**: The ownership changed when Eberspächer sold the new plant to GG Group, indicating a shift from exclusive ownership to a third-party ownership structure.

This timeline captures the key developments and changes at the Eberspächer facility in Ruse, Bulgaria, highlighting its initial plans, subsequent challenges, and eventual sale of the facility.
