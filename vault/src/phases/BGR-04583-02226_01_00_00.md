### Evolutionary Timeline of the Eurowind Energy Facility in Yambol, Bulgaria

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** Eurowind Energy, in a joint venture with Renalfa, announced plans to build a 250 MW solar power plant near the village of Tenovo in Yambol province. The facility will include a power storage system and is part of a broader initiative to install 2 GW of renewable energy capacity in Bulgaria over the next ten years.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** The estimated investment for the 250 MW solar power plant is between EUR 100 million to EUR 150 million. The joint venture, EURA Energy, will develop projects with private capital and may apply for European funds for innovative technologies, particularly in battery storage.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** The construction of the 250 MW solar power plant is set to be completed in 2023. This project marks the first step in a larger plan to develop 2 GW of renewable energy capacity in Bulgaria.
