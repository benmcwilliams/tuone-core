### Evolutionary Timeline of the Solarpro Facility in Silistra Municipality, Bulgaria

**1. Technology and Operations:**
- **Production Capacity:** The St George solar park, which is being constructed, will have a production capacity of **229 MW**. 
- **Technology and Infrastructure:** The facility will consist of nearly **400,000 solar panels** and will be connected to the main transmission grid via a **110kV substation** and two independent connection lines totaling approximately **6 km** in length. 

**2. Investment and Development:**
- **Financing:** The project will be financed through **long-term power purchase agreements (PPAs)** and loan facilities from a consortium of international lending partners and regional commercial lenders. The first PPA for St George is expected to be announced soon, marking it as one of the first PPAs signed in Bulgaria.

**3. Project Status and Milestones:**
- **Announcement Date:** The project was announced in **February 2024**.
- **Construction Start:** Construction work is set to begin shortly after the announcement, with the plant expected to come online in **2025**.
- **EPC Partnership:** CMC Europe and Solarpro are acting as the **engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC)** partners for the project, collaborating with Rezolv Energy to implement the Environmental and Social Action Plan.

**4. Labour:**
- **Job Creation:** The construction phase of the project is expected to create up to **200 jobs**, with a focus on hiring local employees. Additionally, training and skills development programs will be established to support the workforce.

**5. Ownership Structure:**
- **Joint Venture:** The facility is a collaborative effort involving **Solarpro**, **CMC Europe**, and **Green Solar Energy**, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by Solarpro.
