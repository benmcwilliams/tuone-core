## BGR-08244-07120

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Rezolv Energy Facility in Silistra Municipality, Bulgaria

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2023**: Rezolv Energy announced the development of a 229MW solar PV plant named St. George in Silistra Municipality. The facility will be built on a former airport site and will consist of nearly 400,000 solar panels. It is expected to generate an average annual power output of 313GWh, contributing to 13% of Bulgaria's currently-installed solar power capacity.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2024**: The project will be financed through long-term power purchase agreements (PPAs) and loan facilities from a consortium of international lending partners and regional commercial lenders. The first PPA for the St. George project is anticipated to be announced soon, marking it as one of the first PPAs signed in Bulgaria.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2023**: Rezolv Energy acquired the rights to build and operate the St. George solar plant, with construction expected to begin before the end of 2023 and completion projected for early 2025.
- **September 2024**: Construction work is set to commence shortly, with the plant expected to come online in 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2024**: The construction phase of the St. George project is expected to create up to 200 jobs, with a focus on hiring local workers. Training and skills development programs will also be implemented to support the workforce.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2023**: Rezolv Energy is identified as the owner of the facility, indicating full ownership of the St. George solar plant.
