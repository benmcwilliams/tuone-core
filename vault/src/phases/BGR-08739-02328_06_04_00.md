### Evolutionary Timeline of the Jinko Solar Facility in Verila, Bulgaria

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2023
   - **Details:** The Verila solar plant has a production capacity of 123 MW and utilizes 220,000 Tiger Neo bifacial solar modules supplied by JinkoSolar. These modules feature TOPCon N-type cells with a conversion efficiency of 23.23% and a temperature coefficient of -0.29% per degree Celsius. The facility is noted for being the largest solar project in Bulgaria and is significant for the country's clean energy transition.

   - **Details:** The investment from JinkoSolar, a leading solar panel manufacturer, is expected to stimulate greater interest in solar energy within Bulgaria, which has a relatively low solar capacity of 1.5 GW installed as of the previous year. The project is part of a broader trend in Bulgaria's solar sector, with expectations of adding 20 GW of capacity over the next three years.
