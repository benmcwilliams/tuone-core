## BGR-08754-01890

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Renalfa IPP Facility in Tenevo, Bulgaria

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: November 2024
  - The Tenevo facility, operated by Tenevo Solar Technologies EAD, is set to produce solar energy with a generation capacity of **238 MW**. 
  - The facility is expected to generate over **300 GWh** of electricity annually and save **250,000 tonnes** of carbon emissions each year.
  - There are long-term plans to add an additional **250 MW** capacity of behind-the-meter energy storage.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: November 2024
  - The European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD) is providing a loan of **€50 million** for the construction and operation of the solar farm.
  - Raiffeisen Bank International is contributing an additional **€53 million**, bringing the total financing package to **€103 million**.
  - This project is supported by the EBRD’s InvestEU Framework for Sustainable Transition, marking the first use of the InvestEU guarantee in Bulgaria.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: November 2024
  - The Tenevo solar farm project is designated as a significant step towards Bulgaria's net-zero greenhouse gas emissions target by **2050** and aims to reduce reliance on coal generation.
  - It is noted as the first renewable energy plant in Bulgaria over **100 MW** that will sell all its output in the market without a support scheme or a corporate power purchase agreement.

#### 4. Labour
- **Date**: November 2024
  - While specific job creation numbers are not mentioned, the project is expected to strengthen the private sector presence in Bulgaria's energy sector, which may imply job creation opportunities in the renewable energy field.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date**: November 2024
  - The facility is owned by **Tenevo Solar Technologies EAD**, a joint stock company incorporated in Bulgaria. 
  - It is **equally owned** by **Renalfa IPP**, an Austrian joint venture between Renalfa Solarpro Group and RGreen Invest, and **Eurowind Energy**.
