### Evolutionary Timeline of the RGreen Invest Facility in Tenevo, Bulgaria

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2024
   - **Details:** The facility, Tenevo Solar Technologies EAD, is a solar PV plant with a production capacity of 237.6MW. It is notable for being the first solar project in Bulgaria exceeding 100MW to operate fully merchant, without a support scheme or power purchase agreement. There are plans to add an additional 250MW of behind-the-meter energy storage output in the future.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2024
   - **Details:** The project received a financing package totaling €103 million, which includes a loan of up to €50 million from the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD) and €53 million from Raiffeisen Bank International. This financing is part of the EBRD’s InvestEU Framework for Sustainable Transition.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2024
   - **Details:** The financing announcement marks a significant milestone for the project, indicating that construction and operation are supported by the EBRD and Raiffeisen Bank. The project is positioned as a key development in Bulgaria's renewable energy landscape.
