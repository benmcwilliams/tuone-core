## BGR-08754-09848

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Renalfa Group Facility in Tenevo, Bulgaria

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2024
   - **Details:** The facility, operated by Tenevo Solar Technologies EAD, produces solar energy with a capacity of 237.6MW. It is notable for being the first solar project in Bulgaria to operate fully merchant without a support scheme or power purchase agreement. There are plans to add an additional 250MW of behind-the-meter energy storage output in the future.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2024
   - **Details:** The European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD) provided financing of up to €50 million (US$52 million) for the project, with an additional €53 million from Raiffeisen Bank International, totaling €103 million. This financing is part of the EBRD’s InvestEU Framework for Sustainable Transition.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2024
   - **Details:** The project is in the construction and operation phase, marking a significant milestone as it is the first solar PV plant in Bulgaria exceeding 100MW to operate on a fully merchant basis.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** Not specified in the article.
   - **Details:** The article does not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** November 2024
   - **Details:** The facility is a joint venture, equally owned by Renalfa Group, RGreen Invest, and Danish renewables developer Eurowind Energy, under the entity Tenevo Solar Technologies EAD.
