## BGR-08754-10212

### Evolutionary Timeline of Tenevo Solar Technologies EAD Facility in Tenevo, Bulgaria

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2024**: Tenevo Solar Technologies EAD is set to construct and operate a solar farm with a capacity of **238 MW**. The facility is expected to generate over **300 GWh** of electricity annually and save **250,000 tonnes** of carbon emissions each year. There are long-term plans to add an additional **250 MW** of behind-the-meter energy storage capacity.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2024**: The European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD) is providing a loan of up to **€50 million** for the project. Additionally, Raiffeisen Bank International is contributing a parallel financing facility of **€53 million**, bringing the total finance package to **€103 million**. This financing is part of the EBRD’s InvestEU Framework for Sustainable Transition, marking the first use of the InvestEU guarantee in Bulgaria.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2024**: The project is announced as the first renewable energy plant in Bulgaria over **100 MW** that will sell all its output in the market without a support scheme or a corporate power purchase agreement. The construction and operation of the facility are confirmed, with significant emphasis on its role in advancing Bulgaria's green energy transition and achieving net-zero greenhouse gas emissions by **2050**.

#### 4. Labour
- **November 2024**: While specific job creation figures are not mentioned in the articles, the project is expected to strengthen the private sector presence in Bulgaria's energy sector, which may imply job creation opportunities associated with the construction and operation of the solar farm.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **November 2024**: Tenevo Solar Technologies EAD is a joint stock company incorporated in Bulgaria. It is **equally owned** by **Renalfa IPP**, which is a joint venture between Renalfa Solarpro Group and RGreen Invest, and **Eurowind Energy**. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones related to the Tenevo Solar Technologies EAD facility, highlighting its significance in Bulgaria's renewable energy landscape.
