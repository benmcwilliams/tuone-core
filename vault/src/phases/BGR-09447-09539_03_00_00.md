### Evolutionary Timeline of Natsionalna Elektricheska Kompania EAD Facility in Batak, Bulgaria

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2024**: Natsionalna Elektricheska Kompania EAD (NEK) is set to construct two large pumped storage hydropower plants in Bulgaria, specifically in Batak and Dospat. Each plant will have a generation capacity of approximately **800 MW**, totaling **1.6 GW** for both facilities. The expected operational date for both plants is **2032**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2024**: The European Investment Bank (EIB) will support NEK with economic and technological feasibility studies for the construction of the Batak and Dospat plants. Each project is estimated to cost around **€900 million** (approximately **$964 million**).

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2024**: Announcement of the construction of the Batak and Dospat pumped storage hydropower plants. The projects are expected to be operational by **2032**.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2024**: Specific job creation or job loss details related to the construction of the Batak facility have not been disclosed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2024**: The facility will be owned by Natsionalna Elektricheska Kompania EAD (NEK), indicating **full ownership** of the Batak facility by the company.
