### Evolutionary Timeline of Natsionalna Elektricheska Kompania EAD Facility in Dospat, Bulgaria

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2024**: Natsionalna Elektricheska Kompania EAD (NEK) is set to construct a pumped storage hydropower plant in Dospat, which will have a generation capacity of approximately 800 MW. This facility is part of a larger project that includes another plant in Batak, with both expected to be operational by 2032. The plants will enhance Bulgaria's electricity supply and storage capacity, providing flexible management and emergency capacity replacement.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2024**: The European Investment Bank (EIB) will support NEK in the construction of the Dospat plant, with each plant (Dospat and Batak) estimated to cost around €900 million. The EIB will conduct economic and technological feasibility studies to ensure the viability of the projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2024**: Announcement of the construction of the Dospat pumped storage hydropower plant, alongside the Batak plant. Both projects are expected to be operational by 2032.
