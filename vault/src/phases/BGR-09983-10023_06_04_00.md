### Evolutionary Timeline of Smart Solar Technologies AD Facility in Elenino, Bulgaria

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: August 2024
  - Smart Solar Technologies AD is set to construct a solar cell and panel factory in Elenino, Bulgaria. The facility will primarily focus on the production of solar cells and panels, with most of the output intended for export to European markets, including Austria, the Netherlands, Germany, and Italy. The company currently has a production capacity of 2.9 GW of solar modules.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: August 2024
  - The Bulgarian government has approved a memorandum of understanding for the project, which includes an investment of BGN 240 million (approximately $137 million) by Smart Solar Technologies AD in both intangible and fixed tangible assets. Additionally, the investment will be supported by a €34 million ($38 million) grant.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: August 2024
  - The Bulgarian Council of Ministers has signed an agreement with Smart Solar Technologies AD, marking the official start of the project. The facility is designated as a priority investment, and an inter-agency working group will oversee its development.

#### 4. Labour
- **Date**: August 2024
  - The construction of the facility is expected to create 812 new jobs in the region, contributing to local employment and economic growth.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date**: August 2024
  - The articles do not specify the ownership structure in detail, but it can be inferred that Smart Solar Technologies AD will have full ownership of the facility as they are the sole entity responsible for the investment and construction.
