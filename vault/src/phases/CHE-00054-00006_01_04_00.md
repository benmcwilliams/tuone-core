### Evolutionary Timeline of the ABB Facility in Baden, Switzerland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **01-11-2019**: ABB inaugurated a production facility in Baden for energy storage systems used in railways, electric buses, and electric trucks. The facility assembles standardized battery modules from battery cells, enabling ABB to supply complete power trains including energy storage solutions. The products utilize lithium-ion technology, focusing on high energy and power density, and long service life under high loads.

- **04-06-2020**: The facility was officially opened, emphasizing its role in sustainable mobility applications. The energy storage systems are designed for various transport modes, including railways and e-buses, and are crucial for the electrification and decarbonization of transport.

- **16-04-2021**: ABB announced its partnership with Stadler Germany to supply traction converters and battery systems for battery-electric trains. The battery modules for these systems are manufactured at the Baden facility.

- **20-04-2021**: ABB confirmed the contract with Stadler for energy storage systems and traction converters, highlighting that the battery modules will be produced in Baden and integrated into energy storage systems in Minden, Germany.
