### Evolutionary Timeline of the H2 Energy Facility in Gösgen, Switzerland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2019**: Hydrospider, a joint venture between Alpiq and H2 Energy, submitted a planning application for a 2 MW hydrogen production plant at the Gösgen hydroelectric power station. The facility aims to produce hydrogen using 100% hydropower to fuel an initial fleet of 50 fuel cell trucks, part of a larger commitment to supply a total of 1,600 trucks by 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2019**: The establishment of the 2 MW hydrogen production system is part of a broader initiative to develop hydrogen-powered transportation in Switzerland, emphasizing the importance of renewable energy sources for hydrogen production.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **End of 2019**: The commissioning of the commercial hydrogen production plant is scheduled for completion. This marks the establishment of Switzerland's first commercial-scale hydrogen production system.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify job creation or job loss directly associated with the establishment of the facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2019**: Hydrospider is a joint venture where Alpiq and H2 Energy each hold a 50% stake, indicating that the facility is not owned exclusively by either company but rather through a collaborative partnership.
