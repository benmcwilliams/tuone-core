### Evolutionary Timeline of Hydrospider AG Facility in Gösgen, CHE

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** End of 2019
   - **Details:** Hydrospider AG will open Switzerland’s first plant for the commercial production of green hydrogen at the Gösgen hydroelectric power station. The facility will produce climate-friendly hydrogen using 100% hydropower, with an initial production capacity of 2 MW.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** August 2019
   - **Details:** Linde acquired a 10% stake in Hydrospider AG, with H2 Energy and Alpiq each selling 5% of their previous 50% stake. This investment indicates a strategic partnership aimed at enhancing the development of green hydrogen production.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2019
   - **Details:** Hydrospider submitted a building application for the 2 MW plant at the beginning of July. The application was published by the municipality of Niedergösgen shortly thereafter.
   - **Date:** End of 2019
   - **Details:** The facility is set to start commercial operations by the end of the year.
