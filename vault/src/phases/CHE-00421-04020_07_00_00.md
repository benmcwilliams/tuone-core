### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hydrospider Facility in Gösgen, CHE

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2019
   - **Details:** Hydrospider, a joint venture between Alpiq and H2 Energy, plans to develop Switzerland's first commercial hydrogen production system at the Gösgen hydropower plant. The facility will feature a 2 megawatt (MW) hydrogen production system. The hydrogen produced will be used to power an initial fleet of 50 fuel cell electric trucks, with future plans to support a total of 1,600 trucks. The hydrogen generation system is designed to utilize 100% hydroelectricity, making it eco-friendly and carbon-free.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2019
   - **Details:** The project represents a significant investment in renewable energy technology aimed at decarbonizing goods transportation in Switzerland. Specific financial details regarding the investment were not disclosed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2019
   - **Details:** Hydrospider submitted the building application for the 2 MW hydrogen production system at the beginning of July 2019. The commissioning of the facility is scheduled for the end of 2019.

4. **Labour:**
