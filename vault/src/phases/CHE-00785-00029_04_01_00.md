### Evolutionary Timeline of the Alpiq Facility in Niedergösgen, CHE

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** End of 2019
   - **Details:** The facility, through Hydrospider AG, began commercial production of green hydrogen using 100% hydropower from the Gösgen hydroelectric power station. The initial production capacity is a 2 MW plant, which will supply hydrogen for the first 50 of a total of 1,600 fuel cell trucks.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** August 2019
   - **Details:** Linde acquired a 10% stake in Hydrospider AG, which indicates a significant investment in the green hydrogen project. Alpiq and H2 Energy each sold 5% of their previous 50% stake in Hydrospider to Linde.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2019
   - **Details:** Hydrospider submitted a building application for the 2 MW plant at the beginning of July, which was subsequently published by the municipality of Niedergösgen. The plant was set to start commercial operations by the end of 2019.
