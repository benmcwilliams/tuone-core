### Evolutionary Timeline of the H2 Energy Facility in Niedergösgen, CHE

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** End of 2019
   - **Details:** The facility, Hydrospider, will produce climate-friendly hydrogen using 100% hydropower from the Gösgen hydroelectric power station. The initial production capacity is set at 2 MW. The hydrogen produced will be utilized for the first 50 of a total of 1,600 fuel cell trucks, as part of a joint venture with Hyundai.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** August 2019
   - **Details:** Linde acquired a 10% stake in Hydrospider AG, which indicates a significant investment in the facility. H2 Energy and Alpiq each sold 5% of their previous 50% stake to Linde, suggesting a strategic partnership aimed at enhancing the facility's capabilities.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2019
   - **Details:** Hydrospider submitted a building application for the 2 MW plant at the beginning of July. The application was published by the municipality of Niedergösgen shortly thereafter. The facility is expected to start commercial operations by the end of 2019.
