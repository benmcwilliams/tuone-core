1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** End of 2019
   - **Details:** The facility will produce climate-friendly hydrogen using 100% hydropower from the Gösgen hydroelectric power station. The initial production capacity is set at 2 MW, with the hydrogen produced intended for use in 50 out of a total of 1,600 fuel cell trucks.

   - **Date:** August 2019
