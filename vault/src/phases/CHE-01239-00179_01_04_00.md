### Evolutionary Timeline of the Designwerk Facility in Winterthur, CHE

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: Designwerk confirmed the use of lithium iron phosphate (LFP) battery cells in their electric trucks, specifically in the High Cab Semi Lowliner 4x2T, starting from early 2024. The LFP battery system has a capacity of 170 kWh and an energy density of 141 Wh/kg. Two of these systems will be installed in the vehicle, providing a total installed capacity of 338 kWh (311 kWh usable), allowing for a range of up to 250 kilometers.
