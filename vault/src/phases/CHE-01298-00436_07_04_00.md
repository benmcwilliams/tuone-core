### Evolutionary Timeline of the Kyburz Facility in Zurich, CHE

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-09-2020**: Kyburz opens a battery recycling facility in Freienstein, Zurich. The facility is designed to recycle lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO4) batteries used in Kyburz's electric cargo tricycles. The initial production capacity is set to process around 4,000 cells per year, with a long-term goal of reaching up to 24,000 cells per year, which corresponds to the annual production of 3,000 vehicles. The recycling process recovers up to 91% of the metals contained in the batteries and is developed with the support of EMPA and ZHAW, focusing on sustainable practices without the use of chemicals.
