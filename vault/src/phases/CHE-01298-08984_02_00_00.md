### Evolutionary Timeline of the Geo Explorers Facility in Zurich, CHE

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2023
   - **Details:** The facility is involved in evaluating the potential for geothermal heat storage at Zurich Airport. The targeted subsurface layer for this project is a water-bearing gravel formation located at a depth of 300 meters, measuring 1 kilometer wide and 30 kilometers long. The project aims to store excess heat during summer and retrieve it during winter to reduce CO2 emissions from the airport's heating and cooling systems.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2023
   - **Details:** The estimated investment for the geothermal project is between CHF 4 to 8 million (approx. USD 4.5 to 9 million). The Swiss Federal Office of Energy (SFOE) is funding the project as a pilot initiative for geo-energy, contributing a maximum of CHF 1 million.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2023
   - **Details:** Seismic investigations and exploratory drilling have been conducted at three locations to assess the geothermal resource. The findings are promising, but the feasibility of the project will depend on the number of wells required for efficient thermal storage.
