### Evolutionary Timeline of the Green Hydrogen Systems Facility in Innovation Lab, Switzerland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2022**: Green Hydrogen Systems signed an agreement with Gaznat to deliver electrolysis equipment for its Innovation Lab in Switzerland. The order includes the supply of an electrolyzer with a capacity of **0.5 MW**, which, when fully operational, will supply approximately **195 kg of green hydrogen per day**. The hydrogen produced will be used to create synthetic methane with a new methanation reactor developed by Gaznat, the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne, and GRZ Technologies. This synthetic methane will be injected into the distribution grid covering the region of Chablais in Switzerland.
