   - **07-02-2019:** Precision Surfacing Solutions (PSS) acquires most of the production facilities from Meyer Burger Technology AG, including wafer technology operations in Thun. The production capacity specifics for the Thun facility are not detailed, but it is noted that PSS will provide services for wafer customers worldwide.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **07-02-2019:** PSS pays CHF 50 million ($50 million) to acquire the wafer business from Meyer Burger, which includes the production facilities and the workforce in Thun. This investment is aimed at strengthening PSS's activities in key markets and leveraging wafer technology synergies.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
