## CHE-02561-02161

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Axpo Facility in Leuk, Switzerland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** Axpo has installed a photovoltaic (PV) system inside a disused satellite dish operated by Leuk TDC. The facility produces solar power, with the satellite dish system expected to generate approximately 110,000 kWh of electricity annually. Additionally, another solar system on the roof of the data center will contribute an extra 550,000 kWh per year. The facility is located at an elevation of 1,000 meters, which enhances solar yield, especially in winter.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** The installation of the PV systems was carried out under a contracting model by CKW, a unit of Axpo. This model allows Leuk TDC to receive electricity at a fixed price for a specified contract period, shielding them from market price fluctuations without requiring upfront investment in the solar projects.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** The installation of the PV system in the satellite dish has been completed. Leuk TDC has plans to equip two additional satellite dishes with solar systems, having already obtained a building permit for one, with installation set to begin in spring 2023.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** The article does not specify job creation or job loss directly related to the installation of the PV systems at this time.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** The facility is not exclusively owned by Axpo. Under the contracting model, while CKW (part of Axpo) installs and maintains the systems, the ownership of the plant will transfer to Leuk TDC at the end of the contract period.
