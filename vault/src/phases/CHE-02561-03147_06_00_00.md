## CHE-02561-03147

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Leuk TDC Facility in Leuk, Switzerland

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **November 2022**: Axpo, through its unit CKW, installed a photovoltaic (PV) system inside a disused satellite dish operated by Leuk TDC. This system is designed to produce approximately 110,000 kWh of electricity annually. Additionally, another PV system on the roof of the data center is expected to generate an extra 550,000 kWh per year. The facility is located at an altitude of 1,000 meters, which enhances solar yield, especially in winter. Leuk TDC plans to equip two more satellite dishes with solar technology, with a building permit already obtained for one.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **November 2022**: The installation of the PV systems was conducted under a contracting model, allowing Leuk TDC to receive electricity at a fixed price for a specified period without upfront investment in the solar projects. CKW will manage the maintenance of the systems, and ownership will transfer to Leuk TDC at the end of the contract.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **November 2022**: Announcement of the installation of the PV system in the satellite dish and the additional system on the data center roof. Plans for equipping two more satellite dishes with solar technology were also mentioned, with installation set to begin in spring 2023.

4. **Labour**
   - No specific information regarding job creation or job loss was mentioned in the article.

5. **Ownership Structure**
   - Leuk TDC does not own the PV systems exclusively during the contract period; they are installed under a contracting model by CKW. However, ownership of the systems will transfer to Leuk TDC at the end of the contract period. 

This timeline captures the key developments and operational aspects of the Leuk TDC facility as of November 2022.
