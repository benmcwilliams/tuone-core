### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sun-Ways Facility in Neuchâtel, CHE

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2023**: Sun-Ways has developed a patented solution for deploying solar modules between railway tracks, allowing for easy removal for maintenance. The project involves a mechanically detachable PV plant that will occupy a section of track owned by Transports Publics Neuchâtelois SA. The PV system can be pre-assembled in a factory and transported on a special train, rolled out like a carpet between the tracks. The potential output is estimated at up to 1 TWh of solar power, leveraging Switzerland's extensive railway network of nearly 7,000 kilometers.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2023**: Sun-Ways is in discussions with unspecified investors in Europe and the United States to offer its solar solution on an industrial scale, indicating potential future investments tied to the expansion of its technology.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2023**: Sun-Ways is working on a project scheduled to go online in **May 2023**. This marks a significant milestone as it represents the first attempt to deploy solar technology on railways in Switzerland.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
