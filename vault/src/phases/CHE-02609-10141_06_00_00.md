### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sun-ways Facility in Neuchâtel, CHE

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2024**: Sun-ways plans to build an 18 kW pilot photovoltaic (PV) system on a 100-meter linear section of a railway line in Neuchâtel. The system will consist of 48 solar panels, each with an output of 380 W, totaling a capacity of 18 kW. The installation is designed to be removable, allowing for maintenance work on the railway.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2024**: The project is estimated to cost €621,800 ($685,920). The facility will inject power into the local grid, and local electricity provider Viteos, along with DG-Rail, will partner with Sun-ways for the construction of the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **04/2024**: The Swiss Federal Office of Transport (FOT) grants a permit for the installation of the removable solar power plant, marking it as the first of its kind in Switzerland. The project was initially rejected in the summer of 2023 due to a lack of technical references but was later approved after independent assessments and safety analyses were conducted.
