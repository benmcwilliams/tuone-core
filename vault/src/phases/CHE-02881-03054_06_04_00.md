### Evolutionary Timeline of the Insolight Facility in Valais, Switzerland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05/2021**: Insolight unveiled a new translucent monocrystalline solar panel named "Theia" with a nominal power of 106 W and a power conversion efficiency of 20.1%. This panel is designed for agrivoltaics applications and features protective glass and optical lenses to concentrate sunlight. The facility operates a large-scale pilot project occupying 165 m², where the panels are used to grow raspberries and strawberries under pots.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05/2021**: The pilot project was developed in collaboration with Swiss research institute Agroscope and energy company Romande Energie. The project aims to study the impact of light conditions on crop development while simultaneously producing solar energy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05/2021**: The pilot plant was officially announced and deployed in Valais, Switzerland. The project is set to evaluate solar electricity production over a four-year period.
