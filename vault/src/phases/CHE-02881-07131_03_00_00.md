## CHE-02881-07131

### Evolutionary Timeline of the SFR Facility in Valais, Switzerland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 01-07-2022
   - **Details:** The Nant de Drance pumped hydro energy storage (PHES) plant in Valais, Switzerland, officially began operations. The facility has a total energy storage capacity of 20GWh, with six turbines each having a nameplate capacity of 150MW, resulting in a maximum power output of 900MW. The plant is located 600m underground between the Emosson and Vieux Emosson reservoirs. The Vieux Emosson reservoir was raised by 21.5m, doubling its capacity.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 30-06-2022
   - **Details:** The total investment in the Nant de Drance project amounted to CHF2.2 billion (approximately US$2.3 billion). The project involved 60 companies and significant construction efforts over 14 years.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 01-07-2022
   - **Details:** The Nant de Drance plant commenced operations after 14 years of construction. It is set to be officially inaugurated in September 2022.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 30-06-2022
   - **Details:** At the peak of construction, the project employed 650 workers on-site, indicating significant job creation during the development phase.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 30-06-2022
   - **Details:** The ownership of the facility is shared among several stakeholders: Alpiq holds a 39% stake, SFR (Swiss national railway company) has a 36% stake, Industrielle Werke Basel (IWB) owns 15%, and Canton-owned FMV holds 10%. This indicates that the facility is not under exclusive ownership by any single company but is a joint venture among multiple entities.
