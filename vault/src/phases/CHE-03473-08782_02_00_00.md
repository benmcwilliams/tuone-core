### Evolutionary Timeline of the Energie Wasser Bern Facility in Bern, Switzerland

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **March 2023**: The facility is involved in the "Geospeicher" project, which aims to explore geo-storage of heat from waste disposal. The project will utilize excess heat from the waste incineration facility and store it 500 meters underground in a sandstone layer of the Molasse Basin. The system is expected to achieve 80% efficiency after ten years of operation.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **March 2023**: The funding for the project is structured as follows: Energie Wasser Bern (ewb) provides 44% of the funding, the Federal Office of Energy grants a 51% subsidy, and the remaining 5% is sourced from the eco fund of the city of Bern.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **March 2023**: Drilling is ongoing to reach the targeted sandstone layer, with initial drilling reaching 250 meters. Qualitative results from the geo-storage system are expected by the end of 2023. If successful, integration into the energy center is projected for 2026 at the earliest.
