### H2Uri Facility Evolution Timeline - Bürglen, Switzerland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2024**: Axpo and partners broke ground on a new hydrogen production plant under the H2Uri company, located adjacent to the EWA-Energie Uri hydropower plant. The facility is designed to produce up to **260 tonnes of green hydrogen annually**, which will significantly reduce diesel usage by over **one million litres**. The hydrogen produced will be utilized to power the passenger vessel ‘Saphir’ on Lake Lucerne, which is undergoing conversion to a hydrogen fuel cell system.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2024**: The establishment of the H2Uri facility is a collaborative investment by Axpo, Schätzle AG, EWA-Energie Uri, and SGV Holding AG. Specific investment amounts have not been disclosed, but the project is part of Axpo's broader strategy to enhance the hydrogen economy in Switzerland.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2024**: Announcement of the H2Uri company, with Axpo, EWA-Energie Uri, and AVIA Schätzle participating to supply locally produced hydrogen for the passenger vessel on Lake Lucerne.
- **December 2024**: Groundbreaking ceremony for the hydrogen production plant, with plans to have the facility operational by **2025**.

- The articles do not specify exact job creation or job loss figures related to the H2Uri facility. However, the establishment of the facility and the conversion of the passenger vessel suggest potential job creation in construction and operations.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The H2Uri facility is a **joint venture** among four partners: Axpo, Schätzle AG, EWA-Energie Uri, and SGV Holding AG. Each partner holds shares in the company, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by any single entity.
