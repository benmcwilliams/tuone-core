### Evolutionary Timeline of the Axpo Facility in Domat/Ems, Switzerland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2023**: Axpo and Rhiienergie announced the construction of a 2.5 MW hydrogen production facility at the Reichenau hydropower plant in Domat/Ems. The facility is designed to produce approximately 350 tonnes of green hydrogen annually, equivalent to saving about 1.5 million litres of diesel fuel.
- **April 2024**: The facility officially opened, marking it as Switzerland's first 2.5 MW green hydrogen production plant. The hydrogen is produced through water electrolysis using green electricity from the adjacent Reichenau hydropower plant. The facility also includes on-site hydrogen compression for delivery to refuelling stations and industrial customers.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2023**: Axpo and Rhiienergie jointly invested over CHF 8 million (approximately $8.7 million) in the hydrogen production facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **23 January 2023**: Construction began after completing the building permit process.
- **Fall 2023**: The hydrogen production facility was scheduled to commence operations.
- **April 2024**: The facility was officially opened, confirming its operational status.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or job loss figures related to the facility's construction or operation.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Axpo holds a majority stake in the Reichenau hydropower plant, which is directly connected to the hydrogen production facility. The facility is a joint venture between Axpo and Rhiienergie, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by Axpo.
