## CHE-04040-05918

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Rhiienergie Facility in Domat/Ems, Switzerland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2023**: Axpo and Rhiienergie announced the construction of a 2.5 MW hydrogen production facility at the Reichenau hydropower plant in Domat/Ems. The facility is designed to produce approximately 350 tonnes of green hydrogen annually, which is equivalent to saving about 1.5 million litres of diesel fuel for the region.
- **April 2024**: The facility officially opened, marking it as Switzerland's first 2.5 MW green hydrogen production plant. The construction took one year, and the facility includes on-site hydrogen compression capabilities.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2023**: Axpo and Rhiienergie jointly invested over CHF 8 million (approximately $8.7 million) in the hydrogen production facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2023**: After completing the building permit process, construction began on January 23, 2023, with the facility scheduled to go into operation in the fall of 2023.
- **April 2024**: The facility was officially opened, confirming its operational status.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the facility's construction or operation.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is a joint venture between Axpo and Rhiienergie, with Axpo holding a majority share in the Reichenau hydropower plant, which powers the hydrogen production facility.
