### Evolutionary Timeline of the SGV Facility in Lake Lucerne, CHE

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2024:** SGV, in collaboration with Axpo, EWA-energieUri, and AVIA Schätzle, announced plans to produce green hydrogen at a facility in Bürglen, set to commence operations in 2025. The facility will have a production capacity of up to 260 tonnes of green hydrogen per year and a power capacity of 2 MW. The hydrogen produced will be used to power the MS Saphir, a hydrogen passenger vessel owned by SGV.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **February 2024:** The project is supported by Switzerland’s Federal Office of Transport (FOT), indicating governmental investment or subsidy involvement, although specific financial figures were not disclosed.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **February 2024:** Preparatory work for the hydrogen production plant at the Bürglen hydropower site has begun. The conversion of the MS Saphir to a hydrogen fuel cell system is expected to be completed by autumn 2025.
