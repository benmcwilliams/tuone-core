### Evolutionary Timeline of the Axpo Facility in Muttsee Dam, Switzerland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 02-09-2022
   - **Details:** Axpo commissioned a 2.2 MW vertical alpine photovoltaic (PV) project on the Muttsee Dam. This facility is noted as the largest alpine solar plant in Switzerland, expected to supply approximately 3.3 million kWh of electricity annually, with a higher yield during winter months. The installation consists of 5,000 solar modules and is designed to produce around 3 million kWh of electricity per year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 02-09-2022
   - **Details:** The project had a total investment of CHF 8 million (approximately $8.2 million). Axpo secured a power purchase agreement with the supermarket chain Denner in January 2022, marking it as the first bilateral agreement for an unsubsidized solar project in Switzerland.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 02-09-2022
   - **Details:** The construction of the PV plant was completed, and the facility was officially commissioned on this date.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 02-09-2022
   - **Details:** Axpo owns the facility, but IWB, a Basel-based energy company, holds a 49% stake in the project through its Paneco subsidiary, indicating a joint venture ownership structure.
