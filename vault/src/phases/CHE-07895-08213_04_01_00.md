### Evolutionary Timeline of the ABB Group Facility in Abu Qir, Egypt

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2024**: ABB Group, in collaboration with Abu Qir Fertilizers, signed an agreement to install an advanced automated control system at the Abu Qir Ammonia Plant (1). This system aims to reduce natural gas consumption for steam boilers by 2% to 4% and improve overall operational efficiency. The project is part of a broader initiative to enhance energy resource utilization and operational efficiency across the Abu Qir plants (2) and (3).

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2024**: The collaboration with MPS involves supplying green hydrogen generated by water electrolysis powered by renewable energy. This green hydrogen will partially replace natural gas in the production processes of Abu Qir Fertilizers, contributing to compliance with European Union emissions standards and enhancing production capacity at the Abu Qir Ammonia Plant (1).

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2024**: Three significant agreements were signed:
  - An agreement between Abu Qir Fertilizers and MPS for the supply of green hydrogen.
  - An agreement between ABB Group and MPS for the execution of the green hydrogen project.
  - An agreement between ABB Group and Abu Qir Fertilizers for the installation of an advanced automated control system at the Abu Qir Ammonia Plant (1).
