### Evolutionary Timeline of the Electrique du Simplon (EES) Facility in Gondo-Zwischbergen, CHE

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 10-02-2022
   - **Details:** Alpiq announced the construction of the Gondosolar project, an 18MW vertical bifacial solar plant in Gondo-Zwischbergen. The facility is designed to produce approximately 23.3 million kWh of electricity annually, utilizing 4,500 bifacial modules over a 10-hectare area. The project is noted for its high efficiency, producing around twice as much electricity per square meter compared to similar facilities on the Swiss plateau, with 55% of its electricity generated in winter.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 10-02-2022
   - **Details:** The total investment required for the Gondosolar project is CHF 42 million (approximately $45.5 million). The project is contingent upon securing a maximum subsidy of 60% from the federal parliament, which is essential for its economic viability.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 10-02-2022
   - **Details:** Alpiq is in the process of obtaining the necessary approvals and funding for the project. Construction is expected to be completed within three years from the start date, although the exact start date is not specified in the article.
