### Evolutionary Timeline of the Alpiq Facility in Splügen, Switzerland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 12-05-2020
   - **Details:** Construction work has begun on the Hüscherabach small hydropower plant, which has a production capacity of 1.9 MW. The new plant will replace an old facility built in the 1930s and is expected to increase energy generation from 1.1 million kWh to approximately 6.1 million kWh per annum, sufficient to power around 1,500 households. The facility will utilize the Hüscherabach stream and will feature a high-pressure system with a Pelton turbine.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 12-05-2020
   - **Details:** Alpiq and the municipality of Rheinwald are investing approximately CHF 10 million in the construction of the Hüscherabach small hydropower plant. The project has also received support from the Swiss federal investment incentive programme for renewable energy generation.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 12-05-2020
   - **Details:** Construction work has officially started on the Hüscherabach small hydropower plant, with the project expected to last around 18 months. The project is currently in the development phase and is scheduled for implementation over the next few years.
