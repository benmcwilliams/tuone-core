## CHE-07968-09510

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Municipality of Rheinwald Facility in Splügen, CHE

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 12-05-2020
   - **Details:** Construction work has commenced on the Hüscherabach small hydropower plant, which has a production capacity of 1.9 MW. This facility is set to replace an older plant built in the 1930s. The new plant will increase renewable energy production from 1.1 million kWh to approximately 6.1 million kWh per annum, sufficient to power around 1,500 households. The facility will utilize the Hüscherabach stream and will feature a high-pressure system with a Pelton turbine.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 12-05-2020
   - **Details:** The total investment for the construction of the Hüscherabach small hydropower plant is approximately CHF 10 million, shared between Alpiq and the municipality of Rheinwald. The project has also received support from the Swiss federal investment incentive programme for renewable energy generation.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 12-05-2020
   - **Details:** The project is currently in the development phase, with construction expected to last around 18 months. The facility is scheduled for implementation over the next few years.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 12-05-2020
   - **Details:** The article does not specify job creation or job loss related to this project.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 12-05-2020
   - **Details:** The ownership of the facility is shared between Alpiq (60%) and the municipality of Rheinwald (40%) through Wasserkraftwerk Hüscherabach AG.
