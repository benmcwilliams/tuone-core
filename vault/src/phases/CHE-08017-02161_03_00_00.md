### Evolutionary Timeline of the Axpo Facility in Pradapunt, CHE

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - The Pradapunt hydropower plant is designed to produce 42 GWh of hydropower annually. It aims to address the gap in the power plant cascade on the Plessur and mitigate the negative environmental impacts caused by discharge fluctuations from the Litzirüti power plant.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - The 'Wasserkraft Plessur' consortium, which includes Axpo, Arosa Energie, and IBC Energie Wasser Chur, is planning to submit a revised license application in the upcoming year (2024). However, specific investment amounts tied to expansions or capacity have not been disclosed yet.
