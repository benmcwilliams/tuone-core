### Evolutionary Timeline of the Axpo Facility in Alp da Rueun, Switzerland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10/2023**: Axpo announced plans to build a second solar park named **Ovra Solara Rueun** in Alp da Rueun, with a production capacity of **30MW** and an expected output of **44GWh** of renewable power annually, sufficient for over **9,400 households**. This facility will cover an area of approximately **500,000 square meters**. The project aims to ensure that the site can coexist harmoniously with existing land uses, including ski slopes and hiking trails.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **10/2023**: The announcement of the Ovra Solara Rueun project is part of Axpo's broader ambition to generate a total of **66GWh** of sustainable electricity in the Ilanz/Glion region, which will also include the **Ovra Solara Camplauns** site (15MW capacity, producing 22GWh). Specific investment amounts for the Ovra Solara Rueun project have not been disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10/2023**: Axpo informed the local community about the two solar projects, including Ovra Solara Rueun. The public is expected to vote on the projects in the coming weeks. Assuming a positive outcome and obtaining the necessary building permits, construction is slated to begin in **spring 2025**, with partial commissioning anticipated by **late 2025**.

#### 4. Labour
- **10/2023**: While specific job creation numbers have not been detailed, the projects are expected to contribute to local employment opportunities during the construction phase and potentially in ongoing operations.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **10/2023**: The articles do not specify whether Axpo will have exclusive ownership of the Ovra Solara Rueun facility or if it will be part of a joint venture. However, it is indicated that Axpo is the developer and operator of the project.
