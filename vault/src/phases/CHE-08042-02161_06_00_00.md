### Evolutionary Timeline of the Axpo Facility in Swiss Alps, CHE

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **08-05-2023**: Axpo announced plans to build a 10 MW solar plant named the Ovra Solara Magriel project on an 80,000 m² surface at an altitude of 2,100 meters in the Swiss Alps. This facility is intended to provide power to the mountain railway and a ski area. Additionally, Axpo is planning to add another 10 MW solar project called NalpSolar, which will complement the existing AlpinSolar project, a 2.2 MW facility already in production.

- **12-06-2023**: Axpo reported inflow funds of CHF1.5 billion in the first half of the 2022/23 financial year, which will be used to invest in Swiss renewables and grid infrastructure.
