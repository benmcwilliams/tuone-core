## CHE-08057-04570

### Evolutionary Timeline of the BKW Facility in Mühleberg, Switzerland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **1972**: The Mühleberg facility, comprising a single 373 MWe boiling water reactor, began operations.
- **20 December 2019**: The facility was shut down.
- **6 January 2020**: Dismantling operations commenced.
- **15 September 2020**: The facility was considered permanently out of service when its operating license was replaced by a decommissioning order.
- **April 2022**: The first used fuel elements were sent to the interim storage facility in Würenlingen, with a total of 66 shipments required to move all fuel.
- **September 2022**: The milestone of removing all 418 fuel elements was reached, 16 months ahead of the original schedule.
- **Late June 2022**: BKW submitted an application for the second decommissioning phase to the Swiss Federal Nuclear Safety Inspectorate (ENSI).
- **End of 2030**: Mühleberg is expected to be free of radioactive material, with conventional dismantling set to begin in 2031.
- **2027**: BKW plans to submit an application for conventional dismantling to the authorities.
- **2034**: The site is expected to be available for other uses.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2023**: BKW contracted Uniper Nuclear Services to dismantle, disassemble, and package two moisture separator reheaters at Mühleberg. The financial details of this contract were not disclosed.
- **End of 2025**: The UfG consortium (Uniper Nuclear Services, Framatome, and GNS) will start work on dismantling and packaging components, including the reactor pressure vessel.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2020**: Dismantling operations began.
- **September 2022**: Completion of the first decommissioning phase with the removal of all fuel elements.
- **March 2023**: Contract awarded for dismantling of moisture separator reheaters.
- **End of 2025**: Scheduled start for dismantling of the reactor pressure vessel.
- **2031**: Conventional dismantling is set to begin.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify job creation or job loss directly related to the dismantling operations or contracts awarded.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- BKW owns the Mühleberg facility exclusively.
