## CHE-08057-08911

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Framatome GmbH Facility in Mühleberg, CHE

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Mühleberg facility, which began operations in 1972, is a single 373 MWe boiling water reactor. It was shut down on December 20, 2019, and has been undergoing decommissioning since January 6, 2020. The facility is currently in the process of dismantling various components, including the reactor pressure vessel (RPV) and other associated parts. The dismantling operations are divided into three phases, with the first phase completed in September 2021, 16 months ahead of schedule.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The financial details regarding the dismantling contract have not been disclosed. However, the contract for the removal of the reactor vessel and other components involves the UfG consortium, which includes Framatome GmbH. The second decommissioning phase, which involves dismantling all remaining plant components that have come into contact with radioactivity, is expected to be submitted for approval by BKW to the Swiss Federal Nuclear Safety Inspectorate in late June 2022.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **January 6, 2020:** Dismantling operations began.
   - **September 2021:** Completion of the first decommissioning phase (removal of all 418 fuel elements).
   - **March 2023:** Uniper Nuclear Services contracted to dismantle and package two moisture separator reheaters.
   - **End of 2025:** Work on the dismantling of the reactor pressure vessel is scheduled to start.
   - **By the end of 2030:** Mühleberg is expected to be free of radioactive material.
   - **2027:** BKW plans to submit an application for conventional dismantling to the authorities.
   - **2031:** Conventional dismantling is set to begin.
   - **2034:** The site is expected to be available for other uses.

4. **Labour:**
   - The articles do not specify job creation or job loss directly related to the dismantling operations at the Mühleberg facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The Mühleberg facility is owned by BKW, and the dismantling operations are being conducted by the UfG consortium, which includes Framatome GmbH, indicating that Framatome GmbH does not have exclusive ownership of the facility.
