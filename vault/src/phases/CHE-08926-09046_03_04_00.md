### Evolutionary Timeline of the Grande Dixence SA Facility in Bieudron, Switzerland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Capacity**: The Bieudron power station is noted as Switzerland's most formidable hydroelectric facility with a production capacity of 1,200 MW.
- **Renovation**: A comprehensive renovation of the Grande Dixence facility took place over a six-year period from 2016 to 2023. This included a significant overhaul of the entire downhill conduit connecting Lac des Dix to the Rhône plain, as well as retrofitting all six generating units at the Fionnay and Nendaz power stations with new valves, turbines, alternators, and automatic control systems.
- **Operational Resumption**: Operations gradually resumed at the beginning of 2023, with the expectation that both plants would be fully operational by the end of the winter season in 2023-2024.
