### Evolutionary Timeline of the Grande Dixence SA Facility in Fionnay, CHE

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2016-2023**: Comprehensive renovation of the Grande Dixence Hydroelectric Project, which includes the Fionnay and Nendaz power stations. The renovation involved a complete overhaul of the downhill conduit connecting Lac des Dix to the Rhône plain, subdivided into 89 distinct sub-projects.
- **2023**: Gradual resumption of operations began at the start of the year, with systematic reintegration of the six generating units at both power stations. The facilities are expected to be fully operational by the end of the winter season in 2023-2024.
- **Renovation Details**: Each generating unit underwent retrofitting, including new valves, turbines, alternators, automatic control systems, and auxiliary services. The penstock was overhauled, with significant replacements and refurbishments of main valves.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Total Investment**: CHF 240 million allocated by Grande Dixence SA and its shareholders for the renovation project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2022**: The entire downhill line was reopened, allowing water to be supplied to the generating plants.
- **2023**: The generating units began systematic reintegration into operation, contributing to Switzerland's electricity supply, particularly during the winter.
