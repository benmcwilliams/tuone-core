### Evolutionary Timeline of the Grande Dixence SA Facility in Nendaz, Switzerland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2016-2023**: Comprehensive renovation of the Grande Dixence Hydroelectric Project, which includes the overhaul of the downhill conduit connecting Lac des Dix to the Rhône plain. The facility consists of two power stations: Fionnay and Nendaz. Each of the six generating units has undergone retrofitting, including new valves, turbines, alternators, automatic control systems, and auxiliary services.
- **September 2022**: The entire downhill line was reopened, allowing water to be supplied to the generating plants.
- **Start of 2023**: Gradual resumption of operations for the generating units, with full operational status expected by the end of the winter season 2023-2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Total Investment**: CHF 240 million allocated by Grande Dixence SA and its shareholders for the renovation project, which was divided into 89 distinct sub-projects.
