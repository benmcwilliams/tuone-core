### Evolutionary Timeline of the Meyer Burger Facility in Hauterive, Switzerland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2014**: Meyer Burger officially opened its heterojunction (HJ) solar cell pilot production line in collaboration with CSEM at its subsidiary, Meyer Burger Research AG in Hauterive. The pilot line has a production capacity of 600 kilowatts. The facility focuses on the industrialized manufacturing of high-performance solar cells, specifically targeting a PV module efficiency of 21% with a production cost below 0.6 CHF/Wp (US$0.62/Wp). The technology involves a low-temperature PECVD process developed in collaboration with ETH Lausanne, which allows for reduced energy costs and less silver usage.
