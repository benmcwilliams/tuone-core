### Evolutionary Timeline of Viteos SA Facility in Lake Neuchâtel, CHE

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** January 2013
   - **Details:** Viteos SA is constructing three floating laboratories on Lake Neuchâtel, designed to utilize Concentrated Solar Power (CSP) technology. Each floating laboratory has a diameter of 25 meters and is equipped with 100 photovoltaic (PV) panels, which are arranged back-to-back at a 45° incline. The laboratories are designed to track the sun throughout the day, rotating 220° to maximize solar exposure. The facility aims to demonstrate the effectiveness of CSP on water and explore its adaptability to other solar technologies.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** January 2013
   - **Details:** Viteos SA plans to invest over CHF 100 million (approximately US$108 million) in the development of the floating laboratories and associated renewable energy projects. This investment is part of a broader strategy to enhance renewable energy output.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** January 2013
   - **Details:** The construction of the floating laboratories is currently underway, with a completion target set for August 2013. The facilities will be connected to the electricity grid via Viteos inverters.
