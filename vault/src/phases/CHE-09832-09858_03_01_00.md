1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is the **27 MW Robbia hydropower plant** located in Valposchiavo, Switzerland. 
   - The plant consists of a machine hall, technical and storage rooms, and an operations office, equipped with four horizontal-axis machine groups.
   - The facility is undergoing a **complete renovation**, with plans to renew around **90% of the system**. This includes the replacement of machine groups with three identical sets, renewal of the crane system, and optimization of the headrace system to minimize hydraulic losses.
