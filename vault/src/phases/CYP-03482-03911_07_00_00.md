### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ecotricity Holdings Facility at the University of Cyprus, CYP

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **September 2023**: The University of Cyprus, in collaboration with Ecotricity Holdings, installed a nanogrid system for a single building on campus. The nanogrid features:
     - A 40 kWp building-integrated photovoltaic (BIPV) system.
     - 66 kWh (60 kW) of energy storage capacity.
     - A 21 kW EV charging station.
     - An energy management system.
   - The facility aims to minimize energy consumption costs and eliminate the carbon footprint of the power system. It is noted as the first hybrid nanogrid system in Cyprus, capable of operating in both grid-connected and islanding modes.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - The project was primarily financed by the European Union. Ecotricity Holdings was selected through a tender process by the University of Cyprus.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **September 2023**: The installation of the nanogrid system was announced and completed, marking the operational start of the facility.
