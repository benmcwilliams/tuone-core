#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2020**: The Hyundai plant in Nošovice begins production of the Kona Electric, specifically the 150-kW model with a 64 kWh battery. The initial production capacity is set at over 30,000 units in the first year, with 150 electric cars produced daily.
- **August 2023**: Hyundai starts series production of the updated Kona Electric model, planning to produce around 21,000 units by the end of 2023 and an additional 50,000 in 2024. The new model features a 114.6 kW powertrain and a battery capacity starting at 48.4 kWh, with a maximum driving range of 514 km for the Long Range variant.
