### Evolutionary Timeline of the Vitesco Facility in Ostrava, Czech Republic

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** January 2024
   - **Details:** Vitesco Technologies is establishing a new facility in Ostrava, primarily focused on producing components for electric vehicles, particularly power electronics. The facility will cover 38,000 square meters and is expected to go online by the end of 2024. The products will include an advanced power electronics system that integrates an inverter, onboard charger, rapid charging module, and energy distribution unit.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** January 2024
   - **Details:** Vitesco is investing 188 million euros in the new facility. This investment is part of a larger strategy, with the company planning to invest over 576 million euros in sustainable mobility initiatives in the Czech Republic by 2027. Additionally, Vitesco has a long-term supply agreement with Onsemi for silicon carbide semiconductors, valued at 1.75 billion euros over ten years.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** January 2024
   - **Details:** The announcement of the new plant in Ostrava marks the beginning of the project. Construction is set to commence, with the facility expected to be operational by the end of 2024.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** January 2024
   - **Details:** The facility will initially create 150 jobs, with plans to increase employment to over 1,000 by 2027. An additional 300 jobs will be generated by a research and development center located on-site.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** January 2024
   - **Details:** Vitesco Technologies will own the facility exclusively, as there is no mention of joint ventures or partnerships in the provided information.
