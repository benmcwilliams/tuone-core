### Evolutionary Timeline of the VSB – Technical University of Ostrava Facility in Ostrava, CZE

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 10-05-2024**
  - The facility is designed to produce green hydrogen using a combination of photovoltaic (PV) panels and wind turbines. The proposed system includes:
    - **Photovoltaic Panels**: Capacity of 298 kW
    - **Wind Turbines**: 22 units, each with a capacity of 100 kW
    - **Electrolyzer**: Capacity of 1000 kW
    - **Hydrogen Tanks**: Capacity of 800 kg
    - **Batteries**: 30 strings of 1 kWh lead-acid batteries
  - The facility aims to meet a hydrogen demand of 10 kg per hour (240 kg per day) and is expected to produce approximately 85,595 kg of hydrogen annually. The annual energy generation is estimated at 300,425 kWh from PV and 6,697,566 kWh from wind.

### Summary
The VSB – Technical University of Ostrava facility in Ostrava, Czech Republic, is focused on developing a green hydrogen production system powered by renewable energy sources. As of May 2024, the facility is in the design and analysis phase, with significant details on technology, production capacity, and economic viability outlined, but lacks information on job impacts and ownership structure.

### Technology and Operations
- "The proposed system consists of wind turbines, PV panels, an electrolyzer, an inverter, hydrogen tanks, and batteries."
- "The optimal design of the plant determines the size of the components as follows: photovoltaic panels with a capacity of 298 kW, 22 wind turbines with 100 kW each, 30 strings of 1 kWh lead acid battery, electrolyzer of 1000 kW capacity, and hydrogen tanks with a capacity of 800 kg."
- "The PV unit can generate up to 300,425 kWh/year and the wind turbine 6,697,566 kWh/year."
- "The electrolyzers' annual energy consumption is around 3,972,059 kW, and their annual production is estimated at 85,595 kg."

### Investment and Development
- "The levelized cost of hydrogen (LCH) is 2.89 € ($3.12)/kg of hydrogen while net present cost (NPC) is €5.49 million excluding compressor unit costs."
- "The wind turbine and electrolyzer system have the highest NPC."

### Project Status and Milestones
