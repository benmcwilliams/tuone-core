### Evolutionary Timeline of the ORLEN Group Facility in Litvínov, Czech Republic

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2020**: The ORLEN Group announced plans to construct hydrogen refueling stations at two Czech Benzina stations, including one in Litvínov. These stations are part of a broader initiative to develop environmentally friendly hydrogen technology, with hydrogen produced during the processing of crude oil at UNIPETROL refineries in Litvínov and Kralupy.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2020**: The ORLEN Group plans to invest over PLN 25 billion in projects aimed at reducing environmental impact and developing alternative fuels and low carbon technologies. This investment is part of their strategy to lead in the energy transformation in Poland and Central Europe.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2020**: Announcement of the construction of hydrogen refueling stations at the Benzina-Grupa ORLEN fuel service stations in Litvínov and Prague, with the expectation that these stations will be operational in the following year (2021).

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2020**: While specific job creation numbers were not mentioned, the construction of hydrogen refueling stations and the development of hydrogen technology imply potential job creation in the construction and operational phases of the facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **September 2020**: The facility is part of the ORLEN Group, with UNIPETROL, a Czech company within the group, overseeing the construction of the hydrogen refueling station. The ownership structure indicates that the ORLEN Group has full ownership of the facility in Litvínov.
