### Evolutionary Timeline of the CEZ Facility in Štěchovice, Czech Republic

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2022**: CEZ Group completed construction of a 22kW pilot floating photovoltaic project on the upper reservoir of its pumped-hydro storage power plant in Štěchovice. The facility operates at varying heights during the charge and recharge cycles of the pumped-hydro plant, with a height difference of up to nine meters. The photovoltaic system is designed with air-filled floats capable of supporting over six tons, ensuring stability against adverse weather and water movement. The Štěchovice pumped-hydro storage facility can supply a total of 200MWh of electricity in four hours.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2022**: The pilot floating photovoltaic project represents an investment in innovative solar technology, although specific financial details regarding this project were not disclosed in the articles. CEZ Group has previously invested in solar technology, including a €10 million investment in the German start-up Zolar in September 2019.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2022**: CEZ Group announced the completion of the pilot floating photovoltaic project, marking it as Czechia's first floating solar plant. The company expressed intentions to potentially increase the output of the pilot installation five times during the summer if the initial results are promising.
