#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **1992**: The ZF facility in Klášterec began operations, producing power electronics components.
- **February 2024**: ZF opened its first "zero-emission factory" at the Klášterec site after a conversion period of approximately 1.5 years. The facility is part of the Electrified Powertrain Technology division and serves as a blueprint for ZF plants globally. The plant utilizes around 3,400 solar modules, generating approximately 20% of its annual electricity needs from photovoltaic (PV) sources. The facility operates without fossil fuels, employing heat pumps for heating and utilizing natural ambient cold for air conditioning.

#### 2. Investment and Development

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
