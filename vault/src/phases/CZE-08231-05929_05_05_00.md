## CZE-08231-05929

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Rolls-Royce SMR Facility in Dětmarovice, Czech Republic

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2024**: Rolls-Royce SMR is selected for a small modular reactor (SMR) project in the Czech Republic. The Rolls-Royce SMR design is a 470 MWe small pressurized water reactor, which will provide consistent baseload generation for at least 60 years. The facility will primarily focus on the assembly of pre-fabricated, pre-tested modules, significantly reducing project risk and potentially shortening build schedules.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2024**: The Czech government, through ČEZ (the Czech nuclear power company), is establishing a strategic partnership with Rolls-Royce SMR. This partnership is expected to create opportunities for local companies to participate in the development and implementation of the SMR technology, contributing to the global supply chain.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2024**: The Czech Ministry of Trade announces that Rolls-Royce SMR has emerged as the best candidate for the SMR project, with plans for the first reactor to be located near the existing Temelin nuclear power plant in the 2030s. The government is conducting a safety assessment of Rolls-Royce SMR as part of the selection process.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2024**: The strategic partnership is expected to create jobs in the Czech Republic, as local companies will be involved in the development and supply chain for the SMR technology.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **September 2024**: The facility is part of a strategic partnership between ČEZ and Rolls-Royce SMR, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by either party. Specific terms of the cooperation are still under negotiation.

This timeline captures the key developments related to the Rolls-Royce SMR facility in Dětmarovice, focusing on its technology, investments, project milestones, labor implications, and ownership structure as of September 2024.
