### Evolutionary Timeline of the ČEZ Group Facility in Temelín, Czech Republic

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** ČEZ Group plans to build its first small modular reactor (SMR) near the existing Temelín nuclear power plant. This initiative aims to enhance energy security in Czechia and is part of a broader strategy to transition to cleaner energy sources. The SMRs are intended to provide clean, affordable, and reliable electricity, contributing to decarbonization efforts.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** The Czech government and ČEZ Group have reached an agreement with Rolls-Royce SMR, which has been named the preferred supplier for the development of SMRs. Specific investment amounts or subsidies related to this project have not been disclosed yet, as discussions are ongoing to finalize contract terms.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** The announcement marks a significant milestone in the development of SMRs in Czechia. The completion of the first SMR is planned before the commissioning of a new large nuclear unit, which is expected to be built before 2040. The facility is also exploring additional sites for future SMRs, including Dětmarovice and Tušimice.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** The development of SMRs is expected to create jobs in the region, although specific job creation numbers have not been provided in the article.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** ČEZ Group is pursuing this project in partnership with Rolls-Royce SMR, indicating a collaborative approach rather than exclusive ownership of the facility. Further details regarding the ownership structure will be clarified upon the finalization of agreements.

This timeline captures the key developments related to the ČEZ Group facility in Temelín, focusing on the introduction of small modular reactors and the strategic partnership with Rolls-Royce SMR.
