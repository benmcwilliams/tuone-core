### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDF Facility at Dukovany Power Plant II, Czech Republic

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 01-11-2023
   - **Details:** The facility is set to include a new nuclear unit (Dukovany Unit 5) as part of the Dukovany Power Plant II. The new unit is scheduled for trial operation in 2036 and will be built adjacent to the existing power plant. Bids have been submitted by Westinghouse Electric, EDF, and KHNP for the construction of this unit.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 01-11-2023
   - **Details:** The bids submitted by the three companies are part of a competitive process to secure contracts for the new nuclear unit. The evaluation of these bids will lead to final contracts being awarded before November 2024, indicating significant investment in the facility's expansion.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 01-11-2023
   - **Details:** The bidding process for the new nuclear unit was initiated in March 2022, with the deadline for bids extended to 31 October 2023. The project is currently in the evaluation phase, with a report to be submitted to the Ministry of Industry and Trade and the Czech government for final approval.
