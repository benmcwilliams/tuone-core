### Evolutionary Timeline of the TÜV Rheinland Facility in Aachen, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 08-06-2022
   - **Details:** TÜV Rheinland opened its Battery Test Centre in Aachen, described as the most modern independent test centre in Europe. The facility is designed to conduct electrical tests, durability simulations, and safety tests, including destructive testing of batteries. It can test traction batteries weighing up to 800 kilograms and with energy content up to 150 kWh. The facility includes seven climatic chambers for simulating extreme temperatures and humidity, as well as specialized test chambers for various mechanical and environmental stresses.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 08-06-2022
   - **Details:** The total investment in the facility amounted to 24 million euros, which was made by TÜV Rheinland and its partners. This investment was specifically tied to the establishment of the Battery Test Centre.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 08-06-2022
   - **Details:** The Battery Test Centre was officially opened on this date, marking the completion of the project that began with its announcement in October 2020. The facility is now operational and ready to conduct various battery tests.

4. **Labour:**
