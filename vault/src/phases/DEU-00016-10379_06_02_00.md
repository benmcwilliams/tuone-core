1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2007
   - **Details:** WACKER SCHOTT Solar GmbH, a joint venture between Wacker Chemie AG and SCHOTT AG, laid the cornerstone for a new silicon solar wafer manufacturing facility in Jena, Germany. The facility is expected to reach a production capacity of 100MW by the end of 2008. The manufacturing equipment will be installed in a two-storey building with a floorspace of over 7,500m².
