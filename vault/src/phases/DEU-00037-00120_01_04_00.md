### Evolutionary Timeline of the CATL Facility in Arnstadt, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **02/2019**: CATL announces plans to invest €1.8 billion in its German battery factory, initially targeting a production capacity of 14 GWh by 2022.
- **04/2022**: The facility's production capacity is adjusted to 8 GWh for 2022 due to delays, with plans for future expansion to 24 GWh.
- **12/2022**: CATL officially starts series production of battery cells at the Arnstadt facility, achieving an initial capacity of 8 GWh. Production of battery modules began in Q3 2021.
- **03/2023**: CATL appoints a new CEO for Europe, indicating ongoing operational developments and management structure changes.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **02/2019**: CATL announces an investment increase from €240 million to €1.8 billion for the Arnstadt facility due to rising demand for battery cells.
- **04/2022**: The state of Thuringia grants a partial permit for the first expansion stage of the plant, facilitating the ramp-up of production.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10/2019**: Groundbreaking ceremony for the Arnstadt facility takes place.
- **04/2022**: CATL receives an operating license for the first expansion stage of the plant.
- **12/2022**: Series production of battery cells officially begins at the facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **02/2019**: Initial job creation estimates are set at 600 positions.
- **04/2022**: The workforce is expected to grow to about 1,500 employees by the end of the year, with a total of up to 2,000 jobs anticipated in the medium term.
- **12/2022**: By the end of the year, 500 employees from the region are reported to be working at the facility, with additional support from specialists from China.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is owned exclusively by CATL, as indicated by the company's direct investments and operational management in the facility.
