## DEU-00037-03485

### Evolutionary Timeline of SolarWorld Industries GmbH Facility in Arnstadt, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2017**: SolarWorld Industries GmbH officially starts production at its facility in Arnstadt, Thuringia. The facility focuses on monocrystalline ingot and solar cell production. The company has approximately 600MW of mono ingot/wafer capacity and 1,000MW of nameplate cell and module capacity in Germany. The facility is transitioning to higher efficiency P-type monocrystalline PERC (Passivated Emitter Rear Cell) technology, including bifacial cells and modules.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2017**: The formation of SolarWorld Industries GmbH and the acquisition of production plants from the insolvent SolarWorld AG were facilitated by investments from Qatar Solar Technologies (QST), which holds a significant share in the new company.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2017**: SolarWorld Industries GmbH begins production at the Arnstadt facility.
- **March 2018**: SolarWorld Industries GmbH files for insolvency for the second time within a year, citing decreased market prices and the planned phasing out of anti-dumping measures by the EU against Chinese solar imports.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2017**: A total of 515 former employees from SolarWorld AG transition to SolarWorld Industries GmbH, including 294 employees specifically from the Arnstadt facility. The workforce at the Arnstadt facility is part of a larger transfer of employees from the previous SolarWorld AG operations.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **August 2017**: SolarWorld Industries GmbH is majority owned by Dr. Ing. Eh Frank Asbeck (51%) and Qatar Solar Technologies (49%). The facility is not exclusively owned by one entity but is a joint venture between these two stakeholders.
