### Evolutionary Timeline of the Bluecell Facility in Arnstadt, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **June 2014:** Bluecell GmbH acquired the 100 MW solar cell production equipment from the insolvent German producer Sunways.
   - **May 2017:** Bluecell halted manufacturing activities at the Arnstadt facility due to strong price pressures in the market. The production equipment was set to be sold by the end of 2017.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **June 2014:** The acquisition of the production equipment from Sunways marked the initial investment into the facility, although specific financial details of the acquisition were not disclosed.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **May 2017:** Bluecell ceased production at the Arnstadt facility, with the closure being communicated to the 84 employees a few weeks prior.
   - **End of 2017:** Plans were made to auction off the production equipment, which was provided by Centrotherm, Jonas & Redmann, and Baccini.

4. **Labour:**
   - **May 2017:** The closure of the facility resulted in the loss of 84 jobs, as the employees were informed of the shutdown.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
