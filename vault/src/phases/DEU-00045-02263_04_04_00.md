### H-TEC Facility in Augsburg, DEU - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The H-TEC facility in Augsburg produces PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolysis stacks, which are essential components of electrolyzers used for hydrogen production. 
   - The facility is involved in the development and production of the S450 electrolysis stacks, which are designed for large-scale hydrogen production.
   - A new manufacturing facility is being established in Hamburg to automate the production of PEM electrolysis stacks, with a potential total electrolysis capacity of 5 GW, indicating a significant scale-up in operations.

2. **Investment and Development:**
