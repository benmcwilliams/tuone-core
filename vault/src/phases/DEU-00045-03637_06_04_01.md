## DEU-00045-03637

### Evolutionary Timeline of Tube Solar AG Facility in Augsburg, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2020**: Tube Solar AG produces lightweight thin-film solar tubes primarily for agrivoltaic use. The facility aims to expand its annual production capacity to **250 MW**. The solar tubes are designed to allow light and water to permeate for optimal plant growth, providing partial shading to crops and protection from adverse weather conditions.
- **07/2022**: Tube Solar AG, in collaboration with the Centre for Solar Energy and Hydrogen Research Baden-Württemberg (ZSW), achieves a **power conversion efficiency of 14%** for a perovskite-based photovoltaic film used in their solar tubes. The film is produced using roll-to-roll technology and slot-die coating, which minimizes material waste and operational costs.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09/2020**: The Bavarian Minister of Economic Affairs announces a grant of **€10.8 million** to Tube Solar AG to support the development of its agrivoltaic modules. This funding is aimed at enhancing the production capabilities of the facility.
- **06/2022**: Tube Solar AG increases its share capital from **€12 million** to **€13.2 million** through the issuance of new shares. The proceeds are intended to finance further growth and investments in highly automated production processes for their photovoltaic modules.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/2020**: Tube Solar AG begins production of its agrivoltaic solar tubes at a former Osram fluorescent tube production site in Augsburg.
- **07/2022**: The collaboration with ZSW marks a significant milestone in achieving higher efficiency for their solar tubes, indicating progress in technology development.
