## DEU-00047-00792

### Evolutionary Timeline of the TÜV Rheinland Facility in Avantis, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **October 2020:** TÜV Rheinland, in collaboration with ConAC, announced the establishment of a test laboratory for electric vehicle (EV) batteries in Avantis. The facility is set to become one of the largest and most modern independent battery test centers in Europe. It will focus on testing battery modules and packs, including controls and wiring, rather than individual cells. The facility will be capable of testing battery systems weighing up to 800 kilograms and will support charging and discharging at up to 500 kW nominal power. 
   - **September 2021:** The test laboratory is scheduled to start operations.
   - **April 2021:** Construction work is set to begin on an extension building adjacent to the main facility, which will include test facilities for destructive testing.
   - **June 2022:** The extension building is expected to be operational, housing facilities for vibration testing and destructive tests.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **October 2020:** The estimated investment for the 2,000 square meter facility is 22 million euros. This investment is aimed at establishing a state-of-the-art testing facility for EV batteries.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **October 2020:** Announcement of the test laboratory's establishment.
   - **September 2021:** Planned start of operations for the test laboratory.
   - **April 2021:** Commencement of construction for the extension building.
   - **June 2022:** Expected operational date for the extension building.

4. **Labour:**
   - **October 2020:** When fully operational, the test center is projected to employ 25 people.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **October 2020:** The facility will be operated by a joint venture, TÜV Rheinland Automotive Component Testing GmbH, where TÜV Rheinland holds a majority stake of 74.9%, while ConAC holds 25.1%. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones related to the TÜV Rheinland facility in Avantis, DEU, focusing on its technology, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
