## DEU-00047-00985

### Evolutionary Timeline of the ConAC Facility in Avantis, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2020**: ConAC, in collaboration with TÜV Rheinland, announced the establishment of a test laboratory for electric vehicle (EV) batteries in Avantis. The facility is set to become one of the largest independent battery test centers in Europe, focusing on testing battery modules and packs rather than individual cells. The facility will be equipped to handle battery systems weighing up to 800 kilograms and will conduct various tests, including electrical, mechanical, environmental simulations, and misuse tests. 

- **September 2021**: The test laboratory is scheduled to start operations. The existing building will be equipped with climate chambers for environmental simulations, corrosion chambers, and other test facilities.

- **April 2021**: Construction began on an extension building adjacent to the main facility, which is expected to be operational by June 2022. This extension will include facilities for destructive testing, such as a bunker for tests involving drop, pressure, crushing, fire simulation, and nail penetration.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2020**: The total investment for the facility is estimated at 22 million euros. This investment supports the establishment of the test laboratory and its operational capabilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2020**: Announcement of the test laboratory's establishment.
- **September 2021**: Scheduled start of operations for the test laboratory.
- **April 2021**: Commencement of construction for the extension building.
- **June 2022**: Expected operational date for the extension building.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2020**: When fully operational, the test center is projected to employ 25 people, contributing to job creation in the region.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **October 2020**: The facility is operated as a joint venture, with TÜV Rheinland holding a majority stake of 74.9% and ConAC holding 25.1%. ConAC is a subsidiary of PEM Aachen, which is associated with Achim Kampker, a co-founder of StreetScooter.
