## DEU-00051-00818

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Valmet Facility in Bad Friedrichshall, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2019**: Announcement of the establishment of a Battery Test Centre (BTC) in Bad Friedrichshall, which is part of Valmet Automotive's strategy to focus on e-mobility.
- **November 2020**: The BTC in Bad Friedrichshall becomes operational. This facility is designed to enhance testing capacities for battery technology.
- **February 2021**: Valmet Automotive begins the expansion of the BTC in Bad Friedrichshall, adding six additional fireproof test rooms to increase testing capabilities for high-voltage batteries.
- **July 2021**: The expansion of the BTC is reported to be progressing rapidly, with testing scheduled to begin in September 2021. The facility will cover endurance and lifetime tests for high-voltage batteries, including cells, modules, and packs.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2021**: Valmet Automotive announces an increase in engineering activities and investments in its Bad Friedrichshall site to strengthen battery systems development and testing capabilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2019**: Announcement of the Battery Test Centre in Bad Friedrichshall.
- **November 2020**: Official opening of the BTC.
- **February 2021**: Announcement of the expansion of the BTC.
- **July 2021**: Progress report on the expansion, with testing set to begin in September 2021.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2021**: Valmet Automotive announces the creation of new jobs in Germany, including positions for specialists in battery system testing at the Bad Friedrichshall facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **As of November 2020**: Valmet Automotive remains the sole owner and shareholder of the battery business, which includes the Bad Friedrichshall facility. The battery business was spun off as an independent company named IONCOR in 2023, but Valmet Automotive retains ownership of the facility.
