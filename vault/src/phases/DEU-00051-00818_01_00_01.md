### Evolutionary Timeline of the Valmet Facility in Bad Friedrichshall, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2019**: Announcement of the establishment of a Battery Test Centre (BTC) in Bad Friedrichshall, which is part of Valmet Automotive's strategy to enhance its capabilities in battery technology.
- **November 2020**: Launch of a new Battery Test Centre in Weihenbronn, which complements the existing BTC in Bad Friedrichshall, expanding testing capacities.
- **February 2021**: Valmet Automotive begins the expansion of the Bad Friedrichshall BTC, adding six fireproof test rooms to enhance testing capabilities for high-voltage batteries.
- **July 2021**: Completion of the expansion of the BTC in Bad Friedrichshall, allowing for endurance and lifetime tests on high-voltage batteries, including cells, modules, and packs.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2020-2021**: Valmet Automotive has made significant investments in expanding its battery testing and engineering capabilities in Germany, particularly in Bad Friedrichshall and Munich, although specific financial figures for these expansions are not disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2020**: The BTC in Bad Friedrichshall is operational, providing essential testing services for battery technology.
- **February 2021**: Announcement of the ongoing expansion of the BTC in Bad Friedrichshall, with completion expected in September 2021.
- **July 2021**: The expansion of the BTC is reported to be progressing rapidly, with testing scheduled to begin shortly.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2021**: Valmet Automotive announces the creation of new jobs in Germany, including positions for specialists in battery testing at the Bad Friedrichshall facility, as part of its expansion efforts.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **As of November 2020**: Valmet Automotive remains the sole owner and shareholder of the battery business, which includes the Bad Friedrichshall facility. The battery business was spun off as an independent entity under the name IONCOR, but Valmet retains full ownership.
