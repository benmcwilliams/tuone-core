### Evolutionary Timeline of the State Research Institute for Viticulture and Pomiculture (LVWO) Facility in Bad Friedrichshall, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: The LVWO facility at the Heuchlingen experimental fruit farm in Bad Friedrichshall commenced operations of its third pilot plant. This facility integrates agrivoltaics with the cultivation of soft fruits, specifically raspberries, blueberries, currants, strawberries, gooseberries, and blackberries, over an area of nearly 2,000 square meters. The agrivoltaic system has a production capacity of 113 kW and employs closed circuits for water and nutrients, utilizing rainwater collection and solar-powered processing for irrigation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: The facility is part of the Model Region Agri-photovoltaics for Baden-Württemberg research project, which involves collaboration with the Fraunhofer Institute for Solar Energy Systems and 11 other project partners.
