- **July 2010**: The Zentrum für Sonnenenergie- und Wasserstoff-Forschung Baden-Württemberg (ZSW) collaborates with Manz Automation AG and Würth Solar GmbH & Co. KG to enhance the production of CIGS (copper indium gallium (di)selenide) solar modules. The facility aims to achieve a CIGS efficiency of 15% and improve the yield of its product line.
