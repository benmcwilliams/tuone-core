#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Electrolyzer Development**: Ecoclean collaborated with Germany's Zentrum für Sonnenenergie- und Wasserstoffforschung Baden-Württemberg to develop a production-ready electrolyzer known as the P200. This electrolyzer consists of two modular units with an output of 1 MW and features a modular system based on alkaline pressure electrolysis. The system can scale from 1 MW to 20 MW.
