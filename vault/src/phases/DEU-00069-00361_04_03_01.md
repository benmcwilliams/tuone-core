### Evolutionary Timeline of the Horiba FuelCon Facility in Barleben, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: Horiba FuelCon specializes in manufacturing test, production, and diagnostic systems for fuel cells and batteries, catering to the automotive and supplier industries.
- **Production Capacity**: The facility's production area is 7,000 square meters, with an additional 3,000 square meters designated for office space.
- **Expansion**: The facility is undergoing an expansion to strengthen its role as a global competence center within the Horiba Group and to support the planned e-mobility campus.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Amount**: Approximately 30 million euros are to be invested in the new building by the end of 2021.
- **Total Investment**: A total of 36 million euros was invested in the expansions at both the Barleben and Leichlingen sites.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Announcement of Expansion**: The expansion was announced on November 27, 2019.
- **Completion Timeline**: The new building was expected to be completed by the end of 2021, although there was a delay, and the opening occurred at the end of April 2022.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The expansion is set to create 250 new jobs, increasing the total number of employees from 110 to 360.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: Horiba FuelCon is a subsidiary of the Japanese Horiba Group, indicating that it operates under the ownership of the Horiba Group following its takeover.
