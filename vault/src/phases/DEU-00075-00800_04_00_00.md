### Evolutionary Timeline of the Uniper Facility in Bavaria, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2024**: Uniper opened the HyStorage facility in Bavaria, which is designed for hydrogen storage. The facility has a storage capacity of almost 500,000 norm cubic meters (17.6 million cubic feet) of green hydrogen. It is among the first of its kind, utilizing a gas mixture of natural gas and hydrogen stored in a pore storage facility. The facility is set to begin trial operations following a gas tightness test scheduled for September 24, 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2024**: Uniper announced plans to invest a low double-digit million euro amount in the HyStorage project. Additionally, the project received funding of approximately EUR 2.4 million ($2.6 million) from the Lower Saxony Ministry for the Environment, Energy, and Climate Protection as part of the hydrogen directive, aimed at supporting pilot and demonstration projects in the hydrogen economy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2024**: The HyStorage facility was officially opened. The facility is in the trial operations phase, with initial gas filling and operation planned once approved. The company aims to conduct various injection and withdrawal cycles over the coming year to test hydrogen compatibility of equipment and materials.
