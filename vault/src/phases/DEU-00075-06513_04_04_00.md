- **September 2022**: Siemens commissioned an 8.75 MW PEM electrolysis plant at the Energy Park in Wunsiedel, Bavaria. This facility can generate up to 1,350 tons of green hydrogen annually. The plant is powered solely by solar and wind energy. There are considerations for expanding the plant's capacity to 17.5 MW.

The hydrogen electrolysis plant is set to produce green hydrogen using renewable electricity from a newly constructed photovoltaic plant and later from nearby wind turbines.
