### Eavor Facility in Bavaria, Germany - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - The Eavor facility in Bavaria is set to utilize Eavor-Loop technology, developed by the Canadian start-up Eavor. This innovative technology allows for the transformation of sub-surface heat from the Earth’s core into renewable energy without the need to discover underground hot-water reservoirs.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - In 2022, Chubu Electric Power, a major Japanese utility, invested in Eavor and announced plans to acquire a 40% stake in the geothermal energy project in Bavaria. This investment is aimed at promoting the commercialization of Eavor-Loop technology in Germany.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - The project is in the development phase, with significant announcements made in July 2023 regarding the collaboration and investment by Chubu Electric Power. The facility is expected to be the first of its kind in Germany, focusing on geothermal power and district heating.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - The facility is not exclusively owned by Eavor; it is a joint venture with Chubu Electric Power holding a 40% stake in the project.
