### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hydrogenious LOHC Facility in Bavaria, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Process**: Hydrogenious LOHC utilizes a process where hydrogen is chemically bound to a thermal oil called benzyltoluene. This liquid organic hydrogen carrier (LOHC) allows for safe storage and transportation of hydrogen under ambient conditions, similar to diesel. At the off-taker site, hydrogen is released from the LOHC in high purity, and the carrier oil can be reused.
