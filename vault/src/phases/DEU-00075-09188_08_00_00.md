1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 04-09-2018
   - **Details:** IST3 Investment Foundation acquired two wind farms in Bavaria, Germany, with a total production capacity of 53 MW. The facilities include the Reichertshull project featuring 11 Nordex N131/3300 turbines and the Workerszeller Forst facility comprising five of the same model.
