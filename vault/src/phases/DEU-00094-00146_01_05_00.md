### Evolutionary Timeline of Circu Li-ion Facility in Berlin, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2023**: Circu Li-ion develops a new process for upcycling lithium-ion batteries, integrating hardware and AI software. The facility aims to automate battery recycling, significantly reducing CO2 emissions by up to 48% compared to conventional methods. The company plans to expand its Berlin branch alongside its main production facility in Karlsruhe to 2000 m².

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2023**: Circu Li-ion secures €8.5 million in seed funding. This funding includes €4.5 million in equity from BonVenture and other industry experts, along with €4 million from the EIC Accelerator and partnerships with recycling companies and OEMs.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2023**: Circu Li-ion announces plans to expand its Berlin facility as part of its growth strategy, aiming to enhance its production capabilities and operational efficiency.
- **September 2024**: Circu Li-ion begins a pilot phase in collaboration with Amazon to optimize the reuse and recycling of electric vehicle batteries. This pilot will last six months and will utilize Circu Li-ion's technology to process batteries from Amazon's delivery network.

#### 4. Labour
