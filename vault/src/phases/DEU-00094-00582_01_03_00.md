## DEU-00094-00582

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Northvolt Facility in Berlin, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Production Capacity:** Northvolt plans to build a battery cell factory named Northvolt Three in Heide, Schleswig-Holstein, with a final production capacity of 60 GWh.
   - **Operations Timeline:** The facility is expected to go into operation by the end of 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Funding:** Northvolt has been granted more than 155 million euros in funding from the German government, with 70% from the federal budget and 30% from the state of Schleswig-Holstein.
   - **Total Project Cost:** The entire project is estimated to exceed 4 billion euros, with Northvolt aiming to raise about 2 billion euros through its own means and the remainder through investors.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Funding Notification:** On December 5, 2022, the German Federal Minister of Economic Affairs, Robert Habeck, officially handed over the notification of IPCEI funding to Northvolt.
   - **Construction Timeline:** Northvolt plans to start construction in 2023, with production expected to begin at the end of 2025.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Job Creation:** The facility is projected to create up to 3,000 jobs locally in Schleswig-Holstein.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Ownership Status:** The articles do not specify whether Northvolt will have exclusive ownership of the facility or if it will be a joint venture. However, it is indicated that Northvolt is independently pursuing the project, having withdrawn from a previous joint project with Volkswagen in Salzgitter.
