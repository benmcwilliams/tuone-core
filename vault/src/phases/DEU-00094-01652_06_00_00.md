### Evolutionary Timeline of the First Solar Facility in Berlin, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: 11-06-2015
  - **Details**: First Solar, in collaboration with skytron energy, developed an operations and maintenance (O&M) business focused on optimizing the performance of solar power plants. The facility in Berlin became one of three global operating centers for First Solar, alongside locations in Arizona and Australia. The technology involved includes the 'skycontrol' system for grid stability and the PVGuard SCADA system for constant monitoring and fault detection, aimed at reducing downtime and enhancing the levelized cost of electricity (LCOE).
