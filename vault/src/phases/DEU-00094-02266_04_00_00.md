### H2 Mobility Deutschland Facility Timeline (Berlin, DEU)

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Capacity**: H2 Mobility Deutschland operates more than 90 hydrogen refueling stations and aims to expand this network to 300 stations by 2030.
- **Updates**: The facility is focused on upgrading its refueling station network to meet the increasing demand from commercial vehicles and those in intensive use.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Total Investment**: H2 Mobility Deutschland secured a total of €110 million for its expansion efforts.

  - **Industrial Shareholders Contribution**: An additional €40 million will be contributed collectively by industrial shareholders including Air Liquide, Daimler Truck, Hyundai, Linde, OMV, Shell, and TotalEnergies.
