### Evolutionary Timeline of Borosil Renewables Facility in Berlin, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2022**: Borosil Renewables announced the acquisition of Interfloat Group, which includes GMB Glasmanufaktur Brandenburg GmbH (GMB) near Berlin. The facility has a current production capacity of 300 tonnes per day (TPD). Post-acquisition, Borosil plans to increase this capacity to 500 TPD by 2023. Overall, Borosil's solar glass output will grow to 750 TPD, with a total manufacturing capacity of 2,600 TPD across its facilities by 2025, enabling the supply of solar glass for over 15 GW of PV modules.
  
#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2022**: The acquisition of Interfloat Group was part of Borosil's strategy to enhance its manufacturing capabilities in Europe. The company committed to investing in increasing the capacity at GMB’s plant to 500 TPD.
- **October 2022**: Following the acquisition, Borosil announced that its total solar glass manufacturing capacity would increase to 1,300 TPD with the commissioning of a new furnace in India, which is expected to be operational in the next two months.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2022**: Borosil Renewables signed a binding offer to acquire 100% equity in Interfloat Group.
- **October 2022**: Borosil completed the acquisition of an 86% stake in Interfloat Group, making GMB and Interfloat step-down subsidiaries of Borosil Renewables.
