## DEU-00094-03271

### Evolutionary Timeline of the PI Berlin Facility in Berlin, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2017**: PI Berlin operates an IEC-accredited test laboratory in Berlin, providing quality inspections for solar projects and equipment. The facility focuses on laboratory testing and quality assurance in the photovoltaic (PV) sector.
- **January 2019**: PI Berlin releases a study analyzing over 250 solar PV panel quality control audits, highlighting the variability in quality among manufacturers and the importance of quality management tools.
- **June 2019**: PI Berlin conducts tests on PERC modules, addressing light-induced degradation (LID) and light and elevated temperature-induced degradation (LeTID), showcasing its capabilities in analyzing module performance.
- **May 2022**: PI Berlin develops a root cause analysis (RCA) procedure to detect failures in operational inverters, combining operational data review, field inspections, and on-site measurements.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2022**: Kiwa Deutschland acquires PI Berlin, enhancing its capabilities in the solar PV field. The acquisition is aimed at unlocking further growth and reinforcing services in the Iberian Peninsula and Latin America.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2017**: Announcement of the establishment of a subsidiary in India to meet the demand for laboratory testing and quality assurance in solar projects.
- **May 2022**: Formal acquisition by Kiwa Group, making PI Berlin a wholly owned subsidiary while retaining its brand identity.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2022**: The acquisition by Kiwa is expected to lead to job creation and expansion of services, particularly in the areas of factory, laboratory, and field services, benefiting customers in greenfield projects and those refurbishing existing assets.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2022**: PI Berlin becomes a wholly owned subsidiary of Kiwa Deutschland, transitioning from individual private shareholders to a single owner while maintaining its operational structure and brand identity.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones of the PI Berlin facility, focusing on its technological advancements, investments, project statuses, labor implications, and ownership changes.
