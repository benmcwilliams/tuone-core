### Evolutionary Timeline of We Do Solar Facility in Berlin, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/05/2022**: We Do Solar, a Berlin-based startup, introduced its innovative balcony solar modules at Intersolar Europe. The modules weigh 1.6 kg and can be easily installed by users without professional help. Each kit includes eight solar panels with a total output of 640 Wp and a 600 W MPPT PV inverter. The modules are designed for easy installation on balcony railings or other sunny locations.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **17/05/2022**: The company, founded in September 2021, has attracted several business angels for initial investments. While specific investment amounts were not disclosed, the founders indicated plans to seek further investment for growth.

#### 4. Labour
- **17/05/2022**: The company has developed a business model that allows employers to rent solar kits for their employees, creating a virtual power plant. This model is expected to enhance job creation indirectly by promoting sustainability initiatives within companies.
