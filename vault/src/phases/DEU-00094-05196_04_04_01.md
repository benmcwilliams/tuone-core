## DEU-00094-05196

### Evolutionary Timeline of HMS Bergbau AG Facility in Berlin, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2023**: HMS Bergbau AG, through its Oil & Gas division, signed a contract with McPhy Energy for the supply of four McLyzer 3200-30 electrolyzers, totaling a capacity of 64 MW. These electrolyzers will be installed at the Radeland Compression station in Brandenburg, contributing to the conversion of a natural gas pipeline into a hydrogen pipeline. The electrolyzers are expected to produce up to 10,000 tons of low-carbon hydrogen per year, utilizing electricity from nearby wind and photovoltaic plants.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2023**: The contract with McPhy Energy is structured in two phases. The first phase involves the supply of one McLyzer 3200-30 (16 MW), with a final investment decision expected in the first half of 2024. The second phase includes three additional electrolyzers to be commissioned in 2027, with a final investment decision anticipated by the end of 2025.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2023**: The contract signing marks a key milestone in the project “Flow – making hydrogen happen,” which aims to facilitate the hydrogen ramp-up across Europe. The first electrolyzer is expected to be operational by the end of 2025, with subsequent units following in 2027.
