### Evolutionary Timeline of TS Group GmbH Facility in Bitterfeld, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2018**: TS Group GmbH takes over the business of the insolvent PV manufacturer Calyxo, which specializes in the production of cadmium telluride (CdTe) thin film PV modules. Production at the Bitterfeld-Wolfen site will continue under the Calyxo brand.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2018**: TS Group GmbH plans to invest millions at the Bitterfeld-Wolfen production site following the acquisition of Calyxo.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07/01/2018**: The transfer of business operations from Calyxo to TS Group GmbH takes effect retroactively. The agreement for the transaction is signed on July 3, 2018.

#### 4. Labour
- **04/2018**: All 130 employees of the former Calyxo facility are retained by TS Group GmbH, ensuring job continuity at the Bitterfeld-Wolfen site.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **04/2018**: TS Group GmbH exclusively owns the facility after acquiring the entire business of Calyxo, including all patents and production facilities.
