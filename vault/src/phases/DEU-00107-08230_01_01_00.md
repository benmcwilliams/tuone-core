### Evolutionary Timeline of the AMG Critical Materials Facility in Bitterfeld, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2024**: AMG Critical Materials successfully opened Europe’s first lithium hydroxide refinery in Bitterfeld-Wolfen, Germany. The facility began production of battery-grade lithium hydroxide at the first of its five planned modules, with an annual capacity of 20,000 tonnes (t), sufficient for approximately 500,000 electric vehicle (EV) batteries. AMG plans to expand production to a total capacity of 100,000 tonnes per annum (tpa) by 2030.
