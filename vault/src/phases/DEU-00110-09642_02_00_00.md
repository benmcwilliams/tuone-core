## DEU-00110-09642

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Oil Dynamics GmbH Facility in Bochum, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: Oil Dynamics GmbH provided a tailor-made pumping system for the geothermal heating and cooling project at the MARK 51°7 site. This system includes electric submersible pumps (ESPs) installed in two wells. The facility utilizes mine water for district heating and cooling, with temperatures of 27 to 28 degrees Celsius reported from existing boreholes. The system is designed to meet 70% to 75% of the heating and cooling needs for all connected buildings.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The Fraunhofer IEG received over EUR 27 million in funding from federal and state governments for a five-year period, with EUR 18 million allocated for investments in real estate and initial equipment for the research laboratory.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: The project at the MARK 51°7 site was reported as the first major industrial zone with an integrated heating and cooling system that combines geothermal energy, district networks, underground storage, and large heat pumps. A follow-on project is already in the works to heat pit water from a depth of 50 meters using a large heat pump.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2023**: The workforce of Fraunhofer IEG has doubled from 40 to 80 employees over the past three years, indicating job creation linked to the expansion of the facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
