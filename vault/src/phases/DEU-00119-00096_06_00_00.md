### Evolutionary Timeline of the Belectric Facility in Brandenburg, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **June 2022:** Belectric, in collaboration with AMPYR Solar Europe, agreed to install over 200MW of solar PV capacity in Germany within two years. The first two solar plants in Brandenburg will have an installed capacity of 85MW and 65MW, respectively. Belectric will handle the engineering, procurement, construction (EPC), and operations and maintenance (O&M) of these sites.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **June 2022:** AMPYR Solar Europe secured a €400 million (US$455 million) loan facility to develop over 2GW of solar PV across Europe by 2025, which includes the projects in Brandenburg.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **June 2022:** Construction works for the two solar farms in Brandenburg are expected to begin by the end of the year.
