## DEU-00119-00219

### Evolutionary Timeline of the EnBW Facility in Brandenburg, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2019**: EnBW and Energiekontor AG signed a power purchase agreement (PPA) for an 85 MW solar PV plant in Brandenburg. The project is notable for being the first PPA of its kind in Germany, with expectations to produce approximately 1.3 TWh of electricity over the contract period of 15 years. The plant is to be built on a 120-hectare agricultural site and is expected to be commissioned in 2020.
- **August 2022**: EnBW began construction on the Prötzel 2 wind farm in Brandenburg, which will have a capacity of 38 MW, featuring nine Enercon E138 turbines. The commissioning of the wind turbines is scheduled for the end of July 2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2019**: The PPA signed between EnBW and Energiekontor for the 85 MW solar plant indicates a significant investment in subsidy-free solar power development in Germany, marking a shift in financing strategies for renewable energy projects in the country.
- **August 2022**: The construction of the 38 MW wind project indicates further investment in renewable energy infrastructure in Brandenburg, supported by a contract awarded by the Federal Network Agency.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2019**: Announcement of the PPA for the 85 MW solar PV plant, with plans for construction and commissioning in 2020.
- **August 2022**: EnBW officially turned the sod for the Prötzel 2 wind farm, marking the start of construction with an expected completion date for commissioning in July 2023.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2022**: The construction of the Prötzel 2 wind farm is expected to create jobs during the construction phase, although specific job creation numbers are not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2019**: EnBW is involved in a partnership with Energiekontor AG for the solar PV plant, indicating a joint venture structure for this project.
- **August 2022**: The Prötzel 2 wind farm is developed exclusively by EnBW, indicating full ownership of this facility.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones of the EnBW facility in Brandenburg, Germany, focusing on its solar and wind energy projects.
