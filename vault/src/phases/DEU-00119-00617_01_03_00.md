### Evolutionary Timeline of the Porsche Facility in Brandenburg, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **01-09-2023**: Porsche is exploring the establishment of a large battery cell factory in Brandenburg, potentially expanding to over 20 gigawatt-hours. This facility would complement their existing pilot plant near Reutlingen, which has a production capacity of up to 1.3 GWh.
- **30-05-2024**: Plans for the battery factory in Brandenburg are reportedly on hold as Porsche's Supervisory Board questions the necessity of the gigafactory due to market conditions.
- **05-07-2024**: Porsche is considering acquiring a majority stake in Varta's battery subsidiary, V4Drive, which aims for a production capacity of at least 2 GWh. This indicates Porsche's ongoing interest in battery production despite the uncertainty surrounding the Brandenburg facility.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05-07-2024**: The potential investment for the Brandenburg facility is estimated to be between 2 to 3 billion euros for the planned 20 GWh capacity. Porsche is seeking partners for this investment, indicating a collaborative approach rather than sole investment.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **01-09-2023**: Initial discussions and considerations for the Brandenburg site are reported, with a decision expected by the end of 2023.
- **30-05-2024**: The project status shifts as Porsche's Supervisory Board decides to put the plans for the Brandenburg factory on hold, citing market uncertainties.
- **05-07-2024**: The ongoing negotiations with Varta for a stake in V4Drive suggest a pivot in strategy, focusing on partnerships in battery technology rather than immediate construction in Brandenburg.

#### 4. Labour
- **30-05-2024**: The hesitation to proceed with the Brandenburg factory could imply potential job losses or a lack of job creation in the region, as the establishment of a battery production facility would typically lead to significant employment opportunities.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **05-07-2024**: Porsche has fully owned its battery subsidiary, Cellforce, since its inception. However, for the Brandenburg facility, Porsche is looking for partners, indicating that it may not pursue full ownership of the new site.

This timeline captures the evolution of the Porsche facility in Brandenburg, highlighting the company's strategic considerations, investment plans, and operational developments in the context of the clean technology sector.
