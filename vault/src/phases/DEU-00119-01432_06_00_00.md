### Evolutionary Timeline of AMPYR Solar Europe Facility in Brandenburg, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **June 2022:** AMPYR Solar Europe (ASE) and Belectric agreed to install over 200MW of solar PV capacity in Germany within two years. The first two solar plants in Brandenburg will have an installed capacity of 85MW and 65MW (across six sub-areas).

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **February 2022:** AMPYR closed a €400 million (US$455 million) loan facility with CarVal Investors to develop more than 2GW of solar PV across Europe by 2025. This investment supports the development of the solar plants in Brandenburg.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **June 2022:** Construction works for the two solar farms in Brandenburg are expected to begin by the end of the year. The project includes the construction of a 25km long transmission line and a substation.
