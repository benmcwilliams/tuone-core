### Evolutionary Timeline of the GP Joule Facility in Brandenburg, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2022**: GP Joule, in collaboration with Trina Solar, completed the construction of a 170 MW solar project on a former lignite opencast mine in Brandenburg, Germany. This facility is designed for utility-scale photovoltaic (PV) applications.

- **Autumn 2023**: The facility began operations, marking its status as one of the largest solar plants in Germany, with a total capacity of 180 MW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2024**: A power purchase agreement (PPA) was signed between RWE Supply & Trading and Salzgitter Group for the supply of up to 64 GWh per year from the Boitzenburger Land solar farm, which is part of the GP Joule facility. This contract is set to begin in 2027 and runs for seven years. The facility operates without requiring a feed-in tariff due to direct marketing via PPAs.
