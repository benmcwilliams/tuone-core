### Evolutionary Timeline of the TU Braunschweig Facility in Braunschweig, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2023**: TU Braunschweig inaugurated the CircularLab, a research facility focused on recycling lithium-ion batteries. This lab is part of the Battery LabFactory Braunschweig (BLB), which includes two other facilities: ProductionLab and DiagnosisLab. The BLB covers a significant portion of the life cycle and circular production of lithium-ion batteries, from material synthesis to recycling.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2023**: A total investment of three million euros was made through an ERDF fund from the state of Lower Saxony, the EU, and TU Braunschweig for the CircularLab. Additionally, federal and state governments contributed another ten million euros for the procurement of plant technology and analytical equipment.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2021**: Ground-breaking ceremony for the CircularLab.
- **Autumn 2022**: Arrival of the first systems for the CircularLab.
- **November 2023**: Official opening of the CircularLab.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or loss figures related to the CircularLab or the overall Battery LabFactory Braunschweig. However, the facility is expected to contribute to sustainability and independence from critical raw materials, which may imply job creation in research and development.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- TU Braunschweig acts as the client and investor for the CircularLab, indicating that it has full ownership of the facility.
