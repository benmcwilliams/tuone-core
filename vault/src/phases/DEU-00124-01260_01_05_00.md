### Evolutionary Timeline of the Redux Saubermacher Facility in Bremerhaven, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: June 2018
   - **Details**: The facility produces recycled materials from lithium-ion batteries, capable of processing all types of these batteries with a production capacity of 10,000 tons per year. The recycling process includes discharge, disassembly, thermal treatment, and mechanical processing, achieving a recycling rate of approximately 60% to 70%, which is higher than usual targets. The facility aims for a long-term goal of a 100% recycling rate.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Date**: June 2018
   - **Details**: Saubermacher and Redux Recycling invested a total of 3 million euros in the establishment of the battery recycling plant.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Date**: June 2018
   - **Details**: The facility was officially opened after years of joint research and development, taking six years to develop the idea into a scale model and an additional two years to realize the large-scale plant.
