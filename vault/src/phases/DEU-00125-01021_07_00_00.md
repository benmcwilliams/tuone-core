#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2022**: The facility operates a fleet of 14 hydrogen-powered Coradia iLint regional trains, which have replaced 15 diesel trains on the route between Cuxhaven, Bremerhaven, Bremervörde, and Buxtehude. The hydrogen filling station built in Bremervörde has a storage capacity of 1,800 kilograms of hydrogen, supported by six hydrogen compressors and two dispensers for refueling the trains.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2022**: The total project volume for the hydrogen train network is over 93 million euros. The facility is part of a broader initiative to transition from diesel to hydrogen technology in public transport.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2018**: The first two pre-series Coradia iLint trains were put into service on the route between Cuxhaven and Buxtehude.
- **August 2022**: The complete fleet of 14 Coradia iLint trains enters passenger service, marking a significant milestone in the project.
