### Evolutionary Timeline of the EnBW Facility in Bruchsal, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2012**: EnBW took over operational control of the Bruchsal geothermal power plant, marking it as the first geothermal power plant in Baden-Württemberg.
- **2021**: EnBW announced a pilot project in partnership with the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT) to explore lithium extraction from geothermal fluids.
- **April 2023**: Researchers from KIT and EnBW developed a lithium-ion sieve to adsorb lithium from geothermal brines, indicating ongoing research into lithium extraction.
- **June 2024**: EnBW and LevertonHELM successfully produced lithium carbonate with a purity of over 99.5% from the geothermal plant's thermal water using Direct Lithium Extraction (DLE) technology.
