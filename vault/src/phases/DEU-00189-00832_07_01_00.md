- **June 2020**: Volkswagen began testing the ID.3 electric cars with 150 employees across three locations in Saxony, including Chemnitz. The facility was involved in the quality assurance process for the ID.3 before its market launch.
- **September 2022**: Volkswagen announced plans to adjust production at the Chemnitz engine plant to include eMobility components, specifically thermal management systems for electric vehicles. This marked the beginning of the facility's transformation into a key plant for e-mobility.
- **November 2023**: The Chemnitz plant was confirmed to supply components for thermal management systems for the upcoming SSP electric platform, with production expected to start in 2026, and series production planned for 2028.
