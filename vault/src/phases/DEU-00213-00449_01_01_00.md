1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** January 2024
   - **Details:** Lanxess, in collaboration with IBU-Tec Advanced Materials, is developing innovative iron oxides for LFP (lithium/iron/phosphate) batteries. The focus is on improving the electrochemical properties of these batteries, including energy density, charging speed, and longevity. Lanxess operates the world's largest plant for iron oxide in Krefeld-Uerdingen and has nearly 100 years of experience in this field.
