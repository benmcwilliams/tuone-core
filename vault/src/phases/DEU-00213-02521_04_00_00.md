### Evolutionary Timeline of the Frames Facility in Cologne, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2021**: Frames announced the development of a modular and skid-mounted Liquid Organic Hydrogen Carrier (LOHC) Storage Plant in Cologne, which will be the first industrial-scale LOHC plant in Europe. The facility is designed to store five tons of hydrogen per day. The hydrogen will be chemically bonded to the LOHC material benzyl toluene, allowing for transport under ambient conditions using existing logistics infrastructures.
