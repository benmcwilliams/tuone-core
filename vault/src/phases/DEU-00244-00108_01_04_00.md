## DEU-00244-00108

### Evolutionary Timeline of the BorgWarner Facility in Darmstadt, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2021:** BorgWarner announces its intention to take over Akasol, a German battery systems manufacturer. Akasol focuses on battery systems for larger and commercial vehicles and has a production plant in Darmstadt, which includes administration, technology, and development centers. A similar plant is being constructed in the USA.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **February 2021:** BorgWarner offers to acquire Akasol for approximately 730 million euros, which includes a premium of 23.4% on the average share price. This acquisition is aimed at leveraging BorgWarner’s global business platform to enhance Akasol's growth strategy in the e-mobility sector.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **February 2021:** BorgWarner secures a majority of shares in Akasol, exceeding 59% of the shares, which allows them to proceed with the acquisition.
   - **June 2021:** The acquisition of Akasol by BorgWarner is completed, with BorgWarner securing around 89% of the shares.

4. **Labour:**
   - **February 2021:** Akasol's CEO Sven Schulz indicates that the partnership with BorgWarner will allow Akasol to continue operating independently, suggesting stability in job creation and retention at the Darmstadt facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **June 2021:** Following the completion of the acquisition, BorgWarner becomes the majority owner of Akasol, holding approximately 89% of the shares. Akasol continues to operate under its brand, maintaining its management team, including CEO Sven Schulz.
