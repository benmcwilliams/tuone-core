### Evolutionary Timeline of the Schmid Group Facility in Darmstadt, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **June 2012**: The Schmid Group, in collaboration with Schott Solar, developed the TinPad system, which provides an alternative to reduce the critical cost of silver used in solar cell manufacturing. This system allows for tin contacts to be applied to six-inch solar cells, replacing the backside with tin, resulting in a cost saving of approximately US$0.06 per wafer. The system was ready for manufacturing, with initial shipments expected to commence in September 2012.
