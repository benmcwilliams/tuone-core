### Evolutionary Timeline of the ZSW Facility in Dettingen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2022**: The Center for Solar Energy and Hydrogen Research Baden-Württemberg (ZSW) announced the start of the EcoLyzer BW project in collaboration with Ecoclean. The facility is set to manufacture around 80 alkaline water electrolysis systems per year, with the first systems expected to begin production in 2023. Each electrolyzer will have a capacity of 1 megawatt, divided into two blocks of 0.5 megawatts each, capable of producing approximately 20 kilograms of hydrogen per hour at a pressure level of 16 bar.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2022**: The state environment ministry of Baden-Württemberg is funding the EcoLyzer project with €2.1 million, which will be allocated until 2024 to support the development and production of the electrolysis systems.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2022**: The EcoLyzer BW project officially commenced, marking the beginning of the facility's operations related to the production of electrolysis systems. The first series production is scheduled for 2023.
