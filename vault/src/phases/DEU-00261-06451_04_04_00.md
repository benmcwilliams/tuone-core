#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2022**: The ZSW facility, in collaboration with Ecoclean, announced the start of the EcoLyzer BW project. This project aims to manufacture around 80 alkaline water electrolysis systems per year at the Dettingen site for the international market. The first systems are scheduled to begin production in 2023. The electrolyzer will consist of two blocks, each with a capacity of 0.5 megawatts, and is expected to produce approximately 20 kilograms of hydrogen per hour at a pressure level of 16 bar.
