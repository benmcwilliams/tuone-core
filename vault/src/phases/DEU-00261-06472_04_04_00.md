### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ecoclean Facility in Dettingen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2022**: The EcoLyzer BW project was announced, indicating that Ecoclean will manufacture approximately 80 alkaline water electrolysis systems per year at the Dettingen site. This production is set to cater to the international market, with the first systems expected to begin production in 2023. The electrolyzer will feature two blocks, each with a capacity of 0.5 megawatts, and is designed to produce about 20 kilograms of hydrogen per hour at a pressure level of 16 bar.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2022**: The state environment ministry of Baden-Württemberg is funding the EcoLyzer project with €2.1 million, which will be allocated until 2024. This funding supports the development of the first series production of electrolysis systems in the region.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2022**: The EcoLyzer BW project officially commenced, marking the start of a significant initiative for hydrogen production at the Dettingen facility. The production of the first systems is scheduled for 2023.
