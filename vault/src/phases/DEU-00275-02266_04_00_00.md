### H2 Mobility Deutschland Facility Timeline - Dresden, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Inauguration of Hydrogen Charging Stations**: On **October 20, 2023**, H2 Mobility Deutschland inaugurated hydrogen charging stations in Dresden and Berg, near Hof. The hydrogen used at these stations is sourced from Rießner Gase, produced through PEM electrolysis powered by solar and wind energy.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Hydrogen Production Plant**: The hydrogen production plant in the Wunsiedel energy park, in collaboration with the Wunsiedel municipal company and Siemens, is noted as Bavaria's largest, currently producing **960 tons of hydrogen per year**. There are plans for a second expansion phase to increase the electrolyzer's capacity to **17.5 MW**.
