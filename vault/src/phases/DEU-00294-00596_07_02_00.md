- **May 2018**: The Opel facility in Eisenach is set to start production of the Grandland X SUV, including a plug-in hybrid electric vehicle (PHEV) version, from mid-2019.

- **May 2019**: The first Grandland X is expected to roll off the assembly line at the Eisenach plant by the end of August 2019.
- **November 2023**: Opel confirms that the Grandland will receive an all-electric successor, set to begin production at the Eisenach plant in the second half of 2024.
