### Evolutionary Timeline of the EnBW Facility in Emden, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 07-12-2017
   - **Details:** EnBW Energie Baden-Württemberg AG announced the construction of an Operations & Maintenance (O&M) base in Emden for servicing its offshore wind farms in the North Sea. The facility will support the 497MW Hohe See and 112MW Albatros offshore wind farms, as well as the 900MW He Dreiht offshore wind farm. The O&M base will include a two-story building with offices, social rooms, a warehouse for spare parts and tools, and sufficient parking space. Helicopter service operations will be utilized for maintenance.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 07-12-2017
   - **Details:** The construction of the facility is set to begin in the spring of 2018. The investment specifics related to the construction were not detailed in the article, but it is noted that the He Dreiht offshore wind farm will be developed without subsidies.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 07-12-2017
   - **Details:** Construction of the O&M base is scheduled to start in spring 2018. The facility is expected to begin operations in 2019, coinciding with the commissioning of the Hohe See and Albatros offshore wind farms. The He Dreiht offshore wind farm is scheduled to go into operation in 2025.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 07-12-2017
   - **Details:** The facility will create 40 jobs in Emden for maintenance and service work starting in 2019. Additionally, the operation of the He Dreiht offshore wind farm is expected to create 60 new jobs.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 07-12-2017
   - **Details:** EnBW Energie Baden-Württemberg AG is the sole owner of the facility, as there is no mention of joint ventures or partnerships in the article.
