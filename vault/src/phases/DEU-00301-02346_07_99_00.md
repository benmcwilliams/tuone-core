### Evolutionary Timeline of the Livista Facility in Emden, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: Livista Energy Europe announced plans to build a lithium refinery in Emden, Lower Saxony. The facility will produce up to 40,000 tonnes of battery-grade lithium products annually, including 30,000 tonnes of lithium hydroxide and 10,000 tonnes of lithium carbonate. This production capacity is projected to supply enough battery-grade lithium for the production of 850,000 electric vehicles per year. The facility will refine various lithium sources, including spodumene rock, brines, and recycled battery materials.
- **October 2023**: Livista secured a 32-hectare site at Wybelsumer Polder, close to the harbor, for the construction of the refinery. The facility is expected to begin operations in 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The federal state of Lower Saxony pledged its support for the implementation of the project at the Emden site, emphasizing the strategic importance of the location for clean energy and the electric vehicle industry.
