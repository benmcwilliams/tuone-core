## DEU-00305-00120

### Evolutionary Timeline of the CATL Facility in Erfurt, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-06-2018**: CATL announces plans to establish a battery cell manufacturing facility in Erfurt, Germany, targeting an initial production capacity of 14 GWh, with potential expansions to 24 GWh in the future.
- **09-07-2018**: CATL confirms the facility will focus on producing battery cells primarily for the electric vehicle (EV) industry.
- **10-07-2018**: The facility will also include a research and development center for smart manufacturing technology.
- **30-10-2019**: Groundbreaking ceremony for the facility takes place, with production expected to start in early 2022.
- **27-08-2022**: CATL begins series production of battery cells at the Erfurt facility, initially producing 8 GWh per year, with plans to ramp up to 14 GWh.
- **12-12-2022**: CATL officially announces the start of series production of battery cells in Germany.
- **30-06-2023**: CATL's battery test and validation center in Erfurt receives certification from Volkswagen, enhancing its service capabilities for European customers.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-06-2018**: CATL plans to invest €240 million in the facility, which later increases to €1.8 billion due to rising demand for battery cells.
- **20-06-2019**: CATL announces the increased investment of €1.8 billion, reflecting the growing demand for battery cells in Europe.
- **10-09-2019**: The German government provides subsidies to support CATL's investment in the facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **11-06-2018**: CATL's plans for the Erfurt facility are confirmed, with significant contracts already in place with major automakers like BMW and Daimler.
- **10-07-2018**: CATL officially announces the construction of the facility in Erfurt.
- **29-10-2019**: Groundbreaking ceremony for the facility is held.
- **12-12-2022**: CATL starts series production of battery cells at the Erfurt facility.
- **30-03-2023**: CATL announces plans to ramp up production capacity at the Erfurt facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **09-07-2018**: CATL announces the creation of approximately 600 jobs at the Erfurt facility.
- **10-07-2018**: The investment is expected to create up to 2,000 jobs in the region.
- **12-12-2022**: By the end of 2022, CATL plans to employ around 1,500 local workers, with additional specialists from China assisting in the setup.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **07-06-2018**: CATL is establishing the facility as its first production site outside of China, indicating full ownership of the facility.
- **10-07-2018**: CATL confirms that it will operate the facility independently, focusing on collaborations with European automakers.

This timeline captures the key developments in the establishment and evolution of the CATL facility in Erfurt, Germany, highlighting its technological advancements, investments, project milestones, labor impacts, and ownership structure.
