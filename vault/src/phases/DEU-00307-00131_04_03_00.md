#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2021**: Cellcentric, a joint venture between Daimler Truck and the Volvo Group, was established on March 1, 2021, with the aim of mass-producing fuel cell systems. The fuel cell systems are currently in the prototype stage, and customer trials for fuel cell trucks are expected to begin in about three years.
