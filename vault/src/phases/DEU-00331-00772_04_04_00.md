### Evolutionary Timeline of ThyssenKrupp Facility in Frankfurt, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: Thyssenkrupp Nucera announced plans for an initial public offering (IPO) to raise up to $645 million for reinvestment into its alkaline water electrolysis (AWE) technology. The facility focuses on producing green hydrogen through AWE, which is noted for being more cost-effective than traditional electrolysis methods.
- **May 2023**: Thyssenkrupp Nucera announced it would provide over 30 electrolyzers for Europe’s first commercial green steel plant, set to begin operations in 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2023**: Following the IPO, Thyssenkrupp reported gross proceeds of around €526 million ($573 million) from the share offering, which will be invested in the growth of its hydrogen business. The IPO is expected to enhance financial flexibility and support projected growth in AWE sales, which the company aims to increase to between $648 million and $756 million by the end of fiscal year 2023/24.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: Announcement of the IPO plans for Thyssenkrupp Nucera.
- **July 2023**: Successful listing of Thyssenkrupp Nucera on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange, marking a significant milestone in the company's growth strategy.
