## DEU-00333-00848

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Vulcan Energy Facility in Frankfurt-Höchst, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2024**: Vulcan Energy starts production of climate-neutral lithium chloride at its Lithium Extraction Optimization Plant (LEOP) in Landau, Germany. The facility aims to produce 24,000 tonnes of lithium hydroxide monohydrate (LHM) per year from lithium chloride through a multi-stage process.
- **August 2024**: Vulcan begins commissioning of the Central Lithium Electrolysis Optimization Plant (CLEOP) in Frankfurt-Höchst, where lithium chloride will be converted to battery-grade lithium hydroxide. The CLEOP is designed for pre-commercial optimization and training.
- **November 2024**: Vulcan produces lithium hydroxide for the first time in Frankfurt-Höchst, marking it as the first sustainably produced lithium hydroxide in Europe.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2024**: Vulcan announces over EUR 40 million in investment for the lithium extraction project, which includes the LEOP and CLEOP facilities.
- **September 2023**: Vulcan receives a building permit for the CLEOP, indicating progress in the development of the facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2023**: Vulcan announces results of a definitive feasibility study for its lithium project, outlining plans for production capacity.
- **August 2024**: CLEOP begins commissioning, with power fed to the plant for the first time.
- **November 2024**: The first lithium hydroxide is produced at CLEOP, completing the initial phase of the project.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2024**: The LEOP facility has been operational for training personnel and optimizing processes, indicating job creation related to the commissioning of both the LEOP and CLEOP facilities.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Vulcan Energy appears to have full ownership of the CLEOP facility in Frankfurt-Höchst, as there is no mention of joint ventures or partnerships regarding ownership in the provided articles. The company is focused on establishing a domestic supply chain for lithium in Europe.
