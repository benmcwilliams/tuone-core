## DEU-00338-00409

### Evolutionary Timeline of JT Energy Systems Facility in Freiberg, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 08-08-2019
   - **Details:** JT Energy Systems will take over the former Solarworld plant in Freiberg, Germany, to create the largest European production center for batteries and charging systems in intralogistics. The facility will assemble battery systems and chargers for electric forklifts and lift trucks. The modules will be sourced from a highly automated production facility in Glauchau, Saxony. The operational start for the Freiberg plant is planned for the first quarter of 2020.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 08-08-2019
   - **Details:** The acquisition of the Freiberg plant is part of JT Energy Systems' strategy to expand its lithium-ion battery and charger production capacity in response to increasing global demand for electric material handling equipment.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 08-08-2019
   - **Details:** Announcement of the takeover of the Freiberg plant and plans for operational start in the first quarter of 2020.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 08-08-2019
   - **Details:** The facility is expected to create around 200 jobs in the short term as a result of the operations planned at the Freiberg site.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 08-08-2019
   - **Details:** JT Energy Systems is a joint venture founded by Jungheinrich and Triathlon Holding, indicating that the facility is not under exclusive ownership but rather a collaborative effort between the two companies.
