## DEU-00338-03508

### Evolutionary Timeline of Solarworld Industries GmbH Facility in Freiberg, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **08/2017**: Solarworld Industries GmbH aims to continue cell and module production at its German facilities, including Freiberg. The facility is involved in the production of solar cells and modules.
- **01/2018**: Solarworld signs an exclusive distribution partnership with Inosolar for the delivery of solar modules totaling over 60 MW for the Turkish market, indicating ongoing production capabilities at the Freiberg facility.
- **05/2018**: Production in Arnstadt is halted due to low raw material availability, but module production in Freiberg continues.
- **09/2018**: Module production at the Freiberg facility is suspended due to a lack of raw materials, with employees being relocated to a transfer company.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **08/2017**: An agreement is reached with an investor group, including the Qatar Foundation, to take over Solarworld's operations, which includes the Freiberg facility. The purchase price is primarily the replacement of liabilities, indicating a financial restructuring rather than direct investment in expansion.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **08/2017**: The insolvency administrator signs a purchase and transfer agreement to continue production at the Freiberg facility, pending creditor approval.
- **05/2018**: The production line in Arnstadt is closed, but module production in Freiberg is still operational.
- **09/2018**: Module production in Freiberg is halted, and negotiations with a potential European PV company for the site are ongoing.

#### 4. Labour
- **08/2017**: The agreement to continue operations includes the transfer of approximately 280 employees at the Freiberg facility.
- **09/2018**: By the end of September, 120 of the remaining 150 module production workers are relocated to a transfer company, indicating significant job loss at the Freiberg facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **08/2017**: Solarworld Industries GmbH is in the process of being taken over by a newly registered company, which includes the Qatar Foundation as an investor. This indicates a shift in ownership structure but does not confirm exclusive ownership by any single entity.
