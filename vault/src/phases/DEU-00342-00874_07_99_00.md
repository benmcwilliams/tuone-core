1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2019:** ZF Friedrichshafen AG opened a new test center for driveline technology in Friedrichshafen. This facility is designed to test electric, hybrid, and internal combustion engine drives. It includes battery simulators that supply the necessary energy for electric and hybrid drives. The facility is part of ZF's broader Research and Development Center, which also includes existing transmission test benches and a test bench for laser-based Lidar technology, crucial for automated driving functions.
