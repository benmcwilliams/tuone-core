   - **August 2022**: BMW began producing hydrogen fuel cells for the iX5 Hydrogen at its Hydrogen Competence Center in Garching, Munich. The iX5 Hydrogen features a fuel cell output of 125 kW and a total system output of 275 kW when combined with an electric motor.
