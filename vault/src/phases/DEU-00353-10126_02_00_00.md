### Evolutionary Timeline of Strabag Umwelttechnik GmbH Facility in Garching, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2019**: The facility is involved in the construction of a geothermal power plant with a total capacity of 4.3 MW. The plant will utilize three gas-powered cogeneration units, each with a capacity of 900 kW. The facility is designed to operate with Isobutan as the working fluid and features water cooling and black start capability, allowing it to function independently of the external electric power network.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2019**: The Bavarian state government has initiated a master plan for the expansion of geothermal energy, which includes promised funding to support the development of the facility. This funding is expected to enhance the efficiency and operational capabilities of the plant.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2019**: The managing directors of Silenos Energy GmbH, which oversees the project, announced that the construction work is ongoing and that the commissioning of the power plant is expected to take place in August/September 2020. The project has been on schedule, with the first electricity production anticipated within a year.
