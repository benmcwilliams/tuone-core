### Evolutionary Timeline of the Samsung Facility in Gera-Cretzschwitz, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 11-09-2023
   - **Details:** Samsung, in collaboration with SungEel HiTech, plans to build a battery recycling plant in Gera-Cretzschwitz. The facility will have the capacity to recycle 20,000 tons of lithium-ion batteries annually. The plant will feature two production lines, with the first expected to start operations in early 2025 and the second in early 2027. The recycling process will involve dismantling, crushing, and drying old batteries, followed by a mechanical separation of materials to recover raw materials for new batteries.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 11-09-2023
   - **Details:** The total investment for the battery recycling plant is €45 million. 

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 11-09-2023
   - **Details:** Construction of the facility is scheduled to begin in March 2024. The project was initially planned for a different location in Rudolstadt but was moved to Gera-Cretzschwitz due to local objections. The Gera city council supported the project with a narrow majority.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 11-09-2023
   - **Details:** The facility is expected to create approximately 100 jobs upon completion.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 11-09-2023
   - **Details:** The facility will be a joint venture between Samsung and SungEel HiTech, indicating that it is not exclusively owned by Samsung.
