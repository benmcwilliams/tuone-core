### Evolutionary Timeline of the Samsung Facility in Gera-Cretzschwitz, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2023**: Samsung and SungEel HiTech announced plans to build a battery recycling plant in the Gera-Cretzschwitz industrial park. The facility will have the capacity to recycle **20,000 tons of lithium-ion batteries annually**. The process will involve dismantling, crushing, and drying old batteries on-site, followed by a mechanical separation of materials to recover raw materials for new batteries.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2023**: The total investment for the battery recycling plant is estimated at **€45 million**. 

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2024**: Construction of the battery recycling plant is scheduled to begin.
- **Early 2025**: The first of two production lines is expected to commence operations.
- **Early 2027**: The second production line is anticipated to start operations.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2023**: The facility is projected to create **100 jobs** upon completion.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **September 2023**: The facility will operate as a **joint venture** between Samsung and SungEel HiTech.
