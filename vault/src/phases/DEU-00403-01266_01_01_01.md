## DEU-00403-01266

### Evolutionary Timeline of Rock Tech Facility in Guben, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **11-10-2021**: Rock Tech Lithium announces plans to build a production plant for battery-grade lithium hydroxide in Guben, Brandenburg, with a production capacity of approximately 24,000 tonnes per year, sufficient for around 500,000 electric vehicles.
- **02-02-2022**: The company confirms the Guben plant will be the first of five planned production sites in Europe.
- **08-03-2022**: Rock Tech emphasizes its intention to process lithium from its Georgia Lake mine in Ontario, Canada, at the Guben facility.
- **27-03-2023**: Groundbreaking ceremony for the Guben converter plant takes place, with the facility expected to start operations in mid-2025 and produce qualified battery-grade lithium hydroxide from 2026.
- **10-05-2024**: Rock Tech announces that the Guben facility will produce around 24,000 tonnes of battery-grade lithium hydroxide annually, with plans to source 50% of raw materials from battery recycling by 2030.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **11-10-2021**: Rock Tech estimates a total investment of up to 470 million euros for the Guben site, contingent on state funding commitments.
- **08-03-2023**: The estimated cost for the Guben converter is reported to be around 683 million USD (approximately 648 million euros).
- **13-06-2024**: Rock Tech receives up to 100 million euros in funding from the Brandenburg state government after being denied federal funding.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **11-10-2021**: Announcement of the Guben facility construction.
- **27-03-2023**: Groundbreaking ceremony for the Guben converter plant.
- **12-06-2024**: Final approval from Brandenburg's state government for the lithium hydroxide converter.
- **10-05-2024**: Rock Tech begins construction of the Guben facility with preliminary approvals.

#### 4. Labour
- **11-10-2021**: The Guben facility is expected to create approximately 160 jobs.
- **08-03-2023**: The number of jobs created is updated to around 170 as construction progresses.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Rock Tech Lithium is a German-Canadian company headquartered in Vancouver, and it is expected to have full ownership of the Guben facility, as there are no indications of joint ventures or partnerships regarding ownership mentioned in the articles.
