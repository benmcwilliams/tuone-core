### Evolutionary Timeline of the Siemens Facility in Görlitz, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **July 2019:** Siemens announced the establishment of a hydrogen research laboratory at the Görlitz facility, which will serve as a competence center for hydrogen technologies. The facility will focus on the production, storage, and use of hydrogen, as well as research into decarbonized industrial processes aimed at reducing CO2 emissions in energy-intensive industries. Siemens plans to convert the site into a CO2-neutral factory by 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **July 2019:** An investment of approximately 30 million euros was announced for the development of the hydrogen research laboratory and the innovation campus.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **July 2019:** Siemens, the State of Saxony, and the Fraunhofer Institute signed a declaration of intent to strengthen the Görlitz site and support structural change in the region. This marked a significant turnaround from the plant's near closure in November 2017.

4. **Labour:**
