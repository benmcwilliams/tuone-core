## DEU-00422-00326

### Evolutionary Timeline of Gotion High-Tech Facility in Göttingen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2021**: Gotion High-Tech announced plans to convert an existing Bosch plant in Göttingen into its first battery production center in Europe, targeting a production volume of 300 GWh by 2025. The facility will produce lithium-ion battery cells.
- **June 2022**: Gotion revealed that the Göttingen facility would be developed in two phases, with an initial production target of 3.5 GWh scheduled to start in September 2023. The total planned capacity for the facility is 18 GWh, with 6 GWh from the brownfield site and 12 GWh from a greenfield expansion.
- **September 2023**: Gotion commenced production of battery packs at the Göttingen facility, with the first products expected to be delivered to customers in October 2023. The production line is highly automated, with nearly 70% automation overall.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2021**: Gotion acquired the Bosch plant in Göttingen, although specific financial details regarding the purchase were not disclosed.
- **June 2022**: The company indicated that the redevelopment of the Göttingen site would begin at the end of 2022, with an estimated production value of €2 billion once fully operational.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2021**: Announcement of the acquisition of the Bosch plant and plans for conversion into a battery production facility.
- **September 2023**: Official start of battery pack production in Göttingen, marking the facility's operational milestone.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2023**: Gotion's expansion in Göttingen is expected to create numerous jobs, although specific numbers have not been disclosed. The facility is part of Gotion's broader strategy to localize battery production in Europe, which is anticipated to generate significant employment opportunities.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Gotion High-Tech is the sole owner of the Göttingen facility, having acquired all shares in Robert Bosch Aftermarket Solutions GmbH as part of the transaction for the plant. Gotion is also a partner of Volkswagen, which holds a 25.6% stake in the company, indicating a strategic partnership but not joint ownership of the Göttingen facility.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic moves made by Gotion High-Tech regarding its facility in Göttingen, Germany, highlighting its focus on expanding battery production capabilities in Europe.
