### Evolutionary Timeline of the Aurubis Facility in Hamburg, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2022**: Aurubis commenced test operations of a new pilot plant for battery recycling in Hamburg. The facility utilizes a hydrometallurgical process to extract metals such as lithium, nickel, cobalt, manganese, and graphite from 'black mass', a residue from dismantled lithium-ion batteries. The pilot plant is modular in design, and the company plans to develop an industrial-scale battery recycling plant within the next five years.

#### 2. Investment and Development

This investment aligns with the company's strategy to enhance resource recycling and close the cycle of valuable metals for electric vehicles.
