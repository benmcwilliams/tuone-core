### Evolutionary Timeline of the Northvolt Facility in Hamburg, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2023**: Northvolt and EMR opened a recycling facility in Hamburg, Germany. The facility is designed to process **10,000 tonnes of battery packs per year**. It focuses on discharging and dismantling complex traction batteries from electric vehicles. The facility is equipped with specially developed battery unloading and dismantling solutions, and the recovered materials will be sent to Northvolt’s recycling facilities for further processing.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2023**: Northvolt raised an additional **$1.2 billion** in capital to finance its expansion in Europe and North America, which includes the establishment of the Hamburg facility. This capital increase brings Northvolt's total debt and equity to over **$9 billion**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2023**: The Hamburg recycling facility was officially opened, marking it as the first recycling plant in the city. This facility is part of Northvolt's broader strategy to establish a circular battery industry in Europe.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2023**: The opening of the Hamburg facility is expected to contribute to job creation, although specific numbers regarding job creation or loss were not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **August 2023**: The facility in Hamburg is operated jointly by Northvolt and EMR, indicating a **joint venture** structure rather than full ownership by Northvolt.
