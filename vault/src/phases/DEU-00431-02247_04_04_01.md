### Evolutionary Timeline of the Gasnetz Hamburg Facility in Hamburg, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2021**: The Hamburg Hydrogen Initiative announced plans for a **100 MW electrolyser** as part of the Hamburg Green Hydrogen Hub. This facility aims to produce hydrogen from wind and solar power at the Hamburg-Moorburg power plant site.
- **July 2024**: The Hamburg Green Hydrogen Hub project, which includes the construction of the **100 MW electrolyzer**, is confirmed to be underway at the former Moorburg coal-fired power plant site.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2021**: The Hamburg Hydrogen Network, which includes Gasnetz Hamburg, submitted an application for funding under the EU's **Important Projects of Common European Interest (IPCEI)** program. This application is crucial for financial support to implement the hydrogen production and distribution projects.
- **July 2024**: The German government announced it would provide **over €250 million** for the Hamburg Green Hydrogen Hub project, which includes funding for the construction of the **first 40 km of the HH-WIN hydrogen distribution network**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2021**: The Hamburg Hydrogen Initiative was unveiled, detailing the collaborative efforts of multiple companies, including Gasnetz Hamburg, to establish a hydrogen network in Hamburg.
- **July 2024**: The project status is confirmed with funding secured for the construction of the **100 MW electrolyzer** and the **40 km hydrogen distribution network**.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2021**: While specific job creation numbers were not mentioned, the announcement of the hydrogen network and the planned projects suggest potential job creation in the construction and operational phases of the facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2021**: Gasnetz Hamburg is part of a collaborative network involving multiple companies, including Shell, Vattenfall, and Mitsubishi Heavy Industries, indicating a **joint venture** structure for the hydrogen production and distribution projects rather than exclusive ownership.
