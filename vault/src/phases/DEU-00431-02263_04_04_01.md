## DEU-00431-02263

### Evolutionary Timeline of H-TEC Facility in Hamburg, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: H-TEC Systems began construction of a manufacturing facility in Hamburg for PEM electrolyzer stacks. The facility aims to automate production with a potential total electrolysis capacity of up to **5 GW**. The production line is expected to be operational by **2024**.
- **August 2024**: H-TEC announced that it would start production of **1 GW** of electrolyzers in **September 2024**, with plans to scale up to **5 GW** by the end of the decade.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: The construction of the automated factory in Hamburg is funded by Germany's Federal Ministry of Education and Research (BMBF) as part of the PEP.IN research project, which aims to make green hydrogen affordable and competitive.
- **August 2024**: H-TEC Systems announced a rebranding to "Quest One" and highlighted the significant growth in its workforce and production capabilities since its acquisition by MAN Energy Solutions in 2019.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023**: Groundbreaking for the H-TEC Systems Stack Manufacturing and Development Center in Hamburg.
- **September 2024**: Official opening of the production facility, marking the start of automated series production of electrolyzer stacks.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2024**: The workforce at H-TEC Systems has increased more than tenfold since its acquisition by MAN Energy Solutions, indicating significant job creation as the facility expands its operations.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- H-TEC Systems is a subsidiary of **MAN Energy Solutions**, indicating that it operates under a joint ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones of the H-TEC facility in Hamburg, focusing on its technological advancements, investments, project milestones, labor impacts, and ownership structure.
