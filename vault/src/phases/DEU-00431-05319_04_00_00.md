### Evolutionary Timeline of the INNIO Facility in Hamburg, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: November 2019
   - **Details**: INNIO, in collaboration with HanseWerk, announced a pilot project aimed at utilizing hydrogen technology in Combined Heat and Power (CHP) plants. The facility will feature a Jenbacher CHP plant with an electrical output of 999 kilowatts, initially operating on natural gas but designed to run on variable hydrogen/natural gas mixes or 100% green hydrogen. The transition to hydrogen-powered generation is expected to begin in the summer of 2020.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Date**: November 2019
   - **Details**: The project is part of a broader initiative by the city of Hamburg to ensure that by 2035, customers in the power, heat, and transportation sectors can be supplied almost entirely with green hydrogen. Specific investment amounts were not detailed in the article.
