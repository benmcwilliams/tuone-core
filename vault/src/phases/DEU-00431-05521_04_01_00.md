### Evolutionary Timeline of the Luxcara Facility in Hamburg, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2023**: Luxcara joins the Hamburg Green Hydrogen Hub (HGHH) project in partnership with Hamburger Energiewerke. The project plans to install a 100 MW electrolyzer powered by renewable energy sources, with production commencement targeted for 2026. The facility is expected to produce approximately 10,000 tons of green hydrogen per year.

- **September 2024**: Siemens Energy is contracted to provide and install six electrolyzers for the 100 MW system at the HGHH. Construction is set to begin in 2025, with commercial operations expected to start in 2027.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2023**: The consortium, including Luxcara, submits a funding application for the national Important Project of Common European Interest (IPCEI) to the German government for review, indicating a push for financial support for the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2023**: Announcement of Luxcara's involvement in the HGHH project, with plans for a 100 MW electrolyzer.
- **September 2024**: Confirmation of Siemens Energy's role in supplying electrolyzers, with construction scheduled to begin in 2025 and commercial operations expected by 2027.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2024**: While specific job creation figures are not disclosed, the construction and operation of the 100 MW electrolyzer system are expected to create jobs in the region, contributing to local employment during both the construction and operational phases.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **September 2023**: Luxcara is involved in a partnership with Hamburger Energiewerke for the HGHH project, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership of the facility.
