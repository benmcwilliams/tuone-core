### Evolutionary Timeline of Hamburger Energiewerke Facility in Hamburg, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2024**: Hamburger Energiewerke, in partnership with Luxcara, announced the construction of a **100 MW electrolyzer** for green hydrogen at the site of the former Moorburg coal-fired power plant. This facility is part of the **Hamburg Green Hydrogen Hub (HGHH)** project.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2024**: The German government, along with the city of Hamburg, is providing **more than €250 million** in funding for the Hamburg Green Hydrogen Hub project. This funding is part of a broader initiative where the German government allocated **€4.6 billion** for 23 green hydrogen projects across the country.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2024**: The announcement of the Hamburg Green Hydrogen Hub project was made, detailing the construction of the 100 MW electrolyzer. This project is part of the third wave of the **IPCEI Hydrogen** program, which received EU approval in February 2024.
