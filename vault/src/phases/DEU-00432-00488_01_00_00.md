### Evolutionary Timeline of the MAN Facility in Hamburg-Bergedorf, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2018:** MAN Truck & Bus, in collaboration with Hamburg’s public transport provider VHH, initiated a project to test the second life of electric bus batteries in a stationary storage facility. The facility aims to utilize used eBus batteries as energy storage units to support an electric bus charging station at the depot. This prototype is designed to manage power consumption peaks during bus charging (peak shaving) by storing energy during off-peak times.
