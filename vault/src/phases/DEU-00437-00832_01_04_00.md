- **09/2018**: Volkswagen announced plans to electrify its facilities in Germany, including the Hannover plant, to manufacture the I.D. Buzz electric vehicle starting in 2022. The facility will not be exclusively for electric vehicles, as it will also continue producing other models.

- **01/2019**: The Components division of Volkswagen will focus on charging infrastructure at the Hannover plant starting in 2020.
