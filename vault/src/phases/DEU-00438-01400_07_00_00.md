### Evolutionary Timeline of the ÜSTRA Hannoversche Verkehrsbetriebe AG Facility in Hanover, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2021**: ÜSTRA Hannoversche Verkehrsbetriebe AG is involved in a project to recycle old eCitaro batteries. The facility will implement a stationary energy storage system with a capacity of approximately 500 kWh, utilizing around 20 battery systems previously used in eCitaro city buses. This system will be integrated into ÜSTRA’s new rectifier substation to support light rail vehicles and electric buses.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2021**: The project is part of the GUW+ model project, which is funded by the Federal Ministry of Transport and Infrastructure. The project aims to demonstrate the second-life application of batteries and improve the eco-balance of electric buses.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2021**: The construction of the energy storage system is set to be operational by the fourth quarter of 2021. The project began in 2019 and is expected to run for three years.

#### 4. Labour
