### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nexans Facility in Hanover, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **08-10-2018**: Nexans announces that it will manufacture 220kV land export cables at its Hanover facility for the Triton Knoll offshore wind farm project in the UK. The cables will consist of two circuits, each comprising three individual XLPE-insulated AC cables with an aluminum conductor. The first cable drums are scheduled for delivery in the first quarter of 2019.
- **07-11-2018**: Nexans secures a contract to supply and install 66kV array cables for the Borssele 1&2 offshore wind farm in the Netherlands. The cables will be produced at the Hanover facility, with delivery expected in 2019. The project will utilize three-phase 66kV copper cables, marking the first use of Nexans' new 66kV cables for Ørsted's offshore wind farms.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **08-10-2018**: Nexans is awarded a contract worth over €35 million for the Triton Knoll project, which includes the supply of land export cables and accessories. This investment is tied to the production capacity and operational capabilities of the Hanover facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
