### Evolutionary Timeline of Northvolt Facility in Heide, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2022**: Northvolt announces the construction of the 'Northvolt Drei' battery factory in Heide, Schleswig-Holstein, with an annual production capacity of 60 GWh, sufficient for approximately one million electric vehicles (EVs). Plans for a battery recycling plant on-site are also mentioned.
- **May 2023**: Northvolt confirms that the factory will produce lithium-ion batteries and aims to cover 50% of its raw material needs from recycling by 2030.
- **January 2024**: The production start is now scheduled for 2026, with full capacity expected by 2029.
- **March 2024**: Groundbreaking ceremony for the factory takes place, marking the official start of construction.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2022**: Northvolt receives IPCEI funding of over €155 million from the German government, with €108 million from the federal budget and €46 million from Schleswig-Holstein.
- **December 2023**: The German government allocates an additional €600 million in funding for the factory, including a convertible bond.
- **January 2024**: The EU approves a total of €902 million in state aid for the factory, which includes a €700 million direct grant and a €202 million guarantee.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2022**: Announcement of the Northvolt Drei factory.
- **May 2022**: Confirmation of funding from the German government.
- **March 2024**: Groundbreaking ceremony for the factory.
- **January 2024**: Northvolt commits to building the factory after securing necessary funding.
- **2026**: Production is expected to start, with full capacity reached by 2029.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2022**: Northvolt anticipates creating around 3,000 jobs at the facility by the time production starts.
- **January 2024**: The project is expected to create thousands of additional jobs in the industry and service sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Northvolt is the sole owner of the Northvolt Drei facility, with no mention of joint ventures or partnerships specifically related to this facility.
