## DEU-00454-04007

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Holcim Facility in Heide, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **04-08-2020:** The facility is set to produce hydrogen through a 30 megawatt (MW) electrolysis plant as part of the WESTKUESTE 100 project. This plant will utilize electricity from wind turbines to extract carbon-free hydrogen, which will replace fossil fuel-based hydrogen at the Heide refinery. The hydrogen produced will also serve various raw material needs, including heat, kerosene, and plastics.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **04-08-2020:** The total investment for the hydrogen electrolysis plant is 89 million euros, which includes 30 million euros in funding from the German economy ministry and 59 million euros from the project partners.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **04-08-2020:** Announcement of the construction of the 30 MW hydrogen electrolysis plant at the Heide oil refinery, marking a significant step towards commercial-scale carbon-free energy production.

4. **Labour:**
   - The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with this facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is part of a joint venture involving multiple partners, including Holcim, EDF, Orsted, and others. Therefore, Holcim does not exclusively own the facility; it is part of a collaborative effort with other companies and local entities.
