### Evolutionary Timeline of the Open Grid Europe Facility in Heide, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 04-08-2020
   - **Details:** The facility will produce hydrogen through a 30 megawatt (MW) electrolysis plant. This plant is designed to extract carbon-free hydrogen by passing electricity from wind turbines through water. The hydrogen produced will be used by the Heide refinery instead of fossil fuel-based hydrogen and will also serve the raw material needs for various partners, including applications in heating, kerosene, and plastics.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 04-08-2020
   - **Details:** The total investment for the project is 89 million euros, which includes 30 million euros in funding approved by the German economy ministry. The partners are contributing an additional 59 million euros.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 04-08-2020
   - **Details:** The project, known as WESTKUESTE 100, has received approval and is set to construct the electrolysis plant as a first step towards developing commercial-scale carbon-free energy.
