### Evolutionary Timeline of the Porsche Facility in Heilbronn, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **09/2021:** Porsche, in collaboration with Customcells, is planning to establish a battery cell factory with a production capacity of 100 MWh per year, sufficient to power approximately 1,000 cars. The factory will focus on high-performance battery cells featuring a silicon anode, intended for use in racing cars and limited edition sports cars.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **09/2021:** While specific investment amounts are not detailed, the establishment of the factory is part of a joint venture between Porsche and Customcells, with Porsche holding an 83.75% stake in the venture, indicating significant financial commitment from Porsche.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **09/2021:** The Porsche Board of Directors is in the process of deciding on the location for the battery cell factory, with Heilbronn being one of the considered sites. The decision is expected to be made in the coming weeks.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **09/2021:** The facility will be part of a joint venture named Cellforce Group, with Porsche holding 83.75% ownership, indicating that Porsche will have a controlling interest in the facility.
