   - **05-01-2022**: RWE deployed a second-life stationary energy storage system using decommissioned lithium-ion batteries from Audi's electric vehicles (EVs) at its site in Herdecke. The system has a production capacity of approximately 4.5MWh. The project utilizes 60 batteries, each weighing about 700kg, housed in a 160 square meter hall. The batteries, which still retain about 80% of their capacity, are intended for use in frequency regulation applications to help stabilize the German grid.
