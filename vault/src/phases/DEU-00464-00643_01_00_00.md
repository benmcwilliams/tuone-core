### Evolutionary Timeline of the RWE Facility in Herdecke, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06-02-2018**: RWE completed the construction of a battery storage facility in Herdecke, featuring over 550 lithium-ion battery modules with a total capacity of approximately 7MWh. The facility is designed to provide grid services, including power control and grid stabilization.
- **03-01-2022**: RWE commissioned a second energy storage system utilizing used lithium-ion batteries from Audi electric vehicles. This system has a capacity of around 4.5MWh and is located at the RWE pumped storage power plant at Lake Hengstey. The project aims to explore larger storage systems in the future.
- **05-01-2022**: The second-life energy storage system, utilizing 60 decommissioned Audi EV batteries, was confirmed to be operational. The batteries still retain over 80% of their capacity and are housed in a 160-square-metre lightweight hall.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06-02-2018**: The total investment for the initial battery park was about 6 million euros, fully funded by RWE through Belectric's construction and design services.
- **03-01-2022**: The second-life energy storage project is part of RWE's broader strategy to increase its energy storage capacity, targeting 3GW by 2030. Specific investment details for this project were not disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06-02-2018**: Completion of the first battery storage facility.
- **10-2021**: Installation of the second-life battery systems began.
- **11-2021**: Individual components of the second-life storage system were commissioned.
- **12-2021**: The complete second-life energy storage system became operational.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or job loss figures related to the expansions or operations of the RWE facility in Herdecke.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- RWE owns the facility exclusively, as indicated by the articles discussing RWE's projects and investments without mention of joint ventures or partnerships in ownership. The second-life battery project is a collaboration with Audi, but RWE retains full ownership of the facility.
