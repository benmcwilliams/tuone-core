## DEU-00474-00695

### Evolutionary Timeline of the SVOLT Facility in Heusweiler, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2020**: SVOLT announces plans to build a battery cell factory in Überherrn and an assembly facility for modules and packs in Heusweiler. The assembly facility is intended to convert battery cells into modules and packs for car manufacturers. Production in Heusweiler was initially scheduled to start in mid-2022.
- **September 2022**: Due to delays at the Saarland sites, production in Heusweiler is now expected to begin by February 2024, with the cell factory in Überherrn delayed to 2027.
- **December 2022**: SVOLT signs a lease for the Heusweiler facility, with the first part of the building (Production 1) set to be operational by April 2023 and final completion by mid-2024.
- **May 2024**: SVOLT announces it will not build the planned cell factory in Überherrn, but intends to proceed with the assembly of modules and packs in Heusweiler.
- **October 2024**: Reports indicate that SVOLT has cancelled its plans for the assembly facility in Heusweiler, citing financial difficulties and a reassessment of its location strategy.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2020**: SVOLT announces an investment of up to €2 billion for the two sites in Saarland (Überherrn and Heusweiler).
- **December 2022**: The lease agreement for the Heusweiler facility is signed, underpinning SVOLT's long-term plans in Saarland.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2020**: Announcement of the Heusweiler assembly facility.
- **September 2022**: Delay in production start to February 2024.
- **December 2022**: Lease signed for the Heusweiler facility, with phased operational milestones.
- **May 2024**: Confirmation that the cell factory in Überherrn will not be built, but assembly in Heusweiler was still planned.
- **October 2024**: Cancellation of the assembly facility in Heusweiler confirmed.

#### 4. Labour
- **November 2020**: Up to 2,000 jobs were projected to be created across the two sites in Saarland.
- **October 2024**: Reports indicate that SVOLT authorized a law firm to dismiss all employees at the Heusweiler site, with around half of the initial 50 employees already dismissed.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **November 2020**: The facility in Heusweiler is part of SVOLT's European expansion strategy, but specific ownership details regarding the lease and potential future ownership were not disclosed.
- **October 2024**: With the cancellation of plans, the ownership structure remains uncertain as SVOLT withdraws from the European market.

This timeline captures the key developments and changes regarding the SVOLT facility in Heusweiler, DEU, highlighting the challenges faced and the eventual cancellation of the project.
