### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lion Smart Facility in Hildburghausen, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2023**: Lion Smart announced that it will equip its second-generation battery packs with cells from the Chinese manufacturer SVOLT, including both NCM- and LFP-based cells. The facility in Hildburghausen is currently building the first generation of its battery packs, which were inaugurated in June 2023. The production capacity is approximately 4.8 million battery cells annually, translating to 200 battery packs per day or 50,000 packs per year, with operations running in three shifts. This capacity was revised upwards from an initial plan of 40,000 packs per year in a two-shift operation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: Lion Smart invested several million euros in the new facility in Hildburghausen, where production was moved from Dingolfing, Lower Bavaria. This investment supports the establishment of the new production line for battery packs.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2022**: Announcement of the move of production from Dingolfing to Hildburghausen.
- **June 2023**: Inauguration of the series production facility in Hildburghausen.
- **August 2023**: Announcement of the agreement with SVOLT to source battery cells, with plans to start delivering battery packs with these cells in the second half of 2024.
