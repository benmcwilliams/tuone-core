### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fenecon Facility in Iggensbach, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03/2024**: Fenecon opened the CarBatteryReFactory in Iggensbach, which produces large-scale stationary storage systems from new, disused electric vehicle batteries. The facility has a production capacity of approximately 500 large storage systems and 30,000 home storage units per year. The factory also relocated the production of management systems for home storage systems to this new plant. The commercial and industrial storage systems have capacities ranging from 82 kWh to 1,288 kWh.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **11/2022**: Construction of the CarBatteryReFactory began. The initial budget for the first expansion phase was set at 22 million euros, which later increased to 25 million euros.
- **03/2024**: Funding for the facility included 1.7 million euros from the Bavarian Economic Development Agency and 4.5 million euros from the EU Innovation Fund.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03/2024**: The CarBatteryReFactory was officially opened, marking the start of large-scale production of industrial models in February 2024, following the commencement of home storage production in January 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **03/2024**: The opening of the facility is expected to create 100 permanent jobs at the plant.
