### Evolutionary Timeline of the Porsche Facility in Ingolstadt, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2022**: Porsche confirmed the postponement of the all-electric Macan's market launch to 2024. Pre-series production was already underway in Leipzig, alongside the current combustion engine model. The facility is set to produce the electric Macan and a new electric luxury SUV, which will be positioned above the current Macan and Cayenne models. The new SUV is expected to start production in 2026.
- **December 2024**: The Northvolt bankruptcy raised concerns about delays in the introduction of new electric models from Porsche, particularly affecting the electric Boxster and Cayman, which were planned to launch by the end of 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2023**: Audi inaugurated the Incampus technology park in Ingolstadt, which is a joint venture with the city. This facility will support the development of software and electronic architecture for Porsche's electric models, including the Macan, which is based on the new Premium Platform Electric (PPE).

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2022**: Announcement of the delay for the electric Macan's market launch to 2024 due to software development issues at Volkswagen’s subsidiary, Cariad.
- **September 2023**: The Incampus technology park was opened, which will play a crucial role in the development of electric models for both Audi and Porsche.
- **December 2024**: Reports indicated that the Northvolt bankruptcy could delay the launch of the electric Boxster and Cayman models.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2023**: The Incampus technology park is home to over 2,000 employees working on tech stacks for all brands of the Volkswagen Group, including Porsche. This indicates job creation in the region related to the development of electric vehicle technologies.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **September 2023**: The Incampus technology park is maintained by a joint venture between Audi and the city of Ingolstadt, indicating that Porsche does not exclusively own this facility but collaborates with Audi and local government entities for technological advancements.

This timeline captures the key developments and changes at the Porsche facility in Ingolstadt, focusing on its operations, investments, project milestones, labor implications, and ownership structure.
