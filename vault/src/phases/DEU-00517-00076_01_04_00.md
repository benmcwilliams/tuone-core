## DEU-00517-00076

### Evolutionary Timeline of the BMW Facility in Irlbach, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2023**: BMW announced plans to build a large assembly plant for high-voltage batteries in Bavaria, specifically to supply its vehicle plants in Munich, Regensburg, and Dingolfing. The facility will assemble round cells into battery packs for the New Class electric vehicles, with series production expected to start in 2026.
- **February 2023**: BMW confirmed the acquisition of a 105-hectare plot in Irlbach and Straßkirchen for the battery assembly plant. The plant will not produce battery cells but will assemble them from cells sourced from a gigafactory in Hungary.
- **September 2023**: Preliminary visualizations of the first construction phase were presented, indicating the plant's role in securing the future of BMW's Bavarian vehicle plants by supplying high-voltage batteries.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2023**: BMW's investment in the facility is tied to the acquisition of the 105-hectare plot, with plans to use only half of the area in the initial phase, leaving room for future expansion. The company is also expected to incur business tax payments that will rise significantly by the end of the 2020s due to the establishment of the plant.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2023**: BMW confirmed plans for the battery assembly plant, with construction expected to start in early 2024 and production slated to begin in 2026.
- **February 2023**: The location for the battery assembly plant was officially secured with the purchase of land.
- **September 2023**: The local land-use planning process was initiated, with a second round of public consultation planned for October.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2023**: Upon completion of the initial construction phase, the facility is expected to employ around 1,600 people, with approximately 70% of these coming from existing BMW locations. The company also plans to offer about 50 apprenticeships at the new site.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2023**: BMW has full ownership of the facility, having purchased the land outright. Additionally, the company holds purchase rights for approximately 29 hectares of adjacent land, indicating a strategy for potential future expansion.
