### Evolutionary Timeline of Customcells Facility in Itzehoe, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2019**: Customcells participates in the "Forschungsfabrik Batterie" initiative aimed at developing battery cell production technologies for industrial scale.
- **July 2021**: Customcells announces a joint venture with Porsche for high-performance battery cell production, indicating a focus on motorsport applications.
- **November 2021**: Customcells begins expanding its production facilities in Itzehoe to increase capacity, although specific future capacity is not disclosed.
- **July 2022**: Customcells acquires a new plot of land in Itzehoe for expansion, planning to build a new office and production/storage hall.
- **January 2024**: Customcells inaugurates a new headquarters in Itzehoe, which includes a research center and is designed to support future battery cell development.
- **July 2024**: Customcells signs a multi-year supply agreement with Group14 for silicon anode material, indicating a shift towards advanced battery technologies.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: Customcells completes a financing round with new shareholders, including Porsche, raising between €20 to €30 million to expand its Itzehoe and Tübingen sites.
- **November 2022**: Customcells raises €60 million in a Series A financing round to expand activities in electrification and R&D.
- **January 2024**: The new headquarters in Itzehoe is built with a focus on resource efficiency and powered by renewable energy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2021**: Expansion of production facilities in Itzehoe begins.
- **July 2022**: Groundbreaking for a new office building in Itzehoe is planned for August 2022.
- **January 2024**: The new headquarters is officially opened.
- **March 2024**: Customcells opens its first international location in Detroit, Michigan, marking its entry into the US market.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2021**: The workforce is expected to grow from 13 to 80 employees by 2025 due to the joint venture with Porsche.
- **July 2022**: Customcells employs around 110 people at the Itzehoe site, with plans to increase to 160 by mid-2023.
- **January 2024**: The new headquarters will accommodate around 160 employees.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2021**: Customcells remains majority-owned by its founders after the financing round, with Porsche and other investors acquiring shares from the technology consultancy P3 Group.
- **March 2024**: Customcells has reorganized after leaving the joint venture with Porsche, indicating a shift in ownership dynamics and strategic direction.
