### Evolutionary Timeline of the Tozero Facility in Karlsfeld, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2023**: Tozero opens its first hydrometallurgical pilot battery recycling plant in Karlsfeld, near Munich. The facility utilizes proprietary hydrometallurgical recycling technology to recover critical battery materials such as lithium, graphite, nickel, cobalt, and manganese. The plant is designed to meet the EU’s minimum lithium recovery rate requirements of 80% by 2031.
- **April 2024**: Tozero begins delivering its first batch of recycled lithium to commercial customers, emphasizing that its hydrometallurgical process exceeds European Commission recycling targets with recovery rates significantly higher than traditional methods. The company aims to produce hundreds of tonnes of recycled lithium by 2026.
