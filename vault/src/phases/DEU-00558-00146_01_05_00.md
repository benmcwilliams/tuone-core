### Evolutionary Timeline of the Circu Li-ion Facility in Karlsruhe, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2023**: Circu Li-ion develops a new process for upcycling lithium-ion batteries, integrating hardware and AI software. The facility in Karlsruhe is set to expand to 2000 m² to enhance production capabilities. The automated battery upcycling process is designed to save up to 48% in CO2 emissions compared to conventional methods. The facility will offer “clean upcycling” services as “Disassembly-as-a-Service.”

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2023**: Circu Li-ion secures €8.5 million in seed funding. This funding includes €4.5 million in equity from BonVenture and other industry experts, along with €4 million from the EIC Accelerator and partnerships with recycling companies and OEMs. This investment is aimed at supporting the expansion of the Karlsruhe facility and enhancing operational capabilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2023**: Announcement of the facility's expansion plans and the goal to recycle three billion batteries by 2035. The company also aims to build the “world’s largest data repository for battery recycling.”
- **September 2024**: Circu Li-ion begins a pilot phase in collaboration with Amazon to optimize the reuse and recycling of electric vehicle batteries. This pilot will utilize Circu Li-ion’s technology to process batteries from Amazon’s delivery network.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2023**: Circu Li-ion employs 34 people across its facilities in Karlsruhe, Berlin, and Luxembourg. The company plans to double its workforce within the next twelve months as part of its growth strategy following the recent funding.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **October 2023**: Circu Li-ion operates as a start-up and is not mentioned as being in a joint venture; thus, it can be inferred that the company has full ownership of its facility in Karlsruhe.
