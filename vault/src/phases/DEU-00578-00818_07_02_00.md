## DEU-00578-00818

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Valmet Facility in Kirchardt, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03/2021**: Valmet Automotive announced plans to build a battery assembly plant in Kirchardt, Germany, to produce battery systems for electric vehicles (EVs) starting in 2022. The facility will have 11,500 square meters of production space.
- **02/2023**: Series production of battery systems commenced at the Kirchardt plant, marking it as Valmet's first battery plant in Germany. The facility manufactures high-voltage systems for electric vehicles and plug-in hybrids.
- **02/2023**: The initial production program involved small-series production of modules, packs, and the battery management system (BMS) for a plug-in hybrid electric vehicle (PHEV) variant of an unnamed sports car brand. Pre-series production for a major order from another German OEM is scheduled to start in summer 2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **03/2021**: The decision to locate the battery plant in Kirchardt was influenced by a contract with a German automaker as a Tier 1 supplier for a battery program, emphasizing the need for local production to minimize transport routes.
- **02/2023**: The Kirchardt facility was built to support Valmet's strategy as a leader in battery system production, with a focus on sustainability, as production has been CO₂ neutral from the start.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03/2021**: Announcement of the construction of the battery plant in Kirchardt.
- **02/2022**: The plant was expected to begin operations by the end of 2022.
- **02/2023**: Official start of series production at the Kirchardt facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **03/2021**: The Kirchardt facility is expected to create around 160 jobs.
- **02/2023**: The plant initially employed around 60 people, with plans to recruit additional staff to reach a workforce of approximately 150 by 2024.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **03/2021**: Valmet Automotive is the sole owner of the Kirchardt facility, as indicated by the company's statements regarding its operational independence and strategic direction.
