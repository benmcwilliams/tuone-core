## DEU-00578-00818

### Evolutionary Timeline of Valmet Facility in Kirchardt, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03/2021**: Valmet Automotive announced plans to build a battery assembly plant in Kirchardt, Baden-Württemberg, to produce battery systems for electric vehicles starting in 2022. The facility will have an area of 11,500 square meters and is strategically located near Audi's Neckarsulm plant to minimize transport routes.
- **02/2023**: Series production of battery systems commenced at the Kirchardt facility, marking it as Valmet's first battery plant in Germany. The plant manufactures high-voltage systems for electric vehicles and plug-in hybrids.
- **02/2023**: The initial production program involved small-series production of modules, packs, and the battery management system (BMS) for a PHEV variant of an unnamed sports car brand. Pre-series production for a major order from another German OEM is scheduled to start in summer 2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **03/2021**: The decision to establish the Kirchardt facility was influenced by a contract with a German automaker, which necessitated local production to conserve resources and reduce transport distances.
- **02/2022**: The facility was expected to begin operations by the end of 2022, with significant investments made to ensure it met the requirements of the contract with the German OEM.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03/2021**: Announcement of the construction of the battery plant in Kirchardt.
- **02/2022**: Expected operational start date for the facility was set for late 2022.
- **02/2023**: Official start of series production at the Kirchardt plant.

#### 4. Labour
- **03/2021**: The Kirchardt facility was projected to create approximately 160 jobs.
- **02/2023**: The workforce at the plant began with around 60 employees, with plans to increase to about 150 by 2024 as recruitment for various positions was underway.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **03/2021**: Valmet Automotive is the sole owner of the Kirchardt facility, as indicated by the company's announcements regarding its operations and expansions.
