### Evolutionary Timeline of the Covestro Facility in Krefeld-Uerdingen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2021**: Covestro signed a long-term corporate power purchase agreement (PPA) with EnBW for solar-generated electricity, securing 63 MW of capacity from EnBW’s Weesow-Willmersdorf solar farm. The solar power will be utilized at Covestro's sites in Krefeld-Uerdingen and Dormagen for the production of mass-balanced plastics, including polycarbonates and thermoplastic polyurethanes, using alternative raw materials. This aligns with Covestro's strategy to transition to renewable energy and enhance sustainability in its production processes.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2021**: The PPA with EnBW represents a significant investment in renewable energy for Covestro, supporting its goal of climate-neutral production. The agreement is part of Covestro's broader strategy to convert its energy supply to renewable sources, which is crucial for the company's sustainability initiatives.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2021**: The PPA with EnBW is announced, marking the first solar power PPA for Covestro in Europe. The agreement is set to commence at the beginning of 2022, indicating a milestone in Covestro's commitment to renewable energy.
