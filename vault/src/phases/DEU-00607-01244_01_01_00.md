## DEU-00607-01244

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Primobius Facility in Kuppenheim, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2023**: Primobius was awarded a contract to build a shredding plant ('Spoke') for lithium-ion batteries at Mercedes-Benz in Kuppenheim. The facility is expected to process up to ten tonnes of batteries per day, with an annual capacity of 2,500 tonnes.
- **January 2024**: Primobius announced the construction of a hydrometallurgical refinery ('Hub') in Kuppenheim, which will process the black mass produced by the shredding plant. This Hub will also have an annual capacity of 2,500 tonnes and aims to recover battery materials such as lithium, nickel, and cobalt.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2024**: Mercedes-Benz is investing an unspecified amount in the double-digit million euro range for the construction of the plant. Additionally, the German Federal Ministry for Economic Affairs and Climate Protection is funding the recycling factory as part of a scientific research project called 'LiBinfinity.' The contract awarded to Primobius for the Hub construction is valued at approximately 18.8 million euros.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2023**: Mercedes-Benz laid the symbolic foundation stone for the battery recycling plant in Kuppenheim.
- **August 2023**: Primobius was awarded the contract for the shredding plant, marking its first commercial supply contract with an international electric car manufacturer.
- **Q4 2023**: Installation of the shredding plant is scheduled to begin.
- **Q1 2024**: Commissioning of the shredding plant is expected to occur as planned.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or job loss figures related to the investments or expansions at the Kuppenheim facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Primobius is a joint venture between the German SMS Group and the Australian battery material firm Neometals. Therefore, the facility is not owned exclusively by either company but is a collaborative effort between the two entities.
