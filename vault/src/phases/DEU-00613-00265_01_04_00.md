### Fenecon Facility in Künzing, DEU - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Production:** The facility in Künzing, Germany, has been involved in the production of large containerized storage units from electric vehicle (EV) batteries since 2017, albeit in small-batch production. The facility also produces management systems for home storage systems.
   - **Production Capacity:** The new CarBatteryReFactory in Iggensbach, which is an expansion of operations, has a production capacity of approximately 500 large storage systems and 30,000 home storage units per year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Investment Amount:** The first expansion phase of the CarBatteryReFactory was initially budgeted at 22 million euros but ultimately reached 25 million euros.
   - **Funding Sources:** The Bavarian Economic Development Agency provided 1.7 million euros, and an additional 4.5 million euros was received from the EU Innovation Fund.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Construction Start:** Construction of the CarBatteryReFactory began in November 2022.
   - **Production Start:** The home storage line commenced operations in January 2024, followed by the large-scale production of industrial models in February 2024.
   - **Official Opening:** The factory was officially opened on March 4, 2024.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Job Creation:** The new facility is expected to create 100 permanent jobs.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Ownership Status:** The articles do not specify whether Fenecon owns the facility exclusively or if it is a joint venture. However, it is implied that Fenecon operates the facility independently as part of its expansion efforts.
