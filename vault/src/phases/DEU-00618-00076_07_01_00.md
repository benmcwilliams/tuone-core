- **February 2020**: BMW announces that the Landshut facility will produce components for electric drives, specifically electric motors and battery modules, as part of its broader electrification strategy.

- **April 2024**: BMW announces a €200 million investment to expand production capacity for the central housing of the electric drive unit for the Neue Klasse. This expansion will increase annual production capacity for fifth and sixth-generation aluminum housings by approximately 30%.
