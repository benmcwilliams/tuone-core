### Evolutionary Timeline of the Horiba FuelCon Facility in Leichlingen, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Products:** Horiba FuelCon specializes in manufacturing test, manufacturing, and diagnostic systems for fuel cells and batteries, catering to the automotive and supplier industries.
   - **Production Capacity:** The facility in Leichlingen is part of a broader expansion that includes a new building in Barleben, which will feature 7,000 square meters of production space and 3,000 square meters of office space.
   - **Updates:** The expansion in Leichlingen was completed alongside the Barleben site expansion, enhancing the company's capabilities to meet increasing demands for battery and fuel cell technology.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Total Investment:** A total of 36 million euros was invested in the expansions at both the Leichlingen and Barleben facilities.
   - **Specific Investments:** The investment in the Barleben site is specifically noted to be around 30 million euros, aimed at increasing production capacity and creating a competence center for e-mobility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Announcement Date:** The expansion of the Leichlingen facility was announced on November 27, 2019.
   - **Completion Timeline:** The Barleben site expansion was expected to be completed by the end of 2021, although the Leichlingen expansion was also completed around the same time.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Job Creation:** The expansion is set to create approximately 250 new jobs, increasing the total number of employees from 110 to 360 at the Barleben site, which reflects the growth and demand in the clean technology sector.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Ownership Status:** Horiba FuelCon is a subsidiary of the Japanese Horiba Group, indicating that the facility is part of a larger corporate structure rather than being independently owned.
