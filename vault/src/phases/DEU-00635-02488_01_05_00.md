### Evolutionary Timeline of the Vitesco Facility in Limbach-Oberfrohna, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **May 2024:** Vitesco Technologies, in collaboration with Swiss electric vehicle manufacturer Kyburz, begins to implement a battery recycling process at the Limbach-Oberfrohna site. This process focuses on direct recycling of lithium-ion batteries, specifically targeting lithium iron phosphate (LFP) batteries. The facility aims to adapt the recycling process for series production, leveraging Vitesco's expertise in process automation and production technology.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **May 2024:** The partnership with Kyburz signifies an investment in developing a new recycling plant called ‘Liberty’ at the Limbach-Oberfrohna site. This investment is aimed at creating a sustainable business area in battery recycling, especially as the production of diesel injectors is set to be phased out by 2028.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **May 2024:** Announcement of the cooperation agreement between Vitesco and Kyburz to industrialize the battery recycling process at the Limbach-Oberfrohna facility. The project is positioned as a promising future technology for the site.

4. **Labour:**
   - **May 2024:** The implementation of the battery recycling process is aimed at securing jobs in the long term, especially as the facility transitions away from diesel injector production. The site manager, Carsten Czenkusch, emphasizes the importance of this new technology for job security.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **May 2024:** Vitesco Technologies is the owner of the facility in Limbach-Oberfrohna, with no indication of joint ventures or shared ownership in the context of the battery recycling project.
