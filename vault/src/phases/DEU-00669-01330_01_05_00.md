### Evolutionary Timeline of The Battery Lifecycle Company GmbH Facility in Magdeburg, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-04-2023**: The Battery Lifecycle Company GmbH (BLC) was established as a joint venture between Rhenus Automotive and TSR Automotive, focusing on the reuse and recycling of lithium-ion batteries from the mobility sector. The facility will handle all processing stages, including inspection, deep discharging, repairing, and dismantling of batteries, primarily sourced from the automotive industry. A dismantling line is planned for the TSR site in Magdeburg, which will be the first fully automated facility for discharging and dismantling battery modules in Europe, set to begin operations at the end of 2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05-04-2023**: The dismantling line in Magdeburg will utilize technology supplied by Bosch Rexroth, which will enable the transport of battery material up to 150 kilograms at a speed of 18 meters per second. The investment in this technology is part of the broader strategy to enhance battery recycling capabilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05-04-2023**: Announcement of the establishment of BLC and the plans for a dismantling line in Magdeburg, with operations expected to commence by the end of 2023.

#### 4. Labour
- **05-04-2023**: While specific job creation numbers were not mentioned, the establishment of the facility and the planned dismantling line suggest potential job creation in the areas of battery recycling and logistics, leveraging the expertise of both Rhenus Automotive and TSR Automotive.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **05-04-2023**: The facility is a joint venture, with TSR Automotive holding 65% ownership and Rhenus Automotive holding 35%. This indicates a collaborative approach to the operations and management of the facility.
