### Evolutionary Timeline of the Manz Facility in Mannheim, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **May 2022:** The facility is set to produce lithium-ion battery cells and assemble them into complete battery systems. A pilot line for battery cell production and assembly is planned at the Daimler Truck site in Mannheim. The "InnoLab Battery" will cover around 10,000 square meters and will house over 60 systems for cell assembly and electrode production.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **May 2022:** Daimler Truck announces a strategic investment to acquire a 10% stake in Manz as part of a capital increase. This investment is aimed at developing innovative battery technology and production processes for trucks and buses. The order placed with Manz for the first systems for cell assembly and electrode production is valued in the low double-digit million euro range.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **May 2022:** The cooperation agreement between Daimler Truck and Manz is signed, marking the beginning of the partnership focused on battery technology. The construction of the InnoLab Battery in Mannheim is underway, with plans to incorporate research findings into Daimler Truck’s battery-electric product platform by the end of 2024.

4. **Labour:**
   - **May 2022:** The establishment of the InnoLab Battery and the associated systems is expected to create new job opportunities, although specific job creation numbers are not detailed in the article.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **May 2022:** Daimler Truck will hold a 10% stake in Manz, indicating a joint venture structure for the facility, rather than full ownership by either company.
