## DEU-00679-00521

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mercedes-Benz Facility in Mannheim, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2019**: Daimler Trucks & Buses announces a supply agreement with CATL for battery cell modules for electric trucks, with battery systems to be developed and assembled at the Mannheim plant. The facility will focus on battery technologies and high-voltage systems.
- **07/2021**: Daimler Truck realigns its production strategy, with Mannheim specializing in battery technologies and high-voltage systems, integrating electric axle systems, e-motors, inverters, and fuel cell systems into its operations.
- **05/2022**: Daimler Truck announces a partnership with Manz to establish a pilot line for battery cell production and assembly at the Mannheim site, aiming to develop innovative battery technology for trucks and buses.
- **07/2023**: The topping-out ceremony for the InnoLab Battery laboratory is held, marking the establishment of a pilot line for the production of battery cells and assembly of batteries, with completion expected in August 2023.
- **07/2024**: The Battery Technology Centre (BTC) opens, allowing for the production of commercial vehicle-specific battery cells and next-generation truck batteries, with over 10,000 square meters dedicated to product and process development.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09/2019**: Daimler announces a mid-double-digit million investment in the Mannheim plant to support battery assembly for the eActros.
- **05/2022**: The partnership with Manz includes a capital increase, with Daimler Truck acquiring a 10% stake in Manz, facilitating the development of battery technology and production processes.
- **07/2023**: The construction of the InnoLab Battery involves significant renovations and expansions, including a 3,500-square-meter extension to accommodate new systems for battery production.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/2019**: Announcement of the supply agreement with CATL and the intention to start battery assembly for the eActros.
- **07/2021**: Realignment of production strategy, confirming Mannheim's role in battery technologies.
- **05/2022**: Announcement of the pilot line for battery cell production in partnership with Manz.
- **07/2023**: Topping-out ceremony for the InnoLab Battery, with the facility expected to be operational by August 2023.
- **07/2024**: Opening of the Battery Technology Centre, marking the transition to in-house production of battery cells and packs.

#### 4. Labour
- **09/2019**: The investment and expansion plans are expected to create jobs related to the new battery assembly operations.
- **07/2024**: The establishment of the BTC and the InnoLab Battery is anticipated to lead to job creation in the areas of battery technology development and production.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **09/2019 - 07/2024**: The facility is owned exclusively by Daimler Truck, with strategic partnerships (e.g., with CATL and Manz) to enhance its capabilities in battery technology and production processes.
