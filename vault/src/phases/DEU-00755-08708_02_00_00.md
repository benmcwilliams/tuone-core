### Ebersberg-Munich Energy Agency Facility Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is involved in harnessing geothermal energy, specifically tapping into a deep, Upper Jurassic thermal water reservoir in the greater Munich area. Currently, there are 42 boreholes generating a total thermal output of around 400 MW, with an estimated remaining potential of 1 GW. The facility aims to explore this potential further through a large-scale 3D seismic campaign covering about 1000 square kilometers.
