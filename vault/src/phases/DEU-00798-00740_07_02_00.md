### Evolutionary Timeline of the StreetScooter Facility in North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2017**: StreetScooter announces the opening of a second factory in North Rhine-Westphalia, which will double its annual production capacity to 20,000 electric vans. At least 50% of these vans will be produced for third parties.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2019**: Plans for the establishment of Germany's first battery cell factory are discussed, with StreetScooter as a participant in a consortium. The project requires subsidies of 250 million euros for initial production capacity of one gigawatt, with a long-term goal of increasing to eight gigawatts at an investment of 1.2 billion euros.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10/2019**: The German Federal Ministry of Economic Affairs indicates that a decision on the establishment of industrial battery cell production in Germany will be made within the year. StreetScooter's involvement in the consortium is highlighted, with potential locations including the Ford plant in Cologne-Niehl and Euskirchen.
