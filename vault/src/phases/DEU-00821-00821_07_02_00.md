## DEU-00821-00821

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Varta Facility in Nördlingen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2022**: Varta announced a pause in the construction of a factory for electric car battery cells (V4Drive) due to cost reasons. The pilot plant continues to operate, providing cells for Porsche, the first customer. Pilot production of the V4Drive cells in 21700 format began at the end of 2021, with mass production initially scheduled for 2023, targeting a production capacity of at least 2 GWh. However, the ramp-up to this capacity is now uncertain due to economic challenges.
  
- **October 2024**: Porsche initiated the construction of a further production plant for booster cells in Nördlingen, which will continue as planned. This facility will support the supply of battery cells used in Porsche's performance hybrid drive.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2024**: Porsche and major shareholder Michael Tojner each invested 30 million euros in Varta as part of a reorganization concept to secure financing for Varta AG until the end of 2027. This investment is aimed at strengthening the supply chain for Porsche's battery needs.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2022**: Varta paused the construction of the new factory for the V4Drive battery, with plans to resume only after securing binding customer commitments.
  
- **October 2024**: Porsche's acquisition of a majority stake in Varta's subsidiary V4Drive Battery was confirmed, and the construction of a new production plant for booster cells was initiated.

#### 4. Labour
- **November 2022**: Varta implemented temporary short-time work at the Nördlingen production site due to the challenging economic environment and cost-reduction measures.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **October 2024**: Porsche acquired a majority stake in the Varta subsidiary V4Drive Battery, indicating a shift in ownership structure where Varta is no longer the sole owner of this business unit, but rather operates in a joint venture with Porsche.

This timeline captures the key developments and changes at the Varta facility in Nördlingen, focusing on technology, investment, project milestones, labor impacts, and ownership structure.
