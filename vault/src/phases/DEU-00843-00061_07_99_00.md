### Evolutionary Timeline of the Audi Facility in Osnabrück, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Current Production:** The facility primarily produces the open-top version of the T-Roc. It is recognized as a “niche convertible location” within the Volkswagen Group.
   - **Future Considerations:** There are discussions about potentially producing an electric convertible at this facility, with both Volkswagen and Audi considering this option.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Ownership History:** The plant has been owned by Volkswagen since 2009, when it was acquired from the insolvency estate of Wilhelm Karmann GmbH. It operates as an independent subsidiary, Volkswagen Osnabrück GmbH, which is the center of competence for convertible and small-series production.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Future Planning:** A planning round is scheduled for December 9, 2021, where the supervisory board will discuss the potential for an electric convertible, which could clarify the facility's future projects.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Current Workforce Status:** The facility is currently considered underutilized, but specific job creation or loss figures related to the potential electric convertible project have not been disclosed.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Ownership Status:** The facility is fully owned by Volkswagen AG, operating as an independent subsidiary (Volkswagen Osnabrück GmbH).
