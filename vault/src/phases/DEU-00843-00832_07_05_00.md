### Volkswagen Facility in Osnabrück, DEU - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Production**: The facility primarily produces the open-top version of the T-Roc. 
- **Future Considerations**: There are discussions about producing an electric convertible, potentially based on the ID.3 model. The facility is recognized as a “niche convertible location” and is considered underutilized.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Ownership**: The Osnabrück facility has been owned by Volkswagen since 2009, when it was acquired from the insolvency estate of Wilhelm Karmann GmbH. It operates as an independent subsidiary, Volkswagen Osnabrück GmbH, which is part of Volkswagen AG.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date of Publication**: 01-12-2021
  - **Announcement**: Group CEO Herbert Diess indicated the possibility of producing an electric convertible at the Osnabrück plant. The supervisory board was scheduled to meet on 9 December 2021 to discuss upcoming planning, which could clarify the future of the e-convertible project.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
