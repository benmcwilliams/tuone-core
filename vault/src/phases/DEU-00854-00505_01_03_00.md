## DEU-00854-00505

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Manz Facility in Parsdorf, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2021**: Manz was awarded a contract to set up a battery production line at BMW’s Parsdorf site. The facility will produce lithium-ion battery cells, with equipment for coating electrode material and assembling battery cells. The production equipment is designed to be scalable for future capacity expansions.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2021**: The total investment for the pilot plant is expected to be 110 million euros, supported by funding from the Free State of Bavaria (30% of funding) and the Federal Government (70% of funding), amounting to 60 million euros for battery research as part of the "Important Projects of Common European Interest" (IPCEI).

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **04/2021**: Announcement of the contract for the battery production line.
- **End of 2022**: The plant is scheduled to start operations, as per BMW's timeline.
- **12/2024**: Manz files for insolvency due to over-indebtedness and inability to secure further funding, impacting its operations and future projects.

#### 4. Labour
- **12/2024**: At the time of filing for insolvency, Manz reported having 1,435 employees. The insolvency proceedings may lead to job losses, although specific numbers are not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **04/2021**: Manz is responsible for the construction of the battery production line for BMW, indicating a contractual relationship but not exclusive ownership of the facility. The facility is part of BMW's broader strategy to enhance its battery production capabilities.

This timeline captures the key developments related to the Manz facility in Parsdorf, focusing on its technology, investments, project milestones, labor implications, and ownership structure.
