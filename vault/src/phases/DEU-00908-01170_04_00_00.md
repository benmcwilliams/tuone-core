1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2020
   - **Details:** The facility produces hydrogen and is equipped with Linde’s Ionic Compressor technology. The hydrogen filling station has a production capacity of approximately 200 kilograms of hydrogen per day, sufficient to refuel 40 to 50 hydrogen vehicles daily. 

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** May 2020
   - **Details:** The hydrogen filling station in Rastatt was developed as part of the joint venture H2 MOBILITY, which includes partners such as Air Liquide, Daimler, Linde, OMV, Shell, and TOTAL. The project is supported by the European Commission under the Connecting Hydrogen Refueling Stations project (COHRS), indicating financial backing for the establishment of the hydrogen infrastructure.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** May 2020
   - **Details:** The hydrogen filling station in Rastatt was officially opened, marking it as the second refueling option for hydrogen vehicles in the Karlsruhe/Baden-Baden area. This facility is part of a broader initiative to expand the hydrogen refueling network in Germany, aiming for a total of 100 stations.
