### Evolutionary Timeline of the H2 MOBILITY Facility in Rastatt, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2020
   - **Details:** The H2 MOBILITY facility in Rastatt, located at the TOTAL filling station on Karlsruher Straße, began operations as a hydrogen filling station. It is equipped with Linde’s Ionic Compressor technology and has a production capacity of approximately 200 kilograms of hydrogen per day, sufficient to refuel 40 to 50 hydrogen vehicles daily. This facility supports the growing hydrogen infrastructure in Germany, particularly along the Rhine Valley Motorway.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** May 2020
   - **Details:** The Rastatt hydrogen filling station was developed as part of a joint venture involving H2 MOBILITY, TOTAL, and Linde. The project is supported by the European Commission under the Connecting Hydrogen Refueling Stations project (COHRS), which is part of the trans-European Transport Network (TEN-T CEF). This indicates a significant investment in the hydrogen infrastructure aimed at enhancing the supply of hydrogen for fuel-cell electric vehicles (FCEVs).

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** May 2020
   - **Details:** The facility was officially opened and began operations, marking it as the 83rd public hydrogen filling station in Germany and the 14th in Baden-Württemberg. This expansion is part of H2 MOBILITY's goal to establish 100 hydrogen filling stations across Germany.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** May 2020
   - **Details:** The article does not specify exact job creation or loss figures related to the Rastatt facility. However, the establishment of the hydrogen filling station contributes to the overall growth of the hydrogen economy, which is likely to create jobs in the sector.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** May 2020
   - **Details:** The H2 MOBILITY facility in Rastatt is part of a joint venture. H2 MOBILITY Deutschland GmbH & Co.
