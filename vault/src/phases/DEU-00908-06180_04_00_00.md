### Evolutionary Timeline of the TOTAL Facility in Rastatt, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2020**: The TOTAL filling station in Rastatt, located at Karlsruher Straße 16, began offering hydrogen refueling capabilities. The facility has a production capacity of approximately 200 kilograms of hydrogen per day, sufficient to refuel 40 to 50 hydrogen vehicles daily. The station utilizes Linde’s Ionic Compressor technology and is part of a broader initiative to expand hydrogen infrastructure in Germany.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2020**: The hydrogen filling station in Rastatt is part of the H2 MOBILITY joint venture, which includes TOTAL and other partners. The project is supported by the European Commission under the Connecting Hydrogen Refueling Stations project (COHRS), indicating financial backing for the development of hydrogen infrastructure.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2020**: The filling station was officially opened, marking the establishment of one of the 83 public hydrogen filling stations in Germany, with 23 located at TOTAL stations. This facility represents a significant milestone in the expansion of hydrogen refueling options in the Karlsruhe/Baden-Baden area.
