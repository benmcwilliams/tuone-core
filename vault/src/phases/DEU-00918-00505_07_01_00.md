## DEU-00918-00505

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Manz Facility in Reutlingen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2018**: Manz AG reported stable solar sales, generating €105 million in its solar business, primarily from CIGS (Copper Indium Gallium Selenide) contracts with Chinese partners. The company focused on follow-up CIGS orders expected by the end of 2019.
- **07/2022**: Manz announced its exit from the solar business, discontinuing all activities in the CIGS thin-film area after writing off €23.2 million due to a failed fab project in China. The company decided to focus on growth in automotive, electromobility, battery manufacturing, electronics, energy, and medical technology sectors.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2021**: Manz was awarded a contract to set up a battery production line for BMW, with the total order volume described as "well into the double-digit million euro range." This project is part of a broader investment strategy to penetrate the battery production market.
- **12/2021**: Manz received an order from Britishvolt for machines for the first expansion stage of a factory in Northumberland, England, with an order volume exceeding €70 million. This project aims to manufacture lithium-ion cells with an initial volume of four GWh.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10/2020**: Manz announced plans to partially resume operations at its Reutlingen facility after a temporary shutdown due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Approximately 75% of employees returned to work.
- **11/2021**: Manz began setting up a battery production line for BMW, with operations expected to start by the end of 2022.
- **07/2022**: Manz officially exited the solar business, marking a significant shift in its operational focus.

#### 4. Labour
- **04/2020**: Following the COVID-19 pandemic, Manz indicated that some employees would continue to work from home, affecting assembly roles at client production sites. The company aimed to maintain a workforce capable of processing current orders.
- **07/2022**: The exit from the solar business likely resulted in job losses, although specific figures were not disclosed.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Throughout the timeline, Manz AG has maintained full ownership of its facility in Reutlingen, Germany, as indicated by its direct involvement in projects and operations without mention of joint ventures or partnerships affecting ownership.

This timeline captures the key developments and changes at the Manz facility in Reutlingen, reflecting its transition from solar technology to a focus on battery production and other sectors.
