## DEU-00918-00617

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Porsche Facility in Reutlingen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2020**: Porsche and Customcells announce plans to build a battery cell factory in Tübingen, focusing on high-performance storage units for electric sports cars. Initial production capacity is not specified, but a medium-term capacity in the gigawatt range is conceivable.
- **07/2021**: Cellforce Group, the joint venture between Porsche and Customcells, appoints BASF as the exclusive cell development partner. The factory is expected to start production in 2024 with an initial capacity of at least 100 MWh per year, enough for around 1,000 vehicles.
- **10/2021**: The location for the Cellforce factory is confirmed to be in Reutlingen-Nord/Kirchentellinsfurt, with production scheduled to start in 2024.
- **12/2021**: The foundation stone for the facility is laid, with plans to produce high-performance lithium-ion pouch cells.
- **10/2022**: Cellforce announces plans to increase production capacity to at least 1 GWh, equivalent to battery cells for around 10,000 vehicles.
- **05/2023**: Reports indicate that Porsche has taken full ownership of Cellforce and plans to expand production capacity to 20 GWh.
- **07/2023**: Porsche confirms plans for a large-scale battery cell factory, with production capacity potentially reaching 20 GWh.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12/2021**: The German federal government and the state of Baden-Württemberg commit around €60 million in funding for the Cellforce project.
- **05/2022**: Porsche participates as a lead investor in Group14 Technologies, securing access to silicon anodes for the Cellforce Group.
- **06/2023**: Reports suggest that Porsche may invest over €1 billion in the Reutlingen facility to support the expansion to 20 GWh.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10/2021**: Foundation stone laid for the Cellforce factory in Reutlingen.
- **12/2021**: Construction begins on the facility.
- **04/2024**: Production is scheduled to start, initially with a small volume.
- **05/2023**: Porsche takes full ownership of Cellforce, indicating a shift in strategy for battery production.

#### 4. Labour
- **07/2021**: The workforce is expected to grow from 13 to 80 employees by 2025 as the facility ramps up production.
- **10/2022**: The facility is expected to employ a significant number of workers as production capacity increases.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **07/2021**: Cellforce Group is initially a joint venture with Porsche holding 83.75% ownership.
- **05/2023**: Porsche fully acquires Cellforce, transitioning from a joint venture to complete ownership of the facility.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Porsche facility in Reutlingen, Germany, focusing on its technology, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
