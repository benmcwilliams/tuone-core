### Evolutionary Timeline of the Porsche Facility in Reutlingen, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: Porsche plans to produce high-performance battery cells at the Reutlingen facility, with an initial production capacity of 20 GWh, significantly up from the previously discussed 100 MW and later 1 GWh. The facility aims to supply 180,000 to 200,000 electric cars annually.
- **July 2023**: The production capacity is confirmed to be limited to 1.3 GWh at the Reutlingen site, with discussions of a larger facility elsewhere.
- **September 2023**: The Reutlingen facility is still under consideration for expansion, but the focus is shifting towards a potential second site for larger-scale production.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: Porsche is expected to invest over one billion euros in the Reutlingen facility.
- **July 2023**: Plans for a new large-scale battery production facility are discussed, with potential investments of two to three billion euros, although the location may shift to North America.
- **September 2023**: The decision regarding the second site for expansion is expected by the end of 2023, with the Reutlingen facility's capacity being limited.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: Porsche takes complete ownership of Cellforce, the joint venture with Customcells, which was initially established in 2021.
- **Spring 2024**: Series production at the Reutlingen facility is set to begin, initially with a small volume.
- **End of 2023**: A decision regarding the potential second site for a larger battery cell factory is anticipated.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2023**: The expansion of the facility and production capacity is expected to create jobs, although specific numbers are not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2023**: Porsche acquires full ownership of Cellforce, transitioning from a joint venture with Customcells to exclusive ownership.
