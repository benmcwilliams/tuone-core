## DEU-00918-00617

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Porsche Facility in Reutlingen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2020**: Porsche and Customcells announce plans to build a battery cell factory in Tübingen, focusing on high-performance storage units for electric sports cars. Initial production capacity is not specified, but a medium-term capacity in the gigawatt range is conceivable.
- **07/2021**: Cellforce Group, the joint venture between Porsche and Customcells, appoints BASF as the exclusive cell development partner. The factory is expected to start production in 2024 with an initial capacity of at least 100 MWh per year, enough for around 1,000 high-performance electric cars.
- **11/2022**: The foundation stone for the Cellforce plant in Reutlingen-Nord/Kirchentellinsfurt is laid. Plans are announced to increase production capacity tenfold to over 1 GWh.
- **06/2023**: Porsche takes over Cellforce and plans to expand production capacity to 20 GWh, significantly increasing the scale of operations beyond the initial plans.
- **09/2023**: Reports indicate that Porsche is considering a second large-scale battery cell factory, potentially in Brandenburg, while maintaining operations in Reutlingen.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07/2021**: The joint venture Cellforce Group is established, with Porsche investing in Customcells to support the development of high-performance battery cells.
- **05/2022**: Customcells raises €60 million in a Series A financing round, with Porsche participating, aimed at expanding production capacities and R&D.
- **06/2023**: Porsche is expected to invest over €1 billion in the Reutlingen facility to support the expansion to 20 GWh capacity.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10/2021**: The foundation stone for the Cellforce plant is laid in Reutlingen.
- **05/2022**: Production is scheduled to start in spring 2024, initially with small volumes, ramping up to large-scale production in two to three years.
- **06/2023**: Porsche takes full ownership of Cellforce, indicating a shift in strategy to produce high-performance battery cells independently.

#### 4. Labour
- **07/2021**: The workforce at Customcells is expected to grow from 13 to 80 employees by 2025 due to the joint venture's operations.
- **06/2023**: The expansion plans at the Reutlingen facility are expected to create additional jobs, although specific numbers are not detailed.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **07/2021**: Cellforce Group is initially a joint venture with Porsche holding 83.75% ownership.
- **06/2023**: Porsche fully acquires Cellforce, transitioning from a joint venture to complete ownership of the facility.
