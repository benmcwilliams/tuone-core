## DEU-00918-00970

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Cellforce Facility in Reutlingen, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2021**: Cellforce Group, a joint venture between Porsche and Customcells, announced plans to build a battery cell factory in Reutlingen-Nord/Kirchentellinsfurt. The facility will initially produce battery cells for 1,000 vehicles per year, with a target capacity of 100 MWh per year, scheduled to start production in 2024.
- **July 2022**: Cellforce confirmed that the factory will produce high-performance pouch cells for special automotive applications, focusing on electric racing cars and high-performance road vehicles from Porsche.
- **October 2022**: The foundation stone for the facility was laid, and it was announced that production capacity would be increased tenfold to at least 1 GWh, equivalent to battery cells for around 10,000 vehicles.
- **May 2023**: Cellforce is set to utilize Siemens’ Xcelerator portfolio for automation and digitalization in the production process, aiming for a carbon net-zero battery cell factory.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2021**: The project received funding of approximately 60 million euros from the German federal government and the state of Baden-Württemberg as part of the European IPCEI project EuBatIn.
- **October 2022**: The announcement of the tenfold capacity increase indicates a significant investment in expanding the facility's capabilities, although specific financial details were not disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2021**: Announcement of the facility's location and planned construction.
- **March 2022**: Confirmation of the electric 718 sports car, which will likely use the battery cells produced at Cellforce.
- **October 2022**: Foundation stone laid for the facility, marking the official start of construction.
- **May 2023**: Strategic partnership with Siemens announced, indicating progress in the development of the facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2021**: The establishment of the facility is expected to create jobs in the region, although specific numbers have not been disclosed.
- **October 2022**: The announcement of increased production capacity suggests potential for job creation as operations scale up.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **December 2021**: Cellforce is a joint venture between Porsche and Customcells, indicating shared ownership rather than exclusive ownership by either company.
