### Evolutionary Timeline of Rheinmetall PolyCharge GmbH Facility

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: January 2022
   - **Details**: Rheinmetall PolyCharge GmbH was established as a joint venture to develop, produce, and market DC link capacitors, which are crucial components in power electronics for electric vehicles. These capacitors enable smaller, lighter, and more heat-resistant inverters, contributing to the electrification strategy of Rheinmetall. The facility will focus on producing NanoLam capacitors, which are 50% smaller and lighter than conventional capacitors.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: January 2022
   - **Details**: The financial details of the joint venture were not disclosed, but Rheinmetall emphasized its strategic positioning in the automotive supply sector and military markets to meet the growing demand for eMobility and Green Energy components.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: January 2022
   - **Details**: The joint venture, Rheinmetall PolyCharge GmbH, was officially founded, with operations based at Rheinmetall’s Sensors and Actuators Division. This marks a significant step in Rheinmetall's transformation strategy towards electrification technology.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: January 2022
   - **Details**: The establishment of the joint venture is expected to create job opportunities, although specific numbers were not mentioned in the article.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: January 2022
   - **Details**: Rheinmetall PolyCharge GmbH is a joint venture with Rheinmetall holding 75% ownership and PolyCharge America holding 25%. Additionally, Rheinmetall acquired a 13% stake in PolyCharge America. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic positioning of the Rheinmetall PolyCharge GmbH facility as of January 2022.
