### Evolutionary Timeline of the PSA Group Facility in Rüsselsheim, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **03-06-2020:** The PSA Group, in collaboration with Punch Powertrain, announced the formation of a new joint venture named "Punch Powertrain PSA e-transmissions." This joint venture will focus on developing, manufacturing, and supplying dual-clutch transmissions (DT22) for mild hybrid and plug-in hybrid vehicles. The Opel plant in Rüsselsheim is identified as a potential customer for these hybrid transmissions. The facility is set to transition towards producing electrified models, including the next generation of the Opel Astra, which will be partially electrified by the end of 2021.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **03-06-2020:** PSA Group confirmed investments aimed at making the Rüsselsheim plant a sustainable site for vehicle production, including electrified models. Specific financial details regarding the investments were not disclosed, but the focus is on localizing the EMP2 platform to support this transition.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **03-06-2020:** Announcement of the new joint venture with Punch Powertrain, with operations expected to start in the third quarter of 2020. The Rüsselsheim plant is set to produce a new model from the DS Automobiles brand starting in summer 2021, alongside the ongoing production of the Opel Insignia and the next generation of the Opel Astra.

4. **Labour:**
   - **03-06-2020:** The transition to producing electrified vehicles at the Rüsselsheim facility is expected to involve discussions regarding the workforce, particularly concerning the "declining production" of the M1X transmission, which is set to end in 2021. However, specific job creation or job loss figures were not provided in the article.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **03-06-2020:** The facility operates under a joint venture model with Punch Powertrain holding a majority stake of 61% in the new joint venture. PSA Group retains a significant role in the operations and production strategy at the Rüsselsheim facility, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership.
