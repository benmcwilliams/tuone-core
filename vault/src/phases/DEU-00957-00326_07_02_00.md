### Evolutionary Timeline of Gotion High-Tech Facility in Salzgitter, DEU

- **09/2021**: Gotion High-Tech is confirmed as a partner for Volkswagen's battery cell factory in Salzgitter, where unit cells for the volume segment are to be built starting in **2025**.
- **09/2021**: Gotion aims for a battery cell production volume of **300 GWh by 2025**, significantly increasing from a previous target of **100 GWh**. The facility in Göttingen, Germany, will be converted from an existing Bosch plant to support this production.
- **12/2021**: Volkswagen announces plans to produce battery cells in Salzgitter, with Gotion High-Tech as a production partner, focusing on the development of a "unit cell" for electric vehicles.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09/2021**: Gotion High-Tech's ambitious target of **300 GWh** production capacity by **2025** indicates significant investment plans, although specific financial details regarding the Salzgitter facility are not disclosed.
- **09/2023**: Gotion High-Tech and InoBat Auto sign a pre-joint venture agreement to build a battery cell factory in Central Europe, with construction expected to start in **2024** and commissioning planned for **2026**. This indicates ongoing investment in European operations.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/2021**: Gotion High-Tech is officially announced as a partner for the Salzgitter facility, with production of unit cells set to begin in **2025**.
- **12/2021**: Volkswagen confirms that Salzgitter will be the first production site for the new unit cell technology.
- **09/2023**: Gotion and InoBat outline plans for a new battery cell factory in Central Europe, with construction set to begin in **2024**.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
