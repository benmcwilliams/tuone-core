## DEU-00957-00582

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Northvolt Facility in Salzgitter, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2019**: Northvolt and Volkswagen announced a joint venture to establish a battery cell factory in Salzgitter, Germany, with an initial production capacity of **16 GWh** per year, expandable to **24 GWh**.
- **September 2019**: Pilot production for battery cells commenced at Volkswagen’s Salzgitter plant, aiming to scale up to **16 GWh** by the end of **2023**.
- **February 2020**: Plans to expand the production capacity from **16 GWh** to **24 GWh** were confirmed, with a building application to be submitted in March.
- **December 2021**: The first lithium-ion battery cell rolled off the production line at Northvolt's Swedish facility, marking a significant milestone for the company, although this event is not directly related to Salzgitter.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2019**: Volkswagen announced an investment of around **$1 billion** in the joint venture with Northvolt for the Salzgitter facility.
- **August 2020**: The German government provided a state guarantee of **€443 million** to support the financing of Northvolt’s battery cell factory in Sweden, indirectly benefiting the Salzgitter project due to the interconnected nature of the operations.
- **July 2020**: Northvolt secured **$1.6 billion** in loans from various banks to finance its battery cell factories, including the Salzgitter facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2019**: Announcement of the joint venture between Northvolt and Volkswagen for the Salzgitter facility.
- **September 2019**: Launch of pilot production at the Salzgitter plant.
- **March 2020**: Building application for the expansion of the Salzgitter facility from **16 GWh** to **24 GWh** was planned.
- **End of 2023**: Expected start of commercial production of battery cells at the Salzgitter facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2019**: The establishment of the Center of Excellence (CoE) at the Salzgitter plant began with **300 employees**, with an additional **700 jobs** expected to be created as production ramps up, totaling **1,000 new jobs**.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2019**: The facility is a **50/50 joint venture** between Northvolt and Volkswagen, indicating shared ownership and collaboration in the production of battery cells.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones related to the Northvolt facility in Salzgitter, Germany, focusing on its technology, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
