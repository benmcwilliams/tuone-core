### Evolutionary Timeline of the QuantumScape Facility in Salzgitter, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2021**: QuantumScape announced plans to produce solid-state batteries at a potential pilot plant in Salzgitter, in collaboration with Volkswagen. The initial production capacity is set at **1 GWh**, with plans to expand to a total capacity of **21 GWh** for series production.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2021**: Volkswagen indicated that state support would be necessary for the establishment of the pilot plant in Salzgitter, highlighting the importance of financial backing for the project to compete in the European and international markets. Volkswagen had previously invested **$100 million** in QuantumScape, which was tied to the development of their battery technology.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2021**: QuantumScape and Volkswagen are in discussions regarding the construction of the pilot plant in Salzgitter, with a final decision expected by the end of Q4 2021. The plant is referred to as **QS-1**.
- **July 2021**: QuantumScape announced advancements in their battery technology, including the development of ten-layer battery cells, which are part of the ongoing efforts to prepare for mass production slated for **2024 or 2025**.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2021**: While specific job creation figures were not mentioned, the establishment of the pilot plant and subsequent expansions are expected to create jobs in the region, particularly as production ramps up.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2021**: QuantumScape is in a joint venture with Volkswagen for the development of the facility. Volkswagen owns a third of QuantumScape, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by QuantumScape.
