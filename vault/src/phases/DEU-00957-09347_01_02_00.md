### Evolutionary Timeline of the Koenig & Bauer AG Facility in Salzgitter, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: Koenig & Bauer AG partners with PowerCo, a subsidiary of Volkswagen Group, to develop and industrialize a dry coating procedure for battery electrode manufacturing. This technology aims to reduce energy consumption by approximately 30% and eliminate two of the four traditional process steps in electrode manufacturing. The new process allows for the direct application of powdery materials onto foils, enhancing energy density and fast-charging capabilities of batteries. The technology is expected to exit the development stage by the end of 2024, with production anticipated to start in 2026/2027.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The partnership between Koenig & Bauer and PowerCo includes a Joint Development Agreement to create a roller press for large-scale powder coating of electrodes. The new technology is projected to save hundreds of millions of Euros annually by reducing energy consumption and floor space requirements in Gigafactories.
