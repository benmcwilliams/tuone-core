### Evolutionary Timeline of the Webasto Facility in Schierling, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2019**: Webasto begins battery production at the Schierling facility, focusing on assembling battery packs for electric vehicles using purchased cells from Samsung SDI. The production of a self-developed standard battery system for commercial vehicles is set to start in early 2020.
- **2020**: The facility starts producing battery packs for commercial vehicles.
- **November 2024**: Webasto integrates a battery storage system at the Schierling plant, utilizing thirty used commercial vehicle batteries to store electricity from the facility's solar power system. The initial storage capacity is one megawatt hour, with plans for additional containers to enhance energy management.
