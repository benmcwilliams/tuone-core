### Evolutionary Timeline of the H-TEC Facility in Schleswig-Holstein, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: June 2021
   - **Details**: H-Tec Systems is implementing a hydrogen production plant in Schleswig-Holstein, Germany, which will utilize a ME 450/1400 proton exchange membrane (PEM) electrolyser to produce green hydrogen. The facility aims to optimize local wind energy production by using excess power from wind turbines to produce hydrogen, rather than shutting down the turbines. The plant currently serves around 20,000 proWindgas customers of Greenpeace Energy and allows for the storage of renewable gas in the existing gas network or conversion back into electricity.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: June 2021
   - **Details**: The project involves a partnership with AEG Power, which will supply the Thyrobox DC 3 systems to provide power to the electrolysers. The investment focuses on creating a flexible and efficient power supply for the hydrogen production process.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: June 2, 2021
   - **Details**: Announcement of the collaboration between H-Tec Systems and AEG Power for the hydrogen production plant. The project is characterized as a turnkey solution, indicating that it is in the development phase with plans for construction and operation.
