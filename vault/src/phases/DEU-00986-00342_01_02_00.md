### Evolutionary Timeline of the Group14 Facility in Schwarze Pumpe, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2023**: Group14 acquires Schmid Silicon, focusing on the monosilane plant in Schwarze Pumpe. This facility is crucial for producing monosilane gas, a key precursor for Group14's SCC55 silicon battery technology. The acquisition aims to enhance Group14's ability to meet the increasing demand for silicon battery technology in Europe.
- **August 2024**: Group14's SCC55 product, a silicon-carbon composite anode, is highlighted as having five times the capacity of traditional graphite anodes and providing up to 50% more energy density. The facility is expected to support the production of this advanced battery material.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2023**: The financial details of the acquisition of Schmid Silicon are not disclosed, but the acquisition is positioned as a strategic move to strengthen Group14's supply chain in Europe and support its expansion efforts.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2023**: Group14 announces the acquisition of Schmid Silicon, marking the beginning of its operations in Schwarze Pumpe.
- **August 2024**: Group14 is in the planning stages to restart the silane gas factory in Schwarze Pumpe, with an expected timeline of 18 to 24 months for the facility to become operational.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2023**: Group14 plans to retain all current employees of Schmid Silicon and aims to increase the workforce fivefold once the plant is operational. The company also intends to provide training to attract additional employees from the traditional energy sector to the clean energy sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2023**: Group14 acquires Schmid Silicon, indicating that Group14 will have full ownership of the facility in Schwarze Pumpe. The acquisition is part of a broader strategy to enhance its manufacturing capabilities and supply chain in Europe.
