## DEU-01066-00076

### Evolutionary Timeline of the BMW Facility in Straßkirchen, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: BMW acquired a 105-hectare plot in Straßkirchen and Irlbach for a battery assembly plant. The facility will assemble high-voltage batteries for BMW's "New Class" electric car models, with production capacity set at 600,000 batteries per year. Battery cell production will not occur on-site; cells will be sourced from suppliers in Hungary.
- **November 2023**: The facility is confirmed to assemble delivered battery cells into ready-to-install battery packs for distribution to nearby vehicle plants in Munich, Dingolfing, and Regensburg.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2023**: The acquisition of the land marks the initial investment in the facility. The specific financial details of the investment have not been disclosed.
- **November 2023**: BMW is investing approximately €400 million in converting its Munich plant for electric vehicle production, which indirectly supports the battery assembly facility by transitioning to electric vehicle manufacturing.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2023**: BMW announces the acquisition of land for the battery assembly plant.
- **September 2023**: A referendum held among local residents results in over 75% approval for the construction of the battery assembly plant.
- **October 2023**: BMW plans a second public presentation of the construction plans and expert reports on the site’s impact.
- **April 2024**: Construction officially begins with the first excavators moving onto the site.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2023**: Upon completion of the initial construction phase, BMW plans to employ around 1,600 people at the facility. Approximately 70% of these employees will be transferred from existing BMW locations, and about 50 apprenticeships will be offered in cooperation with the Dingolfing plant.
- **November 2023**: The transition of 1,200 employees from combustion engine production to electric vehicle production is noted, indicating a shift in workforce focus within BMW's operations.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2023**: BMW exclusively owns the facility, having purchased the land outright. There are no indications of joint ventures or shared ownership in the articles reviewed.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic decisions surrounding the BMW facility in Straßkirchen, highlighting its role in the company's transition to electric vehicle production and the local community's involvement in the project.
