### Evolutionary Timeline of Q-Cells International Facility in Straßkirchen, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: July 2009
   - **Details**: Q-Cells International, in partnership with MEMC Electronic Materials, is constructing a solar park in Straßkirchen, Bavaria, with a total capacity of around 50 MWp. This facility will be the largest ground-mounted photovoltaic (PV) system in Germany utilizing crystalline solar cell technology. It will consist of approximately 225,000 solar modules installed over an area of about 135 hectares. The expected output will power around 15,000 households and reduce CO2 emissions by approximately 35,000 tons annually.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: July 2009
   - **Details**: Both Q-Cells and MEMC will invest up to $100 million each in the joint venture to cover construction costs. MEMC's investment will occur during the third and fourth quarters of 2009, while Q-Cells is expected to provide similar financial support for bridging finance during the construction phase.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: July 2009
