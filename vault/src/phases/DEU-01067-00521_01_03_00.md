- **09/2021**: Mercedes-Benz acquires a stake in the Automotive Cells Company (ACC), aiming for a significant increase in battery cell production capacity.

- **10/2020**: The transformation fund aims to secure jobs during the transition to electric vehicle production.
- **09/2021**: The partnership with ACC is expected to create thousands of jobs in battery cell production in Germany.
