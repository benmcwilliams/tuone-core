### Evolutionary Timeline of the Stadtwerke Stuttgart Facility in Stuttgart, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2024**: Stadtwerke Stuttgart (SWS) announced the Green Hydrogen Hub Stuttgart (GH 2 S) project, which will produce high-purity hydrogen from green electricity. The facility will feature three electrolyzers capable of producing up to 1,000 tons of hydrogen annually, with operations expected to commence by the end of 2026. The project also includes the construction of hydrogen filling stations and a logistics concept for hydrogen supply.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2024**: SWS secured €5 million in funding from the Committee for Business, Infrastructure and Administration of the Stuttgart Region Association for the HydroPulse Stuttgart sub-project. The total volume for the HydroPulse project is around €11 million, with 80% allocated for the construction and development of hydrogen filling stations and their connection to the H2 GeNeSiS pipeline.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2024**: Announcement of the HydroPulse Stuttgart sub-project, which is part of the larger Green Hydrogen Hub Stuttgart initiative. The groundbreaking ceremony for the GH 2 S project is scheduled for spring 2025, with production expected to start by the end of 2026.
