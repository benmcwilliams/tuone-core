## DEU-01068-00110

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Bosch Facility in Stuttgart-Feuerbach, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2023**: Bosch commenced volume production of hydrogen fuel cell power modules at the Stuttgart-Feuerbach plant. These modules are designed for Nikola trucks in the United States, marking a significant step in the decarbonization of the trucking industry. The facility focuses on producing fuel cell systems that can operate flexibly with hydrogen, eco/biogas, or natural gas.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2023**: Bosch announced plans to recycle fuel cell stacks, targeting the recovery of platinum group metals (PGMs) used in fuel cells. This initiative is part of Bosch's broader strategy to enhance the economic viability of fuel cells and reduce carbon emissions associated with platinum mining.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2020**: Bosch opened a fuel cell pilot plant in Wernau, Germany, based on Solid Oxide Fuel Cell (SOFC) technology. This facility included three fuel cell devices for stationary applications, contributing to the energy supply of the Wernau plant.
- **July 2023**: Series production of the fuel cell drive system began at the Stuttgart-Feuerbach site, with the first customer being Nikola for their H2 truck, expected to enter the North American market in Q3 2023.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2023**: The production of hydrogen fuel cell power modules is expected to create job opportunities at the Stuttgart-Feuerbach facility, although specific numbers were not disclosed.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Bosch operates the Stuttgart-Feuerbach facility as a wholly-owned site, focusing on the production and development of hydrogen fuel cell technologies. There is no indication of joint ventures or shared ownership in the articles reviewed. 

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic initiatives of the Bosch facility in Stuttgart-Feuerbach, highlighting its role in advancing hydrogen fuel cell technology and contributing to sustainable energy solutions.
