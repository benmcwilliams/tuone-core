### Evolutionary Timeline of the Customcells Facility in Tübingen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2020**: Customcells, in collaboration with Porsche, plans to build a battery cell factory in Tübingen. The facility aims to produce high-performance storage units with high energy density and low internal resistance, targeting a production capacity in the gigawatt range in the medium term.
- **07/2021**: The joint venture Cellforce Group, formed by Porsche and Customcells, is set to produce high-performance battery cells with an initial capacity of at least 100 MWh per year, scheduled to start in 2024.
- **09/2021**: The facility in Tübingen is confirmed to be the site for the Cellforce Group's battery production, focusing on high-performance cells with a silicon anode.
- **12/2022**: Customcells announces plans to digitize battery production at the Tübingen site as part of the TwinTRACE project, aiming to enhance quality and traceability in battery cell production.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07/2021**: Customcells completes a financing round, bringing in three new shareholders, including Porsche, to support the expansion of production capabilities in Tübingen.
- **11/2022**: Customcells raises €60 million in a Series A financing round to expand activities in electrification, R&D, and internationalization, with existing investors including Porsche participating.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2020**: Initial plans for the battery cell factory are announced, pending EU approval for state funding.
- **07/2021**: Cellforce Group is officially formed, with plans for the Tübingen facility to produce high-performance battery cells.
- **04/2021**: Production at the Tübingen site is scheduled to start in 2024, with a capacity of 100 MWh per year.
- **10/2021**: The foundation stone for the Cellforce battery production plant is laid in Reutlingen-Nord/Kirchentellinsfurt, with plans to increase capacity to over 1 GWh.

#### 4. Labour
- **07/2021**: The workforce at the Cellforce facility is expected to grow from an initial 13 employees to up to 80 by 2025.
- **11/2022**: Customcells anticipates expanding its workforce at the Tübingen site as part of its growth strategy.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **07/2021**: Customcells announces that Porsche holds an 83.75% stake in the Cellforce Group, indicating a majority ownership structure for the facility.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Customcells facility in Tübingen, Germany, focusing on its technology, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
