## DEU-01150-00158

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Customcells Facility in Tübingen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2020**: Customcells, in collaboration with Porsche, plans to build a battery cell factory in Tübingen. The facility aims to produce high-performance storage units with high energy density and low internal resistance, targeting a production capacity in the gigawatt range in the medium term.
- **07/2021**: The joint venture Cellforce Group, formed by Porsche and Customcells, is set to develop high-performance battery cells. Initial production capacity is planned at **100 MWh per year**, sufficient for around **1,000 high-performance electric cars** and racing vehicles, with production scheduled to start in **2024**.
- **10/2021**: Cellforce orders electrode coating technology from Dürr for the planned factory, indicating a focus on efficiency and quality in production.
- **04/2023**: Customcells initiates the TwinTRACE project to digitize battery production, aiming to enhance quality and traceability in the manufacturing process.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07/2021**: BASF is appointed as the exclusive cell development partner for Cellforce, providing NCM cathode materials and recycling production waste, emphasizing sustainability.
- **05/2022**: Customcells raises **€60 million** in a Series A financing round to expand its activities, particularly in electrification for aviation and further research and development.
- **12/2022**: Customcells announces plans for at least two additional battery cell factories in Germany, indicating a significant expansion of production capabilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06/2021**: The Cellforce Group is officially established, with Tübingen as the likely location for the battery factory.
- **10/2021**: The foundation stone for the Cellforce battery production plant is laid in Reutlingen-Nord/Kirchentellinsfurt.
- **11/2022**: Cellforce announces plans to increase production capacity at the Reutlingen plant to over **1 GWh**.
- **04/2023**: The digital twin project for battery cell production is launched, enhancing the facility's technological capabilities.

#### 4. Labour
- **07/2021**: The workforce at the Cellforce facility is expected to grow from an initial **13 employees** to **up to 80** by **2025**.
- **11/2022**: Customcells anticipates an increase in employment at the Itzehoe site, with plans to grow from **110 to 160 employees** by mid-2023, indicating a broader impact on job creation linked to the Tübingen facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **07/2021**: The Cellforce Group is **83.75% owned by Porsche**, with Customcells retaining a minority stake. This structure indicates a strong influence of Porsche in the joint venture's operations and strategic direction.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones of the Customcells facility in Tübingen, highlighting its technological advancements, investment strategies, project milestones, labor implications, and ownership structure.
