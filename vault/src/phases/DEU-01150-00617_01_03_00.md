### Evolutionary Timeline of the Porsche Facility in Tübingen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2020**: Porsche and Customcells plan to build a battery cell factory in Tübingen, focusing on high-performance storage units for electric sports cars. The production capacity is not specified, but a gigawatt range is conceivable in the medium term.
- **04/2021**: Porsche aims to produce high-performance batteries in Tübingen, emphasizing that battery cells are a core technology for the German car industry. Specific production capacity details are not provided.
- **09/2021**: The planned factory is confirmed to have a production capacity of 100 MWh per year, sufficient for approximately 1,000 cars. The factory will focus on high-performance cells with a silicon anode.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12/2020**: The project requires EU approval to qualify for state funding as an IPCEI project. The German government plans to invest around three billion euros in various battery cell projects, which may include this facility.
- **04/2021**: The joint venture Cellforce Group is being established, with the aim of producing battery cells for Porsche's electric vehicles, although specific investment amounts are not disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2020**: Announcement of the intention to build a battery cell factory in Tübingen.
- **04/2021**: Confirmation of the joint venture Cellforce Group, with plans to produce battery cells for Porsche's electric vehicles.
- **09/2021**: The Porsche Board of Directors is expected to finalize the location for the factory soon, with Tübingen being a strong contender. The factory's production capacity is confirmed at 100 MWh per year.

#### 4. Labour
- **09/2021**: The establishment of the factory is expected to create jobs, although specific numbers are not mentioned in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **09/2021**: Porsche holds 83.75% ownership of the Cellforce Group, indicating a majority stake in the joint venture responsible for the battery cell factory.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Porsche facility in Tübingen, focusing on its technology, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
