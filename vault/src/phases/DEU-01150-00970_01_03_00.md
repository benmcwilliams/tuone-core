### Evolutionary Timeline of the Cellforce Facility in Tübingen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: Cellforce, a joint venture between Porsche and Customcells, is established to develop and produce high-performance battery cells specifically for Porsche. The facility is set to have an initial production capacity of at least **100 MWh per year**, aimed at producing batteries for approximately **1,000 high-performance electric cars and racing cars**. Production is scheduled to start in **2024**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: Customcells completes a financing round that brings in three new shareholders, including Porsche. The investment is reported to be in the range of **20 to 30 million euros**. The funds are intended for the expansion of the Tübingen site and further development of technologies and research projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2021**: The establishment of Cellforce Group GmbH is announced, with operations expected to start in **2024**. The facility is designed to produce high-performance battery cells, with a focus on motorsport applications and potentially limited-edition road-going sports cars.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2021**: The workforce at the Cellforce facility is projected to grow from an initial **13 employees** to up to **80 employees** by **2025** as a result of the joint venture and expansion efforts.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2021**: Cellforce Group GmbH is **83.75% owned by Porsche**, with Customcells retaining a minority stake. The managing directors include representatives from both Porsche and Customcells, indicating a collaborative management structure.
