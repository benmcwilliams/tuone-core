### Heliatek Facility Timeline - Ulm, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **April 2012**: Heliatek GmbH achieved a world record efficiency of 10.7% on a 1.1 cm² organic photovoltaic (OPV) cell. The company plans to begin production of its OPV modules in 2012, with an initial production capacity of 2-3 megawatts (MW) as a proof of concept. The goal is to ramp up production capacity to 15 MWp. The production will utilize a low temperature, roll-to-roll vacuum process, which is unique to Heliatek.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **April 2012**: Heliatek initiated its third financing round, aiming to raise €60 million from current and new investors to establish a second roll-to-roll production line with a capacity of 75 MW. Production for this new line is scheduled to begin in 2014.
