### Evolutionary Timeline of Nikola Corporation Facility in Ulm, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2021:** Nikola Corporation, in collaboration with IVECO, began producing the Nikola Tre battery-electric vehicles (BEVs) at their joint-venture manufacturing facility in Ulm, Germany. The facility is designed to produce both BEV and hydrogen fuel cell vehicle (FCV) versions of Nikola trucks for the European market. The vehicles are based on the IVECO S-Way platform.
