### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mercedes-Benz Facility in Unterkücken, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 06-04-2019
   - **Details:** Mercedes-Benz Cars began construction of a new battery sub-plant at the Brühl site in Unterkücken, Stuttgart. The facility will assemble batteries for future electric vehicles of the EQ family, covering an area of 12,000 square meters. This sub-plant is part of Mercedes-Benz’s global battery production network, which aims to make the production of electric vehicles greener.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 06-04-2019
   - **Details:** Daimler announced an investment of over one billion euros in the battery production network, which includes the Unterkücken facility. This investment is part of a broader strategy to establish nine factories across seven locations on three continents.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 06-04-2019
   - **Details:** The groundbreaking ceremony for the battery sub-plant was held, marking the official start of construction. The facility is part of a larger initiative to develop the Untertürkheim site into a high-tech location for electrical components.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 06-04-2019
   - **Details:** The facility is expected to require current employees to be retrained for new roles in electric vehicle production, particularly in high-voltage systems. A job security agreement was signed to protect jobs through to the end of 2029, indicating a commitment to maintaining employment levels despite the transition from gasoline and diesel engine production.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 06-04-2019
   - **Details:** The facility is owned exclusively by Daimler under the Mercedes-Benz Cars division, as part of its strategy to control the production of key components for its electric vehicles.
