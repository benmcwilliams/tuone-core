### Evolutionary Timeline of the Varta Facility in V4Drive, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2021**: Varta presented the first V4Drive cell suitable for electric cars in the 21700 format (2.1 cm diameter, 7 cm height). The company aimed to commercialize this technology quickly, with pilot production announced for the end of 2021 and mass production scheduled to begin at the end of 2023, targeting a production capacity of at least 2 GWh.
- **End of 2022**: Varta put its plans in the automotive sector on hold, stating that the construction of the new factory for V4Drive batteries would only continue after securing binding customer commitments. However, pilot plant operations continued as planned.
- **August 2023**: Rumors emerged that Varta was resuming work on series production of battery cells for electric cars, indicated by a purchase agreement for anode copper foil with a Korean supplier.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05-07-2024**: Porsche is considering acquiring a majority stake in Varta's subsidiary V4Drive. The deal is still under negotiation, and Varta is examining a possible majority investment from Porsche. The transaction's finalization depends on various factors, including Porsche's review of Varta's balance sheets.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **2021**: Pilot production of V4Drive cells was announced for the end of the year, with mass production expected to start at the end of 2023.
- **End of 2022**: Varta paused its automotive sector plans, delaying the construction of the new factory until binding customer commitments were secured.
- **05-07-2024**: Negotiations between Varta and Porsche regarding a potential majority stake in V4Drive are ongoing.
