### Evolutionary Timeline of the 24M Technologies Facility in Wolfsburg, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 08-12-2021**: Volkswagen Group announced a partnership with 24M Technologies to industrialize 24M's semi-solid battery technology. This technology is an improvement over the conventional dry coating process and aims to optimize battery production by reducing material usage and eliminating several steps in the production process. The introduction of this technology into large-scale production is planned for the second half of the decade.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: 08-12-2021**: Volkswagen is taking a stake in 24M Technologies as part of its strategy to develop and produce battery cells in-house. The specific investment amount is not disclosed, but the partnership aims to generate considerable cost optimization in future battery production.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date: 08-12-2021**: Volkswagen's partnership with 24M Technologies is part of a broader strategy to develop a "unit cell" and build six battery cell factories in Europe by 2030. The first production site for the unit cell is planned to be in Salzgitter, with production starting in 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **Date: 08-12-2021**: The partnership with 24M Technologies is expected to lead to job creation as Volkswagen plans to upscale the technology for automotive applications, although specific job numbers are not mentioned.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date: 08-12-2021**: Volkswagen is not exclusively owning the facility but is entering into a partnership with 24M Technologies, indicating a collaborative approach rather than full ownership. The relationship is characterized as a licensing agreement where Volkswagen will develop and upscale 24M's technology for its own use.
