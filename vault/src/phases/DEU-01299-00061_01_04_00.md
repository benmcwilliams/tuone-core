## DEU-01299-00061

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Audi Facility in Zwickau, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2019**: Audi announces plans for an all-electric compact SUV based on the MEB platform, set to begin production in 2021 at the Zwickau plant.
- **10/2019**: Audi confirms that the production model of the Q4 e-tron concept will be manufactured at the Zwickau plant, with series production starting at the end of 2020.
- **03/2021**: Audi officially launches serial production of the Q4 e-tron at the Zwickau plant, marking it as the first electric SUV produced at a German site.
- **04/2021**: Audi unveils the Q4 e-tron and Q4 Sportback e-tron, emphasizing their role in Audi's electrification strategy.
- **01/2022**: Audi reports significant sales of electric vehicles, including the Q4 e-tron, contributing to the brand's electrification efforts.
- **09/2023**: Audi updates the Q4 e-tron for the 2024 model year, introducing a new electric motor and improved charging capabilities.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **03/2021**: The production start of the Q4 e-tron is highlighted as a significant investment in electric vehicle production at the Zwickau facility, although specific financial figures are not disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2019**: Announcement of the all-electric compact SUV production timeline.
- **10/2019**: Official confirmation of the Q4 e-tron production at Zwickau.
- **03/2021**: Start of serial production of the Q4 e-tron.
- **04/2021**: Launch of the Q4 e-tron and Q4 Sportback e-tron models.
- **01/2022**: Audi reports increased sales figures for electric vehicles, including the Q4 e-tron.
- **09/2023**: Introduction of the updated Q4 e-tron for the 2024 model year.
- **11/2023**: Production at the Zwickau plant is interrupted due to a shortage of electric motors, affecting over 1,000 employees.

#### 4. Labour
- **11/2023**: Production shutdown affects significantly more than 1,000 employees at the Zwickau plant due to a shortage of electric motors. VW continues to pay employees during this time and provides training.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility in Zwickau is owned by Volkswagen Group, with Audi producing its electric models (Q4 e-tron and Q4 Sportback e-tron) at this site, indicating a collaborative production environment within the group rather than exclusive ownership by Audi.
