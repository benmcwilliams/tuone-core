## DEU-01299-00992

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Cupra Facility in Zwickau, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2020**: Seat announces the development of the Cupra Tavascan, an all-electric SUV coupe, and confirms that the Cupra el-Born (later renamed Cupra Born) will be produced at the Zwickau plant starting in 2021.
- **February 2021**: The production version of the Cupra Born is revealed, set to go into production in Zwickau in the second half of 2021. The vehicle is positioned as a counterpart to the VW ID.3.
- **May 2021**: Cupra Born is confirmed to enter serial production in September 2021, with specifications including a range of up to 540 kilometers and various battery options (45 kWh, 58 kWh, 77 kWh).
- **September 2021**: Series production of the Cupra Born begins at the Zwickau plant, with a daily production output of up to 1,400 units, resulting in an annual capacity of approximately 330,000 electric cars.
- **June 2022**: Cupra presents an updated concept for the UrbanRebel, an all-electric small car planned for 2025, while continuing production of the Cupra Born at Zwickau.
- **August 2024**: The Cupra Born lineup is revised, introducing new battery options and performance enhancements, including a sporty VZ version with a 240 kW drive.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2021**: The announcement of the Cupra Born's production indicates a strategic investment in electric vehicle manufacturing at the Zwickau facility, aligning with Volkswagen's MEB platform.
- **September 2021**: The start of series production of the Cupra Born signifies a significant investment in the facility to support the production of electric vehicles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2020**: Announcement of the Cupra Tavascan and plans for the Cupra Born.
- **February 2021**: Production version of the Cupra Born is presented.
- **May 2021**: Confirmation of the Cupra Born's production start in September 2021.
- **September 2021**: Series production of the Cupra Born officially begins.
- **November 2021**: The Cupra Born is launched for sales.
- **June 2022**: Concept for the UrbanRebel is presented, indicating future production plans.
- **August 2024**: Revision of the Cupra Born lineup is announced.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2021**: The start of series production of the Cupra Born is expected to create jobs at the Zwickau facility, contributing to the local economy as part of the broader shift towards electric vehicle production.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The Cupra facility in Zwickau is part of the Volkswagen Group, with Cupra being a brand under the Seat umbrella. The facility is not exclusively owned by Cupra but operates under the Volkswagen Group's ownership structure, producing vehicles for multiple brands including VW and Audi alongside Cupra.
