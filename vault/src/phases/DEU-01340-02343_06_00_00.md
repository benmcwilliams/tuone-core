### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lhyfe Facility in Lower Saxony, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Capacity**: Lhyfe is building two production units in Baden-Württemberg and Lower Saxony with a maximum total production capacity of 8 tons of green and renewable hydrogen per day. This hydrogen will be supplied locally for mobility and industrial processes.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Power Purchase Agreement (PPA)**: On **January 26, 2024**, Lhyfe signed a long-term PPA with EDP Renewables (EDPR) for renewable energy supply. This agreement is tied to a solar project developed by Kronos Solar EDPR with a capacity of 55 MWp (39 MWac), expected to be connected to the grid in **2025**. The solar plant will produce 58 GWh of clean energy annually, sufficient to power approximately 18,000 households.

- **PPA Announcement**: The PPA with EDPR is noted as Lhyfe's largest to date, securing renewable power for its future hydrogen production sites in Germany. This agreement is significant as it marks the first long-term corporate contract for renewable energy signed in Germany, specifically with a hydrogen company.
