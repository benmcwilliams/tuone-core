### Evolutionary Timeline of the TerraThermo Limited Facility in Lower Saxony, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **09/2024:** TerraThermo Limited is developing a 12MW “Hot Dry Rock” geothermal power plant as part of Projekt Thermo in Lower Saxony, Germany. This facility aims to deliver low carbon and baseload geothermal power and storage at an industrial scale using enhanced geothermal technologies.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **09/2024:** The facility secures private investment from alfa8, which includes up to €1 million in working capital to advance Projekt Thermo towards financial close, and potential future financing of up to €32.5 million, contingent on certain conditions.
