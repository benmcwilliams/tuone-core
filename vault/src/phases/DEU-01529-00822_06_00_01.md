- **February 2020**: Vattenfall announced a 60 MW photovoltaic (PV) project in Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania, which is expected to be operational by mid-2021. The facility will supply solar power to Deutsche Telekom under a 10-year power purchase agreement (PPA).
