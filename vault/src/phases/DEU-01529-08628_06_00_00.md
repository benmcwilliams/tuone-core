1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2020:** Deutsche Telekom agreed to become the offtaker for a 60MW photovoltaic (PV) project sponsored by Vattenfall in Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania. The installation is planned to be operational by mid-2021 and will supply solar power to Deutsche Telekom's subsidiary, Power & Air Solutions, which specializes in IT infrastructure maintenance.
