#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05/2023**: The Battery Lifecycle Company GmbH (BLC) was established as a joint venture between Rhenus Automotive and TSR Automotive, focusing on the reuse and recycling of lithium-ion batteries from the mobility sector. The facility aims to handle all processing stages, including inspection, deep discharging, repairing, and dismantling of lithium-ion batteries, primarily sourced from the automotive industry. 
- The facility in Rheda-Wiedenbrück has been operational since 2021, where battery packs have been dismantled and assessed for reuse. The facility plans to introduce high-performance components for repairs and reconditioning of battery packs.
- A new dismantling line is planned for the TSR site in Magdeburg, expected to be the first fully automated facility for discharging and dismantling battery modules in Europe, set to begin operations at the end of 2023.
