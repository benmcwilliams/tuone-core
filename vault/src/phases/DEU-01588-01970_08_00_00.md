### Evolutionary Timeline of the Statkraft Facility in Oberhausen, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **June 2023:** Statkraft, in collaboration with Air Liquide, announced a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) to supply renewable energy for Air Liquide's new electrolyser plant in Oberhausen. The facility will produce renewable hydrogen at an industrial scale using a 20 MW water-electrolysis plant, referred to as the "Trailblazer" plant.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **June 2023:** The PPA is part of a strategy to support the financing of new renewable energy projects, including the integration of the electrolyser into Air Liquide's existing infrastructure in the Rhine-Ruhr valley. This agreement is crucial for Air Liquide to achieve full REDII-compliance for their hydrogen production.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **June 2023:** The announcement of the PPA marks a significant milestone for both Statkraft and Air Liquide, indicating the start of a partnership aimed at producing green hydrogen. The renewable energy supply is set to commence in 2024.
