#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2024**: BayWa r.e. successfully deployed a unique combination of wind and solar generation along with battery storage at the Fraunhofer Institute for Chemical Technology (ICT) campus. The facility includes:
  - **690 kWp** of photovoltaic (PV) capacity from three rooftop arrays and one ground-mounted system.
  - An existing **2 MW** wind turbine connected to the PV systems without their own inverters.
