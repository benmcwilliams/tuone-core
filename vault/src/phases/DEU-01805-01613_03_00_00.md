### Evolutionary Timeline of EnBW Energie Baden-Württemberg AG Facility in Forbach, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: EnBW announced the conversion and expansion of the Rudolf-Fettweis-Werk (RFW) in Forbach into a pumped storage power plant. The new facility will feature a pump turbine with approximately 54MW in turbine operation and 57MW in pump power, along with three Francis turbines totaling around 23MW. The existing facility has a total output of approximately 71MW and has been operational since 1914-1926.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: EnBW committed approximately €280 million for the conversion and expansion of the facility. This investment is aimed at enhancing renewable energy generation and ensuring energy security.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2023**: The planning approval notice for the complex construction project was issued after a thorough examination by the regional council in Karlsruhe.
- **July 2023**: Construction company Porr secured a major contract for civil works, valued at €98.3 million, as part of the pumped storage project. 
- **July 2024**: Tunnel construction commenced for the Forbach pumped storage project, which includes significant excavation and construction of caverns and tunnels. The commissioning of the new facilities is set for summer 2027.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2023**: The construction activities, including the excavation of approximately 450,000m³ of rock and the construction of extensive tunnel systems, are expected to create jobs, although specific job creation numbers were not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2023**: EnBW Energie Baden-Württemberg AG is the sole investor and owner of the facility, indicating full ownership of the Rudolf-Fettweis-Werk and the new pumped storage power plant project.
