### Evolutionary Timeline of the Energie Baden-Württemberg (EnBW) Facility in Forbach, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: EnBW took the final investment decision (FiD) for the Forbach pumped storage power plant/new lower reservoir project. The facility will upgrade the existing Rudolf Fettweis hydropower plant into a high-capacity pumped storage power plant. The current capacity of the Rudolf Fettweis hydropower plant is 71MW, featuring four individual hydroelectric power plants constructed between 1914 and 1926. The new powerhouse will include a pump turbine with a generating capacity of 54MW in turbine mode and 57MW in pump mode, along with three Francis turbines with a total output of 23MW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: The project is estimated to involve an investment of €280 million ($305 million). This investment will be utilized for the conversion and expansion of the existing hydropower plant.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2023**: The project received approval from the regional authorities in Karlsruhe, with the approval notice issued on March 1, 2023.
- **Autumn 2023**: Construction works at the site are expected to begin.
- **End of 2027**: The completion of the pumped storage power plant project is anticipated.
