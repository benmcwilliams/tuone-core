### Evolutionary Timeline of the Porr Facility in Forbach, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10/2023**: Porr wins a contract to build caverns and a tunnel system for the conversion of the Rudolf Fettweis hydropower plant into a pumped storage power plant. The facility currently has a total capacity of approximately 71MW and will undergo significant modifications to enhance its efficiency. The project includes the construction of an underground sub-basin, caverns, water reservoirs, and tunnels, with a total tunnel length of 5200m and a combined volume of underground water reservoirs of 200 million liters. The new power plant is expected to begin operations in **2027**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07/2024**: EnBW Energie Baden-Württemberg AG, the project's investor, commits approximately **€280 million** to the conversion of the facility. Porr is awarded Lot 2 “Civil works” valued at **€98.3 million** for the construction of caverns and tunnels.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07/2023**: Porr announces the contract win for the construction of the caverns and tunnel system.
- **07/2024**: Tunnel construction officially starts for the Forbach pumped storage project, marking the beginning of the physical transformation of the facility.
