1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **July 2021**: Siemens begins work on the Wunsiedel facility, which is designed to produce green hydrogen with an electrical capacity of 8.75MW. The plant aims to offset carbon dioxide production by 13,500 tons per year. It is noted to be one of Germany’s largest hydrogen power plants and will supply power to parts of Germany and Czechia.
