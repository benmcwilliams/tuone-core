### Evolutionary Timeline of the WUN H2 Facility in Wunsiedel, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2022**: Siemens commissioned an 8.75 MW PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzer at the Energy Park in Wunsiedel. This facility is capable of producing up to 1,350 tons of green hydrogen annually, utilizing only solar and wind power. There are considerations for expanding the plant's capacity to 17.5 MW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2022**: The facility is operated by WUN H2, a company formed specifically for this project. Siemens Financial Services and Rießner Gase each hold a 45% stake, while the Wunsiedel municipal utility owns the remaining 10%. No specific investment amounts were disclosed in the article.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2022**: The commissioning of the electrolyzer marks a significant milestone as it is recognized as Germany's second-largest electrolyzer. The facility's operational status was confirmed with the announcement of its commissioning.
