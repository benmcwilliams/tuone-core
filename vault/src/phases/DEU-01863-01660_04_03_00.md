### Evolutionary Timeline of the FuelCell Energy Facility in Taufkirchen, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2019
   - **Details:** The facility in Taufkirchen assembles SureSource sub-MW fuel cell systems, specifically the SureSource 250 and 400 models. These systems are designed for distributed power generation and can run on biogas, making them suitable for applications in small wastewater treatment plants, breweries, biomass plants, and landfills. The technology is noted for its superior electrical efficiency, ultra-low emissions (NOx, SOx, PM), and low noise profile compared to traditional engines and turbines.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2019
   - **Details:** The facility benefits from government initiatives and a new renewable heat incentive policy in Europe, which are key drivers for the growth of fuel cell technology in the region. The SureSource systems receive a 60% reduction on the EEG surcharge in Germany, enhancing their competitiveness against the electric grid.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** January 2018
   - **Details:** The first SureSource 400 installation began operation at the Radisson Hotel in Frankfurt, achieving over 90% availability. This installation is operated by FuelCell Energy Solutions, GmbH under a 10-year Power Purchase Agreement and is owned by E.ON.
