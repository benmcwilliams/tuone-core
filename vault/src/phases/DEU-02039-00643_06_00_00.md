### Evolutionary Timeline of the RWE Facility in Garzweiler, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2022**: RWE announced the construction of two solar PV projects at the Garzweiler opencast mine, which include:
  - **Garzweiler Solar PV Plant**: 
    - Capacity: 19.4 MWp
    - Battery Storage: 6.5 MW / 13 MWh
  - **Jackerath Project**: 
    - Capacity: 12.1 MWp
    - Battery Storage: 4.1 MW / 8.1 MWh
  - Combined area of both projects: approximately 38 football fields.
  
- **September 2023**: RWE began operations of the two solar PV projects, confirming the installation of over 58,000 bifacial solar PV modules.

- **January 2024**: RWE completed a 3.2 MW agrivoltaic project on a 7-hectare recultivation area at the Garzweiler site, testing three different agrivoltaic concepts over five years.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2023**: RWE is investing approximately €11 billion in green generation capacity in Germany by 2030, which includes the development of the Garzweiler facility and other renewable projects in the Rhineland lignite region.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2022**: Announcement of the construction of the Garzweiler solar PV projects.
- **September 2023**: Official start of operations for the Garzweiler and Jackerath solar PV projects.
- **January 2024**: Commencement of the agrivoltaic project at Garzweiler.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or job loss figures related to the investments or expansions at the Garzweiler facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- RWE operates the facility, indicating full ownership of the solar PV projects and agrivoltaic systems at the Garzweiler site.
