### Evolutionary Timeline of BENTELER Steel/Tube Facility in Lingen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2023**: BENTELER Steel/Tube is involved in the GEiSt (Grünes Eisen in der Stahlindustrie, Green Iron in the steel industry) project alongside HyIron and RWE. The facility aims to produce low-CO2 tubes using steel produced from green iron. The initial goal is to produce over one ton of green iron per hour using green hydrogen. The facility will utilize iron ore from Namibia to optimize the process and product.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2023**: The construction of a pilot electrolysis plant by RWE, which will produce green hydrogen for the HyIron project, is funded by the Lower Saxony Ministry for the Environment, Energy and Climate Protection with an investment of €8 million.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2023**: The start-up of pilot production at the Lingen site is announced as a major milestone for bringing the “green iron” product to market maturity. The facility is set to begin testing the use of sponge iron in steel production from 2024.
