### Evolutionary Timeline of the HyIron Facility in Lingen, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **August 2023**: The HyIron facility opens a direct reduction plant for green iron, utilizing a proprietary gas-tight rotary kiln technology. This innovative process converts iron ore into elemental iron (Direct Reduced Iron - DRI) using green hydrogen, producing only water vapor as a byproduct. The facility aims to produce more than one ton of green iron per hour initially, with a long-term goal of producing up to two million tons of iron for the German steel industry.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **August 2023**: The construction of a pilot electrolysis plant, which will produce green hydrogen for the HyIron facility, is funded by the Lower Saxony Ministry for the Environment, Energy and Climate Protection with an investment of €8 million. This plant is expected to start operating at the end of 2023.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **August 2023**: The pilot production at the Lingen site is announced as a major milestone in bringing the “green iron” product to market maturity. The facility is part of the GEiSt (Grünes Eisen in der Stahlindustrie, Green Iron in the steel industry) project, which involves collaboration with RWE and BENTELER Steel/Tube.
