### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nowega Facility in Lingen, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - The Nowega facility is part of the GET H2 initiative, which aims to build a cross-border pipeline for green hydrogen. The facility is involved in the production, transport, storage, and industrial acceptance of green hydrogen, contributing to a broader hydrogen value chain in Europe.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - In March 2021, Nowega, along with other partners in the GET H2 initiative, submitted an expression of interest for funding under the IPCEI program (Important Project of Common European Interest) to the Federal Ministry of Economics and Technology. This funding is aimed at supporting the development of the hydrogen infrastructure.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - The overall project is set to connect various locations, including Lingen, Gelsenkirchen, and the Dutch border, with a timeline for implementation between 2024 and 2030. The project aims to avoid CO2 emissions of up to 16 million tonnes by 2030 through the use of green hydrogen in various industrial applications.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
