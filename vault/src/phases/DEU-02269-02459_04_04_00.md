### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sunfire Facility in Prenzlau, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10/2021**: Sunfire, in collaboration with Enertrag, announced the establishment of an electrolysis test field in Prenzlau, Germany. The facility will feature various electrolysers with a total capacity of up to 15 MW, including a new generation pressurized alkali electrolyser of 10 MW capacity. Additionally, the first hydrogen filling station in the Uckermark region will be built at this site, utilizing green hydrogen produced on-site for local public transport.
