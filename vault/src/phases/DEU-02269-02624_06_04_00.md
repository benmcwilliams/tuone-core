#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2014**: Aleo Solar faced significant operational losses, reporting a negative EBIT of €-92 million for 2013. The company was preparing for liquidation and agreed to sell its module production site in Prenzlau to SCP Solar, retaining 200 employees.

- **November 2019**: Aleo Solar announced plans to launch a new solar module with a power output of up to 333 W, produced at its 280 MW facility in Prenzlau. This module would feature a larger cell size, reflecting industry trends.
- **February 2021**: Aleo Solar announced the installation of a new production line dedicated to a 400 W solar panel series for rooftop applications, set to launch in the third quarter of 2021.
