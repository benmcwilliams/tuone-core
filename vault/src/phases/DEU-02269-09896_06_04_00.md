## DEU-02269-09896

### Evolutionary Timeline of SCP Solar GmbH Facility in Prenzlau, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 05-02-2014
   - **Details:** SCP Solar GmbH plans to manufacture high-performance monocrystalline photovoltaic (PV) modules under the aleo brand. The facility will utilize solar cells produced by Sunrise Global Solar Energy at its plant in Yilan, Taiwan, which has a nameplate capacity of 350 MW. Aleo solar was already using Sunrise's cells in its 290 Watt modules.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 05-02-2014
   - **Details:** The acquisition involved a financial structure where SCP Solar would receive €10 million from aleo solar as part of the acquisition. Additionally, Sunrise Global Solar and CHOSHU Industry contributed €13.5 million in equity capital to SCP Solar.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 05-02-2014
   - **Details:** The acquisition of aleo solar by the consortium led by Sunrise Global Solar and CHOSHU Industry was announced. This acquisition included significant parts of aleo solar's module manufacturing and sales operations, which would be transferred to SCP Solar GmbH.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 05-02-2014
   - **Details:** The acquisition is expected to save around 200 jobs at the Prenzlau facility, although it was noted that more jobs could not be saved.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 05-02-2014
   - **Details:** SCP Solar GmbH is a joint venture formed by Taiwan-based Sunrise Global Solar Energy and Japanese CHOSHU Industry, indicating that it does not have exclusive ownership of the facility. The acquisition of aleo solar's operations was part of a broader strategy to integrate cell manufacturing and module production.
