### Evolutionary Timeline of the Badenova Facility in Freiburg, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2022**: Badenova completed the construction of a 2.4 MW rooftop solar array on the roof of the Europa-Park stadium, utilizing around 6,000 heterojunction solar modules from Swiss manufacturer Meyer Burger. The installation covers a roof area of 15,000 m² and is recognized as the world's third-largest photovoltaic system on a football stadium. The system is expected to be grid-connected in September 2022.
