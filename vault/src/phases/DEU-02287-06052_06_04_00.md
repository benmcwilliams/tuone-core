1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2009
   - **Details:** Soitec, a specialist in Silicon-on-Insulator (SOI) semiconductor wafers, announced its acquisition of a major holding in Concentrix Solar, a concentrated photovoltaic (CPV) technology company. 3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2009
