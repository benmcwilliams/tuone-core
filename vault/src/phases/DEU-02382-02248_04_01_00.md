### Evolutionary Timeline of the Gasunie Facility in North Sea, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: The facility is part of the German offshore wind-to-hydrogen initiative, with a focus on developing offshore electrolysis capacity. The designated area, SEN-1, spans over 100 square kilometers in the North Sea and is set to allow for an electrolysis capacity of up to **1 GW** to be tested and connected with a hydrogen pipeline.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: The German offshore wind and hydrogen industry associations, including Gasunie, called for the German government to strategically plan the first offshore green hydrogen tender. This is seen as a stepping stone for further projects aimed at producing large quantities of green hydrogen. The appeal emphasizes the need for a sustainable perspective on supply chains and calls for a total of **10 GW** of generation capacity for offshore green hydrogen by **2035**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: The designation of the SEN-1 area as the first offshore hydrogen area in the North Sea is announced, marking an important milestone for the development of an offshore hydrogen economy in Germany and Europe.
