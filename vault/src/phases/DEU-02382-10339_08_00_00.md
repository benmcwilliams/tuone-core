### Evolutionary Timeline of the Veja Mate Offshore Project GmbH Facility

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Commissioning Date:** May 2017
   - **Production Capacity:** The facility has a total capacity of 402 MW, comprising 67 Siemens 6 MW wind turbines.
   - **Annual Production:** Capable of producing 1.6 billion kWh of electricity per year, sufficient to power approximately 450,000 German households.
   - **Recent Developments:** As of May 2021, Veja Mate Offshore Project GmbH issued a call for tenders for Balance of Plant (BOP) services, which includes maintenance and troubleshooting for the offshore substation, monopiles, transition pieces, subsea inspections, and operation of a 24/7 offshore control center.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Ownership Change:** In 2019, a consortium consisting of funds managed by Commerz Real, wpd invest, KGAL, and the Ingka Group acquired an 80% stake in the facility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Commissioning:** The project was commissioned in May 2017.
   - **Tender Announcement:** On April 5, 2021, Veja Mate Offshore Project GmbH opened a tender for BOP services.

   - The facility is not exclusively owned by Veja Mate Offshore Project GmbH; it is now 80% owned by a consortium that includes Commerz Real, wpd invest, KGAL, and the Ingka Group, while Veja Mate Offshore Project GmbH retains a 20% stake.
