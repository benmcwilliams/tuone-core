### Evolutionary Timeline of Fortescue Future Industries Facility in Wilhelmshaven, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-10-2022**: Fortescue Future Industries (FFI) announced a strategic collaboration with Tree Energy Solutions (TES) to develop a green energy import facility in Wilhelmshaven, Germany. The facility will focus on the import of green hydrogen, with the first delivery expected in 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-10-2022**: FFI will invest €130 million ($127 million) in the project, with an additional €100 million allocated for the construction of the TES terminal in Wilhelmshaven. FFI will also invest €30 million to become a shareholder in Tree Energy Solutions B.V. Furthermore, FFI will hold a 30% stake in Deutsche Grüngas und Energieversorgung GmbH, the project company responsible for building the TES Green Energy Hub.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07-10-2022**: Announcement of the collaboration and investment in the green energy import facility in Wilhelmshaven, Germany. The first delivery of green hydrogen is anticipated in 2026.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Fortescue Future Industries will not own the facility exclusively; it will hold a 30% stake in Deutsche Grüngas und Energieversorgung GmbH, indicating a joint venture structure with Tree Energy Solutions.
