### Evolutionary Timeline of the EH2 Facility in Wilhelmshaven, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2024
   - **Details:** EH2 has been selected as the exclusive electrolysis partner for Uniper’s hydrogen plant in Wilhelmshaven. The facility will produce green hydrogen using a 200MW PEM electrolyser, which is part of a larger plan for 1GW of green hydrogen production. The electrolyser will be built on the site of a former coal-fired power plant and will be integrated with a 2.6 million tonne per year green ammonia terminal. Preliminary front-end engineering design (pre-FEED) work has already commenced.
