### Evolutionary Timeline of the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology Facility in Black Forest, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: June 2022
   - **Facility**: GeoLaB (Geothermal Laboratory in the Crystalline Basement)
   - **Production**: The facility is designed to conduct underground research on geothermal systems, focusing on reservoir technology and borehole safety. It will facilitate controlled high-flow experiments and flow tests in rock to understand geothermal transport processes.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: June 2022
   - **Investment**: The Helmholtz Association is investing EUR 35 million into the GeoLaB project. This funding is aimed at establishing the underground research facility in the Black Forest region.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: June 2022
   - **Announcement**: The establishment of the GeoLaB facility was announced, with plans to excavate a new mine in the Black Forest or Odenwald. A one-kilometer-long tunnel will lead to the caverns where the facility will be located.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: June 2022
   - **Job Creation**: While specific job creation numbers were not mentioned, the establishment of the GeoLaB facility is expected to contribute to the local economy and may create jobs related to construction and research activities.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: June 2022
   - **Ownership**: The facility is a partnership involving the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), the German Research Center for Geosciences (GFZ), and the Helmholtz Center for Environmental Research (UFZ).
