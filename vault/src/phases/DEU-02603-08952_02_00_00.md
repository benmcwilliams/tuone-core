### Evolutionary Timeline of the GFZ German Research Center for Geosciences Facility in Black Forest, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2022**: Announcement of the GeoLaB, an underground research facility aimed at understanding geothermal systems. The facility will focus on reservoir technology and borehole safety, utilizing advanced methods to record thermal, hydraulic, chemical, and mechanical parameters. The facility will be located in a one-kilometer-long tunnel leading to caverns in the Black Forest, specifically designed to conduct controlled high-flow experiments and flow tests in rock.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2022**: The Helmholtz Association commits EUR 35 million to the GeoLaB project, indicating significant investment in the development of geothermal research capabilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2022**: The GeoLaB project is officially announced, with plans for excavation of a new mine in the Black Forest or Odenwald to create the underground research facility. The project aims to address the challenges of geothermal energy development in Germany, particularly concerning induced seismicity.
