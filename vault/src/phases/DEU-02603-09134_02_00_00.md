- **June 2022**: Announcement of the GeoLaB, an underground research facility aimed at understanding geothermal systems. The facility will focus on reservoir technology and borehole safety, utilizing advanced methods to record thermal, hydraulic, chemical, and mechanical parameters. The facility will include a one-kilometer-long tunnel leading to caverns for controlled high-flow experiments and flow tests in rock.
