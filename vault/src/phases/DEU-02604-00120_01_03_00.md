### Evolutionary Timeline of the CATL Facility in Thüringen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10/2018**: CATL announces the establishment of its first manufacturing facility outside of China in Thüringen, Germany. The facility will have an annual production capacity of **14 GWh** of battery cells, primarily for the electric vehicle (EV) industry. The production will focus on automated and intelligent battery manufacturing.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **10/2018**: CATL commits to invest **€240 million** in the Thüringen facility by **2022**. This investment is part of CATL's strategy to expand its presence in Europe, which is seen as a strategic growth region for lithium-ion battery production.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10/2018**: The announcement of the facility marks it as the **first battery cell production site in Europe**. The construction is expected to significantly boost the local economy and attract further investments in battery production in Thüringen.

#### 4. Labour
- **10/2018**: The establishment of the facility is expected to create up to **600 new jobs** in the region, contributing to local employment and economic growth.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **10/2018**: CATL is establishing the facility as a **wholly-owned** subsidiary, indicating full ownership of the Thüringen facility without any joint ventures or partnerships in its operational structure.
