### Evolutionary Timeline of SolarWorld Industries GmbH Facility in Thüringen, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **08/2017:** SolarWorld Industries GmbH, founded by Dr. Ing. Eh Frank Asbeck, acquired the manufacturing and R&D operations of SolarWorld Industries Thüringen GmbH as part of a purchase and transfer agreement during the insolvency proceedings of SolarWorld AG. The facility is involved in the production of photovoltaic (PV) modules.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **08/2017:** The acquisition of the Thüringen facility was part of a broader agreement that included various assets of SolarWorld AG. While specific financial details were not disclosed, it was noted that the purchase price mainly consisted of the repayment of liabilities secured by creditor protection rights. Additionally, it was reported that Qatar Solar Technologies, a subsidiary of the Qatar Foundation, was a shareholder in the new entity alongside Asbeck.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **08/2017:** Following the acquisition, Dr. Asbeck announced that production in Germany, including the Thüringen facility, would restart the week after the acquisition, indicating a swift transition to operational status post-insolvency.

4. **Labour:**
   - **08/2017:** As a result of the acquisition and the restart of production, over 500 jobs were saved at the Thüringen facility. This was a significant development considering the context of the insolvency of SolarWorld AG, which had led to job losses in other related operations.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **08/2017:** SolarWorld Industries GmbH is not solely owned by Dr. Asbeck; it operates as a joint venture with Qatar Solar Technologies, indicating a shared ownership structure in the facility's operations.
