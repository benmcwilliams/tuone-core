## DEU-02604-08686

### EPOD Solar Facility Timeline in Thüringen, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2009
   - **Details:** EPOD Solar, formerly Allora Minerals, acquired OptiSolar's R&D and production assets, which included a manufacturing facility capable of scaling to 1GW in nameplate production capacity. The facility's initial production capacity increased from 5MW to 30MW, with plans to convert single-junction lines to dual-junction lines, expanding capacity to 55MW. The Thüringen facility is planned to produce solar modules at approximately $0.70 per watt peak.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2009
   - **Details:** EPOD Solar received preliminary approval for €23.5 million in grants from Thüringen Aufbaubank for the construction of a 30MW solar manufacturing facility in Thüringen. This funding is part of regional development programs defined by the European Commission.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2009
   - **Details:** The announcement of the construction of a 30MW solar manufacturing facility in Thüringen was made, with orders for equipment to be placed as part of the project.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** July 2009
   - **Details:** At the start of production, the facility is expected to employ approximately 168 workers.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** July 2009
   - **Details:** EPOD Solar (ESI) is a vertically integrated PV firm that has acquired the assets of OptiSolar, indicating that it owns the facility exclusively following the acquisition.
