### Evolutionary Timeline of CS Wismar GmbH Facility in Wismar, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2016**: CS Wismar GmbH restarted PV module assembly operations at the former Sonnenstromfabrik facility in Wismar, Germany. The facility has a production capacity of around **525 MWp** and primarily offers OEM services for companies targeting the U.S. market. The facility has a strong reputation for quality, with a complaint rate of less than **0.02%** per annum over its 20-year production history.
  
- **March 2018**: CS Wismar plans to raise production in 2018, focusing on manufacturing its own modules under the “Sonnenstromfabrik” brand. The company aims to increase its production of its own modules to **100 MW** and double the capacity utilization in the OEM segment, which achieved a total volume of around **35 MW** the previous year. The facility theoretically has capacities for producing **300 MW** of solar modules annually.

#### 2. Investment and Development
