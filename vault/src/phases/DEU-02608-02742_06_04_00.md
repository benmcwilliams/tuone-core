### Evolutionary Timeline of Centrosolar Group AG Facility in Wismar, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: 03-12-2008
   - **Details**: Centrosolar Group AG opened its solar module facility in Wismar, Germany. The facility is one of the largest in the area and is designed to produce 150 MWp of solar modules per year, starting in 2010. The production includes approximately 3,000 modules per day, which is five times higher than the output of the company's previous factory. The facility features five assembly lines dedicated to producing crystalline modules for grid-connected systems, standalone modules for power generation on boats and RVs, and in-roof modules for roof integration. Additionally, customized modules will be manufactured. A 300 kWp PV plant will be installed on the roof of the production and storage hall to support sustainability efforts.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Date**: 03-12-2008
   - **Details**: The total investment for the Wismar facility amounts to around €23 million.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Date**: 03-12-2008
   - **Details**: The facility was officially opened after a construction period of 10 months.

4. **Labour**
   - **Date**: 03-12-2008
   - **Details**: The opening of the facility created 250 new jobs, making it one of the largest employers in Wismar.

5. **Ownership Structure**
