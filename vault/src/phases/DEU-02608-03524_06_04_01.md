### Evolutionary Timeline of Sonnenstromfabrik Facility in Wismar, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2021**: Sonnenstromfabrik announced the launch of three new PV module series featuring half-cell technology and large wafers (182 mm). The new series includes:
  - A full black module series (1,560 x 1,145 mm) with a maximum output of 350 W.
  - A Full Black & Smart module series (1,745 x 1,145 mm) with a power output of up to 400 W.
  - The largest series (2,110 x 1,145 mm) with an output of 490 W, suitable for ground-mounted PV projects. The company also continued production of its existing “Brilliant” and “Solrif” module series.
  
- **February 2022**: Sonnenstromfabrik announced plans to expand its production capacity by adding a new 300 MW solar module line to its existing 200 MW facility in Wismar. This new line will utilize REC Group’s patented split cell and junction box technology for the manufacture of new PERC products.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2022**: The expansion to a new 300 MW line is part of a partnership with REC Group, which includes a mutual cooperation agreement for technology sharing and licensing. This partnership is aimed at enhancing production capabilities and efficiency.
