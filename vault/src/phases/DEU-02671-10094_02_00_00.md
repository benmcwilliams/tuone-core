### Evolutionary Timeline of the Stadtwerke Flensburg Facility in Flensburg, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04-11-2023**: Innargi conducted an evaluation of the geothermal potential in Flensburg, concluding that the geothermal resource is insufficient for district heating at scale. The evaluation revealed that the sandstone reservoirs in the area are too thin and shallow, making geothermal development uncompetitive compared to other carbon-free heating resources.

#### 2. Investment and Development

### Summary
The Stadtwerke Flensburg facility has been involved in evaluating geothermal energy potential in collaboration with Innargi. Despite the initial exploration, the findings indicated that the geothermal resources are not suitable for large-scale heating, leading Stadtwerke Flensburg to consider alternative methods for achieving climate neutrality by 2035. The project did not incur costs for the municipal utility, and there were no reported changes in employment as a result of this evaluation.
