#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-01-2022**: The Fraunhofer Institute for Building Physics IBP is developing a renewable energy module facade designed to integrate with a building's technical equipment. This module facade is capable of supplying solar power while also providing heating, cooling, and ventilation. The system includes a photovoltaic system, a heat pump, and a decentralized ventilation device with heat recovery. Each technical unit of the module facade measures 1.25m wide and 30cm deep, capable of serving a room of approximately 24m².
