### Evolutionary Timeline of the Implenia Fassadentechnik GmbH Facility in Holzkirchen, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-01-2022**: The facility is involved in the development of a PV-assisted heat pump module facade designed to supply power, heating, cooling, and ventilation for buildings. The facade integrates a photovoltaic system, a heat pump, and a decentralized ventilation device with heat recovery. Each technical unit of the module facade measures 1.25m wide and 30cm deep, capable of servicing a room of approximately 24m². The system is designed for minimal invasiveness, allowing for the replacement of old facades without extensive renovations.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- The project is funded by the German Federal Ministry of Economics, indicating governmental investment in the development of this renewable energy module facade.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
