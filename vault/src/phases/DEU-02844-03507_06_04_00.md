### Evolutionary Timeline of Solarwelt GmbH Facility in Langenwetzendorf, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2020
   - **Details:** Solarwelt GmbH is set to open a solar module factory in Langenwetzendorf, with a production capacity of 400 MW. The facility will manufacture 370 W panels featuring half cells, large wafers, and nine busbars. The factory will operate under a four-shift production schedule.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** May 2020
   - **Details:** The facility will utilize German and European-sourced solar production lines, indicating a commitment to local manufacturing and supply chains.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** May 2020
   - **Details:** The facility is planned to start production in the first quarter of 2021. The announcement was made by Heckert Solar, which is associated with Solarwelt GmbH.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** May 2020
   - **Details:** The establishment of the facility is expected to create new jobs, as indicated by the four-shift production schedule.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** May 2020
   - **Details:** Solarwelt GmbH is associated with Heckert Solar, which is owned by the Trinkerl family. The ownership structure suggests that Solarwelt GmbH is not exclusively owned but is part of a family-owned business model involving Heckert Solar.
