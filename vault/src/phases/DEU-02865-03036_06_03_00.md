### Evolutionary Timeline of ISC Konstanz Facility in Konstanz, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03-11-2021**: ISC Konstanz is ramping up production of conductive backsheets (CBS) for back contact solar modules. The facility is developing next-generation ZEBRA interdigitated back contact (IBC) cells, which aim to replace silver metallization with copper screen printing, leading to significant cost reductions without compromising module efficiency. The production capacity of the back-contact production line is noted to be approximately 290 MWp annually based on full cell modules, with variations for half cells.

- **07-06-2024**: ISC Konstanz claims a certified efficiency of 24.12% for its TOPCon back contact (TBC) solar cell, designed under the polyZEBRA concept. This efficiency is the highest achieved at the facility to date. The facility is also working on optimizing processes for a future production line expected to be implemented in 2025, which aims for cell efficiencies exceeding 25%.

#### 2. Investment and Development
