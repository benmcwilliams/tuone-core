## DEU-03006-03245

### Evolutionary Timeline of Nice Solar Energy Facility in Schwäbisch Hall, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - Nice Solar Energy specializes in CIGS (Copper Indium Gallium Selenide) thin-film photovoltaics. 
   - The facility operates a module production line aimed at transitioning research and development into series production.
   - As of the end of 2019, the facility achieved a world record efficiency of 17.6% for its CIGS modules, produced on Manz production systems.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The facility is a joint venture involving multiple shareholders: Manz AG, China Energy Group, Shanghai Electric, and Beijing Future Science Park Development Group. This collaboration was established to advance CIGS thin-film technology.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **October 2021:** Nice Solar Energy filed for insolvency under self-administration at the Heilbronn District Court, citing the coronavirus pandemic as a contributing factor. The company was granted three months to present a restructuring plan while continuing operations without disruption.

4. **Labour:**
   - The facility employs 160 people in Schwäbisch Hall, Germany. The insolvency proceedings do not indicate immediate job losses, as business operations were reported to continue without disruption.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - Nice Solar Energy operates as a joint venture, with ownership shared among Manz AG, China Energy Group, Shanghai Electric, and Beijing Future Science Park Development Group. There is no indication of exclusive ownership by any single entity.
