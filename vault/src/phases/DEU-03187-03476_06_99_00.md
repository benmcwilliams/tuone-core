### Evolutionary Timeline of the SolarCycle GmbH Facility in Bitterfeld_Wolfen Chemical Park, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **09/2011**: SolarCycle GmbH is established to recycle photovoltaic (PV) modules, producing metal products and other valuable materials. The facility will be located in the Bitterfeld/Wolfen Chemical Park and will feature a largely automated, second-generation recycling plant capable of processing crystalline solar modules and other types of solar modules and compound materials. The facility is designed to process up to 30,000 tons of recycling material per year.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **09/2011**: A total investment of €12.7 million is allocated for the project, which will be executed in two phases, with the first phase expected to be completed by the end of 2012.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **09/2011**: Announcement of the establishment of SolarCycle GmbH and the planned construction of the recycling plant, with construction set to begin in autumn 2011.

4. **Labour**
   - **09/2011**: The establishment of the facility is expected to create new jobs, although the specific number of jobs created has not been disclosed.

5. **Ownership Structure**
   - **09/2011**: SolarCycle GmbH is a joint venture, with SolarWorld holding a 24% stake, alongside Preiss-Daimler Chemical Park and other unnamed companies.
