1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 10-06-2021
   - **Details:** Veolia is developing a new process to recycle silicon, silver, and glass from end-of-life photovoltaic (PV) panels. The facility aims to process approximately 5,000 tons of solar modules annually by 2023. The process involves innovative delamination technology that separates solar cells from glass without shredding, allowing for the recovery of materials with high purity.
