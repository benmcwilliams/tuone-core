### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mitsubishi Facility in Moorburg, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2015**: The Moorburg coal power station was opened by Vattenfall, with a generation capacity of nearly 1.6 gigawatts.
- **2021**: Plans were revealed to transform the facility into one of the first 100-megawatt scale green hydrogen sites in Europe, with an initial plan to establish a 100 MW electrolyzer powered by wind and solar, aiming for green hydrogen production to start around 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2021**: The project is applying for funds from the Important Projects of Common European Interest initiative to support the transition to green hydrogen production. The facility is positioned to benefit from public subsidies aimed at facilitating the development of green hydrogen projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **01-02-2021**: Announcement of the transition from coal to green hydrogen, with plans for the establishment of a 100 MW electrolyzer. The project is contingent on planning and financing requirements, with production expected to start around 2025.

#### 4. Labour
