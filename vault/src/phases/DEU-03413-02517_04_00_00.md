### E.ON Facility in Schopsdorf, DEU - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04-08-2019**: E.ON's subsidiary, Avacon, initiates a project in Schopsdorf to raise the hydrogen admixture rate in its natural gas pipeline grid to 20 percent, marking a first in German gas distribution. The project aims to demonstrate the feasibility of blending higher concentrations of hydrogen into natural gas grids and to test the compatibility of household appliances with this higher hydrogen content. Up to 400 heating systems and other customer devices will be tested in the autumn of 2019.

#### 2. Investment and Development
