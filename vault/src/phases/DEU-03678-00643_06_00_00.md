### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in Bedburg, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06-09-2023**: RWE announced the development of a 3.2 MW agrivoltaic demonstration project on a 7-hectare recultivation area at the Garzweiler opencast lignite mine. The project aims to test three different agrivoltaic systems that will allow for simultaneous solar power generation and agricultural use.
- **19-01-2024**: The 3.2 MW agrivoltaic project was completed. The facility will test three different system designs for various crops over five years, including vertical solar modules, movable trackers, and a pergola-like structure for growing raspberries.
- **20-09-2024**: RWE commissioned a new photovoltaic plant with a capacity of 20 MW peak (15.7 MWac) in Bedburg, constructed on recultivated land. This plant is designed to supply green electricity for approximately 5,400 households.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06-09-2023**: The agrivoltaic project is funded by the state of North Rhine-Westphalia through the progres.nrw program for climate protection and energy transition.
- **11-05-2024**: RWE announced plans to invest around €11 billion net in green generation capacity in Germany by 2030, which includes the Bedburg facility and other projects in the Rhineland lignite region.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06-09-2023**: Announcement of the agrivoltaic demonstration project.
- **19-01-2024**: Completion of the 3.2 MW agrivoltaic project.
- **20-09-2024**: Commissioning of the new 20 MW photovoltaic plant.
