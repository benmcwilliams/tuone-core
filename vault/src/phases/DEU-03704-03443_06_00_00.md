## DEU-03704-03443

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sinn Power Facility in Gilching, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2024**: Sinn Power plans to build a 1.8 MW floating PV system with vertically deployed solar modules at a gravel pit in Gilching, Bavaria. The project will utilize approximately 2,500 solar modules and the company's patented “Floating-SKipp” mounting systems, which will be oriented in an east-west direction to optimize electricity production during morning and afternoon hours. The design allows for more solar modules to be installed without exceeding the German Renewable Energy Act (EEG) requirement of covering a maximum of 15% of a water surface.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2024**: The facility is still in the planning phase, with strong support from local agencies, including gwt Starnberg GmbH, the Starnberg district office, and the municipality of Gilching. Specific investment amounts have not been disclosed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2024**: Anchoring and lake preparation work at the Gilching gravel pit is scheduled to begin.
- **September 2024**: Construction of the 1.8 MW floating solar installation is set to commence.
