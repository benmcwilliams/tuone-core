## DEU-03733-00729

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sono Motors Facility in Hof, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2023**: Sono Motors initiated a pilot project in Hof, Bavaria, equipping a bus operated by Stadtwerke Hof with its photovoltaic (PV) retrofit solution, known as the “Solar Bus Kit.” This kit includes 16 semi-flexible solar modules that provide a total output of 1.4 kW. The setup weighs 50 kg and directs solar energy to the bus's battery, which supports its diesel engine.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2023**: The pilot project represents a strategic shift for Sono Motors towards photovoltaic solutions for vehicles, following the discontinuation of the Sion solar EV project in February 2023. Specific investment amounts related to this pilot project have not been disclosed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2023**: The solar-equipped bus has received road approval from the TÜV and is actively operating in Hof. An equivalent mid-hybrid bus, which does not have the photovoltaic kit, is being used as a performance and energy consumption benchmark on selected routes. The pilot project is seen as a stepping stone towards the gradual conversion of the bus fleet in the coming years.
