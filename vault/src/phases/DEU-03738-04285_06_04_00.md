#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2023**: Sunmaxx PVT began construction of a highly automated factory in Ottendorf-Okrilla, with an annual production capacity of 50 MW, equivalent to 120,000 photovoltaic-thermal (PVT) modules.
