### Evolutionary Timeline of the Thyssengas Facility in Gelsenkirchen, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: The facility is involved in the production, transport, storage, and industrial acceptance of green hydrogen as part of the GET H2 initiative.
- **Capacity and Infrastructure**: The project aims to connect various locations for green hydrogen infrastructure from Lingen to Gelsenkirchen and beyond, with a goal to avoid CO2 emissions of up to 16 million tonnes by 2030.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Funding Initiatives**: On March 15, 2021, Thyssengas, along with other partners in the GET H2 initiative, submitted an expression of interest for funding under the IPCEI program (Important Project of Common European Interest) to the Federal Ministry of Economics and Technology.
