1. **Technology and Operations:**

   - **Details:** The facility is part of the Infinity 50 solar park in Benban, which has a production capacity of 64.1 MW. The solar park is designed to produce approximately 110,000 MWh of electricity annually. The construction involves around 200,000 solar arrays spread over a 98.6-hectare area.
