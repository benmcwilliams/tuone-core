### Evolutionary Timeline of the Siemens Facility in Punta Arenas, Chile

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **August 2021:** The Haru Oni project, which includes the Siemens facility, is designed to produce green hydrogen derived from 3.4MW of dedicated wind energy. The facility will produce:
     - 350 metric tonnes/year of green methanol
     - 130,000 liters/year of carbon-neutral gasoline
     - 16 tonnes/year of carbon-neutral LPG
   - The pilot plant is scheduled to begin operations in **May 2022** and is designed for future scaling in two subsequent stages.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **August 2021:** The project is part of a larger investment strategy, with a planned **$800 million** commercial-scale plant to produce 140,000 tonnes/year of methanol, with a final investment decision expected in **2022**.
