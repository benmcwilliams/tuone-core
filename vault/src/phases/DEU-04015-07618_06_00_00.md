## DEU-04015-07618

### Evolutionary Timeline of the CEE Facility in Döllen, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 05-07-2023**: The Döllen solar park, a 154.7 MW solar facility, was officially commissioned. It is designed to produce approximately 160,000 megawatt-hours of clean electricity annually, sufficient to power 36,000 four-person households. The facility is located on a former agricultural area in Döllen, within the municipality of Gumtow in the Prignitz district of Brandenburg.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: 05-07-2023**: The project was funded by CEE Group and Deutsche Anlagen-Leasing (DAL). A power purchase agreement was signed with French utility company Engie to offload the generated power.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date: 05-07-2023**: The Döllen solar park was inaugurated, marking the completion of the project one year after its groundbreaking ceremony. The activation of the facility was symbolized by connecting a ‘green plug’ to the electricity grid.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or job loss figures related to the Döllen solar park. However, the successful commissioning of the facility and the involvement of multiple companies suggest potential job creation during the construction and operational phases.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
