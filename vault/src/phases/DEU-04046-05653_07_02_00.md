### Evolutionary Timeline of the Neapco Facility in Düren, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Initial Production Capacity**: The facility initially had a production capacity of 30,000 electric delivery vans per year.
- **Current Operations**: As of January 1, 2024, Neapco is assembling electric delivery vans for e.Volution, which has taken over operations from Nob Manufacturing GmbH. The facility continues to produce the electric delivery van previously known as Streetscooter, now under the new management of e.Volution.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Acquisition of Equipment**: Neapco acquired assembly equipment from the DHL Group, which was essential for continuing operations at the facility.
- **Strategic Partnership**: The partnership with e.Volution is aimed at enhancing the sustainability and longevity of the vehicles produced.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Ownership Transition**: As of January 1, 2024, e.Volution has taken over the operations and assets of Nob Manufacturing GmbH, marking a significant transition in the facility's management.
- **Production Continuation**: Following the insolvency of B-ON in September 2023, the facility is set to continue operations with the order of 700 units of the electric delivery van from DHL Group.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Security**: The transition to e.Volution and the continuation of operations at Neapco are expected to secure jobs at Nob, Neapco, and other suppliers involved in the production process.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Joint Venture**: Neapco is not the exclusive owner of the facility; it operates in a strategic partnership with e.Volution, which has taken over the operations from Nob Manufacturing GmbH.
