### Evolutionary Timeline of the BASF Facility in Budenheim, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2024
   - **Details:** BASF's new facility in Budenheim will focus on the production of green hydrogen components, specifically newly developed CCMs (Catalyst Coated Membranes) for PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers. The facility is designed to have multi-gigawatt production capacity, supporting the global market for green hydrogen. This facility will enhance BASF's capabilities in precious metals, catalysts, and recycling, positioning it as an integrated solution provider in the green hydrogen sector.
