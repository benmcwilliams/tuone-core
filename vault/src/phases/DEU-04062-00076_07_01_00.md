- **April 2021**: The facility began painting battery cells, expanding from one to four lines by the end of 2021. This process involves coating the aluminium housings of prismatic cells to enhance mechanical robustness and thermal conductivity.
- **October 2022**: Assembly of high-voltage batteries commenced at the EV component factory (plant 6.11) in Leibnizstraße, which covers an area of approximately 45,000 square meters. By this time, around 30,000 high-voltage batteries had been assembled for vehicle production in Regensburg.
