#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03-11-2020**: Belectric is set to build and operate a 172MW solar farm in Tramm and Lewitzrand, Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania. The facility will utilize 420,000 photovoltaic (PV) modules and is expected to generate enough energy to power nearly 50,000 homes annually. The solar plant is scheduled to start commercial operations at the end of 2021.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-11-2020**: Luxcara acquires the 172MW solar farm from Belectric. This project is noted as the largest German photovoltaic project not owned by a utility.
