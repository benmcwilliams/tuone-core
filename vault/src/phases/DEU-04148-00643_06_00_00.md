**1. Technology and Operations:**
- **02/2021:** RWE Renewables, in collaboration with the Fraunhofer Institute for Solar Energy Systems (ISE) and the Brandenburg University of Technology Cottbus-Senftenberg (BTU), initiated the PV2Float project. This project aims to test several floating photovoltaic (PV) systems with different structure designs over a three-year period.
