### Evolutionary Timeline of EDL Anlagenbau Facility in Böhlen-Lippendorf, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **October 2023:** EDL Anlagenbau Gesellschaft mbH (EDL) selected Johnson Matthey's co-developed Fischer Tropsch (FT) CANS technology for its HyKero plant in Böhlen-Lippendorf. The facility is planned to produce 50,000 metric tons (MT) of sustainable aviation fuel (SAF) per year, including eSAF from a power-to-liquids (PtL) route. Additionally, it aims to produce 14,000 MT of sustainable naphtha and approximately 1,000 MT of electrolytic (green) hydrogen annually. The plant is strategically located to serve Leipzig-Halle international airport and is expected to be operational by 2027.
