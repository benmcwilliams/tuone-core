### Evolutionary Timeline of the EnBW Facility in Gundelsheim, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2024**: EnBW commenced construction on a 72MW hybrid energy park in Gundelsheim, which includes a solar component with an installed output of 60.5MW and a wind component planned for future development. The solar park will feature 110,000 solar modules and is designed to generate enough electricity to supply 23,900 households. The facility covers an area of 64 hectares, with 55 hectares dedicated to solar energy production. A 2.25-megawatt-hours battery storage system will also be installed to optimize power usage and enhance grid integration. The wind farm will consist of two Enercon E160 turbines, each with a capacity of 5.6MW, expected to produce 22 million kilowatt hours annually, sufficient for 6,600 households.
