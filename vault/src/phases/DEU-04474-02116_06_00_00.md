### Evolutionary Timeline of the Trina Solar Facility in Weesow-Willmersdorf, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2020
   - **Details:** Trina Solar began supplying 187.5MW of its double-glass Duomax modules for the Weesow-Willmersdorf solar farm, which is noted as Germany’s largest solar project to date. The facility will deploy a total of 465,000 modules by November 2020, with completion targeted by the end of 2020. The dual-glass structure of the modules is designed for better resistance to aging and is expected to provide long lifetimes and durability.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2020
   - **Details:** The Weesow-Willmersdorf solar farm is the first large-scale PV power plant in Germany to be built without incentives through the EEG (Germany’s legal framework for supporting renewables). This indicates a significant investment in solar technology that relies on market competitiveness rather than government subsidies.
