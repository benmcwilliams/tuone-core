#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2023**: MHI Vestas received a 50 MW order from Energiepark Bad Lauchstädt for the Bad Lauchstädt TerraWatt project. The order includes the supply, installation, and commissioning of eight V162-6.2 MW turbines, which will be installed at a hub height of 169 meters. The facility will utilize wind energy to produce green hydrogen at an industrial scale, supported by a large electrolysis plant of up to 30 MW located near the project site.
