#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2023**: GRTgaz Deutschland, in collaboration with OGE and Solar Turbines, successfully tested a Titan 130 SoLoNOx gas turbine at the MEGAL compressor station in Waidhaus. The turbine operated with hydrogen blending rates of up to 25%, marking a significant step towards decarbonizing the gas transmission sector. The Titan 130 gas turbine is designed to deliver a simple-cycle thermal efficiency of 36% and has a capacity of 16.5 MW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2023**: The project involved the setup of a mobile blending plant and a temporary hydrogen supply, which were crucial for the successful testing of hydrogen blends. Preparations for this project began two years prior, indicating a significant investment in infrastructure to support hydrogen blending in gas turbines.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **04/2023**: The successful completion of the hydrogen fuel test at the MEGAL compressor station was announced. The project ran for six weeks and involved various hydrogen blending rates tested under different loads for over 200 hours.
