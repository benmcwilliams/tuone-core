#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2023**: The OGE facility at the MEGAL compressor station in Waidhaus successfully tested blending rates of up to 25% hydrogen in a Titan 130 SoLoNOx Solar Turbines gas turbine. This turbine serves as a prime mover for a natural gas compressor, which is essential for the MEGAL natural gas pipeline system that operates bi-directionally at the German-Czech border.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2023**: Preparations for the hydrogen blending project began two years prior, involving the setup of a mobile blending plant and a temporary hydrogen supply.
