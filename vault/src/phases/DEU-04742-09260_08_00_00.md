## DEU-04742-09260

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Itochu_CITIC Pacific Facility in Butendiek Offshore Wind Farm, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Operational Since**: 2015
- **Installed Capacity**: 288 MW
- **Annual Electricity Production**: Approximately 1,200 GWh
- **Wind Turbines**: 80 Siemens Gamesa turbines, each with a capacity of 3.6 MW.
- **Recent Development**: The facility has entered into a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) to supply electricity for green hydrogen production, marking it as one of the first green PPAs for hydrogen in Germany.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Recent Investment**: The facility signed a PPA on 2 October 2023 with a large multinational company for 62.5% of its total output (around 700 GWh per year) for a duration of six and a half years. This agreement is expected to support the EU's goal of producing 10 million tonnes of green hydrogen by 2030.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2023**: Announcement of the new PPA for green hydrogen production.
- **July 2023**: The PPA is expected to meet the standards set under the EU Delegated Acts on green hydrogen.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The articles do not specify the exact number of jobs created or lost as a result of the new PPA or the green hydrogen production facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: The Butendiek Offshore Wind Farm is owned by a consortium that includes Itochu/CITIC Pacific, along with Wpd, Schroders Greencoat, Ewz, and Industriens Pension. The specific ownership stake of Itochu/CITIC Pacific within the consortium is not detailed in the articles.
