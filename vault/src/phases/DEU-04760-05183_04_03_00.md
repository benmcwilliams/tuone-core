1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **03-02-2023**: H2FLY announced the construction of the "Center of Excellence for Hydrogen in Aviation" at Stuttgart Airport. This facility will focus on the development and testing of hydrogen-electric propulsion systems for passenger aviation. It will include a dedicated hangar with extensive R&D infrastructure, test stands, workshops, and areas for testing hydrogen technologies in aircraft demonstrators. The facility aims to enable the development of components and sub-systems in the megawatt range and the integration and test operation of hydrogen-electric powertrains.
