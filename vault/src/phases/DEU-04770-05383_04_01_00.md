### Evolutionary Timeline of JLEN Environmental Facility in Borna, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **January 2023:** JLEN Environmental announced its investment in the Thierbach green hydrogen project near Borna, Germany, in partnership with HH2E AG. The facility is designed to produce green hydrogen, with an initial capacity of over 6,000 tonnes (200,000 megawatt hours) per year during the first phase, which will include electrolysis and integrated high-capacity battery storage. Future expansions are expected to increase the capacity to over one gigawatt, with annual production exceeding 60,000 tonnes of green hydrogen.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **July 2023:** JLEN Environmental announced a EUR9.2 million investment in the green hydrogen project, alongside a consortium that includes other Foresight Group LLP managed funds and HH2E AG. This initial investment is allocated for detailed engineering designs and procurement of long lead items.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **July 2023:** The consortium approved the preliminary investment decision. The final investment decision is anticipated in the coming months, with construction of the plant expected to commence immediately thereafter.
