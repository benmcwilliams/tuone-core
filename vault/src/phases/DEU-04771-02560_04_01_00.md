### Evolutionary Timeline of the HH2E Facility in Thierbach, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** January 2023
   - **Details:** The HH2E facility in Thierbach is being developed as an industrial-scale green hydrogen production plant. It is projected to have an initial production capacity of approximately 6,000 tons per year (tpy) of green hydrogen. Future expansion phases could increase this capacity to over 60,000 tpy, significantly contributing to the reduction of greenhouse gas emissions.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** January 2023
   - **Details:** HydrogenOne has committed to investing £2.4 million (€2.8 million) in the Thierbach project, alongside other institutional investors and HH2E. This investment is part of a broader commitment by HydrogenOne, which had previously invested £5 million (€6 million) in HH2E, allowing them the right to co-invest in certain hydrogen supply projects in Germany.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** January 2023
   - **Details:** The Thierbach project is currently in the development phase, with the HH2E team progressing towards the procurement of long-lead equipment. A final investment decision is expected later in 2023.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
