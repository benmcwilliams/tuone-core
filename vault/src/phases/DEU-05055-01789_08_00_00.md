### Evolutionary Timeline of the Masdar Facility in Baltic Sea, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2023**: Masdar, in partnership with Iberdrola, announced the co-investment in the Baltic Eagle offshore wind farm, which has a production capacity of **476 MW**. The wind farm will consist of **50 wind turbines**, each with a capacity of **9.53 MW**. The anticipated annual production is **1.9 terawatt-hours (TWh)**, enough to power **475,000 homes** and reduce CO2 emissions by **800,000 tons** annually. The facility is scheduled to begin initial operations at the **end of 2024**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2023**: The Baltic Eagle wind farm is valued at approximately **€1.6 billion**. This investment is part of a strategic agreement between Masdar and Iberdrola, aimed at enhancing green energy security in Europe and supporting the Energy Security and Industry Accelerator (ESIA) agreement between the UAE and Germany.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2023**: Announcement of the strategic agreement and co-investment in the Baltic Eagle offshore wind farm. The project is set to begin initial operations at the **end of 2024**.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2023**: The project is expected to support **thousands of highly skilled jobs** in the supply chain, contributing to job creation in the region as part of the broader investment in renewable energy infrastructure.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2023**: Iberdrola retains a **51% majority stake** in the Baltic Eagle wind farm, while Masdar is a co-investor in the project. This indicates a **joint venture** ownership structure between Masdar and Iberdrola.
