## DEU-05061-02343

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lhyfe Facility in Schwäbisch Gmünd, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **02-06-2022**: Lhyfe announced plans to build a green hydrogen plant in Schwäbisch Gmünd, with an initial production capacity of 10 MW, expected to produce up to 4 tons of green hydrogen per day (approximately 1,000 tonnes annually). The facility will utilize renewable electricity from a wind farm and will supply hydrogen for industrial customers and fuel cell vehicles.
- **13-10-2023**: The construction of the facility was confirmed, with an expected commissioning date in the second half of 2024. The plant will use renewable electricity secured through hydro, wind, and solar power purchase agreements.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **16-10-2023**: The project received funding from the state of Baden-Württemberg and the EU, supporting the construction of a hydrogen filling station in Schwäbisch Gmünd. The project is part of the HyFIVE initiative and has received €6.4 million in funding from the European Regional Development Fund (ERDF).

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **02-06-2022**: Announcement of the project and its operational timeline.
- **13-10-2023**: Confirmation of the start of construction for Germany's largest commercial green hydrogen production plant.
- **16-10-2023**: The project is set to be commissioned in the second half of 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **16-10-2023**: The project is expected to create jobs in the local area, particularly related to the construction and operation of the hydrogen plant and associated infrastructure, although specific job numbers were not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Lhyfe is the sole owner of the facility, as indicated by the absence of any mention of joint ventures or partnerships in the context of ownership.
