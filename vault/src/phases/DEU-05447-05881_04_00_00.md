### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Gas Storage West Facility in Epe, Gronau, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2023:** RWE Gas Storage West submitted planning approval documents for the construction of a hydrogen storage facility in Epe, Gronau. This facility will be Germany's first hydrogen cavern storage facility for commercial purposes, primarily built on RWE’s existing site at Kottiger Hook. The facility will consist of two caverns, with the second cavern added to the original plan.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **February 2023:** RWE has applied for funding for the construction of the hydrogen storage facility under the IPCEI (Important Projects of Common European Interest) Hydrogen framework. However, funding has not yet been approved, and RWE's final investment decision is still pending.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **April 2022:** RWE announced the start of the approval procedure for the hydrogen storage facility.
   - **February 2023:** Planning approval documents were submitted to the Arnsberg District Government. An information day for the public is scheduled for March 28, 2023, to outline the project details.
   - **End of 2026:** The construction of the hydrogen storage facility is expected to be completed.
