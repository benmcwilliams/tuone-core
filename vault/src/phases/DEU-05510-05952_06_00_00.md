### Evolutionary Timeline of the SINN Power Facility in Eching am Ammersee, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 10-03-2023
   - **Details**: SINN Power plans to build a floating solar power plant at the Ammerseewerke wastewater treatment plant in Eching am Ammersee. The facility will utilize almost 11,000 m² of water surface across five wastewater ponds, generating approximately 1,500 MWh of solar power annually. This energy will meet the plant's own needs and supply green energy to the surrounding communities.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: 10-03-2023
   - **Details**: The project is a collaboration between SINN Power and Ammerseewerke, a joint company of several municipalities in Bavaria. Specific investment amounts or subsidies have not been detailed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: 10-03-2023
   - **Details**: The project is in the planning phase, with construction start dependent on the approval process that is about to commence. This project is noted as a pioneering effort, as it represents the first use of polishing ponds for solar power generation in Germany and potentially worldwide.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: 10-03-2023
   - **Details**: The article does not specify job creation or job loss related to this project at this time.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: 10-03-2023
   - **Details**: SINN Power is collaborating with Ammerseewerke, indicating a joint venture structure for this facility, rather than exclusive ownership by SINN Power.
