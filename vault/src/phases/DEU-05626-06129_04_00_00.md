#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2019**: The Städtische Betriebe Haßfurt GmbH successfully put into operation a highly innovative combined heat and power plant (H2-BHKW) that utilizes expanded hydrogen for reconversion of renewable energy. This facility is capable of operating with pure hydrogen without fossil fuel components, marking a significant advancement in municipal energy practices. The CHP module, developed by 2G Energy AG, delivers an electrical output of up to 200 kW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2019**: The project received funding from the Bavarian State Ministry of Economic Affairs, Development and Energy (StMWi) as part of the Bavarian Energy Research Program. The funding supports the development and operation of the hydrogen-based CHP plant over a period of three years.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2019**: The facility was officially put into operation, marking a significant milestone in the project. The project partners include Stadtwerk Haßfurt GmbH, 2G Energy AG, and the Institute for Energy Technology (IfE) at the Ostbayerische Technische Hochschule Amberg-Weiden.
