### Evolutionary Timeline of the TotalEnergies Facility in Leuna, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: TotalEnergies announced a partnership with VNG to utilize green hydrogen in its refining process at the Leuna refinery. The green hydrogen will be sourced from VNG’s Bad Lauchstädt Energy Park, with a 30 MW electrolyzer being constructed to produce the hydrogen. The trial operation of the hydrogen connection is expected to begin in early 2025, with full transport anticipated later that year. This initiative aligns with TotalEnergies' goal to decarbonize all hydrogen used in its European refineries by 2030.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The partnership with VNG involves significant investment in the Bad Lauchstädt Energy Park project, which is in the implementation phase. The project aims to provide the Leuna refinery with access to future European hydrogen infrastructure and international markets.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: TotalEnergies confirmed its role as the first "anchor customer" for the green hydrogen project, which will connect to the Leuna refinery via pipeline.
- **September 2023**: TotalEnergies announced a "massive" green hydrogen tender to replace the entire 500,000 tons of gray hydrogen used annually across its European refineries, including Leuna, by the end of the decade. This initiative aims to avoid approximately 5 million tons of CO₂ emissions.
