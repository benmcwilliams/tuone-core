## DEU-05880-06411

### Evolutionary Timeline of the ib vogt Facility in Riau Archipelago, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2022**: A memorandum of understanding (MoU) was signed between ReNu Energy, its subsidiary Countrywide Hydrogen, and Anantara Energy to explore the feasibility of a large-scale green hydrogen production facility in Indonesia's Riau archipelago. The facility is planned to feature a **10 MW electrolyzer** supported by at least **100 MW of solar power**. The electrolyzer is expected to produce approximately **1,650 tonnes of green hydrogen per year**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2022**: Anantara Energy has committed to invest up to **$6 billion** to develop a **3.5 GW solar and storage facility** in Riau. This investment is part of the broader project that includes the green hydrogen production facility.
- **November 2022**: Countrywide Hydrogen signed a term sheet with Australian superannuation fund HESTA for an investment of up to **$67.1 million** in the company’s green hydrogen projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2022**: The joint venture has secured more than **600 hectares of land** and is in the process of obtaining permitting rights and financing commitments for the development of the **3.5 GW solar plant** with up to **12 GWh of energy storage capacity**. The PV plant is expected to generate an estimated **4 TWh of clean energy annually**.
- **Expected Timeline**: Subject to a positive outcome from the feasibility studies, financial closure for the green hydrogen manufacturing facility is anticipated in **2024**, with production expected to commence in **2025**.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or job loss figures related to the investments or expansions at this facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is part of a **joint venture** involving ReNu Energy, Countrywide Hydrogen, and Anantara Energy, which includes Quantum Power Asia and ib vogt. Therefore, ib vogt does not own the facility exclusively; it is part of a collaborative partnership.
