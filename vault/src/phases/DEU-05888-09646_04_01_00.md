### Evolutionary Timeline of the Open Grid Europe (OGE) Facility in Emsland, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 12-02-2019
   - **Details**: OGE, in collaboration with Amprion, announced a €150 million power-to-gas project named 'hybridge'. The facility will feature a 100 MW electrolyser designed to convert electricity from renewable sources into green hydrogen and green methane through electrolysis. The project aims to bridge the electricity and gas sectors and is expected to be operational by 2023.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: 12-02-2019
   - **Details**: The total investment for the 'hybridge' project is €150 million. This investment is aimed at constructing the electrolyser and developing the necessary hydrogen infrastructure in Emsland.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: 12-02-2019
   - **Details**: The project is currently in the approval phase, with plans to begin construction once regulatory requirements are met. The facility is expected to be operational by 2023.
