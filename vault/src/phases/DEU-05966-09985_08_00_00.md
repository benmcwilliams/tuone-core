### Evolutionary Timeline of the Semco Maritime Facility in Butendiek, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Operational Since:** 2015
   - **Installed Capacity:** 288 MW
   - **Annual Electricity Production:** Approximately 1,200 GWh
   - **Current Operations:** Semco Maritime has been awarded a 10-year service contract for operations and maintenance of the Butendiek offshore wind farm, which includes 80 Siemens Gamesa 3.6 MW turbines. 2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Recent Contract Award:** On 16 November 2023, Semco Maritime announced a 10-year balance-of-plant operations and maintenance contract with Siemens Gamesa, effective from 1 January 2024. This contract signifies a strategic investment in the service sector of offshore wind operations.

   - **Contract Announcement Date:** 16 November 2023
