### RWE Facility in Albbruck, DEU - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: RWE, in collaboration with Badenova, announced plans to build an electrolysis plant at the RADAG run-of-river power plant in Albbruck. The facility will have a production capacity of **50 MW**, capable of producing up to **8,000 metric tons of green hydrogen per year**. The green electricity for this production will be sourced from renewable sources in the region.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: The project is part of a broader initiative to create a hub for producing, transporting, and distributing green hydrogen in the Upper Rhine region. Specific investment amounts for the Albbruck facility were not detailed in the articles, but it is part of RWE's strategic development in green hydrogen.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023**: RWE and Badenova announced the project, with the expectation that significant quantities of green hydrogen will be available from the facility by the **end of 2026**. The construction of a new hydrogen pipeline from Waldshut to Albbruck is also planned to facilitate distribution to industrial and transport customers.

#### 4. Labour
