### InoBat Auto Facility Timeline - Middle Europe, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2023
   - **Details:** InoBat, in collaboration with Gotion High-Tech, plans to establish a gigafactory in Middle Europe with an initial production capacity of 20 GWh. The facility aims to support local automakers, including Volkswagen Group, by localizing battery production to reduce transportation emissions and enhance competitiveness. The potential for future expansion of the gigafactory to nearly 250 acres is also mentioned.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2023
   - **Details:** Gotion High-Tech announced a 25% stake acquisition in InoBat, marking the first investment by a Chinese battery maker in a European startup. This investment is aimed at expediting InoBat's technology into mass production, with Gotion providing raw materials and sharing its R&D and production expertise.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2023
   - **Details:** A pre-joint venture agreement has been signed between Gotion and InoBat to establish the gigafactory. Construction is scheduled to begin in 2024, with full operations expected to launch in 2026.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2023
   - **Details:** The establishment of the gigafactory is projected to create thousands of local jobs, although specific job locations have not yet been determined.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** September 2023
   - **Details:** The facility will be a joint venture between Gotion High-Tech and InoBat, with Gotion holding a 25% stake in InoBat. Volkswagen Group, which owns 24.77% of Gotion, will benefit from the exclusive supply of EV batteries from this facility outside of China.
