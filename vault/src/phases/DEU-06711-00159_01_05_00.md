### Evolutionary Timeline of the Cylib Facility in Chempark Dormagen, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **09/2024:** Cylib has started construction of its first industrial battery recycling plant at Chempark Dormagen. The facility will have an annual recycling capacity of 30,000 tonnes, focusing on recycling raw materials from lithium-ion batteries using a water-based lithium and graphite recovery process. This method aims to reduce the environmental footprint (GHG-potential) by 30% compared to conventional methods.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **09/2024:** Cylib closed a financing round worth 55 million euros to support the scaling up of production on an industrial scale and to expand its interdisciplinary team.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **09/2024:** Construction of the battery recycling plant has commenced, with the facility scheduled to be commissioned in 2026.

4. **Labour:**
   - **09/2024:** The facility is expected to create around 170 jobs in the region upon its opening in 2026.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **09/2024:** Cylib acquired a 236,000 square ft (22,000 m²) site within Chempark Dormagen, which is managed by Currenta. The ownership structure indicates that Cylib owns the facility, but it operates within a chemical park owned by Currenta.
