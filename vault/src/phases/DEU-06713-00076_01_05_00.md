## DEU-06713-00076

### Evolutionary Timeline of the BMW Facility in Kirchroth, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2024
   - **Details:** BMW announced the establishment of a battery cell recycling competence centre in Kirchroth, utilizing a 'direct recycling' process. This facility will mechanically dismantle battery cells and production residuals to recover valuable components such as lithium, cobalt, graphite, manganese, nickel, and copper. The centre will span 2,200 m² and will be integrated into an existing building in the Kirchroth-Nord industrial park.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2024
   - **Details:** BMW is investing approximately 10 million euros for the construction of the battery cell recycling facility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2024
   - **Details:** The construction of the recycling facility is scheduled to begin in the second half of 2025. Following construction, validation of the recycling method in near-series processes will commence.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** November 2024
   - **Details:** Once operational, the new Competence Centre is expected to create around 20 jobs.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** November 2024
   - **Details:** The facility will not be operated directly by BMW, although the company owns the relevant intellectual properties. Instead, it will be managed by Encory GmbH, a joint venture between BMW Group and Interzero Group, with both partners holding a 50% stake in the company. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments regarding the BMW facility in Kirchroth, DEU, based on the provided article.

**1. Technology and Operations:**
- "The BMW Group is building a competence centre for battery cell recycling in Kirchroth in Lower Bavaria."
- "There, the company will employ a process called ‘direct recycling’, which enables residual materials from battery cell production, as well as whole battery cells, to be mechanically dismantled into their valuable components."
- "Unlike conventional methods, the main characteristic of direct recycling is that raw materials from battery cells are not reverted to their original state, but are instead fed back 'directly' into the cell production cycle."

**2. Investment and Development:**
- "For the construction of the new recycling facility, BMW is investing around 10 million euros."

**3. Project Status and Milestones:**
- "Installation work at the building is scheduled to begin in the second half of 2025."
- "Once construction is completed, BMW writes that validation of the recycling method in near-series processes will get underway."

**4. Labour:**
- "Once completed, about 20 people will be employed in the new Competence Center."

**5. Ownership structure:**
- "The centre will not actually be operated by BMW, however, despite owning the relevant intellectual properties, but by Encory GmbH."
- "It is a joint venture of the BMW Group and Interzero Group, which develops and implements logistics and consulting solutions in areas such as collection, recycling, and remanufacturing of vehicle components."
- "Both partners hold a 50 per cent stake in the company."
