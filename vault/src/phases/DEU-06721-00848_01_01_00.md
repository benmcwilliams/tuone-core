### Evolutionary Timeline of the Vulcan Energy Facility in Industriepark Höchst, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2024**: Vulcan Energy produced lithium hydroxide for the first time at the Central Lithium Electrolysis Optimisation Plant (CLEOP) in Industriepark Höchst. This marks the first sustainable lithium hydroxide production in Europe, utilizing geothermal brine as the raw material. The facility aims to produce 24,000 tonnes of lithium hydroxide monohydrate (LHM) annually, as per a feasibility study announced in February 2023. The CLEOP is part of a fully integrated supply chain for lithium production, which includes the Lithium Extraction Optimisation Plant (LEOP) in Landau.

#### 2. Investment and Development

- **November 2024**: The production of lithium hydroxide at CLEOP is part of Vulcan's strategy to create a local source of sustainable lithium in Europe, contributing to the region's independence from critical raw materials.
