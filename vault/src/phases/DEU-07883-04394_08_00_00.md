### Evolutionary Timeline of the 50Hertz Facility in Rostock-Warnemünde, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **02/2024**: 50Hertz announces the development of the Rostock Offshore Quarter (ROQ) to serve as its offshore wind transmission operations hub. The facility will include an administration building, indoor and outdoor warehouses, and berths for supply and transport ships. The site will support operational management of electrotechnical facilities in the Baltic Sea.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **02/2024**: 50Hertz is investing approximately €80 million in the Rostock Offshore Quarter. This investment is aimed at establishing a facility that will enhance their offshore activities in the Baltic Sea.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **02/2024**: A heritable building right contract is concluded between the Hanseatic and University City of Rostock and 50Hertz for a plot of land at the port in Warnemünde, covering an area of approximately 23,000 square meters. The subscription date for the ROQ is scheduled for 2028, with plans to deepen the harbor basin to 10 meters to accommodate heavy-duty ships.

#### 4. Labour
- **02/2024**: The facility is expected to create around 180 jobs, with various units including a maritime coordination center, offshore control center, and offshore logistics center planned to be housed in the administration building.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **02/2024**: The facility is owned exclusively by 50Hertz, as indicated by the heritable building right contract with the local government.
