### Evolutionary Timeline of the Agri Energia Facility in Hallertau, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: July 2023
   - **Details**: Agri Energia has commissioned a pilot agrivoltaic facility in Hallertau, near Munich, Bavaria. The facility combines solar power generation with hop cultivation, covering an area of 1.3 hectares. It is designed to produce enough solar energy to supply approximately 200 households. The installation features a PV system mounted on steel masts, which not only supports the hop plants but also protects them from sunlight and hail while reducing evaporation.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: July 2023
   - **Details**: The project represents an investment of €1.5 million (approximately $1.64 million). It was developed with support from the Fraunhofer Institute for Solar Energy Systems ISE and the Weihenstephan-Triesdorf University of Applied Sciences.
