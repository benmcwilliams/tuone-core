### Evolutionary Timeline of the Statkraft Facility in Berg im Gau, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **September 2021**: The Schornhof solar park, located in Berg im Gau, was officially commissioned with a production capacity of 120 MW. It is noted as the largest ground-mounted photovoltaic (PV) installation in southern Germany. The facility is designed to sell power under two long-term Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) for 90 MW of its capacity. Anumar, the Bavarian photovoltaic company, is responsible for the construction and operation of the facility.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **September 2021**: Anumar invested approximately €60 million in the construction of the Schornhof solar park. The project is supported by a combination of PPAs and funding from the German renewable energy law (EEG).

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **September 2021**: The facility was officially commissioned and began operations. Anumar announced plans to expand the solar park by an additional 60 MW in the following year, increasing the total area from 140 hectares to 200 hectares. The municipality of Berg im Gau has already approved this expansion.
