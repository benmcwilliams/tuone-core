### Evolutionary Timeline of the BASF Facility in Borkum Riffgrund 3, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Borkum Riffgrund 3 offshore wind farm will produce 913 MW of renewable energy using 83 SG 11.0-200 DD offshore wind turbines. The turbines feature a 200-meter diameter rotor and are designed to increase Annual Energy Production by 9% compared to previous models. The installation work is scheduled for 2024, with commissioning expected in 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The project has secured corporate power purchase agreements (CPPAs) for a total of 786 MW, which includes a commitment from BASF for 186 MW. This agreement is intended to support the final investment decision by providing revenue certainty for the project.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **June 2022:** Siemens Gamesa received a firm turbine order from Ørsted for the Borkum Riffgrund 3 offshore wind farm. The project is set to begin installation in 2024 and is expected to be commissioned in 2025.
