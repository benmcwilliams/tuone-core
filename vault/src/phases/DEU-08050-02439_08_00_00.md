### Evolutionary Timeline of Siemens Gamesa Facility in Borkum Riffgrund 3, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2020**: Siemens Gamesa is named the preferred turbine supplier for Ørsted's Borkum Riffgrund 3 offshore wind farm, which has a planned capacity of 900 MW. The facility will utilize the new SG 11.0-200 DD turbine, featuring a 200-meter diameter rotor and an 11 MW generator capacity. The turbine is expected to increase annual energy production by 9% compared to previous models.
- **June 2022**: Siemens Gamesa confirms a firm order for 83 SG 11.0-200 DD turbines for the Borkum Riffgrund 3 project, increasing the total capacity to 913 MW. Installation is scheduled for 2024, with commissioning expected in 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2020**: The preferred supplier agreement includes a five-year service and maintenance contract, contingent upon Ørsted's final investment decision and necessary approvals from German authorities.
- **June 2022**: Ørsted has signed corporate power purchase agreements (CPPAs) for a total of 786 MW with various companies, providing revenue certainty for the project and supporting the final investment decision.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2020**: Announcement of Siemens Gamesa as the preferred supplier for Borkum Riffgrund 3, with installation expected to start in 2024 and commissioning in 2025.
- **June 2022**: Confirmation of a firm order for the turbines, with installation scheduled for 2024 and commissioning anticipated in 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2022**: The project is expected to provide clean energy to over one million households, contributing to energy security in Germany. However, specific job creation or loss figures related to this project have not been detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2022**: The Borkum Riffgrund 3 wind farm is co-owned by Ørsted (50%) and Glennmont Partners (50%). Ørsted will manage the construction and operations under a full-scope EPC contract and will provide maintenance services for 20 years.
