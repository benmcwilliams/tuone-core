### Evolutionary Timeline of the Covestro Facility in Borkum Riffgrund 3, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Borkum Riffgrund 3 offshore wind farm will feature 83 SG 11.0-200 DD offshore wind turbines, with a total production capacity of 913 MW. The turbines have a rotor diameter of 200 meters and are designed to increase Annual Energy Production by 9% compared to previous models.
