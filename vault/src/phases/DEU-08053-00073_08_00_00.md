### Evolutionary Timeline of the BASF Facility in Nordlicht Wind Park Zone, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 06-12-2023
   - **Details:** BASF is in advanced talks to acquire a 49% stake in the Nordlicht 1 and 2 offshore wind farms, which will have a total capacity of 1.6 GW (980 MW for Nordlicht 1 and 630 MW for Nordlicht 2). The wind farms are expected to generate 6 terawatt-hours (TWh) of clean energy annually, sufficient to power 1.6 million German homes. BASF plans to utilize its share of the electricity to power its chemical production sites across Europe, particularly in Ludwigshafen, Germany.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 06-12-2023
   - **Details:** The memorandum of understanding between BASF and Vattenfall indicates a commitment to invest in the Nordlicht 1 and 2 wind farms. A final investment decision is anticipated in 2025, with construction expected to begin in 2026.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 06-12-2023
   - **Details:** The deal between BASF and Vattenfall is set to be signed in the first half of 2024. The wind farms are scheduled to begin operations in 2028.
