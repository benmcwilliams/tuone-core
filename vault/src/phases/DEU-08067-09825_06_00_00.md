### Evolutionary Timeline of RGE Energy Facility in Eberswalde, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2009
   - **Details:** RGE Energy announced plans to install a 15-MW solar system at Eberswalde airport as part of a cooperative agreement with BP Solar. The facility will utilize BP Solar crystalline-silicon PV panels, with a total of 66 MW of installations planned across various projects, including a 46-MWp plant in Koethen, Saxony-Anhalt.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** May 2009
   - **Details:** The project at Eberswalde is part of a larger investment involving hundreds of thousands of PV panels supplied by BP Solar. BP Solar will provide a yield guarantee for the solar modules, ensuring that the energy yields meet the agreed standards based on the feed-in tariff.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** May 2009
   - **Details:** The project launch for the Eberswalde solar system is planned for the third quarter of 2009, with the approval procedure currently in process. Installation of the 220-W modules is expected to be completed by the end of the year.
