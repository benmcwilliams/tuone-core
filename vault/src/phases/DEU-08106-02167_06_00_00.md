### Evolutionary Timeline of the BayWa Facility in Gelsdorf, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2021**: BayWa r.e. and the Fraunhofer Institute for Solar Energy Systems ISE completed the construction of a 258 kW agrivoltaic system in Gelsdorf, Rhineland-Palatinate. This system integrates four different crop protection methods for apple cultivation, utilizing both permanent light-permeable PV modules and tracking PV module technology. The facility occupies 9,100 square meters and is designed to protect crops from environmental factors such as hail and heavy rainfall while generating electricity for agricultural applications.
