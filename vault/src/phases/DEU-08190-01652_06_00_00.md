### Evolutionary Timeline of the First Solar Facility in Cottbus, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2009
   - **Details:** The facility is set to produce solar power with a capacity of 53 MW, utilizing 700,000 thin-film solar modules.
