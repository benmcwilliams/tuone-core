### Evolutionary Timeline of Lausitz Energie Bergbau Facility in Cottbus, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2019**: Lausitz Energie Bergbau, in collaboration with Juwi Group, announced the construction of a 10MW solar farm near Cottbus, specifically on the site of an airfield close to the Welzow-Sud opencast lignite mine. The solar project is expected to generate approximately 10 million kilowatt-hours of electricity annually, connecting to the grid at the Bahnsdorf substation. The solar farm will consist of 27,000 modules supplied by Astronergy and two central inverters from SMA.

- **May 2019**: The solar project represents Lausitz Energie Bergbau's first venture into renewable energy, expanding its generation portfolio beyond its core business of lignite. Specific investment amounts were not disclosed in the article.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
