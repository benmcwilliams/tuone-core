#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2019**: Lausitz Energie Kraftwerke (LEAG) is set to expand its generation portfolio by constructing a 10MW solar plant near Cottbus, specifically on the site of an airfield close to the Welzow-Sud opencast lignite mine.
