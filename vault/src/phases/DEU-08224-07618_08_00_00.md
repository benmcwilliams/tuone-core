### Evolutionary Timeline of the CEE Facility in Nonnweiler, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2019
   - **Details:** The Nonnweiler facility consists of a wind farm with a total production capacity of 16.5MW, featuring six GE 2.75-120 turbines with hub heights of 139 meters. The turbines are supported on Max Bogl hybrid towers. The facility was installed with minimal forest clearance, utilizing a rotating tower crane to reduce the required clearance area by over one hectare.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** May 2019
   - **Details:** CEE Group acquired the Nonnweiler wind farm from Max Bogl, contributing to CEE's total renewable capacity of approximately 710MW. The acquisition aligns with CEE's strategy to grow its core business in Germany and France.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** May 2019
   - **Details:** The acquisition of the Nonnweiler wind farm was finalized, marking it as the second joint project between CEE and Max Bogl, following the acquisition of the Asslar wind farm in 2018. CEE Operations will manage the commercial aspects of the wind farm, while Max Bogl Wind will oversee technical operations.
