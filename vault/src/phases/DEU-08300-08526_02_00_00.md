## DEU-08300-08526

### Evolutionary Timeline of Chubu Electric Power Co., Inc. Facility in Geretsried, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is part of the Eavor-Europe geothermal project, which is the first commercial implementation of the Eavor-Loop technology. 
   - The project will produce 8.2 MWe of energy and is designed to avoid approximately 44,000 tCO2e of greenhouse gas emissions annually. 
   - The technology involves multiple large underground radiators where clean fresh water circulates to carry heat to the surface, allowing geothermal energy deployment without relying on traditional factors like permeability or existing aquifers.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The project received a grant of €91.6 million from the European Innovation Fund (EIF) to support its development.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - Groundbreaking for the Eavor-Europe project occurred in October 2022.
   - Drilling operations are scheduled to commence in July 2023.
   - The first energy production is expected in Q4 2024.

4. **Labour:**
   - The project is anticipated to create up to 600 person-years of jobs related to drilling services and power plant/infrastructure during the construction phase.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is a collaborative project involving Chubu Electric Power Co., Inc., Eavor Technologies Inc., and Enex Power Germany GmbH, indicating a joint venture ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by Chubu Electric Power Co., Inc.
