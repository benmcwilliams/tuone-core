## DEU-08300-08704

### Evolutionary Timeline of Eavor Erdwärme Geretsried GmbH Facility in Geretsried, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10/2022**: Groundbreaking for the Eavor-Europe geothermal project, which will be the first commercial implementation of the Eavor-Loop technology. The facility will utilize multiple large underground radiators to circulate clean fresh water for heat extraction.
- **03/2024**: Drilling has reached a depth of 7000 meters, and construction of the Organic Rankine Cycle (ORC) power plant is ongoing. The project is expected to produce 8.2 MWe and avoid approximately 44,000 tCO2e GHG emissions annually. The first energy production is scheduled for Q4 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **03/2023**: Eavor Erdwärme Geretsried GmbH is awarded a grant of €91.6 million from the European Innovation Fund (EIF) to support the Eavor-Europe project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10/2022**: Groundbreaking for the Eavor-Europe project.
- **07/2023**: Drilling operations are scheduled to commence.
- **03/2024**: Announcement of drilling progress reaching 7000 meters and ongoing construction of the power plant.

#### 4. Labour
- **03/2023**: The project is expected to create up to 600 person-years of jobs related to drilling services and power plant/infrastructure during the construction phase.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Eavor Erdwärme Geretsried GmbH is part of Eavor Technologies Inc. and operates in collaboration with partners such as Chubu Electric Power Co., Inc. and Enex Power Germany GmbH, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership.
