### Evolutionary Timeline of Eavor Technologies Inc. Facility in Geretsried, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2022**: Groundbreaking for the Eavor-Europe geothermal project in Geretsried, Bavaria, Germany. This facility will implement the Eavor-Loop technology, which consists of multiple underground radiators circulating clean fresh water to carry heat to the surface.
- **July 2023**: Drilling commenced for the first commercial Eavor-Loop, with a total of four loops planned to be drilled to a depth of 4500 meters. The facility is expected to generate approximately 64 MW of thermal output and 8.2 MW of electrical output, avoiding around 44,000 tons of CO2 equivalent emissions annually. The first electrical energy production is scheduled for summer 2024, with full completion expected in 2027.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2023**: Eavor Technologies Inc. and Eavor Erdwärme Geretsried GmbH were awarded a grant of €91.6 million from the European Innovation Fund (EIF) to support the Eavor-Europe project. This funding is crucial for the construction and implementation of the geothermal project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2022**: Groundbreaking ceremony for the Eavor-Europe project.
- **July 2023**: Official start of drilling operations for the Eavor-Loop project, marked by a ceremonial event (chisel consecration) at the drilling site.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2023**: The project is expected to create up to 600 person-years of jobs related to drilling services and power plant/infrastructure during the construction phase.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The Eavor Geretsried project is owned by Eavor Technologies Inc., indicating full ownership of the facility by the company.
