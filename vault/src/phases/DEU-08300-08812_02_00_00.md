## DEU-08300-08812

### Evolutionary Timeline of Enex Power Germany GmbH Facility in Geretsried, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is part of the Eavor-Europe geothermal project, which is the first commercial implementation of the Eavor-Loop technology. This technology utilizes multiple large underground radiators to circulate clean fresh water, carrying heat to the surface. 
   - The project is designed to produce 8.2 MWe and aims to avoid approximately 44,000 tCO2e in greenhouse gas emissions annually. It is expected to power around 20,000 homes with clean energy.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The project has been awarded a grant of €91.6 million from the European Innovation Fund (EIF) to support its development. This funding is crucial for the construction and implementation of the Eavor-Loop technology.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - Groundbreaking for the Eavor-Europe project occurred in October 2022.
   - Drilling operations are scheduled to commence in July 2023.
   - An Organic Rankine Cycle (ORC) power plant is being designed and constructed concurrently with drilling operations, with the first energy production expected in Q4 2024.

4. **Labour:**
   - The project is anticipated to create up to 600 person-years of jobs related to drilling services and power plant/infrastructure during the construction phase.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is a collaborative effort involving Eavor GmbH, Chubu Electric Power Co., Inc., and Enex Power Germany GmbH, indicating a joint venture ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by a single entity.
