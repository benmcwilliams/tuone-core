- **08/2022**: Turboden's Organic Rankine Cycle (ORC) system is selected for the Eavor-Loop project in Geretsried. The project will consist of four loops and a single ORC generator, with full operational capacity expected in 2026. The system is designed to avoid 44,000 tonnes of CO2 emissions annually.

- **07/2023**: Drilling starts for the first commercial Eavor-Loop in Geretsried. The project will involve drilling four loops to a depth of 4,500 meters, with the first electrical energy supply expected by summer 2024. The entire facility is scheduled for completion in 2027.
