## DEU-08300-10261

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Turboden S.p.A. Facility in Geretsried, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is involved in the Eavor-Europe geothermal project, which is the first commercial implementation of the Eavor-Loop technology. This technology utilizes multiple large underground radiators to circulate clean fresh water for heat extraction.
   - The project is designed to produce 8.2 MWe of energy and aims to avoid approximately 44,000 tCO2e of greenhouse gas emissions annually. It is expected to power around 20,000 homes with clean energy.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - Eavor Technologies Inc. and Eavor Erdwärme Geretsried GmbH received a grant of €91.6 million from the European Innovation Fund (EIF) to support the Eavor-Europe project.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - Groundbreaking for the Eavor-Europe project occurred in October 2022.
   - Drilling operations are scheduled to commence in July 2023.
   - The first energy production from the facility is anticipated in Q4 2024.

4. **Labour:**
   - The project is expected to create up to 600 person-years of jobs related to drilling services and power plant/infrastructure during the construction phase.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is a collaborative effort involving Eavor and its project partners, including Chubu Electric Power Co., Inc. and Enex Power Germany GmbH. The specific ownership structure regarding Turboden S.p.A. is not detailed, but it is implied that Turboden is a key collaborator in the design and construction of the Organic Rankine Cycle (ORC) power plant associated with the project.
