### Evolutionary Timeline of the Siemens Gamesa Facility in Veja Mate, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Operational Since:** 2017
   - **Production Capacity:** 402 MW
   - **Wind Turbines:** 67 wind turbines installed
   - **Energy Generation:** Capable of generating clean energy for approximately 450,000 households.
   - **Environmental Impact:** Displaces around 950,000 tons of carbon emissions annually.
   - **Awards:** Recognized as the Best Offshore Wind Power Plant in Operation at the Helgoland Offshore Economic Forum for two consecutive years.
