### Evolutionary Timeline of the Commerz Real Facility in Veja Mate, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Operational Since:** 2017
   - **Production Capacity:** 402 MW
   - **Wind Turbines:** 67 turbines
   - **Energy Generation:** Supplies clean energy to approximately 450,000 households.
   - **Environmental Impact:** Displaces 950,000 tons of carbon emissions annually.
   - **Awards:** Recognized as the Best Offshore Wind Power Plant in Operation at the Helgoland Offshore Economic Forum for two consecutive years.
   - **Maintenance Provider:** Siemens Gamesa Renewable Energy (SGRE) is contracted for technical maintenance until 2032.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Ownership Acquisition:** On June 7, 2022, Commerz Real, along with KGAL and Ingka Group, acquired the remaining 20% stake in the Veja Mate wind farm from Siemens Financial Services, achieving full ownership.
   - **Previous Stake Acquisition:** In February 2019, the consortium (Commerz Real, Ingka Group, KGAL, and ALH Group) acquired an 80% stake from Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners, Highland Group Holdings, and Siemens Financial Services.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Full Ownership Announcement:** June 7, 2022 - KGAL, Commerz Real, and Ingka Group announced they had taken full ownership of the Veja Mate offshore wind farm.
   - **Operational Status:** The facility has been operational since 2017.
