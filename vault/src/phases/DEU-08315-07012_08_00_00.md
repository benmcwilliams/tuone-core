### Evolutionary Timeline of the KGAL Facility in Veja Mate, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Operational Since:** 2017
   - **Production Capacity:** 402 MW
   - **Wind Turbines:** 67 turbines generating clean energy for approximately 450,000 households.
   - **Environmental Impact:** Displaces 950,000 tons of carbon emissions annually.
   - **Awards:** Recognized as the Best Offshore Wind Power Plant in Operation at the Helgoland Offshore Economic Forum for two consecutive years.
   - **Maintenance Provider:** Siemens Gamesa Renewable Energy (SGRE) is contracted to provide technical maintenance until 2032.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Full Ownership Acquisition:** On June 7, 2022, KGAL, Commerz Real, and Ingka Group acquired the remaining 20% stake in the facility from Siemens Financial Services, completing their ownership of the wind farm. This acquisition is part of the renewable energy fund KGAL ESPF IV.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Initial Stake Acquisition:** In February 2019, a consortium including Commerz Real, Ingka Group, KGAL, and ALH Group acquired an 80% stake in the Veja Mate offshore wind farm from Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners, Highland Group Holdings, and Siemens Financial Services.
   - **Complete Ownership Announcement:** June 7, 2022 - KGAL and partners announced they had taken full ownership of the Veja Mate wind farm.
