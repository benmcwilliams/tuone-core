### Evolutionary Timeline of the IBC Facility in Grimmen, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2010
   - **Details:** Construction began on the Grimmen PV park, which is a solar power plant with a rated power of approximately 7MWp. The facility will feature 30,480 polycrystalline solar modules, expected to produce a total of 6,239,256 kWh of electricity per year. The installation will utilize mounting systems driven into the ground rather than anchored to a cement foundation.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** October 2010
   - **Details:** The land for the facility, a 19-hectare clay pit, is leased from Grimmener Ton & Umwelt. The site has been designated as a commercial conversion site, indicating investment in the development of renewable energy infrastructure in the region.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** October 2010
   - **Details:** The opening ceremony for the construction of the facility was attended by Federal Chancellor Dr. Angela Merkel. The project is expected to be completed within weeks, with plans for grid connection by the end of 2010.
