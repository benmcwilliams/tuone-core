### Evolutionary Timeline of the Conergy Facility in Uckerland, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2012
   - **Details:** Conergy installed a 1MW photovoltaic (PV) system in Uckerland, Germany. The system consists of over 4,000 Conergy P series modules covering more than 8,000 square meters of roof area atop six newly-built barn roofs. It is designed to generate approximately 950,000 kWh of electricity annually, which will be used for on-site consumption and to supply around 240 households in the surrounding area. This output is expected to meet about one-third of the total annual power needs of the barns.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2012
   - **Details:** The project was developed in collaboration with SolEnergy, with Conergy acting as the general contractor responsible for all stages, including engineering, planning, and construction. The financial specifics of the investment were not detailed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** December 2012
   - **Details:** The installation of the 1MW PV system was completed and operational as of December 2012. This project was one of the first to respond to the new German Renewable Energy Sources Act (EEG) requirements, which would affect remuneration for energy production starting in 2014.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
