#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10-12-2024**: The drilling of a geothermal doublet in Neuruppin has been completed. The facility is expected to start contributing to district heating by 2027. The wells were drilled to depths of around 2000 meters, with initial tests indicating a potential extraction capacity of 330 cubic meters per hour, exceeding the initial estimate of 200 cubic meters per hour. The geothermal system is anticipated to supply more than the initially estimated 65-70% of the district heating supply.
