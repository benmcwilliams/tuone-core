   - The plant was connected to the grid in 1983 but has been out of service for several years due to various issues, including a transformer fire in 2007 and subsequent faults.
   - The facility was officially taken offline following the Fukushima accident in March 2011, as part of Germany's decision to phase out nuclear power.
