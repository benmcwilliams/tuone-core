### Evolutionary Timeline of the First Solar Facility in Senftenberg, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 06-07-2010
   - **Details:** The facility produces solar power using approximately 240,000 CdTe thin film modules from First Solar. The initial production capacity is 18MW, with 14.4MW already connected to the grid at the time of the article.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 06-07-2010
   - **Details:** The project is developed by Phoenix Solar and owned by Luxcara Asset Management. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 06-07-2010
   - **Details:** The first phase of the project (14.4MW) has been completed and connected to the grid. The second construction phase is planned for the second half of 2010, as it is not subject to the additional feed-in tariff cut due to the legal finalization of the building plan before the cutoff date of March 25.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** Not specified in the article.
   - **Details:** There is no information provided regarding job creation or job loss associated with this facility in the article.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 06-07-2010
   - **Details:** The facility is not exclusively owned by First Solar; it is owned by Luxcara Asset Management, with Phoenix Solar as the builder.
