## DEU-08503-02275

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hanwha Group Facility in Finsterwalde, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **July 2011:** The facility, which includes the Finsterwalde II and III PV power plants, has a combined installed capacity of 40MWp. When combined with the previously sold Finsterwalde I PV power plant, the total installed capacity of the entire site reaches 82MWp. The plants utilize 171,888 crystalline solar modules on a 237.7-acre site that was formerly an opencast lignite mine. Q-Cells supplied and installed the solar modules, and the plants were connected to the grid in September 2010.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **July 2011:** Q-Cells sold the Finsterwalde II and III PV power plants to Blue Forrest Solar Holding, a joint venture between DIF Infrastructure and the NIBC European Infrastructure Fund. The sale reflects a strategic investment in renewable energy infrastructure.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **September 2010:** The Finsterwalde II and III PV power plants were connected to the grid.
   - **July 2011:** Q-Cells completed the sale of the Finsterwalde II and III PV power plants to Blue Forrest Solar Holding.

4. **Labour:**
   - The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the sale or operation of the Finsterwalde facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **July 2011:** The facility is no longer exclusively owned by Q-Cells, as it was sold to Blue Forrest Solar Holding, indicating a joint venture ownership structure involving DIF Infrastructure and the NIBC European Infrastructure Fund. Q-Cells retains a role in managing the operation and maintenance of the plants under a long-term contract.
