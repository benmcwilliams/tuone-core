## DEU-08562-00219

### Evolutionary Timeline of the EnBW He Dreiht Offshore Wind Farm, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2023**: EnBW made the final investment decision (FID) for the He Dreiht offshore wind farm, which will have a total production capacity of 960 MW. The project will consist of 64 wind turbines, each with an individual output of 15 MW, utilizing Vestas’s new 15 MW model with a rotor diameter of 236 meters. The wind farm is designed to generate green electricity for approximately 1.1 million German households.
- **August 2023**: EnBW selected 8.2 Consulting AG to provide manufacturing surveillance services for the nacelle manufacturing of the wind turbines, indicating a focus on quality assurance across multiple European locations.
- **August 2024**: EnBW announced the completion of foundation installations for the wind farm, utilizing the Thialf floating crane to install 64 steel monopile foundations, each measuring 70 meters in length and weighing 1,350 tons. The installation of the 15 MW wind turbines is scheduled to begin in spring 2025, with operations expected to start by the end of 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2023**: The final investment decision marked a significant commitment to the project, which is being developed without state subsidies, indicating a strong financial backing and confidence in the project's viability.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2023**: Final investment decision (FID) made for the He Dreiht offshore wind farm.
- **August 2024**: Completion of foundation installations for the wind farm, marking a significant construction milestone. The project is on track to begin operations by the end of 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2024**: The construction site for the He Dreiht wind farm has seen over 500 people working at peak times during the foundation installation phase, indicating substantial job creation associated with the project.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- EnBW Energie Baden-Württemberg AG (EnBW) is the sole owner of the He Dreiht offshore wind farm, as indicated by the absence of any mention of joint ventures or partnerships in the articles.
