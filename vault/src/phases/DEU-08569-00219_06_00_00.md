### Evolutionary Timeline of the EnBW Facility in Wessow-Willmersdorf, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2019
   - **Details:** EnBW announced the construction of a 175 MW solar plant in Wessow-Willmersdorf, Brandenburg. The facility will consist of 465,000 solar modules installed across a 164-hectare site, with an expected annual electricity generation of approximately 180 million kWh.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** October 2019
   - **Details:** EnBW finalized its investment plan for the solar plant, marking it as the largest subsidy-free PV plant under development in Germany at that time. The project was acquired from Procon Solar GmbH in mid-2018.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** October 2019
   - **Details:** EnBW's board approved the plans for the facility, with construction set to begin in early 2020. The company aimed to make the project fully operational within the same calendar year.
