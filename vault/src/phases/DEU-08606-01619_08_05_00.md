### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enercon Facility in Aurich, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **08/2021**: Enercon began assembling the prototype of its E-nacelle at the Mechatronics Centre of Excellence in Aurich. The E-nacelle integrates electrical systems for the E-160 EP5 E3 turbine, with a machine house measuring 14 meters, nearly double the length of its predecessor. Series production was expected to start in Q4 2021, with over 160 units planned for 2022.
- **09/2023**: Enercon delivered a version of the E-nacelle prototype to its first customer project, specifically for the E-175 EP5 model. The E-nacelle underwent factory acceptance tests at the Aurich site before shipment.
