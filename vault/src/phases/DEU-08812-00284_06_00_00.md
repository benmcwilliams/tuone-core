### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fraunhofer Facility in Kressbronn am Bodensee, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2024**: The Fraunhofer Institute for Solar Energy Systems (Fraunhofer ISE) conducted a study on an agrivoltaic facility in Kressbronn am Bodensee. The facility features semi-transparent, double-glass photovoltaic (PV) modules installed above pome fruit orchards. 
  - **Southern Zone**: Panels with a power output of 260 W, 40% light transparency, and an approximate total power capacity of 147 kW.
  - **Northern Zone**: Panels with a power output of 170 W, 51% light transparency, and an approximate capacity of 95 kW.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones

This study is significant as it is the first to evaluate the impact of agrivoltaic systems on global navigation satellite systems (GNSS) used in precision agriculture.
