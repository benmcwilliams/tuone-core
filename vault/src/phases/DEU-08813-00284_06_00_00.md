### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fraunhofer Facility in Lake Constance, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2017**: The Fraunhofer ISE facility near Lake Constance conducted a pilot project on Agrophotovoltaics (APV), demonstrating the feasibility of dual land use for agriculture and solar power. The facility utilized a 194 kW PV system with 720 bifacial solar modules, producing 1266 kWh of electricity per installed kW, which is significantly higher than the German average of 950 kWh/kW. The pilot project tested various crops, including winter wheat, potatoes, celeriac, and clover grass, with promising results regarding crop yield and energy production.
