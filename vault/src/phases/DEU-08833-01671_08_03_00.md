### Evolutionary Timeline of the General Electric Facility in Salzbergen, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2017**: GE's facility in Salzbergen is involved in manufacturing turbines for wind farms, specifically supplying turbines from its 3MW range totaling 1.2GW for projects in Spain. The nacelles for these turbines are produced at the Salzbergen facility.
- **September 2018**: GE unveils the 5.3-158 onshore wind turbine as part of the new Cypress platform, which offers a 50% increase in annual energy production compared to the previous 3MW platform. A prototype of the 4.8MW unit is in production at the Salzbergen facility.
- **June 2019**: The nacelles for the 100MW Primorskaya 2 wind farm in Ukraine will be built at the Salzbergen facility, showcasing its role in international projects.
- **December 2020**: The facility continues to produce nacelles for the Cypress turbines, specifically for the Cuevas de Velasco wind project in Spain, which will utilize the new two-piece blade design.
