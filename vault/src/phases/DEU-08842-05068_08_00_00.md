## DEU-08842-05068

### Evolutionary Timeline of the GE Vernova Facility in Arkona Basin, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2024
   - **Details:** GE Vernova has been awarded a contract to build a 2 GW offshore grid connection as part of the Ostwind 4 project in the Arkona Basin. The facility will utilize a bipolar HVDC transmission system operating at 525 kV to transport offshore wind energy to the mainland. GE Vernova will provide various technologies including voltage-sourced converters (VSC), high-voltage transformers, g3 gas-insulated switchgear (GIS), air-core reactors, and civil works for the onshore converter station.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2024
   - **Details:** The project involves significant investment in infrastructure, including the offshore converter platform and the onshore converter station, which will be developed by the GE Vernova–Drydocks World consortium. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** December 2024
   - **Details:** The Ostwind 4 project is provisionally scheduled for completion by the end of 2031. The construction of the offshore converter platform and onshore converter station is part of this timeline.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
