### Evolutionary Timeline of the ROMONTA Facility in Amsdorf, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 20, 2012
     - The GERO Solarpark, a 28MW solar power plant, was inaugurated in Amsdorf. The facility is part of a joint venture between GETEC green energy AG and the ROMONTA group. 
     - The facility has an electrical generating capacity of around 120MW, with 75MW originating from renewable sources. It is designed to cover the annual energy requirements of approximately 8,000 households and save around 15,000 tonnes of carbon emissions annually.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 2012
     - The total investment for the GERO Solarpark project was approximately €50 million. This investment was primarily secured by GETEC green energy AG, allowing for the successful commissioning of the solar power plant.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2012
     - The GERO Solarpark was officially inaugurated, marking the completion of the project which began construction less than seven weeks prior. The project was realized quickly despite the challenging political environment regarding solar power subsidies.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The GERO Solarpark is a joint venture between GETEC green energy AG and the ROMONTA group. Therefore, ROMONTA does not own the facility exclusively; it shares ownership with GETEC.
