### Evolutionary Timeline of the Gasag Facility in Spree-Neiße, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2017**: Gasag, through its subsidiary SpreeGas, completed the construction of a 10 MW solar park in the district of Spree-Neiße. This facility is part of a broader initiative to enhance renewable energy production in the region.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2017**: Gasag announced an investment of approximately €32 million (around $37.7 million) for the development of three solar plants in Brandenburg, which will collectively have a capacity of 40 MW. This investment is aimed at expanding their renewable energy portfolio.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2017**: Gasag confirmed the completion of the 10 MW solar installation in Spree-Neiße and outlined plans for additional solar projects totaling 40 MW over the next two to three years.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2017**: Gasag indicated that local companies would be contracted during all phases of the new solar projects, suggesting potential job creation in the region as a result of these developments.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **August 2017**: The facility in Spree-Neiße is owned by Gasag through its subsidiary SpreeGas, indicating full ownership of the solar park by the company.
