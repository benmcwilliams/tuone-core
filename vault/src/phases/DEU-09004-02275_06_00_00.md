### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hanwha Group Facility in Finsterwalde II, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2010
   - **Details:** The Finsterwalde II facility, along with Finsterwalde III, is part of a solar photovoltaics plant located in Brandenburg, Germany. Together with Finsterwalde I, these plants have a combined capacity of 81 MWp, making it the largest operational solar PV plant in the world at that time. Q-Cells SE, the original developer, continues to manage operations and maintenance under a long-term contract.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** October 2010
   - **Details:** Good Energies and NIBC Infrastructure Partners acquired Finsterwalde II and III from Q-Cells SE. This acquisition is part of a broader strategy to enhance their renewable energy portfolio, although specific investment amounts were not disclosed.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** October 2010
   - **Details:** The acquisition of Finsterwalde II and III was announced, with the expectation of financial closure by the end of 2010. The plants were already fully operational at the time of the acquisition.
