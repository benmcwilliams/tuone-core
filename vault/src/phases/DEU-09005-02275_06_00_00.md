### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hanwha Group Facility in Finsterwalde III, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2010
   - **Details:** The Finsterwalde III facility, along with Finsterwalde II and Finsterwalde I, forms part of the largest operational solar photovoltaic (PV) plant in the world, with a combined capacity of 81 MWp. Q-Cells SE developed and constructed these plants, and they are fully operational. Q-Cells continues to manage operations and maintenance under a long-term contract.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** October 2010
   - **Details:** Good Energies and NIBC Infrastructure Partners acquired Finsterwalde II and Finsterwalde III from Q-Cells SE. This acquisition is part of a broader strategy to build out their renewable energy portfolio, although the specific financial details of the transaction were not disclosed.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** October 2010
   - **Details:** The acquisition of Finsterwalde III was finalized as part of a deal that included Finsterwalde II. The transaction was subject to closing conditions, with financial close expected by the end of 2010.

4. **Labour:**

   - **Details:** The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the acquisition of Finsterwalde III.

5. **Ownership Structure:**

Instead, it is owned by Good Energies and NIBC Infrastructure Partners, indicating a joint ownership structure.
