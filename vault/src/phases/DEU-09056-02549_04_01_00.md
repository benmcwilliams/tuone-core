### Evolutionary Timeline of the VNG Facility in Piesteritz, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 06-12-2024
   - **Details:** VNG, in collaboration with HyCC, announced plans for a 500MW green hydrogen facility named GreenRoot, to be located near the Piesteritz agro-chemical park. The facility aims to be one of Germany's largest green hydrogen production sites, with operations expected to begin in 2029. The project will also explore synergies for delivering heat to the municipal utility company of Lutherstadt Wittenberg.
