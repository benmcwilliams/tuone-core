### Evolutionary Timeline of Hydroshaft GmbH Facility in Loisach River, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: July 2020
   - **Details**: The Hydroshaft GmbH facility, the world’s first ‘shaft hydropower plant’, began operations on the Loisach River. The plant features a turbine concealed in a shaft in the river bed, allowing fish to pass freely during their migration. It has a fall height of 2.5 meters and produces enough electricity for approximately 800 households. The exact generating capacity was not disclosed.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: July 2020
   - **Details**: The development of the facility was part-financed by the German Federal Ministry for Economics and Energy and the Bavarian Environment Ministry. The project was developed by a team at the Technical University of Munich (TUM).

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: Spring 2020
   - **Details**: The facility began supplying electricity to the grid. The concept was proven effective during a flooding event, and numerous prototype tests confirmed that most fish could swim safely over the shaft.

4. **Labour**:

5. **Ownership Structure**:
