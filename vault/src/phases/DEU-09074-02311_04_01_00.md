### Evolutionary Timeline of ITM Power Facility at Leuna Chemical Complex, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** January 2021
   - **Details:** ITM Power announced the sale of a 24 megawatt (MW) electrolyser to be used at Linde’s Leuna Chemical Complex. This electrolyser will produce green hydrogen for Linde’s industrial customers through an existing pipeline network. The facility is set to be the world’s largest proton exchange membrane (PEM) electrolyser plant.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** January 2021
   - **Details:** The electrolyser will be built by ITM Linde Electrolysis GmbH, a joint venture between Linde and ITM Power. This project signifies a substantial investment in green hydrogen technology, although specific financial figures are not provided in the article.
