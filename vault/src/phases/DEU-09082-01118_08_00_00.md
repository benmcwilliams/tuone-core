### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility at Baltic Eagle Offshore Wind Farm, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **October 2024:** The Baltic Eagle offshore wind farm, with a total capacity of 476MW, has completed the installation of 50 wind turbines, each with a capacity of 9.53MW. The facility is expected to provide renewable energy to approximately 475,000 households annually and reduce carbon dioxide emissions by around 800,000 tonnes per year. The project is part of Iberdrola’s Baltic Hub, which will have a total capacity of over 1.1GW once completed by 2026.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **July 2023:** Masdar acquired a 49% stake in the Baltic Eagle project from Iberdrola, valuing the project at nearly €1.6bn. The overall investment for the Baltic Hub, which includes Baltic Eagle, is around €3.7bn.
