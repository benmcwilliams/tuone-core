## DEU-09188-01830

### Evolutionary Timeline of Northland Power's Deutsche Bucht Facility

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2018**: Northland Power's Deutsche Bucht offshore wind farm is set to feature a total of 33 MHI Vestas Offshore Wind V164-8.4MW wind turbines, with 31 on monopiles and 2 on mono bucket foundations. The facility has a total production capacity of 269 MW.
- **July 2019**: The installation of the turbines began in early June, with 21 MHI Vestas 8.4MW machines installed by this date. The final fabrication of the two mono bucket foundations is ongoing.
- **August 2019**: Northland Power completes the installation of 31 MHI Vestas turbines on monopile foundations. The commissioning of these machines is ongoing, with plans to install the two turbines on mono bucket foundations in the fourth quarter.
- **November 2019**: Installation of the first of the two mono bucket foundations is paused due to a technical issue, but this does not affect the 31 turbines already installed.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2018**: The Deutsche Bucht project has a total investment of EUR 1.4 billion. Financial close was reached in July 2018, following the necessary permits from the Federal Maritime and Hydrographic Agency of Germany.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2018**: Financial close on the Deutsche Bucht Mono Bucket pilot demonstrator project is reached.
- **October 2018**: Announcement of the installation of mono bucket foundations, with installation expected to start in the second quarter of 2019.
- **June 2019**: Installation of turbines begins.
- **July 2019**: First power is delivered from the wind farm.
- **August 2019**: Completion of installation of 31 turbines on monopile foundations.
- **November 2019**: Installation of the first mono bucket foundation is paused due to a technical issue.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the Deutsche Bucht facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **October 2018**: The Deutsche Bucht offshore wind farm is owned exclusively by Northland Power, marking it as the company's third North Sea offshore wind farm, alongside Gemini and Nordsee 1.
