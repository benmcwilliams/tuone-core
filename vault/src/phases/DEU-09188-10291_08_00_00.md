#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2018**: Universal Foundation is contracted to assist with the installation of two mono bucket foundations at the Deutsche Bucht offshore wind farm, which has a total capacity of 269 MW. The mono buckets will support MHI Vestas V164-8.4 MW turbines. The project aims to demonstrate the effectiveness of these new foundation structures under commercial operating conditions.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2018**: Financial close on the Deutsche Bucht Mono Bucket pilot demonstrator project was reached after the Federal Maritime and Hydrographic Agency of Germany granted the necessary permit. Universal Foundation is investing heavily in technological standardization and industrialization to enhance the commercial viability of the mono bucket technology.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2018**: Installation of monopile foundations at the Deutsche Bucht site commenced.

- **Late 2019**: The entire Deutsche Bucht wind farm is scheduled for commissioning.
