### Evolutionary Timeline of the Universal Foundation Facility in Borkum, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **November 2019:** The Universal Foundation facility developed the world's first Mono-Bucket units for offshore wind energy. These units are designed to be mated to 8.4MW MHI Vestas V164 turbines as part of the 269MW Deutsche Bucht wind farm located in the North Sea off the island of Borkum. The Mono Bucket concept features a hollow, cylindrical steel base that embeds into the seafloor using suction pressure, gravity, and advanced installation techniques. Each mono-bucket weighs 1,100 tonnes and has a base diameter of 18.5 metres, with skirts penetrating 16.5-18.5 metres into the sandy seabed.
