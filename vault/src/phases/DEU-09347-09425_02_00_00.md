### Technology and Operations
- "The Allach Th2 well was drilled deviated to the north over a length of more than one kilometer at an inclination of around 65 degrees."
- "It had a final depth of 3,104 mMD and 2,110 m TVD, penetrating into the Malmkarst."
- "Based on a short-term pumping tests done over two weeks, the well supports a production rate of about 100 L/s with a very slight pressure drop and a temperature of over 70 °C."
