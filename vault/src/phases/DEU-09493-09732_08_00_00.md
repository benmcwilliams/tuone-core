## DEU-09493-09732

**Evolutionary Timeline of the Petrofac Facility in the German North Sea, DEU**

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **August 2018:** Petrofac is involved in the hook-up and commissioning of TenneT’s 900MW BorWin3 HVDC platform (BorWin gamma). The facility will convert alternating current produced by offshore wind turbines to direct current for transmission to the German national grid via a 160-kilometre cable link. The jacket foundation for the platform was installed in May 2018.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **August 2018:** The project involves collaboration with SeaRenergy, which will provide an integrated Offshore Support Services package to Petrofac. This includes platform management and logistics support during the hook-up and commissioning phases.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **May 2018:** The jacket foundation for the BorWin gamma platform was installed at the site in the German North Sea.
   - **August 2018:** Announcement of the partnership with SeaRenergy for support services during the commissioning of the HVDC platform. The commissioning of the BorWin3 grid connection is expected in 2019.
