**Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Renewables GmbH Facility in German North Sea, DEU**

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **06-01-2022:** RWE Renewables GmbH, in partnership with Northland Power Inc., agreed to co-develop a cluster of offshore wind projects in the German North Sea with a total gross capacity of 1.3 GW. The cluster includes:
     - 433 MW N-3.8 (Nordsee 2)
     - 420 MW N-3.5 (Nordsee 3)
     - 480 MW N-3.6 (Delta Nordsee)
   - The projects are expected to achieve commercial operations between 2026 and 2028.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The joint venture established between RWE and Northland Power aims to unlock synergies in development, construction, and operating costs, enhancing returns for the projects. Specific investment amounts were not detailed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **06-01-2022:** Announcement of the joint venture and co-development agreement for the offshore wind cluster.
   - The auction for the Nordsee 3 and Delta Nordsee leases is scheduled for 2023.

4. **Labour:**
   - The article does not specify job creation or job loss related to the investments or expansions at this facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The joint venture will have RWE holding a 51% interest and Northland Power holding a 49% interest. RWE previously held a 15% share in the special purpose companies (Nordsee Two GmbH and Nordsee Three GmbH) established for the development of Nordsee 2 and Nordsee 3, respectively.
