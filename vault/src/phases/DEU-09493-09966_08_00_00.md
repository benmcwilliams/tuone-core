1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **August 2018:** SeaRenergy is contracted to provide integrated Offshore Support Services for the BorWin3 HVDC platform (BorWin gamma). The facility is involved in the management and support during the hook-up and commissioning phases of the HVDC platform, which is designed to convert alternating current from offshore wind turbines to direct current for transmission to the German national grid.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **August 2018:** The project involves significant infrastructure, including the installation of a jacket foundation for the HVDC platform, which was completed in May 2018. The jacket and pinpiles were fabricated and installed by Heerema Fabrication Group and Heerema Marine Contractors, respectively, under a contract with Petrofac.
