## DEU-09508-01830

### Evolutionary Timeline of Northland Power Facility at Deutsche Bucht Offshore Wind Farm, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-06-2023**: Northland Power initiated a pilot project at the Deutsche Bucht Offshore Wind Farm, utilizing autonomous marine survey technologies. The facility, with a production capacity of 252 MW, will employ Subsea Europe Services' Autonomous Surveyor Uncrewed Surface Vessel (USV) and A.IKANBILIS Hovering Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (HAUV) for offshore wind foundation surveys and inspections. The pilot project aims to optimize operations and maintenance (O&M) strategies with minimal human involvement.

- **24-08-2023**: The pilot project concluded successfully, demonstrating the effectiveness of the "mothership concept" using the Service Operations Vessel (SOV) for deploying the USV and HAUV. The integration of these technologies allowed for enhanced operational workflows and data acquisition capabilities, with full commercial readiness for a combined USV/HAUV solution expected by Q1 2024.
