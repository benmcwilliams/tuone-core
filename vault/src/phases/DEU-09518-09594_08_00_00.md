   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** The Bajgora wind park is a 105 MW facility consisting of 27 turbines supplied by General Electric. The project is designed to significantly contribute to Kosovo's renewable energy capacity, equating to one-tenth of the country's domestic power capacity. The wind park is expected to have a lifespan of 25 years and aims to save approximately 247,000 tons of CO2 emissions annually.

   - **Date:** July 2020
