### Evolutionary Timeline of the OPTERRA Facility in Karsdorf, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2022**: OPTERRA, a leading cement manufacturer in Germany, signed a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with Statkraft to receive an additional 300GWh of renewable power from onshore wind and solar sources. This agreement allows OPTERRA to transition its plant operations in Karsdorf (Saxony-Anhalt) to a more renewable energy supply. The PPA includes the provision of 15GWh of wind power from April 2022 to June 2023, and from July 2023 to December 2032, around 30GWh of solar power will be supplied annually. This renewable power will cover up to 20% of the annual electricity demand for the Karsdorf and Wössingen cement plants.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2022**: The PPA with Statkraft represents a significant investment in renewable energy for OPTERRA, supporting its sustainability goals and reducing CO2 emissions. The wind power is sourced from projects that no longer receive EEG subsidies, ensuring the continued operation of these wind farms.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2022**: Announcement of the new PPA with Statkraft, marking a significant step in OPTERRA's commitment to sustainability and renewable energy.
- **December 2021**: Prior to the April 2022 announcement, OPTERRA had already signed a renewable power PPA with Statkraft for a term of four years (2022 to 2025) with a total supply volume of 120GWh of wind power.
