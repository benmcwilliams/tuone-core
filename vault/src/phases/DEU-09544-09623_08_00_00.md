### Evolutionary Timeline of the OPTERRA Facility in Wössingen, DEU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2022**: OPTERRA signed a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with Statkraft to receive an additional 300GWh of renewable power from onshore wind and solar sources. This agreement allows OPTERRA to transition its plant operations to a renewable energy supply. The facility will receive 15GWh of wind power from April 2022 to June 2023, and from July 2023 to December 2032, around 30GWh of solar power annually. This renewable power will cover up to 20% of the annual electricity demand for the Wössingen cement plant.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2022**: The PPA with Statkraft represents a significant investment in renewable energy for OPTERRA, supporting its sustainability goals and reducing CO2 emissions. The wind power is sourced from projects that no longer receive EEG subsidies, ensuring the continued operation of these wind farms.

- **April 2022**: Announcement of the new PPA with Statkraft, marking a step forward in OPTERRA's commitment to sustainability and renewable energy.
