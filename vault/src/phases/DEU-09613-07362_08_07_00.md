### Evolutionary Timeline of Prysmian Group Facility in Nordenham, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-09-2021**: Prysmian Group's facility in Nordenham is identified as a center of excellence for producing submarine cables. The facility will manufacture submarine cables for the Gruissan floating offshore wind farm in France, including a 25 km 66 kV export cable and additional onshore cables.
- **05-11-2021**: The facility is also designated to produce 320 km of three-core 66 kV offshore array cables for the Coastal Virginia Offshore Wind project in the US.
- **03-07-2023**: The facility is set to produce the armouring and assembling of approximately 280 km of 66 kV inter-array cables for Ørsted’s Hornsea 3 offshore wind farm in the UK.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **23-03-2022**: Prysmian Group announces a €1 billion investment plan aimed at supporting energy transition projects, which includes significant investments in expanding production capacity and technological equipment at key excellence centers, including the Nordenham facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07-09-2021**: Announcement of a €30 million contract for the Gruissan floating offshore wind farm, with production scheduled to start at the Nordenham facility.
- **05-11-2021**: Confirmation of the facility's role in supplying cables for the Coastal Virginia Offshore Wind project, with production expected to be completed by 2026.
- **03-07-2023**: Prysmian Group is awarded a contract for the Hornsea 3 offshore wind farm, with production of inter-array cables scheduled for delivery in 2026.
