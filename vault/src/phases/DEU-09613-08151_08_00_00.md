### Evolutionary Timeline of the Van Oord Facility in Nordenham, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** March 2018
     - Van Oord initiated cable transfer operations for the Borkum Riffgrund 2 offshore wind project at the Nordenham facility. The operations involved spooling 36 cable reels and four static tanks of array cable onto Van Oord’s cable laying vessel, Nexus. The Borkum Riffgrund 2 project is designed to have a production capacity of 450MW, utilizing 56 MHI Vestas 8MW turbines, with full commissioning expected in 2019.

   - **Date:** August 2017 (noted for context)
