### Evolutionary Timeline of the SRU Solar Facility in Eisleben, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2012
   - **Details:** SRU Solar, in collaboration with Parabel, completed a 29MW solar farm on a 100ha site in Eisleben. The facility utilizes over 121,000 polycrystalline solar modules from Trina and 1,400 inverters from Siemens and Danfoss. The plant is capable of producing enough electricity to power approximately 7,500 homes, meeting the energy demands of Eisleben.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** October 2012
   - **Details:** The total investment for the solar plant was €40 million. This investment was crucial for the realization of the project, especially after initial uncertainties related to reduced subsidization.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
