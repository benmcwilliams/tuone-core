### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in Epe, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2023:** RWE proposed the construction of the first hydrogen cavern storage facility in a re-purposed salt mine at Kottiger Hook near Epe. The facility will repurpose an existing natural gas cavern for hydrogen storage. Each of the two planned caverns will have a capacity of up to 28 million cubic meters of hydrogen. The project aims to complete construction by the end of 2026 and begin hydrogen supply by 2027.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **February 2023:** RWE has submitted a bid for funding under the EU’s Important Projects of Common European Interest scheme, which, if approved, could lead to additional funding from the Federal Ministry of Economics and Climate Protection and the state of North Rhine-Westphalia. However, funding for the project is still pending approval.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **April 2022:** RWE started the approval process for the facility, consulting various stakeholders.
   - **October 2022:** RWE announced the addition of a second cavern to the facility, contrary to original plans.
   - **February 2023:** RWE submitted an operating plan to the Arnsberg district government, including an environmental impact assessment for the facility. A meeting with local stakeholders is scheduled for March 28, 2023.
