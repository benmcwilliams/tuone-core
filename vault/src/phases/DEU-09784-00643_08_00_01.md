### Evolutionary Timeline of the RWE Facility in Kaskasi, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2022**: The Kaskasi offshore wind farm, with a capacity of 342 MW, began construction. The installation of the 1,400-tonne offshore substation topside was completed, marking a significant milestone in the project. The facility will utilize innovative technologies, including vibro pile driving to reduce underwater noise emissions and recyclable rotor blades from Siemens Gamesa.
- **March 2023**: RWE commenced operations of the Kaskasi offshore wind farm, which consists of 38 wind turbines. The facility has the potential to supply power to over 400,000 households. The turbines were successively commissioned over four months, with the last ones operational by the end of 2022.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2022**: RWE raised a €1 billion debt package to finance the Kaskasi project, among others. This funding was provided by BayernLB and Denmark’s Export Credit Agency EKF, aimed at replacing existing financing and supporting the construction of the wind farm.
- **March 2023**: The total investment in the Kaskasi wind farm was reported to be nearly €800 million.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2022**: Installation of the offshore substation topside was completed.
- **December 2022**: The Kaskasi wind farm was officially put into service, with all 38 turbines delivering clean energy.
- **March 2023**: The facility was inaugurated in the presence of the German Federal Minister for Economic Affairs and Climate Action, marking its official operational status.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2023**: The Kaskasi wind farm's operation is expected to contribute to job creation in the renewable energy sector, although specific job numbers related to this facility were not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **March 2022 - March 2023**: RWE is the sole owner and developer of the Kaskasi offshore wind farm, as indicated by the consistent references to RWE's involvement in all aspects of the project.
