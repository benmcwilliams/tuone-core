### Evolutionary Timeline of the Siemens Gamesa Facility in Kaskasi, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-04-2020**: Siemens Gamesa received a firm order from innogy SE to supply 38 SG 8.0-167 DD Flex wind turbines for the Kaskasi offshore wind power plant, with a total capacity of 342 MW. Each turbine has a capacity of up to 9 MW and features a rotor diameter of 167 meters. The installation work for the turbines is scheduled to begin in Q3 of 2022.
