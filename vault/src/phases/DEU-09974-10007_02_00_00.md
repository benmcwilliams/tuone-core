### Evolutionary Timeline of the Silenos Energy Facility in Bruck, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2020
   - **Details:** The Silenos Energy facility in Bruck is a geothermal power and heat project. It is designed to have an installed power generation capacity of 4.31 MW and a thermal capacity of 6.88 MW for district heating. The facility aims to supply heat to the nearby municipality of Garching an der Alz, meeting the demand of up to 14,000 households.
