### Evolutionary Timeline of Smartenergy Renewables Deutschland Facility in Luckenwald, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **November 2013:** Smartenergy Renewables Deutschland was relaunched, focusing on building integrated PV (BIPV) modules and becoming an independent power producer. The facility produces utility cSi modules and BIPV modules. The utility modules have an output of between 290Wp and 300Wp and are initially used for Smartenergy's own PV projects. The BIPV modules utilize high-efficiency mono-cSi technology, designed for compatibility with various roof types and facades.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
