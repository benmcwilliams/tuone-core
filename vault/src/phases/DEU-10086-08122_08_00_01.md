1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2022:** TenneT is involved in the BorWin6 offshore grid connection project, which will link 980 MW of offshore wind capacity to the German grid. The project includes the construction of offshore and onshore converter stations, with the offshore converter station named BorWin kappa. The project will utilize 66 kV three-phase cables to connect offshore wind turbines directly to the converter platform, eliminating the need for multiple transformer stations. The project is set to go into operation in 2027, with an increased capacity of 980 MW from the initially planned 930 MW.
