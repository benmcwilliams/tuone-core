### Evolutionary Timeline of the Trianel Erneuerbare Energien Facility in Buchenau, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **January 2017:** The Trianel Erneuerbare Energien facility commissioned the 17.25MW Buchenau wind farm in Hesse, Germany. The wind farm consists of five Vestas V126 3.45MW turbines with a tip height of 200 meters. It is expected to generate approximately 46 million kilowatt hours (kWh) of electricity annually.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **January 2017:** The Buchenau wind farm was developed by ABO Wind and acquired by Trianel in August 2016. The acquisition is part of Trianel's broader strategy to expand its renewables portfolio, which also includes a 6MW solar photovoltaic plant in Uchtdorf, Saxony-Anhalt.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **January 2017:** The Buchenau wind farm was officially commissioned. This project contributes to Trianel's goal of operating at least 275MW of wind and solar plants across Germany by 2018.
