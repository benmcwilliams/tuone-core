### Evolutionary Timeline of the Uniper Facility in Elsfleth, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2023**: Uniper announced plans to build two solar power plants with a combined capacity of 317 MW near its Energy Transformation Hub in Lower Saxony, Germany. The plants will consist of a 300 MW facility and a 17 MW facility, designed to supply electricity to a planned green hydrogen production facility at the hub. The hydrogen will be stored in dedicated caverns at the same location.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2023**: Uniper aims to invest more than €8 billion ($8.68 billion) between 2023 and 2030 in power generation assets, including solar and wind. This investment is part of a broader strategy to transition to carbon-free energy generation.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2023**: The project received a development decision from the municipality of Elsfleth, marking the formal beginning of the urban land use plan process for the largest solar project (300 MW). The necessary legal framework will be established through the development plan and amendments to the land use plan.
