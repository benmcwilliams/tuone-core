### Evolutionary Timeline of the Vattenfall Facility in Borkum Island, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **06-12-2023:** Vattenfall announced plans for two offshore wind farms, Nordlicht 1 and Nordlicht 2, with a total installed capacity of 1,610 MW. Nordlicht 1 will have a capacity of 980 MW, while Nordlicht 2 will have a capacity of 630 MW. These wind farms are expected to produce enough electricity to meet the annual requirements of over 1.6 million average German households.
