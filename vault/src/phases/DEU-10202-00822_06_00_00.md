### Evolutionary Timeline of the Vattenfall Facility in Tützpatz, Germany

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: Vattenfall announced plans for a 76MW agrivoltaics (agriPV) project in Tützpatz, Germany, which will utilize bifacial solar modules and agricultural practices over 95 hectares. This project marks Vattenfall's first commercial-scale agriPV initiative.
- **September 2023**: The project capacity was updated to 79MW, and construction officially began. The facility will be the largest agriPV plant in Germany upon completion, covering 93 hectares. The project aims to combine sustainable agriculture with solar power generation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2023**: Vattenfall made a final investment decision to proceed with the Tützpatz project, which is being developed without state support and on a commercial scale.
- **July 2023**: Vattenfall acquired a 4GW solar PV portfolio in Germany, increasing its total solar development portfolio in the country to 7GW, indicating a significant investment in expanding its solar capabilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2023**: Announcement of the Tützpatz agrivoltaics project and plans for construction in early summer 2023.
- **July 2023**: Confirmation that construction of the Tützpatz project is expected to start in summer 2023.
- **September 2023**: Construction officially commenced at the Tützpatz facility, with plans to commission the plant in 2024.
