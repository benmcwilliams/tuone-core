### Evolutionary Timeline of the Vulcan Energy CLEOP Facility, Germany

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Production:** The CLEOP facility produces lithium hydroxide from geothermal brine.
   - **Production Capacity:** The facility is part of Vulcan's Phase One commercial plant, designated Lionheart, which will supply approximately 24,000 tonnes per annum of lithium hydroxide, sufficient for around 500,000 electric vehicles (EVs) annually.
   - **Updates:** The CLEOP facility complements the upstream Lithium Extraction Optimization Plant (LEOP) in Landau, which was officially opened in late 2023. Both facilities utilize Adsorption-type Direct Lithium Extraction (A-DLE) technology and aim to exclude fossil fuels in their operations.
