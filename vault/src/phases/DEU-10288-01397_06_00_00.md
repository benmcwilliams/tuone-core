### Evolutionary Timeline of the juwi Facility at Agnew Gold Mine, DEU

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **May 2020**: The Agnew Hybrid Renewable Power Station was completed, marking it as Australia's largest hybrid microgrid. The facility integrates wind, solar, gas, and diesel generation along with battery storage. The project includes:
     - An 18 MW wind farm
     - A 10,710-panel solar plant generating 4 MW
     - A 13 MW/4 MWh battery system
     - An off-grid 21 MW gas/diesel engine power plant
   - The facility is capable of covering up to 70% of the mine's power requirements with renewable energy under favorable weather conditions.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - The project received a backing of **$13.5 million** from the Australian Renewable Energy Agency (ARENA) to support its development.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **November 2019**: The first stage of the project, a 23 MW power station integrating solar with gas and diesel generation, powered up.
   - **May 2020**: All five wind turbines were successfully integrated and operational.

5. **Ownership Structure**
   - EDL was contracted to design, construct, own, and operate the microgrid under a **10-year agreement** with Gold Fields. The project was delivered in partnership with **juwi**, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by a single entity.
