### Evolutionary Timeline of the European Energy Facility in Brussels, DNK

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **11-10-2019**: The European Energy facility is involved in the "Silver Frog" project, which aims to produce green hydrogen using a proposed 2 GW solar module factory. The facility will support the deployment of 10 GW of renewable energy generation capacity, including wind power, to produce 100% green hydrogen. The project anticipates producing approximately 800,000 tons of green hydrogen over eight years.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **11-10-2019**: The project seeks backing from the EU as one of the Important Projects of Common European Interest, which would provide significant funding opportunities. The emphasis on developing new European production capacities for solar cells and modules is a key aspect of the proposal.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **11-10-2019**: The "Silver Frog" project is pitched at the Hydrogen for Climate Conference in Brussels, with the intention of securing designated status from the European Commission in the following year. The project aims to decarbonize heavy industries such as chemicals and steelmaking.

#### 4. Labour
