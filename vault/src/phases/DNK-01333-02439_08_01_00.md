### Evolutionary Timeline of Siemens Gamesa Facility in Aalborg, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2018**: Siemens Gamesa's blades for the Coastal Virginia Offshore Wind project are produced at the Aalborg facility. The project involves two 6-MW SWT-6.0-154 wind turbines.
- **02/2019**: Initial turbine blades for the 180MW Formosa 1 phase 2 project are cast at the Aalborg facility, ready for installation in 2019.
- **09/2019**: Construction begins on the world’s largest wind turbine blade test stand at the Aalborg site, aimed at testing future generations of rotor blades.
- **08/2020**: The blade test center in Aalborg becomes the first non-commercial in-house facility to gain IECRE approval, enhancing its credibility in testing wind turbine components.
- **09/2021**: Siemens Gamesa launches the world’s first recyclable wind turbine blade, produced at the Aalborg facility, marking a significant innovation in sustainability.
- **06/2023**: Blades for the Hollandse Kust Zuid offshore wind farm are produced at the Aalborg facility, contributing to the world's first subsidy-free offshore wind farm.

- **08/2020**: The investment in the test stand is noted to represent additional savings for Siemens Gamesa’s clients, indicating a strategic investment in R&D.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones

- **08/2020**: The blade test center receives IECRE approval, marking a milestone in its operational capabilities.
