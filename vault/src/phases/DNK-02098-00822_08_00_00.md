### Evolutionary Timeline of Vattenfall Facility in Esbjerg, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2021**: Vattenfall announces plans to build a new warehouse for wind turbines at the Port of Esbjerg. The facility will include a 2,100 square meter indoor warehouse and an 8,200 square meter outdoor storage area. It will serve as a central hub for spare parts for Vattenfall's wind farms in the UK, Scandinavia, and Northern Europe. The warehouse will stock critical components such as gearboxes, generators, transformers, shafts, blades, array cables, and switchgear.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2021**: Thyboron Invest is contracted to construct the warehouse. The investment is part of Vattenfall's strategy to enhance operational efficiency and support the growth of wind farms across Northern Europe.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **End of June 2021**: Construction of the warehouse begins.
- **June 2022**: The warehouse is expected to be operational, marking the completion of the construction phase.
