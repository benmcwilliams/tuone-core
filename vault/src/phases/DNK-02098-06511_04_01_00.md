## DNK-02098-06511

### Evolutionary Timeline of H2 Energy Esbjerg Facility in Esbjerg, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Capacity**: The facility is designed to produce up to **90,000 tons of hydrogen per year**.
- **Technology**: The plant will convert renewable electricity from offshore wind into **green hydrogen** to support the decarbonization of heavy industry and road transportation.
- **Scheduled Operations**: The facility is set to start operations in **2027**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Amount**: H2 Energy Esbjerg, a joint venture of Trafigura and H2 Energy, intends to invest around **1 billion euros** in the hydrogen production scheme.
- **Contract Award**: ABB has been contracted for electrical engineering services, although the value of this contract was not disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Announcement**: The project was publicly announced in **October 2023**.
- **Construction Start**: Specific construction start dates are not mentioned, but the project is actively moving forward with engineering services being booked in the **third quarter of 2023**.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The project is expected to create up to **60 permanent jobs** upon completion.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: The facility is a **joint venture** between Trafigura and H2 Energy, indicating that it is not exclusively owned by a single entity.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the H2 Energy Esbjerg facility, highlighting its significance in the clean technology landscape in Denmark.
