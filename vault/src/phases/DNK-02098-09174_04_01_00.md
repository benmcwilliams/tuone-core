### HØST PtX Facility Evolution Timeline - Esbjerg, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 12-12-2024
   - **Details:** HØST PtX Esbjerg has received environmental approvals to install up to 1 GW of electrolyser capacity. The facility aims to generate sustainable green fuels, primarily hydrogen. This marks a significant step in the development of large-scale Power-to-X projects in Denmark.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 12-12-2024
   - **Details:** The project is moving towards a final investment decision (FID) to begin construction. The facility's development is contingent on securing continued support from national agencies and transmission system operators (TSOs).

   - **Details:** The facility has achieved a major milestone by obtaining environmental approvals. It has also secured large areas of land for the future plant, access to green power through a grid connection agreement with Energinet, and a water supply from a nearby wastewater treatment plant operated by DIN Forsyning.
