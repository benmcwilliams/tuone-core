### Evolutionary Timeline of the Better Energy Facility in Holstebro, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2020**: Better Energy is developing a solar project in Holstebro with a planned generation capacity of **206 MW**. This facility is part of a larger initiative in the Nissum Fjord area, which aims to host up to **400 MW** of solar generation capacity.
- **August 2020**: Construction begins on a **200 MW** photovoltaic project, which will be the largest subsidy-free solar plant in northern Europe upon completion. The facility is designed to be directly connected to the Danish electricity transmission network, marking a significant technological advancement.
- **June 2021**: The facility is expected to be operational by mid-2021, with half of its output dedicated to supplying electricity to Bestseller, a subsidiary of Heartland, covering 100% of its electricity demand.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2020**: The solar projects in the Nissum Fjord area, including the one in Holstebro, are noted to be **unsubsidized**, indicating a shift towards market-driven renewable energy projects in Denmark.
- **August 2020**: The project is highlighted as groundbreaking due to its subsidy-free nature and its direct connection to the transmission grid, which is a significant investment in Denmark's renewable energy infrastructure.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2020**: Announcement of the 206 MW solar project in Holstebro.
- **August 2020**: Construction of the 200 MW solar plant begins.
- **Mid-2021**: The facility is scheduled to be operational, marking a significant milestone in Denmark's renewable energy landscape.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2020**: While specific job creation figures are not provided, the construction of the facility is expected to contribute to local employment opportunities during the building phase, as is typical for large-scale renewable energy projects.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **August 2020**: The facility is owned and operated by **Heartland**, a unit of the Danish clothing company **Bestseller**. Better Energy is responsible for the construction and development of the solar plant, indicating a partnership rather than exclusive ownership by Better Energy.
