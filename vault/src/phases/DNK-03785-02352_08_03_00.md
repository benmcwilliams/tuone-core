### Evolutionary Timeline of the MHI Vestas Facility in Odense, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2021**: Vestas announced the installation of the V236-15.0MW offshore prototype wind turbine at the Osterild national test center. The turbine, which will be the tallest and most powerful in the world, has a production output of 80 gigawatt-hours per year. The nacelle for this turbine will be developed and assembled at the Lindo Port facility in Odense.
- **October 2022**: Vestas confirmed that the Lindo Port facility in Odense will also produce nacelles for the V236-15.0MW turbine, alongside the ongoing development and manufacturing of the prototype and test blades at the Nakskov facility.
