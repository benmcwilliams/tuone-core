### Evolutionary Timeline of the Belectric Facility in Ringkøbing, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** January 2022
   - **Details:** Belectric is constructing a solar park in Ringkøbing with a production capacity of 12.6 MWp. The facility will utilize more than 23,000 bifacial modules mounted on single-axis trackers, which allow for solar radiation absorption from both sides of the module, promising low electricity production costs.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** January 2022
   - **Details:** The project is developed on behalf of Encavis AG and is part of a strategic development pipeline of over 500 MW of solar power projects in Denmark. This facility is noted as the first zero subsidy solar park under a partnership agreement with Encavis.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** January 2022
   - **Details:** The construction of the solar park has commenced, with expectations to begin delivering green energy in the second half of 2022.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
