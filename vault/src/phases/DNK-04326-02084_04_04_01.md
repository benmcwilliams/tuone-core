### Evolutionary Timeline of the Everfuel Facility in Fredericia, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2021**: Everfuel announced the construction of Europe’s largest electrolyser, the HySynergy Phase I, with a capacity of 20 MW, aimed at producing approximately 8 tonnes of green hydrogen per day and featuring 10 tonnes of storage capacity. The facility is designed to contribute to reducing carbon emissions in Europe.
- **December 2022**: Everfuel confirmed that the HySynergy facility had started hydrogen production from the first set of electrolyzer stacks. The company plans to commence commercial production in the second quarter of 2023 and aims to develop three additional 100 MW sections by the end of 2025, ultimately increasing the facility's capacity to 1 GW by 2030.
