### Evolutionary Timeline of the Energinet Elsystemansvar Facility in Fredericia, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: November 2020
   - **Details**: The facility is part of the H2RES project, which aims to produce and store renewable hydrogen (H2) using offshore wind power. The project includes the construction of a 2-MW electrolysis plant with hydrogen storage. The expected production capacity is approximately 600 kg of hydrogen per day, sufficient to fuel 20 to 30 buses and test its use in trucks and taxis.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Date**: November 2020
   - **Details**: The H2RES project received funding of Dkr34.6 million (approximately US$5.2 million) from the Energy Technology Development and Demonstration Programme (EUDP) to support the development of the electrolysis plant and hydrogen storage.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Date**: November 2020
   - **Details**: The H2RES project was announced, with plans to utilize energy from Ørsted’s Siemens Gamesa 3.6-MW offshore wind turbines at the Avedøre Power Station for hydrogen production. The project aims to demonstrate the technology and gain experience for future scaling.
