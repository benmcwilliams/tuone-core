### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hydrogen Denmark Facility in Fredericia, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: 05-11-2020
   - **Details**: The Hydrogen Denmark facility, part of the H2RES project, is set to produce renewable hydrogen (H2) using offshore wind power. The facility will feature a 2-MW electrolysis plant with hydrogen storage, aiming to generate approximately 600 kg of hydrogen daily. This production capacity is intended to support road transport applications, including buses, trucks, and potentially taxis.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Date**: 05-11-2020
   - **Details**: The project received funding of Dkr34.6 million (approximately US$5.2 million) from the Energy Technology Development and Demonstration Programme (EUDP) to support the development of the facility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Date**: 05-11-2020
   - **Details**: The H2RES project was officially announced, detailing the construction of the electrolysis plant and the integration of energy from Ørsted’s offshore wind turbines at the Avedøre Power Station. The project aims to demonstrate the technology and gain experience for future scaling.

4. **Labour**
