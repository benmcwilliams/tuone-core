## DNK-04326-06511

### Evolutionary Timeline of H2 Energy Esbjerg Facility in Fredericia, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 11-10-2023
   - **Details:** The H2 Energy Esbjerg facility is set to produce up to 90,000 tons of green hydrogen per year. The facility will convert renewable electricity from offshore wind into hydrogen, supporting the decarbonization of heavy industry and road transportation. The plant is part of a 1GW hydrogen scheme, one of the largest in Europe, and is scheduled to start operations in 2027.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 11-10-2023
   - **Details:** The project is backed by a joint venture of Trafigura and H2 Energy, with an intended investment of around 1 billion euros. ABB has been contracted for electrical engineering work, although the specific value of this contract was not disclosed.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 11-10-2023
   - **Details:** The facility is currently in the development phase, with operations expected to commence in 2027. ABB's involvement indicates progress in the engineering and infrastructure setup for the hydrogen production facility.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 11-10-2023
   - **Details:** The project is expected to create up to 60 permanent jobs upon completion, contributing to local employment and supporting the green energy sector.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 11-10-2023
   - **Details:** The facility is a joint venture between Trafigura and H2 Energy, indicating that it is not exclusively owned by a single company but rather shared between the two entities.
