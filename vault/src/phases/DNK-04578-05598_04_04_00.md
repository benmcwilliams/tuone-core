### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mitsui Facility in Kassø, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Production Capacity:** The facility will produce 42,000 tonnes of e-methanol annually.
   - **Operations:** The facility utilizes renewable energy to generate hydrogen and captured CO₂ to synthesize low-carbon methanol. The electrolyser has a capacity of 52MW and is expected to be installed later this year (2023).
   - **Additional Benefits:** The facility will generate excess heat for district heating, benefiting around 2,500 local households.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Ownership Stake:** Mitsui has acquired a 49% stake in Kassø Midco, the owner of the facility, from European Energy. This acquisition was completed after securing necessary clearances from Danish and European authorities.
   - **Partnership Development:** Mitsui and European Energy are collaborating to develop sustainable e-methanol projects based on renewable energy.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Construction Status:** The e-methanol facility is currently under construction and is expected to be operational in the first half of 2024.
   - **Solar Plant Operation:** The Kassø solar farm, part of the facility, has been operational since the first half of 2023.
