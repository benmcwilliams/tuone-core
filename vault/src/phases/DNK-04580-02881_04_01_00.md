### Evolutionary Timeline of the European Energy Facility in Padborg, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: The facility in Padborg is involved in the production of green hydrogen through Power-to-X (PtX) technology, which converts renewable energy into hydrogen and fuels.
- **Capacity**: The facility is part of a larger initiative where a total capacity of 280 MW is being developed across six projects, with the aim of contributing to Denmark's goal of achieving 4-6 GW of electrolysis capacity by 2030.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Awarded**: On November 1, 2023, it was announced that the Padborg facility would receive more than DKK 910 million (approximately $155 million) as part of a Danish government tender aimed at supporting green hydrogen projects. This investment is part of a broader budget of DKK 1.32 billion ($190 million) allocated for the tender.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Tender Announcement**: The tender for the PtX projects was opened in April 2023, with the results announced on November 1, 2023. The Padborg facility was confirmed as one of the winners of this tender.
- **Contract Signing**: The Danish Energy Agency indicated that the decision regarding Plug Power's bid would not affect the timeline for contract signing with the winning projects, including the Padborg facility.
