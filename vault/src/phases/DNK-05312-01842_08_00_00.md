### Evolutionary Timeline of Ørsted Facility in Idomlund, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06/2022**: Ørsted and Skovgaard Energy signed a letter of intent to develop a multi-phase Power-to-X facility in Idomlund. The initial phase will have an expected electrolysis capacity of **150 MW**, powered by onshore wind and solar PV. The facility has the potential to expand to more than **3 GW** of electrolysis capacity in the future, leveraging offshore wind resources.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06/2022**: The project is part of Denmark's broader ambition to establish **4-6 GW** of electrolysis capacity by **2030**. The facility is strategically located to tap into the future offshore wind capacity in the North Sea, which is expected to support significant scaling of the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06/2022**: Announcement of the partnership between Ørsted and Skovgaard Energy to develop the Power-to-X facility in Idomlund. The project is described as having the potential for significant growth, contingent on the establishment of necessary offshore wind capacity and hydrogen infrastructure.
