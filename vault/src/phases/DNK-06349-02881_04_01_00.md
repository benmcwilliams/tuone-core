### Evolutionary Timeline of the European Energy Facility in Kasso, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2022**: European Energy announced plans for a Power-to-X (PtX) facility in Kasso, which will be the largest e-methanol facility in the world. - **July 2023**: The e-methanol plant is projected to produce up to 42,000 tons of e-methanol annually when operational in 2024.
