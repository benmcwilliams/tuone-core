### Evolutionary Timeline of the Green Hydrogen Systems Facility at Avedøre Power Station, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** January 2021
   - **Details:** Green Hydrogen Systems (GHS) is supplying electrolysis technology for the H2RES offshore wind-to-hydrogen demonstration project at Avedøre Power Station. The facility will feature a 2 MW electrolysis plant powered by two 3.6 MW offshore wind turbines. The expected production capacity is around 1,000 kilograms of green hydrogen per day, intended for road transport in Greater Copenhagen and Zealand.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2019
   - **Details:** The H2RES project received DKK 34.6 million (EUR 4.6 million) in funding from the Energy Technology Development and Demonstration Programme (EUDP) under the Danish Energy Agency, supporting the development of the facility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** January 2021
   - **Details:** GHS announced that the H2RES plant is expected to start production in late 2021 following Ørsted's final investment decision (FID) for the project.
