### Evolutionary Timeline of HOFOR Facility at Avedøre Power Station, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2021**: HOFOR, in collaboration with Ørsted, announced plans for the **Green Fuels for Denmark** project, which aims to produce sustainable fuels with a total electrolysis capacity of **1.3 GW**. The project will utilize power from HOFOR’s **250 MW Aflandshage offshore wind farm**, located in the Oresund Strait. The offshore wind farm's substation will be placed at the Avedøre Power Station, facilitating the integration of renewable energy into the sustainable fuel production process.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2021**: The partnership between HOFOR and Ørsted is aimed at securing investments and subsidies to support the development of the Green Fuels for Denmark project. The project is expected to require a framework that promotes sustainable fuel production, which will involve collaboration with government entities to create incentives for private investments.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2021**: HOFOR and Ørsted entered into an agreement to secure green power for the Green Fuels for Denmark project. The Aflandshage offshore wind farm is currently under development, with expectations to deliver first power by **2024/2025**. The project is structured in three phases, with the first phase targeting **10 MW**, followed by **250 MW**, and ultimately reaching **1,300 MW** in total electrolysis capacity.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2021**: While specific job creation or loss figures were not detailed in the articles, the project is anticipated to contribute to job creation in the renewable energy sector, particularly in the development and operation of the offshore wind farm and the sustainable fuel production facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2021**: The Avedøre Power Station is owned by Ørsted. HOFOR is collaborating with Ørsted on the Green Fuels for Denmark project, indicating a partnership rather than exclusive ownership of the facility by HOFOR. The agreement suggests a joint effort to leverage resources and expertise for the successful implementation of the project.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic initiatives related to the HOFOR facility at Avedøre Power Station, highlighting its role in advancing Denmark's sustainable energy goals.
