### Evolutionary Timeline of Ørsted Facility in Avedøre Holme, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** January 2021
   - **Details:** Ørsted has taken a final investment decision (FID) on its first renewable hydrogen project, named H2RES, located at Avedøre Holme. The facility will utilize nearby offshore wind energy to produce green hydrogen. It will have an installed capacity of 2 megawatts, enabling the production of 1,000 kg of renewable hydrogen, which is intended for use in road transport in Greater Copenhagen and Zealand. The facility is powered by two nearby 3.6 MW offshore wind turbines.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** January 2021
   - **Details:** The project is supported by public co-funding, which Ørsted emphasizes as crucial for driving the decarbonization of the transport sector. The investment is part of Ørsted's broader strategy to contribute to Denmark's decarbonization goals.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
