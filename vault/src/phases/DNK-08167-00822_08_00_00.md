## DNK-08167-00822

### Evolutionary Timeline of Vattenfall's Kriegers Flak Facility, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **29-11-2017**: Vattenfall's Kriegers Flak project is announced as a 600MW offshore wind farm located in the Baltic Sea, consisting of two sections: KFA (200MW) and KFB (400MW). The project will feature 72 Siemens Gamesa turbines, each with a capacity of 8.4MW.
- **20-12-2018**: The final investment decision is made for the Kriegers Flak project, confirming its capacity at 604.8MW and expected full operation by the end of 2021.
- **07-06-2021**: The installation of all 72 wind turbines is completed, marking Kriegers Flak as Denmark's largest offshore wind farm, capable of covering the annual electricity consumption of approximately 600,000 Danish households.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **20-12-2018**: The total investment for the Kriegers Flak project is reported to be €1.7 billion, which includes the combined capacity of Kriegers Flak and Vattenfall’s other projects, Vesterhav Syd & Nord.
- **13-07-2018**: A long-term Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) is signed with Novo Nordisk and Novozymes, covering about 20% of the wind farm's production, set to begin in January 2020.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **29-11-2017**: Announcement of the project and contract awarded to Tekmar Energy for cable protection components.
- **20-12-2018**: Final investment decision made, confirming the project’s capacity and timeline.
- **19-03-2019**: JDR Cables confirms a contract to supply 170km of array cables for the project.
- **23-05-2019**: EEW SPC completes the first monopile for the wind farm's foundations.
- **11-05-2020**: Van Oord's heavy lift vessel arrives at the site to begin foundation installation.
- **07-06-2021**: Completion of turbine installation, with final testing and certification planned before inauguration.

#### 4. Labour
- **13-07-2018**: The PPA with Novo Nordisk and Novozymes is noted to support sustainability and job creation in Denmark, although specific job numbers are not provided.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Vattenfall owns the Kriegers Flak facility exclusively, as indicated by the contracts and agreements made directly with suppliers and partners for the project.
