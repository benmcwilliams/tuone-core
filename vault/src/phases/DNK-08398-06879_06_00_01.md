### Evolutionary Timeline of the European Energy A/S Facility in Vandel/Billund, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **2015:** European Energy A/S completed the Vandel 1 solar project, which became Denmark’s largest PV plant at that time. The installation consists of 126 individual solar plants with a total capacity of 37.9 MW, built on a former NATO military base covering 108 hectares. The project utilized 1,008 string inverters provided by Delta, a Taiwanese power electronics manufacturer.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **2015:** The Vandel 1 project was financed by HSH Nordbank with €64.5 million ($69.2 million).
   - **April 2017:** HSH Nordbank announced it would finance the Vandel 2 project with €23 million ($24.6 million). This project is part of a broader installation that includes the previously completed Vandel 1 project.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2015:** The Vandel 1 project was sold by European Energy A/S to a fund managed by Allianz Global Investors GmbH.
   - **April 2017:** Announcement of financing for the Vandel 2 project, although no specific construction start date was provided.
