### Evolutionary Timeline of the European Energy Facility in Hanstholmvej, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03-08-2020**: The Hanstholmvej solar farm, featuring bi-facial solar modules, is sold to the German investment management company LHI Group. The facility has a production capacity of 50MW and is noted as the first of its kind in Denmark to utilize bi-facial modules, which can absorb sunlight from both sides. It is categorized as a "Category D" facility, meaning it has more than 25MW at a grid-connection point and was the first to connect under new EU regulations effective from April 2019. European Energy is responsible for the construction and will continue to manage operations and administration post-transfer.
