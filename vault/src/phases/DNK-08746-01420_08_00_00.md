## DNK-08746-01420

### Evolutionary Timeline of the TotalEnergies Facility in Kalundborg, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2024:** A construction permit was granted for the Jammerland Bugt Coastal Offshore Wind Farm, which will consist of 16 wind turbines with a total capacity of 240 MW. The turbines will be installed on monopiles and will cover an area of 31.1 square kilometers south of Kalundborg. The wind farm is planned to begin operation in 2029.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **January 2024:** TotalEnergies signed an agreement to acquire an 85% stake in the Jammerland Bugt Offshore Wind Farm from European Energy, which is the developer and currently the sole owner of the project. This agreement indicates a significant investment by TotalEnergies in the offshore wind sector in Denmark.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 2024:** The Danish Energy Agency (DEA) approved the feasibility study report, which included an environmental impact report for the Jammerland Bugt project.
   - **December 2024:** The construction permit for the offshore wind farm was granted, marking a key milestone in the project timeline.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - As of January 2024, TotalEnergies is expected to acquire an 85% stake in the Jammerland Bugt Offshore Wind Farm, making it the majority owner alongside European Energy, which will retain a 15% stake.
