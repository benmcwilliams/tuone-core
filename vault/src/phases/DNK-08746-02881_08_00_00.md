### Evolutionary Timeline of the European Energy Facility in Kalundborg, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 12/2024
   - **Details:** European Energy is developing the Jammerland Bugt Coastal Offshore Wind Farm, which will consist of 16 wind turbines with a total capacity of 240 MW. The turbines will be installed on monopiles and will cover an area of 31.1 square kilometers south of Kalundborg. The project includes environmental protection measures for bats and porpoises during construction and operation.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 12/2024
   - **Details:** European Energy is the sole owner of the Jammerland Bugt project. TotalEnergies is expected to acquire an 85% stake in the project following an agreement signed in January 2024. This agreement also includes TotalEnergies becoming the majority owner of another offshore wind farm, Lillebaelt Syd.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 12/2024
   - **Details:** The construction permit for the Jammerland Bugt Offshore Wind Farm has been granted. The project is planned to begin operation in 2029. The feasibility study report, including an environmental impact report, was approved by the Danish Energy Agency (DEA) in March 2024.
