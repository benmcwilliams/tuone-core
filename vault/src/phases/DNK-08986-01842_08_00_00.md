#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Capacity**: The Aflandshage offshore wind farm is planned to have a capacity of **250 MW**. It is intended to supply green power for the **Green Fuels for Denmark** project, which aims to produce sustainable fuels in the Greater Copenhagen area.
- **Future Developments**: The facility is expected to deliver its first power in **2024/2025**, subject to regulatory approvals.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Context**: The development of the Aflandshage offshore wind farm is part of a broader initiative to support the **Green Fuels for Denmark** project, which has a total planned capacity of **1.3 GW** across multiple phases.
