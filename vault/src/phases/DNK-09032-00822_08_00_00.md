### Evolutionary Timeline of Vattenfall Facility in Vesterhav Nord, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2020**: The Danish Energy Agency approved the construction of the Vesterhav Syd and Vesterhav Nord offshore wind farms, which will consist of a total of 41 Siemens Gamesa 8.4 MW turbines, with Vesterhav Nord having a capacity of 176 MW.
- **November 2023**: The first power from the Vesterhav Syd offshore wind farm (168 MW) was delivered to the Danish grid. Vesterhav Nord is expected to be grid-connected in the first quarter of 2024.
- **Total Capacity**: Combined, Vesterhav Syd and Nord will have a total capacity of 344 MW, sufficient to meet the energy needs of more than 350,000 Danish households.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2021**: Vattenfall made the final investment decision (FID) for the Vesterhav projects, valued at €769 million. This investment is aimed at adding 344 MW of fossil-free electricity capacity to the Danish energy system by 2023.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2018**: Vattenfall announced the final investment decision for the Kriegers Flak offshore wind farm, while the Vesterhav Syd and Nord projects were still awaiting approval from the Danish Energy Board of Appeals.
- **December 2020**: The Danish Energy Agency approved the construction of Vesterhav Syd and Nord.
- **December 2021**: Vattenfall took the final investment decision for the Vesterhav projects.
- **July to September 2023**: Installation of the 20 turbines at Vesterhav Syd was completed.
- **November 2023**: Vesterhav Syd began delivering power to the grid.
- **First Quarter 2024**: Vesterhav Nord is expected to be grid-connected.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2021**: The projects are expected to contribute to local job creation, although specific job numbers were not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Vattenfall owns the Vesterhav Nord facility exclusively, as indicated by the absence of any mention of joint ventures or partnerships in the articles regarding ownership.
