## DNK-09033-00822

### Evolutionary Timeline of Vattenfall's Vesterhav Syd Facility, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2020**: The Danish Energy Agency approved the construction of the Vesterhav Syd and Nord offshore wind farms, which will consist of a total of 41 Siemens Gamesa 8.4 MW turbines, with Vesterhav Syd having a capacity of 168 MW and Vesterhav Nord 176 MW, totaling 344 MW.
- **July-September 2023**: Installation of the 20 turbines at Vesterhav Syd was completed.
- **November 2023**: The first power from the Vesterhav Syd offshore wind farm was delivered to the Danish grid.
- **January 2024**: Vattenfall announced the completion of the commissioning of the 168 MW Vesterhav Syd offshore wind farm, which is now contributing to the energy needs of 170,000 households.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2021**: Vattenfall made the final investment decision (FID) for the Vesterhav Syd and Nord projects, valued at €769 million. This investment aims to add 344 MW of fossil-free electricity capacity to the Danish energy system by 2023.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2018**: Vattenfall's Vesterhav Syd & Nord projects were awaiting a decision from the Danish Energy Board of Appeals, with plans to enter operation by the end of 2020.
- **December 2020**: The Danish Energy Agency approved the construction of the Vesterhav projects.
- **December 2021**: Vattenfall took the final investment decision for the Vesterhav projects.
- **November 2023**: First power delivered to the grid from Vesterhav Syd.
- **January 2024**: Vattenfall completed the commissioning of Vesterhav Syd.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2021**: Vattenfall indicated that the Vesterhav projects would contribute to local job creation as part of their commitment to the green transition in Denmark.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Vattenfall owns the Vesterhav Syd facility exclusively, as indicated by their direct involvement in the investment decisions and project management without mention of joint ventures or partnerships in ownership.
