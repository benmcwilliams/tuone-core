### Evolutionary Timeline of the MHI Vestas Facility in Hammel, Denmark

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2018**: The Hammel facility produces wind turbine converters. Despite job cuts, the production capacity remains relatively unaffected, and the facility will increase its focus on new products, including prototype building, testing, and documentation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2022**: Vestas announces the sale of its converters and controls business, which includes the Hammel facility, to KK Wind Solutions. This strategic move is aimed at enhancing efficiency and focusing resources on core operations.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2022**: Vestas confirms the sale of the Hammel facility as part of a broader transaction involving three factories (Denmark, India, and China) to KK Wind Solutions. The transaction is expected to close in the first quarter of 2023, pending regulatory approvals.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2018**: The Hammel facility will see a reduction of approximately 80 jobs as part of Vestas' global workforce cuts, which total around 400 jobs. The overall production capacity is not expected to be impacted by these layoffs.
- **August 2022**: The sale of the Hammel facility to KK Wind Solutions involves around 280 employees, who will transition to the new ownership.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **August 2022**: Vestas announces the sale of the Hammel facility to KK Wind Solutions, indicating a shift from full ownership to a partnership where KK will exclusively supply converters and control panels to Vestas. This marks a significant change in the ownership structure of the facility.
