2. **Investment and Development:**

   - **Details:** The acquisition of Vestas' converters and controls business represents a significant investment in KK Wind Solutions, enhancing its production capabilities and market position. Specific financial details of the investment were not disclosed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 02-03-2023
   - **Details:** The completion of the sale of Vestas' converters and controls business to KK Wind Solutions marks a major milestone. 4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 02-03-2023
