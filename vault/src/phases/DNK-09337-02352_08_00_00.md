### Evolutionary Timeline of the MHI Vestas Facility in Thisted Municipality, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2018:** Vestas received a 29MW repowering order from Danish utility NRGi Renewables for the Tagmark wind park in Thisted Municipality. The project involves replacing six V66-1.75MW turbines with eight V117-3.45MW turbines, which will more than double the facility’s annual energy production. Deliveries are expected to begin in the third quarter of 2019, with commissioning planned for the fourth quarter of 2019.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **December 2018:** The repowering project was part of Denmark’s first energy-neutral auction, which indicates a significant investment in renewable energy without reliance on state subsidies. The project was noted for delivering the most competitive bid in this auction.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **November 2018:** Vestas won the repowering project in Denmark’s first energy-neutral auction.
   - **Q3 2019:** Turbine deliveries for the repowering project are expected to begin.
   - **Q4 2019:** Commissioning of the new turbines is planned.
