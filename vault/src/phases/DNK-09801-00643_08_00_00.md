### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in Port of Thorsminde, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **April 2022:** RWE has selected the Port of Thorsminde as the operation and maintenance (O&M) base for its Thor offshore wind farm, which has a planned capacity of 1 GW. The wind farm is located approximately 22 km from the port and is expected to generate clean energy for over one million Danish households. The service station at the port is anticipated to become operational by 2026.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **April 2022:** RWE signed a letter of intent (LoI) with the Port of Thorsminde for a 30-year usage agreement to support the O&M works for the Thor offshore wind farm. The project has received a concession from the Danish Energy Agency to develop the wind farm.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **April 2022:** RWE announced plans to establish its base at Thorsminde by Spring 2023, with the first survey works to be carried out from the port. The Thor offshore wind farm is expected to be fully operational by 2027.

4. **Labour:**
   - **April 2022:** The project is expected to create up to 60 permanent jobs for technicians, engineers, nautical personnel, and crew for service vessels in the long run.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The articles do not specify the ownership structure of the facility, but it is implied that RWE is the primary operator and developer of the Thor offshore wind farm, having secured the necessary concessions for the project.
