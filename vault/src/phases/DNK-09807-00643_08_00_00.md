### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in Thor Offshore Wind Farm, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 01-11-2024
   - **Details:** RWE's Thor Offshore Wind Farm will feature 72 SG 14-236 DD wind turbines from Siemens Gamesa. Half of the turbines will utilize CO2-reduced steel towers, and 40 will have recyclable rotor blades. The facility is expected to generate enough clean energy to power over one million households in Denmark. The project includes ongoing cable installation and the construction of an onshore substation in Lemvig. The seabed preparation for foundation installations is set for early 2025, with turbine installation planned for 2026. The facility is projected to be fully operational by the end of 2027.
