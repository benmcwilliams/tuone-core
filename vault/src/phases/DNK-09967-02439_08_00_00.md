#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2024**: Siemens Gamesa is set to install a new offshore wind turbine prototype with a capacity of **21 MW** at the Østerild wind turbine test center. This turbine is reported to be **40% more powerful** than their current largest turbine, which has a capacity of **14 MW** (up to **15 MW** with the Power Boost feature). The installation involves mounting the nacelle on a **170-metre tower**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2024**: Siemens Gamesa received a **EUR 30 million grant** from the European Union for the project titled **Highly Innovative Prototype of the most Powerful Offshore Wind turbine generator (HIPPOW)**. This funding is aimed at the installation, operation, and testing of the new wind turbine prototype at the Østerild National Test Centre.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
