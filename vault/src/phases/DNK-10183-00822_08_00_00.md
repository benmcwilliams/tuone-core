### Evolutionary Timeline of Vattenfall Facility in Hvide Sande Port, Denmark

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2021
   - **Details:** Vattenfall announced the construction of the Vesterhav North and South offshore wind farms, with a combined capacity of 344MW. The Vesterhav Syd will consist of 20 turbines with a capacity of 168MW, while Vesterhav Nord will have 21 turbines with a capacity of 176MW.
