### Evolutionary Timeline of the Cummins Facility in Barcelona, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
   - **Details:** Cummins is collaborating with Iberdrola to develop large-scale green hydrogen plants in Spain and Portugal. A new €50 million PEM electrolyser plant is planned, with an initial production capacity of approximately 500 MW per year, scalable to over 1 GW/year. The facility will include system assembly and testing.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
   - **Details:** The investment for the new electrolyser plant is €50 million. This investment is part of a broader initiative to enhance the green hydrogen value chain in Iberia.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
   - **Details:** A site selection search is underway for the new plant, which is anticipated to open in 2023. The facility will initially cover 22,000 square meters.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** May 2021

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
