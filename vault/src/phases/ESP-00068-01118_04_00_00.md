## ESP-00068-01118

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Barcelona, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2021**: Iberdrola, in partnership with Cummins, announced plans to collaborate on a hydrogen refueling station in Barcelona. This initiative is part of a broader strategy to accelerate the production of green hydrogen in Spain.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2021**: The partnership between Iberdrola and Cummins includes a significant investment in a €50 million ($61 million) electrolyzer plant in Castilla-La Mancha, which will produce green hydrogen. This facility will have a production capacity of 500MW/year, scalable to over 1GW/year.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2021**: Iberdrola and Cummins signed an agreement to build one of the world’s largest green hydrogen facilities in Spain, with the electrolyzer production facility expected to come online in 2023.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2021**: The construction and operation of the new electrolyzer plant in Castilla-La Mancha are anticipated to create approximately 350 new jobs as production ramps up.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2021**: The facility in Barcelona, as part of the collaboration with Cummins, indicates a joint venture approach to developing hydrogen infrastructure, although specific ownership details of the Barcelona facility are not explicitly stated in the articles.
