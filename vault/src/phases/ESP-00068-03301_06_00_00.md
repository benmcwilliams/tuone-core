   - **Date:** April 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is involved in the development of solar pavement technology. A pilot project was initiated at Plaça de Les Glòries Catalanes, featuring a 50-square-meter demonstration of solar paving materials. The solar panels, measuring 340 mm x 340 mm x 61 mm, have a combined power capacity of 9 kW. The project aims to generate approximately 7,560 kWh of electricity annually, sufficient to power three households. The solar pavement will specifically power a public bicycle rental service in Barcelona.
