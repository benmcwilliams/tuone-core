### Ebro-EV Motors Facility Timeline - Barcelona, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Capacity**: The facility aims to produce 50,000 electric vehicles per year by 2027, with plans to increase this capacity to 150,000 units per year by 2029.
- **Products**: The first vehicles to be assembled will be from the Chery brand, specifically the Omoda model, with an electric all-wheel drive pick-up from the Ebro brand expected to follow by the end of 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Amount**: A total investment of 400 million euros is planned for the revitalization of the former Nissan plant in the Zona Franca industrial area.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Joint Venture Announcement**: The partnership between Ebro-EV Motors and Chery was officially announced on April 22, 2024. 
- **Production Start**: Production is set to begin in 2024, with the first model being the Chery Omoda.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The joint venture is expected to create approximately 1,250 jobs in the Barcelona area.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: Ebro-EV Motors holds the majority stake in the joint venture with Chery, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership.
