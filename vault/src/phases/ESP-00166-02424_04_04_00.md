### Evolutionary Timeline of the Repsol Facility in Catalonia, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2021**: Repsol established the Catalonia Hydrogen Valley, an ecosystem involving over a hundred companies and organizations focused on the hydrogen value chain, aiming for climate neutrality and improved business competitiveness.
- **Ongoing**: Repsol has developed its own renewable hydrogen production technology called photoelectrocatalysis, with a pilot plant at the Repsol Technology Lab producing emissions-free hydrogen using only water and sunlight.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **22-09-2021**: Repsol announced an investment of €8.9 million for the construction of a 2.5 MW electrolyser at its Petronor refinery in the Basque Country, which will also support the Catalonia Hydrogen Valley initiative.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **22-09-2021**: Announcement of the electrolyser project, which is part of the Basque Hydrogen Corridor (BH2C) Initiative, aimed at mobilizing €1.431 billion for 40 projects in the hydrogen value chain by 2026.

#### 4. Labour
- **22-09-2021**: The BH2C Initiative is expected to create more than 1300 jobs as part of its development and execution of hydrogen projects.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ongoing**: Repsol is the primary owner of the facility and is collaborating with Enagás and other partners in various projects, including the Catalonia Hydrogen Valley and the development of renewable hydrogen technologies.
