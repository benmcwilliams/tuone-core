### Evolutionary Timeline of the PSA Group Facility in Figueruelas, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2019**: PSA announced that starting in 2020, the Figueruelas plant will include the production of battery packs for electric and hybrid cars. The facility will also begin producing purely electric cars in 2020, alongside other Spanish plants in Vigo and Madrid. The Figueruelas plant is the largest among the three, accounting for half of PSA's annual production in Spain, which is approximately 500,000 units.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2019**: PSA plans to invest €250 million in the Figueruelas facility by 2021. Additionally, the company will benefit from public subsidies amounting to €4 million per year.

- **November 2019**: The announcement was made regarding the inclusion of battery pack assembly and the production of electric vehicles starting in 2020. The facility is set to produce models such as the Opel Corsa-e, Peugeot 2008, and Citroën C4.
