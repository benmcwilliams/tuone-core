### Evolutionary Timeline of the Stellantis Group Facility in Figueruelas, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2023**: Stellantis announced plans to produce a series of low-cost electric vehicles based on the 'CMP Smart Car' platform, which will include models produced at the Figueruelas facility. This includes the Opel Corsa and potentially other models from different Stellantis brands. The facility is expected to support the production of these vehicles, although specific production capacities were not detailed.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2024**: The Spanish Ministry of Industry pledged €133 million in subsidies for a potential battery cell factory at the Figueruelas plant. Stellantis is considering a significant investment of nearly €2.5 billion for this project, which would create around 3,000 jobs. This investment is part of a broader strategy to enhance electric vehicle production capabilities in Spain.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2024**: Stellantis is in the decision-making phase regarding the establishment of a battery complex adjacent to its car plant in Figueruelas, in collaboration with the Chinese cell manufacturer CATL. The project is still under consideration, with no confirmed timeline for construction or production start.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2024**: The proposed battery cell factory is expected to create approximately 3,000 jobs, contributing to local employment and supporting the transition to electric vehicle production.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
