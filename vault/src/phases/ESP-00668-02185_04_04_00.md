### Evolutionary Timeline of the DH2 Energy Facility in Madrid, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2022**: The DH2 Energy facility is part of the HyDeal project, which is set to be the first industrial implementation of the HyDeal Ambition platform. The facility will produce renewable hydrogen for the production of green steel, green ammonia, and green fertilizers. The total installed capacity is expected to reach **9.5 GW of solar power** and **7.4 GW of electrolyzers** by **2030**. Production is planned to start in **2025**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2022**: The HyDeal project is recognized as the largest giga-scale renewable hydrogen project globally, with anchor sponsors including ArcelorMittal, Enagás, Fertiberia, and DH2 Energy. The project aims to implement a green hydrogen system priced at **€1.5/kg**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2022**: The HyDeal project is announced, with production scheduled to start in **2025** and full capacity expected by **2030**. The project has been ranked by IRENA as the largest of its kind globally.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2022**: While specific job creation figures are not mentioned in the articles, the scale of the project and its partnerships suggest significant job creation potential in the renewable hydrogen sector, particularly in the production of green steel, ammonia, and fertilizers.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2022**: The facility is part of a joint venture involving multiple sponsors, including DH2 Energy, ArcelorMittal, Enagás, and Fertiberia. Therefore, DH2 Energy does not own the facility exclusively but is a key partner in the joint venture.
