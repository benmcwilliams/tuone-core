### Evolutionary Timeline of Solaria Energia y Medio Ambiente Facility in Madrid, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **November 2019:** Solaria Energia y Medio Ambiente has a total of 75MW of operational photovoltaic (PV) capacity in Spain, with 35MW being subsidy-backed. The company is developing an additional 400MW of PV capacity. They aim to deploy 650MW of domestic PV annually and have a total pipeline of 3GW. Notably, the Trillo project in Guadalajara was expanded from 450MW to 626MW, making it one of the largest solar plants in Europe.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **November 2019:** Solaria secured a long-term financing agreement with Banco Sabadell for €59.45 million (approximately US$65.46 million) to support the development of four PPA-backed PV plants totaling 108.5MW. This financing is structured over a 16-year period.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **October 2019:** The Trillo project received a favorable grid connection (IVA), a feasibility access permit, and debt financing, leading to its capacity expansion from 450MW to 626MW.
   - **November 2019:** Announcement of the financing agreement with Banco Sabadell for the development of additional PV plants.
