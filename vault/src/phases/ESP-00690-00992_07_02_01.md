### Evolutionary Timeline of the Cupra Facility in Martorell, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-03-2020**: The Cupra Formentor SUV is introduced as a plug-in hybrid electric vehicle (PHEV) with a system output of 180 kW and an electric range of up to 60 km. Production of the Formentor, along with the Cupra Leon, is set to begin in Martorell.
- **08-09-2021**: Cupra announces plans to become a fully electric brand by 2030, with the UrbanRebel electric small car concept to be produced in Martorell starting in 2025.
- **08-06-2022**: An updated UrbanRebel concept is presented, with production planned in Martorell, pending government funding approval.
- **10-11-2022**: Volkswagen and Seat announce a €10 billion investment in e-mobility projects in Spain, including the conversion of the Martorell plant for small electric cars.
- **23-02-2023**: Seat announces a €3 billion investment to convert the Martorell plant for electric vehicle production, aiming to produce the Cupra UrbanRebel and other small electric cars starting in 2025.
- **23-03-2023**: Plans are confirmed to produce the Cupra Raval electric small car alongside the VW ID.2 in Martorell from 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **10-11-2022**: Volkswagen and Seat confirm a €10 billion investment in e-mobility, with a significant portion allocated to the Martorell plant's conversion.
- **23-02-2023**: Seat announces an additional €3 billion investment specifically for the Martorell plant's transformation to support electric vehicle production.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05-03-2020**: Announcement of the Cupra Formentor PHEV, with production starting in Martorell.
- **08-09-2021**: Production of the UrbanRebel electric small car is planned for 2025.
- **10-11-2022**: Confirmation of the €10 billion investment, enabling the conversion of the Martorell plant.
- **23-02-2023**: Announcement of the strategic plan for the Martorell plant's conversion, with production of electric vehicles set to begin in 2025.
- **23-03-2023**: Official confirmation of the Cupra Raval's production alongside the VW ID.2 starting in 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **10-11-2022**: The investment in e-mobility is expected to create up to 140,000 new jobs in the Spanish automotive sector, including at the Martorell facility.
- **23-02-2023**: The conversion of the Martorell plant will involve training for existing employees to adapt to electric vehicle production.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The Cupra facility in Martorell is part of Seat, which is a subsidiary of the Volkswagen Group. The facility is not exclusively owned by Cupra but is part of the broader Volkswagen Group's operations in Spain.
