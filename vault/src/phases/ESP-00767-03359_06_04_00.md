### Evolutionary Timeline of the Rinovasol Facility in Navarra, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2021**: Rinovasol opens a 50 MW facility in Navarra, Spain, specializing in the repair and manufacture of solar modules. The facility produces low-power solar modules with power outputs ranging from 235 W to 300 W. The company emphasizes a circular economy approach, buying, fixing, and certifying panels, and providing a five-year warranty on refurbished products. Rinovasol claims a recycling rate of almost 100% of the components of the solar modules.
