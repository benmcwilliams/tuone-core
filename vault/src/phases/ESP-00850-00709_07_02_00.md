## ESP-00850-00709

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Seat Facility in Pamplona, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06/2021**: Seat is involved in the development of small electric vehicles (EVs) based on the MEB platform, with plans to produce models like the Seat Acandra in early 2025. The Pamplona facility is identified as a potential production site for these electric cars.
- **12/2022**: The Pamplona facility is set to undergo conversion to support the production of electric small cars, as part of a broader strategy to electrify operations at both the Pamplona and Martorell plants.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05/2022**: The Volkswagen Group and Seat announce an increase in their investment in e-mobility in Spain from €7 billion to €10 billion. This investment is aimed at electrifying the Pamplona facility, among others, and developing a comprehensive supplier ecosystem.
- **12/2022**: As part of the €10 billion investment, significant funds are allocated for the conversion of the Pamplona facility to support the production of electric vehicles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05/2022**: The Future: Fast Forward initiative, led by Volkswagen and Seat, applies for the "Strategic Project for Economic Recovery and Transformation" (PERTE) program to establish Spain as a hub for electric vehicles, which includes plans for the Pamplona facility.
- **12/2022**: Seat announces that the Pamplona facility will be part of the transition to producing electric vehicles, with specific plans to manufacture components for the Volkswagen Group's planned BEV small car family.

#### 4. Labour
- **12/2022**: Seat emphasizes its commitment to maintaining stable employment during the transition to electric vehicle production at the Pamplona facility, indicating efforts to secure jobs amidst the transformation.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Throughout the timeline, there is no indication of joint ownership or partnerships affecting the ownership structure of the Pamplona facility. It is implied that Seat, as part of the Volkswagen Group, operates the facility under its corporate structure.

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic decisions regarding the Seat facility in Pamplona, Spain, focusing on its transition towards electric vehicle production and the associated investments and operational changes.
