### Evolutionary Timeline of the Infinity Lithium Facility in San Jose, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **01-05-2019**: Infinity Lithium owns 75% of the San Jose lithium project, which is a lithium deposit located in southwestern Spain. The facility is expected to produce battery-grade lithium hydroxide, contributing to the development of a vertically integrated battery value chain in the EU.
- **28-06-2021**: Infinity Lithium signed a letter of intent with LG Energy Solution to supply battery-grade lithium hydroxide from the San Jose project for at least five years, with an annual supply volume of at least 10,000 tonnes.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **24-06-2020**: Infinity Lithium signed a multi-staged funding agreement with EU-backed EIT InnoEnergy to develop the San Jose project. This funding is part of a broader EU initiative to establish a secure and autonomous supply chain for lithium in Europe.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **28-06-2021**: Infinity Lithium's project faced a setback when approval for the project was revoked by Spanish authorities in May 2021. The company is contesting the validity of this permit withdrawal, which is crucial for moving forward with the supply agreement with LG Energy Solution.
