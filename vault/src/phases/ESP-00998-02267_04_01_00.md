### Evolutionary Timeline of H2B2 Electrolysis Technologies Facility in Setúbal, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Capacity**: The facility is designed to produce green hydrogen at a capacity of 135 kg (1,500 Nm3) per hour.
- **Technology**: The hydrogen production will utilize electrolyzers, with an expected output capacity around 7.5 MW, fed by a 12 MW solar PV plant adjacent to the hydrogen production unit. The hydrogen produced will have a purity level greater than 99.999%, making it suitable for various applications, including injection into the natural gas distribution network.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment**: The project has received €5 million in CAPEX support from the EU-funded Operational Programme for Sustainability and Efficient Use of Resources (PO SEUR) to aid in its development.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Announcement**: On 04-07-2022, Hyperion Renewables announced the launch of an EPC tender for the Green H2 Setúbal Project, with seven companies, including H2B2 Electrolysis Technologies, competing to build the facility.
- **Tender Details**: The project cost is evaluated at €11.5 million, and the tender process includes evaluation based on price, energy efficiency, and delivery deadlines.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: While specific job creation numbers are not mentioned in the article, the construction and operation of the facility are expected to create jobs in the region, particularly in the renewable energy sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Joint Venture**: H2B2 Electrolysis Technologies is involved in a partnership with the Portuguese companies FCC Industrial and RCC for the construction of the facility, indicating a joint venture rather than exclusive ownership.
