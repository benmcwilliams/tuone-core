### Evolutionary Timeline of the Elecnor Group Facility in Setúbal, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - The facility is part of the Hyperion Green H2 Setúbal Project, which aims to produce green hydrogen. The production capacity is set at 135 kg (1,500 Nm3) of green hydrogen per hour, utilizing a solar PV plant with a capacity of 12 MW. The hydrogen produced will be of high purity (>99.999%) and will be used for various applications, including sales to off-takers and injection into the Portuguese natural gas distribution network.
