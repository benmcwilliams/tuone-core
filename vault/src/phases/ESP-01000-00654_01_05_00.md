### Evolutionary Timeline of the Renault Group Facility in Seville, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06-11-2021**: Renault announced plans to establish a Refactory in Seville, which will focus on the circular economy for electric vehicles (EVs). The facility will cover an area of 5,000 square meters and will include operations for reconditioning used vehicles, recycling E-Tech transmissions, repairing electric car batteries, and developing second-life applications. The Re-Trofit division is expected to start operations by the end of 2022, with a production capacity to refurbish over 10,000 vehicles and repair up to 1,000 batteries annually by 2025. Additionally, the Seville plant will manufacture two new models of electrified gearboxes for hybrid vehicles.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06-11-2021**: The establishment of the Refactory in Seville is part of Renault's broader industrial plan for its Spanish plants, which includes investments in five new vehicle models and a new family of engines for other plants in Castilla y León.
