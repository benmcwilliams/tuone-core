## ESP-01000-01118

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Seville, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2022**: Iberdrola announced the development of a new 375 MW photovoltaic plant in Cedillo, Seville. The facility will utilize over 576,000 bifacial photovoltaic modules, allowing for an annual production of more than 582,400 MWh of green energy. This energy output is significant as it would have required over 97 million cubic meters of gas if generated through traditional means.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2022**: The construction of the Cedillo photovoltaic plant is part of Iberdrola's broader investment plan, which aims to install 10,000 new MW of renewable energy in Spain by 2030. The company plans to invest over €1.7 billion in Extremadura alone from 2020 to 2025, contributing to the creation of approximately 7,400 jobs during this period.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2022**: The facility received its Environmental Impact Statement, marking a significant milestone in the project timeline. The construction is expected to boost local supply chains and create up to 800 jobs during peak construction periods.
- **April 2023**: Iberdrola and MAPFRE announced the addition of 150 new photovoltaic MW to their joint venture, Energías Renovables Ibermap, which includes operational plants in Extremadura and Castilla-La Mancha. This brings the total capacity of the joint venture to 445 MW, with Iberdrola holding a 51% stake.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2022**: The construction of the Cedillo photovoltaic plant is projected to create up to 800 jobs during peak construction periods. Additionally, the overall investment plan in Extremadura is expected to generate around 7,400 jobs from 2020 to 2025.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2023**: Iberdrola holds a majority stake (51%) in the joint venture with MAPFRE, which manages renewable energy assets, including the new photovoltaic capacity. This indicates a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership of the facility.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones related to the Iberdrola facility in Seville, highlighting its technological advancements, investment strategies, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
