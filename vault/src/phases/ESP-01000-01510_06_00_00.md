### Evolutionary Timeline of the Canadian Solar Facility in Seville, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 06-06-2019
   - **Details:** Canadian Solar will supply 500 MW of its 144-cell polycrystalline CS3U-P KuMax modules to U.K. developer Solarcentury for the La Cabrera solar project, which is near Seville. The La Cabrera project is part of a larger initiative that includes the 200 MW La Cabrera project and the 300 MW Talayuela Solar project.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 06-06-2019
   - **Details:** The La Cabrera project is being developed without subsidy, indicating a significant investment from Solarcentury and its partners. However, specific financial terms of the module supply agreement were not disclosed.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 06-06-2019
   - **Details:** The La Cabrera project is scheduled for completion in 2020. Authorization from the regional government of Andalucía was obtained in November prior to this announcement, and the project is being developed with the support of Texla, a local engineering company.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 06-06-2019
   - **Details:** The article does not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss related to the La Cabrera project.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 06-06-2019
