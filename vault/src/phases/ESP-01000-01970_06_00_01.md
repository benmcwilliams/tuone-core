## ESP-01000-01970

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Statkraft Facility in Seville, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10/2018**: The Don Rodrigo solar plant, with a production capacity of **170 MW**, is under construction near Seville. It is notable for being one of the first subsidy-free solar projects of this size in Europe. The plant is expected to be commissioned by the end of the year.
- **08/2019**: A second facility, **Don Rodrigo 2**, is announced with a capacity of **50 MW**. Construction is underway, and it is expected to generate approximately **100 GWh** of electricity annually. This facility is also designed to be subsidy-free.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **10/2018**: A **15-year power purchase agreement (PPA)** is signed between BayWa and Statkraft for the Don Rodrigo solar plant, ensuring long-term financing for the project.
- **08/2019**: A **12-year PPA** is signed for the Don Rodrigo 2 solar farm, further solidifying the partnership between BayWa and Statkraft in developing subsidy-free solar projects in Spain.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10/2018**: Construction of the **170 MW Don Rodrigo solar plant** begins, with commissioning expected by the end of the year.
- **08/2019**: Construction of the **50 MW Don Rodrigo 2 solar farm** is underway, with completion also expected by the end of the year.

- The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the construction or operation of the Don Rodrigo facilities.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is a **joint venture** between **BayWa r.e.** and **Statkraft**, with Statkraft being the Norwegian energy company involved in both the Don Rodrigo and Don Rodrigo 2 projects. There is no indication of exclusive ownership by either company.
