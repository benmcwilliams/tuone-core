### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enel Facility in Seville, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2020
   - **Details:** Enel Green Power España (EGPE) broke ground on a 50MW solar project named Los Naranjos in Seville, which is part of a larger 100MW complex. The facility will feature over 128,000 solar panels with individual module capacity of 340Wp. The project includes the construction of nine new switching stations, an electric substation, and a 4.5km underground cable grid.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** April 2020
   - **Details:** Enel is investing €28.2 million (approximately US$30.6 million) in the second 50MW batch of the solar complex. The first 50MW project, Las Corchas, is also under construction with an investment of €30 million (approximately US$32.6 million).

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** April 2020
   - **Details:** The construction of the Los Naranjos solar project commenced in April 2020, following the lifting of a construction freeze due to COVID-19. The entire 100MW complex is expected to be ready by late 2020.
