### Evolutionary Timeline of the Solarpack Facility in Seville, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **July 2019:** Solarpack announced that three of its utility-scale solar projects, known as Grullas-Pedroso, Grullas-Peñaflor, and Monclova, reached financial close. These projects have a combined capacity of 112MW, which will increase Solarpack's operational PV portfolio to a total installed capacity of 129MW once they are operational.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **July 2019:** Solarpack secured funding of €59 million (approximately US$66 million) from Spanish banks Bankia and Bankinter for the three projects. This funding is part of the financial close achieved for the Grullas-Pedroso, Grullas-Peñaflor, and Monclova projects.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **July 2019:** The three projects were reported to be on track to go live by the end of 2019, following the financial close. The projects had previously secured a combined 100MW in contracts from Spain’s auction in July 2017.
