### Evolutionary Timeline of the Abengoa Facility in Seville, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **11/2016**: Abengoa sold four photovoltaic (PV) plants located in Seville and Jaen, with a total production capacity of 9.37 MW (Casaquemada: 1.88 MW, Cabezas de San Juan: 5.70 MW, Copero: 0.90 MW, Linares: 1.89 MW). This sale was part of a non-strategic divestment plan aimed at reducing debt.
- **02/2021**: Abengoa completed the construction of a pilot green hydrogen plant as part of the Grasshopper project, which focuses on producing sustainable energy through hydrogen. The plant is designed for flexible megawatt-scale fuel cell power generation and is currently in the commissioning and start-up phase.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **02/2021**: The Grasshopper project, which includes the pilot hydrogen plant, is funded by the Fuel Cells and Hydrogen 2 Joint Undertaking with a total budget of €4.4 million. This project is part of the EU's Horizon 2020 research and innovation framework.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **11/2016**: Abengoa initiated a divestment plan, selling four PV plants to Vela Energy for €57.2 million, which contributed to its debt restructuring efforts.
- **02/2021**: The pilot plant for the Grasshopper project was completed and is in the commissioning phase, with plans to transfer it to the Netherlands for further integration.
