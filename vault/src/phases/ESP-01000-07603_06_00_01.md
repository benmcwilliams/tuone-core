### Evolutionary Timeline of BayWa r.e. Solar Projects GmbH Facility in Seville, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10/2018**: BayWa r.e. Solar Projects GmbH announced the development of the Don Rodrigo solar plant, a subsidy-free solar facility with a production capacity of **170 MW**. The plant is based on **1500V architecture** and utilizes **Huawei string inverters**. The project is noted as a significant milestone for the European PV market, being one of the first of its kind in Europe.

- **01/2019**: The capacity of the Don Rodrigo solar park was updated to **175 MW**. The plant was conceived in **2012** and is set for commissioning in **Q2 2019**. The project is designed to be independent of government support, making it resilient to policy changes.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **10/2018**: A **15-year power purchase agreement (PPA)** was signed with Statkraft, ensuring the long-term financing of the Don Rodrigo project. The project’s economic viability is attributed to high solar irradiation, optimized system design, and reduced material costs, allowing it to operate without state funding.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
