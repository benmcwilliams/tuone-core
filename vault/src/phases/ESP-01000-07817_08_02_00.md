### Evolutionary Timeline of GRI Renewable Industries Facility in Seville, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2018**: GRI Renewable Industries began production of a support structure (tower) for GE’s Haliade-X 12MW offshore wind turbine prototype at its Seville factory. This marked the facility's involvement in manufacturing components for one of the largest offshore wind turbines in the world.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2019**: GE Renewable Energy announced a commitment of over $400 million to develop and deploy the Haliade-X turbine, which indirectly supports GRI Renewable Industries' operations by providing a significant market opportunity for the towers produced at the Seville facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2019**: GRI Renewable Industries confirmed that production of the Haliade-X tower had commenced at the end of 2018. This production is part of a collaborative effort with GE Renewable Energy to optimize the turbine's design ahead of its commercial production, which is expected to start in 2021.
