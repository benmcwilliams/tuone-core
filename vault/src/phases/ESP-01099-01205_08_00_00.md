## ESP-01099-01205

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Naturgy Facility in Tarragona, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2023**: Naturgy begins construction of the Tres Termes wind farm in Tarragona, with an installed power capacity of 30 MW. This facility is expected to generate renewable energy equivalent to the consumption of 26,700 homes, with a planned production of 87 GWh/year. The company currently operates six wind farms in Catalonia, totaling 172 MW of power.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2023**: Naturgy announces an investment of 40 million euros for the construction of the Tres Termes wind farm. Additionally, the company invested nearly 61 million euros in two other wind farms, Los Barrancs (23 MW) and Punta Redona (27 MW), which were launched in the first quarter of the same year.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2023**: Construction of the Tres Termes wind farm begins, with an expected connection to the grid in the last quarter of 2024. Earlier in 2023, Naturgy launched the Los Barrancs and Punta Redona parks, adding a total of 50 MW to the Catalan energy system.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2023**: The construction of the Tres Termes wind farm is projected to generate around 400 direct and indirect jobs. The earlier projects (Los Barrancs and Punta Redona) generated approximately 650 direct and indirect jobs through the subcontracting of nearly forty companies from the Terra Alta region and the province of Tarragona.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The articles do not specify any joint ventures or partnerships; therefore, it can be inferred that Naturgy owns the facility exclusively.
