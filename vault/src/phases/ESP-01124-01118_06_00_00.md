### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Toledo, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2024**: Iberdrola announced the construction of the Aceca Hybrid Photovoltaic Plant in Toledo, which will have a capacity of 347.5 MW. The plant will consist of 726,180 bifacial monocrystalline photovoltaic modules and will utilize 95 inverters. The total fenced area for this project is 531.4 hectares.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2024**: The total investment for the Aceca Hybrid Photovoltaic Plant is reported to be €192,588,885. This investment is part of a larger initiative where Iberdrola is developing multiple photovoltaic and hybridisation projects across various provinces, with a total investment of €380 million for all projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2024**: Iberdrola obtained authorization for the construction of the Aceca Hybrid Photovoltaic Plant in Toledo, along with other projects in Huelva, Valencia, and Valladolid.
- **August 2024**: Announcement of the Aceca Hybrid Photovoltaic Plant project, detailing its specifications and investment.
