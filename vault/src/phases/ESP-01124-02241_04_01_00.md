### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fusion Fuel Facility in Toledo, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** March 2023
   - **Details:** Fusion Fuel is developing a 2.4MW green hydrogen project in Toledo, Spain, utilizing the latest generation of its HEVO solution. The facility is expected to produce approximately 110 tonnes of green hydrogen per annum.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** March 2023
   - **Details:** The project has been awarded a €3.3 million ($3.5 million) grant from the H2 Pioneros programme, which represents around 57% of the total estimated capital cost of the project. This funding is part of the Spanish Government’s Strategic Projects for Economic Recovery and Transformation (PERTE) programme.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** March 2023
   - **Details:** The project is planned to be built and commissioned during 2024. Fusion Fuel has secured land for the facility and aims to begin the permitting process quickly, with construction expected to start in early 2024.
