### Evolutionary Timeline of the BP Facility in Valencia, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **July 2022**: BP, in collaboration with Iberdrola, announced plans for a green hydrogen project at BP’s Castellón refinery near Valencia. This project is part of a broader initiative to develop hydrogen production hubs in Spain, Portugal, and the UK, with a total capacity of up to 600,000 tonnes per year. The project aims to produce green hydrogen and its derivatives, such as green ammonia and methanol.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **July 2022**: The joint venture between BP and Iberdrola intends to invest up to €1 billion (£840 million) in a network of up to 11,000 rapid and ultra-fast EV public charge points across Spain and Portugal. This investment is part of their strategic collaboration to expand EV charging infrastructure and green hydrogen production.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **July 2022**: BP and Iberdrola submitted plans for the green hydrogen project at BP’s Castellón refinery as part of the Spanish government’s hydrogen value chain PERTE call. The joint venture agreements are expected to be finalized by the end of 2022, pending regulatory approvals.
