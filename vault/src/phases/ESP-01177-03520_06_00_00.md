### Evolutionary Timeline of the Soltec Facility in Valencia, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - Soltec is collaborating with TotalEnergies to build a 94MW solar PV plant in Valencia. The facility will utilize Soltec’s solar tracker technology, specifically the SFOne trackers, which are designed to maximize energy efficiency while minimizing visual impact.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The project requires an investment of over €50 million (approximately US$54 million). Soltec holds a 35% stake in the project through a special purpose vehicle, while TotalEnergies holds the remaining 65%.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - The collaboration for the solar PV plant in Valencia was announced in September 2023. The project is part of a broader effort by both companies to develop several solar PV projects in Spain, with some receiving environmental permits in January 2023, totaling a combined capacity of 401MW.
