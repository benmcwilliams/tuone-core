### Evolutionary Timeline of the Port Authority of Valencia Facility in Valencia, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: 10-08-2023
   - **Details**: The Port Authority of Valencia is testing a vertical photovoltaic (PV) wall on the Dique Norte breakwater, featuring 21 solar modules with a total capacity of 8.6 kW. This pilot project aims to assess the feasibility and efficiency of vertical PV installations, potentially leading to the development of a larger vertical photovoltaic park in Spain.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Date**: 10-08-2023
   - **Details**: The pilot project is being conducted in collaboration with the Center for Research in Project Management, Innovation, and Sustainability (PRINS) at the Polytechnic University of Valencia (UPV). Following the successful completion of the testing phase, the Port Authority of Valencia plans to secure funding for a larger-scale installation.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Date**: 10-08-2023
   - **Details**: The facility is currently in a two-month real-time testing phase to measure energy generation and structural behavior. The pilot project is a precursor to a potential larger installation on the breakwater.
