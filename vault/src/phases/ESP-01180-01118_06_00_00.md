- **August 2024**: Iberdrola announced the construction of the Peñaflor III Hybrid Photovoltaic Plant in Valladolid, with a capacity of 48.12 MW. This facility will utilize 106,923 bifacial monocrystalline silicon solar panels and 14 inverters, hybridized with the existing Peñaflor III wind farm (49 MW). Additionally, a second hybridization project, Peñaflor IV, was authorized with the same capacity and technical specifications.
