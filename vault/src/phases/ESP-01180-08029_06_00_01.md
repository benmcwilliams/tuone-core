1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **September 2023:** Renfe announced plans to build a 20 MW solar photovoltaic (PV) installation near Valladolid. The facility will primarily produce electricity for train traction, with any excess power being sold on the market. This pilot project is part of a broader initiative to develop multiple PV installations along Renfe's rail network.
