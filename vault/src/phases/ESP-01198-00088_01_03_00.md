### Basquevolt Facility Timeline - Vitoria-Gasteiz, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2022**: Basquevolt, an initiative focused on developing solid-state batteries, plans to develop prototype cells and commission a pilot production line by 2025. The facility aims to achieve an annual production capacity of 10 GWh by 2027.
- **June 2023**: Basquevolt reports progress in developing solid-state battery cells, with plans to produce the first 20 Ah cells suitable for electric vehicles by the end of 2023. The technology aims for a high energy density of 1,000 Wh/l or 450 Wh/kg and a potential cost reduction of 30% compared to current lithium-ion batteries. The facility is set to start mass production at a future plant with a capacity of 1 GWh by the end of 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2022**: The Basquevolt initiative is launched with an investment of over €700 million ($739 million) aimed at establishing the facility and its operations.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2022**: Basquevolt is officially launched, with plans for a pilot production line in 2025 and mass production starting in 2027.
- **June 2023**: Basquevolt announces that it will produce its first battery cells before the end of 2023, marking a significant milestone in its development timeline.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2022**: The initiative is expected to generate more than 800 direct jobs as a result of the investment and establishment of the facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2022**: Basquevolt is a collaborative initiative involving the Basque government and several founding investors, including Iberdrola, Enagás, CIE Automotive, EIT InnoEnergy, and CIC energiGUNE. The ownership structure indicates a joint venture rather than exclusive ownership by a single company.
