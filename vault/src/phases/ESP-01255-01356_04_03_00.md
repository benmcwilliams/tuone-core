### Evolutionary Timeline of the Universitat Politecnica de Catalunya Facility in Würzburg, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is involved in the EU-funded INN-BALANCE project, which focuses on optimizing auxiliary components in fuel cell vehicles. The project aims to enhance the design and cooling systems of fuel cells, specifically targeting the manufacturing-oriented design to lower production costs for fuel cell systems. Significant progress has been reported in defining interfaces between components and the fuel cell stack, as well as in the design of stack housing and cooling modules.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The INN-BALANCE project is funded by the Fuel Cells and Hydrogen 2 Joint Undertaking, which is supported by the European Union’s Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the article, but the funding is aimed at advancing fuel cell technology.

Milestones achieved include the specification of main parameters for fuel cell auxiliary components and the design of critical components such as the cooling module and anode module.
