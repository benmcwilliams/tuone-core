### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enel Facility in Zaragoza, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2020
   - **Details:** Enel Green Power España began construction on a 21MW wind project in the Teruel and Zaragoza provinces. The facility will consist of six 3.55MW turbines. The project is part of a broader initiative to support Endesa's goal of achieving 10.2GW of installed renewable capacity by 2022.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** April 2020
   - **Details:** The total investment for the 21MW wind farm is €20 million. This project is part of a larger investment plan by Endesa, which aims to invest approximately €3.8 billion to increase its renewable capacity from 7.4GW in 2019 to 10.2GW by 2022.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** April 2020
   - **Details:** Construction of the 21MW wind farm has commenced, with expectations for it to enter operation before the end of the year.
