### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fusion Fuel Facility in Zaragoza, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05/2023**: Fusion Fuel entered into a €2.46 million ($2.71 million) equipment supply contract with Consejo Superior de Investigaciones Científicas (CSIC) for a 550 kW solar-to-green hydrogen project in Zaragoza. The project includes the development of an on-premises green hydrogen production and dispensing system for mobility, along with supplementary solar photovoltaic generation and an energy storage system. Fusion Fuel will supply 22 HEVO-Solar units, along with equipment for water purification and hydrogen compression and storage to 500 bar.
