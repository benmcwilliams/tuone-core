### Evolutionary Timeline of the Raven SR Facility in Zaragoza, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Technology**: Raven SR's facility will utilize a patented Steam/CO2 Reforming technology to convert various types of waste (municipal solid waste, organic waste, methane) into clean hydrogen and Fischer-Tropsch synthetic fuels.
- **Production Capacity**: The facility is expected to produce 75 kg of transport-grade hydrogen per ton of organic waste, amounting to an estimated daily output of 5,250 kg and an annual total of approximately 1.8 million kg.
- **Operational Date**: The facility is projected to be operational by 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Funding**: In 2022, Raven SR received €2.4 million ($2.6 million) from the European Commission to assist in the startup of the plant. Additionally, the company secured funding from the Ministry for Ecological Transition and the Demographic Challenge in Spain through the Perte H2 Cadena Valor C2 grant.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Project Announcement**: On 15-08-2024, it was announced that Raven SR's waste-to-hydrogen production plant in Zaragoza has been designated as “A Project and Investment of Regional Autonomous Interest” by the Regional Ministry of Presidency, Economy and Justice of the Government of Aragón. This designation is significant as it allows for expedited administrative processes and highlights the project's importance for regional development.
- **Expected Operational Date**: The facility is anticipated to be operational by 2026.
