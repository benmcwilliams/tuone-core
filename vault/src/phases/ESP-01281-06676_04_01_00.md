#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2022**: Researchers at the University of Zaragoza developed a techno-economic model for green hydrogen production via electrolyzers powered by photovoltaic plants. The model includes a case study of a 10 MW solar plant supplying power to a green hydrogen plant with a 5 MW electrolyzer, expected to produce 600 tons of hydrogen annually at a price of €6/kg. The system is designed to prioritize PV power consumption while supplementing from the grid when prices are low.

- **October 2023**: The University of Zaragoza, in collaboration with Atalaya Generacion, developed a model for optimizing the scheduling and management of pumped hydro energy storage (PHES) integrated with renewable energy sources. This model aims to maximize operating profits and reduce energy costs by 27% compared to systems without storage.
