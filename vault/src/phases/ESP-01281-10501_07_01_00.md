## ESP-01281-10501

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Stellantis CATL Facility in Zaragoza, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2024**: Stellantis and CATL announced a joint venture to establish a lithium iron phosphate (LFP) battery cell production facility in Zaragoza, with a targeted production capacity of up to 50 GWh. The facility is set to start operations by the end of 2026, with production phases dependent on the electric vehicle market's evolution in Europe and support from Spanish and EU authorities.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2023**: Stellantis and CATL confirmed an investment of up to €4.1 billion for the battery plant in Zaragoza. This investment is part of a broader strategy to enhance Stellantis' battery offerings in Europe.
- **December 2024**: The overall project for the battery factory is estimated to have an investment volume of around €2.5 billion, with financial support from the Spanish government being negotiated.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2023**: Stellantis and CATL signed a non-binding memorandum of understanding (MOU) for the local supply of LFP battery cells and modules.
- **June 2025**: Construction work is scheduled to begin on the battery plant.
- **March 2028**: Expected completion date for the construction of the facility.
- **End of 2026**: Targeted start date for battery production at the Zaragoza facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **November 2023**: The new battery cell factory is expected to create approximately 3,000 direct jobs in the region, contributing significantly to the local economy.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **November 2023**: The facility will operate as a 50:50 joint venture between Stellantis and CATL, indicating shared ownership and collaboration in the production of LFP batteries.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic decisions surrounding the Stellantis CATL facility in Zaragoza, Spain, highlighting its significance in the context of the evolving electric vehicle market and the broader clean technology landscape.
