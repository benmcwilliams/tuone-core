### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nel Facility in Puertollano, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **04-11-2020**: Nel is set to supply a 20MW PEM electrolyser for a green hydrogen plant in Puertollano, which will utilize a 100MW photovoltaic solar plant and a lithium-ion battery system with a storage capacity of 20MWh. The facility is designed to produce green hydrogen from 100% renewable sources.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **04-11-2020**: The total investment for the green hydrogen plant is reported to be €150 million, with Iberdrola and Fertiberia as the main stakeholders responsible for the development.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **04-11-2020**: The plant is expected to be operational in 2021, marking a significant milestone in the development of green hydrogen production in the region.

4. **Labour**:
   - No specific information regarding job creation or job loss is mentioned in the article.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - The facility is a joint venture between Iberdrola and Fertiberia, with Nel providing the electrolyser technology but not owning the facility itself.
