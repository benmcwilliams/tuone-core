### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fondi Italiani per le infrastrutture SGR S.p.A. Facility in Puertollano, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2019**: Fondi Italiani per le infrastrutture SGR S.p.A. (F2i) announced the construction of a 79 MW merchant solar plant named El Bonal PV park in Puertollano, southern Spain. This facility is part of a larger portfolio of over 1 GW of solar and wind capacity that Renovalia plans to develop in Spain.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2019**: F2i acquired Spanish renewable energy developer Renovalia, which raised its operational renewable energy portfolio to 1 GW. The financial terms of the acquisition were not disclosed, but it was noted that an additional 800 MW of projects are under development.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2019**: The acquisition of Renovalia was finalized, allowing F2i to enter the Spanish PV market and initiate the development of the El Bonal PV park.
