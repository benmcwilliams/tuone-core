### Evolutionary Timeline of Renovalia Energy Group Facility in Puertollano, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 05-08-2019
   - **Details:** Renovalia Energy Group is developing a 79.2 MW solar park named El Bonal in Puertollano, Spain. The facility will consist of 240,000 PV modules, each rated at 330 W, mounted on fixed structures across an area of 103.4 hectares. Once operational, the park is expected to generate approximately 118,000 MWh per year, sufficient to meet the energy needs of around 146,000 people. The project is notable for being Spain's first PV facility to sell all its output on the spot market without long-term energy sales contracts.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 05-08-2019
   - **Details:** A financing package of €29.7 million was secured from Banco Sabadell to build, commission, and operate the El Bonal solar park. This financing is significant as it marks the first time that financing for a 100% merchant project has been closed in the Spanish market.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 05-08-2019
   - **Details:** The commissioning of the El Bonal solar park is scheduled for 2020. The project will be paired with a substation owned by the national grid operator, Red Eléctrica de España.
