### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Romangordo, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2021**: Iberdrola commissioned the Arañuelo III photovoltaic plant with a production capacity of 40 MW. This facility is notable for being Spain's first photovoltaic project to include a storage system, featuring a 3 MW battery with 9 MWh of storage capacity. The Arañuelo complex, located in Romangordo, consists of Arañuelo I, II, and III, totaling 143 MW capacity.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2021**: Iberdrola invested €80 million to develop the Campo Arañuelo complex, which includes the Arañuelo III plant. This investment is part of a broader strategy to enhance renewable energy capacity in the region.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2021**: The Arañuelo III plant was officially commissioned, marking a significant milestone as the first renewable energy project in Spain with integrated battery storage.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2021**: The construction phase of the Campo Arañuelo complex involved 500 workers, with most civil engineering and fieldwork contracted to local companies, thereby stimulating job opportunities in the region.
