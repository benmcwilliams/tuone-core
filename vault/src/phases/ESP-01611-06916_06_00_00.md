### Evolutionary Timeline of the GranSolar Facility in Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum Solar Park, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2017**: GranSolar is part of a consortium that includes Masdar and Fotowatio Renewable Ventures (FRV) to build the third phase of the Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum Solar Park. This phase has a total capacity of 800 MW, contributing to the park's goal of reaching 1 GW by 2020 and 5 GW by 2030.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2017**: The total investment for the Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum Solar Park is expected to reach $13.6 billion, which includes the investments for the various phases of the project, including Phase III.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2017**: Construction for Phase III of the solar park began.
- **April 2018**: The first 200 MW unit of Phase III is scheduled for completion.
- **April 2019**: An additional 300 MW from Phase III is expected to come online.
- **April 2020**: The final 300 MW unit of Phase III is scheduled to be operational.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not provide specific details regarding job creation or job loss associated with the GranSolar facility or the broader project.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- GranSolar is part of a consortium and does not own the facility exclusively. The consortium is led by Masdar, with GranSolar and Fotowatio Renewable Ventures (FRV) as partners.
