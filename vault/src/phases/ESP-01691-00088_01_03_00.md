## ESP-01691-00088

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Basquevolt Facility in Marie Curie Building, Álava Technology Park, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 06/2022
   - **Details:** Basquevolt is focused on the development of solid state batteries, aiming to produce prototype cells and establish a pilot production line by 2025, with full production expected to start in 2027. The facility will leverage a patented composite electrolyte developed by CIC energiGUNE, which will be integral to Basquevolt's technology.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 06/2022
   - **Details:** The initiative is launched with an investment exceeding €700 million (approximately $739 million). This funding is aimed at establishing Basquevolt as a leader in solid state battery technology and supporting the development of sustainable materials and battery cells.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 06/2022
   - **Details:** The Basquevolt initiative was officially launched, marking the beginning of its development phase. Preparatory work had been ongoing for two years prior to this announcement.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 06/2022
   - **Details:** Basquevolt anticipates generating over 800 direct jobs as a result of its operations and investments in the facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 06/2022
   - **Details:** Basquevolt is a collaborative initiative involving the Basque government and several founding investors, including Iberdrola, Enagás, CIE Automotive, EIT InnoEnergy, and CIC energiGUNE. This indicates that the facility is not under exclusive ownership but rather a joint venture among multiple stakeholders. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic plans for the Basquevolt facility as of June 2022, highlighting its focus on solid state battery technology and the collaborative nature of its ownership and investment structure.
