### Evolutionary Timeline of the Stargate Facility in Gran Canaria, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 12-07-2023
   - **Details:** The Stargate facility will produce green hydrogen using a 1 MW Gateway series electrolyser, which is specifically developed for maritime applications. The facility will utilize renewable energy from a 6 MW offshore wind facility to demonstrate the full value chain of green hydrogen. The generated green hydrogen and oxygen will be supplied to the local hospital complex.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 12-07-2023
   - **Details:** Stargate secured a grant of EUR 3 million as part of a larger project budget of EUR 13 million, with EUR 10.7 million funded by the European Union.
