#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2021**: Emalsa, in collaboration with Instituto Tecnologico de Canarias (ITC) and Universidad Europea de Canarias, is studying the feasibility of a **1.53 MW floating solar plant** intended to power the **Piedra Santa desalination plant** in Gran Canaria. The project aims to integrate solar power into the desalination process, potentially covering about **3% of the plant's total power demand**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12/2021**: The planned floating solar project is estimated to require an investment of approximately **€2 million ($2.4 million)**. This investment is tied to the feasibility study and potential future construction of the solar plant.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2021**: The project is currently in the **feasibility-study phase**. No construction or production start dates have been announced yet, as the project is still under evaluation.

#### 4. Labour
