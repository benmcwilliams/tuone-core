### Evolutionary Timeline of the Floating Power Plant Facility in Gran Canaria, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2021**: Floating Power Plant (FPP) announced plans to deploy a 5MW hybrid wind and wave energy system at the Plocan test centre in Gran Canaria. This project aims to demonstrate the world's first multi-megawatt wind and wave energy device, integrating renewable energy solutions for remote islands.
- **June 2024**: FPP purchased a 4.3 MW wind turbine from Siemens Gamesa for its demonstrator project. The project will integrate a 4.3 MW wind turbine, a 0.8 MW wave energy converter, a 1 MW electrolyzer, 48 MWh of energy storage, and a 1.2 MW fuel cell, showcasing a comprehensive renewable energy system.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2024**: The project secured a EUR 26 million grant from the EU Innovation Fund to support its development and deployment at the Plocan test site.
