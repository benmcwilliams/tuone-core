### Evolutionary Timeline of the Instituto Tecnologico de Canarias Facility in Gran Canaria, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: 12-03-2021
  - **Details**: The facility is involved in a feasibility study for a 1.53 MW floating solar plant intended to power the Piedra Santa desalination plant in Gran Canaria. The project aims to integrate a floating PV system with the existing reverse osmosis desalination plant, which is crucial for addressing water scarcity in the Canary Islands.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: 12-03-2021
  - **Details**: The planned floating solar project is expected to require an investment of approximately €2 million ($2.4 million). This investment is aimed at enhancing the energy efficiency of the desalination process by potentially meeting around 3% of the plant's total power demand.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: 12-03-2021
  - **Details**: The project is currently in the feasibility-study phase. The study indicates significant potential for deploying sustainable systems in the water-energy nexus within the Canary Islands.
