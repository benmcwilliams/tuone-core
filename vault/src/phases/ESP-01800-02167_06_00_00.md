## ESP-01800-02167

### Evolutionary Timeline of the BayWa Facility in Andalusia, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10/2018**: The Don Rodrigo solar project, with a capacity of 175MW, is completed. It is noted as one of the most significant subsidy-free solar projects in Europe.
- **05/2020**: The Don Rodrigo 2 solar farm, with a capacity of 50MWp, becomes operational. It is the second subsidy-free solar project in Spain, located near Seville, and features advanced solar technology including GCL's monocrystalline half-cell modules and Huawei inverters.
- **Q4 2025**: The Almodóvar solar park, with a capacity of 95MW, is expected to be powered. This project includes biodiversity measures during construction.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05/2020**: The Don Rodrigo 2 project is backed by a 12-year fixed-price Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with Statkraft, which will purchase a significant portion of the plant's output.
- **07/2024**: The Almodóvar solar park secures a 10-year virtual power purchase agreement (VPPA) with Huhtamaki, indicating ongoing investment in renewable energy projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2018**: The Don Rodrigo project is sold to asset manager MEAG after its completion.
- **01/2020**: The Don Rodrigo 2 project is divested to insurer Talanx after its commissioning.
- **07/2024**: BayWa r.e. sells the 95MW Almodóvar solar PV project to Encavis AG, with expectations for it to be operational by Q4 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **05/2020**: The commissioning of Don Rodrigo 2 occurs during a period of easing lockdown restrictions, suggesting job creation as construction resumes.
- **07/2024**: The ongoing projects, including the Almodóvar solar park, imply job creation in the renewable energy sector, although specific numbers are not provided.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **10/2018**: BayWa r.e. initially owns the Don Rodrigo project before selling it to MEAG.
- **01/2020**: BayWa r.e. sells the Don Rodrigo 2 project to Talanx, indicating a strategy of building and selling projects.
- **07/2024**: The Almodóvar solar park is sold to Encavis AG, continuing the trend of divesting projects after development.
