### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lightsource Facility in Andalusia, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03/2020**: Lightsource BP announced the development of a 250MW cluster of solar farms in Andalusia, which is expected to deliver approximately 500 gigawatt-hours of electricity annually. The facility will utilize bifacial solar panels supplied by Trina Pro and trackers with inverters from Power Electronics. Construction is set to begin shortly after the announcement, with the facility scheduled to be operational by January 2021.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **03/2020**: The project secured a senior debt facility with NatWest Markets to fund the 250MW solar portfolio. This financing is underwritten entirely by NatWest Markets, indicating significant investment in the project. Additionally, a seven-year power purchase agreement (PPA) was established with BP’s European gas and power business for all electricity generated by the facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03/2020**: Lightsource BP confirmed that construction is about to start on the solar cluster, with an operational target date of January 2021. The project was acquired by Lightsource BP in the autumn of the previous year (2019).
