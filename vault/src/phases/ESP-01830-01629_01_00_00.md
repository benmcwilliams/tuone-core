## ESP-01830-01629

### Enlight Renewable Energy Facility Timeline - Cuenca, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2020**: Enlight Renewable Energy initiates its first wind project in Spain, the Gecama wind farm, with a production capacity of 312 MW. The project will utilize Nordex's Delta4000 series turbines, specifically the N149/4.0-4.5 model, designed for local wind conditions.
- **November 2021**: The Gecama project is confirmed to have an installed power of over 300 MW, capable of generating approximately 1,000 GWh annually, sufficient to power around 280,000 homes for a year. The project spans an area of 41 km² and includes extensive infrastructure such as 80 km of roads and various substations.
- **December 2024**: An environmental impact statement is announced for the Gecama Hybrid Plant, which will include a 250.08 MW photovoltaic generation module and a 100 MW battery storage module, aimed at hybridizing with the existing Gecama wind farm.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2021**: The construction of the Gecama wind farm is backed by an investment of 64.3 million euros. This investment is part of the largest single-phase wind project in Spain, highlighting Enlight's commitment to renewable energy development in the region.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2020**: Announcement of the wind turbine supply and installation contract with Nordex for the Gecama project.
- **November 2021**: Construction of the Gecama wind farm is underway, with completion expected by mid-2022. An extension involving the installation of 4 additional turbines is also planned.
- **December 2024**: Environmental impact statement for the Gecama Hybrid Plant is released, indicating progress towards hybridization with the existing wind farm.

#### 4. Labour
- **November 2021**: The construction of the Gecama wind farm is projected to create up to 460 jobs simultaneously during the construction phase.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Enlight Renewable Energy is the sole promoter of the Gecama wind farm, indicating full ownership of the facility. The project represents Enlight's first venture into the Spanish market, establishing its presence in the renewable energy sector in Spain. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones of the Enlight Renewable Energy facility in Cuenca, Spain, focusing on its technological advancements, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
