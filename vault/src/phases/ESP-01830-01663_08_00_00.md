### Evolutionary Timeline of the GE Facility in Cuenca, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 03-12-2020
   - **Details:** GE Renewable Energy secured its first Spanish order for the Cypress turbine for the Cuevas de Valesco wind project in Cuenca. The project has a total capacity of 100MW, consisting of 19 turbines, each with a rated capacity of 5.5MW and a rotor diameter of 158 meters. The project will produce over 300,000 megawatt-hours annually.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 03-12-2020
   - **Details:** The deal includes a 30-year service contract, indicating a long-term investment in the maintenance and operation of the turbines. The project aligns with Spain's National Energy and Climate Plan (PNIEC) to increase wind power capacity.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 03-12-2020
   - **Details:** Announcement of the order for the Cypress turbines for the Cuevas de Valesco wind project, marking a significant milestone as it is the first implementation of this turbine model in Spain.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 03-12-2020
   - **Details:** The production of the Cypress turbine blades will be handled by LM Wind Power, a GE subsidiary in Castellon, Spain, which employs about 650 people. 5. **Ownership Structure:**
