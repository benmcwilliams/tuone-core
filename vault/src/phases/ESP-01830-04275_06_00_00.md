### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sonnedix Facility in Cuenca, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2018**: Sonnedix acquired 12 PV plants in Spain, adding 46.7 MW to its portfolio, which included the 32.9 MW Project Summa located across Badajoz, Albacete, and Cuenca. This acquisition brought Sonnedix's total operating capacity in Spain to 214.2 MW.
- **November 2024**: Sonnedix inaugurated the 150 MW Betierra project in Cuenca, which consists of three 50 MW solar plants sharing interconnection infrastructure. This project marked the company surpassing 1 GW of owned and operated renewable energy capacity in Spain.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2021**: Sonnedix acquired a 300 MW portfolio from RIC Energy, which included the Betierra project in its development stage. The remaining 150 MW of this portfolio was in late development stages.
- **May 2023**: A 10-year Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) was signed with Equinix for the renewable energy generated from the Betierra project.
