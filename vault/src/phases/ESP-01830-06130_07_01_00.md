### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sumitomo Electric Bordnetze Facility in Cuenca, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2024**: Sumitomo Electric Bordnetze (SEBN) announces the construction of a new production facility in Cuenca, Spain, dedicated to manufacturing high-voltage wiring harnesses specifically for Seat and Volkswagen electric cars. This facility will be SEBN's first in Spain and its 23rd globally. The production process will be highly automated to enhance product quality and quantity, addressing the energy and data requirements of electric vehicles.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2024**: The investment in the Cuenca facility is part of SEBN's strategy to secure its position as a major supplier of high-voltage harnesses for the Volkswagen Group. The specific amount of investment is not disclosed in the article.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2024**: Announcement of the new facility's construction. The facility is expected to support the production of high-voltage wiring harnesses for the urban electric car family produced by Seat in Martorell and Volkswagen in Navarra. Production is set to begin at the end of 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2024**: The new facility is projected to create around 350 jobs in the Cuenca region, contributing to local employment and supporting the automotive supply chain.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2024**: SEBN is a subsidiary of the Sumitomo Group, having been taken over in 2006. The facility in Cuenca will be fully owned by Sumitomo Electric Bordnetze, as it is not mentioned as a joint venture or partnership in the article.
