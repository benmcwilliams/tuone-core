## ESP-01830-07472

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nordex Facility in Cuenca, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is involved in the production of wind turbines, specifically supplying 69 foundations for turbines as part of the Gecama Wind Farm project. The project will have an installed power of more than 300 MW, capable of generating approximately 1,000 GWh per year, which is sufficient energy for about 280,000 homes for a year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** The Gecama Wind Farm project has a total investment of 64.3 million euros. Elecnor is responsible for the construction of the Balance of Plant (BOP) and other auxiliary systems, which includes significant infrastructure development.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** The construction of the Gecama Wind Farm is underway, with completion expected by mid-2022. Additionally, Elecnor has been awarded a contract for an extension of the project, which includes the installation of 4 additional turbines.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** The construction works for the Gecama Wind Farm are expected to generate up to 460 jobs simultaneously during the construction phase.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** The Gecama Wind Farm is promoted by Enlight Renewable Energy, with Elecnor handling the construction. The ownership structure specifics regarding Nordex's involvement in terms of exclusivity or joint ventures are not detailed in the article.
