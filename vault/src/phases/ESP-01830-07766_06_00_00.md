### Evolutionary Timeline of the Eranovum Facility in Cuenca, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Production Capacity**: The Sabinar solar project has a total capacity of 238MW, divided into two phases: 
  - **Sabinar I**: 155MW (operational)
  - **Sabinar II**: 83MW (currently under construction)
- **Additional Projects**: The Olmedilla plant, also located in Cuenca, has a capacity of 169MW and has been operational since July 2022.
- **Total Capacity**: Combined, the Sabinar and Olmedilla projects exceed 400MWp, supplying energy to approximately 250,000 households and preventing the emission of 320,000 tonnes of CO2.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Financing Secured**: On **October 4, 2023**, Eranovum, along with partners Noy Fund and Nofar Energy, secured €132 million from a German pension fund for the Sabinar solar project. This financing is significant as it is a substantial amount of senior debt from a single institutional lender, intended to cover a large portion of the project's capital expenditure (Capex).
- **Total Investment**: The combined investment for the Sabinar and Olmedilla projects is over €350 million.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Operational Status**: 
  - **Sabinar I** is operational.
  - **Sabinar II** is under construction as of the latest update in October 2023.
  - **Olmedilla Plant** has been generating clean energy since **July 2022**.
- **Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs)**: In 2022, Eranovum, along with Noy Fund and Nofar Energy, signed two long-term PPAs with Telecor, a subsidiary of El Corte Inglés, to supply approximately half of the group's annual energy consumption.
