## ESP-01830-07960

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Noy Fund Facility in Cuenca, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 04-10-2023**
  - The facility, known as the Sabinar solar project, has a total capacity of **238MW**. It consists of two phases:
    - **Sabinar I**: 155MW (already operational)
    - **Sabinar II**: 83MW (currently under construction)
  - The Sabinar project, along with the **169MW Olmedilla plant**, contributes to a combined capacity of over **400MWp**, supplying energy to approximately **250,000 households** and preventing the emission of **320,000 tonnes of CO2**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: 04-10-2023**
  - Eranovum, in partnership with Noy Fund and Nofar Energy, secured **€132 million** in financing from a German pension fund for the Sabinar project. This financing is a significant portion of the project's capital expenditure (Capex).
  - The total investment for both the Sabinar and Olmedilla projects exceeds **€350 million**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date: 04-10-2023**
  - The Sabinar project is in two phases, with Sabinar I operational and Sabinar II under construction.
  - The Olmedilla plant has been operational since **July 2022**.
  - In **2022**, long-term Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) were signed with El Corte Inglés subsidiary Telecor to cover about half of the group's annual energy consumption from both the Olmedilla and Sabinar plants.
