### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ingeteam Facility in Almaraz, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2022**: The Arañuelo III facility, a 40MW solar plant linked to a 3MW/9MWh battery energy storage system, was inaugurated. This facility is notable for being the first large-scale solar PV power plant integrated with an energy storage system in Spain. The storage system is installed within the solar array using a DC-coupling configuration, allowing for efficient energy management and utilization of excess solar power.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2022**: The project is part of Iberdrola’s larger Campo Arañuelo complex, which includes a total capacity of 143MW across multiple solar systems (Arañuelo I, II, and III). The integration of the battery storage system is part of Iberdrola's strategy to enhance renewable energy capabilities and optimize energy storage.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2022**: The Arañuelo III plant was officially inaugurated, marking its operational status. The facility is designed to produce clean energy sufficient to supply approximately 65,000 homes annually and is expected to avoid emissions of around 41,000 tons of CO2 per year.
