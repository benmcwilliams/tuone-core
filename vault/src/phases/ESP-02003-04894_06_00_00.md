### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ecoenergias del Guadiana Facility in Extremadura, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2020
   - **Details:** The Ecoenergias del Guadiana facility, in partnership with Iberdrola, contributed to the construction of the Nunez de Balboa solar plant, which is the largest photovoltaic (PV) plant in Europe at 500MW capacity. The facility involved the installation of 1.43 million solar panels, 115 inverters, and two substations. The plant is designed to generate enough clean energy to supply approximately 250,000 people annually and prevent around 215,000 tonnes of CO2 emissions.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** April 2020
   - **Details:** The Nunez de Balboa project had a total investment of €300 million (approximately $326 million). This investment was part of Iberdrola's broader strategy to develop 3GW of solar and wind energy in Spain by 2022.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** December 2019
   - **Details:** Construction of the Nunez de Balboa solar plant was completed in December 2019, and it began feeding its first megawatt-hour of power into the Spanish electric grid in April 2020.
