### Evolutionary Timeline of the ib vogt Facility in Extremadura, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2021**: ib vogt completed construction of the 180MWp Bienvenida solar project in Extremadura, Spain. The company has a total capacity of solar projects built or under construction amounting to 2.2GWp, with a development pipeline exceeding 40GWp. The facility is involved in the development, construction, operation, and maintenance of solar energy projects.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2021**: ib vogt signed a €40 million (US$45 million) syndicated loan agreement with Commerzbank AG to support its international growth strategy. This loan is partly covered by the Federal Republic of Germany and the State of Berlin, aimed at enhancing the company's working capital for global operations.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2021**: The completion of the 180MWp Bienvenida solar project in Extremadura was announced. Additionally, ib vogt reached financial close and began work on a 116MWp solar plant in Malaysia.
