### Evolutionary Timeline of the Global Energy Services Facility in Extremadura, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2021
   - **Details:** Global Energy Services (GES) began construction on three solar projects in Extremadura, Spain, with a combined capacity of 131MWp. The projects, named Cáceres 2020, Cáceres 2021, and Cáceres 2023, will utilize a total of 240,800 solar panels and are expected to generate 243,600 MWh annually. The facility will be connected to a 30/220kV step-up substation.
