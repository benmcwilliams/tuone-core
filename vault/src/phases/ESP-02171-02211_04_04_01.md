## ESP-02171-02211

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enagás Facility in La Robla, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2020**: Enagás announced plans for a 150 MW solar project in La Robla, which would include a 32 MW electrolyzer and infrastructure for daily production of 12 tons of green hydrogen. This facility is part of a broader initiative to create environmentally-friendly jobs in areas affected by decarbonization.
- **12/2020**: Enagás and Naturgy revealed plans to construct Spain's largest green hydrogen plant at the site of a closed thermal power plant in La Robla. The facility will feature a 400 MW photovoltaic plant and an electrolyzer of up to 60 MW, aiming to produce up to 9,000 tons of renewable hydrogen annually for local consumption and potential export.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09/2020**: The initial solar project in La Robla was estimated to require an investment of €266 million.
- **12/2020**: While specific investment figures for the green hydrogen plant were not disclosed, it was noted that the project would exceed the €150 million investment of a competing hydrogen complex announced by Iberdrola and Fertiberia.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/2020**: Announcement of the 150 MW solar project and its associated hydrogen production capabilities.
- **12/2020**: Announcement of the partnership between Enagás and Naturgy to develop the green hydrogen plant alongside the 400 MW solar project, with no specific timeline provided for construction or completion.

#### 4. Labour
- **09/2020**: The solar project is part of a plan to create environmentally-friendly jobs in regions impacted by the transition to a decarbonized economy, although specific job creation numbers were not detailed.
- **12/2020**: The announcement of the green hydrogen plant suggests potential job creation, but specific figures were not provided.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **12/2020**: The facility will be a joint venture between Enagás and Naturgy, as they collaborate to develop the green hydrogen plant at the site of Naturgy's former thermal power plant.

This timeline captures the key developments and announcements related to the Enagás facility in La Robla, Spain, focusing on its technological advancements, investments, project milestones, labor implications, and ownership structure.
