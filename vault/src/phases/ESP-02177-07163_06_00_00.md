### Evolutionary Timeline of the Solaria Facility in Basque Country, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2024**: Solaria Energía y Medio Ambiente (Solaria) received administrative construction authorization for the 595MW Garoña photovoltaic (PV) project. This facility is set to generate enough clean energy for 300,000 homes and is part of Solaria's goal to expand its total capacity to 3GW by the end of 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2023**: Solaria secured $1.8 billion (€1.7 billion) in framework financing from the European Investment Bank (EIB) to support the construction of 20 PV power plants across Spain, Italy, and Portugal. This financing includes an initial loan of €278 million for solar plants with a combined capacity of 1.8GW, which will produce 9.29 terawatt-hours of clean energy annually and be operational by the end of 2028.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2024**: Announcement of the authorization for the Garoña solar project, marking a significant milestone in Solaria's expansion efforts.

#### 4. Labour
- **January 2024**: The construction of the Garoña solar park is projected to create 2,400 jobs, with an emphasis on local employment. The project is also expected to contribute to indirect job creation.
