### Evolutionary Timeline of the Acciona Energia Facility in Mallorca, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2022**: ACCIONA Energía inaugurated the "Power to Green Hydrogen Mallorca" project. This included the construction of an electrolysis plant, the development of two photovoltaic plants that feed it, and a green hydrogen refueling station on the island. The electrolyzer is capable of producing more than 330 tons of green hydrogen per year, primarily for direct energy use and injection into the natural gas grid.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **End of December 2020**: ACCIONA, along with Enagás, IDAE, and Cemex, announced the advancement of the Power to Green Hydrogen Mallorca project, indicating a collaborative investment in the electrolyzer and associated infrastructure.
