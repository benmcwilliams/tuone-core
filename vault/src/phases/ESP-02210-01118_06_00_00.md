### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: Iberdrola has partnered with the Oil’Ive Green network to develop agrivoltaic power plants specifically designed for olive cultivation. These plants will be mounted on solar trackers and are intended to protect olive farms from climate-related hazards while enhancing productivity. The agrivoltaic systems will be adapted to support olive trees grown in "fruit hedges" across several hectares.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: The partnership aims to cultivate 50,000 hectares of olive trees by the end of the decade, indicating significant investment in agrivoltaic technology to support this agricultural initiative. Specific financial details regarding the investment were not disclosed.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: The project is in the planning phase, with Iberdrola and Oil’Ive Green identifying farms in regions including Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes to host the agrivoltaic projects. The operational timeline suggests that production from these agrivoltaic plants could begin within three years of installation.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: The agrivoltaic initiative is expected to provide technical and agronomic support to wine growers and olive producers, which may lead to job creation in the agricultural sector as new farms are developed and existing farms are adapted to incorporate agrivoltaic systems.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: Iberdrola is engaged in an exclusive partnership with the Oil’Ive Green network, indicating a collaborative approach rather than full ownership of the facilities. The specific ownership structure of the agrivoltaic plants has not been detailed, but it suggests a joint venture model for the development of these projects.
