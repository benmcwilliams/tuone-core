### Aqualia Facility Evolution Timeline: Isla Verde Wastewater Treatment Plant, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2022**: Aqualia, in collaboration with a consortium including Repsol and other energy and technology companies, announced the Zeppelin project aimed at producing over 135,000 tons of green hydrogen annually. This will be achieved by processing 99 million tons of waste and 50 million tons of municipal wastewater at the Isla Verde Wastewater Treatment Plant.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2022**: The Zeppelin project has a total budget exceeding €7 million, with €4.23 million funded by the Spanish government. This investment is part of a broader initiative to support green hydrogen production in Spain.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2022**: The consortium officially announced the Zeppelin project, which is set to be implemented at the Isla Verde Wastewater Treatment Plant. The project is positioned to contribute significantly to Spain's Hydrogen Roadmap and support the energy needs of the Spanish railway network.
