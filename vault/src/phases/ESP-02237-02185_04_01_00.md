### Evolutionary Timeline of the DH2 Energy Facility in Aragon, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2024**: DH2 Energy announced the development of the Hysencia project in Aragon, which will feature a 35MW electrolysis plant and a 49MWp solar photovoltaic plant. The facility is expected to produce 99GWh of renewable electricity annually to power the electrolyser. Operations are anticipated to begin in **2027**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2024**: The total investment for the hydrogen production network in Extremadura, which includes the Hysencia project, is reported to be €2.25 billion ($2.36 billion). This investment is part of a broader initiative to develop the largest renewable hydrogen production capacity in Spain.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2024**: DH2 Energy received the green light to develop the Hysencia project in Aragon after securing an Integrated Environmental Authorisation.
- **November 2024**: DH2 launched an auction to secure offtake hydrogen from the 35MW plant, calling for companies interested in purchasing the 1,700 tonnes of green hydrogen produced annually.
