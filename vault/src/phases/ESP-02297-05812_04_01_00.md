#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Production**: The Plataforma Solar de Almería is the largest test center in Europe for concentrated solar technologies. It utilizes mirrors to focus sunlight onto a 43-meter tower, achieving temperatures of up to 1,400 °C.
- **Future Developments**: The facility is involved in projects aimed at producing renewable hydrogen using solar energy. The HYDROSOL-beyond project, which started in January 2019 and is set to conclude at the end of 2023, focuses on using the extreme heat generated to initiate chemical processes for hydrogen production without the need for electricity.
