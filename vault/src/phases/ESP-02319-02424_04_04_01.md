## ESP-02319-02424

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Repsol Facility in Bilbao, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2021**: Repsol announced the startup of its first electrolyser in the Basque Country, with a production capacity of 2.5 MW at its Petronor refinery. This facility will produce renewable hydrogen and is part of the Basque Hydrogen Corridor (BH2C) Initiative.
- **2024**: A 10 MW electrolyser is scheduled to be commissioned in the port of Bilbao, in collaboration with Enagás and the Basque Energy Agency (EVE). This electrolyser will produce hydrogen using 100% renewable electricity.
- **2025**: A third electrolyser with a capacity of 100 MW is planned to be commissioned in the Bilbao area to support Petronor's decarbonisation process and the needs of the Basque Hydrogen Corridor.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2021**: The initial electrolyser project involves an investment of €8.9 million, which also covers the construction of necessary infrastructures for the use and distribution of renewable hydrogen.
- **2021-2026**: The BH2C Initiative aims to mobilise €1.431 billion for the execution of 40 projects throughout the hydrogen value chain, with the goal of creating over 1300 jobs.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2021**: The BH2C Initiative was launched, bringing together 80 companies, institutions, and research centres.
- **2H22**: The first electrolyser at the Petronor refinery is expected to start operations.
- **2024**: Commissioning of the 10 MW electrolyser in the port of Bilbao.
- **2025**: Commissioning of the 100 MW electrolyser in the Bilbao area.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2021**: The BH2C Initiative is projected to create more than 1300 jobs as part of its development and execution of hydrogen projects.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Repsol operates the facility in conjunction with its subsidiary Petronor. The facility is not mentioned as a joint venture with other companies, indicating that Repsol has a significant ownership stake in the operations related to the hydrogen production initiatives.
