### Evolutionary Timeline of Haizea Facility in Bilbao, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2019**: Haizea Wind Group confirmed a contract to fabricate 10 monopile foundations for the 30MW Taranto offshore wind farm in Italy. Each monopile is 55 meters long, with a 5-meter diameter and weighing about 400 tonnes.
- **January 2022**: Haizea signed an agreement with Ørsted for the manufacture and supply of XXL monopiles for an undisclosed offshore wind farm, which will double the existing production capacity by extending the fabrication facilities by 56,000 square meters.
- **September 2022**: Haizea and SeAH Wind signed contracts to supply monopile foundations for Ørsted’s 2.8GW Hornsea 3 offshore wind project in the UK, with production starting in late 2024.
- **March 2023**: Haizea was awarded a contract by Iberdrola to supply 50 monopiles for the 1.4GW East Anglia Three offshore wind farm, marking a significant expansion of the facility from 48,800 square meters to over 200,000 square meters.
- **October 2023**: Haizea manufactured the first monopile foundation for Iberdrola’s East Anglia Three offshore wind project.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2022**: The agreement with Ørsted included an investment to duplicate Haizea’s existing production capacity, creating 350 direct jobs.
- **March 2023**: The contract with Iberdrola involved an investment of over €150 million for the expansion of the Haizea facility.
- **August 2024**: Haizea secured a €35 million green loan from the European Investment Bank to enhance manufacturing technologies and R&D for wind turbine components.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2019**: Contract confirmed for the Taranto offshore wind farm.
- **January 2022**: Agreement signed with Ørsted for XXL monopiles, marking the largest contract for Haizea.
- **September 2022**: Contracts signed for Hornsea 3 project.
- **March 2023**: Contract signed with Iberdrola for East Anglia Three, with expansion works commencing.
- **October 2023**: First monopile manufactured for East Anglia Three.

#### 4. Labour
- **January 2022**: The agreement with Ørsted is expected to create 350 direct jobs.
- **March 2023**: The contract with Iberdrola will create an additional 350 direct jobs.
- **October 2023**: Haizea announced plans to hire 295 new workers for its expanded facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Haizea Wind Group operates the facility in Bilbao, with no indication of joint ventures or shared ownership in the articles reviewed. The facility is part of Haizea Wind Group's broader operations, which include manufacturing facilities in Spain, France, and Argentina.
