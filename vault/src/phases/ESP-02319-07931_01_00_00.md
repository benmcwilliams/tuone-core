### Evolutionary Timeline of the Merlin Properties Facility in Bilbao, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 08-04-2021
   - **Details:** Merlin Properties, in collaboration with Edged Energy, announced the development of a carbon-neutral data centre in Bilbao. This facility will support a total load of up to 20MW and will utilize local renewable energy sources, primarily from Merlin’s solar PV installations. The data centre will feature battery energy storage to ensure 24/7 renewable energy use and will consume zero water for cooling, utilizing less energy for cooling and overhead systems compared to average European data centres.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 08-04-2021
   - **Details:** The collaboration between Merlin Properties and Edged Energy is part of a broader initiative to build a network of four renewables-powered data centres across Spain and Portugal, indicating significant investment in renewable energy infrastructure.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 08-04-2021
   - **Details:** The announcement marks the initiation of the project for the data centre in Bilbao, as part of a larger plan that includes similar facilities in Madrid, Barcelona, and Lisbon.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 08-04-2021
   - **Details:** While specific job creation numbers were not mentioned in the article, the collaboration aims to support job creation in the context of the transition towards a low-carbon economy, as highlighted by Iberdrola's chairman.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 08-04-2021
   - **Details:** The facility in Bilbao is a result of a partnership between Merlin Properties and Edged Energy, indicating a joint venture rather than exclusive ownership by Merlin Properties.
