### Evolutionary Timeline of Saitec Offshore Technologies Facility in Bilbao, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2020**: Saitec Offshore Technologies, in collaboration with RWE Renewables, announced the DemoSATH project, which involves testing a floating platform for wind turbines off the Basque Coast. The technology utilizes a twin hull made of modularly prefabricated concrete elements, designed to align with wind and wave directions. The pilot project will feature a 2MW turbine and is expected to provide electricity to approximately 2000 homes.
- **June 2020**: Saitec confirmed the installation and testing of the DemoSATH technology at the Biscay Marine Energy Platform (BiMEP). The structure will be approximately 30 meters wide and 67 meters long, assembled in the port of Bilbao, and towed to an anchorage point 3 km offshore in 85-meter deep water.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2020**: RWE Renewables committed to financing part of the DemoSATH project costs, which aims to test various technological solutions for floating offshore wind, including vessel accessibility and component replacement.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2020**: Announcement of the DemoSATH project, with a planned operational start in Q3 2021.
- **June 2020**: Saitec signed a contract with BiMEP for the testing of DemoSATH technology, with marine operations planned by the end of 2021. The pilot project is set to last three-and-a-half years, including 18 months for planning and construction, followed by a two-year operational phase.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2020**: Saitec's chief technology officer indicated that at least 70% of the contracting budget for the DemoSATH project would be awarded to local industry, suggesting significant job creation opportunities within the Basque region.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2020**: The DemoSATH project is a joint venture between Saitec Offshore Technologies and RWE Renewables, indicating that Saitec does not exclusively own the facility but is collaborating with another entity for this project.
