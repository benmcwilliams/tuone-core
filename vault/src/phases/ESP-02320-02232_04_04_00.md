## ESP-02320-02232

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fertiberia Facility in Huelva, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2020**: Iberdrola and Fertiberia announced a partnership to produce green hydrogen for the Fertiberia plant in Palos de la Frontera, Huelva. The project aims to have enough electrolyzer capacity to produce 800 MW of green hydrogen in four phases by 2027.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2020**: The joint venture Iberlyzer, formed by Iberdrola and Ingeteam, will involve an initial investment of approximately €100 million to produce more than 200 MW of electrolyzers by 2023. The overall investment for the green hydrogen initiative at Fertiberia is projected to be €1.8 billion over the next seven years.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2020**: The announcement of the joint venture Iberlyzer and the green hydrogen project marks a significant milestone, with plans to begin operations in 2021 and produce electrolyzers by 2023.

#### 4. Labour
- **November 2020**: The Iberlyzer project is expected to generate direct qualified employment for 150 people as part of the initial investment and operations.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **November 2020**: The facility is part of a joint venture between Iberdrola and Ingeteam, indicating that it is not solely owned by Fertiberia but involves collaboration with other companies for its operations and development.

This timeline captures the key developments related to the Fertiberia facility in Huelva, focusing on its technological advancements, investments, project milestones, labor implications, and ownership structure as of November 2020.

### Technology and Operations
- "Iberdrola and Fertiberia are planning to have enough electrolyzer capacity to produce 800 MW of green hydrogen in four phases, at the Fertiberia plants in Puertollano (Ciudad Real) and Palos de la Frontera (Huelva), by 2027."
- "The initiative represents an investment of €1.8 billion million in the next seven years."

### Investment and Development
- "The new company is expected to begin operations next year and to produce more than 200 MW of electrolyzers in 2023, with an initial investment of close to €100 million."
- "This initiative started with the launch of the largest green hydrogen complex for industrial use in Europe, which will be operational in just one year in Puertollano, thanks to an investment of €150 million."

### Project Status and Milestones
- "Iberlyzer will begin operations next year and will produce more than 200 MW of electrolyzers in 2023."
- "The project will consist of a 100 MW photovoltaic solar plant, a lithium-ion battery system with a storage capacity of 20 MWh and one of the largest electrolytic hydrogen production systems (20 MW)."

### Labour
- "The project is expected to generate direct qualified employment for 150 people, according to a joint press release from Iberdrola and Ingeteam."

### Ownership Structure
- "Iberdrola and the Spanish inverter maker Ingeteam have set up the joint venture Iberlyzer."
