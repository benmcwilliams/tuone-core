## ESP-02320-02557

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Endesa Facility in Huelva, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **02/2021**: Endesa announced plans to develop a green hydrogen facility in Huelva featuring a 100MW electrolyser powered by 430MW of solar PV. This facility is part of a broader initiative to develop 23 green hydrogen projects across Spain, with a total electrolyser capacity of 340MW aimed at producing 26,000 tonnes of green hydrogen annually.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **02/2021**: The overall investment for the green hydrogen projects across Spain is projected to exceed €2.9 billion. Specific to the Huelva facility, the investment details are tied to the broader hydrogen initiative.
- **03/2021**: Endesa announced the acquisition of a 519MW solar portfolio from Arena Power for approximately €350 million, which will support the energy needs of the Huelva facility and contribute to its green hydrogen production.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **02/2021**: Endesa presented plans to the Spanish government for the development of the green hydrogen facility in Huelva as part of its commitment to renewable energy and decarbonisation.
- **03/2021**: Construction of the solar projects associated with the Huelva facility is expected to begin in 2022, with operational status anticipated by 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **02/2021**: The green hydrogen projects, including the Huelva facility, are expected to create 620 jobs during construction and 320 jobs in operations and maintenance over the next 20 years.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: Endesa, which is owned by the Italian utility Enel, operates the facility through its subsidiary, Enel Green Power España (EGPE). The facility is not a joint venture; Endesa has full ownership of the projects related to the Huelva facility. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic initiatives surrounding the Endesa facility in Huelva, Spain, highlighting its role in the transition to green hydrogen and renewable energy.
