## ESP-02320-04493

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Alter Enersun Facility in Huelva, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Huelva La Luz Project**: Under construction, consisting of 8 photovoltaic plants (Luz I, II, III, IV, V, VI, VII, and VIII) with a total power of 263 MWp, expected to produce 537 GWh/year. Completion is anticipated in July 2024.
- **Puerto La Luz Project**: A photovoltaic installation aimed at supplying renewable energy to green hydrogen plants, with a capacity of up to 200 MW and an estimated production of 360 GWh/year. Currently awaiting environmental and administrative authorization.
- **Villanueva de los Castillejos Solar I**: A project with a capacity of 450 MW, producing over 900 GWh/year, with an investment of approximately 290 million euros.
- **Villanueva de los Castillejos Eólico I**: The first wind farm in Andalusia, estimated at 100 MW, producing 300 GWh of electricity, with an investment of around 130 million euros.
- **30 MWp Photovoltaic Plant for Atlantic Copper**: Planned for construction on Port Authority land, operational by 2026, producing 53 GWh/year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Total Investment**: Approximately 700 million euros for the combined projects in Huelva and Villanueva de los Castillejos.
- **Huelva La Luz Project**: Investment of 160 million euros.
- **Puerto La Luz Project**: Investment of 100 million euros.
- **Villanueva de los Castillejos Solar I**: Investment of approximately 290 million euros.
- **Villanueva de los Castillejos Eólico I**: Investment of around 130 million euros.
- **30 MWp Plant for Atlantic Copper**: Investment of 16 million euros.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Huelva La Luz Project**: Under construction, completion expected in July 2024.
- **Puerto La Luz Project**: Awaiting environmental and administrative authorization.
- **Villanueva de los Castillejos Projects**: Development of both solar and wind projects announced, with significant investments and expected job creation.
- **30 MWp Plant for Atlantic Copper**: Agreement signed, operational by 2026.

#### 4. Labour
- **Huelva La Luz Project**: Expected to create between 450 and 500 jobs during construction and 20 jobs during operation.
- **Puerto La Luz Project**: Job creation anticipated during construction and operation phases.
- **Villanueva de los Castillejos Projects**: Up to 1,000 jobs expected during the construction phase of both the solar and wind projects.
- **30 MWp Plant for Atlantic Copper**: Contributes to the sustainability goals of Atlantic Copper, indirectly supporting job stability in the region.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Alter Enersun**: The facility is owned by Alter Enersun, which is a company owned by CL Grupo Industrial and Senergy Group (Seu Global Energía). The ownership structure indicates that Alter Enersun is not exclusively owned but is part of a larger corporate group.
