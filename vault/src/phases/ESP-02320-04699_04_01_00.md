### Evolutionary Timeline of the Cepsa Facility in Huelva, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2024**: Cepsa is developing a green hydrogen production facility at La Rábida Energy Park in Palos de la Frontera, Huelva. The facility aims for a total production capacity of **400 MW** of green hydrogen as part of a larger plan for **2 GW** by 2030, with **1 GW** planned for Huelva and **1 GW** for Cádiz. The first phase of this project is still pending a final investment decision.
