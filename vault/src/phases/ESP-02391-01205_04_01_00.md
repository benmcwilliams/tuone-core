## ESP-02391-01205

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Naturgy Facility in Asturias, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 08-02-2021
   - **Details**: Naturgy, in collaboration with Enagás, is studying the production of green hydrogen from a combination of a 250 MW floating offshore wind farm and a 100 MW onshore wind farm in Asturias. The project aims to develop an electrolysis and renewable energy plant, with initial deployment on a demo scale of 50 MW offshore and 100 MW onshore wind power, alongside a 5 MW offshore electrolyzer and a 100 MW onshore electrolyzer. The project has the potential to expand to a total of 350 MW of wind power and additional electrolyzer capacity in future phases.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: 08-02-2021
   - **Details**: The project is part of the Green Crane initiative and has been submitted as a candidate for the Important Project of Common European Interest (IPCEI). Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the article, but the project is expected to significantly contribute to the local economy and energy transition.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: 08-02-2021
   - **Details**: The project is in the study phase, with plans for construction and operation that could create over 1,500 jobs. The initiative is aimed at establishing the first large-scale European green hydrogen production and transport chains.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: 08-02-2021
   - **Details**: The project is expected to create more than 1,500 jobs during its construction and operational phases, contributing to local employment and economic growth.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: 08-02-2021
   - **Details**: The facility is a collaborative project between Naturgy and Enagás, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by Naturgy.
