## ESP-02391-02889

### Exiom Facility Timeline in Asturias, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **09-10-2023**: Exiom, in collaboration with Iberdrola, announced the establishment of a tunnel oxide passivated contact (TOPCon) module assembly plant in Asturias, Spain, with an annual nameplate capacity of 500MW. The facility is currently undergoing refurbishment to adapt former steel industry infrastructure for solar module production. The first batch of specialized machinery, primarily laminating machines, has been received.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **28-02-2024**: The Spanish Ministry of Ecological Transition (MITECO) unveiled a €750 million incentive scheme aimed at boosting renewables and storage manufacturing, which includes support for projects like the Exiom facility. This funding is part of Spain’s recovery and resilience plan and is intended to strengthen domestic manufacturing capabilities.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **09-10-2023**: Announcement of the construction of the 500MW TOPCon module assembly plant.
   - **28-02-2024**: Exiom announced expectations for the plant to be operational by Spring 2024, following the receipt of equipment and ongoing refurbishment.

4. **Labour**
   - Specific job creation or job loss details related to the Exiom facility have not been disclosed in the articles.

5. **Ownership Structure**
   - The facility is a joint venture between Exiom and Iberdrola, indicating that Exiom does not own the facility exclusively.
