### Evolutionary Timeline of the HyDeal España Facility in Asturias, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2022:** The HyDeal España facility is launched with a planned electrolysis capacity of 7.4GW, powered by 9.5GW of solar energy, aiming to start production in 2025. This facility is part of a larger 67GW project under the HyDeal Ambition platform, which seeks to reduce the cost of green hydrogen to be competitive with fossil fuels.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **February 2022:** The project has secured guaranteed offtakers, including ArcelorMittal and Fertiberia, indicating significant investment backing.    - **February 2022:** The facility's launch is announced, marking it as the first industrial implementation of the HyDeal Ambition platform.
