#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-07-2021**: Windar, in collaboration with Navantia, is set to construct a new monopile facility in Fene, Galicia, to produce 130 offshore wind turbine monopiles. - **24-09-2024**: Windar begins preparatory work for a new monopile factory in Asturias, with plans to manufacture monopiles up to 12.5 meters in diameter, 3,500 tonnes, and 130 meters in length.
