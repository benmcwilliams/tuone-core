### Evolutionary Timeline of the Repsol Facility in Santander, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2021**: Repsol announced the development of a pilot project to produce green hydrogen using floating photovoltaic (PV) technology. This project, known as the Bahía H2 Offshore project, will focus on designing and constructing a prototype plant for offshore hydrogen and green ammonia production, powered by floating solar arrays. The green fuel produced will be utilized for vessels and equipment in the Port of Santander. The project is set to be completed by **December 2023**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2021**: The total budget for the Bahía H2 Offshore project is **€4.5 million** (approximately **$5.5 million**). This investment is aimed at developing the necessary technology and infrastructure for the production of green hydrogen and ammonia.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2021**: Announcement of the Bahía H2 Offshore project, with a completion target set for **December 2023**. The project will occupy a water surface owned by the Santander Port Authority and will involve testing technological solutions in the port area before potential application in offshore locations.
