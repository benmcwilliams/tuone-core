### Evolutionary Timeline of the Galician Association of Metal Industries and Associated Technologies Facility in Santander, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2024**: The facility is involved in a project selected by the Hydrogen PERTE program, which aims to develop a system utilizing electricity from offshore wind farms for hydrogen and renewable ammonia production. A floating barge will be deployed at the Port of Santander for on-site transformation of offshore wind into fuel for maritime transport and industrial purposes.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2024**: The project is funded with €7.7 million from Spain's Ministry of Industry, Energy and Tourism through IDAE, supporting the development of hydrogen and renewable ammonia production.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2024**: The Atlantic Wind Supercluster, which includes the Galician Association of Metal Industries and Associated Technologies and about 20 partners, has been selected to develop the hydrogen production system. The floating barge is expected to be operational by the end of the year.
