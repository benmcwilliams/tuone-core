## ESP-02448-01118

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Castellón, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-11-2020**: Iberdrola announced the installation of 7,800 PV modules for a solar project at its combined-cycle power plant in Castellón, with a total capacity of 3.5 MW. The project is expected to generate around 50,000 MWh of electricity per year.
- **30-04-2021**: Iberdrola, in collaboration with BP and Enagás, planned to install a 20 MW electrolyzer for green hydrogen production at BP's plant in Castellón, powered by a 40 MW solar plant. Future phases may increase electrolysis capacity to 115 MW.
- **13-09-2024**: Iberdrola reached a final investment decision with BP for a 25 MW green hydrogen project at BP's refinery in Castellón, set to start in late 2026. The project will feature a 25 MW electrolyzer powered by renewable energy.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05-11-2020**: Iberdrola invested €2.3 million ($2.7 million) in the solar project at the combined-cycle power plant.
- **13-09-2024**: The joint venture, Castellón Green Hydrogen S.L., will benefit from €15 million ($16.6 million) in funding from the Spanish government and NextGenerationEU for the green hydrogen project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05-11-2020**: Announcement of the solar project at the combined-cycle power plant.
- **30-04-2021**: Agreement for the largest green hydrogen production project in the Valencian Community.
- **13-09-2024**: Final investment decision for the 25 MW green hydrogen project, with construction set to begin in early 2026.

#### 4. Labour
- **05-11-2020**: The construction of the solar project involved 50 workers from various companies, including regional suppliers.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **13-09-2024**: The joint venture for the green hydrogen project, Castellón Green Hydrogen S.L., is equally owned by Iberdrola and BP.
