### Evolutionary Timeline of the LM Wind Power Facility in Castellón, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09-12-2020**: The LM Wind Power facility in Castellón is identified as a production site for the Cypress two-piece wind turbine blades. This facility is part of a larger project that includes the production of wind turbine towers in Spain and nacelles in Germany. The Cypress platform aims to enhance the efficiency of wind energy generation in Spain.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **31-01-2022**: LM Wind Power, in collaboration with GE Renewable Energy, Endesa, PreZero Espana, and Reciclalia Composite, is involved in a project to establish the first wind blade recycling plant in Spain. The project has a budget of €8.5 million and aims to promote the circular economy by recycling materials from wind turbine blades.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **31-01-2022**: The construction of the recycling facility is set to begin in 2023, with the aim of becoming operational by early 2024. This facility will utilize around 50% of the excess fibreglass generated during the blade manufacturing process at the Castellón plant.

#### 4. Labour
- **31-01-2022**: The new recycling facility is expected to create 30 direct jobs upon becoming operational in 2024, contributing to local employment in the Castellón region.
