### Evolutionary Timeline of the Naturgy Facility in Castilla-La Mancha, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2022**: Naturgy is involved in the construction of renewable generation projects, including a photovoltaic plant named FV Canredondo in Castilla-La Mancha, contributing to a total of 683 MW of new power across various locations in Spain. The facility in Castilla-La Mancha specifically has a production capacity of **21.7 MW**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2022**: The overall investment for the 683 MW projects, including those in Castilla-La Mancha, amounts to **528 million euros**. This investment is part of Naturgy's strategy to expand its renewable energy portfolio.

- **January 2023**: Naturgy receives a favorable Environmental Impact Declaration (DIA) for additional projects, including the Zorita I and II (100 MW) and Ocaña (50 MW) in Castilla-La Mancha, as part of a broader strategy to add **3 GW of new renewable power in Spain** between 2023 and 2025.
