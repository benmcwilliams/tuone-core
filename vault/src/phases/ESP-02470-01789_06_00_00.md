#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-12-2023**: Masdar, in collaboration with Verbund, announced plans to analyze the feasibility of building one of Europe’s largest green hydrogen production plants in Castilla-La Mancha. The facility aims to produce green hydrogen to decarbonize hard-to-abate industrial sectors across Europe, with the potential to displace up to one million tonnes of carbon emissions annually. The plant is expected to be operational by the end of the decade and may be powered by energy from a planned gigawatt-scale solar farm in the region.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
