## ESP-02546-08182

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Windar Facility in Avilés, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06-07-2021**: Windar, in collaboration with Navantia, is set to establish an XXL monopile factory at the Navantia shipyard in Fene, A Coruña. This factory will produce monopiles over 100 meters long and weighing up to 2,500 tonnes, enhancing capabilities in fixed marine foundations.
- **13-07-2021**: Windar is contracted to produce transition pieces for the Baltic Eagle offshore wind farm in Germany, with production starting in October 2021 and completion expected by December 2022.
- **29-11-2022**: Windar is involved in manufacturing 248 piles for the Dieppe le Tréport wind farm in France, with fabrication works beginning in the first quarter of 2023.
- **03-01-2023**: Windar completes the first phase of works at the Saint-Brieuc wind farm in France, having manufactured and installed 65% of the piles and 40% of the offshore wind platforms.
- **19-05-2023**: Windar is contracted to manufacture 95 transition pieces for the East Anglia Three offshore wind farm in the UK, with production expected to begin in June 2023.
- **01-06-2023**: Windar ships the first transition parts for the Vineyard Wind I project in the US, marking significant progress in its offshore wind production capabilities.
- **11-01-2024**: Windar transfers the first transition piece for the East Anglia Three project to the Port of Avilés for completion, with production expected to finish by mid-2025.
- **27-06-2024**: Windar's facility in Avilés is responsible for producing transition pieces for the East Anglia Three project, with ongoing contracts for various offshore wind projects.
- **24-09-2024**: Windar announces plans to construct a new monopile factory in Avilés, with an annual capacity of 100-120 monopiles, aimed at enhancing production for future offshore wind projects.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06-07-2021**: The agreement between Iberdrola and the Navantia-Windar consortium includes a potential investment of up to €400 million for the production of monopiles between 2023 and 2025.
- **19-05-2023**: The contract for the East Anglia Three project is expected to create over 2,000,000 working hours over 23 months, indicating significant investment in labor and resources.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06-07-2021**: Framework agreement signed for the establishment of the XXL monopile factory.
- **13-07-2021**: Contract awarded for the Baltic Eagle offshore wind farm, with production starting in October 2021.
- **29-11-2022**: Capacity reservation agreement for the Dieppe le Tréport wind farm.
- **03-01-2023**: Completion of the first phase of the Saint-Brieuc wind farm.
- **19-05-2023**: Contract signed for the East Anglia Three offshore wind farm.
- **24-09-2024**: Announcement of a new monopile factory construction in Avilés.

#### 4. Labour
- **13-07-2021**: The Baltic Eagle project is expected to create 800 jobs and 1.3 million man-hours.
- **19-05-2023**: The East Anglia Three project is projected to create 400 jobs during the production of transition pieces.
- **24-09-2024**: The new monopile factory is expected to add 600 new jobs to the existing workforce in Avilés.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Windar operates as a joint venture with Navantia for various projects, including the production of monopiles and transition pieces. The facility in Avilés is part of Windar's operations, which is a subsidiary of the CS WIND group as of December 2023, indicating a change in ownership structure.
