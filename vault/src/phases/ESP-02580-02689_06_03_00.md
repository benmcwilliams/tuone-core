### Evolutionary Timeline of Bashmor Engineering S.L. Facility in Bembimbre, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2018:** Bashmor Engineering S.L. acquired the production unit of the previously idled solar cell manufacturer, Cel Celis, in Bembimbre. The facility's production capacity was planned to be doubled from 35 MW to 70 MW within a year. The facility is focused on manufacturing solar cells, with products intended for distribution mainly in Europe and the Middle East.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **March 2018:** Bashmor Engineering purchased the manufacturing unit for €3.3 million and committed to an additional investment of €2 million to restart operations. This investment was financially supported by Banco Santander and Abanca.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 15, 2018:** Bashmor Engineering acquired the production assets of Cel Celis in a public auction.
   - **March 20, 2018:** Announcement of plans to restart manufacturing activities and upgrade the production line over the next two months.

4. **Labour:**
   - **March 2018:** The facility is expected to create around 40 jobs as a result of the restart of manufacturing operations.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **March 2018:** Bashmor Engineering S.L. owns the facility exclusively following the acquisition of Cel Celis's production assets.
