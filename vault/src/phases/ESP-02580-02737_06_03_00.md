### Evolutionary Timeline of the Cel Celis Facility in Bembimbre, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2018:** The facility, previously idled since 2012, was acquired by Bashmor Engineering. The production capacity was planned to be doubled from 35 MW to 70 MW within a year. The facility produces solar cells, with modules intended for distribution mainly in Europe and the Middle East.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **March 2018:** Bashmor Engineering acquired the production assets for €3.3 million and committed to invest an additional €2 million to restart operations. The acquisition was financially supported by Banco Santander and Abanca.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 15, 2018:** Bashmor Engineering acquired the production unit of Cel Celis in a public auction.
   - **March 20, 2018:** Announcement of plans to restart manufacturing activities and upgrade the production line over the next two months.

4. **Labour:**
   - **March 2018:** It was reported that around 40 jobs were expected to be created at the factory following the restart of operations.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **March 2018:** The facility is now owned by Bashmor Engineering, indicating a change in ownership from Cel Celis to Bashmor Engineering following the acquisition.
