### Evolutionary Timeline of the Canadian Solar Facility in Cerro Verde, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2019**: Canadian Solar announced a module supply deal to provide 500 MW of its 144-cell polycrystalline CS3U-P KuMax modules for two large-scale photovoltaic (PV) plants in southern Spain, including the 300 MW Talayuela Solar project located in Cerro Verde. The Talayuela project is expected to generate approximately 588.26 GWh of electricity annually.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2019**: The Talayuela Solar project was designated as a strategic project by the Extremadura government, which facilitated its development. The Spanish Ministry of the Ecological Transition granted final approval for the project in August 2019.
