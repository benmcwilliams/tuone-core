## ESP-02730-01118

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Langreo, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: Iberdrola and Exiom Solution announced plans to build a solar module factory in Langreo, Spain, which will be one of Europe's first “industrial-scale” facilities. The initial production capacity is set at **500 MW** for tunnel oxide passivated contact (TOPCon) modules.
- **April 2023**: The facility is confirmed to be the first large photovoltaic construction plant in Spain, with operations expected to commence in **2023**.
- **November 2023**: The first machinery shipment for the facility was received, primarily consisting of laminating machines.
- **February 2024**: The inauguration of the solar panel factory is scheduled for **spring 2024**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2023**: An initial investment of **€20 million** (approximately $21.3 million) was announced for the construction of the facility.
- **February 2024**: The project received a subsidy of **€1.2 million** from the Institute for the Just Transition (ITJ) to support job-generating business projects in just transition zones.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2023**: Announcement of the partnership between Iberdrola and Exiom to build the facility.
- **April 2023**: Confirmation of the facility's operational start in **2023**.
- **February 2024**: Scheduled inauguration of the factory in **spring 2024**.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2023**: The facility is expected to create **115 direct jobs** in the Asturian region, contributing to the economic revival of the mining areas.
- **February 2024**: The project aims to further support job creation through the ITJ subsidy aimed at promoting alternative development in just transition zones.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2023**: The facility is a joint venture between Iberdrola and Exiom, with Exiom being a manufacturer with **100% Spanish capital** based in Asturias. The partnership aims to enhance competitive manufacturing within the EU solar industry.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones related to the Iberdrola facility in Langreo, Spain, focusing on its technology, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
