### Evolutionary Timeline of the Naturgy Facility in Badajoz, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03/2023**: Naturgy is advancing the construction of the Merengue II wind farm and the Puerta del Jerte photovoltaic plant in Plasencia, Extremadura. The Merengue II wind farm will consist of 11 wind turbines with a total capacity of 50 MW, producing 128 GWh annually. The Puerta del Jerte photovoltaic plant will have a capacity of 30 MW, generating 62 GWh per year. Together, these facilities will add 80 MW to the existing 62 MW already operational in Extremadura.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **03/2023**: The total investment for the Merengue II wind farm and Puerta del Jerte photovoltaic plant is approximately 57 million euros, with 41 million euros allocated for the wind farm and 16 million euros for the photovoltaic plant.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03/2023**: The construction of both facilities is in progress, with the Merengue II wind farm's turbines scheduled for installation in April 2023. The commissioning of both projects is expected in the third quarter of 2023.

#### 4. Labour
- **03/2023**: The construction of the Merengue II wind farm and Puerta del Jerte photovoltaic plant has created over 860 direct and indirect jobs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **03/2023**: Naturgy is the sole owner of the facilities mentioned, as there is no indication of joint ventures or shared ownership in the articles.
