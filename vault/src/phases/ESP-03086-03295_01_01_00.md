### Evolutionary Timeline of the Phi4tech Facility in Badajoz, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2021**: The Phi4tech facility is announced to be part of a project at the Southwest European Logistics Platform in Badajoz, covering a total surface area of 177,000 square meters. The facility is set to produce batteries and cathodes, with an initial production capacity of 2 GW expected by 2023. This capacity is planned to gradually increase to 10 GW by 2025, with an option to expand to 20 GW by 2027.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2021**: The project is expected to involve an investment of €400 million. This investment is part of a broader initiative that includes the construction of a cathode factory and the extraction of lithium and nickel throughout Spain.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2021**: The project is officially announced, marking the beginning of the development phase for the Phi4tech facility in Badajoz.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2021**: While specific job creation numbers are not mentioned in the articles, the scale of the investment and the planned expansions suggest significant job creation potential as the facility develops and ramps up production.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **March 2021**: The project is described as a private initiative involving Phi4tech, Lithium Iberia, and individual partners related to the Aguablanca mine. This indicates that Phi4tech does not own the facility exclusively but is part of a collaborative effort with other entities.
