### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sonnedix Facility in Badajoz, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2018**: Sonnedix announced the acquisition of 12 PV plants in Spain, including the 1.1 MW Olivenza plant in Badajoz, contributing to a total of 214.2 MW operational capacity in Spain.
- **June 2020**: Sonnedix planned a new 50 MW solar plant in Badajoz, which will supply around 100 GWh of green electricity annually starting in 2021.
- **July 2021**: Construction began on the 50 MW Sonnedix Los Frailes solar park in Badajoz, which will cover 111 hectares and utilize over 110,000 monocrystalline solar panels. The project is expected to generate approximately 102 GWh of solar electricity per year.
- **December 2022**: The Sonnedix Los Frailes solar plant became operational.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2020**: Sonnedix secured a 10-year power purchase agreement (PPA) with Statkraft for the 50 MW Badajoz project, marking it as Sonnedix's first unsubsidized project in Spain.
- **July 2021**: The construction of the Los Frailes project was announced, contributing to Sonnedix's Spanish portfolio exceeding 1 GW.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2018**: Acquisition of 12 PV plants finalized, including the Olivenza plant in Badajoz.
- **June 2020**: Announcement of the planned 50 MW solar plant in Badajoz.
- **July 2021**: Construction commenced on the Sonnedix Los Frailes solar park.
- **December 2022**: The Sonnedix Los Frailes solar plant officially began operations.
- **April 2023**: Inauguration ceremony for the 50 MW plant attended by local government officials.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2021**: The construction of the Los Frailes solar park is expected to create approximately 250 jobs during the construction phase.
- **April 2023**: The construction phase also generated around 250 new jobs in Badajoz, aligning with Sonnedix's commitment to the local economy.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2023**: The Los Frailes solar plant is noted as the first project that Sonnedix España has fully developed, built, and managed in Spain, indicating full ownership of the facility by Sonnedix.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones of the Sonnedix facility in Badajoz, Spain, highlighting its growth in technology, investment, project status, labor impact, and ownership structure.
