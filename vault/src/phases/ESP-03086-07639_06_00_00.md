### Evolutionary Timeline of the Cavalum SGPS Facility in Badajoz, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 10-10-2012
   - **Details:** Cavalum SGPS, in partnership with S.A.G. Solarstrom and Valsolar 2006, is set to develop and construct four photovoltaic (PV) plants in Badajoz, Spain. The total output of these plants is projected to be around 440 MWp, with the potential to generate approximately 660,000 MWh of energy annually after completion.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 10-10-2012
   - **Details:** The project has a total investment volume in the three-digit million range. The Extremadura region’s government has guaranteed support for the project, which includes cooperation with local authorities, energy providers, and the network operator REE.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 10-10-2012
   - **Details:** The joint venture agreement is subject to various contractual conditions, with construction anticipated to start in the second half of 2013, pending approval procedures.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 10-10-2012
   - **Details:** Specific job creation or job loss details related to this project have not been mentioned in the article.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 10-10-2012
   - **Details:** S.A.G. Solarstrom will hold a 51% stake in the joint venture company through its subsidiary, indicating that the facility is not under exclusive ownership by Cavalum SGPS but is part of a joint venture.
