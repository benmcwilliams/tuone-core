### Evolutionary Timeline of Castleton Commodities International LLC Facility in Amorebieta-Boroa, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2020
   - **Details:** Castleton Commodities International LLC (CCI) announced the development of a 20 MW green hydrogen plant co-located at the existing Amorebieta-Boroa combined cycle gas turbine (CCGT) power plant. The facility is expected to produce 1500 tons of green hydrogen annually from 100% renewable sources, which will help offset over 12,000 tons of CO2 emissions per year. A portion of the hydrogen will be utilized by the power plant to reduce its CO2 emissions, while the remainder will be injected into Nortegas' gas distribution network.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2020
   - **Details:** The project is backed by CCI in partnership with White Summit Capital, Bizkaia Energía, Nortegas, and SENER. The investment aims to accelerate the energy transition in Europe through the implementation of hydrogen technology.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** December 2020
   - **Details:** The project is the first of its kind to be co-located adjacent to a CCGT and is expected to commence commercial operations by the end of 2022.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
