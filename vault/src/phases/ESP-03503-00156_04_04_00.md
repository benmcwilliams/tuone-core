## ESP-03503-00156

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Cummins Facility in Palos de la Frontera, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
   - **Details:** Cummins is collaborating with Iberdrola to develop a green hydrogen project in Palos de la Frontera. The facility will serve as the electrolyzer supplier for a 230MW green hydrogen project planned for Fertiberia. Cummins is also establishing a new €50 million PEM electrolyzer plant in Spain, with an initial production capacity of approximately 500MW per year, scalable to over 1GW/year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
   - **Details:** The new PEM electrolyzer plant represents a €50 million investment by Cummins. This investment is part of a broader initiative to enhance green hydrogen production capabilities in Spain and Europe.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is anticipated to open in 2023. The project is currently in the site selection phase within the Guadalajara area of Castilla-La Mancha.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
   - **Details:** The opening of the facility is expected to create 350 new jobs as production ramps up.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
   - **Details:** Cummins is collaborating with Iberdrola on this project, indicating a partnership rather than exclusive ownership of the facility. The specific ownership structure of the facility itself is not detailed, but it is part of a joint initiative with Iberdrola. 

This timeline captures the key developments related to the Cummins facility in Palos de la Frontera, focusing on its technology, investments, project milestones, labor implications, and ownership structure as of the latest available information.
