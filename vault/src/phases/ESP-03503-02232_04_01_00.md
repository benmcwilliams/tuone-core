### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fertiberia Facility in Palos de la Frontera, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2020
   - **Details:** Fertiberia, in partnership with Iberdrola, is set to develop green hydrogen technology at its facility in Palos de la Frontera. The collaboration aims to install a significant production capacity of green hydrogen, contributing to the transformation of Spain into a leader in this technology. The project is part of a broader initiative that includes the development of three additional projects between 2023 and 2027, which could potentially deliver 40 times the capacity of the initial plant in Puertollano.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** October 2020
   - **Details:** The partnership between Iberdrola and Fertiberia involves a total investment of €1.8 billion over seven years for the green hydrogen initiative. This investment is aimed at enhancing the production capabilities at Fertiberia's plants, including the one in Palos de la Frontera.
