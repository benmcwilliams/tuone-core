### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nucera Facility in Palos de la Frontera, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: May 2024
   - **Details**: Nucera has been contracted to provide a basic engineering design package for a 400-MW green hydrogen production project at Cepsa’s La Rabida Energy Park. The facility will utilize 15 units of Nucera's 20-MW alkaline Scalum electrolysers, contributing to a total capacity of 300 MW, which will produce up to 47,000 tonnes of green hydrogen annually. The remaining 100 MW will employ Siemens Energy's Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) technology.
