#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2018**: The facility, a hybrid wind-solar project, was completed, combining a 3 MW Vestas V112 wind turbine with a 372 kW solar photovoltaic (PV) array. This project is located at EDP’s Janda III wind farm near Cadiz, Andalucía. The PV power is fed to the turbine as direct current, which is then converted for grid export. The project aims to demonstrate the benefits of integrating wind and solar technologies to enhance energy production and reduce costs.
