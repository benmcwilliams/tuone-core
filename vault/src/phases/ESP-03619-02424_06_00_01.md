### Evolutionary Timeline of the Repsol Facility in Cádiz, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03-12-2019**: Repsol announced plans to integrate renewable energy into its refining operations, including the production of green hydrogen. The company revealed a 204MW photovoltaic (PV) project named Sigma in Cádiz as part of a larger 794MW renewable pipeline in Spain.
- **01-03-2023**: Repsol's renewable portfolio in Spain reached a total of 3,200 MW, including the Sigma project in Cádiz, which is part of a broader strategy to achieve 6,000 MW of renewable installed capacity by 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **03-12-2019**: Repsol announced a new 1.6GW renewable energy pipeline, which includes the Sigma PV project in Cádiz. The company aims to increase its low-carbon electricity generation target from 3GW to 7.5GW by 2025.
- **22-04-2020**: The Sigma project in Cádiz was confirmed to have obtained land and guaranteed connection to Spain’s national grid, indicating readiness for development.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03-12-2019**: Announcement of the Sigma PV project in Cádiz as part of a larger renewable energy strategy.
- **22-04-2020**: Confirmation that the Sigma project has secured land and grid connection, indicating progress towards construction.
- **01-03-2023**: The Sigma project is included in Repsol's total renewable capacity of 3,200 MW, indicating it is at an advanced stage of development.

#### 4. Labour
- **22-04-2020**: Repsol indicated that it has significantly grown its team of green energy experts to support its renewable energy projects, including the Sigma project.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
