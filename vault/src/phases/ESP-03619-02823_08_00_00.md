### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDPR Facility in Cadiz, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2018**: EDPR, in collaboration with Vestas, developed a wind/solar hybrid demonstration project at the Janda 3 wind farm in Cadiz. The facility combines a Vestas V112-3.0MW turbine with an additional 372 kW of solar photovoltaic capacity, resulting in a total capacity of 3.3 MW. The solar power is fed directly to the turbine, which converts it for grid export. This hybrid model aims to enhance capacity factor and energy yield.
