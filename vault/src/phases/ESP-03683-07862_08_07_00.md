### Evolutionary Timeline of Haizea Facility in Port of Bilbao, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2022**: Haizea Wind Group manufactures and paints wind turbine towers at its facility in the Port of Bilbao for the Saint-Brieuc offshore wind farm. The towers are then shipped to the Port of Brest for assembly of internal components.
- **March 2023**: Haizea Wind Group is contracted to supply 50 monopiles for the East Anglia Three offshore wind farm in the UK. The production of these monopiles will also take place at the Port of Bilbao facility.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2023**: A foundation stone is laid for the expansion of the manufacturing plant at the Port of Bilbao, with an investment exceeding €150 million. This expansion will increase the facility's area from 48,800 m² to over 200,000 m².

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2022**: Haizea begins manufacturing wind turbine towers for the Saint-Brieuc offshore wind farm, with shipments scheduled to start in October 2022.
- **March 2023**: The contract for supplying monopiles to the East Anglia Three project is awarded, marking a significant milestone in Haizea's operations.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2023**: The expansion of the manufacturing plant is expected to create nearly 350 direct jobs at the Haizea facility in Bilbao.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The Haizea facility in the Port of Bilbao is owned exclusively by Haizea Wind Group, as indicated by the contracts and operations described in the articles.
