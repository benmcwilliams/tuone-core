### Evolutionary Timeline of Acciona Energia Facility in Cordoba, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **July 2011:** Acciona Energy activated the Palma del Río I solar thermal plant in Cordoba, Spain. The facility has a production capacity of 50MW and is comprised of 792 solar collectors spanning 135 hectares. This addition brings Acciona’s Concentrated Solar Power (CSP) portfolio in Spain to a total of 200MW. The facility is designed to generate enough electricity to meet the energy requirements of approximately 70,000 homes in southern Spain.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **July 2011:** A total investment of around €500 million was made for the development of the Palma del Río I and its twin plant, Palma del Río II, which was connected to the grid in December 2010.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **July 2011:** The Palma del Río I solar thermal plant was officially activated.
   - **December 2010:** The twin plant, Palma del Río II, was connected to the grid.
