## ESP-03786-01118

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Soria, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2021**: Iberdrola commissioned the Herrera II wind complex in Castilla y León, consisting of three farms (La Huesca, Valdesantos, and Orbanejoa) with a total installed capacity of **63 MW** from **14 wind turbines**. The facility is designed to supply electricity to approximately **60,000 households** annually and prevent the release of **50,000 tons of CO2** per year. The wind turbines installed are the **SG 4.5-145**, which are the largest onshore wind turbines in Spain, with a unit power of **4.5 MW**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2021**: Iberdrola invested **€70 million** in the development of the Herrera II wind complex, contributing to the local industrial sector and employing around **800 people** during the construction phase. The company has plans to invest over **€2 billion** in renewable energy projects in the region by 2025.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2021**: The Herrera II wind complex was officially commissioned, marking a significant milestone in Iberdrola's renewable energy projects in Spain. The facility is part of a broader investment cycle where Iberdrola aims to develop more than **2400 MW** of renewable energy in the region.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2021**: The development of the Herrera II wind complex created approximately **800 jobs** during the construction phase, with a significant portion of the work being entrusted to local companies.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2023**: Iberdrola and MAPFRE established a co-investment vehicle named **Energías Renovables Ibermap**, which includes **295 MW** of wind power and **150 MW** of photovoltaic capacity. Iberdrola holds a **51%** majority stake in this joint venture, while MAPFRE holds **49%**. This indicates that while Iberdrola has a significant ownership interest in the renewable energy projects, it is not exclusively owning the facility in collaboration with MAPFRE.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones of the Iberdrola facility in Soria, Spain, focusing on its technology, investments, project status, labor impact, and ownership structure.
