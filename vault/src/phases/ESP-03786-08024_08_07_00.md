   - **Date:** 08-03-2021
   - **Details:** The facility is part of a pioneering project aimed at developing comprehensive technology for recycling components of old wind turbines. The project includes the establishment of a plant in the Environmental Business Park of Soria, focusing on advanced recycling systems for composite materials, particularly wind turbine blades and nacelle lids.
