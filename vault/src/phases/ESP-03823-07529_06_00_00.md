### Evolutionary Timeline of the ABO Energy España Facility in Yebra, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2024**: Construction began on the Valdezorita solar farm, a ground-mounted photovoltaic system located in Yebra, Castilla La-Mancha. The facility is designed to have a total nominal output of approximately **50MW** and an annual production capacity of around **100GWh**, utilizing **87,000 solar modules**. 
- **Expected Commissioning**: The solar farm is scheduled to be commissioned in **early 2025**. This project marks ABO Energy España's first turnkey project in Spain and is noted as the largest in the company's history to employ tracker technology, which optimizes the angle of the panels towards the sun for enhanced energy yield.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Volume**: The project is financed with an investment volume in the **double-digit million-euro range**, supported by funds from the German **KfW bank** and in cooperation with **DAL Deutsche Anlagen-Leasing**. 
- **Power Purchase Agreement (PPA)**: ABO Wind has secured a long-term pay-as-produced PPA with a large international offtaker for the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2024**: Construction of the Valdezorita solar farm commenced.
- **Summer 2024**: Installation of mounting systems and solar panels is scheduled to take place.
- **Early 2025**: The commissioning of the solar farm is expected.

#### 4. Labour
- The project is anticipated to economically benefit the rural area by providing large-scale contracts to Spanish subcontractors, although specific job creation or loss figures have not been detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: ABO Energy España is a subsidiary of the German company **ABO Wind**. The facility is not mentioned as a joint venture, indicating that ABO Energy España likely has full ownership of the facility.

The ABO Energy España facility in Yebra, Spain, is a significant solar energy project with a capacity of 50MW, expected to be operational by early 2025.
