## ESP-03896-02135

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Acciona Energy Facility in Lloseta, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2019**: Announcement of the green hydrogen production plant in Lloseta, which is set to be a pioneering project for Spain and Europe. The facility is designed to produce 10 MW of renewable hydrogen, primarily powered by solar energy. The hydrogen produced will be used to fuel public buses and other transportation vehicles.
- **12/2021**: The facility begins producing renewable hydrogen, with an annual production capacity of at least 300 tonnes. The production utilizes an electrolyser powered by renewable energy from two photovoltaic plants located in Lloseta and Petra. The hydrogen production is certified using blockchain technology via the GreenH2Chain® platform.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09/2019**: The estimated budget for the Power to Green Hydrogen Mallorca project is €50 million, funded by the four private companies involved: Acciona, Enagás, Cemex, and Redexis.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/2019**: The project is announced and is in the public consultation phase, with administrative proceedings to follow.
- **12/2021**: Confirmation of hydrogen production at the facility after receiving integrated environmental authorization.

#### 4. Labour
- **09/2019**: The announcement of the facility comes in the context of job creation opportunities following the end of production at the Cemex de Mallorca unit, indicating a potential for new employment in the region through the new hydrogen facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **09/2019**: The facility is a collaborative project involving multiple companies: Acciona, Enagás, Cemex, and Redexis, indicating a joint venture rather than exclusive ownership by Acciona.
