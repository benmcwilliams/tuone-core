## ESP-03896-02211

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enagas Facility in Lloseta, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2019**: Announcement of the Power to Green Hydrogen Mallorca project, which will be a pioneering green hydrogen production plant in Lloseta, with a planned capacity of 10 MW. The facility is intended to produce hydrogen powered by solar energy, primarily for public transport and local industries.
- **12/2021**: The facility begins producing renewable hydrogen, with an annual production capacity of at least 300 tonnes. The production utilizes an electrolyser powered by renewable energy from two photovoltaic plants located in Lloseta and Petra.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09/2019**: The estimated budget for the project is €50 million, funded by the four private companies involved: Enagás, Cemex, Acciona, and Redexis.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/2019**: The project is announced and is in the public consultation phase, with administrative proceedings to follow.
- **12/2021**: Hydrogen production is confirmed to have started at the facility after receiving integrated environmental authorization.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or loss figures related to the facility's operations or investments.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is a collaborative project involving multiple companies: Enagás, Acciona, Cemex, and IDAE. Therefore, it is not exclusively owned by Enagás but is part of a joint venture among these entities.
