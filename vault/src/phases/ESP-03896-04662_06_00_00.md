### CEMEX Facility in Lloseta, Spain - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06-08-2021**: Construction began on a green hydrogen production plant in Lloseta, with a production capacity of 300 tonnes of green hydrogen annually. The facility is part of the Power to Green Hydrogen Mallorca (P2GH2M) consortium and includes the installation of two photovoltaic plants. The Lloseta plant will have an area of over 6 hectares with 16,660 solar panels, generating 8.59MWp and producing 13,516MWh annually.

- **25-12-2021**: The facility confirmed the production of renewable hydrogen, utilizing an electrolyser powered by renewable energy from the two photovoltaic plants. The hydrogen production is certified using blockchain technology via the GreenH2Chain® platform.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06-08-2021**: The construction of the green hydrogen plant and associated photovoltaic plants indicates significant investment in renewable energy infrastructure, although specific financial figures are not provided in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06-08-2021**: Announcement of the start of construction for the green hydrogen plant and photovoltaic installations.
- **25-12-2021**: Confirmation of the commencement of renewable hydrogen production at the facility after receiving integrated environmental authorization.

#### 4. Labour
- **06-08-2021**: The construction phase is expected to involve local companies, indicating potential job creation, although specific job numbers are not mentioned.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **25-12-2021**: The facility is a collaborative effort involving Acciona Energía, Enagás, CEMEX, and IDAE, indicating a joint venture ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by CEMEX.
