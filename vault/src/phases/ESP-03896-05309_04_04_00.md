## ESP-03896-05309

### Evolutionary Timeline of the IDAE Facility in Lloseta, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2021**: The facility in Lloseta, Mallorca, began producing renewable hydrogen, with a production capacity of at least 300 tonnes annually. The hydrogen production utilizes an electrolyser powered by renewable energy from two photovoltaic plants (8.5MW in Lloseta and 5.85MW in Petra). The hydrogen production is certified as 100% renewable through blockchain technology via the GreenH2Chain® platform developed by Acciona Energía.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2021**: The facility is part of the Power to Green Hydrogen Mallorca project, a collaboration between Acciona Energía, Enagás, CEMEX, and IDAE. The project received integrated environmental authorisation from the government of the Balearic Islands, allowing it to proceed with commissioning tests and production.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 23, 2021**: Hydrogen production was confirmed after receiving the necessary environmental authorisation.
- **End of December 2021**: The facility began commissioning tests, with plans to start industrial-scale hydrogen production at the beginning of 2022.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or job loss figures related to the facility's operations or expansions.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is not exclusively owned by IDAE; it is a joint venture involving Acciona Energía, Enagás, CEMEX, and IDAE, indicating a collaborative ownership structure among these companies.
