## ESP-03948-01118

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Occitanie, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: Iberdrola has partnered with the Oil’Ive Green network to develop agrivoltaic power plants specifically designed for olive cultivation. These plants will be mounted on solar trackers and are intended to protect olive farms from climate-related hazards while enhancing productivity. The agrivoltaic systems will be adapted to support olive growing in "fruit hedges" across several hectares.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: The partnership aims to cultivate 50,000 hectares of olive trees by the end of the decade, indicating significant investment in agrivoltaic technology to support this agricultural initiative. The specific financial details of the investment were not disclosed.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: The project is in the announcement phase, with plans to identify farms in regions including Occitanie for hosting agrivoltaic projects. The operational timeline suggests that production could start from the third year of operation.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: The agrivoltaic project is expected to provide technical and agronomic assistance to wine growers and olive producers, which may lead to job creation in the agricultural sector, although specific job numbers were not mentioned.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: Iberdrola is involved in an exclusive partnership with the Oil’Ive Green network, indicating a collaborative approach rather than full ownership of the agrivoltaic facilities.
