### Yara Clean Ammonia Facility Timeline - San Roque, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2023
   - **Details:** Yara Clean Ammonia, in partnership with Cepsa, announced plans to construct a new green ammonia plant in San Roque, Cádiz. The facility will have an annual production capacity of up to 750,000 tons of clean ammonia. This initiative is part of a broader strategy to establish a clean hydrogen maritime corridor connecting the ports of Algeciras and Rotterdam, aimed at decarbonizing European industry and maritime transport.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2023
