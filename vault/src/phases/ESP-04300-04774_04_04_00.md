## ESP-04300-04774

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Copsesa Facility in Torrelavega, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **10/2023:** Announcement of a joint project between RIC Energy and Copsesa to establish a green hydrogen production plant on the Sniace land in Torrelavega. The plant will have an electrolysis capacity of 500 megawatts, with potential for expansion.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **10/2023:** The project is estimated to require an investment of approximately 750 million euros. This investment is aimed at developing the green hydrogen plant and its derivatives.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **10/2023:** The project is in the early stages, with plans to acquire the Sniace land through public auction. The City Council and the Ministry of Industry have been collaborating on this project since October 2022 to assess its viability.

4. **Labour:**
   - **10/2023:** The project is expected to create around 250 jobs, contributing to the local economy and promoting green employment opportunities.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **10/2023:** The facility will be a collaborative effort between RIC Energy and Copsesa, indicating a joint venture rather than exclusive ownership by either company.

This timeline captures the key developments and plans for the Copsesa facility in Torrelavega, focusing on its transition towards a green hydrogen production facility.
