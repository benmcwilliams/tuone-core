## ESP-04300-05875

### Evolutionary Timeline of the RIC Energy Facility in Torrelavega, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2023
   - **Details:** RIC Energy, in collaboration with Copsesa, announced plans to establish a green hydrogen production plant on the Sniace land in Torrelavega. The facility will have an electrolysis capacity of 500 megawatts, with potential for expansion. The project aims to produce green hydrogen and its derivatives, contributing to a cleaner energy economy.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2023
   - **Details:** The estimated investment for the green hydrogen plant is approximately 750 million euros. This investment is crucial for the development and expansion of the facility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2023
   - **Details:** The project is currently in the early stages, with plans to acquire the Sniace land through public auction. The project has been in development since October 2022, with ongoing efforts to prove its viability.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2023
   - **Details:** The project is expected to create around 250 jobs, contributing to employment opportunities in Torrelavega and supporting the transition to a sustainable energy economy.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2023
   - **Details:** The facility will be a collaborative project between RIC Energy and Copsesa, indicating a joint venture rather than exclusive ownership by RIC Energy.

This timeline captures the key developments and plans for the RIC Energy facility in Torrelavega, highlighting its significance in the transition to sustainable energy and its potential impact on the local economy.
