### Evolutionary Timeline of the Cummins Facility in Guadalajara, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: May 2021
   - **Details**: Cummins is developing a new €50 million PEM electrolyser plant in Guadalajara, Spain. The facility will initially have a production capacity of approximately 500MW per year, with plans to scale up to more than 1GW/year. The plant will focus on system assembly and testing of electrolysers.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: May 2021
   - **Details**: The facility represents a €50 million investment by Cummins in collaboration with Iberdrola, aimed at promoting the green hydrogen value chain in Spain and Portugal.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: May 2021
   - **Details**: A site selection search is underway in the Guadalajara area, with the facility anticipated to open in 2023. The project is part of a broader collaboration between Cummins and Iberdrola, which includes a 230MW green hydrogen project in Palos de la Frontera.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: May 2021
   - **Details**: The facility is expected to create 350 new jobs as production ramps up.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: May 2021
