### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ferrovial Facility in Gerona, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: Ferrovial, in partnership with RWE, announced plans to develop floating offshore wind projects off the Spanish coast, including Gerona. The partnership aims to contribute to Spain's target of 3 GW of floating offshore wind capacity by 2030. Ferrovial has submitted expressions of interest for four wind farms in Spain, with a total installed capacity of 1,750 MW, which includes the Gerona facility.
