## ESP-04337-08157

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Verbund Green Power Iberia Facility in Granada, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2022**: Verbund Green Power Iberia inaugurated the Pinos Puente Solar Park in Granada, with a production capacity of 147.6 MWp, generating approximately 260 GWh of emission-free electricity annually.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2023**: Verbund Green Power Iberia, in collaboration with Capital Energy, commissioned the 39 MW Loma de los Pinos Wind Farm in Seville. This facility is part of a broader investment strategy in renewable energy projects across Andalusia.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2023**: Announcement of the Loma de los Pinos Wind Farm, which is the first of three wind farms (including El Barroso and Ayamonte) scheduled to come into operation in Andalusia within the same year.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2023**: The construction of the Loma de los Pinos Wind Farm created approximately 220 direct jobs during peak construction periods. In the operational phase, the facility will provide permanent employment for eight professionals from the local area.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2023**: The Loma de los Pinos Wind Farm is jointly owned by Verbund Green Power Iberia (70%) and Capital Energy (30%).
