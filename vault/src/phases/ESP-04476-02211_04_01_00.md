### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enagás Facility in Ciudad Real, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Project Name:** Erasmopower2X
   - **Production Capacity:** The facility will install a 2 MW electrolyzer powered by a 4 MW photovoltaic plant, producing up to 330 tons of green hydrogen per year.
   - **Additional Infrastructure:** A hydrogen refuelling station with a capacity of up to 80 kg of hydrogen per day will be constructed, supplied by a pipeline from the electrolyzer plant.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Total Budget:** The project has a budget of €7 million.
   - **Funding Sources:** The project is a joint initiative involving public and private sector collaboration, including contributions from the regional government, the University of Castilla-La Mancha, and several companies such as Repsol, Iberdrola, Siemens-Gamesa, and Enagás.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Announcement Date:** The project was announced on 12-05-2023.
   - **Completion Timeline:** The project is expected to be completed by the end of 2023.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Job Creation:** The project aims to create new job opportunities in the hydrogen value chain, although specific numbers are not provided in the article.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Joint Venture:** The Erasmopower2X project is a joint project involving multiple stakeholders, including Enagás, indicating that the facility is not owned exclusively by Enagás but is part of a collaborative effort.
