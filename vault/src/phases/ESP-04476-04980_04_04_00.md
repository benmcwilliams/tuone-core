### Evolutionary Timeline of the Erasmopower2X Facility in Ciudad Real, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 12-05-2023
   - **Details**: The Erasmopower2X facility is designed to produce green hydrogen through a 2 MW electrolyzer, powered by a 4 MW photovoltaic plant. The facility aims to produce up to 330 tons of green hydrogen per year. The hydrogen produced will be utilized to power a fleet of 12 fuel cell buses, support the production of green ammonia, and supply hydrogen to local industries. Additionally, a hydrogen refuelling station with a capacity of up to 80 kg of hydrogen per day will be constructed, facilitating the deployment of more fuel cell vehicles in the region.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: 12-05-2023
   - **Details**: The total budget for the Erasmopower2X project is €7 million. This funding is part of a collaborative effort involving public and private sector partnerships, including contributions from the regional government and various companies.

   - **Date**: 12-05-2023
