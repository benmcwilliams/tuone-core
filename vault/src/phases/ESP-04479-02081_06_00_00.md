### Evolutionary Timeline of Enel Facility in Carmona, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2020**: Enel Green Power España (EGPE) commenced construction on the **Los Naranjos** solar farm, a **50MW** facility in Carmona, Seville province. The plant will consist of **128,520 panels** with a capacity of **340Wp each**, expected to generate **100 GWh** annually.
- **End of 2020**: The Los Naranjos facility is projected to be completed by the end of the year, alongside another **50MW** solar plant, **Las Corchas**, which has been under construction since late 2019. Together, these plants will form a **100MW** complex.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2020**: The investment for the Los Naranjos project is **€28.2 million** (approximately **US$30.6 million**). The Las Corchas project has a separate investment of **€30 million** (approximately **US$32.6 million**).
- **Total Investment**: The combined investment for both solar plants amounts to **€58.2 million**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2020**: Construction of the Los Naranjos solar farm officially began.
- **End of 2020**: Both the Los Naranjos and Las Corchas solar plants are expected to be operational by the end of the year.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2020**: Enel Green Power emphasizes the incorporation of local labor in its projects, aligning with its **Creating Shared Value (CSV)** model, which prioritizes local hiring for construction and related services.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is owned by **Enel Green Power España**, a subsidiary of **Endesa**. There is no indication of joint ventures or shared ownership; thus, it can be inferred that Enel has full ownership of the facility.
