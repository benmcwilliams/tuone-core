### Evolutionary Timeline of the Endesa Facility in L'Ampolla, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2024**: Endesa, in collaboration with the Tarragona Water Consortium (CAT), launched the first phase of a photovoltaic installation at the CAT's headquarters in Constantí, which includes 1,902 solar panels generating 1,610 MWh per year. The total capacity of the project is 4.7 MWp, with plans for a second phase at the L'Ampolla Drinking Water Treatment Station (ETAP) to be completed by the end of 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2024**: Endesa is investing €5.6 million for the installation of the two photovoltaic plants, with the financing model allowing CAT to avoid any initial investment. Endesa will handle the construction, operation, and maintenance of the facilities for a duration of 10 years.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2024**: The first phase of the project at Constantí is launched, with the second phase at L'Ampolla already underway and expected to be operational by the last quarter of 2024.
