### Uriel Renovables Facility Timeline - Alcalá de Guadaira, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **April 2024:** Uriel Renovables completed the development of the first megawatt of its photovoltaic solar installation at the Montemarta Cónica Comprehensive Urban Solid Waste Treatment Center. This facility combines energy generated from landfill biogas with solar modules, marking it as the first hybrid photovoltaic-biogas plant in Spain. The total capacity of the photovoltaic installation will reach three megawatts, with an annual electrical energy generation of 2,050 MWh in the first phase, increasing to 6,365 MWh upon completion of the second phase.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **April 2024:** The project represents an investment of three million euros. This investment is tied to the development of the hybridization of biogas and photovoltaic technologies in Andalusia.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **April 2024:** The first phase of the project was completed, with the first megawatt of photovoltaic capacity operational. The second phase, which will add an additional two megawatts, is currently under construction and is expected to be operational by autumn 2024.
