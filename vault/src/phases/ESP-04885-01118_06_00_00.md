#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-06-2019**: Iberdrola announced plans for the **Francisco Pizarro project**, a **590 MW** solar park located in the municipalities of Torrecillas de la Tiesa and Aldeacentenera, Extremadura. The project will cover a **1,300-hectare** area and is set to be commissioned in **2022**.
- **12-08-2022**: The **Francisco Pizarro project** officially began operations, marking it as **Europe’s largest photovoltaic project** and Iberdrola's largest facility of its type globally. The plant consists of approximately **1.5 million photovoltaic modules** and is expected to supply energy to over **334,000 homes**.
