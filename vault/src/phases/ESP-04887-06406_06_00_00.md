### Evolutionary Timeline of the aventron Facility in Bargas, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-07-2019**: aventron announced the construction of a 50MW solar plant in Bargas, Toledo province. The facility is designed to produce 90GWh of annual clean energy. This project is part of a larger 100MW subsidy-free pipeline that aventron is developing in collaboration with Synergia Energy Solutions.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05-07-2019**: The project secured financial backing from Banco Sabadell, with a debt-to-equity ratio of approximately 50%. This financial arrangement supports the construction of the Bargas plant.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05-07-2019**: Construction works for the 50MW Bargas plant were launched after the financial close was achieved.
- **19-07-2019**: A five-year Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) was signed with Statkraft, which will acquire 330GWh of clean energy from the Bargas solar park starting in 2020. The project is slated to reach commercial operation by July 2020.
