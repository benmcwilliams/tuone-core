### Evolutionary Timeline of the Grupo Eco Facility in Hinojosa del Valle, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2017
   - **Details:** Grupo Eco announced the development of the Planta Solar Núñez de Balboa, a 500 MW solar project located in Hinojosa del Valle, along with Usagre and Bienvenida in the Extremaduran province of Badajoz. The facility is expected to produce approximately 850 GWh of electricity annually and will utilize 1,535,637 solar panels with a power output of 325 W each, along with 273 inverters, each with a capacity of 1.58 MW. The project will also include a substation with a capacity of 30/400 kW and a 400 kW transmission line spanning 12.3 km.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2017
   - **Details:** The total investment for the Planta Solar Núñez de Balboa is estimated at $300 million. The project received environmental approval from Spain's Ministry of Agriculture and Fisheries, Food and Environment, and is awaiting further approval from the Ministry of Energy.
