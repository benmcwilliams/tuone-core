### Evolutionary Timeline of the SABIC Facility in La Aljorra, Cartagena, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **June 2023:** SABIC is set to construct the largest on-site solar photovoltaic (PV) plant in Europe at its Cartagena industrial complex. The facility will have a production capacity of 100 MW and will include over 260,000 solar modules. This initiative aims to transition the facility to operate 100% on renewable energy.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **June 2023:** The construction of the solar PV plant requires an investment of more than €60 million. This investment is part of a long-term renewable energy purchase agreement (PPA) between SABIC and Iberdrola, which will supply renewable electricity for the next 25 years.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **June 2023:** Planning consent has been obtained to begin construction of the solar PV plant. The commissioning of the facility is expected in 2024. This project is a significant milestone in SABIC's journey towards achieving carbon neutrality and sustainability.
