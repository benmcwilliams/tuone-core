### Evolutionary Timeline of the aventron Facility in Murcia, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-07-2019**: aventron announced the launch of construction for a 50MW solar plant in the Murcia province, as part of a 100MW subsidy-free pipeline alongside co-developer Synergia Energy Solutions. The facility is designed to produce 90GWh of annual clean energy.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05-07-2019**: The 50MW Bargas plant in Toledo, which is part of the same pipeline, secured a debt package from Banco Sabadell, although specific funding volumes were not disclosed. The debt-to-equity ratio for the project is approximately half-and-half.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05-07-2019**: Construction for the 50MW Bargas plant commenced, with an expected completion time of nine months, targeting commercial operation by July 2020. The Murcia plant is a successor project to Bargas.
