### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Revilla Vallejera, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: Iberdrola began the construction of Spain's first hybrid wind and solar plant in Revilla Vallejera, which includes two photovoltaic plants with capacities of 41 MW and 33 MW, hybridising the existing 69 MW BaCa – Ballestas and Casetona wind power complex. The total capacity of the hybrid plant is 74 MW, with more than 170,000 photovoltaic modules.
- **September 2023**: The construction of the hybrid plant was completed, and the facility entered the commissioning process. The hybrid plant optimises grid use and reduces environmental impact by sharing infrastructure with the existing wind power complex.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: Iberdrola allocated over €40 million for the construction of the hybrid wind and solar project, which aims to enhance renewable resources and make efficient use of existing locations.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023**: Announcement of the construction of the hybrid plant.
- **September 2023**: Completion of the hybrid plant and commencement of the commissioning process.
