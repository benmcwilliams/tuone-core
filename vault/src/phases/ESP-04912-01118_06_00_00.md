### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Torrecillas de la Tiesta and Aldeacentenera, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2022**: Iberdrola has begun the commissioning phase of the Francisco Pizarro project, which is the largest photovoltaic plant in Europe and Iberdrola's largest facility of this technology worldwide. The facility has an installed capacity of **590 MW** and consists of nearly **1.5 million photovoltaic modules**. It is designed to generate enough clean energy to supply more than **334,000 homes** and prevent the emission of **150,000 tonnes of CO2** annually.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2022**: The project required an investment of more than **€300 million**. Iberdrola has secured the project's viability through long-term power purchase agreements (PPAs) with major companies such as Danone, Bayer, and PepsiCo, ensuring the supply of 100% renewable electricity to their facilities in Spain.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2022**: The Francisco Pizarro project was officially put into operation, marking it as the largest photovoltaic plant in Europe and Iberdrola's largest globally.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2022**: The construction of the facility created more than **1,500 jobs** during peak construction periods, with **60%** of these positions filled by local workers.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
