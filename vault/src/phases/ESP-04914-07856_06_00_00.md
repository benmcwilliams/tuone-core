### Evolutionary Timeline of the Grupo Eco Facility in Usagre, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: December 2017
  - **Facility**: Planta Solar Núñez de Balboa
  - **Production Capacity**: 500 MW solar park
  - **Expected Annual Production**: Approximately 850 GWh
  - **Environmental Impact**: Project expected to avoid the emission of 303,450 tonnes of CO2.
  - **Infrastructure**: Includes a substation with a capacity of 30/400 kW and a 400 kW transmission line of 12.3 km.
