### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Vallejera, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: Iberdrola is constructing Spain's first hybrid wind and solar plant in Vallejera, Burgos. The facility will include two photovoltaic plants with capacities of 41 MW and 33 MW, hybridising with the existing 69 MW BaCa – Ballestas and Casetona wind power complex. The total capacity of the hybrid facility will exceed 170,000 photovoltaic modules.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: Iberdrola is investing over €40 million in the construction of the hybrid facility, which aims to optimise grid use and reduce environmental impact by combining wind and solar energy at a single site.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023**: Announcement of the construction of the hybrid facility, which is part of a broader strategy to enhance renewable energy resources in Spain. The project is currently under construction.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2023**: The development of the hybrid facility will involve 360 professionals, contributing to local employment and economic dynamism in the region of Castile and León.
