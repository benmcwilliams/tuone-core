### Evolutionary Timeline of the Endesa Facility in A Coruña, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** February 2021
   - **Details:** Endesa is developing a green hydrogen project in A Coruña featuring a 100MW electrolyser powered by six wind farms with a combined capacity of 611MW. The overall plan includes a total of 23 green hydrogen projects across Spain, with a total electrolyser capacity of 340MW aimed at producing 26,000 tonnes of green hydrogen annually.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** February 2021
   - **Details:** The total investment for the green hydrogen projects across Spain is more than €2.9 billion. This investment is part of Endesa's strategy to transition from thermal to renewable energy generation.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** February 2021
   - **Details:** The A Coruña project is noted as the most advanced among the planned installations. The project is part of a broader initiative following Spain's government approval of a hydrogen roadmap targeting 4GW of installed electrolysis capacity by 2030.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** February 2021
   - **Details:** The development of the green hydrogen projects, including the facility in A Coruña, is expected to create 620 jobs during the construction phase and 320 jobs in operations and maintenance over the next 20 years.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** February 2021
   - **Details:** Endesa is owned by the Italian utility Enel, indicating that the facility is not under full ownership by Endesa but is part of a larger corporate structure.
