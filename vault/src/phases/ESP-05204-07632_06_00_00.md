#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: March 2021
  - **Details**: A feasibility study was published for a 1.53 MW floating solar plant intended to power the Piedra Santa desalination plant in Gran Canaria. The project aims to integrate a floating PV system with the Planta Desaladora de Ósmosis Inversa “Las Palmas III.” The floating solar panels are expected to improve the efficiency of the desalination process by stabilizing seawater temperatures.
