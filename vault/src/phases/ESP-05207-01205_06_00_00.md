### Evolutionary Timeline of the Naturgy Facility in Ocaña, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 12-04-2024**  
  Naturgy began the construction of the Ocaña photovoltaic plant as part of a project that includes two other plants (Zorita I and Zorita II) in Castilla-La Mancha. The combined production capacity of these three plants will be 150 MWp, with the Ocaña plant contributing to this total. The facilities are expected to produce around 310 GWh of electricity annually, sufficient to power approximately 86,000 homes and displace conventional electricity generation, thereby avoiding over 172,000 tons of CO2 emissions per year. The entry into operation is scheduled for 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: 12-04-2024**  
  Naturgy announced an investment of more than 76 million euros for the construction of the three photovoltaic plants, including the Ocaña facility. This investment is part of Naturgy's broader commitment to renewable energy development in the region.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date: 12-04-2024**  
  The construction of the Ocaña photovoltaic plant officially commenced as part of the larger project involving Zorita I and Zorita II. The facilities are scheduled to be operational by 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **Date: 12-04-2024**  
  The construction and operation phases of the three new photovoltaic plants, including Ocaña, are expected to create more than 1,000 jobs.
