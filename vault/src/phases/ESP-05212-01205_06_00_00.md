### Evolutionary Timeline of the Naturgy Facility in Zorita de los Canes, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 12-04-2024
   - **Details:** Naturgy has begun the construction of two photovoltaic plants, Zorita I and Zorita II, located near the former ‘José Cabrera’ Nuclear Power Plant. The facilities will help displace conventional electricity generation and avoid over 172,000 tons of CO2 emissions per year.
