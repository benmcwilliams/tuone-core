## ESP-05264-02134

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Acciona Energia Facility in Lumbier, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: November 2023
  - **Details**: Acciona announced the construction of the Waste2Fiber plant in Lumbier, Navarra, which will focus on recycling wind turbine blades. The facility will utilize a proprietary thermal treatment technology for recycling composite materials, preserving the properties of reinforcement fibers and transforming waste into secondary raw materials. 
  - **Production Capacity**: The plant will have a processing capacity of 6,000 tons of material per year, covering all stages from dismantling and fragmentation of blades to quality control of final products.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: November 2023
  - **Details**: The Waste2Fiber plant has been awarded €5.3 million in funding from the Spanish Government’s Proyectos Estratégicos para la Recuperación y Transformación Económica (PERTE) program, with additional support from the Navarra Government.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: November 2023
  - **Details**: The construction of the Waste2Fiber recycling facility is set to begin in the first half of 2024, with the plant expected to be operational by 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **Date**: November 2023
  - **Details**: The project is expected to create over 100 jobs upon completion of the facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date**: November 2023
  - **Details**: The facility is a collaboration between Acciona, Acciona Energía, and RenerCycle, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by Acciona.
