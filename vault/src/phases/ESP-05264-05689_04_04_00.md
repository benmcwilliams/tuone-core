## ESP-05264-05689

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nordex Electrolyzers Facility in Lumbier, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2024**: Nordex Electrolyzers inaugurated a test bench at its facility in Lumbier, presenting its first in-house-developed prototype of a 500 kW pressurized alkaline electrolyzer. This prototype can produce more than 10 kg/h of hydrogen with an energy consumption of less than 50 kWh/kg. It is designed to handle power changes and frequent stops, differentiating it from competitors. The company aims to finalize the design of a serializable megawatt-scale product by 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2024**: The electrolyzer development plan is partially financed with €12 million from the European Commission through the IPCEI (Important Project of Common European Interest) under the Hy2Tech program. This financial support is crucial for the implementation of strategic projects in Navarra.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2024**: The inauguration of the test bench and the presentation of the 500 kW prototype mark significant milestones for the facility. The prototype was designed, manufactured, and assembled entirely in Navarra, Spain.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2024**: The facility currently employs 26 people, with plans for growth as technological development milestones are achieved. The company is focused on job creation in line with its expansion and development goals.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2024**: Nordex Electrolyzers is a joint venture between the Government of Navarra and Nordex Group. The regional development agency SODENA holds a 15% stake in the company, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by Nordex Group. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic initiatives of the Nordex Electrolyzers facility in Lumbier, Spain, highlighting its focus on innovation in clean technology and hydrogen production.
