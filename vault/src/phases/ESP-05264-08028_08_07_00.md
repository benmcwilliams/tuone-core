### Evolutionary Timeline of the RenerCycle Facility in Lumbier, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: November 2023
  - **Facility**: Waste2Fiber, a wind blade recycling plant.
  - **Production Capacity**: 6,000 tons of material per year.
  - **Technology**: Utilizes proprietary thermal treatment technology for recycling composite materials from wind turbine blades, preserving the properties of reinforcement fibers and transforming composites into secondary raw materials with high added value.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: November 2023
  - **Investment**: The project has been awarded €5.3 million in funding from the Spanish Government’s Proyectos Estratégicos para la Recuperación y Transformación Económica (PERTE) program.
  - **Support**: The project has the backing of the Navarra Government.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: November 2023
  - **Announcement**: Construction of the Waste2Fiber plant announced by ACCIONA, ACCIONA Energía, and RenerCycle.
- **Date**: First half of 2024
  - **Construction Start**: Construction of the recycling facility is set to begin.

#### 4. Labour
- **Date**: November 2023
  - **Job Creation**: The facility is expected to create over 100 jobs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date**: November 2023
  - **Ownership**: The facility is a collaborative project involving ACCIONA, ACCIONA Energía, and RenerCycle, indicating a joint venture rather than exclusive ownership by a single company.
