1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **January 2024:** Opengy began construction of the Neosol project, the first ground-based photovoltaic (PV) plant with grid connection in the Community of Madrid. The facility will have a production capacity of 10 megawatts (MWp) and will cover an area of 10 hectares. It is planned to install 14,588 photovoltaic modules, generating an average of 16,822 megawatt hours (MWh) per year, sufficient to supply renewable energy to approximately 4,800 homes and avoid the emission of 4,500 tons of CO2 annually.
