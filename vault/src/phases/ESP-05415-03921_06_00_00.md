### Evolutionary Timeline of the Encavis Facility in Talayuela, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2019**: Encavis, in collaboration with Solarcentury, announced the development of the **300 MW Talayuela solar project**. The project secured a **10-year power purchase agreement (PPA)** for electricity supply.
- **January 2021**: The **300 MW Talayuela photovoltaic plant** was successfully connected to the Spanish grid, marking its operational status. ### Summary
The Encavis facility in Talayuela, Spain, is a **300 MW solar project** developed in partnership with Solarcentury. It secured a **10-year PPA** in September 2019 and was connected to the grid in January 2021, contributing to Encavis' total operational capacity of **500 MW** in Spain. The ownership structure includes a **20% stake by Solarcentury**, indicating a joint venture rather than exclusive ownership by Encavis.
