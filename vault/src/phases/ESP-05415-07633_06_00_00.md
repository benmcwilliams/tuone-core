### Evolutionary Timeline of the Canopy Energies Facility in Talayuela, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2017:** The facility is planned to be a 300 MW solar power plant located near the municipality of Talayuela, in the province of Cáceres. 2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **December 2017:** The estimated cost for the development of the facility is around €300 million. The project is being developed by an international consortium that includes Canopy Energies, Solarcentury, and Genia Global Energy.

The project was submitted for approval to Spain’s Ministry of Industry, Energy and Tourism in May 2017. Construction is expected to begin in late 2018, with completion anticipated 12 months after the start of construction.
