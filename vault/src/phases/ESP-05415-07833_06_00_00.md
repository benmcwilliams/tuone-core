### Evolutionary Timeline of the Genia Global Energy Facility in Talayuela, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2017:** The facility is planned to be a 300 MW solar power plant located near the municipality of Talayuela, in the province of Cáceres. The project will require approximately 830 hectares of land and will include the construction of a substation and a 400 kW transmission line to connect to the local power network.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **December 2017:** The estimated cost for the development of the facility is around €300 million. The project is being developed by a consortium that includes Genia Global Energy, Solarcentury, and Canopy Energies.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2017:** A memorandum of understanding was signed between the Department of Energy and Infrastructure for the Extremadura region and the international consortium for the development of the solar plant. The project was submitted for approval to Spain’s Ministry of Industry, Energy and Tourism in May 2017, with construction expected to begin in late 2018 and completion scheduled for 12 months after the start of construction.

4. **Labour:**
   - No specific information regarding job creation or job loss has been mentioned in the available article.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is not exclusively owned by Genia Global Energy; it is part of a joint venture with Solarcentury and Canopy Energies.
