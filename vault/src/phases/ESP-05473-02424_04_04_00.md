### Evolutionary Timeline of the Repsol Facility in Abanto-Zierbena, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2021**: Repsol announced the startup of its first electrolyser in the Basque Country, specifically at its Petronor refinery, with a production capacity of **2.5 MW**. This facility will produce renewable hydrogen and will support the nearby Margen Izquierda Technology Park.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2021**: The project involves an investment of **€8.9 million**, which includes the construction of necessary infrastructures for the use and distribution of the renewable hydrogen produced.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **2H22**: The electrolyser is scheduled to start operations.
- **June 2021**: The Basque Hydrogen Corridor (BH2C) Initiative was launched, which Repsol is a part of, aiming to mobilize **€1.431 billion** until 2026 for 40 projects in the hydrogen value chain.

#### 4. Labour
- The BH2C Initiative aims to create **more than 1300 jobs** as part of its projects, contributing to the economic recovery and decarbonisation efforts in the Basque Country.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Repsol operates the facility, but it is part of a broader consortium involving multiple companies and institutions under the BH2C Initiative. The specific ownership structure of the facility itself is not detailed, but Repsol is a leading entity in the project.
