### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Basque Country, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Announcement and Project Details:** The Ekienea project, a 100MW solar PV plant, was announced in 2020. It is a private-public collaboration involving Iberdrola, the Basque Energy Agency (EVE), the regional council of Alava, and the Mondragon Group.
   - **Production Capacity:** The facility is designed to have a production capacity of 100MW and is the largest solar farm in the northern Spanish region.
   - **Operational Context:** The Ekienea project is located near the existing 24MW Ekian solar PV plant, which has been operational since 2020.
   - **Environmental Considerations:** The project spans 200 hectares, with half allocated for the solar PV plant and the other half for environmental compensation and forest preservation.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Investment Amount:** The project required an investment of €70 million (US$78 million).
   - **Future Investments:** Iberdrola plans to invest €15.5 billion (US$17.2 billion) in renewables by 2026, aiming to commission 6GW of solar PV and onshore wind by 2027.
