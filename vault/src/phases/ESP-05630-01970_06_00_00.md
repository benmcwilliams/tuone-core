## ESP-05630-01970

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Statkraft Facility in Southern Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2019**: Statkraft signed a five-year Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) to acquire 330 GWh of clean energy from the 50 MW Bargas solar park, which is under construction in Toledo province, Spain. The Bargas plant is part of a subsidy-free solar project pipeline that includes another 50 MW facility in Murcia.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2019**: The Bargas solar park is backed by a debt package from Spanish financier Banco Sabadell, with a debt-to-equity ratio of approximately 50:50. This financial backing supports the construction and operational goals of the facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2020**: The Bargas solar park is slated to reach commercial operation. The project is part of a broader initiative by aventron, the Swiss developer, to achieve a total installed renewable capacity of 1 GW, with an interim target of 600 MW by late 2020.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the Bargas solar park or Statkraft's operations in Spain.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Statkraft does not own the Bargas facility exclusively; it is a part of a project developed by aventron in collaboration with Synergia Energy Solutions. Statkraft acts as the offtaker for the energy produced by the facility.
