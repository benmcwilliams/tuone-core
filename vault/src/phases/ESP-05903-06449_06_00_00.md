### Evolutionary Timeline of the Canal de Isabel II Facility in Pinto, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: Expected to start operations in **2024**.
   - **Production**: The facility will produce green hydrogen using recycled water, with an initial capacity of approximately **80,000 kilograms of hydrogen per year**.
   - **Technologies Used**: The plant will utilize electrolysis for hydrogen production, combined with solar generation and biogas cogeneration, leveraging waste from the treatment plant itself. It will also implement tertiary treatment to enhance wastewater purification.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Investment Amount**: The project involves an investment of **€7.3 million** (approximately **$7.8 million**).
   - **Completion Timeline**: The project is expected to be completed within **13 months** from the announcement date.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Announcement Date**: The project was announced on **25-05-2023**.
   - **Construction Start**: The construction is expected to begin shortly after the announcement, with operations slated to commence in **2024**.

4. **Labour**
   - **Job Creation**: Specific job creation details have not been provided in the articles, but the establishment of the facility is expected to create jobs related to the construction and operation of the plant.

5. **Ownership Structure**
   - **Ownership**: The facility is owned exclusively by **Canal de Isabel II**, a water-management company owned by the city of Madrid.
