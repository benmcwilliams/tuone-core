## ESP-05930-02211

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enagás Facility in Albacete, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** February 2024
   - **Details:** Enagás is partnering with Hive Energy to develop the Hive H2 Albamed project, which will produce renewable hydrogen. The facility will utilize up to 9 GW of solar and wind energy and will have an electrolysis capacity of 4 GW by 2030. The first phase of the project will include 1.1 GW of photovoltaic (PV) capacity, 284 MW of wind capacity, and 800 MW of electrolysis capacity, expected to come online in the first half of 2028.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** February 2024
   - **Details:** The total investment for the Hive H2 Albamed project is estimated to exceed €10 billion ($10.7 billion). The project will be developed in four phases, with a final investment decision (FID) for the first phase planned by the end of 2025.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** February 2024
   - **Details:** The project is currently in the planning stages, with the first facility expected to be operational in the first half of 2028. A Pre-FEED study has been contracted to support the development of the green hydrogen and ammonia production plant.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** February 2024
   - **Details:** As of April 2024, approximately 100 people are already working on the infrastructure for the project.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** February 2024
   - **Details:** Enagás is partnering with Hive Energy and local authorities for the development of the facility, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership.
