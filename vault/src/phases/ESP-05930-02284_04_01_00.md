## ESP-05930-02284

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hive Facility in Albacete, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2024**: Hive Energy is in the process of securing approval to deploy **800 MW of electrolysis capacity** and **1.1 GW of photovoltaic (PV)** capacity in Albacete. The broader plan includes installing **9 GW of wind and solar power** and **4 GW of electrolyzers** by **2030**. The facility aims to produce more than **1 million tons of green hydrogen and ammonia** by **2030**. The first phase of the project will consist of **1.1 GW of PV**, **284 MW of wind**, and **800 MW of electrolysis capacity**, expected to come online in the **first half of 2028**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2024**: The total investment for the Hive H2 Albamed project is estimated at more than **€10 billion ($10.7 billion)**. The project will be developed in **four phases**, with a **final investment decision (FID)** planned by the end of **2025**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2024**: Hive Energy is actively working on the Hive H2 Albamed project, which is part of a larger initiative to enhance renewable energy production in the region. The project is set to be finalized in **four phases**, with the first facility expected to be operational by **2028**.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2024**: As part of the project development, **100 people** are already employed in the infrastructure work, indicating job creation associated with the project.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2024**: Hive Energy is the primary developer of the facility, partnering with Spanish gas supplier **Enagás** and local authorities for the project. The ownership structure details regarding exclusivity or joint ventures have not been explicitly stated, but it indicates a collaborative effort with local entities.
