### Evolutionary Timeline of IM2 Energía Solar Facility in Andalucía, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Capacity**: The IM2 Energía Solar facility is part of a larger portfolio that includes 14 solar photovoltaic (PV) projects with a total capacity of 212 MWp. 
- **Construction Start**: Construction activities for the most advanced projects within this portfolio are set to begin in 2022. IM2 Energía Solar, as the engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) subsidiary, will oversee these activities.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Agreements**: 
  - In April 2022, Umbrella signed a portfolio-wide merchant term loan construction financing agreement with Incus Capital for €106.5 million to fund the construction of the solar portfolio.
  - In June 2022, Umbrella closed an investment agreement with RGreen Invest, which includes a commitment to invest up to €42 million through the Infragreen IV Fund for the construction of the projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Financial Close**: Financial close for the first 27 MWp project is expected to be achieved shortly after the publication of the article in July 2022.
- **Project Infinity**: This initiative represents a key milestone in Umbrella's strategy to integrate further across the energy value chain and establish itself as an Independent Power Producer (IPP) with proprietary operating assets.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: While specific job creation numbers are not detailed in the article, the construction of the solar projects is expected to create jobs, given the scale of the projects and the involvement of IM2 Energía Solar, which has experience in managing over 150 solar PV projects.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: The facility is part of a joint venture structure, with Umbrella owning and developing the portfolio of solar projects. RGreen Invest has acquired a minority stake in the joint venture vehicle that will own and operate the 14 special purpose vehicles (SPVs) associated with the projects.
