### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ib Vogt Facility in Segovia, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06-12-2023**: Ib Vogt announced the development of the **174MWp Serbal solar power plant** located west of Segovia, which will produce approximately **328GWh** of clean solar electricity annually. The facility will utilize a state-of-the-art design featuring a **single-axis tracking system** and **bifacial modules**.
- **15-02-2024**: Ib Vogt confirmed the construction of a **418MW solar portfolio** in Segovia, which includes the **174MW Serbal**, **134MW Castano**, and **110MW Pato** solar projects. All three projects will feature high-efficiency bifacial modules and single-axis tracker systems.
- **18-04-2024**: The **134MW Castano solar plant** is expected to be fully operational by **December 2024**. It will also utilize bifacial solar panels on a single-axis tracker system to maximize energy production.
- **19-04-2024**: The **Castaño Solar** project, part of the collaboration with Apple, will produce **105MW** of electricity and is expected to be operational by the end of **2024**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **15-02-2024**: Ib Vogt achieved financial close on a project financing debt facility for the **418MW solar portfolio**, raising approximately **€350 million**. The financing was provided by Nord/LB, Santander CIB, and BBVA.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06-12-2023**: Announcement of the **Serbal solar power plant** development.

- **19-04-2024**: Confirmation of the **Castaño Solar** project, which will be operational by the end of **2024**.
