### Evolutionary Timeline of the Madrileña Red de Gas Facility in Valdemoro, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2022**: Madrileña Red de Gas (MRG) and Pryconsa announced the development of a green hydrogen project aimed at supplying renewable hydrogen for heating and domestic hot water to 100 homes in Valdemoro, Madrid. The facility is designed to generate at least 24.5 tons of hydrogen annually, with an estimated consumption of 250 kg of hydrogen per household.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2022**: MRG has committed to covering the additional costs associated with using hydrogen instead of natural gas, ensuring that residents will not experience a difference in cost. The project is still in the planning phase, with a focus on evaluating the technical and economic models for hydrogen production.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: Construction of the hydrogen production facility is scheduled to begin.
- **Q4 2025**: The first shipments of hydrogen are expected to commence.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2022**: While specific job creation or loss figures were not mentioned, the project is expected to create jobs related to the construction and operation of the hydrogen facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **September 2022**: The facility is a joint project between Madrileña Red de Gas and Pryconsa, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by MRG.
