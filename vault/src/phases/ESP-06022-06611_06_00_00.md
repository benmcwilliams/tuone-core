### Evolutionary Timeline of the Pryconsa Facility in Valdemoro, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: September 2022**  
  Pryconsa, in collaboration with Madrileña Red de Gas (MRG), announced the development of a green hydrogen project aimed at supplying renewable hydrogen for heating and domestic hot water to 100 homes in Valdemoro. The facility is expected to generate at least 24.5 tons of hydrogen annually, with each household consuming approximately 250 kg of hydrogen per year. The preferred technology for hydrogen generation will be a polymer electrolyte membrane (PEM) electrolyzer, which will be powered by a solar plant through a power purchase agreement (PPA).

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: September 2022**  
  The project is in the planning stages, with MRG agreeing to cover the additional costs associated with using hydrogen instead of natural gas, ensuring that residents will not experience a difference in energy costs. The financial specifics regarding investments or subsidies have not been detailed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date: September 2022**  
  The project was officially announced, with construction scheduled to begin in May 2023. The first shipments of hydrogen are expected in the last quarter of 2025.
