### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enel Facility in Villar de los Navarros, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2020**: Enel Green Power is developing the Tico Wind Farm in Villar de los Navarros, which will supply 79 MW of sustainable electricity to Novartis. The project is expected to be operational in early 2022.
- **May 2021**: Construction begins on the Tico Wind Farm, which will have a total capacity of 180 MW and is projected to generate approximately 471 GWh per year. The facility will consist of 43 wind turbines, with varying capacities of 3.65 MW and 5 MW.
- **May 2022**: The Tico Wind Farm achieves grid connection, marking it as Enel Green Power's largest wind farm in Spain.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2021**: The investment for the Tico Wind Farm is reported to be €181 million. This investment is part of Enel's commitment to enhancing renewable energy generation in Spain.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2020**: Announcement of the Tico Wind Farm project, expected to be operational in early 2022.
- **May 2021**: Construction of the Tico Wind Farm begins.
- **May 2022**: The Tico Wind Farm is connected to the grid.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2022**: During the construction phase of the Tico Wind Farm, peak employment reached 330 workers, with 30% of the workforce sourced from Villar de los Navarros and surrounding areas.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Enel Green Power España operates the Tico Wind Farm as part of its portfolio, indicating full ownership of the facility. The facility is part of the broader Enel Group, which is dedicated to renewable energy development globally.
