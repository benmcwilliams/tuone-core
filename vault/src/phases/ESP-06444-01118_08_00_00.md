## ESP-06444-01118

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Navarre, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2022**: Iberdrola announced the establishment of EnergyLOOP, a company focused on recycling components used in renewable energy installations, including wind turbine blades. The facility will recover materials such as glass and carbon fibers and resins for reuse in various sectors (energy, aerospace, automotive, textiles, chemicals, and construction).

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2022**: The EnergyLOOP project is supported by the Government of Navarre, emphasizing its strategic importance in promoting renewable energy and circular economy initiatives in the region. Specific investment amounts for the EnergyLOOP project were not disclosed, but it is part of Iberdrola's broader commitment to sustainability and innovation in renewable energy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2022**: Iberdrola and FCC Ámbito announced the development of Spain's first blade recycling plant in Navarre, marking a significant milestone in the country's renewable energy landscape.
- **August 2021**: Iberdrola commissioned the Herrera II wind complex in Castilla y León, which consists of three farms with a total installed capacity of 63 MW. This project is part of Iberdrola's ongoing efforts to expand its renewable energy footprint in Spain.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2021**: The development of the Herrera II wind complex employed around 800 people, with a significant portion of the civil works entrusted to local companies. The broader investment plans in the region are expected to create jobs for approximately 18,000 people.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2022**: Iberdrola is involved in a joint venture with FCC Ámbito for the EnergyLOOP project, indicating a collaborative ownership structure for the recycling facility. The ownership details for the Herrera II wind complex were not specified, but Iberdrola is known for its extensive investments and operations in renewable energy facilities across Spain.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Iberdrola facility in Navarre, focusing on its technological advancements, investments, project statuses, labor impacts, and ownership structure.
