   - **Date:** June 2022
   - **Details:** FCC Ámbito, in collaboration with Iberdrola, plans to establish Spain's first blade recycling plant in Navarre. The facility will focus on recycling wind turbine blade components, primarily glass and carbon fibers, and resins. The project aims to recover these materials for reuse in various sectors, including energy, aerospace, automotive, textile, chemical, and construction.
