#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2019**: Iberdrola announced the development of a 50MW solar photovoltaic (PV) plant in Andévalo, located in the municipality of Puebla de Guzmán, Huelva. This facility is designed to produce clean energy volumes of 82GWh annually, primarily to power Heineken's operations in Spain. The plant utilizes approximately 147,000 polycrystalline silicon 340Wp modules.
