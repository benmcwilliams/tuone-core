### Evolutionary Timeline of the AB InBev Facility - Budweiser Solar Farm, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 09-01-2020
   - **Details:** AB InBev entered into a 10-year virtual power purchase agreement (VPPA) with Baywa r.e. to transition its European operations to renewable energy. The Budweiser Solar Farm is expected to produce 250GWh of electricity annually for the corporation.
