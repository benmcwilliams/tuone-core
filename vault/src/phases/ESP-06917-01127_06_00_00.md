### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ingeteam Facility in Mula, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 05-09-2018
   - **Details:** Ingeteam is supplying inverters for the Mula PV plant, which is part of Europe's largest PV project with a capacity of 500MW. The facility is expected to produce over 750 million kWh of electricity annually, enough to supply approximately 450,000 people. The project involves the delivery of 238 central PV inverters from the INGECON SUN PowerMax B Series, each with a rated power output of 1,690kW. Ingeteam is also providing transformers, switchgear, and auxiliary services, along with the installation and commissioning of 74 inverter stations.
