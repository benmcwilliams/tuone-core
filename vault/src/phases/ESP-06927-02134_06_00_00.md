### Evolutionary Timeline of Acciona Energia Facility in Palma del Río, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **July 2011:** Acciona Energy activated the Palma del Río I solar thermal plant, which has a production capacity of 50MW. The facility consists of 792 solar collectors covering an area of 135 hectares. This plant, along with Palma del Río II (connected to the grid in December 2010), contributes to Acciona’s total Concentrated Solar Power (CSP) portfolio in Spain, which reaches 200MW. The combined output of these plants is sufficient to meet the energy needs of approximately 70,000 homes in southern Spain.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **July 2011:** A total investment of around €500 million was made for the development of both Palma del Río I and Palma del Río II plants.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2010:** Palma del Río II was connected to the grid.
   - **July 2011:** Palma del Río I was activated.
