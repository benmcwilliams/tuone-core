### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Ayora, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2024**: Iberdrola announced the construction of the **Cofrentes I photovoltaic plant** in the Valencian municipalities of Ayora, Zarra, Jarafuel, Jalance, and Cofrentes. The plant will have a capacity of **154.66 MW** and will utilize bifacial monocrystalline silicon modules from Trina, with a total of **77,700 to 101,724 modules** depending on the final configuration. The facility will also include **45 inverters** from Sungrow and nine transformation centers of varying capacities.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2024**: The investment for the Cofrentes I photovoltaic plant is projected to be **over 100 million euros**. This investment is part of a broader initiative by Iberdrola to develop multiple photovoltaic and hybridization projects across Spain, totaling **380 million euros** for five projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2024**: Iberdrola received administrative and prior construction authorization for the Cofrentes I photovoltaic plant, marking a significant milestone in the project timeline.
