### Evolutionary Timeline of Acciona Energy Facility in Celada Fusión, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 05-05-2021
   - **Details:** Acciona began construction on the 48MW Celada Fusión wind farm, which will utilize 10 Nordex 149 wind turbines installed on 125-metre-high steel towers. The facility is expected to produce an estimated 136,000 MWh of clean electricity annually, contributing to the decarbonization of the Spanish electricity mix and avoiding the emission of 60,000 tons of CO2 per year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 05-05-2021
   - **Details:** The project requires an investment of over €45 million. The facility is part of Acciona's sustainability innovation projects, which include the use of biomass ashes from their Briviesca plant in construction, promoting a circular economy.

   - **Date:** 05-05-2021
   - **Details:** Construction works for the Celada Fusión wind farm have commenced, with operations expected to start in the first quarter of 2022. The project is strategically located near existing Acciona-operated wind farms (Celada I and Celada V).
