**1. Technology and Operations:**
- **September 2024:** Viscofan announced the installation of a 48MWp self-consumption solar system at its Cáseda facility. This system is designed to provide renewable energy for the next 30 years and is one of the largest photovoltaic plants for industrial self-consumption in Spain. The system will cover approximately 30% of the electricity needs of Viscofan's facilities in Navarre, utilizing electric boilers to consume the generated energy. The project aims to reduce carbon dioxide emissions by 50,306 tonnes annually and is expected to decrease emissions from the Cáseda plant by more than 20%.
