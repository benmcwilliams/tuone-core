### Evolutionary Timeline of the Anpier Facility in Navarra Canal, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2021**: The Navarra regional government approved a solar project proposed by Anpier, with a planned capacity of **160 MW**. The project aims to deploy solar panels over the Navarra Canal, which is one of Spain's largest artificial irrigation canals. This initiative is expected to double the photovoltaic capacity currently installed in the region. The project is designed to reduce water evaporation by **30%** and enhance the efficiency of solar panels due to the cooling effect of the water.

- **June 2022**: The project is moving forward with the development of the first **9-km** portion of the planned **198-km** solar plant. This pilot section is expected to generate approximately **28.5 GWh** of green electricity annually, covering **52%** of the energy demand for the regional government’s buildings.
