### Evolutionary Timeline of the Solar Millennium Facility in Guadix, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2009
   - **Details:** Solar Millennium, through its subsidiary Flagsol, began development on the Andasol 3 solar thermal plant, which has a production capacity of 50 MW. The facility utilizes parabolic trough technology and is designed to generate approximately 170 GWh of electricity annually. The plant will feature over 1.5 million m² of collector surface area and will install close to 210,000 parabolic mirrors.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2009
   - **Details:** The project is a collaboration involving Stadtwerke München, RWE Innogy, RheinEnergie, and MAN Ferrostaal. Stadtwerke München holds a 48.9% share in the project company Marquesado Solar S.L., while RWE Innogy and RheinEnergie jointly hold 25.1%. The remaining 26% is owned by an investment holding company of MAN Ferrostaal and Solar Millennium.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2009
   - **Details:** Construction of the Andasol 3 plant is at an advanced stage, with collector mountings foundations being cast and preparations for collector assembly underway. The plant is expected to be completed in 2011.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** July 2009
   - **Details:** The article does not specify job creation or job loss related to the Andasol 3 facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** July 2009
   - **Details:** The facility is not exclusively owned by Solar Millennium; it is part of a joint venture with Stadtwerke München, RWE Innogy, RheinEnergie, and MAN Ferrostaal, with varying ownership stakes as detailed above.
