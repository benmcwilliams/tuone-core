## ESP-06991-01118

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Castelló, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **December 2023**: The Castellón Green Hydrogen company was created as a joint venture between BP and Iberdrola to develop a 25 MW green hydrogen project at the BP refinery in Castelló. 
   - **Expected Operations**: The green hydrogen plant is anticipated to be operational in the second half of 2026, producing 2,800 tonnes of green hydrogen annually. The electrolyser will be powered by renewable electricity from Iberdrola, supplying 200 GWh per year from photovoltaic and wind projects. This initiative aims to decarbonize some refinery operations, reducing CO2 emissions by 23,000 tonnes per year.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Initial Investment**: In June 2024, BP and Iberdrola injected an initial capital of 300,000 euros into the joint venture.
   - **Further Investment**: On March 12, 2024, they injected an additional 900,000 euros, bringing the total capital of Castellón Green Hydrogen to over 1.2 million euros. The total investment for the project is estimated at 70 million euros, with 15 million euros coming from the Next Generation funds of the European Union.
   - **Long-term Investment**: The overall investment for the global green hydrogen hub project is projected to be 2 billion euros.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Joint Venture Formation**: The Castellón Green Hydrogen company was established in December 2023.
   - **Investment Decisions**: The final investment decision is expected to be made in June 2024, following the initial capital injections.
   - **Construction Timeline**: The construction of the 25 MW green hydrogen project is set to commence, with operations expected to start in the second half of 2026.

4. **Labour**
   - The project is expected to create up to 500 jobs as a result of the investments and development of the green hydrogen facility.

5. **Ownership Structure**
   - The facility is a joint venture between BP and Iberdrola, indicating that neither company has exclusive ownership of the facility.
