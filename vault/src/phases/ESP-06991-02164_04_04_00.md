### Evolutionary Timeline of the BP Facility in Castelló, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2023**: The Castellón Green Hydrogen company was created as a joint venture between BP and Iberdrola to develop a green hydrogen project at the BP refinery in Castelló. The facility will produce green hydrogen with an electrolyser capacity of 25 MW, expected to generate 2,800 tonnes of green hydrogen annually. The project aims to decarbonize refinery operations, reducing CO2 emissions by 23,000 tonnes per year.
- **Expected Operational Date**: The green hydrogen plant is anticipated to be operational in the second half of 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Total Investment**: The total investment for the green hydrogen project is estimated at €70 million, with €15 million sourced from the Next Generation funds of the European Union.
- **Funding Milestones**:
  - **June 2024**: BP and Iberdrola injected an initial €300,000 into the joint venture.
  - **March 2024**: A further injection of €900,000 was made, bringing the total capital of Castellón Green Hydrogen to over €1.2 million.
- **Additional Funding**: The project has also secured €15 million from the Spanish Recovery, Transformation and Resilience Plan for innovative renewable hydrogen initiatives.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2023**: Establishment of the Castellón Green Hydrogen company.
- **March 2024**: Announcement of the €900,000 capital injection into the joint venture.
- **June 2024**: Initial €300,000 investment made by BP and Iberdrola.
- **Expected Completion**: The green hydrogen plant is projected to be operational by the second half of 2026.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The project is expected to create up to 500 jobs as a result of the investment and development of the green hydrogen facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Joint Venture**: The facility is a joint venture between BP and Iberdrola, indicating shared ownership rather than exclusive ownership by either company.
