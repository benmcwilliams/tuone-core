## ESP-06991-07638

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Castellón Green Hydrogen SL Facility in Castelló, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2023**: Castellón Green Hydrogen SL was created as a joint venture between BP and Iberdrola to develop a green hydrogen project.
- **Expected Operational Date**: The 25 MW green hydrogen plant is anticipated to be operational in the second half of **2026**.
- **Production Capacity**: The facility will produce **2,800 tonnes of green hydrogen per year**.
- **Decarbonization Impact**: The project aims to decarbonize some operations of the BP refinery, avoiding the emission of **23,000 tonnes of CO2 per year**.
- **Water Usage**: BP has requested a concession for **8,000 million litres of water per year** to support the project, opting for reused water from the local Wastewater Treatment Plant.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Initial Investment**: In **June 2024**, BP and Iberdrola injected **300,000 euros** into the joint venture.
- **Subsequent Investment**: On **03-12-2024**, they injected an additional **900,000 euros**, bringing the total capital to **1.2 million euros**.
- **Total Project Investment**: The overall investment for the green hydrogen project is estimated at **70 million euros**, with **15 million euros** coming from the Next Generation funds of the European Union.
- **Global Project Investment**: The green hydrogen hub project is part of a larger initiative involving a total investment of **2 billion euros**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Creation of Joint Venture**: Castellón Green Hydrogen SL was established in **December 2023**.
- **Investment Announcements**: The initial investment of **300,000 euros** was made in **June 2024**, followed by an additional **900,000 euros** in **December 2024**.
- **Construction Timeline**: The construction of the 25 MW green hydrogen project is part of the first phase of the global green hydrogen hub, with operations expected to start in the second half of **2026**.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The project is expected to create up to **500 jobs** as a result of the investments and construction activities.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Joint Venture**: Castellón Green Hydrogen SL is a joint venture between BP and Iberdrola, indicating that neither company has exclusive ownership of the facility.
