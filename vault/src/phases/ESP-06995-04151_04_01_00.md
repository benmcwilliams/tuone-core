## ESP-06995-04151

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ohmium Facility in Southern Europe, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: October 2024
   - **Details**: Ohmium is developing an industrial-scale floating green hydrogen and ammonia production facility. The facility will utilize 300 MW of Ohmium’s PEM electrolyzer solutions installed on a 268-meter FPSO (Floating Production, Storage and Offloading) vessel. The facility aims to produce up to 55,000 tonnes of green hydrogen and nearly 300 kton of green ammonia annually, utilizing nearshore solar and wind power along with treated seawater.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: October 2024
   - **Details**: The project has received support from the Dutch government, indicating financial backing for its development. Specific investment amounts tied to expansions or capacity were not disclosed.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: October 2024
   - **Details**: The project is currently in the development phase, with an anticipated production start for green ammonia by 2029. The project has received approval in principle (AiP) for its design from the classification society DNV.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: October 2024
   - **Details**: There are no specific details provided regarding job creation or job loss associated with this facility at this time.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: October 2024
   - **Details**: The facility is led by SwitcH2 BV, with BW Offshore as a shareholder. The ownership structure indicates a joint venture rather than exclusive ownership by Ohmium.
