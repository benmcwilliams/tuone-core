### Evolutionary Timeline of the SwitcH2 BV Facility in Southern Europe, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 10/2024
   - **Details:** The facility is set to develop an industrial-scale floating green hydrogen and ammonia production facility utilizing 300 MW of Ohmium’s PEM electrolyzer solutions installed on a 268-meter FPSO vessel. The facility will produce up to 55,000 tonnes of green hydrogen and nearly 300 kton of green ammonia annually, using nearshore solar and wind power along with treated seawater.

   - **Date:** 10/2024
