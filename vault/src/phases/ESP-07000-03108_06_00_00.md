### Evolutionary Timeline of the Jinko Power Facility in Alcalá de Guadaíra, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2019**: The Jinko Power facility, known as the La Isla project, is under construction near Seville, with a production capacity of 182.5 MW. The project will utilize 536,790 polycrystalline modules, each with an output of 340 W, and central inverters with a capacity of 2.5 MW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2019**: The total investment in the La Isla project is estimated at €125 million. The project was selected in a renewable energy auction held by the Spanish government in July 2017.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2019**: The La Isla project is confirmed to be under construction and is expected to be completed during the second half of the year.

#### 4. Labour
- The article does not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the La Isla project.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2019**: Initially, the project was wholly owned and developed by Jinko Power International. However, following the investment by Ardian Infrastructure and White Summit Capital AG, the ownership structure is indicated to involve these investors, although specific details on the current ownership stake are not disclosed.
