### Evolutionary Timeline of the BayWa Facility in Don Rodrigo, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-04-2018**: BayWa r.e. announced the start of construction for the **175 MW Don Rodrigo solar power plant** in Southern Spain, which is set to be one of the largest solar projects in Europe and one of the first to achieve grid parity.
- **31-12-2018**: The **175 MW Don Rodrigo solar power plant** was completed and sold. It is noted as the first subsidy-free solar power plant of this size in Europe, utilizing a power purchase agreement instead of subsidies.
- **08-01-2020**: BayWa r.e. sold the **50 MW Don Rodrigo 2 facility**, which is located 3 km away from the original Don Rodrigo plant. This facility also operates under a private power purchase agreement.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **31-12-2018**: The completion of the Don Rodrigo facility was supported by a **€100 million bridging loan** from Nord LB, indicating significant financial backing for the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07-04-2018**: Announcement of the start of construction for the Don Rodrigo project.
- **31-12-2018**: Completion of the Don Rodrigo solar power plant and announcement of its sale to MEAG, an asset management company owned by Munich Re.
- **08-01-2020**: Sale of the Don Rodrigo 2 facility to Talanx AG, marking the second subsidy-free solar project sold by BayWa in Spain.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the Don Rodrigo facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **31-12-2018**: BayWa r.e. sold the Don Rodrigo facility to MEAG, indicating that BayWa no longer holds ownership of this facility but remains responsible for its technical operations.
- **08-01-2020**: BayWa sold the Don Rodrigo 2 facility to Talanx AG, continuing its role in operating the facility on behalf of the new owner.
