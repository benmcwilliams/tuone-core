- **Date: 20-02-2020**: Solarcentury announced the construction of the 200 MW Cabrera solar facility in Andalusia, which is designed to produce approximately 380 GWh of electricity annually. The facility will consist of 554,000 solar modules and 9,500 single-axis trackers, divided into four 50 MW installations across 400 hectares.
