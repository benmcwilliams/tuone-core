### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility in Puerto Real, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2023**: Ocean Winds signed a reserved capacity agreement with Navantia Seanergies for the manufacture of components for future offshore wind farms, including floating structures. The production will primarily occur at Navantia Seanergies’ facilities in Puerto Real and Fene, with an average delivery of 8 to 12 floating foundations per year from 2027 to 2031.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2023**: The agreement with Navantia Seanergies is part of a broader commitment to offshore wind energy, although specific investment amounts tied to this agreement were not disclosed. The collaboration aims to enhance the production capabilities for offshore wind components.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2023**: The signing of the agreement marks a significant milestone in Ocean Winds' strategy to expand its offshore wind capabilities in Spain and Portugal. The agreement is set to generate an average of 2 million hours of work per year, indicating a robust operational timeline from 2027 to 2031.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2023**: The agreement is expected to create an average of 1,000 direct jobs, primarily in the facilities located in Fene and Puerto Real, as a result of the increased production activities associated with the offshore wind projects.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **March 2023**: Ocean Winds is a joint venture created by EDPR and ENGIE, indicating that it does not have exclusive ownership of the facility but operates in collaboration with Navantia Seanergies for the production of offshore wind components.
