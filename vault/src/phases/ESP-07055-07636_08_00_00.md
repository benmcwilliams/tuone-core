## ESP-07055-07636

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Capital Energy Facility in Brañadesella, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Installed Capacity**: The Brañadesella wind farm has an installed capacity of **25 MW**.
- **Energy Production**: It is expected to supply more than **70,000 MWh** of clean electricity annually, sufficient to meet the energy needs of approximately **28,000 Asturian homes**.
- **Environmental Impact**: The facility will prevent the emission of approximately **26,000 tons of CO2** into the atmosphere each year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Amount**: The construction of the Brañadesella wind farm will mobilize an investment of more than **20 million euros**.
- **Local Contributions**: The facility will contribute over **75,000 euros** annually to local coffers and approximately **400,000 euros** to the GDP each year.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Announcement Date**: On **February 20, 2023**, Capital Energy signed a new Territories Project agreement with the Boal City Council, linked to the Brañadesella wind farm.
- **Construction and Operation**: The facility is in the construction phase, with job creation initiatives and local employment agreements already in place.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The construction phase is expected to create more than **110 direct jobs** during peak periods. In the operation and maintenance phase, it will provide stable employment for **three professionals** and favor the creation of **two indirect jobs**.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: Capital Energy is the owner of the Brañadesella facility, as indicated by its commitment to the project and the agreements made with the local council.
