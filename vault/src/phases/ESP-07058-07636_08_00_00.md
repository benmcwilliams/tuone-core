### Evolutionary Timeline of the Capital Energy Facility in Escobosa de Almazán, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: May 2023
  - Capital Energy is developing multiple wind farms in Spain, including the Torrecilla wind farm located in Escobosa de Almazán. The Torrecilla facility will have a capacity of over 15 MW and is expected to produce around 60,000 MWh of clean energy annually, sufficient to supply approximately 23,000 homes and prevent the emission of 22,000 tons of CO2.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: May 2023
  - Capital Energy awarded a contract worth 5.5 million euros to Faramax Trafo, SL for the supply of transformers for six wind farms, including those in Escobosa de Almazán. This investment is part of the broader development of the wind farms, which collectively have a capacity of about 265 MW.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: May 2023
  - The construction of the Torrecilla wind farm in Escobosa de Almazán is part of a larger project that includes several wind farms across Castilla-La Mancha, Aragón, and Castilla y León.
