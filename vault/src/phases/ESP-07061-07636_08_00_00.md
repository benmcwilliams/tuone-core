## ESP-07061-07636

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Capital Energy Facility in Lebrija, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2023**: The Loma de los Pinos onshore wind farm, with a production capacity of 39 MW, was launched. It is equipped with seven General Electric turbines and is capable of producing approximately 100,000 MWh of clean energy annually. This output is sufficient to power over 36,000 homes in Andalusia and will prevent the emission of more than 35,000 tonnes of CO2 per year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2023**: The facility has a long-term power purchase agreement (PPA) with Statkraft, indicating a strategic investment in renewable energy production. The construction phase involved significant investment, although specific financial figures were not disclosed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2023**: The Loma de los Pinos wind farm was officially commissioned, marking it as the first of three wind farms to be operational in Andalusia this year, alongside El Barroso in Jerez de la Frontera and Ayamonte in Huelva.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2023**: The construction of the wind farm created approximately 220 jobs during peak construction periods. In the operational phase, the facility will employ eight professionals for ongoing maintenance and operations.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2023**: The ownership of the Loma de los Pinos wind farm is shared between Verbund Green Power Iberia and Capital Energy, with Verbund holding a 70% stake and Capital Energy holding 30%.
