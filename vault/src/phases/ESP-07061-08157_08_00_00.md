## ESP-07061-08157

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Verbund Green Power Iberia Facility in Lebrija, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2023**: The Loma de los Pinos wind farm, with a capacity of 39 MW, was launched. It is equipped with seven turbines supplied by General Electric and is capable of producing approximately 100,000 MWh of clean energy annually. This output is sufficient to power over 36,000 homes in Andalusia and will prevent the emission of more than 35,000 tons of CO2 per year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2023**: The facility has a long-term power purchase agreement (PPA) signed with Statkraft, indicating a strategic investment in renewable energy production.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2023**: The Loma de los Pinos wind farm was officially launched, marking it as the first of three wind farms planned for operation in Andalusia within the year, alongside El Barroso (Jerez) and Ayamonte (Huelva).

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2023**: The construction phase of the Loma de los Pinos wind farm created approximately 220 direct jobs during peak construction periods. In the operational phase, the facility will provide permanent employment for eight professionals from the local area.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2023**: The ownership of the Loma de los Pinos wind farm is shared between Verbund Green Power Iberia and Capital Energy, with Verbund holding a 70% stake and Capital Energy holding 30%.
