### Evolutionary Timeline of the Capital Energy Facility in Montealegre del Castillo, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: Capital Energy begins construction of the La Herrada wind farm in Montealegre del Castillo, which will have an installed capacity of **51 MW** from **10 Siemens Gamesa SG 5.0 – 145 wind turbines** (5.2 MW each). The facility is expected to supply approximately **149,000 MWh** of clean energy annually, enough for around **56,000 homes**, and will prevent the emission of about **55,000 tons of CO2** per year.
- **November 2023**: The facility's construction progresses with the arrival of the Siemens Gamesa wind turbines. The La Herrada wind farm will now have a total capacity of **52 MW** and is projected to supply over **132,000 MWh** of clean energy annually, serving nearly **51,000 homes** and avoiding around **50,000 tons of CO2** emissions.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: Capital Energy awards a contract worth **5.5 million euros** to Faramax for the supply of transformers for the La Herrada and other wind farms, indicating a commitment to local manufacturing and investment in renewable energy infrastructure.
- **November 2023**: The construction of the La Herrada wind farm involves an investment of approximately **52 million euros**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: Construction of the La Herrada wind farm officially begins.
- **November 2023**: The facility receives its Siemens Gamesa wind turbines, marking a significant milestone in the construction process.

#### 4. Labour
- **November 2023**: The construction of the La Herrada wind farm is expected to create around **160 direct jobs** during peak construction periods. Additionally, it will provide stable employment for **7 professionals** during the operation and maintenance phase, along with **6 indirect jobs**.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The articles do not specify any joint ventures or partnerships; therefore, it can be inferred that Capital Energy owns the La Herrada facility exclusively.
