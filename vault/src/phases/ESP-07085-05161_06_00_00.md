## ESP-07085-05161

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Grupo Cobra Facility in Trujillo, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2018
   - **Details:** Grupo Cobra is responsible for the construction of a solar plant with a production capacity of 50 MW. The facility will consist of 151,500 photovoltaic (PV) modules of 330 Wp, mounted on a horizontal single-axis solar tracking system, along with 27 inverters. The total area occupied by the facility will be 161.2 hectares.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2018
   - **Details:** The financing for the construction of the 50 MW La Solanilla PV project was secured by Fotowatio Renewable Ventures (FRV) from Banco Sabadell. However, the specific amount of financing was not disclosed.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2018
   - **Details:** Construction of the facility is scheduled to start at the end of 2018, with a requirement to begin delivering power by the end of 2019. The project was selected by the Spanish government in a previous auction.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** November 2018
   - **Details:** Approximately 300 jobs are expected to be created during the construction phase of the facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** November 2018
   - **Details:** Grupo Cobra is acting as the Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) contractor for the facility, which indicates that it is involved in the construction but does not imply exclusive ownership. The facility is part of a project led by FRV, a unit of Abdul Latif Jameel Energy.
