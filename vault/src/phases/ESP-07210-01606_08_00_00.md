   - **April 2020:** Elecnor commissioned a 50MW wind farm in Cofrentes, consisting of 13 turbines of 3.83MW each, produced by GE Renewable Energy. The facility is expected to generate 155,000 MWh of energy annually, sufficient to power 43,000 homes and offset 66,000 tonnes of CO2 each year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **April 2020:** The project required an investment of €53 million, financed by Banco Sabadell through its Project Finance programme, which supports major projects in energy generation.
