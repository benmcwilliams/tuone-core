### Evolutionary Timeline of the Naturgy Facility in Batea, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2023**: Naturgy begins construction of the Tres Termes wind farm in Batea and Vilalba dels Arcs, with an installed power capacity of 30 MW. This facility is part of a broader plan to reach nearly 250 renewable MW in Catalonia. The Tres Termes wind farm is expected to generate renewable energy equivalent to the consumption of 26,700 homes, with a planned production of 87 GWh/year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2023**: Naturgy announces an investment of 40 million euros for the construction of the Tres Termes wind farm. This investment is part of a larger commitment to renewable energy development in Catalonia, following the incorporation of four new wind farms into its portfolio.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2023**: Construction of the Tres Termes wind farm begins, with an expected connection to the grid in the last quarter of 2024. This facility is in addition to the launch of two other wind farms (Los Barrancs and Punta Redona) earlier in the year, which together added 50 MW of renewable power to the Catalan energy system.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2023**: The construction of the Tres Termes wind farm is projected to generate around 400 direct and indirect jobs. Additionally, the earlier projects (Los Barrancs and Punta Redona) generated approximately 650 direct and indirect jobs during their construction.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The articles do not specify the ownership structure of the Tres Termes wind farm, but it can be inferred that Naturgy owns the facility exclusively, as it is described as part of Naturgy's commitment to renewable energy development in Catalonia.
