### Evolutionary Timeline of the Naturgy Facility in Vilalba dels Arcs, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2023**: Naturgy begins construction of the Tres Termes wind farm in Vilalba dels Arcs, with an installed power capacity of 30 MW. This facility is part of a broader initiative to reach nearly 250 renewable MW in Catalonia. The Tres Termes wind farm is expected to generate renewable energy equivalent to the consumption of 26,700 homes, with a planned production of 87 GWh/year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2023**: Naturgy announces an investment of 40 million euros for the construction of the Tres Termes wind farm. This investment is part of a larger commitment to renewable energy development in Catalonia, which includes the recent launch of two other wind farms (Los Barrancs and Punta Redona) and various hybridization projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2023**: Construction of the Tres Termes wind farm begins, with an expected connection to the grid in the last quarter of 2024. This project follows the earlier launch of the Los Barrancs (23 MW) and Punta Redona (27 MW) wind farms in the first quarter of the same year.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2023**: The construction of the Tres Termes wind farm is projected to generate around 400 direct and indirect jobs. Additionally, the earlier projects (Los Barrancs and Punta Redona) created approximately 650 direct and indirect jobs through the subcontracting of nearly forty companies from the Terra Alta region and the province of Tarragona.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
