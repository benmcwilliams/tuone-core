- **Date**: 10-06-2024
  - Elewit, through its Hesstec unit, announced plans to build a hybrid storage project in Lanzarote, featuring an 18.8 MVA virtual synchronous compensator and a 16 MW hybrid solution based on ultracapacitors. This facility is designed to be one of the largest systems of its type in Europe, aimed at providing stability to the Lanzarote-Fuerteventura electrical system.
