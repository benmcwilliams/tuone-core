## ESP-07231-01663

### Evolutionary Timeline of the GE Facility in Cubillos del Sil, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility will be the first wind blade recycling plant in Spain, focusing on providing a second life to wind turbine blade materials through circular economy practices. It aims to recycle fibreglass, carbon fibre, and other by-products for reuse in the wind power sector and other industries.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The total investment for the facility is €8.5 million. The project is part of Endesa's Futur-e Plan for the Compostilla industrial complex.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Announcement Date:** January 31, 2022 - GE, LM Wind Power, PreZero Espana, and Reciclalia Composite announced their partnership to establish the recycling facility.
   - **Construction Start:** Scheduled to begin in 2023.
   - **Operational Start:** Expected to be operational by early 2024.

4. **Labour:**
   - The facility is projected to create 30 direct jobs upon becoming operational.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is a collaborative project involving multiple partners: GE Renewable Energy, LM Wind Power, Endesa, PreZero Espana, and Reciclalia Composite. It is not solely owned by GE but is part of a joint venture with these companies.
