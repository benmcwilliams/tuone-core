## ESP-07231-02557

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Endesa Facility in Cubillos del Sil, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2022**: Endesa, in partnership with PreZero Espana and Reciclalia Composite, announced the establishment of the first wind blade recycling plant in Spain, located in Cubillos del Sil. The facility aims to recycle disused wind blades, applying circular economy principles to provide a second life to blade materials, including fibreglass and carbon fibre. The facility is designed to process over 2,000 wind blades per year, equating to approximately 6,000 tons of fibreglass composite.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2022**: The construction of the facility is backed by an investment of €8.5 million. The project is part of Endesa's Futur-e Plan for the Compostilla industrial complex and has received recognition and support from the European Union’s HORIZON EUROPE Framework Program.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2022**: Announcement of the project and construction plans.
- **June 2023**: Formation of Grineo, a company created to manage the recycling of blades at the facility. Construction is expected to begin in 2024, with the facility projected to be operational by early 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **January 2022**: The facility is expected to create approximately 30 direct jobs upon becoming operational.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **January 2022**: The facility is a collaborative project involving Endesa, PreZero Espana, and Reciclalia Composite, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by Endesa.
