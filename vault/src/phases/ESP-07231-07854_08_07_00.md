### Evolutionary Timeline of the Grineo Facility in Cubillos del Sil, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: June 2023
   - **Details**: The Grineo facility is designed to be the first wind blade recycling plant in the Iberian Peninsula. It aims to recycle over 2,000 disused wind blades annually, processing approximately 6,000 tons of fiberglass composite from wind turbines. The facility will convert these materials into raw materials for various applications, including the automotive industry, construction, and ceramics.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: June 2023
   - **Details**: The project has received recognition and funding from the European Union’s HORIZON EUROPE Framework Program, which supports research and innovation. This funding is part of the BLADES2BUILD project, involving 14 leading companies and research centers across Europe.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: June 2023
   - **Details**: The company Grineo was officially constituted to manage the recycling of wind blades at the facility. Construction of the plant is expected to begin in 2024.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: June 2023
   - **Details**: The facility is projected to create around 30 permanent jobs once operational.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: June 2023
   - **Details**: The Grineo facility is a joint venture between PreZero Spain and Endesa, indicating that it is not exclusively owned by a single company.
