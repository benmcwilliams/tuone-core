## ESP-07231-07992

### Evolutionary Timeline of PreZero Espana Facility in Cubillos del Sil, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: The facility will be the first wind blade recycling plant in Spain, aimed at providing a second life to wind turbine blade materials through circular economy practices.
- **Production Capacity**: Specific production capacity details are not provided, but the facility will recycle excess fibreglass generated during the manufacture of blades and incorporate recycled materials back into the industry.
- **Updates**: The facility will also focus on the re-use of recycled fibreglass, carbon fibre, and other by-products for various applications, including wind power and other sectors.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Amount**: The construction of the facility is backed by an investment of €8.5 million.
- **Support**: The project is part of Endesa's Futur-e Plan for the Compostilla industrial complex and involves partnerships with GE Renewable Energy and LM Wind Power.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Announcement Date**: The project was announced on January 31, 2022.
- **Construction Start**: Construction is set to begin in 2023.
- **Operational Date**: The facility is expected to be operational by the beginning of 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The facility is projected to create 30 direct jobs upon becoming operational.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: The facility is a collaborative project involving multiple partners: PreZero Espana, Endesa, GE Renewable Energy, LM Wind Power, and Reciclalia Composite. Therefore, it is not under exclusive ownership by PreZero Espana but rather a joint venture with shared responsibilities among the partners.
