## ESP-07231-07993

### Evolutionary Timeline of PreZero Spain Facility in Cubillos del Sil, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: PreZero Spain, in collaboration with Endesa, announced the construction of the first wind blade recycling plant in the Iberian Peninsula, located in Cubillos del Sil (León). The facility aims to recycle over 2,000 disused wind blades annually, processing approximately 6,000 tons of fiberglass composite. The recycled materials will be repurposed for various industries, including automotive, construction, and ceramics, promoting a circular economy.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The project has received recognition and funding from the European Union’s HORIZON EUROPE Framework Program, which supports research and innovation initiatives. This funding is part of the BLADES2BUILD project, involving 14 leading companies and research centers across Europe.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: The establishment of Grineo, a company created to manage the recycling of wind blades at the facility, was announced. Construction of the plant is expected to begin in 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2023**: The new wind blade recycling plant is projected to create around 30 permanent jobs upon completion.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2023**: The facility is a joint project between PreZero Spain and Endesa, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by either company. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic initiatives surrounding the PreZero Spain facility in Cubillos del Sil, highlighting its role in advancing clean technology and promoting sustainability in the wind energy sector.
