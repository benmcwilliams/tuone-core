### Evolutionary Timeline of the Reciclalia Composite Facility in Cubillos del Sil, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility will be the first wind blade recycling plant in Spain, focusing on recycling materials from wind turbine blades. It aims to apply circular economy principles by reusing and incorporating recycled fibreglass, carbon fibre, and other by-products back into the industry for wind power and other sectors.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The total investment for the facility is €8.5 million. The project is part of Endesa's Futur-e Plan for the Compostilla industrial complex.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Announcement Date:** January 31, 2022 - The partnership to build the facility was announced, involving GE, LM Wind Power, PreZero Espana, and Reciclalia Composite.
   - **Construction Start:** Scheduled to begin in 2023.
   - **Operational Start:** Expected to be operational by the beginning of 2024.

4. **Labour:**
   - The facility is projected to create 30 direct jobs once it becomes operational.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
