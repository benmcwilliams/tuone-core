### Evolutionary Timeline of the Endesa Facility in Enguídanos, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2021**: Endesa, through its renewable energy unit Enel Green Power España (EGPE), began construction of the Campillo Wind Cluster, which includes three wind farms: Campillo I, II, and III. 
  - **Production Capacity**: 
    - Campillo I: 76.8 MW
    - Campillo II: 91.2 MW
    - Campillo III: 91.2 MW
  - **Total Production**: Approximately 660 GWh per year, exceeding the annual consumption of a city like Cuenca.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2021**: Endesa announced an investment of around 256 million euros for the construction of the Campillo Wind Cluster.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2021**: Construction of the Campillo Wind Cluster commenced, involving continuous dialogue with local representatives and authorities to minimize environmental impact and maximize social benefits.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2021**: The construction of the three wind farms is expected to employ around 350 people from the local area, aligning with Endesa's Shared Value Creation plan.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Endesa, through its subsidiary Enel Green Power España, is responsible for the construction and operation of the Campillo Wind Cluster, indicating full ownership of the facility.
