### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enel Facility in Totana, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2018**: Enel Green Power España (EGPE) breaks ground on the Totana solar farm, which has a system rating of **84.7 MW**. The facility will consist of approximately **248,000 solar modules** and is expected to generate around **150 GWh** of electricity annually. The project is part of a larger **339 MW solar project pipeline** awarded to Enel in a government auction.
- **September 2019**: The **85 MW Totana solar plant** is connected to the grid, marking it as the first project to come online from the renewables auction held by the Spanish government in July 2017.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2018**: Enel announces an investment of **€59 million** (approximately **$69 million**) for the construction of the Totana solar farm. This investment is part of a broader strategy to enhance Spain's renewable energy generation capacity.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2018**: Construction begins on the Totana solar farm.
- **September 2019**: The Totana solar plant is officially plugged into the grid, ahead of its planned operational date in the third quarter of 2019.
