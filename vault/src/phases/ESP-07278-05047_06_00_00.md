### Evolutionary Timeline of the Foresight Facility in Las Torres de Cotillas, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2017:** Foresight Group began construction of a 3.9 MW unsubsidized solar power facility named Torre de Cotillas 1 in Las Torres de Cotillas, Murcia. This facility is part of a broader 18 MW development in the region. Once operational, it is expected to generate 7 GWh of green electricity annually, which will be sold under a long-term power purchase agreement (PPA) with Energya-VM.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **December 2017:** The project is part of Foresight's strategy to develop a multi-megawatt portfolio in Southern Europe without regulatory support. The facility's revenue will be derived solely from the sale of the generated power, marking a significant step in the company's investment strategy in the solar sector.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2017:** Construction of the Torre de Cotillas 1 facility commenced. The project is notable for being Foresight's second unsubsidized solar asset under construction in Iberia within the same year. The grid connection is anticipated in the first half of 2019.
