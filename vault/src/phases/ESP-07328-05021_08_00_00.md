### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ferrovial Facility in Pontevedra, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: Ferrovial, in partnership with RWE, announced plans to develop floating offshore wind projects off the Spanish coast, including the Pontevedra area. The partnership aims to contribute to Spain's target of achieving 3 gigawatts (GW) of floating offshore wind capacity by 2030. Ferrovial has submitted expressions of interest for four wind farms in Spain, with a total installed capacity of 1,750 MW, including projects in Pontevedra.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2023**: The partnership between Ferrovial and RWE is part of a broader strategy to capitalize on the growing offshore wind market in Spain, which is seen as a highly attractive market with significant development opportunities. The Spanish government has prioritized floating wind technology due to favorable conditions.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2023**: The announcement of the partnership and the submission of expressions of interest for four wind farms, including the Pontevedra project, marks a significant milestone in the development of offshore wind energy in the region.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2023**: The partnership is expected to create new, future-proof jobs in the local communities as the offshore wind projects are developed, contributing to local industry and employment.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2023**: The facility in Pontevedra is part of a joint venture between Ferrovial and RWE, indicating that Ferrovial does not own the facility exclusively but is collaborating with RWE to develop and operate the offshore wind projects.
