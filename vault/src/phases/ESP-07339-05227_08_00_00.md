### Evolutionary Timeline of Heineken Facility in Puebla de Guzmán, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2019
   - **Details:** Heineken signed a power purchase agreement (PPA) with Iberdrola to fully power its Spanish operations through a new utility-scale solar plant. The facility will utilize 147,000 polycrystalline silicon 340Wp modules and is designed to produce 50MW of solar energy, supplying approximately 82GWh of clean energy annually. The solar installation will be integrated within Iberdrola’s existing 292MW wind farm, El Andévalo.

   - **Date:** September 2019
   - **Details:** The PPA with Iberdrola represents a significant investment in renewable energy, aimed at supporting Heineken's sustainability goals. The project is part of a broader initiative to transition to renewable energy sources across Heineken's operations in Spain.

   - **Date:** September 2019
   - **Details:** The solar plant is set to become operational in 2020, marking a key milestone in Heineken's commitment to renewable energy. The project is part of Heineken's strategy to ensure that all links in its production chain are powered by renewable energy by 2023.
