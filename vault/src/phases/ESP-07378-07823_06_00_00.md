1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **April 2018:** Gas Natural Fenosa announced the construction of a 49 MW photovoltaic (PV) project in La Nava, located between Almodovar del Campo and Puertollano in Ciudad Real, Castilla La Mancha. This facility marks the company's first solar project in Spain's central region. The plant is expected to exceed the annual electricity demand for the residents of the two towns.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **April 2018:** The company invested approximately €34 million in the La Nava PV project. This investment is part of a broader strategy, as Gas Natural Fenosa was awarded 250 MW of PV projects in Spain’s July 2017 auction, which would require around €165 million of investment.
