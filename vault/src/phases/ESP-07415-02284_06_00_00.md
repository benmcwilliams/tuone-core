### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hive Facility in Espejo, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2018**: Hive Energy received planning approval for a 45.6 MW solar farm in Espejo, Cordoba, marking it as the first subsidy-free photovoltaic plant in the Andalucia region. The project, named El Salobral, is designed to provide renewable energy to approximately 32,000 homes and will connect to the national grid via a nearby 132kV Endesa substation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2018**: Hive Energy announced plans to sell power generated from the El Salobral project to the spot market. The company indicated that it owns 14 solar projects totaling 896 MW, with grid approval from Spain’s grid operator, Red Eléctrica de España (REE), and has a pipeline of over 20 additional projects anticipated to begin planning and construction in the next two years.
