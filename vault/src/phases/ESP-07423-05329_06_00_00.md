### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ib Vogt Facility in Castile and Leon, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2024**: Ib Vogt has commenced construction on a solar portfolio comprising three projects in Castile and Leon, specifically around Segovia. The total production capacity of the portfolio is 418 MW, divided into:
  - **Serbal**: 174 MW
  - **Castano**: 134 MW
  - **Pato**: 110 MW
  All three solar arrays utilize high-efficiency bifacial modules and single-axis tracker systems to maximize power output. Ib Vogt will provide full engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) services and will manage operation and maintenance (O&M) and asset management post-construction.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2024**: The facility achieved financial close on a project financing debt facility amounting to approximately €350 million. The financing was provided by Nord/LB, Santander CIB, and BBVA. The projects have long-term pay-as-produced fixed price Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) in place with investment-grade offtakers.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2024**: Construction has officially started on all three solar sites.
- **December 2024**: Commercial operation for the Serbal and Castano projects is slated to begin.
- **Q1 2025**: The Pato project is expected to be fully operational.
