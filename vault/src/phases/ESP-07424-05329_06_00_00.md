### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ib Vogt Facility in Toro, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2022**: Ib Vogt announced the construction of a 150MW solar plant, known as the Garnacha project, located near Toro in the province of Zamora, Spain. The facility will cover an area of 224 hectares and will consist of over 270,000 solar panels mounted on north-south single-axis trackers for optimal performance. The plant is part of Ib Vogt's Independent Power Producer (IPP) platform.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2022**: Financial close for the project was reached on August 25, 2022, with a total investment of €118 million. This includes a €90 million long-term project financing facility provided by Norddeutsche Landesbank Girozentrale (NORD/LB) and Santander Corporate & Investment Banking (Santander CIB).

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2022**: Construction of the Garnacha project began, with commercial operation planned for October 2023. The project is expected to support the region's transition towards clean energy.
