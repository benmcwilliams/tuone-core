### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Caravaca de la Cruz, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2019**: Iberdrola commissioned a lithium-ion energy storage system with a capacity of 3 MWh in Caravaca de la Cruz. This facility is designed to improve the quality of electricity supply, particularly for nearby solar projects, and can provide up to five hours of electricity during supply interruptions. The intelligent system installed can manage grid operations based on consumption and production capacity.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2019**: The investment for the energy storage system was approximately €2 million. This project was realized by the i-DE distribution company, a subsidiary of Iberdrola.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2019**: The energy storage facility was officially commissioned and operational, aimed at stabilizing the grid and supporting local solar energy generation.
