### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Castejón, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 05-11-2020
   - **Details:** Iberdrola announced plans to install solar power at its combined-cycle power plants in Spain, including the facility in Castejón. The project will involve the installation of solar arrays with a total capacity of 32.5 MW across multiple locations, including Castejón. Specifically, the facility will have a solar project with a capacity of 3.5 MW. The solar arrays are expected to generate approximately 50,000 MWh of electricity annually.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 05-11-2020
   - **Details:** Iberdrola is investing €2.3 million (approximately $2.7 million) in the solar project at its combined-cycle power plant in Castejón.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 05-11-2020
   - **Details:** The project at the Castejón facility is part of a broader initiative to enhance combined-cycle power plants with solar energy. The commissioning of the solar project is scheduled for the end of 2020. Administrative authorization for the project was secured at the end of July 2020.
