### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Ekian, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **2020:** Announcement of the Ekienea project, a 100MW solar PV plant in the Basque country, Spain. The facility is designed to be the largest solar farm in northern Spain and is located near the existing 24MW Ekian solar PV plant, which has been operational since 2020.
   - **August 2024:** Iberdrola secures grid access for the Ekienea project, which spans 200 hectares, with half of the area dedicated to the solar PV plant and the other half for environmental compensation and forest preservation.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **2020:** The Ekienea project required an investment of €70 million (US$78 million).
   - **2024:** Iberdrola announced plans to invest €15.5 billion (US$17.2 billion) in renewables by 2026, which includes commissioning 6GW of solar PV and onshore wind by 2027.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **2020:** Announcement of the Ekienea project.
   - **2023:** The project was expected to be operational.
   - **August 2024:** Iberdrola secures grid access for the Ekienea solar PV plant.
