## ESP-07458-07947

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Navantia Seanergies Facility in Fene, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2022**: Navantia Seanergies and Windar Renovables announced the establishment of a new XXL monopile factory at the Fene shipyard, aimed at producing monopiles for offshore wind farms. The factory will produce monopiles with lengths of approximately 90 meters, diameters between 8 to 10 meters, and weights up to 2,000 tonnes.
- **March 2023**: A reserved capacity agreement was signed with Ocean Winds for the manufacture of components for floating offshore wind farms, with production expected to average between 8 to 12 floating foundations per year from 2027 to 2031.
- **April 2023**: Navantia Seanergies and Windar secured a contract to manufacture 45 monopiles for Iberdrola's 1400MW East Anglia 3 offshore wind farm, marking the largest contract for monopiles to date for the facility.
- **August 2023**: The first batch of 14 XXL monopiles for the Moray West offshore wind farm was loaded out, marking the first production from the new factory.
- **July 2024**: A contract was awarded to manufacture 21 monopiles for Iberdrola’s Windanker offshore wind farm in Germany, further solidifying the facility's role in the offshore wind market.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2022**: The establishment of the XXL monopile factory involved an investment of over €36 million.
- **April 2023**: The factory underwent adaptations to accommodate the production of monopiles, including new equipment and facilities for bending thick sheet metal and painting.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2022**: The contract for the Moray West offshore wind farm was awarded, with production set to start in December 2022.
- **March 2023**: The reserved capacity agreement with Ocean Winds was signed, covering future projects from 2027 to 2031.
- **April 2023**: The contract for 45 monopiles for the East Anglia 3 project was secured, with production scheduled to begin in June 2023.
- **August 2023**: The first batch of monopiles for the Moray West project was loaded out.
- **July 2024**: A contract for 21 monopiles for the Windanker offshore wind farm was awarded, with manufacturing planned to start in July 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2022**: The Moray West project is expected to generate 280,000 hours of work, averaging 150 direct jobs per month during production.
- **April 2023**: The East Anglia 3 project is projected to create around 450 jobs over 15 months.
- **March 2023**: The agreement with Ocean Winds is expected to generate an average of 1,000 direct jobs over the five-year period.
- **July 2024**: The Windanker project will generate around 210 jobs during the manufacturing phase.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is operated as a joint venture between Navantia Seanergies and Windar Renovables, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by a single entity.
