## ESP-07458-08182

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Windar Facility in Fene, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **02/2021**: Iberdrola signs a €400 million framework agreement with Navantia and Windar for the manufacture and supply of 130 XXL monopile foundations for offshore wind farms, with production capacity enhancements planned at the Fene shipyard.
- **07/2022**: The facility begins production of its first order for 14 monopiles for the Moray West wind farm in Scotland, marking the first execution of contracts from the new factory.
- **04/2023**: Navantia and Windar secure a contract to manufacture 45 monopiles for Iberdrola's East Anglia 3 offshore wind farm, the largest contract for this type of foundation to date.
- **07/2024**: The consortium is awarded a contract to deliver 21 monopiles for Iberdrola’s Windanker offshore wind farm in Germany.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **02/2021**: The agreement with Iberdrola includes an investment to set up a factory for XXL monopiles at the Navantia shipyard in Fene, enhancing production capabilities.
- **07/2022**: An investment of over €36 million is confirmed for the new factory, which will allow for the production of monopiles with a maximum weight of 2,000 tons.
- **04/2023**: The factory's investment is reiterated, with a focus on adapting facilities for bending thick sheet metal and incorporating advanced manufacturing technology.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **02/2021**: Announcement of the construction of the XXL monopile factory at the Fene shipyard.
- **07/2022**: Start of material collection for the first monopile order, with production commencing in December 2022.
- **06/2023**: Production of monopiles for the East Anglia 3 project begins, with a timeline of 15 months for completion.
- **07/2024**: Manufacturing work for the Windanker project is scheduled to start following the final investment decision.

#### 4. Labour
- **07/2022**: The Moray West project is expected to generate 280,000 hours of work, averaging 150 direct jobs per month during production.
- **04/2023**: The East Anglia 3 project is projected to create around 450 jobs across Navantia, Windar, and their auxiliary industry.
- **07/2024**: The Windanker project will generate approximately 210 jobs during its manufacturing phase.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is operated as a joint venture between Navantia and Windar Renovables, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by either company.
