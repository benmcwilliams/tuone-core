- **Cofrentes I Photovoltaic Plant**: Iberdrola is developing the Cofrentes I photovoltaic plant, which will have a capacity of 154.66 MW. The plant will utilize bifacial monocrystalline silicon modules from Trina, with a total of 77,700 to 101,724 modules, depending on the final configuration.
