### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in La Gomera, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2021:** Iberdrola announced it secured 36.3MW of solar capacity through the ‘SolCan’ programme, which is part of a broader initiative to develop solar projects in the Canary Islands. The facility in La Gomera is one of the projects contributing to the total capacity of 255MW across the islands.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **March 2021:** The facility in La Gomera is part of a larger funding initiative where Spain’s government and the European Union will provide €20 million (US$24 million) to support the development of 65 new solar projects in the Canary Islands. This funding is aimed at reducing electricity prices and increasing the share of renewables in the islands' energy mix.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 2021:** It was announced that the developments, including the La Gomera facility, are expected to be commissioned by the end of 2022. This is part of the ‘SolCan’ programme, which represents the third phase of Spain’s renewable energy island programme.
