## ESP-07477-01118

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in New Aquitaine, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: Iberdrola has partnered with the Oil’Ive Green network to develop agrivoltaic power plants specifically designed for olive cultivation. These plants will be mounted on solar trackers and are intended to protect olive farms from climate-related hazards while enhancing productivity. The agrivoltaic systems will be adapted to support olive trees grown in "fruit hedges" across several hectares.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: The partnership aims to cultivate 50,000 hectares of olive trees by the end of the decade, indicating significant investment in agrivoltaic technology to support this agricultural initiative. Specific financial details regarding the investment were not disclosed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: The project is in the planning phase, with Iberdrola and Oil’Ive Green identifying farms in New Aquitaine and other regions to host the agrivoltaic projects. The agrivoltaic plants are expected to start production from the third year of operation.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: The agrivoltaic initiative is expected to provide technical and agronomic assistance to wine growers and olive producers, which may lead to job creation in the agricultural sector as farmers diversify into olive cultivation and benefit from the agrivoltaic systems.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: 06-12-2023
   - **Details**: Iberdrola is in an exclusive partnership with the Oil’Ive Green network, indicating a collaborative approach rather than full ownership of the facilities. The partnership focuses on developing agrivoltaic solutions in synergy with olive growing.
