## ESP-07488-01118

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in San Cebrián de Mazote, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2024**: Iberdrola announced the construction of the Peñaflor III Hybrid Photovoltaic Plant with a capacity of 48.12 MW, which will utilize 106,923 bifacial monocrystalline silicon solar panels and 14 inverters. This plant will be hybridized with the existing Peñaflor III wind farm (49 MW).
- **August 2024**: A second hybridization project, Peñaflor IV, was authorized, also with a capacity of 48.12 MW, to be hybridized with the Peñaflor IV wind farm in San Cebrián de Mazote, featuring similar technical specifications as Peñaflor III.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2024**: The total investment for the Peñaflor III project is reported at €38,847,591, while the Peñaflor IV project has an execution budget of €27,210,337. These investments are part of a larger €380 million investment across five photovoltaic and hybridization projects in various provinces.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2024**: Iberdrola obtained authorization for the construction of the Peñaflor III and Peñaflor IV projects.
- **December 2024**: The Spanish government approved the prior administrative authorizations for both Peñaflor III and Peñaflor IV installations, confirming their capacity of 48.118 MW each.
