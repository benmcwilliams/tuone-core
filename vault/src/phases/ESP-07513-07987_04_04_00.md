### Evolutionary Timeline of the Porcelanosa Facility in Villarreal, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **April 2021:** Porcelanosa, in collaboration with Iberdrola, initiated the GREENH2SKER project aimed at decarbonizing its production process. The project intends to replace up to 50% of the natural gas used in the facility with green hydrogen. This will be achieved by installing an electrolyser powered by a solar PV system, along with a high-efficiency structure to utilize excess waste heat from production lines.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **April 2021:** The GREENH2SKER project has been presented to the European Innovation Fund Small Scale Projects programme for potential investment. Iberdrola is significantly increasing its investment in renewable energy, targeting €150 billion by the end of the decade, which may indirectly support the facility's transition to green hydrogen.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **April 2021:** Announcement of the GREENH2SKER project, marking the first partnership between Iberdrola and a ceramic manufacturer for a hydrogen initiative. The project is part of a broader strategy to decarbonize thermal energy-intensive industries.
