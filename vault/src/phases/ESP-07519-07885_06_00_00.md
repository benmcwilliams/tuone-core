## ESP-07519-07885

### Ibox Energy Facility Timeline in Posadas, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2020
   - **Details:** Ibox Energy announced the launch of a 50MW photovoltaic (PV) solar project in Posadas, Spain. The facility is designed to produce approximately 100GWh of electricity annually. The construction is being managed by PowerChina Group.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** April 2020
   - **Details:** The total investment for the 50MW solar plant is €37 million (approximately US$40 million). Financing is provided by Ibox Energy and Triodos Bank, marking Triodos' first solar Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) financing in Spain.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** April 2020
   - **Details:** Construction of the facility began shortly after April 13, 2020, when Spain lifted a construction freeze due to COVID-19. The project is expected to be operational by the last quarter of 2020, with a commitment to complete it within approximately six months.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** April 2020
   - **Details:** The construction phase will require around 200 workers at its peak. Safety measures have been implemented to protect workers during the COVID-19 pandemic.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** April 2020
   - **Details:** The facility is a partnership between Ibexia Development and Cox Energy, indicating a joint venture ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by a single entity.
