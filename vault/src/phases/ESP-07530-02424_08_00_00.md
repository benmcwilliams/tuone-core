#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **01-10-2020**: Repsol announces the completion of the grid connection for the first turbines at its 335MW Delta wind complex in Aragon, Spain. The facility is set to begin full commercial operations at the end of 2020. Additionally, Repsol's portfolio includes Delta 2, which comprises 26 wind farms located in Huesca, Zaragoza, and Teruel, totaling 860MW capacity.
