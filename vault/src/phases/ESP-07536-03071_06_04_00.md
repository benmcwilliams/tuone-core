### Isofoton Facility Timeline in Southeastern Ohio, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 05-10-2010
   - **Details:** The Isofoton facility is part of the Turning Point Solar project, which will produce solar photovoltaic (PV) power. The facility is associated with a 49.9MW solar power plant, which is one of the largest in North America. Isofoton, along with other companies, will build manufacturing facilities to provide key components for the solar array.

   - **Date:** 05-10-2010
   - **Details:** The project is contingent upon governmental incentives and agreements. A memorandum of understanding has been signed between American Electric Power Ohio, New Harvest Ventures, and Agile Energy for a 20-year power purchase agreement (PPA) for the electricity generated by the solar project. Specific terms of the PPA were not disclosed.
