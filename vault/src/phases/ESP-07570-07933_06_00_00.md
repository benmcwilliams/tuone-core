### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mirova Facility in Menorca, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2024**: Mirova, an affiliate of Natixis Investment Managers, announced the acquisition of a **60MWp solar park** in Menorca, Spain, with the potential for an additional **30MW of storage capacity**. The facility is located in the municipality of Mahon. The solar PV plant is expected to produce approximately **92,000 MWh annually**, which is about **20% of the island's annual electricity demand**. The project is described as "ready-to-build" and is set to start construction by **mid-2024**, with an expected commissioning date in **early 2026**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2024**: The acquisition was executed through the **Mirova Energy Transition 5 (MET5)** fund, indicating a significant investment in clean energy infrastructure aimed at decarbonizing energy production, storage, and consumption in Europe.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Mid-2024**: Construction of the solar PV plant is expected to commence.
- **Early 2026**: The commissioning of the solar PV plant is anticipated.
