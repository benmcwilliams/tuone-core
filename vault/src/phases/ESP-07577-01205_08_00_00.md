### Evolutionary Timeline of the Naturgy Facility in Agüimes, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: Naturgy commissioned the Agüimes wind farm with a production capacity of **10.75 MW**. This facility is equipped with **three wind turbines** and is expected to produce **32 GWh/year** of energy, which is equivalent to the annual consumption of more than **9,300 homes**. The facility will also prevent the emission of over **21,800 tons of CO2** annually.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The construction of the Agüimes wind farm required an investment of **10.6 million euros**. Additionally, the overall investment for the two new wind farms (Agüimes and Camino de la Madera) amounted to **20.5 million euros**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: Naturgy announced the commissioning of the Agüimes wind farm, marking it as one of the twelve wind farms managed by the company in the Canary Islands. The facility is part of a broader initiative that includes plans for additional solar generation capacity in Agüimes by the end of 2023.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2023**: The construction of the Agüimes wind farm generated nearly **150 jobs**, both direct and indirect, through collaboration with **16 companies** on the island.
