## ESP-07578-01205

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Naturgy Facility in Camino de la Madera, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: The Camino de la Madera wind farm, equipped with four wind turbines, has a production capacity of 9.2 MW and is expected to generate 27.5 GWh of energy annually, which is equivalent to the annual electricity consumption of approximately 7,800 homes. The facility contributes to reducing annual CO2 emissions by over 13,700 tons.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The construction of the Camino de la Madera wind farm involved an investment of nearly 10 million euros. This investment is part of a larger project that includes another wind farm in Agüimes, with a total investment of 20.5 million euros for both facilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: Naturgy officially commissioned the Camino de la Madera wind farm, marking its operational status alongside the Agüimes wind farm. This commissioning is part of a broader initiative where Naturgy manages a total of twelve wind farms in the Canary Islands, with a combined installed capacity of 94.8 MW.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2023**: The construction of the Camino de la Madera wind farm generated nearly 150 jobs, both direct and indirect, through collaboration with 16 companies on the island.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2023**: Naturgy owns the Camino de la Madera facility exclusively, as it is part of their portfolio of renewable energy projects in the Canary Islands.
