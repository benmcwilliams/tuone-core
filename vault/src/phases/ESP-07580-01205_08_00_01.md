### Evolutionary Timeline of the Naturgy Facility in Gran Canaria, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: Naturgy commissioned two new wind farms in Gran Canaria: Agüimes (10.75 MW) and Camino de la Madera (9.2 MW). The total installed capacity of Naturgy's wind farms in the Canary Islands reached 94.8 MW. The Agüimes wind farm is expected to produce 32 GWh/year, while Camino de la Madera will produce 27.5 GWh/year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The construction of the Agüimes and Camino de la Madera wind farms represented a total investment of €20.5 million, with Agüimes requiring €10.6 million and Camino de la Madera nearly €10 million. Additionally, Naturgy announced a global investment of €20 million for five new photovoltaic plants in Agüimes, which will add 30 MW of solar capacity.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: Naturgy announced the commissioning of the Agüimes and Camino de la Madera wind farms, marking the start of operations for these facilities. The Agüimes wind farm is noted as the third highest contributor to Naturgy's production in the Canary Islands.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2023**: The construction of the two new wind farms generated nearly 150 jobs, both direct and indirect, through collaboration with 16 companies on the island.
