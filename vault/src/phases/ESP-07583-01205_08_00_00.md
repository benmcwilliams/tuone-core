### Evolutionary Timeline of the Naturgy Facility in La Pobla Massaluca, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2023**: Naturgy begins construction of the Tres Termes wind farm in the province of Tarragona, which will have an installed power of **30 MW**. This facility is part of a broader plan to reach nearly **250 renewable MW** in Catalonia. The Tres Termes wind farm is expected to generate **87 GWh/year**, providing energy equivalent to the consumption of **26,700 homes**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2023**: Naturgy announces an investment of **40 million euros** for the construction of the Tres Termes wind farm. Additionally, the company invested nearly **61 million euros** in two other wind farms, Los Barrancs (23 MW) and Punta Redona (27 MW), which were launched in the first quarter of the same year.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2023**: Construction of the Tres Termes wind farm begins, with an expected connection to the grid in the **last quarter of 2024**. Earlier in the year, Naturgy launched the Los Barrancs and Punta Redona parks, adding **50 MW** of renewable power to the Catalan energy system.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2023**: The construction of the Tres Termes wind farm is projected to generate around **400 direct and indirect jobs**. The earlier projects (Los Barrancs and Punta Redona) also contributed to job creation, generating approximately **650 direct and indirect jobs** during their construction.
