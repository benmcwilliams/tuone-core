## ESP-07585-01205

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Naturgy Facility in Plasencia, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03/2023**: Naturgy is advancing the construction of the Merengue II wind farm and the Puerta del Jerte photovoltaic plant in Plasencia. The Merengue II wind farm will consist of 11 wind turbines with a total capacity of 50 MW, producing 128 GWh annually. The Puerta del Jerte photovoltaic plant will have a capacity of 30 MW, generating 62 GWh per year. Together, these facilities will add 80 MW to Naturgy's existing capacity in Extremadura, bringing the total to 142 MW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **03/2023**: The total investment for both projects is approximately 57 million euros, with 41 million euros allocated to the Merengue II wind farm and 16 million euros to the Puerta del Jerte photovoltaic plant.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03/2023**: The construction of both facilities is reported to be in advanced stages, with the wind turbines scheduled for installation in April and the photovoltaic plant at 30% completion. The commissioning of both projects is expected in the third quarter of 2023.
- **11/2023**: The Merengue II wind farm and the Puerta del Jerte photovoltaic plant are officially launched, marking their operational status.

#### 4. Labour
- **03/2023**: The construction of the Merengue II wind farm and the Puerta del Jerte photovoltaic plant has created over 860 direct and indirect jobs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The articles do not specify the ownership structure of the facilities, but it can be inferred that Naturgy owns the facilities as they are described as projects promoted by the company.

### Summary
The Naturgy facility in Plasencia, consisting of the Merengue II wind farm and the Puerta del Jerte photovoltaic plant, represents a significant investment in renewable energy in Extremadura. With a combined capacity of 80 MW, these facilities are expected to produce over 190 GWh of energy annually, contributing to substantial CO2 emissions reductions and job creation in the region. The projects reflect Naturgy's commitment to renewable energy and environmental sustainability.
