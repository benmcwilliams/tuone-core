### Evolutionary Timeline of the Naturgy Facility in Trucafort, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Production**: The Trucafort facility has an installed power capacity of 30 MW.
- **Future Plans**: Naturgy is committed to expanding its renewable energy capacity in Catalonia, aiming to reach a total of 250 MW through various projects, including the Tres Termes wind farm and hybridization with solar plants.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Recent Investment**: On December 12, 2023, Naturgy announced an investment of 40 million euros for the construction of the Tres Termes wind farm, which will add to its renewable capacity in the region.
- **Previous Investments**: Naturgy invested nearly 61 million euros in the Los Barrancs (23 MW) and Punta Redona (27 MW) wind farms, which were launched in the first quarter of 2023.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Current Status**: The Trucafort facility is operational with a capacity of 30 MW.
- **Recent Milestones**: The construction of the Tres Termes wind farm began in December 2023, with an expected connection to the grid in the last quarter of 2024. The Los Barrancs and Punta Redona parks were launched in early 2023.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The construction of the Tres Termes wind farm is expected to generate around 400 direct and indirect jobs. The construction of the Los Barrancs and Punta Redona parks generated approximately 650 direct and indirect jobs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: Naturgy owns the Trucafort facility exclusively, as indicated by its ongoing investments and operational control over the facility and its associated projects in the region.
