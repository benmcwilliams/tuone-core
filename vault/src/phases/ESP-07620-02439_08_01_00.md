### Evolutionary Timeline of Siemens Gamesa Facility in As Somozas, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12-01-2016**: Siemens Gamesa announced the addition of a third production line to its As Somozas blade factory in Galicia, Spain. The facility produces blades for the G114 2MW and G114 2.5MW turbines. The factory was already manufacturing blades for two wind farms located in Cyprus and Turkey.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12-01-2016**: The expansion of the facility with the new production line involved a gradual hiring of about 100 people throughout 2016, indicating a significant investment in both infrastructure and human resources.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **20-03-2018**: Siemens Gamesa secured an order from Iberdrola for seven 2.6-114 turbines for the Chimiche 2 wind farm project in Tenerife, with blades manufactured at the As Somozas facility. This marked a significant project milestone for the facility as it contributed to a specific wind farm development.

- **05-02-2019**: Siemens Gamesa announced contracts to supply 200MW of wind capacity across several regions in Spain, with blades for these projects being produced at the As Somozas facility. This included a total of 63 wind turbines for seven wind farms, further solidifying the facility's role in the company's operations.

#### 4. Labour
- **12-01-2016**: The expansion of the As Somozas facility was expected to create approximately 100 new jobs, increasing the workforce from 183 employees at the time of the announcement.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The articles do not specify any joint ventures or changes in ownership structure, indicating that Siemens Gamesa owns the As Somozas facility exclusively.
