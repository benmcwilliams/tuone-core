### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nervion Naval Offshore Facility in As Somozas, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** February 2023
   - **Details:** Nervion Naval Offshore, a subsidiary of Amper Group, is set to launch an offshore wind component plant in As Somozas. The facility will specialize in the manufacture and assembly of fixed and floating structures for offshore wind power, including primary and secondary components for fixed (jackets) and floating offshore wind turbine structures. The plant is expected to start operating in the second half of 2023.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** February 2023
   - **Details:** The facility will be located in the premises previously occupied by Siemens Gamesa, which sold the plant to Nervion Naval Offshore as part of a reindustrialization commitment after the factory ceased operations. This sale is tied to the establishment of an industrial project aimed at job creation in the area.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** February 2023
   - **Details:** Announcement of the launch of the offshore wind component plant, with operations expected to commence in the second half of 2023. The facility will complement Nervion Naval-Offshore's activities in the Port of Ferrol.
