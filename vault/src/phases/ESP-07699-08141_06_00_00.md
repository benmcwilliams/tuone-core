### Evolutionary Timeline of the University of Malaga Facility in Andalucia, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Project Name**: Agua+S
   - **Production Capacity**: The facility aims to produce 20 cubic hectometers (hm3) of desalinated water per year.
   - **Technology**: The project combines a desalination plant, a pumping station network, and an onshore floating photovoltaic plant. This is the first time these three facilities have been integrated into a single, reproducible design.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Estimated Cost**: The project is estimated to cost around €60 million.
   - **Payback Time**: The payback period for the investment is estimated to be between three and four years.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Announcement Date**: The project was announced in March 2022.
   - **Implementation Timeline**: The developers indicated that the project could be implemented in less than a year from the announcement.
