## ESP-07700-08075

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Solar Millennium AG Facility in Andasol 3, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **30-11-2009**: Solar Millennium AG announced that the Andasol 3 facility is a 50MW parabolic trough power plant currently under construction. The plant features a collector surface area of more than 60,000 sqm and includes a thermal storage facility, allowing for the supply of solar power after sunset on demand.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **30-11-2009**: Solar Millennium opened a fund for investors to finance the Andasol 3 project, offering an attractive 8% return on investment. The fund is aimed at German investors, with a minimum investment of €5,000. This fund is part of Solar Millennium's strategy to finance its projects through closed-end funds.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **30-11-2009**: The construction of the Andasol 3 power plant is confirmed to be underway. The facility is part of a series of projects that includes Andasol 1 and 2, which are already operational.

#### 4. Labour
- No specific information regarding job creation or job loss related to the Andasol 3 facility was mentioned in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **20-07-2009**: Solar Millennium AG sold a 25% stake in the Andasol 1 and 2 plants to ACS/Cobra Group, indicating a partial ownership structure for those facilities. However, the ownership structure for Andasol 3 is not explicitly detailed in the articles, but it is noted that the project is being implemented in partnership with Stadtwerke München, RWE Innogy, RheinEnergie, and MAN Ferrostaal, suggesting a joint venture or partnership model for this facility.
