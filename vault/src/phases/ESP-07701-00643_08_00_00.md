### Evolutionary Timeline of the RWE Facility in Basque Coast, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** February 2020
   - **Details:** RWE Renewables, in collaboration with Saitec Offshore Technologies, announced the establishment of a pilot project called DemoSATH to test floating offshore wind technology. The project will utilize SATH technology, which features a twin hull made of modularly prefabricated concrete elements. The facility aims to collect data on the construction, operation, and maintenance of floating wind turbines. A prototype with a 2MW turbine is planned to be assembled at the port of Bilbao and deployed in deep waters (85 meters) off the Basque Coast.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** February 2020
   - **Details:** RWE Renewables will finance part of the project costs associated with the DemoSATH pilot project. The project is expected to last three-and-a-half years, with 18 months allocated for planning and construction, followed by a two-year operational phase.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** February 2020
   - **Details:** The DemoSATH project is set to begin testing in 2021, with the plant expected to go into operation in the third quarter of 2021. A scaled 1:6 model will be deployed off the coast of Santander in April 2020, and the prototype will be towed to its anchorage point three kilometers off the coast.
