### Evolutionary Timeline of the S.A.G. Solarstrom Facility in Calzadilla de la Serena, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **10-10-2012:** S.A.G. Solarstrom, through a subsidiary, is involved in the development and construction of multiple photovoltaic (PV) plants in Badajoz, Spain, including three projects in Calzadilla de la Serena with capacities of 55MW, 132MW, and 88MW. The total output of the joint venture (JV) is projected to be around 440MWp, with the potential to generate approximately 660,000MWh of energy annually.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **10-10-2012:** The total investment volume for the four PV plants is noted to be in the three-digit million range. The project is supported by the Extremadura region’s government, which has guaranteed cooperation with local authorities and energy providers.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **10-10-2012:** The joint venture agreement is still subject to various contractual conditions, but construction is anticipated to begin in the second half of 2013, pending approval procedures.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **10-10-2012:** S.A.G. Solarstrom will hold a 51% stake in the joint venture company formed with Valsolar 2006 and Cavalum SGPS, indicating a joint ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership.
