### Evolutionary Timeline of Siemens Gamesa Facility in Agreda, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2018**: Siemens Gamesa's Agreda plant begins producing nacelles for wind turbines, specifically for the Chimiche 2 project in Tenerife, with a total capacity of 18MW.
- **September 2018**: The Agreda plant continues to manufacture nacelles for multiple turbine orders totaling 233MW for various wind farms in Spain.
- **December 2018**: Nacelles for 10 turbines (3.465MW each) for a project near Lugo are produced at the Agreda facility.
- **February 2019**: The Agreda plant is confirmed as the production site for nacelles for 63 wind turbines across several projects totaling 200MW.
- **July 2020**: The Agreda plant is noted for assembling nacelles for the Siemens Gamesa 5.X turbine, which is part of the company's strategy to focus on larger turbine models.
- **January 2021**: Nacelles for the Cabo Leones 3 wind farm in Chile are assembled at the Agreda plant, employing close to 200 people.
- **March 2021**: The Agreda facility is responsible for assembling nacelles for a 50MW wind project in Asturias.
- **June 2021**: Nacelles for two wind projects totaling 85MW in Zaragoza are produced at the Agreda plant.
- **July 2022**: The Agreda plant is confirmed as the assembly site for nacelles for a 50MW wind farm project with Kinesis Enerji.
- **September 2022**: Nacelles for the 104MW Buniel wind farm are produced at the Agreda facility.
- **December 2022**: The Agreda plant is designated for nacelle assembly for the 70MW Iglesias wind farm.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2019**: Siemens Gamesa secures contracts for 200MW of wind capacity, which includes significant investment in the Agreda facility for nacelle production.
- **May 2023**: Siemens Gamesa and Repsol sign contracts for 200MW of wind energy projects, which will involve nacelle assembly at the Agreda plant, indicating ongoing investment in the facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2018**: Announcement of nacelle production for the Chimiche 2 project.
- **September 2018**: Confirmation of nacelle production for multiple wind projects.
- **July 2020**: Announcement of the closure of the Aoiz factory, with a shift in focus to the Agreda plant for nacelle assembly.

- **June 2021**: Contracts signed for nacelle production for two wind projects in Zaragoza.
- **May 2023**: New contracts signed with Repsol for nacelle production at Agreda.

#### 4. Labour

- **June 2021**: The ongoing projects and contracts are expected to boost employment at the Agreda facility.
