### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sonnedix Facility in Betierra, Cuenca, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2024**: The Betierra facility, consisting of three 50 MW solar plants, achieved commercial operation. The facility generates nearly 300,000 MWh/year of clean electricity, sufficient to power over 71,000 households and avoid over 107,000 tons per year of CO2 emissions. This project is notable for its shared interconnection infrastructure, a first for Sonnedix’s Spanish portfolio.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2021**: The Betierra facility was part of a larger 300 MW portfolio acquired from RIC Energy, with the remaining 150 MW still in late development stages at that time. The acquisition reflects Sonnedix's commitment to long-term renewable energy projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: Sonnedix signed a 10-year Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with Equinix for the renewable energy generated by the Betierra facility.
- **29-11-2024**: The facility was officially inaugurated, marking the achievement of 1 GW operating capacity in Spain for Sonnedix.
