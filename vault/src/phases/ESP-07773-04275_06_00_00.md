### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sonnedix Facility in Herencia, ESP

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: Sonnedix operates the 36MW Fraile solar farm in Herencia, Ciudad Real. This facility is part of a solar power purchase agreement (PPA) with Sidenor Aceros Especiales, a long steel products producer. The PPA is guaranteed by the Spanish Export Credit Agency (Cesce).

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: The financing for the Fraile solar farm was facilitated by Bankinter and ICO, with Bankinter acting as the interest rate hedge entity. This financing is part of the broader support for renewable energy initiatives in Spain.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023**: Sonnedix signed a 12-year PPA for the output from the 36MW Fraile solar farm, marking it as their first corporate PPA in Spain and the first purchase agreement of an electro-intensive plant through a State-backed guarantee.
- **July 2024**: Sonnedix announced the acquisition of a 42MW solar plant in Carpio del Tajo, Toledo, which is part of a larger strategy to exceed 1GW of operational capacity in Spain by the end of 2024. The Fraile solar farm in Herencia is included in a portfolio acquired from Impax Asset Management in April 2022.
