### Evolutionary Timeline of the SunPower Facility in Alange, ESP

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 08-06-2010
   - **Details:** SunPower is set to construct a 9.1MW (AC) solar power plant in Alange, Badajoz. The facility will utilize SunPower's T0 Tracker systems and Serengeti solar panels, covering a 38-hectare site. SunPower will also provide operations and maintenance services for the plant.
