## ESP-07797-08108

### Evolutionary Timeline of the T-Solar Facility in Orense, Spain

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Capacity**: The T-Solar facility in Orense produces thin-film PV modules with a production capacity of 50MW.
- **Product Type**: The facility specializes in the latest-generation 5.7-m² amorphous-Si:H thin-film PV modules.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment in Peru**: On 22-12-2011, T-Solar signed loan agreements worth US$145 million to finance two PV plants in Peru, which will utilize modules produced at the Orense facility. The total cost of the development is estimated at US$165 million, with various financial institutions providing funding.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Partnership Announcement**: On 01-06-2011, T-Solar announced a strategic partnership with Astonfield Renewable Resources to develop solar PV power plants in India, with the first project being a 5MW plant in Osiyan, Rajasthan, expected to be commissioned by October 2011. This project will utilize modules from the Orense facility.
- **Peru Project Construction**: The two PV plants in Peru, which will use T-Solar's modules, are expected to start generating electricity in the second quarter of 2012.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation in Peru**: The construction of the two PV plants in Peru is expected to create over 160 jobs for the local workforce.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: T-Solar operates as a subsidiary of Isolux Corsán, which is involved in the engineering, procurement, and construction of the projects utilizing the modules from the Orense facility. The ownership structure indicates that T-Solar is not exclusively owned but is part of a larger corporate entity.
