### Evolutionary Timeline of the UPC Facility in Campus Nord, Spain

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** Summer 2025 (planned)
   - **Details:** The UPC facility will install two solar prototypes designed to generate photovoltaic energy while providing shade and filtering air. The prototypes include:
     - **Adaptable Modular Solar Shade (OSMA):** A modular system that prioritizes electricity generation and shade during hot months, retractable for winter sunlight, using glass-crystal monocrystalline PV panels.
     - **Sombra Viva:** A multifunctional solar shelter that offers shade, energy, and air filtering, with adaptable modules to adjust shade based on climate conditions.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2024
   - **Details:** Each prototype team will receive €50,000 (approximately $52,771) in funding to cover 80% of project costs, as part of a competition for innovative photovoltaic solutions promoted by BIT Habitat and supported by the local Energy Agency.
