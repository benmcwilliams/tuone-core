### Evolutionary Timeline of the Geological Survey of Estonia Facility in Tallinn, EST

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **November 2024**: The Geological Survey of Estonia (EGT) is involved in the completion of the first pilot geothermal district heating project at Roosna-Alliku. This project is part of the GEOENEST initiative, which aims to assess geothermal energy's potential for district heating solutions. Earlier in 2024, another pilot project in the Tiskre sub-district of Tallinn began operations, supplying clean and sustainable heat to nearby apartment buildings.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **November 2024**: The Roosna-Alliku geothermal pilot project received funding from the Ministry of Climate of Estonia. This funding is part of the broader GEOENEST initiative, which is implemented by the EGT.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **November 2024**: Completion of the first pilot geothermal district heating project at Roosna-Alliku.
