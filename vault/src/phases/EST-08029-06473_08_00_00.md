### Evolutionary Timeline of the Eesti Energia Facility in Gulf of Riga, Estonia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **30-12-2019**: The Estonian government approved the start of the permitting and environmental impact assessment process for the Gulf of Riga offshore wind farm, which is part of a larger project by Eesti Energia. The facility is planned to comprise up to 160 turbines with a total capacity of up to 1GW. Initial studies have shown the location is suitable for offshore wind farm construction.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **27-04-2021**: Eesti Energia's renewable subsidiary, Enefit Green, signed an agreement with Danish offshore wind developer Ørsted to create a joint company for the development of the offshore wind farm in the Gulf of Riga. This collaboration aims to enhance renewable energy production and is positioned to meet ambitious targets for 2030.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **30-12-2019**: The Estonian government announced the initiation of the construction permit procedure for the Gulf of Riga offshore wind farm, allowing Eesti Energia to continue research and partner search for the project.
