### Evolutionary Timeline of the Estonia Offshore Wind DevCo Facility in Saare 2.1, EST

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Saare 2.1 facility is proposed to host an offshore wind farm with a production capacity of up to 1.7 GW. This capacity was initially filed by CI NMF Estonia Sea I (Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners) as part of their application to the Consumer Protection and Technical Regulation Authority (CPTRA).

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The starting price for the auction of the Saare 2.1 site is approximately EUR 2.5 million, based on its size of about 163.8 km². This price reflects the initial investment required to secure the site for development.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - On **03-04-2024**, the CPTRA announced that it had received ten applications for three offshore wind sites, including Saare 2.1. The auction for these sites is expected to be held in **June 2024**. The applications will be evaluated for compliance with the Building Code before moving to the auction phase.

4. **Labour:**
   - As of the current information, there are no specific details regarding job creation or job loss associated with the Saare 2.1 facility, as the project is still in the application and auction phase.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is associated with Estonia Offshore Wind DevCo, which is part of Ignitis Group. The ownership structure details regarding exclusivity or joint ventures have not been explicitly stated in the available information.
