**1. Technology and Operations:**
- **Date:** 04-10-2024
  - The Kirikmäe solar park, located in Pärnu County, covers an area of 110 hectares and has a production capacity of 77 MW. It is noted to be more than twice the size of Estonia’s largest existing solar facility.
  - The facility will install a total of 117,600 solar panels, each with a power output of 655–665 W. The solar panels are manufactured by Canadian Solar.
  - The park is designed to cover the estimated annual energy needs of approximately 35,000 households.
