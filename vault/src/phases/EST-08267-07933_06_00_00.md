### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mirova Facility in Kirikmäe, Estonia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2024**: The Kirikmäe solar park, developed by Estonian energy company Evecon and asset manager Mirova, officially opened with a production capacity of **77 MW**. The facility spans **110 hectares** and is equipped with **117,600 solar panels** manufactured by **Canadian Solar**, each with a power output of **655–665 W**. The park is designed to meet the annual energy needs of approximately **35,000 households**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2022**: The **Baltic Renewable Energy Platform (BREP)** was established as a joint venture between Evecon and Mirova to finance, construct, and operate a portfolio of solar parks exceeding **100 MWp** in Estonia. This initiative marks a significant investment in the region's renewable energy infrastructure.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2024**: The Kirikmäe solar park was inaugurated, contributing to a total of over **100 MW** of solar production capacity added to the local market within a week, including the opening of the Imavere and Lohu mets solar parks.
