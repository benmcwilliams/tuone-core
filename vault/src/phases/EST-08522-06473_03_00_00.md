### Evolutionary Timeline of the Eesti Energia Facility in Ida-Virumaa, EST

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** August 2022
   - **Details:** Eesti Energia is developing Estonia’s first pumped storage hydroelectric plant at the industrial area of the Estonia mine in Ida-Virumaa. The facility will have a production capacity of up to 225 MW. The project utilizes limestone rubble and closed tunnels from oil shale mining, making it a large-scale circular economy initiative. The upper reservoir will be constructed on a tailings structure, while a closed mine will serve as the lower reservoir. The facility is expected to begin operations in 2026.
