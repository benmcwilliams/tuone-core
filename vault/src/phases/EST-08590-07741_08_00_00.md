### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enefit Green Facility in Nord Pärnu, Estonia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: 10-02-2023
  - **Details**: Enefit Green has awarded Nordex an order for a 255 MW wind farm in Nord Pärnu, which includes the Sopi I, Sopi II, and Tootsi wind farms. The project will utilize 38 N163/6.X wind turbines and is expected to produce approximately 700 GWh of electricity annually, enough to power around 200,000 homes. This wind farm will be the most powerful renewable energy project in the Baltic states to date and will almost double the current wind energy production in Estonia.
