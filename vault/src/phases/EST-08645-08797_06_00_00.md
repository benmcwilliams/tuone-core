### Evolutionary Timeline of the Energy Smart Facility in Kurenurme, Estonia

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **09/2011:** Energy Smart is constructing a 100kW pilot solar energy plant in Kurenurme, Estonia. The facility will feature 11 tracker towers, each equipped with 42 solar panels.
