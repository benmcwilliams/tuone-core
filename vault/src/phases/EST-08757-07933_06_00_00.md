### Mirova Facility in Pärnu County, Estonia - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2024**: The Mirova facility, in collaboration with Estonian energy company Evecon, reached operational status at the Kirikmäe solar park, producing 77.53MW of solar power. This facility is noted as the largest solar park in the Baltics at the time of its completion, effectively doubling the capacity of the previous largest operational solar PV plant in Estonia. The construction involved the installation of 117,600 solar panels provided by Canadian Solar.
