## FIN-00443-02394

### Evolutionary Timeline of the P2X Solutions Facility in Harjavalta, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2021**: P2X Solutions announced plans to establish Finland's first green hydrogen production facility in Harjavalta with a production capacity of 20MW. The facility will generate industrial-scale green hydrogen, providing green electricity for industrial applications and producing oxygen and thermal energy as by-products. Additionally, the hydrogen will be used to create synthetic fuels to decarbonize the transportation sector.
- **March 2023**: It was reported that a methanation plant, supplied by Q Power, is being built in connection with the green hydrogen production plant. Some of the hydrogen produced will be processed into synthetic methane at this facility, which is expected to be delivered and commissioned in 2024 alongside the green hydrogen plant.
- **November 2023**: P2X Solutions confirmed that green hydrogen production at the Harjavalta plant is set to commence in 2024, with the hydrogen being supplied to Danisco Sweeteners for xylitol production.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2021**: P2X Solutions received public funding of approximately €26 million ($29.36 million) to support the establishment of the green hydrogen facility. The total expected funding for the project is around €70 million ($79 million).
  
#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2021**: Announcement of the establishment of the green hydrogen production facility in Harjavalta.
- **Autumn 2022**: Start of construction for the green hydrogen production facility is planned.
- **First half of 2024**: Commissioning of the green hydrogen production plant is scheduled.
- **March 2023**: Announcement of the methanation plant's connection to the green hydrogen facility, with commissioning expected in 2024.
- **November 2023**: Confirmation that green hydrogen production will begin in 2024.
