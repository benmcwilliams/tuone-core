### H-TEC Facility in Helsinki, Finland - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: September 2024
  - The facility will produce green hydrogen using a 3MW production plant. 
  - It will utilize three proton exchange membrane (PEM) electrolysers supplied by H-TEC SYSTEMS, capable of producing up to 1,350 kg of green hydrogen per day.
  - The plant will be integrated with Helsinki’s district heating network and the Vuosaari Harbour, utilizing renewable energy from Helen Oy's portfolio.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: September 2024
  - The project is a partnership between Finnish energy company Helen Oy, MAN Energy Solutions, and H-TEC SYSTEMS. 
  - MAN Energy Solutions is responsible for the engineering, procurement, and construction of the hydrogen plant, indicating significant investment in the facility's development.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: September 2024
  - The announcement of the partnership and the project to build the green hydrogen production plant was made, marking the initiation of the project.

#### 4. Labour

  - Specific job creation or job loss details have not been provided in the articles, but the establishment of the facility is expected to create jobs related to the construction and operation of the hydrogen plant.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date**: September 2024
  - The facility is a collaborative project involving multiple companies: Helen Oy, MAN Energy Solutions, and H-TEC SYSTEMS. H-TEC SYSTEMS is a subsidiary of MAN Energy Solutions, indicating a joint venture rather than exclusive ownership by a single company.
