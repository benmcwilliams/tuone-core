### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fimer Facility in Helsinki, Finland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** Fimer provided its PVS980-58 central inverter solution for the Lemene smart grid project in Lempäälä, southern Finland. The project features a 12 MW smart grid with a 4 MW ground-mount PV system, which will derive 35% of its energy from solar PV. The annual output of the installation is 3,600 MWh, sufficient to power approximately 1,620 apartment buildings. Fimer's technology is designed to perform well in harsh weather conditions, ensuring high energy yields.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** The Lemene project secured nearly €5 million in funding from the Finnish Ministry of Economic Affairs and Employment as part of a broader initiative to enhance energy security through independent smart grids.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** Fimer announced plans to open a new R&D facility in Helsinki, which will be its second research center in the city. This facility will support testing and verification of Fimer’s inverter portfolio for safe operation across various climatic conditions.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** The establishment of the new R&D facility in Helsinki is expected to contribute to job creation, although specific numbers were not provided in the article.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** Fimer operates the facility independently, having acquired ABB's inverter business the previous year, indicating full ownership of its operations in Finland.
