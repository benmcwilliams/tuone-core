### Evolutionary Timeline of the Keliber Facility in Kokkola Industrial Park, Finland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** Keliber plans to mine spodumene from an ore mine and build a lithium hydroxide refinery at Kokkola Industrial Park. The refinery aims to produce battery-grade lithium hydroxide, with an annual production capacity of 15,000 tonnes starting in 2025. The refinery will utilize a caustic soda-based technology for sustainable and energy-efficient processing. A concentrator plant is also planned to be built approximately 60 kilometers away as an intermediate processing step.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** The construction of the lithium hydroxide refinery is a significant milestone in Keliber's lithium project, indicating a move into the implementation phase. The project is expected to enhance the Finnish battery cluster and contribute to the local economy.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** Preliminary work for the construction of the refinery is expected to start in 2022, with the aim to ramp up production by 2025. The project is contingent upon obtaining the necessary environmental permits.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** The project is anticipated to create job opportunities and increase vitality in the Kokkola region, although specific job creation numbers were not provided.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** Keliber is a subsidiary of Sibanye-Stillwater, a multinational mining and metals company. The Finnish Minerals Group, a state-owned company, is the second-largest shareholder in Keliber, indicating a joint ownership structure.
