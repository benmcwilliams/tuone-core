1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: August 2023
   - **Details**: The facility is designed to produce nickel-cobalt-manganese (NCM) 811 and nickel-cobalt-aluminium (NCA) with an initial production capacity of 60,000 tons per year. The design allows for future capacity adjustments to produce NCM523 and NCM622.
