### Evolutionary Timeline of the Valmet Facility in Salo, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **02-03-2019**: Valmet Automotive announces plans to establish large-scale production of battery packs for the automotive industry in Salo, Finland, with an initial workforce of over 300 employees.
- **11-2019**: Production of battery packs begins at the Salo facility, which has been transformed from a former cell phone manufacturing plant. The production capacity is set to gradually increase until the planned capacity is reached at the beginning of 2020.
- **04-11-2020**: Valmet announces an expansion of battery system production capacities at the Salo plant, including an additional production line and expanded logistics area, with completion expected by summer 2021. The workforce is projected to rise to around 400 by 2022.
- **10-03-2021**: Valmet receives a new order to produce high-voltage batteries at the Salo plant, expanding its production capabilities beyond 48-volt batteries for hybrid vehicles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **11-2019**: Production starts at the Salo facility after construction began in May 2019.
- **04-11-2020**: Expansion work begins at the Salo plant, expected to be completed by summer 2021.
- **10-03-2021**: Valmet announces the start of production for high-voltage batteries at the Salo plant, with production already underway for 48-volt batteries.
- **10-09-2021**: Valmet announces the completion of the expansion at the Salo plant, which now includes the capacity to produce high-voltage batteries.

#### 4. Labour
