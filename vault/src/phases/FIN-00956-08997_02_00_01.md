### Evolutionary Timeline of the Geomachine Oy Facility in Salo, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05/2024**: The facility is involved in the drilling of geothermal wells at the Lounavoima waste incineration plant. The project includes the completion of a 2000-meter geothermal well, which is the second well of the project, following a previously drilled and tested 1600-meter well. A third well, also 2000 meters deep, was drilled in winter 2023. Upon completion of the ongoing project, the facility will have a total of six geothermal heat wells with a combined output of up to 14 GWh per year, sufficient to meet the annual heating needs of approximately 700 single-family homes.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06/2024**: A decision was made to invest in a third phase of the geothermal project, which includes the construction of three additional wells. The project received EUR 3 million in energy support from the Ministry of Economic Affairs and Employment, which initiated the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Winter 2023**: Drilling of the third geothermal well (2000 meters) was completed.
- **05/2024**: Ongoing testing of the second well and construction work for the third phase has begun, with completion expected by summer 2025.
