### Evolutionary Timeline of the CarbonScape Facility in Sunila, Finland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** CarbonScape, in collaboration with Stora Enso, announced plans to build a biographite demonstration plant in Sunila, Finland. The facility aims to produce biographite as a sustainable alternative to mined graphite for lithium-ion batteries. The demonstration plant will help in producing larger biographite samples for original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and battery cell makers, facilitating product qualification and operator training. The facility represents a significant scaling up of CarbonScape's biographite production technology, which has been optimized at a pilot plant in New Zealand since 2018.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** CarbonScape secured $18 million (NZ$29.08 million) in funding to support its biographite commercialization efforts. This investment is crucial for the establishment of the demonstration plant and future commercial operations.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** CarbonScape is currently working on an engineering study for the demonstration plant, with construction expected to start in 2025 and sample production anticipated in 2026. The facility is part of CarbonScape's strategy to establish a presence in the European market and support global expansion.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** The announcement of the demonstration plant follows the closure of Stora Enso's pulp production and lignin extraction operations at Sunila in 2023. While specific job creation numbers have not been disclosed, the establishment of the new facility is expected to create jobs in the region, particularly in the areas of production and engineering.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** The facility will be a joint venture between CarbonScape and Stora Enso, with Stora Enso being an investor in CarbonScape. This partnership aligns with their shared vision for advancing sustainable battery technologies.
