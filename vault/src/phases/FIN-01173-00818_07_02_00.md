### Evolutionary Timeline of Valmet Facility in Uusikaupunki, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2020**: Valmet Automotive announced plans to expand its car plant in Uusikaupunki into a battery factory, marking it as the second plant for mass production of battery systems for electric vehicles, alongside Salo. Production was scheduled to start in the second half of 2021.
- **January 2021**: Construction of the battery factory commenced, with mass production of high-voltage systems expected to start in the second half of 2021.
- **September 2021**: The Uusikaupunki plant officially opened as a battery factory, with plans for four production lines to be commissioned in stages through 2024. The total production capacity was set at around 500,000 battery modules and cell packs per year.
- **December 2021**: Valmet introduced a modular battery platform for utility vehicles, with series production scheduled to start in Q3 2022.
- **April 2022**: Valmet completed the first pre-production vehicle of the Lightyear 0 solar car at the Uusikaupunki plant, with plans for series production to begin in late 2022.
- **December 2022**: Series production of the Lightyear 0 commenced, initially at a rate of one unit per week, with plans to ramp up production in Q1 2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2020**: The expansion of the Uusikaupunki plant into a battery factory was announced, with an investment of approximately €120 million.
- **February 2023**: Valmet announced an extensive investment into sustainable battery production, including new assembly systems designed by Kuka.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2020**: Announcement of the battery factory expansion.
- **January 2021**: Construction of the battery factory began.
- **September 2021**: Official opening of the battery factory.
- **December 2021**: Introduction of a modular battery platform.
- **April 2022**: Completion of the first pre-production Lightyear 0 vehicle.
- **December 2022**: Start of series production of the Lightyear 0.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2021**: Valmet announced plans to create 1,000 new jobs in Finland, with 700 positions specifically for the Uusikaupunki site.
- **September 2021**: Initially, 200 employees were working at the Uusikaupunki battery plant, with expectations to increase to 500 by 2024 due to the expansion.
- **April 2023**: Valmet's new assembly system installation was expected to create additional jobs, although specific numbers were not disclosed.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Valmet Automotive operates the Uusikaupunki facility as a wholly-owned entity, focusing on both vehicle manufacturing and battery system production under one roof. The facility is part of Valmet's broader strategy to establish itself as a Tier 1 system supplier for battery systems and modules. 

This timeline encapsulates the significant developments and milestones of the Valmet facility in Uusikaupunki, Finland, highlighting its evolution into a key player in the electric vehicle battery production landscape.
