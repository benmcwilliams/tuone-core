### Evolutionary Timeline of the Low Carbon Facility in Vaasa, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2021**: Low Carbon acquires the 30MW Mörknässkogen wind project near Vaasa, Finland. This facility will consist of five wind turbines and is part of a larger renewables pipeline exceeding 120MW in Finland. 
- **April 2022**: The Mörknässkogen wind farm is set to become Low Carbon’s first operational wind farm in Finland, with commercial operations expected to commence in the fourth quarter of 2023. The facility is projected to produce approximately 100 GWh/year, capable of powering over 29,700 homes and avoiding more than 20,900 tons of CO2e emissions.

#### 2. Investment and Development
