### Evolutionary Timeline of Teollisuuden Voima Facility in Olkiluoto, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2021**: Teollisuuden Voima (TVO) contracted Hitachi ABB Power Grids to provide a 90-megawatt battery energy storage system (BESS) for the Olkiluoto facility. This system is designed to support the energy network during potential production disturbances at the Olkiluoto 3 (OL3) nuclear power plant, which is under construction. The BESS is expected to be commissioned in 2022.
- **December 2021**: The OL3 European Pressurized Water Reactor (EPR) achieved first criticality, marking a significant milestone in its commissioning. The reactor is expected to commence electricity production by the end of January 2022, with a net electrical output of approximately 1,600 MW, contributing nearly 14% of Finland's electricity.
- **April 2022**: The OL3 plant's commissioning tests were affected by restrictions on electricity imports from Russia, which were implemented to secure Finland's power system amid geopolitical tensions.
- **December 2022**: OL3 resumed test production of electricity after a two-month disruption due to technical issues, including cracks found in four feedwater pumps. The plant is expected to start permanent production on March 8, 2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2021**: The investment in the battery energy storage system is part of TVO's broader strategy to enhance the reliability of electricity supply and support Finland's transition to carbon neutrality by 2035.
