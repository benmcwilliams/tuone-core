### Evolutionary Timeline of the Areva-Siemens Consortium Facility in Olkiluoto, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2005**: Construction of the Olkiluoto 3 (OL3) plant began under a fixed-price turnkey contract by the Areva-Siemens consortium.
- **December 2021**: OL3 attained first criticality.
- **March 2022**: The plant was connected to the grid.
- **September 2022**: OL3 was operated at full capacity for the first time, producing 1600 MWe.
- **April 16, 2023**: The trial operation of OL3 ended, and regular electricity production began.
- **May 1, 2023**: Commercial operation of OL3 is set to start, with the plant expected to produce about 15% of Finland's total electricity consumption and 30% from the overall Olkiluoto plant (units 1-3).
- **Maintenance Updates**: Cracks were identified in the impellers of the feedwater pumps, leading to the installation of more robust impellers during a scheduled outage.
