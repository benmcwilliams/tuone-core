- **December 2019**: Valoe's facility in Mikkeli is set to expand its operations to produce interdigitated back contact (IBC) solar modules using cells from the newly acquired factory in Vilnius, Lithuania. The annual production capacity of the Vilnius facility is stated to be either 60 MW or 120 MW, depending on financing availability. The Mikkeli facility is expected to upgrade to produce IBC modules and polymer structures for mobile applications.
