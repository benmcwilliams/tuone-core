#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: September 2021
  - **Details**: The facility is part of a consortium planning to build a 500 MW solar power plant in Palloneva, utilizing 500 hectares of abandoned peatland. Additionally, a 45 MW wind power plant is also planned in the area. The project aims to achieve a levelized cost of energy (LCOE) of less than €0.04/kWh.
