### Evolutionary Timeline of the Alight Facility in Eurajoki, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2023**: Alight announced plans to develop a ground-mounted solar park in Eurajoki, covering 123 hectares with an installed capacity of over 100 MW. This capacity is expected to provide enough electricity for approximately 20,000 homes, making it one of Finland's largest solar parks under development. The project is anticipated to generate around 107 GWh/year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2023**: The estimated cost for the solar park is €50 million (approximately $53.1 million). Alight has confirmed that it has equity financing available and had previously secured €150 million when the European infrastructure fund DIF acquired a majority stake in the company.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2023**: Alight announced the commencement of construction for the solar park in the fourth quarter of 2024, with commissioning planned for the first quarter of 2026. This project marks Alight's first entry into the Finnish market.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2023**: The development and construction of the solar park are expected to create additional jobs in the Eurajoki community, contributing to local employment opportunities.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **September 2023**: Alight is an independent power producer (IPP) and is entering the Finnish market with this project. While specific details about joint ventures or partnerships for this facility were not mentioned, Alight's previous acquisition of funding suggests a strong financial backing for its operations in Finland.
