#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2022**: Fortum signed an agreement with Westinghouse for the design, approval, and delivery of a new type of VVER-440 fuel for the Loviisa plant, building on previous successful collaborations from 2001 to 2007.
- **2023**: During the annual maintenance, an experimental fuel element manufactured by Westinghouse was loaded into Loviisa unit 2. This test element did not contain actual uranium pellets but was successful in its function.
