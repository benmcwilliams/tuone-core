### Evolutionary Timeline of the Umicore Facility in Kokkola, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production**: The Umicore facility in Kokkola produces precursor cathode active materials and refines cobalt used in rechargeable batteries for electric vehicles.
- **Capacity**: The facility's operations are supported by a power purchase agreement (PPA) with Statkraft, which will provide 60 gigawatt hours (GWh) of renewable energy annually from 2024 to 2033.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Power Purchase Agreement**: On **September 15, 2022**, Umicore signed a 10-year PPA with Statkraft for renewable energy from a 96.9MW wind farm in Finland, which is expected to be commissioned in 2023. This agreement is part of Umicore's strategy to reduce its scope 2 emissions and secure energy supplies at a competitive cost.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **PPA Announcement**: The PPA with Statkraft was announced on **September 15, 2022**. The wind farm, consisting of 17 turbines, is expected to be operational in 2023.
