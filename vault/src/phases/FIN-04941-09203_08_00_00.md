### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ilmatar Facility in Alajärvi, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2023**: Ilmatar begins power production from the Alajärvi hybrid project, which will ultimately have a total capacity of 370MW. Initially, eight turbines generating 48MW have been installed. By the end of 2023, the project is expected to reach a capacity of 220MW with 36 turbines operational. Plans include a 150MW solar farm and 25MW of battery storage to be constructed during 2024-2025.
- **01/2024**: All 36 wind turbines at the 221MW wind farm have commenced operation, marking the first asset of the 370MW hybrid renewable energy park.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2023**: Ilmatar secures €19.5m ($21.25m) in EU Recovery and Resilience Facility funding from the Ministry of Economic Affairs and Employment for the hybrid project.
- **01/2024**: The same €19.5m grant is confirmed for the construction of the solar farm and battery storage, which will be built between 2024 and 2025.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **04/2023**: Announcement of the start of power production and the intention to have all wind turbines operational by the end of October 2023, with full production expected by the end of the year.
- **01/2024**: Confirmation that all 36 wind turbines are operational, marking a significant milestone in the development of the hybrid renewable energy park.
