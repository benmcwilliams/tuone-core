### Evolutionary Timeline of the MAN Facility in Kristinestad, Finland

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: August 2023
   - **Details**: MAN Energy Solutions is designing a facility with a total input capacity of 200 MW, aimed at producing 55,000 tonnes of synthetic natural gas (SNG) annually. The facility will convert green electricity, primarily from wind power, into green hydrogen through electrolysis and then into liquefied SNG through methanation.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: August 2023
   - **Details**: The facility is part of a joint venture called Koppö Energia, which is backed by Prime Capital and CPC Finland. Specific investment amounts or subsidies related to the facility's development were not disclosed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: August 2023
   - **Details**: The front-end engineering design (FEED) for the methanation reactor has been commissioned by Koppö Energia from MAN Energy Solutions. The order for the delivery of the reactor system is planned for 2024.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: August 2023
   - **Details**: The article does not specify job creation or job loss associated with the facility at this time.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: August 2023
   - **Details**: The facility is not owned exclusively by MAN Energy Solutions; it is part of a joint venture (Koppö Energia) between Prime Capital and CPC Finland. 

This timeline captures the key developments and details regarding the MAN facility in Kristinestad, Finland, as of August 2023.

**1. Technology and Operations:**
- "MAN Energy Solutions is designing a 200 MW plant in Finland that will produce 55,000 tonnes of synthetic natural gas annually to decarbonize heavy-duty transport."
- "The plant, with a total input capacity of 200 MW, will be built in Kristinestad, Finland for the production of green hydrogen and synthetic natural gas (SNG)."
- "Upon completion, the Kristinestad plant will convert 200 MW of green electricity – primarily produced by wind power – initially into green hydrogen through electrolysis, and ultimately into liquefied SNG through methanation."

**2. Investment and Development:**
- "Koppö Energia has commissioned MAN Energy Solutions to deliver the front-end engineering design (FEED) for the methanation reactor of an industrial-scale power-to-X plant."
