### Koppö Energia Facility Timeline - Kristinestad, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2023**: Koppö Energia is developing a Power-to-X plant with a total input capacity of **200 MW**. The facility will convert green electricity into **green hydrogen** and **synthetic natural gas (SNG)**. The plant is designed to produce **55,000 tonnes of SNG annually**, primarily to decarbonize the heavy-duty transport sector. The facility will utilize up to **500 MW of wind** and **100 MW of photovoltaic power** as part of the Koppö Energy Cluster to ensure emission-free renewable energy supply.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2023**: Koppö Energia has commissioned **MAN Energy Solutions** to deliver the front-end engineering design (FEED) for the methanation reactor of the plant. The order for the delivery of the reactor system is planned for **2024**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2023**: The FEED for the liquefaction and storage of liquefied synthetic methane (LSM) is set to be booked by Wärtsilä in **Q3 2023**. The construction of the facility is part of the Koppö Energy Cluster, which is currently in the planning and design phase.

#### 4. Labour
- There are no specific details regarding job creation or job loss associated with the Koppö Energia facility mentioned in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Koppö Energia is a **joint venture** between **Prime Capital**, a German-based asset manager specializing in alternative energy projects, and **CPC Finland**, a subsidiary of project developer and green power producer, CPC Germania.
