#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2022**: CPC Finland, in partnership with Prime Capital, announced the development of a green hydrogen and methane plant with a total capacity of over 1 GW, specifically an electrolyser capacity of 200 MW. The facility is located close to Kristinestad and is expected to utilize 600 MW of renewable energy projects provided by CPC Finland to meet at least 80% of the plant's electricity demand. Construction is scheduled to start in 2024, with commissioning planned for 2025.

- **September 2022**: Prime Capital's Prime Green Energy Infrastructure Fund (PGEIF) is investing in the joint venture with CPC Finland to develop the green hydrogen/methane plant. #### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2022**: The joint venture between Prime Capital and CPC Finland was officially announced, with plans for construction to begin in 2024 and commissioning set for 2025. The permitting process for the plant has already commenced.
