### Evolutionary Timeline of the Plug Power Facility in Porvoo, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **30-05-2023**: Plug Power announced plans to develop three green hydrogen production plants in Finland, including the Porvoo facility. The total electrolyzer capacity across these plants will be 2.2 GW by the end of the decade. The Porvoo facility will focus on producing hydrogen for local mobility applications, specifically for hydrogen-based fuel cells in transport, with a production capacity of 100 tonnes per day (TPD).

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **30-05-2023**: Plug Power is in discussions with financial investors and debt providers to secure funding for the development of the three hydrogen production plants, including the Porvoo facility. The capital structure is expected to include non-recourse debt, although specific investment amounts tied to the Porvoo facility have not been disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **30-05-2023**: The announcement of the Porvoo facility was part of a broader commitment to install a total of 2.25 GW of electrolytic capacity across Finland by 2030. The company aims to make a final investment decision by 2025-26.

#### 4. Labour
- **30-05-2023**: While specific job creation numbers for the Porvoo facility have not been detailed, the overall development of the three facilities is expected to contribute to local employment opportunities in the hydrogen production sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
