### Oulu Energy Facility Timeline - Laanila, Finland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** February 2024
   - **Details:** Oulu Energy plans to establish a hydrogen plant in the Laanila industrial area with a production capacity of up to 100MW. The facility will include an electrolysis plant, a carbon capture plant, hydrogen and carbon dioxide storage, and a further processing plant that could produce methane or methanol. The project aims to generate approximately 200–400GWh of heat energy annually as a by-product, which could be utilized in the district heating network, covering about 10–30% of Oulu’s annual district heating demand.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** February 2024
   - **Details:** The project is currently in the development phase, led by P2X Solutions, which is also constructing a green hydrogen and synthetic methane production plant in Harjavalta, Finland. An investment decision for the Oulu hydrogen plant is anticipated in autumn 2025.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** February 2024
   - **Details:** The project is in the development phase, focusing on feasibility studies and impact assessments. The plant is expected to be operational by 2028 at the earliest.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
