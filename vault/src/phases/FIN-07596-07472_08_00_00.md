### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nordex Facility in Bjorkliden, Finland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility in Bjorkliden consists of seven wind turbines supplied by Nordex, with a total production capacity of 41 MW. 
   - About 85% of the facility's output is designated for use by Equinix Inc under a 10-year power purchase agreement (PPA) signed in January 2022.

2. **Investment and Development:**
