### Evolutionary Timeline of the VSB Group Facility in Karahka, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2016**: VSB Finland began developing the Karahka wind power project.
- **2022**: The project entered its construction phase when building permits were issued. The facility consists of 25 wind turbines with a total production capacity of 148 MW, spread across approximately 2300 hectares. An innovative construction method was employed for the towers, utilizing 27 concrete tower blocks topped with a steel section to achieve greater hub heights. The concrete components were locally produced by Lujabetoni in Karsamaki.
- **December 2024**: The Karahka wind farm was officially commissioned.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2022**: VSB sold the Karahka project to the Finnish utility company Helen and the Bank of Aland Wind Power Special Investment Fund, while retaining responsibility for construction and commissioning. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the articles.
