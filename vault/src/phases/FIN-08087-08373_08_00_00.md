#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2016**: VSB Finland began developing the Karahka wind power project.
- **2022**: The project entered its construction phase when building permits were issued. The facility consists of 25 wind turbines with a total production capacity of 148 MW, spread across approximately 2300 hectares. The innovative tower construction uses 27 concrete tower blocks with a steel section on top, allowing for greater hub heights.
