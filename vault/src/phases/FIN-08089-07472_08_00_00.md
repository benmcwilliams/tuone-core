### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nordex Facility in Oulainen, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2022**: Nordex Group secured a contract to supply and install 25 N163/5.X wind turbines for the Karahka wind farm, which will have a total capacity of 147.5 MW. The turbines will feature an Advanced Anti-Icing-System and will be delivered in a cold-climate version. The project will utilize Nordex's highest hybrid tower with a hub height of 168 meters and a rotor diameter of 163 meters.
- **February 2023**: Peikko Group was contracted to deliver foundation technology for the Karahka wind park, including steel components for the 25 foundations. The site is expected to be operational by the end of 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2022**: The wind farm is being developed by VSB Uusiutuva Energia Suomi, with ownership held by a consortium of Helen Ltd and the Bank of Åland. The project is part of Helen's strategy to become climate-neutral by 2030.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2022**: VSB Uusiutuva Energia Suomi obtained permission for the construction of the Karahka wind farm.
- **July 2022**: Nordex Group announced the turbine supply contract.
- **February 2023**: Peikko Group's contract for foundation technology was confirmed, with deliveries scheduled between April and October 2023. The wind park is estimated to be operational by the end of 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2023**: The involvement of Peikko Group and NYAB as contractors for the civil work and infrastructure indicates job creation related to the construction and assembly of the wind farm.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2023**: The current owners of the Karahka wind park are Helen Ltd and the Bank of Åland, indicating a joint ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by Nordex.
