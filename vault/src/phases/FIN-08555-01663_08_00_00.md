## FIN-08555-01663

### Evolutionary Timeline of the GE Facility in Pyhajoki, Finland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
   - **Details:** GE Renewable Energy will supply 16 Cypress turbines for the Puskakorpi wind farm, which has a total production capacity of 88MW. The Cypress platform includes power ratings between 4.8 and 6.1MW, with rotor diameters of 158 and 164 meters. The wind farm is expected to generate enough renewable energy to power approximately 55,000 households in Finland.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
   - **Details:** The project was co-developed by Smart Windpower, Mincovest, and GE Renewable Energy, and acquired by Foresight Energy Infrastructure Partners (FEIP). The financing for the project was supported by GE Energy Financial Services, with Societe Generale acting as the sole lender. The project is part of Foresight's first onshore wind investment in Finland.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
   - **Details:** The project reached financial close, allowing construction to commence. The first onshore wind turbine installation is expected to begin in early 2022, with commercial operations anticipated to start in late 2022.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
   - **Details:** While specific job creation numbers were not mentioned, the involvement of local contractors such as Suvic and Ramboll indicates potential job opportunities in the region during the construction phase.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is not exclusively owned by GE; it is part of a joint venture involving GE Renewable Energy, Smart Windpower, Mincovest, and Foresight Energy Infrastructure Partners.
