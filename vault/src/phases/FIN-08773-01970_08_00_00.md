### Summary
The Statkraft facility in Siikajoki, Finland, is linked to the Mastokangas wind farm, which is under construction and expected to produce 215 GWh of renewable electricity annually. A significant 10-year PPA with Neste was signed to facilitate the delivery of this power, supporting Neste's sustainability goals. The project is a collaborative effort with Aquila Capital, indicating a joint venture ownership structure.
