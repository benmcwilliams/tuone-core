### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ilmatar Energy Facility in Piiparinmäki, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2022**: The Piiparinmäki wind farm commenced commercial operations. It has a total production capacity of 211 MW, making it one of the largest constructed unsubsidised wind farms in Europe. The facility utilizes wind turbine technology from Vestas, comprising 13 V150 4.2MW turbines and 28 V150 5.6MW turbines, all installed at a hub height of 150 meters.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2019**: Glennmont Partners acquired the Piiparinmäki project, collaborating with Ilmatar Energy for its development.
- **2020**: Kansai Electric Power Company purchased a 15% minority stake in the project, contributing to its investment structure.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2022**: The Piiparinmäki wind farm officially commenced commercial operations.
- **September 2022**: The facility was officially opened by former Finnish Prime Minister Matti Vanhanen, marking a significant milestone for Glennmont’s third Clean Energy Fund.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The Piiparinmäki facility is jointly owned by Glennmont Partners and Kansai Electric Power Company, with Glennmont being the primary owner. Ilmatar Energy played a crucial role in the development and construction management of the project, while Ilmatar Service is responsible for technical and commercial management.
