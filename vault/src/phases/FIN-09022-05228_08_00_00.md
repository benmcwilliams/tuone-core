### Summary:
The Juurakko facility, developed by VSB Group and operational since March 2022, consists of seven wind turbines with a capacity of 40MW. It represents a significant step in Finland's energy transition, with Helen and Ålandsbanken as co-owners. The project began construction in spring 2021 and was completed on schedule, contributing to Finland's climate goals.
