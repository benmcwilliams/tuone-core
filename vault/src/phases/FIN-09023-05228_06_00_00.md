### Evolutionary Timeline of the Helen Facility in Kalanti, Uusikaupunki, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2022**: Helen Oy acquired a 206MWp solar photovoltaic (PV) project from German developer Ib Vogt. This facility is located in Kalanti, Uusikaupunki, and is designed to produce more than 200GWh of clean energy annually, sufficient to power approximately 28,800 households. The solar farm will feature over 380,000 high-capacity bifacial solar modules built on a fixed-tilt substructure for optimal performance.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2022**: The acquisition of the solar project marks Helen's first acquisition of a ready-to-build solar project and one of the largest in Finland. The project is part of Helen's strategy to increase domestic, emission-free energy production and enhance energy self-sufficiency. Specific financial details regarding the investment were not disclosed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2022**: The solar farm is in the ready-to-build stage, with construction expected to begin in the second quarter of 2023. The commercial operation of the facility is anticipated in late 2024.
