1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 11-01-2021
   - **Details**: Nordex secured a contract to supply wind turbines for the 188MW ‘Karhunnevankangas’ wind farm project in Ostrobothnia, Finland. The facility will utilize 33 of Nordex’s N163/5.X turbines from the Delta4000 series, which are designed for medium wind speeds and feature a hub height of 159m and rotor blade tips reaching 240m. The installation is scheduled for completion in late 2022.
