### Evolutionary Timeline of Ilmatar Energy Facility in Piiparinmaki-Murtomaki, Finland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2018
   - **Details:** Ilmatar Energy acquired a 200MW wind project, the Piiparinmaki-Murtomaki wind farm, which will consist of 50 turbines. The facility is part of a broader plan to construct and operate up to 1GW of wind capacity over the next five years. Full operations are expected to commence in 2021.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2018
   - **Details:** The project is part of a five-year plan backed by FPCI Capenergie 3, a €245 million fund dedicated to renewable energy. The project is intended to be built without subsidies but is positioned to participate in the upcoming renewable energy premium scheme.

   - **Date:** June 2018
