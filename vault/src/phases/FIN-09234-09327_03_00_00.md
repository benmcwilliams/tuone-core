   - **Established:** 1954
   - **Current Production Capacity:** Kemijoki Oy operates 20 hydropower plants generating approximately 4,000–5,000 GWh of electricity annually.
   - **Recent Updates:** 
     - In March 2023, the Kurkiaska power plant implemented the HYCAP (HydroPower Capacity) project, which involves the combined use of electricity storage and power plants to respond swiftly to fluctuations in electricity demand and production.
