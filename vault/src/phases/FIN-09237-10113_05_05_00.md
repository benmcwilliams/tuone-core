### Evolutionary Timeline of the Steady Energy Facility in Lahti, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2024**: Steady Energy is developing the LDR-50 district heating small modular reactor (SMR) with a thermal output of 50 MW. The reactor is designed to operate at around 150°C and below 10 bar (145 psi), with less demanding operating conditions compared to traditional reactors, simplifying safety solutions. The LDR-50 has been under development since 2020.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2024**: The pilot plant investment is estimated to be about EUR 15–20 million (USD 16–22 million). This pilot plant will serve as a full-scale operational model of the Finnish-designed SMR, using an electric element for heat production instead of nuclear fuel.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2024**: Steady Energy is finalizing evaluations of potential sites for the pilot plant, with decisions expected by the end of summer 2024. Proposed locations include Kymijärvi and Teivaanmäki in Lahti.
- **December 2023**: Steady Energy signed a letter of intent with Kuopion Energia for the construction of up to five district heating reactors starting in 2030.
- **October 2023**: A letter of intent was signed with Helen, Helsinki's energy company, for the construction of up to 10 SMRs for district heating.
- **Expected Timeline**: Construction of the first operational LDR-50 district heating plant is expected to begin by 2028, with the first unit operational by 2030.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2024**: The development of the LDR-50 reactor is expected to create jobs, although specific job creation numbers have not been detailed in the articles. The facility aims to provide affordable energy for heating, which may lead to job opportunities in the local energy sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2024**: Steady Energy was spun out from the VTT Technical Research Centre of Finland in May 2023, indicating that it operates independently. The articles do not specify whether Steady Energy will have exclusive ownership of the facility or if it will be a joint venture with other municipal energy companies like Kuopion Energia and Helen.
