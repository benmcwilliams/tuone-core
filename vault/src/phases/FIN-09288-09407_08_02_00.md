## FIN-09288-09407

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lujabetoni Facility in Karsamaki, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production**: The Lujabetoni facility produces concrete components for wind turbine towers.
- **Capacity**: The facility contributed to the construction of the 148MW Karahka wind farm, which consists of 25 wind turbines.
- **Innovations**: The concrete components for the hybrid towers are made up of 27 concrete tower blocks with a steel section on top, allowing for greater hub heights.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Collaborative Project**: The partnership between Lujabetoni and Nordex for manufacturing the concrete parts for the Karahka wind farm was recognized as the 'Wind Power Project of the Year' by the Finnish Wind Power Association.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Development Start**: VSB Finland began developing the Karahka wind power project in **2016**.
- **Construction Phase**: The project entered its construction phase in **2022** when building permits were issued.
- **Ownership Transfer**: In **2022**, VSB sold the Karahka project to Helen and the Bank of Aland Wind Power Special Investment Fund while remaining responsible for construction and commissioning.
- **Commissioning**: The Karahka wind farm was officially commissioned in **December 2024**.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The commissioning of the Karahka wind farm is expected to enable the production of significantly more renewable electricity for the Finnish market, which may lead to job creation in the renewable energy sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: The Karahka wind farm is owned by the Finnish utility company Helen (51% stake) and the Bank of Aland Wind Power Special Investment Fund, indicating a joint ownership structure. Lujabetoni is a supplier for the project but does not own the facility.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and contributions of the Lujabetoni facility in Karsamaki, Finland, particularly in relation to the Karahka wind farm project.
