### Evolutionary Timeline of the Neste Facility in Naantali, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production**: Neste is the world’s largest producer of renewable diesel and sustainable aviation fuel, refined from waste and residues.
- **Renewable Electricity**: As of January 2022, the Naantali facility, along with the Porvoo refinery, began using 100% renewable electricity, achieved through a hydropower agreement with Vattenfall.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Hydropower Agreement**: Neste signed a power purchase agreement (PPA) with Vattenfall for 800 gigawatt-hours (GWh) of renewable electricity annually, which is expected to cover approximately 67% of the electricity consumption at Neste’s sites in Porvoo and Naantali.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2021**: Neste announced the hydropower agreement with Vattenfall, which will start delivering electricity from January 1, 2022, and continue until 2024. This agreement is a significant step towards Neste achieving its goal of 100% renewable electricity in Finland by 2022.
