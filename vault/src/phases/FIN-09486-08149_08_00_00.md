### Evolutionary Timeline of the Valorem Facility in Kalistanneva, Finland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Kalistanneva wind farm is part of the larger Viiati project, which includes a total capacity of 165MW. The facility will consist of 27 wind turbines, each with a rated capacity of 5.5MW. The combined output of the Matkussaari and Kalistanneva wind farms is expected to represent around 1.2% of Finland’s annual electricity consumption.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The total planned investment for the Kalistanneva wind farm is part of a larger €195 million investment for the Matkussaari project. Valorem has secured debt financing of €135 million from AIP Infrastructure 2 and an additional €10 million in VAT funding from OP Bank.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 2022:** Construction works for the Matkussaari wind farm, which includes the Kalistanneva facility, are set to begin.
   - **January 2025:** Estimated commissioning date for the wind farm.

4. **Labour:**
   - The articles do not specify job creation or job loss related to the Kalistanneva facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The management company for the Matkussaari wind farm, which includes the Kalistanneva facility, is Matkussaari Wind Farm Oy, which is 100% owned by Valorem. However, the Kalistanneva wind farm was sold to a Finnish consortium comprised of Helen Oy and Alandsbanken's Wind Power Fund.
