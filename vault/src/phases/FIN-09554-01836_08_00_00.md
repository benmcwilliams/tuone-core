### OX2 Facility in Lestijärvi, Finland - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** OX2 signed an agreement to construct a 455MW onshore wind farm in Lestijärvi, Finland. The facility will consist of 69 turbines, each with a capacity of 6MW, and is expected to produce approximately 1.3 TWh of electricity annually. This project will be the largest onshore wind farm in Finland and one of the largest in Europe.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** The total investment for the project is €650 million. OX2 will develop the wind farm under a fixed price agreement without any government subsidies.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** Construction is set to start immediately with an estimated completion date in 2025. OX2 will also be responsible for building the grid infrastructure, including a substation and a 58km 400kV transmission line.

4. **Labour:**
