### Evolutionary Timeline of the Posiva Facility in Onkalo, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2024**: Trial operation begins at Posiva’s used fuel repository in Onkalo. The trial run involves placing disposal canisters in the repository to test the facility's systems and operations. The disposal system includes iron-copper canisters, bentonite buffers, and a tunnel backfilling material. The trial includes the deposition of four canisters in a final disposal tunnel and the retrieval of a damaged canister.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Ongoing**: Posiva has been preparing for the disposal of used nuclear fuel for over 40 years, indicating a long-term investment in the facility's development. The facility's operations are contingent upon receiving an operating license from the Ministry of Economic Affairs & Employment, which is currently under review.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2022**: The Radiation and Nuclear Safety Authority (STUK) begins its review of Posiva's application for an operating license.
- **End of 2023**: STUK is expected to provide its opinion on the application, although they requested an extension until the end of 2024 due to the project's slower-than-anticipated progress.
- **2024-2070**: Posiva expects to commence the disposal of used nuclear fuel in the facility once the operating license is granted.
