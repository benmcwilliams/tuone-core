### Evolutionary Timeline of the Statkraft Facility in Raahe, Finland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2021**: Statkraft is involved in the construction of the Mastokangas wind farm located in the municipalities of Raahe and Siikajoki. This facility is expected to produce approximately 215 GWh of wind power annually, which will supply about 18% of the electricity consumption at Neste's Porvoo oil refinery.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09/2021**: Statkraft has signed a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with Neste for a duration of 10 years. This agreement is part of the efforts to support Neste's target of achieving carbon-neutral production by 2035. The PPA is also linked to the construction of the Mastokangas wind farm, which is being developed by Aquila Capital.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/2021**: The Mastokangas wind farm is currently under construction, with power deliveries to Neste's Porvoo refinery expected to commence in mid-2022.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
