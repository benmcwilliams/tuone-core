### Evolutionary Timeline of Taaleri Energia Facility in Murtotuuli, Finland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2021
     - Taaleri Energia began construction on the Murtotuuli wind farm, which is part of a larger project that includes another wind farm, Isoneva. The facility will utilize 42 Vestas V162-6.0 MW turbines, contributing to a combined installed capacity of 252 MW for both projects. The expected annual electricity production is approximately 675 GWh.
   - **Expected Operational Date:** Q4 2022
     - Both wind farms are anticipated to be operational by the fourth quarter of 2022.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2021
     - Taaleri Energia acquired the Murtotuuli wind farm using capital from its Taaleri II SolarWind fund. The acquisition was part of a larger investment strategy to enhance its Nordic portfolio with fully commercial, subsidy-free projects.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** April 2021
