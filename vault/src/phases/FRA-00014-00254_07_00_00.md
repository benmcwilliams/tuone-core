### Evolutionary Timeline of the FEV Facility in Alsdorf, FRA

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10/2020**: FEV announced the opening of a high-voltage battery testing centre named eDLP in Sandersdorf-Brehna, Saxony-Anhalt. The facility spans 12,000 square meters and includes 54 climate chambers with an electrical output of 30,000 kW. It is designed for testing modules and complete high-voltage batteries for both passenger cars and commercial vehicles. The facility features a unique 350 kN shaker with a climate hood for combined mechanical and electrical tests under varying ambient conditions (-40 to 100 degrees Celsius).

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **10/2020**: The project received over 6 million euros in support from the state of Saxony-Anhalt. This investment is aimed at enhancing FEV's capabilities in offering development services for high-voltage batteries.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10/2020**: FEV announced that the eDLP facility would start operations in the third quarter of 2020. The facility was officially opened on **09/26/2019**, with the aim of conducting performance and electrical tests, including environmental and mechanical impacts.

#### 4. Labour
