### Evolutionary Timeline of the TotalEnergies Facility in Bordeaux, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2020:** TotalEnergies announced the construction of the largest battery-based energy storage project in France. The facility will utilize Saft’s Intensium Max 20 High Energy solution, comprising 11 integrated 2.3 MWh containers. This system is designed to provide fast reserve services to support the stability of the French power grid and is part of the government’s initiative to enhance electrical capacity.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **March 2020:** The project represents an investment of approximately €15 million. This investment is aligned with TotalEnergies' strategy to develop stationary energy storage solutions, which are essential for the integration of renewable energy sources.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 2020:** The project is scheduled for commissioning in late 2020. TotalEnergies was awarded more than 40% of the storage capacities in the first call for tenders organized by RTE (France’s Electricity Transmission Network), marking a significant milestone in the project’s development.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - Saft, the company responsible for manufacturing the battery containers, is a wholly-owned affiliate of TotalEnergies, indicating that TotalEnergies has full ownership of the facility.
