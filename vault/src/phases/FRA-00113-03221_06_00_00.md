### Evolutionary Timeline of NPC Incorporated Facility in Bordeaux, FRA

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2021**: NPC Incorporated provided a solar module disassembly line to Envie, a French company specializing in recycling and repair of waste electrical and electronic equipment. The disassembly line has an annual production capacity of 28 MW and is designed to enable the reuse of various components from PV panels, including frames, junction boxes, intact broken glass, solar cells, and EVA sheets. The technology utilizes heat blade cutting at 300°C, allowing for the separation of module components without crushing them. The facility claims to have disassembled around 25,000 modules at the end of their lifecycle using this equipment.
