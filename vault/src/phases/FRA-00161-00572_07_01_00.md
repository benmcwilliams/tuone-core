### Evolutionary Timeline of Nidec PSA Emotors Facility in Carrières-sous-Poissy, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05/2018**: The Nidec PSA emotors facility was established as a joint venture between PSA Group and Nidec, focusing on the development and sale of electric motors for battery electric vehicles (BEVs) and hybrid electric vehicles (HEVs). The facility aims to achieve an annual production capacity of 900,000 electric machines by 2022.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05/2018**: The joint venture was initiated with a total investment of 220 million euros, with an initial share capital of 15 million euros.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05/2018**: The joint venture was officially completed, marking the establishment of Nidec PSA emotors in Carrières-sous-Poissy.
- **12/2018**: The facility's electric motors were set to be integrated into the GAC Aion S electric car, indicating the commencement of production activities and the facility's role in supplying components for electric vehicles.

#### 4. Labour
- **05/2018**: The facility started with an initial workforce of 40 employees, with plans to hire an additional 30 engineers over the summer, indicating a commitment to expanding its workforce to support operations.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **05/2018**: The facility operates as a joint venture, with both PSA Group and Nidec holding equal ownership stakes of 50% each in Nidec PSA emotors.
