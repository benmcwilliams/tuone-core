### Evolutionary Timeline of the Valeo Facility in Cléon, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10/2022**: Valeo completed the acquisition of Siemens’ 50% stake in the Valeo Siemens eAutomotive (VSeA) joint venture, resulting in Valeo gaining full ownership (100%) of the company. VSeA was integrated into Valeo’s Powertrain Systems Business Group. The facility focuses on developing and producing electric drive systems, including a new generation of electric drive systems with a power output of 200 kW, set to begin production in 2027.
- **07/2022**: Renault commissioned a new production line for electric motors at its Cléon plant, initially producing the ‘ePT-160kW’ motor with a capacity of 120,000 motors per year, which could be expanded to 240,000 units in the future. The facility is also set to produce the ‘ePT-100-kW’ motor starting in 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07/2022**: Renault announced a €620 million investment in the Cléon plant to support the creation of the ElectriCity cluster, aimed at establishing France as a global center for electric mobility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **10/2022**: Valeo now holds 100% ownership of the Valeo Siemens eAutomotive joint venture, following Siemens' divestment of its stake. This full ownership allows for greater integration and strategic alignment within Valeo’s Powertrain Systems Business Group.
