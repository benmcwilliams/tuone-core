## FRA-00264-07764

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Eoliennes en Mer Dieppe Le Tréport Facility

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Project Overview**: The Eoliennes en Mer Dieppe Le Tréport (EMDT) facility is an offshore wind farm located 15 km off Tréport and 17 km off Dieppe, France. It will consist of 62 wind turbines.
- **Production Capacity**: The facility is expected to supply electricity to approximately 850,000 people annually, which is about two-thirds of the population of Seine-Maritime.
- **Construction Timeline**: The construction phase is set to last three years, starting in 2023 and expected to be commissioned in the second half of 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Amount**: The total investment for the EMDT project is around €2.7 billion.
- **Financing Structure**: The financing involves a combination of public and private funding, led by the Japan Bank for International Cooperation (JBIC) and a syndicate of 16 banks from various countries, including France, Japan, the UK, Spain, Germany, and the Netherlands.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Final Investment Decision**: The final investment decision was announced on April 28, 2023, allowing the project to move into the construction phase.
- **Construction Start**: Construction officially began following the final investment decision, with maritime work scheduled to start in 2024.
- **Key Milestones**: 
  - 2024: Installation of piles.
  - 2025: Installation of jackets and the electrical substation.
  - 2026: Transport and installation of foundations and inter-array cables, followed by the installation of wind turbines.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The construction phase is expected to create approximately 1,300 direct jobs in France, contributing to the local economy and the development of the French offshore wind energy industry.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: The EMDT facility is a joint venture, primarily owned by Ocean Winds (a 50-50 joint venture between ENGIE and EDP Renewables), along with Sumitomo Corporation and la Banque des Territoires as shareholders. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones of the Eoliennes en Mer Dieppe Le Tréport facility, highlighting its significance in the offshore wind energy sector in France.
