## FRA-00272-10502

### Evolutionary Timeline of AESC Facility in Douai, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Initial Production Capacity**: The AESC facility in Douai is set to launch with a production capacity of **9 GWh** in **2025**. 
- **Future Expansion Plans**: The facility is planned to expand gradually to a capacity of **24-30 GWh by 2030**. Originally, there were plans for a capacity of **43 GWh**, but these were downsized at the end of **2021** due to a change in the site location and project scope.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Funding**: On **13-10-2023**, the European Investment Bank (EIB) announced it would provide **€450 million** in support for the Douai facility, which includes **€337.2 million** as a direct loan and **€112.8 million** in indirect loans. This funding is aimed at realizing the first phase of the plant.
- **Total Investment**: The total investment for the Douai facility is projected to be **€1.3 billion**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Construction and Production Timeline**: The facility is expected to start production in **2025**. The project is part of the Renault ElectriCity industrial cluster, which aims to produce **400,000 electric vehicles per year** starting in **2025**.
- **Revised Capacity Plans**: The initial plan for a **43 GWh** capacity was revised to **30 GWh or more by 2029**, and later adjusted to **24-30 GWh by 2030**.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The construction and operation of the Douai facility are expected to create new jobs with high added value, contributing to the local economy and supporting the European decarbonization efforts.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: AESC is a subsidiary of Envision, which is the majority shareholder. The facility in Douai is not exclusively owned by AESC but is part of a broader strategy to supply multiple automotive manufacturers, including Renault.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic decisions regarding the AESC facility in Douai, France, highlighting its role in the evolving landscape of clean technology and electric vehicle production.
