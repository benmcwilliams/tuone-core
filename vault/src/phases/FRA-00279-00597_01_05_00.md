## FRA-00279-00597

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Orano Facility in Dunkirk, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05/2023**: Orano, in partnership with XTC New Energy Materials, announced plans to build a cathode material factory in Dunkirk. This facility will focus on producing cathode active materials for electric vehicle batteries and precursors such as cobalt, nickel, manganese, and lithium. Additionally, Orano plans to establish a battery recycling plant nearby to support the circular economy in battery production.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05/2023**: A joint investment of €1.5 billion was announced for the construction of the cathode material factory and the battery recycling plant. This investment is part of a broader strategy to revitalize the industrial sector in the Hauts de France region.
  
#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05/2023**: French President Emmanuel Macron announced the joint venture between Orano and XTC for the cathode material production during a visit to Dunkirk.
- **10/2023**: Orano entered into a joint venture with Stellantis to develop a commercial recycling entity for lithium-ion batteries, with production expected to begin in early 2026. This facility will utilize Orano's hydrometallurgical technology to recover materials from batteries.

#### 4. Labour
- **05/2023**: The joint venture between Orano and XTC is projected to create approximately 1,700 jobs in the Dunkirk region, contributing to local employment and economic revitalization.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **05/2023**: The facility will be a joint venture, with Orano and XTC New Energy Materials sharing ownership. The cathode material plant will be majority-owned by XTC, while the precursor production facility will be majority-owned by Orano. The battery recycling plant will be developed by Orano independently.

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic initiatives surrounding the Orano facility in Dunkirk, highlighting its role in the emerging battery manufacturing and recycling ecosystem in northern France.
