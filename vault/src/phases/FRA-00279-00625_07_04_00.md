#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10/05/2023**: ProLogium announces plans for a solid-state battery cell factory in Dunkirk, which will be its largest facility globally, with a planned production capacity of **48 GWh**. The factory will also include a research and development center.
- **19/06/2023**: ProLogium introduces a new large-footprint lithium ceramic battery (LLCB) at a trade fair, which will be delivered to European car manufacturers for testing by the end of 2023. The Dunkirk facility is expected to start mass production of solid-state battery cells in early **2027**.
- **27/09/2024**: ProLogium's Dunkirk facility is set to incorporate carbon nanotubes supplied by OCSiAl, enhancing the performance of its solid-state batteries.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **10/05/2023**: ProLogium announces an investment of **€5.2 billion** for the Dunkirk facility, with total project costs estimated at **$5.7 billion**.
- **07/08/2023**: The European Commission approves a **€1.5 billion** grant from the French government to support ProLogium's research and development of solid-state batteries, part of the "Prometheus" project running until the end of **2029**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10/05/2023**: Official announcement of the Dunkirk factory, with construction scheduled to begin in the **second half of 2024** and production expected to start by the end of **2026**.
- **19/06/2023**: ProLogium confirms the timeline for the Dunkirk facility, with mass production anticipated to begin in early **2027**.
- **04/10/2024**: The facility is on track for mass production of solid-state batteries, with the project expected to be completed by the end of **2026**.

#### 4. Labour
- **10/05/2023**: The Dunkirk facility is projected to create up to **3,000 jobs** as part of its operations.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- ProLogium is establishing the Dunkirk facility as its **first European factory**, indicating **full ownership** of the site. There are no indications of joint ventures or partnerships specifically related to the ownership of this facility.
