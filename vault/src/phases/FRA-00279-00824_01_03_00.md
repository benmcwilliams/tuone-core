### Evolutionary Timeline of the Verkor Facility in Dunkirk, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03-02-2022**: Verkor announces plans to build its first battery cell factory in Dunkirk, with an initial production capacity of **16 GWh** scheduled to start in **2025**. The facility is expected to expand to **50 GWh** by **2030**.
- **12-11-2023**: Construction officially begins on the battery cell factory in Dunkirk, with operations set to commence in **2025** at the planned capacity of **16 GWh**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06-07-2022**: Verkor secures **€250 million** in financing, including contributions from the European Investment Bank, to support the construction of the Dunkirk facility.
- **10-09-2023**: Verkor raises over **€2 billion** in fresh capital, which includes **€650 million** in grants from the French government and additional subsidies from the Hauts-de-France region and Dunkirk.
- **31-10-2023**: The EU approves nearly **€660 million** in state aid for Verkor, supporting its research project and pilot battery production.
- **27-05-2024**: Verkor secures **€1.3 billion** in financing from various banks to complete the Dunkirk factory.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03-02-2022**: Announcement of the Dunkirk facility and its production timeline.
- **29-03-2022**: Confirmation of the construction start in **2023**.
- **14-01-2023**: Verkor and Renault enter a commercial partnership for battery cell supply.
- **12-11-2023**: Official start of construction for the battery cell factory in Dunkirk.

#### 4. Labour
- **03-02-2022**: The project is expected to create **1,200 direct jobs** and over **3,000 indirect jobs** during its first phase.
- **12-11-2023**: The factory's construction is anticipated to contribute to the same job creation figures.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Verkor is a **joint venture** with Renault as its primary partner, having entered a strategic partnership in **2021** that evolved into a commercial partnership in **2023**. Renault is the main customer for the battery cells produced at the Dunkirk facility.
