### Evolutionary Timeline of the Verkor Facility in Dunkirk, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03-02-2022**: Verkor announces plans to build its first battery cell factory in Dunkirk, with an initial production capacity of **16 GWh** scheduled to start in **2025**. The facility is expected to expand to **50 GWh** by **2030**.
- **11-01-2023**: Verkor officially starts construction of the battery cell factory in Dunkirk, confirming the initial capacity of **16 GWh** and the planned expansion to **50 GWh** by **2030**.
- **12-11-2023**: Verkor secures **€1.3 billion** in financing to complete the construction of the Dunkirk facility, which is expected to open in **2025**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **29-03-2022**: Plastic Omnium invests **€20 million** in Verkor to develop and manufacture battery modules, ensuring preferential access to **4 GWh** of battery cells per year from the Dunkirk facility starting in **2025**.
- **07-11-2022**: Verkor secures **€250 million** in financing, including contributions from the European Investment Bank, to support the construction of the Dunkirk factory.
- **09-2023**: Verkor raises over **€2 billion** in fresh capital, including **€650 million** in grants from the French government and additional subsidies from local authorities, to fund the Dunkirk facility and R&D activities in Grenoble.
- **10-2023**: The EU approves nearly **€660 million** in state aid for Verkor, supporting its research project and pilot battery production.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03-02-2022**: Announcement of the Dunkirk facility and its production timeline.
- **11-01-2023**: Official start of construction for the Dunkirk battery cell factory.
- **31-10-2023**: EU approval for state aid to support Verkor's R&D activities.
- **17-11-2023**: Verkor celebrates the official start of construction for the Dunkirk facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **03-02-2022**: The project is expected to create **1,200 direct jobs** and over **3,000 indirect jobs** during its first phase.
- **11-01-2023**: Confirmation of job creation associated with the Dunkirk facility, with expectations of **1,200 direct** and **3,000 indirect jobs**.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **03-02-2022**: Verkor is identified as the owner of the Dunkirk facility, with strategic partnerships, notably with Renault, for battery supply and development. The partnership with Renault was initially established in **2021** and formalized into a commercial partnership in **April 2023**.
