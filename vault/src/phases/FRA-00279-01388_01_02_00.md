## FRA-00279-01388

### Evolutionary Timeline of the XTC Facility in Dunkirk, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05/2023**: XTC New Energy Materials, in collaboration with French industrial group Orano, announced plans to build a battery material factory in Dunkirk. The facility will focus on the production of cathode materials for lithium-ion batteries. Specific production volumes have not yet been disclosed.
- **Future Plans**: The joint venture will include two production facilities and a research and development center, with operations expected to be established by 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05/2023**: The total investment for the joint venture between XTC and Orano is set at **1.5 billion euros**. This investment is part of a broader strategy to enhance the local battery manufacturing ecosystem in Dunkirk.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05/2023**: French President Emmanuel Macron announced the establishment of the XTC facility during a presentation of Prologium’s plans for a battery plant in the same region. This marked the official confirmation of the project.
- **Future Milestones**: The facility is expected to be operational by **2026**, with the development of two production plants and a research center.

#### 4. Labour
- **05/2023**: The joint venture between XTC and Orano is projected to create **1,700 jobs** in the Dunkirk region, contributing to local employment and economic revitalization.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **05/2023**: The facility will be a **joint venture** between XTC New Energy Materials and Orano, with XTC being the majority owner of one of the plants dedicated to cathode active materials, while Orano will have majority ownership of the plant focused on precursors for cathode materials.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic initiatives surrounding the XTC facility in Dunkirk, highlighting its role in the emerging battery manufacturing landscape in northern France.
