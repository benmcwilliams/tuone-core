### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mobilize Facility in Flins, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-12-2022**: Mobilize, a brand of Renault Group, is focused on developing mobility services, including car-sharing, vehicle subscriptions, and ride-hailing. The facility in Flins is part of Renault's Refactory plant, which is being expanded to support vehicle reuse, recycling, and refurbishing. Mobilize plans to produce new vehicles, including the Duo and Bento, designed for sharing services. The facility will also produce mobile energy storage systems using second-life batteries in collaboration with Betteries AMPS GmbH.

- **26-07-2023**: Production of the Renault Zoe electric car will cease on 30 March 2024 at the Flins plant. The Zoe has been a pioneer in electric vehicles and vehicle-to-grid (V2G) technology. It will be replaced by the Mobilize Duo and Bento, which will be integrated into operations at Flins, emphasizing circular economy principles and the concept of the car as a revenue platform.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05-12-2022**: Renault Group has launched a new company, "The Future is Neutral," to support its reuse and recycling initiatives at the Flins facility. This indicates a strategic investment in expanding the facility's capabilities to support the circular economy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05-12-2022**: Mobilize announces its innovative approach to mobility services and the establishment of the Flins facility as a key site for vehicle refurbishing and energy services.
  
- **26-07-2023**: The end of production for the Zoe is officially announced, marking a significant milestone in the transition of the Flins facility towards new models and services.

#### 4. Labour
- **05-12-2022**: Mobilize's strategy involves a workforce of about 500 people, primarily tech engineers and software engineers, to support the development of its technology platform and services. The collaboration with Betteries AMPS GmbH for second-life battery applications may also create additional job opportunities related to energy storage solutions.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **05-12-2022**: Mobilize operates as a brand under the Renault Group, indicating that the facility in Flins is owned exclusively by Renault Group. There is no mention of joint ventures or external ownership in the articles provided.
