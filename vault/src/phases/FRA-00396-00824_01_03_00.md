## FRA-00396-00824

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Verkor Facility in Grenoble, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2021**: Renault confirms a partnership with Verkor to develop high-performance batteries for electric vehicles, with plans for a battery factory in France. Initial production capacity is set at 10 GWh for Renault, with potential expansion to 20 GWh by 2030.
- **July 2021**: Verkor announces plans to build the Verkor Innovation Center (VIC) in Grenoble, which will focus on battery cell development and production, with an initial capacity of 150 MWh.
- **June 2023**: The VIC officially opens, serving as a pilot production facility and R&D center for Verkor.
- **November 2023**: Verkor begins construction of a battery cell factory in Dunkirk, with an initial capacity of 16 GWh, set to expand to 50 GWh by 2030.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: Verkor raises €100 million in funding to support the development of the VIC and battery production capabilities.
- **November 2022**: Verkor secures €250 million in financing, including contributions from the European Investment Bank, to support the VIC and prepare for series production.
- **September 2023**: Verkor raises over €2 billion in capital, including €650 million in grants from the French government and additional support from the European Investment Bank.
- **May 2024**: Verkor secures €1.3 billion in financing for the Dunkirk factory, bringing total capital raised for the facility and R&D activities in Grenoble to over €3 billion.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2021**: Renault announces its partnership with Verkor and plans for a battery factory.
- **July 2021**: Verkor confirms the establishment of the VIC in Grenoble.
- **June 2023**: The VIC begins operations.
- **September 2023**: Verkor starts construction of the Dunkirk battery cell factory.
- **2025**: Production at the Dunkirk factory is scheduled to commence.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2021**: The partnership with Renault is expected to create nearly 4,500 direct jobs in France by 2030.
- **November 2022**: The Dunkirk factory is projected to create around 1,200 direct jobs and 3,000 indirect jobs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2021**: Renault acquires a 20% stake in Verkor, indicating a joint ownership structure. Verkor operates as a startup with multiple investors, including EIT InnoEnergy, Groupe IDEC, Schneider Electric, and Capgemini.
