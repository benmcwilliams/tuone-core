- **02/2022**: Rosi Solar develops a novel pyrolysis-based solution for recycling end-of-life PV modules, capable of recovering high-purity silicon, silver, and copper. The facility's technology focuses on thermal and chemical separation processes to isolate metals while retaining their purity.
