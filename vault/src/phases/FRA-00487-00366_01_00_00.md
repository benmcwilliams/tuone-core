### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hydrovolt Facility in Hordain, France

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **03/2024**: Hydrovolt announces plans to establish battery recycling operations in Hordain, France. The facility will initially serve as a collection point for discharging and dismantling electric vehicle and industrial batteries. The existing facility has an area of approximately 3,000 square meters. Operations are expected to commence in **2025**. Hydrovolt aims to recycle around **70,000 tons** of battery packs by **2025** and **300,000 tons** by **2030**.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **03/2024**: Hydrovolt makes a final investment decision to set up the recycling operations in Hordain. Specific investment amounts have not been disclosed, but the decision is part of Hydrovolt's strategy to expand its presence in the European battery recycling market.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **03/2024**: Announcement of the establishment of the Hydrovolt facility in Hordain, with operations expected to start in **2025**. The project is still subject to necessary approvals.

4. **Labour**
   - **03/2024**: No specific job creation or job loss figures have been provided yet regarding the new facility in Hordain.

5. **Ownership Structure**
   - Hydrovolt is a **joint venture** between the Swedish battery manufacturer **Northvolt** and the Norwegian aluminium producer **Norsk Hydro**. Therefore, the facility in Hordain is not owned exclusively by either company but is a collaborative effort between the two.
