1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **03/2024**: Hydrovolt, a joint venture between Northvolt and Norsk Hydro, announced plans to establish battery recycling operations in Hordain, France. The facility will utilize an existing site of approximately **3,000 square meters** suitable for recycling.
