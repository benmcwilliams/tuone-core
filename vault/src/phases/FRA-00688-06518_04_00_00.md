### Evolutionary Timeline of the H2Med Facility in Marseille, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2022**: The H2Med project, formerly known as BarMar, is announced as the first green hydrogen corridor connecting the Iberian Peninsula to Marseille, France. The project will focus solely on transporting green hydrogen, with an operational target set for 2030. The pipeline will be capable of transporting approximately 2 million tons of hydrogen per year, which is expected to meet 10% of the EU's hydrogen consumption by that year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12/2022**: The estimated cost for the Barcelona-Marseille section of the H2Med pipeline is projected to be €2.5 billion. The project is expected to be presented as a Project of Common Interest (IPCEI) by December 15, 2023, which would allow access to European funding sources, potentially covering up to 50% of eligible initiatives.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2022**: The leaders of Spain, France, and Portugal officially unveil the H2Med energy interconnection project during a meeting in Alicante, Spain. The project includes two main sections: one from Celorico, Portugal, to Zamora, Spain (248 kilometers), and the other from Barcelona to Marseille (455 kilometers). The project is part of the RePowerEU Plan.
