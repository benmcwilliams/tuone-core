### Evolutionary Timeline of Parc Eolien Offshore de Provence Grand Large Facility in Marseille, FRA

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 03-10-2022**: The facility is developing a floating offshore wind farm named Provence Grand Large, which will consist of three 8 MW Siemens Gamesa wind turbines. The wind farm will be installed 40 kilometers west of Marseille and 17 kilometers off the coast of Port Saint Louis du Rhône. The floating foundations are designed by SBM Offshore and IFP Energies Nouvelles, and the project is set to operate in water depths of around 100 meters. The total production capacity of the pilot project is 25 MW.
