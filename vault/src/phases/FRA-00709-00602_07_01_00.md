## FRA-00709-00602

### Evolutionary Timeline of the PSA Group Facility in Metz, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2018**: PSA Group announces a joint venture with Punch Powertrain named Punch Powertrain PSA e-transmissions, to be based at the Metz facility. The facility is set to produce up to 600,000 electrified powertrains of the e-DCT type with a 48-volt configuration, specifically adapted for PSA’s mild hybrids. Initial production will focus on the current electric dual-clutch transmission Hybrid DT2 until the new production lines are established.
- **June 2020**: A second joint venture is formed between PSA and Punch Powertrain, focusing on developing and supplying the DT22 dual-clutch transmission for mild hybrid and plug-in hybrid vehicles. Operations for this joint venture are expected to start in the third quarter of 2020.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2018**: PSA announces an investment of 80 million euros into the joint venture with Punch Powertrain, aimed at enhancing the production capabilities for electrified powertrains at the Metz facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2018**: Announcement of the joint venture and its objectives, awaiting official permission from responsible agencies.
- **June 2020**: Confirmation of the establishment of a second joint venture, with operations expected to commence in Q3 2020.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2018**: The cooperation between PSA and Punch Powertrain is expected to create 400 jobs at the Metz facility as part of the joint venture's operations.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2020**: Punch Powertrain holds a majority stake of 61% in the new joint venture, indicating a joint ownership structure between PSA and Punch Powertrain for the operations at the Metz facility.
