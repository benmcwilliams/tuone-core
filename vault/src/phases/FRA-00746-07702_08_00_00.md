### Evolutionary Timeline of the EOLFI Facility in Montpellier, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2019**: EOLFI specializes in floating offshore wind power and has developed over 200 renewable energy projects, both onshore and offshore, in five countries. The facility is involved in a pilot floating wind project off the coast of Brittany, which includes the installation of three wind turbines on semi-submersible floats in deep water.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2019**: The acquisition of EOLFI by Shell is a significant investment aimed at enhancing Shell’s renewable power business, particularly in offshore wind. This acquisition is expected to leverage Shell's resources to scale up EOLFI's floating wind projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12-11-2019**: Shell agrees to acquire EOLFI, marking a significant milestone in the expansion of Shell's offshore wind business in France. The acquisition is expected to facilitate the development of floating wind technology and projects.

#### 4. Labour
- **2019**: EOLFI employs over 65 professionals across its offices, including Montpellier. The acquisition by Shell is anticipated to support job retention and potential job creation as the facility expands its operations under Shell's ownership.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **12-11-2019**: Following the acquisition, EOLFI will become a wholly owned subsidiary of Shell, indicating full ownership by Shell and integration into its New Energies division. 

This timeline captures the key developments and changes at the EOLFI facility in Montpellier, France, as it transitions under Shell's ownership and expands its focus on floating wind technology.
