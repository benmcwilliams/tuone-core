### Energies du Sud Facility in Montpellier, FRA

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Capacity**: The joint venture was to install an electrolyzer capable of creating **800 kg per day of hydrogen**.
- **Updates**: The project included a **2.8 MW solar power plant**, hydrogen storage, and distribution stations to refuel buses. However, the project was ultimately **cancelled** due to financial concerns.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Funding**: The project was expected to cost **€29 million**, funded in part by contributions from regional and national transportation authorities and the **European Union**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
