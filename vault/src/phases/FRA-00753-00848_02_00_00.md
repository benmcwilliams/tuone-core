#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **01-06-2023**: Vulcan Energy, in collaboration with Stellantis, initiated a geothermal project aimed at decarbonizing the Opel main plant in Rüsselsheim, Germany, and expanding geothermal energy assets for the Mulhouse facility.
