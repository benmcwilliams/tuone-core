## FRA-00770-00697

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Saft Facility in Nersac, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2020**: The Saft facility in Nersac is set to begin pilot production of battery cells, which is a precursor to series production. The technology aims for maximum energy efficiency and a lower CO2 balance compared to competitors.
- **April 2021**: The facility is part of the Automotive Cells Company (ACC), a joint venture focused on developing and producing battery cells and modules. The pilot production is a key step towards larger-scale production planned for Douvrin and Kaiserslautern.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2020**: Total announces an investment of approximately 200 million euros for the pilot production at the Nersac facility. The overall investment for the battery cell production project, including the facilities in Douvrin and Kaiserslautern, is nearly 5 billion euros, with 1.3 billion euros in financial support from French and German authorities.
  
#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2020**: Official go-ahead for pilot production at the Saft facility in Nersac, scheduled to start in mid-2021.
- **April 2021**: The facility's pilot production is confirmed as part of a broader strategy to increase battery production capacity in Europe, with plans for series production in Douvrin starting in late 2023 and in Kaiserslautern by the end of 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2020**: The project is expected to create around 2,000 jobs at the Opel site in Kaiserslautern, which will be supported by the battery cells produced at the Nersac facility. The job creation at Nersac itself is not specified but is implied as part of the broader project.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **January 2020**: Saft, a subsidiary of Total, is involved in a 50:50 joint venture with PSA (now Stellantis) called Automotive Cell Company (ACC) for the development of EV battery manufacturing in Europe. Saft's share in the joint venture is expected to decrease to 33% during the commercial production phase.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Saft facility in Nersac, France, focusing on its technology, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
