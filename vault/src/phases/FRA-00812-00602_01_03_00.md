- **09/2019**: Announcement of the establishment of a Franco-German battery cell consortium involving PSA (through its German subsidiary Opel) and French battery manufacturer Saft. The consortium plans to lay the foundation for a pilot plant in Nouvelle-Aquitaine by the end of 2019, with two factories for series production of battery cells to be built in the first half of the 2020s—one in France starting in 2022 and another in Germany starting in 2024.

#### 4. Labour
