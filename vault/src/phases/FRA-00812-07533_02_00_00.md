### Evolutionary Timeline of the ADEME Facility in Nouvelle-Aquitaine, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2023**: France’s Ministry of Energy Transition published a national action plan for geothermal energy, aiming to meet renewable heating and cooling needs. The regional strategy for Nouvelle-Aquitaine targets a geothermal energy output of 3,500 GWh by 2030.
- **2024**: The regional ecosystem around the Avenia geothermal cluster is being developed, promoting both surface and deep geothermal energy.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2022-2028**: The Nouvelle-Aquitaine Region allocated a budget of EUR 196 million for renewable energy development, prioritizing geothermal energy.
- **2024**: ADEME financed 71 feasibility studies and 31 investment projects, providing a total of EUR 7.9 million in aid.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **End of 2023**: The national action plan for geothermal energy was officially published.
- **2024-2027**: A 3-year action plan was established to develop the geothermal sector in Nouvelle-Aquitaine in alignment with the national plan.
- **June 19-20, 2025**: National geothermal days are scheduled to be held in Biarritz, organized by the AFPG with ADEME as a partner.
