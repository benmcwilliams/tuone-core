## FRA-00853-00782

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Toyota Facility in Paris, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2023**: Toyota announced plans to use converted fuel cell coaches for transportation during the 2024 Paris Olympic and Paralympic Games. The facility will produce hydrogen fuel cell modules for the conversion of ten used Iveco coaches, which will replace diesel engines with zero-emission technology. These coaches are expected to transport over 5,000 visitors during the Games and will continue to operate in the Ile de France region post-Games.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2023**: Toyota's investment in the conversion of the coaches into hydrogen fuel cell buses is part of its broader strategy as the worldwide mobility partner of the International Olympic Committee (IOC) and the International Paralympic Committee (IPC). The specific financial details of the investment were not disclosed, but the initiative represents a significant commitment to hydrogen technology and sustainable transport solutions.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2023**: Announcement of the use of hydrogen fuel cell modules in ten converted coaches for the Paris 2024 Games. The coaches will be converted to hydrogen fuel cell electric vehicles, with the conversion process involving the replacement of diesel engines and gearboxes with hydrogen fuel cell technology and electric motors.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2023**: The conversion of the coaches into hydrogen fuel cell buses is expected to create jobs related to the conversion process and ongoing operations. However, specific job creation numbers were not mentioned in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **October 2023**: Toyota is involved in a partnership with GCK, an industrial company group, for the conversion of the coaches. After the Games, the converted coaches will be purchased by French company B.E. Green, indicating that Toyota does not exclusively own the facility but is collaborating with other entities for this project.

This timeline captures the key developments related to the Toyota facility in Paris, focusing on its operations, investments, project milestones, labor implications, and ownership structure as of October 2023.
