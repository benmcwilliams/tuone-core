### EOLFI Facility Timeline in Paris, FRA

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - EOLFI specializes in floating offshore wind power and has developed over 200 renewable energy projects (both onshore and offshore) in five countries since its inception in 2004. The facility is involved in a pilot floating wind project off the coast of Brittany, which includes the installation of three wind turbines on semi-submersible floats in deep water where fixed foundations are unsuitable.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The acquisition of EOLFI by Shell represents a significant investment aimed at enhancing Shell’s renewable power business, particularly in offshore wind. This acquisition is expected to leverage Shell's resources and expertise to scale up EOLFI's operations.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2019:** Shell agrees to acquire EOLFI, marking a significant milestone in the expansion of Shell's offshore wind business in France. The acquisition is expected to facilitate the development of the pilot floating wind project in Brittany, pending necessary approvals.

4. **Labour:**
   - EOLFI employs over 65 professionals across its offices in Paris, Lorient, Marseilles, and Montpellier. The acquisition by Shell may lead to job stability and potential growth in employment as the facility expands its operations under Shell's ownership.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - Following the acquisition, EOLFI will become a wholly owned subsidiary of Shell, indicating full ownership by Shell of the facility.
