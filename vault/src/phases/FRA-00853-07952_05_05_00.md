### Evolutionary Timeline of the Newcleo Facility in Paris, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: Newcleo develops small modular reactors (SMRs) powered by reprocessed nuclear waste. The facility is in the process of constructing a manufacturing plant for reprocessed nuclear waste and a prototype reactor.
- **Production Capacity**: The first reactor is expected to be operational in France by the end of 2031, with a non-nuclear precursor prototype ready by 2026 in Italy.
- **Updates**: Newcleo is also investing in a mixed uranium/plutonium oxide (MOX) plant to fuel its reactors.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Total Investment**: As of September 2024, Newcleo has secured over €535 million in investments, including €135 million in new investments announced on September 30, 2024.
- **Future Investments**: Between 2025 and 2027, Newcleo plans to invest an additional €135 million in Italy for a materials research and development center.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Headquarters Relocation**: Newcleo is relocating its headquarters from London to Paris, which is expected to enhance its project development and funding opportunities.
- **Joint Venture**: A joint venture with NextChem's parent company, Maire SpA, is planned, where Newcleo will take a 40% stake. This agreement is expected to be finalized by February 2025.
- **Project Timeline**: The first reactor is expected to be operational by the end of 2031, with the first commercial power plant's final investment decision anticipated around 2029.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The facility is expected to contribute to job creation as it expands its operations and develops new projects across Europe, although specific job numbers related to the Paris facility have not been detailed.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: Newcleo is expected to have full ownership of the facility in Paris, with the joint venture with NextChem representing a strategic partnership rather than shared ownership of the facility itself.
