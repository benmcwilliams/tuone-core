## FRA-00870-00278

### Evolutionary Timeline of Forsee Power Facility in Poitiers, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2018**: Forsee Power announces the establishment of a factory in Grand Poitiers, located in an old diesel engine plant. The facility is set to produce up to 2,500 battery systems for electric buses annually, with production starting in late 2018. The factory will feature three automated production lines and will be equipped with photovoltaic panels to generate 1.8 MW of clean electricity.
- **05/2022**: The Poitiers plant officially opens with five assembly lines for e-bus batteries, achieving a production capacity of 1 GWh, equivalent to over 2,500 bus batteries per year. The company anticipates increasing this capacity to 4 GWh by 2027, which would allow for the production of batteries for 10,000 electric buses annually.
- **03/2023**: Forsee Power collaborates with Blue Solutions and Bluebus to develop a new solid-state battery system, expected to be ready by 2026. The new battery systems will utilize NMC lithium-ion technology and will be integrated into Forsee Power's existing battery systems.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2018**: Forsee Power's expansion plans include a significant investment to increase battery production capacity tenfold by 2021, supported by the renovation of the existing facility.
- **05/2022**: The opening of the production site is marked as a significant step in the company’s industrial and commercial development, with support from the State, the New Aquitaine region, the Greater Poitiers agglomeration, and the Revitalization Fund.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **04/2018**: Announcement of the factory's establishment and planned production capacity.
- **Late 2018**: Production is set to commence at the new facility.
- **05/2022**: Official opening of the Poitiers production site.
- **03/2023**: Announcement of collaboration with Blue Solutions and Bluebus for the development of a new battery system.

#### 4. Labour
- **04/2018**: Forsee Power plans to create 300 jobs as part of its expansion, with the first 100 workers expected to start in 2018.
- **05/2022**: The facility employs over 200 people, with expectations for increased employment as production capacity grows.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **04/2018**: Forsee Power is noted to have moved into a facility that was previously owned by the Greater Poitiers agglomeration, which was renovated for their use. The ownership structure indicates that Forsee Power operates the facility but does not own it exclusively, as it was acquired and renovated by the local government.
