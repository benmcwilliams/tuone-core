## FRA-00870-00278

### Evolutionary Timeline of Forsee Power Facility in Poitiers, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2018**: Forsee Power announces the establishment of a factory in Grand Poitiers, set to produce up to 2,500 battery systems for electric buses annually. The facility will run 3 automated production lines and will be refurbished from an old diesel engine plant.
- **12/2018**: Production is set to start at the new factory, which will also feature photovoltaic panels generating 1.8 MW of clean electricity.
- **03/2022**: Forsee Power extends its partnership with Iveco France, supplying the new ZEN 42 battery pack, which offers up to 20% higher energy density than the previous ZEN 35 model.
- **05/2022**: The Poitiers plant officially opens with five assembly lines, achieving a production capacity of 1 GWh, equivalent to over 2,500 bus batteries per year. Plans are in place to increase capacity to 4 GWh by 2027.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2018**: Forsee Power's expansion plans are supported by the Greater Poitiers agglomeration, which acquired and renovated the site.
- **05/2022**: The opening of the facility marks a significant step in Forsee Power's industrial and commercial development, with support from the State, New Aquitaine region, and the Revitalization Fund.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **04/2018**: Announcement of the factory's establishment and planned production capacity.
- **12/2018**: Expected start of production at the new facility.
- **05/2022**: Official opening of the Poitiers production site.
- **03/2023**: Collaboration with Blue Solutions and Bluebus to develop a new solid-state battery system, with production capabilities at the Poitiers facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **04/2018**: Forsee Power plans to create 300 jobs, with the first 100 workers starting in 2018 as production begins.
- **05/2022**: Over 200 employees are reported to be working at the Poitiers site, with expectations of increased employment as production capacity grows.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **04/2018**: Forsee Power is establishing the facility in a site acquired and renovated by the Greater Poitiers agglomeration, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership.
