   - **Production Focus:** The facility will produce an electric SUV, codenamed CR3, based on the STLA Medium platform. This vehicle will be available exclusively as a Battery Electric Vehicle (BEV).
   - **Production Capacity:** The facility is set to incorporate a battery assembly workshop and a plastic injection moulding workshop, marking the first of its kind in a French Stellantis plant. The workshops will manufacture components for the new electric SUV.
