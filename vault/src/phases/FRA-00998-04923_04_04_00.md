#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Capacity**: The facility is designed to produce green hydrogen with a capacity of 135 kg (1,500 Nm3) per hour. The hydrogen will be produced using electrolyzers fed by a 12 MW solar PV plant.
- **Technology**: The hydrogen produced will have a high purity level (>99.999%) and will be suitable for various applications, including sale to off-takers and injection into the Portuguese natural gas distribution network.
