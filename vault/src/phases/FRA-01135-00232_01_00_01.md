### Evolutionary Timeline of the Eramet Facility in Trappes, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2023**: Eramet, in collaboration with BASF and SUEZ, is involved in the ReLieVe project aimed at recycling lithium-ion batteries for electric vehicles. The facility in Trappes is part of this initiative, with operations expected to begin in summer 2023. The facility focuses on developing efficient recycling processes for critical metals such as nickel, cobalt, manganese, and lithium.
- **November 2023**: Eramet opens a pilot plant for recycling electric car batteries at its research centre in Trappes. This pilot plant is a 1:1,000 scale model of a larger recycling plant planned in Dunkirk, intended to test and optimize recycling processes on a pre-industrial scale.
