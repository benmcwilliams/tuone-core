### Evolutionary Timeline of the Symbio Facility in Haguenau, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-06-2022**: Schaeffler and Symbio announced the establishment of a joint venture named Innoplate, dedicated to the production of bipolar plates for fuel cells with proton exchange membrane (PEM). The facility in Haguenau will serve as the headquarters and the first plant for this venture. The production is set to begin in early 2024, with a target of producing approximately 50 million bipolar plates by 2030. The facility aims to employ over 120 people.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-06-2022**: The joint venture has begun expanding the manufacturing building at the Haguenau site and has ordered additional equipment to ensure the start of production in early 2024. Specific investment amounts were not disclosed in the article.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07-06-2022**: The joint venture, Innoplate, received the necessary regulatory approvals for its establishment. Preparations for the facility's expansion and equipment acquisition are underway to meet the production timeline.

#### 4. Labour
- **07-06-2022**: The facility is projected to create over 120 jobs by 2030 as part of its operational goals.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **07-06-2022**: The facility is a joint venture, with Schaeffler and Symbio each owning 50% of Innoplate. This structure indicates that neither company has exclusive ownership of the facility.
