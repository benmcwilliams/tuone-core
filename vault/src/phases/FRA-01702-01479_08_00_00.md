2. **Investment and Development**
   - **April 2023**: The battery storage project was co-financed by the European Union through its European Regional Development Fund (ERDF). This funding was part of a broader initiative to demonstrate the technical feasibility of integrating energy storage with wind energy.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
