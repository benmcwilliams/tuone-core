### Evolutionary Timeline of NAWA Technologies Facility in Aix-en-Provence, France

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **June 2021**: NAWA Technologies began production of Vertically Aligned Carbon Nanotube (VACNT) material at its Aix-en-Provence facility. The facility utilizes a Chemical Vapor Deposition (APCVD) process to grow VACNT simultaneously on both sides of a substrate, achieving lengths of over 200 micrometers on a 30 cm wide aluminum foil. The facility focuses on applications for high-performance batteries, ultracapacitors (NAWACap), and advanced reinforced composites (NAWAStitch). The production process is designed to be scalable and efficient, with plans for future expansions in foil width and productivity.
