### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nidec Facility in Vingeanne, France

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: March 2020
   - **Details**: Nidec has initiated the construction of the first energy storage system in mainland France as part of a pilot project named ‘Ringo’. The facility will utilize a lithium-ion battery with a storage capacity of 12 MW/24 MWh, equivalent to the energy produced by five wind turbines. The project aims to optimize energy flow management on the transmission grid, particularly during peak demand times.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: March 2020
   - **Details**: The total investment for the Ringo project is €80 million (approximately $89 million), funded by Nidec in collaboration with the French transmission system operator RTE.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: January 2020
   - **Details**: Construction work on the Ringo energy storage system commenced in January 2020. The commissioning and testing of the facility are anticipated to begin in March 2021.
