### Evolutionary Timeline of the TotalEnergies Facility in Mardyck, FRA

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** March 2020
   - **Details:** TotalEnergies announced the construction of France’s largest battery storage project in Mardyck, located in Dunkirk’s port district. The facility will have a storage capacity of 25 MW/25 MWh, utilizing Saft's Intensium Max 20 High Energy battery solution, which includes 11 integrated 2.3 MWh containers. The project aims to support the expansion of renewable energy and stabilize the domestic power grid.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** March 2020
   - **Details:** The estimated cost of the Mardyck battery storage project is 13 million Euros (approximately $14.5 million U.S.). This investment is part of TotalEnergies' strategy to enhance its stationary energy storage solutions.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** March 2020
   - **Details:** The Mardyck storage project is scheduled for commissioning later in 2020. TotalEnergies was noted as the leading winner of the first call for tenders organized by RTE (France’s Electricity Transmission Network), with over 40% of the storage capacities allocated.
