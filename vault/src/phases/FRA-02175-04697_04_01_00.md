### Centrax Facility in Saillat-sur-Vienne, France - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Production Focus:** The facility is involved in the development of a power-to-X-to-power demonstrator utilizing an advanced hydrogen gas turbine. The project aims to produce and store hydrogen from renewable electricity, which will be mixed with natural gas for energy generation.
   - **Production Capacity:** The facility operates a 12 MWe combined heat and power plant, which produces steam for the manufacturing needs of Smurfit Kappa.
   - **Upgrades:** An existing Siemens SGT-400 industrial gas turbine will be upgraded to convert stored hydrogen into electricity and thermal energy, with the goal of achieving up to 100% hydrogen operation.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Total Budget:** The project has a total budget of approximately €15.2 million.
   - **Funding:** The European Union is contributing €10.5 million under the Horizon 2020 program to support the project.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Official Launch:** The project was officially launched on May 1, 2020.
   - **Phases of Development:**
     - **May 2020:** Contract finalization and start of engineering development.
     - **2021:** Installation of the hydrogen production, storage, and supply facility at the pilot demonstration site.
     - **2022:** Installation of the gas turbine for natural gas/hydrogen mixtures and initial demonstration of the advanced pilot plant concept.
     - **2023:** Pilot demonstration with up to 100% hydrogen for carbon-free energy production from stored excess renewable energy.
