### Evolutionary Timeline of the German Aerospace Center (DLR) Facility in Saillat-sur-Vienne, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Project Launch**: The facility will implement the world's first industrial-scale power-to-X-to-power demonstrator with an advanced hydrogen turbine, utilizing an existing Siemens SGT-400 industrial gas turbine.
- **Production Capacity**: The facility aims to demonstrate the conversion of stored hydrogen into electricity and thermal energy, with the goal of operating on up to 100% hydrogen.
- **Operational Goals**: The project aims to save up to 65,000 tons of CO2 per year at baseload operation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Total Budget**: The project has a total budget of approximately €15.2 million.
- **EU Contribution**: The European Union will contribute €10.5 million under the Horizon 2020 program.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Official Launch**: The project was officially launched on May 1, 2020.
- **Phases of Development**:
  - **May 2020**: Contract finalization and start of engineering development.
  - **2021**: Installation of the hydrogen production, storage, and supply facility at the pilot demonstration site.
  - **2022**: Installation of the gas turbine for natural gas/hydrogen mixtures and initial demonstration of the advanced pilot plant concept.
  - **2023**: Pilot demonstration with up to 100% hydrogen for carbon-free energy production from stored excess renewable energy.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The project involves multiple stakeholders, including Engie Solutions, Siemens Gas and Power, and others, which may lead to job creation in the region, although specific job numbers are not mentioned in the article.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Joint Venture**: The facility is part of a consortium made up exclusively of European companies and bodies, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by the DLR. Engie Solutions is responsible for building the hydrogen production and storage facility, while Siemens Gas and Power will supply the electrolyzer and upgrade the gas turbine.
