## FRA-02175-05520

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lund University Facility in Saillat-sur-Vienne, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2020**: The HYFLEXPOWER project was officially launched, marking the beginning of the world's first industrial-scale power-to-X-to-power demonstrator with an advanced hydrogen turbine at the Smurfit Kappa site. The facility aims to upgrade an existing Siemens SGT-400 industrial gas turbine to convert stored hydrogen into electricity and thermal energy. The goal is to eventually operate the facility with up to 100% hydrogen, significantly contributing to a carbon-free energy mix.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Total Project Budget**: Approximately €15.2 million, with €10.5 million funded by the European Union under the Horizon 2020 program. This investment supports the development of hydrogen production and storage technologies, as well as the upgrade of the gas turbine.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2020**: Official launch of the HYFLEXPOWER project.
- **2021**: Installation of the hydrogen production, storage, and supply facility at the pilot demonstration site.
- **2022**: Installation of the gas turbine for natural gas/hydrogen mixtures and initial demonstration of the advanced pilot plant concept.
- **2023**: Pilot demonstration with up to 100% hydrogen for carbon-free energy production from stored excess renewable energy.
