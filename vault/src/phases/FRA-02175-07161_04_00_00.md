### Evolutionary Timeline of the Smurfit Kappa Facility in Saillat-sur-Vienne, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **02/2020**: Announcement of the HYFLEXPOWER project, the world's first industrial-scale power-to-X-to-power demonstrator utilizing an advanced hydrogen turbine at the Smurfit Kappa facility. The project aims to upgrade an existing Siemens SGT-400 industrial gas turbine to convert stored hydrogen into electricity and thermal energy, with the goal of achieving up to 100% hydrogen operation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **02/2020**: The total budget for the HYFLEXPOWER project is approximately €15.2 million, with €10.5 million funded by the European Union under the Horizon 2020 program. This investment is aimed at developing and demonstrating a green hydrogen-based power supply.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05/01/2020**: Official launch of the HYFLEXPOWER project, which will last for four years and be divided into several phases.
- **2021**: Installation of the hydrogen production, storage, and supply facility at the pilot demonstration site is scheduled.
- **2022**: Installation of the gas turbine for natural gas/hydrogen mixtures and initial demonstration of the advanced pilot plant concept is planned.
- **2023**: Pilot demonstration with up to 100% hydrogen for carbon-free energy production from stored excess renewable energy is expected to take place.
