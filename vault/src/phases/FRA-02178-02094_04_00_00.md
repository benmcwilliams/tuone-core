1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **July 2020:** Hydrogène de France, in collaboration with Teréga, announced the HyGéo project, a pilot hydrogen storage initiative utilizing disused salt caverns. The facility aims to explore various applications of hydrogen, including power-to-power, power-to-mobility, power-to-industry, and power-to-gas.
