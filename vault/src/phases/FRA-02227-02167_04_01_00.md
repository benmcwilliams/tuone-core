### Evolutionary Timeline of the BayWa Facility in Pôle Mécanique de la Haute Saintonge, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2022**: BayWa r.e. is developing a hybrid solar-storage-hydrogen project at the Pôle Mécanique de la Haute Saintonge. The facility will initially feature a **40 MWp solar project** with an annual generation capacity of **52 GWh**. The specifics of the energy storage system's capacity have not been disclosed. The project aims to eventually produce green hydrogen in partnership with Lhyfe, utilizing solar generation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2022**: BayWa r.e. is seeking investment from the local authority to support the project’s development. The local authority is expected to become a **majority shareholder** during the construction and operational phases.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2022**: The Community of Communes of Haute-Saintonge selected BayWa r.e. to develop the hybrid project. A one-year study on local usage potential for green hydrogen will be conducted before finalizing plans for its production.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2022**: While specific job creation or loss figures are not mentioned, the project is positioned as an investment opportunity for local authorities, which may imply potential job creation during the construction and operational phases.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **August 2022**: BayWa r.e. intends for the local authority to invest in the project and become a **majority shareholder** during the construction and operation phases, indicating a **joint venture** ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by BayWa r.e.
