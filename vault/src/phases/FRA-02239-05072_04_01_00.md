#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07/2021**: Announcement of the world's first offshore green hydrogen production plant, powered by electricity from a floating wind turbine. The facility will be installed at the SEM-REV demonstration site off the coast of Le Croisic, linked with the Floatgen wind turbine. The offshore hydrogen production platform is designed to have a capacity ranging from ten to several hundred megawatts, with plans for large-scale industrial deployment by 2024.
