### Evolutionary Timeline of the Engie Facility in Saucats, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **11-01-2021**: Engie and Neoen announced the Horizeo project, which will feature a 1 GW solar plant, battery storage, a green hydrogen production unit, a data center, and an agrivoltaic facility. The project aims to demonstrate the maturity and competitiveness of solar energy for direct supply to industrial companies.
- **12-01-2021**: The project is described as Europe’s largest solar-plus-storage plant, with a total investment of €1 billion. The electricity generated will be sold through power purchase agreements (PPAs) to unspecified customers.
- **24-08-2021**: The project management report was validated by France's National Commission for Public Debate (CNDP). The solar plant will cover over 900 hectares and is expected to be the largest in Europe in terms of installed capacity. The project includes a 40 MW Li-ion battery storage unit and a green hydrogen production unit with a capacity of around 10 MW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12-01-2021**: The total investment for the Horizeo project is estimated at €1 billion, with specific allocations: €650 million for the solar plant, €20 million for the battery storage unit, €40 million for the hydrogen production unit, and €10 million for the agrivoltaic facility.
- **24-08-2021**: The investment breakdown is reiterated, emphasizing the significant financial commitment to various components of the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **11-01-2021**: Announcement of the Horizeo project.
- **12-01-2021**: Confirmation of the project’s scope and investment details.
- **24-08-2021**: The CNDP initiated a public debate on the project, which began on September 9, 2021, and concluded on January 9, 2022. This included six public meetings and thematic workshops to discuss the project's impacts.

#### 4. Labour
- **24-08-2021**: While specific job creation figures are not mentioned, the project is expected to have significant economic and social impacts, which may include job creation during construction and operation phases.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **11-01-2021**: The facility is a joint venture between Engie and Neoen, with both companies collaborating on the development of the Horizeo project. There is no indication of exclusive ownership by either company.
