### Evolutionary Timeline of the Genvia Facility in Béziers, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/04/2021**: Genvia, a joint venture, is set to produce electrolysers at the Schlumberger’s Cameron plant in Béziers, Hérault. The facility is expected to start producing its first units in July 2021. Genvia utilizes an electrolysis technology developed by CEA.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09/04/2021**: The Occitanie Region has invested €3.5 million for a 6.5% stake in Genvia as part of its commitment to hydrogen technologies, which is part of France's Green Hydrogen Plan with a budget of €150 million up to 2030.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/04/2021**: Genvia officially inaugurated its facility in Béziers on the eve of the Easter weekend. The first production units are scheduled to be completed by July 2021.

- The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the Genvia facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Genvia is a joint venture involving Schlumberger New Energy, CEA, Vicat, Vinci, and the Occitanie Region, indicating that it is not solely owned by one entity but rather a collaborative effort among multiple stakeholders.
