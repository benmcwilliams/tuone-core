## FRA-02314-01652

### Evolutionary Timeline of the First Solar Facility in Blanquefort, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2009
   - **Details:** First Solar, in partnership with EDF Energies Nouvelles, announced plans for a 100MW CdTe thin film solar panel manufacturing facility in Blanquefort, near Bordeaux. The facility will feature two operating manufacturing lines.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2009
   - **Details:** The estimated cost for the new plant is approximately €100 million (US$150 million).

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** December 2009
   - **Details:** The announcement was made regarding the planned facility, with expectations for construction to begin in the second half of 2010 and full production ramp-up by early 2012.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** December 2009
   - **Details:** The facility is expected to create approximately 400 jobs in the region.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** December 2009
   - **Details:** The facility is a joint venture between First Solar and EDF Energies Nouvelles, indicating that it is not exclusively owned by First Solar. 

This timeline captures the initial announcement and expectations surrounding the First Solar facility in Blanquefort, France, as of December 2009.
