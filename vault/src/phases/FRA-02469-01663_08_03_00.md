### Evolutionary Timeline of the GE Facility in Saint-Nazaire, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2019**: GE Renewable Energy is assembling the Haliade-X 12 MW wind turbine at its factory in Saint-Nazaire. The turbine is designed to generate 67 GWh of power annually using a 220-meter rotor.
- **July 2019**: The first Haliade-X nacelle is rolled out from the Saint-Nazaire facility, marking a significant step towards launching the offshore wind turbine into the market.
- **October 2019**: Production of turbines for the 480-MW Saint-Nazaire offshore wind farm begins, with 80 units of Haliade 150-6MW turbines planned for the project.
- **April 2022**: The commissioning of 80 Haliade 150-6MW machines at the Saint-Nazaire offshore wind farm is overseen by marine service operator Valemo.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2019**: GE is investing close to $400 million in the development of the Haliade-X 12 MW turbine.
- **June 2020**: The Haliade-X prototype receives a provisional type certificate from DNV GL, indicating that it meets high safety and quality standards.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2019**: GE signs a memorandum of understanding with Eolien Maritime France (EMF) for the Haliade 150-6MW turbines for offshore wind farms, while also producing turbines for other projects.
- **July 2019**: The first Haliade-X nacelle is unveiled at the Saint-Nazaire facility.
- **October 2019**: Nacelle assembly for the 480-MW Saint-Nazaire offshore wind project is set to begin in Q2 2020.
- **November 2019**: The Haliade-X prototype starts generating power in Rotterdam.
- **June 2020**: The Haliade-X prototype is on track to meet full type certification requirements.
- **April 2022**: The first turbine for the Saint-Nazaire offshore wind farm is installed, marking the project's progress.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2019**: The Saint-Nazaire facility has already created over 500 jobs, with an additional 200 jobs expected to be added when the factory is fully operational.
- **April 2022**: The construction phase of the Saint-Nazaire offshore wind farm generates over 1,000 local jobs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2019**: GE Renewable Energy operates the facility, which is dedicated to manufacturing offshore wind turbine generators and nacelles.
