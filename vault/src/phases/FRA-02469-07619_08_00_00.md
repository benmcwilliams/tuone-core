### Evolutionary Timeline of the CPP Investments Facility in Saint-Nazaire, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** The Saint-Nazaire offshore wind farm has entered the operational phase. It has a production capacity of 480 MW, which can meet 20% of Loire-Atlantique’s annual electricity consumption and provide energy for approximately 700,000 people. The facility includes an electrical substation and 80 nacelles produced locally.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** The project was developed in partnership with EDF Renewables and Enbridge, indicating significant investment from these companies, including CPP Investments. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2019
   - **Details:** Construction works for the Saint-Nazaire offshore wind farm began.
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** The facility officially began operations.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** During the construction phase, the project employed an average of 2,300 people in France, with 1,200 jobs in the Pays de la Loire region. Post-operation, around 100 people will be employed at the site for ongoing operation and maintenance activities.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** The facility is a joint venture involving CPP Investments, EDF Renewables, and Enbridge, indicating that CPP Investments does not own the facility exclusively but is part of a partnership.
