### GravitHy Facility Evolution Timeline - Fos-sur-Mer, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **2022:** GravitHy was launched as a sustainable iron firm, focusing on the production of low-carbon hot briquetted iron (HBI) from direct reduction iron (DRI) ore pellets.
   - **2024:** The facility is planned to have a production capacity of two million tonnes per year of HBI. The production process will utilize grid-connected nuclear power to produce hydrogen for processing DRI pellets into HBI. The project aims to reduce carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions by over 90% compared to traditional iron-making methods.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **2024:** GravitHy entered into definitive agreements with Rio Tinto for the supply of iron ore, which is crucial for the facility's operations. The project has been designated as an Industrial Project of Major National Interest by the French Government, indicating potential for significant investment and support.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **2024:** GravitHy selected IDOM to develop engineering works for the plant. The facility is expected to be commissioned in **2028**.
