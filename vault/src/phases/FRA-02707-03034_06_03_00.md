### Evolutionary Timeline of the INES Facility in Cadarache, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03/2020**: The INES facility achieved a 24.63% conversion efficiency for a heterojunction (HJT) solar cell based on a standard M2 wafer. This was accomplished through a collaboration with Enel Green Power, utilizing an improved busbar-free screen printing metallization process developed with equipment from Meyer Burger.
- **10/2022**: Enel Green Power and INES installed 36 DC/DC maximum power point trackers (MPPT) at the Cadarache site to enhance energy production. The demonstrator unit has been operational since July 2021 and has shown a 1% energy production gain.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **03/2020**: The collaboration between INES and Enel Green Power indicates a significant investment in research and development for high-efficiency solar technologies, although specific monetary values are not disclosed.
- **10/2022**: The installation of the 36 DC/DC MPPT converters at the Cadarache site represents an investment in enhancing the operational efficiency of solar panels.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones

- **07/2021**: The demonstrator unit for the DC/DC MPPT converters began operation.
