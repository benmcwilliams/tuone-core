### Evolutionary Timeline of the Engie Facility in Central France, FRA

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2022**: Engie, in collaboration with the French National Research Institute for Agriculture, Food and the Environment (INRAE), inaugurated the Camelia vertical agrivoltaic pilot project. The facility features 252 vertically installed bifacial solar panels with an installed power of 89 kW and an expected annual production of 100 MWh. The installation covers 1 hectare of land, with vertical rows spaced 12 to 18 meters apart. The project is designed to study the impact of vertical bifacial solar panels on grasslands and electricity production.
