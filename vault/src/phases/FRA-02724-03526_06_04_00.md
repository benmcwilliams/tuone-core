## FRA-02724-03526

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Soren Facility in Saint-Loubès, France

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: October 2022
   - **Details**: The Soren facility, in collaboration with Envie 2E Aquitaine, inaugurated a solar module recycling facility. This facility utilizes a hot blade delamination process, allowing it to process 4,000 tons of solar panels per year. The technology enables the recovery of 95% of materials used in solar panels, extending the lifecycle of components.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Date**: October 2022
   - **Details**: The facility was established with an investment of €2 million (approximately $1.9 million). This investment supports the development of the first solar module recycling facility of its kind in Europe.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Date**: October 2022
   - **Details**: The facility was officially inaugurated in October 2022. It is noted as the first of its kind in Europe, marking a significant milestone in solar panel recycling.

4. **Labour**
   - **Date**: October 2022
   - **Details**: The facility is expected to employ 25 people, contributing to job creation in the region.

5. **Ownership Structure**
   - **Date**: October 2022
   - **Details**: The facility is a joint venture between Soren (formerly PV Cycle France) and Envie 2E Aquitaine, indicating that Soren does not own the facility exclusively.
