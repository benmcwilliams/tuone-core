### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fonroche Energie SARL Facility in Agen, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2009**: The facility, a joint venture between Spanish manufacturer Instalaciones Pevafersa SL (24% ownership) and French company Fonroche Energie SARL (74% ownership), was established to manufacture solar modules. 
- **End of 2009**: The first production line for crystalline modules was commissioned with an annual capacity of **20 MW**.
- **April 2010**: A second production line was added, contributing to an increase in production capacity.
- **End of 2009**: The total production capacity was expanded to **70 MW**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2009**: The Fonroche Pevafersa consortium invested a total of **€5.5 million** in the initial setup of the factory.
- **Future Expansion**: An additional investment of **€3.5 million** was planned for further expansion to increase capacity to **42 MW**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **2009**: The facility was officially commissioned and began operations with the first production line.
- **April 2010**: The second production line was announced to start, enhancing the facility's output capabilities.

#### 4. Labour
- **End of 2009**: The facility employed more than **200 people** as a result of its operations and expansions.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility operates as a **joint venture** between Instalaciones Pevafersa SL (24% ownership) and Fonroche Energie SARL (74% ownership), indicating that Fonroche does not own the facility exclusively.
