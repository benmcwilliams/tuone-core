### Evolutionary Timeline of Instalaciones Pevafersa SL Facility in Agen, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2009**: Instalaciones Pevafersa SL, in partnership with Fonroche Energie SARL, established a facility in Agen, France, to manufacture solar modules. The first production line for crystalline modules was commissioned with an annual capacity of **20 MW**.
- **April 2010**: A second production line was planned to follow the first.
- **End of 2009**: The facility's production capacity was expanded to **70 MW**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2009**: The joint venture between Pevafersa and Fonroche invested a total of **€5.5 million** in the factory. An additional **€3.5 million** was earmarked for the expansion to **42 MW**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **End of 2009**: The first production line was operational, and the facility was officially commissioned.
- **April 2010**: Announcement of the second production line to be added.

#### 4. Labour
- **End of 2009**: The facility employed more than **200 people** as a result of its operations and expansions.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **2009**: The facility operates as a joint venture, with Instalaciones Pevafersa SL holding **24%** ownership and Fonroche Energie SARL holding **74%** ownership.
