## FRA-03090-03298

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Photowatt Facility in Bourgoin-Jailleu, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2018**: Photowatt, a unit of EDF EN, announced plans to expand its ingot and wafer production capacity from 50 MW to 500 MW. The facility will also produce solar modules, with an existing capacity of around 100 MW. All production will take place at the Bourgoin-Jailleu factory.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09/2018**: The expansion is part of EDF's broader 30 GW solar plan, which is expected to involve an investment of €25 billion. This plan aims to enhance renewable energy capacity in France and is aligned with government goals for energy mix rebalancing.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/2018**: Photowatt announced the creation of a new company, Photowatt Crystal Advanced, to facilitate the expansion of its ingot and wafer business. This new entity will be 60% owned by Photowatt, 30% by Canadian Solar, and 10% by ECM Greentech.

#### 4. Labour
- **09/2018**: The expansion and requalification program will involve all 313 employees at the facility, indicating a focus on job retention and potential job creation as a result of the increased production capacity.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **09/2018**: Photowatt is primarily owned by EDF EN, which is 85% owned by the French government. The new joint venture, Photowatt Crystal Advanced, will have a mixed ownership structure with Photowatt holding 60%, Canadian Solar 30%, and ECM Greentech 10%.
