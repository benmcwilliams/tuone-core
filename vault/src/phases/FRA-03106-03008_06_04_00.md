### Evolutionary Timeline of the Holosolis Facility in Sarreguemines, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Announcement of Production Plans**: In May 2023, Holosolis announced plans to build a 5GW solar module assembly plant in Sarreguemines, France. 
- **Production Start**: Commercial production is scheduled to start in 2025, with full production capacity expected to be reached by 2027.
- **Technology Collaboration**: Holosolis signed a strategic collaboration agreement with Fraunhofer ISE to support the setup of its PV production line, focusing on cost-effective and efficient PV modules based on tunnel oxide passivated contact (TOPCon) solar cells.
