## FRA-03106-03330

### Evolutionary Timeline of the REC Group Facility in Sarreguemines, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2020**: REC Group is considering establishing a **2 GW heterojunction solar module factory** in Sarreguemines, France. This facility aims to produce advanced solar panels, specifically the **REC Alpha solar panel** which utilizes heterojunction technology.
- **July 2022**: REC Solar wins an EU grant to support the construction of the **2 GW heterojunction (HJT) solar manufacturing plant** in Sarreguemines, named the **RISE (REC Innovation at Sarreguemines Enterprise)** project. The facility will primarily produce **bifacial heterojunction modules** and may explore tandem-cell technologies in the future.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2022**: The European Union selects REC Solar for a grant under its second Innovation Fund for large-scale projects, contributing to the funding of the **2 GW HJT plant**. This grant is part of a larger €1.8 billion investment allocated for various clean-tech projects across Europe.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2020**: Initial discussions and considerations for the **2 GW factory** in Sarreguemines are reported, indicating a competitive landscape with Germany also vying for solar manufacturing facilities.
- **July 2022**: The project is officially recognized with the EU grant, marking a significant milestone in the establishment of the facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2022**: While specific job creation numbers are not detailed, the establishment of a **2 GW manufacturing facility** is expected to create numerous jobs in the region, contributing to local employment in the clean technology sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2020**: It is noted that REC Group is owned by **Elkem**, which in turn is owned by **China National BlueStar Corp**, and ultimately by **ChemChina**, indicating that the facility may not be exclusively European-owned but rather part of a larger Chinese state-owned enterprise structure.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic decisions regarding the REC Group facility in Sarreguemines, France, highlighting its technological focus, investment backing, project milestones, potential job impacts, and ownership context.
