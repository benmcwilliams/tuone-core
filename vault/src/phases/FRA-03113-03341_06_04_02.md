### Evolutionary Timeline of the Recom Facility in Lannion, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06/2017**: Recom acquires the Sillia VL facility in Lannion, which had a production capacity of 50 MW. Plans are announced to triple this capacity to 150 MW by the end of 2017.
- **07/2017**: Manufacturing activities resume at the Lannion facility, with a pipeline of new projects totaling 20 MW.
- **09/2018**: Recom announces plans to expand the Lannion facility's capacity to 300 MW by the end of the year.
- **05/2024**: The annual production capacity of the Sillia plant is reported to have been expanded to 500 MW before its closure.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06/2017**: Recom's acquisition of Sillia VL includes plans for modernization and expansion of the Lannion facility.
- **09/2018**: Recom announces a significant investment to increase production capacity in Europe, including the Lannion facility.
- **05/2024**: Recom provides €30 million in financing to increase the production capacity of Recom Sillia from 50 MW to 500 MW.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06/2017**: Recom acquires Sillia VL and announces plans for capacity expansion.
- **07/2017**: Manufacturing activities at the Lannion facility resume after a break.
- **09/2018**: Plans to increase the facility's capacity to 300 MW are announced.
- **04/2024**: Recom Sillia is placed in liquidation, leading to the closure of the Lannion facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **06/2017**: Recom confirms the employment of Sillia’s 44 staff at the factory post-acquisition.
- **05/2024**: Following the liquidation of Recom Sillia, 32 employees receive dismissal letters, with a mention of a procedure for accelerated relocation.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **06/2017**: Recom acquires Sillia VL, thus assuming full ownership of the Lannion facility.
- **05/2024**: Recom Sillia, a subsidiary of Recom, is placed in liquidation, indicating a shift in operational control and ownership dynamics.
