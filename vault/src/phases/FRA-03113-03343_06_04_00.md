### Evolutionary Timeline of the Recom Solar Facility in Lannion, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2017
   - **Details:** Recom Solar, through its Italian unit Recom Italia, acquired the 50 MW production site of the French module producer Sillia VL in Lannion. The facility has a potential order intake of 300 MW, indicating significant production capacity and demand.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2017
   - **Details:** The acquisition of the Lannion facility was part of Recom's strategic investment to expand its manufacturing capabilities in Europe. The financial specifics of the acquisition were not disclosed, but it was a critical move to enhance Recom's presence in the solar module market.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** June 2017
   - **Details:** The acquisition of the Lannion facility was officially announced as Recom was chosen as the preferred bidder for Sillia VL, which had filed for insolvency in March 2017. The acquisition did not include Sillia's other factory in Venissieux.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** June 2017
   - **Details:** Following the acquisition, the 44 employees at the Lannion facility were retained and maintained their jobs. In contrast, the 128 employees at the Venissieux facility received severance payments due to the insolvency.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** June 2017
   - **Details:** Recom Solar, through its Italian unit, acquired the Lannion facility, indicating full ownership of the site. There was no mention of joint ventures or shared ownership in the acquisition details.
