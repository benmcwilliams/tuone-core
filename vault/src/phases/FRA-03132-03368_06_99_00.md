### Evolutionary Timeline of Rosi Solar Facility in La Mure, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2022**: Rosi Solar has developed an industrial solution for recycling end-of-life photovoltaic (PV) modules, capable of recovering high purity silicon, silver, and copper. The facility in La Mure will process laminates without glass to extract these materials. The plant is designed to recycle approximately 3,000 tons of solar panels per year, yielding about three tons of silver and 90 tons of silicon.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2022**: The facility's technology has received support from various institutions, including the French environment and energy agency Ademe and the French national investment bank BPIFrance. The company has also engaged in partnerships with Veolia and European Union programs to enhance its recycling capabilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2021**: Construction of the La Mure facility began, following the selection of Rosi Solar's technology by Sorem in a call for tenders to upgrade end-of-life PV modules.
- **Q4 2022**: The commissioning of the La Mure facility is planned.
