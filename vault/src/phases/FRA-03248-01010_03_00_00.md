### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDF Facility in Hautes-Alpes, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **June 2023:** EDF Group inaugurated the Lazer floating solar power plant, marking it as the first facility of its kind for EDF Renewables in France. The plant combines photovoltaic and hydroelectric production, effectively doubling the renewable electricity production capacity of the existing hydroelectric site. The facility has over 50,000 solar panels with an installed capacity of 20MWp, which is expected to meet the annual electricity consumption needs of approximately 12,500 people.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **2017-2018:** The project began its journey in 2017 and won the Energy Regulation Commission's call for tenders in 2018. A crowdfunding campaign in 2021 raised €179,000 from local residents to support the project.
   - **2021:** Construction commenced, involving geotechnical studies and the installation of anchors for the floating solar panels.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **June 2023:** The Lazer floating solar power plant became operational. The project is expected to operate for approximately three decades.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
