### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sunwind Facility in Hautes-Alpes, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: Sunwind has developed a light, curved solar panel called eV+ specifically designed for use at ski resorts, ski lifts, and high-altitude areas. The panels are 20% efficient, measure 1,020 mm x 2,000 mm, and utilize PERC solar cells. They can be integrated into PV systems with a power range from 7 kW to 12 kW depending on the surface size. The company has solarized a ski lift station at the Serre-Chevalier ski resort in Hautes-Alpes by replacing polycarbonate canopies with these semi-rigid photovoltaic panels, which are five times lighter than traditional glass modules.

- **Future Plans**: Sunwind is working with CEA-Liten to develop its own solar panel encapsulation line, aiming for a production capacity of 40 MW to 50 MW per year. The company intends to produce a 100% European or French product within two years and plans to expand its market to include building facades and photovoltaic noise barriers.
