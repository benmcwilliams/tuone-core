## FRA-03250-03583

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Systovi Facility in Carquefou, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2023**: Systovi ramped up the annual capacity of its module factory in Carquefou, doubling its production capacity from 40 MW to 80 MW. This expansion involved the inauguration of a new assembly line, which allows for the manufacturing of the latest Optymo series of Systovi panels, utilizing M10 cells with outputs of up to 400 W. The new modules are primarily aimed at the rooftop market and feature a thicker aluminum frame for better durability.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2023**: Systovi invested €1.5 million ($1.59 million) in the new assembly line to facilitate the increase in production capacity and improve the quality of its solar panels.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2023**: Announcement of the new assembly line and capacity increase to 80 MW.
- **March 2024**: Systovi announced it is seeking a buyer for its design, production, and commercial activities due to a sharp drop in orders attributed to increased competition from Chinese suppliers and market challenges.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2024**: Systovi's announcement to seek a buyer includes a commitment to preserving employment, indicating potential job security concerns due to the company's financial struggles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **March 2024**: Systovi is identified as a subsidiary of the Cetih group since 2018, indicating that it does not have exclusive ownership of the facility but operates under the umbrella of a larger corporate entity.
