### Evolutionary Timeline of the Next2Sun Facility in Channay, FRA

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is a 240 kW agrivoltaic demonstrator plant located in Channay, Côte-d'Or, Bourgogne-Franche-Comté, France. It utilizes Next2Sun's mounting structure technology for bifacial vertical solar panels. The solar modules for the project were supplied by Hevel Solar, a Russian heterojunction panel maker. This configuration aims to maximize performance during peak times, particularly at dawn and dusk, while also stabilizing the electrical network.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** The project received crowdfunding amounting to €50,000, with 42% of the funds raised from local residents of Channay and Nicey. This indicates community involvement and support for the project.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** The facility was officially commissioned and energized by TotalEnergies, marking a significant milestone in the development of agrivoltaic projects in the region. The project was developed in partnership with Next2Sun, following an exclusive partnership agreement signed in March 2020.
