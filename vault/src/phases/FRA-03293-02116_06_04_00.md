### Evolutionary Timeline of the Trina Solar Facility in Aramon, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2019**: Trina Solar announced the supply of 16 MW of bifacial, double glass Duomax Twin panels to EDF Renewables for two large-scale PV projects in France. The Aramon facility will specifically utilize a 5 MW solar park, which is part of the larger project selected through tenders held by the French energy regulator CRE. The bifacial panels feature monocrystalline PERC cells and are designed to increase overall power generation through front and rear power generation, with an additional energy gain of up to 25%.
