### Evolutionary Timeline of the Trina Solar Facility in Saint Pargoire, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2019**: Trina Solar announced the supply of 16 MW of bifacial, double glass Duomax Twin solar panels to EDF Renewables for two large-scale PV projects in France. The specific project in Saint Pargoire is an 11 MW PV facility. The bifacial panels feature monocrystalline PERC cells and are designed to increase overall power generation through front and rear power generation, with an additional energy gain of up to 25%.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2019**: The projects, including the one in Saint Pargoire, were selected through tenders held by the French energy regulator CRE. The use of Trina's bifacial panels, which comply with a French policy for low carbon footprints, indicates a strategic investment in sustainable technology.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2019**: Trina Solar confirmed the delivery of approximately 45,000 dual-glass bifacial Duomax Twin panels for the projects, with completion expected by the end of the quarter. This marks the first supply agreement for bifacial modules in France for Trina Solar.
