### Evolutionary Timeline of the Urbasolar Facility at Disneyland Paris, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2020**: Announcement of a 30MW solar plant to be developed by Urbasolar at Disneyland Paris, which will include 67,500 solar panels over 17 hectares, generating 31 GWh/year, meeting 17% of the resort's power consumption.
- **April 2022**: A third of the 17MW solar array is completed, with 46,000 out of 86,000 photovoltaic panels installed, producing 10 GWh of solar electricity per year. The final completion is scheduled for 2023.
- **August 2024**: The last section of a 36.1 MW solar carport is commissioned, covering 20 hectares of parking space, with all electricity produced being sold, indicating no self-consumption.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2020**: The project is part of Disneyland Paris's environmental strategy, although specific investment amounts are not detailed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2020**: Construction of the solar facility begins.
- **Spring 2021**: The first phase of the solar carport is expected to be completed.
- **April 2022**: A third of the solar array is operational.
- **2023**: Final completion of the solar carport is expected.
- **August 2024**: The last section of the solar carport is commissioned.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Urbasolar is a subsidiary of Swiss utility Axpo, which purchased Urbasolar in May 2019. The facility is developed under the ownership of Urbasolar, indicating that it is not exclusively owned by Disneyland Paris but rather developed by Urbasolar as part of a partnership with Axpo.
