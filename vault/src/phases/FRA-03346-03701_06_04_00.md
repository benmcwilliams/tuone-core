### Evolutionary Timeline of the Voltec Solar Facility in Alsace, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2020**: Voltec Solar has an annual PV module production capacity of **200 MW** at its factory in Alsace. The facility focuses on manufacturing solar modules with a low environmental impact, prioritizing low-carbon manufacturing processes.
- **May 2022**: Voltec Solar introduces a new bifacial solar module for agrivoltaic applications, maintaining the **200 MW** production capacity. The new module features various levels of transparency and a power conversion efficiency of **13.40%**.
- **November 2022**: Voltec Solar partners with the Institut Photovoltaïque d’Île-de-France (IPVF) to create a solar panel gigafactory as part of the ‘France PV Industrie’ project. The facility aims to introduce **4T perovskite/silicon tandem technology**, targeting a production capacity of **500 MW by 2023**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2020**: Voltec Solar and Systovi announce the Belenos project, aiming for a business volume of around **€250 million** within five years. This project is expected to create several hundred direct jobs.
- **November 2022**: The partnership with IPVF aligns with France's **2030 investment plan**, which aims to boost solar PV manufacturing and reduce dependency on Chinese-made solar equipment.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2020**: Announcement of the Belenos project, indicating a strategic alliance between Voltec Solar and Systovi.
- **May 2022**: Presentation of the new bifacial solar module at the Smarter E fair in Munich.
- **November 2022**: Announcement of the gigafactory project in collaboration with IPVF, with plans to market the new tandem technology by **2025** and scale up to **5 GW** of production by **2030**.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2020**: The Belenos project is expected to create several hundred direct jobs as a result of the collaboration between Voltec Solar and Systovi.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2020**: Voltec Solar is **100%-owned** by the Strub Group, indicating full ownership of the facility.
