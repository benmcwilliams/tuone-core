## FRA-03346-07918

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lithium de France Facility in Alsace, FRA

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: The facility aims to extract "low carbon" lithium from geothermal waters in northern Alsace, alongside developing geothermal energy solutions.
- **Production Start**: Planned to begin production in **2027**.
- **Capacity and Infrastructure**: The facility will require the installation of drilling wells and a refinery to support lithium extraction.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Needs**: The estimated financial requirement for France’s first lithium project is projected to exceed **one billion euros**.
- **Funding Sources**: The company plans to finance its projects through debt and fundraising, leveraging the capital raised from its IPO, which is expected to exceed **150 million euros**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Company IPO Announcement**: On **20-09-2023**, Arverne announced its intention to go public, which will help finance its lithium and geothermal projects.
- **Research Permits**: Arverne has been awarded five exclusive research permits in France for lithium extraction, with additional permits under review.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The company currently employs nearly **150 employees** and anticipates job growth as it expands its operations, particularly with the upcoming projects in lithium extraction and geothermal energy.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: Arverne is the parent company of Lithium de France and holds more than **60%** ownership of the facility. The company is also supported by significant investments from partners such as Equinor and Hydro.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic plans for the Lithium de France facility, highlighting its focus on sustainable lithium extraction and geothermal energy solutions.
