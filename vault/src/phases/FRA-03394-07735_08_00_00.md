### Evolutionary Timeline of Enbridge Eolien France 2 S.à.r.l Facility in Provence, FRA

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2023
   - **Details:** The facility is part of the Provence Grand Large project, which features three Siemens Gamesa 8 MW wind turbines installed on tensioned line floats. The total production capacity of the floating offshore wind farm is 24 MW, which is expected to produce enough electricity to meet the annual consumption of 45,000 inhabitants. The wind turbine components were manufactured at Siemens Gamesa’s factory in Le Havre, and the floating foundations were assembled at Eiffage Métal’s site in Fos-sur-Mer.
