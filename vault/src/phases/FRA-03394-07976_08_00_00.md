## FRA-03394-07976

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Parc Eolien Offshore de Provence Grand Large Facility

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2023
   - **Details:** The facility features three Siemens Gamesa 8 MW wind turbines installed on tensioned line floats. The total production capacity of the floating offshore wind farm is 24 MW, which is expected to produce enough electricity to meet the annual consumption of 45,000 inhabitants. The wind turbine components were manufactured at Siemens Gamesa’s factory in Le Havre, while the floating foundations were assembled at Eiffage Métal’s site in Fos-sur-Mer.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** October 2023
   - **Details:** The project is part of a pilot initiative for floating wind technology, indicating significant investment in innovative offshore wind solutions. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** October 2023
   - **Details:** The project has reached the milestone of completing the installation of the wind turbines, with the last two being towed offshore and anchored. The commissioning of the pilot project is planned for early 2024. An electrical connection is currently being installed to transport electricity to the coast.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** October 2023
   - **Details:** The article does not specify job creation or job loss associated with the project.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** October 2023
