### Evolutionary Timeline of the Neoen Facility in Lot-et-Garonne, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2018**: Neoen is involved in planning a massive 1.2 GW photovoltaic (PV) facility in the Lot-et-Garonne Department, which will be developed on approximately 1,800 hectares of agricultural land across four municipalities: Allons, Boussès, Sauméjan, and Pompogne. The project is expected to be divided into sections ranging from 20 MW to over 200 MW.
- **January 2023**: Neoen announces it will provide power to TDF under a long-term corporate power purchase agreement (PPA) starting January 1, 2026. The anticipated annual production from this project is estimated at nearly 83 GWh.
