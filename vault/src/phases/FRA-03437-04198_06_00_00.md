## FRA-03437-04198

### Evolutionary Timeline of the RECIT Facility in Saint-Joachim, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** March 2024
   - **Details:** RECIT, in collaboration with Brier’energie, is installing solar shades above a cemetery in Saint-Joachim. The installation will cover 8,000 square meters and consist of 5,000 photovoltaic panels, with a total production capacity of 1.3 MW. The project is designed for self-consumption and aims to supply electricity to the 4,000 inhabitants of the commune starting in the summer of 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** March 2024
   - **Details:** The total investment for the installation is €3.35 million ($3.6 million), which will be fully financed by the municipality using tax capital gains from a 7% property tax increase due to inflation.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** March 2024
   - **Details:** The project is currently in the planning and installation phase, with a prototype assembly scheduled to begin on March 18, 2024, covering a small area of 180 square meters to test the integration of the solar plant into its environment.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
