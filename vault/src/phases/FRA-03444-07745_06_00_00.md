### Enercoop Aura Facility Timeline (Southern France, FRA)

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2024**: The Enercoop Aura facility, in collaboration with Araymond and HeliosLite, inaugurated the Coeur de Savoie floating solar power plant. This facility features a 260 kW demonstrator utilizing a new aluminum floating structure designed to minimize its footprint on water and material needs. The floats and anchors were assembled on-site using a mobile micro-factory, which allows for reduced transport impacts and on-site storage needs. The HeliosFloat technology, developed by HeliosLite, is protected by an international patent and marketed under an exclusive 11-year licensing agreement with Araymond. The facility aims to optimize costs for future deployments targeting a capacity of 1 MW, which is four times the size of the current demonstrator.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2024**: Enercoop Aura funded the floating photovoltaics project, viewing it as an opportunity to develop new areas and provide low-cost electricity to consumers through the energy supplier Enercoop. The project aims to achieve a levelized cost of energy between €80 and €85 per MWh for future expansions.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
