### Evolutionary Timeline of the Engie Facility in Châteauneuf-les-Martigues, FRA

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **January 2021:** Engie, in partnership with Total, announced the development of the Masshylia project, which will be France's largest renewable hydrogen production site. The facility will feature a 40 MW electrolyser capable of producing 5 tons of green hydrogen per day, with plans to expand production capacity to 15 tons per day in the future. The facility will be powered by solar farms with a total capacity exceeding 100 MW and will integrate several innovative technologies for hydrogen production and storage.
