### ENOGIA Facility in Dholera, FRA - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **07-05-2019:** The facility is involved in the production of geothermal energy, specifically through the use of an Organic Rankine Cycle (ORC) technique. The initial geothermal power plant is set to have a capacity of 10-20 kilowatts. Two geothermal borewells have been drilled at Dholera, with water temperatures recorded between 47-60 degrees Celsius.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **07-05-2019:** The project is part of a Rs 13 crore initiative funded by the Gujarat government, aimed at drilling wells to depths of 1,500 meters to harness geothermal energy.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **07-05-2019:** The facility is preparing to start operations next month (June 2019) with the geothermal power plant at Dholera. The Centre of Excellence for Geothermal Energy (CEGE) is coordinating the initiative, which includes extensive exploration activities in Gujarat.
