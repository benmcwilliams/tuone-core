### Evolutionary Timeline of the Institut Photovoltaïque d’Île-de-France Facility

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2022**: The Institut Photovoltaïque d’Île-de-France (IPVF) announced a partnership with Alsace-based Voltec Solar to create a solar panel gigafactory focused on the development of 4T perovskite/silicon tandem modules. This technology aims to achieve a 30% efficiency at the photovoltaic module level, significantly higher than traditional technologies. The facility plans to scale up to a production capacity of 5GW by 2030.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2022**: The project aligns with France’s government commitment to boost solar PV manufacturing through a ten-year scaling plan, as part of the France 2030 investment plan aimed at making France a leader in green and renewable energies by 2030.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2022**: The announcement of the partnership and the gigafactory project was made, with plans to market the new technology by 2025 and achieve a production capacity of 500MW by 2023.
