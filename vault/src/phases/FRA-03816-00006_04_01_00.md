1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 07-02-2023
   - **Details**: The ABB facility in Auxerre is involved in the production and distribution of green hydrogen. It features a 1 MW hydrogen station designed to supply hydrogen to five buses of the Leo network (Transdev), as well as light commercial vehicles and trucks. This operation aims to remove 2,200 tons of CO2 emissions from the road each year. The facility utilizes the ABB Ability OPTIMAX for Green Hydrogen energy management system (EMS), which optimizes electrical costs of hydrogen production by up to 16% and enhances the efficiency and safety of electrolyzer modules.
