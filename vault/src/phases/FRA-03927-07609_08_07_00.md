## FRA-03927-07609

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Boskalis Facility in Le Havre, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production**: The facility is involved in the design, construction, and installation of concrete gravity-based foundations for the Fecamp offshore wind farm.
- **Production Capacity**: The Fecamp offshore wind farm has a total capacity of 500 MW, with the foundations contract valued at €552 million.
- **Expansions/Updates**: The foundations will be built at the Bougainville shipyard in the Grand Port Maritime in Le Havre and will involve the preparation of the seabed and ballasting of the installed foundations.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Total Project Cost**: The total capital cost for the Fecamp offshore wind farm is estimated at €2 billion.
- **Financial Structure**: The majority of the financing will be through non-recourse project-level debt.
- **Foundation Contract Value**: Boskalis holds a 19% share of the €552 million foundations contract.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Construction Start**: Construction of the Fecamp offshore wind farm is set to kick off in June 2020.
- **Commissioning Date**: The project is scheduled for commissioning in 2023.
- **Foundation Manufacturing Start**: Manufacturing of the gravitational foundations is set to begin in the summer of 2020.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The construction of the Fecamp project will create over 1,400 local jobs, with 100 full-time positions during the project's 25-year lifetime. The manufacturing of the foundations will provide work for around 600 people, and Siemens Gamesa's new plant in Le Havre will create 750 jobs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Joint Ownership**: The Fecamp offshore wind farm is a joint venture with EDF Renewables (35% ownership), Enbridge (35% ownership), and WPD (30% ownership). Boskalis is a contractor responsible for specific aspects of the project rather than owning the facility.
