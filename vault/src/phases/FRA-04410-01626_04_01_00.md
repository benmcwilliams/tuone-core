### Evolutionary Timeline of the Engie Facility in Houdain-Divion, France

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: June 2019
   - **Details**: The Engie facility in Houdain-Divion is equipped with McPhy technologies, specifically the McLyzer and McFilling systems. It produces clean hydrogen on-site through electrolysis using renewable electricity from certified French sources. The facility has an initial production capacity of over 200 kg of clean hydrogen per day, with the potential to increase capacity by 30% without altering the facility's total surface area. The facility supports the refueling of six hydrogen buses on a new High Level Service Bus (BHNS) line.

   - **Date**: June 2019
