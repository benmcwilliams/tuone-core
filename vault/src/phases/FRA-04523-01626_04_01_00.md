### Evolutionary Timeline of the Engie Facility in Chateauneuf-les-Martigues, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2021**: Engie, in partnership with Total, announced the construction of France’s largest renewable hydrogen production site at Total’s La Mede biorefinery in Chateauneuf-les-Martigues. The facility will feature a 40 MW electrolyser capable of producing 5 tonnes of green hydrogen per day, with potential expansion to 15 tonnes per day. This hydrogen will be used to support biofuel production at the biorefinery and is expected to reduce CO2 emissions by 15,000 tonnes annually.

- **January 2021**: The Masshylia project has applied for subsidies from French and European authorities to support its development. The project is recognized as innovative and of significant interest to regional institutions.
