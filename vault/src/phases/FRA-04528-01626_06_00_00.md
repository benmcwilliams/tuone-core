### Evolutionary Timeline of Engie Facility in La Batie-Montsaleon, France

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **October 2020**: Engie began commercial operation of a 12MW photovoltaic park in La Batie-Montsaleon. The facility is designed to produce 18.5 gigawatt-hours of electricity annually to supply the Val de Durance electrical network. The site covers 22 hectares and incorporates naturalistic design elements, including reseeding local flora between solar panel rows and allowing small animals to pass through fences.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **October 2020**: The total investment for the solar farm is €12.7 million. Tax benefits from the project are estimated at €74,000 per year, which will be distributed among the Sisteronais-Buech communes and the Hautes-Alpes department.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **October 2020**: Engie announced the commencement of commercial operations for the photovoltaic park, marking a significant milestone in the project.

4. **Labour**

5. **Ownership Structure**
