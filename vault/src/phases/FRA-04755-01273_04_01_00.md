## FRA-04755-01273

### Evolutionary Timeline of the SUEZ Facility in Créteil, France

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: February 2021
   - **Details**: SUEZ, in collaboration with SIPEnR, announced plans to construct a green hydrogen production and distribution plant at the Energy-from-Waste (EfW) unit in Créteil. The facility, named H2 Créteil, will utilize an electrolysis process to convert electricity generated from the combustion of household waste into hydrogen. The initial production capacity is set at 500 kg/day, sufficient to fuel approximately 500 cars traveling 100 km daily. The facility aims to eventually expand its capacity to one tonne of hydrogen per day.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Date**: February 2021
   - **Details**: The project is part of a joint venture between SUEZ and SIPEnR, indicating a collaborative investment in the development of the facility. Specific financial details regarding the investment were not disclosed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Date**: February 2021
   - **Details**: The announcement of the project was made on February 3, 2021, with plans for commissioning by the end of 2022. This marks a significant milestone as it is the first green hydrogen facility of its kind in France.

4. **Labour**

5. **Ownership Structure**
