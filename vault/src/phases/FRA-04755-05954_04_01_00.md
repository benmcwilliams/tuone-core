### Evolutionary Timeline of the SIPEnR Facility in Créteil, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** February 2021
   - **Details:** SIPEnR, in collaboration with SUEZ, announced plans to construct a green hydrogen production and distribution plant at the Energy-from-Waste (EfW) unit in Créteil. The facility will utilize electricity generated from the combustion of household waste to produce hydrogen through an electrolysis process. The initial production capacity is set at 500 kg/day, sufficient to meet the needs of 500 cars traveling 100 km per day. The facility is expected to eventually expand its capacity to distribute up to one tonne of hydrogen per day.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** February 2021
   - **Details:** The project is part of a joint venture between SUEZ and SIPEnR, indicating a significant investment in the development of green hydrogen technology in the region. Specific financial details regarding the investment were not disclosed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** February 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is scheduled to be commissioned by the end of 2022. This marks a significant milestone as it is the first green hydrogen production plant in France.
