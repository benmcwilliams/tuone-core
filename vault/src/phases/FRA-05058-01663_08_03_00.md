## FRA-05058-01663

### Evolutionary Timeline of the GE Facility in Montoir-de-Bretagne, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2020**: GE Renewable Energy produced the first nacelle for the 480 MW Saint-Nazaire offshore wind project. This nacelle is part of a series of 80 Haliade 150-6 MW wind turbines being assembled at the Montoir-de-Bretagne facility. The wind farm is located 12 to 20 kilometers off the coast and is expected to be fully operational in 2022.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2020**: The Saint-Nazaire offshore wind project is developed jointly by EDF Renewables and Enbridge, contributing to the local economy and creating over 1,000 jobs during the construction phase. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2020**: Announcement of the production of the first nacelle for the Saint-Nazaire offshore wind farm.
- **2021**: Earthworks are finalized for the storage and assembly of wind turbine components at the port of Saint-Nazaire. The wind turbines will be stored there starting in 2021, with construction activities including the installation of foundations and burying of inter-array cables beginning in the same year.
- **Spring 2022**: Expected installation of the first wind turbines offshore, with a progressive commissioning process lasting until the end of 2022.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2020**: The Montoir-de-Bretagne nacelle assembly plant employs over 500 GE Renewable Energy employees, with an additional 200 employees at the Nantes office. It is anticipated that 200 new jobs will be created when the Saint-Nazaire facility is fully operational. The project is expected to generate over 1,000 local jobs during the construction phase.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **September 2020**: The facility is operated by GE Renewable Energy, which is part of a consortium with EDF Renewables and Enbridge for the Saint-Nazaire project. The articles do not specify whether GE has exclusive ownership of the facility, but it indicates a collaborative development with the other companies involved. 

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the GE facility in Montoir-de-Bretagne, focusing on its role in the Saint-Nazaire offshore wind project.
