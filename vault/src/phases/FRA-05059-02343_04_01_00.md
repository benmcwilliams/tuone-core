### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lhyfe Facility in Port-du-Bec, France

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **October 2021**: Lhyfe begins producing renewable green hydrogen from an electrolyser powered directly by wind turbines, achieving a production capacity of 300 kg of hydrogen daily. Plans are announced to scale up production to one tonne per day. The hydrogen produced will supply four hydrogen refuelling stations, with additional operators in sectors such as haulage, logistics, and industry expected to be announced soon.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **October 2021**: Lhyfe announces a €50 million funding round to support its initiatives, with €30 million allocated to strengthen deployment and R&D teams in France and beyond. This funding is raised from SWEN Capital Partners, Banque des Territoires, and original partners.
