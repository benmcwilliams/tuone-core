### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nantes – Saint Nazaire Port Facility in Saint Nazaire, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2022**: Lhyfe launched the Sealhyfe project at the Nantes – Saint Nazaire Port, which aims to deliver the world’s first offshore renewable hydrogen production prototype with a production capacity of 1 MW. This prototype will be connected to a floating wind turbine. Lhyfe is also planning to develop and operate offshore renewable hydrogen production platforms, with a vision of achieving an additional installed capacity of approximately 3 GW by 2030-2035.
