### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lhyfe Facility in Vendée, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2020**: Lhyfe secures a contract with ABB for the installation of an automation solution at its green hydrogen production plant in Vendée. This facility is designed to produce green hydrogen on an industrial scale.
- **September 2021**: Lhyfe produces 627 kg of renewable hydrogen using an electrolyser powered by a wind turbine, marking a world first for the industry. The facility aims to ramp up production to 300 kg per day and eventually to 1 tonne per day.
- **2022**: The first green hydrogen production facility in Vendée is inaugurated, and Lhyfe begins producing and delivering hydrogen to customers.
- **October 2023**: Lhyfe announces plans to expand hydrogen production, focusing on a combination of renewable sources, particularly offshore wind. The company is deploying modular 100% green industrial hydrogen production plants.
