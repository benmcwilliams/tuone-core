1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Production:** The facility is involved in the manufacturing of monopiles for offshore wind turbines. These monopiles are seven metres in diameter and can reach lengths of up to 68 metres, designed to support the foundations of wind turbines.
   - **Capacity:** The facility is part of the 498 MW Îles d’Yeu and Noirmoutier offshore wind farm project, which will include 61 Siemens Gamesa 8 MW wind turbines.
   - **Updates:** As of November 2024, 17 of the 61 planned inter-array cables have been installed, with a total length of 90 kilometres, essential for electricity transport from the turbines to the substation.
