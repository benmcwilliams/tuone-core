### Exosun Facility Timeline - Landes, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 01-10-2013
   - **Details:** Exosun signed a contract to supply a 12MW solar plant, which will be the largest tracker-equipped plant in France. The facility will utilize 72 of Exosun’s Exotrack HZ trackers and is designed to power approximately 5,000 homes. The plant is part of France’s national solar scheme.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 01-10-2013

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**

   - **Details:** The Langele solar plant is currently in the construction phase and is scheduled to be grid connected by mid-2014. This project marks the fourth for La Compagnie du Vent and GDF Suez to reach construction under the 2012 national solar scheme.
