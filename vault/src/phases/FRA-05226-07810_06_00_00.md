1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 01-10-2013
   - **Details:** The facility, known as the Langele solar plant, is a 12MW solar power plant equipped with advanced solar tracking technology. It is designed to power approximately 5,000 homes and is set to be the largest tracker-equipped solar plant in France upon completion. The plant will utilize 72 Exotrack HZ trackers to enhance efficiency and financial returns.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 01-10-2013
   - **Details:** The Langele solar plant is part of France's national solar scheme, which allocated 105 solar plants to various operators following a tender launched in 2012. The project is a collaboration between La Compagnie du Vent and GDF Suez, indicating significant investment in solar technology and infrastructure.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
