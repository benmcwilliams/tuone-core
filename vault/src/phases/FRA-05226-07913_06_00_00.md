1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 01-10-2013
   - **Details:** La Compagnie du Vent is developing the Langele solar plant, which will have a production capacity of 12MW. This facility will be equipped with 72 Exotrack HZ solar trackers, making it the largest tracker-equipped solar plant in France upon completion. The plant is designed to power approximately 5,000 homes and is part of France's national solar scheme.
