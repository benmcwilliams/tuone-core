### Evolutionary Timeline of the Corsica Sole Facility in Folelli, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06/2024**: Corsica Sole is constructing its first renewable hydrogen production unit in Folelli, Corsica. The facility will feature a 150 kW electrolyzer powered exclusively by photovoltaic (PV) electricity during peak hours. The electrolyzer is designed to produce two tons of hydrogen per year (approximately 40 kg per week) and will be directly connected to the existing 12 MW Folelli solar power plant. The project aims to utilize excess electricity from the solar plant, which has experienced energy losses since its commissioning in 2017.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06/2024**: Corsica Sole is financing the hydrogen production project to introduce hydrogen for heavy maritime mobility and temporary energy applications in the Bastia region. The company aims to limit investment costs by avoiding oversizing the electrolyzer and storage equipment. Future plans include scaling up to larger projects, potentially involving an electrolyzer of up to 2.5 MW with battery storage.
