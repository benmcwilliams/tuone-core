### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lhyfe Facility in Buléon, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-09-2023**: Lhyfe Bretagne, a green hydrogen production site in Buléon, is under construction. It will have a production capacity of up to 2 tonnes of hydrogen per day (approximately 575 tonnes per year) with an installed electrolysis capacity of 5 MW. The facility will utilize electricity from the 13.2 MW Buléon wind farm operated by VSB Energies Nouvelles.
- **15-12-2023**: The Lhyfe facility in Buléon is inaugurated, marking it as the first renewable hydrogen production site in Brittany. It is designed to supply green hydrogen for regional mobility and industrial processes.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-09-2023**: Lhyfe signs a 16-year corporate power purchase agreement (PPA) with VSB Energies Nouvelles for the electricity generated by the Buléon wind farm, which will exclusively supply the Lhyfe Bretagne site.
- **27-10-2023**: Lhyfe is actively seeking to secure additional power purchase agreements with various renewable energy producers to support its hydrogen production initiatives.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07-09-2023**: Announcement of the construction of Lhyfe Bretagne in Buléon, with operations expected to start in late 2023.
- **15-12-2023**: Official inauguration of the Lhyfe Bretagne facility, confirming its operational status and readiness to supply hydrogen.
