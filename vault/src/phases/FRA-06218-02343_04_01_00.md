- **2021**: Lhyfe established a production site in Pays de la Loire, France.

- **2024**: The Brachy wind park, which will supply electricity to Lhyfe, is set to be repowered to a total power of 15MW, increasing its electricity generation capacity by 70% while maintaining the same number of turbines.
