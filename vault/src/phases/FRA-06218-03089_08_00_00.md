## FRA-06218-03089

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Jan De Nul Facility in Brittany, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2024**: Jan De Nul, in partnership with Hellenic Cables, has been awarded a framework agreement by RTE to provide export cables for two floating wind farms in southern Brittany, totaling 750 MW. The facility will be responsible for the design, manufacturing, installation, and protection of three high-voltage alternating current (HVAC) 225 kV cable systems, spanning a total of 150 kilometers. The project includes the embedding of cables in the seabed and the supply of 240 km of onshore cables manufactured in Greece.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2024**: The framework agreement with RTE signifies a significant investment in the development of floating offshore wind energy in France. The engineering, procurement, construction, and installation (EPCI) contract is set to be finalized during the period of 2025-2026, indicating a commitment to substantial financial and resource investment in this clean technology.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2024**: The announcement of the framework agreement marks a key milestone for Jan De Nul and Hellenic Cables, as they prepare to execute the projects under the EPCI contract. The projects are aimed at supporting the development of two floating wind farms, with construction and production expected to commence in the following years.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2024**: While specific job creation or loss figures are not mentioned in the articles, the scale of the project and the involvement of Jan De Nul and Hellenic Cables suggest that the execution of the framework agreement will likely lead to job creation in the region, particularly in roles related to cable production, installation, and marine operations.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **December 2024**: The facility operates as a joint venture between Jan De Nul and Hellenic Cables, indicating that it is not exclusively owned by Jan De Nul but rather a collaborative effort to fulfill the contract with RTE for the floating wind farms.
