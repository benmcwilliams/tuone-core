### Evolutionary Timeline of the Recom Facility in Brittany, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 05-07-2017
   - **Details:** Recom acquired the Sillia facility, which had a production capacity of 50MW/year with three independent production lines for both poly and monocrystalline solar modules. The company announced plans to expand the facility's capacity to 150MW by the end of the year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 05-07-2017
   - **Details:** Recom committed to upgrading the Sillia facility to 150MW, which would involve investments in modern equipment to enhance production capabilities.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 05-07-2017
   - **Details:** Recom announced the expansion of the Sillia facility from 50MW to 150MW, which was part of fulfilling commitments related to Sillia’s 197MW project development portfolio. The expansion was expected to be completed by the end of 2017.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 05-07-2017
   - **Details:** Recom guaranteed the existing commitments made to the Brittany factory’s 44 employees for the next two years following the acquisition of Sillia.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 05-07-2017
   - **Details:** Recom owns the facility exclusively after acquiring Sillia, which had gone into administration prior to the acquisition.
