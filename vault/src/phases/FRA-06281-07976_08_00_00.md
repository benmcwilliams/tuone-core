### Evolutionary Timeline of the Parc Eolien Offshore de Provence Grand Large Facility

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **September 2023:** The facility, known as the Provence Grand Large floating offshore wind farm, comprises three Siemens Gamesa 8 MW wind turbines, totaling a production capacity of 24 MW. The turbines are installed on tensioned line floats designed by SBM Offshore and IFP Energies Nouvelles. The project is located 40 kilometers west of Marseille and 17 kilometers off the coast of Port-Saint-Louis-du-Rhône, in water depths of around 100 meters.
