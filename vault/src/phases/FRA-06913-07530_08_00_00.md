### Evolutionary Timeline of the ABS Group Facility in Leucate/Le Barcarès, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **April 2018:** ABS Group is contracted to provide design verification services for the Les Eoliennes Flottantes du Golfe du Lion (EFGL) floating wind farm pilot project. The facility will produce energy using four GE Haliade 150-6 MW turbines mounted on Principle Power’s WindFloat semi-submersible floaters. The total production capacity of the project is 24 MW, located approximately 17 km off the coast in water depths of 65 to 80 meters.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **November 2016:** The French Environment and Energy Management Agency (ADEME) approved the EFGL floating wind project as part of a tender for the development of floating wind energy in France, which indicates prior investment and planning efforts leading up to the contract awarded to ABS Group.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **April 2018:** ABS Group wins the contract for design verification services, marking a significant milestone in the development of the EFGL project. This includes evaluating the design basis and detailed design of the wind turbine assembly and associated components.
