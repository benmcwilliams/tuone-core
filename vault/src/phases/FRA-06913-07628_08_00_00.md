## FRA-06913-07628

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Caisse des Dépôts Facility in Leucate_Le Barcarès, FRA

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2018**: The facility is part of the Les Éoliennes Flottantes du Golfe du Lion (EFGL) floating wind farm pilot project, located approximately 17 km off the coast of Leucate/Le Barcarès in the Mediterranean Sea. The project has a production capacity of 24 MW, comprising four GE Haliade 150-6 MW turbines mounted on Principle Power’s WindFloat semi-submersible floaters, designed for water depths of 65 to 80 meters.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2016**: The French Environment and Energy Management Agency (ADEME) approved the EFGL project as part of a call for tenders for the development of floating wind, launched in August 2015. This approval indicates a commitment to investment in the floating wind technology sector.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2018**: A consortium including Caisse des Dépôts, ENGIE, and EDP Renewables awarded ABS Group a contract for design verification services for the EFGL project. This contract includes comprehensive evaluations and analyses necessary for the certification of the floating wind farm.
- **July 2018**: The consortium selected Shoreline to provide a simulation solution for the EFGL project, which will assist in various operational aspects such as pre-assembly, installation, and major component replacements.
