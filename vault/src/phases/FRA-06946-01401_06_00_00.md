### Evolutionary Timeline of the Akuo Facility in Cintegabelle, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: Akuo commissioned its second operational floating solar power plant in Cintegabelle, with a production capacity of **8.7 MWp**. The facility is located on the **Cap Vert lake**, a **19-hectare** man-made lake created from a former gravel quarry. This floating photovoltaic power plant is designed to provide local and decentralized renewable energy to over **2000 homes**, avoiding more than **900 tonnes of CO2 emissions annually**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The project received financing from several French lenders, including **Arkea Banque et Entreprises et Institutionnels**, **Caisse d'Epargne Aquitaine Poitou Charentes**, and its subsidiary **Hélia Conseil**, along with the **Energy MAIF Transition fund**. Additionally, Akuo secured a **20-year power purchase agreement (PPA)** with **Octopus Energy** for the electricity generated by the facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: The Cintegabelle floating solar project became operational, marking a significant milestone for Akuo as it is their second floating solar installation in France. The facility was officially inaugurated in **September 2023**.
