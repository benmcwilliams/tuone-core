## FRA-06950-01401

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Akuo Facility in Piolenc, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2018**: Akuo Energy announces plans for a 17 MW floating solar power plant, O'MEGA 1, to be located on a lake at a former quarry in Piolenc. This facility is set to be the largest floating PV installation in France and Europe, utilizing Hydrelio technology from Ciel&Terre.
- **October 2019**: The O'MEGA 1 floating solar plant is inaugurated, featuring 47,000 solar panels across 17 hectares, capable of supplying power to approximately 4,733 homes.
- **January 2022**: A fire incident occurs at the facility due to a short circuit caused by weather conditions. Despite the incident, 15 MW of the 17 MW capacity remains operational.
- **July 2022**: The O'MEGA 1 Bis extension project is completed, increasing the facility's capacity from 17 MW to 22 MW.
- **July 2022**: The extended plant generates a total of 25,500 GWh annually.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2018**: Natixis Energeco agrees to part-finance the O'MEGA 1 project with approximately €12.8 million ($14 million).
- **October 2019**: The project is backed with €12.8 million in debt and €4.2 million in equity, part of which is sourced through crowdfunding.
- **Fall 2021**: A crowdfunding campaign via AkuoCoop raises €1.185 million for the project, reflecting local community engagement.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2018**: Construction of the O'MEGA 1 project begins.
- **October 2019**: The O'MEGA 1 floating solar plant is officially inaugurated.
- **January 2022**: A fire incident occurs, leading to temporary operational challenges.
- **July 2022**: The O'MEGA 1 Bis extension is completed, increasing the capacity to 22 MW.

#### 4. Labour
- **January 2021**: The construction of the Lazer floating solar plant by EDF, which is larger than O'MEGA 1, is expected to mobilize 40 people on-site for 13 months. This indicates a competitive labor environment in the region, although specific job creation or loss related to O'MEGA 1 is not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **September 2018**: Akuo Energy is identified as the manufacturer and exclusive distributor of the Hydrelio product in France, indicating full ownership of the facility. The project also involves local community investment, but there is no indication of joint ownership or partnerships beyond local crowdfunding.
