### Evolutionary Timeline of the GE Grid Solutions Facility in Fécamp, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2020
   - **Details:** The facility is designed to transform up to 500MW of offshore wind energy. It will collect and transform electricity produced by 71 wind turbines of the Fécamp offshore wind farm and transfer it to the shore network. GE Grid Solutions is responsible for the design, manufacturing, and commissioning of high & medium voltage electrical equipment and protection control systems.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2020
   - **Details:** The construction of the Fécamp offshore wind farm electrical substation is part of a consortium project involving EDF Renouvelables, Enbridge, and WPD Offshore. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the article.
