### Evolutionary Timeline of the Global Wind Service Facility in Fécamp, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **April 2023:** Global Wind Service (GWS) was awarded the contract for the complete pre-assembly scope for the 497 MW Fécamp offshore wind farm. The facility will support the assembly of 71 Siemens Gamesa 7 MW wind turbines. GWS's responsibilities include the pre-assembly of tower components and the assembly of the HSBM tool on the nacelles.
