## FRA-06993-05863

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Qair Facility in Gruissan, France (EolMed Project)

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-10-2020**: The EolMed project, a 30 MW floating wind farm pilot, is announced. It will consist of three Vestas 10 MW wind turbines installed on Ideol’s Damping Pool floating foundations, located 18 km off the coast of Gruissan at an average depth of 60 meters.
- **09-03-2021**: Bureau Veritas is awarded a contract for project certification, ensuring reliability and sustainability for the EolMed project.
- **21-05-2021**: BW Ideol signs contracts worth EUR 4 million for engineering and licensing related to the EolMed project.
- **01-12-2021**: BW Ideol acquires a 5% stake in the EolMed project, confirming its role as a co-developer.
- **14-06-2023**: JDR Cable Systems is contracted to supply, test, and terminate the 66 kV dynamic inter-array cables for the project.
- **02-04-2024**: Mooreast secures an order to supply proprietary anchors for the EolMed project, which is set to be one of the first floating wind energy projects in France.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-10-2020**: Total acquires a 20% stake in the EolMed project, bringing financial and technical expertise to the development.
- **14-06-2023**: The project reaches financial close with EUR 85 million in financing secured from the European Investment Bank (EIB).

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07-10-2020**: Announcement of the EolMed project.
- **February 2021**: The project receives full consent.
- **April 2023**: Construction begins with the arrival of the first steel blocks for the floating foundations.
- **Mid-2024**: The project is expected to be commissioned.
- **June 2025**: Installation of the floating turbines is anticipated to start.

#### 4. Labour
- Specific job creation or loss details related to the EolMed project have not been explicitly mentioned in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The EolMed project is co-owned by Qair (majority shareholder), Total (20% stake), and BW Ideol (5% stake). Qair is the primary developer of the project, with Total and BW Ideol as partners.
