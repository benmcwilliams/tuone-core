## FRA-06993-07332

### Evolutionary Timeline of the BW Ideol Facility in Gruissan, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2021**: BW Ideol signed contracts valued at over EUR 4 million for the EolMed project, which includes the installation of three Vestas 10 MW wind turbines on Damping Pool floating foundations. The project has a total production capacity of 30 MW and is located 18 kilometers off the coast of Gruissan. It is scheduled to be operational in 2023.
- **June 2023**: The project will utilize 66 kV dynamic inter-array cables supplied by JDR Cable Systems, which will connect the floating wind turbines to the floating electrical hub. The project is expected to generate approximately 100 million kWh per year, enough to power 50,000 people.
- **April 2024**: Mooreast secured an order to supply proprietary anchors for the EolMed project, which will be used to moor the three floating wind turbines. The project is set to be one of the first three floating offshore wind farms in France.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2021**: BW Ideol acquired a 5% stake in the EolMed project, confirming its role as a co-developer and floating foundation EPCI provider. The project reached financial close in March 2022, securing EUR 85 million in financing from the European Investment Bank (EIB).

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2021**: The EolMed project received full consent.
- **Early 2022**: The project was progressing towards a final investment decision (FID) with construction expected to start soon.
- **April 2023**: Construction of the floating foundations began in Port-La Nouvelle.
- **June 2025**: Installation of the floating structures is expected to start, with final commissioning anticipated in the third quarter of 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- Specific job creation or job loss details related to the EolMed project have not been explicitly mentioned in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The EolMed project is co-owned by Qair, TotalEnergies (which holds a 20% stake), and BW Ideol (which holds a 5% stake). This indicates a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by BW Ideol.
