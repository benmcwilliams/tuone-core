## FRA-06994-07332

### Evolutionary Timeline of the BW Ideol Facility in La Ciotat, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Operations**: BW Ideol is a leader in offshore floating wind technology, with a patented floating offshore wind technology and engineering capabilities. The facility is involved in the design, execution, and development of floating wind projects.
- **Production Capacity**: BW Ideol has two full-scale offshore floating wind turbines operational in France and Japan, with a significant project pipeline.
- **Updates**: The company retains full ownership of its technology and related intellectual property, including the floater EPCI business line and the Floatgen demonstrator.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Announcement**: On July 7, 2023, BW Ideol and ADEME Investissement announced a joint venture for offshore floating wind development, with ADEME Investissement committing EUR 40 million to fund BW Ideol’s project development activities. The initial investment of EUR 17.85 million is expected to be made in September 2023, with further capital injections based on project funding calls.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Joint Venture Formation**: The agreement to create the BW Ideol Projects Company SAS was finalized on July 7, 2023. This involves the transfer of BW Ideol’s co-development project portfolio to the new company.
- **Financial Closing**: The first financial closing is planned for September 2023, contingent upon the completion of the reorganization and necessary approvals.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Retention**: BW Ideol will retain all its teams, including those in engineering, business development, project execution, supply chain, legal, innovation, and finance, as part of the reorganization.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership Details**: Following the first financial closing, BW Ideol will own 75.8% of the newly formed BW Ideol Projects Company, while ADEME Investissement will gradually increase its holding as projects are matured and financed. BW Ideol retains full ownership of its other business activities and assets.
