### MHI Vestas Facility in Mediterranean Sea, FRA - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is involved in the production of floating offshore wind energy, specifically through the deployment of three Vestas V164-10 MW wind turbines. These turbines are mounted on Principle Power’s WindFloat semi-submersible floating foundations. The project, known as the Eoliennes Flottantes du Golfe du Lion (EFGL), has a total production capacity of 30 MW.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The EFGL floating wind project has attracted significant financial backing from various institutions, including KfW IPEX-Bank, Crédit Agricole CIB, Banco Santander, Mizuho Bank, the European Investment Bank (EIB), and the Danish Export Credit Agency (EKF). This financing marks a notable investment in floating offshore wind technology, with the final investment decision reached at the beginning of 2022.
