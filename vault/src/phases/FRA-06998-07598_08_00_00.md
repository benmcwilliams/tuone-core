### Evolutionary Timeline of the Banque des Territoires Facility Off the Coast of Oléron Island, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **05-10-2023:** Ocean Winds and Banque des Territoires announced their joint bid for the 1GW AO7 South Atlantic offshore wind tender. The project will utilize bottom-fixed offshore wind technology and is located approximately 40km off the coast of Oléron island. This facility is part of France's broader strategy to achieve a 2050 target of 40GW of installed offshore wind capacity.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **05-10-2023:** The announcement of the joint bid for the AO7 tender indicates a commitment to invest in the development of offshore wind capacity, although specific investment amounts tied to this project have not been disclosed.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **05-10-2023:** The partnership between Ocean Winds and Banque des Territoires for the AO7 tender marks a significant milestone, extending their collaboration that began in 2014. They have previously worked together on three offshore wind farms currently under construction, which collectively exceed 1GW in capacity.

4. **Labour:**
   - **05-10-2023:** The facility's development is expected to support local employment, with a focus on engaging with local stakeholders, including fisheries, to ensure responsible and sustainable offshore development. However, specific job creation numbers have not been provided.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **05-10-2023:** The facility is a joint venture between Ocean Winds (which is itself a partnership between EDP Renewables and Engie) and Banque des Territoires. Therefore, it is not exclusively owned by either entity but rather operates under a collaborative framework.
