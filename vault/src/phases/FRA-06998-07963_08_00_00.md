### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility Off the Coast of Oléron Island, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 05-10-2023
   - **Details:** Ocean Winds, in partnership with Banque des Territoires, is participating in the 1GW AO7 South Atlantic offshore wind tender. The facility will be a bottom-fixed offshore wind project located approximately 40km off the coast of Oléron Island. This project is part of France's broader goal to achieve 40GW of installed offshore wind capacity by 2050.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 05-10-2023
   - **Details:** The partnership between Ocean Winds and Banque des Territoires aims to leverage their combined experience from previous offshore wind projects to secure investment for the AO7 tender. The specific financial details of the investment for this project have not been disclosed, but it is part of a strategic initiative to enhance offshore wind capacity in France.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 05-10-2023
   - **Details:** Ocean Winds and Banque des Territoires have announced their joint bid for the AO7 tender, marking a significant milestone in the development of offshore wind energy in the region. This follows their previous participation in the AO6 tender and their ongoing work on three other offshore wind farms currently under construction.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 05-10-2023
   - **Details:** The project emphasizes engagement with local stakeholders, particularly in the fisheries sector, to support local employment and ensure responsible and sustainable offshore development. However, specific job creation or loss figures related to this project have not been provided.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 05-10-2023
   - **Details:** Ocean Winds is a joint venture between EDP Renewables and Engie. The facility will not be owned exclusively by Ocean Winds but will involve collaboration with Banque des Territoires, indicating a joint ownership structure for the project.
