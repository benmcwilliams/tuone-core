### Evolutionary Timeline of MHI Vestas Facility in Port-La Nouvelle, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **October 2024:** The facility received components for the Vestas V164-10.0 MW wind turbines, specifically for the Les Eoliennes Flottantes du Golfe du Lion (EFGL) floating offshore wind farm. The components include towers, nacelles, and 80-meter-long blades, which are stored on the quayside for assembly on floating foundations.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **March 2022:** A consortium comprising Ocean Winds and the Banque des Territoires signed an agreement with Euroports for port services during the construction of the EFGL wind farm at Port-La Nouvelle. This agreement includes logistical support for the assembly of wind turbines.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 2022:** The agreement for port services was established.
   - **Beginning of 2023:** The export cable connecting the pilot wind farm to the onshore power grid was installed by DEME’s French subsidiary SDI.
