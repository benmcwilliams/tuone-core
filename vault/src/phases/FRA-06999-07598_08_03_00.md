## FRA-06999-07598

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Banque des Territoires Facility in Port-La Nouvelle, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2022**: The facility is part of the Les Eoliennes Flottantes du Golfe du Lion (EFGL) consortium, which includes Ocean Winds and the Banque des Territoires. They are developing a 30 MW floating wind pilot project in the Mediterranean Sea, consisting of three 10 MW floating wind turbines supported by Principle Power’s WindFloat semi-submersible floating foundations.
- **August 2024**: The last sets of blades for the three V164 wind turbines, manufactured by Vestas, arrive at the Port-La Nouvelle wind terminal. The blades are 80 meters long and weigh 34 tonnes each.
- **October 2024**: Additional components, including towers and nacelles for the Vestas V164-10.0 MW wind turbines, are delivered to the wind terminal in Port-La Nouvelle. The assembly of the wind turbines on their floating foundations is scheduled for late spring 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2022**: The EFGL consortium reaches a final investment decision for the 30 MW floating wind project, setting it on the path to be operational by the end of 2023. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2022**: An agreement is signed between EFGL and Euroports for port services during the construction of the wind farm.
- **March 2023**: Work begins on connecting the wind farm to the national grid by French transmission system operator RTE.
- **April 2025**: The floating foundations are expected to be delivered, followed by the final assembly of the wind turbines.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2024**: Over 300 people are mobilized at Eiffage Métal’s industrial site in Fos-sur-Mer for the construction of the floating foundations.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is developed as a consortium, indicating a joint venture between Ocean Winds and the Banque des Territoires, rather than exclusive ownership by either entity.
