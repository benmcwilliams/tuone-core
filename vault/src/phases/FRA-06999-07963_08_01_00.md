### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility in Port-La Nouvelle, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2022**: The facility is part of the Les Eoliennes Flottantes du Golfe du Lion (EFGL) consortium, which is developing a 30 MW floating wind pilot project in the French Mediterranean Sea. The project involves the construction and operation of three 10 MW floating wind turbines supported by Principle Power’s WindFloat semi-submersible floating foundations.
- **August 2024**: The last sets of blades for the pilot project’s three V164 wind turbines, manufactured by Vestas, arrive at the Port-La Nouvelle wind terminal. The blades are 80 meters long and weigh 34 tonnes each, with a height of 186 meters at the tip once installed.
- **October 2024**: Additional components for the wind turbines, including towers and nacelles, arrive at the wind terminal. The floating foundations are under construction at Eiffage Métal’s industrial site in Fos-sur-Mer, with over 300 workers involved. The floating foundations are expected to be delivered in April 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2022**: Ocean Winds and the Banque des Territoires reach a final investment decision for the 30 MW pilot project, setting it on the path to be built and operational by the end of 2023. 

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2022**: An agreement is signed with Euroports for port services during the construction of the wind farm at Port-La Nouvelle.
- **March 2023**: The French transmission system operator RTE begins work to connect the wind farm to the national grid.
- **August 2024**: The last sets of blades for the wind turbines are delivered to the wind terminal.
- **October 2024**: Towers and nacelles for the wind turbines arrive at the terminal, with preparations for assembly underway.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2024**: Over 300 people are mobilized at Eiffage Métal’s industrial site for the construction of the floating foundations.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is developed by a consortium comprising Ocean Winds and the Banque des Territoires, indicating a joint venture ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by Ocean Winds.
