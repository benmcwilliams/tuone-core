### Boralex Facility in Santerre, France - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2020
   - **Details:** Boralex commissioned the 15MW Santerre wind farm, which consists of seven Vestas V100 wind turbines, each with a power output ranging from 2MW to 2.2MW. This facility contributes to Boralex’s total capacity in France, which reached 993MW, and a global capacity of 2055MW. The wind farm operates under a 20-year purchase contract.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** Late 2019 (refinancing announcement)
   - **Details:** The Santerre wind farm was financed through a credit facility that was part of Boralex’s refinancing of its French assets. Specific investment amounts were not disclosed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2020
   - **Details:** The Santerre wind farm was officially commissioned. The project faced a month-long delay in its connection phase due to the COVID-19 pandemic but was completed on budget.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2020
   - **Details:** The opening of a new maintenance base in Abbeville, La Somme, created six local jobs. The Santerre wind farm will be operated by the crew from this maintenance base.
