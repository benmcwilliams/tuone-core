1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: December 2019
   - **Details**: The Seuil du Cambresis facility features a total production capacity of 24MW, utilizing seven Vestas V117 3.45MW turbines. It is expected to generate approximately 74,500 megawatt-hours of electricity annually.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: December 2019
   - **Details**: The project was financed through Boralex's credit facility, which was part of a refinancing deal announced on November 28, 2019. The facility is supported by a feed-in premium agreement for 15 years, contributing about €4.8 million to Boralex's annualized earnings.

   - **Date**: December 2019
   - **Details**: The Seuil du Cambresis wind farm was officially commissioned in December 2019. This project marked the fifth wind farm installed by Boralex in that year, contributing to their strategic plan to reach 1,000MW of installed capacity in France by 2020.
