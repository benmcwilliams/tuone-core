### Evolutionary Timeline of the Celsius Energy Facility in Ferney-Voltaire, France

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: November 2023
   - **Details**: The facility will utilize geothermal technology to provide low-emissions heating and cooling. It will involve drilling 173 wells to a depth of approximately 230 meters. The system is designed to distribute 20 GWh of heat and 6 GWh of cold annually, serving 195,000 square meters of various infrastructures including offices, business facilities, housing, and public infrastructure.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: November 2023
   - **Details**: The project has received a total investment of EUR 40 million, which includes EUR 30 million from the local public company Terre d’innovation and EUR 10 million from the French Environment and Energy Management Agency (ADEME).

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: November 2023
   - **Details**: The project is described as the largest shallow geothermal project in France and is characterized as a "green field" project aimed at serving newly constructed buildings. Specific milestones such as construction start or production start were not detailed in the article.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: November 2023
   - **Details**: The project is expected to generate approximately 4,500 jobs as a result of its development.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: November 2023
   - **Details**: Celsius Energy is working in partnership with Dalkia on this project, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership.
