## FRA-07076-07671

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Dalkia Facility in Ferney-Voltaire, France

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: November 2023
   - **Details**: The facility is involved in a geothermal heating and cooling project. It will provide low-emissions heating and cooling to 195,000 square meters of office space, business facilities, housing, and public infrastructure. The project will distribute 20 GWh of heat and 6 GWh of cold annually, equivalent to 2,000 hours of heat and 1,600 hours of cold per year. The project is noted as the largest shallow geothermal project in France.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: November 2023
   - **Details**: The local public company Terre d’innovation has invested EUR 30 million into the project. Additionally, the French Environment and Energy Management Agency (ADEME) is contributing EUR 10 million.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: November 2023
   - **Details**: The project is currently in the development phase, with Celsius Energy, in partnership with Dalkia, working on the geothermal heating project. Celsius will drill 173 wells to depths of about 230 meters as part of the project.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: November 2023
   - **Details**: The project is expected to generate 4,500 jobs.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: November 2023
   - **Details**: The facility is a partnership between Celsius Energy and Dalkia, indicating a joint venture rather than exclusive ownership by Dalkia.
