### Evolutionary Timeline of Q Energy Facility in Haute-Marne, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2023**: Q Energy announced the construction of a floating solar project named "Les Ilots Bladin" in Haute-Marne, with a production capacity of **74.3 MW**, making it the largest floating PV project in Europe. The project was initially planned for **66 MW** but was expanded due to design improvements. The facility will utilize **over 134,000 solar panels** and is set to be built on a former quarry site.
- **September 2024**: Construction is underway, covering **127 hectares** of former gravel pits, with **pre-commissioning** scheduled for the **first quarter of 2025**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2024**: Q Energy secured **€50.4 million** (approximately **$55.7 million**) in financing for the floating solar plant. This financing was arranged by **Crédit Agricole Transitions & Energies** and **Bpifrance**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2023**: Q Energy began construction on the floating solar plant, with an expected completion time of **18 months** and commissioning planned for **early 2025**.
- **September 2024**: The project is confirmed to be under construction, with financing secured and pre-commissioning planned for the **first quarter of 2025**.
