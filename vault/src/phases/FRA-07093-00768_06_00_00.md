### Evolutionary Timeline of the Tesla Facility in Giuncaggio, FRA

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04-10-2019**: The facility in Giuncaggio, Corsica, produces solar energy with a capacity of **5 MW** and **7.5 MWh** of storage using **Tesla Powerpack batteries**. The project is part of a larger initiative by Corsica Sole to install **20 MW of solar capacity** and **15 MWh of storage** in the region. The facility is designed to operate independently if necessary and features a power management system developed by Nidec Industrial Solutions.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04-10-2019**: The project received financing of **€20 million** from regional bank Caisse d’Epargne Provence Alpes Corse, French investment bank BPI, and the Eurofideme 3 fund managed by Mirova. This investment supports the development of the solar plant and battery system.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **04-10-2019**: The Giuncaggio facility is one of four planned solar-plus-storage projects intended to generate enough electricity to power **4,000 homes**. The facility was commissioned in the summer of 2019, alongside the **1.5 MW/2.3 MWh Pancheraccia project**. The Giurone project (4.8 MW/7 MWh) was commissioned the previous year, and another **1.5 MW/2.3 MWh** installation is set to be commissioned in the summer of 2020.
