### Evolutionary Timeline of the Corsica Sole Facility in Giuncaggio, FRA

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04-10-2019**: The Corsica Sole facility in Giuncaggio, FRA, features a solar plant with a capacity of **5 MW** and **7.5 MWh** of storage using **Tesla Powerpack batteries**. The facility is designed to operate independently if necessary and utilizes a power management system developed by **Nidec Industrial Solutions** alongside an innovative energy management device from Corsica Sole. The facility is part of a larger plan to install **20 MW of solar capacity** and **15 MWh of storage** within the year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04-10-2019**: The project received financing totaling **€20 million** from various sources, including **Caisse d’Epargne Provence Alpes Corse**, **BPI**, and the **Eurofideme 3 fund** managed by **Mirova**. This investment supports the development of the solar plant and its associated storage capabilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **04-10-2019**: The Giuncaggio project is the first of four planned solar-plus-storage projects intended to generate enough electricity to power **4,000 homes**. The facility was commissioned in the summer of 2019, alongside the **1.5 MW/2.3 MWh Pancheraccia project**. The **4.8 MW/7 MWh Giurone project** was commissioned the previous year, and a fourth installation of **1.5 MW/2.3 MWh** is set to be commissioned in the summer of 2020.
