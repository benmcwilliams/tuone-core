### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDPR Facility in Leucate, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2019
   - **Details:** The facility, part of the Eoliennes Flottantes du Golfe du Lion (EFGL) project, is set to produce 30 MW of floating wind energy using three V164-10.0 MW turbines from MHI Vestas Offshore Wind. This project will feature the world’s most powerful wind turbines in commercial operation once installed in 2022. The project was initially planned as a 24 MW wind farm with four GE Haliade 6 MW units but was updated to the current configuration.

   - **Date:** November 2019
   - **Details:** The project is scheduled for installation in 2022. The assembly of the turbines will take place at Port La Nouvelle, with the turbines to be towed into position more than 16 km off the coast of Leucate and Le Barcares.
