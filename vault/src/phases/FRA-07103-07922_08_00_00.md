### Evolutionary Timeline of MHI Vestas Offshore Wind Facility in Leucate, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **November 2019:** MHI Vestas Offshore Wind was selected to supply V164-10.0 MW turbines for the Eoliennes Flottantes du Golfe du Lion (EFGL) project, which is a 30MW floating wind pilot project in the French Mediterranean. The project will feature the world’s most powerful wind turbines in commercial operation once installed in 2022. The project will utilize three V164-10.0 MW turbines mounted on the semi-submersible WindFloat platform.
