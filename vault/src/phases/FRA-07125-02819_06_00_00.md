### Evolutionary Timeline of the DuPont Facility in Cernay, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **June 2014:** DuPont completed the installation of a 4.5MW solar array at its Cernay facility. This array is the largest of the company's 13 PV installations on its own land and the largest ground-mounted PV site in the Alsace region. The project includes 18,400 JinkoSolar panels utilizing DuPont’s Solamet metalisation pastes and Tedlar polyvinyl fluoride film-based backsheets.
