### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDF Facility in Chinon, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Capacity**: The EDF facility in Chinon operates 900 MWe pressurized water reactors, which are part of the existing fleet of 32 reactors that came into commercial operation between 1977 and 1988.
- **Updates**: The facility is part of EDF's Grand Carénage life extension programme, which aims to upgrade plants to improve performance and extend operational life beyond 40 years. The nuclear safety regulator, ASN, has set conditions for the continued operation of these reactors, ensuring safety for an additional 10 years.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Programme**: EDF announced a total investment of approximately EUR 55 billion by 2025 for the Grand Carénage programme, which was later revised to EUR 45 billion in 2018 and adjusted to EUR 49.4 billion in 2020.
