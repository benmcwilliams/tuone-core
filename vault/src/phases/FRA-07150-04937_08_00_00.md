### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enbridge Facility in Fecamp, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **02/2020**: The Fecamp offshore wind farm, with a production capacity of 500 MW, is announced. The project will utilize 71 Siemens Gamesa 7.0-154 turbines, which will be installed between 13km and 22km off the coast. The project is scheduled for commissioning in 2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **02/2020**: The total capital cost of the Fecamp project is estimated at €2 billion, primarily financed through non-recourse project-level debt. The project is supported by a 20-year power purchase agreement granted by France in June 2018.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **02/2020**: The consortium, including Enbridge, reaches financial close, and construction is set to begin. The project is confirmed to create over 1,400 local jobs during construction and 100 full-time positions over its 25-year lifetime.

#### 4. Labour
- **02/2020**: The construction of the Fecamp offshore wind farm is expected to create over 1,400 local jobs, with 100 full-time positions established for the project's operational phase.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **02/2020**: The ownership of the Fecamp project is shared among the consortium members: EDF Renewables (35%), Enbridge (35%), and WPD (30%).
