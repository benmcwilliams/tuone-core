1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **June 2023:** EDF Renewables commissioned a 20MWp floating solar (FPV) plant at the Lazer dam hydropower plant. This facility combines solar PV and hydroelectric power generation, marking the first of its kind for EDF in France. The project includes 50,000 solar panels and is designed to double the renewable electricity generation at the site.
