### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDF Facility in Mohammed bin Rashid Maktoum Solar Park, FRA

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-11-2018**: The Dubai Water and Electricity Authority (DEWA) announced an expansion of Phase IV of the Mohammed bin Rashid Maktoum Solar Park by an additional 250 MW of photovoltaic (PV) capacity. This phase originally included a 700 MW concentrated solar power (CSP) plant, which will now also incorporate the PV section.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05-11-2018**: The power purchase agreement (PPA) for the project was amended to reflect the new PV section's tariff of $0.024/kWh, marking it as the lowest price ever recorded for a large-scale solar project in the Emirates.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05-11-2018**: Announcement of the expansion of Phase IV to include a 250 MW PV section alongside the existing 700 MW CSP section. The project will feature the world's tallest solar tower at 260 meters and the largest thermal energy storage capacity of 15 hours.
