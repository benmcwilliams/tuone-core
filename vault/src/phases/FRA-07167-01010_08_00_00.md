### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDF Facility in Provence Grand Large, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: The Provence Grand Large pilot project, a collaboration between EDF Renewables and Maple Power, is set to feature floating wind turbines with a capacity of 8.4 MW. The first floating foundation has been loaded out, which will support the wind turbines.
- **April 2024**: The project is confirmed to consist of three Siemens Gamesa 8 MW wind turbines installed on tensioned line floats, designed by SBM Offshore and IFP Energies Nouvelles. The installation of the wind turbines was completed in October 2023, and the project is expected to generate electricity equivalent to the consumption of 45,000 people.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2024**: The Provence Grand Large project is part of a broader strategy by EDF and Maple Power, which includes securing a 250 MW floating offshore wind farm in the Golfe de Fos area. This project is part of the ongoing development of offshore wind energy in France, aligning with national carbon neutrality objectives.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: The first floating foundation for the Provence Grand Large project is loaded out.
- **October 2023**: Installation of the wind turbines is completed.
- **April 2024**: Announcement of the Golfe de Fos 1 project, which is a continuation of the developments initiated by the Provence Grand Large pilot project.
