### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDF Energies Facility in Provence Grand Large, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2019:** The Provence Grand Large facility is identified as a floating offshore wind project, comprising three Siemens Gamesa 8.4MW turbines mounted on a float system developed by SBM Offshore and IFP Energies Nouvelles. The total production capacity of the facility is 25MW. 

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **March 2019:** Prysmian Group is awarded a EUR 30 million contract to develop a turnkey cable system for the Provence Grand Large project. This includes the supply of 3km of inter-array cables, 19km of export cables, and onshore cables for a 9km route. This investment is significant as it marks the first turn-key project for a floating wind farm developed by Prysmian.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 2019:** The project is noted as the first pre-commercial floating offshore wind farm ever developed in France, with commissioning scheduled for 2021.

4. **Labour:**
   - No specific information regarding job creation or job loss related to the Provence Grand Large facility is provided in the available article.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The Provence Grand Large facility is owned exclusively by EDF Energies.
