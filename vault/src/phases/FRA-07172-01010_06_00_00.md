### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDF Facility in Souillac, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 08-04-2021
   - **Details:** EDF Renouvelables announced the construction of a 20 MW photovoltaic solar plant located in Souillac and Lachapelle-Auzac. The plant is expected to produce approximately 25 GWh of electricity annually, which will supply power to SNCF’s unit, SNCF Energie, under a 20-year power purchase agreement (PPA). The project aligns with EDF's solar plan to achieve a 30% market share in France by 2035.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 08-04-2021
   - **Details:** The project is part of EDF's CAP 2030 strategy, which aims to double the renewable capacity of the EDF group from 28 GW to 60 GW between 2015 and 2030. Specific investment amounts for the Souillac facility were not disclosed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 08-04-2021
   - **Details:** Construction of the solar power plant is expected to begin in 2021, with commissioning scheduled for 2023. This marks the initiation of the project under the signed PPA with SNCF Energie.
