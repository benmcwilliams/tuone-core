### Evolutionary Timeline of the Engie Facility in Dieppe - Le Tréport, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2022**: The Dieppe – Le Tréport offshore wind farm is developed by the Éoliennes en Mer Dieppe Le Tréport consortium, which is led by a joint venture between Engie and EDP Renewables. The facility will consist of 62 Siemens Gamesa 8 MW wind turbines, with an expected commissioning date by July 2024. Once operational, the wind farm is projected to produce an average of 2,000 GWh per year, sufficient to power approximately 850,000 people.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2022**: The contract for the supply and installation of the export cable for the wind farm, valued at around EUR 100 million, was awarded to Nexans. This contract includes the installation of 47 kilometers of offshore and 18 kilometers of onshore power export cable, which is essential for connecting the wind farm to the onshore power grid.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2022**: Announcement of the contract awarded to Nexans for the cable installation, with the installation of subsea cables expected to start in the second half of 2024. The commissioning of the wind farm is slated for July 2024.
