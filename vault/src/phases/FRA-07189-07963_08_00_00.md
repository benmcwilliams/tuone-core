### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility in Îles d’Yeu and Noirmoutier, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 07-04-2023
   - **Details:** The facility will produce clean electricity through a 496MW offshore wind farm, generating enough electricity for nearly 800,000 people annually. The project is located 11 km off the Island of Yeu and 16 km off the Island of Noirmoutier. The installation process is expected to start in 2025, with construction anticipated to take 2.5 years.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 07-04-2023
   - **Details:** The project has reached a final investment decision (FID) with a total investment of €2.5 billion. The development is a joint effort by Ocean Winds, Sumitomo, Banque des Territoires, and Vendée Energie through the company Eoliennes en Mer des îles d’Yeu et de Noirmoutier (EMYN).

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 07-04-2023
   - **Details:** The FID marks a key milestone, allowing the consortium to move forward to the construction stage. Initial offshore operations are expected to begin in the summer of 2023, with maritime works including the installation of monopile foundations and construction of the electrical substation starting in spring 2024.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 07-04-2023
   - **Details:** The construction of the offshore wind facility is projected to create up to 1,600 direct jobs in France, significantly contributing to the country’s offshore wind energy sector.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 07-04-2023
   - **Details:** Ocean Winds operates as a 50/50 joint venture between Engie and EDP Renewables, indicating that the facility is not owned exclusively by Ocean Winds but rather in partnership with these companies.
