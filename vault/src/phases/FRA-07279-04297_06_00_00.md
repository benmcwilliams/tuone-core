### Evolutionary Timeline of the TSE Facility in Marville, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The TSE facility in Marville, developed in partnership with Enerparc, has a total installed capacity of 152 MW. It is built on a 155-hectare site that was previously a NATO military airport. The facility utilizes 364,000 PV modules, including bifacial modules, and is capable of generating approximately 160 GWh of electricity annually, which meets about 90% of the annual consumption of the nearby city of Verdun.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** TSE invested €65 million in the project, which included €57 million in debt financing and the remainder financed through equity from shareholders.
