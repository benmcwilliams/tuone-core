### Evolutionary Timeline of GDF Suez Facility in Curbans, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 11-02-2010
   - **Details:** GDF Suez signed a contract to build the largest photovoltaic (PV) power facility in France, located in Curbans. The facility is designed to have an installed capacity of 33 MWp, utilizing approximately 145,000 PV panels. It is expected to produce over 43 million kWh of energy annually and will cover an area of 150 acres at an altitude of 3,280 feet. GDF Suez will be responsible for the construction, operation, and maintenance of the facility for a period of 20 years.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 11-02-2010
   - **Details:** The financial investments for the project will be supported by financial partners EuroFideme 2 (Natixis Environnement & Infrastructure) and SEIEF (Dexia), alongside GDF Suez.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 11-02-2010
   - **Details:** The project contract was signed, and commissioning is scheduled for August 2011.
