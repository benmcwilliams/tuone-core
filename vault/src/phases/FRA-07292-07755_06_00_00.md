### Evolutionary Timeline of the Engie Green Facility in Volgelsheim, FRA

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2022**: The Volgelsheim solar project, with a production capacity of **22 MW**, is under development. The facility will be equipped with **41,472 photovoltaic modules** and will occupy an area of **20 hectares** on a former military site.
- **May 2023**: The **22 MW Volgelsheim photovoltaic park** is inaugurated. The project is co-developed by Engie Green and SAEM Vialis, and it enhances biodiversity through various ecological measures, including sheep grazing for maintenance and habitat creation for local wildlife.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: The solar farm required an investment of **€15 million**. This investment was part of the project secured in the **2019 call for tenders** for the "energy transition of the territory of Fessenheim" by the Ministry of Ecological Transition.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2022**: Announcement of the commissioning scheduled for **August 2022**.
- **May 2023**: Official inauguration of the **22 MW Volgelsheim photovoltaic park**.
