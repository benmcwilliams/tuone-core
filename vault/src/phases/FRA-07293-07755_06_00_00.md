#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2022**: Engie Green, in collaboration with SAEM Vialis, began developing the 22MW Volgelsheim solar project in the Haut-Rhin region. The project will utilize 41,472 photovoltaic modules and occupy a total area of 20 hectares.
- **May 2023**: The 22MW Volgelsheim photovoltaic park was inaugurated. It was built on former military ground and includes biodiversity enhancement measures.
