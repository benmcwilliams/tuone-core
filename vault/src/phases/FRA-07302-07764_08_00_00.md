## FRA-07302-07764

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Eoliennes en Mer Dieppe Le Tréport Facility

**Facility Overview:**
- **Company:** Eoliennes en Mer Dieppe Le Tréport (EMDT)
- **Location:** Le Tréport, France
- **Country:** France

---

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023:** The EMDT facility is set to produce electricity through an offshore wind farm consisting of 62 wind turbines. The facility will supply electricity to approximately 850,000 people annually, which is about two-thirds of the population of Seine-Maritime. The project is expected to be commissioned in the second half of 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023:** The total investment for the EMDT project is approximately €2.7 billion. The financing structure includes international public and private financing led by the Japan Bank for International Cooperation (JBIC) and a syndicate of 16 banks from various countries.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023:** The final investment decision (FID) was announced, marking the official start of construction for the EMDT wind farm. The construction phase is expected to last three years, with maritime work beginning in 2024. The project has been in development since winning a government tender in 2014.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2023:** The construction phase of the EMDT wind farm is projected to create approximately 1,300 direct jobs in France.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2023:** The EMDT facility is a joint venture, primarily owned by Ocean Winds (a 50-50 joint venture between ENGIE and EDP Renewables), along with Sumitomo Corporation and la Banque des Territoires as shareholders.
