### Exosun Facility Evolution Timeline: Porette de Nérone, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 02-04-2012
   - **Details:** Exosun completed the construction of a 3.8MW solar power plant in Porette de Nérone. The facility is equipped with Exotrack HZ solar trackers and is expected to generate nearly 6,235MWh of electricity annually. The plant spans 20.5 acres and includes 39 rows of trackers and 11,817 monocrystalline modules. Exosun is responsible for the operation and maintenance of the plant for two years.
