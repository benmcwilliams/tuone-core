### Exosun Facility Evolution Timeline - Saint-Hélène, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2014:** Exosun signed a deal with Fonroche Energie to provide 12MW of trackers for the 'Le Betout' solar plant in Saint-Hélène. The facility is projected to produce 16,900 MWh of power annually. Exosun will supply and install 81 Exotrack HZ horizontal single-axis trackers, which will support 40,640 solar panels over approximately 850,000 square feet.
