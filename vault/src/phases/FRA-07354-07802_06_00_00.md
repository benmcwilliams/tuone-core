### Evolutionary Timeline of the French Ministry for Ecological and Solidary Transition Facility in Provence Alpes Côte d’Azur, FRA

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-08-2018**: The French Ministry for Ecological and Solidary Transition announced the allocation of 79.5 MW of solar capacity in Provence Alpes Côte d’Azur as part of a larger initiative that included 720 MW across 103 solar projects in the fourth round of a series of tenders for utility-scale solar projects. The projects range in size from 500 kW to 17 MW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-08-2018**: The solar projects selected in this tender are entitled to receive a “complement de rénumeration” under the new incentive mechanism introduced by the Energy Transition for Green Growth Law in August 2015. This mechanism allows PV plant owners to sell power to the electricity market while receiving an ex post tariff to complement their sales.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07-08-2018**: The announcement of the 79.5 MW solar capacity allocation in Provence Alpes Côte d’Azur was part of the fourth tender in a series of six planned by the French government, which aims to allocate a total of 4 GW of solar capacity.
