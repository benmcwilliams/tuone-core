### Evolutionary Timeline of the GazelEnergie Facility at Le Bourget Airport, France

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 06-02-2020
   - **Details**: GazelEnergie, in collaboration with Urbasolar, is involved in the development of solar power plants to supply electricity to Le Bourget Airport, along with Paris-Charles-de-Gaulle and Orly airports.
