### Evolutionary Timeline of the GazelEnergie Facility at Orly Airport, France

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 06-02-2020
   - **Details**: GazelEnergie, in collaboration with Urbasolar, is developing solar power plants to supply electricity to Orly Airport and two other Paris airports (Charles-de-Gaulle and Le Bourget). The solar plants are expected to generate a total of 47 GWh per year, which will cover approximately 10% of the electricity demand for the three airports. The commercial operations of these facilities are set to begin in 2021.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: 06-02-2020
   - **Details**: The project is part of a 21-year Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) signed between Groupe ADP (the airport operator) and GazelEnergie, along with Urbasolar. This agreement signifies a significant investment in renewable energy infrastructure for the airports.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: 06-02-2020
