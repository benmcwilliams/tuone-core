### Evolutionary Timeline of the GazelEnergie Facility at Paris-Charles-de-Gaulle Airport, FRA

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 06-02-2020
   - **Details:** GazelEnergie, in collaboration with Urbasolar, is developing three solar photovoltaic (PV) plants to supply electricity to Paris-Charles-de-Gaulle Airport, along with Orly and Le Bourget airports. The combined expected output of these plants is 47 GWh per year, which will cover approximately 10% of the electricity demand for the three airports. The solar plants are set to begin commercial operations in 2021.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 06-02-2020
   - **Details:** The project is part of a 21-year Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) signed between Groupe ADP (the airport operator) and GazelEnergie, along with Urbasolar.
