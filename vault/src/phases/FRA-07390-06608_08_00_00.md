### Evolutionary Timeline of the Principle Power Facility in Leucate-Le Barcarès, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is part of the Eoliennes Flottantes du Golfe du Lion (EFGL) floating wind farm, which will consist of three 10 MW Vestas wind turbines installed on WindFloat foundations developed by Principle Power. The total production capacity of the pilot project is 30 MW. The wind farm is scheduled to be commissioned at the end of 2023 and will operate for 20 years.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The project reached its final investment decision at the beginning of 2022, allowing the developers to sign contracts with industrial and financial partners. The financing for the project involves KfW IPEX-Bank, Crédit Agricole CIB, Banco Santander, Mizuho Bank, the European Investment Bank (EIB), and the Danish Export Credit Agency (EKF).

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **June 2022:** The project entered the construction phase after signing all contracts for financing. Global Maritime was awarded the Marine Warranty contract for the construction phase, which includes foundations, wind turbines, and cables. The project is scheduled to be commissioned by the end of 2023.
