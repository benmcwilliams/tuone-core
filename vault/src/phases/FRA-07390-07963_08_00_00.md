### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility in Leucate-Le Barcarès, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is developing the Eoliennes Flottantes du Golfe du Lion (EFGL) floating wind farm, which will consist of three 10 MW Vestas wind turbines installed on WindFloat foundations. The total production capacity of the facility is 30 MW. The wind farm is scheduled to be commissioned at the end of 2023 and will operate for 20 years.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The project reached its final investment decision at the beginning of 2022, allowing the developers to sign contracts with industrial and financial partners. The financing for the project involves KfW IPEX-Bank, Crédit Agricole CIB, Banco Santander, S.A., Mizuho Bank, European Investment Bank (EIB), and the Danish Export Credit Agency (EKF).

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **June 2022:** The project entered the construction phase after signing all contracts for financing. Global Maritime was awarded the Marine Warranty contract for the construction phase, which includes foundations, wind turbines, and cables. The project is scheduled to be commissioned by the end of 2023.

4. **Labour:**
   - The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is developed by Ocean Winds in partnership with Banque des Territoires (groupe Caisse des Dépôts). Therefore, it is not under exclusive ownership by Ocean Winds but rather a joint venture with Banque des Territoires.
