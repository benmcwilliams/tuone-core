### Evolutionary Timeline of Haizea Breizh Facility in Port of Brest, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2022**: Haizea Breizh, a subsidiary of Haizea Wind Group, began assembling internal components for 62 wind turbine towers intended for the Saint-Brieuc offshore wind farm. The assembly of components such as platforms, cables, and ladders is conducted at the new workshop in the Port of Brest, which was under construction at that time. The towers themselves are manufactured and painted at Haizea's factory in the Port of Bilbao, Spain.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2022**: The establishment of the new workshop in Brest indicates a significant investment in the facility to support the assembly operations for the offshore wind farm project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2022**: Haizea Breizh began working on the polder in Brest to install the new workshop, which is expected to be operational by mid-August 2022. The assembly of the wind turbine towers is part of the larger Saint-Brieuc offshore wind farm project, which is scheduled for completion in 2023.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2022**: As part of the assembly operations, Aguado Wind France is set to train local employees at the new site, indicating job creation and skill development for the local workforce.
