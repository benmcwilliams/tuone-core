### Evolutionary Timeline of the Solvay Facility in Tavaux, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2022**: Solvay announced a partnership with Iberdrola to develop a solar project that will produce approximately **75 GWh of electricity** annually. This facility will be one of the largest solar installations in Europe, utilizing **172 hectares of land**, with **77 hectares** dedicated to the solar facility itself, comprising around **100,000 photovoltaic modules**. The project aims to provide **60% of the green electricity** produced to Solvay’s industrial sites in Tavaux and Saint Fons.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2022**: The solar project is part of a **20-year corporate power purchase agreement** between Solvay and Iberdrola, indicating a significant investment in renewable energy infrastructure to support Solvay’s operations.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Expected Operation Date**: The solar facility is anticipated to be operational by the **end of 2025**. This timeline marks a significant milestone in Solvay's commitment to reducing its energy footprint and advancing its sustainability goals.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
