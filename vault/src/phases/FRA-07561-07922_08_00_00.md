### Evolutionary Timeline of MHI Vestas Offshore Wind Facility in Le Barcares, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2019
   - **Details:** MHI Vestas Offshore Wind was selected to supply V164-10.0 MW turbines for the Eoliennes Flottantes du Golfe du Lion (EFGL) project, a 30MW floating wind pilot project in the French Mediterranean. The project will feature the world’s most powerful wind turbines in commercial operation, with three V164-10.0 MW turbines installed atop the semi-submersible WindFloat platform.
