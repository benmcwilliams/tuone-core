### Evolutionary Timeline of the Neoen Facility in Goyder South Wind Farm, South Australia

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2023:** The Goyder South wind farm, developed and owned by Neoen, is equipped with 75 wind turbines and has a total production capacity of 412 MW. This facility is part of the larger Goyder Renewables Zone project, which has approval for a total of 1.2 GW of wind power, 600 MW of solar, and 900 MW of battery storage capacity.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **March 2023:** The construction of the first phase of the Goyder South wind farm has begun, with ElecnorGroup participating in the construction alongside GE Renewable Energy. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 2023:** Construction work on the first stage of the Goyder South wind farm has commenced. The project is part of Neoen's broader Goyder Renewables Zone initiative.

4. **Labour:**
   - **March 2023:** The construction phase of the Goyder South wind farm is expected to create over 400 jobs. Once operational, the facility will provide an additional 12 full-time positions.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **March 2023:** Neoen is identified as the owner and long-term operator of the Goyder South wind farm, indicating full ownership of the facility.
