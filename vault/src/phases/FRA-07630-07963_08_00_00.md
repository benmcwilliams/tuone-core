### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility in Dieppe Le Tréport, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: Ocean Winds, in partnership with Sumitomo Corporation and Banque des Territoires, reached a final investment decision (FID) for the Dieppe Le Tréport offshore wind project, which will have a production capacity of **496 MW**. The project will consist of **62 wind turbines** and is expected to be commissioned in **2026**. The wind farm will be located **15 km off Tréport** and **17 km off Dieppe**.
- **2024**: Maritime work will commence with the installation of monopiles.
- **2025**: Installation of jackets and the electrical substation will take place.
- **2026**: The wind turbines, produced by Siemens Gamesa, will be installed, and the construction phase is expected to create **1,300 direct jobs** in France.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: The total investment for the Dieppe Le Tréport project is **€2.7 billion**, supported by a syndicate of **16 banks**, led by the Japan Bank for International Cooperation (JBIC). This financing is structured through shareholders backed financing and non-recourse debt.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023**: Final investment decision (FID) announced for the Dieppe Le Tréport offshore wind project.
- **2023**: The project was a winner in the 2014 call for tenders launched by the French state in 2013 and has been in development since then.
- **2024-2026**: Timeline established for various construction phases, including maritime work, installation of foundations, and turbine installation.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2023**: The construction phase of the wind farm is projected to create **1,300 direct jobs** in France.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Ocean Winds is a **joint venture** between **EDP Renewables** and **Engie**, indicating that it does not own the facility exclusively but rather shares ownership with its partners. The partnership with Banque des Territoires for the project further emphasizes the collaborative nature of the ownership structure.
