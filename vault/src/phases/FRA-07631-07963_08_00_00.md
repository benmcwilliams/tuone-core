### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility in Golfe du Lion, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Projects**: Ocean Winds is involved in multiple offshore wind projects, including the Dieppe Le Tréport, Îles d’Yeu and Noirmoutier fixed-bottom offshore wind farms, and the Éoliennes Flottantes du Golfe du Lion (EFGL) floating wind pilot project. 
- **Capacity**: The combined capacity of these projects exceeds 1 GW. The facility is also participating in the AO7 South Atlantic offshore wind tender for a new 1 GW wind farm off the Ile d’Oléron, which is expected to be announced in 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Joint Ventures**: Ocean Winds has partnered with Banque des Territoires to bid for the AO7 tender, which aims to enhance offshore wind capacity in France. The partnership is a continuation of their collaboration on existing projects and reflects a commitment to local development and sustainability.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Tender Participation**: In October 2023, Ocean Winds and Banque des Territoires announced their joint bid for the 1 GW AO7 South Atlantic offshore wind tender, with the winner expected to be announced in 2024. 
- **Current Projects Under Construction**: The Dieppe Le Tréport and Îles d’Yeu and Noirmoutier projects are currently under construction, alongside the EFGL pilot project.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The partnership emphasizes the importance of local job creation and community engagement, particularly in relation to the fisheries and local stakeholders, as part of their commitment to responsible and sustainable offshore development.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Joint Venture**: Ocean Winds is a joint venture between EDP Renewables and Engie, indicating that the facility is not owned exclusively by one company but rather through a collaborative partnership.
