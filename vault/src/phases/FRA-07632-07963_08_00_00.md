### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility in Ile d’Oléron, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2023**: Ocean Winds, in partnership with Banque des Territoires, is involved in the French AO7 South Atlantic offshore wind tender for a proposed 1 GW wind farm off the Ile d’Oléron. This project is part of a competitive dialogue process initiated by the French government for offshore wind development.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2023**: The partnership with Banque des Territoires aims to enhance local supply chains and create job opportunities through their joint experience in offshore wind projects. Specific investment amounts or subsidies related to the AO7 project have not been disclosed, but the focus is on promoting local opportunities and fostering a sustainable industry.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2022**: The AO7 South Atlantic tender was launched, with the winner expected to be announced in 2024. Ocean Winds is a prequalified developer for this project.
- **04/2023**: Ocean Winds and Banque des Territoires are already partners on three offshore wind projects under construction, which include the Dieppe Le Tréport and Îles d’Yeu and Noirmoutier fixed-bottom offshore wind farms, as well as the Éoliennes Flottantes du Golfe du Lion floating wind pilot project.

#### 4. Labour
- **04/2023**: The partnership emphasizes the creation of local job opportunities and engagement with local stakeholders, particularly in the fisheries sector, to support responsible and sustainable offshore development.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **04/2023**: Ocean Winds and Banque des Territoires are collaborating on the AO7 project, indicating a joint venture approach for this facility. There is no indication of exclusive ownership by Ocean Winds, as they are working in partnership with Banque des Territoires.

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic focus of the Ocean Winds facility in Ile d’Oléron, highlighting its commitment to local engagement and sustainable offshore wind energy development.
