### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility in Noirmoutier, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Projects**: Ocean Winds is involved in the development of the Noirmoutier bottom-fixed offshore wind farm, part of a larger initiative that includes the Dieppe Le Tréport and Îles d’Yeu projects. Collectively, these projects have a capacity exceeding 1 GW.
- **Future Plans**: Ocean Winds is participating in the AO7 South Atlantic offshore wind tender for a new 1 GW wind farm off the Ile d’Oléron, with the winner expected to be announced in 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Joint Ventures**: Ocean Winds has partnered with Banque des Territoires for the development of the Noirmoutier project and other offshore wind farms. This partnership is aimed at enhancing local supply chains and promoting sustainable industry practices in France.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Current Status**: As of October 2023, Ocean Winds and Banque des Territoires are actively working on three offshore wind projects under construction, including the Noirmoutier facility.
- **Tender Participation**: In October 2023, Ocean Winds announced its joint bid with Banque des Territoires for the AO7 South Atlantic offshore wind tender, which was launched in December 2022.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation Focus**: Ocean Winds emphasizes its commitment to local job creation and engagement with local stakeholders, particularly in the fisheries sector, to ensure responsible and sustainable offshore development.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Joint Venture**: Ocean Winds is a joint venture between EDP Renewables and Engie, indicating that the facility is not owned exclusively by one entity but rather through this partnership structure.
