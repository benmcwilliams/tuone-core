## FRA-07637-07963

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility in Îles d'Yeu and Noirmoutier, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is developing a 496MW offshore wind farm located 11km off the island of Yeu and 16km off the island of Noirmoutier. 
   - The project involves the construction and installation of wind turbines produced by Siemens Gamesa at their Le Havre facility.
   - The first offshore operations are scheduled to begin in summer 2023, with installation operations planned for 2024 and 2025. The site is expected to be commissioned in the second half of 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - A total investment of approximately €2.5 billion has been secured for the project, with financial close reached on April 5, 2023.
   - The financing is backed by a consortium including Ocean Winds, Sumitomo Corporation, Banque des Territoires, and Vendée Energie, along with support from the Japan Bank for International Cooperation (JBIC) and a syndicate of 16 banks.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **April 2023:** Final investment decision reached, allowing the project to progress to the construction phase.
   - **Spring 2024:** Maritime work will commence, including the installation of monopile foundations and laying of submarine cables.
   - **2024-2025:** Effective start of installation operations, with wind turbines expected to be installed in 2025.
   - **Second half of 2025:** Expected commissioning of the wind farm.

4. **Labour:**
   - The articles do not specify job creation or job loss directly associated with the investments or expansions at this facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is not owned exclusively by Ocean Winds; it is part of a consortium that includes Sumitomo Corporation, Banque des Territoires, and Vendée Energie, operating under the development company Eoliennes en Mer des îles d'Yeu et de Noirmoutier (EMYN).
