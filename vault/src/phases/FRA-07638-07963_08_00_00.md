### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility in Îles d’Yeu, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Production Capacity**: The facility is part of the broader offshore wind projects, including the Îles d’Yeu wind farm, which is one of three projects under construction with a combined capacity of over 1 GW.
- **Updates**: Ocean Winds is involved in the development of the Dieppe Le Tréport, Îles d’Yeu, and Noirmoutier bottom-fixed offshore wind farms, as well as the Éoliennes Flottantes du Golfe du Lion floating wind pilot project.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Joint Ventures**: Ocean Winds has partnered with Banque des Territoires for the French AO7 South Atlantic offshore wind tender, which aims to develop a 1 GW wind farm off the Ile d’Oléron. This partnership is an extension of their collaboration on existing projects.
- **Future Plans**: The AO7 tender was launched in December 2022, with the winner expected to be announced in 2024. The French government aims to reach a target of 40 GW of installed offshore wind capacity by 2050.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Current Projects**: As of October 2023, Ocean Winds and Banque des Territoires are actively working on three offshore wind farms under construction: Dieppe Le Tréport, Îles d’Yeu, and Noirmoutier.
- **Tender Participation**: Ocean Winds is participating in the AO6 tender for two floating wind sites in the French Mediterranean, each with a planned capacity of about 250 MW, potentially leading to a total capacity of 1.5 GW.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The partnership between Ocean Winds and Banque des Territoires emphasizes the importance of local job creation and community engagement, particularly in relation to the fisheries and local stakeholders, as they develop the offshore wind projects.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Joint Venture**: Ocean Winds is a joint venture between EDP Renewables and Engie, indicating that the facility is not owned exclusively by one company but rather through this collaborative structure.

### Summary
The Ocean Winds facility in Îles d’Yeu is part of a significant offshore wind initiative in France, with ongoing projects that contribute to the country's renewable energy goals. The collaboration with Banque des Territoires highlights a commitment to local engagement and sustainable development, while the joint venture ownership structure reflects a strategic partnership aimed at enhancing offshore wind capacity in the region.
