### Evolutionary Timeline of the Oil'Ive Green Facility in Villemoustaussou, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: The Oil'Ive Green facility aims to cultivate olive trees for the production of edible oil and biofuels.
- **Production Capacity Goal**: The facility plans to cultivate 50,000 hectares of olive trees by the end of the decade (2030).
- **Innovative Approach**: The facility will implement agrivoltaic power plants on solar trackers to enhance olive cultivation, providing protection against climate-related hazards and improving productivity.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Partnership**: Oil'Ive Green has formed an exclusive partnership with Iberdrola, a Spanish energy producer, to develop agrivoltaic solutions that will support olive growing and diversify wine-growing areas.
- **Investment in Agrivoltaics**: The agrivoltaic power plants will be specifically designed to adapt to olive growing, which is expected to generate economic benefits and support the sustainability of the agricultural sector.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Announcement Date**: The partnership and project details were announced on June 12, 2023.
- **Implementation Timeline**: The agrivoltaic projects will be identified and developed in various regions, including New Aquitaine, Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes, Provence-Alpes-Côte d’Azur, and Occitanie, with the aim of starting production from the third year of operation.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation Potential**: The agrivoltaic plants are expected to provide technical and agronomic assistance to wine growers, which may lead to job creation in the agricultural sector as operators develop and diversify their activities.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: The facility is part of the Oil'Ive Green network of producers, which is collaborating exclusively with Iberdrola for the agrivoltaic project, indicating a partnership model rather than full ownership by a single entity.
