#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2019**: The Gron facility is involved in the production of land cables for the Provence Grand Large floating offshore wind farm project. The facility is responsible for manufacturing 9km of land cables as part of a €30m turnkey contract for EDF Renouvelables.
- **November 2020**: The Gron plant is again highlighted for its role in producing 15km of land cables for the 1.4GW Sofia offshore wind farm project in the UK. The cables will utilize 100% recyclable P-Laser insulation.
