### Evolutionary Timeline of the Prysmian Group Facility in Montereau, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03/2023**: Prysmian Group has been awarded a contract to supply inter-array submarine cables for the Hornsea 3 offshore wind farm in the UK. The project involves approximately 280 kilometers of 66 kV inter-array cables with XLPE insulation, which will cover about 50% of the overall wind farm’s requirements. All cable cores will be produced at Prysmian’s centres of excellence located in Montereau and Gron, France.
