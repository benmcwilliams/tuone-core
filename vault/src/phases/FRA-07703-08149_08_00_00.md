### Evolutionary Timeline of the Valorem Facility in South Atlantic French Coast, FRA

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is involved in offshore wind energy production, specifically participating in the AO7 South Atlantic offshore wind tender in France. The project will utilize bottom-fixed offshore wind technology and is located approximately 40km off the coast of Oléron island. The overall goal is to contribute to France's target of achieving 40GW of installed offshore wind capacity by 2050.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - In August 2023, Valorem announced plans to jointly participate in the AO7 tender alongside RWE.
