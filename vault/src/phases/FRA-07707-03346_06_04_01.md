### Evolutionary Timeline of the Reden Solar Facility in Roquefort, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2024**: Reden Solar inaugurated a new 200 MW solar module production line at its facility in Roquefort, replacing an outdated 65 MW production line that had been in operation for 15 years. The new line features advancements such as 166 mm half-cells and 10 to 16 busbar connection points, moving away from the previous 72-cell modules with five busbars. The facility produces mono passivated emitter and rear cell (PERC) panels with efficiencies exceeding 21.7%. The production capacity is up to 300,000 panels per year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2024**: Reden Solar invested €4 million (approximately $4.2 million) in the new production line equipment, which includes machinery designed by Spanish manufacturer Mondragon and recustomized machinery from Eastern countries.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2024**: The new production line was inaugurated, marking a significant upgrade in the facility's capabilities and technology.

#### 4. Labour
