### Evolutionary Timeline of the Reden Facility in Roquefort, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06/2024**: Reden launched a module assembly line with an annual nameplate capacity of **200MW**, significantly increasing its previous capacity of **60MW**. The facility produces **Passivated Emitter and Rear Contact (PERC)** modules, chosen over the dominant **tunnel oxide passivated contact (TOPCon)** technology due to reliability concerns and the company's familiarity with glass backsheet technology. The modules produced will primarily be used for **agrivoltaics (agriPV)** applications, which constitute **90%** of Reden's portfolio.

- **06/2024**: Announcement of the launch of the new module assembly line with a capacity of **200MW**. The facility aims to integrate the entire value chain of being an independent power producer (IPP), including project development, operation, maintenance, and module production.
