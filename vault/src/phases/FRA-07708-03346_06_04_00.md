### Evolutionary Timeline of the Reden Solar Facility in Roquefort, Lot-et-Garonne, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2024**: Reden Solar inaugurated a new 200 MW solar module production line at its facility in Roquefort. This line has the capacity to produce up to 300,000 solar modules per year, primarily for the company's own renewable electricity projects. The new line replaces an outdated 65 MW production line that had been in operation for 15 years. The updated technology includes advancements such as 166 mm half-cells and 10 to 16 busbar connection points, improving efficiency and performance.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2024**: The company invested €4 million (approximately $4.2 million) in the new production line equipment, which includes machinery designed by Spanish manufacturer Mondragon and recustomized machinery from Eastern countries.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2024**: The new production line was officially inaugurated, marking a significant milestone in the facility's operational capabilities.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2024**: The new production line requires about 10 employees to operate, contributing to job creation at the facility.
