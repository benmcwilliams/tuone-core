### Evolutionary Timeline of the Rhodia Facility in Belle-Etoile, France

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2010**: Rhodia announced plans to install 12,000m² of photovoltaic (PV) panels at the Belle-Etoile site. The project will have a peak capacity of 2MW and is expected to produce two million kilowatt hours of electricity annually. The solar panels will be integrated into the roof design of all warehouses at the facility, making it one of France's largest industrial rooftop solar power projects.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2010**: The project is a partnership between Rhodia, Solairedirect (the solar module manufacturer), and OFI Asset Management. Specific investment amounts were not disclosed in the articles, but the collaboration indicates a significant financial commitment to the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2010**: Construction of the 2MW solar project is set to begin.
- **Late 2011**: The project is scheduled for completion.
