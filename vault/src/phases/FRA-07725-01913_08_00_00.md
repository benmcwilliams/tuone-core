### Evolutionary Timeline of the SSE Facility in Chaintrix-Bierges, FRA

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2023
   - **Details:** SSE Renewables has broken ground on the 27MW Chaintrix-Bierges and Vélye Wind Farm, which is SSE's first onshore wind farm in France. The facility will consist of eight Siemens Gamesa SG 3.4-132 wind turbines, each with a production capacity of 3.4MW. The project is expected to generate enough low-carbon energy to power over 14,000 homes annually over its 35-year lifetime.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2023
   - **Details:** The total capital investment for the Chaintrix-Bierges project exceeds €30 million. This investment is aimed at supporting the local economy in the Grand-Est region of France.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2023
   - **Details:** The project has officially entered the construction phase, with a target commissioning date set for the end of 2024. The project has been in development for approximately eight years.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
