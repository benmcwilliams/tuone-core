### Summary
The TotalEnergies facility in Rabigh 2 is a 300 MW solar PV project that is currently in the bidding phase of the Saudi Arabian government's renewable energy program. The facility is part of a joint venture with Al Jomaih Energy and Water Company and KEPCO, reflecting a collaborative investment approach. The project is actively seeking to secure its place in the competitive landscape of renewable energy in Saudi Arabia, as evidenced by its recent bid submission.
