### Evolutionary Timeline of the Voltalia Facility in Taconnaz, France

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **November 2019:** The Taconnaz facility, a 4.5 MW run-of-the-river hydropower plant, has produced its first kilowatt hours. The plant is designed to operate without a dam, minimizing visual impact and greenhouse gas emissions. It is expected to supply power to approximately 10,400 inhabitants, covering 90% of the combined population of Chamonix and Les Houches.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The facility benefits from a 20-year contract with the Régie Électrique des Houches, ensuring a stable revenue stream. The project reflects a collaboration with local authorities, as the municipalities of Chamonix and Les Houches hold a 33% stake in the project, sharing in the value created through dividends.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **November 2019:** The facility has completed construction after one year and three months of work, with final commissioning occurring before the winter season. The plant is set to reach full production capacity in spring 2020.
