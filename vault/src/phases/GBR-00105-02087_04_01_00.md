### H2SITE Facility Evolution Timeline - Birmingham, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2022
   - **Details:** H2SITE is developing a facility in Birmingham to convert ammonia into hydrogen. The pilot plant is designed to produce 200 kg of hydrogen per day, which will supply an existing hydrogen refueling station. The facility aims to create the most efficient ammonia-to-hydrogen conversion unit in the world.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2022
   - **Details:** The initiative is financed with €7.8 million ($8.2 million) from the UK Department of Business, Energy and Industrial Strategy. This funding is part of a broader commitment to achieve a hydrogen production capacity of up to 10 GW by 2030.
