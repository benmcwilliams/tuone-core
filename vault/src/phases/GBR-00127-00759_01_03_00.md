### Evolutionary Timeline of the Tata Group Facility in Bridgwater, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05/2023**: Tata Group announces plans to construct a battery cell factory in Bridgwater, UK, at Gravity Business Park. The facility is expected to produce battery cells for Jaguar Land Rover (JLR) and aims to localize cell production to enhance the local supply chain.
- **07/2023**: The UK government confirms Tata Group's plans to build its first battery cell factory outside India in Bridgwater. The factory will have an initial annual capacity of 40 GWh and is scheduled to begin operations in 2026.
- **03/2024**: The facility is described as a "gigafactory" that will supply almost half of the battery production needed by the UK automotive sector by 2030. It will also produce batteries for stationary energy storage solutions.
- **07/2024**: Agratas, Tata Group's battery business, appoints Sir Robert McAlpine as the lead engineering and delivery provider for the Bridgwater project. The facility is expected to be the largest of its kind in the UK.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05/2023**: Reports indicate that Tata's investment in the Bridgwater facility will be part of a larger incentive package worth nearly £800 million, which includes government subsidies and investments in other UK facilities.
- **07/2023**: Tata Group announces an investment of over £4 billion in the Bridgwater battery cell factory.
- **03/2024**: The UK government agrees to a support package reportedly worth hundreds of millions of pounds to help secure the delivery of the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05/2023**: Initial announcement of the Bridgwater facility and its significance for the UK automotive industry.
- **06/2023**: Confirmation of the Bridgwater site as the chosen location for the battery cell factory.
- **07/2023**: Official announcement from the UK government regarding the construction of the factory.
- **03/2024**: Preparatory works for the facility begin, with construction expected to start soon.
- **07/2024**: Sir Robert McAlpine is appointed as the delivery partner, marking a significant step forward in the project.

#### 4. Labour
- **05/2023**: The Bridgwater facility is expected to create up to 9,000 jobs.
- **07/2023**: The factory is projected to create up to 4,000 direct jobs upon its opening in 2026.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
