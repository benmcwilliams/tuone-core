## GBR-00127-00759

### Evolutionary Timeline of Tata Group Facility in Bridgwater, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05/2023**: Tata Group announces plans to build a battery cell factory in Bridgwater, Somerset, UK, to produce cells for Jaguar Land Rover (JLR) and potentially other manufacturers. The factory is expected to have an initial production capacity of 40 GWh, with plans to supply almost half of the UK's battery production needs by 2030.
- **07/2023**: The UK government officially confirms Tata's decision to build the battery cell factory in Bridgwater, with operations scheduled to begin in 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05/2023**: Reports indicate that Tata's investment in the Bridgwater facility will exceed £4 billion, with the UK government expected to provide a subsidy package worth hundreds of millions of pounds, potentially up to £500 million.
- **07/2023**: Tata confirms the investment of £4 billion into the Bridgwater facility, which is part of a broader £15 billion investment plan to upgrade its UK manufacturing facilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05/2023**: Initial discussions and negotiations regarding the Bridgwater site are reported, with Tata favoring the UK over Spain for the new battery plant.
- **07/2023**: The UK government officially announces the location of the battery cell factory in Bridgwater, confirming Tata's plans after months of speculation.
- **07/2023**: Tata finalizes plans for the Bridgwater facility, marking it as a significant investment in the UK automotive sector.
- **08/2023**: Tata appoints Sir Robert McAlpine as the lead engineering and delivery partner for the Bridgwater project, indicating progress towards construction.

#### 4. Labour
- **05/2023**: The Bridgwater facility is expected to create up to 9,000 jobs, with later reports indicating that the factory will create approximately 4,000 direct jobs.
- **07/2023**: The announcement of the factory's construction is expected to generate thousands of additional jobs in the wider supply chain.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **07/2023**: Tata Group is confirmed to be the owner of the Bridgwater facility, with no indication of joint ventures or shared ownership mentioned in the articles. The facility is part of Tata's broader strategy to enhance its electric vehicle production capabilities through its subsidiaries, including Jaguar Land Rover.
