### Evolutionary Timeline of Tata Group Facility in Bridgwater, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05/2023**: Tata Group announces plans to build a battery cell factory in Bridgwater, Somerset, UK, with an initial production capacity of 40 GWh, aimed at supplying cells for Jaguar Land Rover (JLR) and potentially other manufacturers.
- **07/2023**: The UK government confirms the establishment of the battery cell factory, which is expected to be one of the largest in Europe, with production scheduled to start in 2026.
- **07/2024**: Tata appoints Sir Robert McAlpine as the lead engineering and delivery provider for the Bridgwater facility, which is projected to supply nearly half of the UK's battery manufacturing capacity by the early 2030s.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05/2023**: Reports indicate that Tata's investment in the Bridgwater facility will exceed £4 billion, with the UK government expected to provide hundreds of millions in subsidies, potentially amounting to £800 million in total incentives.
- **07/2023**: The investment is confirmed to be part of a broader £15 billion plan by Tata to upgrade its UK manufacturing facilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05/2023**: Initial announcement of the Bridgwater battery cell factory.
- **07/2023**: Official confirmation from the UK government regarding the factory's establishment.
- **2026**: Scheduled start of production at the Bridgwater facility.
- **07/2024**: Preparatory works for the factory are underway, with construction set to begin soon.

#### 4. Labour
- **05/2023**: The Bridgwater facility is expected to create up to 9,000 jobs, with later reports indicating around 4,000 direct jobs.
- **07/2023**: The project is anticipated to generate thousands more jobs in the wider supply chain.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **05/2023**: Tata Group is the sole owner of the Bridgwater facility, with its battery subsidiary Agratas set to operate the plant. There are discussions about potential partnerships for technology supply, but Tata maintains full ownership of the facility.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic decisions surrounding the Tata Group's battery cell factory in Bridgwater, GBR, highlighting its significance in the UK's transition to electric vehicle manufacturing.
