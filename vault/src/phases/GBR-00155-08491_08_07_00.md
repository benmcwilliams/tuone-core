- **Date: 11-05-2023**: Cambridge Vacuum Engineering (CVE) developed an innovative electron beam welding technology called Ebflow. This technology allows for welding offshore wind turbine monopile foundations significantly faster than conventional methods, achieving speeds at least 25 times faster than submerged arc welding (SAW). The process uses 90% less energy, costs 88% less, and produces 97% less CO2 emissions compared to traditional methods.
