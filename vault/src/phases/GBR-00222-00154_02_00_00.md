### Evolutionary Timeline of the Cornish Lithium Facility in Cornwall, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2021**: Cornish Lithium is focused on the environmentally sustainable extraction of lithium from geothermal waters and hard rock in Cornwall. The company is constructing a demonstration plant for lithium processing and conducting a feasibility study for its Trelavour project.
- **October 2024**: Cornish Lithium opens a demonstration plant aimed at producing 10,000 tonnes of lithium hydroxide annually from 2027, utilizing granite extracted from an old clay pit. The facility is expected to help reduce reliance on carbon-intensive materials from other countries.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2021**: Cornish Lithium secures up to £18 million ($24 million) from TechMet to fast-track its lithium projects, including the construction of a demonstration plant and a feasibility study for the Trelavour project.
- **August 2023**: Cornish Lithium secures £53.6 million ($67 million) to open its first mine in Britain, led by the UK Infrastructure Bank as part of a larger funding round of up to £168 million.
- **October 2024**: The demonstration plant is funded in part by a £15 million investment from various sources, including the National Wealth Fund and the UK Government’s automotive transformation fund.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2021**: Announcement of the construction of a demonstration plant and feasibility study for the Trelavour project.
- **October 2024**: Official opening of the demonstration plant in Cornwall.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2024**: The opening of the demonstration plant is expected to create over 300 well-paid jobs for local people, contributing significantly to the local economy.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **November 2021**: Cornish Lithium is a start-up that has previously used crowdfunding to raise capital. The facility is owned exclusively by Cornish Lithium, with no mention of joint ventures or partnerships in the context of ownership.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Cornish Lithium facility, highlighting its focus on sustainable lithium extraction and the significant investments and job creation associated with its operations.
