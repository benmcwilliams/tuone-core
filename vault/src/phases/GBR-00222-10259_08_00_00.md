### Evolutionary Timeline of the Tugdock Facility in Cornwall, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **01-06-2023**: Tugdock has developed a technology known as the Tugdock Submersible Platform (TSP), which allows for the construction and loading of floating offshore wind turbine substructures in ports that lack sufficient water depth and assembly space. The TSP can be delivered in modular form, assembled at the port, and towed to deeper water for launching. The technology is designed to support the deployment of commercial-scale floating wind turbines and has an effective load capacity of up to 25,000 tonnes.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **01-06-2023**: Tugdock formed a partnership with Crowley, which strategically invested in Tugdock to facilitate the introduction of its technology to the U.S. floating offshore wind market. This collaboration aims to enhance logistics capabilities for offshore wind energy development.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **01-06-2023**: Tugdock's technology is highlighted as a solution to logistical challenges in the floating offshore wind sector, with no need for planning or environmental permissions, thus accelerating project schedules.
- **04-10-2023**: Tugdock collaborates with RWE and Sarens to test floating wind deployment and integration solutions. This partnership aims to investigate the quayside offload and launch of floating offshore wind platforms in the Celtic Sea, utilizing local port infrastructure.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
