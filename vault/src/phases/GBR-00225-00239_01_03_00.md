- **March 2024**: Eve Energy, a Chinese battery cell manufacturer and partner of BMW, is planning to establish a battery factory at Coventry Airport. - **March 2024**: Eve Energy is in discussions to invest approximately £1.2 billion in the Coventry facility. The project is contingent on securing several hundred million pounds in subsidies, which are currently under negotiation.

The facility's construction timeline has not been explicitly detailed, but it is noted that the West Midlands Gigafactory joint venture had previously announced plans for a cell factory in Coventry in 2021, with production expected to start in 2025.

#### 4. Labour
