## GBR-00225-00412

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Jaguar Land Rover Facility in Coventry, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2021**: Coventry City Council announced plans to develop a battery ‘gigafactory’ at Coventry Airport, aiming for production to start by 2025. The facility is intended to produce and recycle batteries, supporting Jaguar Land Rover's transition to an all-electric car brand by 2025.
- **July 2021**: The planning application for the gigafactory was submitted, with plans for a 530,000-square-metre plant that would create 6,000 new jobs.
- **March 2024**: The West Midlands Gigafactory joint venture was confirmed as the main tenant at Greenpower Park, planning a 60 GWh battery cell factory.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2021**: The UK government pledged up to £500 million in stimulus funding for the gigafactory initiative.
- **July 2021**: The initiators expected private investment of up to £2 billion for the gigafactory project.
- **March 2024**: Greenpower Park is expected to attract foreign investment of £2.5 billion, with the gigafactory being a significant part of this investment.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2021**: Announcement of plans for a gigafactory at Coventry Airport.
- **July 2021**: Submission of the planning application for the gigafactory.
- **March 2024**: Official launch of Greenpower Park, confirming the gigafactory's establishment.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2021**: The joint venture aims to create over 4,000 new jobs.
- **July 2021**: The gigafactory project is expected to create 6,000 new jobs.
- **March 2024**: Greenpower Park is projected to create an additional 6,000 jobs through various initiatives, including the gigafactory.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2021**: The facility is being developed through a public-private partnership (PPP) involving Coventry City Council and Coventry Airport, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by Jaguar Land Rover.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Jaguar Land Rover facility in Coventry, focusing on its transition towards battery production and the establishment of a gigafactory to support its electrification goals.
