### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lotus Facility in Coventry, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2022**: Hyperbat, a joint venture between Williams Advanced Engineering and Unipart, secured a multi-million-pound contract to supply 90kWh lithium-ion battery packs for the Lotus Evija, the first British-made all-electric hypercar. The battery packs will be manufactured in Coventry, utilizing a new production line opened by Unipart Manufacturing Group. The facility is designed for low-volume, high-performance vehicle production, focusing on handmade, small unit numbers rather than mass production.
