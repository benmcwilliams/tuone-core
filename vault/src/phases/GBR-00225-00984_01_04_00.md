### Evolutionary Timeline of the Comau Facility in Coventry, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2021**: The UK Battery Industrialisation Centre (UKBIC) in Coventry announced that its Module and Pack suite of specialist battery manufacturing equipment became operational. This facility is designed to provide testing facilities for new battery technologies and training for experts. The Module and Pack assembly line has a production capacity of 50 modules and 2.5 packs per 8-hour shift, featuring both manual and automatic workstations. The facility aims to bridge the gap between laboratory-scale battery technology and mass production.
- **April 2021**: Comau, a subsidiary of Stellantis, was confirmed as the partner responsible for developing and deploying the semi-automated battery module and pack assembly line. Comau provided the technology and know-how for the entire battery manufacturing process, including cylindrical cell preparation and pouch cell stacking.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2021**: The UKBIC facility represents a £130 million investment aimed at advancing battery technology in the UK. This investment supports the development of an open-access battery development facility that covers the entire production process.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2021**: The Module and Pack assembly line was officially opened and became operational.
- **April 2021**: Comau was announced as the partner for the battery manufacturing process, confirming the operational status of the assembly line and the ongoing installation of additional battery manufacturing equipment.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2021**: The facility allows British companies to bring their employees for training on the assembly line, indicating potential job creation opportunities as companies develop their battery technologies.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **2021**: The facility is operated as a partnership between UKBIC and Comau, with Comau providing the technology and expertise. It is not stated whether Comau has exclusive ownership, but it is clear that they play a significant role in the operation and development of the facility.
