#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2020**: The Port of Dundee is identified as the construction hub for the Neart na Gaoithe offshore wind farm, developed by EDF Renewables and ESB. The facility is focused on supporting offshore renewable energy projects, specifically wind energy.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2020**: A £40 million redevelopment program is announced for the Port of Dundee, which includes the construction of a new heavy-lift quayside and upgrades to over 15 hectares of land. This investment aims to enhance the port's capabilities for offshore renewable energy and oil & gas sectors.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2020**: Construction specialist Graham is contracted to deliver the new wharf and land upgrades at the Port of Dundee. The project includes replacing the existing Caledon East Wharf and upgrading Prince Charles Wharf, with completion expected by the end of 2021. The total quayside available will be 485 meters, allowing large semi-submersible vessels and wind installation vessels to berth.

- **December 2020**: The redevelopment of the Port of Dundee is expected to create jobs and support the supply of low carbon electricity, contributing to both social and economic benefits for Dundee and Scotland.
