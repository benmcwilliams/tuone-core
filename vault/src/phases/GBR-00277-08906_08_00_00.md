## GBR-00277-08906

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Forth Ports Facility in Dundee, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06-10-2020**: Forth Ports has invested "tens of millions of pounds" in enhanced port facilities at the Port of Dundee, including a strengthened and enlarged quay. An additional heavy lift quayside is under construction to support offshore wind projects.
- **09-02-2022**: Forth Ports acquires OM Heavy Lift (OMHL), enhancing its ability to provide a full-service package for offshore renewables projects, including marine services, quayside infrastructure, and heavy lift logistics. OMHL will operate across Forth Ports' Scottish operations, including Dundee.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06-10-2020**: Forth Ports has committed significant private sector funds to upgrade port facilities in Dundee, aiming to support offshore wind projects.
- **09-02-2022**: The acquisition of OMHL is part of Forth Ports' strategic investment to strengthen its position in the offshore renewables sector, following previous investments including a £10 million investment in 2017 for a new quayside designed for offshore wind and oil & gas decommissioning.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **28-02-2022**: The Neart na Gaoithe (NnG) offshore wind farm project, which the Port of Dundee supports, is set to deliver its first turbine components in March 2022. The project has been delayed to 2024 due to the Covid-19 pandemic, but the port is prepared to handle the assembly of turbine towers and storage of blades.

#### 4. Labour
- **28-02-2022**: The NnG project will create 50 new jobs at the operations and maintenance building in Eyemouth, contributing to local employment as the project progresses.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **06-10-2020**: Forth Ports operates the Port of Dundee and has made significant investments in its facilities. The acquisition of OMHL indicates a strategy to enhance its service offerings, suggesting that Forth Ports maintains full ownership of the facility and its operations.

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic moves of the Forth Ports facility in Dundee, highlighting its role in supporting offshore wind projects and its ongoing investments in infrastructure and services.
