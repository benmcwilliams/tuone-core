### Evolutionary Timeline of the Vertex Hydrogen Facility in Ellesmere Port, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2022**: Vertex Hydrogen, a joint venture between Essar and Progressive Energy, submitted plans to build the UK’s first low carbon hydrogen production hub at the Stanlow Manufacturing Complex in Ellesmere Port. The facility will utilize technology to capture carbon dioxide emissions and produce low carbon hydrogen, with a planned production capacity of over 1 GW, beginning in 2026.
- **February 2022**: Vertex Hydrogen announced that the Front End Engineering Design (FEED) of the plant was funded by the UK Government’s BEIS hydrogen supply competition. The facility will utilize technology from Johnson Matthey, achieving over 85% thermal efficiency and approximately 97% carbon capture.
- **August 2022**: Vertex Hydrogen awarded a project management services contract to Worley for the 350 MW low-carbon hydrogen production plant at Stanlow, marking it as one of the first large-scale low-carbon hydrogen plants globally.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2023**: Essar Group announced a $2.4 billion investment in the UK, focusing on the Stanlow site, which includes the Vertex Hydrogen project. This investment aims to reduce industrial emissions in Northwest England by around 20% and is part of Essar's broader energy transition strategy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2022**: Vertex Hydrogen submitted plans for the hydrogen production hub.
- **February 2022**: The FEED of the plant was funded by the UK Government’s BEIS hydrogen supply competition.
- **August 2022**: Vertex Hydrogen awarded a project management contract to Worley for the hydrogen production plant.
- **2026**: Planned start of hydrogen production at the facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2023**: Essar's investment plans are expected to create new highly skilled job opportunities at the Stanlow site, contributing to the Northern Powerhouse economy.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **January 2022**: Vertex Hydrogen is a joint venture between Essar and Progressive Energy, indicating shared ownership rather than exclusive ownership by a single entity.
