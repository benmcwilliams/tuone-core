### Evolutionary Timeline of the Progressive Energy Facility in Ellesmere Port, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2022**: Vertex Hydrogen, a joint venture between Essar Oil UK and Progressive Energy, announced plans to develop the UK’s largest hydrogen hub at the Stanlow Manufacturing Complex in Ellesmere Port. The facility will utilize technology from Johnson Matthey to produce high purity low carbon hydrogen from natural gas, achieving over 85% thermal efficiency and approximately 97% carbon capture.
- **January 2024**: Plans for a 1.35GW blue hydrogen production facility were approved, consisting of two plants (HPP1 and HPP2). HPP1 will have a capacity of 350MW, while HPP2 will target 1,000MW, with an overall target capacity exceeding 4,000MW by 2030. The hydrogen produced will be used locally by the Essar refinery and other major manufacturers.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2022**: The Front End Engineering Design (FEED) of the plant was funded by the UK Government’s BEIS hydrogen supply competition, supporting the development of the hydrogen hub.
- **January 2024**: The project is expected to significantly reduce carbon emissions in the North West by 2.5 million tonnes annually, equivalent to removing 1.1 million cars from the roads.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2022**: Announcement of the hydrogen hub development as part of the HyNet cluster.
- **January 2024**: Approval of plans for the hydrogen production facility by Cheshire West & Chester Council. Construction for HPP1 is expected to start in 2024, with hydrogen production anticipated by 2027.

#### 4. Labour
- **January 2024**: The project is expected to support UK jobs by enabling local industrial and power generation businesses to transition to low carbon energy, although specific job creation numbers were not detailed.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2022**: The facility is a joint venture between Essar Oil UK and Progressive Energy, indicating shared ownership rather than exclusive ownership by either company.
