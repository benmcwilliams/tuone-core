## GBR-00296-06861

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Encirc Facility in Ellesmere Port, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 11-01-2024
   - **Details:** Plans for a 1.35GW blue hydrogen production facility at the Stanlow Manufacturing Complex in Ellesmere Port have been approved. The facility will consist of two plants (HPP1 and HPP2). HPP1 will have a capacity of 350MW, while HPP2 will target 1,000MW, with an overall target capacity of over 4,000MW by 2030. The hydrogen produced will be utilized locally by the Essar refinery and other major manufacturers, including Encirc.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 11-01-2024
   - **Details:** The hydrogen hub is part of the HyNet industrial decarbonisation cluster, which aims to reduce carbon emissions in the North West by 2.5 million tonnes annually. The project is expected to create the first low carbon refining operations and glass and chemicals manufacturing sites globally.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 11-01-2024
   - **Details:** Cheshire West & Chester Council approved the plans for the hydrogen production facility. Construction for HPP1 is expected to start in 2024, with low carbon hydrogen production anticipated by 2027.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 11-01-2024
   - **Details:** The project is expected to support UK jobs by enabling local industrial businesses to decarbonise their processes.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 11-01-2024
   - **Details:** EET Hydrogen, responsible for the facility, is a joint venture between Essar Oil UK and Progressive Energy.
