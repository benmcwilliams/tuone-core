### Evolutionary Timeline of the NextPower UK ESG Facility in Glasgow, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **November 2024:** NextPower UK ESG (NPUK) acquired a 29MW, 2-hour duration standalone battery energy storage system (BESS) in Glasgow, named Rutherglen. This facility is designed to provide constraint management services to the National Energy System Operator (NESO) and has the potential for future capacity augmentation. This acquisition marks NPUK's first standalone BESS project, contributing to a total of 49MW of battery capacity in their portfolio.
