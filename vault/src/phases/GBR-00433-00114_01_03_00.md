## GBR-00433-00114

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Britishvolt Facility in Hams Hall, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2022**: Britishvolt announced plans to build a battery cell research and development center at its Hams Hall site. This facility is designed to develop new battery types and test existing ones, supporting the gigafactory in Blyth, Northumberland. The Hams Hall facility will focus on creating higher performance and more affordable battery cells.
- **June 2022**: Britishvolt revealed that the Hams Hall facility would be used to test manufacturing methods for the gigafactory, aiming to produce “higher performance, production-ready batteries of the future” while lowering costs for electric vehicles (EVs).
- **August 2022**: The company confirmed that the Hams Hall facility would act as a “mini replica” of the Blyth gigafactory, which is expected to produce enough lithium-ion cells for approximately 300,000 EV battery packs annually.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2022**: Britishvolt announced a £200 million investment for the Hams Hall facility, which is part of a broader strategy to support the gigafactory in Blyth and enhance the UK’s battery ecosystem.
- **June 2022**: The investment in the Hams Hall facility is aimed at creating more than 150 jobs and facilitating the development of new cell formats and chemistries.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2022**: Britishvolt announced the construction of the Hams Hall facility, with completion expected by the end of Q3 2023.
- **June 2022**: Construction and setup of equipment at Hams Hall were projected to be completed by the end of Q3 2023.
- **August 2022**: The company confirmed ongoing plans for the Hams Hall facility, despite challenges faced by the Blyth gigafactory project.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2022**: The Hams Hall facility is expected to create 150 jobs for the local area, contributing to the local economy and workforce development.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2022**: Britishvolt is the sole owner of the Hams Hall facility, having partnered with Prologis for the development but retaining full ownership of the site and its operations.
