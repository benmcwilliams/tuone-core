- **July 2019**: LEVC reported that its electric black taxis had captured a 10% market share in London since their launch in January 2018. By this date, 2,500 TX black cabs were operating in the UK, significantly reducing emissions and fuel consumption.

- **2014**: Geely, the Chinese parent company, acquired LEVC (formerly London Taxi Company) and has since invested over £500 million to transform it into an electric vehicle manufacturer. This investment has been crucial for the development and production of the TX electric taxis.
