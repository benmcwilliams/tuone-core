### Evolutionary Timeline of the Circulor Facility in London, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2020**: Circulor, a British blockchain specialist, is involved in developing technology to trace the origin of raw materials in the automotive supply chain, particularly cobalt used in electric vehicle batteries. This technology is already being utilized in Volvo's battery supply chain.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09/2020**: Volvo Cars invests in Circulor through its investment fund, Volvo Cars Tech Fund. The specific amount of the investment is not disclosed. #### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/2020**: The investment by Volvo is part of a broader strategy to improve supply chain transparency and ethical sourcing in the automotive industry. Circulor's technology is already operational in the supply chain for cobalt, with plans to expand its application.
