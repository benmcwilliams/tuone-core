### Evolutionary Timeline of the Octopus Facility in London, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2022**: Octopus Energy partnered with Xlinks to develop a 10.5 GW wind-solar project in Morocco, which will connect to the UK via a submarine cable. The facility is expected to supply 3.6 GW of reliable, clean power to the UK for an average of 20 hours a day, enough to power approximately 7 million heat pumps annually.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2022**: The partnership with Xlinks is part of Octopus Energy's broader strategy to invest in renewable energy projects, although specific investment amounts for the Morocco project were not disclosed.

- **December 2022**: Octopus Energy signed a power purchase agreement (PPA) with Shell Energy Europe to procure 2.4 TWh of wind energy annually from the Dogger Bank offshore wind farm, which is a significant investment in renewable energy procurement.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2022**: Announcement of the partnership with Xlinks for the Morocco project, marking a significant milestone in Octopus Energy's expansion into international renewable energy projects.

- **December 2022**: Signing of the PPA with Shell Energy Europe, indicating a commitment to secure renewable energy supply from the Dogger Bank project, which is currently in its development phase.

#### 4. Labour

However, specific job creation numbers related to Octopus Energy's operations were not mentioned.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ongoing**: Octopus Energy operates as a full owner of its renewable energy assets, including solar farms and wind projects across 11 countries. The company is also involved in joint ventures, such as the one with Xlinks for the Morocco project, but retains full ownership of its operational facilities in the UK.
