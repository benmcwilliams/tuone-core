### Evolutionary Timeline of the Newcleo Facility in London, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2023**: Newcleo announced a partnership with the Nuclear AMRC to explore various technical and business operations related to their advanced modular reactor technology. The facility is set to produce a 30 MWe lead-cooled fast reactor (LFR) to be deployed in France by 2030, followed by a 200 MWe commercial unit in the UK by 2033. Additionally, Newcleo plans to invest in a mixed uranium/plutonium oxide (MOX) plant to fuel its reactors.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2023**: Newcleo signed a cooperation and investment agreement with the Tosto Group to advance the industrialization of its reactors. This agreement includes collaboration on manufacturing, fabrication, and installation processes.
- **June 2022**: Newcleo contracted France's Orano for feasibility studies on establishing a MOX production plant, indicating prior investment efforts to support their reactor technology.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2023**: Newcleo's partnership with the Nuclear AMRC marks a significant step in their delivery roadmap, focusing on the design and construction of their reactors.
- **April 2024**: Newcleo's application for the justification of their reactor design in the UK was accepted for consideration by the government, marking the first application for advanced nuclear technology in the UK. This is a crucial milestone in the regulatory process for their planned 200 MWe reactor.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2023**: The partnership with the Nuclear AMRC and the investment agreements are expected to support job creation in the UK nuclear sector, although specific job numbers were not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
