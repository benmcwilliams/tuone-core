### Evolutionary Timeline of the Brunel University London Facility in London, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: Starting 01-10-2024
  - **Project**: GEOFLEXheat
  - **Description**: The facility will focus on harnessing waste geothermal heat for industrial use. The project aims to develop a system that combines a heat pipe heat exchanger, a high-temperature heat pump, and thermal energy storage to utilize low-temperature geothermal brine effectively. The system will produce upgraded water temperatures up to 110°C steam for industrial applications and district hot water.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: 20-08-2024
