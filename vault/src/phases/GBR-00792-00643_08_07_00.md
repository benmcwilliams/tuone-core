### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in Norfolk, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2024**: RWE awarded contracts to Lamprell Energy Limited for the supply of transition pieces for the Norfolk Vanguard offshore wind projects (Norfolk Vanguard West and Norfolk Vanguard East). The combined total installed capacity of these projects is 2.8 GW, with each project planned to have a capacity of 1.4 GW. The transition pieces will be fabricated using Lamprell's serial production line, which was commissioned in 2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2024**: RWE acquired the three offshore wind projects in the Norfolk Offshore Wind Zone, which includes Norfolk Vanguard West, Norfolk Vanguard East, and Norfolk Boreas. This acquisition is part of RWE's commitment to expanding its green energy portfolio in the UK.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2024**: RWE and Lamprell marked the signing of the contract for the transition pieces at the WindEnergy Hamburg 2024 conference. The projects have already secured seabed rights, grid connections, Development Consent Orders, and all other necessary permits. Onshore construction of substations and the underground cable route between Necton and Happisburgh has commenced.
