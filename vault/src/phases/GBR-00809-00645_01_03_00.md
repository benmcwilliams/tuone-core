## GBR-00809-00645

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Recharge Industries Facility in Northumberland, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: Recharge Industries acquires Britishvolt, which had planned to build a battery cell factory in Northumberland. The focus of the facility will shift from producing battery cells for major car manufacturers to batteries for stationary energy storage and later for high-performance sports cars. The factory is expected to start operations by the end of 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2023**: The acquisition deal was completed, but specific financial details, including the purchase price, were not disclosed. The facility's planned investment was initially projected at £4 billion for the battery factory.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2023**: Recharge Industries announced plans to begin work on the factory within the next six to twelve months, with production expected to start by the end of 2025. The building permit for the site had reportedly been granted.
- **November 2023**: Reports emerged that Recharge Industries had not made the final payment of £8.57 million due on 5 April 2023, raising concerns about the viability of the project and the potential for insolvency.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2023**: The acquisition included the transfer of remaining employees from Britishvolt to Recharge Industries. The facility could potentially create up to 8,000 local jobs once operational.
- **November 2023**: Reports indicated that about 26 employees from Britishvolt joined Recharge, but most had since left due to ongoing financial issues and unpaid salaries.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2023**: Recharge Industries, an Australian battery startup owned by US investor group Scale Facilitation, acquired Britishvolt. The facility is under the ownership of Recharge Industries, but the financial stability and operational control have been questioned due to ongoing legal and financial challenges.

This timeline captures the key developments and challenges faced by the Recharge Industries facility in Northumberland, highlighting its evolution from acquisition to potential operational issues.
