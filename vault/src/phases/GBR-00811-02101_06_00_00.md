### Martifer Facility in Nottingham, GBR - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** March 2014
   - **Details:** Martifer Solar is involved in the construction of a 78.4MWp portfolio of photovoltaic (PV) power plants in the UK, which includes a facility in Nottingham. The projects consist of five PV plants with capacities ranging from 9MW to 24.67MW, totaling 308,418 solar modules installed in ground-mounted fixed structures across approximately 155 hectares. The combined output is expected to generate around 69.05GWh of energy annually, sufficient to supply the average consumption of 38,815 inhabitants and offset approximately 31,582 tons of CO2 emissions each year. Martifer Solar will also provide operation and maintenance (O&M) services for optimal performance.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** March 2014
   - **Details:** The construction of the PV power plants is part of a larger portfolio developed for Lightsource Renewable Energy, indicating significant investment in solar energy infrastructure in the UK. The total ground-mount PV pipeline in the UK was noted to exceed 5GW, highlighting the scale of investment in solar technology.
