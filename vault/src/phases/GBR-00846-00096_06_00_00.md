### Evolutionary Timeline of the Belectric Facility in Oxfordshire, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 07-11-2014
   - **Details:** Belectric, in collaboration with First Solar, began construction of a 46MW solar farm in Oxfordshire. This project will increase the total large-scale PV capacity deployed by the partnership in the UK to 80MW. The facility will utilize over 483,000 of First Solar’s thin-film modules. Upon completion, it is expected to generate approximately 45MWh of electricity annually, enough to power around a quarter of Oxford’s homes. The land will continue to be used for grazing sheep post-construction.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 07-11-2014
   - **Details:** The project is part of a joint venture formed in 2013 between Belectric and First Solar, indicating a collaborative investment in large-scale solar energy development in the UK.
