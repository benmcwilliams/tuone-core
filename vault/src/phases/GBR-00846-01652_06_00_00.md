### Evolutionary Timeline of the First Solar Facility in Oxfordshire, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 07-11-2014
   - **Details:** First Solar, in collaboration with Belectric, began construction of a 46MW solar farm in Oxfordshire. This project will increase the total large-scale PV capacity deployed by the partnership in the UK to 80MW. The facility will utilize over 483,000 of First Solar’s thin-film modules. Upon completion, it is expected to generate approximately 45MWh of electricity annually, enough to power around a quarter of Oxford’s homes. The land will continue to be used for grazing sheep post-construction.
