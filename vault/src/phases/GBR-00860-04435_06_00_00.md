### Evolutionary Timeline of the Absolute Solar and Wind Facility in Perth, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2020
   - **Details:** The facility is involved in the installation of a combined solar power and battery storage system near Perth’s Waste Water Treatment Works at Sleepless Inch on the River Tay. The project includes the installation of 2,520 solar panels with a combined generating capacity of just over 1MW. It also features four vanadium flow batteries (VFBs) capable of storing up to 0.8MWh of energy. The system is designed to enable around 94% of the renewable power generated to be used on-site, significantly reducing the carbon footprint and energy costs.
