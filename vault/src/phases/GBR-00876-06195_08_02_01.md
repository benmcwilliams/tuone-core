#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2021**: Tata Steel's Port Talbot facility is involved in supplying steel for the foundations of the Dogger Bank offshore wind farm, which is set to be the world's largest offshore wind farm. The facility produces UK-made steel that is processed into hollow sections at Corby and Hartlepool sites for use in the wind farm's transition pieces.
- **September 2023**: The UK Government announces plans for Tata Steel to construct a new electric arc furnace at the Port Talbot site to transition to greener steel production. This new technology aims to replace the existing coal-powered blast furnaces, significantly reducing carbon emissions.
