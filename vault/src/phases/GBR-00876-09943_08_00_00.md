## GBR-00876-09943

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sarens Facility in Port Talbot, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 11-12-2024
   - **Details:** Sarens, in collaboration with Tugdock, is developing a heavy lift Operations & Maintenance (O&M) hub at ABP's port of Port Talbot. The facility will support heavy lift equipment used in floating offshore wind projects in the Celtic Sea. Services will include crane rental, heavy lifting, engineered transport, assembly areas, storage, training, and consultancy.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 11-12-2024
   - **Details:** The project has secured funding from The Crown Estate as part of the Supply Chain Accelerator, a £50 million fund aimed at supporting UK supply chain projects for the offshore wind sector. This funding is intended to accelerate and de-risk the development of the O&M hub.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 11-12-2024
   - **Details:** The announcement of the funding and the development of the O&M hub marks a significant milestone for the facility, positioning it as a key player in the floating offshore wind market.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 11-12-2024
   - **Details:** The establishment of the Sarens and Tugdock O&M hub is expected to create significant employment opportunities in Port Talbot, contributing to regional economic development.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 11-12-2024
   - **Details:** The facility is a joint project between Sarens and Tugdock, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by Sarens.
