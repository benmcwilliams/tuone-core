### Evolutionary Timeline of the Uniti Facility in Silverstone Park, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2018**: Uniti announced plans to construct a pilot factory in Silverstone Park for the production of the Uniti One, a two-seater electric vehicle (EV). The facility is intended to serve as a pilot program for future licensed factories globally. The production is expected to be ready by 2020. The Uniti One features a 22 kW battery, a range of 300 km, and a top speed of 130 km/h.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2018**: Uniti indicated plans for a crowdfunding campaign via Crowdcube to raise funds for the facility and its operations. The campaign successfully raised one million euros, contributing to the development of the Uniti One.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2018**: Announcement of the pilot factory construction in Silverstone Park, with production readiness targeted for 2020. The facility will be developed in cooperation with MEPC.
- **July 2019**: Uniti opened online orders for the Founders’ Edition of the Uniti One, indicating that production was progressing towards the rollout of the vehicle.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2018**: While specific job creation numbers were not mentioned, the establishment of the facility is expected to create jobs in the local area as part of the manufacturing and operational setup.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **October 2018**: Uniti is establishing the facility in cooperation with MEPC, indicating a partnership rather than exclusive ownership. The details of the ownership structure beyond this partnership have not been specified in the articles.
