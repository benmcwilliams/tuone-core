### Evolutionary Timeline of the RWE Facility in Sofia, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2021**: RWE greenlights the Sofia Offshore Wind Farm, which will feature 100 SG 14-222 DD offshore wind turbines with a total capacity of 1.4 GW. Onshore enabling works are set to begin in Spring 2021, with offshore construction planned for 2023.
- **March 2023**: RWE announces the selection of Siemens Gamesa’s RecyclableBlade for 44 of the 100 turbines, totaling 132 individual recyclable blades. The blades will measure 108 meters long, marking the first deployment of this variant. The project is located 195 km off the UK coast at Dogger Bank and is scheduled for full commissioning in 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2022**: RWE raises a €1 billion debt package to finance the Sofia project, which will partly replace existing financing. This funding is provided by BayernLB and Denmark’s Export Credit Agency EKF, supporting RWE's strategy to expand its renewable energy capacity.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2021**: RWE announces the greenlighting of the Sofia Offshore Wind Farm, with construction activities planned to commence.
- **December 2022**: Onshore works for the Sofia project have started, while offshore installation is expected to begin in 2023.
- **March 2023**: RWE confirms the agreement with Siemens Gamesa for the supply of recyclable blades, marking a significant milestone in the project's development.
