## GBR-01037-00759

### Evolutionary Timeline of Tata Group Facility in Somerset, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2023**: Tata Group announced plans to build its flagship European electric vehicle (EV) battery plant in Somerset, UK, with a production capacity of **40 gigawatt hours (GWh)**. This facility is expected to supply nearly half of the UK's EV battery production needs by 2030.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2023**: Tata confirmed an investment of **£4 billion ($5.1 billion)** into the Somerset battery plant. The UK Government is expected to provide **hundreds of millions of pounds** in subsidies, with reports suggesting up to **£500 million** in support.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2023**: The deal for the battery factory was finalized after months of negotiations with the UK government. Production at the site is scheduled to begin in **2026**.
- **August 2023**: Reports indicated that Tata Motors and Japanese battery manufacturer AESC are likely to form a partnership for technology supply related to the battery cells produced at the Somerset facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2023**: The establishment of the battery plant is projected to create **4,000 jobs** in the UK, along with thousands more in the wider supply chain.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2023**: Tata Group is the sole owner of the facility, as it is a direct investment by Tata for its operations in the UK.
