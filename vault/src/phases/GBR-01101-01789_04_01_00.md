### Evolutionary Timeline of the Masdar Facility in Teesside, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2021**: BP, ADNOC, and Masdar announced plans to develop a 2GW low carbon hydrogen facility named H2Teesside in Teesside, UK. This facility is expected to contribute significantly to the UK Government’s target of 5GW hydrogen production by 2030. Production is anticipated to start in **2027**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2022**: ADNOC announced it would take a **25% stake** in the design stage of the blue hydrogen project, while Masdar would participate in the green hydrogen project. This partnership marks ADNOC's first investment in the UK.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2021**: Initial announcement of the H2Teesside project, with intentions to expand as the project progresses.
- **May 2022**: The project advanced to the design phase, confirming its status as the largest blue hydrogen project in the UK.

#### 4. Labour
- No specific job creation or job loss details were mentioned in the articles regarding the Masdar facility in Teesside.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is not exclusively owned by Masdar; it is a joint venture involving BP, ADNOC, and Masdar, with ADNOC holding a **25% stake** in the project.
