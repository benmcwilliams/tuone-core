## GBR-01101-07150

### Evolutionary Timeline of SeAH Wind Facility in Teesside, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2022**: SeAH Wind submitted plans for a monopile factory in Teesside, with a production capacity of 100 to 150 monopiles per year. The facility will produce monopiles up to 120 meters in length, 15.5 meters in diameter, and weighing 3000 tonnes.
- **06/2022**: Planning approval was granted for the £300m facility, which will include a 93,324 square meter main manufacturing building, an administration office, maintenance station, paint facility, and power stations.
- **07/2022**: Construction began on the facility, which is expected to be the first XXL monopile foundation fabrication facility in the UK.
- **12/2023**: SeAH Wind secured a contract to supply monopiles for Vattenfall’s 2.8 GW Norfolk Vanguard offshore wind farm, with production set to start in 2026 and steelwork running until the end of 2027.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2022**: The facility is part of a £300m investment, located on a 36-hectare site within Teesside’s Freeport.
- **07/2022**: The investment increased to £400m as construction commenced.
- **12/2023**: The total investment was further increased to £650m, highlighting the facility's significance in the UK’s offshore wind sector.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **04/2022**: SeAH Wind submitted plans for the facility.
- **06/2022**: Planning approval was granted.
- **07/2022**: Construction officially began.
- **12/2023**: SeAH Wind announced a significant contract with Vattenfall for the Norfolk Vanguard project, marking a major milestone for the facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **04/2022**: The project is expected to create 1500 jobs during construction and an additional 750 jobs once operational.
- **07/2022**: The facility is projected to create 750 direct jobs and 1500 indirect jobs in the supply chain during construction.
- **12/2023**: The contract with Vattenfall is expected to secure over 750 new jobs, further emphasizing the facility's role in local employment.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **04/2022**: SeAH Wind is identified as the owner of the facility, with no mention of joint ventures or partnerships in the initial articles. The facility is part of SeAH Steel Holdings, indicating full ownership by SeAH Wind.
