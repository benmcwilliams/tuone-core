## GBR-01101-08671

### Evolutionary Timeline of the EEW Facility in Teesside, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2017**: EEW OSB (formerly OSB) is contracted to manufacture 86 transition pieces for Ørsted’s Hornsea Project One, contributing to the production of monopiles and transition pieces for offshore wind farms. The facility is noted for its role in the growing offshore wind industry, securing hundreds of jobs in Teesside.
- **October 2018**: EEW OSB secures its first export order, providing 35 transition pieces for Ørsted’s Borssele 1 + 2 offshore wind farm in the Dutch North Sea. This contract highlights the facility's capability to engage in international projects.
- **September 2020**: The facility is involved in the production of 30 transition pieces for Ørsted’s Hornsea 2 offshore wind farm, with the first load out recently completed.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2018**: The contract for the Borssele 1 + 2 project is described as a multi-million pound order, indicating significant financial investment tied to the facility's operations and capacity utilization.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2017**: Announcement of the contract for Hornsea Project One, marking a significant milestone for the facility.
- **October 2018**: Securing the contract for Borssele 1 + 2, marking the facility's first export order.
- **September 2020**: The facility completes the first load out of transition pieces for Hornsea 2, indicating progress in project execution.

#### 4. Labour
- **November 2017**: The Hornsea Project One contract is noted to secure hundreds of jobs in Teesside, showcasing the facility's impact on local employment.
- **October 2018**: The Borssele 1 + 2 contract secures 180 jobs in Teesside, ensuring high utilization of the facility for the first half of 2019.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **November 2017**: EEW Special Pipes Constructions GmbH (EEW SPC) takes full ownership of EEW OSB, which was previously a joint venture between EEW SPC and Bladt Industries established in 2014. This transition marks a significant change in the ownership structure of the facility.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones of the EEW facility in Teesside, GBR, focusing on its operations, investments, project statuses, labor impacts, and ownership changes.
