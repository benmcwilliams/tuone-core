### Kellas Facility in Teesside, GBR - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **April 2023:** Kellas Midstream, in partnership with RWE, announced plans to evaluate large-scale green hydrogen production in Teesside. The proposed site aims for "gigawatt-scale" production, with a phased development based on customer demand. Kellas also operates the H2NorthEast blue hydrogen project at the same location, which plans to deliver 355MW by 2027 and scale up to over 1GW by 2030.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **April 2023:** RWE is planning to invest £15 billion in green energy projects across the UK by 2030, which includes the development of hydrogen plants in various locations, including Teesside.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **April 2023:** A memorandum of understanding (MoU) was signed between Kellas and RWE to collaborate on green hydrogen production in Teesside. The project is part of the East Coast Cluster initiative aimed at decarbonizing industrial emissions.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
