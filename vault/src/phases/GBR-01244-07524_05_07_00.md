### Evolutionary Timeline of the Westinghouse Electric Company Facility in Wolverhampton, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: Initial testing completed for the Generation IV lead-cooled fast reactor (LFR) design at the Passive Heat Removal Facility (PHRF) in Wolverhampton. The facility is part of the Advanced Modular Reactor (AMR) programme, which aims to support the development of LFR technology. The facility includes two experimental setups: the Versatile Loop Facility (VLF) and the PHRF, designed to collect data for the reactor's materials, systems, and components.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: The UK Government's Department for Business, Energy & Industrial Strategy (BEIS) invested £10 million ($12.5 million) to support the testing of LFR technology at the Wolverhampton facility. This funding is aimed at enabling Westinghouse to assess the feasibility of key materials and systems for the LFR.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
