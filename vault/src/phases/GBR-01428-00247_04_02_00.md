### Evolutionary Timeline of the ExxonMobil Facility in Southampton, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is set to transform into a hydrogen hub, with an initial hydrogen production capacity of 4.3 TWh per year. The project aims to decarbonize the region and introduce hydrogen technologies, contributing to the development of an international hydrogen supply chain.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2021
   - **Details:** ExxonMobil, along with SGN and Green Investment Group (GIG), signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) to develop the hydrogen hub. The feasibility study led by SGN and GIG indicates that the project will stimulate the local economy through the conversion of the natural gas network.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** December 2021
   - **Details:** The announcement of the Southampton Hydrogen Hub marks a significant milestone in the facility's evolution, with production set to commence as early as 2030.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** December 2021
   - **Details:** The hydrogen hub is expected to support specialist jobs in the area, contributing to local employment as the project develops.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** December 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is part of a collaboration involving ExxonMobil, SGN, and GIG, indicating a joint venture structure for the hydrogen hub development.
