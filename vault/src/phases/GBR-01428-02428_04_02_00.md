## GBR-01428-02428

### Evolutionary Timeline of the SGN Facility in Southampton, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2021**: The SGN facility is set to become part of the Southampton Hydrogen Hub, which aims to produce hydrogen with an initial production capacity of **4.3 TWh per year**. This facility will focus on decarbonizing the local industrial sector and converting the natural gas network to hydrogen technologies.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2021**: SGN, along with ExxonMobil and the Green Investment Group (GIG), signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to explore hydrogen production and carbon capture in the Southampton industrial cluster. The feasibility study indicates that hydrogen demand could rise to **37 TWh by 2050**, with initial carbon capture facilities projected to capture approximately **2 million tonnes of CO2 per year**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2021**: The announcement of the Southampton Hydrogen Hub and the signing of the MoU marks a significant milestone in the project, with production expected to commence as early as **2030**.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2021**: The development of the hydrogen hub is expected to support **specialist jobs** in the area, contributing to local economic stimulation through the introduction of hydrogen technologies.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **December 2021**: The facility is part of a collaborative effort involving SGN, ExxonMobil, and GIG, indicating a **joint venture** structure rather than exclusive ownership by SGN.

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic plans for the SGN facility in Southampton, highlighting its role in advancing hydrogen technology and contributing to the UK's clean energy goals.
