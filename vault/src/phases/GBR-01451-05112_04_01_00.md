## GBR-01451-05112

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Getech Facility in Edinburgh, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 11-03-2022
   - **Details:** Getech, through its unit H2 Green, is involved in the development of a green hydrogen network across the Highlands of Scotland. The facility in Inverness, developed in collaboration with SGN Commercial Services, is expected to produce eight tonnes of hydrogen per day, which can service approximately 800 heavy goods vehicles (HGVs) daily. This facility is part of a broader plan to establish a regional hydrogen network that includes production, storage, and delivery infrastructure.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 11-03-2022
   - **Details:** Highland Council has committed £100,000 to support the initial work on the green hydrogen network. This investment is aimed at facilitating the development of the hydrogen production and infrastructure necessary for the decarbonisation initiatives in the region.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 11-03-2022
   - **Details:** A memorandum of understanding (MoU) was signed between H2 Green and Highland Council to create a network of green hydrogen hubs. The MoU marks a significant milestone in the establishment of a hydrogen hub in Inverness, which is the first planned facility in this network. The project is currently in the negotiation phase for a joint venture agreement to deliver the decarbonisation plan.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 11-03-2022
   - **Details:** The development of the hydrogen network and facility is expected to support job creation in the Highlands, as stated by Highland Council leaders. The project aims to attract external investment, which will further enhance job opportunities and supply chain developments in the region.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 11-03-2022
   - **Details:** H2 Green is a unit of Getech, indicating that Getech has a direct involvement in the facility's operations. The facility in Inverness is being developed in collaboration with SGN Commercial Services, suggesting a joint venture structure for this specific project. 

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic initiatives related to the Getech facility in Edinburgh, focusing on its role in the green hydrogen sector in Scotland.
