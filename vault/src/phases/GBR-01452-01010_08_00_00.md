### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDF Facility in Leith, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2020**: The Port of Leith is actively involved in supporting EDF Renewables’ Neart na Gaoithe (NnG) offshore wind project, which has a production capacity of 450 MW, supplying low carbon electricity for approximately 375,000 homes. The facility is utilized for the storage of offshore wind farm foundation jackets and subsea project work.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2020**: A seven-figure private investment is made to expand the Port of Leith by an additional 25 hectares, enhancing its capabilities with over 3 km of deep water quaysides. This investment aims to support the growing offshore renewables market and complements ongoing upgrades at the Dundee facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2020**: Offshore construction for the NnG project begins, with the Port of Leith designated as the Marine Logistics Base for the pile casings, which are essential for the foundations of the project’s turbines and substations.

#### 4. Labour
- **November 2020**: The investment in the Port of Leith is expected to drive employment opportunities, as it aims to enhance local content in Scotland's offshore wind sector, supporting job creation through increased activity and project work.
