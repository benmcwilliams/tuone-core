1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is involved in supporting the Neart na Gaoithe (NnG) offshore wind farm project, which has a production capacity of 450 MW of low carbon energy. The Port of Leith is utilized for the storage of offshore wind farm foundation jackets and other subsea elements related to the development of offshore wind farms.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - In November 2020, a seven-figure private funding investment was announced to enhance the Port of Leith's capabilities in the offshore renewables market. This investment aims to bring an additional 25 hectares of land to the market, linked to over 3 km of deep water quaysides.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - The construction of the Neart na Gaoithe offshore wind farm began in August 2020, with the Port of Leith serving as the Marine Logistics Base for the pile casings that will form part of the foundations for the project's 54 turbines and two substations.
