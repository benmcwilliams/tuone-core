### Evolutionary Timeline of the Forth Ports Facility in Leith, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2020**: The Port of Leith aims to enhance its position in the offshore renewables market with a seven-figure private investment to bring an additional 25 hectares of land linked to over 3km of deep water quaysides. This investment supports the storage of offshore wind farm foundation jackets and project work associated with offshore windfarms.
- **January 2024**: Forth Ports confirms that a new 250,000-square metre quayside at Leith, part of a £50m investment, will be operational by August 2024. This site will serve as a marshalling area for advanced delivery and storage, enhancing manufacturing capabilities for offshore wind projects.
- **December 2024**: Forth Ports secures a £50m investment for the Port of Leith to support the delivery of the 1100MW Inch Cape offshore wind project, enhancing marine access and infrastructure.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2020**: A seven-figure private funding investment is announced to expand the Port of Leith.
- **January 2024**: A £50m investment is confirmed for the new quayside at Leith.
- **December 2024**: Another £50m investment is secured for infrastructure improvements related to the Inch Cape project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2020**: Announcement of the investment to expand the Port of Leith and support offshore wind projects.
- **January 2024**: Confirmation that the new quayside will be operational by August 2024.
- **December 2024**: Forth Ports announces the securing of the Inch Cape project, marking a significant milestone in their offshore wind capabilities.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2024**: The investment related to the Inch Cape project is expected to create up to 50 new and upskilled green energy jobs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ongoing**: Forth Ports Limited owns the facility exclusively, as indicated by their direct investments and acquisitions related to the facility's operations and expansions.
