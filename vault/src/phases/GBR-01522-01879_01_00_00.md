### Evolutionary Timeline of the Quinbrook Facility (Project Fortress) in Kent, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2021**: Quinbrook acquired a consented 350MW solar-plus-battery project named Project Fortress in Kent, UK. Once operational, it is expected to be the largest single-site solar PV installation in the UK, generating enough renewable power to meet the needs of approximately 100,000 UK homes. The facility will also include battery storage to enhance the reliability and security of the UK power grid.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2022**: Quinbrook secured a Contract for Difference (CfD) for Project Fortress, which will support the deployment of low-carbon power generation. This 15-year contract was awarded under the fourth round of the UK Government’s renewable energy auction, allowing Quinbrook to proceed with construction. The project was noted as the largest solar PV project to secure a CfD in this round.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2021**: Announcement of Project Fortress with expectations to commence construction in the first half of 2022.
- **July 2022**: Confirmation that construction is planned to start by the end of 2022 following the securing of the CfD.
