## GBR-01522-05450

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Kimberly-Clark Facility in Kent, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: 12-04-2023
  - **Details**: Kimberly-Clark has partnered with HYRO to produce clean hydrogen on-site to power its factories in Kent and Wales. The facility modifications will include the installation of electrolyzers powered by renewable energy sources (wind and solar) to generate clean hydrogen, as well as hydrogen-ready boilers. The combined capacity of the two facilities is 22.5 MW, which is expected to significantly reduce carbon emissions.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: 12-04-2023
  - **Details**: HYRO, a joint venture between Octopus Energy Generation and RES, aims to invest £3 billion ($3.7 billion) into green hydrogen plants across the UK, which includes the modifications at Kimberly-Clark's facilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: 12-04-2023
  - **Details**: The announcement of the partnership between Kimberly-Clark and HYRO marks a significant milestone in the transition to clean fuels at the Kent facility. The project aims to decarbonize the manufacturing process by switching from gas to clean hydrogen.

#### 4. Labour
- **Date**: 12-04-2023
  - **Details**: The partnership is expected to create jobs as the transition to clean fuels is implemented, although specific job creation numbers were not provided in the article.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date**: 12-04-2023
  - **Details**: The facility is not exclusively owned by Kimberly-Clark; it is part of a partnership with HYRO, which is a joint venture between Octopus Energy Generation and RES.
