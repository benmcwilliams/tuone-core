## GBR-01523-01232

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Pivot Power Facility in Sandwell, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2021**: Pivot Power has broken ground on a **50MW battery storage facility** in Sandwell. The facility will store enough electricity to power over **100,000 homes for two hours** once fully operational. It will connect to the transmission network at National Grid’s Bustleholme substation, enabling it to automatically charge and discharge to balance supply and demand and manage energy intermittency. The facility is part of Pivot Power’s Energy Superhub network, which aims to deliver up to **2GW of transmission-connected battery storage** across the UK.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2021**: The Sandwell battery storage facility is part of Pivot Power's second Energy Superhub project, which is designed to enhance the integration of renewable energy and increase the resiliency of the electricity system. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the articles, but the project is part of a broader initiative to deploy up to **40 similar sites** throughout the UK.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2021**: Work has officially started on the **50MW/100MWh battery storage project** in Sandwell. This facility is designed to enable mass-scale, rapid electric vehicle (EV) charging and will replicate core elements of the Energy Superhub Oxford project.
