### Everoze Facility Timeline - Bristol, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is a **20MW battery storage asset** located on Feeder Road in Bristol. 
   - The project was originally intended to be a **diesel-fuelled STOR plant** but was transformed into a battery storage project following community opposition.
   - The construction of the Feeder Road battery energy storage project was completed by March 2023, and it is currently being commissioned.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - Thrive Renewables has invested **£29 million** in clean energy infrastructure in the Bristol area, which includes the Feeder Road battery project.
   - An additional investment of approximately **£100,000** is planned for upgrades to enhance the site's biodiversity.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 2021:** Thrive Renewables acquired the Feeder Road battery project.
   - **March 2023:** The construction of the Feeder Road battery storage project was completed, and it is in the commissioning phase.

4. **Labour:**
   - The project aims to create **new green jobs** as part of the community ownership initiative and the overall development of clean energy infrastructure in Bristol.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - Thrive Renewables is the owner of the facility and is offering local residents the opportunity to co-own a share of the battery storage asset, marking a community ownership model for this project.
