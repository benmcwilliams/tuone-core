### Evolutionary Timeline of the Tevva Facility in Tilbury, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2023**: Tevva commenced deliveries of its 7.5t battery-electric truck to commercial fleet customers, focusing on urban delivery. #### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2023**: A proposed merger with ElectraMeccanica was announced, which is expected to enhance Tevva's production capabilities and market reach. Concurrently, a $6 million credit facility was approved to provide Tevva with additional working capital to accelerate vehicle deliveries.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones

- **August 2023**: The merger with ElectraMeccanica was unanimously approved by both companies' Boards of Directors, with the transaction expected to close in the fourth quarter of 2023.

#### 4. Labour

- **August 2023**: Following the merger with ElectraMeccanica, Tevva shareholders will own 76.5% of the combined company, while ElectraMeccanica shareholders will own 23.5%. The combined entity will operate as Tevva, Inc., indicating a shift in ownership structure post-merger.
