### Evolutionary Timeline of the NAC International Facility in Sheffield, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: NAC International is involved in a project to develop a corrosion-resistant canister for the deep disposal of used nuclear fuel. This project is a collaboration with Deep Isolation EMEA Ltd, the Nuclear Advanced Manufacturing Research Centre (NAMRC), and the University of Sheffield. The canister is designed for safe encapsulation of used fuel assemblies in deep borehole repositories located 1-3 km underground. The project aims to establish a manufacturing supply chain for these canisters, which is crucial for the deployment of small modular reactors (SMRs).
