## GBR-01605-02756

### Evolutionary Timeline of the China National Building Material Company Facility in Liverpool, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2016:** The facility is part of a joint venture that will produce building integrated photovoltaics (BIPV) pre-fabricated homes. The production capacity is set to ramp up to 25,000 BIPV homes annually by 2025. The first batch of properties will be constructed in Liverpool using a design developed by Barcelona Housing Systems, which incorporates low-carbon and energy-efficient features.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **December 2016:** A joint venture deal worth £2.5 billion ($3.12 billion) was signed between Your Housing Group (YHG) and WElink, with support from CNBM. This investment will facilitate the establishment of six prefab home factories across the U.K., including the one in Liverpool.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2016:** Announcement of the joint venture and the plan to create six factories over the next five years, with the first pilot schemes to be unveiled in 2017, delivering an initial 2,000 solar-powered homes.

4. **Labour:**
   - **December 2016:** The project is expected to create a total of 1,000 jobs across the U.K. as a result of the joint venture and the establishment of the factories.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **December 2016:** The facility is part of a joint venture involving China National Building Material Company (CNBM), British renewable energy specialists WElink, and Your Housing Group (YHG). Therefore, CNBM does not own the facility exclusively but is a key partner in the joint venture.
