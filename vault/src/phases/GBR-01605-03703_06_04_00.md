## GBR-01605-03703

### Evolutionary Timeline of the WElink Facility in Liverpool, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Production Focus:** The facility will produce building integrated photovoltaics (BIPV) pre-fabricated homes.
   - **Production Capacity:** The project aims to deliver up to 25,000 BIPV homes annually by 2025.
   - **Initial Production:** Five pilot schemes are set to unveil in 2017, delivering an initial 2,000 solar-powered homes.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Investment Amount:** A joint venture deal worth £2.5 billion ($3.12 billion) was signed to support the project.
   - **Support and Financing:** The project is backed by China National Building Material Company (CNBM), which will also finance the construction of the six factories across the U.K.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Announcement Date:** The joint venture was announced on December 19, 2016.
   - **Construction Timeline:** The construction of six prefab home factories is planned over the next five years, with the first batch of properties in Liverpool.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Job Creation:** The partnership is expected to create a total of 1,000 jobs across the country as a result of the project.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Joint Venture:** The facility is part of a joint venture between WElink and Your Housing Group (YHG), with support from CNBM. It is not exclusively owned by WElink but is a collaborative effort with multiple stakeholders involved.
