### Evolutionary Timeline of Your Housing Group Facility in Liverpool, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2016:** Your Housing Group (YHG) enters a joint venture with WElink and China National Building Material Company (CNBM) to produce building integrated photovoltaics (BIPV) pre-fabricated homes. The facility in Liverpool will be part of a larger initiative to create six factories across the U.K. that will produce up to 25,000 BIPV homes annually by 2025. The first batch of properties will be constructed in Liverpool using a design developed by Barcelona Housing Systems, focusing on low-carbon and energy-efficient homes.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **December 2016:** The joint venture is backed by a £2.5 billion ($3.12 billion) investment, which aims to support the construction of the six factories and the production of solar-powered homes. This investment is part of a broader strategy to enhance the U.K.'s solar industry and address housing needs.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2016:** The joint venture is officially announced, with plans to unveil five pilot schemes in 2017, delivering an initial 2,000 solar-powered homes. Full production is expected to ramp up to 25,000 homes annually by 2025.

4. **Labour:**
   - **December 2016:** The partnership is projected to create approximately 1,000 jobs across the U.K. as a result of the establishment of the factories and the production of the homes.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **December 2016:** The facility is part of a joint venture between Your Housing Group (YHG) and WElink, with support from CNBM. This indicates that YHG does not own the facility exclusively but is part of a collaborative effort with other entities.
