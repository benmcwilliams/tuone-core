## GBR-01610-01510

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Canadian Solar Facility in England, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** February 2018
   - **Details:** Canadian Solar sold a portfolio of 24 operational solar PV plants with a total capacity of 142 MWp. The plants are capable of generating approximately 142,000 MWh of solar power annually, sufficient to power around 46,000 homes and reduce CO2 emissions by 56,800 tons per year. The sale included 272,000 Canadian Solar modules, and the company will continue to provide operations and maintenance services for these plants.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** February 2018
   - **Details:** The sale of the solar portfolio was valued at approximately $267.7 million. This transaction helped Canadian Solar reduce its non-recourse project debt by about $172.2 million, allowing the company to strengthen its balance sheet.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** February 2018
   - **Details:** The sale of the 24 operational solar PV plants to Greencoat Solar II LP was announced. The plants are part of the U.K.’s Renewable Obligation Certificate (ROC) and Feed-in Tariff (FIT) schemes, which provide inflation-linked solar power generation revenues for a period of 20 years.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** February 2018
   - **Details:** The article does not specify job creation or job loss directly related to the sale of the plants. However, Canadian Solar's commitment to provide operations and maintenance services may imply ongoing employment opportunities related to these services.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** February 2018
   - **Details:** The facility is no longer exclusively owned by Canadian Solar, as the 24 solar plants were sold to Greencoat Solar II LP, a subsidiary of Greencoat Capital LLP. Canadian Solar retains operational and maintenance responsibilities for the plants.
