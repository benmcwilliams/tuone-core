## GBR-01610-08938

### Evolutionary Timeline of the GE Hitachi Nuclear Energy Facility in England, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2022**: GE Hitachi Nuclear Energy (GEH) submitted a Generic Design Assessment (GDA) entry application for its BWRX-300 small modular reactor (SMR) to the UK Department for Business, Energy and Industrial Strategy. The BWRX-300 is a 300 MWe water-cooled, natural circulation SMR featuring passive safety systems.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2024**: GEH is receiving funding from the UK government's Future Nuclear Enabling Fund programme to cover its costs and those of the regulators for the GDA process.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2024**: Step 1 of the GDA for the BWRX-300 began, focusing on agreeing the scope and schedule for Step 2.
- **December 2024**: Step 1 of the GDA has been successfully completed, allowing GEH to proceed to Step 2, which will assess the adequacy of the BWRX-300 design for deployment in England and Wales. Step 2 is expected to be completed by December 2025.
