- **06-12-2023**: Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners (CIP) announced the commencement of construction for the Coalburn 1 project, a 500MW/1GWh Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) located in Coalburn, Scotland. This facility is set to be one of the largest of its kind in Europe, designed to reduce reliance on fossil fuels during peak demand and lower carbon dioxide emissions. The project will utilize Canadian Solar’s SolBank energy storage solution and is expected to begin operations in Q4 2025.
