### Holtec Britain Facility Timeline - South Yorkshire, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2024**: Holtec Britain selected South Yorkshire for its new Small Modular Reactor (SMR) factory. The facility will produce the SMR-300, a pressurized water reactor with a capacity of approximately 300 MW of electrical power or 1050 MW of thermal power for process applications. The SMR-300 has undergone several design evolutions, including the incorporation of forced flow capability.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2023**: Holtec secured GBP30 million from the UK government’s Future Nuclear Enabling Fund to initiate the generic design assessment (GDA) process for the SMR-300.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2024**: Holtec announced the selection of South Yorkshire as the location for the SMR factory, which is part of a broader plan to deploy around 5 GWe of SMRs in serial production in the UK by 2050. The company is working to finalize its factory business plan to support its Final Investment Decision, which is expected to be made in 2029.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2024**: The new factory is projected to create hundreds of well-paid, high-skilled jobs in South Yorkshire, contributing significantly to the local economy with an estimated GBP1.5 billion (USD2 billion) in Gross Value Added.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The articles do not specify the ownership structure of the facility, but it is implied that Holtec Britain will have a significant presence and commitment to the UK market through local sourcing and partnerships.
