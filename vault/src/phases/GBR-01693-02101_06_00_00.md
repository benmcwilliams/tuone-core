### Martifer Facility in Cambridgeshire, GBR - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** March 2014
   - **Details:** Martifer Solar is building a portfolio of PV power plants in the UK, including projects in Cambridgeshire. The total capacity of the portfolio is 78.4MWp, with individual project capacities ranging from 9MW to 24.67MW. The combined output is expected to generate approximately 69.05GWh of energy annually, sufficient to supply the average consumption of 38,815 inhabitants and offset around 31,582 tons of CO2 emissions each year. Martifer Solar will also provide operation and maintenance (O&M) services for optimal performance.
