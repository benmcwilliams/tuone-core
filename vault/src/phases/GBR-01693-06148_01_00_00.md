### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sunnica Energy Facility in Cambridgeshire, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2019**: The Sunnica Energy Farm is proposed as a 500 MW solar-plus-storage project, which will include large-scale storage capacity. The facility will consist of two sections: Sunnica East in West Suffolk and Sunnica West in East Cambridgeshire. The project aims to utilize the latest solar and storage technologies, including lithium-ion batteries and solar modules ranging from 250 W to 400 W or more.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2021**: Sunnica Energy submits an application for a Development Consent Order (DCO) to the Planning Inspectorate, which is required for projects over 50 MW and qualifies as a nationally significant infrastructure project.
