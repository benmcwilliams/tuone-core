## GBR-01706-01010

### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDF Facility in Fife, GBR (Neart na Gaoithe Offshore Wind Farm)

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2022**: The Neart na Gaoithe (NnG) offshore wind farm, with a production capacity of 450MW, is under construction 15km off the coast of Fife. The project will consist of 54 turbine towers, each 90 meters tall, and will utilize Siemens Gamesa's wind turbine technology.
- **October 2024**: The facility achieves its first power generation, supplying electricity to the UK’s national grid. The wind farm features 54 Siemens Gamesa 8MW wind turbine generators and two offshore transformer platforms. Once fully operational, it is expected to power approximately 375,000 households and eliminate over 400,000 tonnes of CO2 emissions annually.
- **November 2024**: The facility is expected to be fully commissioned by summer 2025, with ongoing turbine installation and commissioning.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2022**: A £40 million investment in the Port of Dundee is highlighted, facilitating the delivery and assembly of turbine components for the NnG project.
- **October 2024**: The project has engaged with various Scottish businesses, contributing to local economic benefits and supporting 24 community initiatives across the east of Scotland.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2022**: The commissioning of the NnG project is delayed to 2024 due to the Covid-19 pandemic. The new Contract for Difference start date is set for June 2024.
- **March 2022**: The first turbine blades, each 83.5 meters long, arrive at the Port of Dundee, marking a significant milestone in the construction process.
- **October 2024**: The facility generates its first power, marking the start of electricity supply to the national grid.
- **November 2024**: The project is anticipated to be fully commissioned by summer 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2022**: The project’s new operations and maintenance building at Eyemouth is set to create 50 new jobs.
- **October 2024**: The operations and maintenance base currently employs 27 staff, with expectations of generating around 50 jobs over the wind farm's 25-year lifespan.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **March 2022**: The Neart na Gaoithe project is jointly owned by EDF Renewables and ESB, indicating a partnership rather than exclusive ownership by a single entity.
