## GBR-01706-01592

### Evolutionary Timeline of the ESB Facility in Fife, GBR (Neart na Gaoithe Offshore Wind Farm)

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2022**: The Neart na Gaoithe (NnG) offshore wind farm, a joint project by EDF Renewables and ESB, is under construction 15 km off the coast of Fife. The facility is designed to produce 450 MW of renewable energy using 54 Siemens Gamesa 8 MW wind turbines. The first power generation is expected in 2023, with full commissioning delayed to 2024 due to the Covid-19 pandemic.
- **October 2024**: The facility successfully generates its first power, supplying electricity to the UK’s national grid. The project is expected to power approximately 375,000 households and eliminate over 400,000 tonnes of CO2 emissions annually.
- **November 2024**: The offshore wind farm continues to progress, with turbine installation and commissioning expected to be completed by summer 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2022**: The Port of Dundee has seen a £40 million investment to support the NnG project, including the construction of a custom-built wind turbine marshalling facility.
- **October 2024**: The project has engaged with various Scottish businesses, contributing to local economic benefits and supporting 24 community initiatives across the east of Scotland.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2022**: The project is delayed to 2024 for commissioning due to pandemic impacts. The first turbine components are scheduled for delivery to the Port of Dundee.
- **March 2022**: The first 18 turbine blades arrive at the Port of Dundee, marking a significant milestone in the construction process.
- **October 2024**: The facility achieves its first power generation, marking a critical milestone in the project timeline.
- **November 2024**: The project is expected to be fully commissioned by summer 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2022**: The operations and maintenance building at Eyemouth is set to create 50 new jobs.
- **October 2024**: The operations and maintenance base currently employs 27 staff, with an expectation of generating around 50 jobs over the wind farm's 25-year lifespan.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **March 2022**: The Neart na Gaoithe project is jointly owned by EDF Renewables and ESB, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by a single entity. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones of the Neart na Gaoithe offshore wind farm facility, highlighting its technological advancements, investment efforts, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
