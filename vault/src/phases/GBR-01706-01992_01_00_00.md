### Evolutionary Timeline of the TagEnergy Facility in Fife, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2022**: TagEnergy acquired a 100% stake in the Pitkevy battery storage facility, which has a production capacity of 49.9MW/99.8MWh. This facility is TagEnergy's first split-contract project, with Tesla providing the battery systems.
- **April 2024**: Construction of the Pitkevy facility is underway, with operations scheduled to begin at the end of 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2024**: TagEnergy reached financial close on the Pitkevy facility, financed by a non-recourse green loan package. This financing was originally secured for the Lakeside Battery Energy Storage System project but was adapted to include the Pitkevy site. The financial backing comes from Santander UK, Rabobank, and Triple Point.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2022**: TagEnergy announced the acquisition of the Pitkevy battery storage facility.
- **February 2024**: Financial close was reached for the Pitkevy facility.
- **April 2024**: Construction of the Pitkevy facility commenced, with an operational start date projected for the end of 2024.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
