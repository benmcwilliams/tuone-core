## GBR-01706-02439

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Siemens Gamesa Facility in Fife, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2024**: The Siemens Gamesa facility is involved in the production of 54 wind turbine generators (WTGs), each with a capacity of 8 MW, for the Neart na Gaoithe (NnG) offshore wind farm. The total production capacity of the wind farm is 450 MW. The facility has completed the installation of the foundation jackets and is in the process of turbine installation and commissioning, which is expected to be completed by summer 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2018**: EDF Renewables secured the offshore wind farm project through a competitive bidding process.
- **November 2019**: ESB acquired a 50% share in the NnG project, indicating a significant investment in the development of the facility.
- **August 2020**: Offshore construction work commenced, following onshore construction that began in November 2019.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2024**: The NnG offshore wind farm began generating its first power and supplying electricity to the UK’s national grid.
- **Summer 2025**: The wind farm is anticipated to be fully commissioned, with turbine installation and commissioning expected to be completed in the coming months.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2024**: The operations and maintenance base at Eyemouth currently employs 27 staff, primarily local workers. Over the 25-year lifespan of the wind farm, it is projected that around 50 jobs will be generated at the facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The NnG offshore wind farm is owned jointly by EDF Renewables UK and ESB, indicating a joint venture ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by Siemens Gamesa.
