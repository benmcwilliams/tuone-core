### Evolutionary Timeline of the TagEnergy Facility in Abernethy, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **November 2021:** TagEnergy acquired a 60% stake in the Jamesfield Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) project in Abernethy as part of a joint venture with Harmony Energy. The facility is designed to have a capacity of 49 MW and utilizes two-hour duration Tesla Megapack lithium-ion batteries, along with Tesla’s Autobidder AI software for real-time trading and control.
   - **March 2024:** The Jamesfield BESS was confirmed to be online, with commissioning starting gradually from November 2023 and construction completed in early March 2024.
