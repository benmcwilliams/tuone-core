### Exagen Facility Timeline: Brockworth Road, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2024
   - **Details:** Exagen received planning permission to build a 20MW/40MWh battery energy storage facility known as the Brockworth Road Energy Reserve. The facility will have an export capacity of approximately 20MW and a duration of two hours. It is designed to help reduce reliance on imported gas and enhance energy security by integrating more renewable energy sources into the grid.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** April 2024
   - **Details:** The project is supported by funding from the Octopus Energy Development Partnership, which is part of Octopus Energy Generation. This partnership indicates a financial backing that aids Exagen in expanding its project pipeline.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** April 2024
   - **Details:** Tewkesbury Borough Council granted planning permission for the facility, marking a significant milestone in the project's development. The facility is part of Exagen's strategy to secure planning permission and enter construction for 500MW of UK sites annually.
