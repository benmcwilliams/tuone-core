## GBR-02005-02438

### Evolutionary Timeline of the ScottishPower Facility in Highlands, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2021**: ScottishPower, in collaboration with the Port of Cromarty Firth (PCF), is involved in establishing a "green hydrogen hub" in the Highlands. The facility aims to distribute green hydrogen produced in Norway to customers in the Highlands and across the UK. The production capacity is linked to Gen2 Energy's plant in Norway, which is expected to produce 11,700 tonnes of green hydrogen annually starting December 2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2021**: The memorandum of understanding (MoU) signed between PCF and Gen2 Energy is seen as a significant step towards developing substantial production infrastructure for green hydrogen in Scotland. This initiative is expected to attract investments and support the transition to clean energy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2021**: Plans to establish a "world-leading" green hydrogen hub on the Cromarty Firth were announced as part of the North of Scotland Hydrogen Programme (NSHP), which includes ScottishPower and several distillers.
- **May 2021**: The MoU with Gen2 Energy marks a significant milestone in creating a hydrogen value chain between Norway and the UK.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2021**: The establishment of the green hydrogen hub is anticipated to create skilled jobs and business opportunities in Scotland, contributing positively to the local economy and energy transition plans.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2021**: The facility is part of a collaborative effort involving ScottishPower and the Port of Cromarty Firth, indicating a partnership rather than exclusive ownership by ScottishPower. The involvement of multiple stakeholders, including Gen2 Energy and various distillers, suggests a joint venture approach to the development of the hydrogen hub. 

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic moves related to the ScottishPower facility in the Highlands, focusing on its role in the emerging green hydrogen sector.
