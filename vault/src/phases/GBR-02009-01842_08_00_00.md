### Evolutionary Timeline of Ørsted's Hornsea Facility, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10-08-2018**: Ørsted's Hornsea Project Two is announced, with a production capacity of 1.4GW, featuring 165 Siemens Gamesa turbines. The project is located 89 kilometers off the Yorkshire Coast and is expected to be fully operational by 2022.
- **14-02-2019**: The first turbine of the Hornsea 1 offshore wind farm, with a capacity of 1218MW, is installed and begins delivering power to the grid. This project uses 174 Siemens Gamesa 7MW turbines.
- **28-05-2021**: The first turbine of the Hornsea 2 offshore wind farm is installed. Hornsea 2 will feature 165 Siemens Gamesa 8MW turbines, with a total capacity of 1.4GW, and is expected to be completed in 2022.
- **31-03-2023**: Ørsted announces the Hornsea 3 project, which will further increase the combined capacity of Hornsea 1, 2, and 3 to over 5GW, making it one of the largest offshore wind zones globally.

- **10-08-2018**: Ørsted's Hornsea Project Two won a Contract for Difference in September 2017, allowing it to be built at the lowest price for offshore wind in the UK.
