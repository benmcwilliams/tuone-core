1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Cleve Hill Solar Park has a production capacity of 373MW and is described as the UK’s largest solar and battery energy storage project. The facility includes a proposed battery energy storage system (BESS) with an intended capacity of 150MW, although this application was recently refused.

2. **Investment and Development:**
