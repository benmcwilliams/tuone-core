### GT Energy Facility in Salisbury, GBR - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: GT Energy, a subsidiary of IGas Energy, is set to develop a geothermal heat plant for Salisbury District Hospital. The facility aims to fulfill the hospital's full heat requirements, which exceed 20 GWhth per annum. The project will involve the creation of an Innovation Partnership to collaborate on the development phases of the geothermal project.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: GT Energy plans to apply for funding through the Public Sector Decarbonisation Scheme (PSDS) to support the geothermal project, although the project's progression is not dependent on receiving this grant.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: GT Energy is in the initial phases of the geothermal project, which includes geological feasibility studies, consenting, and construction of wells and the energy center. The expected start of heat supply is projected for early 2026, subject to planning and regulatory approvals.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2023**: The project is anticipated to create jobs during the construction and operational phases, although specific job creation numbers have not been detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2023**: GT Energy is expected to own and operate the geothermal facility exclusively, as indicated by the statements from the COO of IGas and the Director of Estates at Salisbury NHS Trust.
