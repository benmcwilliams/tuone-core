## GBR-02069-01913

### Evolutionary Timeline of the SSE Facility in Warrington, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2023:** SSE Renewables announced a final investment decision to construct a 150MW/300MWh battery energy storage system (BESS) at the site of the former Fiddler’s Ferry coal-fired power station in Warrington, Cheshire. The facility will utilize the existing grid connection and is designed to operate for up to two hours at a time. This project marks SSE's fourth BESS project, contributing to a total pipeline capacity of 670MW for projects currently under construction.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **December 2023:** The announcement of the final investment decision indicates a significant financial commitment to the 150MW BESS project, although specific investment amounts were not disclosed in the articles.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2023:** SSE Renewables made a final investment decision to proceed with the BESS project.
   - **January 2024:** SSE Renewables officially greenlit the construction of the 150MW BESS project at the Fiddler’s Ferry site.

4. **Labour:**
   - The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the construction of the BESS facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is owned by SSE Renewables, which previously owned and operated the Fiddler’s Ferry coal-fired power station before its decommissioning in March 2020. There is no indication of joint ownership or partnerships mentioned in the articles.
