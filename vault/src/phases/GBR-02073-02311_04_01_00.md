1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **April 2021:** ScottishPower submitted a planning application for the UK's largest electrolyser, a 20MW unit, to be installed at the Whitelee facility. This electrolyser will produce up to 8 tonnes of green hydrogen per day, sufficient to fuel over 550 buses for daily trips between Glasgow and Edinburgh. The facility will also include a combined solar and battery energy storage scheme of up to 40MW and 50MW respectively.

2. **Investment and Development:**
