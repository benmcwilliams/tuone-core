## GBR-02091-01969

### Evolutionary Timeline of Statera Energy Facility in Thurrock, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2023**: Statera Energy announced the development of a 300MW/600MWh battery energy storage system (BESS) combined with a 270MW flexible generation plant (gas-fired). The facility has planning consent for a total of 900MW of energy storage and generation. The BESS is designed to provide two hours of discharge capacity and is expected to be one of the largest in the UK and Europe.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2023**: Statera secured up to £300 million in debt financing from Lloyds Bank, with participation from NatWest, Santander, and Siemens Financial Services. The financing is divided into two phases: £144 million for the BESS and £156 million for the gas plant. This funding is crucial for the development of the facility, which is classified as a nationally significant infrastructure project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2023**: Statera won a capacity market auction, securing 150MW of battery storage and 270MW of flexible generation at a price of £63 per kilowatt.
- **November 2023**: The company announced that operations for the BESS and flexible generation projects are expected to begin in late 2026.
