## GBR-02093-01970

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Statkraft Facility at Scurf Dyke Solar Farm, Driffield, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Production Capacity:** The Scurf Dyke Solar Farm has a production capacity of 80 MWp (megawatts peak) for solar energy. Additionally, there is an adjacent 8 MW storage facility.
   - **Updates:** Statkraft has agreed to optimize the adjacent storage facility as part of their operations.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Ownership Transition:** The facility was acquired by FP Lux Group, managed by re:cap global investors AG, from German renewables company BayWa r.e. AG in January 2023. BayWa r.e. continues to provide engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) services.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Power Purchase Agreement (PPA):** On July 10, 2024, Statkraft signed a PPA for the Scurf Dyke Solar Farm, which includes provisions for managing power generation during negative price periods.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Job Creation/Loss:** The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or loss associated with the facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Ownership Status:** Statkraft does not exclusively own the facility; it is owned by FP Lux Group, with BayWa r.e. involved as the EPC service provider.
