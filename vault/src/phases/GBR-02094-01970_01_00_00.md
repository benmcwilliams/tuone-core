### Evolutionary Timeline of the Statkraft Facility in York, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2020**: Statkraft was selected to manage a hybrid solar and battery energy storage project in York, which is claimed to be the UK’s largest solar and battery energy storage project. The facility includes a **35 MW solar farm** and **27 MWh battery storage**. It is noted for being the most technically advanced solar project in the UK, utilizing both **single-axis trackers** and **bifacial solar panels**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2020**: The partnership between Statkraft and Warrington Renewables (the owner of the facility) is highlighted as a collaboration that aims to deliver low carbon, subsidy-free renewable power. The specific financial investments related to this project are not detailed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2020**: Statkraft signed an agreement to manage the York hybrid solar and battery site, marking the beginning of their operational involvement.
- **December 2022**: Statkraft announced the expansion of its relationship with Warrington Borough Council, indicating ongoing management and optimization of the York facility as part of a broader strategy to optimize multiple renewable energy and battery sites across the UK and Ireland.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility in York is **owned by Warrington Renewables**, which is wholly owned by the local authority, Warrington Borough Council. Statkraft does not own the facility but manages it under a contractual agreement.
