### Evolutionary Timeline of the Riversimple Facility in Aberdeen, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: The facility is intended to produce the Rasa hydrogen fuel cell-powered car, which is described as “the most energy efficient car on the planet.”
- **Production Capacity**: Riversimple plans to manufacture 5,000 cars per year at the proposed facility.
- **Unique Engineering**: The Rasa utilizes a proton exchange membrane fuel cell, generating electricity from hydrogen and oxygen, with a range of 300 miles and an efficiency of over 200 mpg equivalent.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Considerations**: Riversimple is exploring the establishment of a manufacturing plant in Aberdeen, contingent on receiving the right incentives, particularly those associated with the North East Scotland Green Freeport initiative.
- **Green Freeport Status**: The company has indicated that the success of the green freeport bid would significantly enhance the attractiveness of the region for investment.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Initial Announcement**: On **July 20, 2022**, Riversimple announced its interest in establishing a manufacturing facility in Aberdeen, contingent on the availability of incentives and the success of the green freeport bid.
- **Active Discussions**: The company is in active talks regarding the potential location and establishment of the facility, highlighting the attractiveness of Aberdeen due to existing hydrogen initiatives.

#### 4. Labour

- **Ownership Status**: The articles do not specify whether Riversimple would own the facility exclusively or if it would involve joint ventures or partnerships. However, the company is actively considering the establishment of operations in collaboration with regional partners.
