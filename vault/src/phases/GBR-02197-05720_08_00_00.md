## GBR-02197-05720

### Evolutionary Timeline of the ORE Catapult Facility in Aberdeen, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2022**: Announcement of the National Floating Wind Innovation Centre (FLOWIC) to be located at Aberdeen's Energy Transition Zone. The facility aims to accelerate the commercialization of floating offshore wind technology, focusing initially on digital simulation and modeling, and later on testing and validation of key components such as moorings, anchors, dynamic cables, and electrical systems.
- **March 2024**: Official opening of FLOWIC, equipped with advanced technologies including a dynamic cable flex fatigue rig, a large-scale anchor test rig, a floating offshore wind scale motion simulator (Hexapod), and a virtual reality studio for engineers. The facility is designed to support the development of a floating wind supply chain and enhance the UK's position in the global floating wind market.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2022**: The facility is co-funded with a total investment of £9 million (€10.8 million), with £4 million from the Energy Transition Zone (ETZ) for capital costs and £5 million from ORE Catapult for operational expenses.
- **March 2024**: The opening of FLOWIC is supported by funding from both the Scottish Government and Innovate UK, emphasizing the facility's role in the commercialisation of floating offshore wind technology.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2022**: Announcement of the establishment of the National Floating Wind Innovation Centre in Aberdeen.
- **March 2024**: Official opening of the FLOWIC by Scotland’s First Minister, marking its readiness to support the floating offshore wind sector.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2024**: The operation of FLOWIC is expected to create jobs as supply chain businesses transition towards offshore wind, with projections indicating that floating offshore wind could generate over 29,000 jobs in the UK by 2050.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2022**: The facility is a collaborative project between ORE Catapult and ETZ Limited, indicating a joint venture structure for the ownership and operation of the facility. ORE Catapult is a key player in the management and operational aspects of the centre. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones of the ORE Catapult facility in Aberdeen, highlighting its role in advancing floating offshore wind technology in the UK.
