### Evolutionary Timeline of the Port of Aberdeen Facility, Aberdeen, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2023:** The Port of Aberdeen is involved in a project to build and showcase the world’s first liquid hydrogen autonomous vessel and bunkering infrastructure. This initiative aims to contribute to the decarbonisation of the maritime industry. The project will utilize Unitrove's mobile fuelling technology at the Port of Aberdeen, supporting the operation of hydrogen-powered autonomous ships for cargo transport.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **February 2023:** The project received a £3.8 million grant from the UK Government as part of the £5.4 million initiative funded through the Clean Maritime Demonstration Competition Round 3 (CMDC3). This funding is aimed at supporting the development of innovative technologies for maritime decarbonisation.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **February 2023:** The HI-FIVED consortium, which includes the Port of Aberdeen, announced plans to deliver the project by autumn 2024. The project aims to establish a green shipping corridor between Aberdeen and the Orkney and Shetland islands.
