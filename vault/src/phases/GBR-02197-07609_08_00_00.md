### Evolutionary Timeline of the Boskalis Facility in Aberdeen, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2017**: Boskalis, as the Offshore Balance of Plant contractor, was involved in the production and delivery of 21 anchors for the wind turbine foundations at the Aberdeen Bay wind farm (also known as the European Offshore Wind Deployment Centre - EOWDC). The facility is designed to support the installation of eleven MHI Vestas wind turbines, with a total capacity of 92.4 MW.
- **September 2018**: The Aberdeen Bay wind farm officially opened, featuring two MHI Vestas V164 8.8 MW turbines and nine 8.4 MW turbines. The project includes innovative technologies such as suction bucket jacket foundations and 66kV cabling.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2018**: Vattenfall and the EU invested €3 million to study the environmental impact of the offshore wind farm on marine life, which is part of the broader development of the EOWDC.
