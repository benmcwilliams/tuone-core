### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility in Aberdeen, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** Ocean Winds, in partnership with Aker Offshore Wind, submitted joint bids for a total of 6GW of floating wind energy in the ScotWind leasing round. The project will utilize steel-based semi-submersible floaters employing Principle Power’s Windfloat technology. This initiative aims to harness Scotland's deepwater resources for clean energy production.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The project is expected to involve billions of pounds of investment in local supply chains and infrastructure, with a commitment to a minimum of 40% supply chain content in Scotland and 60% in the UK. Early investment priorities have already been established through agreements with suppliers.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The announcement of the joint bids for the ScotWind leasing round was made during the Floating Offshore Wind 2021 conference in Aberdeen. This marks a significant milestone in the development of floating offshore wind energy in Scotland.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The project is projected to create thousands of highly-skilled local jobs, contributing significantly to the local economy and supply chain.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** Ocean Winds is in a joint venture with Aker Offshore Wind for this facility, indicating that it does not have exclusive ownership of the facility.

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic plans for the Ocean Winds facility in Aberdeen, highlighting its role in advancing floating offshore wind technology in Scotland.
