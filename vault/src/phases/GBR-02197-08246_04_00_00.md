### Evolutionary Timeline of the Aberdeen Hydrogen Hub Facility, Aberdeen, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10/2020**: The Aberdeen Hydrogen Hub, led by Aberdeen City Council in collaboration with Opportunity North East (ONE) and Scottish Enterprise, aims to establish a commercial operation capable of delivering up to **3.5 tonnes of hydrogen per day** for public and private vehicle fleets and potentially rail networks.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12/2020**: The Scottish Government pledges **£100 million** over the next five years to support the hydrogen sector, which includes the development of the Aberdeen Hydrogen Hub. This investment is part of a broader ambition to generate **5 gigawatts (GW)** of renewable and low carbon hydrogen by **2030**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2020**: The Scottish Government publishes its Hydrogen Policy Statement, which outlines the framework for the hydrogen sector in Scotland, including the establishment of the Aberdeen Hydrogen Hub as a regional collaboration project.

#### 4. Labour
- **12/2020**: The Scottish Government's investment and the development of the hydrogen sector, including the Aberdeen Hydrogen Hub, are expected to create new jobs in hydrogen across the north-east, contributing to economic recovery and job security in the region.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **10/2020**: The Aberdeen Hydrogen Hub is a **collaborative project** involving multiple partners: Aberdeen City Council, Opportunity North East (ONE), and Scottish Enterprise, indicating a **joint venture** ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by a single entity.
