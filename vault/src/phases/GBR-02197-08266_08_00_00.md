### Aker Offshore Wind Facility in Aberdeen, GBR - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** Aker Offshore Wind, in partnership with Ocean Winds, submitted joint bids for a total of 6GW of floating wind energy in the ScotWind leasing round. The project aims to utilize steel-based semi-submersible floaters employing Principle Power’s Windfloat technology. The facility is designed to harness Scotland's deepwater resources for clean energy production.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The partnership indicated that the project would involve billions of pounds of investment in local supply chains and infrastructure, with a commitment to a minimum of 40% supply chain content in Scotland and 60% in the UK. This investment is expected to create thousands of jobs and significantly boost Scotland's economy.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The announcement of the joint bids for the ScotWind leasing round was made during the Floating Offshore Wind 2021 conference in Aberdeen. This marks a significant milestone in the project’s development, as it outlines the intention to develop floating offshore wind energy in Scotland.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The project is projected to create thousands of highly-skilled local jobs through the investments in local supply chains and infrastructure, as stated by Sian Lloyd-Rees, managing director of Aker Offshore Wind UK.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is a joint venture between Aker Offshore Wind and Ocean Winds, indicating that Aker does not own the facility exclusively but rather shares ownership with its partner.
