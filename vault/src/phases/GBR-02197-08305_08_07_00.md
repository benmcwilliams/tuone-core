### Archer Wind Facility Evolution Timeline - Aberdeen, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2024**: Archer Wind commenced the fabrication of the floating foundation for a 3 MW wind turbine as part of the Culzean pilot project. This project involves the installation of a single floating wind turbine located 220 kilometers off the coast of Aberdeen, designed to cover approximately 20% of the Culzean gas platform's energy needs.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2024**: Archer Wind was awarded a contract by TotalEnergies for the Culzean floating wind project, indicating a significant investment in offshore wind technology. The project was selected in the Crown Estate Scotland’s Innovation and Targeted Oil & Gas (INTOG) leasing round in 2023.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2024**: The first plate for the floating foundation was cut on November 19, marking the transition from planning to tangible progress in the project.
- **December 2024**: Delmar Systems was contracted to provide a total mooring solution for the floating wind turbine, further advancing the project’s development.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
