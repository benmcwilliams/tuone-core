## GBR-02197-08697

### Evolutionary Timeline of ETZ Ltd Facility in Aberdeen, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2022**: The ETZ Ltd facility is set to host the National Floating Wind Innovation Centre, located at Aberdeen's Energy Transition Zone at the Altens Industrial Estate. The centre will focus on accelerating the commercialisation of floating offshore wind technology, with an initial emphasis on digital simulation and modelling, followed by testing and validation of key components such as moorings, anchors, dynamic cables, and electrical systems.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2022**: The facility is backed by a total investment of £9 million (€10.8 million) for the National Floating Wind Innovation Centre. ETZ Ltd contributes £4 million towards capital costs, while the Offshore Renewable Energy (ORE) Catapult provides £5 million for operational expenses. This investment is part of a broader £26 million funding from the Scottish Government aimed at supporting the Energy Transition Zone.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2022**: The formal launch of the ETZ Ltd facility and the National Floating Wind Innovation Centre occurs, marking a significant milestone in the establishment of a hub for floating offshore wind technology in the UK.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2022**: The establishment of the National Floating Wind Innovation Centre is expected to contribute to job creation in the North East of Scotland, as it aims to support the development of a floating wind supply chain and enhance operations and maintenance practices in the offshore wind sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2022**: ETZ Ltd is involved in a collaborative effort with ORE Catapult for the development of the National Floating Wind Innovation Centre, indicating a partnership rather than exclusive ownership of the facility. The facility operates under a co-investment model, highlighting a joint venture approach to its establishment and operations. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic initiatives associated with the ETZ Ltd facility in Aberdeen, focusing on its role in advancing floating offshore wind technology.
