### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sarens Facility in Aberdeen, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **03-12-2024:** Sarens announced the opening of a Centre of Excellence in Aberdeen, Scotland, focused on offshore wind projects. The facility will enhance the city’s heavy lifting and engineered transport capabilities, supporting the growth of the UK’s offshore wind sector. The centre will be equipped with heavy-duty capabilities, including inspection pits, brake testing equipment, and a wash bay for the maintenance and repair of Sarens PSG’s fleet of cranes and other heavy lifting equipment.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **03-12-2024:** Sarens is investing £1.6 million in the new facility. This investment is part of a broader commitment, with Sarens having invested £12 million in equipment and operational facilities to support its operations in the offshore wind sector.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **03-12-2024:** Announcement of the Centre of Excellence in Aberdeen, with the facility expected to go live in the following year. The site is located in Aberdeen’s Energy Transition Zone at the Altens Industrial Estate, following a completed purchase from Forbes Homes.

4. **Labour:**
   - **03-12-2024:** The establishment of the Centre of Excellence will create 25 new jobs initially, contributing to the local economy and supporting the UK Government's target of achieving zero-carbon electricity generation by 2030.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **03-12-2024:** Sarens owns the facility exclusively, having completed the purchase from Forbes Homes, indicating full ownership of the Centre of Excellence in Aberdeen.
