### Evolutionary Timeline of the ABB Facility in Dogger Bank, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **30-10-2019**: ABB, in collaboration with Aibel, is set to deliver offshore converter platforms and a High Voltage Direct Current (HVDC) power transmission system for the Dogger Bank offshore wind farms. The facility will support the 3,600 MW Dogger Bank offshore wind farms, specifically the Creyke Beck A and B projects, each with a capacity of 1.2 GW. ABB will provide its HVDC light converter system, which will convert alternating current (AC) from the wind farms to direct current (DC) for transmission to the UK power grid.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **30-10-2019**: The project is moving towards a final investment decision expected in 2020, indicating significant financial commitments are anticipated for the development and construction phases led by SSE Renewables.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
