#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: The Dogger Bank Wind Farm, which includes the Vargronn facility, is set to produce a total of 3.6 GW of offshore wind energy through three phases: Dogger Bank A, B, and C, each with a capacity of 1.2 GW. The facility will utilize 277 GE Haliade-X offshore wind turbines, marking it as the largest offshore production facility in the world upon completion.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The project is a joint venture involving SSE Renewables (40%), Equinor (40%), and Vargronn (20%). SSE Renewables is leading the development and construction efforts, while Equinor will operate the facility for its expected operational life of around 35 years.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: The world's largest offshore wind jack-up vessel, Jan de Nul's Voltaire, arrives in the UK to assist in the construction of the Dogger Bank Wind Farm. The vessel is scheduled to install the first GE Haliade-X offshore wind turbine in early July 2023. The project is expected to achieve first power for Dogger Bank A in the summer of 2023, with completion anticipated in 2026.
