### Ceres Facility in Horsham, GBR - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 11-01-2022**: Ceres Power, based in Horsham, is noted for its next-generation fuel cell technology. The company has partnered with Korean conglomerate Doosan to build a 50MW factory for the production of its fuel cells. This facility aims to enhance the production capacity of Ceres' solid oxide technology, which can operate as both a fuel cell and an electrolyzer.

- **Date: 14-11-2022**: Ceres Power's solid oxide technology is highlighted as a unique approach that uses a ceramic electrolyte printed on steel sheets, allowing for low-cost and highly manufacturable fuel cells. The technology is flexible, capable of running on various fuels, and is designed for scalability, with aspirations for gigafactory-scale production.
