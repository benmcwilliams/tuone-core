### Foresight Facility Timeline - U.K., GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **06-08-2018:** Foresight Solar Fund completed the acquisition of a portfolio of 15 operational solar plants across the U.K. with a total capacity of 114 MW. This acquisition increased the total capacity managed by the fund to 788 MW across 43 PV projects. The plants range in size from 3.6 MW to 13.2 MW and are accredited under the U.K.’s Renewable Obligation Certification scheme.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **06-08-2018:** The acquisition cost was approximately £47 million ($60.7 million), funded by a share placement that raised around £48.1 million in June 2018. The portfolio has a debt facility worth £107.2 million provided by the Royal Bank of Scotland, with plans to refinance these and other U.K. projects within 12 months.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **06-08-2018:** The acquisition of the 15 solar projects was announced, marking a significant milestone as it positioned Foresight as the largest U.K.-listed dedicated solar energy investment company by installed capacity.
