### Evolutionary Timeline of Above Surveying Facility in U.K., GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06/2024**: Above Surveying announced the addition of large-scale photovoltaic (PV) condition monitoring to its Solargain digital twin tool. This enhancement was made possible through partnerships with Kiwa PI Berlin and 2DegreesKelvin. The Solargain tool now supports various functions including design and topographic mapping, construction project management, thermographic panel inspection, and operations and maintenance (O&M). The digital twin technology integrates drone thermography data and provides analytics to assist PV asset owners and operators.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06/2024**: The expansion of Solargain's functionality was enabled by collaborations with Kiwa PI Berlin and 2DegreesKelvin, focusing on quality assurance and engineering. This partnership aims to enhance the quality of data from manufacturing pre-shipment audits to operational monitoring.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06/2024**: Above Surveying's CEO, Will Hitchcock, highlighted the need for digital tools and drones to manage the growing complexity of solar PV assets. The company has been operational since 2015 and has conducted over 5,000 surveys globally. The recent updates to Solargain represent a significant milestone in the facility's technological capabilities.
