### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lavo Facility at Thornton Science Park, University of Chester, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2022**: The Lavo facility is set to demonstrate an innovative energy storage system based on storing green hydrogen in a patented metal hydride. This system includes a water purifier and an electrolyzer, designed for integration with solar PV systems. The facility aims to illustrate the scalability of Lavo’s modular, stackable hydrogen energy storage device.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2022**: The UK government, through the Department for Business Energy and Industrial Strategy (BEIS), provided financial backing of $264,000 (£141,000) to support the installation of the demonstration facility. This funding is part of the BEIS’s Longer Duration Energy Storage Demonstration program, which is part of a larger $1.87 billion (£1bn) Net Zero Innovation Portfolio aimed at accelerating the commercialization of innovative energy storage technologies.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2022**: GHD, a cornerstone investor in Lavo, announced the installation of the demonstration facility at the University of Chester’s Thornton Science Park. The project is intended to demonstrate the feasibility of a grid-connected energy storage system based on Lavo’s hydrogen technology.
