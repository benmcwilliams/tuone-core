### Evolutionary Timeline of the Shell Facility at St Fergus Gas Terminal, Aberdeenshire, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2021**: Shell, in partnership with Storegga and Harbour Energy, announced plans to establish a 20 MW blue hydrogen production facility at the St Fergus gas terminal. This facility is part of the Acorn project, which aims to utilize North Sea natural gas to produce blue hydrogen while capturing and storing associated CO2 emissions under the sea. The project is noted as one of the largest and most mature CCS (Carbon Capture and Storage) and hydrogen projects in the U.K.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2021**: The partnership between Storegga, Shell, and Harbour Energy was formalized, marking a significant milestone in the development of the Acorn project at the St Fergus gas terminal. The project aims to store at least 5 metric tons of CO2 per year by 2030.
