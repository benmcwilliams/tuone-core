## GBR-02353-01420

### Evolutionary Timeline of the TotalEnergies Facility in Orkney, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2021**: A consortium including TotalEnergies proposed a hydrogen production project on the Orkney Islands, specifically at the Flotta terminal. The facility aims to leverage abundant offshore wind resources to produce green hydrogen, positioning Orkney as a hydrogen hub. The project is linked to the development of new offshore wind farms in the area.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2021**: TotalEnergies, in partnership with Macquarie’s Green Investment Group and Renewable Infrastructure Development Group (RIDG), announced a £140 million initiative to support the local supply chain and infrastructure development related to the West of Orkney Windfarm project. This investment is aimed at enhancing the capabilities of suppliers and developing local skills, with a commitment to ensuring that a significant portion of the project’s content is sourced from Scotland.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2022**: The consortium submitted the Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) scoping report for the up to 2GW West of Orkney offshore wind farm. This report is a critical step in the project development process and outlines the studies and surveys planned for the project. The wind farm is intended to supply renewable power to the proposed Flotta Hydrogen Hub.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2021**: The hydrogen hub project is expected to create new jobs for individuals transitioning from the oil and gas industry, contributing to local economic development in Orkney.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **October 2021**: The facility is part of a joint venture involving TotalEnergies, Macquarie’s Green Investment Group, and RIDG. There is no indication of exclusive ownership by TotalEnergies; rather, it is a collaborative effort among the consortium partners. 

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic initiatives related to the TotalEnergies facility in Orkney, highlighting its focus on green hydrogen production and the integration with offshore wind energy projects.
