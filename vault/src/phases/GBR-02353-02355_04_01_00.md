## GBR-02353-02355

### Evolutionary Timeline of Macquarie’s Green Investment Group Facility in Orkney, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2021**: A consortium including Macquarie’s Green Investment Group, TotalEnergies, and Renewable Infrastructure Development Group (RIDG) proposed a hydrogen production facility at the Flotta terminal in Orkney. This facility aims to leverage the abundant offshore wind resources in the North Sea to produce green hydrogen, positioning Orkney as a hub for hydrogen production.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2022**: The consortium announced a £140 million initiative during the initial phase of the West of Orkney offshore wind farm project. This investment is aimed at developing the supply chain, driving skills development, and creating opportunities for businesses in Caithness and Orkney, which will support the hydrogen production facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2021**: The proposal for the green hydrogen hub was submitted, marking the initial step towards establishing the facility.
- **March 2022**: The consortium submitted the Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) scoping report for the West of Orkney offshore wind farm, which is intended to supply renewable power to the Flotta Hydrogen Hub once operational.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2021**: The hydrogen hub project is expected to create new jobs, particularly for those transitioning from the oil and gas industry, as indicated by local leaders' comments on the potential economic benefits for Orkney.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **October 2021**: The facility is part of a joint venture involving Macquarie’s Green Investment Group, TotalEnergies, and RIDG, indicating that it is not solely owned by Macquarie but rather a collaborative effort among multiple stakeholders. 

This timeline captures the key developments related to the Macquarie’s Green Investment Group facility in Orkney, highlighting its technological ambitions, investment strategies, project milestones, labor implications, and ownership structure.
