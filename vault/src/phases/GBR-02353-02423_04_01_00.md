### Evolutionary Timeline of the Renewable Infrastructure Development Group (RIDG) Facility in Orkney, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2021**: A proposal was submitted for a hydrogen production facility at the Flotta terminal in Orkney. The facility aims to leverage abundant offshore wind resources in the North Sea to produce green hydrogen. The project is part of a consortium that includes RIDG, Macquarie’s Green Investment Group, and TotalEnergies. The facility is envisioned to play a significant role in the energy transition and could serve both national and international hydrogen demand.

#### 2. Investment and Development

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2021**: The consortium, including RIDG, submitted a proposal for the green hydrogen hub. The project is in the evaluation phase, with ongoing assessments such as cable routing and nearshore geophysical surveys to understand the practical aspects of project delivery.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
