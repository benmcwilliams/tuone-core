## GBR-02370-07116

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Renewable Infrastructure Development Group Facility in Flotta, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12-10-2021**: A consortium including Renewable Infrastructure Development Group (RIDG), TotalEnergies, and Macquarie’s Green Investment Group (GIG) proposed to use electricity from a planned offshore wind project to power a green hydrogen production facility on the island of Flotta. The facility aims to leverage the renewable power generated by the proposed 2 GW West of Orkney Windfarm to produce green hydrogen, positioning the Flotta Terminal as a potential hydrogen hub.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12-10-2021**: The consortium's proposal for the green hydrogen production facility is supported by local stakeholders, including the European Marine Energy Centre (EMEC) Hydrogen. The project is contingent on securing a market for green hydrogen and discussions with the Scottish and UK governments regarding necessary mechanisms for realization.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12-10-2021**: The Offshore Wind Power Limited (OWPL) consortium has completed cable routing assessments and nearshore geophysical surveys as part of the project development process. The proposal was submitted during the ScotWind leasing round, indicating an early stage of project planning and development.

#### 4. Labour
- **12-10-2021**: The project aims to retain and upskill the current workforce at the Flotta Terminal while creating long-term skilled jobs during both the construction phase and hydrogen operations. This indicates a focus on job creation as the facility transitions to a diversified energy hub.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **12-10-2021**: The facility is part of a consortium involving RIDG, TotalEnergies, and GIG, in collaboration with Repsol Sinopec, which owns the Flotta Terminal. This suggests a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by RIDG.

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic plans for the Renewable Infrastructure Development Group facility in Flotta, GBR, as of the latest article published on October 12, 2021.
