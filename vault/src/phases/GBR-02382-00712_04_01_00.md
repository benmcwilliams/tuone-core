### Evolutionary Timeline of the Shell Facility in the North Sea, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2022:** The NortH2 consortium, which includes Shell, is investigating the feasibility of producing hydrogen offshore at a multi-gigawatt scale in the Dutch part of the North Sea. The project aims to establish a multi-gigawatt wind farm connected to an onshore hydrogen facility, with plans to produce green hydrogen using electricity from approximately 4 GW of offshore wind by 2030 and 10 GW by 2040. The study will explore various technologies, including electrolysis on offshore platforms and at the base of wind turbines.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **December 2022:** The feasibility study being conducted by Ramboll will assess the investment required for the NortH2 project, including suitable technologies and environmental impacts. The results of this study are expected to guide future investments in offshore hydrogen production in the Dutch North Sea.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2022:** The feasibility study is set to be released in January 2023, which will outline options for investment and technology integration for the NortH2 project.
