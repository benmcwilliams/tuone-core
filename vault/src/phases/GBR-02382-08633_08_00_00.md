### Evolutionary Timeline of the Dogger Bank Wind Farm Facility

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 29-06-2022
   - **Details:** The Dogger Bank Wind Farm is a significant offshore wind project with a total production capacity of 3600 MW. The facility will utilize Seajacks' NG2500X class vessels to accommodate technicians during the offshore substation platform hook-up and commissioning across all three phases of the project.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 29-06-2022
   - **Details:** Seajacks UK announced an investment to upgrade its NG2500X vessel to accommodate 140 persons for the project. This upgrade will be conducted at PD Ports Teesport UK facility, emphasizing the maximization of local and UK content.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 29-06-2022
   - **Details:** Seajacks UK signed three contracts with Dogger Bank Wind Farm for the use of its vessel, with operations commencing in Q2 2023. The vessel is scheduled to return to the wind farm in both 2024 and 2025 for ongoing construction activities.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 29-06-2022
   - **Details:** The upgrade of the vessel and the associated operations are expected to create job opportunities, particularly in the local area, as the project will be managed by onshore teams from Great Yarmouth.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 29-06-2022
   - **Details:** The Dogger Bank Wind Farm is a joint venture involving SSE Renewables, Equinor, and Eni Plenitude, indicating that the facility is not owned exclusively by a single company.
