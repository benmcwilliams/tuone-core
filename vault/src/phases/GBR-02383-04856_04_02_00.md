### Evolutionary Timeline of the EET Hydrogen Facility in Cheshire, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2024**: EET Hydrogen is establishing the Hydrogen Production Plant 2 (HPP2) at the Stanlow Manufacturing Complex in Cheshire. This facility will have an annual hydrogen production capacity of nearly 230,000 tons, making it one of the UK's largest low carbon hydrogen plants upon operation. The facility will utilize the Advanced KM CDR Process™ for CO2 capture, developed by Mitsubishi Heavy Industries (MHI) in collaboration with Kansai Electric Power Co., Inc.
