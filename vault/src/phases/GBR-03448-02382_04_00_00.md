### Evolutionary Timeline of the Northern Gas Networks Facility in Low Thornley, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2021:** Northern Gas Networks is set to deliver the UK’s first homes with hydrogen-powered household appliances at its energy solutions test-bed facility in Low Thornley. The facility will showcase two semi-detached properties equipped with a rotating range of new hydrogen appliances, including boilers, hobs, cookers, fires, and barbecues. The initiative aims to demonstrate the use of 100% hydrogen for domestic heating and cooking.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **February 2021:** The project is supported by £250,000 ($350,000) in funding from the UK government’s Hydrogen for Heat (Hy4Heat) programme, aimed at promoting hydrogen as a viable energy source for home heating.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **February 2021:** Announcement of the project, with the homes scheduled to open in April 2021. The hydrogen houses are intended to have a lifespan of 3 years, with the potential to extend up to 10 years.
