### Evolutionary Timeline of the BOC Facility at Saltend Chemicals Park, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** January 2023
   - **Details:** The facility is part of the H2H Saltend project, which is a 600MW low-carbon hydrogen production plant with carbon capture capabilities. It is designed to store 890,000 tonnes of CO2 per year and aims to reduce emissions at the Saltend Chemicals Park by up to one third by replacing natural gas with low-carbon hydrogen in various industrial facilities. The plant will utilize Linde Engineering’s hydrogen and air separation technologies in conjunction with Johnson Matthey’s LCH technology.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** January 2023
   - **Details:** The project is part of the wider Zero Carbon Humber scheme, which aims to create regional infrastructure for hydrogen transport and carbon capture. Specific investment amounts were not detailed in the article, but the project is positioned as a significant step towards achieving net-zero emissions in the Humber region by 2040.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** January 2023
   - **Details:** The front-end engineering design (FEED) contract for the H2H Saltend project was awarded to Linde Engineering, while BOC was awarded the operation and maintenance service contract. The plant is scheduled to be operational by 2027.
