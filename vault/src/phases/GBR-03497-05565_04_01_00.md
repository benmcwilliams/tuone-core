## GBR-03497-05565

### Meld Energy Facility Timeline - Saltend Chemicals Park, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 07-06-2023
   - **Details:** Meld Energy plans to build a green hydrogen production facility at Saltend Chemicals Park, which will be the UK’s largest green hydrogen facility. The initial production capacity is set at 100 megawatts (MW), with potential expansion to over 200 MW in a second development stage. The hydrogen will be produced using electrolysis, utilizing renewable energy to split water into hydrogen and oxygen.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 07-06-2023
   - **Details:** Meld Energy has pledged an investment of between £180 million and £240 million for the project, pending design costs. The company is also seeking support from the UK’s Net Zero Hydrogen Fund to facilitate the development.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 07-06-2023
   - **Details:** The project is expected to be operational by early 2027. Following the submission of the planning application, Front End Engineering Design (FEED) work is slated to begin in November 2023, with construction expected to start less than a year later.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 07-06-2023
   - **Details:** The facility is projected to create approximately 1,380 jobs during the construction phase, with around 20 permanent positions once the plant becomes operational.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 07-06-2023
   - **Details:** Meld Energy is partially owned by World Kinect Sustainability Ventures, a subsidiary of World Fuel Services Corporation, which acquired a 50% stake in Meld Energy in late 2022. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and plans for the Meld Energy facility at Saltend Chemicals Park, highlighting its significance in the UK's transition to green hydrogen and decarbonisation efforts.
