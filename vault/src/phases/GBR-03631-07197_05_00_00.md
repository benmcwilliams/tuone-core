### Evolutionary Timeline of the UK Atomic Energy Authority Facility in Cumbria, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2023**: The Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Collaboration (RAICo) facility, a partnership involving the UK Atomic Energy Authority, successfully demonstrated the SMuRF (Sensor-Mediated Robotic Fleet) system. This system enables collaboration among wheeled, four-legged, and airborne robots for tasks in hazardous environments, particularly in nuclear decommissioning. The robots can create a 3D digital twin of environments, enhancing situational awareness for human operators.
