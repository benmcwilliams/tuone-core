### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nuclear Decommissioning Authority Facility in Cumbria, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2023**: The Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Collaboration (RAICo) facility in Cumbria successfully demonstrated the SMuRF (Sensor-Mediated Robotic Fleet) system. This system enables collaboration among wheeled, four-legged, and airborne robots to perform tasks in hazardous environments, such as nuclear decommissioning. The robots can create a 3D digital twin of the environment, allowing for effective navigation and task execution with minimal human oversight.
