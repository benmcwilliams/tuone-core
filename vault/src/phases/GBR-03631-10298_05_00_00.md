## GBR-03631-10298

### Evolutionary Timeline of the University of Manchester Facility in Cumbria, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2023**: The Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Collaboration (RAICo) facility in Cumbria, a collaboration involving the University of Manchester, successfully demonstrated the SMuRF (Sensor-Mediated Robotic Fleet) system. This system enables wheeled, four-legged, and airborne robots to collaborate for tasks in hazardous environments, such as nuclear decommissioning. The robots can share sensor data and operate with a high level of autonomy, creating a 3D digital twin of the environment for effective navigation and task execution.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2023**: The SMuRF system was deployed in a practical demonstration at the RAICo facility, where it successfully completed an inspection mission in a simulated radioactive storage facility. This marks a significant milestone in the application of robotic technology for nuclear facility monitoring and maintenance.
