### Evolutionary Timeline of the PowerHouse Energy Facility in Protos, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2019
   - **Details:** The facility is designed to process 35 tonnes per day of waste plastics, producing up to two tonnes of hydrogen per day using the DMG® technology. The preliminary engineering stage has been completed, and the design has been enhanced to nearly double the hydrogen output.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2019
   - **Details:** Peel L&P Environmental has committed to funding the next stage of development for the FMG® plant at Protos, leading up to a financial close. This investment reflects significant commercial confidence in the DMG technology.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
