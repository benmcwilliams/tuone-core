1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** August 2022
   - **Details:** Precision Gear Company (PGC) specializes in the collection, maintenance, and refurbishment of turbine gearboxes, which are critical components in renewable energy infrastructure. Following its acquisition by Galetech Group, PGC will collaborate with Optinergy to provide a comprehensive service that includes an inventory of multi-brand gearboxes and components, repair and restoration services, management, and climate-controlled storage of parts. This service aims to reduce downtime for wind farm operators, ensuring stable and efficient renewable energy production.
