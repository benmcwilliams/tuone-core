### Evolutionary Timeline of the HyNet North West Facility in Runcorn, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2021**: HyNet North West, in collaboration with InterGen, announced plans to develop a zero-carbon power station at InterGen’s Rocksavage Power Plant. The facility will blend hydrogen with natural gas, with the goal of transitioning to 100% net zero power generation by 2028. The project aims to decarbonize the North West and North Wales region by replacing fossil fuels with clean hydrogen and capturing carbon emissions.
