### Evolutionary Timeline of the Banks Renewables Facility in Dinnington, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2023**: Banks Renewables proposed the Common Farm Solar Energy Park, which will have an installed capacity of **49MW** for solar energy generation and a **50MW** battery storage facility. The project will be located on a **116-hectare** site to the west of the Todwick Road Industrial Estate in Dinnington.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2023**: The project includes a **£2 million benefits package** aimed at supporting local community projects, with an annual community benefits contribution of **£50,000** over the project's lifetime.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: The Rotherham Metropolitan Borough Council’s planning committee voted in favor of the Common Farm solar energy park project, with a majority of **eight to one**. The planning application was subsequently referred to the Secretary of State for further consideration.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2023**: The project is expected to bring jobs and investment to the local area, although specific job creation numbers were not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is owned by **Banks Renewables**, a subsidiary of **Banks Group**, indicating that the company has **full ownership** of the facility.
