### Evolutionary Timeline of OnPath Energy Facility in Dinnington, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2024**: OnPath Energy is developing the Common Farm Solar Energy Park in Dinnington, which will have an installed capacity of up to **49MW**. This capacity is sufficient to power approximately **18,800 homes** and will displace over **11,470 tonnes of CO2** annually. Additionally, a **50MW battery storage facility** will be integrated with the Thurcroft electricity sub-station, enhancing grid efficiency and supporting renewable energy generation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2024**: The project includes an annual **£50,000 community benefits package**, potentially totaling over **£2 million** throughout its lifetime, aimed at supporting local community and environmental initiatives.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2024**: OnPath Energy appointed AECOM as the lead consultant for the Common Farm Solar Energy Park, overseeing various aspects of construction, including planning and environmental management. Construction is expected to begin in **summer 2025** and conclude by the end of **2026**.
- **December 2024**: An archaeological dig was completed at the site, covering over **5000 square meters**. This was part of the preparatory work for construction, following an initial investigation that included digging over **130 trial trenches**.

#### 4. Labour
- **November 2024**: The construction phase is anticipated to provide opportunities for regional sub-contractors and suppliers, contributing to local job creation.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **November 2024**: OnPath Energy is the sole developer of the Common Farm Solar Energy Park, indicating **full ownership** of the facility.
