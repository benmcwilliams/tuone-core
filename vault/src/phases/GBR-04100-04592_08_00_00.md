### Evolutionary Timeline of the Baxi Facility in Methil, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2023:** Baxi announced it will supply fully hydrogen boilers to the H100 Fife Project, which is designed as a hydrogen-to-homes demonstration. The project aims to provide hydrogen to households in the Buckhaven and Denbeath areas of Methil through a new network, set to go live in 2024. The facility will include two hydrogen show homes for public demonstrations of domestic hydrogen appliances, including Baxi’s 100% pure hydrogen boilers. The project is supported by a 7MW offshore wind turbine that will generate clean electricity for hydrogen production.
