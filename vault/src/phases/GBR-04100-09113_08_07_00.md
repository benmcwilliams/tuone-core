## GBR-04100-09113

### Evolutionary Timeline of Harland & Wolff Facility in Methil, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2021**: Harland & Wolff announced that it would use its Methil facilities to fabricate and load-out eight jacket foundations for the Neart na Gaoithe (NnG) offshore wind project. This marked the beginning of operations at the facility after its acquisition from BiFab.
- **February 2023**: The contract with Saipem for the manufacture and delivery of jacket foundations was terminated, with the scope reduced from eight to four foundations. This change indicated a shift in operational capacity and project scale.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2021**: The contract with Saipem for the NnG project was a significant investment opportunity for Harland & Wolff, as it was expected to create around 290 jobs and establish the facility's role in the offshore wind sector.
- **February 2023**: The contract value was finalized at GBP 16 million after the scope reduction, indicating a financial adjustment in response to the project's changes.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2021**: Harland & Wolff signed a contract with Saipem to start work on the NnG project, with fabrication work commencing on 1 July 2021.
- **February 2023**: The contract was terminated due to project delays and economic changes, marking a significant milestone in the facility's operational history.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2021**: The contract with Saipem was expected to create approximately 290 direct and indirect jobs in Scotland, highlighting the facility's potential impact on local employment.
- **February 2023**: Following the termination of the contract, Harland & Wolff announced a rationalization of the Methil facility’s workforce, reducing it to approximately 115 core personnel to align resources with the reduced level of work.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2021**: Harland & Wolff, owned by InfraStrata, acquired the Methil facilities from BiFab, indicating a change in ownership and operational control of the site. The facility is fully owned by Harland & Wolff under InfraStrata.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and changes at the Harland & Wolff facility in Methil, GBR, focusing on its operations, investments, project milestones, labor impacts, and ownership structure.
