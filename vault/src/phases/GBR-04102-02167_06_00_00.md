### Evolutionary Timeline of the BayWa Facility in Bracks, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **July 2023:** BayWa r.e. commenced commercial operations at the Bracks solar farm, which has an installed capacity of **30MW**. The facility is designed to generate enough clean energy to meet the needs of nearly **8,000 British households** and offset approximately **11,000 tonnes of CO₂ emissions annually**. 
   - The company plans to allow **sheep grazing** at the solar farm as part of a biodiversity initiative starting next year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **September 2022:** Construction of the Bracks solar plant began.
   - **End of 2022:** The Bracks solar plant was sold to the **Railways Pension Scheme**, managed by Railpen. BayWa r.e. agreed to provide long-term operations and maintenance services, along with commercial and asset management services.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **July 2023:** Official announcement of the commencement of commercial operations at the Bracks solar farm.
