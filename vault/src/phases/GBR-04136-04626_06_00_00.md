#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Acquisition and Capacity**: On **12-01-2021**, Bluefield Solar Income Fund (BSIF) acquired the Bradenstoke Solar Park, which has a production capacity of **70MWp**. The facility was built in early **2015** and is located on a former RAF airbase. It began generating revenue for BSIF starting **1 April 2021**. Following this acquisition, BSIF's total installed capacity across its UK solar assets reached **612MWp**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Acquisition Cost**: The acquisition of the Bradenstoke Solar Park was completed for **£89 million**.
