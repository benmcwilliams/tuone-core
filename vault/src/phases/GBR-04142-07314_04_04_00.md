### Evolutionary Timeline of the CPH2 Facility in Doncaster, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06-09-2022**: CPH2 has developed membrane-free electrolyser (MFE) technology, which utilizes cryogenic separation to produce pure hydrogen and pure oxygen. The facility is capable of producing MFE220 units, with each 1-MW model producing approximately 450 kg of hydrogen per day. Under a new licensing agreement, KCA Deutag's unit Bentec will manufacture up to 30 MFE220 units for CPH2 at its facility in Germany.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06-09-2022**: Kenera Energy Solutions, part of KCA Deutag, is already a significant investor in CPH2, having supported the company during its IPO. The licensing agreement is expected to provide valuable manufacturing capacity for CPH2's MFE technology, facilitating a faster rollout as the hydrogen market expands.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06-09-2022**: CPH2 signed a licensing agreement with KCA Deutag, allowing Bentec to build and sell CPH2's MFE units across Europe and the Middle East. The agreement is effective for ten years following the completion of the initial 30 MFE units.

#### 4. Labour
- The article does not specify any direct job creation or job loss associated with the licensing agreement or the manufacturing of MFE units.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- CPH2 does not own the facility exclusively; it has entered into a licensing agreement with KCA Deutag, allowing them to manufacture and sell its technology. Kenera has secured non-exclusive and exclusive licenses for various regions, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than full ownership by CPH2.
