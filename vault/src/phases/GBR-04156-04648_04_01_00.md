### Evolutionary Timeline of the Budweiser Facility in Samlesbury Brewery, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2023**: Budweiser and Protium announced plans to develop an on-site green hydrogen energy production plant at the Samlesbury Brewery. The project aims to decarbonize the brewery and produce green hydrogen to meet all thermal demand, heating, and transportation needs through renewable hydrogen.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2023**: Protium will fund, build, and operate the green hydrogen production facility. The facility will include an electrolyzer powered by renewable energy and a refueling station for hydrogen-powered vehicles and equipment.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2023**: The companies plan to submit a planning application for the green hydrogen project in the coming weeks, following consultations with local stakeholders. The facility is expected to be operational by the end of 2025.
