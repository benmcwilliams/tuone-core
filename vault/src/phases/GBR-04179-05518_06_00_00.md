### Evolutionary Timeline of Luminous Energy Facility at Columboola Solar Farm, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2020
   - **Details:** The Columboola Solar Farm is set to produce 162MW of solar energy. The facility is being constructed by Hana Financial Investment, with Stirling and Wilson as the builders. The project is expected to come online by the end of 2021.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2020
   - **Details:** Luminous Energy, a UK-based project developer, announced the financial close for the Columboola Solar Farm project, which includes its sale to Hana Financial Investment. This indicates a significant investment in the renewable energy sector in Queensland.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2020
   - **Details:** The Queensland government announced the commencement of construction for the Columboola Solar Farm. The project is part of a broader initiative to enhance renewable energy generation in the region, aiming for 50% renewable generation by 2030.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2020
   - **Details:** During the construction phase of the Columboola Solar Farm, it is expected to create 400 job opportunities in the region, contributing to local employment and economic recovery post-Covid.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** September 2020
   - **Details:** Luminous Energy does not exclusively own the facility; it has sold the project to Hana Financial Investment, indicating a transfer of ownership for the construction and operation of the solar farm.
