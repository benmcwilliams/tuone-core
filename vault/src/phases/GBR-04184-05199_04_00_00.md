## GBR-04184-05199

### Evolutionary Timeline of the HSE Science and Research Centre Facility in Buxton, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05/2019**: The HSE Science and Research Centre in Buxton unveiled the world’s first 100% hydrogen testing facility. This facility is designed to conduct controlled tests to establish safety evidence for converting the UK gas network from natural gas to hydrogen. The facility aims to support the transition to a hydrogen-based gas grid, which is crucial for meeting the UK’s decarbonisation targets.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05/2019**: Ofgem awarded £9 million to Northern Gas Networks through the Network Innovation Competition to support the establishment of the H21 hydrogen testing facility. This investment is part of a broader initiative to innovate and decarbonise heating in the UK.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05/2019**: The H21 hydrogen testing facility was officially launched, marking a significant milestone in the UK’s efforts to explore hydrogen as a clean energy source. The facility is part of the H21 project, which aims to deliver clean energy to nearly 4 million homes and 40,000 businesses in the North of England by 2034.

#### 4. Labour
- **05/2019**: The project is expected to create and sustain thousands of high-quality jobs as the UK transitions to a hydrogen-based gas grid. The facility's operations and the broader H21 project are anticipated to contribute significantly to job creation in the clean energy sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **05/2019**: The facility is part of a collaborative effort involving multiple partners, including Northern Gas Networks, Cadent, SGN, Wales & West Utilities, HSE Science and Research Centre, and DNV-GL. This indicates a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by a single company. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones related to the HSE Science and Research Centre facility in Buxton, focusing on its role in advancing hydrogen technology and contributing to the UK's decarbonisation goals.
