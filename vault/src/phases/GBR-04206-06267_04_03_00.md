### ULEMCo Facility Evolution Timeline - Bentley Works, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **09/2022**: ULEMCo, in collaboration with Cementation Skanska and the Building Research Establishment (BRE), initiated the ZECHER project (Zero Carbon Hydrogen Construction Equipment for Real-world use). This project aims to develop the world’s first dual-fuel hydrogen and diesel piling machine. The project will convert a Soilmec SR30 rotary and CFA piling rig, which typically consumes 100 litres of diesel per day, leading to emissions of 262 kg CO2. The goal is to demonstrate that this conversion can save up to 50% CO2 emissions and reduce NOx and particulate emissions.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **09/2022**: The ZECHER project received a grant of £418,613 from Phase 1 of the UK government’s Red Diesel Replacement programme to support the development of hydrogen fuel for construction equipment.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **09/2022**: The project was announced, focusing on the conversion of existing construction machinery to dual-fuel systems, with the aim of providing a proof of concept for hydrogen use in construction.
