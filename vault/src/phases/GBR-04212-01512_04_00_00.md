#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2022**: Centrica Business Solutions began a pilot project to inject hydrogen into its existing 49MW gas-fired peaking plant at Brigg, Lincolnshire. This facility is designed to operate during peak demand times or when renewable generation is low, typically functioning for less than three hours a day. The integration of hydrogen is aimed at reducing the carbon intensity of the power station.

- **December 2022**: Centrica announced a 12-month trial to burn hydrogen as a fuel at the Brigg facility. The hydrogen used in this trial will be produced through pyrolysis, resulting in emerald hydrogen, which is blended with natural gas to further reduce carbon emissions. The trial aims to inform future applications of this technology across gas-fired thermal power plants.
