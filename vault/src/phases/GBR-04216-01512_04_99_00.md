1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **April 2023:** Centrica Business Solutions began work on a 20MW hydrogen-ready gas-fired peaking plant in Redditch, Worcestershire, UK. The facility will utilize eight UK-assembled containerized engines capable of burning natural gas and a blend of natural gas and hydrogen. The plant is designed to provide backup power during peak electricity demand or when renewable generation is low, with the capacity to power approximately 2,000 homes for a full day.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **April 2023:** Centrica has purchased a previously decommissioned power plant for the development of the Redditch facility. The investment is part of Centrica's broader strategy to deliver around 1GW of flexible energy assets, which includes the redevelopment of legacy power stations and a focus on renewable energy assets.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **April 2023:** Construction has commenced on the peaking plant, with expectations for it to be fully operational later in the year. This project is part of Centrica's plans to enhance grid stability and reliability as the UK transitions to a renewable-led energy system.
