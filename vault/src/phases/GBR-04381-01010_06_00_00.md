## GBR-04381-01010

### EDF Facility in Pitstone, GBR - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2023**: EDF Renewables UK, through its acquisition of SAS Energy, has taken over the installation of more than 2,100 roof-mounted solar PV panels at the Safran manufacturing facility in Pitstone. This installation is expected to cover approximately 47% of the site's daytime electricity usage from May to September. EDF Renewables UK currently has over 45MW of solar projects in construction and development.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2023**: The acquisition of SAS Energy, which includes 45 projects in development and construction, is part of EDF's strategy to strengthen its position in the commercial and industrial (C&I) solar sector. This acquisition is expected to enhance EDF's offerings in solar, electric vehicle charging, and battery storage for commercial clients.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **15 September 2023**: SAS Energy became a wholly owned subsidiary of EDF Renewables UK. The acquisition allows EDF to accelerate growth and build on its existing project pipeline.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2023**: The acquisition of SAS Energy brings a team of 30 solar experts into EDF Renewables UK, enhancing its workforce and capabilities in the C&I sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **September 2023**: EDF Renewables UK now fully owns SAS Energy, which allows for a more integrated approach to managing solar projects, including those at the Pitstone facility.
