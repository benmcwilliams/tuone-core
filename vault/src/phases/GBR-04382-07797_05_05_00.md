### Evolutionary Timeline of the Framatome Facility in Sizewell, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2023**: The UK Environment Agency issued three environmental permits to NNB Generation Company (Sizewell C) Limited for the new nuclear power plant (NPP) planned at Sizewell. The facility will host two EPR reactors with a total generation capacity of 3.2 GWe, sufficient to power approximately 6 million homes in the UK. The design is a replica of the Hinkley Point C NPP.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2022**: The UK government announced an investment of GBP 679 million (USD 845 million) to become a 50% partner with EDF in the Sizewell C project.
- **Summer 2023**: An additional GBP 511 million of funding was made available to the project to facilitate progress towards a final investment decision.
- **January 2024**: A further GBP 1.3 billion of government funding was approved for necessary infrastructure work, including roads and rail lines.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2023**: Environmental permits were issued, marking a significant milestone in the regulatory process for the Sizewell C NPP.
- **April 2024**: Framatome signed multi-billion euro contracts for the delivery of nuclear heat production systems and safety instrumentation and control systems for the Sizewell C facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
