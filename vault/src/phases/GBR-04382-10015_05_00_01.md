## GBR-04382-10015

### Evolutionary Timeline of Sizewell C Company Facility in Sizewell, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2023**: Sizewell C is planned to feature two European Pressurized Reactors (EPRs) with a total production capacity of 3.2 GW of electricity, sufficient to power approximately six million homes. The facility aims to replicate the Hinkley Point C plant currently under construction in Somerset.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2023**: The UK government announced the release of an additional £341 million to support the Sizewell C project, building on a previous investment of £870 million. This funding is intended to prepare the site for construction, procure key components, and expand the workforce. The project is also supported by a Regulated Asset Base (RAB) finance model to attract private investors.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2023**: The government confirmed that the funding would help make the Sizewell C site "shovel-ready," allowing for quicker commencement of construction. The project is positioned as a critical part of the UK's nuclear energy revival, with a focus on energy security and low-carbon power generation.
- **November 2023**: Plans for a temporary desalination plant were announced to provide a continuous source of water during construction. This plant will produce up to 4,000 cubic meters of potable water per day, utilizing zero-carbon electricity from the nearby Sizewell B power station.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2023**: The project is expected to create thousands of skilled jobs across the country, contributing significantly to local employment and the economy.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The Sizewell C Company is a joint venture involving EDF Energy and other stakeholders, with the UK government also holding a significant financial stake in the project. The specific ownership structure details were not provided in the articles, but it is indicated that the project is a collaborative effort rather than solely owned by one entity. 

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Sizewell C facility, highlighting its technological capabilities, financial backing, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
