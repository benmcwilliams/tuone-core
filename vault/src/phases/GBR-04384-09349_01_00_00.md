### Evolutionary Timeline of the Kona Energy Facility in Heysham, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2022**: Kona Energy secured planning consent for a **200MW battery storage facility** in Heysham, Lancashire. The facility is designed to ease grid constraints, increase renewable energy usage, and offset **45,000 tons per annum (tpa)** of carbon emissions. It is expected to be one of the largest battery storage facilities in Europe and will provide local grid services such as inertia and reactive power support.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2022**: The Gore Street Energy Storage Fund (GSF) acquired the **200MW construction-ready energy storage project** from Kona Energy. The acquisition is expected to minimize capital expenditure and operating costs while opening new revenue opportunities. GSF aims to connect the project to the grid by the **fourth quarter of 2026**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2022**: Planning approval was granted for the **200MW battery storage facility** in Heysham.
- **November 2022**: GSF finalized the acquisition of the facility, marking it as GSF's largest acquisition to date.
- **Expected by Q4 2026**: The project is due to be connected to the national grid.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
