### Evolutionary Timeline of the ERM Facility in UK Waters, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2022
   - **Details:** The ERM Dolphyn project aims to produce green hydrogen at scale using moored floating substructures. Each structure will host an offshore wind turbine and localized hydrogen production facilities, generating hydrogen from seawater powered by offshore wind energy. The facility is designed to produce a demonstration unit with a capacity of 10MW, expected to be operational by summer 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** May 2022
   - **Details:** The project received additional funding from the UK Government’s Department for Business, Energy and Industrial Strategy (BEIS) as part of the Net Zero Innovation Portfolio Low Carbon Hydrogen Supply 2 Competition.
