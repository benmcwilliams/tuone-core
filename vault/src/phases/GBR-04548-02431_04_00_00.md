## GBR-04548-02431

### Evolutionary Timeline of the SSE Thermal Facility in Aldbrough, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: SSE and Equinor announced plans to develop one of the world’s largest hydrogen storage facilities at the existing Aldbrough site. The facility aims to store low-carbon hydrogen with an initial expected capacity of at least 320 GWh.
- **March 2024**: The Aldbrough Hydrogen Pathfinder Project is expected to integrate a salt cavern storage facility (20 GWh), an electrolytic hydrogen production facility (35 MWe), and a low carbon hydrogen power production through Open Cycle Gas Turbine (OCGT) of up to 50 MWe net capacity. The project will deliver up to 4,000 kg/h of hydrogen from the caverns.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2023**: The Aldbrough Hydrogen Project was selected by the UK Government for the final stage of the Net Zero Hydrogen Fund, which supports the development and deployment of new low-carbon hydrogen production. This selection indicates significant governmental backing for the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2021**: Announcement of the Aldbrough Hydrogen Storage facility development.
- **January 2022**: Contracts awarded to Atkins and Environmental Resources Management (ERM) for feasibility studies and environmental assessments, marking an important milestone in the project.
- **March 2023**: An Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) Scoping Report was published.
- **2024**: Construction works are expected to begin, taking around 36 months, with production anticipated to start in 2028.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2021**: SSE Thermal indicated that the development of the hydrogen storage facility would safeguard and create crucial jobs and investment across the region, although specific job numbers were not detailed.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The Aldbrough Gas Storage facility, which is being upgraded for hydrogen storage, is co-owned by SSE Thermal and Equinor. The partnership indicates a joint venture structure for the facility's operations and developments.
