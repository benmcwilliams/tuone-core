## GBR-04551-01636

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Equinor Facility in Keadby, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **02-11-2021**: Equinor announced the development of the H2H Saltend low carbon hydrogen production plant with a capacity of 600 MW, which will produce hydrogen from natural gas using carbon capture and storage (CCS). This facility is part of a larger plan to achieve a total hydrogen production capacity of 1.8 GW, which includes the Keadby Hydrogen power station, expected to be the first large-scale facility to use 100% hydrogen for power generation.

- **15-08-2022**: The H2H Saltend project was selected to proceed as one of the first large-scale hydrogen projects in the UK, with plans to enable fuel switching by 2026-2027, resulting in a CO2 emissions reduction of nearly 1 million tonnes annually. Equinor is also planning a second hydrogen production facility in the Humber with a capacity of 1.2 GW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **28-06-2022**: Equinor and SSE Thermal acquired Triton Power for £341 million, which includes the Saltend power station. This acquisition is part of their strategy to transition to hydrogen, with plans to have the Saltend power station using 30% hydrogen by 2027 and eventually 100% hydrogen thereafter.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **02-11-2021**: Announcement of pre-FEED study contracts for the H2H Saltend project, with a final investment decision expected in late 2023.
  
- **15-08-2022**: Confirmation that the H2H Saltend project will proceed to the due diligence stage of the Phase-2 cluster sequencing process, with operations expected to start in 2026-2027.

#### 4. Labour
- **15-08-2022**: Equinor's projects, including H2H Saltend and Keadby, are expected to preserve existing jobs and create new ones as part of the transition to low-carbon solutions.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **28-06-2022**: Equinor and SSE Thermal will own Triton Power, including the Saltend power station, in a 50:50 joint venture, indicating a shared ownership structure for the facility.
