### Evolutionary Timeline of the EET Hydrogen Facility in Stanlow, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 09/2023
   - **Details:** The EET Hydrogen facility at Stanlow is set to produce "blue" hydrogen. The combined capacity of the two hydrogen plants (HPP1 and HPP2) at the Stanlow complex will total up to 1,350 MW. HPP1 is expected to produce approximately 350 MW of hydrogen starting in 2026, while HPP2 is planned to have a capacity of up to 1,000 MW.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 09/2023
   - **Details:** HPP2 has received funding from the Net Zero Hydrogen Fund. This funding is part of the broader investment strategy for the hydrogen production facilities at the Stanlow complex.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 09/2023
   - **Details:** KBR has been awarded a contract for the front-end engineering design (FEED) of HPP2, marking a significant milestone in the development of the facility. HPP1 has already been selected by the UK Government as one of two initial large-scale hydrogen projects, with production expected to commence in 2026.
