### Evolutionary Timeline of the Essar Facility in Northwest England, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: Essar Group announced the formation of the Essar Energy Transitions (EET) division, focusing on hydrogen, ammonia, and biofuel projects. The facility at Stanlow will play a crucial role in this initiative, with plans to develop 1GW of blue hydrogen capacity for the UK market and a commitment to achieve a 75% reduction in carbon emissions by the end of the decade.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2023**: Essar earmarked approximately $3.6 billion for a range of low carbon energy transition projects over the next five years. This includes $2.4 billion specifically allocated for the Stanlow site in Northwest England.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2023**: The launch of the EET division is marked as a major milestone in Essar's commitment to low carbon energy, with the aim to transform the Stanlow refinery into one of the most sustainable refineries in Europe.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2023**: The establishment of the EET division and the associated investments are expected to eliminate around 20% of the industrial carbon dioxide emissions in Northwest England, which may lead to job creation in the clean technology sector, although specific job numbers were not detailed in the announcement.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2023**: Essar Group operates the facility at Stanlow through its refining and marketing business, Essar Oil UK, indicating full ownership of the facility. The EET division consolidates various existing ventures under Essar, reinforcing its exclusive control over the facility's operations.
