## GBR-04608-02428

### Evolutionary Timeline of the SGN Facility at Levenmouth Campus, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2024**: SGN, in partnership with Fife College, announced the opening of the UK’s first hydrogen training facility for gas engineers at Levenmouth Campus. This facility aims to upskill over 200 experienced Gas Safe registered engineers to work with hydrogen gas in domestic properties. The training will support the world-first green hydrogen trial, H100 Fife, which will supply green hydrogen to several hundred homes in Levenmouth through a new hydrogen gas network.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2022**: SGN began developing training courses in collaboration with Fife College and key stakeholders, indicating an investment in workforce development for the hydrogen sector.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2024**: Announcement of the opening of the hydrogen training facility, set to commence in the first half of 2024. This facility is part of the H100 Fife project, which is the first trial globally to deliver green hydrogen gas into homes for heating.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2024**: The establishment of the training facility is expected to create a new net zero workforce in Fife by upskilling over 200 existing Gas Safe registered engineers, enhancing local employment opportunities in the clean energy sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is a collaborative effort between SGN and Fife College, indicating a joint venture structure for the training facility, rather than exclusive ownership by SGN. 

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the SGN facility at Levenmouth Campus, focusing on its role in advancing hydrogen technology and workforce training in the UK.
