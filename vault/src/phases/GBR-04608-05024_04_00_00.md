## GBR-04608-05024

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fife College Facility at Levenmouth Campus, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2024**: Fife College, in partnership with SGN, announced the opening of the UK’s first hydrogen training facility for gas engineers at the Levenmouth Campus. This facility aims to upskill over 200 experienced Gas Safe registered engineers to work with hydrogen gas in domestic properties. The training will focus on the installation, testing, and commissioning of hydrogen supply systems and appliances, including hydrogen boilers, hobs, and meters.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2022**: SGN began developing training courses in collaboration with Fife College and key stakeholders, indicating an investment in the training infrastructure necessary for the hydrogen facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2024**: The facility is set to open in the first half of 2024, marking a significant milestone as the first of its kind in the UK. The training facility will support the H100 Fife project, which is a world-first green hydrogen trial supplying hydrogen to homes in Levenmouth.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2024**: The establishment of the training facility is expected to create a new net zero workforce in Fife by upskilling over 200 existing Gas Safe registered engineers, thereby enhancing local employment opportunities in the clean energy sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is a collaborative effort between SGN and Fife College, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by either entity. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones related to the Fife College facility at Levenmouth Campus, focusing on its role in advancing hydrogen technology and training in the UK.
