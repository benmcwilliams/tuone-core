### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hygen Energy Facility in Herne Bay, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2021**: Hygen Energy, in collaboration with Wood, announced plans for a facility in Herne Bay, Kent, designed to produce green hydrogen using electricity from existing offshore wind farms. The facility is expected to have a production capacity of between eight and nine tonnes of green hydrogen per day. Production is planned to commence by mid-2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2021**: The Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between Hygen Energy and Wood signifies a commitment to develop solutions for hydrogen production plants in the UK, starting with the Herne Bay facility.
