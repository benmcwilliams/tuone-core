### Evolutionary Timeline of the Wood Facility in Herne Bay, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2021**: Wood signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Hygen Energy to provide the conceptual design for an electrolyser at the Herne Bay facility. The facility is planned to produce green hydrogen using electricity from existing offshore wind farms, targeting a production capacity of between eight and nine tonnes of green hydrogen per day. Production is expected to commence by mid-2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2021**: The MoU between Wood and Hygen Energy is a strategic partnership aimed at accelerating green hydrogen production in the UK, contributing to the UK government's hydrogen strategy and net-zero carbon emissions targets. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the articles.
