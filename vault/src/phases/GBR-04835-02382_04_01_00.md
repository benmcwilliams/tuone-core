## GBR-04835-02382

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Northern Gas Networks Facility in Bradford, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Proposed Facility**: The facility aims to produce hydrogen power, specifically designed to support local businesses and residents in refueling hydrogen-powered and electric vehicles.
   - **Production Capacity**: Once operational, the facility is projected to generate approximately 16 tonnes of hydrogen per day, sufficient to power all the buses in Bradford.
   - **Additional Features**: The facility will include an array of solar panels for electric charging points and an on-site skills and training center for engineering education.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Funding Application**: Northern Gas Networks submitted an application for £6.8 million to the BEIS Net Zero Hydrogen Fund to assist in developing the site. This funding is expected to cover 20% of the project costs, with the remainder sourced from the private sector.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Consultation Events**: Public consultations are scheduled for September 2023 to gather community input on the project.
   - **Planning Application**: A planning application is anticipated to be submitted to Bradford Council later in 2023.
   - **Construction Timeline**: If approved, construction is expected to commence in mid-2024, with the facility projected to be operational by 2025.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Job Creation**: The construction of the facility is expected to create around 30 jobs. 

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Partnership**: The facility is a collaborative project between Northern Gas Networks, Hygen, and N-Gen. Hygen builds, owns, and operates commercial-scale hydrogen production facilities, while N-Gen focuses on energy development and decarbonization solutions. Therefore, Northern Gas Networks does not own the facility exclusively; it is part of a joint venture. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and plans for the Northern Gas Networks facility in Bradford, highlighting its role in advancing clean energy solutions in the region.
