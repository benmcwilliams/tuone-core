### Evolutionary Timeline of ITM Power Facility in Bessemer Park, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-11-2019**: ITM Power introduces Bessemer Park as the largest PEM electrolyzer factory in the world. The facility has an annual production capacity of 1 GW.

- **09-11-2021**: ITM Power announces plans for a second UK factory in Tinsley, which will have a capacity of 1.5 GW per annum. This new facility is intended to complement the existing capacity at Bessemer Park and is part of a strategic plan to increase total electrolyser capacity to 5 GW by the end of 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09-11-2021**: ITM Power acquires land for the new factory at a cost of £13.4 million. The overall investment for the new facility is expected to be between £50 million and £55 million, which includes £16 million for the construction of the shell and £20-25 million for fit-out and power supply.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05-11-2019**: Announcement of the Bessemer Park facility as the largest PEM electrolyzer factory.
  
- **09-11-2021**: ITM Power signs heads of terms with the University of Sheffield for the acquisition of land for the second factory, with plans for it to be operational by the end of 2023.

#### 4. Labour
- **09-11-2021**: ITM Power's expansion plans, including the new factory, are expected to support the local economy through job creation and supply chain support, although specific job numbers are not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **09-11-2021**: ITM Power is acquiring the land for the new factory, indicating full ownership of the Bessemer Park facility and the planned second factory in Tinsley. There is no mention of joint ventures or shared ownership in the articles.
