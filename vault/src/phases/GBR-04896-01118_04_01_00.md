### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Felixtowe, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 10-08-2022
   - **Details:** Iberdrola plans to build a green hydrogen plant at the port of Felixtowe, which will be operational in 2026. The facility will initially produce 14,000 tonnes of renewable hydrogen (H2) per year, with the potential to double its capacity in the future. The hydrogen produced will be used to fuel vehicles and machinery at the port, including up to 1,300 green hydrogen trucks and trains transporting goods. The facility will also have the potential to produce green ammonia or ethanol for shipping and export markets.
