## GBR-04983-00412

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Jaguar Land Rover Facility in Whitley, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2023
   - **Details:** Jaguar Land Rover (JLR) opened the "Future Energy Lab" at its development center in Whitley, Coventry. The facility is designed to develop and test electric drives for future Battery Electric Vehicle (BEV) models. It spans 30,000 square meters and includes offices, a workshop for prototype production of Electric Drive Units (EDU), and various test benches, including extreme weather climate chambers capable of simulating conditions from -40 to +55 degrees Celsius.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** October 2023
   - **Details:** JLR has invested £250 million (approximately €287 million) in the Future Energy Lab as part of its broader billion-dollar investment in the electrification of its luxury brands.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** October 2023
   - **Details:** The Future Energy Lab was officially opened, marking a significant milestone in JLR's Reimagine strategy aimed at enhancing the development and testing capabilities for electric vehicles. The facility is integral to the company's plan to introduce nine all-electric models by 2030.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** October 2023
   - **Details:** The facility currently employs over 200 e-car engineers, with plans to create an additional 150 jobs as part of the expansion and operational needs of the Future Energy Lab.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** October 2023
   - **Details:** The facility is owned exclusively by Jaguar Land Rover, which has been rebranded as JLR, indicating a full ownership structure without joint ventures.
