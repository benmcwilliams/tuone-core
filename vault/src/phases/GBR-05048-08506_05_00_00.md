#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **1962**: The Berkeley nuclear power plant began operations, utilizing two Magnox reactors to generate electricity.
- **Late 1980s**: The two Magnox units were shut down after over 25 years of operation.
- **1992**: Berkeley became the first Magnox site to complete defueling.
- **2010**: The units were sealed and placed in 'safestor', marking a significant milestone in the decommissioning process.
- **2023**: A major decommissioning project was initiated to clean up and demolish the four 'blower house' structures surrounding the reactor buildings. This project is expected to take eight years and involves the removal of residual metallic low-level waste and asbestos cleaning.
