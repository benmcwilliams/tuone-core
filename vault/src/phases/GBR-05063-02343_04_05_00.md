- **March 2023**: Lhyfe and Centrica announced a collaboration to develop offshore renewable green hydrogen in the Southern North Sea. The pilot production site aims to demonstrate an end-to-end hydrogen production, storage, and distribution system, leveraging Lhyfe's expertise in green hydrogen production and Centrica's experience in gas storage and infrastructure.
