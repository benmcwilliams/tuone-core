#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 05-12-2022**: Low Carbon announced the construction of the Fox Covert Solar Farm in Buckinghamshire, which will have an intended capacity of **23.4MW**. #### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: 05-12-2022**: The Fox Covert Solar Farm is part of a larger initiative where Low Carbon is targeting **1GW of solar capacity** across three sites in the UK, funded through a multi-bank financing facility from **NatWest, Lloyds Bank, and AIB**. This is part of Low Carbon's broader goal to create **20GW of new renewable energy capacity by the end of the decade**.

- **Date: 05-12-2022**: Construction at the Fox Covert Solar Farm is set to commence imminently. The project is one of three solar farms being developed simultaneously, with the other two located in Derbyshire and Essex, which will have capacities of **28.8MW** and **23MW**, respectively.
