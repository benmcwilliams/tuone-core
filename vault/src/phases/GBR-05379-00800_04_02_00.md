### Evolutionary Timeline of the Uniper Facility in Killingholme, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12-04-2022**: Uniper, in collaboration with Shell, announced plans for a blue hydrogen production facility at the Killingholme site, with a production capacity of up to 720 megawatts. The facility will utilize gas reformation technology combined with carbon capture and storage (CCS) to mitigate emissions. The project aims to capture approximately 1.6 million metric tonnes of carbon annually.
- **12-03-2024**: Uniper and Phillips 66 announced plans for a separate green hydrogen project at Killingholme, which will initially produce up to 120 MW of electrolytic hydrogen, with potential for future expansion.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12-04-2022**: The UK Government has set a target to capture 10 million tonnes of CO2 annually by 2030 through CCS, supporting the Humber Hub Blue project as part of its industrial decarbonisation support programme. The project is expected to receive government funding as it progresses through the phases of development.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12-04-2022**: The Humber Hub Blue project was announced, with plans to progress to front-end engineering and design by 2023. The project is expected to take final investment decisions in 2024 and become operational by 2027.
- **12-03-2024**: The green hydrogen project with Phillips 66 is in the planning stages, with hydrogen transportation to Phillips 66's Humber Refinery expected to begin in 2029.

#### 4. Labour
- **12-04-2022**: The development of the hydrogen production hub at Killingholme is expected to create high-quality jobs and stimulate economic growth in the Humberside region, as stated by UK energy minister Greg Hands.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **12-04-2022**: The facility is a joint venture between Uniper and Shell, as indicated by their cooperation agreement to develop the blue hydrogen production facility. The green hydrogen project with Phillips 66 also suggests a collaborative ownership structure for that initiative.
