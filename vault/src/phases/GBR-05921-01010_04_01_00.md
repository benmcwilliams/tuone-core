### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDF Facility in UK Power Plant, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2024**: EDF collaborates with Hadean Energy to deploy a 5 kW pilot electrolyzer at its UK power plant. The electrolyzer utilizes tubular solid oxide electrolysis (SOE) technology, which is claimed to produce hydrogen using 30% less electricity compared to existing technologies. The SOE technology operates by electrolyzing water with a combination of heat and electricity, achieving a production efficiency of less than 40 kWh per kilogram of hydrogen.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2024**: Hadean Energy secures a grant of AUD 10 million (approximately $6.64 million) through the Australia-UK Renewable Hydrogen Innovation Partnerships program to fund the development of the 5 kW electrolyzer for the EDF facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2024**: Announcement of the collaboration between Hadean Energy and EDF to test the electrolyzer technology at the UK power plant. The electrolyzer is expected to be built at Hadean's facility in Melbourne before being transported to the UK for installation.
