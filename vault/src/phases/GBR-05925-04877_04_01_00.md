#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05/2024**: ERM launched offshore trials for its Dolphyn Hydrogen process, which integrates electrolysis, desalination, and hydrogen production on a floating wind platform. The hydrogen produced is transported to shore via pipeline and does not require an electrical connection.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05/2024**: The joint research project between Southern California Gas (SoCalGas) and Evoloh resulted in enhancements to the electrolyzer manufacturing process and anion exchange membrane (AEM) electrolyzer technology. #### 3. Project Status and Milestones
