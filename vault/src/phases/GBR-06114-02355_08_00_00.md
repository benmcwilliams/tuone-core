### Evolutionary Timeline of Macquarie’s Green Investment Group Facility in Orkney Islands, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: September 2021**
  - The facility is part of a consortium that has proposed the **West of Orkney Windfarm**, aiming for a production capacity of **2GW**. This offshore wind farm is designed to power more than **2 million homes**. The project is currently under assessment by Crown Estate Scotland.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: September 2021**
  - The consortium, which includes Macquarie’s Green Investment Group, TotalEnergies, and Scottish developer RIDG, has committed to enhancing local port infrastructure through memorandums of understanding with Orkney Harbour Authority and Scrabster Harbour Trust. This investment aims to position the area competitively for future offshore wind projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date: September 2021**
  - The consortium's bid for the West of Orkney Windfarm is part of the **ScotWind leasing round**. The project has undergone extensive site investigations and has finalized a grid connection agreement with National Grid, allowing for generation to commence in **2029**.

#### 4. Labour
- **Date: September 2021**
  - The project is expected to create **sustainable long-term skilled local jobs** for decades, particularly through the development of port infrastructure and operations related to the offshore wind farm.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date: September 2021**
  - The facility is part of a **joint venture** involving Macquarie’s Green Investment Group, TotalEnergies, and Scottish developer RIDG, indicating that it is not exclusively owned by Macquarie’s Green Investment Group.
