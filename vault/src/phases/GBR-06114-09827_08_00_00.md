### Evolutionary Timeline of the RIDG Facility in Orkney Islands, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** RIDG, as part of a consortium with TotalEnergies and Macquarie’s Green Investment Group, announced a bid for a 2GW offshore wind farm named the West of Orkney Windfarm. This facility is designed to power over 2 million homes and is currently under assessment by Crown Estate Scotland. The project includes plans for significant infrastructure enhancements at local ports to support offshore wind operations.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The consortium has committed to funding collaborative design and supply studies and investing in improving the infrastructure of Orkney and Scrabster harbors, which are crucial for the operational phase of the offshore wind project.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The West of Orkney Windfarm project is in the proposal stage, with site investigations already undertaken. A grid connection agreement with National Grid has been finalized, allowing for the commencement of generation in 2029.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The project is expected to create sustainable long-term skilled local jobs, particularly through the development of port infrastructure and operations related to the offshore wind farm.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is part of a consortium, indicating that RIDG does not own the facility exclusively but is involved in a joint venture with TotalEnergies and Macquarie’s Green Investment Group. 

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic plans for the RIDG facility in Orkney Islands, highlighting its role in advancing offshore wind energy in the region.
