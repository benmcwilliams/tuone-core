## GBR-06194-01913

### Evolutionary Timeline of the SSE Facility in Hartlepool, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 03-11-2021
   - **Details:** The facility is involved in the marshalling operations for the Seagreen project, which is a 1075MW offshore wind farm. The project will utilize Vestas V164-10.0 MW turbines, with the Able Seaton Port in Hartlepool serving as the pre-assembly site for turbine parts, including blades, nacelles, and supporting towers. The facility is expected to support approximately 100 full-time employees when in full production.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 03-11-2021
   - **Details:** The Seagreen project has a total investment of £3 billion (€3.5 billion). The project is significant as it is set to be the world’s deepest fixed bottom offshore wind farm and Scotland's largest renewable energy project.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 03-11-2021
   - **Details:** The installation of turbine foundations has commenced, and the campaign to fit turbine parts is expected to begin in late 2021. First power is anticipated in spring 2022, with full commissioning of the 114-turbine wind farm expected in 2023.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 03-11-2021
   - **Details:** The facility is projected to create approximately 100 full-time jobs, including roles for technicians, crane personnel, supervisors, quality inspectors, and warehouse workers.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 03-11-2021
   - **Details:** The facility is part of a joint venture between SSE Renewables and TotalEnergies for the Seagreen project.
