### Evolutionary Timeline of the Cornish Lithium Facility in Redruth, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 10-08-2020
   - **Details:** Cornish Lithium is piloting the extraction of lithium from geothermal brines using Direct Lithium Extraction (DLE) technology. The facility aims to construct Europe’s first geothermal lithium recovery pilot plant at the United Downs Deep Geothermal Project near Redruth. The project will utilize nanofiltration technology to selectively remove lithium compounds from geothermal waters, promoting environmentally friendly extraction methods.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 10-08-2020
   - **Details:** The project secured £4 million ($5.23 million) in funding as part of a larger £14.3 million initiative from the U.K. government’s Getting Building Fund, aimed at job creation and infrastructure development. This funding is intended to support the construction of the pilot plant and accelerate the demonstration of sustainable lithium production.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 10-08-2020
   - **Details:** The announcement of the funding and the pilot plant construction marks a significant milestone for Cornish Lithium, indicating the project's progression towards operational status.
