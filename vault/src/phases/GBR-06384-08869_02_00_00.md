   - **Date**: July 2023
   - **Details**: Exergy is set to design and construct the UK's first deep geothermal power plant located near Redruth, Cornwall. The facility aims to deliver approximately 3 MWe of baseload renewable electricity and up to 10 MWth of zero-carbon heat for the Langarth Garden Village housing development. The technology will utilize a highly efficient radial outflow turbine and an air-cooled condensing system, ensuring no water consumption and minimal environmental impact.
