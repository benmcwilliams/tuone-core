### Evolutionary Timeline of the Geothermal Engineering Facility in Redruth, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2023**: Geothermal Engineering Limited (GEL) partners with Exergy to design the UK's first deep geothermal power plant at the United Downs site near Redruth, Cornwall. The facility aims to deliver approximately 3 MWe of baseload renewable electricity and up to 10 MWth of zero-carbon heat for the Langarth Garden Village development. The power plant will utilize a closed-loop ORC (Organic Rankine Cycle) system, which is designed to be efficient and environmentally friendly, with no vapour emissions and minimal visual impact.
