## GBR-06400-01913

### Evolutionary Timeline of the SSE Facility in Port of Tyne, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2020**: The facility is designated as the operations and maintenance (O&M) hub for the 3600MW Dogger Bank offshore wind farm, which is being constructed in three phases of 1.2GW each. The facility will include office space and a warehouse.
- **March 2021**: The design for the O&M base is completed, incorporating environmentally friendly materials, EV chargers, solar panels, and efficient heating systems. The facility will serve as the main hub for operating the wind farm, which is expected to provide around 5% of the UK’s electricity demand.
- **November 2021**: Construction begins on the 3400 square metre O&M base, which will include offices and a warehouse for spare parts. The facility is expected to be completed in Q4 2022.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2020**: The total capital investment for the Dogger Bank project is approximately £9bn between 2020 and 2026.
- **December 2020**: An option agreement is signed between the Port of Tyne and the developers (SSE Renewables and Equinor) for the O&M facility, allowing for initial works to commence in early 2021.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2020**: Construction of the Dogger Bank wind farm begins.
- **May 2020**: Announcement of the O&M hub at Port of Tyne.
- **December 2020**: Site clearance work is set to begin, with initial works expected to commence in early 2021.
- **March 2021**: Planning application for the O&M base is submitted to South Tyneside Council.
- **November 2021**: Groundbreaking for the O&M base occurs, with completion expected in Q4 2022.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2020**: The project is expected to generate over 200 direct jobs in the region, with opportunities for local companies in the supply chain.
- **March 2021**: It is noted that 120 of the 200 jobs will be related to wind turbine operation and maintenance.
- **November 2021**: Recruitment for roles such as wind farm technicians, project planners, engineers, and management is set to ramp up in 2022.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2020**: The Dogger Bank Wind Farm is a 50:50 joint venture between Equinor and SSE Renewables, with Eni expected to join as a partner in the future. The O&M facility will be developed and operated under this joint venture agreement. 

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the SSE facility at the Port of Tyne, focusing on its technology, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
