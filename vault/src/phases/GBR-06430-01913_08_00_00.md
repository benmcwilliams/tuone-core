### Evolutionary Timeline of the SSE Facility in Hunterston, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2019**: SSE Renewables concluded the decommissioning of the Hunterston National Offshore Wind Turbine Testing Facility. The facility was instrumental in testing offshore wind turbine technology, specifically a 6MW Siemens turbine, which operated for six years. The facility provided key data that enabled the deployment of 84 Siemens Gamesa 7MW turbines at the Beatrice offshore wind farm.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2019**: The controlled felling of the last remaining turbine at the Hunterston facility was scheduled for September 19, 2019, and was successfully completed shortly thereafter. The decommissioning process involved a controlled explosion due to the inability to safely dismantle the turbine by crane.
