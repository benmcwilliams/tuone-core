### Evolutionary Timeline of the Wirsol Energy Facility in Cleve Hill, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: May 2020
  - The Cleve Hill solar farm, developed by a joint venture of Hive Energy and Wirsol Energy, received government approval to proceed. The facility is set to be the largest photovoltaic (PV) plant in the UK, with a production capacity of up to **350MW**. It will also include battery storage, enabling it to generate clean renewable electricity to power over **91,000 homes** and reduce CO2 emissions by **68,000 tonnes annually**.
