### Evolutionary Timeline of Ørsted Wind Power A/S Facility in Hornsea 3, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2022**: Ørsted Wind Power A/S was awarded a Contract for Difference (CfD) for the Hornsea 3 offshore wind farm, which has a production capacity of 2.9 GW. The facility will consist of up to 231 wind turbines and is located 120 kilometers off the north Norfolk coast. Once operational, the combined capacity of Hornsea 1, 2, and 3 will exceed 5 GW, making it one of the largest offshore wind zones globally, capable of powering approximately five million UK homes.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2022**: The awarding of the CfD indicates a significant investment commitment towards the development of the Hornsea 3 project, aligning with the UK Government's goal to achieve 50 GW of offshore wind power by 2030.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2023**: Ørsted announced the contract with Prysmian Group for the supply of inter-array submarine cables, which will cover about 50% of the wind farm's cable requirements. The cables are scheduled for delivery in 2026.
- **July 2023**: The contract with Prysmian Group was confirmed, detailing the design, manufacture, supply, termination, and testing of approximately 280 kilometers of 66 kV inter-array cables.
