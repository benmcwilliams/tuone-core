### Evolutionary Timeline of the TotalEnergies Facility in Montrose, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is part of the operations and maintenance base for the Seagreen wind farm, which has a production capacity of 1075 MW. The base will include an operations building, a repurposed warehouse, a communications tower, and a CTV pontoon for vessels servicing the wind farm.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The construction of the operations and maintenance base is a collaborative effort between SSE Renewables and TotalEnergies, with Pert-Bruce Construction appointed for the development. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **November 2020:** Construction of the operations and maintenance base began in Montrose, with an expected completion timeline of around one year.

4. **Labour:**
   - The project is expected to safeguard 25-30 jobs over the year within Pert-Bruce Construction and its wider supply chain.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is a joint venture between SSE Renewables and TotalEnergies, as indicated by their collaboration on the Seagreen wind farm project. 

This timeline captures the key developments related to the TotalEnergies facility in Montrose as of the information available from the article published on November 24, 2020.
