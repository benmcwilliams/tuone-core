1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 07-02-2022
   - **Details:** The ESB facility in Montrose is set to serve as the operations and maintenance base for the 1.1GW Inch Cape offshore wind farm. The base is expected to be operational by early 2025, coinciding with the commissioning of the first turbines at the wind farm.
