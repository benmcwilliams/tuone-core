1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2020
   - **Details:** SSE Renewables and Total began the construction of an operations and maintenance (O&M) base for the 1075MW Seagreen wind farm, located 27km off the Angus coast. 2. **Investment and Development:**

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2020
   - **Details:** Construction of the O&M base commenced in November 2020 and is expected to take around one year to complete.
