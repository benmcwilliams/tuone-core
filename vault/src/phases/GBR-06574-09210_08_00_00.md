### Evolutionary Timeline of the Inch Cape Facility in Montrose, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 07-02-2022
   - **Details:** The Inch Cape facility will serve as the operations and maintenance (O&M) base for the 1.1GW Inch Cape offshore wind farm, which is expected to be one of Scotland’s largest sources of renewable power. The facility will support the operation and maintenance of large wind turbines deployed in water depths of up to 57 meters. The base is set to include infrastructure upgrades such as offices, a warehouse, a dedicated pontoon for crew transfer vessels, dockside cranes, and a communications mast. The facility is expected to be operational by early 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 07-02-2022
   - **Details:** The developers, Red Rock Power and ESB, have applied for a long-term energy contract under the UK government's Contracts for Difference (CfD) allocation round. If successful, this will trigger an initial investment program of £5.2 million at Montrose Port, with infrastructure work commencing in 2023.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 07-02-2022

4. **Labour:**
