### Evolutionary Timeline of the Red Rock Power Facility in Montrose, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 07-02-2022
   - **Details:** Red Rock Power, in partnership with ESB, has selected Montrose Port as the operations and maintenance (O&M) base for the 1.1GW Inch Cape offshore wind farm. The facility will support the maintenance of the wind farm, which is expected to be one of Scotland’s largest sources of renewable power, operational for at least 30 years. The facility will include infrastructure upgrades such as offices, a warehouse, a dedicated pontoon for crew transfer vessels, dockside cranes, and a communications mast.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 07-02-2022
   - **Details:** The developers have applied for a long-term energy contract under the UK government's Contracts for Difference (CfD) allocation round. If successful, this will trigger an initial investment program of £5.2 million at Montrose Port, with infrastructure work expected to start in 2023.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 07-02-2022
   - **Details:** The initial work on the infrastructure upgrade is set to begin in 2023, with the base expected to be operational by early 2025, coinciding with the commissioning of the first turbines at the Inch Cape wind farm.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 07-02-2022
   - **Details:** Once operational, the Montrose base is expected to support up to 56 direct, full-time equivalent jobs, including positions for turbine technicians, asset managers, and office staff.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 07-02-2022
