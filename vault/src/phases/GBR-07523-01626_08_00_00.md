## GBR-07523-01626

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Engie Facility in Moray Firth, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **November 2024:** A planning application was submitted for the Caledonia offshore wind farm, which aims to generate 2GW of power in the outer Moray Firth. The project will include up to 140 turbines, each with a maximum capacity of 25MW. This development is expected to more than double the generation capacity of Ocean Winds in the Moray Firth region.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **August 2024:** Offshore wind engineering consultancy Wood Thilsted was selected to create the structural concept design for the project’s turbine foundations, indicating ongoing investment in the project’s development.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **November 2024:** The submission of the planning application to the Scottish government’s Marine Directorate marks a significant milestone after three years of development work, which began in 2021. The site for the Caledonia offshore wind farm was awarded in 2022 through the ScotWind process.

4. **Labour:**
   - No specific information regarding job creation or job loss was mentioned in the articles.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is part of Ocean Winds, a 50:50 joint venture between EDP Renewables and Engie, indicating that Engie does not own the facility exclusively but shares ownership with EDP Renewables.
