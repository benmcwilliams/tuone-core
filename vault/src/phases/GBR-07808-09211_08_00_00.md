### Evolutionary Timeline of the Inch Cape Offshore Facility in Angus, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2024
   - **Details:** The Inch Cape Offshore facility will produce renewable electricity using 72 V236-15.0 MW wind turbines, totaling a production capacity of 1.1 GW. The turbines will be installed on monopile and jacket foundations in water depths ranging from 34m to 64m. The facility is expected to generate more than 5 terawatt hours of renewable electricity annually.
