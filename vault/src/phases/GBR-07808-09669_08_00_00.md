### Evolutionary Timeline of the PD&MS Group Facility in Angus, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2022**: PD&MS Group secured a three-year Balance of Plant (BoP) statutory inspection framework agreement to support SSE Renewables on the Seagreen offshore wind farm, which has a total installed capacity of 1,575 MW. This facility is expected to deliver enough green energy to power more than 1.6 million homes. PD&MS will embed its inspection technicians into Seagreen’s team to work across all wind turbine generators and the offshore substation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2022**: The agreement with SSE Renewables is part of PD&MS's strategy to leverage local technicians for cost and green efficiencies, aligning with local content targets. This indicates a focus on regional investment and development in the clean energy sector.

- **November 2022**: PD&MS announced the securing of the framework agreement for the Seagreen offshore wind farm, which is a significant milestone as it positions PD&MS as a serious contender for future BoP statutory inspection work, especially with the ramp-up of ScotWind-related development projects.
