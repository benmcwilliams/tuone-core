## GBR-07816-08516

### Evolutionary Timeline of Certex UK Facility at Seagreen Offshore Wind Farm, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: September 2022
   - **Details**: Certex UK has been awarded a framework contract for inspection and maintenance work at the Seagreen Offshore Wind Farm, which consists of 114 V164-10 MW turbines, totaling a production capacity of 1075 MW. The facility will provide enough green energy to power over 1.6 million homes. Certex UK's work will focus on the Balance of Plant (BoP) equipment, including transition jackets, personal protective equipment (PPE), davit cranes, anchor points, and ladders. The contract is for three years, with a potential two-year extension.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: September 2022
   - **Details**: The Seagreen Offshore Wind Farm is a £3 billion joint venture between TotalEnergies (51%) and SSE Renewables (49%). This investment supports the development of Scotland's largest and the world's deepest fixed bottom offshore wind farm.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: September 2022
   - **Details**: Certex UK won the contract through a tender process, marking a significant milestone in the operational phase of the Seagreen Offshore Wind Farm. The facility is currently operational, with first power announced recently.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: September 2022
   - **Details**: Certex UK will deploy a two-person team working in rotation via a service operation vessel (SOV) to cover offshore scopes year-round. This indicates job creation related to the maintenance and inspection services provided at the wind farm.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: September 2022
   - **Details**: The Seagreen Offshore Wind Farm is a joint venture, with TotalEnergies holding a 51% stake and SSE Renewables holding 49%. Certex UK does not own the facility but operates under a contract to provide services.
