### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in Grimsby, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** RWE announced plans to build a new Operations and Maintenance (O&M) hub at Grimsby’s Royal Dock, which will serve as the base for servicing the 1.4GW Sofia offshore wind farm. The facility will support major component exchanges and offshore repairs. It will include a joint control room for 24/7 monitoring, shared office space, and separate warehouse facilities.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** RWE is investing in the new O&M hub, which will expand the existing Triton Knoll offshore wind farm base. The construction is set to start in early 2022 and run until the end of 2022. RWE will also lease an additional 1.3 acres of land for this expansion.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** Construction of the new facility is scheduled to begin in early 2022, with completion expected by the end of 2022. First occupation of the facility is anticipated later in 2023.

4. **Labour:**

   - **Details:** The new facility is expected to accommodate around 140 RWE employees, with the potential creation of approximately 60 new jobs, in addition to indirect jobs required to support the operations.
