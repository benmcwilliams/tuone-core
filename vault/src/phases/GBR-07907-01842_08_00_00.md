### Evolutionary Timeline of Ørsted Facility in Grimsby, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2018**: Ørsted appointed Hobson & Porter as the principal contractor for the extension of its offshore wind operations center at the Royal Docks in Grimsby. The facility is designed to be the UK’s largest offshore wind operations and maintenance (O&M) hub, serving projects like Hornsea Project One and Two. It will feature high-tech Service Operation Vessels (SOVs) capable of servicing multiple wind turbines daily.
- **September 2019**: Ørsted officially opened its UK East Coast O&M hub, which is now the world's largest O&M hub, servicing four operational projects: Race Bank (573MW), Lincs (270MW), Westermost Rough (210MW), and Gunfleet Sands (172MW). It will also support Hornsea Project One (1.2GW) and Hornsea Project Two (1.4GW).
- **September 2020**: The Wind of Hope SOV was launched for maintenance work on Hornsea 2, expected to arrive at the Grimsby facility in 2021. This vessel can accommodate up to 90 people, including 64 wind farm technicians.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2019**: The opening of the O&M hub was the result of a £10 million investment at Grimsby Fish Dock and its surrounding quayside. This investment underscores Ørsted's commitment to the region and the offshore wind sector.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2018**: Announcement of the contract with Hobson & Porter for the O&M hub extension.
- **September 2019**: Official opening of the O&M hub, marking it as the world's largest facility of its kind.
- **December 2023**: Ørsted took the final investment decision on Hornsea 3, which will be operated from the Grimsby hub and is expected to be completed around the end of 2027.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2019**: The O&M hub employs over 350 people, contributing to local job creation in the offshore wind sector. 
- **December 2023**: Hornsea 3 is projected to support up to 5,000 jobs during its construction phase and an additional 1,200 permanent jobs during its operational phase.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Ørsted owns the facility exclusively, as indicated by their direct investments and operational management of the O&M hub and associated projects.
