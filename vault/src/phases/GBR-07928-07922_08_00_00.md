## GBR-07928-07922

### Evolutionary Timeline of MHI Vestas Offshore Wind Facility at Able Seaton Port, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-11-2020**: MHI Vestas Offshore Wind established a full turbine logistics and pre-assembly hub at Able Seaton Port (ASP) for the Triton Knoll offshore wind farm project. The facility is involved in the handling and assembly of offshore wind turbine components, specifically the V164-9.5 MW turbines, which are the largest turbines installed in commercial projects globally. The project will utilize 90 turbines, each capable of powering a typical home for up to 29 hours with a single rotation of its 80 m long blades.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05-11-2020**: A multi-million pound investment was made to transform Able Seaton Port into a specialist assembly and construction facility for the Triton Knoll project. This investment is aimed at establishing wind turbine handling facilities and enhancing the local offshore supply chain.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **05-11-2020**: The first turbine blades for the Triton Knoll project arrived at Able Seaton Port, marking the first time offshore wind turbine components were received at this port. The site preparation for the project began in November 2019, and the facility is set to support the installation of the turbines, with installation expected to begin early the following year.

#### 4. Labour
- **05-11-2020**: The establishment of the turbine logistics and pre-assembly hub at ASP has led to the presence of technicians from local suppliers, including Global Wind Service, Dawson, and Boston Energy, alongside MHI Vestas employees. This indicates job creation in the region as a result of the project.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **05-11-2020**: The Triton Knoll project is jointly owned by RWE, J-Power, and Kansai Electric Power, with RWE managing the construction and long-term operation and maintenance of the wind farm. MHI Vestas is a supplier for the project but does not own the facility exclusively. 

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the MHI Vestas Offshore Wind facility at Able Seaton Port, highlighting its role in the Triton Knoll offshore wind farm project.
