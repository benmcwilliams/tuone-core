### Aker Clean Hydrogen Facility in Shetland, GBR - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 04-11-2021
   - **Details:** Aker Clean Hydrogen, in collaboration with Aker Offshore Wind and DNV, announced the Northern Horizons project aimed at producing green hydrogen using offshore wind energy. The facility will utilize floating offshore wind turbines to generate electricity for multiple floating installations that will produce hydrogen. The project is designed to support a net zero hydrogen refinery on Shetland, which will produce various zero carbon energy solutions, including ammonia, liquid hydrogen, and synthetic fuels.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 04-11-2021
   - **Details:** The project is expected to involve billions of pounds in investment during its construction and operation phases. It aims to create a robust supply chain for hydrogen production in Scotland, aligning with the Scottish Government's target of 5GW of hydrogen production by 2030.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
