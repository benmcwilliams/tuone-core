### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mainstream Facility in Shetland, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **August 2023:** Mainstream Renewable Power, in collaboration with Ocean Winds, announced the development of a second floating offshore wind farm off the east coast of Shetland. This new project will add 500MW to the existing capacity, bringing the total capacity of the Arven floating offshore wind farm to 2.3GW. The initial site was a 1.8GW project, with both sites located 22km offshore in water depths of 100m.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **November 2022:** Mainstream and Ocean Winds entered a seabed lease agreement with Crown Estate Scotland for the 1.8GW project under the ScotWind Offshore Auction. The collaboration for the new 500MW site indicates significant investment in the region's renewable energy infrastructure.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **August 2023:** The announcement of the second 500MW floating offshore wind farm marks a significant milestone in the development of the Arven offshore facility. The project is expected to commence operations in the early 2030s.
