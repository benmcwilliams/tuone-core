### Aker Offshore Wind Facility in Shetland, GBR - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 04-11-2021
   - **Details**: Aker Offshore Wind, in collaboration with Aker Clean Hydrogen and DNV, announced the Northern Horizons project aimed at using offshore wind to produce hydrogen. The project plans to harness 10GW of renewable energy in the North Sea to produce green hydrogen, which will be transmitted to a net zero hydrogen refinery on Shetland. The facility will utilize floating offshore wind turbines to generate the necessary electricity.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: 04-11-2021
   - **Details**: The project is expected to involve billions of pounds in investment during its construction and operation phases. It aligns with the Scottish Government's ambition to develop a hydrogen economy and targets 5GW of hydrogen production by 2030.
