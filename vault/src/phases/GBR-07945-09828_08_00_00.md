### Evolutionary Timeline of the RJ McLeod Facility in Shetland, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** RJ McLeod has been awarded the civils contract for the Viking Wind Farm, which will consist of 103 turbines with a total production capacity of 443 MW. The facility is expected to be the UK’s largest onshore wind farm in terms of annual electricity output, estimated to reach almost 1.9 terawatt-hours per year. The construction includes building access tracks, a new road, construction compounds, and bases for the turbines.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2020
   - **Details:** SSE Renewables announced an investment of £580 million in the Viking Wind Farm project, which RJ McLeod is contracted to support through civil engineering works.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** August 2020
   - **Details:** Construction of the Viking Wind Farm is scheduled to begin in August 2020, with completion expected in 2024.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** The peak construction phase is expected to support around 400 jobs, with an estimated 35 local jobs during the operational lifetime of the wind farm.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** The Viking Energy partnership, which includes limited investment from the Shetland Charitable Trust and developers of the Burradale Wind Farm, is involved in the project. The ownership structure indicates that RJ McLeod is not the exclusive owner of the facility but is a contractor working under the Viking Energy partnership.
