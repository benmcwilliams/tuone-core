### Evolutionary Timeline of the Viking Energy Facility in Shetland, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** The Viking Wind Farm, developed by Viking Energy, is set to be the UK's largest onshore wind farm, with a production capacity of 443 MW and an estimated annual electricity output of almost 1.9 terawatt-hours. The facility will consist of 103 turbines.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2020
   - **Details:** SSE Renewables announced an investment of £580 million in the Viking Wind Farm project, marking a significant financial commitment to the development.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** RJ McLeod was awarded the civils contract for the construction of the wind farm, which includes building access tracks, a new road, construction compounds, and turbine bases. Construction is scheduled to begin in August 2020 and is expected to be completed in 2024.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** The construction phase is expected to support around 400 jobs at peak construction, with an estimated 35 local jobs during the operational lifetime of the project.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** July 2020
   - **Details:** The Viking Energy partnership includes limited investment from the Shetland Charitable Trust and developers of the Burradale Wind Farm, indicating that the facility is not under exclusive ownership by a single entity but rather a joint venture arrangement.
