## GBR-07970-00032

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Altilium Facility - ACT 2 EV Battery Recycling Plant, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 05-12-2024
   - **Details**: The ACT 2 EV Battery Recycling Plant showcases Altilium’s EcoCathode process, making it the only facility in the UK capable of producing large volumes of precursor cathode active materials (P-CAM) and cathode active materials (CAM) from recycled EV battery waste. The facility spans 18,000 ft² and has a production capacity to process 300 kg of black mass waste per day, equivalent to one EV battery. The facility aims to produce high volumes of battery materials, including high-nickel NMC811 CAM, for qualification with automotive customers. The EcoCathode process has been validated, recovering over 95% of cathode metals from waste EV batteries.
