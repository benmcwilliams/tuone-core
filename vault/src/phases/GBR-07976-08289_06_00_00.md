### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ameresco Sunel Energy Facility in Cowley Complex, County Durham, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2024**: The Cowley Complex is reported to consist of three connected solar sites with a total production capacity of 120MW, divided into 60MW, 43MW, and 17MW. It is noted as the largest plant in Sonnedix’s 300MW UK portfolio and is expected to become operational in the second half of 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2023**: Sonnedix secures a €500 million loan facility to finance the construction of renewable energy projects in Europe and the UK, including the Cowley Complex. This loan allows for more efficient and flexible clustering, contracting, and construction of their development pipeline.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2024**: Construction of the Cowley Complex has begun, marking a significant milestone in Sonnedix’s UK growth strategy. The project is expected to be operational by the second half of 2025. The Contracts for Difference (CfD) scheme, under which the entire UK portfolio is contracted, will become effective in 2026 following the completion of the Cowley Complex.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2024**: The construction of the Cowley Complex is expected to create over 100 green jobs in the local area, contributing positively to the local economy.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2024**: The Cowley Complex is part of Sonnedix’s portfolio, which is 100% contracted under the UK Government’s Contracts for Difference (CfD) scheme.
