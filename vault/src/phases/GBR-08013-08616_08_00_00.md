## GBR-08013-08616

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Delmar Systems Facility in Culzean, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2024**: Delmar Systems is set to provide complete mooring systems for a 3 MW floating wind turbine as part of the Culzean Floating Wind Pilot Project. The turbine will be positioned two kilometers west of the Culzean production complex in the UK central North Sea. The project aims to cover approximately 20% of the Culzean gas platform's electricity needs, supplementing existing gas turbine generation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2024**: The Culzean Floating Wind Pilot Project was selected in Crown Estate Scotland’s Innovation and Targeted Oil & Gas (INTOG) leasing round in 2023, indicating a strategic investment in renewable energy technology and infrastructure.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2024**: Fabrication of the foundation for the floating wind turbine commenced.
- **December 2024**: Delmar Systems was awarded the mooring scope for the TotalEnergies 3 MW Culzean Floating Wind Pilot Project, with the project scheduled to be fully operational by the end of 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify any direct job creation or job loss associated with the Culzean Floating Wind Pilot Project at this time.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is part of a collaborative project involving Delmar Systems, Archer Wind, and TotalEnergies. While Delmar Systems is responsible for the mooring systems, the overall project is being executed by Archer Wind on behalf of TotalEnergies, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by Delmar Systems.
