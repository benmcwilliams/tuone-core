### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocergy Facility in Culzean, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2024**: Ocergy's modular, light semi-submersible floater hull design is selected for a floating offshore wind pilot project by TotalEnergies. This project will feature a 3 MW floating wind turbine positioned two kilometers west of the Culzean production complex. Once operational, it is expected to cover approximately 20% of Culzean's energy needs, supplementing the electricity generated by gas turbines.
