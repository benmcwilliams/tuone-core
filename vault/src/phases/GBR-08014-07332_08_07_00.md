## GBR-08014-07332

### Evolutionary Timeline of the BW Ideol Facility in Ardersier, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2021**: BW Ideol partners with Ardersier Port Authority to explore the manufacturing of concrete floating wind substructures using BW Ideol’s damping pool technology. The facility aims to establish a serial production line dedicated to this purpose.
- **December 2021**: Work begins to transform Ardersier into the UK’s largest manufacturing center for floating concrete foundations. The facility will include a 34ha hub for manufacturing concrete foundations, with plans for a "green steel" mill powered by offshore wind.
- **July 2023**: BW Ideol retains exclusive access to the Port of Ardersier for a floater production line, reaffirming its commitment to local manufacturing of floating wind foundations.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2021**: A £20 million capital dredge project is initiated to reopen the Ardersier port, which is expected to facilitate the construction of the floating wind manufacturing hub. This project is part of a broader investment strategy to create a sustainable floating wind industry in Scotland.
- **July 2023**: BW Ideol announces a joint venture with ADEME, with an initial investment of EUR 17.85 million for the co-development of floating offshore wind projects, although this is separate from the Ardersier facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2021**: Partnership agreement signed between BW Ideol and Ardersier Port Authority for exclusive access to the port.
- **December 2021**: Construction work begins on the floating wind manufacturing hub at Ardersier.
- **July 2023**: BW Ideol confirms its full ownership of the floater production line at Ardersier amidst the formation of a joint venture for other projects.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2021**: The establishment of the floating wind manufacturing hub is expected to create thousands of long-term jobs, contributing to the UK’s green jobs initiative and supporting the offshore wind market.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2023**: BW Ideol retains full ownership of its activities and assets related to the Ardersier facility, including the exclusivity agreement with the port for the floater production line. The company is involved in a joint venture for other projects but maintains exclusive control over the Ardersier facility.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic decisions regarding the BW Ideol facility in Ardersier, highlighting its role in advancing floating wind technology and contributing to the UK's green energy goals.
