## GBR-08058-07963

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility in Port of Nigg, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06-09-2023**: Ocean Winds, a joint venture between Engie and EDP Renewables, is involved in the marshalling of 62 transition pieces (TPs) for the Moray West offshore wind farm at the Port of Nigg. The facility is responsible for the storage and marshalling of these TPs, which are critical components for the offshore wind project. The TPs are expected to start arriving later in 2023, with installation scheduled for completion in 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06-09-2023**: Ocean Winds is making significant early investments through the ‘Caledonia’ project to enhance the capability and capacity of Scottish ports, including the Port of Nigg. This investment is aimed at supporting the infrastructure necessary for offshore wind projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **28-11-2023**: The first batch of transition pieces for the Moray West offshore wind farm arrives at the Port of Nigg. This marks the beginning of a rolling program for the delivery and installation of all 62 TPs, which will be integrated with J-Tube cages fabricated by Global Energy Group (GEG). The installation of the TPs is part of the critical infrastructure for the wind farm, which is expected to power approximately 650,000 households in Scotland once completed.

#### 4. Labour
- **06-09-2023**: The expansion of facilities at the Port of Nigg, supported by investments from Ocean Winds, is expected to create job opportunities in the region, particularly in the logistics and support services for offshore wind projects.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **06-09-2023**: Ocean Winds operates as a 50:50 joint venture between Engie and EDP Renewables, indicating that the facility is not owned exclusively by one entity but rather shared between the two companies. Additionally, Lithuania’s Ignitis Group holds a minority share in the Moray West project, which further illustrates the collaborative ownership structure involved in this facility. 

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Ocean Winds facility at the Port of Nigg, highlighting its role in the Moray West offshore wind project.
