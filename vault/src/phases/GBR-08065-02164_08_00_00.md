## GBR-08065-02164

### Evolutionary Timeline of the BP Facility in Port of Leith, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2022**: BP and EnBW signed a reservation and collaboration agreement with Forth Ports for the Port of Leith to serve as the marshalling port for the 2.9 GW Morven offshore wind farm. The facility will provide a bespoke offshore wind berth and a significant land bank for the laydown and marshalling of offshore wind farm components. The facility is expected to be operational by the end of 2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2022**: Forth Ports announced a £50 million investment into the new offshore wind hub at the Port of Leith, which is part of the broader development for the Morven offshore wind project. This investment is aimed at transforming the port into a renewables hub and supporting future Scottish offshore wind projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2022**: The agreement between BP, EnBW, and Forth Ports marks a significant milestone in the development of the Morven offshore wind project, which was awarded during the ScotWind leasing round earlier in the year. The project is expected to generate enough energy to power over three million homes once completed.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2022**: The establishment of the offshore wind hub at the Port of Leith is projected to create up to 1,000 high-quality, long-term direct jobs and around 2,000 indirect jobs. Additionally, BP and EnBW plan to create up to 120 new direct jobs at their operational center in Aberdeen, contributing approximately £40 million per year to the local economy.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2022**: Forth Ports owns and operates the Port of Leith, which will serve as the marshalling port for the Morven offshore wind project developed by BP and EnBW. The ownership structure indicates a collaboration rather than exclusive ownership by BP or EnBW. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones related to the BP facility at the Port of Leith, highlighting its role in the Morven offshore wind project and its anticipated impact on local employment and investment in renewable energy.
