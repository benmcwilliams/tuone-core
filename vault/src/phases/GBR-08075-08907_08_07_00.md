### Evolutionary Timeline of the Forth Ports Group Facility in Rosyth, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2022
   - **Details:** The facility is set to include offshore renewable manufacturing and green power generating capacity as part of a new development plan. The proposal envisions the development of nearly 150 hectares of land at Rosyth, focusing on activities such as shipping logistics, advanced manufacturing, shipbuilding, and the assembly of offshore wind and low carbon energy generation.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** October 2022
   - **Details:** The development plan has the potential to deliver £1 billion of investment. This investment is tied to the establishment of the Forth Green Freeport, which aims to enhance green manufacturing and logistics capabilities in the region.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** October 2022
   - **Details:** The proposal for the new development plan at Rosyth was revealed, which includes plans for a new freight terminal and a proposed rail freight connection. The project is part of a broader initiative involving multiple partners, including Babcock International and Scarborough Muir Group.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** October 2022
   - **Details:** The development is expected to create approximately 7,000 new direct green jobs, primarily in shipping logistics, advanced manufacturing, shipbuilding, and offshore wind assembly.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** October 2022
   - **Details:** The facility is part of a consortium involving Forth Ports Group, Babcock International, and Scarborough Muir Group, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by Forth Ports Group.
