## GBR-08095-01469

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Banks Renewables Facility in South Lanarkshire, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **08-01-2019**: The Kype Muir wind farm, with a capacity of 88MW, is connected to the grid via a 132kV underground cable. The facility consists of 26 turbines and is operational.
- **18-08-2021**: Construction begins on the Kype Muir Extension, which will add 15 turbines (200 meters tall) to the existing wind farm, increasing the total capacity to 155MW. The new turbines are expected to produce an average output of 15GWh per year, significantly higher than the existing turbines' average of 9GWh.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **24-02-2021**: Greencoat UK Wind agrees to acquire a 49.9% interest in the Kype Muir Extension for £51.4 million, contingent upon the wind farm becoming operational (targeted for Q4 2022). Additionally, Greencoat will provide construction finance of up to £47 million.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **08-01-2019**: The Kype Muir wind farm is completed and operational.
- **18-08-2021**: Construction of the Kype Muir Extension begins, with plans for the project to go live by the end of 2023.

#### 4. Labour
- **18-08-2021**: The construction of the Kype Muir Extension is expected to support around 90 jobs. Additionally, contracts worth up to £10 million are anticipated for local suppliers through the Connect2Renewables initiative.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **24-02-2021**: Banks Renewables retains a majority stake in the Kype Muir Extension, with Greencoat UK Wind acquiring a 49.9% interest. The ownership structure indicates a joint venture for the extension project, while Banks Renewables exclusively owns the original Kype Muir wind farm.
