#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2021**: Construction work began on the Kype Muir 2 extension project, which will feature 15 new 200-metre turbines. The new turbines are expected to have an average output of 15 GWh per year, significantly higher than the existing turbines' average of 9 GWh per annum.

- **August 2021**: Preparatory work began on the Kype Muir extension site, with turbine erection work scheduled to start by summer 2022. The project is expected to go live by the end of 2023.
