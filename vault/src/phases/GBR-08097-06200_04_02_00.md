### Evolutionary Timeline of Technip Energies Facility in Stockton-on-Tees, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **August 2024:** Technip Energies secured a FEED contract from BP for the H2Teesside hydrogen production facility. The facility is expected to produce approximately 160,000 tonnes of low carbon hydrogen per annum. It will feature a hydrogen production plant with up to 1.2GW thermal capacity, hydrogen distribution pipelines, an air separation unit, and carbon dioxide capture and compression facilities.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **August 2024:** A final investment decision on the blue hydrogen production facility is anticipated in 2025. The project is part of a broader initiative to establish Teesside as a leading hydrogen hub in the UK, which may involve significant investments from BP and ADNOC as partners.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **August 2024:** Technip Energies was awarded the FEED contract, marking a critical milestone in the development of the H2Teesside project. The project is expected to progress towards EPC contracts and construction in the near future.

4. **Labour:**
   - **August 2024:** The H2Teesside project is expected to generate high-quality jobs and support local education and skills training, contributing to the development of a skilled hydrogen supply chain within the UK.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **August 2024:** The facility is a collaborative project between BP and ADNOC, indicating a joint venture ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by Technip Energies.
