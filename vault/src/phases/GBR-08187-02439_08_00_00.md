## GBR-08187-02439

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Siemens Gamesa Facility in Moray West, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2023**: The Moray West offshore wind farm is set to feature 60 Siemens Gamesa 14.7 MW wind turbines. The project has a total capacity of 882 MW, expected to meet the power requirements of approximately 650,000 households in Scotland for the next 25 years.
- **June 2024**: The wind farm will utilize Siemens Gamesa’s SG 14-222 DD wind turbines, which are equipped with a "Power Boost" feature allowing each turbine to generate up to 14.7 MW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2022**: Lamprell signed a contract with Ocean Winds to supply 62 transition pieces for the Moray West offshore wind farm, marking a significant investment in the facility's infrastructure.
- **2021**: A contract was signed with Nexans for the manufacturing of two export cables, indicating prior investment in the project's electrical infrastructure.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2023**: The first batch of transition pieces for the Moray West offshore wind farm was dispatched from Lamprell’s facility in the UAE, marking a significant milestone in the project.
- **November 2023**: Nexans began pull-in work on the two export cables at the offshore site.
- **December 2023**: The first offshore substation platform was installed, with the second unit installed at the beginning of 2024.
- **June 2024**: Installation of all transition pieces was completed, and the wind turbines are currently being installed.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2024**: The integrity management contract awarded to 2H for the Moray West offshore wind farm is expected to create jobs related to the management and maintenance of the wind farm's infrastructure.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The Moray West offshore wind farm is developed and majority-owned by Ocean Winds, with Lithuania’s Ignitis Group holding a minority share in the project. Siemens Gamesa is a key technology provider for the wind turbines but does not own the facility.
