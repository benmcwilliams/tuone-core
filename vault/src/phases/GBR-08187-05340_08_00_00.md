### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ignitis Group Facility in Moray West, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Offshore Wind Farm Production:** The Moray West facility is an 882 MW offshore wind farm located approximately 22 kilometers off the Moray coast in northeast Scotland. It will consist of 60 SG 14-222 DD wind turbines supplied by Siemens Gamesa, along with two offshore substations.
   - **Transition Pieces (TPs):** The facility will utilize 62 transition pieces, which will be manufactured and delivered by Lamprell under a contract with Ocean Winds.
   - **Installation Timeline:** Installation of the TPs is set to begin shortly after their arrival at the Port of Nigg later in 2023, with the wind farm expected to be fully operational by 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Financial Close:** The Moray West project reached financial close a few months prior to the article's publication in September 2023, marking it as the first project to achieve this milestone from the UK’s Contract for Difference (CfD) Allocation Round 4.
   - **Port of Nigg Investment:** The Port of Nigg has benefitted from over GBP 120 million in investment from Global Energy Group (GEG) to support offshore wind projects, including Moray West.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Agreement with Port of Nigg:** On September 6, 2023, Moray West signed an agreement with GEG to use the Port of Nigg for marshalling the transition pieces prior to installation.
   - **Installation Schedule:** The offshore installation works are scheduled to begin later in 2023, with completion expected in 2024.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
