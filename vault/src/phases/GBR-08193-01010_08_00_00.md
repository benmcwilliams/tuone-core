## GBR-08193-01010

### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDF Facility in Eyemouth, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **09/2020:** Approval was granted for the construction of an operations and maintenance (O&M) building for the Neart na Gaoithe (NnG) offshore wind farm project. The facility includes a three-storey main building (1,040 square metres) and a two-storey storage warehouse (538 square metres). The NnG wind farm has a production capacity of 450MW and is expected to supply low carbon electricity for approximately 375,000 homes, offsetting over 400,000 tonnes of greenhouse gas emissions annually.
   - **01/2023:** The O&M base was officially opened, which will service the 450MW offshore wind project. The facility will conduct servicing of turbine components and inspections of blades, foundations, and electrical cables.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **09/2020:** The construction of the O&M facility is part of the broader £1.8 billion investment in the NnG offshore wind farm project.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **09/2020:** Scottish Borders Council granted permission for the construction of the O&M facility.
   - **01/2023:** The O&M base was opened, marking a significant milestone in the project. First power from the NnG wind farm is expected in summer 2023, with full operational status anticipated by the end of 2023.

4. **Labour:**
   - **09/2020:** The project is expected to create up to 50 permanent jobs over its 25-year lifespan, along with training, retraining, and apprenticeship opportunities for local residents.
   - **01/2023:** Recruitment for the 50 permanent jobs has already begun as part of the facility's operational launch.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is part of a consortium between EDF Renewables and ESB, indicating a joint venture ownership structure for the O&M facility associated with the NnG offshore wind farm project.
