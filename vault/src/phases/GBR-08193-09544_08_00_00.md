### Evolutionary Timeline of Neart na Gaoithe Offshore Wind Limited Facility in Eyemouth, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility will support the operations and maintenance (O&M) of the 450MW Neart na Gaoithe offshore wind farm located off east Scotland. The O&M base will include a three-storey main building (1040 square meters) and a two-storey storage warehouse (538 square meters) designed to house office, warehousing, and staff welfare facilities.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The planning permission for the O&M facility was approved by the Scottish Borders Council, indicating a commitment to the development of the facility, although specific investment amounts were not detailed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2020
   - **Milestone:** The Scottish Borders Council approved the plans for the O&M base at Eyemouth Harbour, marking a significant step towards the establishment of the facility necessary for the 25-year lifespan of the Neart na Gaoithe project.

4. **Labour:**
   - The O&M facility is expected to create training, retraining, and apprenticeship opportunities for residents of Eyemouth and the surrounding area, contributing to local job creation.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is a joint venture between Neart na Gaoithe Offshore Wind Limited, EDF Renewables, ESB, and Eyemouth Harbour Trust, indicating that it is not solely owned by one entity but rather a collaborative effort among multiple stakeholders.
