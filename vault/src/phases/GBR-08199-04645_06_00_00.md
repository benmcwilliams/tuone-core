### Evolutionary Timeline of the British Solar Renewables Facility in Shotwick, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2015**: The Shotwick Solar Farm, part of British Solar Renewables (BSR), was commissioned. It has a production capacity of 72.2 MWp and is one of the largest solar PV plants in the UK.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2018**: British Solar Renewables signed a five-year Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with Shell Energy Europe Ltd for the off-take of energy from the Shotwick Solar Farm. The agreement is part of a broader strategy by Shell to invest in renewable energy, aligning with their commitment to low-carbon generation.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2018**: The PPA with Shell was announced, confirming that Shell would be the sole off-taker of energy from the Shotwick Solar Farm. This agreement is part of BSR's strategy to secure long-term contracts for energy produced at their facilities.
