### Protium Facility in Islay, GBR - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** Protium has initiated a project to decarbonise Bruichladdich Distillery using a dynamic combustion chamber (DCC) technology, which is designed to eliminate operational emissions. The DCC will heat the stills for whisky production, marking a significant step towards Bruichladdich's goal of decarbonisation by 2025. The project, named HyLaddie, aims to demonstrate the feasibility of switching from fossil fuels to sustainable hydrogen energy.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** The project has secured £2.65 million in funding from the Green Distilleries Competition, part of the UK Government's Net Zero Innovation Portfolio. This funding will support the installation of the DCC and a zero-emission hydrogen boiler.

Phase 2 of the project is expected to be completed by summer 2023, which will involve the production of hydrogen and oxygen from a larger commercial-scale electrolyser located offsite.
