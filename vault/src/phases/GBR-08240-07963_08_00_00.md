## GBR-08240-07963

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility in Buckie, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: Ocean Winds announced the construction of a new Operations and Maintenance (O&M) facility in Buckie, Scotland, to support the 882 MW Moray West offshore wind farm. This facility will enhance Ocean Winds' offshore wind generation capacity in the Moray Firth to 1,800 MW, effectively doubling their operational capacity.
- **May 2023**: Construction officially commenced on the O&M base, which will cover a 2,000 square meter area and include advanced technology such as a fully equipped workshop, warehouse, office, and training facilities. This facility is designed to efficiently manage and maintain the offshore wind farms.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: The Moray West offshore wind farm reached financial close, indicating significant investment in the project. The facility is part of a broader strategy to expand Ocean Winds' operational footprint in the region.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023**: Contract signed between CHAP Group and Ocean Winds for the construction of the O&M facility.
- **May 2023**: Construction kicked off at the Buckie harborside site, marking a significant milestone in the development of the facility.
- **June 2023**: The facility is scheduled for completion in 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2023**: Recruitment for the O&M base began, with 11 of the anticipated 60 long-term local roles already filled and undergoing training. The facility is expected to create additional employment opportunities across the regional supply chain.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2023**: The Moray West Offshore Wind Farm is owned by Ocean Winds, with Ignitis Group as a minority shareholder. This indicates that Ocean Winds does not have exclusive ownership of the facility but is the primary owner. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones related to the Ocean Winds facility in Buckie, GBR, focusing on its operational, investment, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
