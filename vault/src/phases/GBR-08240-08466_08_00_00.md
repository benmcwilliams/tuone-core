### Evolutionary Timeline of CHAP Group Facility in Buckie, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: CHAP Group and Ocean Winds signed a contract to construct a new Operations and Maintenance (O&M) facility in Buckie, Scotland. This facility will serve the 882 MW Moray West offshore wind farm, which is expected to come online in 2024. The new facility will feature advanced technology, including a fully equipped workshop, warehouse, office, and training facilities. This facility will strategically extend Ocean Winds' operational footprint along the Banff & Buchan coast and will more than double the offshore generation capacity the firm can operate and maintain.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: The Moray West offshore wind farm reached financial close, indicating significant investment in the project. The facility is part of a broader expansion that will increase Ocean Winds' offshore wind generation capacity in the Moray Firth to 1,800 MW.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023**: Announcement of the contract between CHAP Group and Ocean Winds for the construction of the O&M facility.
- **April 2023**: Construction of the O&M base is set to commence soon, with completion scheduled for 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2023**: The construction of the new O&M base is expected to create significant employment opportunities for local residents, as stated by Andy McNair, Pre-Construction Director at CHAP Group.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2023**: The Moray West offshore wind farm is owned by Ocean Winds, with a minority share held by Ignitis Group. The ownership structure of the O&M facility itself is not explicitly stated, but it is implied that CHAP Group is responsible for its construction and operation in partnership with Ocean Winds.
