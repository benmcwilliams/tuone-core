1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The United Downs Deep Geothermal Power (UDDGP) project is the first geothermal power plant in the UK, developed and operated by Geothermal Engineering Ltd. (GEL).
   - The facility has successfully drilled two deep directional wells: a production well to a depth of 5,275 meters (the deepest in the UK) and an injection well to 2,393 meters.
   - The project aims to produce both power and heat from the hot granite rocks beneath Cornwall.
   - The geothermal heating project at Langarth Garden Village will supply heat to 3,800 homes, with potential for additional supply to the western side of Truro.
