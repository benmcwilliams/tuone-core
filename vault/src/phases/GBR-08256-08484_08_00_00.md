### Evolutionary Timeline of the CWind Facility in East Anglia, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2020**: CWind is contracted by ScottishPower Renewables to provide subsea maintenance for the East Anglia ONE offshore wind project. This includes maintenance of the foundations and offshore substation, subsea surveys, corrective maintenance, and pre-engineering studies. The facility is part of the East Coast hub, which is central to CWind's operations for this project. The East Anglia ONE wind farm consists of 102 Siemens Gamesa 7 MW turbines, with a total production capacity of 714 MW. The wind farm delivered its first power to the UK grid in early September 2019, with full commissioning expected in 2020.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2020**: The contract awarded to CWind is valued at up to GBP 8.2 million for a three-year term, with options for two additional one-year extensions. This investment supports the growth of CWind's East Coast hub and its operations related to the East Anglia ONE project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2019**: East Anglia ONE offshore wind farm delivered its first power to the UK grid.
- **April 2020**: CWind's contract for subsea work is officially announced, marking a significant milestone in the operational phase of the East Anglia ONE project.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2020**: The contract is expected to create new employment opportunities within CWind and its supply chain, particularly as the company aims to transition workers from traditional industries, such as fishing, into the offshore wind sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
