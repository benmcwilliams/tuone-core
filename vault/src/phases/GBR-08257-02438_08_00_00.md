### Evolutionary Timeline of the ScottishPower Facility at East Anglia ONE Wind Farm, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Production Capacity:** The East Anglia ONE Wind Farm is designed to provide energy to up to 630,000 homes annually.
   - **Operations:** The facility consists of 102 turbines and is involved in various engineering services, including subsea inspections and maintenance.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Total Investment:** The project represents a £2.5 billion investment.
   - **Partnerships:** The facility is a joint venture between ScottishPower Renewables and Macquarie's Green Investment Group (GIG).

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Contract Award:** In 2019, CWind was awarded a multi-year contract to provide below-water services for the wind farm.
   - **Completion of Works:** As of November 2020, CWind completed its 2020 campaign for the East Anglia ONE project, indicating ongoing operational activities and support.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Job Creation:** The project supports local employment opportunities through its collaboration with East Coast-based contractors like CWind, which emphasizes the importance of local talent in the supply chain.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Joint Venture:** The East Anglia ONE Wind Farm is a joint venture between ScottishPower Renewables and Macquarie's Green Investment Group, indicating that ScottishPower does not own the facility exclusively.
