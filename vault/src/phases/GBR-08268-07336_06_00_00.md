#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: The Larks Green solar farm, with a production capacity of **50 MW** and equipped with **152,400 solar modules** installed on a **200-acre plot**, was completed and connected to the National Grid. This facility is notable for being the first solar farm in the UK to connect directly to the high-voltage transmission network, allowing for greater energy transport distances.
- **November 2024**: The facility expanded to include a **49.5 MW / 99 MWh Battery Energy Storage System (BESS)**, making it the first transmission-connected co-located solar and battery storage facility in the UK. This addition allows for energy storage during peak generation and delivery to the grid when demand is high.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones

- **November 2024**: The BESS was successfully energized, further solidifying the facility's status as a pioneering project in the UK’s renewable energy landscape.
