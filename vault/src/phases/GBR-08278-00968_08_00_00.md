### Evolutionary Timeline of the Carlton Power Facility in Wigan, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 05-02-2024
   - **Details:** Carlton Power is collaborating with Kraft Heinz to develop a green hydrogen facility at the Kitt Green plant in Wigan. The facility will have a production capacity of up to 20MW, which is expected to replace up to 50% of the plant’s gas demand. The green hydrogen will be produced using an electrolyser powered by renewable electricity, primarily from wind and solar sources.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 05-02-2024
   - **Details:** The project is estimated to cost £40 million. It is contingent on securing financial support from the UK Government’s Hydrogen Allocation Round (HAR2) under the Hydrogen Production Business Model (HPBM). The project aims to reduce carbon emissions by 16,000 tonnes per year.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 05-02-2024
   - **Details:** The project is in the planning phase, with a target operational date set for 2026, pending planning approval and financing. A submission to the Department for Energy Security and Net Zero (DESNZ) is planned for this year as part of the HAR2 process.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 05-02-2024
   - **Details:** The project is expected to create long-term green jobs in the Wigan area, contributing to the local economy and supporting the transition to net zero.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 05-02-2024
   - **Details:** The facility is a collaboration between Carlton Power and Kraft Heinz, indicating a joint venture structure for this specific project.
