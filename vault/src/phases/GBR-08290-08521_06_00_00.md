### Evolutionary Timeline of the Twitch Hill Solar Farm Facility (Cheswell Grange, GBR)

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2020**: Development of the Twitch Hill Solar Farm began in collaboration with Low Carbon Alliance and Cheswell Grange as part of a regenerative environmental plan.
- **July 2022**: The project received planning permission for a solar facility designed to deliver clean, renewable energy to the grid. The facility is located on 36 hectares of primarily Grade 3b agricultural land, which will be repurposed for solar energy production. To maximize land use, sheep will graze the land beneath and between the solar arrays.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: Lynher Energy, a joint venture between Napier Park Global Capital and Ethical Power, purchased the 26MW solar farm from Cheswell Grange. This acquisition aligns with their long-term strategy of investing in sustainable green infrastructure.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **2020**: Initiation of the development of the Twitch Hill Solar Farm.
- **July 2022**: Obtained planning permission for the solar facility.
- **April 2023**: Completion of the sale of the solar facility to Lynher Energy.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2023**: Neil and Susanna Harley of Cheswell Grange expressed that the sale of the solar facility would support the fight against climate change and help secure the future of their business and employees during challenging times for farmers. However, specific job creation or loss figures were not provided.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2023**: Cheswell Grange sold the solar facility to Lynher Energy, indicating that Cheswell Grange no longer holds ownership of the facility. Lynher Energy is a joint venture, suggesting that the facility is not under exclusive ownership but rather shared between Napier Park Global Capital and Ethical Power.
