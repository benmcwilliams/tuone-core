### Evolutionary Timeline of the Low Carbon Alliance Facility at Twitch Hill Solar Farm, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2020**: Low Carbon Alliance and Cheswell Grange began working on the development of the Twitch Hill Solar Farm as part of a regenerative and environmental plan.
- **July 2022**: The project received planning permission for a solar facility designed to deliver clean, renewable energy to the grid. The facility is situated on 36 hectares of primarily Grade 3b agricultural land, which will be repurposed for solar energy production. To maximize land use, sheep will graze beneath and between the solar arrays.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: Lynher Energy, a joint venture between Napier Park Global Capital and Ethical Power, purchased the 26MW solar farm from Cheswell Grange. This acquisition aligns with their long-term strategy of investing in sustainable green infrastructure.
