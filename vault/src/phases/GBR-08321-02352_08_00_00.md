### Evolutionary Timeline of the MHI Vestas Facility in Sanquhar, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2016**: Community Windpower (CWP) signed Vestas to supply turbines for the Sanquhar wind farm, which will have a production capacity of 31MW, featuring nine Vestas V112 3.45MW turbines. The project is expected to be operational in 2017.
- **July 2017**: CS Wind began delivering turbine towers for the Sanquhar project, confirming the use of nine Vestas V112 3.45MW machines. The facility is designed to provide electricity to cover half of Nestlé’s operations in the UK and Ireland.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2016**: A £40 million finance package was secured from the German export bank KfW Ipex to fund the Sanquhar wind farm project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2016**: CWP reached financial close on the Sanquhar wind farm project, allowing for operations to commence in 2017.
- **February 2017**: CWP and Vestas agreed to an early order of turbine towers from CS Wind UK’s Campbeltown plant, facilitating the project timeline.
- **July 2017**: CS Wind started delivering turbine towers for the Sanquhar project, marking a significant milestone in the construction phase.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is a project of Community Windpower (CWP) in collaboration with Vestas for turbine supply. There is no indication of joint ownership or partnerships beyond the supply agreements, suggesting that CWP retains primary ownership of the Sanquhar facility.
