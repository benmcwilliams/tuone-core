### Evolutionary Timeline of the Conrad Energy Facility in Herefordshire, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: Conrad Energy began work on the **45MW Larport solar farm** in Herefordshire, marking its first solar farm project. The facility is designed to provide enough power to support **10,400 homes** and is expected to save approximately **9,432 tonnes of carbon dioxide** annually. The solar farm will span **120 acres**, with a portion dedicated to biodiversity enhancement, including natural grassland and wildflower meadows.
- **June 2024**: The Larport solar farm was officially switched on, commencing power production. The facility will supply electricity to BNP Paribas under a **15-year power purchase agreement (PPA)**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: Conrad Energy secured an offtake agreement for the green power generated on-site through a PPA with BNP Paribas, indicating a strategic investment in renewable energy and sustainability.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2016**: Conrad Energy initially achieved planning permission for a diesel generator in the area, which was later converted to a gas facility before transitioning to a solar plant based on community feedback.
- **May 2023**: Groundbreaking for the Larport solar farm took place, with construction led by Ethical Power as the engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) contractor.
- **June 2024**: The solar farm was officially commissioned and began producing power.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
