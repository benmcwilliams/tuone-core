#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2019**: The Beatrice offshore wind farm, with a production capacity of 588MW, officially opened. It consists of 84 turbines, each with a capacity of 7MW, supplied by Siemens Gamesa. The facility is located 13km off the coast of Caithness in the North Sea.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2019**: The Beatrice project represents a significant investment by SSE and its partners, contributing £2.4 billion to the UK economy over its lifetime. The project had already contributed £460 million to the Scottish economy during its development and construction phases.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2019**: The Beatrice offshore wind farm was completed.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2019**: The Beatrice facility is expected to create an average of 800 jobs annually during its operational phase, with 370 of those jobs located in Scotland.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2019**: The Beatrice offshore wind farm is developed by SSE Renewables (40%), Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners (35%), and Red Rock Power Limited (25%). This indicates that SSE does not have exclusive ownership of the facility but is a significant partner in a joint venture.
