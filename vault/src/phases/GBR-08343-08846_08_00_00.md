### Evolutionary Timeline of the Equitix Investment Management Facility in Beatrice Offshore Wind Farm, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Beatrice offshore wind farm has a total production capacity of 588 MW. The facility is primarily focused on generating renewable electricity through offshore wind technology.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - On **January 15, 2021**, Equitix Investment Management acquired a 17.5% stake in the Beatrice offshore wind farm from Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners (CIP). The financial details of the acquisition were not disclosed, but it was part of a broader investment strategy to enhance the portfolio's robustness.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - The acquisition of the stake by Equitix was part of a transaction where CIP sold a total of 35% of the wind farm, with The Renewables Infrastructure Group (TRIG) also acquiring a 17.5% stake. The completion of these deals was expected to occur in the coming weeks following the publication date.
