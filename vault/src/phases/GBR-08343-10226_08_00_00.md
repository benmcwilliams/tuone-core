### Evolutionary Timeline of The Renewables Infrastructure Group Facility in Beatrice Offshore Wind Farm, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Beatrice offshore wind farm has a total production capacity of 588 MW. The facility is primarily focused on generating renewable electricity through offshore wind technology.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - On **January 15, 2021**, The Renewables Infrastructure Group (TRIG) announced the acquisition of a 17.5% equity stake in the Beatrice offshore wind farm from Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners (CIP). This investment represents approximately 12% of TRIG's investment portfolio. The acquisition is financed through a drawdown of TRIG's recently renewed revolving credit facility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - The acquisition of the stake by TRIG is subject to regulatory and lender consents, which were expected to be received in the weeks following the announcement. This acquisition marks TRIG's third investment in a UK offshore wind farm and its fifth in the offshore wind sector.

Therefore, TRIG does not own the facility exclusively; it is part of a joint ownership structure.
