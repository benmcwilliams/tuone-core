## GBR-08354-08565

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Corus Facility in Shotton, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** August 2009
   - **Details:** The facility is involved in a joint project with Dyesol to commercially manufacture dye solar cells (DSC) on steel. The goal is to integrate DSC onto strip steel in a coil coating line. The facility has a dedicated technology team of 30 people working on this project.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** August 2009
   - **Details:** The project received £5 million in support from the Welsh Assembly Government (WAG) to advance the development of the BIPV (Building Integrated Photovoltaics) project.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** August 2009
   - **Details:** The project has entered its fifth stage of development, with expectations to complete the next phase of production development by May 2010. This phase will include production trials, acceptance testing, and placement on demonstration sites.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** August 2009
   - **Details:** The facility employs a technology team of 30 people dedicated to the BIPV project.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** August 2009
   - **Details:** The facility operates as a joint venture between Corus and Dyesol, with Dyesol being the sole supplier of DSC materials to Corus for this project.
