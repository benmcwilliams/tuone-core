### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDPR Facility in Fraserburgh, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **June 2018:** Moray Offshore Windfarm (East) Ltd selected Fraserburgh as the preferred Operations and Maintenance (O&M) port for the 950MW Moray East offshore wind project. The facility will serve as the principal onshore base for operating the wind farm and conducting routine maintenance. The site will include a two-storey building, berthing facilities for support vessels, a pontoon for crew transfer vessel (CTV) access, and a storage and unloading area, covering approximately 1,880m².

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **2017:** The Moray East project was awarded a tariff of GBP 57.50/MWhr under a Contract for Difference (CfD) by the UK Government, indicating financial support for the development of the wind farm.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **June 2018:** An options agreement was signed with Fraserburgh Harbour to secure the necessary land for constructing the O&M facilities. The wind farm is scheduled for commissioning in 2022.
