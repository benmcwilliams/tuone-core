## GBR-08412-07963

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ocean Winds Facility in Fraserburgh, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2021**: Ocean Winds (OW) begins recruiting for the operations and maintenance (O&M) phase of the 950 MW Moray East offshore wind farm. The facility in Fraserburgh will support the O&M team, which will grow to service the wind turbines.
- **June 2023**: Ocean Winds announces the construction of a new 2,000 square metre O&M base in Buckie, Scotland, to support the upcoming 882 MW Moray West offshore wind farm. This new facility will complement the existing base in Fraserburgh, enhancing the operational capacity to manage the growing number of offshore wind farms in the Moray Firth.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: Ocean Winds and CHAP Group sign a contract for the new O&M facility in Buckie, which is expected to enhance operational capabilities and support the Moray West offshore wind farm. The financial close for the Moray West project was reached shortly before this announcement.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2021**: Recruitment for the O&M phase of Moray East begins, with the construction teams expected to hand over to the O&M team in 2022.
- **June 2023**: Groundbreaking for the new Moray West O&M base occurs, with completion scheduled for 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2021**: Ocean Winds announces the creation of 20 new posts for the O&M phase of Moray East, in addition to approximately 60 posts needed for servicing the wind turbines.
- **June 2023**: Recruitment for the new O&M base in Buckie is underway, with eleven of the 60 long-term local roles already filled and undergoing training.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2023**: The Moray West Offshore Wind Farm is owned by Ocean Winds, with a minority share held by Ignitis Group. The ownership structure indicates that Ocean Winds has a significant stake in the facility, while the Fraserburgh facility is primarily associated with the Moray East project. 

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Ocean Winds facility in Fraserburgh, highlighting its role in supporting the Moray East and Moray West offshore wind farms.
