### Evolutionary Timeline of the Diamond Generating Europe Limited Facility in Fraserburgh, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **June 2018:** Diamond Generating Europe Limited (DGE) is part of a joint venture, Moray Offshore Windfarm (East) Ltd, which has selected Fraserburgh as the preferred Operations and Maintenance (O&M) port for the 950MW Moray East offshore wind project. The facility will serve as the principal onshore base for operating and maintaining the wind farm, which will consist of 100 MHI Vestas 9.5MW wind turbines, scheduled for commissioning in 2022. The site will include a two-storey building, berthing facilities for support vessels, a pontoon for crew transfer vessel (CTV) access, and a storage and unloading area.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **2017:** The Moray East project was awarded a tariff of GBP 57.50/MWhr under a Contract for Difference (CfD) by the UK Government, indicating financial support for the development of the wind farm.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **June 2018:** An options agreement was signed between Moray East and Fraserburgh Harbour to secure the necessary land for constructing the O&M buildings and facilities, covering an area of approximately 1,880m².

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
