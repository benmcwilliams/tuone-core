### Evolutionary Timeline of Moray Offshore Windfarm (East) Ltd Facility in Fraserburgh, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **June 2018:** Moray Offshore Windfarm (East) Ltd selected Fraserburgh as the preferred Operations and Maintenance (O&M) port for the 950MW Moray East project. The facility will serve as the principal onshore base for operating the wind farm and conducting routine maintenance. The site will cover approximately 1,880m² and include a two-storey building, berthing facilities for support vessels, a pontoon for crew transfer vessel (CTV) access, and a storage and unloading area.
