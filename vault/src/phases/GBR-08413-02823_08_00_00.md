### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDPR Facility in Moray East, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2018**: The Moray East offshore wind farm, developed by EDPR, Engie, and Mitsubishi Corp, is set to produce 950MW of renewable energy. The project will feature 100 MHI Vestas 9.5MW turbines.
- **June 2019**: DEME Offshore is contracted to use the Port of Nigg as the staging point for the jacket foundations for the Moray East project, indicating operational preparations for the installation of the wind farm's infrastructure.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2019**: Global Energy Group has invested £90 million at the Port of Nigg since acquiring it in 2011, with £50 million allocated for port infrastructure and £40 million for heavy plant and equipment.
