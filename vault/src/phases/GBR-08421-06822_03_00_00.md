## GBR-08421-06822

### Evolutionary Timeline of Drax Facility in Cruachan, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2020**: Drax Group announced a £1 million upgrade to the Cruachan hydroelectric pumped storage plant, focusing on replacing the existing programmable logic controller (PLC) to enhance efficiency. The plant has a capacity of 440 MW and can generate power in less than a minute.
- **March 2023**: Drax appointed Studio Pietrangeli to optimize plans for a 600 MW expansion of the Cruachan facility, which will involve constructing a new underground plant adjacent to the existing one.
- **July 2024**: Drax confirmed the construction of a 600 MW underground plant, marking it as the first new pumped storage hydro scheme in the UK in 40 years. The existing facility's capacity will be increased to 480 MW through an £80 million upgrade.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2020**: Drax Group committed £1 million for the control system modernization at Cruachan.
- **April 2024**: Drax announced an £80 million investment for refurbishing the Cruachan site, which has been operational since the 1960s.
- **July 2024**: Drax's expansion plans for the new 600 MW plant are moving forward, with ongoing exploratory ground works and seismic surveys.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2022**: Drax applied for development consent from the Scottish Government to double the generating capacity at Cruachan.
- **March 2023**: Development consent was granted by the Scottish Government for the expansion project.
- **June 2024**: Seismic surveys began as part of the expansion project, marking a significant milestone in the development process.
- **July 2024**: Drax appointed Voith Hydro to conduct a Front-End Engineering and Design (FEED) study for the new plant.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2023**: The expansion project is expected to support hundreds of jobs during its construction phase, with around 900 jobs anticipated over six years.
- **July 2024**: The construction of the new plant is projected to create significant employment opportunities, contributing to the Scottish economy.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Drax Group exclusively owns the Cruachan facility and operates it as part of its portfolio of renewable electricity generation assets in Scotland.
