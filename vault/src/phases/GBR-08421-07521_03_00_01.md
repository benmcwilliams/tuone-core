### Evolutionary Timeline of the Voith Hydro Facility in Cruachan, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **July 2024:** Drax has appointed Voith Hydro to advance plans for a new 600MW underground pumped storage hydro plant near the existing Cruachan facility. This project, referred to as Cruachan 2, aims to enhance the UK's pumped storage hydro capacity, which is essential for integrating renewable energy and strengthening energy security.
   - **Ongoing:** Drax is also completing an £80 million upgrade to the existing Cruachan facility, which will increase its generating capacity by 40MW, bringing the total capacity to 480MW.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **July 2024:** The upgrade of the existing Cruachan facility involves an investment of £80 million. The new Cruachan 2 project is expected to attract significant private investment, contingent on updated policy and market support from the UK Government.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **July 2024:** The Cruachan 2 project has received development consent from the Scottish Government. Exploratory ground works and seismic surveys are currently underway to gather geological data for the project. Voith Hydro is tasked with completing a Front-End Engineering and Design (FEED) study for the new plant.

4. **Labour:**
   - **July 2024:** The Cruachan 2 project is expected to support hundreds of jobs and provide a boost to the Scottish economy, as stated by Drax’s Development Manager.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **July 2024:** Voith Hydro is involved in the Cruachan 2 project as a partner with Drax, indicating a collaborative effort rather than exclusive ownership. Drax is the primary company behind the facility's developments.
