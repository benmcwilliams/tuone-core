1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Galloway hydro scheme, which includes the Glenlochar Barrage, is a historic hydropower facility that controls the flow of water from Loch Ken to Tongland power station. The barrage is 103 meters long and features six sluice gates. The facility is part of the first large-scale integrated hydroelectric complex built in Britain, consisting of six power stations, eight dams, and a network of tunnels, aqueducts, and pipelines.
