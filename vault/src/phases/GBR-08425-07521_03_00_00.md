### Evolutionary Timeline of the Voith Hydro Facility in Oban, GBR

   - **09/2024:** Voith Hydro is selected by Drax to conduct a front-end engineering and design (FEED) study for the proposed 600MW Hollow Mountain underground pumped storage hydro power plant (Cruachan 2) adjacent to the existing 400MW Cruachan power station.
