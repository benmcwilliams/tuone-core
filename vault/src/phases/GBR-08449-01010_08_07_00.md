### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDF Facility in Neart na Gaoithe, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Neart na Gaoithe facility is a 450MW wind farm located off the east coast of Scotland. 
   - The facility's operations involve the fabrication of jackets for wind turbine foundations, which are critical components for the wind farm's infrastructure.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - In April 2021, a contract valued at £26.50 million was placed with Harland and Wolff for the fabrication of eight jackets. However, due to various issues, the contract was later settled for £16 million.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **April 2021:** Original contract placed with Harland and Wolff for jacket fabrication.
   - **September 2022:** Saipem removed four partially completed bases from Harland and Wolff.
   - **December 2022:** Saipem signed a revised contract valued at £23 million for the fabrication of the remaining four jackets, which were also later removed from Harland and Wolff.
   - **February 2023:** Harland and Wolff reached a £16 million contract settlement with Saipem for the incomplete work on the jackets.

4. **Labour:**
   - Following the contract settlement, Harland and Wolff announced a rationalization of its workforce at the Methil facility to approximately 115 core personnel to align resources with the level of work being undertaken.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is owned by EDF Renewables, and the work for jacket fabrication was contracted out to Harland and Wolff, indicating that Harland and Wolff does not have ownership of the facility but is a contractor for specific operations related to the project.
