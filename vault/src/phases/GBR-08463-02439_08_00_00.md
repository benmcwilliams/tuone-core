### Evolutionary Timeline of the Siemens Gamesa Facility in London Array, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The London Array wind farm, powered by 175 Siemens Gamesa 3.6MW turbines, has a total installed capacity of 630MW. The facility began commercial operations in 2013 and generates enough clean energy to power nearly 500,000 British homes while helping avoid around 900,000 tonnes of CO₂ emissions.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - Ørsted, the previous majority owner, sold a 25% stake in the London Array to Caisse de dépôt et placement du Québec (CDPQ) in 2014 for £644m. In July 2023, Ørsted reached a £717m deal to offload its remaining 25% stake to Schroders Greencoat.
