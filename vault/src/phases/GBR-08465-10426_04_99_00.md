### Evolutionary Timeline of the Wilton Universal Group Facility in Haverton Hill, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** Wilton Universal Group acquired a manufacturing facility on Haverton Hill, extending to 23 hectares adjacent to its existing Teesside base, creating a combined area of 45 hectares. The facility includes large workshops, a blast and paint facility, slipways, and a wet dock, aimed at servicing projects in offshore wind, tidal, hydrogen, and other green energy sectors. The facility is designed to maximize logistics and storage capabilities for current and future clients.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The acquisition was supported by finance from FW Capital and a grant from the Tees Valley Mayor and Combined Authority. This financial backing was crucial for the expansion and development of the facility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The acquisition of the EEW OSB Teesside site was officially announced, marking a significant milestone in Wilton Universal Group's expansion strategy. The facility is expected to enhance the company's capacity to secure more work in key markets, particularly in the offshore renewables sector.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The acquisition is projected to create up to 200 new jobs at the site, contributing positively to local employment in the region.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** Wilton Universal Group now owns the facility, having acquired it from EEW Special Pipe Constructions, indicating full ownership of the site. The acquisition was described as a harmonious transition, reflecting a positive relationship between the two companies.
