### Evolutionary Timeline of Eden Geothermal Limited Facility in Eden Project, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: The Eden Geothermal facility began operating, marking the UK's first deep geothermal energy project in 37 years. The facility utilizes a well that reaches a vertical depth of almost 5km, allowing it to harness water heated by the Earth's core, with temperatures reaching up to 200°C. The facility generates heat delivered via a 3.8km heat main to supply the Eden Project's new nursery, Growing Point, as well as its biomes and offices. The well is the longest geothermal well in the UK, with an actual length of almost 5.3km, and operates as a single well coaxial system.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: Eden Geothermal Limited (EGL) secured £24 million in funding from the European Regional Development Fund and Cornwall Council, along with commercial funding from investment trust GCP Infrastructure Investment to support the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: The facility officially began operations, generating heat for the Eden Project. The drilling for the well was completed in 2021, and the project is noted as a demonstration and research initiative aimed at showcasing the potential of deep geothermal energy in the UK.
