### Evolutionary Timeline of Eden Geothermal Ltd Facility in Eden Project, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: The Eden Geothermal facility officially began operations as the UK’s first operational deep geothermal heating plant since 1986. The facility utilizes a single well coaxial system, with a vertical depth of 4,871 meters and a measured depth of 5,277 meters, making it the longest geothermal well in the UK. The geothermal heat system delivers heat at around 85 degrees Celsius through a 3.8 km heat main to supply the Eden Project and its new nursery, Growing Point.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Funding**: The project was funded with £24 million from a combination of sources, including the European Regional Development Fund, Cornwall Council, and commercial funding from GCP Infrastructure Investments Limited. This funding was crucial for the research and development of the geothermal project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: The geothermal heating plant was switched on, marking a significant milestone in renewable energy for the UK. The drilling process was completed in 2021, leading to the establishment of the geothermal heating system.
