### Evolutionary Timeline of REG Power Management Ltd Facility in New Cumnock, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - REG Power Management Ltd, in collaboration with ESB, is developing a wind farm and battery storage facility adjacent to the former Greenburn opencast coal mine near New Cumnock. The project has undergone revisions, reducing the number of turbines from 16 to 14, with increased blade tip heights from 149.9 meters to 180 meters for 11 turbines and 167.5 meters for 3 turbines. The facility is expected to be fully operational towards the end of 2027.
