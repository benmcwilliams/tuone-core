#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-07-2023**: ESVAGT, in collaboration with Ørsted, announced the development of a second methanol-powered Service Operation Vessel (SOV) to be launched in 2026. The SOV will utilize dual fuel engines capable of running on renewable e-methanol, leading to an estimated annual carbon emissions reduction of approximately 4,500 tonnes.
