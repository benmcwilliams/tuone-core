1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** February 2016
   - **Details:** Construction of Europe’s largest floating solar PV array is underway, with a production capacity of 6.3MWp. The installation will cover approximately 10% of the Queen Elizabeth II reservoir and will consist of over 23,000 solar panels. The energy produced is intended to power a nearby water treatment facility. This project marks Lightsource's first floating solar installation, expanding their experience beyond rooftop and ground-mounted systems.
