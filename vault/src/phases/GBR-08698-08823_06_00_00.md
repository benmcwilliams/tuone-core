### Ennoviga Solar Facility Timeline - Queen Elizabeth II Reservoir, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** February 2016
   - **Details:** The facility is constructing Europe’s largest floating solar PV array with a production capacity of 6.3 MWp. The installation will cover approximately 10% of the Queen Elizabeth II reservoir and will consist of over 23,000 solar panels. The energy produced will be utilized to power a nearby water treatment facility.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** February 2016
   - **Details:** The project is a collaboration between Thames Water, Ennoviga Solar, and Lightsource. It is designed to help Thames Water meet its target of self-generating a third of its power by 2020. The project qualifies for a 1.5 ROC rate under the UK renewable obligation scheme, which is higher than the rate for ground-mounted systems.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** February 2016
   - **Details:** Construction of the floating solar PV array has commenced and is scheduled for completion by the end of March 2016. This marks the first floating solar installation for Lightsource, which has previously focused on ground and rooftop solar projects.
