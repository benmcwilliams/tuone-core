### Evolutionary Timeline of the Greencoat Facility in Hornsea 1, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2019:** Hornsea 1, an offshore wind farm, entered full commercial operation. It has a production capacity of 1,200 MW, comprising 174 Siemens 7 MW turbines. The facility benefits from a 15-year Contract for Difference (CfD) at a price of £175.25 per megawatt-hour.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **May 2022:** Greencoat UK Wind announced its intention to acquire a 12.5% stake in Hornsea 1 from Global Infrastructure Partners (GIP) for approximately £400 million (€470 million). The acquisition is expected to be funded from cash flow and, if necessary, the company's revolving credit facility.
   - **August 2022:** Greencoat UK Wind completed the acquisition of the 12.5% stake in Hornsea 1 for the same total consideration of approximately £400 million (€470 million).

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **May 2022:** Announcement of the acquisition agreement for a 12.5% stake in Hornsea 1.
