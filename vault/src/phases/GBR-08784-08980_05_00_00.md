### Evolutionary Timeline of the General Fusion Facility in Culham, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: January 2023
   - **Details**: General Fusion is set to construct a demonstration facility for its magnetised target fusion technology at the UK Atomic Energy Authority’s (UKAEA) Culham campus. The facility will be a 70% scale model of a commercial power plant, housed in a 10,500m² building. The technology aims to combine aspects of magnetic confinement and inertial confinement fusion, utilizing a hydrogen plasma injected into a liquid metal-lined vessel, which is then rapidly compressed to achieve fusion temperatures.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: January 2023
   - **Details**: The facility will be constructed on a lease from the UKAEA, with construction expected to start in the summer of 2023. The project is part of the UK’s broader strategy to enhance fusion research and innovation.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: January 2023
   - **Details**: General Fusion has received permission to construct the demonstration facility. Construction is anticipated to begin in summer 2023, with commissioning expected in 2026 and full operational status by early 2027.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: January 2023
   - **Details**: The project is expected to create approximately 60 long-term jobs at the site, along with around 200 jobs during the construction phase.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: January 2023
