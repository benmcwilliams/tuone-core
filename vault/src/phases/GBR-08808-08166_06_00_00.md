## GBR-08808-08166

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Voith Facility in Snowdonia, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2023
   - **Details:** The facility, known as the Dorothea Lakes Pump Storage project, will have a production capacity of 450 MW. It is designed to integrate wind, solar, and hydroelectric power, allowing for the storage of electricity produced during low demand for use during peak demand periods. The facility aims to replace fossil fuel generation and enable more renewable electricity.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2023
   - **Details:** The total investment for the project is £800 million (approximately US$1 billion), with £500 million ($644.8 million) allocated specifically for construction. It is noted that 70% of the project cost will be dedicated to civil works.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2023
   - **Details:** The acquisition of the Dorothea Lakes Pump Storage project by Fortune Hydro AG and Voith was announced. The project is positioned to contribute to the Welsh Government's goal of achieving net zero emissions by 2050. The facility is expected to generate significant economic development and new business opportunities in the local community.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** July 2023
   - **Details:** The project is expected to create 600 jobs during the construction phase and an additional 20 jobs for the operational phase, which is projected to last 100 years.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** July 2023
   - **Details:** Fortune Hydro UK will own and manage the Dorothea Lakes Pump Storage project, while Voith will provide the technology. Once operational, the facility will be run by Green Highland, a Scottish operator, under a service level agreement.
