### Evolutionary Timeline of the Galloper Facility in Harwich, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Galloper facility is an operations and maintenance (O&M) base for the 353MW Galloper offshore wind farm, which consists of 56 Siemens Gamesa 6.3MW turbines. The facility will support the wind farm's operations for over 20 years.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The construction of the O&M base involves an investment of £10 million. 

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **November 2018:** A ground-breaking ceremony was held on 16th November 2018, marking the start of construction for the new O&M base at the port of Harwich. The construction is expected to take about 12 months.

4. **Labour:**
   - The construction of the facility is projected to create 120 direct and indirect jobs. Once operational, the base will house a team of around 60 people dedicated to the operation and maintenance of the wind farm.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The Galloper facility is owned by a consortium that includes Innogy, Siemens Financial Services, Sumitomo, ESB, and is managed by Green Investment Group and Macquarie Infrastructure and Real Assets.
