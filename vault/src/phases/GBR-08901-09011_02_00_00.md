### Evolutionary Timeline of Geothermal Engineering Ltd. Facility in Langarth Garden Village, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility will produce geothermal heat for a district heating network, marking it as the first deep geothermal district heating network in the UK.
   - It is expected to supply geothermal heat to 3,800 homes at Langarth Garden Village, with potential for additional supply to the western side of Truro.
   - The project utilizes heat from a completed 5-km deep geothermal well at United Downs, which is part of the United Downs Deep Geothermal Power (UDDGP) project.

2. **Investment and Development:**

   - This funding will assist Cornwall Council and GEL in developing detailed plans for the geothermal heating network.
