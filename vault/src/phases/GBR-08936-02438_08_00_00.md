### Evolutionary Timeline of the ScottishPower Facility in East Anglia 1, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The East Anglia 1 facility is an offshore wind farm with a production capacity of 714 MW. 

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - On **November 11, 2020**, The Renewables Infrastructure Group (TRIG) announced its acquisition of a 14.3% equity stake in the East Anglia 1 offshore wind farm from the Green Investment Group. This investment is part of TRIG's strategy to increase its offshore wind portfolio, which is expected to represent approximately 29% of its overall investments.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - The investment transaction is expected to complete by the **first quarter of 2021**, pending consent from The Crown Estate. This marks TRIG's fourth investment in the offshore wind sector and its second in the UK.

4. **Labour:**
   - The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with this investment or the facility's operations.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is not exclusively owned by ScottishPower; it involves a joint investment structure where TRIG holds a 14.3% stake, and InfraRed European Infrastructure Income Fund 4 (IREIIF4) will acquire a 5.7% indirect equity interest alongside TRIG.
