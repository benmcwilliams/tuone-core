## GBR-08937-01420

### Evolutionary Timeline of the TotalEnergies Facility at Flotta Terminal, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2021**: Plans were announced to create a green hydrogen hub at the Flotta Terminal, leveraging offshore wind power for the production of zero-emission hydrogen. The facility aims to produce green hydrogen on an industrial scale, with the potential to become one of the world’s first green hydrogen plants. The proposed West of Orkney Windfarm, with a planned capacity of 2 gigawatts (GW), is expected to power the hydrogen production.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12/2021**: The consortium, including TotalEnergies, Macquarie’s Green Investment Group, and the Renewable Infrastructure Development Group (RIDG), is pursuing funding and approval for the hydrogen hub project, which is anticipated to be a multi-billion-pound development. Specific investment figures are not detailed, but the project is expected to significantly contribute to the local economy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2021**: The project is currently at the pre-Front End Engineering Design (FEED) stage, with expectations for funding and approval in the following year. The first hydrogen production and export are projected for 2028/2029. The consortium has submitted a proposal to Crown Estate Scotland for the development of the West of Orkney Windfarm.

#### 4. Labour
- **12/2021**: The development of the Flotta Hydrogen Hub is expected to create “hundreds of skilled jobs” during both the construction and operational phases. The project aims to retain and upskill the current workforce at the Flotta Terminal, which has been operational since 1976.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **12/2021**: The Flotta Terminal is owned by Repsol Sinopec, which is collaborating with TotalEnergies and other partners on the hydrogen hub project. The ownership structure indicates a joint venture approach, with multiple stakeholders involved in the development of the facility.

This timeline captures the key developments and plans for the TotalEnergies facility at Flotta Terminal, highlighting its transition towards green hydrogen production and the collaborative efforts involved in this initiative.
