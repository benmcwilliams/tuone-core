## GBR-08937-02390

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Offshore Wind Power Limited Facility at Flotta Terminal, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2021**: The Offshore Wind Power Limited (OWPL) consortium proposed the development of a green hydrogen production hub at the Flotta Terminal as part of its ScotWind bid. The facility aims to utilize renewable power from the proposed West of Orkney Windfarm, which has a planned installed capacity of 2 gigawatts (GW), capable of powering over two million homes. The hydrogen production is expected to be on an industrial scale.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12/2021**: The OWPL consortium, which includes TotalEnergies, Macquarie’s Green Investment Group, and Renewable Infrastructure Development Group (RIDG), is in the pre-Front End Engineering Design (FEED) stage for the project. The total investment for the offshore wind and hydrogen schemes is projected to be in the "multi-billion pound" range, although specific funding details are yet to be finalized.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2021**: OWPL submitted a proposal to Crown Estate Scotland to develop the N1 plan option area west of Orkney. The results of the ScotWind leasing process are expected to be released in early 2022. If successful, the first hydrogen production and export are anticipated to commence in 2028/2029.

#### 4. Labour
- **12/2021**: The development of the Flotta Hydrogen Hub is projected to create "hundreds of skilled jobs" during both the construction and operational phases. The facility's transition to green hydrogen production is expected to provide long-term employment opportunities, although specific job figures have not been disclosed.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **12/2021**: The Flotta Terminal is owned by Repsol Sinopec, which is collaborating with OWPL and Uniper on the hydrogen hub project. The partnership indicates a joint venture approach to the facility's development, with OWPL working closely with Repsol Sinopec and Uniper to explore opportunities within the energy transition.

This timeline captures the key developments and plans for the Offshore Wind Power Limited facility at Flotta Terminal, highlighting its focus on green hydrogen production and the collaborative efforts of various stakeholders involved in the project.
