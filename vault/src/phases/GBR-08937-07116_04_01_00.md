## GBR-08937-07116

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Renewable Infrastructure Development Group Facility at Flotta Terminal, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2021**: Plans are announced for the development of a green hydrogen hub at Flotta Terminal, leveraging offshore wind power for the production of zero-emission hydrogen. The facility aims to produce hydrogen on an industrial scale, with the potential to supply both national and international markets.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12/2021**: The project is described as having the potential to form a "multi-billion pound" development, although specific investment figures are not detailed. The consortium, including TotalEnergies, Macquarie’s Green Investment Group, and the Renewable Infrastructure Development Group, is working on securing funding and approvals for the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2021**: The facility is currently at the pre-Front End Engineering Design (FEED) stage, with expectations for funding and project approval in the following year. The first hydrogen production and export are anticipated to begin in 2028/2029. The consortium has submitted a proposal to Crown Estate Scotland for the development of the West of Orkney Windfarm, which is expected to power the Flotta Hydrogen Hub.

#### 4. Labour
- **12/2021**: The project is expected to create "hundreds of skilled jobs" during both the construction and operational phases of the Flotta Hydrogen Hub. The facility's transition to green hydrogen production is also linked to potential job retention and upskilling of the current workforce, as Repsol Sinopec aims to minimize staff reductions during the energy transition.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **12/2021**: The Flotta Terminal is owned by Repsol Sinopec, which is collaborating with the consortium (including RIDG) to explore the energy transition. The partnership indicates a joint effort rather than exclusive ownership by any single entity.

This timeline captures the key developments and plans for the Renewable Infrastructure Development Group facility at Flotta Terminal, highlighting its transition towards green hydrogen production and the collaborative efforts involved in this significant project.
