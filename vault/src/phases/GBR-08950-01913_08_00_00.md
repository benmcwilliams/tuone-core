### Evolutionary Timeline of the SSE Facility in Clyde, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2016**: The Clyde wind farm, operational with a production capacity of 350MW, consists of 152 Siemens 2.3MW turbines and has a net load factor of 33%. The facility is part of a larger complex that will eventually total 522MW, with an under-construction extension, Clyde 2, featuring 54 Siemens 3.2MW turbines, expected to come online to meet Renewables Obligation deadlines.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2016**: Greencoat UK Wind and pensions partners acquired a 49.9% stake in the operational Clyde wind farm for £355 million. This investment is part of a strategy to optimize SSE's wind farm pipeline and recycle capital for future investments.
- **March 2019**: SSE reported that the sale of stakes in the Clyde wind farm, among others, contributed to over £1 billion in cash proceeds from asset disposals, indicating ongoing financial maneuvers to support capital and investment expenditures.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2016**: The deal for the 49.9% stake acquisition was completed, allowing SSE to continue operating the assets while Siemens provided turbine operation and maintenance.
- **March 2019**: SSE confirmed that the Clyde facility, now featuring 206 turbines, is one of Europe’s largest operational wind farms, indicating its successful operational status and the completion of the initial phases.
