### Evolutionary Timeline of Tesla Facility at Lakeside Energy Park, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **December 2024**: The Lakeside Energy Park's 100 MW Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) is now the largest transmission-connected BESS project in the UK. This facility is designed to enhance the capacity and flexibility of the energy network by absorbing surplus clean electricity and discharging it back when needed.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **December 2024**: The project involved significant upgrades to the National Grid's Drax 132 kV substation to accommodate the new clean power from the Lakeside facility. This included extending busbars, upgrading protection and control systems, and installing an operational tripping scheme.

   - **December 2024**: The Lakeside BESS project was successfully energized and connected to the National Grid, marking a key milestone in the facility's operational status.
