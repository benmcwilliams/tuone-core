### Evolutionary Timeline of the Habitat Energy Facility at Lakeside Energy Park, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2024**: The Lakeside Energy Park facility, with a production capacity of **100 MW**, is established as the **largest transmission connected Battery Energy Storage System (BESS)** project in the UK. The facility is designed to enhance the capacity and flexibility of the energy network by absorbing surplus clean electricity and discharging it back to the grid when needed.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2024**: The project involved significant upgrades to the National Grid's Drax 132 kV substation to accommodate the new facility. This included extending busbars, upgrading protection and control systems, and installing an operational tripping scheme, although specific investment amounts are not detailed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2024**: The Lakeside BESS project is officially connected to the grid, marking a key milestone in the energy transition towards net-zero targets in the UK. The energisation of the facility is highlighted as a significant achievement in the project timeline.
