### Evolutionary Timeline of the RES Facility at Lakeside Energy Park, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2024**: The Lakeside Energy Park's 100 MW Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) is now the largest transmission-connected BESS project in the UK. This facility enhances the capacity and flexibility of the energy network by absorbing surplus clean electricity and discharging it when needed.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2024**: The connection of the Lakeside BESS to the National Grid involved significant upgrades to the Drax 132 kV substation, including extending busbars, upgrading protection systems, and installing operational tripping schemes. These upgrades were essential to accommodate the additional clean power from the BESS.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2024**: The Lakeside Energy Park BESS project was successfully energized and connected to the National Grid, marking a significant milestone in the facility's operational status.
