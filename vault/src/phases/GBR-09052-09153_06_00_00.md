   - **May 2016:** The Hounslow facility, located at Western International Market, installed a 1.73MW solar array with a battery storage solution. The system consists of 6069 LG Solar panels (285W each), a SolarEdge inverter, DC power optimisers, and four 60kW Tesvolt lithium-ion batteries.
