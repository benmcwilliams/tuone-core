### Evolutionary Timeline of Inch Cape Offshore Limited Facility in Port of Dundee, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2022
   - **Details:** Inch Cape Offshore Limited (ICOL) has appointed Vestas as the preferred supplier for 72 V236-15.0 MW offshore wind turbines for the 1.1 GW Inch Cape wind farm, located 15 kilometers off the Angus coast in East Scotland. The project will utilize the most powerful turbines available on the market, following the removal of the 1 GW capacity cap in August 2021. The facility will serve as Scotland’s largest single source of renewable power, generating enough energy to meet the annual demand of over one million homes. Port of Dundee has been selected as the pre-assembly and marshalling site for the construction of the wind farm.
