### Evolutionary Timeline of the Muir Construction Facility in Eyemouth Harbour, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 11-06-2021
   - **Details:** Muir Construction has been contracted to design and construct an operations and maintenance (O&M) base for the Neart na Gaoithe (NnG) Offshore Wind project at Eyemouth Harbour. The facility will include a three-storey main O&M building with a total area of 1,040 square metres and a two-storey storage warehouse of 538 square metres. The O&M base will support the servicing of the NnG wind farm once it becomes operational.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 11-06-2021
   - **Details:** The project is part of the NnG offshore wind farm, which is a significant infrastructure project for Scotland. The specific financial investment details for the construction by Muir Construction are not disclosed in the article, but the project is expected to bring socio-economic benefits to the local community.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 11-06-2021
   - **Details:** Muir Construction has started enabling works for the facility, with construction expected to commence in June 2021. The O&M base is part of a 25-year lease agreement signed between NnG (jointly owned by EDF Renewables UK and ESB) and Eyemouth Harbour Trust.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 11-06-2021
   - **Details:** The O&M base is projected to create up to 50 permanent jobs. Additionally, there will be training, retraining, and apprenticeship opportunities available for residents of Eyemouth and the surrounding area.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 11-06-2021
   - **Details:** The NnG offshore wind project is jointly owned by EDF Renewables UK and ESB. Muir Construction is contracted for the construction of the facility but does not own it exclusively.
