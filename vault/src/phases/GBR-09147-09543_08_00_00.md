### Neart na Gaoithe (NnG) Facility Timeline - Eyemouth Harbour, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Facility Type**: Operations and Maintenance (O&M) Base for the NnG Offshore Wind project.
- **Production Capacity**: The facility will support the servicing of the NnG wind farm, which is expected to have a significant operational lifespan of 25 years.
- **Facility Details**: The O&M base includes a three-storey main building of 1,040 square metres and a two-storey storage warehouse of 538 square metres. A new pontoon will also be constructed to facilitate access for crew transfer vessels.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investments**: The construction of the O&M base is part of the broader investment in the NnG project, which emphasizes the use of the Scottish supply chain. Specific financial details regarding the investment were not disclosed in the article.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Announcement Date**: 11-06-2021
- **Construction Start**: Enabling works have begun, with construction expected to start in June 2021.
- **Pontoon Installation**: Fabrication of the pontoon is set to begin in June 2021, with installation planned for later in the year.
- **Lease Agreement**: A lease was signed with Eyemouth Harbour Trust in February 2021, confirming the harbour as the O&M base for the project's 25-year lifespan.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The O&M base is expected to provide up to 50 permanent jobs. Additionally, there will be training, retraining, and apprenticeship opportunities available for local residents.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: The NnG project is jointly owned by EDF Renewables UK and ESB.
