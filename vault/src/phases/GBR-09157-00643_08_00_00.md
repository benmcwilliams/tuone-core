### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in Mynydd y Gwair, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 04-04-2023
   - **Details:** RWE operates the Mynydd y Gwair onshore wind farm, which, along with Brechfa Forest West, has a combined capacity of over 90 MW. The facility serves as a base for the operation and maintenance of onshore wind turbines, with a new Onshore Wind Service Centre established in Neath to support these operations. The centre includes a workshop for inspecting and maintaining wind turbine components, a warehouse for spare parts, and workspace for operational planning and training.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 04-04-2023
   - **Details:** RWE has invested £350,000 in the new Onshore Wind Service Centre in South Wales. Additionally, RWE has invested £250 million into three onshore wind projects in Wales, which includes the Mynydd y Gwair facility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 04-04-2023
   - **Details:** The announcement of the new Onshore Wind Service Centre marks a significant milestone in RWE's commitment to enhancing its operational capabilities in South Wales. The facility is intended to support the ongoing operations of the Mynydd y Gwair wind farm.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 04-04-2023
   - **Details:** The facility currently employs five technicians, with plans to hire additional staff, including a new apprentice, as part of RWE's commitment to developing a skilled workforce in the region.
