## GBR-09161-00643

### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in Brechfa Forest West, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2023**: RWE opened a new Onshore Wind Service Centre in Neath, South Wales, with an investment of £350,000. This facility will support the operation and maintenance of the Brechfa Forest West and Mynydd y Gwair onshore wind farms, which have a combined capacity of over 90 MW. The service centre includes a workshop for inspecting and maintaining wind turbine components, a warehouse for spare parts, and workspace for operational planning and training.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2023**: RWE has invested £250 million into three onshore wind projects in Wales, including the Brechfa Forest West and Mynydd y Gwair wind farms. The new service centre represents a further commitment to investment in the region.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **04/2023**: Announcement of the opening of the Onshore Wind Service Centre, which will serve as a central base for the operation and maintenance of onshore wind turbines in South Wales.

#### 4. Labour
- **04/2023**: The facility currently employs five technicians, with plans to hire additional staff, including a new apprentice, as part of RWE’s commitment to developing a skilled workforce in the region.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- RWE is the sole owner of the facility, as it is part of their operations in South Wales, where they are the largest power producer and renewable energy generator.
