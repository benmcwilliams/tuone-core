### Evolutionary Timeline of the Innova Facility in Elms Farm, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 02-12-2024
   - **Details:** The Elms Farm Solar Park has a production capacity of 27.8MWp. It is part of a joint venture called ISG Renewables, which is a collaboration between Innova and Schroders Greencoat. The facility has been successfully energised, marking it as the fourth solar DNO-level project for Innova.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** May 2024
   - **Details:** The Elms Farm Solar Park was acquired by ISG Renewables. Prior to this, planning permission was granted in 2022, and construction began in 2023. A significant investment was made to secure a 15-year corporate power purchase agreement (CPPA) with Anglian Water, which will offtake 100% of the energy generated.
