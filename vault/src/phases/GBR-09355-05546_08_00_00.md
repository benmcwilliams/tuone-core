### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mainstream Facility in Shetland Islands, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2022**: Mainstream Renewable Power and Ocean Winds were selected as the preferred bidders for a 1.8GW offshore wind farm site offshore from the Shetland Islands. The facility, named the Arven Offshore Wind Farm, is designed to produce enough clean energy to power over two million homes and offset three million tonnes of carbon emissions annually. The project will be developed on an industrial scale, leveraging Mainstream's expertise in floating offshore wind technology.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2022**: A seabed lease agreement was signed with Crown Estate Scotland for the Arven Offshore Wind Farm, valued at £36 million ($40.4 million). This fee is part of the option agreement for the site, indicating a significant financial commitment to the development of the facility.
