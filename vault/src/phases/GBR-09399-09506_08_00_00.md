#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2021**: The Crossdykes wind farm, developed by Muirhall Energy, became operational. It has a total installed capacity of 48MW, consisting of 10 Nordex turbines, each with a capacity of 4.8MW. It is noted as one of the largest unsubsidised wind farms in the UK.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2022**: Octopus Renewables Infrastructure Trust (ORIT) announced the acquisition of a 51% ownership interest in the Crossdykes wind farm, with the remaining 49% being acquired by another Octopus-managed fund.
