### Evolutionary Timeline of the Pentland Floating Offshore Wind Farm Facility in Dounreay, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 07-12-2022
   - **Details:** The Pentland Floating Offshore Wind Farm (PFOWF) is designed to produce 100 MW of renewable energy. The facility will utilize Stiesdal Offshore’s TetraSub as the floating foundation technology, which is noted as the world’s first fully industrialized floating offshore technology. The project aims to test and demonstrate floating wind technology solutions to reduce costs and accelerate industrialization.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 07-12-2022
   - **Details:** The projected lifetime expenditure for the Pentland floating wind farm is GBP 419 million, with an aim for 40-60% of the lifetime content to be sourced from the UK.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 07-12-2022
   - **Details:** The Highland Council’s North Planning committee approved the offshore consent application for the PFOWF. The next step involves the onshore application, which is scheduled for review by Councillors in January 2023. If consent is granted by Marine Scotland, construction is expected to begin in 2024, with the wind farm anticipated to be fully operational by 2026.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 07-12-2022
   - **Details:** The project is expected to create 1,300 full-time equivalent (FTE) jobs during the construction phase and 85 FTE jobs during the operational phase.
