#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date:** 11-10-2022  
  - The Pentland Floating Offshore Wind Farm (PFOWF) will utilize Stiesdal Offshore’s TetraSub as the floating foundation technology. This project is designed to have a production capacity of 100 MW, which is expected to provide enough green energy for approximately 70,000 homes annually, covering about 65% of the Highland Council area. The TetraSub technology is noted for being the world’s first fully industrialized floating offshore technology, allowing for lightweight and cost-effective floating foundations made from factory-made modules.
