## GBR-09694-09744

### Evolutionary Timeline of Pict Offshore Facility in Inverkeithing, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2020**: Pict Offshore begins manufacturing the "Get Up Safe" (GUS) motion-compensated lifting system at its facility in Inverkeithing, Fife. This system is designed for offshore wind turbine foundations, eliminating the need for external ladders and enhancing safety for technicians. The GUS system is included in the installation of 165 wind turbine foundations at the Hornsea 2 offshore wind farm.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2016**: Development of the GUS system commences, supported by a £250,000 Regional Selective Assistance grant from Scottish Enterprise.
- **2021**: Pict Offshore secures its first export opportunity with Ørsted for the GUS system to be deployed at three offshore wind farms in the US, indicating the technology's international market potential.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2020**: Announcement of the multi-million pound deal with Ørsted for the deployment of the GUS system at Hornsea 2.
- **December 2021**: Pict Offshore signs a deal with Ørsted to deploy the GUS system at the South Fork, Revolution Wind, and Sunrise Wind offshore wind farms in the US, marking the first export order for the company.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2020**: Following the deal with Ørsted, Pict Offshore doubles its headcount, enhancing its production and manufacturing capabilities at the Inverkeithing facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **October 2020**: Ørsted takes a minority stake in Pict Offshore during the collaboration to develop the GUS system, indicating a joint venture aspect in the ownership structure of the facility.
