### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in Neath, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2023
   - **Details:** RWE opened a new Onshore Wind Service Centre in Neath, which serves as the central base for the operation and maintenance of onshore wind turbines in South Wales. The facility supports the Brechfa Forest West and Mynydd y Gwair onshore wind farms, which have a combined capacity of over 90 MW. The centre includes a workshop for inspecting and maintaining wind turbine components, a warehouse for spare parts, and workspace for operational planning and training.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** April 2023
   - **Details:** RWE invested £350,000 in the establishment of the Onshore Wind Service Centre. This investment is part of RWE's broader commitment to the region, which includes a total investment of £250 million into three onshore wind projects in Wales.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** April 2023
   - **Details:** The Onshore Wind Service Centre was officially opened, marking a significant milestone in RWE's operations in South Wales. The facility is designed to enhance the efficiency and safety of maintenance operations for the nearby wind farms.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** April 2023
   - **Details:** The facility currently employs five technicians, with plans to hire additional staff, including a new apprentice. This reflects RWE's commitment to developing a skilled workforce in the region.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Details:** RWE owns the facility exclusively, as it is part of their operations in South Wales, where they are the largest power producer and renewable energy generator.
