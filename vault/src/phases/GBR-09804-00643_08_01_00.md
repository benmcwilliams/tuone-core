### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE's Sofia Offshore Wind Farm, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **08-09-2021**: RWE's Sofia offshore wind farm is set to have a production capacity of 1,400 MW, featuring 100 offshore turbines, each with a capacity of 14 MW. The offshore converter platform, which is a critical component of the facility, will consist of a 10,000-tonne topside and a 7,000-tonne jacket foundation, to be installed 220 km from the shore. Fabrication of the platform began in the third quarter of 2021, with offshore installation expected to complete by the end of 2024.
- **04-10-2024**: The first turbine blade for the Sofia offshore wind project is unveiled, manufactured at the Siemens Gamesa factory in Hull, UK. Offshore installation commenced earlier in 2024, and the project is on track for completion in 2026.
