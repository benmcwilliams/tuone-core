#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2011**: The ZephIR 300 Lidar, a remote sensing wind measurement tool, was deployed on the Cavendish platform to collect wind data for potential offshore wind farm development in the Dogger Bank area. This Lidar was capable of measuring wind speeds and directions from 10 meters above the platform up to 200 meters.
- **2013**: The Lidar was redeployed for a confidential meteorological project requiring highly accurate wind data for weather monitoring in the UK.
- **2014**: The Lidar was used on an existing substation within an offshore wind farm, demonstrating its versatility and acceptance in the industry.
- **2017**: The Lidar was deployed on the Fred. Olsen Windcarrier jack-up vessel, Brave Tern, to monitor wind speeds during crane lift operations for the installation of wind turbines at the Wilkinger Wind Farm.
- **2018**: The Lidar returned to onshore operations, providing standalone, bankable wind resource assessments for proposed multi-turbine wind farm sites.
