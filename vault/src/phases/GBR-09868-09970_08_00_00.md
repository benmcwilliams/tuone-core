### Evolutionary Timeline of Seagreen Wind Energy Limited Facility in Offshore Scotland, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2022
   - **Details:** The Seagreen wind farm has a production capacity of 1.1 GW and is equipped with 114 Vestas V164-10.0 MW wind turbines. As of April 2022, 21 turbines had been installed, marking the first installation of a double-digit megawatt capacity wind turbine offshore in Europe. The facility is recognized as Scotland’s largest and the world’s deepest fixed-bottom offshore wind farm.
