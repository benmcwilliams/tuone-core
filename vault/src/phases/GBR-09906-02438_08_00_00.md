- **Date: 12-11-2024**: ScottishPower Renewables announced a £1bn agreement with Siemens Gamesa to supply 64 SG 14-236 DD offshore wind turbines, each with a capacity of 15MW, for the East Anglia 2 (EA2) offshore wind farm.
