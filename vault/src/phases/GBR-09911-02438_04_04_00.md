### Evolutionary Timeline of the ScottishPower Facility in Felixstowe, GBR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 08-08-2022
   - **Details:** ScottishPower announced plans for a 100 megawatt (MW) green hydrogen plant at the Port of Felixstowe. The facility is designed to produce green hydrogen, which will be used to fuel approximately 1,300 hydrogen trucks at the port. The electrolyser facility will occupy an area "around the size of a football pitch" and is expected to come online in 2026. The project may also explore the production of green ammonia.

2. **Investment and Development:**

The NZHF, worth up to £240 million, aims to fund the development and deployment of low carbon hydrogen production to mitigate investment risks and reduce project costs.
