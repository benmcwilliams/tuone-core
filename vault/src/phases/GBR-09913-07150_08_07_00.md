## GBR-09913-07150

### SeAH Wind Facility Timeline at Able Marine Energy Park, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07/2021**: SeAH Wind announced plans to establish a monopile foundation factory at Able Marine Energy Park (AMEP) with a production capacity of approximately 240,000 tonnes. The facility is expected to be the sole supplier of monopiles in the UK, responding to increasing global demand.
- **11/2021**: SeAH Wind secured a multi-million-pound contract with Orsted to supply monopile foundations for the Hornsea 3 offshore wind project, marking the facility's first major contract. The factory is anticipated to become the world’s largest monopile production facility, capable of producing extra-large monopiles for new generation wind turbines.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07/2021**: SeAH Wind committed to a total investment of £260 million over three years for the establishment of the monopile factory at AMEP. This investment is supported by grant funding from the UK government’s Offshore Wind Manufacturing Investment Support scheme.
- **09/2021**: The total investment in the UK offshore wind industry exceeded £900 million, highlighting the significance of SeAH Wind's investment in the context of the broader industry growth.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07/2021**: Announcement of the factory establishment and investment commitment.
- **11/2021**: Confirmation of the contract with Orsted for the Hornsea 3 project, which is contingent upon the project securing a Contract for Difference and reaching a Final Investment Decision. Manufacturing operations are expected to start in 2023.

#### 4. Labour
- **07/2021**: The establishment of the factory is projected to create up to 750 direct jobs by 2030, contributing significantly to local employment in the Humber region.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- SeAH Wind is a UK-based subsidiary of SeAH Steel Holdings and is expected to have full ownership of the facility at AMEP, as indicated by its commitment to being the sole monopile supplier in the UK offshore wind supply chain. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones related to the SeAH Wind facility at Able Marine Energy Park, reflecting its strategic importance in the UK offshore wind sector.
