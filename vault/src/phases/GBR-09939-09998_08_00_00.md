### Evolutionary Timeline of the Shepherd Offshore Facility at Swan Hunter Shipyard, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-01-2021**: Shepherd Offshore announced plans to redevelop the Swan Hunter Shipyard for the offshore wind sector. The facility aims to support offshore wind projects, providing access to significant project sites such as Dogger Bank, Hornsea, and Firth of Forth. The site includes infrastructure such as access roads, two quays, two river berths, and a wet berth facility.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-01-2021**: The redevelopment of the Swan Hunter site is being pursued in partnership with North Tyneside Council, indicating a collaborative investment approach to attract offshore wind companies and stakeholders.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
