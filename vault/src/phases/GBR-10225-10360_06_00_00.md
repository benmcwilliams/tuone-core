### Evolutionary Timeline of the Vogt Solar Facility in Bourn, GBR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2010**: Vogt Solar announced plans for a 5MW crystalline-based solar park in Bourn, Cambridge. The facility will utilize approximately 22,000 crystalline modules on a 36-acre plot. The solar farm is expected to generate enough energy to power around 1,200 homes and save an estimated 55,000 tonnes of CO2 emissions over its 25-year lifetime.
