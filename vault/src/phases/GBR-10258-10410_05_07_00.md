   - **Date:** 11-01-2023
   - **Details:** The facility is proposed to develop an Advanced Radioisotope Technology for Health Utility Reactor, known as Project ARTHUR. It aims to produce medical isotopes essential for the diagnosis and treatment of diseases, particularly cancer. The facility is envisioned as a "global centre of excellence in nuclear medicine" and is intended to supply the National Health Service (NHS) in Wales and the rest of the UK with medical isotopes. The project is expected to have a commitment of 60 to 70 years.
