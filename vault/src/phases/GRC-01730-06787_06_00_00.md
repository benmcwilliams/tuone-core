### Evolutionary Timeline of Cero Development Facility in Domokos and Stylida, Greece

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **02/2024**: Cero Development Hellas received approval to add a battery energy storage system with a capacity of 749 MWh to its existing project for a 370 MW photovoltaic plant, named the Dristello solar power plant, located in Domokos and Stylida. The battery system will deliver electricity to the grid but will not be able to receive power from it. The nominal battery availability is projected to be 95%.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **02/2024**: The addition of the battery storage system is part of a broader strategy to enhance the photovoltaic project amid stagnating electricity consumption in Greece and challenges with grid capacity. The project is expected to benefit from the increasing necessity of energy storage for participation in renewable energy auctions.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **02/2024**: Cero Development Hellas received the green light from the Regulatory Authority for Energy, Waste and Water (RAEWW) to modify its producer certificate for the 370 MW photovoltaic project by incorporating battery energy storage.
