### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hive Facility in Central Greece, GRC

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2024**: Hive Energy announced the sale of four solar projects with a combined capacity of 267MW to German solar developer Juwia. These projects are located in Central Greece and were developed to ready-to-build status by Hive Energy. Juwi confirmed that construction would begin in the first quarter of 2024.
