### Evolutionary Timeline of the Juwi Facility in Central Greece, GRC

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **November 2023:** Juwi acquired a solar PV portfolio consisting of three projects with capacities of 160MW, 67MW, and 40MW, totaling 267MW. The projects were in a ready-to-build status at the time of acquisition.
   - **July 2024:** Juwi signed an agreement to sell the 267MW solar PV portfolio to investment group Foresight and management company Mirova. Juwi will remain involved in the projects as the engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) contractor, as well as for operation and maintenance (O&M) once commissioned. The installation will include approximately 430,000 bifacial solar PV modules, over 800 string inverters, and nearly 16,000 single-axis tracker systems.
