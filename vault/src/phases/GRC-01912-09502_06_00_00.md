1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **May 2023:** Motor Oil Group, through its subsidiary Motor Oil Renewable Energy (MORE), acquired a majority stake in Unagi, which owns interests in 16 solar projects with a total capacity of 1.9GW located across Macedonia and central Greece. This acquisition is part of Motor Oil's strategy to enhance its renewable energy portfolio, aiming for a total of 2GW by 2030. MORE already has an installed portfolio of 800MW across various wind, solar, and hydroelectric projects.
