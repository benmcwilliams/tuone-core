### Evolutionary Timeline of the Deutsche Werte Holding Facility in Thessaloniki, GRC

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2019
   - **Details:** Deutsche Werte Holding (DWH) has acquired an undisclosed stake in Maximus Terra, a Greek PV specialist. The facility is involved in deploying a solar pipeline, particularly focusing on a 200MW photovoltaic (PV) project split into two 100MW projects located in Xanthi and Florina, Greece. The expected annual turnover from every 25MW of solar is approximately €2 million (US$2.23 million), with a potential total turnover of around €340 million (US$379.75 million) if DWH opts to construct and maintain the PV parks themselves.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** May 2019
   - **Details:** DWH's investment in Maximus Terra is part of a strategy to tap into the renewable energy market in Greece, which is seen as having "highly attractive" conditions. The investment is aimed at leveraging the 1.36GW worth of renewables assets that Maximus Terra has, which includes wind, biomass, and biogas plants, alongside the solar projects.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** May 2019
   - **Details:** DWH's involvement is expected to lead to the commencement of the 200MW PV pipeline "very soon." The projects are in the finalization or construction phase, with a focus on ensuring all aspects of the renewable pipeline are thoroughly audited and scrutinized.
