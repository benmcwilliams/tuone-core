#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2020**: PPC Renewables is developing a 230 MW photovoltaic complex in Eordea, Greece, which is part of a larger initiative to convert lignite mining areas into solar power plants. The complex is divided into three parts, with the largest unit being a 200 MW solar power plant. The facility will utilize solar trackers to optimize energy production.

- **July 2020**: The total budget for the 230 MW solar power complex is reported to be EUR 131.5 million. #### 3. Project Status and Milestones
