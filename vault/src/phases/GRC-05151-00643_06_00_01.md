#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **01-08-2023**: RWE and PPC joint venture Meton Energy announced the construction of three solar projects totaling **259 MW** in the Amynteo Cluster 2, located on the site of the former Amynteo opencast lignite mine. The total capacity of solar farms being developed by the joint venture in Greece is up to **2 GW**. The projects are scheduled to start operations by the **end of 2024**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **01-08-2023**: The total investment for the three solar projects is **€196 million**, financed through:
  - **€98 million** from EU funds (NextGenerationEU fund, part of the Recovery & Resilience Facility Plan "Greece 2.0").
  - **€59 million** in commercial debt financing from Alpha Bank, Eurobank, and the National Bank of Greece.
  - **€39 million** in equity.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **01-08-2023**: Meton Energy made the final investment decision for the construction of the three solar projects. Construction is set to begin in **autumn 2023**, with operations expected to commence by the **end of 2024**.
