### Evolutionary Timeline of the Meton Energy SA Facility in Amynteo, Greece

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 02-08-2023
   - **Details:** Meton Energy SA is executing the Amynteo Cluster II, which consists of three photovoltaic (PV) parks with a total capacity of 280 MW. This is in addition to the five PV parks known as Amynteo Cluster I, which are currently under construction. The PV farms will be installed within the former Amynteo open pit lignite mine.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 02-08-2023
   - **Details:** The total investment for the Amynteo Cluster II is EUR 196 million (USD 215.1 million). This includes EUR 98 million sourced from Greece’s Recovery and Resilience Facility (RRF), EUR 59 million from commercial debt financing (Alpha Bank SA, Eurobank SA, and National Bank of Greece SA), and EUR 39 million from shareholders’ equity.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 02-08-2023
   - **Details:** Construction for the Amynteo Cluster II is set to begin in the autumn of 2023, with plans to bring the plants online by the end of 2024. The entire output from these three plants will be purchased by PPC under a 15-year power purchase agreement (PPA).

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 02-08-2023
   - **Details:** The article does not specify job creation or job loss related to the Amynteo Cluster II project.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 02-08-2023
   - **Details:** Meton Energy SA is a joint venture between RWE Renewables Europe & Australia and PPC Renewables, indicating that the facility is not under exclusive ownership but rather a collaborative partnership.
