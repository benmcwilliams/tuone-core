### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Renewables Europe & Australia Facility in Amynteo, Greece

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 02-08-2023
   - **Details:** The facility is involved in the production of solar energy through photovoltaic (PV) parks. The Amynteo Cluster II will add 280 MW of capacity, complementing the existing five schemes that will add 210 MWp of capacity in the same region. The PV farms will be installed within the former Amynteo open pit lignite mine.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 02-08-2023
   - **Details:** The total investment for the Amynteo Cluster II is EUR 196 million (USD 215.1 million). This includes EUR 98 million sourced from Greece’s Recovery and Resilience Facility (RRF), EUR 59 million from commercial debt financing (Alpha Bank SA, Eurobank SA, and National Bank of Greece SA), and EUR 39 million from shareholders’ equity.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 02-08-2023
   - **Details:** Construction for the Amynteo Cluster II is set to begin in the autumn of 2023, with plans to bring the plants online by the end of 2024. The project is being executed by Meton Energy SA, a joint venture between RWE Renewables Europe & Australia and PPC Renewables.
