### Evolutionary Timeline of the Meton Energy Facility in Amynteo, GRC

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **01-08-2023**: Meton Energy, a joint venture between RWE and PPC, announced the final investment decision for the construction of three solar projects totaling **259 MW** in the Amynteo Cluster 2, located on the site of the former Amynteo opencast lignite mine. This is in addition to the previously established Amynteo Cluster 1, which comprises **175 MW**. The total capacity of solar farms being developed by the joint venture in Greece is up to **2 GW**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **01-08-2023**: The total investment for the three solar projects in Amynteo Cluster 2 is **€196 million**. This investment will be financed through:
  - **€98 million** from EU funds (NextGenerationEU fund as part of the Recovery & Resilience Facility Plan "Greece 2.0").
  - **€59 million** in commercial debt financing from Alpha Bank, Eurobank, and the National Bank of Greece.
  - **€39 million** in equity.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **01-08-2023**: Construction work for the three solar projects is scheduled to begin in **autumn 2023**, with all three sites expected to start operations by the **end of 2024**. This marks a significant milestone following the earlier investment decision for the first five projects of the Amynteo pipeline.
