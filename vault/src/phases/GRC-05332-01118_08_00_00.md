### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Kozani, Greece

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2022**: Iberdrola's 50.4MW Askio III wind farm begins operations. It features 12 V150 4.2MW wind turbines, with a hub height of 105m and rotor diameter of 150m. This facility is part of Iberdrola's total installed wind power capacity of 308MW in Greece.
- **09/2023**: The 38MW Askio II wind farm is commissioned, consisting of nine Vestas V150 wind turbines, each with a 4.2MW capacity. This facility operates alongside the Askio III project, bringing the total operational capacity in the region to 421MW.
