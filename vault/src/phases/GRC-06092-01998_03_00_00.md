## GRC-06092-01998

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Terna Energy Facility in Aetolia-Acarnania, Greece

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Production Capacity**: Terna Energy operates a pumped hydroelectric energy storage project in Aetolia-Acarnania, which upon completion is expected to have a total installed capacity of **680 MW (production)** and **730 MW (pumping)**. This project is set to be Greece’s largest grid-connected energy storage investment.
- **Additional Projects**: Terna Energy is also constructing **101 MW of PV capacity** in Greece and has a **2 GW solar pipeline** for which it seeks licenses. The company operates **8.5 MW of solar capacity** and has a significant presence in the wind energy sector, owning about **1.2 GW of operating onshore wind power capacity**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment in Energy Storage**: The Amphilochia pumped hydroelectric energy storage project is expected to exceed **€500 million** in investment. Terna Energy has submitted a **€120 million loan request** to the European Investment Bank for this project.
- **Public Subsidy Support**: In February 2024, Terna Energy was awarded contracts in Greece’s second energy storage tender, which includes a **40 MW project** and a **12 MW installation** under its Heron subsidiary, receiving public subsidy support for capital and operational expenditure.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Construction Status**: The Amphilochia pumped hydroelectric energy storage project is currently under construction.
- **Acquisition Announcement**: On **24-06-2024**, it was announced that Masdar signed a definitive agreement to acquire **67% of Terna Energy** shares, with plans to launch a mandatory tender offer for 100% ownership following regulatory approvals.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The construction and operation of the Amphilochia project and other expansions are expected to create jobs, although specific numbers are not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership Transition**: As of **June 2024**, Masdar is in the process of acquiring **67%** of Terna Energy, with intentions to acquire **100%** of the shares, indicating a shift from Terna Energy's previous ownership structure to a majority ownership by Masdar.
