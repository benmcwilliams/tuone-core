#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Production Capacity**: Terna Energy operates the 330 MW Kafireas project on Evia Island, which is its largest wind project, consisting of a cluster of wind farms.
- **Future Plans**: The company is pursuing plans in offshore wind power and is constructing the Amphilochia pumped hydroelectric energy storage project, expected to have a total installed capacity of 680 MW (production) and 730 MW (pumping). Additionally, Terna Energy is building 101 MW of photovoltaic (PV) capacity and has a 2 GW solar pipeline for which it seeks licenses.
