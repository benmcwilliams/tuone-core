- **Production**: The facility is part of the Vermio wind farm, which includes Vermio North and Vermio South. It is designed to generate carbon-free energy.
- **Production Capacity**: The combined capacity of the three wind farms (Vermio North, Vermio South, and others) is expected to power the equivalent of nearly 200,000 Greek homes annually.
- **Updates**: Construction of the wind farms has already started, with power generation expected to begin in 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Total Investment**: The collaboration between Aer Soléir and Amazon involves a transformative investment exceeding $1 billion.

- **Financing**: The financing for the project includes €249 million in commercial debt from the National Bank of Greece and funds from the European Recovery & Resilience Fund.

- **Announcement Date**: The investment and project details were announced on November 21, 2024.

- **Expected Production Start**: Power generation is anticipated to begin in 2026.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: Aer Soléir is the developer and owner of the facility, indicating full ownership of the wind farm projects in collaboration with Amazon. The partnership also involves support from Quantum Capital Group and other financial institutions.
