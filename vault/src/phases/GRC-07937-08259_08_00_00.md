### Aer Soléir Facility in Vermio South, Greece - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production**: The Aer Soléir facility in Vermio South is part of a larger project that includes utility-scale wind farms. It is designed to generate carbon-free energy to support local electricity needs and Amazon's operations.
- **Production Capacity**: The combined capacity of the Vermio wind farm (which includes Vermio South and Vermio North) is expected to generate enough energy to power approximately 200,000 Greek homes annually.
- **Updates**: The construction of the wind farms has commenced, with power generation anticipated to start in 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Total Investment**: The collaboration between Aer Soléir and Amazon involves a transformative investment exceeding $1 billion.
- **Specific Allocations**: Aer Soléir has allocated more than €500 million specifically for wind projects across Greece, with €1.8 million earmarked for reforestation and biodiversity programs in the Vermio region.
- **Financing**: The financing for the project includes €249 million in commercial debt from the National Bank of Greece and funds from the European Recovery & Resilience Fund.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Announcement Date**: The investment and project details were announced on November 21, 2024.
- **Construction Start**: Construction of the wind farms has already begun as of the announcement date.
- **Production Start**: Power generation is expected to begin in 2026.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The construction of the Vermio wind farm has already created over 100 construction jobs, with expectations of up to 300 roles during peak construction activity.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: Aer Soléir is the developer and owner of the Vermio wind farm, indicating full ownership of the facility. The project is supported by investors including Quantum Capital Group and 547 Energy, but Aer Soléir retains exclusive ownership.
