#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2022**: Cero Generation announced the development of the Delfini solar photovoltaic (PV) project in Prosotsani Drama, Greece, with a production capacity of **100MW**. The facility is expected to generate **142GWh** of electricity annually and offset **63,000 tons** of carbon dioxide emissions per year.
- **September 2024**: The Delfini solar PV plant reached **commercial operations**, marking a significant milestone for Cero Generation as it became operational without leveraging government subsidies.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2022**: Cero Generation achieved **financial close** for the Delfini project, indicating that necessary funding and investment arrangements were finalized. The project is also noted to be among the first in Greece to utilize a private power purchase agreement (PPA) without government subsidies.
- **September 2024**: The project benefited from the European Union’s **Next Generation Recovery and Resilience Facility**, which supports renewable energy initiatives.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2022**: Construction works commenced for the Delfini solar PV facility, with an expected completion date set for the **third quarter of 2023**.
- **September 2024**: The facility officially reached **commercial operation**, confirming its readiness to generate electricity and fulfill its PPA obligations.
