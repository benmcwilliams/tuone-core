## GRC-07978-01998

### Evolutionary Timeline of Terna Energy Facility in Amfilochia, Greece

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 07-05-2021
   - **Details:** Terna Energy announced the construction of a pumped storage hydropower system in Amfilochia, with a production capacity of 680 MW in turbine mode and 730 MW for pumping. The facility will consist of two upper reservoirs (Agios Georgios and Pyrgos) and a lower reservoir (Kastraki lake). The annual output is projected to be 816 GWh.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 07-05-2021
   - **Details:** The total investment for the Amfilochia pumped storage project is valued at EUR 500 million. The project has received strategic investment and Project of Common Interest (PCI) status from the European Commission.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 07-05-2021
   - **Details:** Terna Energy reached a final investment decision for the project, with construction scheduled to start in October 2021 and expected to last up to four years.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 07-05-2021
   - **Details:** The construction phase is expected to create approximately 1,200 jobs, with 100 permanent jobs anticipated upon completion of the facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 07-05-2021
   - **Details:** The facility is owned by Terna Energy, which is part of the GEK Terna Group, indicating full ownership of the Amfilochia facility.
