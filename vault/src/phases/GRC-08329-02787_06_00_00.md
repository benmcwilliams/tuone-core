### Evolutionary Timeline of the Conergy Facility in Panagia Domokou, Greece

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2012
   - **Details:** Conergy announced the construction of a 1MW rooftop solar power plant on the factory roof of Nomikos A.B.E.K. in Panagia Domokou. The installation will cover approximately 47,000 square meters and will utilize 4,000 PowerPlus modules and over 60 Conergy IPG T inverters. The facility is expected to generate around 1.4 million kWh of electricity annually, sufficient to power more than 350 households, while also reducing CO2 emissions by over 1,200 tonnes.
