### Evolutionary Timeline of the Deutsche Werte Holding Facility in Florina, Greece

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2019
   - **Details:** Deutsche Werte Holding (DWH) has become a shareholder in Maximus Terra, a Greek PV specialist. The facility in Florina is part of a 200MW solar pipeline, which is split into two 100MW projects (Xanthi and Florina). The expected annual turnover from every 25MW of solar is approximately €2 million (US$2.23 million). DWH has the option to either realize short-term profits or construct and maintain the PV parks, which could unlock around €340 million (US$379.75 million) in turnover.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** May 2019
   - **Details:** DWH's investment in Maximus Terra allows them to access a portfolio of 1.36GW worth of renewable assets, including the Florina facility. The licensing fees for the projects are around €100,000 (US$111,681) per MW.

   - **Date:** May 2019
   - **Details:** The Florina solar project is part of a pipeline that is expected to break ground "very soon." The project is included in a broader strategy to capitalize on attractive conditions in the Greek renewable market.
