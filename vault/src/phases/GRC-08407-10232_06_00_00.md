### Evolutionary Timeline of the Thrace Group Facility in Xanthi, Greece

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: Thrace Group developed the Helios Reflect System (HRS), a reflective, permeable membrane made of polypropylene resins, designed to enhance the energy yield of bifacial photovoltaic (PV) projects. The HRS was installed in two 1 MW bifacial PV plants in Xanthi, Northern Greece. The installations demonstrated a 69% increase in albedo reflectance and a total energy yield rise of at least 4.5%.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The installation cost for the HRS was reported at €0.014 ($0.015)/W, which includes materials, labor, and accessories. The facility is capable of installing approximately 2,000 square meters of HRS per working day with a team of at least three workers.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: The HRS was installed in two 1 MW bifacial PV plants in Xanthi, with performance measurements compared to a control 1 MW installation in the same area over a year. The project showcased significant improvements in energy yield and albedo reflectance.
