## GRC-08473-05766

### Evolutionary Timeline of the PPC Facility in Lesvos, Greece

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** PPC Renewables, a subsidiary of the state energy company PPC, is set to utilize geothermal potential in Lesvos. The facility will produce geothermal energy with a capacity of 8 MW in Lesvos, alongside 5 MW in three other geothermal fields (Milos-Kimolos-Polyaigos, Nisyros, and Methana). The facility will be managed by a special purpose company named “Geothermal Target Two – Single Person SA,” which will handle financing, construction, and management of the power plants.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** PPC Renewables has entered into a partnership with ELECTOR of the ELLAKTOR group as a strategic partner for the geothermal projects. The strategic partner will hold a majority percentage in the special purpose company, which indicates a significant investment in the geothermal energy sector.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** The international tender for selecting a strategic partner was completed, and ELECTOR was awarded the position. The first exploratory drilling is scheduled to begin in Milos by the end of 2020, with the goal of having the power plant operational by 2025.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** While specific job creation numbers are not mentioned, the establishment of the special purpose company and the construction of the geothermal plants are expected to create jobs in the region, particularly during the construction and operational phases.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** PPC retains ownership of the exploitation contracts for the geothermal fields. The facility is not fully owned by PPC, as it has entered into a partnership with ELECTOR, which will hold a majority stake in the special purpose company responsible for the project.
