## GRC-08474-08679

### Evolutionary Timeline of the ELECTOR Facility in Methana, Greece

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** ELECTOR, part of the ELLAKTOR group, was awarded the position of strategic partner by PPC Renewables for the energy utilization of four geothermal fields, including Methana. The facility will produce geothermal energy with a total capacity of 5 MW for the Methana field, alongside 8 MW in Lesvos and 5 MW in Nisyros and Milos-Kimolos-Polyaigos. The goal is to start exploratory drilling in Milos by the end of 2020, with the power plants expected to be operational by 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** The special purpose public limited company, “Geothermal Target Two – Single Person SA,” will be established to undertake financing, construction, and management of the power plants. The strategic partner (ELECTOR) will enter with a majority percentage in this company, indicating a significant investment in the geothermal projects.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** The project is in the decisive stage of utilizing geothermal potential, with the first exploratory drilling planned for Milos by the end of 2020. The construction of the power plants is scheduled to be completed and operational by 2025.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** PPC Renewables retains ownership of the exploitation contracts and will carry out the drilling and necessary work to supply geothermal fluid to the power plants. ELECTOR will have a majority stake in the special purpose company formed for the project, indicating a joint venture structure rather than full ownership by ELECTOR.
