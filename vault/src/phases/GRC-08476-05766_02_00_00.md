### Evolutionary Timeline of the PPC Facility in Nisyros, Greece

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** PPC Renewables, a subsidiary of the state energy company PPC, is set to utilize geothermal potential in Nisyros as part of a broader project involving four geothermal fields (Lesvos, Milos-Kimolos-Polyaigos, Nisyros, and Methana). The facility will produce geothermal energy with a planned capacity of 5 MW for Nisyros, alongside other fields. The power plants will be constructed and operational by 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** PPC Renewables has established a special purpose public limited company named “Geothermal Target Two – Single Person SA” to manage the financing, construction, and operation of the geothermal power plants. The strategic partner, ELECTOR, will hold a majority stake in this company, indicating a collaborative investment approach.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** The international tender for selecting a strategic partner was completed, with ELECTOR awarded the position. The first exploratory drilling in the geothermal field of Milos is scheduled to start by the end of 2020, with subsequent developments in Nisyros planned for the second year, contingent on local community consultations.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** While specific job creation figures are not mentioned, the establishment of the geothermal power plants and the associated drilling and construction activities are expected to create jobs in the region, particularly during the construction phase.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** PPC Renewables retains ownership of the exploitation contracts for the geothermal fields, while the special purpose company, in which ELECTOR holds a majority stake, will manage the construction and operation of the power plants.
