### Evolutionary Timeline of the ELECTOR Facility in Nisyros, Greece

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** The ELECTOR facility, part of a strategic partnership with PPC Renewables, is involved in the energy utilization of geothermal fields. The facility will contribute to the development of geothermal power plants with a total capacity of 5 MW in Nisyros, as part of a broader project that includes other geothermal fields (Lesvos, Milos-Kimolos-Polyaigos, and Methana). The goal is to start exploratory drilling in Milos by the end of 2020, with the power plants expected to be operational by 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** The partnership involves the establishment of a special purpose public limited company, “Geothermal Target Two – Single Person SA,” which will handle financing, construction, and management of the geothermal power plants. The strategic partner, ELECTOR, will hold a majority percentage in this company.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 28-06-2020
   - **Details:** The project is in the decisive stage of utilizing geothermal potential, with the first exploratory drilling planned for Milos by the end of 2020. The construction of the power plants is scheduled to be completed and operational by 2025.

   - **Details:** The facility is not exclusively owned by ELECTOR; it is part of a joint venture with PPC Renewables. PPC retains ownership of the exploitation contracts and will conduct the drilling and necessary work to supply geothermal fluid to the power plants.
