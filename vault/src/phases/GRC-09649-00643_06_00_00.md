### Evolutionary Timeline of the RWE Facility in Orycheio Dei Amynteo, Greece

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **09-02-2024:** RWE and PPC's joint venture, Meton Energy, took a final investment decision (FID) to build a 450MW solar farm in Orycheio Dei Amynteo, part of the Amynteo portfolio which will total 1GW capacity. This facility will be the third cluster of the Amynteo portfolio, complementing the existing clusters I and II, which are under construction with a total capacity of 490MW. The solar farm is expected to be commissioned by the end of 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **09-02-2024:** The total investment for the Orycheio Dei Amynteo solar project is €255.4 million. This includes €127.7 million from the EU’s NextGenerationEU Recovery and Resilience Facility plan, €76.6 million in debt financing from Alpha Bank, Eurobank, and the National Bank of Greece, and a shareholders’ equity contribution of €51.1 million.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **09-02-2024:** The final investment decision was announced, and construction is expected to begin in the spring of 2024. The project is part of a larger initiative that includes eight solar projects already under construction, which will be connected to the grid in 2024.

4. **Labour:**
   - The articles do not specify job creation or job loss related to the Orycheio Dei Amynteo facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is a joint venture between RWE Renewables Europe & Australia (51% ownership) and PPC Renewables (49% ownership), under the name Meton Energy.
