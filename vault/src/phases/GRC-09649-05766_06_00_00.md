1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **09-02-2024:** The Orycheio Dei Amynteo solar farm, part of the Amynteo portfolio, is set to have a production capacity of 450MW. This facility is part of a larger portfolio that will total 1GW, with the first two clusters (I and II) already under construction, comprising eight solar projects with a total capacity of 490MW. The solar farm is expected to be commissioned by the end of 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **09-02-2024:** The total investment for the Orycheio Dei Amynteo solar farm is €255.4 million. This includes €127.7 million from the EU’s NextGenerationEU Recovery and Resilience Facility plan, €76.6 million in debt financing from Alpha Bank, Eurobank, and the National Bank of Greece, and a shareholders’ equity contribution of €51.1 million.

   - **09-02-2024:** The final investment decision (FID) for the Orycheio Dei Amynteo solar farm has been taken. Construction is expected to begin in the spring of 2024 and the facility is projected to be operational by the end of 2025.
