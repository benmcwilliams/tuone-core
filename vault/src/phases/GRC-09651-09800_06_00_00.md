### Evolutionary Timeline of Public Power Corp. Facility in Ptolemaida, Greece

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date of Publication: 05-02-2024**
  - Public Power Corp. (PPC) announced the construction of a **550 MW solar plant** at a former lignite mine in Ptolemaida. 
  - The facility will utilize approximately **950,000 PV panels** of bifacial technology on single-axis trackers.
  - Upon completion, the plant is expected to generate **1 TWh** of electricity, sufficient to meet the needs of approximately **200,000 households**.
  - The project is part of a broader initiative to install **3 GW of solar capacity** in mining regions.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date of Publication: 05-02-2024**
  - The total budget for the solar plant is **€368 million** ($397.6 million).
  - Funding includes a long-term loan of **€294.4 million**, with **€184 million** from the Recovery and Resilience Fund (RRF) and **€110.4 million** from Eurobank and Piraeus Bank. 
  - The remaining **€73.6 million** will be financed by PPC's own funds.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date of Publication: 05-02-2024**
  - The project is expected to be completed by **2025**.
  - The announcement of the project marks a significant milestone in PPC's transition towards renewable energy.
