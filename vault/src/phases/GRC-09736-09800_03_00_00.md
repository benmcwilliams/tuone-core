### Evolutionary Timeline of the Public Power Corp. Facility in Mesochora, Greece

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2021:** The Mesochora hydropower plant is envisaged to have a production capacity of **161.6 MW** and is expected to generate an estimated annual output of **362 GWh**. The facility will consist of three units and aims to produce cheap and green electricity while also providing flood protection by storing over **250 million cubic meters** of water.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **December 2021:** Public Power Corp. (PPC) has invested **EUR 280 million** in the Mesochora project so far. The project had faced delays primarily due to environmental concerns and issues related to the diversion of the Acheloos river.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2021:** The Greek government announced the revival of the Mesochora hydropower project after long delays, with the Ministry of the Environment and Energy approving the environmental conditions necessary for the project to proceed. The completion of the plant is considered a priority for the government.
