### Evolutionary Timeline of the SPI Solar Facility in Rhodope Prefecture, Greece

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** February 2012
   - **Details:** SPI Solar announced the development of a 1MW fixed-ground-mount solar facility in Rhodope Prefecture, Greece. The facility will utilize high-performance solar modules from LDK and will be responsible for operations and maintenance over a 20-year Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with the Greek Public Power Corporation.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** February 2012
   - **Details:** The project is part of SPI Solar's broader expansion in Greece, which includes a total of 7.4MW under development across three projects within a year. Specific investment amounts for the Rhodope facility were not disclosed, but it is part of a strategic expansion in a favorable business environment.
