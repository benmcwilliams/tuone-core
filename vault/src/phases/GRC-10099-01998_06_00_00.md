### Evolutionary Timeline of the Terna Energy Facility in Pournariou, Greece

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: January 2022
   - **Details**: Terna Energy is developing the first floating photovoltaic (PV) plant in Greece, located on the artificial lake Pournariou in the Arta region. The facility will have a production capacity of 103 MW and is expected to generate approximately 156.2 GWh annually, resulting in a CO2 saving of 150,000 tonnes. The project will cover an area of 114.4 hectares, which is 5.6% of the reservoir.

   - **Details**: The total budget for the floating PV plant at Pournariou, along with two additional planned floating PV plants (one on lake Kastrakiou with 120 MW capacity and another on lake Stratou with 42 MW capacity), is EUR 170 million.
