### Evolutionary Timeline of the Valorem Facility in Magoula, Greece

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2022
   - **Details:** The Magoula facility is a wind farm located on Askio Mountain, utilizing six Vestas V150-4.5MW wind turbines, totaling a production capacity of 27MW. The facility is set to be operational in 2024.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2022
   - **Details:** The total investment for the Magoula wind farm is €40 million. Valorem secured long-term bank financing of nearly €31 million from Alpha Bank, which includes a VAT tranche and a Debt Service Reserve Facility.

   - **Details:** Construction of the Magoula wind farm is set to start shortly, with commissioning forecasted for the first quarter of 2024. The facility has secured a feed-in tariff in July 2020 through an auction organized by Greece’s Regulatory Authority for Energy (RAE).
