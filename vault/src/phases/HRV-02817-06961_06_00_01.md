#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06-02-2019**: Hrvatska elektroprivreda (HEP) issued a call for developers for utility-scale solar projects, indicating plans to construct or acquire solar power plants exceeding 2 MW capacity. The facility on Cres is part of this initiative, with two solar parks under development: the 6.5 MW Orlec Trinket project and the 4.14 MW Orlec Trinket West Solar Power Plant. These installations will be the first non-integrated solar plants in HEP’s portfolio.

- **09-04-2019**: HEP announced a plan to expand its solar power generation portfolio, which includes the construction of four PV plants with a combined capacity of 11.6 MW on the islands of Cres and Vis. This expansion is part of a larger goal to achieve a total solar capacity of 350 MW by 2030.
