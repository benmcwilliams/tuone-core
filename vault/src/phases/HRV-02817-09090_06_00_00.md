### Evolutionary Timeline of HEP Hrvatska elektroprivreda Facility in Cres, HRV

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **June 2020:** HEP Hrvatska elektroprivreda began construction of the SE Cres solar power plant, which has a production capacity of 6.5 MW. The facility will consist of 17 sub-units of 500 kW each, utilizing 20,330 solar panels supplied by Croatian manufacturer Solvis d.o.o. The project is designed to ensure sufficient electricity supply during the peak tourist season and will be located on a 17-hectare plot of land.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **June 2020:** The total investment for the initial four PV plants, including SE Cres, is approximately HRK 80 million ($11.8 million). HEP plans to invest HRK 150 million annually in solar projects until 2023, aiming for a total investment of around HRK 750 million and annual capacity additions of about 20 MW.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **June 2020:** Construction of the SE Cres solar power plant commenced, with commercial operations scheduled to begin by the end of the year. This project is part of HEP's broader plan to expand its solar power generation portfolio, which includes a total of 350 MW.
