### Evolutionary Timeline of the H2 - Hydrogen Cell Association Facility in Vis, HRV

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06/2023**: The H2 - Hydrogen Cell association, in collaboration with the City of Vis, is developing a hydrogen filling station, which will produce green hydrogen for boats and utility vehicles. The hydrogen will be generated using electricity from the local solar power plant and waste treatment processes. The facility aims to utilize electrolyzers to separate water into hydrogen and oxygen gases.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06/2023**: The City of Vis plans to secure EUR 2.9 million for the construction of the hydrogen filling station. Additionally, the local authority is set to invest EUR 6.7 million by 2025 on utility vehicles and vessels that will utilize hydrogen. The project has been submitted for public funding to European and Croatian investment funds.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07/2021**: The City of Vis signed a letter of intent with the H2 - Hydrogen Cell association to initiate the hydrogen filling station project.
- **06/2023**: The project is in the planning stages, with all necessary studies and documentation completed in cooperation with the H2 association. The goal is to start hydrogen production and build the filling station by 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **06/2023**: While specific job creation or loss figures are not mentioned, the development of the hydrogen filling station and associated projects is expected to create jobs related to the construction and operation of the facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **06/2023**: The facility is a collaborative project between the City of Vis and the H2 - Hydrogen Cell association, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by one entity.
