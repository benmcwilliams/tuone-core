## HRV-04768-06961

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hrvatska elektroprivreda Facility in Korlat, HRV

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **October 2024:** Announcement of a new 99 MW solar project to be constructed near Korlat, Croatia. This facility will be integrated with an existing 58 MW wind farm built in 2021, creating a hybrid energy project. The solar plant is set to become the largest in Hrvatska elektroprivreda's renewable energy portfolio.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **October 2024:** The European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD) and the European Investment Bank (EIB) have signed loan agreements totaling €62 million ($67.2 million) for the construction of the solar plant. This includes €31.6 million from the EBRD and €30.4 million from the EIB.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **October 2024:** Construction of the solar park is scheduled to begin in the first quarter of 2025, with commissioning expected in 2026.

4. **Labour:**
   - No specific information regarding job creation or job loss has been mentioned in the articles.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is owned exclusively by Hrvatska elektroprivreda (HEP), a Croatian state-owned utility.
