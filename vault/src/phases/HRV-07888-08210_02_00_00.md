### A14 Energy Limited Facility Timeline - Ernestinovo, HRV

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is focused on geothermal energy production, specifically aiming to develop a 10-MW geothermal power plant using Organic Rankine Cycle (ORC) technology. 
   - As of December 2023, three deep exploration wells have been drilled, and 2D seismic data has been collected and processed, indicating potential for geothermal development.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The British company Star Energy, through its subsidiary GT Energy Croatia Limited (GTEC), acquired a controlling stake in A14 Energy Limited earlier in 2023. This acquisition is part of their strategy to enter the Croatian geothermal market.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2023:** Star Energy mobilized a rig to the Ernestinovo geothermal site for drilling operations, which are expected to last about a month, with results anticipated by January 2024. The operation includes re-entry into the Ernestinovo-3 well.
