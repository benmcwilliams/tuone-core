### Evolutionary Timeline of the British Star Energy Facility in Ernestinovo, HRV

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2023:** British Star Energy, through its subsidiary GT Energy Croatia Limited (GTEC), is engaged in drilling operations at the Ernestinovo geothermal site. The facility aims to develop a 10-MW geothermal power plant utilizing Organic Rankine Cycle (ORC) technology. Three deep exploration wells have been drilled, and 2D seismic data has been collected, indicating potential for geothermal energy production.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **2023:** British Star Energy entered the Croatian geothermal market by acquiring a controlling stake in A14 Energy Limited, which holds the exploration license for the Ernestinovo area. This acquisition is part of their strategy to expand into the geothermal sector in Croatia.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2023:** The drilling operation at the Ernestinovo site is mobilized, with expectations for results by January 2024.
