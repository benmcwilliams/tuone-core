### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nordex Facility in Sinj, Croatia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2023**: Nordex received an order for 56 MW of wind power in Croatia, specifically for the “Visoka” wind farm. This order includes eight N163/6.X wind turbines, which are part of the Delta4000 series. The turbines will be installed on tubular steel towers with a hub height of 113 meters. The turbines are scheduled to go into operation at the end of 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2023**: The order for the N163/6.X turbines is part of a joint investment by ENCRO d.o.o and Adris Grupa, contributing to the growth of renewable energy generation capacity in Croatia. The project is aligned with the EU’s Recovery and Resilience Facility, indicating a significant investment in renewable energy infrastructure.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2023**: Announcement of the order for the N163/6.X turbines for the Visoka wind farm, with delivery and installation planned for autumn 2024. The project marks a significant milestone as it is the first order for the N163/6.X turbines from Croatia.
