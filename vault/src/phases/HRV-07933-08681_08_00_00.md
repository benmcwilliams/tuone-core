## HRV-07933-08681

### Evolutionary Timeline of the ENCRO d.o.o Facility in Sinj, HRV

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2023
   - **Details:** ENCRO d.o.o, in collaboration with Adris Grupa, has ordered eight N163/6.X wind turbines for the "Visoka" wind farm, totaling 56 MW of wind power capacity. The turbines will be installed on tubular steel towers with a hub height of 113 meters. The wind farm is scheduled to go into operation at the end of 2024. ENCRO currently has 141 MW in operation, 164 MW under construction, and a pipeline of 1,000 MW.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2023
   - **Details:** The order for the turbines includes a Premium Service covering a period of 30 years, ensuring high availability and optimal performance of the wind farm. This project is part of ENCRO's efforts to enhance renewable energy generation capacity in Croatia, contributing to the EU’s Recovery and Resilience Facility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2023
   - **Details:** The "Visoka" wind farm project has been announced, with construction expected to start in autumn 2024. This marks a significant milestone as it is the first order for N163/6.X turbines in Croatia.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** November 2023
   - **Details:** While specific job creation or loss figures are not mentioned in the article, the expansion of renewable energy projects typically contributes to job creation in construction and operational phases.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** November 2023
