### Evolutionary Timeline of the Bukotermal Facility in Varazdin County, Croatia

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2024
   - **Details:** Bukotermal has initiated the design and permitting phase for a proposed 16-MW geothermal power project. This facility will be the second geothermal power plant in Croatia and the first in the northern region. The project will utilize the Lunkjovec-1 well for reinjection of cooled fluids and plans to drill two new injection wells. The geothermal resource at the site has been assessed to support a maximum output of 16 MW, with an additional capacity to supply around 90 MW of thermal energy for agrothermal applications.
