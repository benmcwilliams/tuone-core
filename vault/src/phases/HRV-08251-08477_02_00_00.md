### CROSCO Facility Evolution Timeline - Slatina, Croatia

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **August 2024:** CROSCO begins drilling operations for the Slatina 2 geothermal project, targeting a total depth of 5000 meters. The facility is planned to produce geothermal energy with a capacity of 20 MW using Organic Rankine Cycle (ORC) technology. Once completed, it will be the largest ORC power plant in the European Union.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **August 2024:** The Slatina 2 geothermal project is set to require an investment of over EUR 140 million.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **2019:** Initial plans to build the geothermal power plant in the Slatina 2 field were announced after Geo Power Zagocha was awarded exploration rights.
   - **2022:** Further exploration work was conducted, and the Enna group took over the Babina Greda 1 geothermal project.
