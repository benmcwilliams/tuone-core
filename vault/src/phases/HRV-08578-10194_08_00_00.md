### Evolutionary Timeline of Taaleri Energia Facility in Zadar, Croatia

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** August 2022
   - **Details:** Taaleri Energia, in partnership with Encro, acquired a 111MW wind farm in Zadar, Croatia. The facility consists of 25 Nordex wind turbines and is expected to supply nearly 317GWh of electricity annually to the Croatian grid. It has the capacity to power approximately 85,000 households and offset around 78,000 tons of carbon emissions each year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** August 2022
   - **Details:** The acquisition of the Zadar wind farm is part of Taaleri Energia's Taaleri SolarWind II fund, marking the eleventh investment from this fund. The project signifies Taaleri's commitment to expanding its presence in South-East Europe, which is identified as a key target market for renewable energy investments.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** August 2022
   - **Details:** Construction works at the Zadar wind farm began in the second quarter of 2021, with the project expected to come online in the fourth quarter of 2023. Encro, as the original developer, will provide construction management and technical services, while Nordex will maintain the turbines under a 30-year contract.
