## HRV-08982-02971

### Evolutionary Timeline of the HEP Facility in Kosinj, Croatia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2020**: HEP (Hrvatska Elektroprivreda) initiated works on the Kosinj hydropower system and HPP Senj 2, with a total capacity of 412 MW. The projects aim to provide green electricity, primarily peak energy, and are designed to prevent floods, supply water, and boost irrigation. Upon completion, the total capacity of the hydropower system will reach 650 MW, generating 132 GWh of electricity annually, with a significant portion being peak energy.

- **July 2021**: The Kosinj hydropower system was declared a strategic investment project, which will enhance the electricity output of the Senj hydropower system by 230 GWh (22%) compared to its current production. The project includes the construction of the Kosinj reservoir, dams, and a hydropower plant, along with the reconstruction of the existing Sklope hydropower plant.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2020**: HEP announced an investment of EUR 450 million for the Kosinj and Senj 2 projects, marking them as one of the largest modern projects in Croatia.

- **July 2021**: The estimated cost for the Kosinj hydropower system was reported to be 1.54 billion kuna (approximately EUR 205 million). The total investment for both the Kosinj and Senj 2 projects is projected to be 3.4 billion kuna.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2020**: HEP began construction on the Kosinj hydropower system and HPP Senj 2.

- **July 2021**: The project was officially recognized as a strategic investment, which is expected to expedite its construction.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2021**: The project is anticipated to create new jobs and improve conditions for agricultural production, contributing to economic growth and lowering unemployment in the region.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- HEP (Hrvatska Elektroprivreda) is the sole owner of the facility, as indicated by the absence of any mention of joint ventures or partnerships in the articles.
