## HRV-08983-02971

### Evolutionary Timeline of the HEP Facility in Senj, Croatia

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2020
   - **Details:** HEP (Hrvatska Elektroprivreda) is developing the hydropower system Kosinj and hydropower plant Senj 2, with a total capacity of 412 MW. The existing hydropower system Senj (HES Senj) has an installed capacity of 216 MW and an average annual production of about 100 GWh. The new projects will increase the total production capacity to 650 MW and generate an additional 32 GWh annually, bringing the total to 132 GWh, primarily as peak energy.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** October 2020
   - **Details:** HEP has initiated investments estimated at EUR 450 million for the construction of the Kosinj hydropower system and HPP Senj 2. By the end of 2020, HEP plans to spend around EUR 53 million on these projects.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** October 2020
   - **Details:** HEP has started works on the Kosinj and Senj 2 projects, marking the beginning of one of the largest hydropower projects in modern Croatia. The projects are expected to be completed within six years.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** October 2020
   - **Details:** The construction of the water reservoir for the projects will necessitate the relocation of several hundred people from villages in the Kosinj valley. HEP has begun constructing a residential building in Perušić to secure homes for those affected.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** October 2020
   - **Details:** HEP is the sole owner of the facility, as it is fully responsible for the development and operation of the Kosinj hydropower system and HPP Senj 2.
