### Evolutionary Timeline of the Andrada Group Facility in Alsózsolca, Hungary

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **30-08-2023**: The Andrada Group announced plans to establish a battery recycling plant in Alsózsolca, Hungary. The facility is designed to recycle **10,000 tonnes of electric car batteries annually**. The company claims to utilize an innovative Li-ion recycling technology developed in-house, which meets and exceeds Germany's strict quality standards for safety and environmental compatibility.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **30-08-2023**: The Andrada Group will invest **10 billion forints (approximately 26 million euros)** into the battery recycling plant. Additionally, the Hungarian government is supporting the project with **4.7 billion forints (about 12 million euros)**.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **30-08-2023**: The announcement of the facility's establishment was made, but a specific schedule for the commissioning of the factory has not yet been disclosed. The plant will be located in an existing industrial park, which is noted to have no direct or indirect negative impact on the environment.

4. **Labour**:
   - **30-08-2023**: The project is expected to create **200 new jobs**, with **70 positions** to be filled by the end of the year. The Group indicated that these roles would primarily be for local engineers and technicians.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
