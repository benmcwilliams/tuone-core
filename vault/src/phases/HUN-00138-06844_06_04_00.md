### Evolutionary Timeline of the EcoSolifer Facility in Brussels, HUN

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 11-10-2019**: The EcoSolifer facility is part of the "Silver Frog" project, which proposes the construction of a 2 GW solar module factory. This facility will produce solar modules that will be used to generate renewable energy, specifically to support the production of 100% green hydrogen. The project aims to produce approximately 800,000 tons of green hydrogen over eight years.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: 11-10-2019**: The project seeks to secure designated status and backing from the EU, which would provide significant funding opportunities. The proposal is part of a broader initiative to bring solar module manufacturing back to Europe, aligning with the EU’s ‘Green Deal’.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date: 11-10-2019**: The "Silver Frog" project was pitched at the Hydrogen for Climate Conference in Brussels, where it was presented as a joint proposal by four European partners, including EcoSolifer. The project aims to be designated as one of the Important Projects of Common European Interest, which would facilitate its development.

#### 4. Labour
- **Date: 11-10-2019**: The project is expected to create at least 6,000 jobs over its duration, contributing significantly to employment in the region as part of the green hydrogen production initiative.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date: 11-10-2019**: The EcoSolifer facility is part of a joint venture involving multiple partners, including Meyer Burger, Hydrogenics, and European Energy, indicating that EcoSolifer does not own the facility exclusively but is part of a collaborative effort.
