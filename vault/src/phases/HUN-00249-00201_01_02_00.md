## HUN-00249-00201

### Evolutionary Timeline of the EcoPro BM Facility in Debrecen, Hungary

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09-12-2021**: EcoPro BM announces plans to build its first factory outside Korea in Debrecen, Hungary, with a production capacity of 108,000 tonnes of cathode materials for electric car batteries annually. The facility is expected to produce enough materials for 1.35 million electric cars. The first construction phase is set to be completed in the second half of 2024, with the second phase following a year later.
- **25-04-2023**: Construction of the EcoPro BM factory officially begins. The facility will employ 631 people and is confirmed to be on schedule for its planned completion phases.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09-12-2021**: EcoPro BM announces an investment of 715 million euros in Hungary for the construction of the facility. A government subsidy is also anticipated, pending EU Commission approval.
- **25-04-2023**: The investment amount is updated to 728 million euros, reaffirming the commitment to the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09-12-2021**: Announcement of the factory's construction and its intended capacity.
- **25-04-2023**: Ground-breaking ceremony marks the official start of construction for the facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **25-04-2023**: The facility is expected to create 631 jobs upon completion, contributing to local employment in the Debrecen area.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **09-12-2021**: EcoPro BM is establishing a joint venture with Samsung SDI for cathode materials, indicating that while EcoPro BM is the primary owner of the facility, it collaborates closely with Samsung SDI for supply and operational purposes.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones related to the EcoPro BM facility in Debrecen, Hungary, focusing on its technology, investment, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
