## HUN-00417-00061

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Audi Facility in Győr, Hungary

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2017**: Audi announces that all upcoming electric vehicles (EVs) will be powered by technology produced at the Győr facility, with production set to start shortly thereafter.
- **July 2018**: Audi officially begins serial production of electric motors at the Győr factory, with an initial production capacity of around 400 motors per day. The facility employs a modular assembly concept, enhancing flexibility.
- **October 2020**: Audi and E.ON unveil a 160,000 m² rooftop solar PV system at the Győr factory, making it carbon neutral. The system has a peak output of 12 MW and is expected to supply over 9.5 GWh of renewable energy annually.
- **February 2021**: Preparations begin for the production of new electric drives based on the Premium Platform Electric (PPE), with a new production area of 15,000 m² being set up.
- **December 2022**: Audi announces plans to upgrade all factories for EV production by 2029, including the Győr facility, which is expected to produce a new family of electric drive systems for the MEB ECO platform starting in 2025.
- **June 2023**: Audi plans to produce a compact electric model in Győr, with the facility currently producing internal combustion and electric engines.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2018**: The upgrade of the Győr facility for electric motor production involved investments in the double-digit million area.
- **October 2020**: The solar PV system installation represents a significant investment in renewable energy infrastructure at the facility.
- **February 2021**: The establishment of the new production area for PPE drives indicates further investment in the facility's capabilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2017**: Announcement of electric motor production starting soon.
- **July 2018**: Official start of serial production of electric motors.
- **October 2020**: Launch of Europe’s largest rooftop solar PV system at the facility.
- **February 2021**: Preparations for PPE electric drive production begin.
- **December 2022**: Announcement of plans to produce new electric drive systems by 2025.
- **June 2023**: Plans to produce a compact electric model in Győr are confirmed.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2018**: Initial employment of 100 employees for electric motor production, with plans to hire an additional 30 by the end of the year.
- **February 2021**: The new production area for PPE drives is expected to secure jobs for about 700 employees.
- **June 2023**: Audi trains around 2,000 employees in electrification since 2022 in preparation for increased electric vehicle production.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is operated by **Audi Hungaria**, a subsidiary of Audi, indicating that Audi has full ownership of the Győr facility.
