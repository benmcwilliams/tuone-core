## HUN-00419-00702

### Evolutionary Timeline of Samsung Facility in Göd, Hungary

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2017**: Construction of the Samsung SDI battery factory in Göd, Hungary, is completed. The facility initially has an annual production capacity of **30 GWh**.
- **February 2021**: Samsung SDI announces plans to expand the factory's production capacity to over **40 GWh**.
- **January 2023**: Samsung SDI is considering building a new battery cell factory in Göd to produce cylindrical battery cells exclusively for BMW's New Class electric cars.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2021**: Samsung SDI announces an investment of approximately **740 million euros** (around **900 million USD**) for the expansion of the Göd facility. This investment is partly financed through a capital increase of Samsung SDI Hungary Zrt and loans.
- **April 2023**: EcoPro BM begins construction of a new plant in Debrecen, Hungary, to produce **108,000 tonnes** of cathode materials annually, which will supply Samsung SDI's Göd facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2021**: Announcement of the expansion of the Göd facility.
- **April 2023**: Groundbreaking for EcoPro BM's plant in Debrecen, which will supply materials to the Göd facility.
- **December 2023**: EcoPro BM signs a major supply agreement with Samsung SDI for cathode materials worth approximately **30.9 billion euros**, valid from **2024 to 2028**.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2023**: The EcoPro BM plant in Debrecen is expected to employ **631 people**, contributing to the local job market and supporting the operations of the Göd facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **2020**: Samsung SDI and EcoPro BM establish a joint venture for cathode materials, indicating a collaborative ownership structure for the supply chain related to the Göd facility.
