### Evolutionary Timeline of the BYD Facility in Komárom, Hungary

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2016**: Reports indicate that BYD has begun building an electric bus factory in Komárom, Hungary, with an initial production capacity of 100 units per year, aimed at export to Western Europe.
- **October 2016**: BYD confirms the establishment of an assembly facility for electric buses and forklifts in Komárom, with a planned production capacity of 400 electric vehicles (EVs) per year, with potential future production of electric trucks.
- **April 2017**: BYD officially opens its first plant in Europe in Komárom, commencing production of electric buses with an annual output of up to 400 units. Plans for future production of electric trucks and forklifts are also mentioned.
- **July 2023**: BYD announces a fivefold increase in production capacity at the Komárom facility, raising the output from 200 to 1,000 electric buses annually.
- **October 2023**: BYD expands its e-mobility business in Hungary, launching three new electric car models in the market, indicating a diversification of its product offerings beyond buses.
