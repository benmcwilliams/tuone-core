### Evolutionary Timeline of the LG Magna e-Powertrain Facility in Miskolc, Hungary

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Announcement of Facility**: On 04-09-2023, LG Magna e-Powertrain announced the establishment of its first powertrain plant in Europe, located in Miskolc, Hungary. The facility is designed to produce electric motors initially, with plans to expand into manufacturing electrified powertrain solutions, including inverters and onboard chargers.
- **Production Capacity**: The facility will cover an area of 284,000 square feet (26,400 sqm) and is scheduled to start production in 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Government Support**: The facility has received strong support from the Hungarian government and local authorities, although specific funding commitments have not been disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Construction Timeline**: The facility is scheduled for completion in 2025, with production expected to start in 2026. This marks a significant milestone for LG Magna e-Powertrain as it expands its operations in Europe.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The facility is expected to employ about 200 staff at the initial start of production in 2026.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Joint Venture**: The facility is part of a joint venture between LG Electronics and Magna International, indicating that it is not solely owned by LG but rather shared between the two companies.
