### Evolutionary Timeline of the Schaeffler Facility in Szombathely, Hungary

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** Schaeffler opened a new plant in Szombathely, marking it as the company's first pure e-mobility plant worldwide. The facility serves as a center of excellence for producing components and systems for electrified drives. The plant covers an area of approximately 15,000 square meters and features a high degree of automation and digitalization. The production will focus on electric motors and hybrid transmissions.
   - **Production Capacity Goals:**
     - By 2023: Increase annual capacity to 800,000 products in multi-shift operation.
     - Between 2026 and 2029: Target an annual production of 1.8 million products for automotive partners.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The establishment of the Szombathely plant is part of Schaeffler's strategic growth in the e-mobility sector, with expectations of generating new orders between 2 and 3 billion euros annually from 2022 onwards.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Milestone:** Official opening of the Szombathely plant, which began construction in 2020. This facility is the second plant for Schaeffler in western Hungary.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The opening of the Szombathely plant is expected to create 150 new jobs focused on e-mobility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Details:** The facility is owned exclusively by Schaeffler AG, as indicated by the context of the articles.
