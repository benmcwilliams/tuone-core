### Evolutionary Timeline of the MOL Facility in Százhalombatta, Hungary

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2024**: MOL has commenced hydrogen production at its 10 MW electrolysis unit in Százhalombatta, Hungary. The facility has a production capacity of 1,600 tons of hydrogen per year and is designed to serve the Danube Refinery. This facility is noted as the region's largest capacity green hydrogen plant. The technology implemented will gradually replace the natural gas-based production process, which currently contributes to one-sixth of MOL Group's total carbon dioxide emissions.
