### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sanyo Facility in Dorog City, Hungary

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 11-03-2011
   - **Details:** Sanyo officially opened its new solar module production facility in Dorog City, Hungary, with a production capacity of 150 megawatts (MW). This facility is the third production line for Sanyo Hungary and was established to increase the production capacity of its parent company’s HIT solar modules. The annual production of modules at the facility almost doubled from 165 MW to 315 MW, contributing to Sanyo’s worldwide annual module production capacity of 680 MW. The facility covers an area of 14,000 square meters and features the latest production equipment.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 11-03-2011
   - **Details:** A total investment of approximately €19 million (around 900 million Forint) was made into the facility, with roughly €8 million coming from the Hungarian government in the form of a subsidy.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 11-03-2011
   - **Details:** The facility was officially opened, marking the start of operations for the new production line.

4. **Labour:**

   - **Details:** The opening of the facility resulted in the creation of around 300 jobs.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Details:** The facility is owned exclusively by Sanyo, as it is a subsidiary of the parent company.
