### Evolutionary Timeline of the Paks Nuclear Plant Facility in Paks, Hungary

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - As of July 2023, the Paks Nuclear Plant operates with four VVER reactors, each generating approximately 500MW, totaling around 2GW of output. However, due to a heatwave, three of the four blocks reduced their operating capacity by a total of 240MW.
   - An expansion project is planned to add two additional VVER reactors, each with a capacity of 1.2GW, which will more than double the plant's current output.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - In April 2023, Hungary's foreign minister announced a deal with Russia’s Rosatom for the expansion project, estimated to cost $13.2 billion (€12 billion) for the addition of two reactors.
