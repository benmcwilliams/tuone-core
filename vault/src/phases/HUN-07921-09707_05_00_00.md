### Evolutionary Timeline of the Paks Nuclear Power Plant Facility in Paks, Hungary

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Current Production Capacity:** The Paks Nuclear Power Plant currently has four VVER reactors, each generating approximately 500MW, totaling around 2GW of output.
   - **Reduced Capacity:** As of July 2023, due to high water temperatures in the Danube River, three of the four reactors began operating at a reduced capacity, lowering output by a total of 240MW.
   - **Expansion Plans:** An expansion project is underway to add two additional VVER reactors, each with a capacity of 1.2GW, which is expected to more than double the plant's output to over 4GW.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Expansion Investment:** In April 2023, Hungary's foreign minister announced a deal with Russia's Rosatom for the addition of two reactors at an estimated cost of $13.2 billion (€12 billion).
