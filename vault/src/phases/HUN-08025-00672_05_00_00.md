### Evolutionary Timeline of the Rosatom Facility at Paks II Nuclear Power Plant, Hungary

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **August 2023:** Rosatom's subsidiary, Atomstroyexport, commenced main-stage construction of two new VVER-1200 power units at the Paks II nuclear power plant, with a total power-generating capacity of 2.4 GW. The construction involves excavation for power unit 6, with significant soil removal and site preparation activities ongoing.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **August 2023:** The estimated investment for the Paks II project is €10 billion ($10.8 billion), funded by Russian banks. Hungary is set to repay this loan by 2046.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **August 2023:** The project transitioned to its second stage following the agreement on amendments to the construction contract on August 18, 2023. The main construction works began with the excavation of the pit for power unit 6, which is expected to continue until the end of autumn 2023. The main license for the construction was also secured in August 2023.
