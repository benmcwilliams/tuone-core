1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **August 2023:** Atomstroyexport, a subsidiary of Rosatom, commenced the main-stage construction of two new VVER-1200 power units at the Paks II Nuclear Power Plant, with a total power-generating capacity of 2.4 GW. The construction involves excavation for power unit 6, with significant soil removal and preparation work ongoing.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **August 2023:** The estimated investment for the Paks II project is €10 billion ($10.8 billion), funded by Russian banks. Hungary is set to repay this loan by 2046.

   - **August 2023:** Construction work began following amendments to the construction contract on August 18, 2023. The project transitioned to its second stage, with excavation work for power unit 6 starting, which involves removing a million cubic meters of soil.
