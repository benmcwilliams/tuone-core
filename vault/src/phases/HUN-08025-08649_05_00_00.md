### Evolutionary Timeline of the Duna Aszfalt Facility at Paks II Nuclear Power Plant, Hungary

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **August 2023:** Main-stage construction of two new VVER-1200 power units (units 5 and 6) commenced, with a total power-generating capacity of 2.4 GW. Excavation for power unit 6 began, covering an area equivalent to four soccer fields, with a million cubic meters of soil removed.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **August 2023:** The estimated investment for the Paks II project is €10 billion ($10.8 billion), funded by Russian banks. Hungary is set to repay the loan by 2046.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **August 2023:** Construction works began following amendments to the construction contract on August 18, 2023. The project transitioned to its second stage, with the main license for construction secured in the same month.
