### Evolutionary Timeline of the MET Group Facility in Kaba, Hungary

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2024**: Construction of the MET Kaba 2 solar site, with a production capacity of 23 MWp, commenced. The facility will cover an area of almost 32 hectares and will feature more than 33,000 solar panels. The project will utilize AI technology for project management, including drone data analysis for construction oversight.
- **August 2024**: The MET Kaba 2 Solar Park was inaugurated, confirming a production capacity of 23.4 MWp. The facility is designed to supply energy to approximately 13,000 local households and has an expected operational lifetime of at least 30 years. The solar park consists of two smaller installations, the Titán and Rhea sites, connected to the grid via the Kaba High Voltage Substation.

- **August 2024**: The Kaba 2 solar project is noted as a result of an in-house, greenfield project development effort initiated after the commissioning of the first MET Kaba Solar Park in 2021. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the articles.

- **August 2024**: Completion and inauguration of the MET Kaba 2 Solar Park.
