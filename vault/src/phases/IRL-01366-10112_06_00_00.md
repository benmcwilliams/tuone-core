### Evolutionary Timeline of Statkraft Ireland Facility in Dublin, Ireland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2023**: Four solar farms totaling **199 MW** near Dublin entered commercial operations. These farms were developed and constructed by Statkraft Ireland under the oversight of Octopus Renewables Infrastructure Trust (ORIT).
- **October 2024**: A fifth solar farm of **42 MW** was acquired by ORIT, bringing the total capacity of the five-site complex to **241 MW**. This complex is expected to contribute to approximately **2.5%** of Ireland's national PV target of **8 GW** by 2030.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2024**: The acquisition of the initial four solar farms was completed for a total cost of **€160 million**, partially financed by a **€80.6 million** debt facility from Allied Irish Banks and La Banque Postale.
- **October 2024**: The total acquisition cost for the five-site complex was **€198 million**, with **€38 million** attributed to the fifth site. This was financed through a **€104 million** 20-year debt facility, which included an additional drawdown of **€23 million** for the incremental site.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2023**: The four solar farms became operational and were officially acquired by ORIT.
- **October 2024**: The fifth solar farm was acquired following its energization, completing the five-site complex.
