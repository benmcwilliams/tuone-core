1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **October 2023**: Siemens Energy announced the deployment of a hybrid project in Shannonbridge, combining a synchronous condenser and a battery energy storage system (BESS) with a capacity of 160MWh. The synchronous condenser can inject 4000MW of inertia into the grid, providing low-carbon inertia and rapid frequency response capabilities. This project marks the first instance of these two technologies being combined with a single grid connection.
