   - **Details:** Halston Environmental and Planning received approval to construct an energy storage facility in Shannonbridge. The facility will cover approximately five acres and is designed to provide system support services to the electricity grid. It will consist of an open area battery Energy Storage System (ESS) compound containing 169 battery and medium voltage power station enclosures, an IPP building, asynchronous condenser compound, synchronous condenser building, five control modules, associated bunded transformers, and an electrical substation connected to the existing Shannonbridge 220kV substation.
