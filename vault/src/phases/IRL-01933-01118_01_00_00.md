### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in County Meath, Ireland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2022
   - **Details:** The facility is a 50MW commercial-scale battery system with a power capacity of 25MWh. It consists of over 4,000 modules housed in 16 containers, occupying an area equivalent to a football pitch. This facility is the first renewable project by Iberdrola in Ireland in over 25 years. The battery system is part of the DS3 programme aimed at reinforcing the grid to accommodate increased renewable generation. Iberdrola plans to expand the storage system to double its capacity to 100MW in the future.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2022
   - **Details:** The project required an investment of €28 million ($28.3 million). Iberdrola has also announced plans to invest up to €100 million ($100 million) in new onshore renewable and storage projects in Ireland by 2025.

   - **Date:** July 2022
   - **Details:** The battery system was officially commissioned, marking the culmination of the main initiative of the DS3 programme. It will serve EirGrid for six years, contributing to the security and sustainability of the Irish electricity grid.
