### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in Lisdrumdoagh, Ireland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2021**: RWE announces the development of a 60 MW battery storage facility at Lisdrumdoagh, Co. Monaghan, capable of storing enough electricity to power approximately 125,000 homes. This facility is designed to provide rapid delivery of electricity to the grid, helping to balance intermittency in electricity generation and providing backup during power outages.
- **June 2022**: The Lisdrumdoagh facility goes live, marking it as RWE's largest battery storage project in Ireland. It is the second battery storage facility RWE has brought online in the country, following the 8.5 MW facility in Stephenstown, Balbriggan.
