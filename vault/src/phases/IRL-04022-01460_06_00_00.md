### Evolutionary Timeline of the Aura Power Facility in Duleek, Ireland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2024**: The Gaskinstown solar array, with a total capacity of **93 MWp**, is being developed on an **81-hectare** site near Duleek, County Meath. The facility will utilize **high-capacity bifacial modules** on **fixed-tilt support structures** to optimize land use and efficiency. Construction is expected to be completed by **September 2024**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2024**: The joint venture, Highfield Solar (which includes Aura Power), reached financial close on a **€65 million** project financing debt facility for the Gaskinstown solar plant. This funding was provided by **Coöperatieve Rabobank U.A.** The project was successful in the **Renewable Electricity Support Scheme (RESS 2)** auction in **2022**, which guarantees an annual community fund of approximately **€150,000** for 15 years.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2024**: The Gaskinstown project is confirmed to be under construction, with work on the grid connection infrastructure already underway. The project is expected to be completed by **September 2024**. It was awarded one of the highest solar PV capacities in the RESS 2 auction.
