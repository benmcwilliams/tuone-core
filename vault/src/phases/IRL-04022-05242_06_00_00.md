### Evolutionary Timeline of Highfield Energy Facility in Duleek, Ireland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: Highfield Solar, a joint venture including Highfield Energy, announced the development of the Gillinstown solar plant near Duleek, with a capacity of 141 MW. The project will utilize high-capacity bifacial modules on fixed-tilt support structures for optimized land use and efficiency. Construction was expected to be completed by October 2022.
- **February 2024**: Highfield Solar reached financial close on a 93 MWp solar plant named Gaskinstown, located near Duleek. This project will also utilize bifacial modules on fixed-tilt support structures and is expected to be completed by September 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: The joint venture secured €160 million in project financing for the Gillinstown and Rosspile solar plants, with lenders including Cooperatieve Rabobank, Landesbank Baden-Wurttemberg, and Norddeutsche Landesbank.
- **February 2024**: Highfield Solar secured a €65 million financing facility for the Gaskinstown project, again from Cooperatieve Rabobank. This project was part of the second Renewable Electricity Support Scheme (RESS 2) auction in 2022.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2021**: Announcement of the Gillinstown solar plant project, with construction expected to be completed by October 2022.
- **February 2024**: Financial close reached for the Gaskinstown solar project, with construction expected to be completed by September 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2024**: The Gaskinstown project will provide an annual community fund of approximately €150,000 for local communities, clubs, and societies for the duration of the RESS support (15 years), indicating potential job creation and community benefits.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2021**: The facility is part of a joint venture established in 2014 between Highfield Energy, Ib Vogt, and Aura Power, indicating that Highfield Energy does not own the facility exclusively but shares ownership with its partners.
