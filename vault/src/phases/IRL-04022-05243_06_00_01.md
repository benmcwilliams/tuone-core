## IRL-04022-05243

### Evolutionary Timeline of Highfield Solar Facility in Duleek, Ireland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: Highfield Solar, a joint venture involving Ib Vogt, Highfield Energy, and Aura Power, announced plans for the Gillinstown solar plant near Duleek, with a capacity of 141 MW. The project will utilize high-capacity bifacial modules on fixed-tilt support structures for optimized land use and efficiency. Construction was expected to be completed by October 2022.
- **February 2024**: Highfield Solar reached financial close for the Gaskinstown solar PV plant, which will have a capacity of 93 MWp. The project will also utilize bifacial modules on fixed-tilt support structures and is expected to be completed by September 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: The joint venture secured €160 million in project financing for the two solar plants (Rosspile and Gillinstown) in Ireland, with lenders including Cooperatieve Rabobank, Landesbank Baden-Wurttemberg, and Norddeutsche Landesbank.
- **February 2024**: Highfield Solar secured €65 million in project financing for the Gaskinstown solar plant from Coöperatieve Rabobank U.A., marking the second partnership with the bank following the financing of the Rathnaskilloge project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2021**: Announcement of the Gillinstown project with expected completion in October 2022.
- **February 2024**: Financial close for the Gaskinstown project was achieved, with construction expected to be completed by September 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2024**: The Gaskinstown project will provide an annual community fund of approximately €150,000 for local communities, clubs, and societies for the duration of the RESS support (15 years), indicating potential job creation and community benefits.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Established in 2014**: Highfield Solar is a joint venture between Ib Vogt, Highfield Energy, and Aura Power, indicating that the facility is not under exclusive ownership but rather a collaborative effort among the three companies.
