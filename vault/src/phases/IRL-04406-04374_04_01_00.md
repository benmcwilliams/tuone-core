### Evolutionary Timeline of the Worley Facility in Aghada, Ireland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2021
   - **Details:** Worley has been appointed to design a 50MW green hydrogen production facility in Aghada. The facility will produce green hydrogen through electrolysis powered by renewable energy, with a production capacity of over 20 tonnes of green hydrogen per day. Once operational, it is expected to remove 63,000 tonnes of carbon emissions annually.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2021
   - **Details:** The project is part of a broader initiative to support Ireland's Climate Bill, which aims for a 51% reduction in emissions by 2030. Specific investment details are not provided in the article, but the project is positioned as a significant step towards establishing Ireland as a leader in green energy.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** June 2021
   - **Details:** The project is referred to as Ireland’s first commercial-scale green hydrogen production facility. It is expected to be operational before the end of 2023.

4. **Labour:**

   - **Details:** The project is anticipated to generate green, local jobs, contributing to employment in the region.
