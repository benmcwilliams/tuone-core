1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2021
   - **Details:** RWE is establishing its first test centre for airborne wind energy generators in County Mayo. The test centre will also evaluate systems from other Airborne Wind Energy (AWE) developers over an eight-year operational lifetime.
