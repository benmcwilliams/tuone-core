#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2021**: Greencoat Renewables acquired the Cordal wind farm, which features 28 GE 3.2MW turbines, totaling an operational capacity of 89.6MW. The wind farm had reached full commercial operations in May 2018.
- **February 2022**: Greencoat Renewables acquired the Tullahennel wind farm, consisting of 13 GE 2.85MW turbines, operational since September 2018. 

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2021**: The acquisition of the Cordal wind farm was funded by Greencoat's existing revolving credit facility. This acquisition increased Greencoat's total installed capacity to 650MW.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2018**: The Cordal wind farm reached full commercial operations.
- **September 2018**: The Tullahennel wind farm became operational.
- **April 2021**: The acquisition of the Cordal wind farm was expected to close.
