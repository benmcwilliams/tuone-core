### Highfield Energy Facility Timeline - Foulksmills, Ireland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **July 2021:** Highfield Solar, a joint venture involving Highfield Energy, Ib Vogt, and Aura Power, announced the development of two solar plants in Ireland, including the **141MW Rosspile** plant located on a **140-hectare site** near Foulksmills, County Wexford. The second plant, **Gillinstown**, is also 141MW and located in County Meath. Both projects will utilize high-capacity bifacial modules on fixed-tilt support structures for optimized land use and efficiency.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **July 2021:** The joint venture reached financial close on a **€160 million project financing debt facility** for the two solar plants. The financing was provided by Cooperatieve Rabobank, Landesbank Baden-Wurttemberg, and Norddeutsche Landesbank, each contributing one-third of the senior debt.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **July 2021:** Construction on the grid connection infrastructure for the Rosspile plant had already commenced. The solar construction for Rosspile is expected to be completed by **July 2022**, while the Gillinstown plant is expected to be completed by **October 2022**.
   - The projects were successful participants in Ireland’s first competitive Renewable Electricity Support Scheme (RESS 1) auction run by the Department of the Environment, Climate and Communications in **2020**.
