1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **July 2021**: Highfield Solar, in a joint venture with Ib Vogt and Aura Power, announced the development of two solar plants in Ireland, including the **141MW Rosspile** plant located on a **140-hectare** site near Foulksmills, County Wexford. The facility will utilize high-capacity bifacial modules on fixed-tilt support structures for optimized land use and efficiency. Construction for Rosspile is expected to be completed by **July 2022**.
