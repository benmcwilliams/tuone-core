### Evolutionary Timeline of SSE Facility in Galway Wind Park, Ireland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 07-01-2016
   - **Details:** SSE and Coillte completed a financing deal for the second phase of the Galway Wind Park, which has a production capacity of 105 MW. The full project, including phase one (64 MW), totals 169 MW. Siemens is supplying 58 SWT-3.0-101 turbines for the entire project.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 07-01-2016
   - **Details:** A €176 million finance package was arranged for the second phase of the wind farm. This financing deal is noted as the largest-ever finance package for a single wind farm in Ireland, involving Spanish bank BBVA, Dutch bank Rabobank, and German bank Nord LB.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 07-01-2016
   - **Details:** Construction on phase two of the Galway Wind Park is set to begin in January 2016, with the full project expected to be completed by Q3 2017. Phase one is already under construction.
