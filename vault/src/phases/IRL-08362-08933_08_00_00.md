### Evolutionary Timeline of the FuturEnergy Ireland Facility in Ballyvourney, IRL

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 11-10-2024
   - **Details:** FuturEnergy Ireland, in partnership with SSE Renewables, submitted a planning application for the Cummeennabuddoge wind farm, which will consist of 17 turbines with a total capacity of 122MW. The facility is expected to generate enough energy to power 81,900 homes annually, with a potential output of 375,000 megawatt hours of electricity per year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 11-10-2024
   - **Details:** The project is in the planning phase and is contingent upon receiving necessary planning consents and a final investment decision from the co-development partners, SSE Renewables and FuturEnergy Ireland. Specific investment amounts have not been disclosed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 11-10-2024
   - **Details:** The planning application for the Cummeennabuddoge wind farm has been submitted to An Bord Pleanala, Ireland’s national independent planning body. The project is currently in the planning process, which has included extensive consultations with local residents since winter 2020.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 11-10-2024
   - **Details:** The construction phase of the wind farm is expected to create approximately 130 jobs, contributing to local employment during the project's development.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 11-10-2024
   - **Details:** The facility is a co-development project between FuturEnergy Ireland and SSE Renewables, indicating a joint venture ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by either company.
