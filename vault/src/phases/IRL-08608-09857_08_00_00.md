### Rengen Power Facility Timeline: Curraghderrig, Ireland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2017
   - **Details:** The Rengen Power facility in Curraghderrig produces wind energy with a total production capacity of 4.6MW. 2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2017
   - **Details:** The project received an equity investment of €8.7 million from Scottish Equity Partners (SEP) through its Environmental Capital Fund (ECF). Additionally, a long-term senior debt facility was provided by Bank of Ireland to finance the construction of the wind farm.
