#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: September 2019
  - Hanwha Energy Corporation, in partnership with Lumcloon Energy, is developing a **200 MW battery storage facility** in County Offaly. The facility consists of **two 100 MW lithium-ion storage units** located near Ferbane and Shannonbridge. This facility aims to stabilize the grid and support the integration of renewable energy sources into Ireland's electricity system.

- **Date**: September 2019
  - The total investment for the facility is **€300 million**. This investment is part of Ireland's broader initiative to enhance its grid capabilities to accommodate a growing share of renewable energy.

  - Construction of the facility officially began in September 2019, as announced by Ireland’s Minister for Communications, Climate Action and Environment, Richard Bruton. The project was initially announced in April 2018 by Lumcloon Energy in collaboration with Hanwha Energy.
