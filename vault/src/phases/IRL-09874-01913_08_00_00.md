### Evolutionary Timeline of SSE Facility in Arklow Harbour, Ireland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-03-2019**: SSE is assessing Arklow Harbour as a potential operations and maintenance (O&M) hub for the 520MW Arklow Bank offshore wind farm. The facility will support around 60 full-time employees and various companies across the supply chain.
- **08-06-2020**: SSE confirms Arklow Harbour as the preferred location for the O&M base, which will accommodate 80 full-time employees. The facility will include a new office, warehouse buildings, and berthing for up to four crew transfer vessels.
- **07-06-2021**: SSE unveils plans for the O&M facility at Arklow Harbour, designed as a ‘Nearly Zero Energy Building’ to support the offshore wind farm. The facility will include warehousing for turbine components, office space, and staff welfare facilities.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-06-2021**: SSE announces an expected investment of around €15 million for the development of the O&M facility at the Old Shipyard site in Arklow Harbour.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07-03-2019**: SSE begins assessing locations for the O&M hub, with a study to be completed in a few months.
- **08-06-2020**: SSE selects Arklow Harbour as the site for the O&M base and begins discussions with Wicklow County Council for lease agreements and planning permission.
- **07-06-2021**: SSE opens a public consultation on the plans for the O&M facility, with intentions to lodge a planning application with Wicklow County Council later that summer.

#### 4. Labour
- **07-03-2019**: The development of the service base is expected to create around 60 full-time jobs and support various supply chain roles.
- **08-06-2020**: The O&M base will employ 80 full-time staff once operational, with additional jobs in the supply chain.
- **07-06-2021**: Approximately 60 construction roles will be created during the delivery phase of the O&M facility, with thousands of indirect jobs expected over the project's lifetime.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **08-06-2020**: SSE Renewables is moving forward with the development of the facility, indicating that they will have full ownership of the O&M base at Arklow Harbour, as they have entered into land option agreements for the site.
