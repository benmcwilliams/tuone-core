#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09-06-2020**: Landsvirkjun announced plans to develop a hydrogen production facility at the Ljósifoss Hydropower Station. The facility will initially have a production capacity of 10MW, housed in a 700m² building, with plans for phased expansion based on demand. The hydrogen will be produced through the electrolysis of water using renewable power, aiming to supply the entire public transportation fleet in the Reykjavík area.
