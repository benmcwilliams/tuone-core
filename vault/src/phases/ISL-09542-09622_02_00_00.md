- **Production**: The ON Power facility in Hengill primarily produces electricity and hot water through geothermal energy extraction from high-temperature areas.

- **Innovations**: The CarbFix method, developed in 2014, allows for the mineralization of carbon dioxide and hydrogen sulfide, significantly reducing emissions from the facility.
