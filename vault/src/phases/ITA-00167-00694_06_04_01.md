### Evolutionary Timeline of the STMicroelectronics Facility in Catania, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04-01-2010**: A joint venture named 3Sun is formed between Sharp, Enel Green Power, and STMicroelectronics to produce thin-film solar modules. The facility, located in a former 300mm semiconductor fab owned by STMicroelectronics, is set to have an initial production capacity of 160MW, with a targeted ramp-up to 480MW annual capacity over several years.
- **09-12-2022**: STMicroelectronics announces the introduction of new silicon carbide (SiC) power modules, with plans to expand manufacturing capacity through a new SiC substrate production facility in Catania, scheduled to open in 2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04-01-2010**: The joint venture partners (Sharp, Enel, and STMicroelectronics) are set to invest €320 million for the establishment of the facility.
- **02-08-2010**: The project receives an additional €49 million funding from CIPE (the Italian Joint Ministerial Committee for Economic planning) to support the development of the 3Sun plant.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **04-01-2010**: Announcement of the formation of the joint venture and the establishment of the facility.
- **02-08-2010**: The facility is officially named 3Sun, and production is scheduled to begin in the second half of 2011.
- **09-12-2022**: Announcement of the upcoming SiC substrate production facility, with an opening planned for 2023.

#### 4. Labour
- **04-01-2010**: The establishment of the 3Sun facility is expected to create jobs, although specific job creation numbers are not detailed in the articles.
- **09-12-2022**: The expansion for the SiC substrate production facility is anticipated to create additional job opportunities, but specific figures are not provided.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **04-01-2010**: The facility is a joint venture (3Sun) between Sharp, Enel Green Power, and STMicroelectronics, indicating that STMicroelectronics does not own the facility exclusively but shares ownership with its partners.
