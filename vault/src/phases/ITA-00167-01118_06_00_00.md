### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Catania, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2025**: Iberdrola's new photovoltaic plant in Catania and Enna, Sicily, is set to become operational. The facility will have a renewable energy generation capacity of **242.78 MWp**, sufficient to meet the annual energy needs of approximately **154,000 Italian households**. The plant will utilize the latest technologies to ensure high energy efficiency and minimal environmental impact.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 3, 2024**: The European Investment Bank (EIB) and Iberdrola signed a **€100 million green loan agreement** to finance the construction of the photovoltaic plant. This financing is **70% backed by SACE's Archimede Guarantee**, which aims to enhance the competitiveness of the national system.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 3, 2024**: Announcement of the green loan agreement and details about the photovoltaic plant's operational timeline and capacity.
- **December 20, 2024**: Confirmation that the facility will be operational in **2025**.

#### 4. Labour
- The construction and management of the photovoltaic plant are expected to **create new job opportunities** in local communities, contributing to the economic development of the region.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is owned **exclusively by Iberdrola**, as indicated by the context of the articles discussing the company's direct involvement in the project and financing arrangements.
