   - **Date:** September 2015
   - **Details:** The facility features a grid-connected solar-smoothing storage system with a capacity of 1MW/2MWh. It is integrated with a 10MWp photovoltaic (PV) plant operated by Enel Green Power. The storage system utilizes General Electric’s Durathon batteries, which are molten salt-based (sodium-metal halide). The purpose of this facility is to enhance power quality from the PV system and optimize the variable generation fed into the grid.
