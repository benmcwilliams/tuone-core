## ITA-00167-02081

### Evolutionary Timeline of Enel's 3Sun Facility in Catania, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04-01-2010**: Enel Green Power, Sharp, and STMicroelectronics announce a joint venture to establish a thin-film solar module plant in Catania, targeting an initial capacity of 160 MW, with plans to expand to 480 MW by 2016.
- **06-04-2011**: The facility, named 3Sun, is granted €49.06 million in state aid to support the establishment of a 240 MW photovoltaic module production plant.
- **08-07-2011**: The 3Sun facility is inaugurated, with an initial production capacity of 160 MW, and the potential to ramp up to 480 MW.
- **11-03-2017**: Enel announces plans to convert the facility to produce bifacial heterojunction modules, with an expected capacity of 240 MW by 2019.
- **11-06-2019**: Production of heterojunction modules begins at the 3Sun facility, with a capacity of 200 MW.
- **20-05-2021**: Enel Green Power announces plans to scale up the facility to 3 GW by 2024, with the first 400 MW expected to be operational by September 2023.
- **01-04-2022**: Enel secures €560 million for the expansion of the 3Sun facility, which will include bifacial heterojunction PV cells and a tandem cell structure.
- **19-06-2023**: 3Sun launches new photovoltaic modules, M40, M40 BOLD, and B60, based on heterojunction technology, expected to be released to the market in 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **02-08-2010**: The joint venture receives €49 million in funding from CIPE to support the development of the 3Sun facility.
- **06-04-2011**: The European Commission grants €49.06 million in regional investment aid for the establishment of the 240 MW plant.
- **11-03-2017**: Enel announces an investment of €80 million to convert the facility to bifacial heterojunction technology.
- **01-04-2022**: Enel Green Power announces a total investment of €600 million for the expansion of the 3Sun facility, with €118 million from the EU's Innovation Fund.
- **24-01-2024**: Enel secures €560 million for the expansion of the 3Sun facility, with additional funding from the European Investment Bank and Italian banks.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **04-01-2010**: Announcement of the joint venture to establish the 3Sun facility.
- **08-07-2011**: Inauguration of the 3Sun facility in Catania.
- **06-04-2011**: Construction work begins on the 240 MW plant.
- **11-03-2017**: Announcement of the conversion to bifacial heterojunction technology.
- **11-06-2019**: Start of production of heterojunction modules.
- **20-05-2021**: Announcement of plans to scale up to 3 GW by 2024.
- **01-04-2022**: Grant agreement signed with the EU for the 3 GW expansion.
- **19-06-2023**: Launch of new photovoltaic modules at the facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **08-07-2011**: Initial employment of 280 people at the 3Sun facility.
- **20-05-2021**: Enel Green Power announces plans to create 700 new jobs by the end of 2024, with an additional 1,000 indirect jobs.
- **19-06-2023**: The workforce at the 3Sun facility is expected to expand to about 900 people by 2024.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **07-2014**: Enel Green Power takes full control of the 3Sun facility, having previously been a joint venture with Sharp and STMicroelectronics.

This timeline captures the significant developments in technology, investment, project milestones, labor impacts, and ownership structure of the Enel 3Sun facility in Catania, Italy, reflecting its evolution as a key player in the solar manufacturing sector.
