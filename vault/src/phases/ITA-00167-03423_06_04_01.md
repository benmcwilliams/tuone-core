## ITA-00167-03423

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sharp Facility in Catania, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2010**: A joint venture named 3Sun is formed between Sharp, Enel Green Power, and STMicroelectronics to produce thin-film solar modules. The facility is set to have an initial production capacity of 160MW, with a targeted ramp-up to 480MW by 2016.
- **07/2014**: Sharp exits the 3Sun joint venture, which had been producing a-Si thin-film modules at the Catania facility. The facility was supplying modules to Enel Green Power projects in Southern Europe. 

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2010**: The joint venture partners announce a total investment of €320 million for the establishment of the facility.
- **02/2010**: The project receives an additional €49 million funding from CIPE (the Italian Joint Ministerial Committee for Economic Planning) to support the development of the 500MW thin-film plant.
- **07/2014**: Sharp incurs a cost of approximately US$141 million to exit the joint venture, indicating financial challenges faced by the facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **04/2010**: Announcement of the formation of the 3Sun joint venture and plans for the facility.
- **2011**: Production at the Catania facility is expected to start in the second half of the year, with initial ramp-up planned for early 2011.
- **07/2014**: Sharp officially exits the 3Sun joint venture, marking a significant change in the operational status of the facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **07/2014**: The exit from the joint venture by Sharp suggests potential job losses, although specific figures regarding job creation or loss are not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **04/2010**: The facility is established as a joint venture (3Sun) with equal ownership between Sharp, Enel Green Power, and STMicroelectronics.
- **07/2014**: Sharp relinquishes its 50% ownership in the 3Sun joint venture to Enel through a share repurchase, indicating a shift in ownership structure where Enel becomes the sole owner of the facility.

This timeline captures the key developments and changes at the Sharp facility in Catania, Italy, focusing on technology, investment, project milestones, labor implications, and ownership structure.
