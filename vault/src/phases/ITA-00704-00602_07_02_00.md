### Evolutionary Timeline of the PSA Group Facility in Melfi, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: The facility in Melfi is set to produce the Jeep Renegade Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV).
- **Production Capacity**: The facility has a workforce of approximately 7,300 employees, although recent reports indicate that more than half of these employees were on short-time work.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Merger Announcement**: On 31-10-2019, PSA and Fiat Chrysler announced their merger, which aims to leverage synergies in technology and operations, particularly in electric mobility. The merger is expected to generate savings of €3.7 billion annually without closing any plants.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Merger Agreement**: Following initial discussions, a binding merger agreement was signed on 18-12-2019, with completion expected within 12 to 15 months, pending regulatory approval. This merger is anticipated to create the fourth-largest global automaker.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Impact**: The merger discussions have raised concerns about job security at the Melfi facility. Reports suggest that more than half of the workforce was on short-time work, and analyses indicate that around 10,000 employees across the merged entity may be considered superfluous.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Joint Venture**: The merger between PSA and Fiat Chrysler will result in a 50:50 joint venture, meaning that the facility will not be exclusively owned by PSA but will be part of a larger entity with shared ownership.
