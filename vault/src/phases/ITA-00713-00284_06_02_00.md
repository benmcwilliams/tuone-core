### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fraunhofer Facility in Milan, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2022**: The Fraunhofer Institute for Solar Energy Systems ISE, leading a research consortium, developed a proof of concept for a new wafer production line capable of producing between 15,000 to 20,000 wafers per hour. This represents a doubling of the usual production capacity. The improvements were achieved through several process enhancements, including:
  - Implementation of an ‘on-the-fly laser equipment’ for high-speed processing.
  - Use of rotary screen printing for metallisation instead of flatbed screening.
  - Combined diffusion and thermal oxidisation processes for wafers, allowing stacked processing.
  - Increased belt speed in inline furnace processes to triple the standard speed without compromising solar cell efficiency.
