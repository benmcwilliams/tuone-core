## ITA-00713-00650

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Reefilla Facility in Milan, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2021**: Reefilla was founded in Turin as part of the I3P startup incubator, focusing on electric mobility solutions. The facility in Milan offers predictive mobile charging services for commercial fleets and private users.
- **July 2023**: Reefilla developed the "Fillee" power banks, which utilize re-used battery parts from electric vehicles. These power banks are delivered to clients' vehicles by electric vans, providing a sustainable charging solution through photovoltaic recharging centers.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2023**: FPT Industrial launched a pilot project in collaboration with Reefilla to manage the lifecycle of electric vehicle batteries, emphasizing sustainability and compliance with European regulations. This partnership aims to enhance the circular economy by reconditioning and reusing batteries.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2023**: The partnership between FPT Industrial and Reefilla was officially announced, focusing on the second life of spent Iveco batteries. The eBS37 battery packs will be reconditioned by Reefilla for use in their mobile charging service.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2023**: The collaboration between FPT Industrial and Reefilla is expected to create job opportunities related to the reconditioning and recycling of battery components, although specific job creation numbers were not mentioned in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2023**: Reefilla operates as an independent startup, and while it collaborates with FPT Industrial, there is no indication of joint ownership or exclusive ownership of the facility by either company. The partnership is framed as a cooperative effort to enhance sustainability in electric mobility.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones of the Reefilla facility in Milan, highlighting its technological advancements, collaborative efforts, and operational focus on sustainability in electric mobility.
