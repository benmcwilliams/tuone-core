### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fimer Facility in Milan, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - Fimer specializes in the production of inverters for solar systems. The facility operates two inverter factories in Italy, including one near Milan.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - In November 2024, McLaren Applied Group acquired Fimer through its subsidiary, MA Solar Italy Ltd. This acquisition is expected to enhance technological synergies and operational efficiency, although specific investment amounts were not disclosed.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **September 2022:** Fimer announced an industrial relaunch after entering composition-with-creditors proceedings.
   - **February 2023:** Fimer filed for creditor protection at a tribunal in Arezzo.
   - **June 2023:** Fimer presented a new industrial plan to the tribunal.
   - **July 2023:** Fimer's board selected Clementy Group as a strategic partner for acquisition, pending a decision by the Court of Milan.
   - **November 2024:** McLaren Applied Group completed the acquisition of Fimer.

4. **Labour:**
   - In July 2024, it was reported that 55 out of 65 employees at the Milan site were collectively dismissed, indicating significant job loss prior to the acquisition.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - As of November 2024, the Fimer facility is owned by MA Solar Italy Ltd., a subsidiary of McLaren Applied Group, following its acquisition. Prior to this, Fimer was undergoing restructuring and creditor protection processes.
