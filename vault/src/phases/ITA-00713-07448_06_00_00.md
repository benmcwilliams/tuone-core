### Evolutionary Timeline of LT Renewables Facility in Milan, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 08-07-2020
   - **Details:** LT Renewables acquired the operations and maintenance (O&M) and engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) arms of Enerray, adding 240 MWp of solar generation capacity to its portfolio. This acquisition brought LT Renewables' total project capacity under management to 500 MWp, up from 220 MWp at the start of the year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 08-07-2020
   - **Details:** The acquisition of Enerray was part of a broader context where the Italian government and the European Union provided substantial funding for the renewable energy sector through the Green New Deal. This initiative aims to add 1.1 GWp of new clean energy capacity annually for the next decade.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 08-07-2020
   - **Details:** LT Renewables announced the acquisition of Enerray, which included around 250 projects across Italy. The acquisition was part of a consolidation trend in the Italian solar industry.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 08-07-2020
   - **Details:** The spokesman for LT Renewables stated that no jobs would be lost at Enerray as a result of the acquisition.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 08-07-2020
   - **Details:** LT Renewables acquired the O&M and EPC arms of Enerray, indicating that LT Renewables now has full ownership of the operations and maintenance activities associated with the acquired capacity.
