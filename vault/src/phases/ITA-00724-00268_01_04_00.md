## ITA-00724-00268

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fiat Chrysler Facility in Mirafiori, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **11/2019**: Fiat Chrysler announces a €700 million investment to establish a production line for the electric Fiat 500 at the Mirafiori plant. The facility is set to produce 80,000 units annually starting in Q2 2020. The production capacity may be expanded if necessary.
- **04/2021**: Production of the all-electric Fiat 500 is reduced from 300 to 210 units per day due to battery supply difficulties. The production will shift to a single shift operation starting on 19 April 2021.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **11/2019**: The €700 million investment for the electric Fiat 500 is part of a larger €5 billion investment plan by Fiat Chrysler for its Italian plants by 2021.
- **02/2020**: Maserati announces an €800 million investment for the production of electric vehicles, with some of this investment linked to the Mirafiori plant, which will also serve as a battery assembly center.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03/2020**: The production start of the electric Fiat 500 is announced but has not yet begun due to the COVID-19 pandemic, which leads to a temporary shutdown of the plant.
- **04/2021**: The production of the electric Fiat 500 is officially reduced due to battery supply issues.

#### 4. Labour
- **04/2021**: The reduction in production raises concerns about employment at the Mirafiori site, particularly regarding the extension of 160 temporary workers hired in October 2020, as the reduced production may not support their continued employment.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is owned exclusively by Fiat Chrysler Automobiles (FCA), with no mention of joint ventures or shared ownership in the articles.
