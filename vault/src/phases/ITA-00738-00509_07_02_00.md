#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2020**: Maserati began testing its first all-electric drive system at the Modena Innovation Laboratory, with plans to fit this system into models starting in 2021. The initial models to be equipped with electric motors are the GranTurismo and GranCabrio.
- **February 2020**: Maserati announced its strategy to electrify all new models, including hybrid and battery-electric drives, with production entirely in Italy. The GranTurismo and GranCabrio EVs are set to begin production in 2021.
