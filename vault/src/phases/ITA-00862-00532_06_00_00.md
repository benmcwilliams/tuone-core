### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mitsubishi Facility in Piedmont, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: March 2013
  - **Details**: Mitsubishi Corporation, along with INCJ and Solar Ventures, acquired Solar Holding, which has a photovoltaic (PV) portfolio of 42 MWp in Italy. The facility consists of 19 plants located in Piedmont, Sardinia, and Apulia, capable of meeting the energy needs of approximately 20,000 households.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: March 2013
  - **Details**: The acquisition of Solar Holding marks Mitsubishi's first investment in Italy. The three shareholders (Mitsubishi, INCJ, and Solar Ventures) plan to acquire an additional 100-150 MWp of PV plants in Italy, aiming to become one of the largest players in the Italian solar market. INCJ has the capacity to invest up to ¥2 trillion (approximately US$21 million).

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: March 2013
  - **Details**: The acquisition of Solar Holding was completed, establishing a joint venture among Mitsubishi, INCJ, and Solar Ventures. This partnership is expected to enhance the development and maturity of the Italian solar market.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure

  - **Details**: Mitsubishi Corporation owns a 50% stake in Solar Holding, while INCJ holds 35% and Solar Ventures holds 15%. This indicates a joint venture ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by Mitsubishi.
