## ITA-00862-07497

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Solar Ventures Facility in Piedmont, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2010**: Solar Ventures, in partnership with SunPower, signed a contract to design and build three solar power plants in the Piedmont region, totaling **11.1 MW** over **30 hectares**. Construction was set to begin in late July 2010, with completion aimed by the end of the year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2013**: Mitsubishi Corporation, along with Innovation Network Corporation of Japan (INCJ) and Solar Ventures, acquired **Solar Holding**, which has a PV portfolio of **42 MWp** in Italy. This acquisition was part of a strategic investment to enhance the solar market in Italy, with plans to acquire an additional **100-150 MWp** of PV plants.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2010**: Announcement of the partnership between Solar Ventures and SunPower for the construction of the solar power plants in Piedmont.
- **March 2013**: Completion of the acquisition of Solar Holding, which includes 19 plants managed by Solar Ventures in Piedmont, Sardinia, and Apulia.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not provide specific details on job creation or job loss related to the investments or expansions at the Solar Ventures facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **March 2013**: Following the acquisition of Solar Holding, the ownership structure is as follows:
  - Mitsubishi Corporation: **50%**
  - Innovation Network Corporation of Japan (INCJ): **35%**
  - Solar Ventures: **15%**
  
This indicates that Solar Ventures does not exclusively own the facility but is part of a joint venture with Mitsubishi and INCJ.
