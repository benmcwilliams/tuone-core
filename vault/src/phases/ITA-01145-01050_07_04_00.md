### Evolutionary Timeline of FPT Industrial Facility in Turin, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2022**: FPT Industrial collaborates with Microvast to supply complete battery systems for the Iveco Bus Crossway LE Electric. The facility in Turin is responsible for assembling battery packs with a capacity of 400 to 466 kWh, which are designed for electric city and intercity buses. This project is part of a broader initiative to electrify commercial vehicles.

- **07/2023**: FPT Industrial continues to enhance its operations by assembling eBS37 battery packs at its ePowertrain plant in Turin. These batteries are used in Iveco’s eDaily and minibuses and are later reconditioned for use in mobile charging services by the startup Reefilla.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12/2022**: The partnership with Microvast is part of a long-term agreement established in 2019, indicating prior investment in the collaboration aimed at advancing battery technology for commercial vehicles.

- **07/2023**: The collaboration with Reefilla represents an investment in sustainable practices, focusing on the second life of electric vehicle batteries, which aligns with European regulations on sustainability.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2022**: Announcement of the collaboration with Microvast for the Iveco Bus Crossway LE Electric, with first deliveries expected in mid-2023.

- **07/2023**: Launch of a pilot project with Reefilla to utilize spent Iveco batteries for mobile charging services, marking a significant milestone in battery lifecycle management.

#### 4. Labour
- **07/2023**: The partnership with Reefilla and the ongoing projects at FPT Industrial are expected to create job opportunities related to the assembly and reconditioning of batteries, although specific job creation numbers are not mentioned.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- FPT Industrial is a brand of the Iveco Group, indicating that it operates under the umbrella of a larger corporate structure. The facility in Turin is not exclusively owned by FPT Industrial but is part of the broader Iveco Group, which became an independent publicly traded company in early 2022 after previously being part of CNH Industrial.
