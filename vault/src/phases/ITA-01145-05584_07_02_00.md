### Evolutionary Timeline of the Micro Mobility Facility in Turin, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-09-2021**: Micro Mobility Systems presented the Microlino 2.0 at the IAA Mobility. The vehicle will be available in three editions (Urban, Dolce, Competizione) and with three battery options (6, 10.5, and 14 kWh) allowing ranges of 95, 175, and 230 kilometers. Production was set to start in 2021.
- **06-01-2022**: Production of the Microlino was postponed to mid-March 2022 due to global supply chain bottlenecks. The first 500 vehicles were to be a special model.
- **04-05-2022**: The final assembly line in Turin was put into operation, with plans to produce a Microlino every 20 minutes after ramp-up. The facility is 3,000 square meters and covered with solar panels.
- **25-07-2023**: The Microlino began sales in France, expanding its market presence after being available in Germany and Switzerland.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-09-2021**: Micro Mobility established a new business unit for production in Italy, partnering with CECOMP as a minority shareholder. The facility in Turin was moved into in August 2021, with a production capacity expected to reach around 7,500 vehicles per year.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07-09-2021**: Announcement of the Microlino 2.0 and production plans.
- **06-01-2022**: Postponement of production start to mid-March 2022.
- **04-05-2022**: Start of the final assembly line in Turin, with production of pre-series vehicles ongoing.
- **25-07-2023**: Launch of Microlino in France, marking the expansion of the vehicle's availability.

#### 4. Labour
- **04-05-2022**: The assembly line in Turin is expected to employ up to 100 people, indicating job creation associated with the facility's operations.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **07-09-2021**: Micro Mobility Systems produces the Microlino at the Turin facility, having established a new business unit for production in Italy with CECOMP as a minority shareholder, indicating a joint venture structure for the facility.
