### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mitsubishi Facility in Sardinia, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: March 2013
  - **Details**: Mitsubishi Corporation, along with its partners, acquired Solar Holding, which has a photovoltaic (PV) portfolio of 42 MWp in Italy. The facility is part of a larger network of 19 plants located in Piedmont, Sardinia, and Apulia, managed by Solar Ventures. The energy production capacity of these plants is sufficient to meet the energy needs of approximately 20,000 households.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: March 2013
  - **Details**: The acquisition of Solar Holding marks Mitsubishi's first investment in Italy. The joint venture consists of Mitsubishi owning 50%, Innovation Network Corporation of Japan (INCJ) holding 35%, and Solar Ventures with 15%. The companies plan to acquire an additional 100-150 MWp of PV plants in Italy to strengthen their position in the market.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: March 2013
  - **Details**: The acquisition of Solar Holding was announced, marking a significant milestone for Mitsubishi in entering the Italian solar market. The companies involved expressed intentions to further develop and mature the Italian solar market through this investment.
