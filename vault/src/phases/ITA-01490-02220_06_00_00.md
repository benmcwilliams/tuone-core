### Evolutionary Timeline of the Eni Facility in Sardinia, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2021:** Eni completed a 31 MW solar plant at one of its chemical industry sites in Sardinia. The company is also planning to install 220 MW of photovoltaic (PV) power plants in the country, along with a 14 MW floating PV project. Additionally, Eni launched a partnership with Enel to bring online 20 MW of hydrogen capacity between 2022 and 2023.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **March 2021:** Eni, in collaboration with Cassa Depositi e Prestiti Equity (CDP Equity), announced a joint venture named GreenIT, which will invest around €800 million in renewable energy projects in Italy by 2025. This investment is expected to deploy approximately 1 GW of renewable energy capacity.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 2021:** The joint venture GreenIT was established, with plans to build large-scale renewable energy plants, utilizing properties owned by the Italian government.
