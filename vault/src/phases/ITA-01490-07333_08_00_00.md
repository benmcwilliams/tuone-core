### Evolutionary Timeline of the BlueFloat Energy Facility in Sardinia, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2022**: BlueFloat Energy, in partnership with Falck Renewables, announced the development of the Tibula Energia project, a 975MW offshore wind farm consisting of 62 floating wind turbines. The facility is expected to generate over 3.4 TWh of electricity annually and will be located between 25 and 40 kilometers off the northeastern coast of Sardinia.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2022**: The companies applied for a maritime concession on May 6, 2022, to begin the authorization process for the project. The project aims to support sustainable growth in local communities through various initiatives, although specific investment amounts were not detailed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2022**: The joint venture filed for a maritime concession and began exploratory research into local sea and wind conditions, environmental impacts, and industrial infrastructures. The companies plan to hold meetings with local communities to discuss the project before starting the authorization process with Italy’s Ministry of the Ecological Transition.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2022**: The Tibula Energia project is expected to create nearly 3,200 full-time jobs during its manufacturing, assembly, and construction phases. Once operational, the facility will employ over 180 people for maintenance.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2022**: The facility is a joint venture between Falck Renewables and BlueFloat Energy, indicating that neither company has exclusive ownership of the facility.
