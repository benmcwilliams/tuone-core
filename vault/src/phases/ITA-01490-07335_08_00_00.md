### Evolutionary Timeline of the CDP Equity Facility in Sardinia, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Project Name:** Ichnusa Wind Power Project
   - **Production Capacity:** 504 MW
   - **Technology Type:** Floating wind power
   - **Location:** Approximately 35 km off the coast of Sardinia
   - **Significance:** This facility is part of Italy’s initiative to reach 20 GW of operational wind power by 2030 and is designed to support local supply chain development.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Ownership Structure:** The project is a joint venture where CDP Equity holds a 49% stake, while Plenitude (Eni) owns 51%. The overall development is led by Copenhagen Offshore Partners (COP) on behalf of Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners.
   - **Development Context:** The Ichnusa project is part of a larger portfolio of almost 3 GW of floating wind projects being developed in Italy.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Milestone Achieved:** On **June 12, 2023**, COP submitted the Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) for the Ichnusa Wind Power Project to the Italian Ministry of Environment and Energy Security. This marks the project as the first floating wind project off the Sardinian coast and the third in Italy to reach this stage.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
