### Evolutionary Timeline of the Copenhagen Offshore Partners Facility in Sardinia, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Project Name**: Ichnusa Wind Power Project
- **Production Capacity**: 504 MW
- **Technology**: Floating wind power
- **Location**: Approximately 35 km off the coast of Sardinia
- **Significance**: This facility is noted as Italy’s first offshore wind project off the Sardinian coast and part of a larger floating wind portfolio of nearly 3 GW in Italy.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Structure**: The project is developed by Copenhagen Offshore Partners (COP) on behalf of Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners and GreenIT, a joint venture where Plenitude (Eni) holds 51% and CDP Equity holds 49%. 
- **Government Target**: The project aligns with the Italian government's target of achieving 20 GW of operational wind power by 2030.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Milestone Achieved**: On **June 12, 2023**, COP submitted the Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) for the Ichnusa Wind Power Project to the Italian Ministry of Environment and Energy Security. This marks a significant step in the project’s development.
