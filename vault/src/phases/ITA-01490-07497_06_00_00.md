### Evolutionary Timeline of the Solar Ventures Facility in Sardinia, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2013**: Solar Holding, which includes a PV portfolio of 42MWp in Italy, is managed by Solar Ventures. The facility's energy production capacity is stated to meet the energy needs of approximately 20,000 households. The facility is part of a larger network of 19 plants located in Piedmont, Sardinia, and Apulia.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2013**: Mitsubishi Corporation, Innovation Network Corporation of Japan (INCJ), and Solar Ventures acquire Solar Holding. The ownership structure post-acquisition is Mitsubishi (50%), INCJ (35%), and Solar Ventures (15%). This investment marks the first foray into the Italian market for Mitsubishi and INCJ, with plans to acquire an additional 100-150MWp of PV plants in Italy.
