## ITA-01847-03341

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Recom Facility in Padua, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 03-07-2019
     - Recom has a cell production plant in Padua, Italy, which is under consideration for a technological upgrade. No current capacity details were provided.
   - **Date:** 10-05-2024
     - Recom is consolidating its European cell and module manufacturing in Padua, Italy, following the liquidation of its subsidiary, Recom Sillia, in France. The specific capacity for the Padua facility was not disclosed, but it is expected to resume module production operations after a technological upgrade.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2024
     - Recom provided 30 million Euros in financing to its subsidiary Recom Sillia to increase its production capacity from 50 MW to 500 MW before the plant's closure. This investment reflects the company's strategy to consolidate operations in Padua.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2024
     - Recom Sillia's plant in France was placed in liquidation on April 12, 2024. The decision was made to relocate module production to the Padua facility, indicating a strategic shift in operations.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2024
     - The closure of the Recom Sillia plant in France resulted in the dismissal of 32 employees. However, there is mention of a procedure allowing for their accelerated relocation, suggesting potential job opportunities at the Padua facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2024
     - Recom-Italia, a subsidiary of Recom, acquired the Sillia business out of bankruptcy in 2017. The consolidation of operations in Padua indicates that Recom maintains ownership of the facility, although the specific ownership structure (full ownership or joint venture) is not explicitly stated in the articles.
