### RWE Facility in Sicily, Italy - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **08/2021**: RWE completed the construction of the Alcamo 2 wind farm in Sicily, with a production capacity of **13.6 MW**. The facility consists of four **GW 136-3.4 MW Goldwind turbines**. The wind farm has a power purchase agreement (PPA) with the local paper manufacturer **Sofidel**, which will purchase all guarantees of origin generated by the wind farm’s output.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **08/2021**: The construction of the Alcamo 2 wind farm was enabled by the PPA with Sofidel, allowing RWE to build the facility without relying on state subsidies. This agreement supports Sofidel's climate goals, aiming for a **40% reduction in emissions per tonne of paper by 2030** compared to 2018 levels.
