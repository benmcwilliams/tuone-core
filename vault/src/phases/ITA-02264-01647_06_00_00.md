### Evolutionary Timeline of the Falck Renewables Facility in Sicily, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2019
   - **Details:** Falck Renewables agreed to acquire a 70% stake in Big Fish SPV S.r.l., which holds the rights to a large-scale solar PV project with a capacity of 195MW in Sicily. The project has already secured a connection point and preliminary land rights, with plans to apply for building permits in Q4 2019. If completed, this project will significantly expand Falck's renewable portfolio, which exceeds 1GW globally.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2019
   - **Details:** Falck Renewables will pay Canadian Solar €60,000 for the 70% stake, covering the vehicle’s corporate capital share. The total price could increase to €145,000 if project capacity increases. The financing for the project will be done on a pro-rata basis by the promoters of Big Fish SPV S.r.l.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2019
   - **Details:** The acquisition of the 70% stake in Big Fish SPV S.r.l. was announced, with plans to apply for building permits in Q4 2019. The project is in the preliminary stages, having secured necessary rights but not yet started construction.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** July 2019
   - **Details:** Falck Renewables will hold a 70% stake in the facility through Big Fish SPV S.r.l., indicating a joint ownership structure. There is an option for Falck to acquire the remaining 30% share once construction is approved.
