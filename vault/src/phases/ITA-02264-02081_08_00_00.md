## ITA-02264-02081

### Evolutionary Timeline of Enel's 3Sun Facility in Sicily, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-06-2018**: Enel Green Power launched the Wind Big Data Boost project, testing new micro electro mechanical sensors on turbines in Sicily to improve predictive maintenance.
- **13-05-2021**: The 3Sun facility in Sicily had a production capacity of 200MW for bifacial heterojunction modules, with plans to scale up to 3GW by the second half of 2023.
- **01-04-2022**: Enel announced the expansion of the 3Sun facility to 3GW, with the first 400MW expected to be operational by September 2023 and full capacity by July 2024.
- **15-04-2022**: The facility will produce bifacial heterojunction PV cells and is expected to exceed 30% efficiency with future tandem cell structures.
- **23-06-2022**: 3D Micromac was contracted to supply laser cutting technology for the expansion of the 3Sun facility, which aims to increase production capacity to 3GW by 2024.
- **14-06-2023**: 3Sun developed a silicon-perovskite tandem PV cell with a recorded efficiency of 27.1% in partnership with French research institutions.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **01-04-2022**: Enel Green Power announced a total investment of around €600 million for the 3Sun facility, with EU funding of nearly €118 million.
- **16-01-2024**: 3Sun secured €560 million in financing for its heterojunction module plant, supported by the European Investment Bank and Italian banks.
- **20-02-2023**: The project received a €600 million investment grant from the European Commission as part of its Innovation Fund for large-scale projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **13-05-2021**: Announcement of plans to scale production capacity from 200MW to 3GW.
- **01-04-2022**: Confirmation of the expansion timeline, with the first 400MW operational by September 2023 and full capacity by July 2024.
- **09-06-2023**: Reports of Enel in talks to sell a minority stake in the 3Sun facility to NextEnergy Capital.
- **04-03-2024**: The facility is expected to reach its full operational capacity of 3GW.

#### 4. Labour
- **01-04-2022**: The expansion of the 3Sun facility is expected to create approximately 1,000 direct and indirect jobs by 2024.
- **09-06-2023**: The facility is projected to create 900 direct jobs and 1,000 indirect jobs upon completion.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **09-06-2023**: Enel is reportedly in talks to sell a minority stake in the 3Sun facility, indicating that it does not exclusively own the facility but is open to joint ventures or partnerships.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones of Enel's 3Sun facility in Sicily, highlighting its technological advancements, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
