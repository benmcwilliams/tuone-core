#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2019**: European Energy is developing a portfolio of solar projects in Italy, with a total capacity of 1 GW. Approximately 300 MW of this capacity is under development, with half of it ready to build. The facility in Sicily is part of this portfolio.
