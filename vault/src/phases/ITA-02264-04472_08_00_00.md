## ITA-02264-04472

### Aker Solutions Facility in Sicily, ITA - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: September 2024
   - **Details**: Aker Solutions has been contracted by Renexia to design underwater substations for the 2.8 GW Med Wind floating offshore wind project in the Mediterranean Sea. The project involves the design of eight modules to be placed on the seabed of the Strait of Sicily, facilitating the connection of floating wind turbines to the power grid. The facility aims to generate approximately 9 TWh of electricity annually, sufficient to meet the energy needs of 3.4 million households.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: September 2024
   - **Details**: The project is part of a larger investment in the Med Wind floating offshore wind farm, which is expected to be built in phases to reach its full capacity of 2.8 GW. The design phase is supported by previous oceanographic studies that have identified suitable areas for construction.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: September 2024
   - **Details**: Aker Solutions has commenced the design phase for the underwater substations as part of the Med Wind project. This marks the first involvement of Aker Solutions in an offshore wind project in the Mediterranean Sea.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: September 2024
   - **Details**: The construction of the wind farm is expected to create jobs in Sicily over the six years of construction and throughout the 25 years of maintenance activities.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: September 2024
   - **Details**: The facility is part of a collaboration where Aker Solutions is contracted by Renexia, indicating that Aker Solutions does not own the facility exclusively but is involved in a contractual partnership for the design and engineering aspects of the project.
