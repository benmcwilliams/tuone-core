1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 10-12-2019
   - **Details:** STEAG Group announced plans for a 440MW solar complex in Sicily, to be deployed by its subsidiary STEAG Solar Energy Solutions (SENS). The complex will consist of six solar parks located within a 40-kilometre radius in the Palermo and Trapani provinces. The facility is expected to produce enough energy to power over 350,000 households. A first section of the complex is projected to complete construction by the end of 2020.
