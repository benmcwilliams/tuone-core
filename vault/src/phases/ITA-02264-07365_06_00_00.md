### Evolutionary Timeline of the SENS Italia Facility in Sicily, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: SENS Italia began construction of a 135MW agri-PV project in Sicily, covering an area of 230 hectares. This facility is designed to operate both for energy production and agriculture, with plans to plant olive and fig trees and set up beehives alongside the solar panels. This project is the first of seven planned ground-mounted agri-PV farms in Sicily, which together will have a total output of around 700MW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The construction of the 135MW agri-PV project follows a contract signed with Peridot Solar, a subsidiary of FitzWalter Capital, which is an investor in the solar project. Specific investment amounts or subsidies were not detailed in the article.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: The construction activity for the 135MW agri-PV project commenced, with an expected operation date by the end of the year. SENS Italia has been involved in planning and developing this large-scale project since 2019 in collaboration with Sicilia e Progresso (S&P).
