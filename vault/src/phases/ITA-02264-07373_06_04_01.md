### Evolutionary Timeline of the 3D Micromac Facility in Sicily, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2022**: 3D Micromac supplies its microCELL MCS laser cutting technology to Enel Green Power’s 3Sun gigafactory in Sicily. This technology is used for cutting high-efficiency heterojunction solar cells. The microCELL MCS system has a throughput of over 6,000 wafers per hour and can cut various sizes of mono-crystalline and polycrystalline silicon wafers into half-cells or shingled cells.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2022**: Enel Green Power announces an expansion of the 3Sun gigafactory's production capacity from 200MW to 3GW by July 2024. The total investment required for this expansion is €600 million (approximately US$631 million), with €118 million (approximately US$124.1 million) coming from EU funding via the Innovation Fund for large-scale projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2022**: The 3Sun gigafactory had a production capacity of 200MW.
- **June 2022**: Announcement of the supply contract for the laser cutting technology and the intention to ramp up production capacity to 3GW by July 2024.
