### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enel Facility in Rome, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10-03-2022**: Enel Group, through its subsidiary Enel X Global Retail, announced a partnership with Santander to provide solar PV, lithium battery storage, and energy efficiency solutions for residential and commercial sectors. Enel aims to triple its renewable capacity from 54GW to 162GW by 2030 and plans to decommission its thermal fleet by 2040.
- **26-06-2023**: Enel X secured a tender to build a 22MW solar photovoltaic plant at Leonardo da Vinci Airport in Rome. The plant will cover 340,000m² and include 55,000 solar panels, with an expected annual generation of 32GWh of renewable energy. It will also feature a 10MWh storage system using recycled batteries from electric vehicles.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **26-06-2023**: The solar plant project at Fiumicino Airport is part of the partly EU-funded PIONEER airport sustainability project, which focuses on integrating second-life batteries into a reliable power system.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10-03-2022**: Announcement of the partnership with Santander and plans for solar and storage solutions.
- **26-06-2023**: Enel X won the tender for the solar plant at Fiumicino Airport, with construction expected to begin by the end of 2023 and completion scheduled for 2024.
