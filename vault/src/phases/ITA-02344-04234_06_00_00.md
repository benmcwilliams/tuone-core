#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: August 2023**
  - A group of researchers from Sapienza University of Rome, along with other institutions, investigated the economic competitiveness of floating photovoltaic (FPV) systems compared to ground-mounted photovoltaic (GPV) systems. The study highlighted that moving a monofacial PV array from land to water can increase performance by nearly 5%. The research also indicated that using bifacial modules and trackers could further enhance performance.
