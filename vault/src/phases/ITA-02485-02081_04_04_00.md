### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enel Facility in Sarroch, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2021**: Enel Green Power and Saras announced plans to set up a green hydrogen production facility at the Saras refinery in Sarroch. The facility will utilize a **20 MW electrolyzer** powered by renewable energy produced on-site. The hydrogen produced will serve as a raw material for the refinery's operations.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2021**: The partnership between Enel Green Power and Saras is aimed at developing the green hydrogen project, indicating a commitment to invest in sustainable energy solutions. Specific investment amounts were not disclosed, but the project is part of a broader strategy to decarbonize the refining process.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2021**: A memorandum of intent was signed between Enel Green Power and Saras to formalize their collaboration on the green hydrogen project. This memorandum signifies the commitment to accelerate the energy transition and develop the necessary infrastructure for hydrogen production.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2021**: While specific job creation numbers were not mentioned, the development of the green hydrogen facility is expected to contribute positively to job creation in the region, particularly in the context of energy transition and decarbonization efforts.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2021**: The facility is a collaboration between Enel Green Power and Saras, indicating a **joint venture** structure for the green hydrogen project. Enel does not own the facility exclusively; rather, it partners with Saras to develop and operate the hydrogen production capabilities.

This timeline captures the key developments related to the Enel facility in Sarroch, Italy, focusing on the establishment of green hydrogen production and the collaborative efforts between Enel and Saras.
