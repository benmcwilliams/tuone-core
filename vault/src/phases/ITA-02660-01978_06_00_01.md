## ITA-02660-01978

### Evolutionary Timeline of the SunPower Facility in Montalto di Castro, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2009**: SunPower announces plans to build a 100MW solar power plant in Montalto di Castro, Italy, with a 24MW first phase expected to be completed by the end of 2009.
- **12/2009**: The first phase of the Montalto di Castro solar power plant, a 24MW facility, is completed and connected to the grid on November 30, 2009, ahead of schedule. The plant utilizes 78,720 SunPower solar panels and tracker systems.
- **12/2010**: SunPower closes a €195.2 million solar bond issuance to fund the addition of 44MW to the facility, bringing the total capacity to 72MW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09/2009**: The project is financed by a consortium of international and Italian banks, with a total investment of €120 million for the first phase.
- **12/2010**: SunPower secures a €195.2 million loan to finance the final two phases of the Montalto di Castro solar park.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **11/2009**: Construction of the first phase (24MW) is completed and connected to the grid.
- **10/2010**: The second phase (8MW) is commissioned.
- **12/2010**: The solar bond issuance for the final phase (44MW) is closed, with plans for further development.

#### 4. Labour
- **12/2009**: More than 200 local workers were trained in advanced PV technology during the construction of the first phase, contributing to job creation in the community.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **09/2009**: The facility is built in collaboration with SunRay Renewable Energy, which will own the plant. SunPower provides technology and project management services but does not own the facility exclusively.
