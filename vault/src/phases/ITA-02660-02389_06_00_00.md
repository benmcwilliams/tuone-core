### Evolutionary Timeline of the Octopus Facility in Montalto di Castro, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2018**: Octopus has 110 MW of solar projects under construction in Italy, including five operational, unsubsidized PV sites totaling 68 MW located near Montalto di Castro. These sites are part of a larger 173 MW Italian PV portfolio.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2018**: Octopus completed a €23 million refinancing for its projects, which includes the five operational PV sites near Montalto di Castro.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2018**: Octopus signed a five-year private power purchase agreement (PPA) with EGO Group for the electricity generated from its five operational PV sites totaling 68 MW. This agreement is valid until September 2023.
- **April 2019**: The municipality of Montalto di Castro is identified as a key area for solar development, with plans for several large solar parks, including the existing 84 MW facility completed in 2010 and the first grid parity projects built by Octopus in 2017.
