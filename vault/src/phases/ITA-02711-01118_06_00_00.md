### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Southern Italy, ITA

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: Iberdrola announced plans to develop a portfolio of solar and wind projects with a combined capacity of nearly **400 MW**. This includes the construction of small and medium-sized solar photovoltaic (PV) plants and wind power plants, along with necessary grid connection infrastructure. The projects are expected to generate enough green energy to meet the needs of up to **260,000 households**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2023**: Iberdrola secured a **€150 million ($161 million)** loan from the European Investment Bank (EIB) to expedite the development of these renewable energy facilities. The total investment for the projects is projected to exceed **€300 million**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2023**: The EIB and Iberdrola signed an agreement to accelerate the development of renewable energy plants in Italy. This includes the construction of the aforementioned **400 MW** portfolio of wind and solar projects. Iberdrola has a project pipeline of almost **3 GW** in Italy, which includes a **23 MW** PV plant already in operation and four licensed, ready-to-build projects with a combined capacity of **116 MW**.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2023**: The construction of the new infrastructure is expected to support approximately **600 temporary jobs** each year during the construction phase, contributing to economic growth and employment in the regions of southern Italy.
