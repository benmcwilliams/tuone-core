#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10/2021**: Fly Solartech Solutions Srl developed a lightweight cylindrical solar panel designed for integration onto PV-powered street lighting lamp posts. - **01/2024**: The company unveiled new 350 W balcony TOPCon solar modules, featuring a power conversion efficiency of 17.87%. These modules are designed for easy installation on balconies and terraces and are produced at the factory in Tolmezzo.
