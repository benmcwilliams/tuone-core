1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** FuturaSun installed an 8.84 kWp solar-plus-storage system to power a panoramic elevator in Cuneo. The system includes 26 FuturaSun Next Pro 340 W monocrystalline photovoltaic panels, totaling a surface area of 45 square meters. The elevator utilizes energy from the solar array, batteries, and gravitational storage generated while the elevator moves.
