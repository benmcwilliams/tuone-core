## ITA-03030-07350

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hope Group Facility in Apulia, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **29-09-2022**: Hope Group, in partnership with Galileo, announced the development of the **Lupiae Maris** project, a **525MW floating wind project** off the coast of Apulia. The project will consist of **35 floating wind turbines**, each with a capacity of **15MW**. The facility will be located several kilometers from the coast, utilizing floating offshore wind foundations to access deeper seabeds and exploit stronger wind resources in the Southern Adriatic Sea.
- **24-02-2023**: The joint venture also announced the **Barium Bay** offshore wind farm, which will have a capacity of **1.1GW** and will feature **74 wind turbines** capable of generating over **three billion kilowatt-hours** of clean energy. This facility will be located **40km from shore**, stretching from Barium to the north of Barletta.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **16-01-2023**: The Lupiae Maris project filed its **Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)** with the Italian Ministry of Environment and Energy Security, marking a significant step towards obtaining planning permission for the facility. The project aims to generate approximately **1.5 billion renewable kWh per year**, sufficient to meet the annual energy needs of over **500,000 families**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **29-09-2022**: Announcement of the joint venture and project initiation for Lupiae Maris.
- **16-01-2023**: Submission of the EIA for the Lupiae Maris project.
- **24-02-2023**: Announcement of the Barium Bay project, with plans to submit an EIA later in the year and commence a **24-month wind measurement campaign**.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or loss figures related to the Lupiae Maris or Barium Bay projects. However, the projects are described as opportunities for local communities to develop new industrial capabilities and contribute to the energy transition, suggesting potential job creation.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is developed as a **joint venture (JV)** between **Hope Group** and **Galileo**. There is no indication of exclusive ownership by either company; both are collaborating on the projects.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Hope Group facility in Apulia, Italy, focusing on the Lupiae Maris and Barium Bay projects.
