#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2019**: Solar-Konzept is planning several unsubsidized solar parks with a total generation capacity of **800 MW** across the southern Italian regions of Apulia and Basilicata. The projects are designed to incorporate newly planted Mediterranean forest on approximately **25%** of their footprint.
