## ITA-03213-03514

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Solitek Facility in Benevento, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2023**: Solitek announced plans to build a solar module factory in Benevento, Italy, with an annual production capacity of **600 MW**. The facility will produce solar panels specifically for **agrivoltaics** applications. Additionally, there are plans for a future expansion to include **1 GWh** of residential battery capacity per year.
- **December 2023**: Solitek launched a new product line of solar carports featuring **370 W glass-glass frameless modules**. This product is designed for both residential and commercial applications.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2023**: The initial investment for the Benevento facility is projected to be **€50 million** (approximately **$53.4 million**). This project will be financed through a combination of subsidies from the **European Commission** and the company's own investment.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2023**: Announcement of the new manufacturing facility in Benevento, Italy.
- **Expected Q2 2024**: The facility is anticipated to begin manufacturing operations.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2023**: Solitek plans to create up to **300 jobs** in the Benevento region as part of the facility's development, aiming to revitalize a low-employment area.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **March 2023**: Solitek is a unit of **Global BOD**, indicating that the facility is not owned exclusively by Solitek but is part of a larger corporate structure. The facility is a direct holding of Global BOD, which also owns the ophthalmic lens manufacturing company Bod Lenses.
