### Evolutionary Timeline of the Bluenergy Facility in Udine, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2024**: Bluenergy, in partnership with Udinese Calcio, began the installation of a solar panel system on the roof of the Friuli stadium. The system will consist of more than 2,400 solar panels, producing a daily average of 3,000 kilowatt hours (kWh). The project aims to cover part of the stadium's energy needs and is expected to be operational by October 2024.
- **July 2024**: The facility is officially developing a 1.1 MW photovoltaic (PV) system on the stadium. The project may include a 300 kW battery storage system to enhance efficiency.
