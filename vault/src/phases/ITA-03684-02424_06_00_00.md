### Evolutionary Timeline of the Repsol Facility in Puglia, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: Repsol announced plans to develop renewable energy projects totaling 1,768 MW in Italy, which includes 943 MW of wind power and 825 MW of solar power. The company is set to begin construction of its first two photovoltaic solar plants in the Puglia region, with a combined capacity of 11 MW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2022**: Repsol acquired Asterion Energies for €560 million ($594 million), which included a portfolio of renewable assets totaling 7,700 MW. This acquisition is part of Repsol's strategy to expand its renewable energy footprint in Italy and other regions.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: Repsol confirmed that more than 60% of its renewable projects in Italy are in advanced stages of development. The company is expected to start construction on the first two solar plants in Puglia shortly.
