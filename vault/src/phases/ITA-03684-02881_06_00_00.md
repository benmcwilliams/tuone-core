### Evolutionary Timeline of the European Energy Facility in Puglia, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2019**: European Energy is developing a total of 300 MW of solar plant capacity in Italy, with around half of this capacity (approximately 150 MW) ready to build. The company has a project pipeline in Italy of about 1 GW, primarily located in Puglia and Sicily.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2019**: The facility secured a 12-year Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with Axpo Italia, which ensures the bankability of the projects and supports the realization of substantial parts of the Italian project pipeline.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2019**: Announcement of the PPA and readiness of 150 MW of solar projects for construction. The company indicated that permitting activities for many hundreds of megawatts were already underway in the Italian market.

- **January 2019**: While specific job creation numbers were not mentioned, the readiness of projects for construction suggests potential job creation associated with the development and operation of the solar facilities.

- **January 2019**: European Energy has a mixed ownership structure for its projects in Italy. It has some projects in partnership with local entities, such as a 400 MW portfolio in a 50-50 partnership with Limes Renewable Energy, while also having projects where it is the sole owner.
