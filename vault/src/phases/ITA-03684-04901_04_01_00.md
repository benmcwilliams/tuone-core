### Evolutionary Timeline of the Edison Facility in Puglia, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: February 2024
   - **Details**: The Edison facility is part of a larger initiative to establish a 'Green Hydrogen Valley' in Puglia. The facility will produce green hydrogen using renewable energy sources. The project will include 260 megawatts (MW) of solar photovoltaic (PV) generation capacity and 160 MW of electrolysis capacity to produce green hydrogen. This hydrogen will be utilized in the direct reduced iron (DRI) production process to decarbonize steel-making in Italy's largest steel plant located in Taranto and other Italian steel mills.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: February 2024
   - **Details**: The European Commission has awarded financing of up to 370 million euros ($402 million) to support the establishment of the Green Hydrogen Valley in Puglia. This funding is part of the important projects of common European interest (IPCEI) aimed at promoting green hydrogen initiatives.
