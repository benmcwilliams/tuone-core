### Evolutionary Timeline of the Platina Partners Facility in Puglia, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **09/2010:** Platina Partners finalized the acquisition of 10MW of solar PV projects from the Degennaro Group. The facility consists of ten 1MW solar PV plants located in the Puglia region, marking Platina's first operational plants in Italy. Following this acquisition, Platina's portfolio includes a total of 162MW of renewable energy projects, with 31MW being operational solar PV projects.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **09/2010:** The investment in the 10MW solar PV projects is highlighted as a strategic move by Platina Partners to enter the Italian market, which is recognized as a key European solar market due to its supportive regulatory environment for solar PV installations.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **11/2010:** The first of the ten solar PV plants was connected to the grid in November, with all plants expected to be connected by the end of the year.
