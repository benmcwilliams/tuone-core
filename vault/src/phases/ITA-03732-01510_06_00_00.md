### Evolutionary Timeline of the Canadian Solar Facility in Lazio, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2020**: Canadian Solar announced a portfolio of 12 unsubsidized solar projects totaling 151 MW across Italy, including Lazio. The projects will utilize Canadian Solar's HiKu CS3W-425 modules and are expected to generate approximately 287 GWh of solar power annually once operational.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2020**: Canadian Solar secured a €55 million credit facility from Intesa San Paolo to fund the construction of the 151 MW portfolio. This financing is part of the company's commitment to invest in the Italian solar market, particularly during the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2020**: The first projects in the portfolio are expected to break ground in the third quarter of 2020. This portfolio is noted to be among Italy's first subsidy-free utility-scale PV projects.
