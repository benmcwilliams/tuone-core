## ITA-03732-07341

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enfinity Facility in Lazio, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2022**: Enfinity Global reached financial close on solar PV projects totaling 112MW in Lazio, expected to produce 191GWh annually. This included two projects of 72MW and a third project of 40MW.
- **December 2023**: Enfinity Global secured financing for four utility-scale solar PV plants with a total capacity of 101MW, expected to generate 172GWh annually.
- **February 2024**: Enfinity Global agreed on a €25 million finance facility for a 134MW solar portfolio in Lazio, which will generate 225GWh annually, contracted through a 10-year PPA with Statkraft.
- **December 2024**: Enfinity Global secured €165 million for a 147MW solar PV portfolio in Lazio, expected to be operational by 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2022**: Enfinity Global secured €142 million (US$150 million) in financing for the initial 112MW solar projects.
- **December 2023**: The company secured €118 million (US$127.88 million) for the construction of four solar plants totaling 101MW.
- **February 2024**: Enfinity Global secured €25 million for the 134MW solar portfolio.
- **December 2024**: The company secured €165 million for the 147MW solar portfolio, bringing total financing in 2024 to €865 million.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2022**: Financial close for 112MW solar projects; operational expected in early 2024.
- **January 2023**: Enfinity signed a 10-year PPA with Statkraft for 191GWh of electricity.
- **April 2023**: Announcement of an additional 22MW solar facility, increasing the total capacity under the PPA with Statkraft to 134MW/225GWh.
- **February 2024**: Construction of the 134MW solar portfolio began, with progressive commissioning expected starting in Q2 2024.
- **December 2024**: Financing secured for a 147MW solar portfolio, expected to be operational by 2026.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
