### Evolutionary Timeline of the REC Systems Facility in Lazio, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 03-02-2011
   - **Details:** REC Systems, in collaboration with ABB, began construction of a solar power plant with a production capacity of 24.2 MW. The facility will utilize 100,000 REC modules to generate approximately 37 million kWh of electricity annually.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 03-02-2011
   - **Details:** The financing for the project, which is noted as Italy’s largest lease-financed development, is provided by Unicredit Leasing, Leasint, and BNP Paribas Leasing Solutions.

   - **Date:** 03-02-2011
   - **Details:** Construction of the solar power plant commenced after financing was secured. The project had received authorization in March 2010.
