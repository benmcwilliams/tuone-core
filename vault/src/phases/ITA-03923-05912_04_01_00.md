### Evolutionary Timeline of the Renhive Facility in Mantua, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2023**: The Mantua Hydrogen Valley project, involving Renhive, aims to produce over 1,500 tons of hydrogen annually using renewable sources. The facility will include a dedicated photovoltaic park with an installed power of approximately 14MW, directly connected to the hydrogen production plant. Additionally, a 10 MW electrolyser will be installed by Sapio for hydrogen production.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12/2023**: The total project investment is over €60 million, financed by the National Recovery and Resilience Plan (PNRR) (€30 million) and the European Interregional Innovation Investment tender (€9 million). The project is expected to unlock over €30 million in private capital.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2023**: The Mantua Hydrogen Valley project has been officially unveiled, with various initiatives planned, including the construction of the photovoltaic park and hydrogen production plant. The project also includes the establishment of a hydrogen refueling station and a tri-modal refueling station at the Port of Valdaro.

#### 4. Labour
- **12/2023**: The project is expected to create new job opportunities as it progresses, although specific job creation numbers have not been detailed in the article.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **12/2023**: The facility is part of a collaborative project involving multiple partners, including Renhive, Sapio, and Agire, along with 16 other partners from various sectors. The ownership structure appears to be a joint venture rather than exclusive ownership by Renhive.
