### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sapio Facility in Mantua, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 12-05-2023
   - **Details**: The Sapio facility is part of the Hydrogen Valley project in Mantua, which aims to produce over 1,500 tons of renewable hydrogen annually. The facility will feature a 10 MW electrolyser installed in an abandoned industrial area, directly connected to a dedicated photovoltaic park with an installed power of approximately 14MW. The project will also include a hydrogen refueling station with a delivery capacity of up to 1 ton per day, supplying light vehicles, local public transportation, and heavy-duty vehicles.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: 12-05-2023
   - **Details**: The total project is valued at over €60 million, with funding sourced from the National Recovery and Resilience Plan (PNRR) (€30 million) and the European Interregional Innovation Investment tender (€9 million). The project is expected to unlock over €30 million in private capital.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: 12-05-2023
   - **Details**: The Hydrogen Valley project has been officially unveiled, with plans for construction and operation of the hydrogen production facility and associated infrastructure. The project includes various initiatives such as the construction of a photovoltaic park and a tri-modal refueling station at the Port of Valdaro.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: 12-05-2023
   - **Details**: The project is expected to create new job opportunities as it progresses, although specific numbers regarding job creation have not been detailed in the article.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
