## ITA-03950-04901

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Edison Facility in Brindisi, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** Edison is involved in the development of a green hydrogen project as part of the Puglia Green Hydrogen Valley initiative. The Brindisi facility will feature a production plant for green hydrogen using electrolysers with a capacity of 60 MW, powered by a photovoltaic (PV) farm. The combined capacity of three planned plants (Brindisi, Taranto, and Cerignola) will be 220 MW, with an expected annual production of approximately 300 million cubic meters of renewable hydrogen.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The project aims to accelerate the uptake of green hydrogen in Italy's energy mix, contributing to national and European climate neutrality targets by 2050. The initiative is expected to involve investments in research and development (R&D) to foster the hydrogen industry in the Puglia region.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** A memorandum of understanding (MoU) was signed among Edison, Snam, Saipem, and Alboran Hydrogen for the joint development of the green hydrogen project. The authorization process for the Brindisi project is already underway, indicating progress towards construction.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The project anticipates job creation through the establishment of a special purpose company involving regional stakeholders, although specific job numbers were not mentioned in the article.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The facility will be part of a special purpose company where Edison, Snam, and Alboran will each hold 30% stakes, while Saipem will hold a 10% stake, indicating a joint venture ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by Edison.
