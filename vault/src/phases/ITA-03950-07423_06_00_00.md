### Evolutionary Timeline of Eni New Energy SpA Facility in Brindisi, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2019**: Eni New Energy SpA announced plans to develop Italy's first utility-scale floating solar plant with a capacity of **14.19 MW**. The facility will be located on an artificial water basin in Brindisi, Apulia, and will consist of two separate floating arrays featuring **39,424 crystalline modules** with an output of **360 W** each, along with five central inverters.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2019**: The estimated cost for the floating solar project is approximately **€14.7 million**.
