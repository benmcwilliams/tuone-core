## ITA-03951-04589

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Barilla Facility in Foggia, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2020**: Barilla is set to implement a solar thermal technology pilot plant as part of the HiFlex project. The facility will utilize a solar tower with approximately 500 movable mirrors (heliostats) to concentrate sunlight on a receiver that heats ceramic particles to temperatures of up to 1,000°C. This technology aims to provide renewable electricity and heat for Barilla's pasta production processes.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2020**: The HiFlex project, which includes Barilla and the German Aerospace Center (DLR) among its 11 partners, has received EUR 13.5 million (approximately USD 16 million) in funding from the Horizon 2020 program. This investment is specifically tied to the development of the solar thermal technology at the Foggia facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2020**: The announcement of the pilot installation of the solar tower near Barilla's factory in Foggia marks a significant milestone in the project. The special receiver developed by DLR has been tested in Germany and is scheduled to be delivered to Foggia in the following year for installation.
