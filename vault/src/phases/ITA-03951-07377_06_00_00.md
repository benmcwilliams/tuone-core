- **December 2019**: A2A announced plans to start building its first large-scale, subsidy-free photovoltaic (PV) plant in the early months of 2020 in the province of Foggia. #### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2019**: A2A acquired a 1 GW solar development pipeline from Chinese PV manufacturer Talesun. This acquisition is part of A2A's broader strategy to develop subsidy-free solar projects across Italy, including the planned 10 MW plant in Foggia.
