### Evolutionary Timeline of the Solar-Konzept Facility in Foggia, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2019**: Solar-Konzept is planning several unsubsidized solar parks with a total generation capacity of **800 MW** across the southern Italian regions of Apulia and Basilicata. The facility in Foggia is part of this pipeline, which includes projects with capacities ranging from **16 MW to 98 MW**. The company has secured land and grid connection approvals for all projects.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2019**: The total investment for the planned facilities is estimated at around **€180 million**. Solar-Konzept is seeking power purchase agreements (PPAs) for its projects, as they are being developed without subsidies.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2019**: Solar-Konzept has applied for environmental impact assessments for several solar parks, including three **20 MW** solar parks near Melfi in Basilicata and a **20 MW** facility near Taranto in Apulia. Additionally, they plan to apply for another **140 MW** of capacity in the Apulian provinces of Foggia, Bari, and Taranto. The company aims to obtain final authorizations by the end of **2020** and plans to build the power plants between **2021 and 2022**.
