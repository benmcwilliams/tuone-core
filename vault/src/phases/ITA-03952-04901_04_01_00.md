## ITA-03952-04901

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Edison Facility in Taranto, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** Edison, in collaboration with Snam, Saipem, and Alboran Hydrogen, announced the development of the Puglia Green Hydrogen Valley project, which includes the construction of three green hydrogen production plants in Brindisi, Taranto, and Cerignola. The combined capacity of these plants will be 220 MW, powered by a total photovoltaic (PV) production of 380 MW. Once operational, the plants are expected to produce approximately 300 million cubic meters of renewable hydrogen annually, primarily for local industries and potentially for blending into the local gas network.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The project aims to accelerate the uptake of green hydrogen in Italy's energy mix, contributing to national and European climate neutrality targets by 2050. The initiative is expected to underpin investments in research and development (R&D) to foster the hydrogen industry in the Puglia region.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** A memorandum of understanding (MoU) was signed among the partners for the joint development of the green hydrogen project. The authorization process for the Brindisi project is already underway, which will involve the construction of a 60 MW green hydrogen production plant powered by a PV farm.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The project anticipates the creation of a special purpose company to execute the project, which will involve key regional players and is expected to create jobs related to the hydrogen production industry in the Puglia region.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The special purpose company will have Edison, Snam, and Alboran each holding a 30% stake, while Saipem will hold a 10% stake. This indicates that Edison does not own the facility exclusively but is part of a joint venture with other companies.
