## ITA-04402-02823

### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDPR Facility in Serracapriola, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2021**: EDPR began operations of the **28 MW Serracapriola wind farm**, which consists of seven wind turbines. This facility marked a significant addition to EDPR's operational capacity in Italy, bringing it to **299 MW**.
- **July 2024**: EDPR connected its first **10 MWp utility-scale photovoltaic solar project**, named **Boccadoro**, to the national grid. This solar project utilizes over 18,000 bifacial panels and is expected to produce **17.5 GWh** of clean energy annually, supplying energy to over **6,000 local homes**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2024**: EDPR announced plans to achieve over **1 GW of renewable build-out capacity** in Italy, which includes investments in both wind and solar projects. The company is committed to diversifying its renewable energy sources and accelerating the energy transition in Italy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2021**: The Serracapriola wind farm became operational, being one of the first projects from the Italian auction held in October 2019 to connect to the grid.
- **July 2024**: The Boccadoro solar project was connected to the grid, marking a milestone in EDPR's commitment to renewable energy in Italy.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2024**: The expansion of renewable energy projects, including the Boccadoro solar project, is expected to create local jobs as part of EDPR's commitment to business development in Italy.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- EDPR operates the Serracapriola facility under its own brand, indicating **full ownership** of the wind and solar projects in the region. The company has been expanding its presence in Italy since entering the market in 2010 through EDP Renewables. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones of the EDPR facility in Serracapriola, Italy, highlighting its growth in renewable energy production and commitment to sustainability.
