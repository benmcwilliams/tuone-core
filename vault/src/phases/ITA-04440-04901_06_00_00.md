## ITA-04440-04901

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Edison Facility in Cerignola, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** Edison, in collaboration with Snam, Saipem, and Alboran Hydrogen, announced the development of the Puglia Green Hydrogen Valley project, which includes the construction of three green hydrogen production plants in Brindisi, Taranto, and Cerignola. The combined capacity of these plants will be 220 MW, powered by a total photovoltaic (PV) production of 380 MW. The plants are expected to produce approximately 300 million cubic meters of renewable hydrogen annually, primarily for local industries and potentially for blending into the local gas network.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The project aims to accelerate the uptake of green hydrogen in Italy, contributing to national and European climate neutrality targets by 2050. The initiative is expected to involve significant investments in research and development (R&D) to foster the hydrogen industry in the Puglia region.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** A memorandum of understanding (MoU) was signed among the partners for the joint development of the green hydrogen project. An authorization process is already underway for the Brindisi project, which will include the construction of a 60 MW green hydrogen production plant powered by a PV farm.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The project anticipates job creation through the establishment of a special purpose company involving regional stakeholders and the development of competencies in the hydrogen industry.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The special purpose company for the project will have Edison, Snam, and Alboran each holding 30% stakes, while Saipem will hold a 10% stake. This indicates a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by Edison.
