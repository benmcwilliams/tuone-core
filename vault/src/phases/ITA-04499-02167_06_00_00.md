## ITA-04499-02167

### Evolutionary Timeline of the BayWa Facility in Tarquinia, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2023**: BayWa r.e. announced the development of a solar farm in Tarquinia, Lazio, with an installed capacity of approximately **53MW**. The facility is designed to provide renewable energy to more than **35,000 households**.
- **October 2024**: The construction of the solar farm was completed, and the final capacity was reported as **51.4MW**. The facility consists of over **94,000 fixed solar modules**. BayWa r.e. will also be responsible for the operation and maintenance (O&M) of the solar farm.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2023**: BayWa r.e. secured a **€56 million** structured financing deal from Nord/LB for the Tarquinia solar project. This financing marked the first of BayWa r.e.'s solar projects in Italy to utilize structured financing.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2023**: Announcement of the project and financing agreement with Nord/LB. Construction was expected to begin in the following months.
- **October 2024**: Completion of construction at the Tarquinia solar park was announced, with the facility now operational.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2024**: The project is expected to create jobs related to the operation and maintenance of the solar farm, although specific job creation numbers were not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- BayWa r.e. is the **exclusive owner** of the Tarquinia facility, acting as the general contractor and developer, responsible for all phases of the project from planning to construction and ongoing operations.
