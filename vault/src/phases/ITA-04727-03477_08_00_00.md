#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2024**: SolarDuck, in collaboration with Green Arrow Capital and New Developments, announced the development of a hybrid offshore project in the Gulf of Taranto. The facility will feature a total capacity of 540 MW, comprising 120 MWp of floating photovoltaic (PV) solar panels and 420 MW of floating wind turbines. The project will utilize SolarDuck’s elevated platform technology, designed to withstand significant wave heights and ensure safe access for maintenance.
