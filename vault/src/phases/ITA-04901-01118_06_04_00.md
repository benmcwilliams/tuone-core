## ITA-04901-01118

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Limes 15, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Production Capacity**: The Limes 15 facility will have a production capacity of 36 MW.
- **Future Expansion**: An additional 60 MW could potentially be added to the Fenix project, which is part of Iberdrola's broader initiative in Italy, bringing the total capacity to 305 MW.
- **Technology**: The facility will utilize bifacial solar panels and is expected to generate around 400 GWh of electricity annually, enough to power over 140,000 families.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Details**: Iberdrola has signed an agreement with IB Vogt for the construction of the Limes 15 facility, which is part of a larger investment in solar energy projects in Italy, including the Fenix project.
- **Total Capacity Under Construction**: In 2024, Iberdrola will have a total of 330 MW under construction across various projects in Italy, including Limes 10 and Limes 15.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Construction Start Date**: Construction for Limes 15 is scheduled to begin in the first half of 2024.
- **Operational Timeline**: The Fenix project, which includes Limes 15, is expected to be operational by 2025.
- **Related Projects**: Alongside Limes 15, the Limes 10 facility (18 MW) will also begin construction in the same timeframe.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The construction phase of the Limes 15 facility is expected to create approximately 500 jobs. Once operational, it will provide more than 100 stable jobs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: Iberdrola is the sole owner of the Limes 15 facility, as indicated by their direct involvement in the construction and operation of the solar plants in Italy.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and future plans for the Iberdrola facility in Limes 15, Italy, highlighting its role in the company's renewable energy strategy in the region.
