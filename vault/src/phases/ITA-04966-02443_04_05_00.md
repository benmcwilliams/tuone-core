### Evolutionary Timeline of the Snam Facility in Reggio Emilia, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: October 2021
   - **Details**: Snam, in collaboration with Iris Ceramica, announced the development of the world’s first hydrogen-powered ceramic factory in Reggio Emilia. The facility will utilize green hydrogen produced from solar energy, with a photovoltaic system of 2.5MW installed on the roof. The operations will initially use a blend of green hydrogen and natural gas, with plans to transition to 100% hydrogen in the future.

2. **Investment and Development**:
