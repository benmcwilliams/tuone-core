### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iris Ceramica Facility in Reggio Emilia, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** Iris Ceramica, in collaboration with Snam, announced the development of the world’s first hydrogen-powered ceramic factory. The facility will utilize green hydrogen produced from solar energy to power its operations. A photovoltaic system with a capacity of 2.5MW will be installed on the roof, along with an electrolyser and a renewable hydrogen storage system for onsite production. The facility aims to transition from a blend of green hydrogen and natural gas to running entirely on 100% hydrogen, significantly reducing carbon emissions.
