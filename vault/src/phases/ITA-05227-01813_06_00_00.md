### Evolutionary Timeline of the Neoen Facility in Lombardy, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03/2024**: Neoen SA announced the construction of two solar projects in Lombardy with capacities of 8 MWp and 7.6 MWp, contributing to a total of 24.7 MWp across three solar projects in Italy. These projects are Neoen's first in the Italian photovoltaic sector and are expected to generate enough electricity to supply over 9,000 homes annually. The commercial operations are planned to start at the beginning of 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **03/2024**: The construction of the solar projects is part of Neoen's strategy to develop a portfolio of solar and energy storage projects in Italy, leveraging government-designated areas for accelerated project development. The company aims to secure power purchase agreements (PPAs) for a significant portion of the electricity generated.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03/2024**: Construction has officially begun on the two solar farms in Lombardy and one in the Marche region. The commissioning of these facilities is scheduled for early 2025. The projects were developed in under 24 months, showcasing a rapid execution timeline.

#### 4. Labour
- **03/2024**: While specific job creation numbers were not mentioned, the construction of the solar projects is expected to contribute to local employment through the engagement of Intec Energy Solutions as the engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) contractor.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **03/2024**: Neoen will maintain full ownership of the solar assets once they become operational, distinguishing its business model of developing, owning, and operating its projects in the long term.
