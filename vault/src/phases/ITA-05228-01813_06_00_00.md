### Evolutionary Timeline of the Neoen Facility in Marche, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03/2024**: Neoen SA announced the construction of a solar plant in Marche with a generation capacity of **9.1 MWp**. This facility is part of a trio of solar projects in Italy, totaling **24.7 MWp** across three locations (two in Lombardy and one in Marche). The solar plants are expected to begin commercial operations in **early 2025**. Once operational, they will generate enough electricity to supply over **9,000 homes annually** and offset approximately **37,000 tonnes of CO₂** emissions each year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **03/2024**: The construction of the solar facility in Marche is part of Neoen's broader strategy to develop solar and energy storage projects in Italy. The company has been active in the Italian market since **2021** and has been leveraging government-designated areas for accelerated project development. The projects are expected to secure power purchase agreements (PPAs) for a significant portion of the generated electricity.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03/2024**: Neoen officially began construction on the solar plant in Marche, marking it as their first solar project in the Italian photovoltaic sector. The commissioning of the facility is scheduled for **early 2025**.

#### 4. Labour
- **03/2024**: While specific job creation numbers were not detailed in the articles, the construction of the solar facility is expected to contribute to local employment opportunities through the engagement of contractors like Intec Energy Solutions, which has secured a turnkey construction contract for the projects.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **03/2024**: Neoen will maintain **full ownership** of the solar facility in Marche once it becomes operational, aligning with their business model of developing and owning their projects for long-term operation.
