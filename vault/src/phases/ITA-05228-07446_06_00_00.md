### Evolutionary Timeline of Jiangsu Zongyi Co. Ltd Facility in Marche, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2012
   - **Details:** Jiangsu Zongyi Co. Ltd built and connected a 13.5MW rooftop solar array in Marche, Italy. This facility is one of Italy’s largest integrated photovoltaic plants, covering an area of approximately 200,000 square meters. The facility produces enough electricity to power around 4,500 households. The company’s total connected PV capacity in Italy reached 22MW with this addition, and there is an additional 7MW capacity planned for future development.

2. **Investment and Development:**

   - **Details:** The project was valued at €55 million, with assistance from UK law firm Watson, Farley & Williams in acquiring the project.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
