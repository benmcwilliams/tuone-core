## ITA-05444-00643

### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in San Severo, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2023**: RWE commissioned the San Severo wind farm, which has a production capacity of 54 MW, consisting of 12 turbines, each with a capacity of 4.5 MW. This facility is designed to supply green electricity to up to 55,000 Italian households.
- **Future Plans**: RWE is also constructing the Mondonuovo wind farm, which will have a capacity of 53 MW, scheduled for commissioning in mid-2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2023**: RWE announced a final investment decision (FID) for the San Severo project, with a total investment volume of approximately €180 million for both San Severo and the Mondonuovo wind farms.
- **Community Investment**: RWE initiated a community investment program for the San Severo project, allowing local residents to invest between €250 and €5,000, totaling €200,000, with a guaranteed return of up to 9% gross per annum.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2023**: RWE confirmed that both the San Severo and Mondonuovo projects had received building permits and construction was planned to start in autumn 2023.
- **December 2023**: The San Severo wind farm was officially commissioned after a year of construction.
- **April 2024**: Construction of the Mondonuovo wind farm began, with a planned commissioning in 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2024**: During the construction phase of the Mondonuovo wind farm, an average of 150 RWE employees and external service providers were involved, indicating job creation associated with the construction activities.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- RWE operates the San Severo facility as a wholly owned project, with no indication of joint ventures or shared ownership mentioned in the articles.
