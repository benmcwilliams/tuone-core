### Evolutionary Timeline of the Renantis Facility in Terzo di Aquileia, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: Renantis announced the construction of a 32.6 MW agrivoltaic plant in Terzo di Aquileia, which will combine clean energy production with agricultural activities, including beekeeping and the cultivation of native crops. The plant is expected to produce around 50 GWh of energy annually, sufficient for over 16,500 households. The facility will utilize advanced agricultural technology to enhance sustainability in beekeeping.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2023**: A crowdfunding campaign was launched to finance part of the agrivoltaic project. The campaign allows local residents to invest between €200 and €10,000, with an expected annual return of 6.5% for participants.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2023**: Construction work for the Terzo di Aquileia project is set to begin. The project will cover a 52-hectare site, with 45 hectares dedicated to renewable energy production and agricultural activities.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2023**: The project is expected to create job opportunities related to the construction and operation of the agrivoltaic plant, although specific job creation numbers were not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2023**: Renantis is the sole owner of the facility, as indicated by the details surrounding the crowdfunding campaign and the management of the project.
