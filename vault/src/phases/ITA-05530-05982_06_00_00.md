## ITA-05530-05982

### Evolutionary Timeline of Sangraf International Facility in Narni, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2024**: Sangraf International, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Sanergy Group, has ordered two Enapter AEM Nexus electrolysers with a total capacity of 1.5MW. The facility is focused on producing graphite electrodes for smelting processes in the steel, iron, and non-ferrous metals industries. Sangraf is currently building its own hydrogen production facility, which will be connected to a 2.6MWp photovoltaic system at its plant in Narni. The aim is to reduce the proportion of natural gas in its production processes by 10%.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2024**: The facility is part of Italy's National Recovery and Resilience Plan, which allocates around EUR 3.6 billion for the development of the hydrogen supply chain, with EUR 500 million specifically earmarked for the creation of Hydrogen Valleys, including the one in Narni.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2024**: Sangraf International is in the process of constructing its hydrogen production facility in Narni, which will utilize the ordered electrolysers and photovoltaic system.
