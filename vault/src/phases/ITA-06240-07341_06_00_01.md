## ITA-06240-07341

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enfinity Facility in Viterbo, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2023**: Enfinity Global announced a 10-year power purchase agreement (PPA) with Statkraft for a 112MW solar power portfolio in Viterbo, capable of producing 191GWh of electricity annually. The facility is expected to be operational in Q1 2024.
- **April 2023**: Enfinity Global signed an additional PPA with Statkraft, adding a 22MW solar facility to the existing portfolio, bringing the total capacity to 134MW and total energy production to 225GWh.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2022**: Enfinity Global closed project financing worth €142 million ($154.6 million) for the construction of the 112MW solar portfolio. This financing was provided in two tranches from Santander Corporate and Investment Banking and BayernLB, along with a third tranche from Nomura for a 40MW facility.
- **April 2023**: The new 22MW facility was financed through €22.6 million (US$24.8 million) in non-recourse debt financing, VAT financing, and letter of credit facilities from Santander Corporate and Investment Banking and BayernLB.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2023**: Announcement of the PPA for the 112MW solar portfolio and confirmation of its expected operational status in Q1 2024.
- **April 2023**: Announcement of the second PPA, which includes the addition of a 22MW facility, increasing the total capacity to 134MW.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify any job creation or job loss associated with the investments or expansions at the Viterbo facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Enfinity Global exclusively owns the facilities involved in the PPAs with Statkraft, as indicated by the statements regarding the development and ownership of the solar facilities.
