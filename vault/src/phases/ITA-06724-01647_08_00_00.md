### Evolutionary Timeline of the Falck Renewables Facility Offshore from Sardinia, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **May 2022:** Falck Renewables, in partnership with BlueFloat Energy, announced the development of the Tibula Energia project, a floating wind farm offshore from Sardinia. The facility will consist of 62 floating wind turbines with a total production capacity of 975 MW, generating more than 3.4 TWh of electricity annually. The project aims to offset over 1.6 million tonnes of carbon dioxide emissions each year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **May 2022:** The companies applied for a maritime concession for the Tibula Energia project, indicating the initiation of the investment and development phase. Specific investment amounts were not disclosed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **May 2022:** The announcement of the Tibula Energia project was made, with plans to engage local communities and begin the authorization process with Italy’s Ministry of the Ecological Transition. The project is in the exploratory phase, focusing on local sea and wind conditions, environmental impacts, and industrial infrastructures.

4. **Labour:**
   - **May 2022:** The Tibula Energia project is expected to create nearly 3,200 full-time jobs during its manufacturing, assembly, and construction stages. Once operational, the facility will employ more than 180 people for plant maintenance.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **May 2022:** The facility is a joint venture between Falck Renewables and BlueFloat Energy, indicating that it is not exclusively owned by Falck Renewables.
