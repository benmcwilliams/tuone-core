### Evolutionary Timeline of the BlueFloat Energy Facility Offshore from Sardinia, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2022
   - **Details:** The facility, known as the Tibula Energia project, will consist of 62 floating wind turbines with a total production capacity of 975 MW. It is expected to generate more than 3.4 TWh of electricity annually and will significantly reduce carbon dioxide emissions by offsetting over 1.6 million tonnes each year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** May 2022
   - **Details:** Falck Renewables and BlueFloat Energy applied for a maritime concession for the Tibula Energia project on May 6, 2022. The companies have been conducting exploratory research into local conditions and environmental impacts, indicating a commitment to sustainable development and community engagement.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** May 2022
   - **Details:** The project is in the initial stages, with plans to discuss the project with local communities and begin the authorization process with Italy’s Ministry of the Ecological Transition. The partnership between Falck Renewables and BlueFloat Energy was established in October of the previous year to develop floating wind farms offshore from Italy.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** May 2022
   - **Details:** The Tibula Energia project is expected to create nearly 3,200 full-time jobs during its manufacturing, assembly, and construction phases. Once operational, the facility will employ over 180 individuals for maintenance.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** May 2022
   - **Details:** The facility is a joint venture between Falck Renewables and BlueFloat Energy, indicating that neither company has exclusive ownership of the Tibula Energia project.
