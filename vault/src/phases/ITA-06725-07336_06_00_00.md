### Evolutionary Timeline of Cero Generation Facility in Pontinia, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2022**: Cero Generation achieved financial close for the 70MW Pontinia solar farm, which is one of the first large-scale agrivoltaic solar projects in Italy. The facility is designed to maximize performance and efficiency using single-axis trackers, bifacial modules, and string inverters. Approximately 65% of the 135 hectares will also be used for agricultural crops.
- **November 2023**: The Pontinia facility began operations, producing clean energy for the grid. The facility is backed by two long-term virtual power purchase agreements (PPAs) with Heineken (64GWh/y) and Philips (16GWh/y).

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2022**: The project reached financial close, marking it as one of the largest grid-parity solar projects in Italy, indicating a significant investment in subsidy-free solar PV projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2022**: Construction commenced on the Pontinia solar farm.
- **Q1 2023**: The facility was expected to reach commercial operation.
- **November 2023**: The facility officially began operations.

#### 4. Labour
- The collaboration with a social farm, Fattoria Solidale del Circeo, is aimed at providing work placements for vulnerable or socially excluded individuals, teaching them biodynamic and fuel-efficient farming methods, thus supporting their reintegration into the community.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Cero Generation operates the Pontinia facility as an independent portfolio company of Macquarie’s Green Investment Group (GIG), indicating that Cero has full ownership of the facility. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones of the Cero Generation facility in Pontinia, Italy, highlighting its innovative approach to agrivoltaics and its commitment to sustainability and social responsibility.
