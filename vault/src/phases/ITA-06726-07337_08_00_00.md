### Evolutionary Timeline of the Copenhagen Offshore Partners Facility in Civitavecchia, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is part of the **Ichnusa Wind Power Project**, which is a **504MW floating wind project** planned approximately **35km off the coast of Sardinia**. 
   - It is designed to support local supply chain development by the second half of the decade and is part of a larger portfolio of almost **3GW** of floating wind projects in Italy, which includes a **500MW** site in Latium off the coast of Civitavecchia.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The project is being developed by **Copenhagen Offshore Partners (COP)** on behalf of the project’s owners, which include **Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners** and **GreenIT** (a joint venture owned 51% by **Plenitude (Eni)** and 49% by **CDP Equity**). 
   - The project contributes to the Italian government's target of **20GW of operational wind by 2030**.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - On **12-06-2023**, COP submitted the **Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)** for the Ichnusa Wind Power Project to the Italian Ministry of Environment and Energy Security. This marks the project as Italy’s third floating wind project to reach EIA submission and the first off the Sardinian coast.

4. **Labour:**
   - The project is expected to support local supply chain development, which may lead to job creation in the region, although specific job creation numbers have not been detailed in the article.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is not exclusively owned by COP; it is part of a joint venture involving **Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners** and **GreenIT**.
