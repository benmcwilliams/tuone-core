### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enel Facility at Leonardo da Vinci Airport, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **June 2023**: Enel X has been awarded a tender by Aeroporti di Roma (ADR) to construct a 22MW solar plant at Leonardo da Vinci Airport. This facility will cover an area of 340,000 square meters and is designed to meet the airport's self-consumption energy requirements. The solar energy plant will be complemented by a 10MWh storage system utilizing second-life electric vehicle batteries, part of the Pioneer project aimed at managing energy demand peaks.
