### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sogin Facility in Campania, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2023:** Sogin has begun dismantling the pressure vessel of the Garigliano boiling water reactor, which is noted as the most complex dismantling activity in the decommissioning process. The facility, originally a 150 MWe boiling water reactor, was connected to the grid in January 1964 and was shut down in 1982. The removal of the vessel head allows for subsequent dismantling operations to be conducted underwater, providing a natural radiation shield.
