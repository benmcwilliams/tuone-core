### Evolutionary Timeline of the Tirreno Power Facility in Campania, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Tirreno Power facility produces solar energy through a photovoltaic (PV) plant with a total capacity of 6.9 MW. 
   - The solar farm was connected in two phases: the first half was connected in December 2010, and the second half followed in February 2011. The first phase received a feed-in tariff rate of €0.346/kWh, while the second phase received a rate of €0.297/kWh.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - In January 2014, the solar investment vehicle ForVEI acquired the 6.9 MW PV plant from Tirreno Power. This acquisition was part of ForVEI's strategy to enhance its portfolio of solar PV assets, which was reported to exceed 60 MW post-acquisition.
   - ForVEI announced a doubling of its commitment to the Italian solar sector to €75 million (US$102.6 million) in 2014, aiming to grow its portfolio to over 100 MW of solar assets.
