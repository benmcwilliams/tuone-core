### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hope Group Facility in Barium Bay, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2023:** The Barium Bay facility is set to produce more than three billion kilowatt-hours of clean energy through a 1.1GW floating wind farm. The facility will consist of 74 wind turbines and is located 40km from the shore in the Adriatic Sea, stretching from Barium to the north of Barletta.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **February 2023:** The facility is a joint venture (JV) between European renewable energy companies Galileo and Hope Group, indicating a collaborative investment in the development of the offshore wind farm.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **February 2023:** The Barium Bay JV is in the process of completing environmental and technical studies and plans to submit an Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) application later in the year. Additionally, a 24-month wind measurement campaign at sea is set to begin in partnership with Eolos Floating Lidar Solutions.

4. **Labour:**
   - **February 2023:** The project is expected to create new job opportunities for the local community, contributing to the development of new capabilities for the energy transition.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **February 2023:** The facility is a joint venture (JV) between Hope Group and Galileo, indicating that it is not exclusively owned by either company but rather shared between them.
