### Evolutionary Timeline of the Renexia Facility in Trapani, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2024**: Renexia signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with MingYang to establish a production site for 18.8MW turbines for the Med Wind floating wind farm project. The agreement aims to create a floating wind industrial chain, with the factory designed to produce turbines that will reduce the number of units needed from 190 to 148, thereby lowering environmental impact and maintenance costs.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2024**: The MoU includes a significant investment of €500 million to establish the new production site. This investment is part of a broader effort to support the National Integrated Energy and Climate Plan and the Mattei Plan for Africa.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2024**: The agreement marks a crucial milestone in the development of the Med Wind project, with plans to start installations in the first section of the wind farm by the first half of 2026. The new company formed by Renexia and MingYang is tasked with identifying a suitable area for the production site within 90 days.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2024**: The establishment of the new production site is expected to create approximately 1,100 jobs, contributing significantly to local employment in the Trapani area.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **August 2024**: The facility will be operated by a new company established through a partnership between Renexia and MingYang. This indicates a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by Renexia. The partnership aims to involve domestic companies and foster investment from both local and international players.
