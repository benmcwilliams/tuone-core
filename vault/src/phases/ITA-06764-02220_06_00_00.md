#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03-07-2018**: Eni announces the construction of a **26 MW solar plant** at its industrial site in Assemini, Sardinia. The solar park is designed to cover approximately **70% of the energy needs** of the nearby salt production site operated by Conti Vecchi.
