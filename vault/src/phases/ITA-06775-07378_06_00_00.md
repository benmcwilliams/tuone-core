### Evolutionary Timeline of the AES Solar Energy Facility in Cellino San Marco, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2010
   - **Details:** The facility is a 43MW solar photovoltaic (PV) project located in Puglia, Italy. It is noted as the largest solar PV project in Italy to reach financial close. The facility will utilize First Solar thin-film panels for its installation.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** April 2010
   - **Details:** AES Solar Energy secured €173 million in long-term project financing to fund the construction of the Cellino San Marco project. This financing covers approximately 85% of the estimated project costs at completion and involves five banks: Société Générale, Unicredit Mediocredito Centrale, BNP Paribas, Crédit Agricole Corporate and Investment Bank, and Dexia Crediop.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** April 2010
   - **Details:** Construction on the Cellino San Marco project has already begun, with an expected completion date by the end of 2010. If completed on time, the project will qualify for a 20-year regulated feed-in tariff under the ‘Conto Energia’ scheme.
