## ITA-06780-07466

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Monsud SpA Facility in Rotondella, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Production**: The facility is involved in the cementation of approximately 3 cubic meters of liquid uranium-thorium solution, referred to as the "final product," resulting from experimental reprocessing operations.
- **Storage Capacity**: The facility includes a temporary storage building for approximately 170 items resulting from the cementation process and casks containing 64 fuel elements awaiting transfer to a national repository.
- **Construction Details**: The ICPF project involves the construction of two structures: a process building for cementing activities and an annexed temporary storage facility, which is already completed.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Contract Award**: On June 20, 2023, a contract was awarded to a consortium comprising Ansaldo Nucleare and Monsud SpA for the construction of the ICPF facility, following a tender launched on December 30, 2022.
- **Historical Development**: Preliminary activities for the plant began in 2004, including the design and qualification of the cementitious matrix for final products.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **2004**: Preliminary activities initiated for the implementation of the plant.
- **2007-2010**: Creation of a mock-up prototype of the cementation cell for product qualification and training.
- **2014-2017**: Excavation works and construction of a barrier to support surrounding soil, along with foundation work for the interim storage facility.
- **2017**: Termination of the agreement with the winning contractor due to default.
- **2023**: Announcement of the contract award and the expected increase in decommissioning activities by more than 4%, with an overall progress of 35%.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The entry into operation of the plant is expected to raise safety levels and significantly increase decommissioning activities, which may lead to job creation, although specific numbers are not provided in the article.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: The facility is part of a consortium involving Monsud SpA and Ansaldo Nucleare, indicating a joint venture structure for the project.
