   - **November 2024**: De Nora, in partnership with Maffei Sarda Silicati, announced the construction of a 50-tonne hydrogen plant in Ossi. The facility will utilize De Nora’s 1MW Dragonfly® pressurised alkaline electrolyser to produce hydrogen, which will be powered by a 1.5MW photovoltaic plant. The hydrogen produced will be used to partially replace fossil fuels in Maffei Sarda Silicati's industrial processes, particularly in the drying of sands.
