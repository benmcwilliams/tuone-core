1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2011
   - **Details:** The facility produces solar energy through a solar park with a capacity of 7.56 MW. It utilizes 32,202 Sanyo HIT solar modules installed on Ideematec safetrack tracking systems. The installation was completed in December 2010, and the plant was connected to the grid in April 2011.
