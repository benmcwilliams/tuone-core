### Evolutionary Timeline of the Edison Facility in Pescopagano, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2024
   - **Details:** Edison, in partnership with Webuild, is focusing on two pumped storage projects in Pescopagano, which are part of a broader strategy to increase its installed green capacity from 2GW to 5GW by 2030. The projects will enhance the storage of excess renewable energy, ensuring grid stability and reducing waste. They will involve the creation of new reservoirs and improvements to existing ones.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2024
   - **Details:** The partnership aims to attract significant private investment estimated at €1.2 billion for the pumped storage projects in southern Italy, which includes Pescopagano. This investment is expected to benefit the local economy and the Italian civil and manufacturing sectors.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2024
   - **Details:** The pumped storage projects are currently pending approval from the Italian Environment and Energy Security Ministry and regional authorities. Plans are in place to participate in upcoming tenders organized by Terna for new storage capacity.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** July 2024
   - **Details:** While specific job creation figures are not mentioned, the projects are expected to create new jobs and improve existing employment opportunities in the local economy as a result of the significant investment and development activities.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** July 2024
   - **Details:** The facility is a result of a partnership between Edison and Webuild, indicating a joint venture structure for the pumped storage projects in Pescopagano.
