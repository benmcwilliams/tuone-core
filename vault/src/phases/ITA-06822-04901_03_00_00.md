### Evolutionary Timeline of the Edison Facility in Villarosa, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2024**: Edison, in partnership with Webuild, announced plans to develop pumped storage projects in Villarosa, Sicily. These projects are part of Edison's strategy to increase its installed green capacity from 2GW to 5GW by 2030. The pumped storage facilities will store excess renewable energy, enhancing the stability of the electrical grid and reducing energy waste. The projects will involve creating new reservoirs and improving existing ones to increase their capacity and safety.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2024**: The partnership between Edison and Webuild aims to attract significant private investment in southern Italy, estimated at €1.2 billion. This investment is intended to benefit the local economy and the Italian civil and manufacturing sectors, contributing to the overall development of the hydroelectric supply chain in Italy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2024**: The pumped storage projects in Villarosa are pending approval from the Italian Environment and Energy Security Ministry and regional authorities. The companies plan to participate in upcoming tenders organized by Terna for new storage capacity.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2024**: The development of the pumped storage projects is expected to create new jobs and improve existing employment opportunities in the local area, although specific job creation numbers were not detailed in the announcement.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2024**: The facility in Villarosa is part of a joint venture between Edison and Webuild, indicating that it is not solely owned by Edison but rather a collaborative effort between the two companies.
