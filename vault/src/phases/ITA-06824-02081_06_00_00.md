### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enel Facility in Adrano, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2011**: Enel Green Power announced the construction of a new 9MW photovoltaic (PV) power plant at the historic site in Adrano, where the first concentrated solar power (CSP) system, Eurelios, was established in 1981. The new PV plant is expected to generate over 14 million kilowatt-hours annually. The decommissioned components of the Eurelios system will be sent to the Museum of Industry and Work in Brescia.
- **August 2011**: The 9MW Adrano PV plant was completed and commissioned, contributing to a total of 14.3MW added to Enel's solar portfolio across three new plants in Italy. The Adrano plant is noted for its historical significance as the site of Enel's first solar energy project.

#### 2. Investment and Development
