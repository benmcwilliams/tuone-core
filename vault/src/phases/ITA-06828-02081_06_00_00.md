### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enel Facility in Borgo San Lazzaro, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 03-07-2013
   - **Details:** Enel Green Power (EGP) completed a photovoltaic (PV) plant in Borgo San Lazzaro, part of a project that included another plant in Spineto, Campania. The total installed capacity of the two plants is 21 MW, with the Borgo San Lazzaro plant contributing to this capacity. The plants are expected to generate up to 30 million kWh per year, sufficient to power approximately 15,000 households. The PV plants utilize thin-film panels manufactured at 3Sun’s factory in Catania.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 03-07-2013
   - **Details:** The land for the PV plants was assigned to EGP in November 2011 by the Italian Ministry of Defence, utilizing two lots totaling 190 hectares of Italian Army land.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 03-07-2013
   - **Details:** The completion of the Borgo San Lazzaro PV plant is part of a broader expansion of EGP's solar energy capabilities in the Salerno Province. This facility joins EGP’s first solar plant in Salerno, which was built in 1993 and had its capacity doubled in 2011.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
