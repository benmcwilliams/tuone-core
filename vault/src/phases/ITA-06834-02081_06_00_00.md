### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enel Facility in Enea-Portici Experimental Area, Italy

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2021**: Enel Green Power, in partnership with the Italian research agency ENEA, initiated a pilot project at the Enea-Portici experimental area. The facility aims to integrate photovoltaic (PV) electricity production with microalgae cultivation. The PV plant will have a production capacity of approximately **7 kW**, utilizing both mono and bifacial panels. The microalgae cultivated will include high-value species such as Acutodesmus obliquus, Nannochloropsis gaditana, and Haematococcus pluvialis.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2021**: The project is part of Enel's broader investment strategy in the agrivoltaic sector, aligning with Italy’s National Recovery and Resilience Plan. Specific financial details regarding investments or subsidies for this pilot project were not disclosed in the article.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2021**: The pilot project was announced and is currently being deployed at the Enea-Portici experimental area. The primary goal is to evaluate the interactions between solar energy generation and microalgae yield.
