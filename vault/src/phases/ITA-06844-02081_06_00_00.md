### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enel Facility in Spineto, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 03-07-2013
   - **Details**: The Enel facility in Spineto, part of a network expansion in Salerno Province, produces solar energy through a photovoltaic (PV) plant. The total installed capacity of the two new PV plants, including Spineto, is 21 MW. The plants are expected to generate up to 30 million kWh per year, sufficient to power approximately 15,000 households. The PV panels used in the construction are thin-film panels manufactured at the 3Sun factory in Catania.

   - **Details**: The two PV plants, including the one in Spineto, were built on land assigned to Enel Green Power (EGP) by the Italian Ministry of Defence. The land was previously owned by the Italian Army and totals 190 hectares.
