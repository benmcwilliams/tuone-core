### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enel Facility in Valbondione, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2024:** Enel announced the integration of a 4 MW/8 MWh lithium-ion Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) with the existing 43.4 MW Dossi pumped storage hydroelectric power plant. This project, known as BESS4Hydro, aims to enhance the efficiency and flexibility of hydroelectric energy storage by allowing the battery to act as a secondary reservoir, storing electricity and injecting it into the grid.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The BESS4Hydro project is backed by the European Union’s Innovation Fund, indicating financial support for the integration of the battery storage system with the pumped storage plant.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2024:** Enel announced the retrofit of the battery storage at the Dossi pumped storage hydroelectric power plant, marking a significant milestone in the facility's evolution.
