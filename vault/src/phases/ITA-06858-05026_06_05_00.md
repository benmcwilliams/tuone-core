### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fimer Facility in Arezzo, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - Fimer specializes in the production of inverters for solar systems. The facility in Arezzo is part of Fimer's operations, which includes two inverter factories in Italy and one in India.
   - In September 2022, Fimer announced an industrial relaunch after entering composition-with-creditors proceedings, indicating a focus on revitalizing its production capabilities.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The acquisition of Fimer by McLaren Applied Group through its subsidiary MA Solar Italy Ltd. was announced on November 30, 2024. This acquisition is expected to enhance technological synergies and operational efficiency, although specific investment amounts were not disclosed.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - In February 2023, Fimer filed for creditor protection at the tribunal in Arezzo, which was part of its restructuring efforts.
   - The acquisition by McLaren Applied was authorized by the Ministry of Business and Made in Italy (MIMIT) and is seen as a significant turning point for the company, allowing it to continue its core business and expand operations.

4. **Labour:**
   - The acquisition is expected to help maintain the workforce at the Arezzo facility. However, in July 2024, it was reported that 55 of the 65 employees at a different site near Milan were collectively dismissed, indicating challenges in job retention across the company.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - As of November 30, 2024, Fimer is no longer independently owned; it is now owned by McLaren Applied Group through its subsidiary MA Solar Italy Ltd. This acquisition marks a shift in ownership from Fimer's previous independent status to being part of a larger corporate structure.
