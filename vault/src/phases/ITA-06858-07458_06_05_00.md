### Evolutionary Timeline of McLaren Applied Group Facility in Arezzo, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Acquisition of Fimer:** On **November 30, 2024**, McLaren Applied Group, through its subsidiary MA Solar Italy Ltd., acquired Italian inverter manufacturer Fimer. This acquisition includes Fimer’s business assets and aims to expand McLaren's core operations, particularly in inverter design and production for solar systems. The facility in Arezzo is expected to maintain its workforce and continue its core business operations.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Acquisition Details:** The acquisition of Fimer by MA Solar Italy Ltd. is a strategic investment aimed at fostering technological synergies and operational efficiency. The financial details of the acquisition were not disclosed, but it is part of a broader plan to support business continuity and expand activities in new sectors.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Acquisition Announcement:** The acquisition of Fimer was officially announced on **November 30, 2024**. This marked a significant milestone in the restructuring process of Fimer, which had been undergoing creditor protection proceedings since February 2023.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Workforce Maintenance:** The acquisition is expected to help maintain the workforce at the Arezzo inverter production site, especially after the collective dismissal of 55 out of 65 employees at another site near Milan in July 2024. This indicates a focus on job preservation at the Arezzo facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Ownership by McLaren Applied Group:** The facility in Arezzo is now under the ownership of MA Solar Italy Ltd., a subsidiary of McLaren Applied Group. This acquisition indicates that McLaren has full ownership of the facility following the purchase of Fimer's assets.
