### Evolutionary Timeline of M+W Zander Italia S.r. Facility in Rieti, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2007
   - **Details:** M+W Zander Italia S.r. was commissioned by SOLSONICA S.p.A. to retrofit an existing IC memory module assembly plant into a photovoltaic (PV) cell manufacturing facility. The facility will produce polycrystalline silicon solar cells, with an initial production area of 3,500 square meters and an additional 2,000 square meters for service. The initial production capacity is set at 30 MWp/year, with future plans to expand to 120 MWp/year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2007
   - **Details:** The project involves significant upgrades to the facility, including enhancements to gas and chemical distribution systems, electrical systems, compressed air distribution, and a demineralized water distribution plant. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** January 2008
   - **Details:** Tool move-in and start of commissioning are scheduled for January 2008, with production planned to commence in mid-2008. This marks the first PV project undertaken by M+W Zander in Italy.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** Not specified
   - **Details:** The article does not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with this facility's establishment or expansion.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** November 2007
   - **Details:** The facility is not owned exclusively by M+W Zander; it is a retrofit of a plant owned by EEMS S.p.A., with SOLSONICA S.p.A. as the operator for the PV cell manufacturing.
