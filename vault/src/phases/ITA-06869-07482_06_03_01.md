### Evolutionary Timeline of SOLSONICA S.p.A. Facility in Rieti, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2007
   - **Details:** SOLSONICA S.p.A. is converting part of an existing IC memory module assembly plant into a PV cell manufacturing plant. The facility will produce polycrystalline silicon solar cells with an initial production capacity of 30 MWp/year, planned to expand to 120 MWp/year in the future. The production area will cover 3,500 square meters, with an additional 2,000 square meters designated for service.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2007
   - **Details:** The facility's conversion is being managed by M+W Zander Italia S.r.l., which is tasked with retrofitting the plant. The project involves collaboration with Air Product Italia S.r.l. and Sapio Produzione Idrogeno e Ossigeno S.r.l. to upgrade essential systems for the new operations.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2007
