### Summary
The RWE facility in Morcone, Italy, is an advanced Agri-PV plant with a planned capacity of 9.8 MWac. RWE has secured investment through the Italian Resilience and Recovery Plan and plans to commence construction in spring 2025. The facility is fully owned by RWE, contributing to their broader strategy in the Italian renewable energy market.
