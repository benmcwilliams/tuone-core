### Evolutionary Timeline of the S.A.G. Solarstrom Facility in Canaro, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **July 2010:** S.A.G. Solarstrom announced the construction of a 48MWp ground-mounted photovoltaic power plant in Canaro, Veneto, Italy. This facility is the largest project planned by S.A.G. Solarstrom to date. The plant will cover an area of 150 hectares and will include the construction of a transformer substation to connect the system directly to the high-voltage grid.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **July 2010:** The project will utilize financial resources from the refinancing of completed projects. Specific investment amounts were not detailed in the article, but the project is expected to contribute to S.A.G.'s sales forecast of €190 to €210 million for 2010.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **July 2010:** All necessary approvals for the construction of the power plant have been obtained, allowing work to commence. The project is scheduled for implementation in 2010 and 2011.
