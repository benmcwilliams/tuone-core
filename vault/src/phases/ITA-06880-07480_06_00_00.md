### Evolutionary Timeline of the SAG Solarstrom Facility in Serenissima, ITA

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **04/2012:** SAG Solarstrom's Serenissima facility is a 48MW photovoltaic (PV) plant located in Northern Italy. It features 200,000 ground-mounted solar modules capable of producing over 64 million kWh of electricity annually. This facility is noted as the largest PV installation built by SAG Solarstrom to date.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **04/2012:** The financing for the Serenissima project included an €80 million commitment from Deutsche Bank, which was crucial during the financial crisis. Additionally, a €118 million loan agreement was part of the sale contract signed on December 31, 2011.

   - **03/2012:** The sale of the 48MW Serenissima PV plant was nearing completion, although it faced delays due to high administrative efforts. The completion was expected shortly after the publication of the articles.
