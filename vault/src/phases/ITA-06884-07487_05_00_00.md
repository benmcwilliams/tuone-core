1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **June 2024:** The facility is in the process of constructing the DT2 storage facility, which will measure 70 metres in length, 18 metres in width, and 13 metres in height. This facility will house approximately 1800 cubic metres of low and medium-level radioactive waste, specifically from the dismantling activities of the former nuclear power plant. The dismantling work of the reactor vessel systems and components began in December 2023. The DT2 facility will include an operational handling area, a service building, and a storage area equipped with an overhead crane for remote handling of waste containers.
