### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sogin DT2 Facility, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **July 2024:** Preparatory activities for the dismantling of the Garigliano Reactor Vessel are underway. The facility is focused on the decommissioning of the Garigliano nuclear power plant, specifically producing interim storage solutions for low and medium-level radioactive waste generated from the dismantling activities. The DT2 facility, once operational, will have a capacity to house approximately 1,800 cubic meters of waste. The facility measures 70 meters in length, 18 meters in width, and 13 meters in height.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **July 2024:** Sogin has completed the laying of the foundation slab for the DT2 facility, indicating ongoing investment in the infrastructure necessary for the interim storage of radioactive waste.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **July 2024:** The removal of the vessel head from the Garigliano reactor is in progress, with components being cut and stored for future transfer to a national repository. The executive design for the removal of the internals of the vessel has already started. The DT2 facility is expected to be commissioned in the first half of 2026.
