### Evolutionary Timeline of the Winaico Facility in Giave, Italy

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **August 2012:** Winaico completed a 16MWp photovoltaic (PV) project in Giave, covering approximately 30 hectares. The facility is designed to generate 26,252,226 kWh of solar power annually. The PV system utilizes 67,704 Winaico WSP-235P6 modules installed atop greenhouses.
