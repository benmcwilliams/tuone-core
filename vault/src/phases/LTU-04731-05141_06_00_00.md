### Evolutionary Timeline of the Green Genius Facility in Seduva, Lithuania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2023**: Green Genius announced plans to construct a solar power plant in Seduva, Lithuania, with a capacity of **78 MW**. The facility will cover an area of **140 hectares** and is expected to produce **245 GWh** of electricity annually, sufficient to power approximately **82,000 households**. The plant will also contribute to a reduction of around **102,000 tons of CO2** emissions each year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2023**: Green Genius confirmed an investment of **€70 million** for the construction of the Seduva solar power plant. This investment is part of a larger **€179 million** investment into two solar projects in the Baltic states, which includes a second project in Latvia.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2023**: Green Genius secured permits for the construction of the Seduva solar power plant. The construction is scheduled to begin by **mid-2024**, with the facility expected to start operations in **2025**.
