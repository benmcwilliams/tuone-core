### Evolutionary Timeline of the PASSER Group Facility in Klaipeda, Lithuania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2023**: The PASSER Group's new fabrication facility in Klaipeda officially went into operation. The facility is designed to produce cable manufacturing and handling equipment, including a vertical lay-up machine (VLM) for JDR Cable Systems' subsea cable manufacturing facility in Blyth, UK. The facility has expanded its production capacity from 2,000 square meters to approximately 7,000 square meters to accommodate the growing demand in the subsea cable market, particularly for offshore wind and interconnector projects.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2024**: The facility is part of PASSER Group's strategy to enhance its production capabilities in response to the significant increase in offshore wind and renewable energy projects. The investment in the new workshop emphasizes energy efficiency, with plans for solar panel installation and advanced filter systems to ensure a 'green' operation.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones

#### 5. Ownership Structure
