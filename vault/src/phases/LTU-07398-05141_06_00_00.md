### Evolutionary Timeline of the Green Genius Facility in Utena, Lithuania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2023**: Green Genius entered into a power purchase agreement (PPA) with Svyturys-Utenos Alus (SUA) to develop, construct, commission, and operate two solar-plus-storage systems. The first system, a 1.5MW solar power plant with a 2MWh battery storage, is to be installed at the SUA brewery in Utena by **May 2024**. The second system, a 5MW off-site utility-scale solar PV plant with a 4MWh battery storage, will be developed near Butrimonys and is expected to start operations by **January 2025**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2023**: The total value of the InnoSolveGreen project is €7.5 million, with €2.6 million funded by the European Union Emissions Trading System – Innovation Fund. Green Genius is responsible for the remaining funding.
- **October 2023**: Green Genius secured an additional €6.9 million from Citadele Bank to fund the solar power plant on the brewery's roof, as part of the energy-as-a-service solution.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2023**: Announcement of the PPA and the InnoSolveGreen project.
- **May 2024**: Expected installation completion date for the 1.5MW solar power plant at the SUA brewery in Utena.
- **January 2025**: Expected operational start date for the 5MW off-site solar PV plant near Butrimonys.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or job loss figures related to the investments or expansions at the Utena facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Green Genius operates the facility under a power purchase agreement with Svyturys-Utenos Alus, which is part of the Carlsberg Group. The ownership structure indicates that Green Genius does not own the facility exclusively but is involved in a partnership arrangement for the development and operation of the solar-plus-storage systems.
