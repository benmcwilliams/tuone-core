### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lietuvos Energija Facility in Kruonis, LTU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2019**: Lietuvos Energija announced the deployment of a floating solar plant at the Kruonis pumped storage hydroelectric power plant. The initial capacity of the floating solar plant is set at **60 kW**, with potential expansion up to **250 MW** due to the availability of approximately **300 hectares** of water surface for the project. This facility will provide reliable frequency control regulation services to the hydropower plant.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2019**: The Lithuanian Business Support Agency provided additional funding of **€235,000** for the floating PV project at the Kruonis facility. This funding is part of the development efforts in collaboration with the Kaunas University of Technology.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2019**: Announcement of the floating solar project at the Kruonis facility, with expectations for completion by **2021**. This project is noted as the first of its kind in Lithuania.
