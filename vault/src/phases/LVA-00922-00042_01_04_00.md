### Evolutionary Timeline of Anodox Energy Systems Facility in Riga, Latvia

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2022:** Anodox Energy Systems announced plans to build two battery plants in Latvia. The first facility will focus on assembling battery packs for electric vehicles, while the second plant will produce LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) high-performance cells, marking it as the first of its kind in Europe. The Riga facility aims to complete the battery production cycle from raw materials to finished products.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **March 2022:** A total investment of 50 million euros was announced for the establishment of the two plants in Latvia. This investment is tied to the construction of the assembly plant in Riga and the subsequent cell production facility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 2022:** Announcement of the construction of the battery assembly plant in Riga, with plans for it to go into operation by December 2022. The timeline for the second plant's production start was not specified.

4. **Labour:**
