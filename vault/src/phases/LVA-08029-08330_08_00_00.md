### Evolutionary Timeline of the Augstsprieguma Tikls Facility in Gulf of Riga, LVA

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **April 2021:** Augstsprieguma Tikls (AST) is involved in the planning of the Elwind project, which is an offshore wind farm with a potential capacity of up to 1GW. The facility will be responsible for the transmission infrastructure necessary to connect the wind farm to the onshore grid. The project is expected to be completed by 2030.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **April 2021:** The Elwind project may be eligible for EU co-financing from the Connecting Europe Facility of Renewable Energy Structural Funds, contingent on further studies and conditions to be determined after 2022.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **April 2021:** AST and Elering (the Estonian transmission system operator) announced their collaboration to examine options for connecting the Elwind offshore wind farm. Preliminary cable routes have been identified, and detailed studies are planned to be completed by spring 2022.
