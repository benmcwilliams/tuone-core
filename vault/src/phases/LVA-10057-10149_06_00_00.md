- **01-11-2024**: Sunly, an Estonian independent power producer, began construction on three utility-scale solar PV parks in Latvia, including an 81MW solar park in the Madona region. This facility is part of a larger portfolio with a combined capacity of 225MW across the Valmiera, Krāslava, and Madona regions. The Madona facility is an expansion of an existing 11MW solar facility.
