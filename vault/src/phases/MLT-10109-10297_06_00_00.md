### Evolutionary Timeline of the University of Malta Facility in Malta's National Waters, MLT

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: January 2025
   - **Details**: The University of Malta, in collaboration with Tiangong University, proposed the development of an offshore mooring and power platform (OMPP) that integrates a virtual power plant (VPP). The facility is designed to operate through a 200 MW floating wind farm and a 300 MW floating PV plant, utilizing battery and compressed air systems for energy storage. The OMPP will feature a 24 MVA platform-to-ship (P2S) facility with four berths, each providing up to 6 MVA, and is designed to accommodate up to four ships, each up to 200 m long.
