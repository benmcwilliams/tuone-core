#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2024**: Stellantis Group and Leapmotor finalize their joint venture, Leapmotor International, which will commence operations in September 2024. The facility will focus on the production and sale of electric vehicles (EVs), specifically the T03 and C10 models, with plans to introduce at least one new model each year for the next three years. The joint venture aims to sell 500,000 electric cars outside China by 2030.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2023**: Stellantis announces a significant investment of 1.5 billion euros to acquire a 20% stake in Leapmotor, aimed at boosting sales in both China and Europe. This investment is part of the establishment of the joint venture, which will have exclusive rights to export, sell, and manufacture Leapmotor products outside Greater China.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2024**: The joint venture, Leapmotor International, is set to begin operations in September 2024. The management team will be based in Amsterdam, led by CEO Tianshu Xin. The joint venture will establish 200 sales outlets by the end of 2024, expanding to 500 by 2026.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2024**: The establishment of the joint venture and the planned expansion of sales outlets are expected to create job opportunities, although specific job creation numbers have not been disclosed.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2024**: The joint venture, Leapmotor International, is structured as a partnership where Stellantis holds a 51% stake and Leapmotor holds a 49% stake, indicating a joint venture ownership model rather than exclusive ownership by Stellantis.
