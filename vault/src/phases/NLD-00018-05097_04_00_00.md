### Evolutionary Timeline of the GasLog Facility in Amsterdam, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2023**: GasLog is involved in a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with ACWA Power, Zenith Energy Terminals, and the Port of Amsterdam to study a green hydrogen export corridor. The focus is on developing liquid green hydrogen transportation from ACWA’s production sites to Amsterdam. GasLog is working on vessels capable of carrying liquid hydrogen.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2023**: The Dutch government has allocated 9 billion euros for green hydrogen initiatives up to 2030, with 300 million euros specifically reserved for imports. This funding supports the feasibility study for the green hydrogen corridor involving GasLog.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2023**: The MoU signed during the Saudi-EU Investment Forum marks the beginning of a feasibility study for the liquid green hydrogen corridor. Depending on the study's results, a joint development agreement (JDA) may follow.
- **April 2024**: The Port of Amsterdam signs two new agreements to develop a green hydrogen corridor between Amsterdam and the port of Bilbao, Spain. One agreement involves a joint study agreement (JSA) with GasLog, Petronor, and EnBW to evaluate the feasibility of a liquid hydrogen corridor.
