### Rable Group Facility in Delft, NLD - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2023**: Rable Group unveiled the RABLE2go system, a 600 W two-module residential rooftop PV system. This system features two 350 W Canadian Solar panels and a Hoymiles HMS600 inverter, allowing for quick installation (20 minutes) without special permitting or modifications. The system is designed for lightweight applications, weighing 55 kg and measuring 270 cm x 173 cm x 34 cm. It is marketed as a solution for households facing long wait times for professional solar installers and is sold on a pre-order basis to prevent overproduction.
