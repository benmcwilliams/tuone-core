### Evolutionary Timeline of the Solliance Facility in Delft, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2017**: Solliance improved perovskite roll-to-roll (R2R) processing, achieving new conversion efficiency records. The facility produced small solar cells with a maximum stabilized efficiency of 13.5% and demonstrated the capability to produce various module sizes with high yield. The R2R process was noted for its reproducibility, which is crucial for future manufacturing reliability.
- **September 2022**: The Solliance consortium achieved a record efficiency of 30.1% for a semi-transparent perovskite solar cell combined with a crystalline silicon solar cell in a four-terminal tandem configuration. This marked a significant increase from previous efficiencies, showcasing advancements in the technology and its potential for mass production.
