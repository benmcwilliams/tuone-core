### Evolutionary Timeline of the Solarge Facility in Eindhoven, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-09-2021**: Solarge developed a 500 W solar panel, the Solarge DUO, which is 50% lighter than conventional glass-backsheet modules. The panel is designed for flat rooftops with limited weight-bearing capacity and features a unique mounting system that eliminates the need for ballast. The facility in Eindhoven is currently producing these modules through a pilot line.
- **13-12-2023**: Solarge completed a 225 kW rooftop system using lightweight modules made from strong polymers, suitable for commercial buildings with limited load-bearing capacity. The modules weigh 5.5 kg/m² and are part of the Solo line, ranging from 480 W to 490 W.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **15-09-2021**: Dutch construction company Vorm invested in the new solar module factory being developed by Solarge in Eindhoven. The factory is planned to have a production capacity of 300 MW and is set to become operational in March 2022.
