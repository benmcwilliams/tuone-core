### Evolutionary Timeline of Belectric Facility in Emmen, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2018**: Belectric officially inaugurated the "Zonnepark Lange Runde," a 14 MW solar power plant in Emmen, Drenthe. The facility consists of approximately 118,000 solar modules covering 17 hectares, generating enough electricity to power around 4,000 households annually. This project utilized a 1,500V high voltage DC cabling system, marking its first use in the Benelux region.
