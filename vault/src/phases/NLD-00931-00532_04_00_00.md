### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mitsubishi Facility in Rotterdam, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03-08-2021**: Mitsubishi Corporation, in collaboration with Chiyoda Corporation, initiated a feasibility study for the commercial-scale import of hydrogen from overseas sources to a terminal in the port of Rotterdam. The project aims to utilize Chiyoda's Spera Hydrogen technology, which involves the transportation and storage of hydrogen using Methylcyclohexane (MCH). The goal is to import 100 to 200 kilotons per annum (ktpa) of hydrogen by 2025 and 300 to 400 ktpa by 2030.
- **23-06-2023**: Mitsubishi established Eneco Diamond Hydrogen in Rotterdam to focus on developing green hydrogen and associated renewable energy projects in Europe.
