### Evolutionary Timeline of zepp.solutions Facility in Rotterdam, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2023**: zepp.solutions announces the construction of a new factory near Rotterdam dedicated to producing Y50 and X150 fuel cell modules. The facility aims to increase production capacity to 1,000 fuel cell systems per year. The fuel cell technology is designed to replace diesel engines, contributing to a reduction of CO2 emissions by 111 to 332 metric tons per system annually.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2023**: The facility receives a total investment of €3 million from the Rotterdam Energy Transition Fund and a private investor. Additionally, it secures a €2 million grant from the Ministries of Economic Affairs and Climate (EZK) and Social Affairs and Employment (SZW), along with support from local municipalities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2023**: The announcement of the factory construction is made, with plans to produce fuel cell systems for various applications, including the Rotterdam water taxi and excavators from Liebherr. The first two trucks featuring zepp’s fuel cell systems (X150) are scheduled to be operational later in the year.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2023**: The establishment of the new facility is expected to create approximately 100 sustainable jobs in the hydrogen sector within the Greater Rijnmond region.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **August 2023**: zepp.solutions is identified as the owner of the facility, with no mention of joint ventures or shared ownership in the articles. The company, founded in 2017 as a spinoff from TU Delft’s hydrogen racing team, operates independently in this venture.
