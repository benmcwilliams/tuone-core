### Evolutionary Timeline of the Honeywell Facility in Rotterdam, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2024**: The Honeywell facility in Rotterdam is set to utilize Honeywell UOP’s eFining™ methanol-to-jet technology to produce electrofuels (eFuels). The facility will have a production capacity of over **250,000 tonnes per year** of sustainable aviation fuel (eSAF), which is a non-fossil, synthetic fuel made from green hydrogen and biogenic carbon. The eFuels will be produced by combining locally produced green hydrogen and imported methanol.
