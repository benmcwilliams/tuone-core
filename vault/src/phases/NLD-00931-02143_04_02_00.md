### Evolutionary Timeline of the Air Products Facility in Rotterdam, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10-05-2022**: Air Products, in collaboration with Schenk Tanktransport and TNO, announced plans to develop hydrogen trucks and a public hydrogen refueling station in the Port of Rotterdam. This project aims to create the largest hydrogen refueling station in the Netherlands, initially supplying three zero-emission trucks.
- **07-11-2023**: Air Products announced plans to build Europe’s largest blue hydrogen production plant in Rotterdam, which is expected to be operational by 2026. This facility will produce blue hydrogen using natural gas combined with carbon capture technology, serving ExxonMobil's Rotterdam refinery and other customers through Air Products' hydrogen pipeline network.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-11-2023**: The blue hydrogen plant is part of a larger investment strategy, with Air Products planning to build, own, and operate a carbon capture and CO2 treatment facility at its existing hydrogen production plant in Rotterdam. The facility will be connected to the Porthos system for CO2 transport and storage.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10-05-2022**: Announcement of the collaboration for the hydrogen refueling station project.
- **07-11-2023**: Announcement of the plans for the blue hydrogen production plant, expected to be operational by 2026.

#### 4. Labour
- **07-11-2023**: While specific job creation numbers were not mentioned, the establishment of the blue hydrogen plant and associated facilities is expected to create jobs in the region, particularly in the construction and operational phases.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **07-11-2023**: Air Products will build, own, and operate the new blue hydrogen production plant, indicating full ownership of this facility.
