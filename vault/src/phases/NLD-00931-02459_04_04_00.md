### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sunfire Facility in Rotterdam, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-07-2022**: Sunfire is installing a 2.6 MW high-temperature electrolyzer at Neste’s renewable products refinery in Rotterdam. This electrolyzer is based on Sunfire’s Solid Oxide Electrolysis Cell (SOEC) technology, which operates at temperatures of 850 °C and is designed to produce more than 60 kg of green hydrogen per hour. The facility will ultimately consist of 12 electrolysis modules, making it the world’s largest high-temperature electrolyzer installed in an industrial environment.
- **17-04-2023**: Sunfire achieves mechanical completion of the electrolysis system at the Neste refinery. The MultiPLHY project, which includes the installation of the electrolyzer, is entering its commissioning phase. The system is expected to achieve an electrical efficiency of up to 85% AC to LHV H2 and will be integrated into Neste’s refinery processes for the production of high-quality biofuels.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **17-04-2023**: The MultiPLHY project is funded by the EU Clean Hydrogen Partnership, indicating significant investment in the development of this facility and its technology.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
