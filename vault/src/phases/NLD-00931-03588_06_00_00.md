### Evolutionary Timeline of the TNO Facility in Rotterdam, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: November 2021
   - **Details**: TNO installed a pilot thin-film floating solar system at a lake near Rotterdam as part of the Solar@Sea II project. The system consists of two floats measuring 7×13 meters, topped with 20kWp of flexible solar modules. The design aims to reduce resistance to waves, allowing for lighter and cheaper production compared to traditional rigid floats. The project is set to run until summer 2022, with future plans to develop a commercial floating solar system of 1-5MWp by around 2024, intended to be colocated with North Sea wind parks.
