### Evolutionary Timeline of the Shell Nederland Facility in Rotterdam, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 07-05-2020
   - **Details:** Shell Nederland, in collaboration with Eneco through their joint venture CrossWind, is planning to develop a green hydrogen plant in the Port of Rotterdam. The facility aims to produce green hydrogen using renewable electricity from the Hollandse Kust Noord offshore wind farm, which has a capacity of 759MW and is expected to be operational by 2023. The hydrogen plant is projected to have a capacity of 200MW and aims to produce 50,000-60,000 kg of hydrogen per day. The hydrogen produced will be used to decarbonize fuel production at the Shell refinery in Pernis.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 07-05-2020
   - **Details:** Both Shell and Eneco have issued guarantees for investments in the construction and operation of the Hollandse Kust Noord offshore wind farm. However, the final investment decision for the hydrogen plant has not yet been made.
