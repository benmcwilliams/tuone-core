### Evolutionary Timeline of the HyCC Facility in Rotterdam, Netherlands

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** February 2022
   - **Details:** The H2-Fifty project is a planned 250MW green hydrogen plant located in the port area of Rotterdam. It aims to produce green hydrogen to replace fossil-based feedstock used at BP’s Rotterdam refinery and other industries in the port area. The project has the potential to displace up to 350,000 tons of carbon emissions annually.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** February 2022
   - **Details:** The project is a joint development agreement between BP and HyCC, following a feasibility study that indicated its potential contribution to decarbonizing the region's industry. The project is supported by the Topsector Energy (TSE), a Dutch government initiative, and has been nominated for the Important Projects of Common European Interest (IPCEI) Hydrogen programme.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** February 2022
   - **Details:** The companies plan to select a technology supplier within the year, further develop the plant design, and begin environmental studies for the licensing process. A final investment decision for the plant is expected to be made in the following year.
