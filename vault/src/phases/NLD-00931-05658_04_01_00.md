### Neste Facility in Rotterdam, NLD - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: Sunfire achieved mechanical completion of a 2.6 MW high-temperature electrolyzer at Neste's Rotterdam refinery as part of the MultiPLHY project. This electrolyzer will generate green hydrogen for the production of high-quality biofuels, with a production capacity of over 60 kg of green hydrogen per hour. The technology used is based on Solid Oxide Electrolysis Cell (SOEC) technology, which operates at 850 °C and utilizes industrial excess heat for high conversion efficiency.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2022**: Neste announced a $1.9 billion expansion at its Rotterdam facility aimed at increasing the production of sustainable aviation fuel. This investment is part of Neste's broader strategy to enhance its renewable energy capabilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023**: The MultiPLHY hydrogen project at the Rotterdam facility entered its commissioning phase after the installation of twelve electrolysis modules, marking a significant milestone in integrating green hydrogen production into the refinery's operations.
