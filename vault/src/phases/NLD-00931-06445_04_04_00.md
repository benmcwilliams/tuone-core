### Evolutionary Timeline of Battolyser Systems Facility in Rotterdam, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2023**: Battolyser Systems has recently started production of its patented dual battery-electrolysis system in the Rotterdam area. The facility currently produces systems with a capacity of a couple of megawatts. The company aims to manufacture 50 MW systems by 2024 and 200 MW systems by 2025. The technology utilizes nickel-iron electrodes combined with alkaline electrolysis, achieving up to 85% system efficiency and up to 90% at the stack level.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2023**: Battolyser Systems signed a €40 million financing agreement with the European Investment Bank (EIB) during World Hydrogen Week. This funding is intended to scale the production facility in Rotterdam towards mass production of its combined electricity storage and electrolyzer stack system. The company is also preparing for another investment round within the next 18 months.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2023**: Battolyser Systems announced plans to develop a second production facility in the Rotterdam area, which is expected to be commissioned in 2026. This facility will be co-owned by the Rotterdam port authority and will add 1 GW of production capacity.
