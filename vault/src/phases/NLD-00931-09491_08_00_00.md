## NLD-00931-09491

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mooreast Facility in Rotterdam, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: Mooreast specializes in total mooring solutions, particularly for the offshore renewable energy sector, including floating offshore wind farms. The facility in Rotterdam operates as Mooreast Europe, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Mooreast Holdings Ltd.
- **Production Capacity**: The facility is part of Mooreast's broader strategy to enhance its production capabilities, particularly in response to the growing demand for floating offshore wind energy solutions.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Announcements**: On 19 June 2024, Mooreast announced plans to acquire a 98,919 sqm facility from a subsidiary of Seatrium Limited, which will quadruple its production capacity in Singapore. This investment is part of a broader strategy to support the fast-growing floating offshore renewable sector.
- **Funding**: On 13 June 2024, Mooreast signed a non-binding term sheet to secure a S$20.01 million convertible loan from SG-RT FUND to provide essential working capital for FY2024 and support its expansion and growth strategies.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Recent Developments**: On 02 April 2024, Mooreast secured an anchor order for a floating offshore wind project in Southern France, marking its 15th offshore wind-related project since 2013. The anchors are expected to be delivered by October 2024.
- **Future Plans**: The company is actively discussing opportunities for subsea foundation production and establishing a manufacturing facility in Aberdeen, Scotland, alongside its regional marketing office in the Netherlands.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The expansion and establishment of new facilities, particularly in Taiwan and Singapore, are expected to create job opportunities as Mooreast enhances its operational capacity to meet the demands of the offshore renewable energy sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: Mooreast Europe operates as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Mooreast Holdings Ltd., indicating full ownership of the facility in Rotterdam.
