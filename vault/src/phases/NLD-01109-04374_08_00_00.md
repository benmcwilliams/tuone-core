### Evolutionary Timeline of the Worley Facility in The Hague, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2021**: Worley is awarded a services contract by Shell to support the development of a new 200 MW electrolysis-based hydrogen plant in Rotterdam, Netherlands. This facility is expected to be one of the largest commercial green hydrogen production facilities globally, with operations scheduled to start by 2023. The plant is estimated to produce between 50,000 to 60,000 kilograms of green hydrogen per day. The hydrogen produced will be used to decarbonize Shell’s nearby refinery in Pernis and support the heavy transportation industry.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2021**: The project is part of the NortH2 initiative, which integrates renewable energy from the Hollandse Kust Noord offshore wind farm, currently under development by Shell and the CrossWind consortium. The wind farm is expected to generate at least 3.3 TWh per year and will power the electrolysis plant.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2021**: Announcement of Worley’s involvement in the project and the timeline for operations to commence in 2023. The Hollandse Kust Noord wind farm is also scheduled to be operational in 2023.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
