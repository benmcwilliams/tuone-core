### Evolutionary Timeline of the McDermott Facility in The Hague, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2022**: McDermott, in consortium with GEIRI/C-EPRI, is awarded a contract for the BorWin6 offshore grid connection project. The facility will be responsible for engineering, procurement, construction, installation, and commissioning of an HVDC offshore converter platform and an onshore converter station. The offshore converter platform will connect 980 MW of offshore wind capacity to the German grid. The engineering and project management will be executed from McDermott’s HVDC centre of excellence in The Hague.
