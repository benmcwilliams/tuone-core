The initiative includes plans for approximately 15GW of offshore wind auctions annually, targeting a total of nearly 100GW by 2030. This initiative is designed to enhance predictability in the wind energy sector and improve cooperation among member countries.

- **November 2023**: The NSEC Action Agenda was agreed upon by the Energy Ministers of the NSEC, which outlines actions for grid development, market design, financing, and auction design.
