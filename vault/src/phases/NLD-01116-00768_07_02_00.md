### Evolutionary Timeline of the Tesla Facility in Tilburg, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2015**: Final assembly operations for Tesla Model S and Model X began in Tilburg, Netherlands. The facility includes a paint shop, parts warehouse, and a remanufacturing center for refurbishing old parts.
- **2018**: The Tilburg facility assembled 5,622 Model S and 2,963 Model X vehicles.
- **March 2021**: Tesla announced plans to close the assembly plant in Tilburg due to updates in the Model S and Model X production processes, which made assembly in the Netherlands "no longer possible on an industrial scale."

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones

#### 4. Labour
