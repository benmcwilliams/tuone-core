### ElectricBrands Facility in Born, NLD - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: ElectricBrands announced that it will have its light electric vehicles, the XBUS and Evetta, produced at the VDL Nedcar plant in Born, Netherlands. The production of the Evetta is planned to begin with a pilot series in early 2024, followed by the XBUS in late 2025. The XBUS will offer nine variants, including pick-up, van, bus, and camper, with a basic version featuring a 15 kWh battery providing a range of 200 km, expandable to 600 km with additional battery packs.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2023**: The partnership with VDL Nedcar as a contract manufacturer indicates a significant investment in the production capabilities for ElectricBrands' vehicles, although specific financial figures are not disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2023**: ElectricBrands confirmed the production of the Evetta and XBUS at VDL Nedcar, with the Evetta's pilot series production set for early 2024 and the XBUS to follow in late 2024 or early 2025.
- **September 2023**: ElectricBrands updated the timeline for the XBUS production start to late 2024 or early 2025, with first deliveries planned for 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2023**: The announcement of production at VDL Nedcar suggests potential job creation, leveraging the existing workforce of approximately 4,000 employees at VDL Nedcar for the production of ElectricBrands' vehicles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2023**: ElectricBrands does not own the facility exclusively; it is a contract manufacturing arrangement with VDL Nedcar, indicating a partnership rather than full ownership.
