### Evolutionary Timeline of the Shell Facility in Groningen, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2020:** The NortH2 project was launched, aiming to produce green hydrogen using electricity from offshore wind. The project plans to utilize about 1 GW of offshore wind by 2027, 4 GW by 2030, and over 10 GW by 2040. The infrastructure will include offshore wind farms, an electrolyser plant, pipelines for hydrogen delivery, and underground storage in salt caverns.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **February 2021:** The NortH2 consortium, which includes Shell, Gasunie, Equinor, and RWE, is in the Assess Phase, which will inform further decisions leading to a final investment decision planned for 2024. The project is supported by Groningen Seaports and the Province of Groningen.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **February 2020:** Launch of the NortH2 project by Shell and partners.
   - **December 2020:** Equinor and RWE joined the NortH2 consortium.
   - **January 2021:** A technical integration and optimization contract was awarded to Mott MacDonald to develop potential technical solutions for the project.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
