### Evolutionary Timeline of the Royal HaskoningDHV Facility in Groningen, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2021:** Royal HaskoningDHV was awarded a contract for environmental consultancy services for the NortH2 project, which aims to produce green hydrogen using electricity from offshore wind. The project plans to achieve a production capacity of 1 GW by 2027, 4 GW by 2030, and over 10 GW by 2040. The infrastructure will include offshore wind farms, an electrolyser plant, pipelines for hydrogen delivery, and underground storage in salt caverns.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **February 2020:** The NortH2 project was launched by a consortium including Shell, Groningen Seaports, Gasunie, and the province of Groningen, with further developments and investments expected as the project progresses towards its final investment decision planned for 2024.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **February 2020:** Launch of the NortH2 project.
   - **December 2020:** Equinor and RWE joined the NortH2 consortium.
   - **January 2021:** A technical integration and optimization contract was awarded to Mott MacDonald to assist in the project's development.
   - **February 2021:** Royal HaskoningDHV's involvement as a consultant for environmental and safety aspects was announced.
