   - **April 2022:** The facility is involved in testing a mobile agrivoltaic system called H2arvester. This system is designed to improve soil quality and biodiversity in agricultural fields. It consists of movable solar arrays that can cover up to 10% of an agricultural field without losing its agricultural purpose. The system can also be combined with an electrolyzer for hydrogen generation.
