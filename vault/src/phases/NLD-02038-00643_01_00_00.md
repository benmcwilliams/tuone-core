### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in Eemshaven, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2020**: RWE signs a power purchase agreement for a 54 MW wind farm (Waddenwind) near Eemshaven, with full commissioning planned for January 2022.
- **February 2021**: RWE announces two new onshore wind farms (Oostpolderdijk and Westereems 3) near Eemshaven, totaling 16.5 MW capacity, to be commissioned in 2021.
- **June 2022**: RWE acquires a 1.4 GW gas-fired power plant (Magnum) in Eemshaven, which is hydrogen-ready, enhancing the facility's capabilities.
- **September 2023**: RWE announces the construction of a 35 MW battery storage system at its biomass plant in Eemshaven, expected to supply control energy from 2025.
- **November 2024**: RWE secures permits to build a 100 MW electrolyzer in Eemshaven, contributing to the OranjeWind offshore wind project.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2022**: RWE's acquisition of the Magnum plant is valued at €500 million ($534 million), aimed at strengthening its hydrogen project cluster in Eemshaven.
- **September 2023**: RWE announces a significant investment in the battery storage project, which is part of its strategy to integrate renewable energy sources.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2020**: Announcement of the Waddenwind facility, with operations starting in early 2021.
- **January 2022**: Full commissioning of the Waddenwind facility.
- **February 2021**: Commissioning of the two new onshore wind farms planned for 2021.
- **September 2023**: Start of construction for the battery storage system.
- **November 2024**: RWE secures permits for the 100 MW electrolyzer, linked to the OranjeWind project.
- **December 2024**: RWE and TotalEnergies select Eemshaven as the construction base for the OranjeWind offshore wind project, with construction starting in early 2026.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2022**: The acquisition of the Magnum plant is expected to provide job security and development opportunities for existing employees.
- **December 2024**: The OranjeWind project is anticipated to create jobs during the construction phase, although specific numbers are not detailed.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2022**: RWE acquires the Magnum plant, indicating full ownership.
- **November 2024**: The electrolyzer project is part of RWE's broader strategy, indicating exclusive ownership of the facility's developments.
- **December 2024**: RWE collaborates with TotalEnergies for the OranjeWind project, indicating a joint venture for this specific offshore wind project while maintaining full ownership of other facilities in Eemshaven.
