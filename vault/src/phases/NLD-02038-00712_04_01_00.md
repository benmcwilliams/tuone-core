## NLD-02038-00712

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Shell Facility in Eemshaven, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2020**: Shell, in collaboration with Gasunie and Groningen Seaports, announced the NortH2 project, which aims to construct a purpose-built offshore wind farm to supply electricity for the industrial-scale production of green hydrogen. The project plans to produce green hydrogen using renewable electricity generated by a 3GW to 4GW offshore wind farm by 2030, scaling up to 10GW by 2040. The initial production capacity of green hydrogen is expected to be around 800,000 tonnes per year by 2040.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2020**: The NortH2 project anticipates requiring European and national subsidies for its initial phases, which will support the decarbonization of energy in the region. The project is expected to involve significant investments, although specific financial figures were not disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2020**: The NortH2 project was officially launched with a feasibility study expected to conclude by the end of 2020. The partners aim to install the first offshore wind turbines by 2027, with the first hydrogen production anticipated in the same year.
- **December 2020**: The NortH2 project was recognized as one of the world's largest green-hydrogen projects, with a planned output of one million tonnes of hydrogen per year by 2040. The project is currently in the feasibility study phase, which is expected to be completed by July 2021.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2020**: The NortH2 project is projected to create thousands of jobs in the northern Netherlands as it develops the green hydrogen industry, contributing to local economic growth and employment opportunities.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2020**: The NortH2 project is a consortium involving Shell, Gasunie, Groningen Seaports, and other partners. It is not solely owned by Shell but is a collaborative effort among multiple stakeholders, indicating a joint venture structure for the facility.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Shell facility in Eemshaven, focusing on its technology, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
