### Evolutionary Timeline of the MHI Vestas Facility in Eemshaven, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2019**: MHI Vestas establishes a new facility operated by DHSS in Eemshaven to serve as a hub for commissioning and operations and maintenance (O&M) for the 269MW Deutsche Bucht offshore wind farm. The facility includes a temperature-controlled warehouse, fluid-tight yard, and office facilities. This facility is strategically located to efficiently support the offshore wind farm project featuring 33 V164-8.4MW turbines.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2024**: RWE and TotalEnergies announce the use of Buss Terminal Eemshaven for the OranjeWind offshore wind farm, which will consist of 53 V236-15.0 MW turbines. The project is expected to generate 3 terawatt hours (TWh) of electricity annually, enough to supply over one million Dutch households. The investment details specific to this facility are not disclosed in the articles, but the scale of the project indicates significant financial backing.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2024**: RWE and TotalEnergies confirm that construction activities for the OranjeWind offshore wind project will commence in early 2026, with turbine installation scheduled to start in early 2027 and full commissioning expected by the end of 2027. The Buss Terminal will facilitate the offloading, storage, handling, and pre-assembly of major turbine components and inter-array cabling.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2024**: While specific job creation or loss figures are not mentioned, the announcement of the OranjeWind project implies potential job creation at the Buss Terminal Eemshaven due to the scale of operations required for the assembly and construction of the wind farm.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **March 2019**: MHI Vestas operates the facility in collaboration with DHSS, indicating a partnership rather than exclusive ownership. 
- **December 2024**: The Buss Terminal Eemshaven is utilized by RWE and TotalEnergies for the OranjeWind project, suggesting that the facility is part of a broader logistics and operational network rather than being owned exclusively by any single entity.

This timeline captures the key developments and operational milestones of the MHI Vestas facility in Eemshaven, highlighting its role in supporting significant offshore wind projects in the region.
