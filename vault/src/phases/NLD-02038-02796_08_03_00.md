- **Production**: The facility is involved in the assembly and construction of offshore wind turbines for the OranjeWind project, which will generate approximately 3 terawatt hours (TWh) of green electricity annually, enough to supply over one million Dutch households.

- **Updates**: The facility will handle the transportation and installation of inter-array cables and secondary steel components for the turbine foundations.
