### Evolutionary Timeline of BOW Terminal Facility in Eemshaven, Netherlands

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **October 2018:** BOW Terminal announces the establishment of a new logistics hub in Eemshaven, focusing on offshore wind activities. The facility will support four markets: heavy lift, decommissioning, oil & gas, and offshore wind. The terminal will be located along a 525-meter quay at the Wilhelmina Harbour, which will be upgraded to support a quay strength of 10 tons/m² and has a water depth of 16 meters, suitable for deepwater vessels.
   - **Operational Date:** The facility is expected to be operational by the second quarter of 2019.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **October 2018:** BOW Terminal has agreed to purchase 22 hectares of land in Eemshaven from Groningen Seaports, marking a significant investment in the expansion of its offshore wind activities.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **October 2017:** A Letter of Intent was signed between BOW Terminal and Groningen Seaports to establish the offshore wind hub.
   - **October 2018:** Official announcement of the expansion and purchase of land, with the contract signing scheduled for October 23, 2018, during the Offshore Energy Exhibition & Conference in Amsterdam.

4. **Labour:**
   - **October 2018:** The Managing Director of BOW Terminal, Ludolf Reijntjes, indicates that the new facility will provide opportunities for local companies and residents, suggesting potential job creation, although specific numbers are not provided.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **October 2018:** BOW Terminal is indicated to be the owner of the facility, with no mention of joint ventures or shared ownership in the articles.
