### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in OranjeWind, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2022**: RWE secured the development and construction rights for the OranjeWind offshore wind farm, which will have a production capacity of **760 MW**. The facility will incorporate **floating solar panels** and a **subsea lithium-ion battery** to enhance efficiency and support green hydrogen production.
- **September 2023**: RWE signed grid connection agreements with TenneT for the OranjeWind facility, confirming the project's capacity of **800 MW** and outlining the integration of innovative technologies such as a **LiDAR system** for accurate power production predictions. The facility is set to be located **53 km off the Dutch coast** and is expected to start producing renewable electricity in **2027**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2023**: RWE announced an investment in a **battery storage system** with an installed power capacity of **35 MW** and a storage capacity of **41 MWh**. This system will be constructed at RWE’s biomass plant in Eemshaven and is expected to support the OranjeWind project by supplying control energy starting in **2025**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2022**: RWE won the right to build and operate the OranjeWind offshore wind farm in the Dutch tender for the Hollandse Kust West (HKW) VII site.
- **September 2023**: RWE signed offshore grid connection agreements with TenneT, marking a significant milestone in the project and ensuring the transport of electricity generated by the wind farm. The agreements also outline the planning for grid stability and the delivery of clean power.
