   - **Date**: September 2024
   - **Details**: The facility has launched the largest heat pump in the Netherlands, located at the Utrecht sewage treatment plant. This heat pump generates heat up to 75°C for the local heating network by utilizing purified hot wastewater. It extracts residual heat from approximately 65 million liters of purified wastewater daily, which comes from household sources such as showers and dishwashers. The system is capable of providing sustainable heat for 20,000 homes annually.
