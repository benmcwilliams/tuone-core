### Evolutionary Timeline of the Virya Energy Facility in Terneuzen, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** Virya Energy, in collaboration with Vision Hydrogen, announced the development of a scalable green hydrogen production facility in Terneuzen. The facility will initially operate at a capacity of 26MW, producing up to 3.5 million kilograms of green hydrogen annually. When fully scaled to 75MW, the output could reach 10.5 million kilograms per year. The facility will utilize proven, commercially available electrolysis technology.
