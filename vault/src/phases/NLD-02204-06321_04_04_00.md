### Evolutionary Timeline of the Vision Hydrogen Facility in Terneuzen, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** Vision Hydrogen, in collaboration with Virya Energy, announced the development of a scalable green hydrogen production facility in Terneuzen. The facility will initially operate at a capacity of 26MW, producing up to 3.5 million kilograms of green hydrogen per year. When fully scaled to 75MW, the output could reach up to 10.5 million kilograms annually. The facility will utilize proven, commercially available electrolysis technology.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** The projected development costs for the initial 25MW phase of the project are estimated to be between €35 million ($39 million) and €45 million ($50 million).
