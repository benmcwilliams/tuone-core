## NLD-02209-02146

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Alliander Facility in Oosterwolde, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10-10-2020**: Green Hydrogen Systems (GHS) is contracted to supply three electrolysers (GHS HyProvide™ A90) with a combined capacity of 1.4 MW for a Power-to-X pilot project at Ecomunitypark in Oosterwolde. The facility will convert excess solar or wind energy into hydrogen, which will be stored and sold for various applications.
- **28-03-2022**: The construction of the Netherlands' first green hydrogen production facility is completed. This facility is connected to a 50 MW solar park operated by Groenleven and is expected to produce 100,000 kg of hydrogen annually, sufficient for approximately 10 million clean kilometers of car journeys. Hydrogen production is set to begin in June 2022.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **10-10-2020**: The project is described as a landmark pilot project, indicating significant investment in the development of green hydrogen technology and infrastructure in the region.
- **28-03-2022**: The facility is part of a broader initiative to address grid congestion issues in the northern Netherlands, suggesting that investments are also aimed at improving grid efficiency and capacity.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10-10-2020**: Announcement of the contract with GHS for the supply of electrolysers.
- **28-03-2022**: Completion of construction of the hydrogen production facility, with production expected to start in June 2022.
