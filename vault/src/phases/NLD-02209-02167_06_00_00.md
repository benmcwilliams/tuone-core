- **December 2018**: BayWa r.e. completed its first floating solar project, the 2.1MWp Weperpolder plant, in the Oosterwolde municipality.
- **October 2019**: BayWa r.e. announced the completion of the 14.5MWp Sekdoorn floating solar installation near Zwolle, which is part of its growing portfolio in the Netherlands. The facility utilizes self-designed floating platforms and glass-glass solar modules for enhanced durability.
- **October 2020**: BayWa r.e. and Alliander announced plans for a pilot project in Oosterwolde, consisting of a 50 MW solar park connected to a utility-scale electrolyzer. This project aims to test the integration of solar power with hydrogen production to alleviate grid constraints.
