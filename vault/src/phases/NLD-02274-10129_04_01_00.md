## NLD-02274-10129

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Strohm Facility in Offshore, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: April 2023
   - **Details**: The Strohm facility is planned to produce hydrogen and ammonia, linked to an adjacent wind farm by 2027. The facility will utilize seawater for the electrolysis process to produce green hydrogen, which can be transported through existing oil and gas pipelines or newly installed Thermoplastic Composite Pipe (TCP). The facility aims to address challenges in energy supply and water quality for electrolysis.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: April 2023
   - **Details**: The facility secured a grant of EUR 3 million from the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO) under the MOOI scheme for its development. This funding is part of the OFFSET project consortium, which includes SwitcH2, BW Offshore, and TU Delft.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: April 2023
   - **Details**: The project received Approval in Principle (AiP) from DNV, confirming the technical feasibility of the design. The project is currently in the basic design phase.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: April 2023
   - **Details**: The article does not specify job creation or job loss related to the facility at this time.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: April 2023
