### SwitcH2 Facility Evolution Timeline - Offshore, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: April 2023
   - **Details**: The SwitcH2 facility is planned to produce green hydrogen and ammonia. It will be linked to an adjacent wind farm by 2027. The facility aims to address challenges in large-scale production of green hydrogen, such as intermittent energy supply and high-quality water supply for electrolysis, by utilizing seawater as feed for the electrolysis process. The facility will employ Thermoplastic Composite Pipe (TCP) technology for hydrogen storage and transportation.

   - **Date**: April 2023
   - **Details**: The facility secured a grant of EUR 3 million from the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO) under the MOOI scheme for its development. This funding is part of the OFFSET project consortium, which includes SwitcH2 as the developer.
