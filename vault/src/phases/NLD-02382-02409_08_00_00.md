### Evolutionary Timeline of the PosHYdon Facility, North Sea, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: The PosHYdon facility is identified as the world's first offshore green hydrogen pilot on a working platform. It will utilize electricity generated by offshore wind turbines to power a hydrogen plant on the platform. The facility will convert seawater into demineralized water and perform electrolysis to produce green hydrogen. The production capacity is set at a maximum of 400 kg of green hydrogen per day using a 1 MW electrolyzer.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: The Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO) awarded a subsidy of €3.6 million to the PosHYdon project. This funding is intended to kickstart all activities for the pilot project, with the remaining budget to be funded by consortium partners.
