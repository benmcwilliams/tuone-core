## NLD-02382-07074

### NortH2 Facility Evolution Timeline (North Sea, NLD)

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2021**: The NortH2 project aims to produce green hydrogen using electricity from offshore wind. The initial target is to generate 1 GW of offshore wind energy by 2027, scaling up to 4 GW by 2030, and exceeding 10 GW by 2040. The facility will include offshore wind farms, an electrolyser plant, pipelines for hydrogen delivery, and underground storage in salt caverns.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2021**: The project is supported by a consortium including Shell, Gasunie, Equinor, and RWE, with backing from Groningen Seaports and the Province of Groningen. The consortium is in the Assess Phase, which will inform a final investment decision planned for 2024.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2020**: The NortH2 project was launched by Shell, Groningen Seaports, Gasunie, and the Province of Groningen.
- **December 2020**: Equinor and RWE joined the NortH2 consortium.
- **January 2021**: A technical integration and optimization contract was awarded to Mott MacDonald to evaluate technical solutions for the project.
- **December 2022**: Ramboll was selected to investigate the feasibility of producing hydrogen offshore at a multi-gigawatt scale, with results expected in January 2023.
