### Evolutionary Timeline of Sif Group Facility in Maasvlakte, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-10-2022**: Sif Group completed the first two of the 70 TP-less monopiles for the Hollandse Kust Noord offshore wind farm. The monopiles, weighing a combined 64,356 tonnes, are designed to eliminate the need for transition pieces, allowing for faster installation and cost reductions. These monopiles will support Siemens Gamesa’s SG 11.0-200 DD 11 MW wind turbines.

- **10-02-2023**: The final TP-less monopile was installed at the Hollandse Kust Noord offshore wind farm. Each monopile weighs between 788 tons and 960 tons, and the installation was described as an immense operation. The monopiles were manufactured at Sif’s Maasvlakte facility.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-10-2022**: Sif Group secured a contract with Van Oord for the fabrication and supply of monopiles for the Hollandse Kust Noord project, which is part of a larger offshore wind initiative without government subsidies.
