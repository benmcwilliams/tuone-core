   - **March 2019:** The Gigawatt Elektrolysefabriek project was launched, aiming to produce green hydrogen at a gigawatt scale from wind and solar parks in the Netherlands. The goal is to ramp up hydrogen production technology from a few megawatts to gigawatt capacity to supply sustainable hydrogen to Dutch industry.
