- **February 2017**: Nuon announced plans to add 70 MW of solar capacity at six wind farms across the Netherlands, including Haringvliet. The solar projects will consist of over 250,000 solar panels. The largest solar plant is planned for Wieringermeerpolder, where a wind farm is already under construction. The integration of solar and wind technologies aims to optimize infrastructure use and enhance energy generation efficiency.
