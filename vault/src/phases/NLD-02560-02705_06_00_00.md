### Evolutionary Timeline of the Blue21 Facility in Weurt, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2022**: The Blue21 facility is involved in the development of the Hide and Shine Floating Solar (HAS FPV) technology, which is designed to be highly resistant to storms and harsh weather conditions. The system allows solar modules to be submerged partially or fully, with the capability to submerge up to two meters during severe storms. The first pilot project will utilize 50 solar modules at a water reservoir in Weurt, aimed at researching and developing the system's behavior under various weather conditions.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2022**: The consortium, which includes Blue21, is building the HAS FPV system using existing market components. The project is expected to incur higher initial construction costs but aims to reduce long-term expenses related to weather insurance and maintenance.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2022**: The consortium plans to deploy the first pilot project at the water reservoir in Weurt in the coming weeks. This pilot is part of a broader research initiative that began in 2018, when Waterschap Rivierenland partnered with Blue21 and others to develop floating PV technology.
