### Evolutionary Timeline of the Delft University of Technology (TU Delft) Facility in Weurt, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2022**: The facility is involved in the development of the Hide and Shine Floating Solar (HAS FPV) technology, which is designed to be highly resistant to storms and harsh weather conditions. The system allows solar modules to be submerged partially or fully, with the capability to submerge up to two meters during severe storms. The first pilot project, utilizing 50 solar modules, is set to be deployed at a water reservoir in Weurt.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2022**: The development of the HAS FPV technology is part of a research project coordinated by Waterschap Rivierenland, involving a consortium of Dutch companies, including Easyfix Solar, EQAenergy, and Aveco de Bondt. The project aims to create a modular raft system that may incur higher initial construction costs but could lead to lower weather insurance and construction costs over time.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2022**: The consortium plans to deploy the first pilot project at the water reservoir in Weurt within a few weeks. This pilot aims to research and develop the system's balancing, floating, and control behavior under various weather conditions, particularly storm scenarios.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
