### Evolutionary Timeline of the Genap Facility in Arnhem, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-04-2022**: Genap introduced the Genap Energy Cover, which utilizes HyET Solar Powerfoil thin-film solar modules with an initial efficiency of 12.0%. The facility focuses on producing lightweight solar solutions for agricultural water storage and reservoirs, particularly targeting the greenhouse and horticulture markets in the Netherlands. A test setup with a capacity of 12kWp was demonstrated, with a generation density starting at 60W/m2, expected to rise to 120W/m2 within a year, and a long-term goal of 165W/m2. The facility aims to optimize the membrane coverage with PV to reach 90%.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-04-2022**: The Genap Energy Cover is noted to have a capital cost that is less than half of other floating PV solutions available, indicating a significant investment in developing a cost-effective solar solution.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
