### Haskel Facility in Arnhem, Netherlands - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: April 2021
   - **Details**: Haskel developed, installed, and deployed new hydrogen refuelling equipment at Total Nederland in Arnhem. The facility supports both passenger cars and heavy-duty vehicles, providing hydrogen at two pressure levels: 700 bar for cars and 350 bar for heavy-duty vehicles. The core technology implemented is the H-Drive hydraulic-drive gas booster, which enhances the efficiency and speed of refuelling operations.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: April 2021
   - **Details**: The development of the refuelling station is part of the H2Nodes project, which received a total contribution of €14.5 million ($19.9 million) from the Connecting European Facility (CEF). This investment aims to bolster hydrogen refuelling infrastructure in the Netherlands.
