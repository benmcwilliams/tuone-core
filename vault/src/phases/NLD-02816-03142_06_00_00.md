### Evolutionary Timeline of the LTO Noord Facility in Oude-Tonge, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2022**: The LTO Noord facility in Oude-Tonge is involved in the development of a mobile agrivoltaic system called H2arvester. This system consists of four solar cars hosting a total of 168 solar panels, designed to improve soil quality and biodiversity while allowing for agricultural production. The system is capable of moving slowly (10 meters per hour) and can be repositioned during harvesting. The project is currently in the pilot testing phase, with two prototypes being tested—one in Oude-Tonge and another in Lelystad.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2022**: The local farmer, Jacob Jan Dogterom, invested €166,000 in the mobile agrivoltaic system. Additionally, InnovationQuarter, the regional economic development agency for the Province of Zuid-Holland, subsidized approximately half of the project's costs. The project is part of the H2GO Program, which supports the integration of electricity and green hydrogen.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2022**: The H2arvester project was inaugurated by Netherlands' Agriculture Minister, Henk Staghouwer. The pilot project is set to operate for one year to assess its impact on agricultural yield and land use.
