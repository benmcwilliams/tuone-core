#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2022**: The facility is involved in the development of a mobile agrivoltaic system called H2arvester. This system consists of four solar cars hosting a total of 168 solar panels and an irrigation system. The mobile solar arrays are designed to improve soil quality and biodiversity while allowing agricultural production to continue. Each solar car measures 12×6 meters and can move at a speed of 10 meters per hour. The system is capable of covering up to 10% of an agricultural field without losing its agricultural purpose.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2022**: The local farmer, Jacob Jan Dogterom, invested €166,000 in the H2arvester system located in Oude-Tonge. Additionally, InnovationQuarter, the regional economic development agency for the Province of Zuid-Holland, subsidized around half of the project's costs. The project is part of the H2GO Program, which supports the integration of electricity and green hydrogen.
