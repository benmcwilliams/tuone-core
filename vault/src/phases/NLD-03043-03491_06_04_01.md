### Evolutionary Timeline of the Solarge Facility in Weert, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06-05-2022**: Solarge announces the installation of a 100 MW solar module production line, designed for lightweight, 100% recyclable solar modules without PFAS. Production is set to start by the end of 2022.
- **25-05-2023**: The facility officially opens its first large-scale production line capable of manufacturing 200,000 lightweight solar panels annually. The panels are designed for rooftops with limited load-bearing capacity and are made from premium lightweight materials.
- **25-04-2024**: Solarge plans to expand its production capacity to 300 MW at the existing facility and announces plans for a new 500 MW building-integrated PV (BIPV) module factory, aiming for a total capacity of 1 GW after 2026.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **25-04-2024**: Solarge raises €3 million in venture capital from new and existing investors to support the expansion of its production capacity in Weert.
- **02-08-2024**: The province of Limburg invests in Solarge, contributing to the company's plans for increasing production capacity and constructing a new facility. The specific amount of investment is not disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06-05-2022**: Announcement of the 100 MW production line installation.
- **25-05-2023**: Inauguration of the first large-scale production line for lightweight solar panels.
- **25-04-2024**: Announcement of plans to expand production capacity to 300 MW and the construction of a new 500 MW facility.
- **02-08-2024**: Confirmation of investment from the Limburgs Energie Fonds to support the expansion.

#### 4. Labour
- **25-04-2024**: The expansion plans and new facility construction are expected to create jobs, although specific job creation numbers are not mentioned in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **02-08-2024**: The Limburgs Energie Fonds becomes a co-shareholder in Solarge following its investment, indicating that Solarge is not exclusively owned by a single entity but has external investors involved.
