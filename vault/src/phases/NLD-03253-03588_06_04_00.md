1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: December 2021
   - **Details**: The TNO facility is developing a full-size, recyclable, back-contact solar module. This module is designed according to Design for Recycling (D4R) guidelines, which focus on the recyclability of products from the early stages of their life cycle. The technology involves a novel encapsulant that allows for sophisticated dismantling at the end of the module's life, replacing traditional shredding methods. The back-contact technology integrates cell interconnections and wiring on the back side of the panel, enhancing efficiency.
