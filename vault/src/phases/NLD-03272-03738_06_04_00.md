### Evolutionary Timeline of the Yingli Green Energy Facility in Vaassen, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: 12-06-2017
   - **Details**: Yingli Green Energy, in collaboration with Amtech Group’s Tempress Systems BV, opened one of Europe’s largest bifacial PV solar plants in Vaassen. The facility has a production capacity of close to 400 kW, utilizing 1,428 N-type silicon PANDA bifacial modules manufactured by Yingli. Each module has a nominal peak power ranging from 275Wp to 280Wp, with an expected annual energy production exceeding 400 MWh. The bifacial technology allows for energy generation from both sides of the panel, potentially yielding up to 30% more energy compared to standard monofacial modules.
