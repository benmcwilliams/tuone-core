### Evolutionary Timeline of the Amtech Group Facility in Vaassen, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: 12-06-2017
   - **Details**: The Amtech Group facility, in collaboration with Yingli Green Energy, opened one of Europe’s largest bifacial PV solar plants. The plant has a production capacity of close to 400 kW, utilizing 1,428 N-type silicon PANDA bifacial modules manufactured by Yingli. The expected annual energy production is over 400 MWh. The bifacial modules are designed to generate electricity from both sides, potentially yielding up to 30% more energy compared to standard monofacial modules.
