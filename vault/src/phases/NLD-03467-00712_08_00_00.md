### Evolutionary Timeline of the Shell Facility in Hollandse Kust Noord, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09-05-2020**: Shell and Eneco, through their joint venture CrossWind, bid for the Hollandse Kust Noord offshore wind farm, targeting a production capacity of 759 MW. The facility is expected to generate approximately 3.3 TWh of clean energy annually, sufficient to power over one million Dutch homes.
- **01-06-2021**: Van Oord is selected as the Balance of Plant contractor for the project, responsible for engineering, procurement, construction, and installation of the wind farm's infrastructure.
- **30-06-2022**: Rovco is contracted for subsea investigation works, including UXO (unexploded ordnance) identification, as part of the preparations for the wind farm's construction.
- **21-09-2022**: Vos Prodect Innovations is awarded a contract to supply cable hang-off systems for the wind farm, further detailing the project's infrastructure.
- **20-06-2023**: The first electricity is produced from the wind farm, marking the beginning of its operational phase. The facility is expected to reach full capacity by the end of 2023.
- **20-10-2023**: The final wind turbine is installed, completing the construction of the 69 Siemens Gamesa 11 MW turbines. The facility is on track to be fully operational by the end of 2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09-05-2020**: Both Shell and Eneco provide guarantees for the construction and operation investments of the Hollandse Kust Noord project through their joint venture, CrossWind.
- **20-06-2023**: The project is part of a broader initiative by the Netherlands to add around 1 GW of offshore wind capacity annually, contributing to a target of 21 GW by 2030.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09-05-2020**: Announcement of the bid for the Hollandse Kust Noord offshore wind farm.
- **01-06-2021**: Contract awarded to Van Oord for Balance of Plant activities.
- **30-06-2022**: Contract signed with Rovco for subsea investigation works.
- **21-09-2022**: Contract awarded to Vos Prodect Innovations for cable systems.
- **20-06-2023**: First electricity produced from the wind farm.
- **20-10-2023**: Completion of the installation of all wind turbines, with the project expected to be fully operational by the end of 2023.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The Hollandse Kust Noord facility is developed and owned by CrossWind, a joint venture between Shell and Eneco. This indicates that Shell does not own the facility exclusively but shares ownership with Eneco.
