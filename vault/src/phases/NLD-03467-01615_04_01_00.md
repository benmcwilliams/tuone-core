## NLD-03467-01615

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Eneco Facility at Hollandse Kust Noord, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09-05-2020**: Eneco and Shell, through their joint venture CrossWind, bid for the Hollandse Kust Noord offshore wind farm, targeting a production capacity of 759 MW, expected to generate nearly 3.3 TWh of clean energy annually, sufficient for over one million Dutch homes.
- **01-06-2021**: Van Oord is selected as the Balance of Plant contractor for the project, responsible for engineering, procurement, construction, and installation of the wind farm's infrastructure.
- **30-06-2022**: Rovco is contracted for subsea investigation works, focusing on unexploded ordnance (UXO) mitigation, crucial for the wind farm's construction.
- **21-09-2022**: Vos Prodect Innovations is awarded a contract for cable hang-off systems, part of the inter-array cable infrastructure for the wind farm.
- **08-12-2022**: KBR is contracted to design a baseload power hub that integrates lithium-ion battery storage and green hydrogen electrolysis, enhancing the facility's energy storage capabilities.
- **23-01-2023**: CrossWind orders a fuel cell system from Ballard Power Systems to utilize green hydrogen for energy storage, further diversifying the facility's energy solutions.
- **20-06-2023**: The facility begins producing its first electricity, with plans to ramp up production to its full capacity of 3.3 TWh annually.
- **20-10-2023**: The final wind turbine is installed, marking the completion of the 69 Siemens Gamesa turbines, with the facility expected to be fully operational by the end of the year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09-05-2020**: Both Eneco and Shell provide guarantees for the construction and operation investments of the Hollandse Kust Noord project through their joint venture, CrossWind.
- **08-12-2022**: The design of the baseload power hub by KBR is part of the investment in integrating hydrogen production and energy storage solutions.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09-05-2020**: Bid submission for the Hollandse Kust Noord offshore wind farm.
- **01-06-2021**: Contract awarded to Van Oord for Balance of Plant activities.
- **30-06-2022**: UXO investigation contract signed with Rovco.
- **21-09-2022**: Cable hang-off systems contract awarded to Vos Prodect Innovations.
- **08-12-2022**: KBR contracted for the design of the baseload power hub.
- **23-01-2023**: Fuel cell system ordered from Ballard Power Systems.
- **20-06-2023**: First electricity produced from the wind farm.
- **20-10-2023**: Installation of the final wind turbine completed.

#### 4. Labour
- **20-06-2023**: The project is expected to create numerous jobs during the construction and operational phases, although specific job creation numbers are not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **09-05-2020**: The facility is developed by CrossWind, a joint venture between Shell and Eneco, indicating a shared ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by either company.
