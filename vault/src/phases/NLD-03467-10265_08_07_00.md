### Evolutionary Timeline of the Twentsche Kabelfabriek Facility in Hollandse Kust Noord, Netherlands

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **September 2022:** Twentsche Kabelfabriek (TKF) is involved in the Hollandse Kust Noord (HKN) offshore wind farm project, supplying 140 kilometers of 66 kV inter-array cables and associated accessories. The facility is responsible for the production of cable hang-off systems to secure inter-array cables in the wind turbines. The wind farm will feature 69 Siemens Gamesa 11 MW wind turbines and is designed to have a total capacity of 759 MW.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **July 2021:** TKF signed a contract with Van Oord, the Balance of Plant (BoP) contractor for Hollandse Kust Noord, indicating a significant investment in the infrastructure necessary for the wind farm.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **September 2022:** Vos Prodect Innovations (VPI) was awarded a contract by TKF to supply cable hang-off systems for the HKN project, with delivery planned for the end of Q4 2022. The wind farm is set to be fully operational in 2023.
