### Evolutionary Timeline of the Shell Facility in North of the Netherlands (NLD)

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Project Name**: NortH2
- **Production Goal**: Aims to produce one million tonnes of green hydrogen annually.
- **Capacity**: Plans to install at least 10GW of dedicated offshore wind power by 2040, with an initial target of 4GW of green-hydrogen producing electrolysers by 2030.
- **Technology**: Utilizes electrolysers powered by renewable energy to split water molecules into hydrogen and oxygen.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Partners**: Shell, Equinor, Gasunie, and Groningen Seaports.
- **Financial Aspects**: The project is expected to require significant investments, with discussions ongoing about government support, subsidies, and incentives to make green hydrogen cost-competitive.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Initiation**: The NortH2 project was initiated in February 2020.
- **Key Announcement**: On 08-12-2020, Equinor joined the NortH2 project, marking a significant milestone in the project's development and collaboration among major energy companies.
