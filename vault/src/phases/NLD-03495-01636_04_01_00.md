### Evolutionary Timeline of the Equinor Facility in the North of the Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: 08-12-2020
  - **Production Focus**: The facility is part of the NortH2 project, which aims to produce one million tonnes of green hydrogen annually by utilizing at least 10GW of dedicated offshore wind power by 2040.
  - **Production Capacity**: The project plans to install 4GW of green-hydrogen producing electrolysers by 2030.
  - **Technology**: The facility will use electrolysers powered by renewable energy to split water molecules into hydrogen and oxygen, contributing to the green hydrogen economy.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: 08-12-2020
  - **Investment Context**: The NortH2 project is a significant investment initiative involving multiple partners, including Equinor, Shell, Gasunie, and RWE, aimed at establishing a new hydrogen value chain in Europe.
  - **Future Plans**: The project is expected to require substantial investments to ramp up electrolyser production and develop advanced electrolyser factories.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: 08-12-2020
  - **Announcement**: Equinor joined the NortH2 project, marking a significant milestone in the development of green hydrogen production in the Netherlands.
  - **Feasibility Study**: A feasibility study is currently underway, expected to continue until July 2021, to explore cost reduction strategies and the viability of the project.

#### 4. Labour
