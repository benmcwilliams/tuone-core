#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: 08-12-2020
  - **Details**: The Gasunie facility is part of the NortH2 project, which aims to produce one million tonnes of green hydrogen annually by utilizing at least 10GW of dedicated offshore wind power by 2040. The facility will leverage offshore wind energy to power electrolysers, which split water into hydrogen and oxygen.
