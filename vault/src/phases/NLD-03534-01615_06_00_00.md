### Evolutionary Timeline of the Eneco Facility in Delfzijl, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Early 2017**: The Sunport Delfzijl solar plant, with a production capacity of 30 MW, becomes operational. It consists of 123,000 solar panels spread over 12 hectares and is capable of generating approximately 27 GWh of solar power annually.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-07-2017**: Google signs a 10-year power purchase agreement (PPA) with Eneco to buy all the electricity generated by the Sunport Delfzijl solar plant. This agreement provides economic certainty for Eneco to invest in new renewable energy capacity.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Early 2017**: The Sunport Delfzijl solar plant is officially operational.
