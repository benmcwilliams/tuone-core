### Evolutionary Timeline of the HEAVENN Facility in Veendam, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03-07-2020**: The HEAVENN project is launched, aiming to create a fully integrated green hydrogen value chain in the Northern Netherlands by the end of 2025. The project will utilize a 20 MW electrolyser in Delfzijl and a planned 10 MW electrolyser in Emmen for green hydrogen production. The facility will support various applications, including fuel-cell driven buses, cars, vans, trucks, and heating systems for residential buildings.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **03-07-2020**: The HEAVENN project receives a significant European grant of €20 million from the Fuel Cell and Hydrogen Joint Undertaking (FCH-JU), which is one of the largest ever awarded to a single project under this partnership. This grant will be complemented by €70 million in public and private co-funding at the national level, specifically for the development of green hydrogen generated from renewable electricity.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03-07-2020**: The official kick-off of the HEAVENN project is announced, marking the beginning of the initiative to develop the hydrogen economy in the Northern Netherlands. The project is expected to culminate in a fully operational hydrogen value chain by the end of 2025.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
