## NLD-03652-01842

### Evolutionary Timeline of Ørsted Facility in Sluiskil, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **07-10-2020:** Ørsted and Yara announced the development of a 100MW wind-powered electrolyser plant for renewable hydrogen production at Yara’s Sluiskil plant. The facility aims to produce approximately 75,000 tonnes of green ammonia per year, which is about 10% of the capacity of one of the ammonia plants in Sluiskil. The green ammonia will be used for carbon-neutral fertilizer production and has potential applications as a climate-neutral shipping fuel.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The project seeks public co-funding to support the development and construction of the electrolyser facility. The final investment decision for the plant is anticipated to be made in late 2021 or early 2022, contingent on sufficient co-funding and a confirmed business case.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **07-10-2020:** Announcement of the joint project between Ørsted and Yara to develop the electrolyser plant. The project is positioned as a significant step in the hydrogen roadmap for the Smart Delta Resources cluster in Zeeland.

4. **Labour:**
   - The project is expected to create economic activity and jobs in the region, leveraging the Netherlands' offshore wind resources and hydrogen consumption centers.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility is a joint venture between Ørsted and Yara, indicating that Ørsted does not exclusively own the facility but collaborates with Yara for its development and operation.
