#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2019**: Van Oord began installation work on the 370MW Norther offshore wind farm, utilizing 44 MHI Vestas V164-8.4MW turbines. The first four turbines were loaded at the port of Vlissingen for transport to the project site.
- **May 2020**: Van Oord's jack-up vessel Aeolus was involved in the installation of MHI Vestas V164-9.5MW turbines for the Borssele 3&4 project.
