## NLD-03802-09415

### Evolutionary Timeline of the MARIN Facility in Wageningen, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2023
   - **Details:** The MARIN facility is involved in the development of a floating hydrogen and ammonia production facility linked to an adjacent offshore wind farm, planned for operation by 2027. The facility will utilize seawater for the electrolysis process to produce green hydrogen and ammonia, which can be transported via existing oil and gas pipelines or newly installed Thermoplastic Composite Pipe (TCP).

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** April 2023
   - **Details:** The facility secured a grant of EUR 3 million from the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO) under the MOOI scheme for the OFFSET project, which supports the development of the floating facility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** April 2023
   - **Details:** The project consortium, which includes SwitcH2 and BW Offshore, announced that the facility received Approval in Principle (AiP) from DNV, confirming the technical feasibility of the design. The project is now in the basic design phase.
