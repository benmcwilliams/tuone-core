1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 25, 2019
     - **Details:** A pilot project commenced in Rozenburg, where the world’s first hydrogen-powered domestic boiler was operated under real conditions. The boiler was developed in Italy and is part of a project overseen by the Dutch subsidiary Remeha. The hydrogen boiler is designed to contribute to the decarbonization of heating systems by burning pure hydrogen generated from renewable sources.

2. **Investment and Development:**
