### Evolutionary Timeline of the BayWa Facility in Bomhofsplas, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2020**: BayWa re completed the construction of the **27.4 MW Bomhofsplas floating PV plant** in just seven weeks. This facility utilizes proprietary floating solar technology developed in-house, featuring a unique design that enhances stability and reduces construction costs. The system is designed to optimize energy generation with an east-west orientation and a 12-degree inclination, allowing for **1.5 MW of generation capacity per hectare**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2020**: BayWa re has invested in the development of floating solar technology, achieving significant cost reductions through optimized construction processes. The company plans to expand its floating solar capacity in the Netherlands, targeting an additional **100 MW** of floating solar installations in the near future.
