## NLD-04108-02167

### Evolutionary Timeline of the BayWa Facility in Sellingen, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2021**: Construction began on a 41 MWdc floating solar plant in Sellingen, which is expected to be the largest in Europe and the biggest outside China. The facility utilizes 72,000 solar modules arranged in a unique west-east orientation for stability and efficiency. The plant occupies a surface area of 24 hectares on a former sand extraction lake.

- **July 2021**: The Sellingen solar park was officially commissioned with a capacity of 41.1 MWp, making it one of the largest floating PV projects outside of Asia. This facility, along with the Uivermeertjes project (29.8 MWp), contributes to BayWa r.e.'s growing portfolio of floating solar projects in Europe.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: BayWa r.e. sold the Sellingen facility to a consortium that includes the provincial energy transition fund Energiefonds Overijssel, Blauwvinger Energie, and an unnamed private investor. The financial details of this transaction were not disclosed.

- **February 2021**: Prior to the sale, BayWa r.e. had invested in the construction of the facility, which was part of a broader strategy to expand its floating PV capacity in response to increasing demand.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2020**: Construction of the Sellingen floating solar plant commenced.

- **July 2021**: The facility was officially commissioned, marking a significant milestone in BayWa r.e.'s floating solar project portfolio.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the Sellingen facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **July 2021**: BayWa r.e. sold a 49% stake in its floating solar business to Swiss investor Energy Infrastructure Partners for €530 million, indicating a shift in ownership structure. The Sellingen facility is now part of a consortium, suggesting that BayWa r.e. does not exclusively own the facility.

### Summary
The BayWa facility in Sellingen, Netherlands, has evolved from the commencement of construction in February 2020 to its commissioning in July 2021 as a significant player in the floating solar market. The facility, with a capacity of 41.1 MWp, is part of a strategic investment by BayWa r.e. in renewable energy, which has seen a shift in ownership to a consortium that includes various stakeholders.
