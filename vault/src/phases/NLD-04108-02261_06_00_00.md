### Evolutionary Timeline of the Groenleven Facility in Sellingen, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2021**: Construction began on a 41 MWdc floating solar plant located on a former sand extraction lake in Sellingen, Groningen. The facility utilizes 72,000 solar modules arranged in a unique west-east orientation to enhance stability and resistance to wind and waves. The design allows for efficient maintenance and cable management, requiring only a single medium-voltage cable to connect to the grid.

- **July 2021**: The facility was sold to a consortium that includes the provincial energy transition fund Energiefonds Overijssel, Blauwvinger Energie, and an unnamed private investor. This indicates a significant investment in the project, although specific financial details were not disclosed.
