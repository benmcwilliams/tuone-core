## NLD-04241-02771

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Chint Facility in Sappemeer, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2019**: The Midden-Groningen Solar Park, developed by Chint Solar, was officially powered up. This facility has a production capacity of **103 MWp** and is equipped with **315,000 solar panels**. The site spans **117 hectares** and is notable for being the first PV plant directly linked to the Dutch grid.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2015**: Initial development efforts began by Powerfield, focusing on permitting for the site.
- **Early 2017**: Chint Solar took over the development from Powerfield and continued to advance the project, which faced technical challenges.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2019**: Construction of the Midden-Groningen Solar Park commenced.
- **October 2019**: Construction was completed, and the facility was commissioned by network operator TenneT. The project required the installation of approximately **85,000 posts** to support a **1,000-kilometre cable** for grid connection.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
