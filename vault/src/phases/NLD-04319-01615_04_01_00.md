### Evolutionary Timeline of the Eneco Facility in Q13a-A, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: The PosHYdon project is announced, aiming to install a green hydrogen-producing plant on the Q13a-A platform. The facility will convert seawater into demineralised water and then into green hydrogen via electrolysis. The plant will have a production capacity of 1MW electrolyser, producing a maximum of 400 kilograms of green hydrogen per day.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: The Dutch government, through the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO), awards €3.6 million ($4.2 million) to the PosHYdon consortium, which includes Eneco. This funding is intended to kickstart the project and support the installation of the green hydrogen production plant.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2021**: The PosHYdon project is officially backed by the Dutch government, marking it as the world’s first offshore green hydrogen pilot on a working platform. The project aims to integrate offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen systems at the Q13a-A facility.
