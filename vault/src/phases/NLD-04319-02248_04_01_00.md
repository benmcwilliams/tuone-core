   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The facility at Q13a-A is set to host the world’s first offshore green hydrogen production plant as part of the PosHYdon project. The plant will utilize a 1MW electrolyser to produce a maximum of 400 kilograms of green hydrogen per day. The hydrogen will be produced by converting seawater into demineralised water and then into green hydrogen through electrolysis, powered by electricity generated from onsite offshore wind turbines.
