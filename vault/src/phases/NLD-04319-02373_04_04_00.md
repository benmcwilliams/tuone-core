### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nel Facility in Q13a-A, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The facility at Q13a-A is set to produce green hydrogen through a pilot project named PosHYdon. It will utilize a 1MW electrolyser capable of producing a maximum of 400 kilograms of green hydrogen per day. The production process involves converting seawater into demineralised water, which is then electrolyzed to produce green hydrogen. The facility will be powered by electricity generated from onsite offshore wind turbines.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The Dutch government, through the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO), has awarded €3.6 million ($4.2 million) to support the PosHYdon project. This funding is intended to kickstart the project and assist the consortium in leveraging its own budget for the completion of the installation.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The PosHYdon project was announced, marking the initiation of the world's first offshore green hydrogen production plant at the Q13a-A site. The project aims to integrate offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen systems and is positioned approximately 13km off the coast of Scheveningen in the Dutch North Sea.
