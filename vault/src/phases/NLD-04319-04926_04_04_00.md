### Evolutionary Timeline of the Emerson Automation Solutions Facility in Q13a-A, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The facility at Q13a-A is set to produce green hydrogen through a pilot project named PosHYdon. The production capacity is defined by a 1MW electrolyser, which is expected to generate a maximum of 400 kilograms of green hydrogen per day. The facility will integrate offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen systems, utilizing electricity from onsite offshore wind turbines to convert seawater into demineralised water and then into green hydrogen via electrolysis.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The Dutch government, through the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO), has awarded €3.6 million ($4.2 million) to support the PosHYdon project. This funding is aimed at enabling the consortium to initiate the project and leverage additional budgets for the completion of the installation.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The PosHYdon project was announced as the world’s first offshore green hydrogen production plant, with the facility located approximately 13km off the coast of Scheveningen in the Dutch North Sea. The project aims to gain insights into integrating energy systems at sea and the production of hydrogen in an offshore environment.
