   - **Date**: July 2021
   - **Details**: The facility at Q13a-A is set to produce green hydrogen through a pilot project named PosHYdon. The hydrogen production will utilize electricity generated from onsite offshore wind turbines, with seawater being converted into demineralised water and then into green hydrogen via the electrolysis process.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
