1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The facility at Q13a-A is set to produce green hydrogen through a pilot project named PosHYdon. The hydrogen production will utilize electricity from onsite offshore wind turbines and will involve the conversion of seawater into demineralised water, followed by electrolysis.
