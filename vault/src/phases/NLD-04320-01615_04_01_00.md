### Evolutionary Timeline of the Eneco Facility at Q13a-A Platform, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: The Q13a-A platform, operated by Neptune Energy, is set to host the PosHYdon project, which will install a 1MW electrolyser. This electrolyser is designed to produce a maximum of 400kg of green hydrogen per day by converting seawater into demineralised water and then into hydrogen via electrolysis. The platform is noted as the first fully electrified platform in the Dutch North Sea, utilizing electricity generated from offshore wind.

#### 2. Investment and Development
