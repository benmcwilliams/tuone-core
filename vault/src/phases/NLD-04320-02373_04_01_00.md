### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nel Facility at Q13a-A Platform, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The facility will produce green hydrogen using a 1MW electrolyser installed on the Neptune Energy-operated Q13a-A platform. The electrolyser is expected to produce a maximum of 400kg of green hydrogen per day. The project aims to validate the integration of offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen energy systems.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The PosHYdon project received a subsidy of €3.6 million from the Dutch government through the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO). The remaining budget will be funded by consortium partners, which include Nel Hydrogen.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The PosHYdon project was announced, marking the start of activities for the pilot project on the Q13a-A platform. This project is significant as it is the first offshore green hydrogen pilot and involves the installation of the electrolyser.
