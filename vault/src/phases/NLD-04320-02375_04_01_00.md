#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: The Q13a-A platform, operated by Neptune Energy, is set to host the world’s first offshore green hydrogen pilot project, known as PosHYdon. The facility will install a 1MW electrolyser capable of producing a maximum of 400kg of green hydrogen per day. This platform is notable for being the first fully electrified platform in the Dutch North Sea, utilizing electricity generated from offshore wind to power the hydrogen production process.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: The PosHYdon project secures a grant of €3.6 million from the Dutch government through the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO). - **July 2021**: The announcement of the PosHYdon project marks the start of activities for the pilot installation on the Q13a-A platform. The project aims to validate the integration of offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen energy systems in the Dutch North Sea.
