#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10/2024**: The PosHYdon project aims to produce green hydrogen by integrating three energy systems: offshore wind, offshore gas, and offshore hydrogen. The facility will involve the installation of a hydrogen plant on the Neptune Energy (Eni) Q13a-A platform, located 13 kilometers off the coast of Scheveningen. The first offshore hydrogen production is planned for the fourth quarter of 2024.
