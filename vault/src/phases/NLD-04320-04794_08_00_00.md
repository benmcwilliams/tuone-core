### Evolutionary Timeline of DEME Offshore Facility at Q13a-A Platform, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: The Q13a-A platform, operated by Neptune Energy, is set to host the PosHYdon project, which will involve the installation of a 1MW electrolyser. This electrolyser is designed to produce a maximum of 400kg of green hydrogen per day by converting seawater into hydrogen through electrolysis, powered by electricity generated from offshore wind.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: The PosHYdon project secures a grant of €3.6 million from the Dutch government through the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO). This funding is aimed at enabling the consortium, which includes DEME Offshore and Eneco, to commence activities for the pilot project. The remaining budget will be covered by the consortium partners.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2021**: The announcement of the PosHYdon project marks the beginning of activities for the world's first offshore green hydrogen pilot, which will be installed on the Q13a-A platform. The project aims to validate the integration of offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen energy systems in the Dutch North Sea.
