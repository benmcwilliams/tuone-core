#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-11-2020**: The Q13a platform is set to host the world's first offshore green hydrogen project, known as PosHYdon. This facility will integrate offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen production. The platform will utilize electricity generated by offshore wind turbines to power a hydrogen plant, converting seawater into demineralized water and then into hydrogen via electrolysis. The Q13a platform is noted as the first fully electrified platform in the Dutch North Sea, with an annual CO2 savings of approximately 16.5 kilotonnes.
