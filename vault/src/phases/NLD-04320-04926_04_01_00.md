### Evolutionary Timeline of Emerson Automation Solutions Facility at Q13a-A Platform, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: The Q13a-A platform, operated by Neptune Energy, is set to host the PosHYdon project, which will install a 1MW electrolyser. This facility aims to produce green hydrogen by converting seawater into demineralised water and then into hydrogen via electrolysis. The electrolyser is expected to produce a maximum of 400kg of green hydrogen per day. The Q13a-A platform is noted as the first fully electrified platform in the Dutch North Sea.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: The PosHYdon project received a subsidy of €3.6 million from the Dutch government through the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO). The remaining budget for the project will be funded by consortium partners, which include Emerson Automation Solutions.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2021**: The PosHYdon project is officially announced, with activities set to commence following the government grant. The project aims to validate the integration of offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen energy systems in the Dutch North Sea.
