## NLD-04320-05327

### Evolutionary Timeline of the IV-Offshore & Energy Facility at Q13a-A Platform, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: The facility will produce green hydrogen through the installation of a 1MW electrolyser on the Q13a-A platform. The electrolyser is expected to produce a maximum of 400kg of green hydrogen per day. The Q13a-A platform is noted as the first fully electrified platform in the Dutch North Sea, utilizing electricity generated from offshore wind to power the hydrogen production process.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: The PosHYdon project, which includes the Q13a-A facility, secured a grant of €3.6 million from the Dutch government through the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO). The remaining budget for the project will be funded by consortium partners, which include IV-Offshore & Energy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2021**: The announcement of the PosHYdon project marks the start of activities for the pilot project, which aims to validate the integration of offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen energy systems in the Dutch North Sea. The project is set to progress with the installation of the electrolyser on the Q13a-A platform.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The Q13a-A platform is operated by Neptune Energy. The facility is part of a consortium that includes multiple partners, indicating that IV-Offshore & Energy does not own the facility exclusively but is involved in a collaborative project with other entities.
