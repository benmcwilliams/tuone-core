### Evolutionary Timeline of the InVesta Facility at Q13a-A Platform, Netherlands

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The facility, located on the Q13a-A platform, will produce green hydrogen using a 1MW electrolyser. The electrolyser is expected to produce a maximum of 400kg of green hydrogen per day. The platform is the first fully electrified platform in the Dutch North Sea, utilizing electricity generated from offshore wind to power the hydrogen production process.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The PosHYdon project, which includes the InVesta facility, secured a grant of €3.6 million from the Dutch government through the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO). The remaining budget for the project will be funded by consortium partners.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The PosHYdon project was announced, marking the start of activities for the pilot project on the Q13a-A platform. The project aims to validate the integration of offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen energy systems.
