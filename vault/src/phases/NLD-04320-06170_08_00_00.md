### Evolutionary Timeline of TAQA Offshore B.V. Facility at Q13a-A Platform, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-11-2020**: The Q13a platform is set to host the world's first offshore green hydrogen project, known as PosHYdon. This project integrates offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen production. The facility will utilize electricity generated by offshore wind turbines to power a hydrogen plant, converting seawater into hydrogen through electrolysis. The Q13a platform is noted as the first fully electrified platform in the Dutch North Sea, with an annual CO2 savings of approximately 16.5 kilotonnes.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **05-11-2020**: The PosHYdon project is a collaborative initiative involving multiple partners, including Gasunie, NOGAT B.V., and Noordgastransport B.V. The project aims to develop a large-scale hydrogen infrastructure to support the energy transition in the Netherlands. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the article, but the project is part of a broader public-private research consortium, the North Sea Energy program.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07-2019**: Neptune Energy announces the selection of the Q13a platform for the pilot project to create the first offshore green hydrogen plant in the Dutch North Sea.
- **05-11-2020**: The pilot project is officially announced, with production expected to begin later in 2021.
