- **March 2023**: The Dutch government designated Ten noorden van de Waddeneilanden as the site for the world’s largest offshore hydrogen production project, with a planned electrolysis capacity of **500 MW**. The facility will utilize an existing natural gas pipeline for the transport of green hydrogen to land and will connect to the onshore hydrogen network. A smaller pilot project with an electrolysis capacity of **50 MW to 100 MW** will be developed prior to the main project to test and modify the technology.
