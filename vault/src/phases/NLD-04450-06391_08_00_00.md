### Evolutionary Timeline of Zenith Energy Terminals Facility in Port of Amsterdam, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** March 2023
   - **Details:** Zenith Energy Terminals, in collaboration with Electriq, announced the development of a plant in the Port of Amsterdam to produce a solid hydrogen carrier known as Electriq Powder. This innovative product simplifies the storage, transport, and use of hydrogen, particularly for last-mile, off-grid, and backup applications. The facility will utilize renewable energy generated by Zenith’s on-site wind turbines for the production of green hydrogen. The plant is noted to be the first of its kind globally.
