### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in Geertruidenberg, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2018**: RWE initiated the first phase of Solar Park Amer by installing a 0.5MWp solar project on the roof of its Amer power plant.
- **March 2021**: RWE announced the construction of its first floating solar project, the Amer floating PV project, with a capacity of 6.1MWp, consisting of 13,400 solar panels floating on a lake near the Amer power plant. Additionally, a 2.3MWp ground-mounted PV project was also under construction at the site.
- **July 2022**: The floating solar project was commissioned, marking the completion of the 6.1MWp installation. The floating PV system is part of a total of three solar systems at the Amer power plant, which collectively generate enough electricity to power approximately 2,300 Dutch households.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2021**: RWE raised €2 billion in capital to expand its renewables pipeline, which includes the floating and ground-mounted PV projects at the Amer site.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
