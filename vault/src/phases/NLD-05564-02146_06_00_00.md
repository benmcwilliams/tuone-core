### Evolutionary Timeline of the Alliander Facility in Friesland, Netherlands

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2022:** The facility, a green hydrogen plant powered by a 50MWp solar farm, was commissioned. It is the first hydrogen plant in the Netherlands powered by solar PV. The plant is expected to produce 100,000 kg of hydrogen per year. The facility aims to explore how hydrogen can alleviate grid congestion caused by the increasing integration of renewable energy sources.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **March 2022:** The Dutch government announced a record €13 billion (approximately US$14.3 billion) for its 2022 Sustainable Energy Production and Climate Transition Incentive Scheme, which is open to green hydrogen projects linked to solar initiatives. This funding is relevant to the development of the Alliander facility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 2022:** The facility was officially opened, marking the start of operations for the hydrogen plant. Production of hydrogen is expected to commence in June 2022.
