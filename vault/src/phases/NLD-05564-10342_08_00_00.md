### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ventolines BV Facility in Friesland, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **03-08-2018:** Ventolines BV is developing the Fryslân nearshore wind farm, which will consist of 89 wind turbines, each with a capacity of 4.3MW, totaling 382.7MW. The wind farm is located in the Frisian section of the IJsselmeer Lake, next to the Afsluitdijk dam. The project includes the construction of a nature conservation island to support local biodiversity.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **03-08-2018:** The entire project involves an investment of over EUR 500 million. This investment is tied to the construction and operational setup of the wind farm.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **03-08-2018:** The consortium of Siemens Gamesa Renewable Energy and Van Oord is selected as the preferred contractor for the construction of the wind farm. Construction works are scheduled to start in 2019, with the wind farm expected to be fully operational by mid-2021.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
