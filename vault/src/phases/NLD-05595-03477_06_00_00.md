### Evolutionary Timeline of the SolarDuck Facility in Waal, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2021**: SolarDuck has developed a triangular floating PV platform designed for offshore conditions. Each platform measures 16 x 16 x 16 meters and can be flexibly connected to form larger plants. The facility will deploy its first offshore PV plant on the Waal, consisting of four linked platforms, each fitted with 39 solar panels, totaling an installed capacity of 65 kW. The project is designed to be connected to a 10 kW electrolyzer operated by Voyex.

- **March 2021**: SolarDuck secured €350,000 from the Dutch province of Gelderland to implement its first pilot project in partnership with Voyex. The total cost of the project on the Waal is estimated at €1 million.
