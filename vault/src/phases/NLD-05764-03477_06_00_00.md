### Evolutionary Timeline of the SolarDuck Facility at Hollandse Kust West VII, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2022**: SolarDuck has been awarded the world’s largest hybrid offshore floating solar power plant at the Hollandse Kust West VII offshore wind park. The facility will produce a 5MW floating solar demonstrator with integrated energy storage solutions. The project is scheduled to become operational in **2026**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2022**: SolarDuck has entered into a partnership with RWE, which provides exclusive rights for offshore floating solar technology at the Hollandse Kust West VII site. This partnership is aimed at exploring the commercialization of SolarDuck’s technology, which is expected to facilitate faster scaling and global expansion.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2022**: Announcement of the 5MW floating solar demonstrator project at Hollandse Kust West VII, with an operational target set for **2026**. This project is part of RWE's bid for the offshore wind farm, which has a total capacity of **700MW**.
