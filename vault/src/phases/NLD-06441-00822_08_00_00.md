### Evolutionary Timeline of the Vattenfall Facility in IJmuiden, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2019**: Vattenfall announces the construction of a facility at the port of IJmuiden to support operations and maintenance (O&M) for the 760MW Hollandse Kust 1&2 offshore wind farm. The facility will include a service center with office space, boat landings, and a storage room for spare parts. 
- **Expected Operations**: The facility is designed to ensure optimal performance of the Hollandse Kust 1&2 wind farm, which is scheduled to be operational by 2023.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2019**: Vattenfall indicates that it will invest in the construction of the new facilities at the IJmuiden harbor in conjunction with the port owners. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2019**: Announcement of the facility's location and purpose.
- **Early 2022**: Construction work for the facility is scheduled to begin.
- **2023**: The Hollandse Kust 1&2 wind farm is expected to be operational.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2019**: Vattenfall states that once the facility is complete, it will employ 40 staff members on-site to support the operations and maintenance of the wind farm.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **March 2019**: The articles do not specify any joint ventures or partnerships regarding the ownership of the facility, implying that Vattenfall will have full ownership of the facility in IJmuiden.

### Additional Context
