### Evolutionary Timeline of Windcat Workboats Facility in IJmuiden, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2017**: Windcat Workboats operates a fleet of over 40 crew transfer vessels (CTVs) designed specifically for the offshore wind industry. The facility is strategically located in IJmuiden, which services multiple wind farms, including Luchterduinen and Princess Amalia Offshore Wind Farm. The port is well-equipped with berthing facilities for large offshore wind vessels.

- **December 2020**: Windcat Workboats expands its fleet to 46 CTVs, enhancing its operational capacity in the European offshore wind sector. The company is also involved in the development of the Hydrocat, the world’s first hydrogen-powered CTV, expected to be operational by June 2021.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2020**: Compagnie Maritime Belge (CMB) agrees to acquire Windcat Workboats for £32.8 million in cash, along with assuming approximately £20.4 million of Windcat’s outstanding debt. This acquisition is part of CMB's strategy to diversify into the offshore wind market and develop hydrogen-powered vessels.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2017**: Windcat Workboats is recognized as a pioneer in the offshore wind industry, having established its base in IJmuiden over twelve years prior, taking advantage of the growing offshore wind sector.

- **December 2020**: The acquisition by CMB is expected to close on or before January 12, 2021, marking a significant milestone in Windcat's operational history and future development plans.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2020**: Windcat Workboats employs approximately 180 shore-based and sea-going personnel. The acquisition by CMB is anticipated to enhance job opportunities as the company aims to expand its operations and fleet.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **December 2020**: Windcat Workboats is sold to Compagnie Maritime Belge (CMB), indicating a change in ownership from Seacor Marine Holdings to CMB. Prior to this, Windcat was owned by Seacor Marine, which had supported its growth for nine years. The facility is now part of CMB's strategy to scale up in the offshore wind market. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and changes at the Windcat Workboats facility in IJmuiden, highlighting its growth, technological advancements, and shifts in ownership.
