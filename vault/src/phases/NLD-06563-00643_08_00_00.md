### Evolutionary Timeline of RWE Facility in Moerdijk, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2024**: RWE announced the construction of an ultra-fast battery energy storage system (BESS) at its Moerdijk power plant. The system is designed with an installed capacity of **7.5 MW** and a storage capacity of **11 MWh**. It will utilize lithium iron phosphate (LFP) batteries housed in three shipping containers and will connect to the high-voltage grid via an existing grid connection. The system aims to enhance grid stability by providing or absorbing electricity within milliseconds.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09/2024**: The Moerdijk battery storage project is part of the OranjeWind initiative, a collaborative offshore wind project between RWE and TotalEnergies, aimed at integrating intermittent renewable energy sources into the Dutch energy grid. Specific investment amounts for the Moerdijk facility were not disclosed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/2024**: RWE commenced construction of the ultra-fast BESS at the Moerdijk power plant. The project is set for commissioning by the **end of 2024**, followed by a **two-year pilot phase** in collaboration with the transmission system operator TenneT to refine technical requirements and grid compliance procedures.
