### Evolutionary Timeline of the Blauwwind Facility in Borssele, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2020**: The Blauwwind consortium commenced the installation of 77 MHI Vestas V164-9.5MW turbines at the Borssele 3&4 offshore wind farm, which has a total production capacity of 731.5 MW. The project is located 22 km off the coast of Zeeland and is designed to power approximately 825,000 Dutch households.
- **January 2021**: The Borssele 3&4 wind farm reached full commissioning, marking the completion of construction. The last turbine was installed in November 2020, and the facility was officially commissioned on January 6, 2021.
- **May 2021**: The facility received certification from Lloyd’s Register, allowing it to officially start operations.
