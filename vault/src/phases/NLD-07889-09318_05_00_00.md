1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2023:** KHNP (Korea Hydro & Nuclear Power) is set to begin a feasibility study in January 2024 to assess the compliance of their reactor design with Dutch legislation and regulations. The study will also evaluate the suitability of the Borssele location for two new nuclear reactors, each with a capacity of 1000-1650 MWe. The reactors are projected to contribute 9-13% of the Netherlands' electricity production by 2035.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **December 2023:** The feasibility study is part of a broader initiative by the Dutch government to explore nuclear energy as a key component of its climate and energy policy. The study is expected to last at least six months, with results anticipated in autumn 2024. The Dutch government has indicated that this is the first of three contracts for feasibility studies, with additional studies planned for Westinghouse (USA) and EDF (France).
