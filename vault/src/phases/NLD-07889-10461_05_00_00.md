#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **1973**: The Borssele nuclear power plant, featuring a 482 MWe (net) pressurized water reactor, commenced operations. It is the only nuclear power facility in the Netherlands and accounts for approximately 3% of the country's total electricity generation.
- **2024**: The Dutch government is exploring the feasibility of extending the operation of the existing Borssele plant beyond its scheduled closure in 2033. This includes conducting environmental studies and amending the Nuclear Energy Act.

#### 2. Investment and Development

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones

- **June 2024**: The government confirmed that discussions are ongoing regarding the potential purchase of a stake in the Borssele plant to ensure its continued operation beyond 2033.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
