### Evolutionary Timeline of the BP Facility in Hollandse Kust (West), Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 12-05-2022**: BP submitted bids for the Hollandse Kust (west) VI and VII offshore wind farm leases. The combined capacity of the two wind farm sites is approximately **1.4 GW**, covering an area of **176 square kilometers**. BP plans to integrate offshore wind power with various clean energy technologies, including the production of **green hydrogen** and sustainable aviation fuel.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: 12-05-2022**: BP anticipates investments of up to **EUR 2 billion** for the decarbonization of flexible demand, in addition to investments in offshore wind. This includes a commitment of nearly **EUR 75 million** for eco-innovation initiatives aimed at enhancing the Dutch North Sea ecosystem.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date: 12-05-2022**: BP's bids for the Hollandse Kust (west) sites are part of a competitive process against other companies and consortia, including SSE Renewables, Vattenfall, BASF, Shell, and Eneco. The evaluation criteria for Site VI focus on eco-innovation, while Site VII emphasizes systems integration.

#### 4. Labour
- **Date: 12-05-2022**: BP plans to develop a **skills and entrepreneurship incubator** to support the local workforce in acquiring skills necessary for the new industries associated with the offshore wind and clean energy projects.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date: 12-05-2022**: The ownership structure of the facility is not explicitly stated in the article, but BP is competing for the rights to develop the Hollandse Kust (west) sites, indicating that they may not have exclusive ownership at this stage.
