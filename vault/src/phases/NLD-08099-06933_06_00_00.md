### Evolutionary Timeline of the GroenLeven Facility in Babberich, Netherlands

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **June 2020:** BayWa r.e. inaugurated its largest agrivoltaic power plant in the Netherlands at the GroenLeven facility in Babberich. The facility has a production capacity of **2.67 MW** and utilizes semitransparent solar panels. The PV modules generate approximately **2,403 MWh** per year, which is fully injected into the grid. The project covers **3.2 hectares** and replaced traditional plastic tunnels with a solar structure.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The investment cost for the agrivoltaic project was nearly **€1 million per hectare**, translating to approximately **€1.15/W**. This is notably higher than the typical costs for conventional ground-mounted projects, which range from **€0.50/W to €0.80/W**.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **June 2020:** The facility was extended to a commercial format of **3.2 hectares** after the initial pilot project. The project involved testing two types of solar panels, including semitransparent modules.
