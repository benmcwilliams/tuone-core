### Evolutionary Timeline of VDL Group Facility in Noord-Brabant, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 10-01-2025**: VDL Group, in partnership with Demcon and Thorizon, formalized a collaboration to develop molten salt reactor (MSR) technology. The facility aims to establish an advanced testing facility for MSR technologies, with plans to deliver several component prototypes and a test facility within two and a half years. The Thorizon One plant, a 250 MWt/100 MWe MSR, is targeted for large industrial customers and utilities, with construction expected to begin around 2030.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: 10-01-2025**: The consortium submitted a grant application to the Province of Noord-Brabant to support the development of the MSR technology. This application is part of the broader initiative launched in 2021 called "Nuclear Energy for the Future," aimed at leveraging local manufacturing and research capabilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date: 10-01-2025**: A project agreement was signed in December 2024 between VDL Group, Demcon, and Thorizon to accelerate the development of small modular reactor technology. The consortium reached a significant milestone by submitting a grant application to the province, indicating progress in the project.

#### 4. Labour
- **Date: 10-01-2025**: The project is expected to create new jobs in the region as it fosters innovation and strengthens the competitiveness of the Dutch manufacturing sector. The collaboration aims to provide companies with valuable experience and qualifications to participate in the supply chain for SMRs and large-scale reactors.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date: 10-01-2025**: The facility is part of a consortium involving VDL Group, Demcon, and Thorizon, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by VDL Group. The collaboration leverages the expertise of all partners in high-tech project realization.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the VDL Group facility in Noord-Brabant, focusing on its role in advancing molten salt reactor technology.
