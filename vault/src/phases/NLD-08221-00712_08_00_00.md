## NLD-08221-00712

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Shell Facility in Egmond aan Zee, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2020**: The CrossWind consortium, consisting of Shell and Eneco, is awarded the Hollandse Kust (Noord) offshore wind project. The facility is planned to have an installed capacity of **759 MW**, generating at least **3.3 TWh** of electricity annually. The project is expected to be operational by **2023**.
- **June 2022**: C-Ventus is contracted for Balance of Plant (BoP) services for the **108 MW Egmond aan Zee offshore wind farm**, which consists of **36 Vestas V90-3.0 MW** wind turbines. The facility is the Netherlands' first offshore wind farm, opened in **2007**.
- **December 2022**: The Hollandse Kust Noord project is confirmed to consist of **69 Siemens Gamesa SG 11.0-200 DD** wind turbines, with installation and commissioning expected to be completed in the **second half of 2023**.
- **April 2023**: The first turbine of the Hollandse Kust Noord offshore wind farm is installed, marking a significant milestone in the construction process.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2020**: The Hollandse Kust (Noord) project is developed without government subsidies, indicating a significant investment from the CrossWind consortium (Shell and Eneco) to support the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2020**: Announcement of the CrossWind consortium winning the tender for the Hollandse Kust (Noord) project.
- **October 2022**: Offshore construction for the Hollandse Kust Noord project begins.
- **February 2023**: The last TP-less monopile is placed on the seabed for the Hollandse Kust Noord project.
- **April 2023**: The first wind turbine is installed, with expectations to generate its first power by summer 2023 and become fully operational by the end of the year.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2022**: C-Ventus is contracted for BoP services, which may lead to job creation in the operations and maintenance sector for the Egmond aan Zee offshore wind farm.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2022**: Shell becomes the sole owner of the Egmond aan Zee facility after acquiring Vattenfall’s stake in NoordzeeWind. The facility is now fully owned by Shell, with operational support from Vestas and OutSmart. The Hollandse Kust Noord project is a joint venture between Shell and Eneco, indicating a shared ownership structure for this specific project.
