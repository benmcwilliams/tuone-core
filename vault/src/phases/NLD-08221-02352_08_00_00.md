### Evolutionary Timeline of the MHI Vestas Facility in Egmond aan Zee, Netherlands

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility produces energy through the Egmond aan Zee offshore wind farm, which consists of 36 Vestas V90-3.0 MW wind turbines, totaling a production capacity of 108 MW. The wind farm is located 10 to 18 kilometers off the Dutch coast and was opened in 2007.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - In June 2022, Shell became the sole owner of the Egmond aan Zee wind farm after acquiring Vattenfall’s stake in NoordzeeWind. This acquisition indicates a significant investment in the facility's operational future.
