### Evolutionary Timeline of the Siemens Gamesa Facility in Egmond aan Zee, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2022**: The facility is part of the Hollandse Kust Noord (HKN) offshore wind farm, which will consist of 69 Siemens Gamesa SG 11.0-200 DD wind turbines. The total production capacity of the wind farm is 759 MW, with an expected annual generation of at least 3.3 TWh, sufficient to meet 2.8% of the Netherlands' total electricity demand.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2022**: The HKN project is being developed subsidy-free by the CrossWind consortium, a joint venture between Shell and Eneco. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the articles, but the project represents significant financial backing from these major companies.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2022**: Offshore construction for the HKN wind farm commenced.
- **February 2023**: The last TP-less monopile was placed on the seabed.
- **April 15, 2023**: The first Siemens Gamesa SG 11.0-200 DD wind turbine was installed on its foundation.
- **Summer 2023**: The project is expected to generate its first power.
- **End of 2023**: The wind farm is planned to become fully operational.
