## NLD-08221-08151

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Van Oord Facility in Egmond aan Zee, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: The Hollandse Kust Noord (HKN) offshore wind farm, developed by CrossWind (a joint venture between Shell and Eneco), is located 18.5 kilometers off the coast of Egmond aan Zee. The facility is designed to generate at least 3.3 TWh annually, which will meet approximately 2.8% of the total electricity demand in the Netherlands. The first Siemens Gamesa SG 11.0-200 DD wind turbine was installed on April 15, 2023.
- **December 2023**: Van Oord completed the installation of the 759 MW HKN offshore wind farm, marking it as one of the fastest installed sites of its kind. The project involved the design, engineering, procurement, transport, and installation of 70 turbine foundations and inter-array cables.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2020**: Van Oord was appointed as the balance of plant contractor for the HKN project by CrossWind, indicating a significant investment in the development of the offshore wind farm.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2022**: Offshore construction for the HKN project commenced.
- **February 2023**: The last TP-less monopile was placed on the seabed.
- **April 15, 2023**: The first wind turbine was installed.
- **December 2023**: Van Oord announced the successful completion of the installation of the HKN offshore wind farm.
