### Evolutionary Timeline of the Capital Stage Facility in Melissant, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 04-12-2017**
  - Capital Stage acquired two solar parks in the Netherlands, including the Melissant facility with a production capacity of **10 MW**. The total generation capacity of both parks (Melissant and Ooltgensplaat) is **47.6 MW**. The facilities are expected to be completed and grid-connected by **October 2018**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: 04-12-2017**
  - The total investment for the two solar parks, including project-based debt financing, amounts to **€44.5 million**. The solar parks are entitled to a feed-in tariff of **€0.0107 per kWh** for the first 15 years.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date: 04-12-2017**
  - Announcement of the acquisition of the Melissant solar park, with expectations for completion and grid connection by **October 2018**.
