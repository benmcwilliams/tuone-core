### Evolutionary Timeline of the Capital Stage Facility in Ooltgensplaat, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 04-12-2017**
  - Capital Stage acquired the Ooltgensplaat solar park, which has a production capacity of **37.6 MW**. This facility is part of a total generation capacity of **47.6 MW** when combined with another solar park in Melissant (10 MW). The solar parks are expected to be completed and grid-connected by **October 2018**.

- **Date: 04-12-2017**
  - Capital Stage announced the acquisition of the Ooltgensplaat solar park, marking its entry into the Dutch solar market. The facility is expected to be completed and operational by **October 2018**.
