   - **Date:** January 2019
   - **Details:** The Jolywood facility, known as the Zonnepark Rilland project, is the first utility-scale solar plant in Europe built with n-type bifacial solar modules. It has a production capacity of 11.7 MW and incorporates around 40,000 Jolywood premium Double-Glass Bifacial Solar Modules.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** January 2019
   - **Details:** The project was developed by Cooperation Unisun Energy and is part of the Netherlands' SDE+ program for large-scale renewables, which compensates for the difference between the cost price of renewable energy and the market value of the energy supplied.

   - **Date:** January 2019
   - **Details:** The Zonnepark Rilland project was connected to the grid, marking its operational status. The construction of the power plant was completed in just four months.
