- **12-06-2024**: The Noordzeker consortium, which includes SSE Renewables and AGP (asset manager for Dutch pension fund ABP), secured the IJmuiden Ver Beta area, which will house an offshore wind farm integrated with a large-scale floating solar plant offshore and a large-scale electrolyser on land. The total capacity for this area is 2 GW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12-06-2024**: The Noordzeker consortium is set to pay a total of EUR 21 million per year for 40 years for the rights to develop the IJmuiden Ver Beta area. They are also responsible for covering the environmental impact assessment costs.

- **12-06-2024**: The Noordzeker consortium was announced as the winning bidder for the IJmuiden Ver Beta area in the largest Dutch offshore wind tender, which concluded in March 2024. They are required to refine their project and make a decision on whether to progress with the development by July 2024. They must also provide a bank guarantee or a deposit of EUR 200 million within four weeks of the announcement to secure their right to develop the project.
