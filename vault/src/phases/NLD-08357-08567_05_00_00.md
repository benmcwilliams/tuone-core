### Evolutionary Timeline of the Covra Facility in Nieuwdorp, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2023
     - Construction has begun on the Multifunctional Storage Building (MOG), a new storage facility for low and intermediate-level radioactive waste. 
     - The MOG will have a capacity of 4000 cubic meters and is designed for a lifespan of at least 100 years. 
     - The facility will include a repacking area for drums of radioactive waste and is intended to store historical radioactive waste from NRG and future decommissioning waste from nuclear installations in the Netherlands. 
     - The building is designed to allow for future expansions of storage capacity.

     - The Authority for Nuclear Safety and Radiation Protection (ANVS) granted the final permit to Covra for the construction of the MOG.
