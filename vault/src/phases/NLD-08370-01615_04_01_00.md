### Evolutionary Timeline of the Eneco Facility in Scheveningen, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: The PosHYdon project is announced, involving the installation of a 1MW electrolyser on the Neptune Energy-operated Q13a-A platform, approximately 13 km off the coast of Scheveningen. The electrolyser is designed to produce a maximum of 400 kg of green hydrogen per day. The project aims to validate the integration of offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen energy systems.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: The project secures a grant of €3.6 million from the Dutch government through the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO) to support the pilot. The remaining budget will be funded by the consortium partners, which include Eneco and other companies.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2021**: The PosHYdon project is officially launched, marking the beginning of activities for the pilot installation on the Q13a-A platform. The project aims to gain experience in offshore hydrogen production and the integration of energy systems.
