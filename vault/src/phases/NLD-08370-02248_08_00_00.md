## NLD-08370-02248

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Gasunie Facility in Scheveningen, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: The PosHYdon project was announced, involving the installation of a 1MW electrolyser on the Neptune Energy-operated Q13a-A platform, approximately 13 km off the coast of Scheveningen. The facility aims to produce green hydrogen by using electricity generated from offshore wind to convert seawater into hydrogen via electrolysis. The electrolyser is expected to produce a maximum of 400 kg of green hydrogen per day.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: The project secured a grant of €3.6 million from the Dutch government through the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO) to support the pilot's development. The remaining budget will be funded by consortium partners, which include Gasunie, among others.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2021**: The announcement of the PosHYdon project marks the start of activities for the pilot, which is the world's first offshore green hydrogen pilot. The project aims to validate the integration of offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen energy systems.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
