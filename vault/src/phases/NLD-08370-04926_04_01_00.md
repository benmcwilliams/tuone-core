### Evolutionary Timeline of Emerson Automation Solutions Facility in Scheveningen, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is involved in the PosHYdon project, which includes the installation of a 1MW electrolyser on the Neptune Energy-operated Q13a-A platform, located approximately 13 km off the coast of Scheveningen. The electrolyser is designed to produce a maximum of 400kg of green hydrogen per day. The project aims to validate the integration of offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen energy systems.
