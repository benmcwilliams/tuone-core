### Evolutionary Timeline of the NOGAT Facility in Scheveningen, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: July 2021
   - **Details**: The NOGAT facility is involved in the PosHYdon project, which includes the installation of a 1MW electrolyser on the Neptune Energy-operated Q13a-A platform, located approximately 13 km off the coast of Scheveningen. The electrolyser is designed to produce a maximum of 400 kg of green hydrogen per day by converting seawater into demineralised water and then into hydrogen via electrolysis. The project aims to validate the integration of offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen energy systems.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Date**: July 2021
   - **Details**: The PosHYdon project secured a grant of €3.6 million from the Dutch government through the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO). The remaining budget for the project will be funded by the consortium partners, which include NOGAT and other companies.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Date**: July 2021
   - **Details**: The PosHYdon project was officially announced, marking the start of activities for the pilot project. This project is noted as the world’s first offshore green hydrogen pilot, with plans to integrate various energy systems at sea.
