### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nexstep Facility in Scheveningen, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2021**: The Nexstep facility is involved in the PosHYdon project, which will install a 1MW electrolyser on the Neptune Energy-operated Q13a-A platform, approximately 13 km off the coast of Scheveningen. The electrolyser is designed to produce a maximum of 400 kg of green hydrogen per day. The project aims to validate the integration of offshore wind, gas, and hydrogen energy systems.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2021**: The PosHYdon project secures a grant of €3.6 million from the Dutch government through the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO). This funding is intended to support the startup activities for the pilot project, with additional funding provided by consortium partners.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 2021**: The PosHYdon project is officially announced, marking the beginning of activities to develop the world's first offshore green hydrogen pilot. The project is a collaboration among various partners, including DEME Offshore and Eneco.
