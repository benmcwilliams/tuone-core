### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ecowende Facility at Hollandse Kust West Site VI, NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2023**: Ecowende collaborates with Sif Holding for the construction of the offshore wind farm at Hollandse Kust West site VI, located approximately 53 kilometers off the Dutch coast near IJmuiden. The facility will produce 52 transition piece-less monopile foundations, totaling approximately 70 kilotonnes. The production is set to commence in 2025.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2023**: Sif Holding announced a final investment decision to expand its manufacturing facilities in Rotterdam. Ecowende committed to participate in the financing of this expansion through an Advance Factory Payment, marking its role as a launching client for the expanded factory.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2023**: The final contract for the production of the monopiles was signed, confirming the project in Sif’s order book for 70 kilotonnes. The production is scheduled to start in 2025.
