### Evolutionary Timeline of the Shell Facility in Hollandse Kust (noord), NLD

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: 07-05-2020
  - **Details**: Shell and Eneco, through their joint venture CrossWind, submitted a bid to develop the Hollandse Kust (noord) offshore wind farm. The facility is planned to have an installed capacity of up to 759 MW, generating an estimated 3.3 TWh of electricity per year. The wind farm will be located approximately 18.5 kilometers off the Dutch coast near Egmond aan Zee. The project aims to incorporate innovations for system integration to tackle intermittency in electricity generation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: 07-05-2020
  - **Details**: Both Shell and Eneco have issued guarantees to CrossWind for investments in the construction and operation of the Hollandse Kust (noord) wind farm. The project is part of a zero-subsidy tender round, indicating a significant investment commitment from both companies.
