### Evolutionary Timeline of the Van Oord Facility in Hollandse Kust (Noord), NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 30-07-2020
   - **Details:** Van Oord has been selected as the Balance of Plant contractor for the Hollandse Kust (noord) offshore wind project. The facility will have an installed capacity of 759 MW, generating at least 3.3 TWh of clean energy annually. Van Oord's responsibilities include the engineering, procurement, construction, and installation of the foundations, inter-array cables, and the transportation and installation of wind turbines. The project is expected to be operational by 2023.
