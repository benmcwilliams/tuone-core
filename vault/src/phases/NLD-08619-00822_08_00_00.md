### Evolutionary Timeline of the Vattenfall Facility in Zeewolde, Netherlands

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2019
   - **Details:** Vattenfall signed a long-term purchasing agreement for electricity generated by the repowered onshore Windpark Zeewolde. The facility will undergo repowering, replacing 220 existing wind turbines with 91 larger and more powerful turbines, resulting in an installed capacity of over 300MW. The repowering is expected to be completed by 2021, making it the largest onshore wind farm in the Netherlands, producing more than 850 gigawatt hours annually.
