### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enercon Facility in Zeewolde, Netherlands

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 02-05-2019
   - **Details:** Enercon signed a contract to supply 74 turbines from its new EP3 platform and nine turbines from the existing EP2 platform for the Zeewolde wind farm, totaling a production capacity of 320MW. The repowering project will replace the existing 220 turbines with newer and larger machines, with the largest EP3 turbines rated at 4.2MW and a tip height of 220 meters. The repowering is set to begin in late 2019.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 02-05-2019
   - **Details:** Financing for the repowering project is expected to close by the end of 2019. The project includes balance of plant work, which involves the construction of a new substation and two transformers by ABB Power Grids, to facilitate the connection of the wind farm to the grid.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 02-05-2019
   - **Details:** The repowering project is announced to begin in late 2019, with the expectation that the new infrastructure will be operational by 2020. A long-term purchasing agreement for electricity generated by the repowered wind farm has been signed with Vattenfall.
