- **February 2019**: The Rijkswaterstaat facility in Uden completed the installation of a 400-meter long solar array using bifacial photovoltaic (PV) modules on a noise barrier along the A50 highway. This project is part of the Solar Highways initiative, which aims to integrate solar energy generation into existing infrastructure.

- **February 2019**: The project received €1.4 million in funding from the European Union through the LIFE + program. This funding was aimed at accelerating the use of PV technology on noise barriers across Europe.
