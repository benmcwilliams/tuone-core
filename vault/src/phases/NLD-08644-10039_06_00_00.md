#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2019**: The SEAC facility is involved in the deployment of bifacial photovoltaic (PV) modules on noise barriers along the A50 highway near Uden. A pilot project featuring a 400-meter long solar array has been completed, marking a significant technological advancement in integrating solar energy generation with infrastructure.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2019**: The project received €1.4 million in funding from the European Commission under the LIFE + program. This funding supports the initiative to accelerate the use of PV technology on noise barriers across Europe.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2019**: The completion of the first Solar Highways PV system is announced, with the 400-meter noise barrier equipped with bifacial solar panels. An 18-month test phase is initiated to measure the energy output and assess maintenance needs, energy performance, and financial yield.
