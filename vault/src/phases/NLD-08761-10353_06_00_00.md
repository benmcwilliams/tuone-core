1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is set to produce energy through a floating photovoltaic (PV) array coupled with flow batteries. The energy generated will be used to power the operations of a new road (N434) and a 2.2 km tunnel segment, including ventilation, lighting, and other installations.
