### Evolutionary Timeline of the GE Facility in Wieringermeer, Netherlands

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2021**: The GE facility in Wieringermeer became operational with the TIADE research wind turbine, featuring a full-scale 130-meter diameter turbine. This turbine utilizes a revolutionary two-piece blade design that allows for the outer 12 meters of the blades to be replaced with innovative tips. The project aims to improve the efficiency of next-generation wind turbine rotors and wind farms.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2020**: The TIADE project was established with partial funding from the ‘Topsector Energiesubsidie’ of the Dutch Ministry of Economic Affairs. This funding supports the development of technologies for more efficient wind turbine operations.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2021**: The TIADE research wind turbine was announced as fully operational, marking a significant milestone in the project aimed at validating innovations in wind turbine blade design and aerodynamics.
