### Evolutionary Timeline of Jifmar Offshore Services Facility in Den Helder, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 11-12-2023
   - **Details:** Jifmar Offshore Services acquired Acta Marine Workboats, which includes a fleet of 26 workboat vessels. This acquisition brings Jifmar's total fleet to over 70 vessels. The workboat division will continue to operate under the Jifmar brand from Den Helder.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 11-12-2023
   - **Details:** The acquisition was financed through a capital increase subscribed by Marguerite and Jifmar’s CEO. The financing package includes senior debt arranged by Societe Generale and other banks, as well as asset-backed financing from Santander Alternative Investments.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 11-12-2023
   - **Details:** The acquisition of Acta Marine Workboats marks a significant milestone for Jifmar, establishing a strategic position in the Netherlands and enhancing its global operations.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 11-12-2023
   - **Details:** The acquisition includes the transfer of 200 employees from Acta Marine Workboats to Jifmar, contributing to job retention and potential job creation within the facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 11-12-2023
   - **Details:** Jifmar Offshore Services, a portfolio company of Marguerite since February 2022, now fully owns the workboat division of Acta Marine, enhancing its operational capabilities in the Netherlands.
