#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2023**: The Ter Apel solar farm, developed by Low Carbon, has become fully operational with a production capacity of **26.7 MW**. It is noted as Low Carbon's largest solar project in the Netherlands, with a pipeline of over **120 MW** expected to come online by early **2024**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2023**: The development of the Ter Apel facility has been financed through Low Carbon’s **£540 million** finance facility, which involves seven leading international banks. - **September 2023**: The successful energisation of the Ter Apel solar farm marks a significant milestone for Low Carbon, being the first project from their Dutch pipeline to enter operations. This project is part of their broader strategic goal to achieve **20 GW** of new renewable energy capacity by the end of the decade.
