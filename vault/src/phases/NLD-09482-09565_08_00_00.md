#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 12-06-2024**: The Noordzeker consortium, comprising SSE Renewables and AGP (asset manager for Dutch pension fund ABP), secured a 2 GW offshore wind area named IJmuiden Ver Alpha. #### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: 12-06-2024**: The Noordzeker consortium is set to pay a total of EUR 21 million per year for 40 years as part of the tender agreement. They are also responsible for covering the environmental impact assessment costs associated with the project.

They are required to provide a bank guarantee or a deposit of EUR 200 million within four weeks to proceed with the development. The consortium must make a decision on whether to progress the project by July 2024.
