#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12-08-2019**: Vattenfall announced the construction of a hybrid power plant in Goeree-Overflakkee, combining a 38 MW solar power plant, a 22 MW wind farm, and a 12 MW battery storage system. This facility is noted as Vattenfall's first fully renewable hybrid power plant and aims to serve as a blueprint for future projects, particularly in Germany. The total output capacity of the facility is 60 MW.
