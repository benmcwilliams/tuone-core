### Evolutionary Timeline of Siemens Gamesa Facility in IJsselmeer Lake, NLD

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 03-08-2018
   - **Details:** Siemens Gamesa, in partnership with Van Oord, was selected as the preferred contractor for the construction of the Fryslân nearshore wind farm. The facility will produce a total capacity of 382.7 MW, comprising 89 wind turbines, each with a capacity of 4.3 MW. The turbines will feature a rotor diameter of 130 meters, a hub height of 109 meters, and a blade tip height of 174 meters. Siemens Gamesa will also design, fabricate, and commission the onshore substation and will service the wind farm for at least 16 years.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 03-08-2018
   - **Details:** The entire project involves an investment of over EUR 500 million. Construction works are scheduled to start in 2019.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 03-08-2018
   - **Details:** The final contracts for the construction are expected to be signed later in 2018. The wind farm is planned to be fully operational by mid-2021.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 03-08-2018
