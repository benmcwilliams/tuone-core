### Evolutionary Timeline of the Van Oord Facility in IJsselmeer Lake, Netherlands

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 03-08-2018
   - **Details:** The facility is part of the Windpark Fryslân project, which will produce renewable energy through a nearshore wind farm with a total capacity of 382.7 MW. It will consist of 89 wind turbines, each with a capacity of 4.3 MW. Van Oord is responsible for the Balance of Plant work, including the design, fabrication, and installation of foundations and cables for the wind project. The wind turbines will have a hub height of 109 meters and a blade tip height of 174 meters. The project also includes the construction of a nature conservation island.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 03-08-2018
   - **Details:** The entire Windpark Fryslân project involves an investment of over EUR 500 million. 

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 03-08-2018
   - **Details:** The consortium of Siemens Gamesa and Van Oord has been selected as the preferred contractor for the construction of the wind farm. Construction works are scheduled to start in 2019, with the wind farm expected to be fully operational by mid-2021.
