1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 11-07-2019
   - **Details:** Vattenfall's Hollandse Kust (Zuid) offshore wind project has a total production capacity of 1.4 GW, divided into four zones of 350 MW each. Sif Holding has been contracted to manufacture 114 monopiles for three of the four zones, utilizing 113 KTons of steel for this purpose. The monopiles will be stored at the MV2 facility in Rotterdam during 2020-2021.
