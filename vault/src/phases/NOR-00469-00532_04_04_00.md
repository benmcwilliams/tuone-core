### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mitsubishi Facility in Herøya, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2023**: Mitsubishi Power has initiated the validation process for two 5.5 MW pressurized alkaline electrolyzers at the Herøya Industrial Park. The Norwegian electrolyzer has successfully completed a short-term validation, including a 96-hour baseload run. The electrolyzers are designed to produce hydrogen, with an output of 1,100 Nm3/hour, equivalent to 100 kg of pure hydrogen per hour.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09/2023**: The electrolyzers were supplied by HydrogenPro AS, indicating a partnership that leverages HydrogenPro's technology, which has been previously validated in constructing one of the world's largest single-stack high-pressure alkaline electrolyzers.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/2023**: The facility has completed short-term validation tests for the electrolyzers. Mitsubishi Power plans to incorporate the design of these electrolyzers into the Advanced Clean Energy Storage (ACES Delta) project in Delta, Utah, which is a green hydrogen initiative set to begin commercial operations in 2025.
