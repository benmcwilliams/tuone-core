### Evolutionary Timeline of the Vianode Facility in Herøya, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06-04-2022**: Vianode, a subsidiary of Elkem, is expected to produce graphite for over 20,000 electric vehicles per year at a pilot plant in Herøya. Future plans include a large-scale plant capable of producing graphite for over one million electric vehicles annually, potentially increasing employment to around 300.
- **21-09-2022**: Vianode announces plans to build a factory in Herøya for electric car battery materials, specifically graphite-based anode materials, with an initial production capacity for 20,000 electric vehicles per year starting in 2024. The facility will later scale up to supply materials for two million electric vehicles by 2030.
- **16-10-2024**: Vianode officially opens its first fully-fledged anode graphite production plant, named 'Via ONE', in Herøya. The plant has a capacity of 2,000 tonnes per year, sufficient for 30,000 electric vehicles annually. It features advanced furnaces for synthetic anode graphite production.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06-04-2022**: The total investment for the pilot plant and preparations for a potential large-scale plant is estimated at around €209 million.
- **21-09-2022**: Vianode announces an investment of approximately 2 billion Norwegian kroner (around €195 million) for the new factory in Herøya.
- **16-03-2024**: Vianode receives a grant of €30 million from Innovation Norway as part of the European battery cell production project (EuBatIn). This funding is in addition to a previous €90 million received from the EU for the Herøya site.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06-04-2022**: Announcement of external investors Hydro and Altor acquiring stakes in Vianode, with an investment decision for the pilot plant expected in the first half of 2022.
- **21-09-2022**: Official announcement of the construction of the battery materials factory in Herøya.
- **16-03-2024**: Confirmation that the anode material factory in Herøya will be operational in the second half of 2024.
- **16-10-2024**: Official opening of the 'Via ONE' graphite production plant in Herøya.

#### 4. Labour
- **06-04-2022**: The pilot plant is expected to employ about 100 people, with potential future employment increasing to around 300 for the large-scale plant.
- **16-10-2024**: The 'Via ONE' facility will employ over 100 people when fully operational.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **06-04-2022**: Elkem remains the majority shareholder of Vianode with a 40% stake, while Hydro and Altor each hold a 30% stake, indicating a joint venture structure for the facility. 

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones in the evolution of the Vianode facility in Herøya, Norway, focusing on its technology, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
