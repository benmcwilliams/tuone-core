#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2022**: Nel ASA announced that its electrolyzer manufacturing facility at Herøya is fully automated and currently producing at three shifts, with a capacity of 500 MW. The facility has the potential to expand up to 2 GW.
- **April 2022**: Nel Hydrogen announced plans to build a second production line in Herøya, aiming to double the production of alkaline electrolyzer stacks to 1 GW. This new production line, costing €35 million, is expected to be operational by April 2024.
- **January 2024**: Nel secured a €5 million contract with Samsung C&T for 10 MW of alkaline electrolyzer equipment for an off-grid green hydrogen production project in South Korea, with the electrodes produced at the Herøya facility.
