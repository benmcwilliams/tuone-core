### Yara Clean Ammonia Facility Timeline - Herøya, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2024**: The Yara Clean Ammonia facility inaugurated a 24 MW renewable hydrogen plant at Herøya Industrial Park, which is the largest electrolyser currently in operation in Europe. The facility produces 10 tonnes of hydrogen daily through the electrolysis of water using renewable energy. This process replaces natural gas as feedstock and is expected to cut 41,000 tonnes of CO2 emissions annually. The facility also produces low-carbon footprint fertilizers under the new portfolio called Yara Climate Choice, which includes fertilizers made from both renewable hydrogen and low-carbon ammonia produced using carbon capture storage (CCS).
