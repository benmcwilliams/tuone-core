## NOR-00603-00217

### Evolutionary Timeline of Elkem Facility in Kristiansand, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2020**: Elkem is constructing a pilot plant for battery graphite in Kristiansand, representing an investment of NOK 65 million (over EUR 6 million). The pilot plant is expected to open at the beginning of 2021. This facility aims to produce graphite as anode material for lithium-ion batteries, targeting the electric vehicle (EV) market.
- **May 2021**: Elkem launches a subsidiary named Vianode, dedicated to the development and production of anode materials for electric car batteries. Pilot production on an industrial scale has begun in Kristiansand, with qualification processes initiated with several battery cell manufacturers.
- **August 2021**: Vianode announces plans to rent an additional building at Herøya Industrial Park, increasing production capacity for graphite to a total of 60,000 tonnes per year, with a new "fast-track plant" to meet rising demand.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2020**: Enova, owned by the Norwegian Ministry of Climate and Environment, provides NOK 10 million (approximately EUR 917,900) for the initial planning of the Northern Recharge project, which is linked to the potential large-scale plant for battery materials.
- **May 2021**: Vianode has received NOK 10 million (about EUR 1 million) in funding from the government to support its operations and further applications for the large-scale plant are planned.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2020**: Announcement of the pilot plant construction in Kristiansand.
- **January 2021**: Expected opening of the pilot plant.
- **May 2021**: Establishment of Vianode as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Elkem, with pilot production commencing.
- **August 2021**: Announcement of the expansion of production capacity at Herøya Industrial Park.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2021**: The establishment of Vianode and the pilot production in Kristiansand is expected to create jobs, although specific numbers are not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2021**: Vianode is founded as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Elkem, indicating that Elkem has full ownership of the facility in Kristiansand. However, the company is open to partnerships with external industrial and financial partners for future developments.
