### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nidec Facility in Mo i Rana, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2022**: Freyr's facility, named Giga Arctic, is currently under construction with an initial production capacity of **29 GWh**, set to come online in **2024**. By **2030**, Freyr targets an increased production capacity of **200 GWh**. The facility will produce LFP battery cells, which will be used in stationary battery energy storage solutions (BESS) rather than electric vehicles. The cells will be semi-solid and powered by **100% hydroelectric energy** through a Power Purchase Agreement with Statkraft.

- **June 2022**: The Freyr Board approved the construction of the Giga Arctic factory in Mo i Rana.
- **August 2022**: Freyr announces a joint venture with Nidec to combine battery cells into integrated BESS solutions. The facility is set to begin production in **2024**.
