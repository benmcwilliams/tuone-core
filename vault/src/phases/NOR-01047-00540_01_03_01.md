### Morrow Batteries Facility Timeline - Southern Norway, NOR

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 25-05-2022
   - **Details:** Morrow Batteries is building a battery cell factory in Southern Norway, initially announced with a total production capacity of 32 GWh. This capacity has been updated to 43 GWh, with operations scheduled to start in 2023 and mass production of the first-generation product portfolio planned for 2024. The facility will utilize LNMO-X core technology and will incorporate anode materials supplied by Echion Technologies, with an initial supply of up to 150 tonnes.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 25-05-2022
   - **Details:** Morrow Batteries raised €100 million during its latest investment round, which will finance the first phase of the factory. The investment round was led by Siemens Financial Services and ABB, with participation from existing shareholders including the Danish pension fund PKA, NOAH AS, and Agder Energi Invest.
