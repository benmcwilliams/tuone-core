### Evolutionary Timeline of Windport AS Facility in Agder, Norway

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** March 2023
   - **Details:** Windport AS is collaborating with Brigg Vind (a consortium of Vårgrønn, Å Energi, and Corio Generation) to develop port facilities for offshore wind in Agder, Norway. The planned port facility is intended to support the development and construction of the first phase of the Southern North Sea II (Sørlige Nordsjø II) offshore wind area, which has a production capacity of up to 1,500 MW. The required port capacity for this project is estimated to be approximately 150,000-200,000 square meters.
