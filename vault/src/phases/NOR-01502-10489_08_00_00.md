### Evolutionary Timeline of the Å Energi Facility in Agder, Norway

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** March 2023
   - **Details:** Å Energi is part of a consortium named Brigg Vind, which includes Vårgrønn and Corio Generation. The consortium is collaborating to develop port facilities for offshore wind in Agder, specifically for the first phase of the Southern North Sea II (Sørlige Nordsjø II) offshore wind area. The estimated production capacity for this area is up to 1,500 MW, and the required port capacity for the project is approximately 150,000-200,000 square meters.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** March 2023
   - **Details:** The Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed with Windport AS aims to initiate early-phase planning and investments for the development of innovative and competitive port infrastructure and services necessary for the offshore wind project.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** March 2023
   - **Details:** The MoU signifies the beginning of collaboration for the development of port facilities. Norway's Ministry of Petroleum and Energy plans to tender the Utsira Nord and the first phase of the Southern North Sea II offshore wind areas by the end of the first quarter of 2023.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
