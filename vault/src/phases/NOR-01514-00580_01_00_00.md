1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 02-06-2020
   - **Details**: Norsk Hydro, in collaboration with Northvolt, announced the formation of a joint venture named Hydro Volt to recycle battery materials and aluminium from electric vehicles. There is potential for capacity expansion over time.
