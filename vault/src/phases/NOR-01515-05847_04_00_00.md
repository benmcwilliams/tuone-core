#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: Provaris is developing a fleet of vessels for the transportation of compressed green hydrogen (CH2) across Europe, utilizing a compression technology that is more cost-effective than liquefaction.
- **Production Capacity**: The vessels, named Gaseous Hydrogen (GH2), come in two design variants: H2Neo (26,000m³ capacity) and H2Max (120,000m³ capacity). The facility aims to facilitate the transport of 100,000-200,000 tonnes of CH2 per annum, with potential scalability to 400,000 tonnes.
- **Operational Model**: The hydrogen is produced using renewable energy, compressed to 250 bar, and transported via the vessels to distribution pipelines, ensuring energy efficiency and reduced capital costs.
