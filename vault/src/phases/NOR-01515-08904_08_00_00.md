### Fon Energy Services Facility Timeline - Oslo, Norway

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: March 2022
   - **Details**: Fon Energy Services was established as a joint venture between Akastor and IKM to provide integrated operations and maintenance (O&M) solutions specifically for the offshore wind industry. The facility focuses on inspection and maintenance services for balance of plant equipment, including foundations, inter-array cables, and substations. The services offered include periodic and event-based inspections, preventive and corrective maintenance, major repairs, asset and O&M management, and logistics and vessel management.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: March 2022
   - **Details**: Akastor is set to inject capital into Fon Energy Services as part of the establishment of the joint venture. This investment aims to enhance the O&M segment within offshore wind, which is a significant cost factor throughout the project lifecycle.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: March 2022
   - **Details**: Fon Energy Services was officially launched in March 2022, with a team of approximately 80 employees, including specialist engineers and offshore technicians. The company aims to provide a competitive O&M offering to wind farm developers and operators.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: March 2022
   - **Details**: The establishment of Fon Energy Services resulted in the creation of approximately 80 jobs, including roles for specialist engineers and offshore technicians.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: March 2022
   - **Details**: Fon Energy Services is a joint venture where Akastor and IKM Group each own 44% of the company, while the remaining 12% is owned by the management of the company.
