### Evolutionary Timeline of the Login Eiendom Facility in Oslo, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2019**: A rooftop solar project with a production capacity of **2.5MW** is being developed on a cold storage facility owned by Login Eiendom, utilized by retailer REMA 1000. This project is notable for being Norway's first commercial and industrial (C&I) solar scheme utilizing **1,500V inverters**, which are expected to lower installation costs by **10-15%** due to reduced cabling and labor requirements.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2019**: Initial funding for the rooftop project was secured from the Norwegian government's **Enova program** to cover early expenses. No further state support has been required since then.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2019**: Construction of the 2.5MW rooftop solar unit is underway, with an expected completion date by the end of summer 2019. The project is being designed to align with the energy consumption profile of the cold storage facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2019**: The use of 1,500V inverters is anticipated to reduce the need for expensive electricians due to less cabling, which may impact job requirements in the installation phase.
