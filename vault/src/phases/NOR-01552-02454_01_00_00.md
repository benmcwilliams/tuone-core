### Evolutionary Timeline of the Stena Facility in Ausenfjellet, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2024**: Stena Recycling announced the establishment of Europe's first complete and scalable value chain for the reuse and recycling of electric car batteries at its new facility in Ausenfjellet. The facility is designed to process up to 3,000 tons of high-voltage batteries per year, equivalent to 6,000 to 8,000 electric car batteries. The facility will incorporate advanced infrastructure, including AI algorithms for battery health assessment, innovative disassembly methods, and a modular battery storage bank system for second-life applications.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2024**: The project is supported by Enova, which aids in the development of new technology and the establishment of a value chain aimed at minimizing the environmental impact of electric car batteries.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2024**: The facility is scheduled for completion in the summer of 2024. The project is a collaboration with technology company EV HUB, which has developed solutions for diagnosing and repairing battery systems.

#### 4. Labour
