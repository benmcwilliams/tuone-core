### Evolutionary Timeline of the Norsun Facility in Årdal, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Production**: Norsun operates a hydroelectric-powered facility in Årdal, producing n-type mono wafers and ingots for solar cells.
- **Production Capacity**: As of February 2023, the facility has a production capacity of 1 GW. Plans are in place to quadruple this capacity to 3 GW, supported by EU funding.
- **Future Plans**: Norsun is also preparing for a potential 5 GW plant in the United States.
