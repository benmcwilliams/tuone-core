### Evolutionary Timeline of Store Norske Energi Facility in Svalbard, NOR

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2023**: Store Norske Energi installed a photovoltaic (PV) system at Isfjord Radio, consisting of 360 ground-mounted bifacial solar panels (550 W each) for a total capacity of 198 kW, and rooftop systems with a total capacity of 96 kW. The expected annual energy production is 161,000 kWh from the ground-mounted system and 60,000 kWh from the rooftop systems. The facility also includes a battery storage system of 405 kWh and thermal storage of 12,000 liters of hot water.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2023**: The project is positioned as a pilot for reducing fossil fuel use by 70% at Isfjord Radio, with the aim of serving as a model for renewable energy transitions in Arctic communities. The investment details specific to the project were not disclosed in the articles, but the emphasis on reducing diesel consumption indicates significant financial implications.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones

- **November 2023**: Store Norske Energi announced the successful activation of the northernmost ground-mounted PV plant, confirming the project's completion and operational status.
