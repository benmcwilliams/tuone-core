### Aker Horizons Facility in Rjukan, Norway - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: 02-06-2023
  - Aker Horizons announced plans for a green hydrogen production facility in Rjukan, with a production capacity of 20 MW electrolyzer, enabling the production of approximately 9 tonnes of green hydrogen per day. The facility will leverage existing infrastructure, industrial land, and hydropower available at the site.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: 02-06-2023
  - The facility secured grants and loans totaling up to NOK 135 million from Innovation Norway, which includes up to NOK 85 million in grants and up to NOK 50 million in green growth loans. This financial support is intended to de-risk the project and accelerate the scale-up of hydrogen production.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: 02-06-2023
  - Aker Horizons aims to finalize the investment decision by the end of 2023, with commercial operations expected to commence in 2025. The project is currently in the permitting process, and a pre-FEED (Front-End Engineering Design) for civil works has been completed. Partnership discussions are ongoing, and a letter of intent for a substantial portion of hydrogen offtake has been secured.

#### 4. Labour
- **Date**: 02-06-2023
  - The Rjukan green hydrogen project is expected to generate new green jobs, contributing to local employment opportunities as the facility develops.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date**: 02-06-2023
  - Aker Horizons ASA, through its subsidiary Aker Horizons Asset Development, is leading the project, indicating that the facility is under the company's full ownership structure.
