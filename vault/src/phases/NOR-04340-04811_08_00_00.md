### Evolutionary Timeline of the Deep Wind Offshore Facility in Haugesund, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **01-02-2024**: Deep Wind Offshore partners with Hydepoint to explore offshore hydrogen production integrated with wind energy. The facility aims to produce hydrogen offshore, which will allow wind farms to be built further out to sea, enhancing utilization and profitability. The facility has a pipeline of over 10 GW, including 2 GW under exclusive development in South Korea. A permit application for a wind farm offshore Sweden featuring a maximum of 70 wind turbines was submitted at the end of the previous year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **01-02-2024**: The partnership with Hydepoint is part of a broader EU initiative targeting the production of 200 million tonnes of hydrogen from renewable energy by 2030. This collaboration is expected to optimize the integration of offshore wind and hydrogen production, potentially reducing the need for grid investments.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **01-02-2024**: Announcement of the partnership with Hydepoint for offshore hydrogen production. The facility is in the early project development phase, focusing on the integration of wind and hydrogen production.
