### Hellesylt Hydrogen Hub Facility Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Hellesylt Hydrogen Hub facility is designed to produce green hydrogen using renewable surplus hydro power. The hydrogen will be utilized to power ferries operating on the Hellesylt to Geiranger route. The facility aims to deliver green hydrogen by no later than 2023.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The facility received a grant of NOK 37.6 million (€3.6 million) from the PILOT-E funding scheme to support its development.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2019:** Announcement of the facility's construction, with activities set to begin in January 2020. The project aims to deliver hydrogen by 2023.

4. **Labour:**
