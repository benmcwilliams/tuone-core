### Evolutionary Timeline of Fortescue Future Industries Facility in Svelgen, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2023**: Fortescue Future Industries announced plans for a green ammonia plant near Svelgen, which will utilize surplus renewable energy from the Norwegian transmission grid. The facility aims to produce green hydrogen and green ammonia, targeting operations to begin in 2027, pending final approvals. The plant is designed to replace fossil fuels in hard-to-decarbonize sectors such as long-haul transport, shipping, and heavy industry.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2023**: Fortescue approved an estimated total investment of about $750 million over the next three years for various green energy projects, including the Svelgen facility. This funding is part of a larger €3.6 billion allocation for multiple projects and will be disbursed upon the completion of specific project milestones.
