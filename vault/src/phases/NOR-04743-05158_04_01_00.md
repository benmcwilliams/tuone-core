### Evolutionary Timeline of the Greenstat Facility in Fiskå Industrial Park, Kristiansand, Norway

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** August 2024
   - **Details:** Greenstat is set to commence construction of the Agder hydrogen facility at Fiskå industrial park. The facility will have a production capacity of 20 MW, producing up to 8 tonnes of hydrogen per day. Phase 2 of the project is also underway, which will increase capacity to 40 MW.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** August 2024
   - **Details:** The project is supported by a NOK 148 million ($14.9 million) investment from Enova. Additionally, La Française de l’Energie (FDE) became the largest owner of Greenstat in early 2024, providing the necessary capital for the hydrogen facility's realization.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** August 2024
   - **Details:** Construction of the Agder hydrogen facility is set to begin, with a planned building period of two years. The facility is expected to be operational by the end of 2026. A long-term lease agreement of 30 + 10 years has been established with Elkem for the area and shared services. The final investment decision (FID) for the project was reached at the beginning of 2024.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** August 2024
   - **Details:** Specific job creation or job loss details have not been provided in the article, but the construction and operation of the facility are expected to create jobs in the region.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** Early 2024
   - **Details:** The Agder hydrogen facility is 100% owned by Greenstat through Agder Hydrogen Hub. Everfuel transferred its ownership in Agder to Greenstat at the beginning of 2024.
