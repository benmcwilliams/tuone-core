### Evolutionary Timeline of the Horisont Energi Facility in Hammerfest, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06/2021**: Horisont Energi is developing a fully automated production plant near Hammerfest, Norway. The facility will utilize gas from the nearby Melkøya LNG plant to produce over 1 million tons of ammonia annually. The plant is designed to have a production capacity of 600 tons per day of blue hydrogen and 3,000 tons per day of blue ammonia, with CO2 emissions being stored off the coast of Finnmark, Norway.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12/2021**: The Norwegian government announced a funding package of NOK 1 billion (€98.3 million) for three hydrogen projects, including Horisont Energi, which will receive up to NOK 482 million. This funding is aimed at establishing ammonia production from natural gas with carbon capture technology near Hammerfest.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06/2021**: Horisont Energi is in the planning stages for the Barents Blue project, with a final investment decision expected by the end of 2022. The project aims to start shipping blue ammonia to the Port of Rotterdam by 2025.
