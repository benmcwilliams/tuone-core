### Evolutionary Timeline of Odfjell Oceanwind Facility in Hammerfest, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2024**: The GoliatVIND project is set to be the world’s first demonstration plant featuring turbines and floaters over 15 MW. It will consist of five 15 MW wind turbines installed on Odfjell Oceanwind’s Deepsea Star semi-submersible floating foundation, totaling a production capacity of 75 MW. The project is expected to be built in 2028/2029.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2024**: GoliatVIND is backed by Odfjell Oceanwind, Source Galileo, Kansai Electric Power, and ENEOS Renewable Energy. The project aims to establish Norwegian supplier capacity and reduce technical risks associated with floating offshore wind, which is expected to lower costs for future developments in Norway.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2023**: GoliatVIND submitted a proposal for an Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) programme for the floating offshore wind project outside Hammerfest, Norway.
- **November 2024**: The EIA programme has been approved by the Ministry of Energy, paving the way for a licence application to be submitted shortly. The project is positioned as a key initiative to secure Norway's standing in the floating offshore wind sector.
