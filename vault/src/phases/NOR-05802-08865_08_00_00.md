### Evolutionary Timeline of the Eviny Facility in Lund, Norway

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **January 2024:** Eviny acquired two wind farms, Guleslettene (197MW) and Tellenes (168MW), increasing its total renewable energy capacity by 20%, from 7.7 TWh to 9 TWh. The Guleslettene plant generates 700 GWh annually, while Tellenes generates 520 GWh. The Tellenes wind power plant is located in the Rogaland municipalities of Lund and Sokndal.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **January 2024:** The acquisition of the two wind farms is valued at €537.5 million ($587.3 million). This investment is part of Eviny's strategy to enhance its renewable energy portfolio and is led by its new energy and industrial partnerships division.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **January 2024:** Eviny announced the acquisition of the wind farms, with the transaction expected to be completed by the end of the first quarter of 2024. Tellenes commenced operations in 2017, and Guleslettene began operations in 2021.
