### Evolutionary Timeline of the Equinor Facility at Kårstø Gas Processing Plant, Norway

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: September 2022
   - **Details**: The Kårstø Gas Processing Plant is involved in the HyPilot project, which aims to demonstrate the capability of Hystar’s electrolysers to produce green hydrogen at a lower cost. The facility will host a 1 MW containerised PEM electrolyser, which will operate for 10,000 hours to gather performance data. Equinor's focus within this project is on the electrolyser's ability to operate dynamically, utilizing power from varying offshore wind sources.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: March 2022
   - **Details**: Norway’s state-backed Enova provided a financial support of 38.2 million krone (approximately $3.53 million) to support Hystar’s technology, which is integral to the HyPilot project at the Kårstø facility. This investment is aimed at reducing production costs for green hydrogen.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: September 2022
   - **Details**: The announcement of the HyPilot project, including the commissioning of the PEM electrolyser at the Kårstø Gas Processing Plant, was made. The project is set to commence in the second half of 2023.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: March 2022
   - **Details**: The investment from Enova was highlighted by Minister of Climate and Environment Espen Barth Eide, who emphasized the need for job creation alongside emission reductions through hydrogen production.
