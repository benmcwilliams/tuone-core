### Yara Clean Ammonia Facility Timeline - Kårstø Gas Processing Plant, Norway

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2022
   - **Details:** Yara Clean Ammonia has joined the HyPilot project, focusing on the production of green ammonia. The project involves the commissioning of a 1 MW containerised PEM electrolyser at the Kårstø Gas Processing Plant, aimed at producing green hydrogen efficiently. The electrolyser will operate for 10,000 hours to gather performance data.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
