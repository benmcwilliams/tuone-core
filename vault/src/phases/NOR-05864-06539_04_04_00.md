### Hystar Facility Evolution Timeline at Kårstø Gas Processing Plant, Norway

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2022
   - **Details:** Hystar is involved in the HyPilot project, which includes the commissioning of a 1 MW containerised PEM electrolyser at the Kårstø Gas Processing Plant. The electrolyser aims to produce green hydrogen efficiently, with a focus on demonstrating lower production costs. The project will run the electrolyser for 10,000 hours to gather performance data.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** March 2022
   - **Details:** Norway’s state-backed Enova provided a financial support of 38.2 million krone (approximately $3.53 million) to Hystar for the development of its technology. This investment is aimed at reducing production costs and supporting the green hydrogen initiative.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2022
   - **Details:** The HyPilot project was announced, marking a significant collaboration among Hystar, Yara Clean Ammonia, Equinor, and Gassco. The project aims to showcase Hystar’s electrolysers and their ability to produce green hydrogen.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** March 2022
   - **Details:** The investment from Enova was highlighted by Minister of Climate and Environment Espen Barth Eide, who emphasized the need for job creation alongside emission reductions through hydrogen production.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** September 2022
   - **Details:** The facility operates as part of a collaborative project (HyPilot) involving multiple companies (Hystar, Yara, Equinor, and Gassco), indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership by Hystar.
