### Hystar Facility Evolution Timeline - Høvik, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03/2023**: Hystar announced plans for a fully automated 4 GW electrolyzer factory in Høvik, Norway, set to be operational by 2025, with construction starting in early 2024.
- **08/2024**: Hystar awarded ThyssenKrupp Automation Engineering the contract to supply an automated electrolyzer manufacturing line for its 4.5 GW factory in Høvik, which will improve efficiency and increase capacity from 100 MW.
- **11/2024**: Hystar launched a 1.5 MW green hydrogen field pilot project in collaboration with Equinor, Yara Clean Ammonia, Gassco, and ABB, aimed at gathering performance data for its containerized PEM electrolyzer unit.
