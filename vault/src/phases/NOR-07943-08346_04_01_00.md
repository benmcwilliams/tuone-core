### Evolutionary Timeline of the BKK Facility in Mongstad, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: The BKK facility is involved in the production of liquid green hydrogen (LH2) as a carbon-neutral fuel.
- **Capacity and Operations**: The facility is part of a project that aims to establish a viable LH2 supply chain and bunkering platform, specifically for maritime applications. The hydrogen produced will be used to fuel a new roll-on/roll-off demonstration ferry named Topeka, which is designed to operate on liquid green hydrogen.
