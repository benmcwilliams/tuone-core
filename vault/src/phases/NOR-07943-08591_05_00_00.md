### Evolutionary Timeline of the DL Engineering & Construction Facility in Mongstad, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: September 2024**: DL Engineering & Construction, in collaboration with DL Energy and Norsk Kjernekraft, is involved in a preliminary feasibility study for the construction and operation of Small Modular Reactors (SMRs) in the industrial area of Mongstad. The study will investigate the deployment of four US X-Energy XE-100 reactors, which have an electrical output of 320 MW, with potential expansion to 1 GW depending on future demand. The XE-100 is a high-temperature gas-cooled reactor that can be scaled into a four-pack 320 MWe power plant.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: September 2024**: The project receives funding from the Korea Overseas Infrastructure & Urban Development (KIND) Corporation, which supports the feasibility study. This funding is part of KIND's 2024 grant cycle, where the project was selected among 15 proposals.
