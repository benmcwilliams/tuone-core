### Norsk Kjernekraft Facility Timeline - Mongstad, Norway

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is focused on the development and operation of Small Modular Reactors (SMRs), specifically the deployment of four US X-Energy XE-100 reactors.
   - The initial electrical output is planned to be 320 MW, with potential expansion to 1 GW depending on future demand.
   - The XE-100 reactor is a high-temperature gas-cooled reactor with a thermal output of 200 MWt or an electrical output of 80 MWe, capable of being scaled into a four-pack 320 MWe power plant.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - In September 2024, South Korea's KIND Corporation awarded funding to support a preliminary feasibility study for the SMR project in Mongstad. This funding is part of KIND's initiative to support global Public-Private Partnerships (PPP).
   - The study will assess various factors including site land area, electricity market, regulatory framework, investment costs, and technical analysis.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **September 2024:** Announcement of funding from South Korea for the preliminary feasibility study of the SMR project in Mongstad. The study is expected to be completed by 2025, which will inform the next steps in project development.
