### Evolutionary Timeline of the X-energy Facility in Mongstad, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2024**: X-energy is exploring the feasibility of constructing a nuclear power plant at the Mongstad oil refinery. The proposed technology is the Xe-100, a Generation IV advanced small modular reactor (SMR) designed to operate as a standard 320 MWe four-pack power plant or in units of 80 MWe. The Xe-100 utilizes TRISO fuel, which enhances safety by preventing meltdown and is suitable for various applications, including mining and heavy industry.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2024**: Amazon announced its investment in X-energy, aiming to deploy up to 5 GW of small modular reactors in the USA by 2039. This investment is expected to bolster the feasibility of the nuclear power plant project in Mongstad.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2024**: An agreement was reached to conduct a feasibility study for the nuclear power plant at Mongstad, with plans to potentially build the facility by the mid-2030s, contingent on political support.
- **June 2024**: The Norwegian government appointed a committee to review the establishment of nuclear power in the country, with a report due by April 2026.

#### 4. Labour
