### Evolutionary Timeline of Halden Kjernekraft AS Facility in Halden, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2024**: Halden Kjernekraft AS was established to investigate the construction of a nuclear power plant based on small modular reactors (SMRs) at Halden, where a research reactor previously operated. The facility aims to produce nuclear energy using SMRs, with each reactor having a generating capacity of 300 MWe.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2024**: Norsk Kjernekraft, the parent company of Halden Kjernekraft AS, indicated plans to finance the construction of SMR power plants in collaboration with capital-strong industry and solid financial players. Specific investment amounts or subsidies related to the Halden facility have not been detailed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2024**: The establishment of Halden Kjernekraft AS was announced, marking a significant milestone in the investigation of nuclear power in Halden.
- **June 2024**: The company is actively preparing license applications in accordance with national regulations and international standards, following the International Atomic Energy Agency's approach for milestones.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2024**: The establishment of the Halden facility is expected to create hundreds of jobs, as indicated by the CEO of Norsk Kjernekraft, Jonny Hesthammer. The facility aims to attract a high number of employees necessary for the construction and operation of the plant.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2024**: Halden Kjernekraft AS is a joint venture established by Norsk Kjernekraft, Østfold Energi, and the municipality of Halden. This indicates that the facility is not owned exclusively by one entity but is a collaborative effort among multiple stakeholders.
