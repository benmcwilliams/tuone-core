### Evolutionary Timeline of the Aquila European Renewables Income Fund Facility in Oyfjellet, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2021**: The Oyfjellet facility, also known as The Rock, is a 400MW onshore wind project located in Norway. A wholly-owned subsidiary of Aquila European Renewables Income Fund (AERIF) holds a 13.7% interest in the project.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2021**: AERIF announced a bond refinancing for the Oyfjellet wind farm, comprising a US private placement (USPP) of €235m and a green bond of €80m, totaling €315m on a 100% interest basis. This refinancing was pursued to provide immediate access to debt financing at attractive terms and to fund remaining construction and other project costs.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2021**: AERIF increased its commitment to The Rock by up to €35.6m to provide bridge financing due to conditions in the existing debt financing agreement that prevented the draw-down of senior debt.
- **September 2021**: The bond refinancing was concluded, marking an important milestone for the project as it advanced closer to completion.
