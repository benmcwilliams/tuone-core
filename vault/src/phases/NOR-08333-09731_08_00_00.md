### Evolutionary Timeline of the Petoro Facility in Bergen, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2022**: Petoro, along with TotalEnergies, Shell, and ConocoPhillips, is involved in the Trollvind initiative, which aims to develop a 1GW floating offshore wind farm in the Troll oil field area, approximately 65km west of Bergen. The project is expected to generate 4.3 terawatt-hours of electricity annually starting in 2027. The wind farm will provide power to the Troll and Oseberg oil fields and the Kollsnes plant, addressing electricity shortages in the Bergen area.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2022**: The partners in the Trollvind project indicate that the development will not require additional financial support and estimate that the wind farm can deliver power at a cost of less than Nkr 1 (€0.095) per kilowatt-hour. An investment decision is anticipated in 2023.

- **June 2022**: The Trollvind initiative is announced, with a study initiated to explore the feasibility of the floating offshore wind farm. The project is positioned to support Norway's goal of reducing emissions from the oil and gas sector by electrifying existing installations.
