### Evolutionary Timeline of the Shell Facility in Troll Area, Norway

   - **Date**: June 2022
   - **Details**: The Trollvind floating offshore facility is planned to be built in the Troll area, approximately 65 km west of Bergen. The facility aims to supply clean energy to the Troll and Oseberg offshore fields through an onshore connection point, contributing to the electrification of oil and gas installations.
