### Evolutionary Timeline of the Norsk Hydro Facility in Flekkefjord, Norway

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 02-07-2018
   - **Details:** Norsk Hydro will receive electricity from the 208MW Tonstad wind farm, which is under development. The wind farm will consist of 51 Siemens Gamesa turbines and is designed to supply energy to Norsk Hydro's aluminium plants in Norway.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 02-07-2018
   - **Details:** The Tonstad wind farm project is developed by Engie, with financial structuring support from SUSI Partners. The SUSI Renewable Energy Fund 2 will be the majority investor, holding an 80% stake in the project, while Engie retains a 20% stake.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 02-07-2018
   - **Details:** A 25-year agreement was established for the sale of all output from the Tonstad wind farm to Norsk Hydro, running from 2020 to 2045. Hydro Energi, a wholly owned subsidiary of Norsk Hydro, will manage the energy supply and operational aspects.
