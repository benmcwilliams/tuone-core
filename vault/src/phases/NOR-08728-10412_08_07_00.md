#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: Wergeland Group enters a long-term partnership with Equinor as the preferred supplier for the fabrication of foundations and assembly of turbines for floating offshore wind. The facility at Wergeland Base is designated to serve as a factory for offshore wind, focusing on the Utsira Nord project, which has a total capacity of 1.5 GW, divided into three sites of 500 MW each. The concrete foundations for the wind farm will be produced in Norway.
