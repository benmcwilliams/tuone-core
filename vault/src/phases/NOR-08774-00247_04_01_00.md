### Evolutionary Timeline of the ExxonMobil Facility in Slagen, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2022**: ExxonMobil is studying the potential to repurpose its oil and gas terminal in Slagen for producing and distributing green hydrogen. The facility is currently powered by hydroelectricity and has the capacity to produce up to **20,000 metric tons of green hydrogen** annually. Additionally, it has the capability to distribute up to **100,000 metric tons of green ammonia** per year.
