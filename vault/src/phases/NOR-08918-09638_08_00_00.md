### Evolutionary Timeline of Odfjell Oceanwind Facility in Barents Sea, Norway

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **December 2024**: Odfjell Oceanwind, as part of a consortium, submitted a license application for the GoliatVIND floating offshore wind project. The facility is planned to produce 75 MW of energy through five 15 MW wind turbines installed on Odfjell Oceanwind’s Deepsea Star semi-submersible floating foundation. The project will be located in the Barents Sea, at a depth of 300 to 400 meters.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **December 2024**: The consortium, which includes Odfjell Oceanwind, is working to secure a license by 2025 to ensure competitiveness globally and to meet Enova’s deadlines.
