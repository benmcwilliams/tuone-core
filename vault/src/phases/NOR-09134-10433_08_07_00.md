## NOR-09134-10433

### Evolutionary Timeline of WindWorks Jelsa Facility in Jelsa, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **11-10-2022**: WindWorks Jelsa (WWJ) and Implenia announced plans to build a facility in Jelsa, Norway, for the production of concrete platforms for floating wind turbines. The facility will cover an area of 800,000 square meters and aims to produce large concrete substructures for offshore wind farms.
- **18-08-2023**: The facility aims to build 70 floating offshore wind turbines per year, targeting a total output of 1 gigawatt (GW) annually, sufficient to power approximately 4 million homes.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **18-08-2023**: Implenia committed to two small investment tranches in 2023 and 2024 as part of the project’s initial "pre-production" phase, which will last from 2023 to 2026. The financial details of these investments were not specified, but they are described as limited.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **11-10-2022**: A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) was signed between Implenia and WindWorks Jelsa to develop and operate the facility.
- **18-08-2023**: An investment and shareholder agreement was signed, confirming the development of the facility. The project will be implemented in several phases from 2023 to 2032, with the first phase focusing on pre-production.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or job loss figures related to the investments or expansions at the WindWorks Jelsa facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **18-08-2023**: WindWorks Jelsa was established in 2020 by NorSea, Suldal municipality, and Ryfylke IKS, with Norsk Stein as a partner. Following the recent agreements, both Implenia and NorSea will hold approximately 41% each as equal minority shareholders in WindWorks Jelsa, indicating that the facility is not under full ownership by any single entity. 

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic decisions regarding the WindWorks Jelsa facility, highlighting its focus on floating offshore wind technology and collaborative investment efforts.
