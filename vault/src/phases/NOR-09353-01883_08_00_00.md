   - **Date:** 03-04-2020
   - **Details:** The Buheii onshore wind farm, developed by RES, has a production capacity of 79.8 MW and is equipped with 19 Vestas V150 turbines. Once fully operational, it is expected to generate clean energy and offset 15,000 tonnes of carbon emissions annually.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 03-04-2020
   - **Details:** Financial details regarding the sale of the Buheii wind farm to Macquarie’s Green Investment Group (GIG) have not been disclosed. However, the project is part of GIG's strategy to meet the growing demand for affordable power in Norway's energy-intensive industrial sector.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 03-04-2020
   - **Details:** RES sold the Buheii wind farm to GIG. As part of the agreement, RES will provide construction management and asset management services for the wind farm. The project is set to supply clean power to Eramet Norway until 2038.
