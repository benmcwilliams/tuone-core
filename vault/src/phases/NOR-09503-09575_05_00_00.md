### Evolutionary Timeline of the Norsk Kjernekraft Facility in Skien, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2024**: Norsk Kjernekraft announced its focus on off-grid Small Modular Reactor (SMR) projects. The company plans to build three or four SMRs, each with a capacity of 300 MWe, to deliver a total of 900-1200 MWe of electricity (7.5-10 TWh annually). This capacity is intended to meet the energy needs of a new data center being constructed by Google in Skien, as well as support a green electrolysis factory for hydrogen and provide heat to local industries.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2024**: Norsk Kjernekraft indicated that financing for the SMR projects would occur in collaboration with capital-strong industry partners and solid financial players. The company is preparing license applications in accordance with national regulations and international standards, although specific investment amounts or subsidies were not detailed in the article.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2024**: Norsk Kjernekraft is in the planning stages for the construction of SMR power plants in connection with the Google data center. The company has submitted proposals to the Ministry of Oil and Energy for assessments into the construction of power plants based on multiple SMRs in various municipalities, including Aure, Heim, and Vardø. They are also investigating a site in Øygarden municipality for a potential nuclear power plant comprising up to five SMRs.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2024**: The establishment of the SMR power plants is expected to create many jobs, both directly for the operation of the power plants and indirectly for the industries that will be built in connection with the nuclear power facilities.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2024**: Norsk Kjernekraft aims to build, own, and operate the SMR power plants in Norway in collaboration with power-intensive industries. The article does not specify whether Norsk Kjernekraft will have exclusive ownership of the facility or if it will be a joint venture with other partners.
