### Evolutionary Timeline of the Norwegian Hydrogen Facility in Ørskog, Norway

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: June 2023
   - **Details**: Norwegian Hydrogen announced plans for a 270MW green hydrogen production facility in Ørskog. The facility is expected to produce 40,000 tonnes per year (tpa) of green hydrogen. Initially, the plant was allocated 20MW of electrolytic capacity, with an application for an additional 250MW capacity pending.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Date**: June 2023
   - **Details**: The project is supported by Provaris Energy, which will assist in the transport of hydrogen through compressed solutions. The collaboration aims to facilitate both local and European hydrogen demand.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Date**: June 2023
   - **Details**: The announcement of the facility marks a significant milestone in the development of large-scale green hydrogen production in Norway. An initial memorandum of understanding between Norwegian Hydrogen and Provaris Energy was signed in January 2023 to identify potential sites and conduct technical analyses for production and export.
