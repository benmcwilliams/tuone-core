### Evolutionary Timeline of the Vår Energi Facility in Goliat, Norway

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: Vår Energi is collaborating with Odfjell Oceanwind and Source Galileo to pursue a pilot project for floating offshore wind at the Goliat oil platform. The Goliat platform is currently electrified and powered from shore through a power cable with a capacity of 75MW. The GoliatVind project aims to demonstrate new offshore wind technology and may contribute up to 75MW of installed capacity through the addition of three to five floating wind turbines, each with a capacity of 15MW. Current operations on Goliat require about 50MW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: The GoliatVind project is in the early phase of development, with multiple factors being studied before a concept selection and development decision can be made. This includes regulatory framework conditions and potential incentives from authorities. Vår Energi is exploring opportunities to utilize the existing power cable and capacity on the platform to support the development of renewable power generation.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023**: Announcement of the GoliatVind pilot project for floating offshore wind, which is currently in the early phase of development. The project aims to increase renewable power generation in the Finnmark region and contribute to overall power generation in the Hammerfest area.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
