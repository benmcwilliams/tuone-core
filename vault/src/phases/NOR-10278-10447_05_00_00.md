- **Date**: November 2024
  - **Details**: X-energy is developing the Xe-100, a Generation IV advanced small modular reactor (SMR) designed to operate as a standard 320 MWe four-pack power plant or in scalable units of 80 MWe. The reactor utilizes tri-structural isotropic (TRISO) particle fuel, which enhances safety by preventing meltdown. The Xe-100 is suitable for various applications, including mining and heavy industry, and is designed for accelerated construction and predictable costs.
