## POL-00379-00383

### Evolutionary Timeline of Impact Clean Power Technology Facility in GigafactoryX, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2022**: Impact Clean Power Technology (ICPT) announces the construction of GigafactoryX, which will initially produce 2 GWh of battery systems per year starting in 2024, with a planned expansion to 5 GWh. The facility will focus on battery systems for heavy-duty commercial vehicles and stationary energy storage, utilizing highly automated production lines powered by a photovoltaic system and second-life battery systems.
- **December 2022**: ICPT signs a supply contract with Pepper Motion to manufacture complete battery systems for electric buses and trucks, emphasizing the robustness and modularity of the battery units.
- **November 2023**: ICPT extends its battery supply contract with Solaris, committing to supply next-generation HE 100 systems from 2024 to 2027, which offer improved energy density and performance.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2022**: ICPT contracts Teamtechnik to build an automated assembly line for battery systems at GigafactoryX, indicating a significant investment in production capabilities.
- **July 2022**: ICPT enters an agreement with InoBat Auto for the development and supply of prismatic battery cells, which will be integrated into the battery systems produced at GigafactoryX.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2022**: Announcement of the construction of GigafactoryX.
- **July 2023**: Scheduled start of assembly work for the automated production line at GigafactoryX.
- **2024**: Planned operational start of GigafactoryX with an initial production capacity of 2 GWh.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2022**: The construction and operation of GigafactoryX are expected to create jobs, although specific job creation numbers have not been disclosed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2022**: ICPT is identified as the Polish manufacturer of battery systems, indicating full ownership of the GigafactoryX facility. There is no mention of joint ventures or shared ownership in the articles.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Impact Clean Power Technology facility in GigafactoryX, Poland, based on the provided articles.
