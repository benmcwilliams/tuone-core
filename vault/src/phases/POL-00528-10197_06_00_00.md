### Evolutionary Timeline of the Tauron Facility in Jaworzno, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2020**: Tauron announced the construction of a 5 MW solar project on a former coal-fired power station site in Jaworzno. This project is part of Tauron's Green Return program, which aims to transition from coal to renewable energy sources. The facility is expected to be operational within the same year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2020**: The project is co-financed by the Provincial Fund for Environmental Protection and Water Management (WFOŚiGW) in Katowice, which provided a preferential loan of PLN 3 million (approximately $717,000). This loan allows for early repayment, with savings on interest to be reinvested into further green projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2020**: Despite challenges posed by the Covid-19 pandemic, Tauron confirmed the commencement of construction for the solar project. The engineering, procurement, and construction contract was tendered in September 2020, with Tauron Serwis and Tauron Dystrybucja Serwis units securing the deal.
