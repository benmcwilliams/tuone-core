### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hydrogen Utopia International Facility in Konin, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-09-2021**: Hydrogen Utopia International (HUI), in collaboration with German chemical company Linde and Powerhouse Energy (PHE), announced plans for a facility in Konin, Poland. The facility aims to convert 40 tons of plastic waste into 58 MWh of renewable electricity and clean hydrogen per day. The hydrogen produced will be utilized locally as fuel for city buses, heavy-duty vehicles, and passenger cars.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-09-2021**: The project is subject to funding approval from the EU's Just Transition Fund, which supports energy transition initiatives in regions historically reliant on fossil fuels. The Polish government has committed to match any European funding for the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07-09-2021**: The announcement of the feasibility study for the facility marks a significant milestone, indicating that HUI and its partners are in the final stages of contract negotiations for syngas production.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
