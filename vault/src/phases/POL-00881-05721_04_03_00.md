### Evolutionary Timeline of the ORLEN Facility in Poznań, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: The ORLEN facility is involved in hydrogen technology, specifically producing automotive-grade hydrogen.
- **Production Capacity**: As of 2024, ORLEN has delivered over 90,000 kg of automotive-grade hydrogen to various cities, including Poznań, Wałbrzych, Płock, and Włocławek.
- **Infrastructure Development**: The facility has initiated the launch of public hydrogen refuelling stations and is piloting hydrogen fuel in public road transport across Poland.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Funding Secured**: The Clean Cities program, which is part of ORLEN's hydrogen initiatives, has attracted approximately EUR 76 million in non-repayable funding from the EU's Connecting Europe Facility, along with PLN 20 million from Poland's National Fund for Environmental Protection and Water Management.
- **Significant Grant**: The third phase of the Clean Cities program received the largest hydrogen-related grant ever awarded under the Connecting Europe Facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
