### Evolutionary Timeline of the SK Group Facility in Silesia, Poland

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: 01-04-2019
     - SK Innovation announced plans to construct a new battery separator plant in Silesia, Poland. The facility will produce lithium-ion separators (LiBS) and ceramic coated separators (CCS) with an estimated annual production capacity of 340 million m² for LiBS and 130 million m² for CCS.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Date**: 01-04-2019
     - The total investment for the facility is reported to be 335 million euros. This investment is part of SK Innovation's broader strategy to increase its production capacity to meet the growing demand from the automotive industry.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Date**: 01-04-2019
     - Construction of the plant is scheduled to begin in the third quarter of 2019, with mass production expected to start in the third quarter of 2021.

4. **Labour**
   - **Date**: Not explicitly mentioned in the articles.
     - While specific job creation numbers are not provided, the construction and operation of the facility are expected to create jobs in the region, contributing to local employment
