### Evolutionary Timeline of the SK Group Facility in Stalowa Wola, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **12/2022**: SKC, a subsidiary of SK Group, announced the construction of a copper foil factory in Stalowa Wola, Poland, aimed at producing copper foils for electric vehicle batteries. The facility is set to have an initial production capacity of **50,000 tonnes per year**. There is potential for future expansion to **150,000 tonnes per year**, although no specific timeline for this expansion was provided.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12/2022**: The total investment for the facility is reported to be **900 billion won** (approximately **693 million euros**). This investment is made by SKC and its wholly-owned subsidiary, SK Nexilis.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2022**: Announcement of the factory construction in Stalowa Wola, with operations expected to commence in the **second half of 2024**.
- **02/2023**: Confirmation that the facility is under construction and will start operations as planned in the second half of 2024.
