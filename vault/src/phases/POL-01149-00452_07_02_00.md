### Evolutionary Timeline of the Leapmotor Facility in Tychy, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2024**: Stellantis is set to begin production of the T03 small electric car for Leapmotor at its Tychy facility, utilizing semi-knocked-down technology. The facility also produces the Fiat 600e.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2023**: Stellantis invested €1.5 billion in Leapmotor, acquiring a 20% stake in the company. This investment is tied to the establishment of a joint venture, Leapmotor International, which will have exclusive rights to export, sell, and manufacture Leapmotor products outside Greater China.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2024**: Reports indicate that production for the T03 could start as early as the second quarter of 2024.
- **September 2024**: Leapmotor International is set to commence operations in Europe, with a market launch planned in several countries including France, Italy, Germany, and Spain.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2024**: The establishment of the joint venture and the planned production of electric vehicles are expected to create new job opportunities, although specific numbers have not been disclosed.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **October 2023**: The joint venture, Leapmotor International, is established with Stellantis holding a 51% stake and Leapmotor holding 49%. This indicates a joint ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by either company.
