### Evolutionary Timeline of Stellantis Group Facility in Tychy, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2022**: Stellantis announces plans to produce a compact electric SUV, the Jeep Avenger, at its Tychy plant, targeting an electric range of 400 kilometers.
- **10/2022**: The Jeep Avenger is presented at the Paris Motor Show, featuring a 115 kW electric motor and a 54 kWh battery, with production set to begin in early 2023.
- **02/2023**: Production of the Jeep Avenger officially starts at the Tychy plant.
- **03/2023**: Deliveries of the Jeep Avenger commence.
- **09/2023**: Stellantis begins series production of the Fiat 600e at the Tychy plant, which is exclusively electric.
- **06/2024**: Production of the Leapmotor T03 begins at the Tychy facility, with mass production expected to start in September 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **10/2023**: Stellantis invests €1.5 billion in Leapmotor, acquiring a 20% stake and establishing a joint venture, Leapmotor International, to manufacture and sell Leapmotor products outside Greater China.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **12/2022**: Orders for the Jeep Avenger open, with 20,000 units sold by early April 2023.
- **01/2023**: Full lineup of the Jeep Avenger becomes available for orders.
- **03/2023**: Jeep Avenger deliveries begin.
- **09/2023**: Production of the Fiat 600e starts.
- **06/2024**: Leapmotor T03 production commences, with mass production set for September 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- **02/2023**: The start of production for the Jeep Avenger is expected to create new jobs at the Tychy facility, although specific numbers are not disclosed.
- **06/2024**: The commencement of Leapmotor T03 production is anticipated to further increase job opportunities at the Tychy plant.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **10/2023**: Stellantis holds a 51% stake in the joint venture Leapmotor International, while Leapmotor holds 49%. The Tychy facility operates under Stellantis' ownership, producing vehicles for multiple brands, including Jeep and Fiat, as well as for the joint venture with Leapmotor.
