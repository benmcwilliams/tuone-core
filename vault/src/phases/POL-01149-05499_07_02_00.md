### Evolutionary Timeline of the Leapmotor International Facility in Tychy, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2024**: Production of the Leapmotor T03 commenced at Stellantis’ factory in Tychy, Poland, during the third week of June (10-16 June). The T03 is an electric city car with a targeted WLTP range of 265 km. Mass production is scheduled to start in September 2024. Leapmotor International plans to introduce at least one new model each year, with the Leapmotor A12, a compact SUV, also set to enter production at the Tychy plant.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2024**: The choice of Stellantis’ Polish factory for Leapmotor International's European production was influenced by cost considerations, with production costs per vehicle at approximately €400-500, significantly lower than the €1,000 cost in Italy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2024**: Announcement of the commencement of production for the Leapmotor T03.
- **September 2024**: Scheduled launch of the Leapmotor T03 in the European market.
- **Future Plans**: The Leapmotor A12 is expected to follow as the brand’s second model produced at the Tychy facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2024**: The establishment of the facility and the commencement of production are expected to create jobs, although specific job creation numbers have not been detailed in the article.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2024**: Leapmotor International operates as a joint venture (51/49) led by Stellantis with Leapmotor, managing operations in Europe, India, Asia Pacific (excluding Greater China), the Middle East, Africa, and South America. The joint venture has exclusive rights to export, manufacture, and sell Leapmotor products outside Greater China.
