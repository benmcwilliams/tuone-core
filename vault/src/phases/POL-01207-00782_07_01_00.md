- **July 2019**: Toyota Motor Manufacturing Poland (TMMP) announced plans to produce a second hybrid transmission (e-CVT) at its Walbrzych plant starting in 2021. The facility will also manufacture one of the electric motors integral to Toyota’s hybrid electric powertrain system. This addition aligns with the company's strategy to localize the production of hybrid vehicle components in Europe.
