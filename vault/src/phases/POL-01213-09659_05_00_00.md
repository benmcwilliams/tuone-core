### Evolutionary Timeline of the Orlen Synthos Green Energy Facility in Warsaw, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: Orlen Synthos Green Energy announced seven potential sites for the construction of the country’s first small modular reactors (SMRs). The selected sites include Warsaw, among others, and the company plans to build at least one modern and completely safe nuclear block in Poland by 2030.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: Orlen Synthos Green Energy, along with US financial institutions EXIM Bank and DFC, signed a letter of intent for financing, which includes USD4 billion in financial support for the implementation of the BWRX-300 SMRs in Poland. This financing is specifically tied to the construction of the first two power plants to be developed by Orlen Synthos Green Energy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023**: The company announced the selection of seven optimum locations for further geological surveys for the construction of SMRs, with a commitment to thoroughly analyze the possibility of building the first small modular block near these locations over the next two years.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2023**: While specific job creation numbers were not detailed, the announcement of potential site selections and the planned construction of SMRs indicates a future increase in job opportunities related to the development and operation of these facilities.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2023**: Orlen Synthos Green Energy is a joint venture between chemical producers Synthos Green Energy (SGE) and PKN Orlen, indicating that the facility is not solely owned by one entity but rather a collaboration between the two companies.
