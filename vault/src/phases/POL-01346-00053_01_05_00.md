### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ascend Elements Facility in Zawiercie, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2024**: Ascend Elements and Elemental Strategic Metals announced the establishment of a joint venture named AE Elemental in Zawiercie, Poland, for the recycling of electric vehicle batteries. The facility is designed to process up to **12,000 tonnes of batteries per year**, equivalent to approximately **28,000 electric car batteries**.
- **September 2024**: The recycling plant officially opened in Zawiercie, confirming the previously announced processing capacity. The facility will discharge, dismantle, and crush EV batteries to produce "black mass," which contains valuable materials for battery production. An expansion to process black mass into battery-grade materials is planned to be operational by **2026**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2024**: Both partners in the joint venture, Ascend Elements and Elemental Strategic Metals, agreed to invest in the extraction of lithium from black mass, indicating a commitment to further development in the circular economy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2024**: Announcement of the joint venture AE Elemental and plans for the recycling plant in Zawiercie.
- **September 2024**: Official opening of the recycling plant in Zawiercie, Poland.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
