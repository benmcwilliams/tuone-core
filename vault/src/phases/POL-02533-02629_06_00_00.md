### Evolutionary Timeline of the Aliplast Facility in Lublin, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2021**: Aliplast, a Polish aluminum system provider, installed a photovoltaic sunshade on the facade of its factory in Lublin. This sunshade is equipped with perovskite solar cells and is noted as the world's first building-applied photovoltaics (BAPV) system utilizing perovskite technology. The system is designed to enhance energy efficiency and provide thermal comfort by automatically adjusting its alignment based on live weather data and sun-tracking capabilities.
