## POL-03058-00582

### Evolutionary Timeline of Northvolt Facility in Gdańsk, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2018**: Northvolt announces plans to establish a battery assembly plant in Gdańsk, Poland, with an initial production capacity of **10,000 battery modules per year**, set to begin in **2019**. The facility will focus on complete energy storage solutions, including battery cells, electronic control systems, and thermal management systems.
- **February 2021**: Northvolt announces a **$200 million investment** to expand the Gdańsk facility into Europe’s largest factory for energy storage solutions (ESS). The expansion will occur in two phases, with the first phase targeting **5 GWh** of energy storage systems per year, expected to start construction in **autumn 2021** and production in **2022**. The total capacity after the second phase could reach **12 GWh** per year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2021**: Northvolt commits to a **$200 million investment** for the expansion of the Gdańsk facility, which will enhance its capacity for energy storage systems.
- **July 2020**: Northvolt secures **$1.6 billion** in loans, part of which will support the Gdańsk facility, although the primary focus is on the Swedish gigafactory.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2018**: Announcement of the Gdańsk facility's establishment.
- **2019**: Production begins at the Gdańsk facility.
- **February 2021**: Announcement of the expansion plan for the Gdańsk facility, with construction of the first phase expected to start in **autumn 2021** and production in **2022**.

#### 4. Labour
- **October 2018**: The initial workforce for the Gdańsk facility is set at **100 employees**.
- **February 2021**: Northvolt plans to recruit **500 more employees** for the expanded operations in Gdańsk.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The Gdańsk facility operates as a **joint venture** between Northvolt and South Bay Solutions, with Northvolt being the primary entity managing the facility.
