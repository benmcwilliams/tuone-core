### Evolutionary Timeline of GRI Renewable Industries Facility in Gdańsk, Poland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** January 2023
   - **Details:** GRI Renewable Industries, in collaboration with the Polish Industrial Development Agency (ARP), announced plans to build an offshore wind tower factory in Gdańsk. The facility will have the capacity to manufacture more than 200 offshore towers annually, specifically designed for turbines with capacities of 14MW and above.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** January 2023
   - **Details:** The estimated investment for the facility is over €200 million. This investment aims to meet the increasing demand for wind towers in Poland's offshore wind market and other markets in the Baltic Sea.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** January 2023
   - **Details:** A joint venture was launched to construct the factory, which is planned to start production in 2025. The factory will be located on the island of Ostrów in Gdańsk, featuring direct access to a mooring quay.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** January 2023
   - **Details:** The facility is expected to create approximately 400 jobs upon the start of production.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** January 2023
   - **Details:** The facility will be operated as a joint venture between GRI Renewable Industries and the Polish Industrial Development Agency (ARP), indicating that GRI does not have exclusive ownership of the facility.
