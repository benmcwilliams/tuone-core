### Evolutionary Timeline of PGE Baltica Facility in Gdańsk, POL

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 28-01-2020
   - **Details:** PGE Group has secured environmental permits for two offshore wind projects, Baltica 2 and Baltica 3, with a total capacity of up to 2.5GW. Baltica 2 will have a capacity of 1498MW, and Baltica 3 will have a capacity of 1045MW. The wind farms are expected to generate electricity starting in 2026. The turbines will have capacities of 10MW or more, depending on technological advancements.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 20-12-2019
   - **Details:** PGE and Orsted signed a preliminary agreement for Orsted to acquire a 50% stake in the Baltica 2 and 3 projects, indicating a significant investment in the development of these offshore wind farms.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 28-01-2020
   - **Details:** The environmental permits awarded allow PGE to commence work on the technical aspects of the Baltica 2 and 3 projects. The projects are located approximately 30 km north of the shoreline at Ustka and Leba in Pomerania.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Details:** The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss related to the PGE Baltica facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 20-12-2019
   - **Details:** PGE has established a joint venture with Orsted for the Baltica 2 and 3 projects, with Orsted acquiring a 50% stake. PGE intends to develop offshore projects independently in the future, having built competencies through partnerships.
