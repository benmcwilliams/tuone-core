### Evolutionary Timeline of the PGE Group Facility in Gdańsk, Poland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** January 2020
   - **Details:** PGE Group has secured environmental permits for two offshore wind projects, Baltica 2 and Baltica 3, with a total capacity of up to 2.5 GW. Baltica 2 will have a capacity of 1498 MW, and Baltica 3 will have a capacity of 1045 MW. The wind farms are expected to generate electricity starting in 2026. The turbines will have capacities of 10 MW or more, depending on technological advancements and the selected make and model.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2019
   - **Details:** PGE and Orsted signed a preliminary agreement for Orsted to acquire a 50% stake in the Baltica 2 and 3 projects, indicating a significant investment and partnership for the development of these offshore wind farms.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** January 2020
   - **Details:** The regional director for environmental protection in Gdańsk issued a decision on environmental conditions, allowing PGE to commence work on the technical aspects of the Baltica 2 and 3 projects. All three special purpose companies (EWB1, EWB2, EWB3) have location permits for constructing offshore farms in designated areas of the Baltic Sea.

   - **Details:** PGE Group is not the exclusive owner of the facility. It has entered into a joint venture with Orsted, with Orsted acquiring a 50% stake in the Baltica 2 and 3 projects.
