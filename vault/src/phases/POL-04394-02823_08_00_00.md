#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2023**: EDPR inaugurated the Przykona PV Plant in Greater Poland Voivodeship, which has an installed capacity of 200 MWp (153 MWac). This facility is noted as one of EDPR’s largest photovoltaic (PV) plants in Europe. Additionally, the company implemented the first wind and solar hybrid park in Poland by integrating the 45 MWp Konary photovoltaic plant with the 79 MW Pawlowo wind farm.
