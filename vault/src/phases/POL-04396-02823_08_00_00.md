### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDPR Facility in Lesser Voivodeship, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2023**: EDPR inaugurated the Konary photovoltaic plant with an installed capacity of 45 MWp, integrated into the existing 79 MW Pawlowo wind farm, creating Poland's first wind and solar hybrid park. This facility represents a significant technological advancement in combining solar and wind energy production.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2023**: EDPR ramped up its investments in Poland, which included the inauguration of the Przykona PV Plant (200 MWp) and the Chotków PV Plant (40 MWp). The company has also invested significantly in distributed solar projects, contributing over 95 MWp of solar capacity in Poland.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **2023**: EDPR was awarded a long-term Contract-for-Difference (CfD) for the Recz solar project, which will have an installed capacity of 20 MWp (14 MWac). This project is expected to be operational by 2025 and marks EDPR's first project in the West Pomeranian Voivodeship.
