### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDPR Facility in Przykona, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2022**: Construction of the Przykona solar photovoltaic (PV) plant began. The facility is designed to generate approximately 220 GWh of clean energy annually, utilizing nearly 308,000 bifacial solar panels that maximize electricity production by harnessing solar radiation from both sides.
- **June 2023**: The facility commenced its first energy generation.
- **September 2023**: The Przykona facility was officially inaugurated, marking it as EDPR's largest solar project in Europe and the second largest in Poland. The cumulative solar power capacity of EDPR in Poland exceeded 280 MWp, representing over 90% of its total solar capacity in Europe.
