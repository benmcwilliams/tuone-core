### Evolutionary Timeline of the Energia Przykona Facility in Przykona, POL

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 05-09-2019
   - **Details:** Energia Przykona, a unit of private equity group NeoInvestments, announced plans for a 600MW solar complex in the Przykona commune. The facility is designed to produce 630GWh of energy annually. The first phase will start with a 200MW batch, with plans for energy storage as well.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 05-09-2019
   - **Details:** The project is a partnership involving Energia Przykona, China Sinogy Electric Engineering, and Strategic Swiss Partners. The consortium is preparing to secure grid connection points and building permits, indicating significant investment in the facility's development.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 05-09-2019
   - **Details:** The consortium announced that the first two phases of the solar complex are ready to secure grid connection points, with building permits expected in early 2020. The anticipated operational start date for the facility is March 2022, following a construction period of 18 to 24 months after obtaining permits.
