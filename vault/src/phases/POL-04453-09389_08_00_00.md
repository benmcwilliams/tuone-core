## POL-04453-09389

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lewandpol Group Facility in Kleczew, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2023**: Lewandpol Group announced plans for a 200MW solar and wind energy project in Kleczew, which will include 193MWp of photovoltaic (PV) panels and up to 19.2MW of wind turbines in the first phase. The project is set to be one of the largest renewable energy parks in Central and Eastern Europe and the first large-scale project in Poland to combine solar and wind energy. Future expansions may increase the solar capacity to approximately 250MWp, along with additional wind turbines and energy storage facilities.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2023**: Lewandpol Group secured a loan agreement worth PLN90 million (US$20.7 million) from a fund partly managed by the state-owned Polish Development Fund. This loan is supplemented by a senior financing package of PLN776 million (US$178.8 million) from a consortium of banks including ING, PKO BP, and mBank.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2023**: Announcement of the project and securing of financing.
- **March 2023**: Huawei was confirmed as the technology supplier for the PV and wind energy park, marking the project as the largest renewable energy park in Central and Eastern Europe.
