### Evolutionary Timeline of ST3 Offshore Facility in Szczecin, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2015**: The ST3 Offshore facility officially opened for business. It was designed for the serial production of jacket foundations and transition pieces for offshore wind turbines and substations, focusing on foundations for water depths of up to 60 meters.
- **2015**: The facility was awarded a contract for the Race Bank offshore wind farm with Ørsted, delivering 91 foundations for the 580 MW project in the UK.
- **2016**: ST3 Offshore secured another contract with Ørsted for the production and delivery of 20 suction bucket jacket foundations for the Borkum Riffgrund 2 offshore wind farm in the German North Sea.
- **2017**: The company first filed for restructuring and bankruptcy but withdrew the bankruptcy petition in early 2018 after receiving positive feedback from the District Court regarding its restructuring application.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2012**: The company was founded as Bilfinger Mars Offshore, a joint venture between Bilfinger and MARS Investment Fund, with the construction of the facility starting the same year.
- **2016**: ST3 Holding, owned by the Munich VTC group, acquired a 20% stake in the company, while the remaining 80% was held by Poland’s state-owned Mars investment fund.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2020**: ST3 Offshore applied for bankruptcy after failing to secure new offshore wind contracts and a search for a new backer.
- **March 31, 2020**: The Szczecin-Centrum District Court officially declared ST3 Offshore bankrupt.
- **February 2021**: The company was put up for sale for the first time, with an asking price of PLN 234,680,000 (approximately EUR 52 million).
- **March 20, 2023**: Vestas SPV (RPGZ XXVIII sp. z o.o.) won the auction for ST3 Offshore, purchasing it for PLN 175.5 million (EUR 37.3 million).

#### 4. Labour
- **2020**: The bankruptcy filing and subsequent financial struggles likely resulted in job losses, although specific numbers are not provided in the articles.
- **2023**: The acquisition by Vestas may lead to job creation, but specific details on job numbers post-acquisition are not mentioned.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **2012-2016**: Initially a joint venture (Bilfinger Mars Offshore) with Bilfinger and MARS Investment Fund.
- **2016**: ST3 Holding acquired a 20% stake, indicating a partial ownership structure.
- **2023**: Vestas SPV acquired full ownership of ST3 Offshore through the auction process.
