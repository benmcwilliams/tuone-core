### Evolutionary Timeline of the Equinor Facility in Lipno, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: Equinor announced that its second solar plant, Zagórzyca, has started test production, doubling its solar capacity in Poland to around 120MW. The first solar plant, Stępień, is already in commercial production, and another plant, Lipno, is expected to be commissioned in 2024.
- **June 2024**: Equinor began commercial operations at the Lipno solar plant, which has a capacity of 53MW. The facility is developed and operated by Wento, Equinor's 100% subsidiary. Lipno is part of a broader portfolio that now includes three PV plants and one wind park in Poland, totaling 200MW.
