### Evolutionary Timeline of the Wento Facility in Zagórzyca, Poland

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **June 2023**: The Zagórzyca solar plant, developed and operated by Wento, a 100% subsidiary of Equinor, began test production. The facility has a production capacity of 60MW, which contributes to Equinor's total solar capacity in Poland, now approximately 120MW. The plant consists of 111,000 solar panels spread over an area of around 55 hectares.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **2021**: Equinor acquired Wento and its pipeline of solar projects totaling 1.6GW. This acquisition laid the groundwork for the development of the Zagórzyca facility and other solar projects in Poland.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **June 2023**: The Zagórzyca solar plant was inaugurated, marking it as Equinor's second solar plant in Poland. The first plant, Stępień, was already in commercial production, and another plant, Lipno, is scheduled for commissioning in 2024.

4. **Labour**
   - **June 2023**: Approximately 500 people were involved in the construction of the Zagórzyca solar plant, indicating significant job creation during the development phase.

5. **Ownership Structure**
   - **2021**: Wento is a 100% subsidiary of Equinor, indicating that Equinor has full ownership of the Zagórzyca facility.
