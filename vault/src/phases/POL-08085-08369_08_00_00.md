### Evolutionary Timeline of the Baltic Power Facility in Łeba, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2022**: Baltic Power, a joint venture between PKN Orlen and Northland Power, announced plans for the construction of an operations and maintenance (O&M) base for the 1.2 GW offshore wind farm. The facility will include a social and office building, a warehouse, and a trans-shipment quay for crew transfer vessels. The project is expected to support the operation of approximately 70 wind turbines located about 22 kilometers north of the Baltic coastline.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06/2021**: Poland’s Energy Regulatory Office awarded a contract for difference (CfD) to the Baltic Power project, which is a significant investment milestone for the 1.2 GW offshore wind farm.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/2022**: Baltic Power launched a tender for the O&M base, with the contract expected to start in July 2022 and run until November 2024. The offshore wind farm is anticipated to enter construction in 2023 and be completed in 2026.
- **05/2022**: Baltic Power initiated a prequalification process for tendering offshore construction services and equipment, with construction activities planned between 2024 and 2026.
