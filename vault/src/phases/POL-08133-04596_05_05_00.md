### Evolutionary Timeline of the Bechtel Facility on the Baltic Sea Coast, Poland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2022
   - **Details:** Bechtel, in collaboration with Westinghouse Electric Company, is preparing a front-end engineering design for Poland's first civil nuclear power plant, which will be located on the Baltic Sea coast. The facility will be equipped with three Westinghouse AP1000 reactors.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** April 2022
   - **Details:** Bechtel signed memoranda of understanding (MoUs) with 12 Polish companies to support the development of the nuclear power plants. This collaboration is expected to involve significant investment in local expertise and resources.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** April 2022
   - **Details:** The signing of MoUs marks an important milestone in the project, indicating a commitment to move forward with the development of the nuclear power plant.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** April 2022
   - **Details:** Bechtel's project is expected to create thousands of skilled jobs for Polish workers, as the company plans to engage a large number of Polish companies as subcontractors for the construction and operation of the facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** April 2022
   - **Details:** The facility will not be exclusively owned by Bechtel; it will involve collaboration with Westinghouse Electric Company and various Polish subcontractors, indicating a joint venture approach to ownership and operation.
