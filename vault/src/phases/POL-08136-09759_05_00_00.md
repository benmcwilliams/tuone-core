### Evolutionary Timeline of Polskie Elektrownie Jądrowe Facility in Pomerania, POL

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03/2023**: Polskie Elektrownie Jądrowe (PEJ) is in discussions with Westinghouse and Bechtel for the design of Poland's first large-scale nuclear reactor. The facility will utilize Westinghouse's AP1000 technology, with plans for a total capacity of 3750 MWe, comprising three reactors.
- **07/2023**: PEJ applied for a decision-in-principle for the construction of the first large-scale nuclear power plant in Poland, which will consist of three Westinghouse AP1000 reactors.
- **08/2023**: PEJ submitted an application for a siting decision for the nuclear power plant, marking a key milestone in obtaining necessary administrative approvals for construction.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **11/2022**: The Polish government selected Westinghouse for the construction of the first nuclear power plant, indicating state support for the investment.
- **07/2024**: PEJ, in collaboration with Bank Gospodarstwa Krajowego, initiated a study to assess the potential of the Polish industry to participate in the nuclear power plant project, indicating ongoing investment in local supply chains.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03/2023**: PEJ, Westinghouse, and Bechtel are expected to sign a Memorandum of Understanding to formalize their cooperation for the nuclear power plant project.
- **04/2023**: PEJ applied for a decision-in-principle for the construction of the nuclear power plant, which was granted by the Ministry of Climate and Environment.
- **08/2023**: PEJ applied for a siting decision, a crucial step in the administrative process for the nuclear power plant's construction.
- **2026**: Scheduled start of construction for the first nuclear power plant.
- **2033**: Expected commissioning of the first reactor with a capacity of 1-1.6 GWe.

#### 4. Labour
- **03/2023**: The project is expected to create jobs in the region, particularly in the construction and operational phases of the nuclear power plant, although specific job creation numbers have not been detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **03/2023**: PEJ is a special-purpose vehicle that is 100% owned by the State Treasury, indicating full state ownership of the facility.
