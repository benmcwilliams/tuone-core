### Evolutionary Timeline of Toshiba Energy Systems & Solutions Facility in Lubiatowo-Kopalina, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2024**: Toshiba Energy Systems & Solutions (Toshiba ESS) is collaborating with local firms to support Poland's first nuclear power plant, which will utilize Westinghouse's AP1000 reactor technology. The facility is set to feature three AP1000 reactors, with the aim for the first reactor to enter commercial operation in 2033.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2022**: The Polish government selected the Westinghouse AP1000 reactor technology for the Lubiatowo-Kopalina site. An agreement for the delivery of the plant was signed in May 2023 by Westinghouse, Bechtel, and Polskie Elektrownie Jądrowe (PEJ).

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2024**: Toshiba ESS signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with Rockfin sp zoo and EthosEnergy Poland SA to explore collaboration in equipment supply and maintenance services for the nuclear project. This partnership is a significant step in the implementation of the new nuclear project at Lubiatowo-Kopalina.
- **July 2022**: The Ministry of Climate and Environment issued a decision-in-principle for PEJ to construct the plant.
- **September 2022**: An engineering services agreement was signed between Toshiba ESS, its US affiliate, and Bechtel to collaborate on the supply of steam turbines and generators for the nuclear power plant.
