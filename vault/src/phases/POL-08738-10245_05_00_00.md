### Evolutionary Timeline of Toshiba International (Europe) Ltd Facility in Lubiatowo-Kopalina, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2024**: Toshiba International (Europe) Ltd (TIL) is collaborating with Rockfin sp zoo and EthosEnergy Poland SA to explore equipment supply and maintenance services for Poland's first nuclear power plant, which will utilize the Westinghouse AP1000 reactor technology. The facility aims to feature three AP1000 reactors, with the first expected to enter commercial operation in 2033.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2022**: The Polish government selected the Westinghouse AP1000 reactor technology for the Lubiatowo-Kopalina site. An agreement for the plant's delivery was signed in May 2023 by Westinghouse, Bechtel, and Polskie Elektrownie Jądrowe (PEJ). This indicates significant investment in the nuclear project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: An agreement was signed for the delivery of the nuclear plant, marking a key milestone in the project.
- **July 2023**: The Ministry of Climate and Environment issued a decision-in-principle for PEJ to construct the plant.
- **September 2023**: An engineering services agreement was signed between Toshiba ESS, PEJ, Westinghouse, and Bechtel to finalize a site-specific design for the plant.
