### Evolutionary Timeline of the GE Facility in Porabka Zar, Poland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** April 2023
   - **Details:** GE Renewable Energy secured a contract to upgrade the Porabka Zar pumped hydro storage plant, which has an installed capacity of 500MW. The project involves replacing four 125MW pumped turbines and generators, as well as upgrading the excitation and governing systems. The existing 40-year-old turbines will be replaced with new high-efficiency pumped turbines and motor generators, along with new stay rings and stay vanes specifically designed for the facility. The plant is noted as the second-largest pumped storage power plant in Poland, providing ancillary services to the electricity system.
