### Evolutionary Timeline of the Polimex Mostostal Facility in Osieki Lęborskie, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2024**: Polimex Mostostal, in consortium with GE Vernova, is selected as the general contractor for the onshore connection infrastructure for the 1.5 GW Baltica 2 offshore wind farm. The facility will develop a 275/400 kV high-voltage transformer substation and onshore export cable lines necessary for transmitting power to the Polish Power System. Construction is set to begin in 2024, with testing and activation scheduled for 2026 and 2027.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2024**: The contract for the construction of the onshore infrastructure is part of the larger investment in the Baltica offshore wind farm project, which includes both Baltica 2 and Baltica 3, aiming for a combined capacity of 2.5 GW. The specific financial details of the investment have not been disclosed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2024**: PGE Group and Ørsted announce the selection of Polimex Mostostal and GE Vernova as contractors for the onshore infrastructure of the Baltica 2 project. Construction is planned to start in 2024, with the first electricity expected to be fed into the Polish Power System in 2027.

#### 4. Labour
