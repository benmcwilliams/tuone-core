### Evolutionary Timeline of Geotermia Kolo Facility in Kolo, Poland

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **2021**: Drilling of the first well for the geothermal heating plant commenced.
   - **2022**: Drilling of the second well was completed.
   - **01/2024**: Construction of the geothermal heating plant was completed. The facility includes two wells (production and reinjection), a heating plant building, a filter station, a discharge pool, and a water treatment plant.
   - **03/2024**: The geothermal heating plant officially started operations. The facility is expected to improve energy efficiency and reduce CO2 emissions while providing heating for domestic use and supporting local recreation and tourism.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Mid-2022**: Contracts for funding the geothermal heating project were signed, with a total investment of PLN 108 million (approx. USD 27.4 million). This investment included PLN 28,409,462.50 in non-returnable subsidies from the utility company MZEC, a loan from the National Fund for Environmental Protection and Water Management, and the own funds of Geotermia Kolo.
