### Evolutionary Timeline of the Goldbeck Facility in Zwartowo, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2022**: Goldbeck Solar switched on a **204 MW solar park** in Zwartowo, marking the first phase of a **290 MW scheme** that will include an additional **86 MW facility**. The solar park spans **300 hectares**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2022**: The investment for the 204 MW plant amounted to **PLN 635 million** (approximately **$128.8 million**). The project was supported by loans from the **European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD)**, **Bank Pekao S.A.**, and **PKO Bank Polski**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Q1 2021**: Construction of the solar park began.
- **September 2022**: The facility was officially switched on and became operational.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
