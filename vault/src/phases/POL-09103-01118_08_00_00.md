### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Zopowy, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **30-05-2022**: Iberdrola operates the Zopowy wind farm with a production capacity of 30MW. This facility is part of Iberdrola's broader portfolio in Poland, which includes the Korytnica I wind farm (82.5MW) and the newly constructed Korytnica II wind farm (50.5MW).
- **03-01-2023**: The Korytnica II wind farm, with a capacity of 50.4MW, begins commissioning. This facility will increase Iberdrola's total onshore wind power capacity in Poland to 163MW. The Korytnica II facility is equipped with 14 Vestas V126 wind turbines.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **24-05-2022**: Iberdrola announces the construction of the Korytnica II wind farm, which is part of its strategy to expand its clean energy portfolio in Poland. The project is supported by a power purchase agreement with Statkraft and a contract for difference secured in an auction held in June 2021.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **24-05-2022**: Construction of the Korytnica II wind farm begins, with an expected operational start in Q1 2023.
- **03-01-2023**: The commissioning of Korytnica II starts, with full operation scheduled for the first quarter of 2023. The facility successfully exports its first power on 20 December 2022, two weeks ahead of schedule.
