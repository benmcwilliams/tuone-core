- **February 2023**: A letter of intent was signed between ZE PAK, Polska Grupa Energetyczna (PGE), and Korea Hydro & Nuclear Power to explore the feasibility of constructing a nuclear power plant in Patnow, utilizing South Korean-designed APR1400 reactors.
- **April 2023**: The project is still in the assessment phase, with no specific production capacity mentioned yet.
