### Evolutionary Timeline of the ZE PAK Facility in Patnow, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: ZE PAK, in collaboration with Polska Grupa Energetyczna and Korea Hydro & Nuclear Power, signed a letter of intent to explore the feasibility of constructing a nuclear power plant in Patnow, utilizing South Korean-designed APR1400 reactors. This facility is intended to replace decommissioned coal-fired units as part of ZE PAK's transition to cleaner energy sources.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2023**: ZE PAK and Polska Grupa Energetyczna submitted an application to the Ministry of Climate and Environment for a decision-in-principle regarding the construction of a nuclear power plant in Patnow, which would consist of at least two APR1400 reactors. This application marks a significant step in securing the necessary approvals for investment in the facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2023**: Announcement of the letter of intent to cooperate on the nuclear power plant project in Patnow.
