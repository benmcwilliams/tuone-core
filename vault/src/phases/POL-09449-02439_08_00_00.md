### Evolutionary Timeline of Siemens Gamesa Facility in Baltica 2, Poland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **April 2023:** Siemens Gamesa secured a contract to supply 107 wind turbines, each with a capacity of 14MW, for the Baltica 2 offshore wind project. The total capacity of the project is 1.5GW. The facility is located 40km from the northern coast of Poland and is expected to begin operations in 2027. The project will generate enough clean energy to power 2.4 million Polish homes and displace 5.4 million tonnes of carbon emissions annually. Siemens Gamesa has also established a hub in Gdansk to manage operations in the Baltic Sea.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **April 2023:** The contract for the Baltica 2 project is part of a broader strategy to develop offshore wind power in Poland, with Siemens Gamesa also having three preferred supplier agreements in place, totaling 1.79GW capacity. The project is positioned as the largest offshore wind investment in Poland and one of the largest globally.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
