## POL-09449-09542

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Navantia and Windar Facility in Baltica 2, Poland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is involved in the production of monopile foundations for the Baltica 2 offshore wind farm, which has a capacity of 1.5GW. The entire Baltica Offshore Wind Farm is planned to have a total capacity of 2.5GW.
   - Navantia and Windar have secured a contract for the supply of 77 monopiles for turbines and offshore substations, with production set to begin in 2024.
   - Windar plans to establish a separate facility in Szczecin, Poland, for manufacturing towers, expected to be operational by 2026.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The investment for the Baltica 2 project is significant, being described as the biggest energy project to date based on renewables in Poland. Specific financial figures for the Navantia and Windar contract were not disclosed, but the overall project is part of a larger investment strategy by PGE and Orsted.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **October 2023:** PGE and Orsted finalized a contract with Steelwind Nordenham for the supply of monopiles, marking the completion of Tier 1 contracts for the offshore part of the Baltica 2 project.
   - **2024:** Production of monopiles by Navantia and Windar is scheduled to begin.
   - **2025:** Production of monopile foundations by Steelwind is set to start in the second half of the year.
   - **2026:** The new Windar facility for manufacturing towers in Szczecin is expected to be ready.

4. **Labour:**
   - The project is anticipated to create new job opportunities, particularly as Polish companies express interest in contributing to the Baltica 2 project. The Suppliers Day organized by PGE and Orsted indicated a strong local engagement, which may lead to job creation in the supply chain.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility operated by Navantia and Windar is part of a strategic alliance, indicating a joint venture structure for the production of monopiles for the Baltica 2 project. Specific ownership percentages were not detailed in the article. 

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Navantia and Windar facility in the context of the Baltica 2 offshore wind farm project in Poland.
