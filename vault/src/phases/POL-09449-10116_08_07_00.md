1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Production:** Steelwind Nordenham specializes in the manufacturing of monopiles, which are foundations for offshore wind turbines.
   - **Production Capacity:** The facility will deliver 34 monopile foundations for the Baltica 2 wind farm, which consists of 107 turbines and four offshore substations.
   - **Updates:** Production of the monopiles is scheduled to start in the second half of 2025.

   - **Investment Details:** The Baltica 2 project is part of a larger investment of 1.5GW capacity, which is the biggest renewable energy project to date in Poland. The total capacity of the entire Baltica Offshore Wind Farm is planned to be 2.5GW by the end of the decade.
