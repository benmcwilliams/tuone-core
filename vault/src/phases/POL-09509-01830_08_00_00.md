### Evolutionary Timeline of the Northland Power Facility in Leba, Poland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** January 2024
   - **Details:** Construction has begun on an offshore wind service base in Leba, Poland. This facility will support the offshore development of the Baltic Power project, a joint venture between Orlen Group and Northland Power. The facility will serve as a mother port for vessels transporting equipment and technical personnel for the maintenance of the 1200MW Baltic Power offshore wind farm. The service base will include a farm spare parts warehouse and a workshop, and it will be operational for a minimum of 25 years.
