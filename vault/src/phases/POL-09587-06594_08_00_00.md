### Evolutionary Timeline of the Orlen Group Facility in Świnoujście, Poland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2023 (Construction Start)
   - **Details:** Orlen Group is constructing an onshore turbine installation terminal at the Świnoujście port. The facility will support the Baltic Power and other offshore wind farm projects. Once completed, it will facilitate the installation of over 80 offshore wind turbines annually. The terminal will handle and store essential components for offshore wind turbines, including towers, blades, and nacelles, and will also accommodate offshore substation topsides weighing up to 24,000 tonnes. The site covers almost 20 hectares and will include communication infrastructure, workshop, administration, and office buildings.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** August 2023 (Conditional Investment Decision)
   - **Details:** The project received a conditional investment decision, marking a significant step in the development of the facility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 12-09-2023 (Announcement)
   - **Details:** Orlen Group announced the start of construction for the turbine terminal, with completion expected in late 2024 or early 2025. The contractor for the project is Budimex.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** Expected upon completion (2024/2025)
   - **Details:** The facility is projected to employ approximately 100 staff members once operational.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Details:** The facility is owned exclusively by the Orlen Group, as there is no mention of joint ventures or partnerships in the provided information.
