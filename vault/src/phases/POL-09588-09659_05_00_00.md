### Evolutionary Timeline of Orlen Synthos Green Energy Facility in Dąbrowa Górnicza, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: Orlen Synthos Green Energy (OSGE) announced the selection of Dąbrowa Górnicza as one of seven potential sites for the construction of small modular reactors (SMRs) based on GE Hitachi Nuclear Energy's BWRX-300 technology. The company holds exclusive rights to this technology in Poland.
- **December 2023**: OSGE received decisions-in-principle from the Ministry of Climate and Environment for the construction of a total of 24 BWRX-300 reactors at six locations, including Dąbrowa Górnicza. This marks a significant step towards the establishment of a fleet of zero-emission, stable energy sources.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: OSGE announced a letter of intent for financing with US financial institutions, including EXIM Bank and DFC, indicating a commitment to financial support for the construction of the SMR plants.
- **December 2023**: The decisions-in-principle received from the Ministry of Climate and Environment are part of a broader investment strategy aimed at deep decarbonization of the Polish economy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023**: OSGE shortlisted seven locations for further geological surveys for SMR construction, including Dąbrowa Górnicza.
- **Late April 2023**: OSGE submitted applications for decisions-in-principle for the construction of plants at six locations, excluding Warsaw.
- **December 2023**: The Ministry issued decisions-in-principle for the construction of 24 BWRX-300 reactors at the six selected locations, including Dąbrowa Górnicza.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2023**: OSGE indicated that the construction of SMRs would create jobs and stimulate local economies, although specific job creation numbers were not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2023**: Orlen Synthos Green Energy is identified as a joint venture between Synthos Green Energy (SGE) and PKN Orlen, indicating that the facility is not solely owned by one entity but rather a collaboration between these two companies.
