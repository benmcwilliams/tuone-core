## POL-09588-09907

### Evolutionary Timeline of SK Hi-Tech Battery Materials Poland sp. z o.o. Facility in Dąbrowa Górnicza, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: The facility specializes in the manufacturing of lithium-ion battery separators (LiBS), which are essential components in lithium-ion batteries used for electric vehicles (EVs).
- **Production Capacity**: As of the end of 2022, SK IET's total production capacity for LiBS was 1.53 billion square meters. The facility in Dąbrowa Górnicza is expected to fulfill at least 40% of the EU's LiBS demand by 2025, contributing to approximately 70% of the anticipated total capacity in the EU.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Amount**: A significant investment program totaling $1.7 billion is underway, with a green financing package of $300 million arranged by the International Finance Corporation (IFC) to support the final two phases of this investment.
- **Financing Breakdown**: The financing includes a $200 million loan from IFC, $30 million from Bank Pekao S.A., $20 million from Intesa Sanpaolo, and $50 million from PKO Bank Polski S.A. This financing adheres to green financing principles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Announcement Date**: The partnership and financing details were announced on May 29, 2023.
- **Construction and Expansion**: The investment program will support the construction and expansion of the LiBS production facility, aligning with the growing demand for EV batteries in Europe.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The facility is expected to generate hundreds of jobs in Poland, contributing to the local economy and providing opportunities outside the historically coal-dependent mining industry.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: SK Hi-Tech Battery Materials Poland sp. z o.o. is a subsidiary of Korea's SK IE Technology Co., Ltd (SK IET), indicating that it operates under the ownership of SK IET, but it is not exclusively owned as it is a subsidiary.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic initiatives surrounding the SK Hi-Tech Battery Materials facility in Dąbrowa Górnicza, Poland, highlighting its role in the evolving landscape of the electric vehicle value chain in Europe.
