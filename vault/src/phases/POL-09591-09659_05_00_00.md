### Evolutionary Timeline of the Orlen Synthos Green Energy Facility in Stawy Monowskie, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: Orlen Synthos Green Energy (OSGE) announced the selection of seven potential sites for the construction of small modular reactors (SMRs) in Poland, including Stawy Monowskie. The SMRs will be based on GE Hitachi Nuclear Energy's BWRX-300 technology, for which OSGE holds exclusive rights in Poland.
- **December 2023**: OSGE submitted applications to the Ministry of Climate and Environment for decisions-in-principle for the construction of plants at six locations, including Stawy Monowskie. The ministry issued decisions-in-principle for the construction of a total of 24 BWRX-300 reactors at these locations.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: OSGE announced a letter of intent for financing from US financial institutions, including EXIM Bank and DFC, which declared USD4 billion in financial support for the implementation of the BWRX-300 reactors in Poland. This funding is crucial for the construction of the first two power plants to be developed by OSGE.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023**: OSGE shortlisted seven locations for further geological surveys, including Stawy Monowskie, and planned to analyze the feasibility of building SMRs at these sites over the next two years.
- **December 2023**: The Ministry of Climate and Environment issued decisions-in-principle for the construction of 24 BWRX-300 reactors at six selected locations, marking a significant milestone in the administrative process for nuclear power investments in Poland.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2023**: OSGE indicated that the construction of SMRs would create jobs and stimulate local economies, although specific job creation numbers were not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2023**: Orlen Synthos Green Energy is identified as a joint venture between Synthos Green Energy (SGE) and PKN Orlen, indicating that the facility is not solely owned by one entity but rather a collaboration between these two companies.
