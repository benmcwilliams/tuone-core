### Evolutionary Timeline of the PEC Facility in Sochaczew, Poland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **November 2023:** Announcement of the construction of a geothermal heating plant in Sochaczew. The facility will utilize geothermal energy to provide a clean heat source for the city, aiming to connect seven out of eight PEC boiler rooms to a new heat distribution network.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **November 2023:** The project is backed by PLN 62 million in funding, which includes PLN 33.4 million as a non-repayable grant and PLN 26.8 million as a loan. Additionally, PEC is expected to recover approximately PLN 14.26 million in VAT for the project. The city council is set to decide on a tripartite agreement involving PEC and NFOSiGW to ensure funding support if needed.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **July 2023:** Confirmation from Minister Maciej Malecki that the geothermal project has passed all evaluation stages and will be implemented.
   - **2018:** PEC was one of the first four cities in Poland to receive co-financing for geothermal projects, which included a PLN 10 million subsidy for drilling the first research well.
