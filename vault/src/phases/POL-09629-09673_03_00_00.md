### Evolutionary Timeline of the PGE Facility in Żarnowiec, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2022**: PGE announced plans to build an 820MWh hybrid energy storage system at the Żarnowiec pumped-storage plant. This project will integrate the existing 716MW pumped-storage plant with a new battery energy storage system (BESS), resulting in a total hybrid installation capacity of 921MW. The facility aims to supply energy to approximately 200,000 households for at least 5 hours and provide balancing services for the Polish power system.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2022**: PGE is applying for European funding and seeking investors to finance the hybrid energy storage project. This project is part of PGE's strategic goal to achieve 800MW of new energy storage installed capacity in Poland by 2030.
