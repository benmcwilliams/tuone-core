### Evolutionary Timeline of PGE Energia Odnawialna Facility in Gubin, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: PGE Energia Odnawialna announced the modernization of the Gubin Hydroelectric Power Plant, which was built in 1905. The facility has three hydroassemblies with a Kaplan turbine and a total capacity of about 1.1 MW. The modernization will increase its production capacity by 10%, raising it from 3,634 MWh to approximately 4,000 MWh, which will meet the electricity demand of nearly 2,000 households.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The facility received nearly PLN 2 million in co-financing from the EEA fund for the modernization project. This is in addition to over PLN 6 million previously received from the same source. The funding structure for the modernization project consists of 85% from the EEA financial mechanism (2014-2021) and 15% from the state budget.
