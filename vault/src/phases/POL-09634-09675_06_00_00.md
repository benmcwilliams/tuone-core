#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **05-06-2019**: PGE Energia Odnawialna announced the development of a 5 MW solar project in Świętokrzyskie Osiek, which is expected to come online in mid-2022. The facility will utilize approximately 16,000 solar modules and will occupy 10 hectares of land owned by Grupa Azoty Siarkopol. This project marks the first photovoltaic installation in Poland built specifically for the needs of an industrial customer.
