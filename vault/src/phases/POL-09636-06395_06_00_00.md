### Evolutionary Timeline of Zespół Elektrowni Pątnów-Adamów-Konin SA Facility in Turek, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2018**: ZE PAK SA announced plans to install a 70 MW solar photovoltaic (PV) capacity at a depleted area of the Adamów brown coal mine in Turek. This project was intended to replace the generation capacity lost with the closure of the Adamów coal-fired power station, which had a capacity of 600 MW.
- **March 2021**: ZE PAK SA secured a 15-year power purchase agreement (PPA) for a 70 MW solar plant planned for Brudzew, Greater Poland Voivodeship. This project is part of the company's transition towards renewable energy.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2018**: The total investment for the solar project at the Adamów mine was estimated at approximately PLN 200 million ($52.5 million). However, no funding had been secured at that time.
- **March 2021**: ZE PAK SA secured a loan of PLN 175 million ($45.5 million) from Polish banks for the solar project in Brudzew.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2018**: Announcement of the solar project at the Adamów mine.
- **March 2021**: Secured a PPA for the solar plant and confirmed financing through a loan, indicating progress towards project implementation.
