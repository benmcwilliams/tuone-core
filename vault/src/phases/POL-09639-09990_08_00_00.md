### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sevivon Facility in Jasna, Poland

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2021:** The Jasna wind farm, developed by PNE and its Polish subsidiary Sevivon, has a total production capacity of 132MW. It consists of 39 turbines: 22 Vestas V126 turbines with a capacity of 3.45MW each and 17 Vestas V126 turbines with a capacity of 3.3MW each. The facility is in the testing and commissioning phase, with completion expected in the coming months.
