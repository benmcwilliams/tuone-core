### Evolutionary Timeline of Polskie Elektrownie Jądrowe Facility in Krokowa, POL

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2023**: Polskie Elektrownie Jądrowe (PEJ) submitted an application for a decision-in-principle to construct Poland's first large nuclear power plant. The project aims to utilize US AP1000 reactor technology with a maximum total installed capacity of up to 3750 MWe. The first reactor is expected to have a capacity of 1.0-1.6 GWe, with construction starting in 2026 and commissioning planned for 2033.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: The application for the decision-in-principle is part of PEJ's strategy to secure necessary administrative approvals for the nuclear power plant project, which aligns with Poland's Energy Policy until 2040 and the EU's climate policy. The project is supported by US financial institutions, including EXIM Bank and DFC, which have signed letters of intent for financing.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2023**: PEJ's application for the decision-in-principle marks a significant step towards obtaining administrative approvals for the construction of the nuclear power plant. The project is part of a broader plan announced in September 2021 to build six large pressurized water reactors by 2040.
