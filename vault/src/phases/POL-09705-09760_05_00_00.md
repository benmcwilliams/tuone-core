### Evolutionary Timeline of the Polskie Elektrownie Jądrowe (PEJ) Facility in Lubiatowo, Poland

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2023**: Polskie Elektrownie Jądrowe (PEJ) is in the process of planning a nuclear power plant in Lubiatowo, which will utilize APR1400 reactor technology. The facility is expected to have a maximum total installed capacity, although specific figures are not detailed in the article. The plant is part of Poland's broader strategy to reduce reliance on coal and aims to start construction in 2026, with the first reactor expected to be operational by 2033.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2023**: PEJ, a joint venture formed by ZE PAK and Polska Grupa Energetyczna (PGE), applied for a decision-in-principle regarding the construction of the nuclear power plant. This application is a crucial step in securing further administrative permits and indicates significant investment in the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2022**: The Polish government selected Westinghouse's AP1000 technology for the country's first nuclear power plant, which is part of the broader initiative that includes the PEJ facility.
- **August 2023**: PEJ received a decision-in-principle from the Ministry of Climate and Environment, confirming that the investment project aligns with public interest and state energy policies.
