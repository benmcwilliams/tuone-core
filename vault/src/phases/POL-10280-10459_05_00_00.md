#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2023**: The Zakład Unieszkodliwiania Odpadów Radiotworniczych (ZUOP) facility is identified as responsible for the proper handling of low and intermediate-level radioactive waste from various sectors including nuclear power, industry, medicine, and research and development. The facility will not accept high-level waste or used nuclear fuel.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2023**: ZUOP is designated as the investor and operator for a new repository intended for the storage of low and intermediate-level waste. The facility will utilize experiences from the existing National Radioactive Waste Repository (NRWR) in Różan and will follow examples from proven facilities in Spain and France.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2023**: The Polish Ministry announces the search for a new repository site, with ZUOP as the operator. The selection process for municipalities to host the new repository is set to begin, with a deadline for applications on November 15, 2023. The selection will depend on local community approval and geological assessments.
