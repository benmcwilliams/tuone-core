### Evolutionary Timeline of the IberBlue Wind Facility in Figueira da Foz, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** February 2023
   - **Details:** IberBlue Wind announced plans to build the Botafogo floating offshore wind farm offshore from Figueira da Foz. The facility will have an installed capacity of 990MW, equipped with 55 wind turbines, each with a capacity of 18MW. The project will cover an area of 359km² and will utilize floating platforms anchored to the seabed, allowing for installation 30-50km from the coast.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** February 2023
   - **Details:** While specific investment amounts were not disclosed in the article, the project is part of IberBlue Wind's broader goal to develop at least 2GW of floating wind power capacity offshore from the Iberian Peninsula. The company is collaborating with local and regional institutions to ensure effective integration of the facility into the region.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** February 2023
   - **Details:** The announcement of the Botafogo facility marks the initiation of the project. The construction timeline and specific milestones were not detailed in the article.

4. **Labour:**

   - **Details:** The project is expected to create job opportunities in the Figueira da Foz region, particularly during the construction and operational phases of the wind farm.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
