### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Setúbal, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09/2022**: Iberdrola began construction on four solar farms in Setúbal, totaling 86 MW of installed capacity. The specific capacities are:
  - Algeruz 2: 27.35 MW
  - Conde: 13.51 MW
  - Alcochete 1: 23.89 MW
  - Alcochete 2: 12.72 MW
  - Notably, Alcochete 1 and 2 will utilize bifacial technology, which enhances efficiency by allowing the PV panels to generate electricity from both sides.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09/2022**: The solar plants were awarded in the 2019 auction, indicating prior investment and planning efforts by Iberdrola to secure these projects.
- **07/2024**: Iberdrola announced the completion of two additional photovoltaic plants, Montechoro I (12 MW) and Montechoro II (25 MW), as part of their ongoing investment in renewable energy in Portugal.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09/2022**: Construction of the four solar farms commenced, with an expected completion date within the same year.
- **07/2024**: The completion of the Montechoro I and II photovoltaic plants was announced, marking significant milestones in Iberdrola's expansion of renewable energy capacity in Portugal.

#### 4. Labour
- **09/2022**: During the construction phase of the solar farms, it was reported that 500 jobs would be created at peak periods. This job creation is tied directly to the construction and operational phases of the solar facilities.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The articles do not specify any joint ventures or partnerships; therefore, it can be inferred that Iberdrola owns the facility exclusively.
