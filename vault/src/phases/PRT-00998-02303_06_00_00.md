## PRT-00998-02303

### Evolutionary Timeline of Hyperion Renewables Facility in Setúbal, PRT

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2022**: Hyperion Renewables launched an EPC tender for the Green H2 Setúbal Project, which will produce green hydrogen with a capacity of 135 kg (1,500 Nm3) per hour. The facility will be powered by a 12 MW solar PV plant. The hydrogen produced will have a purity level greater than 99.999%, suitable for various applications, including sale to off-takers and injection into the Portuguese natural gas distribution network.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **04/2022**: The project has an estimated cost of €11.5 million. It received €5 million in CAPEX support from the EU-funded Operational Programme for Sustainability and Efficient Use of Resources (PO SEUR).

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03/2022**: Hyperion Renewables initiated the EPC tender process for the Green H2 Setúbal Project.
- **04/2022**: Seven companies were selected to compete for the construction of the plant, with evaluations based on technical and economic criteria.
