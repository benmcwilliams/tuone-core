### Evolutionary Timeline of the Bondalti Facility in Sines, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **04-01-2022**: Announcement of the GreenH2Atlantic renewable project, which will produce 100MW of green hydrogen. The facility will be equipped with scalable and fast-cycling 8MW electrolyser modules and will interface with a local hybrid renewable power plant. The project aims to transition the former coal-fired power plant area in Sines into a renewable hydrogen production hub.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **04-01-2022**: The project has received €30 million ($33.9 million) in funding from the European Union’s Horizon 2020 – Green Deal Call research and innovation programme to finance the construction of the 100MW electrolyser plant.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **04-01-2022**: Construction works on the plant are expected to begin in 2023, with operations scheduled to start in 2025, pending approval from relevant authorities.

4. **Labour**
   - No specific job creation or job loss details were mentioned in the article.

5. **Ownership Structure**
