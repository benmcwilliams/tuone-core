## PRT-01432-00716

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Siemens Facility in Terceira, PRT

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 07-09-2020
   - **Details:** Siemens Smart Infrastructure, in partnership with Fluence, was awarded a contract to build a battery-based energy storage system on Terceira Island. The system will have a power capacity of 15MW and is designed to be one of the largest stand-alone battery-based energy storage systems in Europe. It aims to regulate frequency and voltage of the electrical grid, increase supply security, and absorb excess energy from renewable sources. The project also includes plans for an expansion of up to 6MW of renewable energy sources, potentially doubling the island's share of renewables from 20-30% to over 60%.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 07-09-2020
   - **Details:** The project is part of a broader initiative to transition Terceira Island to a more sustainable energy mix, supported by the Portuguese energy provider EDA – Electricidade dos Açores. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the article, but the project is significant for enhancing the island's energy independence and sustainability.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
