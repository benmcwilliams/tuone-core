## PRT-01432-01058

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fluence Facility in Terceira, PRT

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: September 2020
   - **Details**: Fluence, in partnership with Siemens, was awarded a contract to build a battery-based energy storage system on Terceira Island. The system, known as Gridstack, has a power capacity of 15MW and is designed to be one of the largest stand-alone battery-based energy storage systems in Europe. It will regulate frequency and voltage, provide spinning reserve, and store excess energy from renewable sources for use during peak consumption.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: September 2020
   - **Details**: The project is part of a broader initiative to transition Terceira to a more sustainable energy mix, aiming to double the island's share of renewables from 20-30% to over 60%. The implementation of this system is associated with a potential expansion of up to 6MW of renewable resources, including geothermal energy.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: September 2020
   - **Details**: The project is scheduled for completion in 2021. It marks a significant milestone in enhancing the energy independence and sustainability of Terceira Island.
