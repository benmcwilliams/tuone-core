### Evolutionary Timeline of the Siemens Fluence Facility in Terceira, PRT

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-09-2020**: Siemens Smart Infrastructure, in partnership with Fluence, was awarded a contract to build a battery-based energy storage system on Terceira Island. The system, known as Gridstack, has a power capacity of **15MW** and is designed to be one of the largest stand-alone battery-based energy storage systems in Europe. It will regulate frequency and voltage, provide spinning reserve, and store excess energy from renewable sources for later use. The project aims to increase the island's renewable energy share from **20-30% to over 60%**.
