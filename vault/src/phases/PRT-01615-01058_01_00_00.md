### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fluence Facility in Madeira, PRT

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** Fluence, in collaboration with Siemens Smart Infrastructure, is set to develop a 22.5MVA/15.6MWh battery storage system on Madeira Island. This facility aims to enhance energy resilience by integrating renewable energy sources and increasing the share of renewables in Madeira’s energy mix to 50%. The battery storage system will be located next to the Vitoria Thermal Power Plant and is expected to be operational by the second quarter of 2022.
