1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2021
   - **Details:** Siemens Smart Infrastructure and Fluence were selected to develop a 22.5MVA/15.6MWh battery storage system on Madeira Island. This facility aims to enhance energy resilience by integrating renewable energy sources and increasing the share of renewables in Madeira’s energy mix to 50%. The battery storage system will be located next to the Vitoria Thermal Power Plant and is designed to support the island's energy grid stability and quality.
