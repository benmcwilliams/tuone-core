## PRT-01784-01587

### Evolutionary Timeline of EDP Facility in Alqueva, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2019**: EDP proposed a 4MW floating solar installation at the Alqueva dam, featuring 10,000 solar panels across five clusters. The project was designed to float on a HDPE structure and included a 30kV inverter unit.
- **07/2020**: EDP completed the first phase of the 4MW project, which included a 1MW floating solar array with 11,000 solar panels, generating 6 GWh annually.
- **05/2022**: EDP inaugurated a 5MW floating solar array at the Alqueva dam, producing 7.5 GWh per year and supplying power to approximately 1,500 families. The project utilized lithium batteries for energy storage.
- **07/2022**: EDP announced plans to add a 1MW/2MWh battery energy storage system to the existing floating solar array, promoting asset optimization and efficiency.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07/2020**: The 1MW phase of the floating solar project involved significant investment, although specific figures were not disclosed.
- **05/2022**: The total investment for the 5MW floating solar array was reported at €6 million (approximately $6.07 million).
- **04/2022**: EDP won a tender for a 100MW floating solar project at Alqueva as part of a larger auction for 263MW of floating solar capacity across Portugal.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **04/2019**: EDP submitted proposals for the 4MW floating solar project, pending planning approval.
- **07/2020**: EDP finalized the first phase of the 4MW project, marking the completion of the initial 1MW installation.
- **05/2022**: EDP officially launched the 5MW floating solar park, which is noted as the largest floating solar installation in Europe on a reservoir.
- **04/2022**: EDP secured a contract for a 100MW floating solar project, expected to be operational by 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **05/2022**: The 5MW floating solar project is expected to create jobs, although specific numbers were not detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- EDP appears to have full ownership of the Alqueva facility, as there are no mentions of joint ventures or partnerships regarding the ownership of the floating solar projects.
