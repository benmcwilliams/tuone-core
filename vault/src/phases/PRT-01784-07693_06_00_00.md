#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03-06-2019**: EDP-Gestão da Produção de Energia, S.A. announced plans for a 4 MW floating solar plant at the Alqueva water reservoir. The facility will utilize approximately 10,000 PERC half-cell solar modules, each with an output of 385 W, covering an area of 3.9 hectares. The expected annual power production is approximately 6 GWh.
