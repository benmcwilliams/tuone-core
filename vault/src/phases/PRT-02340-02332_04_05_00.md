### Keme Energy Facility Timeline - Sines Industrial and Logistics Zone, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date: 08-02-2022**  
  Keme Energy has contracted 4.8 hectares in the Sines Industrial and Logistics Zone to install a green hydrogen production plant with a total capacity of 2.52 MW. The initial phase of the project, which has already been approved, will have a capacity of 1.26 MW. The production will utilize electrochemical electrolysis and will compress and store the hydrogen gas in PED bottles. The hydrogen produced will be used for industrial applications and electricity production.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date: 08-02-2022**  
  The total investment for the Keme Energy facility is €5.2 million (approximately US$5.95 million). This investment is specifically tied to the establishment of the green hydrogen production plant in Sines.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date: 08-02-2022**  
  Keme Energy's project has been approved and is supported by Aicep Global Parques, the manager of the Sines industrial complex. The project is described as a pilot initiative, with potential for replication in other locations across Portugal, contingent on its success.
