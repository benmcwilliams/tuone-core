1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **May 2022:** EDP (Energias de Portugal) launched Europe's largest floating solar park on the Alqueva dam in Moura, with an installed capacity of 5 megawatts (MW). The facility is designed to produce 7.5 gigawatt/hours (GWh) of electricity annually and is complemented by lithium batteries for energy storage, providing 2 GWh. The solar panels are expected to supply power to approximately 1,500 families, covering a third of the energy needs of the nearby towns of Moura and Portel.
