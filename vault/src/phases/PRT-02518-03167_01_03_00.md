### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lux Optimeyes Energy Facility in Moura, PRT

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Production:** The facility will manufacture flexible photovoltaic solar panels and lithium batteries.
   - **Production Capacity:** Specific production capacity details are not provided, but the facility is expected to create more than 40 direct jobs and 200 indirect jobs related to the promotion, sale, development, and installation of the manufactured solutions.
   - **Facility Background:** The facility occupies the site of the old Moura Solar Factory (MFS), which was operational for 10 years before shutting down in January 2019.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Total Investment:** Approximately €5 million.
   - **Local Government Subsidy:** €2.96 million provided by the local government through the Alentejo 2020 regional funding program.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Announcement and Lease Signing:** The project was officially announced on 24-05-2021, with the signing of the lease for the former MFS facility and a memorandum of understanding for the project execution.
   - **Expected Operation Start:** The new factory is anticipated to begin operations by the end of 2021.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Job Creation:** The project is expected to create more than 40 direct jobs, primarily for former employees of the old Moura Solar Factory, and an additional 200 indirect jobs.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Ownership:** Lux Optimeyes Energy is owned by a consortium that includes the Portuguese tourism sector group Lux, led by Rui Torrão and Paulo Torrão, and energy industry veteran Miguel Matias. The ownership structure indicates a joint venture rather than exclusive ownership by a single entity.
