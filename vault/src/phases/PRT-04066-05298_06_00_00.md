### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hyperion Facility in Evora, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2018**: Hyperion signed a 10-year Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with Axpo Ibéria for a solar project with a capacity of **28.8 MW** located in Evora. This project is part of the Vale de Moura scheme.
- **May 2018**: Construction began on the **28.8 MW (DC)** PV plant, which is expected to produce over **52 GWh** of electricity annually and occupy **55 hectares**.
- **End of 2018**: Construction was expected to be completed by the end of the year, with commercial operations scheduled to start in early **2019**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2018**: The project was acquired by French corporate and investment bank **Natixis** shortly after the signing of the PPA.
- **May 2018**: The project received financing from **Banco BPI**, indicating financial backing for the construction of the facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10-04-2018**: Announcement of the PPA and project acquisition by Natixis.
- **30-05-2018**: Official start of construction on the Vale de Moura project.
- **Early 2019**: Scheduled start of commercial operations for the facility.

- Initially, Hyperion was the developer of the facility. However, after the signing of the PPA in January 2018, the project was acquired by **Natixis**, indicating a shift in ownership structure. The current ownership is a joint venture with Natixis as the major stakeholder.
