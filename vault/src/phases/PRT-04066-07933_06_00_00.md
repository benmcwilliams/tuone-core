### Evolutionary Timeline of the Mirova Facility in Evora, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2018
   - **Details:** Construction began on the Vale de Moura solar park, which is a 28.8 MW (DC) photovoltaic (PV) plant located in Evora, Alentejo, Portugal. The facility is designed to produce more than 52 GWh of electricity annually and occupies 55 hectares.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** May 2018
   - **Details:** The project is backed by Mirova, a unit of the French financial group Natixis. It is notable for being the first solar plant in the Iberian Peninsula built without any subsidies. The project received financing from Portugal’s Banco BPI.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** May 2018
   - **Details:** Construction of the facility commenced, with expectations for completion by the end of 2018 and the start of commercial operations scheduled for early 2019. A 10-year Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) was signed with Axpo Ibéria in January 2018.
