## PRT-04352-02345

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Lightsource Facility in Monforte, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2021
   - **Details:** Lightsource BP, in partnership with Dourogás, is forming a joint venture to invest in solar and green hydrogen infrastructure. The facility will produce green hydrogen powered by up to 200MW of solar projects developed by Lightsource BP, which will supply energy to 130MW electrolysers developed by Dourogás. The green hydrogen produced will be injected into Portugal's gas grid.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2021
   - **Details:** The first project in Monforte will receive a €5 million grant from the EU's Portugal 2020 fund to support early-stage deployment of the green hydrogen initiative. This investment is part of a broader ambition by Lightsource BP to develop 25GW of solar power by 2025.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** December 2021
   - **Details:** The announcement of the joint venture marks the initiation of the project in Monforte, which is Lightsource BP's first green hydrogen partnership.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** December 2021
   - **Details:** The partnership is expected to create over 1000 direct and indirect jobs at its peak, contributing significantly to local employment through the development of the facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** December 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is a joint venture between Lightsource BP and Dourogás, indicating that Lightsource BP does not own the facility exclusively but shares ownership with Dourogás.
