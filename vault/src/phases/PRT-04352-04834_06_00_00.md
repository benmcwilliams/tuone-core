### Evolutionary Timeline of the Dourogás Facility in Monforte, PRT

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2021**: Dourogás, in partnership with Lightsource BP, is set to develop a facility that will utilize 130MW electrolysers to convert water into green hydrogen and oxygen. The facility will be powered by up to 200MW of solar projects developed by Lightsource BP. The green hydrogen produced will be injected into Portugal's gas grid.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2021**: The project in Monforte will receive a €5 million grant from the EU's Portugal 2020 fund to support its early-stage deployment. This investment is part of a broader initiative to explore the potential of eight green hydrogen sites in Portugal.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2021**: The announcement of the joint venture between Lightsource BP and Dourogás marks the initiation of the Monforte project, which is part of a larger strategy to decarbonize the economy through green hydrogen production.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2021**: The partnership is expected to create over 1000 direct and indirect jobs at its peak, highlighting the project's potential impact on local employment.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **December 2021**: The facility is a joint venture between Dourogás and Lightsource BP, indicating that it is not solely owned by Dourogás but rather a collaborative effort between the two companies. 

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic initiatives surrounding the Dourogás facility in Monforte, Portugal, as of December 2021.
