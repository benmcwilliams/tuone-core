## PRT-04390-02823

### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDPR Facility in Ansião, PRT

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2024:** EDP Renewables inaugurated a new hybrid energy project combining wind and solar energy. The facility includes the Monte de Vez photovoltaic solar plant with a capacity of 21 MWp, installed next to the existing São João Wind Farm, which has been operational since 2008 with a capacity of 22.8 MW. The combined production capacity of the facility is now 79 GWh of renewable energy per year, sufficient to supply electricity to 23,000 families. The installation includes 36,000 bifacial solar panels alongside 13 wind turbines.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **February 2024:** The project is part of EDP's broader investment strategy in the central region of Portugal, where the company has over 280 MW of wind energy in operation. EDP plans to implement an additional 700 MW in similar hybrid projects in the coming years.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **February 2024:** The hybrid park was officially inaugurated, marking it as the second hybrid operation in Portugal. The project aims to accelerate the energy transition in the central region of the country.
