### Evolutionary Timeline of the Empresa de Desenvolvimento e Infraestruturas do Alqueva Facility in Ferreira do Alentejo, PRT

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: The Empresa de Desenvolvimento e Infraestruturas do Alqueva (EDIA) announced the construction of five floating photovoltaic plants located in the reservoirs of Ferreira do Alentejo, Almeidas, Pias, Penedrão, and Monte Novo. The combined capacity of these plants is 4.5 MW, with an estimated annual production of 7 GWh. The plants will also feed adjacent pumping stations.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: The total investment required for the five floating photovoltaic projects is approximately €4.3 million ($4.6 million). This tender is part of a larger plan, with a global investment of about €60 million anticipated for the installation of around 70 MW across multiple tenders.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: EDIA launched an international tender for the construction of the five floating photovoltaic plants. This is noted as the first of a series of four tenders expected to be launched by the end of summer 2023.
