1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** May 2023
   - **Details:** The facility is part of a project to construct five floating photovoltaic plants, including one at Monte Novo. The combined capacity of these plants is 4.5 MW, with an estimated annual production of 7 GWh. The plants will supply energy to adjacent pumping stations.

   - **Date:** May 2023
