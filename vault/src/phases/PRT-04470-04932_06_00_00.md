### Evolutionary Timeline of the Empresa de Desenvolvimento e Infraestruturas do Alqueva Facility in Penedrão, PRT

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: The Empresa de Desenvolvimento e Infraestruturas do Alqueva (EDIA) announced the construction of five floating photovoltaic plants, including one in Penedrão. The combined capacity of these projects is 4.5 MW, with an estimated annual production of 7 GWh. The plants will serve adjacent pumping stations and include maintenance for three years post-operation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: The total investment for the five floating solar projects is approximately €4.3 million ($4.6 million). This tender is part of a larger initiative, with a total investment of about €60 million planned for the installation of around 70 MW across multiple projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: EDIA launched an international tender for the construction of the floating photovoltaic plants, marking the beginning of the project. This is noted as the first of a series of four tenders expected to be launched by the end of summer 2023.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2023**: While specific job creation or loss figures were not mentioned in the articles, the construction and operation of the floating solar plants are expected to create jobs related to the installation and maintenance of the facilities.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2023**: The facility is controlled by the Ministry of Agriculture of Portugal, indicating that EDIA operates under public ownership rather than exclusive private ownership.
