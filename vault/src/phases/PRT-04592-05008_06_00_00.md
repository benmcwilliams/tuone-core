### Evolutionary Timeline of Exide Technologies Facility in Azambuja, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2019**: Exide Technologies, in collaboration with EDP, initiated a project to install a 3.8 MW solar-plus-storage system at its facilities in Azambuja and Castanheira do Ribatejo. This project includes a hybrid wind-solar component and aims for self-consumption, utilizing a total of 10,000 PV panels.
- **March 2021**: The facility expanded its solar installation, which now includes approximately 11,250 photovoltaic panels and 70 inverters. This setup is one of the largest self-generation installations backed by a Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) in Europe, designed to reduce carbon emissions by 19% at the Azambuja recycling plant.
