## PRT-04593-01587

### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDP Facility in Castanheira do Ribatejo, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2019**: EDP announced the construction of a 3.8 MW solar-plus-storage project, which is the first of its kind in Portugal. This facility will consist of two photovoltaic (PV) units for self-consumption at Exide Technologies' manufacturing sites in Castanheira do Ribatejo and Azambuja. The project will utilize a total of 10,000 PV panels.
- **March 2021**: The facility was reported to have approximately 11,250 PV panels and 70 inverters, capable of producing enough energy to supply over 1,500 homes. The installations are designed to reduce carbon emissions by 23% at the Castanheira do Ribatejo factory.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2019**: EDP's investment in this project is part of its strategy to expand into renewable energy, particularly in solar and storage technologies. The project is significant as it represents EDP's largest solar energy initiative in Portugal.
- **March 2021**: The facility is highlighted as one of the largest self-generation installations backed by a Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) in Europe, indicating substantial investment in renewable energy infrastructure.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2019**: Announcement of the project to build the solar-plus-storage facility.
- **March 2021**: Launch of the solar installation, marking the operational status of the facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **March 2021**: The project is noted to provide a path forward for energy-intensive manufacturers to reduce energy costs and manage carbon emissions, implying potential job creation related to the installation and operation of the solar facilities, although specific job numbers are not mentioned.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **April 2019**: EDP is the primary developer of the facility, working in collaboration with Exide Technologies, which suggests a partnership but does not indicate joint ownership. EDP appears to maintain significant control over the project as the energy provider and operator.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and milestones of the EDP facility in Castanheira do Ribatejo, focusing on its technological advancements, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
