### Evolutionary Timeline of Exide Technologies Facility in Castanheira do Ribatejo, PRT

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2019**: Exide Technologies, in collaboration with EDP, announced a 3.8 MW solar-plus-storage project for self-consumption at its facilities in Castanheira do Ribatejo and Azambuja. The project includes two PV units with a total of 10,000 PV panels, designed to cover energy needs and store surplus energy for grid injection during lower consumption periods.
- **March 2021**: The facility launched a solar installation featuring approximately 11,250 photovoltaic panels and 70 inverters, capable of supplying energy to over 1,500 homes. This installation is noted as one of the largest self-generation setups backed by a Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) in Europe, reducing carbon emissions by 23% at the Castanheira do Ribatejo factory.
