### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fusion Fuel Facility in Elvas, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **March 2023:** Fusion Fuel announced the development of a 1 MW decentralized green hydrogen production facility in Elvas, Portugal. The facility will produce up to 400 kg of green hydrogen per day and will include an integrated hydrogen refueling station for light and heavy-duty commercial vehicles. The project will involve green hydrogen production and compression to 40 bar.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **March 2023:** Fusion Fuel was awarded €3.6 million in grant funding through Component 5 of Portugal’s Recovery and Resilience Plan for the development of the facility. The total capital investment required for the project is approximately €7.2 million.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **March 2023:** The project was officially announced, detailing the collaboration with Galp and the strategic location of the facility on the Portugal-Spain border, which is significant for freight transport.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
