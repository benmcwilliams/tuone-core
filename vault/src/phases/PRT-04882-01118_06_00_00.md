- **August 2022**: Iberdrola's Alcochete 1 solar facility, with a production capacity of **32.89MW**, is part of a larger portfolio of **187MW** secured in solar auctions held in 2019. The facility is expected to enter commercial operation in **2022**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2022**: The Alcochete 1 project is part of a broader investment strategy by Iberdrola, which includes a total investment of **€17.8 million** for the Alcochete 2 PV plant, indicating significant financial commitment to solar energy in the region.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2022**: The Alcochete 1 project is one of three solar projects (including Alcochete 2 and the Conde project) expected to commence commercial operations in **2022**. The other projects are **12.72MW Alcochete 2** and **13.51MW Conde**.

- **August 2022**: The construction of the Alcochete 2 project generated over **200 jobs** during peak construction periods, all filled by local workers. While specific job creation numbers for Alcochete 1 are not mentioned, it is likely that similar local employment opportunities were created.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **August 2022**: Iberdrola is the sole owner of the Alcochete 1 facility, as indicated by its status as the largest winning bidder in the 2019 solar auction in Portugal.
