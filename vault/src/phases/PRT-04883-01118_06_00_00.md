## PRT-04883-01118

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Alcochete 2, PRT

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2022**: The Alcochete 2 solar plant, with a production capacity of **12.72 MW**, was commissioned. It is part of a larger **187 MW portfolio** awarded to Iberdrola in solar auctions held in 2019. The facility utilizes **more than 50,500 fixed single-facial photovoltaic modules**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2022**: The total investment for the Alcochete 2 project was **€17.8 million**. This investment is part of the broader commitment by Iberdrola to expand its renewable energy capacity in Portugal.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2022**: The Alcochete 2 facility was officially commissioned and began commercial operation. It was one of three projects from the 2019 auction expected to enter operation in 2022, alongside the **13.51 MW Conde project** and the **32.89 MW Alcochete 1 project**.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2022**: The construction of the Alcochete 2 facility generated **more than 200 jobs** during peak construction periods, all of which were filled by local workers.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **August 2022**: Iberdrola owns the Alcochete 2 facility **exclusively** as part of its portfolio in Portugal, having been the largest winning bidder in the 2019 solar auction.
