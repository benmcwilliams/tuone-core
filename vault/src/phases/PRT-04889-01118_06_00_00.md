### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Carregado, PRT

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **August 2022:** Iberdrola is in the process of obtaining construction permits for the Carregado solar project, which has a planned production capacity of **64.1 MW**. This project is part of a larger portfolio of solar projects awarded in the 2019 auctions, which totals **187 MW** across various facilities in Portugal.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **August 2022:** The Carregado project is part of a broader investment strategy by Iberdrola, which includes a total investment of **€17.8 million** for the Alcochete 2 PV plant, indicating a significant financial commitment to solar energy development in Portugal.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **August 2022:** The Carregado project, along with Montechoro 1 (11.57 MW) and Montechoro 2 (24.95 MW), is scheduled to be commissioned in **2023**. This timeline aligns with the completion of the other projects awarded in the same auction.

4. **Labour:**
   - **August 2022:** The construction of the Carregado facility is expected to generate local job opportunities, similar to the Alcochete 2 project, which created over **200 jobs** during its peak construction period.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **August 2022:** Iberdrola is the sole owner of the Carregado facility, as indicated by its involvement in the project and its status as the largest winning bidder in the 2019 solar auction in Portugal.
