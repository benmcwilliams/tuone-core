### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Conde, PRT

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **09-02-2022**: Iberdrola began construction of four solar farms in Portugal, including the Conde facility with an installed capacity of **13.51MW**. The solar plants utilize bifacial technology, which enhances efficiency by allowing the photovoltaic (PV) panels to generate electricity from both sides. The construction of these plants is expected to conclude in **2022**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **16-08-2022**: The Conde project is part of a larger portfolio of **187MW** secured in the 2019 solar auction. The Alcochete 2 solar plant, which is part of this portfolio, involved an investment of **€17.8 million**. The specific investment amount for the Conde facility has not been disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09-02-2022**: Announcement of the construction start for the Conde solar facility as part of the four solar farms in Setúbal district.
- **16-08-2022**: The Conde project is expected to enter commercial operation in **2022**, alongside the Alcochete 1 and Alcochete 2 projects.

#### 4. Labour
- **09-02-2022**: During the construction phase of the solar plants, including Conde, **500 jobs** will be created at peak periods. 
- **16-08-2022**: The Alcochete 2 project generated over **200 jobs** during peak construction, all filled by local workers, indicating a similar trend for the Conde facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The articles do not specify the ownership structure of the Conde facility, but it is implied that Iberdrola owns the facility as part of its portfolio of solar projects in Portugal.

### Summary
The Iberdrola facility in Conde, Portugal, is a solar power plant with a capacity of **13.51MW**, utilizing advanced bifacial technology. Construction began in early **2022**, with an expected operational date later that year. The project is part of a larger **187MW** portfolio from the 2019 auction, with significant local job creation during its construction phase. While specific investment details for Conde are not provided, it is part of Iberdrola's broader investment strategy in renewable energy in Portugal.
