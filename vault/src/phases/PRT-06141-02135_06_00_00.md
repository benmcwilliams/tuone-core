### Evolutionary Timeline of Acciona Energy Facility in Amareleja, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2008**: The Amareleja solar plant was finalized by Acciona, featuring a production capacity of 45.8 MW. The facility includes 2,520 solar trackers and 262,080 PV panels.
- **2018**: Acciona's total power generation assets in Portugal, including the Amareleja facility, reached a combined capacity of 165.5 MW, comprising 19 wind power plants totaling 119.7 MW and the 45.8 MW solar park in Amareleja.
- **2023**: The Amareleja project is noted as one of the Iberian Peninsula’s largest solar assets, maintaining its capacity of 46 MWp.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **2018**: Acciona secured a short-term Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with Vidrala, indicating ongoing investment in renewable energy marketing efforts in Portugal.
- **2023**: Acciona signed a ten-year PPA with Grupo Violas to supply 55,000 MWh of green electricity annually, further solidifying its investment in renewable energy solutions.
