#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **64MW Carregado Solar Project**: In early 2023, Iberdrola announced the construction of the Carregado solar plant in Alenquer, which will have a capacity of 64MW. This project is part of a broader initiative where Iberdrola is developing multiple solar facilities in Portugal, including the Estoi solar plant (83MW) in the Algarve, which will include battery storage and is expected to be operational in 2024.

#### 2. Investment and Development

### Summary
The Iberdrola facility in Alenquer, Portugal, is set to include a 64MW solar project known as the Carregado solar plant, with construction announced in early 2023 as part of a broader €3 billion investment in renewable energy in Portugal. The project is expected to create local jobs and is part of Iberdrola's strategy to enhance its renewable energy portfolio in the country.
