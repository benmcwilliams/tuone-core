1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Welink facility, known as the Solara4 plant, is a solar power project with a production capacity of 221 MW. It is located near the Vaqueiros district in the Algarve region of Portugal. 2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The Solara4 project is being developed by the Irish company Welink, with funding sourced from Investec and Kommunalkredit funds. The project is notable for being described as the largest facility in Europe built without public subsidies.
