- **September 2021**: Prosolia announced plans to build a solar facility in Santiago do Cacém with an initial capacity of **1.14 GW**. The facility will utilize **Tesla batteries** for energy storage.
- **February 2023**: The capacity of the solar plant was updated to **1.2 GW**, and it was stated that the project would be the **largest PV plant in Europe**. The project is expected to begin commercial operations in **2025**.

- **February 2023**: Prosolia and Iberdrola secured an **environmental permit** from Portuguese authorities for the construction of the 1.2 GW solar plant. The land for the project has been secured, and construction is expected to begin soon.
