### Evolutionary Timeline of ASM Industries Facility in Aveiro Port, PRT

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2018**: ASM Industries formalized its initiative to build a new industrial production unit for offshore structures in Aveiro Port. The facility will cover a total area of 72,000 m², with 22,000 m² dedicated to fabrication. The facility will produce offshore wind turbine towers and foundations, including transition pieces, monopiles, and pin-piles.
- **March 2019**: The PEMA offshore heavy tubular structure manufacturing lines, which include integrated milling stations and welding lines, began operations. This technology enables efficient and flexible fabrication of offshore structures.
- **October 2020**: ASM Industries commenced the fabrication of 42 offshore wind tower sections for the Fryslan offshore wind project in the Netherlands, marking a significant operational milestone for the new facility.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2018**: ASM Industries sealed an investment worth EUR 29 million for the new production facilities, which includes the automation lines supplied by Pemamek for offshore wind turbine tower and foundation production.
