### Evolutionary Timeline of the Allianz Capital Partners Facility in Ourika, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2018**: Allianz Capital Partners acquired the Ourika solar farm, which has a production capacity of **46 MW**. The facility is equipped with **142,000 PV panels** and covers an area of **100 hectares**. It is noted as **Europe’s largest subsidy-free solar project** and is capable of supplying electricity to approximately **23,000 average households** annually. The facility became fully operational in **June 2018** and was inaugurated on **July 26, 2018**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2018**: The acquisition of the Ourika solar farm involved an investment by WElink Group and its partner China Triumph International Engineering Company (CTIEC), amounting to around **€40 million** for the development of the 46 MW plant. Allianz Capital Partners has entered into a **20-year fixed price power purchase agreement** (PPA) for the electricity generated at the facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **July 26, 2018**: The Ourika solar farm was inaugurated.
