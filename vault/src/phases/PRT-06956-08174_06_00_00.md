### Evolutionary Timeline of the WElink Group Facility in Ourika, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Ourika facility is a solar photovoltaic (PV) project with a production capacity of 46 MW. 
   - It covers an area of 100 hectares and consists of 142,000 PV panels.
   - The facility became fully operational in June 2018 and was inaugurated on July 26, 2018. It is recognized as Europe’s largest subsidy-free solar project.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - WElink Group, in partnership with China Triumph International Engineering Company (CTIEC), invested approximately €40 million in the development of the Ourika solar plant.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **June 2018:** The Ourika solar farm became fully operational.
   - **July 26, 2018:** The facility was officially inaugurated.
   - A 20-year fixed price power purchase agreement (PPA) was established with Allianz Capital Partners to purchase the generated electricity, which is expected to supply around 23,000 average households annually.
