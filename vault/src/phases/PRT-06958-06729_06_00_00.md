### Evolutionary Timeline of the Amorim Cork Composites Facility at Alqueva Hydropower Dam, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2022**: The facility features a newly constructed 5 MW floating solar array at the Alqueva hydropower dam. The floating solar platform consists of 26,600 floating structures made from cork composite, a 100% natural, recyclable, and biocompatible material, combined with recycled polymers. The installation is linked to 2 MWh of lithium-ion batteries.
