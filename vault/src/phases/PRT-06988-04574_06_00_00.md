## PRT-06988-04574

### Evolutionary Timeline of the BNZ Facility in Vila Nova de Famalicao, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: 04-09-2024
   - **Details**: BNZ has begun the construction of a 49MW solar plant in Vila Nova de Famalicao. The facility is designed to produce enough energy to supply the annual electricity needs of approximately 14,000 households. The clean energy generated will avoid the emission of 21,500 tonnes of CO2 equivalent per year.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Date**: 04-09-2024
   - **Details**: This facility marks BNZ's first project in Portugal, with plans to install a total capacity of approximately 600MWp in the country by 2026. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Date**: 04-09-2024
   - **Details**: Construction of the solar power plant has commenced. The plant is expected to open in 2025.

4. **Labour**
   - **Date**: 04-09-2024
   - **Details**: BNZ estimates that the facility will create between 61 to 72 direct and indirect jobs by 2025.

5. **Ownership Structure**
   - **Date**: 04-09-2024
   - **Details**: The facility is owned by BNZ GRS, the solar EPC contractor of Gransolar Group, indicating that BNZ has exclusive ownership of the facility. 

### Additional Context
- BNZ has signed a protocol with the Vila Nova de Famalicao municipal government to undertake environmental conservation actions, including the conservation of cork oak trees, reforestation, and rehabilitation of the Veirão river. This reflects BNZ's commitment to sustainable development and community engagement.
