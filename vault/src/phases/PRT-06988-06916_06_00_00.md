### GranSolar Facility Timeline - Vila Nova de Famalicao, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: 04/2024
   - **Details**: The GranSolar facility, owned by BNZ GRS, is in the process of constructing a 49MW solar plant. This facility is designed to produce enough energy to meet the annual electricity needs of approximately 14,000 households. The clean energy generated is expected to prevent the emission of 21,500 tonnes of CO2 equivalent per year.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Date**: 04/2024
   - **Details**: This project marks the first initiative of BNZ in Portugal, which has plans to install a total capacity of approximately 600MWp in the country by 2026. Specific investment amounts are not detailed in the article.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Date**: 04/2024
   - **Details**: Construction of the solar plant has begun, with an expected completion date in 2025 when the plant is anticipated to start operations.

4. **Labour**
   - **Date**: 04/2024
   - **Details**: The project is estimated to create between 61 to 72 direct and indirect jobs by the time the plant opens in 2025.

5. **Ownership Structure**
   - **Date**: 04/2024
   - **Details**: The facility is owned by BNZ GRS, which is a solar EPC contractor of the Gransolar Group. There is no indication of joint ventures or shared ownership in the provided information, suggesting full ownership by BNZ GRS.

### Additional Notes
- The project includes environmental initiatives such as the conservation of cork oak trees, reforestation with native species, and rehabilitation of the Veirão river, along with community support projects like a natural swimming pool and a children's playground.
- The facility is part of BNZ's broader commitment to renewable energy production and community growth in Portugal.
