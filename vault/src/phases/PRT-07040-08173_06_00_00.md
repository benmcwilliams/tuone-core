## PRT-07040-08173

### Evolutionary Timeline of WElink Energy Facility in Vaqueiros, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06-03-2017**: WElink Energy announces the development of a 220MW solar plant named Solara 4 in Vaqueiros, Algarve. The facility is designed to produce enough energy for approximately 200,000 homes. The project is noted as the largest privately funded PV project in Europe.
- **26-10-2017**: Construction officially begins on the 221MW Solara 4 project, which is set to be Europe’s largest unsubsidised solar plant. The facility will feature 850,000 solar modules and is expected to be operational by mid-2019.
- **26-06-2019**: The project nears completion, with operations expected to start in September. The facility will utilize 661,000 polycrystalline solar modules supplied by Jetion Solar, confirming its status as Europe’s largest subsidy-free solar facility.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06-03-2017**: The total investment for the Solara 4 project is reported to be £200 million. This investment is significant as it demonstrates the viability of large-scale solar projects without government subsidies.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06-03-2017**: Announcement of the project and signing of the EPC agreement with China Triumph International Engineering (CTIEC).
- **26-10-2017**: Construction begins on the Solara 4 project.
- **26-06-2019**: The project is nearing completion, with operations expected to commence in September 2019.

#### 4. Labour
- **06-03-2017**: The construction phase is expected to create 200 jobs, with up to 600 jobs at peak times. Once operational, the facility will provide 30 permanent operation and maintenance jobs.
- **30-10-2017**: During construction, around 300 people are expected to be employed.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **06-03-2017**: WElink Energy is identified as the developer of the facility, indicating that they have exclusive ownership of the project, although they are collaborating with CTIEC for engineering, procurement, and construction services.
