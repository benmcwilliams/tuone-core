## PRT-07077-02742

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Centrosolar Group AG Facility in Vila do Conde, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 05-05-2008
   - **Details:** Centrosolar Group AG, in partnership with Qimonda AG, announced the establishment of a joint venture to manufacture crystalline solar cells at a facility located at Qimonda’s semiconductor packaging plant in Vila do Conde. The facility is designed to have a production capacity of 100 MWp, equivalent to approximately 30 million solar cells per year.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 05-05-2008
   - **Details:** The initial investment for the first phase of the joint venture is approximately €70 million. The financing structure includes 70% funding from local banks and additional support from Portuguese state authorities in line with European funding regulations.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 05-05-2008
   - **Details:** The construction of the facility is scheduled to begin in mid-2008, with equipment installation expected to start in early 2009. Initial production is anticipated to ramp up in the second half of 2009.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 05-05-2008
   - **Details:** The facility is expected to create approximately 150 jobs upon its operational launch.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 05-05-2008
   - **Details:** The facility operates as a joint venture, with Qimonda holding a 51% stake and Centrosolar holding a 49% stake in the project.
