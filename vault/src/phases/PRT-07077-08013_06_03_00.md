## PRT-07077-08013

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Qimonda AG Facility in Vila do Conde, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **05-05-2008:** Qimonda AG, in partnership with Centrosolar Group AG, announced the establishment of a joint venture to build and operate a crystalline solar cell manufacturing facility at Qimonda’s semiconductor packaging plant in Vila do Conde. The facility is designed to produce solar cells with an initial production capacity of up to 30 million solar cells per year, equivalent to approximately 100 MegaWatt peak (MWp). The production is expected to utilize Qimonda's semiconductor process technology to achieve solar cell efficiencies above 16%.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **05-05-2008:** The first phase of the joint venture is expected to involve an investment of approximately €70 million. The financing structure includes 70% funding from local banks and additional support from Portuguese state authorities in line with European funding regulations.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **05-05-2008:** Announcement of the joint venture and plans for construction.
   - **Mid-2008:** Construction of the facility is scheduled to begin.
   - **Early 2009:** The facility is expected to be ready for equipment installation.
   - **Second half of 2009:** Initial production ramp-up is anticipated.

4. **Labour:**
   - **05-05-2008:** The facility is expected to create approximately 150 jobs upon the commencement of operations.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **05-05-2008:** Qimonda AG holds a 51% stake in the joint venture, while Centrosolar Group AG holds a 49% stake, indicating a joint venture ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by Qimonda.
