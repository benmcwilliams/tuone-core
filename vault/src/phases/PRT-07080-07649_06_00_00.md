### Evolutionary Timeline of the China Triumph International Engineering Co. Ltd Facility in Vaquieros, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** The facility, known as the Riccardo Totta Photovoltaic Plant, has a production capacity of 219 MW, making it the largest solar power plant in Portugal and one of the largest unsubsidized solar projects in Europe. The plant is expected to begin full operations by the end of October 2021.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** The total investment for the project was €170 million, which came in under the scheduled budget of €200 million. The project was a collaboration between WElink Group and China Triumph International Engineering Co. Ltd. (CTIEC).

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** The project was completed and inaugurated in October 2021. It was originally expected to be completed in September 2019 but faced multiple delays, including a COVID-19 outbreak among construction workers.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** The construction of the facility created approximately 500 local jobs. During its operational phase, the plant is expected to employ 15-20 individuals.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is a joint venture between WElink Group and China Triumph International Engineering Co. Ltd. (CTIEC), indicating that it is not exclusively owned by either company.
