### Evolutionary Timeline of the WElink Group Facility in Vaquieros, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** The facility, known as the Riccardo Totta Photovoltaic Plant, has a production capacity of 219 MW, making it the largest solar power plant in Portugal and one of the largest unsubsidized solar projects in Europe. The plant is expected to begin full operations by the end of October 2021. Plans for future integration of energy storage systems were also mentioned to optimize energy costs.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** The total investment for the project was €170 million, which came in under the scheduled budget of €200 million. This project represents the largest investment ever made in the municipality of Alcoutim.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** The project was completed and inaugurated in October 2021. It was originally expected to be completed in September 2019 but faced multiple delays, including a COVID-19 outbreak among construction workers.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** The construction of the facility created approximately 500 local jobs. During its operational phase, the plant is expected to employ 15-20 individuals.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** October 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is developed by WElink Group in collaboration with China Triumph International Engineering Co. Ltd. (CTIEC). The ownership structure specifics regarding exclusivity or joint venture were not explicitly detailed in the article, but it indicates a partnership in development.
