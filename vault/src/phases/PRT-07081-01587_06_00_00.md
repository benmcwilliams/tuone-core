## PRT-07081-01587

### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDP Facility in Alto Rabagão, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **03-03-2017**: EDP, in collaboration with Ciel & Terre, installed a **220 kW** floating photovoltaic (PV) system at the Alto Rabagão hydroelectric dam. This project marked the first utility-scale combination of hydroelectric and floating solar technologies. The dam has a hydroelectric capacity of **68 MW**.

- **05-04-2022**: EDP won a tender to develop a **42.5 MW** floating solar project at the Alto Rabagão dam as part of a larger auction for floating solar projects across Portugal. This project is expected to be operational by **2023**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **03-06-2019**: EDP announced plans for a **4 MW** floating solar plant at the Alqueva water reservoir, indicating ongoing investments in floating solar technology. The environmental impact assessment for this project was released, showcasing EDP's commitment to expanding its renewable energy portfolio.

- **05-04-2022**: EDP's successful bid in the floating PV tender included a **negative bid** of **–€4/MWh** for a 15-year contract, indicating a strategic investment approach to secure long-term renewable energy projects.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **03-03-2017**: Installation of the **220 kW** floating PV system at Alto Rabagão completed.
  
- **03-06-2019**: Announcement of the **4 MW** floating solar project at Alqueva, with construction expected to start in early **2020**.

- **26-11-2021**: Announcement of a new auction for **262 MW** of floating solar, including the **42 MW** project at Alto Rabagão.

- **05-04-2022**: EDP confirmed its project at Alto Rabagão as part of the auction results, with an expected operational date in **2023**.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- EDP is the primary owner of the facility, with no indication of joint ventures or shared ownership in the articles. The facility is operated under EDP's renewables unit, EDPR, which indicates full ownership and control over the projects discussed.
