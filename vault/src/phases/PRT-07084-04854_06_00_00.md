## PRT-07084-04854

### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDP Group Facility in Montalegre, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2016
     - The facility began operating a 220kWp floating solar power plant at an existing hydro-electric power station at the Alto Rabagão reservoir. The plant utilizes 840 solar modules mounted on Ciel & Terre's ‘Hydrelio’ platform, covering an area of approximately 2500m².
   - **Expected Production:** The floating solar plant was projected to generate 332MWh of electricity in its first year. By July 2017, it had achieved 160MWh in the first 7 months of operation, exceeding initial expectations.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 2015 (Pilot project initiation)
     - EDP initiated the pilot project in collaboration with Ciel & Terre, with a total investment of around €450,000 for the floating solar installation.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2017
     - The project was highlighted as the first floating solar project operating in Portugal, showcasing the collaboration between EDP and Ciel & Terre.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
