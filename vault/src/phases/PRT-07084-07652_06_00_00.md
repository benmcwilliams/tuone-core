### Evolutionary Timeline of Ciel & Terre International Facility in Montalegre, PRT

   - **Date:** November 2016
     - The facility began operations with a 220kWp floating solar power plant, utilizing 840 solar modules on the ‘Hydrelio’ mounting platform, covering an area of approximately 2500m².
   - **Expected Production:** The plant was anticipated to generate 332MWh of electricity in its first year. By July 2017, it had achieved 160MWh in the first 7 months, exceeding initial expectations.

### Ciel & Terre International Facility in Montalegre, PRT

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- "Ciel & Terre (C&T) International has collaborated with Portuguese energy firm EDP (Energias de Portugal) Group to design and build the first floating solar project at an existing hydro-electric power station."
- "C&T developed a 220kWp floating solar power plant, using 840 solar modules on its ‘Hydrelio’ mounting platform."
- "The pilot project was initiated by EDP back in 2015 and has been operating since the end of November, 2016."
- "C&T noted that the plant was expected to generate 332MWh’s of electricity in its first year."
- "EDP noted that in the first 7 months of operation production of 160MWh had been achieved and higher than expected for this period."
