### Evolutionary Timeline of the General Electric Facility in Alto Lindoso, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2019
   - **Details:** The facility is involved in a demonstration project aimed at evaluating low capital expenditure (CAPEX) opportunities to enhance services at the existing 317-MW high-head, large-output reservoir storage hydropower plant. The project focuses on extending the operating range to achieve near-continuous power output and testing simultaneous pumping and generating through hydraulic short circuit operation.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2019
   - **Details:** The Alto Lindoso project is part of the larger XFLEX HYDRO initiative, which has a total budget of €18 million ($20 million). This funding is aimed at increasing hydropower efficiency and flexibility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** December 2019
   - **Details:** The project was officially launched as part of the XFLEX HYDRO initiative during the UN climate change conference (COP25) in Madrid, Spain. The project will run for four years and includes phases of innovation, demonstration, and deployment.
