   - **Date:** 05-04-2022
   - **Details:** EDP won a project to build a floating photovoltaic (PV) plant with a capacity of 100 MW at the Cabril dam as part of Portugal's first auction for floating PV projects. The project is part of a larger initiative that includes a total capacity of 263 MW across seven different dams. The floating solar plant is expected to come online in 2025.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 05-04-2022
   - **Details:** EDP agreed to a contract for difference (CfD) at a negative price of –€4/MWh for a 15-year period, which means EDP will pay the electrical system €4 for each megawatt-hour generated by the floating solar plant. The grid connection capacity awarded in the auction allows for the installation of up to 154 MW of renewable energy, including 70 MW of floating PV, 14 MW of additional solar, and 70 MW of wind capacity.
