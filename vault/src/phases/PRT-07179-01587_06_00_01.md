### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDP Facility in Castelo de Bode, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2021**: Portugal launched an auction for 262MW of floating solar, with the Castelo de Bode dam set to host a 50MW floating solar project. The project is expected to be operational by 2023.
- **April 2022**: EDP won the largest project in the auction, securing a 100MW floating solar plant at the Castelo de Bode dam. The project is expected to come online in 2025, with the capacity to install up to 154MW of renewable energy, including 70MW of floating photovoltaic solar energy.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2022**: EDP agreed to a negative bid of –€4/MWh for a 15-year contract for the floating solar project, indicating a unique investment strategy where EDP will pay for the electricity generated.
