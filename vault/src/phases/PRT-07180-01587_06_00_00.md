### Evolutionary Timeline of the EDP Facility in Paradela, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 05-04-2022
   - **Details:** EDP won a contract to build a floating photovoltaic (PV) plant with a capacity of 100 MW at the Paradela dam as part of Portugal's first auction for floating PV projects. The project is part of a larger initiative that includes a total capacity of 263 MW across seven different dams. The facility will also have the potential to install an additional 54 MW of renewable energy, including 14 MW of additional solar and 70 MW of hybridized wind capacity.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 05-04-2022
   - **Details:** EDP agreed to a negative bid of –€4/MWh for a 15-year contract under a contract for difference (CfD) regime. This means EDP will pay €4 for each megawatt-hour generated by the floating solar plant over the contract period. The grid connection capacity awarded in the auction is for a period of 30 years.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 05-04-2022
   - **Details:** The floating PV plant at Paradela is expected to come online in 2025. The project was part of a procurement exercise launched in late November 2021, which concluded with the selection of seven projects.
