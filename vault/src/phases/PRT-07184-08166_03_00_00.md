### Evolutionary Timeline of the Voith Facility in Frades 2, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: December 2019
   - **Details**: The Voith facility in Frades 2 is involved in a project to enhance the existing 390-MW pumped storage capacity. The project aims to integrate hydraulic short circuit technology for variable speed machines, which will extend the power range of the facility. The enhancements will focus on improving the flexibility services of the electric power system, including inertia emulation and frequency containment reserve, and will aim to increase annual energy production by reducing auxiliary equipment power consumption.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: December 2019
   - **Details**: The XFLEX HYDRO project, which includes the Frades 2 facility, is part of a larger €18 million ($20 million) initiative funded by the European Commission and a consortium of partners. This investment is aimed at increasing the efficiency and flexibility of hydropower plants across Europe.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: December 10, 2019
   - **Details**: The XFLEX HYDRO project was officially launched at the UN climate change conference (COP25) in Madrid, marking the beginning of the four-year initiative that includes the Frades 2 facility.
