## PRT-07305-07771

### Etermar Energia Facility Timeline - Setubal, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2024**: Etermar Energia, in collaboration with Qair, announced plans to establish a floating foundation manufacturing facility in Setubal. This facility aims to support the construction and operation of floating offshore wind farms, particularly within Portuguese maritime spatial planning zones. Etermar has over five decades of experience in constructing and installing floating structures, utilizing both steel and concrete.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2024**: The memorandum of understanding (MoU) signed between Etermar Energia and Qair outlines a partnership that includes component stockpiling, foundation manufacturing, logistical support, and operation and maintenance services. While specific investment amounts were not disclosed, the establishment of the manufacturing facility indicates significant investment in infrastructure to serve both European and global markets.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2024**: The MoU was signed during a visit to Qair’s Eolmed project, a 30MW floating offshore wind project under construction in Port-La Nouvelle, France. This partnership marks a strategic ambition for both companies to become key players in the floating offshore wind market.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2024**: The establishment of the floating foundation manufacturing facility is expected to create jobs, although specific numbers were not mentioned in the articles. The collaboration with Qair is also positioned as a socio-economic proposal, indicating potential job creation in the region.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **December 2024**: The articles do not specify the ownership structure of the facility, but it is implied that Etermar Energia will have a significant role in the operations, given their extensive experience and the establishment of the manufacturing facility at their premises in Setubal. The partnership with Qair suggests a collaborative approach rather than exclusive ownership.

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic moves of the Etermar Energia facility in Setubal, Portugal, as reported in the articles.
