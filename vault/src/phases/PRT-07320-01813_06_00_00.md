1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2022
   - **Details:** Neoen has begun construction on a solar park in Portugal, which includes the Rio Maior solar farm (204MWp) as part of a larger 272MWp facility. The facility is expected to produce around 500GWh of electricity once fully operational. The construction will maintain local biodiversity, preserving 6,000 cork oak trees in protected areas.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2022
   - **Details:** The project is part of a larger investment in renewable energy, with 80% of the power produced to be purchased by the Portuguese state through two 15-year power purchase agreements (PPAs) expected to commence in 2025.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** December 2022
   - **Details:** Construction has officially begun on the solar park, which is touted as Portugal’s largest PV plant. The facility is expected to be commissioned in early 2024.
