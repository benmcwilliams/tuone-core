## PRT-07393-08185

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Younicos Facility in Graciosa, PRT

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2012**: Younicos, in collaboration with EDA, announced the development of a renewable energy system on Graciosa, which will be the world's first MW-scale system based on up to 100% solar and wind power. The system will include:
  - A 500 kW solar plant
  - A wind park with a total capacity of 4.5 MW
  - A 2.8 MW lithium-ion battery storage system
  - An intelligent energy management system
- **November 2017**: The facility's capacity was updated to include 4.5 MW of wind power and 1 MW of solar power, integrated with the battery storage and energy management system.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2012**: An initial investment of €25 million was secured for the project through a special project company established by Younicos.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2012**: Announcement of the project with a projected operational date in 2014.
- **November 2017**: Confirmation of the facility's operational status and its integration into the Azores' renewable energy framework.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not provide specific information regarding job creation or job loss associated with the Younicos facility in Graciosa.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is a joint venture between Younicos and EDA, with Younicos responsible for setting up and financing the project. There is no indication of exclusive ownership by either party.
