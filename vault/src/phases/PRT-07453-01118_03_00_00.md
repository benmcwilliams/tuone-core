### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility at Daivões Power Plant, PRT

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Daivões Power Plant is part of the Tâmega Hydroelectric Complex, which includes three power plants: 
     - Daivões Power Plant: 118 MW capacity
     - Gouvães Pumping Hydroelectric Plant: 880 MW capacity
     - Alto Tâmega Hydroelectric Power Plant: 160 MW capacity (scheduled for commercial operation by March 2024).
   - The entire Tâmega Hydroelectric Complex will have a total installed capacity of 1,158 MW once all plants are operational.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The Tâmega Hydroelectric Complex represents a total investment of over €1.5 billion. This investment supports the development of hydroelectric storage capacity, which is crucial for integrating renewable energy sources into the electricity system.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Operational Status:** The Daivões Power Plant has been operational since 2022.
   - **Upcoming Milestones:** The Alto Tâmega Hydroelectric Power Plant is expected to enter commercial operation by the end of March 2024, with the second turbine scheduled to connect to the grid in February 2024.
