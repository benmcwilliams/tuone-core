### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Future Wind Farm, PRT

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2022**: Iberdrola commissioned its first solar farm in Portugal, the **12.72MW Alcochete 2 PV plant**. This facility is part of a larger **187MW portfolio** secured in solar auctions held in 2019. The plant features over **50,500 fixed single-facial photovoltaic modules**. 

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2022**: The Alcochete 2 project involved an investment of **€17.8 million**. This investment is part of Iberdrola's broader strategy to expand its renewable energy capacity in Portugal.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2022**: The Alcochete 2 PV plant is one of three projects from the 2019 auction expected to enter commercial operation in 2022. The other two projects are the **13.51MW Conde project** and the **32.89MW Alcochete 1 project**. All three projects are located in the **Setúbal district**.
- **2023**: Additional projects from the same auction, including **Montechoro 1 (11.57MW)**, **Montechoro 2 (24.95MW)**, and **Carregado (64.1MW)**, are in the process of obtaining construction permits and are scheduled to be commissioned, bringing Iberdrola's total PV capacity in Portugal to **187MW**.

#### 4. Labour
- **August 2022**: The construction of the Alcochete 2 PV plant generated **more than 200 jobs** during peak construction periods, all of which were filled by local workers.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The articles do not specify the ownership structure of the facility, but it can be inferred that Iberdrola owns the facility exclusively as it is described as their commissioned project in Portugal.
