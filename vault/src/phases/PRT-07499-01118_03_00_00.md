## PRT-07499-01118

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Tâmega Power Plant System, Portugal

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **10-12-2024**: Iberdrola announced the installation of the largest wind farm in Portugal, integrated into the Tâmega Power Plant System (SET). The wind farm will have an installed capacity of **274 MW** and a production capacity of **601 GWh per year**, sufficient to power **128,000 households**. This project will combine wind energy with hydroelectric energy, utilizing existing infrastructure in Ribeira de Pena.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **10-12-2024**: The total investment for the wind farm project is approximately **350 million euros**. This investment is part of Iberdrola's broader commitment to renewable energy, having invested over **2 billion euros** in renewable energies in Portugal over the last 20 years.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10-12-2024**: Iberdrola received the production license from the Portuguese Directorate General of Energy and Geology (DGEG), marking a significant step towards the construction of the wind farm. The project includes the Tâmega Norte and Tâmega Sul wind farms and is expected to start construction in **early 2025**.

#### 4. Labour
- **10-12-2024**: The project is expected to create approximately **700 jobs** during peak construction times, focusing on areas such as civil engineering, wind turbine assembly, substations, and transmission lines.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The articles do not specify the ownership structure of the facility, but it is implied that Iberdrola is the primary company responsible for the project, potentially in collaboration with the Norwegian sovereign wealth fund, managed by Norges Bank Investment Management, for the wind farm component.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Iberdrola facility in the Tâmega Power Plant System, highlighting its technological advancements, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership context.
