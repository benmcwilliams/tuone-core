### Evolutionary Timeline of the Iberdrola Facility in Tâmega Power Plant, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **10/2024:** Iberdrola has been granted a production license for a 274MW wind project, which will be integrated into the Tâmega power plant system. The project will have a production capacity of 601 GWh per year and will combine wind energy with hydroelectric energy. The Tâmega power plant consists of the Alto Tamega hydroelectric power plant (160MW), the Gouvaes pumped storage plant (880MW), and the Daivoes power plant (118MW), with the latter two operational since 2022.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **10/2024:** The wind project will see an investment of €350 million, part of a broader investment exceeding €1.5 billion for the Tâmega power plant system. Iberdrola has invested more than €2 billion in renewable energies in Portugal over the last 20 years.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **06/2024:** Iberdrola received the final environmental permit to build the wind farm.
   - **10/2024:** Announcement of the production license for the wind project, marking a significant step towards construction.

4. **Labour:**
   - **10/2024:** Local companies such as CJR and Conduril-Socorpena will be involved in developing the necessary infrastructure, indicating potential job creation during the construction phase. However, specific job creation numbers are not provided.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
