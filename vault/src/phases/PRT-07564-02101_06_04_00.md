### Martifer Facility in Ferreiras, PRT - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2012
   - **Production Capacity:** The Ferreiras solar plant has a production capacity of 6.8MWp, utilizing 28,320 solar modules over an area of more than 16 hectares.
   - **Expansion/Updates:** The facility is part of a broader expansion strategy by Martifer Solar, which includes the completion of two significant PV power plants in Portugal, contributing to a total of 22.4MWp capacity across both plants.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2012
   - **Investment Amount:** Martifer Solar had approximately €235 million worth of projects in its backlog at the end of the first half of 2012, indicating significant investment in solar energy projects, including the Ferreiras facility.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2012
   - **Milestone:** The Ferreiras solar plant was completed as part of Martifer Solar's ongoing expansion goals for 2012, alongside the Avalades plant.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2012
   - **Job Creation:** The construction of the Ferreiras solar plant involved 80 workers. Additionally, operation and maintenance services will be provided by more than 10 personnel.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** September 2012
   - **Ownership Status:** The facility is developed, built, and operated by Martifer Solar for a fund managed by BNP Paribas Clean Energy Partners, indicating a partnership rather than exclusive ownership.
