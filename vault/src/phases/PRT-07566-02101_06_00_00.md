## PRT-07566-02101

### Martifer Facility in Silves, PRT - Evolutionary Timeline

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2012
   - **Details:** Martifer Solar completed two significant photovoltaic (PV) power plants in Portugal, including the Avalades plant in Silves. The Avalades plant has a production capacity of 15.6 MWp and covers over 41 hectares, utilizing 66,080 solar modules. The Ferreiras plant, also part of this expansion, has a capacity of 6.8 MWp and employs 28,320 modules over 16 hectares.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2012
   - **Details:** At the end of the first half of 2012, Martifer Solar had approximately €235 million worth of projects in its backlog, indicating significant investment in expanding its PV power plant projects.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2012
   - **Details:** The completion of the Avalades and Ferreiras solar plants marks a milestone in Martifer Solar's expansion goals for 2012. The construction involved a total of 200 workers, with 120 at the Avalades site and 80 at Ferreiras.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2012
   - **Details:** The construction of the two solar plants created jobs for 200 workers (120 in Avalades and 80 in Ferreiras). Additionally, more than 10 people will be employed for operation and maintenance services.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** September 2012
   - **Details:** The projects were developed, built, and operated for a fund managed by BNP Paribas Clean Energy Partners, indicating that Martifer Solar is not the exclusive owner of the facility but is a key partner in the development and operation of the plants.
