### Evolutionary Timeline of the Neoen Facility in Torre Bela, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** December 2022
   - **Details:** Neoen began construction on a 272MWp solar park in Portugal, which includes the Torre Bela solar farm (68MWp) and the Rio Maior solar farm (204MWp). The facility is expected to produce around 500GWh of electricity once fully operational. The construction will maintain local biodiversity by preserving 6,000 cork oak trees and utilizing plots of 20 hectares separated by green corridors.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** December 2022
   - **Details:** The project is part of Neoen's investment in renewable energy in Portugal, with 80% of the power produced being purchased by the Portuguese state through two 15-year power purchase agreements (PPAs) expected to commence in 2025.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** December 2022
   - **Details:** Construction of the solar park has officially begun, with commissioning expected in early 2024. The project is overseen by Spanish construction group Aldesa.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**

   - **Details:** The Torre Bela solar farm is part of a larger project that is co-owned with Aura Power, indicating that Neoen does not have exclusive ownership of the facility.
