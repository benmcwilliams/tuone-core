### Evolutionary Timeline of Siemens Gamesa Facility in Vagos, Portugal

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Acquisition of Ria Blades:** On May 1, 2020, Siemens Gamesa finalized the acquisition of Ria Blades, which includes a turbine blade production plant in Vagos, Portugal. The facility is described as having "best-in-class operational features" and is strategically located for both road and sea transport. This acquisition is intended to enhance Siemens Gamesa's competitiveness in the onshore wind business and serve as an export hub for international markets.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Total Purchase Price:** The acquisition of Ria Blades and other assets from Senvion was completed for a total of €200 million. This investment is aimed at strengthening Siemens Gamesa's manufacturing capabilities and reducing exposure to supply chain issues.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Acquisition Announcement:** On January 9, 2020, Siemens Gamesa announced its intention to close the acquisition of Ria Blades by the end of March 2020.
   - **Completion of Acquisition:** The acquisition was officially completed on May 1, 2020.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
