## ROU-00229-00274

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ford Facility in Craiova, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Production Focus**: The Craiova facility produces internal combustion engine versions of the Ford EcoSport and Puma, and is set to transition to electric vehicle (EV) production.
- **Upcoming Models**: 
  - **2023**: Production of the internal combustion version of the Transit Courier and Tourneo Courier begins.
  - **2024**: The facility will start producing the all-electric versions of the Puma (named Gen-E), Transit Courier, and Tourneo Courier.
- **Battery Assembly**: As of September 2024, Ford plans to assemble batteries for electric cars at the Craiova plant, utilizing cells manufactured at another location.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Joint Venture**: In March 2022, it was announced that Ford Otosan, a joint venture between Ford Motor Company and Koç Holding, would assume ownership of the Craiova plant, enhancing its capacity for electric and commercial vehicle production.
- **Investment in Electrification**: Ford is investing significantly in electrification across Europe, with the Craiova facility playing a crucial role in this strategy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **2021**: Ford announces plans to build electric vehicles in Europe, including the Craiova facility as a potential production site for electric models.
- **2022**: Ford confirms that the electric Puma and Transit Courier will be produced in Craiova starting in 2024.
- **2024**: Production of the electric Puma (Gen-E) and the electric versions of the Transit Courier and Tourneo Courier is set to commence.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The transition to electric vehicle production is expected to create new jobs at the Craiova facility, although specific numbers have not been disclosed.
- **Ownership Transition**: The shift to Ford Otosan ownership may also lead to changes in employment structures, but the overall impact on jobs remains to be clarified.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: As of March 2022, the Craiova plant is under the ownership of Ford Otosan, a joint venture between Ford Motor Company and Koç Holding. This joint venture aims to leverage resources and expertise in electrification and commercial vehicles.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic shifts at the Ford facility in Craiova, Romania, focusing on its transition towards electric vehicle production and the implications for investment, operations, and ownership.
