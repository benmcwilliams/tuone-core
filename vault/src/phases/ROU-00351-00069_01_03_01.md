## ROU-00351-00069

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Avesta Battery and Energy Engineering Facility in Galați, Romania

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **Date**: June 2023
   - **Details**: Avesta Battery and Energy Engineering (ABEE) is establishing a battery cell factory named Romvolt in Galați, Romania. The facility will primarily produce battery cells for the automotive industry, with an annual production capacity of 22 GWh planned upon completion in 2026. Additionally, the facility will also manufacture cells for stationary batteries. In a second phase, by the end of the decade, a recycling plant with a capacity of 50,000 tonnes per year will be built, requiring an additional investment of 200 million euros.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **Date**: June 2023
   - **Details**: ABEE is investing 1.4 billion euros in the construction of the Romvolt battery cell factory. This investment is aimed at supporting the production of battery cells for electric vehicles, particularly for car manufacturers Renault-Dacia and Ford, which have operations in Romania.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **Date**: June 2023
   - **Details**: The announcement of the battery factory was made, but the final agreement for the project has not yet been signed, as local authorities are still processing several requests. The facility is expected to start production in 2026.

4. **Labour**
   - **Date**: June 2023
   - **Details**: The establishment of the Romvolt facility is projected to create up to 8,000 jobs in the Galați region.

5. **Ownership Structure**
   - **Date**: June 2023
   - **Details**: The articles do not specify whether ABEE will have exclusive ownership of the facility or if it will be a joint venture. Further details on ownership structure are not provided in the available information.
