### Evolutionary Timeline of the Avesta Battery and Energy Engineering Facility in Galați, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: Avesta Battery and Energy Engineering (ABEE) announces the construction of a battery cell factory named Romvolt in Galați, Romania. The facility will primarily produce battery cells for the automotive industry, with an annual production capacity of 22 GWh planned upon completion in 2026. The plant will also manufacture cells for stationary batteries. 

- **Future Plans**: By the end of the decade, ABEE plans to invest an additional 200 million euros to build a recycling plant at the site, which will have a capacity of 50,000 tonnes per year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: ABEE commits to an investment of 1.4 billion euros for the construction of the Romvolt battery cell factory in Galați.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: Announcement of the Romvolt factory construction. However, the final agreement for the facility has not yet been signed, as local authorities are still processing several requests.

- **September 2024**: Reports indicate that Ford may source battery cells from the Romvolt factory for assembly at its vehicle plant in Craiova, Romania, although this has not been officially confirmed.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2023**: ABEE states that the Romvolt facility is expected to create up to 8,000 jobs in Galați.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership Status**: The articles do not specify whether ABEE will have exclusive ownership of the facility or if it will involve joint ventures or partnerships. However, it is noted that Ford, which is a potential customer for the battery cells, operates a separate vehicle plant in Craiova under Ford Otosan, a joint venture with Koç Holding.
