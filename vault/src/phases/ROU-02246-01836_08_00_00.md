### OX2 Facility in Dobrogea, Romania - Evolutionary Timeline

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: 11-06-2021
  - OX2 intends to revive four dormant wind power projects in Dobrogea with a combined planned capacity of **402 MW**. The projects are named South Wind Cherchezu, South Wind Cherchezu 2, South Wind Dumbrăveni, and South Wind Viroaga. These projects had lost their licenses five years prior due to government subsidy cuts, and the permits from 2014 had expired.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: 11-06-2021
  - OX2 plans to utilize more than half of the targeted **SEK 3.3 billion (EUR 328 million)** from its initial public offering (IPO) to facilitate growth in these projects, while the remainder will be used to pay off debt.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: 11-06-2021
  - OX2 announced the revival of its wind power projects in Dobrogea, which had been dormant for over a decade. The projects are intended for sale to financial and industrial investors, larger companies, and energy producers.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date**: 11-06-2021
  - OX2 does not own energy production assets but instead develops and sells completed projects to other investors. The company is controlled by Peas Industries and Altor, indicating a joint ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership of the facility.
