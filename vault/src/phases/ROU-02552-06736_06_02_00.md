### Evolutionary Timeline of the Astrasun Facility in Turnu Măgurele, ROU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **May 2022:** Astrasun announced plans to establish an integrated production facility in Turnu Măgurele, Romania, which will include:
     - A wafer manufacturing facility with a capacity of **1.8 GW**.
     - A solar cell production unit with a capacity of **1.5 GW**.
     - A photovoltaic module factory with a capacity of **1.2 GW**.
   - The total combined capacity of the facility will be **4.5 GW** across all three production units.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **May 2022:** Astrasun plans to invest a total of **€267 million** ($284.9 million) in the facility, broken down as follows:
     - **€102 million** for the wafer factory.
     - **€110 million** for the cell production unit.
     - **€55 million** for the module factory.
   - The funding will be sourced from the European Recovery and Resilience Facility (RFF) and is part of the broader National Recovery and Resilience Plan (NRRP) aimed at developing renewable energy supply chains in Romania.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **May 2022:** Announcement of the facility's establishment and planned production capacities.
   - The facility is expected to come online with projects anticipated to start in **2024**.

4. **Labour:**
   - **May 2022:** The establishment of the facility is projected to create **over 800 jobs** across the three production units.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The facility will be owned by **Astrasun Solar Kft.**, indicating **full ownership** by the company without any mention of joint ventures or partnerships in the articles provided.
