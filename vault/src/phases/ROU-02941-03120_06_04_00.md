### Evolutionary Timeline of the Karpat Solar Facility in Transylvania, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2021**: Karpat Solar acquires a 100 MW panel production line from Spanish provider Mondragon Assembly. This facility will be the first solar module factory in Romania, located in Transylvania. The production line is designed for high-efficiency solar modules, with an efficiency of up to 20.5% and power ratings of 360/455 W. The facility aims to produce modules primarily for the Romanian market initially, with plans to expand to the European Union market later.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2021**: The establishment of the facility is part of a larger investment strategy by Karpat Solar to become the first module manufacturer in Romania. The investment in the production line is significant, although specific financial figures are not disclosed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2021**: Karpat Solar announces the purchase of the production line and plans to begin manufacturing before the summer of 2021. The CEO, Csömör Károly, expresses confidence that the facility will be operational in time to serve the Romanian market by autumn 2021.

#### 4. Labour
