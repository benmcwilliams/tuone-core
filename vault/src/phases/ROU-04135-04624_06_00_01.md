## ROU-04135-04624

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Blue Line Energy Facility in Tulcea, Romania

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **10/2023**: Completion of a 1.1 MW photovoltaic power plant integrated into the Beştepte wind park, which has been operational since 2014. The total installed capacity of the hybrid plant is now 7.25 MW. The facility produces a total annual power generation of 24 GWh and contributes to a reduction of carbon dioxide emissions by 20,400 tonnes per year.

2. **Investment and Development**
   - **10/2023**: The construction of the photovoltaic power plant involved an investment of approximately EUR 10 million. This investment is part of a broader strategy to hybridize existing plants and develop new production capacities.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**
   - **10/2023**: The hybrid solar and wind park project is announced as completed and is currently undergoing technological trials. The photovoltaic system is set to be fully integrated into the existing infrastructure.

4. **Labour**
   - **10/2023**: The construction of the photovoltaic plant resulted in an increase in job creation during both the construction phase and the operational period. Various regional, national, and international service and equipment providers were involved in the project.

5. **Ownership Structure**
   - **10/2023**: Blue Line Energy is a renewable energy producer and holds an ESRE license since 2007. The facility is a joint project with Parapet, indicating a collaborative ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by Blue Line Energy.
