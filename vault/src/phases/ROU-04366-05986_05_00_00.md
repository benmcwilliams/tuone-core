### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sargent & Lundy Facility in Doicești, Romania

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: July 2024
   - **Details**: The facility is part of Romania's small modular reactor (SMR) project, which aims for an installed capacity of 462 MW using NuScale technology. The project will consist of six modules, each with an installed capacity of 77 MW.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: July 2024
   - **Details**: The project is supported by a potential financing commitment of up to USD 1 billion from the US International Development Finance Corporation. This investment is crucial for the project's development and implementation.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: July 2024
   - **Details**: The FEED 2 contract was signed between RoPower Nuclear and Fluor, which will provide design and engineering services necessary for the project. This contract is a significant step towards finalizing the cost estimate and schedule for the project.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: July 2024
   - **Details**: The SMR project is expected to create nearly 200 permanent jobs, 1,500 construction jobs, and 2,300 manufacturing and component assembly jobs, along with additional jobs for facility operation and maintenance over the 60-year lifespan of the facility.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: July 2024
   - **Details**: The facility is a joint effort involving multiple stakeholders, including Nuclearelectrica, RoPower Nuclear, Nova Power & Gas (part of the E-INFRA group), Fluor, Samsung C&T Corporation, and Sargent & Lundy. - "The US International Development Finance Corporation restated its interest in considering providing up to USD1 billion of financing for the project."
