## ROU-04366-07123

### Evolutionary Timeline of RoPower Nuclear Facility in Doicești, ROU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2024
   - **Details:** RoPower Nuclear is developing a small modular reactor (SMR) project at the former coal plant site in Doicești, Romania. The project aims for an installed capacity of 462 MW, utilizing NuScale technology with six modules, each having an installed capacity of 77 MW.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** July 2024
   - **Details:** The project is supported by a potential financing commitment of up to USD 1 billion from the US International Development Finance Corporation. This investment is crucial for the project's development and implementation.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2024
   - **Details:** RoPower Nuclear and Fluor signed a FEED 2 contract, which includes design and engineering services necessary for the project. This contract will lead to an updated cost estimate and schedule, along with safety and security analyses required for a final investment decision.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** July 2024
   - **Details:** The SMR project is expected to create nearly 200 permanent jobs, 1,500 construction jobs, and 2,300 manufacturing and component assembly jobs, in addition to jobs related to facility operation and maintenance over the 60-year lifespan of the facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** July 2024
   - **Details:** RoPower Nuclear is a joint venture involving Nuclearelectrica, Nova Power & Gas (part of the E-INFRA group), and Fluor, along with Samsung C&T Corporation and Sargent & Lundy, indicating that RoPower does not have exclusive ownership of the facility.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the RoPower Nuclear facility in Doicești, Romania, as of July 2024.

### Technology and Operations
- "Romania's small modular reactor (SMR) project is aiming for 462 MW installed capacity, using NuScale technology with six modules at the former coal plant site at Doicești, each with an installed capacity of 77 MW."
- "When completed, the plant will be the first of its kind in Europe."

### Investment and Development
- "The US International Development Finance Corporation restated its interest in considering providing up to USD1 billion of financing for the project."

### Labour

### Ownership Structure
