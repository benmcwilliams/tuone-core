### Evolutionary Timeline of Samsung C&T Corporation Facility in Doicești, ROU

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: July 2024
   - **Details**: The facility is part of Romania's small modular reactor (SMR) project, aiming for an installed capacity of 462 MW using NuScale technology. The project will consist of six modules, each with an installed capacity of 77 MW.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: July 2024
   - **Details**: The project is supported by a potential financing of up to USD 1 billion from the US International Development Finance Corporation. This investment is crucial for the development and deployment of the NuScale SMR power plants in Romania.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: July 2024
   - **Details**: The FEED 2 contract was signed between RoPower Nuclear and Fluor, which will provide design and engineering services for the SMR project. This contract is a step towards finalizing the investment decision for the facility.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: July 2024
   - **Details**: The SMR project is expected to create nearly 200 permanent jobs, 1,500 construction jobs, and 2,300 manufacturing and component assembly jobs, along with additional jobs for facility operation and maintenance over its 60-year lifespan.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
   - **Date**: July 2024
   - **Details**: The facility is a joint effort involving multiple stakeholders, including RoPower Nuclear, Nuclearelectrica, Nova Power & Gas (part of the E-INFRA group), Fluor, Samsung C&T Corporation, and Sargent & Lundy.
