#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2023**: Enel Green Power Romania commissioned a 63MW solar PV plant in Călugăreni. This facility covers an area of 88 hectares and utilizes approximately 120,000 bifacial monocrystalline solar panels. The plant is designed to produce an annual electricity output of approximately 99 GWh, making it the largest photovoltaic plant in the company's portfolio to date. Following this commissioning, Enel's total solar installed capacity in Romania reached nearly 100MW, with a total of approximately 600MW across wind and solar generation plants.
