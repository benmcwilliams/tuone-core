## ROU-04625-05465

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Korea Hydro & Nuclear Power Facility in Cernavoda, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2024**: Ansaldo Nucleare signs a memorandum of understanding (MOU) with Societatea Nationala Nuclearelectrica (SNN) for the refurbishment of Cernavoda NPP Unit 1 and the construction of new Units 3 and 4. The existing CANDU 6-type units (Units 1 and 2) have a capacity of 700MW each and began operations in 1996 and 2007, respectively. Together, they provide 20% of Romania’s energy needs.
- **December 2024**: A €1.9bn EPC contract is signed for the refurbishment of Unit 1, which aims to extend its operational life until 2054. The refurbishment includes retubing works and replacing major components, ensuring the unit's safe operation for another lifecycle.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2024**: The refurbishment and expansion project is potentially financed up to €2bn ($2.16bn), supported by the Italian export trade agency SACE.
- **December 2024**: The EPC contract valued at €1.9bn ($1.97bn) is awarded to a consortium that includes Korea Hydro & Nuclear Power, aimed at extending Unit 1's operational life and supporting Romania's decarbonisation goals.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2025**: The refurbishment works for Unit 1 are expected to commence. The project is pending approval from the Canadian government and Nuclearelectrica shareholders.
- **Future Plans**: The completion of Units 3 and 4 is planned, which will be based on the design of Unit 2, with a target operational date around 2031-2032.

#### 4. Labour
- The refurbishment and expansion projects are expected to contribute to job creation and the development of a new generation of nuclear specialists, although specific job numbers have not been detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility is owned by Societatea Nationala Nuclearelectrica (SNN), a Romanian state-owned nuclear power producer. Korea Hydro & Nuclear Power is part of a consortium involved in the refurbishment and expansion projects, indicating a collaborative approach rather than exclusive ownership.
