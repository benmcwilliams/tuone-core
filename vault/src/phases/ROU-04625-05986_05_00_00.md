### Evolutionary Timeline of the Sargent & Lundy Facility in Cernavoda, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Production Capacity**: The Cernavoda nuclear power plant, operated by Societatea Nationala Nuclearelectrica, currently produces 1.4 GW of electricity with two operational units, meeting 20% of Romania’s electricity demand.
- **Expansion Plans**: Plans are in place to add units 3 and 4, which will almost double the facility’s power output. This expansion is part of a broader effort to enhance sustainability and energy security in the region.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment Decisions**: In November 2024, Fluor and its joint venture partners, including Sargent & Lundy, received a limited notice to proceed with the design phase for units 3 and 4. This includes providing essential information for a final investment decision, which encompasses engineering and construction plans, revised cost estimates, and initial nuclear safety assessments.
- **Support for Refurbishment and Expansion**: A memorandum of understanding (MoU) was signed in early 2024 between Ansaldo Nucleare and Societatea Nationala Nuclearelectrica for the refurbishment of Unit 1 and the construction of new units 3 and 4, backed by the Italian export credit agency, SACE.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Key Announcements**: On July 23-24, 2024, U.S. Secretary of Energy Jennifer Granholm highlighted the modernization plans for the Cernavoda nuclear power plant during her visit to Romania, which includes collaboration with U.S. companies Fluor and Sargent & Lundy.
- **Limited Notice to Proceed**: The limited notice to proceed for the design phase of units 3 and 4 was granted in November 2024, marking a significant step towards the expansion of the facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: While specific job creation figures have not been detailed in the articles, the expansion of the Cernavoda nuclear power plant is expected to create jobs associated with the construction and operation of the new units.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: The facility is operated by Societatea Nationala Nuclearelectrica, with Sargent & Lundy being part of a joint venture that includes Fluor, AtkinsRéalis, and Ansaldo Nucleare for the expansion and modernization efforts. The exact ownership structure of the facility itself remains under the control of Societatea Nationala Nuclearelectrica, with Sargent & Lundy involved as a contractor rather than an owner.
