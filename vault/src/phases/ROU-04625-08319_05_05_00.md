### Evolutionary Timeline of the AtkinsRéalis Facility in Cernavoda, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Production**: The Cernavoda nuclear power plant, operated by Societatea Nationala Nuclearelectrica, currently produces 1.4 GW of electricity with two operational units, meeting 20% of Romania’s electricity demand.
- **Future Expansion**: The addition of units 3 and 4 is planned, which will almost double the facility’s power output. The refurbishment of Unit 1 is also scheduled to extend its operational life until 2054.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment for Refurbishment**: On December 20, 2024, a €1.9 billion ($1.97 billion) engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) contract was signed for the refurbishment of Unit 1. This contract involves a consortium that includes AtkinsRéalis and aims to ensure the unit's operation for another lifecycle.
- **Funding for Expansion**: The expansion project for units 3 and 4 is backed by a memorandum of understanding signed in early 2024, which includes support from the Italian export credit agency, SACE.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Limited Notice to Proceed**: On November 18, 2024, Fluor and its joint venture partners, including AtkinsRéalis, received a limited notice to proceed with the design phase of units 3 and 4.
- **EPC Contract Signing**: The EPC contract for the refurbishment of Unit 1 was signed on December 20, 2024, with works expected to commence in February 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The refurbishment and expansion projects are expected to create jobs, although specific numbers have not been disclosed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Joint Venture**: The facility is not exclusively owned by AtkinsRéalis; it operates as part of a joint venture with Fluor, Sargent & Lundy, and Ansaldo Nucleare for the expansion project. Additionally, the refurbishment contract involves collaboration with other companies, indicating a shared ownership structure in the operations and projects at the facility.
