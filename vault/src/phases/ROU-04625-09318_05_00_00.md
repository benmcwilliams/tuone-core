## ROU-04625-09318

### Evolutionary Timeline of KHNP Facility in Cernavoda, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: KHNP signed an EPC contract for the construction of a tritium removal facility at the Cernavoda Nuclear Power Plant. This facility will utilize advanced technology for capturing and processing tritium, enhancing environmental safety and workplace conditions. It is the world's third and the first in Europe.
- **June 2024**: Construction of the tritium removal facility commenced, with an expected completion date of September 2027. The facility will include high-technology areas such as liquid phase isotopic separation and cryogenic distillation.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The EPC contract for the tritium removal facility was valued at approximately KRW 260 billion (USD 200 million).
- **January 2024**: Nuclearelectrica secured a loan agreement of EUR 145 million (USD 159 million) from the European Investment Bank to support the tritium removal facility project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: KHNP signed the EPC contract for the tritium removal facility.
- **June 2024**: Construction officially began on the tritium removal facility after receiving regulatory approvals in May 2024.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or job loss figures related to the tritium removal facility or other projects at the Cernavoda facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- KHNP is involved in the Cernavoda facility as the EPC contractor for the tritium removal facility. The ownership of the facility itself is primarily held by Nuclearelectrica, which is 82.49% state-owned. KHNP collaborates with other companies, including Candu Energy and Ansaldo Nucleare, for various projects at the facility, indicating a partnership rather than exclusive ownership.
