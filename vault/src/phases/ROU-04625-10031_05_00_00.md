### Evolutionary Timeline of the Societatea Nationala Nuclearelectrica Facility in Cernavoda, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Production**: The Cernavoda nuclear power plant, operated by Societatea Nationala Nuclearelectrica, currently produces 1.4 GW of electricity with two operational units, meeting 20% of Romania’s electricity demand.
- **Future Expansion**: Plans are in place to add units 3 and 4, which will almost double the facility’s power output. This expansion is part of Romania's strategy to enhance sustainability and energy security.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Investment for Expansion**: In early 2024, a memorandum of understanding (MoU) was signed between Ansaldo Nucleare and Societatea Nationala Nuclearelectrica for the refurbishment of Unit 1 and the construction of new units 3 and 4. This collaboration is supported by the Italian export credit agency, SACE.
- **Final Investment Decision**: As of November 2024, Fluor and its joint venture partners have been granted a limited notice to proceed with the design phase of units 3 and 4, which includes providing essential information for a final investment decision.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **MoU Signing**: Early 2024 - Ansaldo Nucleare signed an MoU with Societatea Nationala Nuclearelectrica for refurbishment and expansion.
- **Limited Notice to Proceed**: November 2024 - Fluor-led JV received a limited notice to proceed with the design phase for units 3 and 4 at the Cernavoda nuclear power plant.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: The expansion of the facility with the addition of units 3 and 4 is expected to create jobs, although specific numbers have not been detailed in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
