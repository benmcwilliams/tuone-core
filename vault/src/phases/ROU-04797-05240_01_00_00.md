## ROU-04797-05240

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hidroelectrica Facility in Ostrovu Mare, ROU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2021**: Hidroelectrica announced plans to establish a 50 MW green hydrogen production unit on the Danube island of Ostrovu Mare. The facility will utilize water hydrolysis to produce ultra high-purity green hydrogen, with an expected annual production of at least 7,500 tons. The production process aims for an efficiency of 75%, and the hydrogen is to be produced using resources from the Danube and renewable energy generated by a dedicated photovoltaic park.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2021**: The company is seeking a consultant to prepare a feasibility study for the hydrogen production unit, which will also assess the potential connection to the nearby Portile de Fier II hydropower plant as a backup energy source for the photovoltaic park.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2021**: The announcement of the feasibility study marks the initial milestone for the project, indicating the company's commitment to developing the green hydrogen facility.

#### 4. Labour
- **December 2021**: No specific job creation or job loss figures were mentioned in the article regarding the establishment of the hydrogen production unit.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **December 2021**: Hidroelectrica is a majority state-owned company, indicating that it has full ownership of the facility in Ostrovu Mare.
