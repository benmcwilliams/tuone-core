## ROU-05280-05719

### Evolutionary Timeline of the OMV Petrom Facility in Petrobrazi, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: OMV Petrom announced plans to construct a 20 MW hydrogen production facility utilizing water electrolysis technology, powered by renewable energy sources. The goal is to achieve a minimum of 100 MW of renewable hydrogen production capacity by December 31, 2025, with an annual output of at least 10,000 tons of hydrogen.
- **February 2024**: OMV Petrom signed contracts to build two additional green hydrogen production facilities at the Petrobrazi refinery, totaling 55 MW capacity (35 MW and 20 MW). The hydrogen produced will be classified as green hydrogen due to its carbon-free production process.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: OMV Petrom received a grant as part of a larger scheme totaling EUR 148.8 million to promote renewable hydrogen production in Romania. The company plans to invest EUR 74 million in the initial hydrogen production facility.
- **February 2024**: The total investment for the two new green hydrogen projects is approximately EUR 140 million, with a maximum financing amount of EUR 50 million secured from the National Recovery and Resilience Plan (PNRR).

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: Announcement of the hydrogen production facility project and signing of the financing agreement with the Ministry of Energy.
- **February 2024**: Signing of two financing contracts for the construction of the 55 MW green hydrogen production capacity at the Petrobrazi refinery.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2023**: The construction of the hydrogen production facility is expected to create job opportunities during the construction phase, contributing positively to the local economy.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- OMV Petrom is the sole owner of the facility, as indicated by the absence of any mention of joint ventures or partnerships in the articles.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the OMV Petrom facility in Petrobrazi, Romania, focusing on its clean technology initiatives and investments in hydrogen production.
