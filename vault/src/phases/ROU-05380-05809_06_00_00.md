### Evolutionary Timeline of the Photon Energy Facility in Bocșa, ROU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** February 2024
   - **Details:** Photon Energy completed and grid-connected its tenth photovoltaic (PV) power plant in Romania, located near Bocșa, Caraș-Severin County. The facility has a generation capacity of 3.8 MWp and is equipped with 6,160 high-efficiency bifacial photovoltaic modules mounted on single-axis trackers. The expected total annual production is around 5.9 GWh. This power plant expands Photon Energy's Independent Power Producer (IPP) portfolio in Romania to a total of 39.1 MWp and increases its global capacity to 131.1 MWp.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** February 2024
   - **Details:** The Bocșa power plant is part of Photon Energy's broader investment strategy in Romania, where the company anticipates generating approximately EUR 490,000 in revenues in 2024 based on current electricity market prices. The facility operates without governmental support or a power purchase agreement.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** February 2024
