## ROU-05381-05809

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Photon Energy Facility in Teiuș, Romania

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2023
   - **Details:** Photon Energy has commissioned two solar photovoltaic (PV) power plants in Romania, with a combined generation capacity of 9.5 MWp. The plants utilize high-efficiency bifacial solar modules mounted on single-axis trackers, expected to deliver a total of 13.9 GWh of renewable energy annually. The electricity generated will be sold on the energy market without any support or power purchase agreement.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2023
   - **Details:** The company anticipates generating EUR 2.0 million in revenues from the plants based on current forward prices for electricity in Romania over the next 12 months. The commissioning of these plants is part of Photon Energy's strategy to expand its independent power producer (IPP) portfolio, which is expected to grow to over 120 MWp shortly.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2023
   - **Details:** The commissioning of the power plants in Aiud and Teiuș marks an important milestone for Photon Energy in the Romanian renewable energy market. The plants are located on 6.6 and 10 hectares of land, respectively, and are part of a broader plan to develop additional capacity in Romania.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2023
   - **Details:** The article does not specify job creation or job loss directly associated with the commissioning of the Teiuș facility.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** 10-05-2023
   - **Details:** The power plants are owned and operated by special-purpose companies that are fully owned by Photon Energy Group, indicating full ownership of the facility by the company.
