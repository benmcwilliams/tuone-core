## ROU-05679-06205

### Evolutionary Timeline of the TeraPlast Facility in Sărățel, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2023**: TeraPlast announced the development of a new photovoltaic plant in the Industrial Park of Sărățel, designed for a power capacity of approximately 4.56 MWp, with an expected production capacity of around 3.81 MW from inverters. The plant will include an integrated storage facility with a capacity of 920 kW. The project will consist of over 8,000 solar panels.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2023**: TeraPlast signed a financing contract for 5.5 million RON (approximately €1.1 million) through the National Recovery and Resilience Plan (PNRR) to support the photovoltaic plant project. The total budget for the project is 22.5 million RON, indicating that the PNRR funding covers about a quarter of the total investment. Additionally, TeraPlast plans to invest around €3.4 million (US$3.7 million) of its own funds to complete the project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2023**: TeraPlast received the funding and announced the project, with the first step being the publication of a competitive procedure for assigning a supply contract. The construction of the photovoltaic plant is expected to be completed by **June 2024**.
