## ROU-06580-07080

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nuclearelectrica Facility in Doicesti, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: Nuclearelectrica, in collaboration with NuScale Power and RoPower Nuclear, is advancing the deployment of a NuScale VOYGR-6 small modular reactor (SMR) plant at the former coal plant site in Doicesti. The VOYGR-6 plant will have an installed capacity of 462 MWe, utilizing six modules, each with a capacity of 77 MWe.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: A funding announcement of up to USD 275 million was made at the G7 leaders' summit to support the deployment of the NuScale SMR in Romania. This includes potential financial support from the US Export-Import Bank (EXIM) for up to USD 99 million and additional Letters of Interest for up to USD 3 billion from EXIM and USD 1 billion from the US International Development Finance Corporation (DFC).

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: Nuclearelectrica and RoPower Nuclear are conducting a Phase 1 Front-End Engineering and Design (FEED) study for the Doicesti site. The funding will support the Phase 2 FEED work, which includes site characterization and regulatory analyses.
- **April 2024**: The International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) approved the site selection process for the SMR project in Doicesti, confirming that the process meets safety standards. This approval is a critical step before applying for a site license.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2024**: The SMR project is projected to create approximately 200 permanent jobs, 1,500 construction jobs, and 2,300 manufacturing and component assembly jobs, along with additional jobs for facility operation and maintenance over the 60-year lifespan of the facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2023**: The facility is a joint venture between Nuclearelectrica and Nova Power & Gas, under the project development company RoPower Nuclear. Nuclearelectrica is a state-owned company, indicating that it has significant control over the facility, but it is not the sole owner.

This timeline captures the key developments and milestones related to the Nuclearelectrica facility in Doicesti, Romania, focusing on its technological advancements, investments, project status, labor implications, and ownership structure.
