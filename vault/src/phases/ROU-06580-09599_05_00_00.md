## ROU-06580-09599

### Evolutionary Timeline of NuScale Power Corporation Facility in Doicesti, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: 05/2023
  - The facility is set to deploy a NuScale Power Corporation VOYGR small modular reactor (SMR) plant, specifically a VOYGR-6 model, which has a production capacity of 462 MWe. The facility will replace an old coal-fired power plant in Doicesti.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: 05/2023
  - Funding of up to USD 275 million was announced to advance the deployment of the SMR plant. This includes a Letter of Interest from the US Export-Import Bank (EXIM) for up to USD 99 million, aimed at supporting procurement of long lead materials, Phase 2 Front End Engineering and Design work, and other project management activities. Additional potential financial support of up to USD 3 billion and USD 1 billion was indicated from EXIM and the US International Development Finance Corporation, respectively.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: 05/2023
  - NuScale and RoPower Nuclear (a joint venture between Romania's Nuclearelectrica and Nova Power and Gas) are conducting a Phase 1 study to analyze the preferred site for the VOYGR-6 SMR power plant. The new funding will support the Phase 2 study, which will initiate the process of securing authorizations and licenses for the project. The project is expected to be operational by 2029.

#### 4. Labour
- **Date**: 05/2023
  - While specific job creation numbers were not mentioned, the project is expected to create jobs as it transitions from a coal-fired power plant to a modern nuclear facility, indicating a positive impact on local employment.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date**: 05/2023
  - The facility is part of a joint venture between Nuclearelectrica and Nova Power and Gas, indicating that NuScale Power Corporation does not own the facility exclusively but is collaborating with local partners for its development.
