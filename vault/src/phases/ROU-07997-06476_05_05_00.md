   - The ALFRED unit will have a thermal capacity of 300 MWt and is intended to demonstrate the technological and engineering aspects of commercial SMRs. 
   - The reactor will utilize mixed-oxide fuel and operate at temperatures around 550°C, featuring passive safety systems.
