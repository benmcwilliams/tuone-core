### Evolutionary Timeline of the Black Sea Oil & Gas Facility in Black Sea, ROU

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **July 2023**: Black Sea Oil & Gas (BSOG) announced the initiation of the permitting process for a power corridor in the Black Sea, utilizing its existing Midia Gas Development (MGD) infrastructure. This corridor is designed to connect future offshore wind farms to the Transelectrica national power grid (SEN) with a planned capacity of **3 GW**.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2024**: The feasibility study for offshore wind development was completed by the UK-based consultancy OWC, indicating a significant step towards assessing the commercial viability of the offshore wind project. The Romanian government aims to designate offshore wind energy areas and define tender procedures by mid-2025, with plans for the first offshore wind project to be built by **2032**.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **09-05-2024**: BSOG's CEO Mark Beacom announced the completion of the feasibility study and the ongoing assessment of the technical potential for offshore wind development in Romania’s Black Sea. The regulatory procedures for the power corridor are expected to be completed by **mid-2024**.
