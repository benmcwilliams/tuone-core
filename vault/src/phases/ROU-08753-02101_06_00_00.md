### Evolutionary Timeline of the Martifer Facility in Pufesti, Romania

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** August 2012
   - **Details:** Martifer Solar completed the construction of a 1.5MW photovoltaic (PV) plant in Pufesti, Vrancea County, Romania. The facility utilizes 240W polycrystalline MPrime modules installed on fixed structures, covering an area of almost 4 hectares. The output is sufficient to power nearly 1,000 households annually.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 2011 (contract signed)
   - **Details:** Martifer Solar signed a contract with Danish company Eurowind Energy for the distribution of Martifer Solar’s MPrime components in Denmark and other Scandinavian countries, indicating a strategic investment in expanding their market presence.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** July 2012
   - **Details:** The PV plant was completed at the beginning of July 2012.
   - **Date:** August 2, 2012
   - **Details:** The plant was connected to the Romanian national grid, marking it as one of the first large-scale PV plants to achieve this milestone in Romania.
