### GSP Offshore Facility Timeline - Constanta, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **February 2023**: GSP Offshore is involved in the BLOW (Black Sea fLoating Offshore Wind) project, which aims to install a 5 MW floating wind turbine off the Bulgarian coast. The floating turbine will be produced at the Constanta shipyard, showcasing GSP Offshore's capabilities in offshore wind technology.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **February 2023**: GSP Offshore will receive approximately €12.56 million for its services related to the BLOW project, which includes the production and transportation of the floating wind turbine.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **February 2023**: The BLOW project is launched with a budget of €21 million, including over €15 million from the EU. The project aims to test the first floating wind turbine in the Black Sea, with the turbine expected to be operational by 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- **February 2023**: The offshore wind deployment in Romania, including projects like BLOW, is projected to create significant job opportunities. The analysis indicates that 3 GW of offshore wind installed in Romania could generate approximately 22,000 new full-time equivalent (FTE) jobs, with 15,500 of these being direct local jobs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **February 2023**: GSP Offshore is controlled by Gabriel Comanescu, indicating that the company has full ownership of its operations at the Constanta facility.
