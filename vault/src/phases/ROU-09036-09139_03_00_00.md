### Evolutionary Timeline of Hidro Tarniţa SA Facility in Tarnita-Lapustesti, ROU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is planned to be a reversible, pumped hydropower plant with a production capacity between 500 – 1,000 MW. The project aims to establish solutions for the construction of this facility.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - A feasibility study is being contracted to assess the construction of the pumped storage hydroelectric power plant, with initial investment estimates from 2019 at EUR 1.15 billion. The execution of the works is anticipated to take place over seven years.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **July 2023:** Romania's Ministry of Energy announced the initiation of pre-feasibility consultations to contract a feasibility study for the Tarnita-Lapustesti pumped hydropower plant.
   - **2019:** The Romanian Government approved the project, which had been initiated since the 1970s but not implemented until now. Hidro Tarniţa SA was established in 2013 to implement the project.
