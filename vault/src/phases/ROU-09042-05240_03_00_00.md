### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hidroelectrica Facility in Tudor Vladimirescu, Brăila, ROU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **September 2021:** Hidroelectrica announced plans to develop a 45 MW ground-mounted photovoltaic (PV) project in Tudor Vladimirescu, Brăila County. This project marks the company's entry into solar power, complementing its existing portfolio of 6.4 GW of hydropower assets. Additionally, the company plans to deploy 5 MW of floating solar plants at its hydropower facilities.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **September 2021:** The investment for the 45 MW PV project is estimated at approximately $42.6 million (ROL 193 million). This project is part of a broader initiative to deploy a total of 50 MW of PV capacity in Romania.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **September 2021:** Hidroelectrica is in the process of seeking experts to conduct a feasibility study for the 45 MW PV project in Tudor Vladimirescu. This project is significant as it represents the company's first solar power station.
