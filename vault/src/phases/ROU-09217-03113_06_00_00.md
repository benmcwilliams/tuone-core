### Evolutionary Timeline of the Juwi Facility in Satu Mare, Romania

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2013
   - **Details:** Juwi has initiated the construction of a photovoltaic (PV) power plant in Satu Mare, part of a larger project that includes another plant near Bucharest. The total capacity of the two plants is 13 MW, which is expected to power approximately 4,700 households in Romania, generating around 16 million kWh annually.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2013
   - **Details:** Juwi secured a €252 million (US$336 million) loan from a consortium of 13 financial institutions to fund the construction of the solar plants, indicating significant investment in the development of renewable energy infrastructure in Romania.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2013
   - **Details:** Construction has commenced on the Satu Mare PV plant, with expectations for grid connection by November 2013. The project is being managed by Juwi’s subsidiaries in Italy and Bulgaria.
