### Evolutionary Timeline of the Soltec Facility in Giurgiu, Romania

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** July 2023
   - **Details:** Soltec is supplying its SF7 solar trackers for a 130MW solar PV project in Giurgiu, Romania. The project will utilize 3,083 SF7 trackers and 227,240 solar modules. This facility will be the largest solar plant in Romania that Soltec has worked on to date. The SF7 tracker is noted for its efficiency, featuring a 2-port configuration that reduces construction and installation time, requiring 54% fewer piles and 58% fewer parts compared to competitors.
