### Evolutionary Timeline of the RATEN Facility in Mioveni, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Production**: The RATEN facility in Mioveni is involved in the construction of a 300 MWt demonstration unit of the Advanced Lead-cooled Fast Reactor European Demonstrator (ALFRED). This reactor will utilize mixed-oxide fuel and operate at temperatures around 550°C, featuring passive safety systems.
- **Existing Operations**: The facility also houses a fuel manufacturing plant that supports Romania's two operating Candu reactors.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Total Project Cost**: The total cost for the ALFRED project is estimated at EUR 1.0 billion (USD 1.1 billion).
- **Funding for Research and Development**: Belgium has allocated EUR 100 million to the research and development of Small Modular Reactors (SMRs) as part of its energy transition strategy.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **MoU Signing**: On 10-11-2023, a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) was signed in Brussels involving RATEN, SCK-CEN, ENEA, Ansaldo Nucleare, and Westinghouse Electric Company to accelerate the development of lead-cooled SMRs.
- **Construction Plans**: The consortium aims to construct the ALFRED reactor in Pitești, Romania, leveraging previous work done under the Fostering ALFRED Construction (FALCON) consortium.
