### Evolutionary Timeline of the OMV Petrom Facility in Işalniţa, ROU

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** OMV Petrom announced plans to build a 111 MW solar power plant at a former chemical plant in Işalniţa, Dolj county. The facility will utilize 175,214 solar panels, each with a power output of 485 W, and 424 string inverters with a capacity of 185 kVA. The expected annual energy generation is 125,913 MWh.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The project is budgeted at €103.5 million. It will be connected to the local grid via a new 20/110 kV substation.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2021
   - **Details:** The construction of the solar power plant is expected to last around 24 months. The project is adjacent to a coal power plant operated by Complexul Energetic Oltenia and another 4 MW solar park under development.
