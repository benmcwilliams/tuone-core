### Evolutionary Timeline of the Photon Energy Facility in Făget, Romania

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2024**: Photon Energy Engineering Romania completed the Făget 3 solar PV power plant with a capacity of 7.5 MWp. The facility covers 9.1 hectares and is equipped with 12,216 bifacial PV modules mounted on single-axis trackers. The plant is expected to produce approximately 11.1 GWh of electricity annually. This facility is the third PV power plant in Făget, contributing to a total installed capacity of 14.6 MWp across the three plants.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2024**: The Romanian government is providing financial incentives for solar projects, including a rebate of €425,000 ($473,000) per MW installed for solar arrays over 1 MW. This support is part of Romania's strategy to meet its renewable energy targets.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2024**: The Făget 3 solar PV power plant was connected to the Romanian grid, marking the completion of the project. This addition expands Photon Energy's portfolio in Romania to a total of twelve power plants with a combined capacity of 48.4 MWp.
