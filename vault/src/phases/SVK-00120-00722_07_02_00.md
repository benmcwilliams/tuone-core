### Evolutionary Timeline of the Skoda Facility in Bratislava, Slovakia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2019**: Skoda introduced its first electrified models under the new iV subbrand in Bratislava, including the battery electric Citigo e iV and the plug-in hybrid Superb iV. The Citigo e iV features a 36.8 kWh battery with a range of up to 265 km and a 61 kW electric motor.
- **November 2019**: Series production of the Citigo e iV commenced at the Bratislava plant, marking a significant milestone as Skoda's first all-electric model.
- **April 2020**: Production of the Citigo e iV was temporarily suspended due to COVID-19 restrictions, impacting the ability to fulfill orders in the UK.
- **November 2023**: Skoda unveiled the fourth generation of the Superb, which will again be available as a plug-in hybrid (PHEV) with a new 25.7 kWh battery, allowing for an electric range of over 100 km.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2019**: Skoda announced plans to convert production in its Czech factories to green energy by the second half of the next decade, indicating a commitment to sustainable practices in their operations.
