- **May 2019**: Volkswagen announces plans to manufacture a small electric car for under €20,000 in Eastern Europe, with the Bratislava facility being a favored option for production. The facility is currently producing purely battery-operated small cars on an older vehicle platform.

- **April 2020**: Production resumes at the Bratislava plant after a COVID-19 shutdown, with the facility set to produce models including the Skoda Citigo-e and PHEV versions of the Porsche Cayenne and Audi Q7.
- **November 2020**: The facility continues to produce the VW e-Up, Seat Mii electric, and Skoda Citigo iV.
