This package features the unique "eHydroCOM" compressor capacity system, which allows for high regulation precision and a broad regulation range. The HCP 500 is designed to achieve a high mass flow rate of over 250 kg/h (105,802 scf/h), making it suitable for heavy-duty hydrogen refuelling stations and trailer filling facilities.
