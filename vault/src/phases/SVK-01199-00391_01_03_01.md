### Evolutionary Timeline of InoBat Auto Facility in Voderady, Slovakia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **02/2020**: InoBat Auto announces plans for a 10 GWh battery cell factory in Slovakia, with a pilot production line of 100 MWh capacity to be built in Voderady.
- **10/2020**: InoBat orders production equipment from Manz for the 100 MWh production line, scheduled to start operations by the end of 2021.
- **09/2020**: InoBat presents its AI-driven battery cell technology, aiming for energy densities of 265 Wh/kg by late 2021 and 330-350 Wh/kg by the end of 2023.
- **05/2021**: InoBat secures its first commercial vehicle customer, SOR, for battery modules and packs.
- **08/2021**: InoBat partners with Group14 Technologies to produce high-energy-density batteries using lithium-silicon technology.
- **11/2021**: InoBat begins construction of its R&D center and pilot production line in Voderady.
- **01/2022**: InoBat announces plans for a 100 MWh pilot production facility and a 10 GWh factory by 2024.
- **12/2023**: InoBat launches its first battery cell production line in Voderady, capable of producing up to 50,000 cells annually.
- **06/2024**: Official start of production for battery cells in Slovakia, with plans for large-scale production in collaboration with Gotion High-Tech.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **02/2020**: InoBat receives €5 million from the Slovakian government, followed by an additional €10 million from CEZ Group.
- **10/2020**: Manz receives an order worth over €20 million for production equipment for the pilot line.
- **11/2021**: Rio Tinto invests in InoBat to support the development of a battery ecosystem in Europe.
- **01/2022**: Ideanomics invests in InoBat to support the completion of the R&D center and pilot battery plant.
- **10/2023**: Gotion High-Tech acquires a 25% stake in InoBat, marking a significant investment in the company.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **06/2020**: InoBat acquires a former TV factory in Voderady for its operations.
- **10/2020**: Announcement of the 100 MWh production line to be operational by the end of 2021.
- **05/2021**: InoBat announces a partnership with SOR for battery supply.
- **11/2021**: Construction of the R&D center and pilot line begins.
- **12/2023**: InoBat officially launches its pilot production line.
- **2024**: Planned start of a 10 GWh factory.
- **2026**: Expected start of production at the joint Gotion-InoBat Batteries factory in Slovakia.

#### 4. Labour
- **05/2021**: The partnership with SOR is expected to create job opportunities in the region.
- **12/2023**: The pilot line employs 41 operators for 24/7 production.
- **06/2024**: The Gotion-InoBat Batteries factory is expected to create over 1,500 jobs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **10/2023**: Gotion High-Tech acquires a 25% stake in InoBat, indicating a joint venture structure for future projects.
- **12/2023**: InoBat and Gotion plan to establish a joint battery cell factory, further solidifying their partnership.
