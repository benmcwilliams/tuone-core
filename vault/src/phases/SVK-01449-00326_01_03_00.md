### Evolutionary Timeline of Gotion High Tech Facility in Šurany, Slovakia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2023**: Gotion High-Tech and InoBat announced plans for a joint battery cell factory in Šurany, Slovakia, with an initial production capacity of 20 GWh, which can potentially be expanded to 40 GWh.
- **June 2024**: The factory's capacity was confirmed to be 20 GWh initially, with plans to expand to a total capacity of 60 GWh once fully operational.
- **December 2024**: Gotion High-Tech confirmed the establishment of a production facility in Slovakia with a capacity of 20 GWh, focusing on LFP and LMFP battery cells.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2024**: Gotion High-Tech announced a total investment of 2.51 billion euros for battery cell factories in Slovakia and Morocco, with 1.23 billion euros allocated for the facility in Šurany.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2023**: A letter of intent was signed between Gotion, InoBat, and the Slovakian government to establish the factory.
- **June 2024**: An Investment Agreement was signed, confirming the construction of the factory, which is set to begin towards the end of 2024, with operations expected to start in 2026.
- **Expected Timeline**: Production is anticipated to start in the second quarter of 2026, with series production commencing in 2027.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2024**: The construction of the factory is expected to create more than 1,000 jobs during the first phase of development.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **December 2024**: The joint venture, Gotion InoBat Batteries (GIB), was established, with Gotion High-Tech holding an 80% stake and InoBat holding 20%. This indicates that Gotion does not own the facility exclusively but is in a joint venture with InoBat.
