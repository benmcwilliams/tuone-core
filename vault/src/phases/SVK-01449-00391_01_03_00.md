## SVK-01449-00391

### Evolutionary Timeline of InoBat Auto Facility in Šurany, Slovakia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2023**: InoBat and Gotion High-Tech announced plans for a joint battery cell factory in Šurany, Slovakia, with an initial production capacity of 20 gigawatt-hours (GWh), which can potentially be expanded to 40 GWh.
- **June 2024**: The factory's capacity was confirmed to be 20 GWh initially, with plans to expand to 60 GWh once fully operational. The facility will utilize an innovative closed-loop circular value chain.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2023**: The Slovakian government agreed to provide subsidies and support for the construction of the factory, including the provision of a 65-hectare site and connection to the power grid.
- **June 2024**: An Investment Agreement was signed between the Slovak government and the joint venture, Gotion InoBat Batteries (GIB), although the financial details were not disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2023**: A letter of intent was signed between Gotion and InoBat with the Slovak government to establish the factory.
- **June 2024**: The construction of the factory is set to begin towards the end of 2024, with production expected to start in the second quarter of 2026 and series production in 2027.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2024**: The factory is expected to create more than 1,500 jobs during its operation, with the first phase alone projected to create over 1,000 jobs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **December 2023**: InoBat and Gotion established a joint venture named GIB (Gotion-InoBat-Batteries) to manage the factory, indicating a shared ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by either company.
