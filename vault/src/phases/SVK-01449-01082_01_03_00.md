## SVK-01449-01082

### Evolutionary Timeline of Gotion InoBat Batteries Facility in Šurany, Slovakia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2023**: Gotion High-Tech and InoBat announce plans for a joint battery cell factory in Šurany, Slovakia, with an initial production capacity of 20 gigawatt-hours (GWh), which can potentially be expanded to 40 GWh.
- **June 2024**: The factory's capacity is confirmed to be 20 GWh initially, with plans to increase to 60 GWh once fully operational. The facility will utilize an innovative closed-loop circular value-chain.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **November 2023**: A letter of intent is signed with the Slovakian government, which includes government subsidies and the provision of a 65-hectare site for the factory.
- **June 2024**: An Investment Agreement is signed between the government and the joint venture, confirming the establishment of the factory, although the financial details are not disclosed.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2023**: Announcement of the planned factory and signing of the letter of intent.
- **June 2024**: Confirmation of the factory's establishment through the signing of the Investment Agreement. Construction is set to begin towards the end of 2024, with production expected to start in the second quarter of 2026 and series production in 2027.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2024**: The factory is expected to create more than 1,500 jobs during its operation, with the first phase alone projected to create over 1,000 jobs.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **December 2023**: Gotion High-Tech and InoBat establish a joint venture named Gotion InoBat Batteries (GIB) to manage the facility, indicating a shared ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by either company.
