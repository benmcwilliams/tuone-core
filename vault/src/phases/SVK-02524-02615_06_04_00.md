### Agora Solar Facility Timeline - Vranow, SVK

1. **Technology and Operations**
   - **March 2022**: Agora Solar announced plans to build a solar module factory in Vranow with an initial production capacity of 150MW, focusing on glass-glass panels. The facility aims to expand its capacity to 500MW by 2024. The production line is designed to be flexible, allowing for the manufacturing of semi-transparent glass-glass modules suitable for building-integrated photovoltaic (BIPV) systems and agrivoltaics. The company also plans to offer bifacial solar modules with a 30-year performance guarantee.
