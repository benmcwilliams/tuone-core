### Evolutionary Timeline of the Schaeffler Facility in Kysuce, Slovakia

1. **Technology and Operations**:
   - **Date**: 03-07-2023
   - **Details**: Schaeffler opened a new development center in Kysuce focused on developing products and components for e-mobility and chassis applications for automated driving. The facility includes a new building covering 8,000 square meters, with 4,000 square meters dedicated to the development center and another 4,000 square meters for testing facilities and test benches. The adjacent production facility manufactures engine and transmission systems, various bearing variants, actuator systems, components for electric axles and hybrid drives, and chassis actuators.

2. **Investment and Development**:
   - **Date**: 03-07-2023
   - **Details**: Schaeffler is investing approximately 20 million euros in the new development center and the expansion of testing facilities.

3. **Project Status and Milestones**:
   - **Date**: End of 2021
   - **Details**: Construction of the new building began.
   - **Date**: 03-07-2023
   - **Details**: The new development center officially opened, with the first employees moving in after about one and a half years of construction.

4. **Labour**:
   - **Date**: 03-07-2023
   - **Details**: The facility currently employs around 4,300 people, with more than 350 in development. The new center is expected to create around 150 additional jobs, particularly for partially and fully electrified drives.

5. **Ownership Structure**:
