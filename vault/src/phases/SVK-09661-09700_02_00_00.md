## SVK-09661-09700

### Evolutionary Timeline of the PW Geoenergy Facility in Ziar nad Hronom, Slovakia

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 08-12-2023
   - **Details:** The facility is focused on geothermal energy production, with a total expected generation capacity of 20 MWe. The project involves drilling two geothermal wells: a vertical production well (GTZ-1) to a depth of 4000 meters and a deviated reinjection well (GTZ-2) to a vertical depth of 3400 meters. The geothermal water is expected to have a temperature of 130 to 140 degrees °C, which will be used to produce electricity. Additionally, residual heat from the process can be utilized for domestic heating, industrial heating, agriculture, and recreational purposes.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** Mid-2022
   - **Details:** Stredoslovenska energetika Holding (SSE Holding) acquired a stake in PW Geoenergy, which is a subsidiary established for the implementation of the geothermal power plant in Ziar nad Hronom. This investment is tied to the development of the geothermal power project.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** Early 2022
   - **Details:** A pre-tender request for information was published by PW Energy for drilling at the Ziar nad Hronom project.
   - **Date:** 08-12-2023
   - **Details:** The tender for the drilling of the two geothermal wells was announced.
