### Evolutionary Timeline of Stredoslovenska energetika Holding Facility in Ziar nad Hronom, Slovakia

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **08-12-2023:** The Ziar geothermal power project is set to produce electricity with a total expected generation capacity of 20 MWe. The facility will utilize deep geothermal water with temperatures between 130 to 140 degrees °C to generate electricity using modern technological equipment. Additionally, residual heat from the process can be used for domestic heating, industrial heating, agriculture, and recreational purposes.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Mid-2022:** Stredoslovenska energetika Holding (SSE Holding) acquired a stake in PW Geoenergy, a subsidiary established for the implementation of the geothermal power plant in Ziar nad Hronom. This acquisition indicates a commitment to the development of the geothermal project.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **08-12-2023:** A tender was announced for the drilling of two geothermal wells: a vertical production well (GTZ-1) to a depth of 4000 meters and a deviated reinjection well (GTZ-2) to a vertical depth of 3400 meters. This marks a significant step in the project’s development.
