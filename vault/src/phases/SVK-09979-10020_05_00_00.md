### Evolutionary Timeline of the Slovenske Elektrarne Facility in Jaslovske Bohunice, SVK

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Operations**: Slovenske Elektrarne operates two nuclear units at Jaslovske Bohunice, each with a capacity of 505 MW.
- **Recent Developments**: The company completed the 472 MW Unit 3 at the Mochovce plant in 2023 and is currently completing another unit at the same site.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Future Plans**: The Slovak government is planning to build a new nuclear power plant at Jaslovske Bohunice with a capacity of up to 1,200 MW. The project is expected to be fully state-owned, differing from the current operations which are partially state-owned through Slovenske Elektrarne.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2024**: Prime Minister Robert Fico announced plans for a new nuclear unit with an output of up to 1,200 MW at Jaslovske Bohunice.
- **October 2024**: The Slovak government has tasked its economy and finance ministers to develop a plan for the new nuclear power plant by the end of October, including supplier selection and financing details.

#### 4. Labour

#### 5. Ownership Structure
