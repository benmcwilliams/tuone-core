## SVK-09981-10022

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Slovenské elektrárne Facility in Nováky, Slovakia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: Slovenské elektrárne, in collaboration with various partners, submitted a bid for a feasibility study to convert coal-fired power plants, including the Nováky facility, to Small Modular Reactor (SMR) nuclear energy generation. This initiative aims to enhance Slovakia's energy mix by integrating nuclear technologies to replace coal-fired plants.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The joint application for funding from the US government's Project Phoenix included a grant request of EUR 2 million (USD 2.16 million) to support the feasibility study for the conversion of coal plants to SMR technology.

- **September 2023**: Slovenské elektrárne's proposal was selected to receive a share of USD 8 million from the US government under Project Phoenix, aimed at supporting coal-to-SMR conversions. This funding will facilitate feasibility studies at the Nováky site among others.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: The signing of a memorandum to apply for funding marked the first step towards assessing the suitability of SMR technologies for Slovakia, including the Nováky facility.

- **September 2023**: The announcement of successful grant applications under Project Phoenix confirmed that the Nováky site is among those being considered for future SMR development.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2023**: The Project Phoenix initiative emphasizes retaining local jobs through workforce retraining as part of the transition from coal to SMR technology, indicating potential job creation or retention at the Nováky facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2023**: Slovenské elektrárne is identified as the owner of the coal-fired power plants in Nováky, indicating full ownership of the facility. The facility's future development towards SMR technology will remain under the ownership of Slovenské elektrárne.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic initiatives related to the Slovenské elektrárne facility in Nováky, Slovakia, focusing on its transition towards cleaner energy solutions.
