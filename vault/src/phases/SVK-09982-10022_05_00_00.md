## SVK-09982-10022

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Slovenské elektrárne Facility in Vojany, Slovakia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2023**: Slovenské elektrárne, in collaboration with various partners, submitted a bid for a feasibility study to explore the conversion of coal-fired power plants, including the Vojany facility, to Small Modular Reactor (SMR) nuclear energy generation. This initiative aims to enhance Slovakia's energy mix by integrating stable and carbon-free energy sources.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **June 2023**: The joint application for funding from the US government's Project Phoenix includes a grant of EUR 2 million (USD 2.16 million) aimed at supporting the feasibility study for SMR technology at the Vojany site, among others.

- **September 2023**: Slovenské elektrárne's proposal for the SMR feasibility study was selected to receive a share of USD 8 million in US government support under Project Phoenix. This funding will facilitate the assessment of the Vojany site for potential future construction of SMRs.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2023**: The signing of a memorandum to apply for funding marks the first step in evaluating the suitability of SMR technologies for Slovakia, specifically targeting the Vojany facility for potential conversion.

- **September 2023**: The announcement of successful grant applications under Project Phoenix confirms that the Vojany site is among the five Slovak locations being considered for SMR feasibility studies.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2023**: The Project Phoenix initiative emphasizes retaining local jobs through workforce retraining as part of the transition from coal to SMR technology, indicating a focus on job preservation at the Vojany facility during this transformation.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2023**: Slovenské elektrárne is identified as the owner of the coal-fired power plants in Vojany, indicating full ownership of the facility as it explores the transition to SMR technology.

This timeline encapsulates the key developments and strategic initiatives surrounding the Slovenské elektrárne facility in Vojany, Slovakia, focusing on its transition towards cleaner energy solutions through the adoption of small modular reactors.
