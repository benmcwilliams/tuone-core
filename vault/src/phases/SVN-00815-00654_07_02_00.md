## SVN-00815-00654

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Renault Group Facility in Novo Mesto, Slovenia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **November 2023**: Renault's new electric car division, Ampere, announced the production of a new city electric car, initially referred to as "Legend," which is confirmed to be an electric Twingo. The facility in Novo Mesto will produce this vehicle, alongside the current Twingo and its electric version. The new Twingo is expected to consume 10 kWh/100 km and aims to be priced below 20,000 euros.
- **April 2024**: Development of the new electric Twingo has begun, with series production scheduled to start in 2026 at the Novo Mesto facility. The design has been finalized, and the company is preparing for production by selecting suppliers and building prototypes.
- **July 2024**: Renault signed a letter of intent with the Slovenian government to officially confirm the production of the new electric Twingo at the Revoz plant in Novo Mesto, with a target production capacity of 150,000 vehicles per year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **July 2024**: The Slovenian government will provide subsidies to support Renault's investment in electric vehicle production at the Novo Mesto facility. The specific amount of funding is not disclosed, but it is aimed at enhancing Slovenia's competitiveness as an industrial location and supporting the transition to electric vehicle manufacturing.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **November 2023**: Announcement of the new electric Twingo (initially called "Legend") to be produced in Novo Mesto.
- **April 2024**: Confirmation that series production of the new electric Twingo will start in 2026.
- **July 2024**: Official confirmation of the production location and signing of a Memorandum of Understanding with the Slovenian government.

#### 4. Labour
- **July 2024**: The investment in the new electric Twingo production is expected to create new jobs and improve productivity and salaries in Slovenia, as stated by the Slovenian Economics Minister.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The facility in Novo Mesto is owned by Renault through its subsidiary Revoz. There is no indication of joint ventures or shared ownership with other companies for this specific facility.

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic decisions regarding the Renault Group facility in Novo Mesto, Slovenia, focusing on the production of the new electric Twingo and the associated investments and operational plans.
