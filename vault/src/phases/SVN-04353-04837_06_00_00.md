### Evolutionary Timeline of Dravske elektrarne Maribor Facility in Formin, Slovenia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2021**: Dravske elektrarne Maribor (DEM) announced the construction of segment 5 of its 30 MW photovoltaic project, which includes a new 2.7 MW solar power facility to be installed on the left bank of the Drava river canal, downstream from the 136 MW Zlatoličje hydropower plant. This new unit is expected to produce approximately 3 GWh of electricity per year. The facility already has a 777 kW solar power plant on the roofs of its engine room, garage, and near the inlet side of the canal.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2021**: The construction of the new 2.7 MW photovoltaic unit is valued at EUR 2 million. This project is part of a larger initiative to install solar panels with a combined capacity of 30 MW in the area, which would make it the largest solar system in Slovenia.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2021**: Construction of segment 5 of the photovoltaic project is set to begin the week following the article's publication. The project is scheduled for completion by the end of the year. The Municipality of Ptuj approved the construction permit in May 2021.
