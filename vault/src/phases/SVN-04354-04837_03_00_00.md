### Evolutionary Timeline of Dravske elektrarne Maribor Facility in Zlatoličje, Slovenia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **August 2021**: Dravske elektrarne Maribor (DEM) announced the addition of a 2.7 MW photovoltaic facility to its existing 777 kW solar power plant at the Zlatoličje hydropower plant. This new solar unit will be constructed on the left bank of the Drava river canal, downstream from the 136 MW hydropower plant. The new segment is part of a larger 30 MW solar project planned for the area, which aims to produce 3 GWh per year.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **August 2021**: The construction of the 2.7 MW photovoltaic unit is valued at EUR 2 million. This investment is part of a broader initiative to expand solar energy production at the facility.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2021**: Construction of segment 5 of the 30 MW photovoltaic project was set to begin the week following the announcement. The project received a construction permit from the Municipality of Ptuj in May 2021. The completion of the new solar unit is scheduled for the end of 2021.
- **November 2021**: It was reported that DEM had started the construction of the first segment of its 30 MW photovoltaic system at the Zlatoličje hydropower plant.
