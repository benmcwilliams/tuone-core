## SVN-04798-05241

### Evolutionary Timeline of Hidroelektrarne na spodnji Savi Facility in Brežice, Slovenia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2020**: HESS announced plans to add a 6 MW photovoltaic unit to its Brežice hydroelectric plant, making it a hybrid power production system. This solar unit will be the largest of its kind in Slovenia at that time. The existing hydropower plant has a capacity of 47.4 MW, commissioned in 2017. The combined capacity of HESS's four hydropower plants on the lower Sava will be 158.1 MW after the completion of the planned 28 MW Mokrice system.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2020**: The estimated cost for the 6 MW photovoltaic unit is EUR 4.3 million. HESS aims to secure funding from the EU’s Cohesion Fund, which could cover up to 20% of the project costs.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2020**: HESS announced the project to build the photovoltaic unit, which is scheduled to come online in the first half of 2022. The facility will be linked to the existing hydropower plant via cable.
- **March 2021**: HESS had already installed a rooftop photovoltaic unit on its office building, indicating ongoing efforts to diversify energy production.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
