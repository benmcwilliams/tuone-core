### Evolutionary Timeline of the HESS Facility in Brežice, Slovenia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04/2020**: HESS announced the development of a **6 MW solar park** to be located close to its **47.7 MW Brežice hydroelectric plant** on the lower Sava River. The solar park will cover **6 hectares** of a **9-hectare sediment dump area** and will be linked to the hydroelectric plant via a cable line, functioning as its fourth unit. The project will also incorporate a **pumped hydro storage solution**.
