1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2021:** A solar power plant named Blate is planned to be built at the site of the former Trbovlje-Hrastnik coal mine. The facility is expected to have a peak capacity of **1.5 MW**, making it one of the largest solar power plants in Slovenia. The plant will consist of **2,600 monocrystalline ground-mounted panels** of **600 W each**, covering an area of **6,500 square meters**. The facility is set to connect to the grid in the **third quarter of 2022**.
