### Evolutionary Timeline of the Rudis Facility in Trbovlje, Slovenia

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **December 2021:** Rudis announced plans to build a solar power plant named Blate at the site of the former Trbovlje-Hrastnik coal mine. The facility is designed to have a peak capacity of **1.5 MW**, making it one of the largest solar power plants in Slovenia. The installation will consist of **2,600 monocrystalline ground-mounted panels** of **600 W** each, covering an area of **6,500 square meters**. The company plans to connect the power plant to the grid in the **third quarter of 2022**.
