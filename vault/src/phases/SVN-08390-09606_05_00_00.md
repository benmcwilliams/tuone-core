## SVN-08390-09606

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nuklearna Elektrarna Krško Facility in Krško, Slovenia

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-04-2023**: The used fuel dry storage facility at the Krško nuclear power plant was officially commissioned with the placement of the first HI-STORM FW cask. The facility is designed to hold 37 used fuel elements per cask, with a total of 16 casks planned for the first loading campaign, amounting to 592 used fuel elements by late 2023. The facility includes advanced safety features such as seismic restraint and protection against extreme weather.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **12-04-2023**: The construction of the dry storage facility involved rigorous quality assurance and local supply chain management, primarily with Slovenian, Croatian, and Italian companies. The facility was designed to meet high safety standards, including those for earthquake resistance and flood conditions.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **10-2022**: NEK received the operating license for the dry storage facility from the Slovenian Nuclear Safety Administration.
- **03-2020**: NEK completed the process of amending the NPP Regulatory Plan, which included a comprehensive environmental impact assessment.
- **02-2021**: NEK obtained an integral building permit for the dry storage facility.
- **01-2023**: Slovenia's Ministry of the Environment approved the Krško plant's operations for an additional 20 years, extending its operational life until 2043.

#### 4. Labour
- **20-06-2023**: The JEK2 project, which aims to expand the capacity of the Krško facility, is expected to create jobs and skills across the Slovenian economy. The project is anticipated to provide significant benefits, including reducing the need for electricity imports and cutting CO2 emissions.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **20-06-2023**: The Krško facility is owned and operated by Nuklearna Elektrarna Krško (NEK), which is a joint venture between Croatia's Hrvatska elektroprivreda (HEP Group) and Slovenia's GEN Energija.
