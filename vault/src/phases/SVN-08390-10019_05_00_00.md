### Evolutionary Timeline of the Slovenian Radioactive Waste Management Organisation (ARAO) Facility in Krško, Slovenia

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is involved in the management of radioactive waste, specifically focusing on the disposal of spent fuel assemblies from the Krško nuclear power plant. 
   - A study is being conducted to explore the costs associated with deep borehole disposal for the shared inventory of pressurized water reactor spent fuel assemblies from Slovenia and Croatia. 
   - The facility is also considering the construction of a mined repository for this waste, with updates expected in 2024.
