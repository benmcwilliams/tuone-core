### Evolutionary Timeline of the Westinghouse Electric Corporation Facility in NEK, Slovenia

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **1982:** The NEK facility, built in cooperation with Westinghouse Electric Corporation, began operations. It produces over 6 billion kilowatt hours of electricity annually, covering approximately 20% of Slovenia's electrical energy demand and 16% of Croatia's.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **2016:** Slovenia and Croatia agreed to extend the operational lifespan of the NEK facility by 20 years, pushing the end date to 2043.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **October 6, 2023:** The facility was shut down due to a leak in the connection system of the primary circuit.
   - **October 28, 2023:** The government announced that repair works were underway, including the replacement of the damaged pipeline and preventive measures on the safety injection system's pipeline. The startup of NEK is anticipated for November 2023, pending successful repairs and approvals.
