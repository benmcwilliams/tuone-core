### Evolutionary Timeline of the Riksbyggen Facility in Gothenburg, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2018
   - **Details:** The facility is involved in a project utilizing old Volvo bus batteries as stationary energy storage with a capacity of 200 kWh. This pilot project is part of the housing project Viva, where apartment buildings will produce and store their own solar energy. The energy storage system aims to reduce the load on the public electrical grid during peak usage and allows for the sale of excess solar energy to the national energy grid.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2018
   - **Details:** The project is a collaboration between Göteborg Energi, Riksbyggen, and Johanneberg Science Park, indicating a shared investment in the development of this innovative energy storage solution.
