#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2018**: Goteborg Energi is in the process of constructing a 5.5MW solar farm named Save, located near Save airport outside Gothenburg. The facility is situated on an 11-hectare site. The project includes the installation of a substation with switchgear, distribution transformers, relays, control equipment, and inverters provided by ABB.
