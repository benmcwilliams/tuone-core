### Evolutionary Timeline of Stena Recycling Facility in Halmstad, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **06/2021**: Stena Recycling announces plans to build a new battery recycling facility in Halmstad, focusing on a hydrometallurgical process that can recycle 95% of lithium-ion batteries.
- **11/2021**: Construction begins on the new facility, which will have an initial annual capacity of 10,000 tonnes, processing batteries from electric vehicles and electrical appliances.
- **03/2023**: The facility officially opens, maintaining the initial capacity of 10,000 tonnes, with expectations to increase capacity significantly over the next decade.
- **01/2024**: Stena Recycling collaborates with BASF to produce black mass from end-of-life lithium-ion batteries, enhancing the recycling process.
- **02/2024**: Stena Recycling begins processing production scrap from Morrow Batteries, aiming to establish a circular battery value chain.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **06/2021**: Stena Recycling announces a total investment of 250 million kronor (approximately 24.5 million euros) for the new facility.
