### Evolutionary Timeline of the Uniti Facility in Landskrona, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2017**: Uniti, a Swedish startup, announced plans to mass-produce its biodegradable compact electric vehicle (EV) with a production capacity of 50,000 units per year, starting in 2019. The vehicle, named Uniti One, is designed to weigh 400 kg and offers battery options of 11 or 22 kWh, with a range of either 150 or 300 km.
- **May 2018**: Uniti presented a working prototype of the Uniti One, showcasing its compact and affordable design. The vehicle is set to have a 22 kWh battery with a range of 300 km and a starting price of €19,900, with plans for even cheaper versions.
- **September 2018**: Uniti announced plans to manufacture the Uniti One in Australia with an expected annual capacity of up to 10,000 units starting in 2020. The vehicle will also feature inductive charging technology and hardware for future autonomous driving capabilities.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2017**: Siemens invested €3 million in Uniti to support the establishment of a fully automated factory in either Malmö or Landskrona.
- **May 2018**: The company confirmed that the funds raised through crowdfunding (totaling €1 million) were effectively utilized in developing the Uniti One prototype.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2017**: Announcement of plans to build a fully automated factory for mass production of the Uniti One.
- **May 2018**: Uniti began taking pre-orders for the Uniti One and released a video of the working prototype.
- **July 2018**: The Uniti One was spotted on the road for the first time, indicating progress towards production and delivery.
- **September 2018**: Uniti confirmed plans for future production facilities in Australia and India, alongside the existing facility in Landskrona.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2018**: As Uniti ramped up its operations and began taking pre-orders, it indicated potential job creation associated with the production of the Uniti One in Landskrona.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **March 2017**: Uniti is a startup that has received significant backing from Siemens and other partners, but the articles do not specify whether the facility is fully owned by Uniti or if there are joint venture arrangements. However, Siemens' involvement suggests a collaborative partnership in the production process.
