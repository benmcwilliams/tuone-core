### Evolutionary Timeline of the Northvolt Facility in Northern Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 12-06-2021
   - **Details:** Northvolt is expanding the battery cell production capacity of its Northvolt Ett gigafactory in northern Sweden from 40GWh to 60GWh to meet growing demand for electric vehicles. The factory is currently in development and is set to begin production later in 2021. Northvolt aims to establish recycling capabilities to source 50% of its raw material requirements from recycled batteries by 2030.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 12-06-2021
   - **Details:** Northvolt raised $2.75 billion in equity to support the expansion of its battery production capacity and research and development efforts. This funding was co-led by new investors and existing investors, including AP funds 1-4, OMERS Capital Markets, Goldman Sachs Asset Management, and Volkswagen Group. The total amount raised by Northvolt, including this private placement, exceeds $6.5 billion, aimed at achieving an annual production capacity of over 150GWh in Europe by 2030.
