### Evolutionary Timeline of the Northvolt Facility in Skelleftea, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2021**: Northvolt, in a joint venture with Volvo, announced plans to build a gigafactory in Europe with a potential capacity of 50 GWh per year, aimed at producing batteries for electric vehicles. Production is expected to start in 2026.
- **2022**: Northvolt shipped its first battery cells from the Skelleftea facility.
- **August 2023**: Northvolt confirmed ongoing production in Skelleftea, which will be further scaled and supplemented by the 'Revolt Ett' recycling plant.
