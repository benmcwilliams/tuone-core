### Evolutionary Timeline of the ABB Facility in Skellefteå, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **2020**: ABB began delivering power supply infrastructure from the local grid to Northvolt’s battery cell factory in Skellefteå, Sweden.
- **Early 2022**: Northvolt Ett battery cell factory commenced operations, with ABB providing electrification and automation technology.
- **2023**: ABB announced its involvement in the Revolt Ett battery recycling plant, which will recycle 125,000 tonnes of used batteries and waste from battery production annually. ABB will supply switchgear and frequency converters to optimize the recycling processes.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Q1 2023**: ABB received an order from Northvolt for the electrification of the Revolt Ett recycling plant, marking its first order in the battery recycling segment.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **2017**: ABB and Northvolt established a supply and technology partnership, including R&D collaboration.
- **Early 2022**: Northvolt Ett battery cell factory started operations.
- **2023**: Announcement of the expansion of the partnership to include the Revolt Ett recycling plant.

#### 4. Labour
- Specific job creation or job loss details related to the expansions or investments at the ABB facility in Skellefteå were not mentioned in the articles.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- ABB collaborates with Northvolt, indicating a partnership rather than exclusive ownership of the facility. ABB provides technology and support for both the battery cell factory and the recycling plant, but the facilities are owned and operated by Northvolt.
