### H2 Green Steel Facility Timeline - Stockholm, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Production Focus:** H2 Green Steel (H2GS) specializes in producing virtually CO2-free steel using hydrogen and electricity from 100% renewable energy sources, eliminating the need for coking coal.
   - **Production Capacity:** The facility plans to produce around 50,000 tonnes of green steel annually for Mercedes-Benz's European press plants, with an aim to start production in 2025.
   - **Environmental Impact:** H2GS aims for a CO2 footprint of 0.4 tonnes per tonne of steel at the start of delivery, significantly lower than traditional steel production methods.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Ownership and Stake:** Mercedes-Benz has held a stake in H2 Green Steel since 2021, indicating a strategic partnership for sustainable steel sourcing.
   - **Future Plans:** The partnership includes intentions to establish a supply chain for green steel in North America, enhancing the sustainability of the automotive supply chain.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Company Launch:** H2 Green Steel was launched in 2020.
   - **Production Start Date:** Planned production commencement is set for 2025.
   - **Supply Agreement:** On June 8, 2023, Mercedes-Benz announced a supply contract with H2 Green Steel for the delivery of green steel, marking a significant milestone in their collaboration.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Job Creation:** While specific job creation figures were not mentioned in the articles, the establishment of the facility and its operations are expected to contribute positively to local employment in the clean technology sector.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Ownership Status:** H2 Green Steel is a start-up founded by Vargas Holding, which is the largest shareholder. Mercedes-Benz holds a stake in the company, indicating a joint interest but not exclusive ownership of the facility.
