## SWE-01528-02628

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Alight Facility in Central Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2024**: Alight has secured financing for a **220MW solar PV portfolio** in Sweden, marking it as the first of its kind in the Nordics. The facility will consist of small to medium solar parks, each with a capacity of up to **50MW**. The initial projects include:
  - **4MW** in Halland
  - **6MW** in Södermanland
  - **15MW** in Uppland
- The company is also developing two additional solar parks: one **150 hectares** in central Sweden and another **70 hectares** in the south.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2024**: Alight secured up to **€110 million** (approximately **US$123 million**) in senior debt portfolio financing from **Rabobank**. This financing is intended for the development, ownership, and operation of the **220MW** solar parks across Sweden.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **August 2024**: Alight partnered with **Sveaskog**, a state-owned forestry company, to develop solar energy projects, initiating the development of the two solar parks mentioned above.
- **September 2024**: Financial arrangements were completed for the solar park project, with construction expected to begin on the initial three solar parks.
