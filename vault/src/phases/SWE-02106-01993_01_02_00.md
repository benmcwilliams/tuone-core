   - **June 2023:** The Talga facility is set to produce 19,500 tons per annum (tpa) of anode material for lithium-ion batteries. This production will come from an integrated mine and anode refinery located in northern Sweden. The facility is designed to operate using 100% green electricity.

2. **Investment and Development:**
