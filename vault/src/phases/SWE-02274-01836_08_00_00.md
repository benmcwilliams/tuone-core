## SWE-02274-01836

### Evolutionary Timeline of the OX2 Facility in Offshore, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **February 2024:** OX2 and Ingka Investments filed an application to build the Neptunus offshore energy hub, located 50 kilometers off the southern coast of Sweden. This hub is planned to include up to 207 wind turbines with a total installed capacity of 3.1 GW, alongside offshore hydrogen production and a pilot project aimed at oxygenating the Baltic Sea.
   - **February 2024:** The Pleione energy park, part of the partnership between OX2 and Ingka Investments established in 2023, is planned to feature a 1 GW offshore wind farm consisting of 42-70 wind turbines and hydrogen production facilities. This project is set to be developed in conjunction with the 1.8 GW Ran offshore wind project announced by OX2 in September 2023.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **February 2024:** The application for the Neptunus project indicates significant investment potential, although specific financial figures are not disclosed. The Pleione energy park and Ran project are also part of the broader investment strategy by OX2 and Ingka Investments.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **February 2024:** OX2 and Ingka Investments filed for permits for both the Neptunus and Pleione energy park projects. The next milestone for the Pleione-Ran project is to complete the environmental impact assessment (EIA) to enable the filing of the application in spring 2024. If permits for Neptunus are obtained, construction is expected to start in 2030.

4. **Labour:**

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - OX2 and Ingka Investments are collaborating on these projects, indicating a joint venture structure for the Neptunus and Pleione energy park projects. There is no indication of exclusive ownership by OX2.
