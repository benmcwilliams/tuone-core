## SWE-02347-06046

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Skyborn Facility in Söderhamn, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: The new hydrogen production plant, operated by Lhyfe and Skyborn, is announced to be located in Söderhamn, Sweden. It will be connected to Skyborn’s 1 GW offshore wind farm in nearby Storgrundet. The facility is expected to produce approximately 240 tonnes of hydrogen per day, with an installed capacity of 600 MW, making it one of the largest suppliers of renewable hydrogen in Europe. The project aims to support the decarbonisation of the Swedish energy system and has potential for further processing and use of green hydrogen in the area.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: A sales purchase agreement is entered into by Skyborn and Lhyfe with Stora Enso for an industry property of around 40 hectares, which will be utilized for the SoutH2Port project.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: ABB, Lhyfe, and Skyborn announce a collaboration to jointly realise and optimise the SoutH2Port hydrogen project. This partnership is part of a memorandum of understanding (MoU) aimed at integrating offshore wind-generated renewable hydrogen into the energy system. The project is positioned as one of Europe’s largest renewable hydrogen initiatives.

#### 4. Labour
- **May 2023**: The SoutH2Port project is expected to contribute to job creation and business opportunities in the region, enhancing the attractiveness and competitiveness of the local society.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **May 2023**: The facility is operated by Lhyfe and Skyborn, indicating a joint operational structure rather than exclusive ownership by a single entity.
