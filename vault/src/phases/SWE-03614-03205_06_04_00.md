## SWE-03614-03205

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Midsummer Facility in Flen, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **April 2024**: Midsummer announced the establishment of a new solar module production facility in Flen, Sweden, which will produce copper indium gallium selenide (CIGS) solar cells. The factory is designed to have a production capacity of **200 MW** and is scheduled to commence production in **2026**. The facility will focus on producing lightweight and environmentally sustainable solar panels suitable for low-load-bearing commercial and industrial roofs.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **April 2024**: The project is partially funded by a **€32.3 million grant** from the EU Innovation Fund, aimed at supporting the development of the facility in Flen.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **April 2024**: Midsummer selected the location for the factory in Flen and plans to take over the premises in **September 2024**, pending EU approval. The factory is expected to be fully operational by **2028**.

#### 4. Labour
- **April 2024**: Once fully operational, the facility is expected to create approximately **200 jobs** in the region.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **November 2024**: Midsummer is confirmed to have **full ownership** of the facility in Flen, as there are no indications of joint ventures or partnerships related to this specific facility.
