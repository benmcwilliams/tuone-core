- **December 2020**: Alight announced plans to build an 18 MW solar park in Skurup, which is claimed to be Sweden's largest solar park. - **May 2022**: The solar park in Skurup officially started operations. Alight's CEO, Harald Överholm, indicated that the company is capable of creating contracts directly with commercial partners, which drives their development in solar energy.
