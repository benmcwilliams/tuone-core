### Evolutionary Timeline of the Plug Power Facility in Limmared, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: Plug Power announced the deployment of its 5MW containerized electrolyzer modules for the first time in industrial applications. The facility in Limmared aims to produce **2.1 metric tons per day** of green hydrogen by the end of 2023. This production will replace a portion of the natural gas currently used in the manufacturing process at Ardagh Glass Limmared AB, significantly reducing carbon emissions.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: Plug Power secured three 5MW electrolyzer projects, including the one at Ardagh Glass Limmared. The projects are part of a broader initiative to utilize large-scale green hydrogen in various industries, marking a significant investment in clean technology.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: Plug Power confirmed the commencement of projects involving the deployment of electrolyzer technology at Ardagh Glass Limmared, Hydro Havrand in Norway, and APEX Group in Germany. The Limmared project is set to produce green hydrogen by the end of 2023.
