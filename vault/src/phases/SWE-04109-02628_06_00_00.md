### Evolutionary Timeline of the Alight Facility in Southern Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2021**: Alight announced plans to construct an 8 MW solar park in southern Sweden, expected to be completed by April 2022. This facility will supply electricity to Nolato Group, covering approximately 15% of their electricity demand.
- **June 2023**: Alight secured a 12-year power purchase agreement (PPA) for a 64 MW solar plant in Hallstavik, Stockholm County. Construction began shortly after the announcement, with the plant expected to be operational by the second quarter of 2023.
- **September 2024**: Alight announced plans to develop 220 MW of small to medium-sized solar parks across Sweden, with initial projects including a 4 MW park in Halland, a 6 MW park in Södermanland, and a 15 MW park in Uppland.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2024**: Alight secured €110 million in senior debt portfolio financing from Rabobank to support the development, ownership, and operation of the 220 MW solar parks. This financing is noted as a landmark in the Nordic region.
