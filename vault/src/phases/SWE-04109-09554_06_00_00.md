### Evolutionary Timeline of the Niam Facility in Southern Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2022
   - **Details:** Niam, in partnership with Solkompaniet, is developing a solar portfolio with a total capacity of 500 MW in southern Sweden. This portfolio is expected to power approximately 120,000 households. The first solar park under this initiative is the Östra Eneby solar facility, which is currently the most progressed project and is expected to come online by the end of 2024.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2022
   - **Details:** The total investment for the solar projects is more than €300 million (approximately $297.7 million). This investment is part of the framework agreement between Niam and Solkompaniet for developing solar parks in Sweden.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2022
   - **Details:** Niam and Solkompaniet are developing two 80 MW solar facilities, with the Östra Eneby project being the most advanced. The project is expected to be operational by the end of 2024. The companies plan to initiate the development of additional solar parks over the next two years.
