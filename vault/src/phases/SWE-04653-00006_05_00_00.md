### Evolutionary Timeline of the ABB Facility in Oskarshamn, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **October 2024**: ABB and Blykalla announced a partnership to develop an electric Small Modular Reactor (SMR) pilot facility in Oskarshamn. The facility will focus on constructing a testing facility for Blykalla’s SEALER-E, an electric lead-cooled fast reactor (LFR). This facility will simulate the temperatures of nuclear fission without using uranium, allowing for a full-scale simulation of future reactors.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **October 2024**: The partnership between ABB and Blykalla aims to support Sweden's clean energy and decarbonisation goals, although specific investment amounts or subsidies related to this project have not been disclosed in the articles.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2024**: The Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) was signed between ABB and Blykalla to collaborate on the development of the electric SMR pilot facility. The project will include the construction of a testing facility for the SEALER-E prototype.
