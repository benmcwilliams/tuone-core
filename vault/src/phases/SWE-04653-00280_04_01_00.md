### Evolutionary Timeline of the Fortum Facility in Oskarshamn, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2022**: The Oskarshamn facility, operated by OKG (a joint venture between Uniper and Fortum), has been producing hydrogen from electrolysers powered by its own electricity since 1992. The facility currently produces 12 kg of pink hydrogen per day, primarily for use in reactor coolant. Following the permanent closure of Oskarshamn reactors 1 and 2 in 2016 and 2017, the facility has excess hydrogen production capacity, which it is now selling to industrial gases giant Linde.
