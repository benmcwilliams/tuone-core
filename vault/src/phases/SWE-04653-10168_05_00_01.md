### Evolutionary Timeline of the Svensk Kärnbränslehantering AB Facility in Oskarshamn, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Current Operations**: Svensk Kärnbränslehantering AB (SKB) is involved in the management of radioactive waste, specifically through the operation of the Clab intermediate storage facility and the planned construction of a final repository for used nuclear fuel.
- **Production Capacity**: The facility is preparing for the construction of an encapsulation plant in Oskarshamn, which will support the final disposal of used nuclear fuel. The SFR (Short-lived Radioactive Waste Repository) is set to expand from its current capacity of 63,000 cubic meters to 180,000 cubic meters to accommodate low and medium waste.
- **Upcoming Updates**: SKB plans to expand the SFR to include six new rock vaults, with construction expected to begin in autumn 2024, pending environmental approvals.

#### 2. Investment and Development
