### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hybrit Development AB Facility in Svartöberget, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: 08-04-2021
  - **Details**: Hybrit Development AB commenced the construction of a hydrogen storage facility in Svartöberget. This facility is designed to develop technology for the storage of fossil-free hydrogen gas, which is essential for the production of fossil-free iron and steel. The storage facility will be a 100 cubic metre capacity rock cavern located approximately 30 metres underground, specifically adapted to Scandinavian bedrock conditions.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: 08-04-2021
  - **Details**: The total investment cost for the hydrogen storage facility is just over SEK 250 million (€24.4 million). This investment is shared equally among the holding companies (SSAB, LKAB, and Vattenfall) and is supported by the Swedish Energy Agency through the Industriklivet initiative.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: 08-04-2021
  - **Details**: The construction of the hydrogen storage facility has officially started. The facility is expected to be operational between 2022 and 2024.

#### 4. Labour

  - **Details**: The article does not specify exact job creation or job loss figures related to the construction of the hydrogen storage facility.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Date**: 08-04-2021
  - **Details**: The facility is part of a joint initiative (HYBRIT) involving SSAB, LKAB, and Vattenfall, indicating that it is not under exclusive ownership but rather a collaborative project among these companies.
