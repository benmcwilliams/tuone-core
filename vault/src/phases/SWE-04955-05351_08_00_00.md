## SWE-04955-05351

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ingka Investments Facility in Offshore South of Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Project Name**: Neptunus
  - **Production Capacity**: Planned total installed capacity of 3.1 GW, comprising up to 207 wind turbines.
  - **Additional Features**: The energy hub will include offshore hydrogen production and a pilot project to oxygenate the Baltic Sea.
- **Project Name**: Pleione Energy Park
  - **Production Capacity**: Planned capacity of 1 GW, consisting of 42–70 wind turbines and hydrogen production plant(s) on dedicated platforms or on the foundations of the wind turbines.
- **Related Project**: Ran Offshore Wind Project
  - **Production Capacity**: 1.8 GW, developed alongside the Pleione offshore wind farm.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Partnership**: OX2 and Ingka Investments partnered in 2023, which includes the development of the Pleione energy park as part of their transaction.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: February 2024
  - **Milestone**: OX2 and Ingka Investments filed an application to build the Neptunus offshore energy hub.
- **Future Plans**: Expected to apply for a permit for the Pleione energy park, with the next milestone being the completion of the environmental impact assessment (EIA) to enable filing the application in spring 2024.
- **Construction Start**: If permits are obtained, construction for Neptunus is planned to start in 2030.

#### 4. Labour
- **Job Creation**: Specific job creation details have not been provided in the articles, but the development of large-scale offshore wind projects typically leads to job creation during both the construction and operational phases.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **Ownership**: Ingka Investments is part of a partnership with OX2 for the development of these projects, indicating a joint venture structure rather than exclusive ownership.
