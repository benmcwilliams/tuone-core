- **Date: February 2022**: The LKAB facility in Lulea is involved in the production of hot briquetted iron ore using the HYBRIT process, which aims to significantly reduce carbon emissions in steel production by replacing coking coal with hydrogen produced from renewable energy.
- **Current Production Capacity**: The pilot plant has produced a couple of hundred tons of iron ore to date.
- **Future Plans**: There are plans to construct a larger demonstration plant and begin commercial deliveries by 2026.
