### Evolutionary Timeline of the Hitachi Facility in Hofors, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2023**: Hitachi Energy delivered a modular solution to electrify a 20 MW electrolyzer at the Hofors facility. This project is notable as it marks the world's first steel plant to utilize hydrogen for heating before the rolling process. The hydrogen produced will not only be used for steel heating but will also fuel cell-powered trucks, and surplus heat from the plant will support district heating.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2023**: The project involves a modular Grid-to-Stack solution, which includes components such as transformers, rectifiers, control equipment, and high-current connections necessary for converting alternating current from the distribution grid into the direct current required for the electrolyzer. The collaboration for this system's development includes partnerships with Volvo Group, H2 Green Steel, and Nel Hydrogen.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2023**: The announcement of the delivery of the modular eHouse solution and the operational status of the 20 MW electrolyzer signifies a major milestone in the project, establishing the facility as a pioneer in using green hydrogen for steel production.
