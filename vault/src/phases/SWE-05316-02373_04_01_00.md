### Evolutionary Timeline of the Nel Facility in Hofors, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2021**: Nel Hydrogen Electrolyser, a division of Norway-based Nel ASA, announced a collaboration to develop a fossil-free hydrogen facility in Hofors, Sweden. This facility aims to produce green hydrogen to significantly reduce CO2 emissions in the steel industry, specifically targeting a 50% reduction in emissions during steel production. The facility will be the first in the world to heat steel with hydrogen prior to rolling, showcasing innovative technology in the steel manufacturing process.
