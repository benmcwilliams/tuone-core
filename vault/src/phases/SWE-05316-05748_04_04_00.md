### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ovako Facility in Hofors, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **04-09-2023**: Ovako inaugurated its first plant to produce fossil-free hydrogen for heating steel before rolling. The facility features Sweden’s largest electrolysis plant, equipped with a 20MW alkaline electrolyzer system from Nel ASA. This system can produce up to 3,880 Nm³/h of hydrogen, which is over 8 tonnes per day. The hydrogen produced will be used to heat steel at adjacent rolling mills, significantly reducing carbon dioxide emissions to nearly zero. Additionally, the hydrogen will fuel fuel cell-powered trucks, and excess heat will be utilized for district heating.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- The development of the hydrogen plant has received support from the Swedish Energy Agency and the European Union, although specific investment amounts tied to expansions or capacity were not detailed in the article.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
