#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2022**: The Volvo HX04, the world’s first hydrogen fuel cell articulated hauler prototype, is tested at the Volvo CE facility in Braås. This facility is historically significant as it is also where the first articulated hauler, Gravel Charlie, was developed in 1966. The hydrogen refueling station installed by Shell at the test track supports the operation of the HX04, which can be refueled with 12 kg of hydrogen in approximately 7.5 minutes, allowing for about four hours of operation.

This project aimed to support sustainable vehicle strategic research, innovation, and development.
