- **December 2022**: Ørsted acquires the remaining 55% stake in the FlagshipONE e-methanol project from Liquid Wind. The facility is designed to produce 50,000 tonnes of e-methanol per year, focusing on the shipping industry. It will utilize renewable electricity and biogenic carbon dioxide for production. The facility will also integrate excess heat from the e-methanol production process into the district heating supply.
