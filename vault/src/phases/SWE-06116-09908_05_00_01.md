### Evolutionary Timeline of the SKB Facility in Forsmark, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-03-2024**: SKB announced the construction of a new geology building in Forsmark, which will support upcoming major construction projects. The facility currently includes the SFR (final repository for low and intermediate-level waste) and plans to expand its capacity from 63,000 cubic metres to 180,000 cubic metres by excavating six new rock vaults. The geology building will house various operational facilities, including a workshop, chemistry laboratory, and office spaces, designed with sustainability in mind.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-03-2024**: The construction of the geology building has an order value of approximately SEK 55 million (USD 5.3 million), contracted to Norwegian construction firm Veidekke. This investment is part of SKB's broader strategy to expand its operations in Forsmark.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **07-03-2024**: Announcement of the start of construction for the geology building, with completion expected in spring 2025. Operations in the new building are planned to commence in August 2025 after moving existing equipment from old premises.
- **Autumn 2024**: SKB plans to begin work on expanding the SFR repository to triple its capacity.
