### Evolutionary Timeline of the Veidekke Facility in Forsmark, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **07-03-2024**: SKB (Svensk Kärnbränslehantering AB) announced the construction of a new geology building in Forsmark, which will support upcoming major construction projects. The building will cover approximately 2000 square meters and include a workshop, storage for drill cores, a chemistry laboratory, office spaces, and changing rooms. The facility will utilize sustainable construction materials, including green concrete and wood, and will feature a sedum roof.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **07-03-2024**: The construction of the geology building is valued at approximately SEK 55 million (USD 5.3 million), under a turnkey contract signed between Veidekke and SKB in October 2023.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **October 2023**: Veidekke signed a contract with SKB to construct the geology building.
- **07-03-2024**: Construction officially began, with completion expected in the spring of 2025. Operations are planned to start in August 2025 after moving existing equipment from old premises.
