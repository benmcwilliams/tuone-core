**1. Technology and Operations:**
- **June 2023:** Axfood partners with Alight to develop a solar park in Hallstavik, Sweden, with a production capacity of 64 MW. This facility will be the largest solar park in Sweden at the time of commissioning.

- **June 2023:** Construction of the solar park is set to begin immediately after the announcement, with the facility expected to be operational by Q2 of 2024. The project was initially considered for Skåne but was relocated to Hallstavik due to the land's low natural values.
