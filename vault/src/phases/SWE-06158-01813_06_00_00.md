### Evolutionary Timeline of the Neoen Facility in Hultsfred, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2022**: Neoen and Alight announced a 90 MW solar project in Hultsfred, with construction set to begin in the second half of 2023 and commissioning expected by 2025. The project will utilize solar panels with a low carbon footprint.
- **October 2023**: Construction officially began on a 100 MWp solar plant, which is noted as the largest solar project to commence in Sweden to date. The facility will supply 95% of renewable energy and guarantees of origin to H&M under the previously signed PPA.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2022**: Alight received a US$150 million investment from DIF Capital Partners to accelerate its solar pipeline, which includes the Hultsfred project. This investment is aimed at completing the solar projects within the next two years.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2022**: A power purchase agreement (PPA) was signed with H&M for the 90 MW solar project, marking it as the largest PPA in Sweden at that time. 
- **October 2023**: Groundbreaking for the 100 MWp solar project took place, confirming the project's operational timeline for 2025.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or job loss figures related to the Hultsfred facility. However, the construction and operation of the solar plant are expected to contribute positively to local employment in the renewable energy sector.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- The Hultsfred solar project is a joint venture between Neoen and Alight, with Neoen serving as the majority shareholder.
