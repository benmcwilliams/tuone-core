### Evolutionary Timeline of the Alight Facility in Hultsfred, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **December 2022**: Alight and Neoen announced a 90 MW solar project in Hultsfred, with construction set to begin in the second half of 2023 and commissioning expected by 2025. This facility will utilize solar panels with a low carbon footprint.
- **October 2023**: Construction officially began on the 100 MW solar plant, which is noted as the largest solar project in Sweden to date. The facility will provide renewable energy primarily to H&M under a long-term power purchase agreement (PPA).

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2022**: Alight received a US$150 million investment from DIF Capital Partners to accelerate its solar pipeline, which includes the Hultsfred project. This investment is aimed at completing projects within the next two years.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **December 2022**: Alight and Neoen signed Sweden's largest PPA with H&M for the 90 MW solar plant in Hultsfred.
- **June 2023**: Alight announced the start of construction for a 64 MW solar plant in Hallstavik, which is separate from the Hultsfred project but indicates ongoing development activity.
- **October 2023**: Groundbreaking for the 100 MW solar project in Hultsfred took place, marking a significant milestone as the largest solar project under construction in Sweden.
