### Evolutionary Timeline of the NTR Facility in Jönköping, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** Q4 2022 (expected operations start)
   - **Details:** The facility will produce renewable energy through the Norra Vedbo wind farm, which consists of 20 Vestas V150 turbines, each with an installed capacity of 4.3MW, totaling 86MW. Once operational, NTR will manage a total of 165MW of wind energy in the Swedish market.
