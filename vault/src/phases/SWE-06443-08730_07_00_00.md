### Evolutionary Timeline of the Einride Facility in Jönköping, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **May 2019:** Einride's T-pod, a cab-less autonomous electric truck, began commercial operations on public roads in Jönköping. The T-pod is designed to transport goods between a warehouse and terminal at the DB Schenker facility. It operates solely on batteries, offering increased loading capacity, flexibility, and optimized energy consumption.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **November 2018:** Einride and DB Schenker initiated the first installation of the T-pod at the Jönköping facility, marking it as the first commercial installation of an autonomous electric truck in the world. Specific investment amounts were not detailed in the article.
