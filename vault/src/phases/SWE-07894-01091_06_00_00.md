#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2018**: The Göteborg Energi facility in Säve, near Gothenburg, is a solar plant with a production capacity of **5.5 MW**. It is designed to increase the utility's generation capacity from renewable sources.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2018**: The construction of the 5.5 MW solar project is initiated by Göteborg Energi, with ABB providing the necessary infrastructure, including a substation, switchgear, distribution transformers, relays, control equipment, and inverters. This investment is part of Göteborg Energi's strategy to enhance its renewable energy portfolio.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2018**: Construction begins on the solar facility, with the expectation that it will be grid-connected by **November 2018**. The project was announced alongside another 1 MW solar initiative by Göteborg Energi in June 2018.
