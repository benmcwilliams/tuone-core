## SWE-07954-05351

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Ingka Investments Facility in Blekinge, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2024**: OX2 and Ingka Investments submitted a permit application to construct the Neptunus offshore energy hub, which will consist of 207 wind turbines with a maximum height of 420 meters, located 50 km from the coast of Blekinge. The total installed capacity of the project is 3.1 GW, with an estimated annual electricity production of 13-15 TWh, sufficient to meet the electricity needs of the Blekinge and Skåne regions. Additionally, the project includes plans for offshore hydrogen production of up to 370,000 tonnes annually and a pilot project to oxygenate the Baltic Sea.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **January 2024**: The project is in the early development phase, with OX2's project development portfolio including 1.9 GW of the total 3.1 GW capacity. The overall project is part of a larger portfolio that amounted to 34 GW by the end of Q3 2023.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2024**: OX2 and Ingka Investments applied for the necessary permits to construct the Neptunus offshore wind energy hub. If permits are granted, construction is expected to start in 2030. The project is one of six offshore wind farms being developed by OX2 and Ingka Investments in Sweden.

#### 4. Labour
- **No specific job creation or job loss information** has been provided in the articles regarding the Neptunus project at this time.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **January 2024**: The facility is a joint venture between OX2 and Ingka Investments, with no indication of exclusive ownership by either party. Ingka Investments is the investment arm of the Ingka Group, which is the largest IKEA retailer.
