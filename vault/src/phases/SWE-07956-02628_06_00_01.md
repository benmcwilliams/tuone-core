## SWE-07956-02628

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Alight Facility in Halland, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2024**: Alight announced the construction of three solar parks in Sweden, including a 4 MW solar park in Halland. The combined capacity of these parks will be 24 GWh, with the Halland park contributing to this total. The parks are expected to be operational by early 2025.
- **September 2024**: Alight secured financing for a 220 MW solar PV portfolio, which includes the 4 MW project in Halland, alongside a 6 MW project in Södermanland and a 15 MW project in Uppland. This portfolio is part of a broader strategy to develop small to medium-sized solar parks across Sweden.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2024**: Alight secured up to €110 million (approximately US$123 million) in financing from Rabobank for the development of the 220 MW solar portfolio, marking a significant investment in the facility's expansion and operational capabilities.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2024**: Announcement of the long-term power purchase agreements (PPAs) with H&M Group for the construction of the solar parks, including the Halland facility.
- **September 2024**: Financial close on the senior debt portfolio financing with Rabobank, enabling the construction and operation of the solar parks, including the Halland project.
