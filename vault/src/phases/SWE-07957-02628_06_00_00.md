## SWE-07957-02628

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Alight Facility in South Sweden, SWE

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** Alight has secured financing for a 220MW solar PV portfolio in Sweden, marking it as the first of its kind in the Nordics. The facility will consist of small to medium solar parks, each with a capacity of up to 50MW. The initial projects include a 4MW facility in Halland, a 6MW project in Södermanland, and a 15MW project in Uppland. Additional projects are anticipated to follow within two years.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** Alight secured up to €110 million (US$123 million) in financing from Dutch financing firm Rabobank for the development of the 220MW solar PV portfolio. This financing is crucial for the construction, ownership, and operation of the solar parks.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** The financing allows Alight to proceed with the development of its solar projects, with construction already underway for two PV projects located in central and southern Sweden. This initiative is part of a broader goal to achieve a 5GW installed capacity by 2030.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** While specific job creation numbers are not mentioned, the financing and development of the solar portfolio are expected to contribute to job creation in the renewable energy sector in Sweden, particularly as projects are built and operated.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** September 2024
   - **Details:** Alight is the owner of the facility, having partnered with Swedish solar company Svea Solar and state-owned forestry company Sveaskog to develop a larger 2GW portfolio of solar projects. This indicates a collaborative approach for land provision and project development, but Alight retains ownership of the specific 220MW portfolio.
