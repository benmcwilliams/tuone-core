### Evolutionary Timeline of the Alight Facility in Södermanland, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **January 2024**: Alight announced the construction of three solar parks in Sweden under long-term power purchase agreements (PPAs) with H&M Group. The parks will have capacities of 13 MW (Blekinge), 6 MW (Södermanland), and 4 MW (Halland), with a combined expected generation of at least 24 GWh. Construction for the Södermanland park is set to begin in spring 2024 and is expected to be operational by early 2025.
- **September 2024**: Alight secured financing for a 220 MW solar PV portfolio, which includes a 6 MW project in Södermanland. This project is part of a larger initiative to develop small to medium-sized solar parks across Sweden.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2024**: Alight secured up to €110 million (approximately US$123 million) in financing from Rabobank for the development of a 220 MW solar PV portfolio in Sweden. This financing is significant as it represents the first portfolio facility of its kind in the Nordic region.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **January 2024**: Announcement of the signing of long-term PPAs for the construction of solar parks, including the 6 MW park in Södermanland.
- **September 2024**: Financial close on the €110 million financing for the solar portfolio, which includes the 6 MW project in Södermanland, with construction expected to follow within the next two years.

- Alight is an independent power producer (IPP) and will **own and operate** the solar parks, including the one in Södermanland, under the financing agreement with Rabobank. There is no indication of joint ventures or shared ownership in the articles provided.
