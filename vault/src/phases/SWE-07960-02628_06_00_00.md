## SWE-07960-02628

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Alight Facility in Uppland, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **September 2024**: Alight, a Swedish independent power producer, announced the development of a 220MW solar PV portfolio, which includes a 15MW solar park in Uppland. This facility is part of a larger initiative to build, own, and operate small to medium-sized solar parks across Sweden, with each park having a capacity of up to 50MW.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **September 2024**: Alight secured up to €110 million (approximately US$123 million) in senior debt portfolio financing from Rabobank. This financing is earmarked for the development of the 220MW solar portfolio, marking it as the first of its kind in the Nordic region.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **September 2024**: The first phase of the project includes the construction of three solar parks: a 4MW park in Halland, a 6MW park in Södermanland, and a 15MW park in Uppland. The remaining projects are expected to be financed and operational within the next two years.

#### 4. Labour
- **September 2024**: While specific job creation numbers were not mentioned in the articles, the financing and development of the solar parks are expected to contribute to job creation in the renewable energy sector in Sweden.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **September 2024**: Alight is the sole owner of the facility, as indicated by their plans to build, own, and operate the solar parks independently, although they have partnered with Sveaskog for land provision in other projects.
