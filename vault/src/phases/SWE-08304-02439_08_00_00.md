### Evolutionary Timeline of Siemens Gamesa Facility in Ange, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **Date**: 12-10-2020
  - **Details**: Siemens Gamesa secured an order to supply 60 turbines for the Björnberget onshore wind farm in Ange, Sweden. The turbines are of the 5.8-170 model, operating at 6.2MW, and will be installed in 2022. This project is Siemens Gamesa’s largest order for this platform to date, expected to produce around 1.1 TWh of clean electricity annually.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **Date**: 12-10-2020
  - **Details**: The project is part of Prime Capital’s Prime Green Energy Infrastructure Fund, which focuses on wind-powered energy assets in the Nordic countries without reliance on government support. This investment marks the first for the fund, which aims to enhance Prime Capital’s renewable energy portfolio in the Nordics to over 1GW.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **Date**: 12-10-2020
  - **Details**: The Björnberget project is one of the largest onshore wind projects to start construction in Europe in 2020. It is expected to reach commercial operation at the beginning of 2023.
