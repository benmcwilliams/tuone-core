### Evolutionary Timeline of the Prime Capital Facility in Ange, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 12-10-2020
   - **Details:** Prime Capital, in partnership with Enlight Energy, is developing the 372MW Björnberget onshore wind farm in Ange, Sweden. The facility will utilize 60 Siemens Gamesa 6.2MW turbines, specifically the 5.8-170 model, which is Siemens Gamesa’s largest onshore rotor. The project is expected to produce approximately 1.1 TWh of clean electricity annually and is set to reach commercial operation at the beginning of 2023.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 12-10-2020
   - **Details:** The Björnberget project marks the first investment from Prime Capital’s Prime Green Energy Infrastructure Fund (PGEIF), which focuses on wind-powered energy assets in the Nordic countries without reliance on government support. The project is significant as it will increase Prime Capital’s renewable energy portfolio in the Nordics to over 1GW.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** 12-10-2020
   - **Details:** The project is one of the largest onshore wind projects to start construction in Europe in 2020. Turbine installation is scheduled for 2022, with commercial operations expected to begin in early 2023.
