### Evolutionary Timeline of the Enercon Facility in Erstrask, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Erstrask wind farm has a total production capacity of 229.1 MW, comprising two phases:
     - **Phase 1:** 26 Enercon E-103 turbines, each with a capacity of 2.35 MW.
     - **Phase 2:** 42 Enercon E-126 turbines, each with a capacity of 4 MW.
   - The first phase is expected to become operational in the first quarter of 2019, while the second phase is anticipated to start in the first quarter of 2020.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The facility is valued at approximately €190 million.
   - UK-based investor The Renewables Infrastructure Group (TRIG) acquired a 75% interest in the project from Enercon, financing the investment through its revolving acquisition facility without requiring third-party debt.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2018:** Announcement of TRIG acquiring a 75% stake in the Erstrask wind farm, which is currently under construction.
   - **Expected Operational Dates:**
     - Phase 1: Q1 2019
     - Phase 2: Q1 2020

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - Enercon does not own the facility exclusively; it retains a 25% stake while TRIG holds a 75% interest in the Erstrask wind farm.
