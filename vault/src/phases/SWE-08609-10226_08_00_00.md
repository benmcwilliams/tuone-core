### Evolutionary Timeline of The Renewables Infrastructure Group Facility in Erstrask, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The Erstrask wind farm has a total production capacity of 229.1MW, consisting of two phases:
     - **Phase 1:** 26 Enercon E-103 turbines, each with a capacity of 2.35MW, expected to become operational in Q1 2019.
     - **Phase 2:** 42 Enercon E-126 turbines, each with a capacity of 4MW, expected to become operational in Q1 2020.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The facility is valued at approximately €190 million. The Renewables Infrastructure Group (TRIG) acquired a 75% interest in the project from Enercon, financing the investment through its revolving acquisition facility without requiring third-party debt.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **December 2018:** TRIG announced the acquisition of a 75% stake in the Erstrask wind farm, which is currently under construction. The first phase is set to enter operation in Q1 2019, followed by the second phase in Q1 2020.
