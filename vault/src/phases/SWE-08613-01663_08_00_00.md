### Evolutionary Timeline of the GE Facility in Markbygden, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is a wind farm named Markbygden ETT, with a production capacity of 650 MW.
   - It will utilize 179 turbines, each with a capacity of 3.6 MW and equipped with 137-metre rotors.
   - The turbines will include an ice mitigation system developed by LM Wind Power.
   - GE Renewable Energy will provide a 20-year full service package and high voltage switchgear for two collector substations.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The project has raised approximately €800 million in financing.
   - GE Energy Financial Services and the Green Investment Group (GIG) have invested over €300 million in equity.
   - An additional €500 million in debt financing has been secured from various financial institutions, including the European Investment Bank and others.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **07-11-2017:** Announcement of the partnership between GE and GIG to develop and operate the Markbygden ETT wind farm, with construction starting shortly after.
   - Commissioning of the facility is expected in the second half of 2018, with full operational status anticipated by the end of 2019.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
