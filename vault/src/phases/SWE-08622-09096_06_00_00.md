### HSB Södermanland Facility Timeline - Strängnäs, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2019
   - **Details:** HSB Södermanland announced plans to build a 20 MW solar park near the E20 highway in Strängnäs. The facility will have a total installed capacity of 20 MW and an electricity generation capacity of 18 GWh, sufficient to meet the annual electricity consumption for approximately 7,500 apartments. The facility will cover an area of 45 hectares.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2019
   - **Details:** The project is estimated to cost €14 million. HSB Södermanland is developing the solar park in collaboration with Swedish PV company EnergiEngagemang Sverige. 

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2019
   - **Details:** Construction of the solar park is set to begin in March 2020. Upon completion, it will be the largest photovoltaic park in Sweden.
