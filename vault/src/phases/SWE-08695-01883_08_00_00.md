## SWE-08695-01883

### Evolutionary Timeline of the RES Facility in Bjornberget, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Production Capacity:** The Bjornberget wind farm is designed to have a capacity of 372 MW, featuring 60 Siemens Gamesa 6.2 MW turbines.
   - **Electricity Generation:** It is expected to generate approximately 1.1 terawatt-hours of electricity annually.
   - **Construction Details:** The facility will include around 64 km of new and upgraded roads and 130 km of cabling.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Contractors:** RES appointed Peab and SIC as contractors for the balance of plant, indicating significant investment in local construction capabilities.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Announcement Date:** The project was announced on 22-02-2021.
   - **Construction Start:** Construction is scheduled to start in 2021.
   - **Operational Date:** The wind farm is expected to come online in early 2023.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Job Creation:** At peak construction, approximately 200 people will be employed on-site, contributing to local job creation.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Joint Ownership:** The Bjornberget wind farm is jointly owned by Enlight Renewable Energy and Prime Capital’s Prime Green Energy Infrastructure Fund.
