## SWE-08695-06607

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Prime Capital Facility in Bjornberget, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - The facility is a wind farm with a production capacity of 372 MW, featuring 60 Siemens Gamesa 6.2 MW turbines.
   - It is expected to generate approximately 1.1 terawatt-hours of electricity annually.
   - The wind farm is set to come online in early 2023.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - The project is a joint venture between Enlight Renewable Energy and Prime Capital’s Prime Green Energy Infrastructure Fund, indicating significant investment from both entities.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **February 2021:** RES appointed Peab and SIC as contractors for the balance of plant, with construction scheduled to start in 2021.
   - The wind farm is expected to be operational by early 2023.

4. **Labour:**
   - At peak construction, approximately 200 people will be employed on-site, contributing to local job creation during the construction phase.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - The Bjornberget wind farm is jointly owned by Enlight Renewable Energy and Prime Capital’s Prime Green Energy Infrastructure Fund, indicating a shared ownership structure rather than exclusive ownership by Prime Capital.
