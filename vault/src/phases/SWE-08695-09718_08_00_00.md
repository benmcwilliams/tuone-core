### Evolutionary Timeline of the Peab Facility in Bjornberget, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** February 2021
   - **Details:** The facility is part of the Bjornberget wind farm, which has a production capacity of 372 MW. It will feature 60 Siemens Gamesa 6.2 MW turbines and is expected to generate approximately 1.1 terawatt-hours of electricity annually. The construction includes the development of foundations for 35 turbines, with an additional 25 being completed by SIC, along with 64 km of new and upgraded roads and 130 km of cabling.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** February 2021
   - **Details:** The project is a collaboration between Enlight Renewable Energy and Prime Capital’s Prime Green Energy Infrastructure Fund, indicating significant investment in the development of the wind farm.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** February 2021
   - **Details:** Construction is set to begin in 2021, with the wind farm expected to come online in early 2023. The announcement of the project and the appointment of Peab and SIC as contractors marks a significant milestone in the development of the facility.

4. **Labour:**
   - **Date:** February 2021
   - **Details:** At peak construction, approximately 200 people will be employed on-site, contributing to local job creation during the construction phase of the wind farm.

5. **Ownership Structure:**
   - **Date:** February 2021
   - **Details:** The Bjornberget wind farm is jointly owned by Enlight Renewable Energy and Prime Capital’s Prime Green Energy Infrastructure Fund, indicating that Peab does not have exclusive ownership of the facility.
