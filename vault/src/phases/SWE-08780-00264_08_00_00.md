### Evolutionary Timeline of the Faurecia Facility in Klevberget, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **May 2023**: Faurecia, a subsidiary of the FORVIA group, is involved in a project to secure renewable energy through a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with Renewable Power Capital (RPC) for the Klevberget onshore wind farm. The facility will consist of 24 turbines with a total production capacity of 417 GWh. The project is currently under construction, with commercial operations expected to start in November 2023. This wind farm will generate more than 40% of FORVIA’s European plant electricity consumption annually.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **May 2023**: The PPA signed between Faurecia and RPC is a 10-year agreement that secures the clean energy output from the Klevberget facility. This deal is part of FORVIA's strategy to maximize renewable energy usage, aiming to cover over 70% of its energy needs in Europe by the end of 2023.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **May 2023**: The Klevberget onshore wind farm is under construction, with an expected operational start date in November 2023. The project is significant as it marks FORVIA's commitment to decarbonizing the automotive segment and achieving net-zero emissions.
