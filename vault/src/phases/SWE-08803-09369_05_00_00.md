### Evolutionary Timeline of the Kärnfull Next Facility in Nyköping, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **11-09-2023**: Kärnfull Next is planning to develop Europe's first Small Modular Reactor (SMR) campus in Nyköping. The facility will utilize advanced nuclear reactors with a power capacity of up to 300 MW(e) per unit. The completion of the facility is scheduled for 2030. The project aims to produce a significant amount of low-carbon energy and is expected to create future-proof jobs and opportunities for co-location with other high-tech industries.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **09-12-2024**: Kärnfull Next signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Samsung C&T during the Korea-Sweden Strategic Industry Summit. This partnership aims to develop a business model for building SMR power plants by 2032, focusing on project financing, design, licensing, technology selection, and environmental impact assessments. The collaboration is part of the Re:Firm South programme, which seeks to establish multiple SMR parks to achieve economies of scale.
