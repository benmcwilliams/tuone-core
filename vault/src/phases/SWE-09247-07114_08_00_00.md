### Evolutionary Timeline of the Renewable Energy Systems Facility in Lycksele, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **2007:** Renewable Energy Systems (RES) began developing the Lycksele project portfolio in Västerbotten County, Sweden. The portfolio consists of three wind energy projects: Fjällberg, Hornmyran, and Vinliden, with a total production capacity of 290 MW. Once operational, the facility is expected to deliver nearly 1 TWh of clean energy annually to the region and the wider European market.

   - **September 2022:** The sale of the Lycksele project portfolio to Prime Capital was announced, with expectations for the projects to come online in 2025. RES will assist Prime Capital in preparing the portfolio for construction and will provide asset management services once operational.
