## SWE-09259-09369

### Evolutionary Timeline of the Kärnfull Next Facility in Valdemarsvik, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **June 2024**: Kärnfull Next announced plans for an SMR (Small Modular Reactor) campus in Valdemarsvik, which is expected to host between four and six small light water reactors. The facility aims to add between 10-15 TWh of electricity generation per year. The project is part of Kärnfull Next's Re:Firm South SMR programme, which focuses on expanding carbon-free and dispatchable energy production in southern Sweden.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **December 2024**: Kärnfull Next signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Samsung C&T during the Korea-Sweden Strategic Industry Summit. This partnership aims to develop a business model for building SMR power plants by 2032, which will supply electricity directly to data centres in Sweden. The collaboration is expected to include project financing, design, licensing, technology selection, and environmental impact assessments.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **June 2024**: Kärnfull Next entered into an exclusive partnership agreement with landowner Latona Group for exploration rights for nuclear power on the site. The ongoing study showed promising preliminary results, leading to public announcements about the SMR campus plans.
- **December 2024**: The partnership with Samsung C&T represents a significant milestone in the Re:Firm South programme, focusing on the construction and operational aspects of the SMR campus.

#### 4. Labour
- **June 2024**: The project in Valdemarsvik is estimated to create around 500 direct and indirect jobs per year for 70 years, contributing significantly to local job creation.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- **June 2024**: Kärnfull Next is collaborating with Latona Group for the land, indicating a partnership but not exclusive ownership. The facility's development involves multiple stakeholders, including Samsung C&T, which suggests a joint venture approach rather than full ownership by Kärnfull Next.

This timeline captures the key developments and strategic partnerships related to the Kärnfull Next facility in Valdemarsvik, Sweden, highlighting its focus on clean energy production and local economic benefits.
