### Evolutionary Timeline of the OX2 Facility in Hornamossen, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** 06/2019
   - **Details:** The facility is a 43-MW wind farm located in Hornamossen, Jonkoping municipality. OX2 is responsible for the engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) of the wind farm, which has been in development since 2006. Siemens Gamesa Renewable Energy will supply 10 turbines of the SWT-DD-130 4.3MW model, and OX2 will manage the technical and commercial operations of the plant upon its completion in late 2020.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** 06/2019
   - **Details:** Green Investment Group Ltd (GIG), owned by Macquarie Group Ltd, has acquired the wind farm from OX2. The facility will operate under a long-term power purchase agreement (PPA) between GIG and Axpo Nordic.
