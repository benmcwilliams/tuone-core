### Evolutionary Timeline of Macquarie's GIG Facility in Hornamossen, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** June 2019
   - **Details:** Macquarie's Green Investment Group (GIG) acquired a 43-MW wind farm located in Hornamossen, Jonkoping municipality. The facility will consist of 10 Siemens Gamesa SWT-DD-130 turbines, each with a capacity of 4.3 MW. The wind farm is expected to be operational by late 2020.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** June 2019
   - **Details:** The acquisition of the wind farm from OX2 marks a significant investment by GIG in the Swedish renewable energy sector. The facility will operate under a long-term power purchase agreement (PPA) with Axpo Nordic.

   - **Details:** GIG announced the acquisition of the wind farm, which has been in development since 2006. OX2 will serve as the engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) contractor and will manage the technical and commercial operations of the plant upon its completion in late 2020.
