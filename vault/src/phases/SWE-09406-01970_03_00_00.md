### Evolutionary Timeline of the Statkraft Facility in Hammarforsen, Sweden

#### 1. Technology and Operations
- **March 2023**: Statkraft has commissioned NCC to improve dam safety at the Hammarforsen hydropower plant, which has an existing production capacity of 80 MW. The refurbishment includes remodelling a spillway to increase discharge capacity and replacing existing segment gates with two smaller floodgates. The project aims to ensure the facility remains a reliable producer of renewable energy and can meet increasing electricity demand.

#### 2. Investment and Development
- **March 2023**: The order value for the refurbishment project is approximately SEK 160 million (around $15.2 million). This investment is part of an extensive restoration effort to enhance the facility's capacity and safety.

#### 3. Project Status and Milestones
- **March 2023**: Statkraft announces the commissioning of NCC for the refurbishment project, which is scheduled for completion in spring 2025. The project includes various construction activities such as building a cofferdam, demolition work, and concrete casting.

#### 4. Labour
- The articles do not specify exact job creation or job loss figures related to the refurbishment project. However, the involvement of NCC, a construction company, suggests potential job creation during the construction phase.

#### 5. Ownership Structure
- Statkraft owns the Hammarforsen facility exclusively, as indicated by the direct commissioning of NCC for the refurbishment work.
