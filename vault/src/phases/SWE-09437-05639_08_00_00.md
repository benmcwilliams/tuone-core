### Evolutionary Timeline of the NTR Facility in Norra Vedbo, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** The Norra Vedbo facility consists of an 86MW onshore wind farm featuring 20 Vestas V150 turbines, each with an installed capacity of 4.3MW. The facility is designed to produce enough clean energy to serve approximately 69,000 households annually. It utilizes innovative rock anchor foundations, which require 70% less concrete compared to standard foundations, leading to a reduction of over 1,600 tonnes of CO2 emissions (27% drop).

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** The project has a total investment of €115 million, supported by co-investment from Reichmuth Infrastructure Fund and NTR REIF II Fund. Additionally, Skandinaviska Enskilda Banken AB (SEB) provided debt financing for the project.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** November 2022
   - **Details:** The Norra Vedbo wind farm achieved commercial operation in November 2022. It is noted as NTR's fourth active wind project in the Nordic region, contributing to a total of 198MW of wind energy managed by NTR.
