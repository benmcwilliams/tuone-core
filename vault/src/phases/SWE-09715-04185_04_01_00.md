The goal is to transition to a renewable and climate-neutral value chain by 2035. The facility aims to produce 5 million cubic meters of renewable fuels and e-fuels by 2035, leveraging fossil-free hydrogen for decarbonization and increased production of renewable fuels such as HVO, renewable gasoline, and sustainable aviation fuels.
