### Evolutionary Timeline of the Solkompaniet Facility in Norrköping, Sweden

1. **Technology and Operations:**
   - **Date:** September 2022
   - **Details:** Solkompaniet, in partnership with Niam, is developing a solar portfolio with a total capacity of 500 MW in southern Sweden. The Östra Eneby solar facility, located outside Norrköping, is one of two 80 MW solar facilities being developed under this partnership. The facility is expected to power approximately 120,000 households and is projected to come online by the end of 2024.

2. **Investment and Development:**
   - **Date:** September 2022
   - **Details:** The total investment for the solar projects is more than €300 million ($297.7 million). This investment is part of a framework agreement between Niam and Solkompaniet to develop solar parks in Sweden.

3. **Project Status and Milestones:**
   - **Date:** September 2022
   - **Details:** The Östra Eneby solar facility is noted as the most progressed project among the two 80 MW facilities being developed. The project is expected to be operational by the end of 2024.
